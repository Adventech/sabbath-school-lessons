title:  Nyeem ntxiv
date:   04/04/2025
---

Nyeem Niam Phauj Elees Cis Vais, “Ib tug Neeg Amelikas Reformer,” nplooj 319–324, nyob rau hauv phau ntawv Kev Ua Rov Ua Rog Ntawm Qhov Phem thiab Qhov Zoo.

“Cov xibhwb thiab tej neeg tau qhia tias tsis muaj leej twg tuaj yeem nkag siab cov sob lus tshwj xeeb hauv phau Daniyees thiab phau Tshwmsim. Tiamsis Yexus hais kom Nws cov thwjtim nyeem cov sob lus uas Daniyees tau sau txog tej yam uas yuav tshwm sim nyob rau lawv tiam neej. Yexus hais tias, 'Tus uas nyeem zaj no, cia li totaub! [Daniyees 9:27] (Mathais 24:15). Lub npe ntawm Phau Tshwmsim qhia rau peb tias Vajtswv xav kom peb to taub phau ntawv no.

“Yog li ntawd, yuav tsum tsis pub muaj ib leej twg qhia lwm tus tias Phau Tshwmsim yog nyob nraim nkoos tsis muaj leejtwg paub uas tib neeg tsis tuaj yeem nkag siab tau. Phau Tshwmsim yog ib qho nraim nkoos uas Vajtswv tau piav qhia tseg. Phau Tshwmsim yuav pab peb nco txog phau Daniyees. Los ntawm ob phau ntawv muab Vajtswv cov tshooj lus qhia thiab cov lus qhia tseem ceeb rau peb.” Niam Phauj Elees Cis Vais, Phau ntawv Kev rov kev rog ntawm qhov phem thiab qhov zoo, nplooj 341, 324.

**Lus nug:**

`Daniyees 2 qhia li cas rau peb kev txiav txim siab yam muaj hwj chim kom tso siab rau Vajtswv thiab Nws txoj kev paub txog yav tom hauv ntej?`

`Txoj hauv kev zoo tshaj plaws twg yuav los tiv thaiv peb tus kheej los ntawm ntau lub tswv yim cuav uas hais txog phau Vajlug Kub tej sob lus tshwj xeeb?`
