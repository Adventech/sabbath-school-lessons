### Nyeem rau lub lim tiam no
Malakaus 8:22–30; 8:31–38; 9:1–13; 9:30–41; 9:42–50.

> <p>Nqe Cim Nco</p>
> “Yexus txawm hu cov neeg thiab nws cov thwjtim los thiab hais rau lawv tias, “Yog leejtwg xav raws kuv qab mus, cia tus ntawd tswj nws tus kheej thiab kwv nws tus khaublig ntoo raws kuv qab” (Malakaus 8:34).

Nrab Xub Thawj Ntawm Phau Malakaus qhia peb tias Tswv Yexus yog leej twg. Nws tej lus qhia uas muaj hwj chim thiab tej txuj ci tseem ceeb pab kom peb to taub tias Nws yog tus Cawm Seej.

Nrab thib 2 ntawm Phau Malakaus, Tswv Yexus nug Nws cov thwjtim tias lawv ntseeg tias Nws yog leej twg. Tom qab Petus teb tag lawm, Tswv Yexus pib piav qhia tias yuav muaj dab tsi tshwm sim rau Nws ua yog tus Cawm Seej.

Nyob rau ntu kawg ntawm Malakaus 8 txog rau nqe kawg ntawm Malakaus 10, Tswv Yexus qhia Nws cov thwjtim txog qhov yuav tshwm sim rau Nws. Nws hais txog tus ntoo khaub lig. Ces Nws txawm qhia Nws cov thwjtim tej lus qhia tshwj xeeb. Cov lus qhia no tseem ceeb heev rau peb niaj hnub no.

Tswv Yexus tej lus qhia tau pab Nws cov thwjtim uas neeg zoo dua qub. Nws tej lus qhia tseem yuav pab peb niaj hnub no thiab. Tej lus qhia ntawd qhia peb tias peb yuav tsum ua li cas thiaj raws tau Nws qab.
