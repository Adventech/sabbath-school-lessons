Niam Phauj Elees Ci Vais, “The Voice in the Wilderness,” pages 97–108, in The Desire of Ages; and “Heralds of the Gospel,” pages 166-176, in The Acts of the Apostles.

Koj puas paub tias thawj tus tim tswv ceeb tsheej hauv Tshwmsim 14:6, 7 hais ntau yam uas Tswv Yexus hais hauv Malakaus 1:15? Thawj tus tim tswv ceeb tsheej coj Txojmoo Zoo uas nyob mus ibtxhis los qhia rau tej neeg uas nyob thoob plaws hauv lub qab ntuj no rau tiam kawg. Nws sob lus muaj peb ntu uas peb pom hauv Tswv Yexus sob lus hais.

Thawj tus tim tswv ceeb tsheej tshaj tawm sob lus qhia txog qhov pib ntawm Vajtswv teg dej num ua Tus Txiav Txim. Nws caw txhua tus tib neeg nyob thoob plaws hauv qab ntuj no los pehawm Vajtswv thiab ua raws li Vajtswv.

**Sib tham txog cov nqe lus nug**:

`Piv Txojmoo Zoo los ntawm Tswv Yexus hauv Malakaus 1:14, 15 nrog Thawj Tug Tim tswv ceeb tsheej sob lus hauv Tshwm Sim 14:6, 7. Cov sob lus no pab tau koj nkag siab zoo txog koj teg dej num raws li yog ib tug Ntseeg So Hnub Xya (Xevees De Eveestiv niaj hnub no li cas?`
