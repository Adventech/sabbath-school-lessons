---
title:  Vajtswv Ua Raws Li Nws Tej Lus Cog Tseg
date:  14/12/2025
---

`Yausuas 21:43–45 qhia dab tsi rau peb txog Vajtswv thiab Daim Av Uas Tau Cog Lus Tseg? Cov nqe Vajlug Kub no qhia dab tsi rau peb txog Vajtswv lub hom phiaj txhawm los cawm peb dim (2 Timautes 2:11–13)?`

Yausuas 21:43–45 tham txog ib qho tseem ceeb tshaj plaws ntawm cov ntsiab lus nyob rau hauv tag nrho phau ntawv: tus Tswv ua raws li Nws tej lus cog tseg. Cov nqe Vajlug Kub no kuj qhia peb, luv luv, txog txhua yam uas tau tshwm sim hauv phau Yausuas txog tam sim no. Yausuas 21:43 hais txog qhov uas muab daim av faib thiab mus nyob. (Peb tuaj yeem nyeem txhua yam txog tej yam no hauv Yausuas 13–21.) Yausuas 21:44 hais txog kev tsov rog uas cov neeg Yixalayees tau tawm tsam. Nqe no kuj hais tias cov Yixalayees tswj tau daim av tag nrho. (Peb tuaj yeem nyeem ntxiv txog lub ntsiab lus ntawd hauv Yausuas 1–12.) Tag nrho cov keeb kwm yav dhau los no nthuav qhia cov Yixalayees tias Vajtswv ua raws li Nws cov lus cog tseg rau lawv. Cov Yixalayees yuav tsum nco ntsoov tsis txhob hais tias lawv kov yeej tej tsov rog los sis daim av los ntawm lawv tus kheej. Lawv muaj yeej vim Vajtswv ua raws li Nws tej lus cog tseg. Vajtswv cog lus tias yuav tawm tsam pub rau lawv thiab muab daim av ntawd rau lawv.

Vajtswv “tau muab tag nrho daim av uas nws tau cog tseg rau lawv” (Yausuas 21:43). “Tus Tswv tso cai rau cov Yixalayees kov yeej txhua tus yeeb ncuab” (Yausuas 21:44). Tus Tswv “tau cog lus rau lawv cov neeg uas nyob ntau xyoo dhau los tias nws yuav ua li ntawd” (Yausuas 21:44). “Tus Tswv ceev txhua yam lus cog tseg uas Nws tau cog tseg rau cov Yixalayees” (Yausuas 21:45).
