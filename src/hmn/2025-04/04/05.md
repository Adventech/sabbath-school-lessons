---
title:  Tus Tswv Yuav Tawm Tsam Pub Rau Koj
date:  22/10/2025
---

`Vajtswv thawj lub hom phiaj rau cov Yixalayees rau kev ua tsov ua rog yog dab tsi? Nyeem Khiav Dim 14:13, 14, 25 rau lo lus teb.`

Thaum cov Yixalayees tau txais kev nyuaj siab, Mauxes hais tias, “'

“Tsis txhob ntshai! Yuav tsum sawv khov kho, nej thiaj yuav pom tias hnub no tus TSWV yuav cawm nej li cas; nej yuav tsis pom cov neeg Iziv no ib zaug ntxiv li lawm. Tus TSWV yuav tawmtsam pub rau nej, yogli ntawd, nej cia li nyob twj ywm xwb” (Khiav Dim 14:13, 14). Phau Vajlug Kub hais tias cov Iziv nkag siab txog tias Vajtswv tau tua tawm tsam txhawm pub rau Nws haiv neeg: “Peb cia li khiav rov qab tsis txhob caum cov Yixalayees lawm! Rau qhov tus TSWV tuaj lawv tog tawm tsam peb lawm” (Khiav Dim 14:25).

Qhov uas tshwm sim ntawm Hiavtxwv Liab nthuav qhia peb txog Vajtswv txoj hau kev uas Vajtswv yuav pab cov Yixalayees. Vajtswv yuav tawm tsam txhawm pub rau lawv.
