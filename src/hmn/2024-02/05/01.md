### Nyeem rau lub lim tiam no
Ntawv Nkauj 119:103, 104; 2 Khauleethaus 4:1–6; Yauhas 14:25, 26; Efexaus 2:8, 9;

> <p>Npe cim nco</p>
> “Kuv ua tib zoo kawm koj tej lus qhia yog li ntawd kuv yuav tsis ua txhaum rau koj” (Phau Ntawv Nkauj 119:11, ERV).

Cov thawj coj PROTESTANT hauv 1500s muaj ib yam dab tsi peb txhua tus xav tau. Lawv muaj laj thawj ua neej nyob. Ib tug kws tshawb fawb nto moo, Philiv Casmes, hais txog peb qhov kev xav tau rau lub neej. Hauv nws phau ntawv, Tus Eebthis , Casmes hais tias qee tus neeg tsis muaj dab tsi tsim nyog tuag rau. Tsis muaj dab tsi hauv lub neej tiag tiag rau lawv. Yog li, lawv tsis tshua muaj nqis nyob rau.

Tab sis cov txiv neej, poj niam, thiab cov me nyuam ntawm Protestant lawv tsim kho ntawm lawv tus kheej txawv. Lawv tsis lees paub tej lus qhia cuav ntawm pawg ntseeg Kastosliv. Cov kev hloov pauv uas lawv tau ua tau coj mus rau qhov pib ntawm pawg ntseeg Protestant. Cov Protestants muaj qhov laj thawj tseem ceeb rau kev ua neej nyob. Lawv txoj kev ntseeg tseem ceeb heev rau lawv. Lub lim tiam no, peb yuav saib seb qhov tseeb hauv phau Vajlugkub hloov lub neej ntawm cov Protestants li cas nyob rau hauv 1500s thiab muab ib tug laj thawj rau lawv nyob. Peb txoj kev nkag siab txog qhov tseeb hauv phau Vajlugkub yuav pab peb npaj txhij rau qhov kev sim siab zaum kawg hauv lub sijhawm kawg.
