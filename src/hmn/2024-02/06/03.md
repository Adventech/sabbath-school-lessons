---
title:  1,260 Hnub
date:   06/05/2024
---

`Tshimsim tshooj 11:3 thiab Tshim 12: 5,6,14,15 nrog rau phau ntawv Daniyees 7:25. Tag nrho cov lus no siv cov lej sib txawv los tham txog tib lub sijhawm hauv keeb kwm. Txhua nqe vajlug kub ua hai txog tib lub sijhawm twg nyob hauv zaj keeb kwm no?`

Vajtswv pub nw ob tug timkhawv muaj peev xwm thiab muaj zog mu qhia qhov tseeb ua nkawv paub thiab nkawv yuav qhia vajtswv zaj mu kom txog 1,260 hnub lo yog (42 hlis) nkawv hnav tsi tsho ua yog muab tsiaj cov plaub saum taub hau lo ua khaub ncaw hnav (Tshwmsim11:3). 1,260 hnub yog ib yam nkau li 42 hlis. Nyob rau lub caij nyoog ntawv vajtswv tso lub nroog Yeluxalees tuav tswj txhuas haiv neeg ua lawv tsis tua paub vajtswv. Txhuas haiv neeg no lawv yuav mus rau hauv lub nroog ua dawb huv thiab tsim kev pua tsuaj tau 42 hlis. (tshwsim 11:2) txuas haiv neeg ntawv yog vajtswv cov yeeb ncuab thiab lawv rhuav tshem vajlug kub los tau 1,260 hnub or yog 42 hlis (42x30=1,260 hnub ntawm haiv neeg Yudais lo yog xyoo. Vajtswv ob tug timkhawv yog phau vajlugkub qub thiab vajlug kub tshiab. Ob tug timkhawv tsawm tawm vajtswv txoj kev chim tawm tsam cov neeg ua rhuav tshem vajtswv txoj lus ua yog txoj tseeb.
