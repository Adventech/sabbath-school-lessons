---
title:  Ob tug timkhawv raug tua tuag
date:   07/05/2024
---

`Nyeem nyob rau tshwmsim 11:7-9. Cov nqe no hai txog dab tsi yuav tshwm sim rau vajtswv ob tug tim khawv, Vajlug kub qub thiab vajlug kub tshiab?`

Xyoo A.D. 538 lub teb chaw Loo tau poob nw lub hwjchim. Tu vajntxwv kav tawb loo nyob rau lub sib hawm ntawv hu ua Jastinian. Jatinian muab hwjchim rau txiv plig tuav tswj pawg ntseeg thiab cov nom tswv. Nyob rau lub 2/10/1798 Napolian thiaj li txib nw tu thawj tub rog mu rau teb chaw Loo. Mu txhom tut xiv plig thib 6 coj rov mu kaw rau teb chaw Fabkis tom qab ntawv txiv plig nw tau tag sim neej. Phau ntawv Daniyees thiab tshwmsim thiaj li qhia hai tia txoj kev tswj hwm thiab tsim txom pawg ntseeg yuav tag mu tom qab 1,260 hnub lo yog xyoo ua tseeb tiag. Keeb kwm thiaj qhia rau peb pom tia ntawv nw yog ib yam tshwm sim muaj tseeb tag los. Peb yuav tsum ntseeg siab thiab peb tuaj yeem cia siab rau phau vajlug kub.

Tom qab 1,798 muaj ib co yeeb ncuab rov tawm tsam phau vajlug kub dua. Tu yeeb ncuab no yog ib lub hwjchim tsiaj qus uas tshwm tuaj ntawm lub qhov av tuaj. Lub hwjchim no tau tawm tsam qhov tseeb hauv phau Vajlugkub thaum lub sib hawm Fabkis kev tuav tswj. Ua yog pib 1,789, Leej twg yog tu muaj hwjchim no? lub hwjchim ntawv yog lub cim ntawm kev qhia hai tsi muaj vajtswv. Txoj kev ntseeg hai tia tsi muaj Vajtswv no pib thaum Fabkis kev tuav tswj los. Nyob rau lub sib hawm Fabkis kav thiab tswjhwm, Cov neeg Fabkis tau muab Vajlugkub hlawv pov tseg yam ntau kawg nkau thiab Dab tau siv cov neeg phem lo mus tua Vajtswv ob tug Timkhawv.
