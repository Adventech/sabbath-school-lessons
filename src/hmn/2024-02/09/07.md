---
title:  Nyeem Ntxiv
date:   31/05/2024
---

“Lub sijhawm dhau los, muaj ntau cov ntseeg tau kaws hnub ib. Lawv xav tias lawv tau khaws hnub Sabbatho li hauv Vajlugkub hais lawm. Muaj ntau ntau cov ntseeg zoo uas nyob rau ntau lub tshawj, tshawj Txiv Plig los ib yam cov uas pehawm rau hnub ib. Cov ntseeg ntawv yeej ntseeg tias hnub ib yog Vajtswv hnub Sabbatho tseeb tiag. Vajtswv lees xais lawv txoj kev ntseeg. Tiamsis lub neej pem suab thaum cov nomtswv yuav tawm kevcai yuam kom tibneeg txhua qhov kaws (hwm) hnub 1 (ວັນອາທິດ), lawv sawvdaws nyob hauv ntiajteb no yuav nkag siab tias hnub Sabbatho yog hnub xya uas tseeb tiag hauv Vajlugkub hais. Lawv yuav tsum tau xaiv nruab nrab ntawm Vajtswv txoj kevcai thiab Hnub ib txoj kevcai, uas yog lub cim ntawm tus tsiaj. Lub sijhawm ua ke ntawv, cov tibneeg uas tseem ua txhaum yuav tawm tsam Vajtswv txoj kevcai thiab khaws hnub yuav muaj lub cim ntawm tus tsiaj.” Ellen G. White, “kev sov rog ntawm kev phem thiab zoo”, plooj ntawv 449.

**Lus nug**:

`Lub neej tau hloov pauv sai heev. Txhua txhua hnub tau coj kev hloov pauv thiab teebmeem. Vim li cas peb thiaj yuav tsum nyob tos ntsoov thiab zov tos Nws rov qab txhua lub sijhawm? Peb yuav paub tua li cas tias peb npaj ntxiv rau hnub Nws rov qab los?`

`Koj puas muaj phoojywg uas lawv ntseeg Vajtswv rau hnub ib thiab tsis tau nkag siab txog hnub Sabbatho yog hnub tseem ceeb? Koj yuav faib Vajtswv txoj lus tseeb rau lawv tau li cas?`
