---
title:  Hnub Sabnpato thiab txoj kevcai
date:   28/05/2024
---

`Nyeem Tshwmsim 14:6,7; Tshwmsim 4:11; Chivkeeb 2:1-3; thiab Khiavdim 20:8-11. Cov qes Vajlugkub no tau qhia rau peb tias Vajtswv tsim lub ntiajteb tau rau hnub. Kev sib txuas ntawm kev qhia, Hnub Sabbatho, thiab Vajtswv txoj kev cai zoo li cas?`

Yexus yog tsim nyog peb pehawm. Peb pehawm Nws vim Nws tsim peb thiab cawm peb. Vajtswv so rau hnub Sabbatho tom qab Nws tsim lub ntiajteb tau rau hnub. Hnub Sabbatho pab peb nkag siab txog Vajtswv txoj kev hlub tshua rau peb ib yam tus uas tsim peb. Hnub Sabbatho qhia rau peb paub peb tus Cawmseej txoj kev hlub. Li ntawv, hnub Sabbatho pab peb nkag siab txog Vajtswv lub homphiaj cawm neeg txhaum.

Tom qab Nws tsim lub ntuj thiab ntiajteb, Vajtswv so rau hnub xya ua kev zoo siab rau txhua yam Nws tau tsim. Vajtswv tau so ua ib tug qauv rau peb. Hnub Sabbatho yog ib hnub luv luv uas peb siv hnub xya los txhawb nqa Tus uas Tsim peb. Peb pehawm Vajtswv rau hnub Sabbatho, peb qheb lub siab tos txais qhov khoomplig txwjxeeb uas Vajtswv pub rau hnub ntawv. Vajtswv tsis tau pub qhov koom hmoo no rau lwm hnub.

Hnub Sabbatho pab peb txog tus uas tau tsim peb. Tus Tswv hlub peb heev Nws tsis xav kom peb hnov qab Nws. Hnub Sabbath yog ib lub cim txwjxeeb. Thaum peb khaws Hnub Sabbatho uas hnub Dawbhuv, peb qhia tias peb ncajncees rau Vajtswv (Exakees 20:12, 20).
