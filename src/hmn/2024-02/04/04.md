---
title:  Ua siab loj sawv khov kho
date:   23/04/2024
---

`Piv txwv li Teshaujlwm 5:28–32; Ephexus 6:10–12; thiab Tshwmsim 3:11. Puas Muaj txoj cai tseem ceeb dabtsi uas nyob hauv cov nqe vajlug kub no?`

Cov neeg Vodeexes(Waldenses) muaj kev sib nraug zoo nrog vajtswv. Lawv mloog nws tejlus sam hwm(lus txib). Lawv ntseeg hais tias phau vajlug kub yog phau ntawv kevcai(ກົດລະບຽບ) uas qhia lawv txoj kev ua neej. Lawv pehawm tswv Yexus,thiab tsis pehawm tus txiv plig. Cov neeg Vodeexes (Waldenses) nyob hauv lawv lub siab tsua ntseeg cov keeb kwm hauv phau vajlug kub tshiab uas hais txog kev ntseeg thiab muaj peev xwm.

Petus thiab tswv Yexus’ nrog rau lwm tus ntseeg, cov neeg Vodeexe(Waldenses) tau hais tias, “ ‘peb yuav tsum mloog vajtswv lus, tsis yog tib neeg!’ ” (Teshaujlwm 5:29, cov neeg Vodeexe(Waldenses) nkag txog Paulus cov lus qhia zoo, “cia tus tswv ua tus txhawb koj lub zog. Nyob ntawm vajtswv lub tshwj chim” (Ephexus 6:10, NIrV). Lawv raws tus tswv Yexus qab’ cov lus qhia: “tuav yam uas koj muaj cia. Tom qab ntawv thiaj tsis muaj leej twg tshem tau lub mom kub tawm ntawm koj mus” (Tshwmsim 3:11, NIrV). Cov neeg Vodeexes(Waldenses) tsis lees yuav thiab mloog cov loos tej lus qhuab qhia. Cov txiv neej thiab cov pojniam uas muaj peev xwm no txoj kev ntseeg txhawb lawv lub zog sawv ruaj khov rau ntawm qhov tseeb raws li hauv phau vajlug kub.

Lawv faib txojlus tseeb uas lawv paub thiab muab sau tseg ua phau ntawv ua vajlug kub qhia rau cov tib neeg thaum lawv muaj sijhawm. Lawv qhia txoj lus tseeb uas tau khaws cia lawm mus txog pua pua xyoo.
