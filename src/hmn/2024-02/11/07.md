---
title:  Nyeem Ntxiv
date:   14/06/2024
---

“Vajtswv tsis tau quab yuam peb kom ua raw li Nws lossis ntseeg Nws. Tiamsis dab ntxwg nyoog pheej kev quab yuam coj los tswj tib neeg nws tso tsis tau nws txoj kev dag ntxia. Dab ntxwg nyoog siv kev ntshais lossis kev qhuab yuam yuam tib neeg kom ua raws li nws thiab pehawm nws. Dab ntxwg nyoog ua tej no licas? Nws ua hooj lwm nrog cov thawj coj ntawm kev ntseeg thiab tej nom tswv los ntawm kev tawm tej kevcai. Tib neeg tej kevcai no tawm tsam Vajtswv li kevcai..”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, page 591, adapted.

Vajtswv muaj cov tib neeg nyob hauv ntiajteb no, cov ncaj ncees rau Nws. Lawv yuav lees yuav Vajtswv txoj lus, thiab tsua yog Vajtswv txoj lus xwb, los ua txoj kev qhuab qhia thiab lub tswv yim. Ua ntej peb yuav lees yuav ib yam lus qhia txawv twg peb yuav tsum noog kom lawv qhia rau peb saib Vajtswv txoj lus txhawb nqa lawv lub tswv yim ntawv licas.”— Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, page 595, adapted.

**Lus nug**:

`Peb yuav pab tau peb thiab lwm tus kom tsis txhob ntshais nyob rau tiam kawg? Peb yuav pab kom lawv cia siab rau Vajtswv tau licas?`

`Kev nkag siab txog yam yuav tshwmsim rau tiam kawg hloov peb lossis ua rau peb lub neej txawv niaj hnub no licas?`
