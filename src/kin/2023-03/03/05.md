---
title:  Kristo ari Hejuru y'Ubushobozi Bwose
date:   12/07/2023
---

Pawulo yari yarahimbawe no sushyirwa hejuru kwa Yesu, ubu wicaranye na Se ku ntebe ye y'ubwami mu ijuru. Amaze gusobanura umwanya Kristo arimo mu isano afitanye na Se (“yicara .. , iburyo bwa Se ahantu ho mu ijuru” Abefeso 1:20), Pawulo agaruka ku isano afitanye n’ “imbaraga” Nieufatanyije na Se gutegeka, Yesu ari “hejuru cyane” y’abandi bose (Abefeso 1:21).

`Gereranya aho Pawulo avuga imbara: ga mbi zimyuka mu Abefeso 1:21, Abefeso 2:2, no mu Abefeso 6:12. Utekereza ko ari iyihe mpamvu itera Pawulo gushishikarira iby’izi mbaraga?`

Ibyakozwe n’'Intumwa 19:11-20, n'inkuru y'abahungu ba Sikewa barindwi yerekana ko mu gihe cya Pawulo Efeso hari ahantu hakorerwaga imirimo y'ubukonikoni. “Ukwiganza gukabije kw'ibyerekanaga imirimo y'ubukonikoni yabonekaga mu bwami bw'Ubugiriki cyari ikimenyetso cy'uko hariya hantu higanje iby'imyuka byagiraga imbaraga ikora kuri buri rwego rwose rw’ubuzima. Intego y’abakonikoni kwari ugusobanukirwa imyuka ibafasha ikomoka ku myuka igirira nabi abantu no kwiga imikorere yayo yihariye n'imbaraga kimwe n'ubutware imyuka ifite. Binyuze muri ubu bumenyi, bashoboraga kubaka uburyo (bifashishije amagambo avugwa cyangwa yanditswe, imitongero, n'ibindi) kugira ngo bashobore gutegeka imyuka gukora ibihuye n'ibyo umuntu runaka yifuza. Bifashishije amagambo asubirwamo bateguye, uburwayi bwabaga bwatewe n'umwuka mubi, bwashoboraga gukira, [cyangwa] umuntu agatsinda amarushanwa yamagare y'intambara”—Clinton E. Arnold, _Power and Magic: The Concept of Power in Ephesians_, p. 18.

Gushishikarira kuvuga amazina y'imana cyangwa ibinyabubasha mu magambo yatinganijwe neza, cyari ikintu cyerekana imibereho y'ibyiyobokamana yo muri Efeso (Soma mu Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 19:13), ndetse n'iy’abantu bamwe bo muri iki gihe. Pawulo yifuza gusobanura neza isano iri hagati ya Kristo n’ “imbaraga”: Yesu wererejwe “asumba cyane ubutware bwose n'ubushobozi bwose, nimbaraga zose n'ubwami byose” (Abefeso 1:21).

Kugira ngo yiringire ko abamuteze amatwi bumvise neza ko nta yindi mbaraga ibonerwa hanze y'Umwami Yesu, yongeraho amarenga ajyanye imigenzereze yo kuvuga amazina yimana mu mitongero: “n’izina Tyose rivugwa” (Abefeso 1:21). Ubwo Pawulo yari akuye amaso ku bunini bw’isanzure akayerekeza ku burebure bw'igihe, yashimangiye iby uruhererekane rutagira imipaka rw’ubwami bwa Yesu. Ubwami bwe butegeka ububasha bwose, “atari muri iki gihe gusa, ahubwo ari no mu gihe kizaza” (Abefeso 1:21).

`Ni ukuhe kwigaragaza kumeze gutya kw'imbaraga z'ikibi kuboneka muri iki gihe, kandi twakwirinda dute gufatirwa muri ko?`
