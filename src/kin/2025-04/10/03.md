---
title:  Igishushanyo n'icyo Gishushanya
date:   01/12/2025
---

Abasobanuzi ba Bibiliya ntabwo bashobora kugena uko bishakiye ibigize ikigereranyo cyo muri Bibiliya, cyangwa uburyo icyo kigereranyo cyihariye gikoreshwa mu Isezerano Rishya ndetse n'ahandi. Bibiliya ubwayo itanga uburyo bwo kugenzura ndetse n'amahame yerekeranye n'imikoreshereze y' ikigereranyo cyo muri Bibiliya. 

Mu buryo nk'ubwo, Isezerano Rishya rigaragaza ugusohora kw'icyashushanywaga n'igishushanyo mu byiciro bitatu bitandukanye: (1) mu mibereyo ya Kristo, (2) mu itorero, (3) ku iherezo ry'ibihe.

Dushobora kubona ibyo bigereranyo ndetse n'ibyo bigereranya muri Bibiliya, kandi bifasha cyane mu kwereka abasomyi uburyo bwo gusobanukirwa Bibiliya ndetse n'ukuri kwigishwa n'Ijambo ry'Imana kwerekerekeye kuri Yesu, ku gakiza ndetse n'ibyiringiro dufite. 

Reba ibigereranyo bikurikira byo mu Isezerano rya Kera: Isirayeli, Kuva mu Misiri, ndetse n'Ubuturo Bwera. Ni mu buhe buryo buri kigereranyo cyasoboye mu byiciro bitatu: binyuze muri Kristo, mu itorero, ndetse no mu gihe giheruka? 

1. **Isirayeli**
`a. Muri Kristo (Matayo 2: 15):`
`b. Mu itorero (Abagalatiya 6: 16):` 
`c. Mu gihe giheruka (Ibyahishuwe 7:4-8, 14 ):`
2. **Kuva mu Misiri** 
`a. Muri Kristo (Matayo 2: 19-21 ):`
`b. Mu itorero (2 Abakorinto 6: 17):` 
`c. Mu gihe giheruka (Ibyahishuwe 18:4):`
3. **Ubuturo Bwera** 
`a. Muri Kristo (Yohana 1:14, Yohana 2:21, Matayo 26:61):`
`b. Mu itorero (1 Abakorinto 3:16, 17; 2 Abakorinto 6:16):`
`c. Mu minsi iheruka (Ibyahishuwe 3: 12, Ibyahishuwe 11: 19, Ibyahishuwe 21 :3, Ibyahishuwe 21 : 22):`
