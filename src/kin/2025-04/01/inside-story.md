### Mbese, Imana Ishobora Kukwiringira?

"Ariko ndababwira ukuri yuko mu gihe cya Eliya hariho abapfakazi benshi mu Bisirayeli, ubwo imvura yamaraga imyaka itatu n'amezi atandatu itagwa, inzara ikoreka igihugu cyose; nyamara muri bo nta n'umwe Eliya yatumweho, keretse umupfakazi w'i Sarefati ho mu gihugu cy'i Sidoni." Luka 4:25, 26

Ayo magambo yavuzwe n'Umwami wacu Yesu ni ingenzi cyane kuri twe turiho mu gihe cy'imperuka kandi twahawe ukuri kudasanzwe. Nubwo hari abapfakazi benshi, abacuruzi, abashakanye n'abatarashaka bo muri Isirayeli bashoboraga kwita kuri Eliya, nta n'umwe Imana yahisemo koherereza umugaragu wayo. Ahubwo, yabatambutseho kugira ngo isohoze umurimo wayo. Imana yadusigiye ukuri abantu benshi batazi kandi badasobanukiwe neza. Yaduhaye uburyo bwo gukwirakwiza uko kuri hirya no hino. Nitudashyigikira umurimo wo gukwirakwiza uko kuri, Imana izadutambukaho maze ikoreshe abantu tutari twiteze kugira ngo isohoze umurimo wayo. Ibyo bitwibutsa indirimbo itsinda ry'abaririmbyi ryaririmbye mu myaka runaka ishize. Umurongo umwe w'iyo ndirimbo waragiraga uti "Imana ishobora kukwiringira."

> <p>ICYO DUHAMAGARIRWA:</p>
> Mu gihe abandi bagaragaje ko ari abahemu, Imana ikomeze kukwiringira. Mu gihe abandi bananiwe gushyigikira umurimo w'Imana binyuze mu kugarura icyacumi no gutanga amaturo y'isezerano (amaturo ashingiye ku isaranganya rikomatanyije), Imana ikomeze kukwiringira. Mu gihe dutanga icya cumi n'amaturo, nimureke tuvuge tuti "Mana, ushobora kunyiringira."
