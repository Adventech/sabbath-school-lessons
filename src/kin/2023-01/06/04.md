---
title:  "Ibyemezo Bibi Loti Yafashe"
date:   07/02/2023
---

Igihe Aburamu yavaga mu gihugu cy’iwabo yitabye irarika ry'Imana, umuhungu wabo Loti na we yahisemo kujyana na we muri urwo rugendo rwe. Itngiriro 13 havuga ko Imana yahaye Aburahamu umugisha ku rwego rw’uko “yari afite ubutunzi bwinshi bw’amatungo.n’ifeza n’izahabu.” (Itangiriro 13:2). Loti na we yari “afite imikumbi n’amashyo n’amahema” (Itang. 13:5). Bombi bahindutse abatunzi cyane kubw’amashyo n’imikumbi byabo byari byinshi cyane ku buryo batashoboraga guturana begeranye. Kugira ngo birinde intonganya hagati y’abashumba babo, Aburamu yahaye Loti amahirwe ngo ahitemo aho ashaka gutura. Birumvikana Loti yagombye kugira amahirwe ngo ahitemo aho ashaka gutura. Birumvikana Loti yagombye gutunganirwa yari afite yagukeshaga imibanire yari afitanye na Aburahamu. Nyamara nta gushima Loti yigeze agaragariza uwari yaramugiriye neza maze mu bwikanyize bwe yifuza icyo yafataga ko ari ubutaka bwiza bwiza.cyane bwari buhari. 

`Soma Itangiriro 13:10-12, Ni izihe ngingo zumvikana zishobora kuba zarateye Loti gufata icyemezo yafashe?`

Uburyo bworoshye bwose Loti yaba yarashyigikiye icyemezo cye cyo kujya mu mujyi, muri wo ntabwo ibintu byamugendekeye neza cyane, kandi igihe Aburamu yumvaga ibyabaye kuri Loti, ntabwo yavuze ati: “Nta kundi, ni bibi cyane, Loti we. Usaruye ibyo wabibye.” Aho kuvuga atyo, yaje kumutabara (Itangiriro 14). Rimwe na rimwe iyo turangamiye ibintu n’ubutunzi buruseho, ntabwo twiga neza amasomo twagombye kwiga. Loti yahise asubira muri Sodomu! Ariko mu mbabazi zayo nyinshi, Imana yohereje intumwa ngo zijye kuburira Loti n’umuryango we, zibamenyeshe ibyo kurimbuka kw’iyo mijyi kwari kugiye kubaho.

`Soma Itangiriro 18:20-33. Ni iki Imana yabwiye Aburahamu cyari impamvu y’uko ije gusura isi? Aburahamu yitwaye ate ku makuru yavugaga ko Imana ifite umugambi wo kurimbura iyo mijyi y’inkozi z’ibibi?`

Bitewe n’uko Aburahamu yari ahangayikishijwe na Loti n’umuryango we yahendahenze Imana kugira ngo ye kurimbura iyo mijyi mu gihe yabonekamo abantu b’abakiranutsi. Yatangiriye ku bantu 50 maze aramanuka agera ku 10. Bihuje n’imico yayo yuje urukundo, ntabwo Imana yigeze ihagarika gutanga imbabazi kugeza igihe Aburahamu yahagarikiye gusaba! Imana ubwayo n’abamarayika babiri nibo barokoye Loti, umugore we, ndetse n’abakobwa be babiri. Ariko umugore we yarebye inyuma ahinduka inkingi y'umunyu. Loti yinjiye i Sodomu ari umukungu ariko ayisohokamo nta kintu afite. Mbega uburyo tugomba kwigengesera ku bijyanye n'ibyemezo dufata, ariko by’umwihariko igihe dutekereza ku nyungu z’igihe, kigufi bihabanye cyane n’ishusho yagutse (Mariko 8:36, 37).
