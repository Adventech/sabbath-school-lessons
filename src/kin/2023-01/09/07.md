---
title:  "Ibindi Byo Kwigwa No Kuzirikanwa"
date:   03/03/2023
---

Mu rugamba rwabereye i Yeriko, Akani ntabwo ari we muntu wenyine wazanye ifeza n’izahabu n’ibintu by’imiringa n’iby’icyuma abizanye mu nkambi ya Isirayeli. Yosuwa yari yababwiye ko bagomba kuzana ifeza n’izahabu n’ibintu by’imiringa n’icyuma bakabizana mu bubiko, bw’inzu y’Imana (Yosuwa 6:19, 24). Ibindi byose byagombaga gutwikwa. Nyamara Akani ni we muntu wenyine wagize ibyo ahisha mu ihema rye. “Muri izo miliyoni z’Abisirayeli harimo umuntu umwe gusa, wahangaye kugomera itegeko ry’Imana muri iyo saha ikomeye y’insinzi wurubanza [kuri Yeriko]. Kwifuza kwa Akani kwabyukijwe n’ubwiza bw’uwo mwambaro w’igiciro wo mu gihugu cy’i Shinari; ndetse n’igihe wari umugejeje aho agomba kwicwa yawise ‘Umwambaro mwiza w’i Shinari [Babuloni]’. Icyaha kimwe cyari cyateje intabwe yo gukora ikindi, maze afata izahabu n’ifeza byerejwe ububiko bw’ Uwiteka. Yibye Imana ku muganura wo mu gihugu cy’i Kanani.” — Ellen G: White *Abakurambere n'Abahanuzi*, pp. 415-416.

Ku rutonde rw’ibimenyetso byo mu minsi y’imperuka Pawulo yatanze, ibimenyetso bibiri bibanza byerekeranye n’amafaranga Wubutunzi. “Umenye neza ko mu minsi y’imperuka hazaza te bikomeye. Abantu bazaba_bikunda, bakunda ifaranga [kwifuza]” Timoteyo 3:1, 2, BII). Kwikunda no gukunda amafaranga ni ibimenyetso simusiga bigaragaza abantu bo mu minsi iheruka isi - turi mu minsi ya nyuma.

***Ibibazo***:

`Soma 1 Timoteyo 6:5-10: “feyakora koko. gukunda Imana iyo gufatanyije no kugira umutima unyuzwe kuvamo inyungu nyinshi, kuko nta cyo twazanye mu isi kandi nta cyo tuzabasha kuyivanamo. Ariko ubwo dufite ibyo kurya n’imyambaro biduhagije, tunyurwe na byo, kuko abifuza kuba abatunzi bagwa mu moshya no mu mutego, no mu irari ryinshi ry’ubupfu ryangiza, rikaroha abantu mu bibahenebereza bikabarimbuza. Kuko gukunda impiya ari : umuzi w’ibibi byose, hariho abantu bamwe ba: zir. ‘arikiye barayoba, bava mu byo kwizera bihandisha imibabaro myinshi.” Mu itsinda ryanyu, nimuganire ku ngero z’abantu “bavuye' mu byizerwa kandi bakihandisha imibabaro myinshi” bitewe no gukunda amafaranga. Ingero ni nyinshi, ese si ko bimeze? Ni mu buhe buryo dushobora kubona urugero rwiza rwo gushaka amafaranga, tuzirikanako dukeneye amafaranga yo kwifashisha, tudakeneye amafaranga yo kutugusha mu mutego nk’uwo Pawulo avaga aha ngaha?`

`Uretse amafanga, ni ibihe bintu bindi dushobora kwifuza?`

`Ni irihe tandukaniro riri hagati yo kwifuza kwiza no kwifuza kubi? Ni part kwifuza gushobora guhinduka kubi?`
