---
title:  "Umurage Mu Bya Mwuka"
date:   09/03/2023
---

Nubwo bitoroshye kumenya uko ubuzima bwari kumera ku isi iyo abantu bataza gucumura, ikintu kimwe dushobora kumenya dutashidikanya ni uko: nta kwirundanyirizaho imitungo, umururumba, ubukene bw’ ibintu byari kubaho, bikaba bimaze kuyogoza iyi si yacu kuva yabaho. Kumva ko ibyo twaruhiye ari ibyacu, nubwo twaba twarakoranye ubunyangamugayo, twabivunikiye, biba bigaragaza imibereho mu isi y’icyaha. Nyamara ku iherezo, hatitawe ku bwinshi. bw’ibyo twakoze cyangwa tutakoze, hari ingingo y’ingenzi dukwiriye guhora tuzirikana.

`Soma amasomo akurikira. Ni iyihe ngingo ayo ‘masomo yose ahuriycho, kandi ni mu buhe buryo iyo ngingo ikwiriye kugira ingaruka ku byo dukora dukoresheje ubutunzi ubwo ari bwo bwose Imana yaduhaye? (Zaburi 24:1; Abaheburayo 3:4; Zaburi 50:10, Itangiriro 14:19;  Abakolosayi 1:15-17).`

Turi ibisonga nabashinzwe gucunga ibyo Imana yaturagije; ni ukuvuga ko, Imana ari yo Nyiri bintu byose, kandi ni yo iduha ubuzima, kubaho. n’imbaraga zo kuba dufite ibyo dutunze byose. Ntabwo bikwiriye ko dukoresha ibyo Imana yaduhaye, ngo tubanze kwita ku miryango yacu, hanyuma ngo abe aribwo twibuka kugarurira Imana ibisigaye.

“Iyo mutanze ibikoreshwa mu murimo w’Imana, muba mwibikiye ubutunzi mu ijuru, Ubutunzi bwose mubitse mu ijuru buba ‘burinzwe ibiza vibihombo, kandi buriyongera ndetse ntibwangirike kugeza mu bugingo buhoraho.” (*Inama Ku Busonga*, p. 309). Hari inyungu nyinshi zo gutanga  tukiriho. Dore zimwe muri zo:

1. Ugize icyo atanga ashobora kubona umusaruro w’impano atanze- Ashobora kubona urusengero rushya rwubakwa, ashobora kubona umwana ajya ku ishuri, ashobora kubona amavuna aterwa inkunga, nibindi.
2. Umurimo cyangwa umuntu ashobora kubona ubufasha none aha igihe hari igikenewe cyihutirwa.
3. Nta mirwano iba hagati y’abagize umuryango cyangwa inshuti nyuma yo gupfa kwawe. :
4. Bitanga urugero rwiza rw’ indangagaciro z’umuryango zo kugirira abandi ubuntu no kubakunda.
5. Bigabanya ingaruka z’ umusoro ku mutungo.
6. Byizeza ko impano.izahambwa uwo yagenewe (nta kwivanga kw’ inkinko cyangwa abavandimwe batishimiwe). 
7. Bigaragaza ko umutima w’utanga wahindutse ukava’ mu kwikubira ukajya mu kuzirikana abandi. 
8. Bidufasha kwibikira ubutunzi mu ijuru.
