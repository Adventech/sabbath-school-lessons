Ellen G. White yanditse ibice bibiri bivuga kuri iyi ngingo y’ingenzi yo kugabanya abana imitungo, yacu. Soma igitabo cyitwa, “*Testimonies for the Church*”, ingingo ivuga,” To the Aged and Weathy Parents, vol.3, pp.1 16-130 n’ingingo ivuga, “Wills and Legacy”, vol.4, pp.476-485. 

Hari ikindi gice dusanga mu gitabo eyitwa “*Inama ku Busonga*”, pp.293-298 kivuga ibyerekeranye no kwitegura urupfu. Ellen G. White yaranditse ati, “Icyo abantu benshi hanga gukora kugeza igihe bari hafi gupfa, iyo baza kuba ari-abakristo, vari kugikora bagifite imbaraga. Bari kwitanga kandi bakegurira Imana umutungo wabo, ikindi iyo baza kuba barakoze nk’ibisonga byayo, bari kunyurwa no gukora inshingano yabo. Iyo baza gukora nk’abikorera, bari gukora ibyo Imana ibasaba, aho kwikuraho ishyira ku bandi.” *Testimonies for the Church*, vol.4, p.480

Ni iki Ellen G. White yashakaga kuvuga ubwo yavugaga "gukora nk'abiorea"? Mu igenamurage, uwateguye igenamurage ashyiraho umuntu asigira umutungo we akurikije ibyanditswe muri iryo genamurage nyuma yo gupfa kwe. Gukora nk'uwikorera, bivuze ko igihe ukiriho, ari wowe wishimira kubona umusaruro no kumenya ko ufashe neza impano Imana yaguhaye.

Ku bakristo, kugaruka kwa Kristo ni "ibyiringiro by'umugisha." Twese wigeze gutekereza uburyo bizaba binejeje kubona Yesu agarutse ari mu bicu byo mu ijuru. Twese dufite amatsiko yo kuzabwirwa ngo," nuko nuko.” Ariko se byamera bite turamutse dupfuye mbere yuko Yesu agaruka? Niba twarakurikije ubushake bwe bwahishuwe, uyu munsi dushobora kwizera kubona umurimo w’Imana ukomeza kujya mbere itewe nuko tuwushyiramo imbaraga, tuzi neza ko igenamigambi ryacu ry’ umurage rizafasha umurimo gukomeza mv igihe tuzaba tutakiriho.

***Ibibazo***:

`Nubwo dushobora kwibikira ubutunzi mu ijuru uyu munsi, ni kuki bidakwiriye kugereranywa no kugerageza kugura inzira kuki ibyo  ituganisha ku gakiza?`

`Igihe tugira ubuntu dutanga ibyo dufite ubu, tuba dukwiriye kubikorana, ubwenge. Ni kangahe twagiye twumvise abantu by’umwihariko abantu bishyiriraho amatariki, basaba abantu gutanga amafaranga kubera ko igikorwa iki n'iki kigiye kuba ku itariki iyi n’iyi, kandi bakavuga ko kubera ko icyo gihe amafaranga yacu nta cyo azaba akimaze, ibyiza ari uko twa. as aeceaice ang’ wuwo mubwiriza uyu munsi? Ni mu bane b ee antares eens gutandukanya ubu bucakura n’inzira zemewe z’ pub dnshobora gukoresha amafaranga yacu mu murimo w’Ima uburyé dushobora na duhereye none?`
