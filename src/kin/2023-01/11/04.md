---
title:  "Ni Igihe Cyo Kubaho Imibereho Yoroheje?"
date:   14/03/2023
---

Ni iki Abakristo b’ Abadiventisiti bp’ Umunsi wa Karindwi bakwiriye gukora igihe bahanganye n’ibihe bikomeye? Ese dukwiriye gucika intege? Oya rwose, ahubwo dukwiriye gukora ibihabanye ni’ ibyo. Kuko tuzi yuko iherezo ry’isi ryegereje kandi Kristo akaba ari hafi kugaruka, dukwiriye gukoresha umutungo wacu mu kubwira abandi Inkuru Nziza y’Ubutumwa Bwiza kandi tukababwira n’icyo Imana yateguriye abayikunda. Dusobanukiwe ko umunsi umwe uri hafi kuza, ibintu byose byo ku isi bikazakongorwa n’umuriro.

`Soma 2 Petero 3:3-12. Niiki Petero atubwira muri ayo magambo?`

Ijambo ry’Imana ritubwira ko Imana itazohereza imodoka’zo kuza gutwara ubutunzi bwacu mu ijuru, Ubutunzi bwacu bwose’ buzakongorwa n’umuriro mu itanura ry’umuriro uheruka igihe ingaruka zose z‘icyaha Wikibi zizakongorwa n’umuriro hagasigara inkovu zo mu biganza bya ‘Yesu zonyine. None se ni iki twakagombye gukoresha umutungo wacu? “Ubu ni igihe bene Data bakwiriye kugabanya ku ‘butunzi' bwabo aho kubwongera. Turi bugufi kujya mu gihugu kirushijeho kuba cyiza, ndetse ni igihugu cyo mu ijuru. Nuko rero, nimureke twe kuba abaturage bazaguma kuri iyi si, ahubwo turundarunde bikeya cyane mu byo tubona ko bidufitiye akamaro.” *Inama ku Busonga*, p.56, Nubwo hashize imyaka irenga ijana Ellen G. White yanditse aya magambo, amahame dusanga muri: yo aracyafite agaciro: igihe gihora ari gito, kuko turama igihe gito. Mbese kurama imyaka 60, cyangwa 80, cyangwa 100 byaba bimaze iki niba tutabikiwe ubugingo buhoraho? Ubuzima bwawe bushobora kurangira mbere yuko urangiza gusoma icyigisho cy’iki cyumweru, maze ikindi kintu gikurikiyeho wabasha kumenya kikaba ari ukugaruka kwa Yesu. (Ubuzima ni bugufi cyane kurusha ibintu byose, mbese si ko biri?)

Nk’Abakristo b’Abadiventisite b’Umunsi wa Karindwi, buri gihe tugomba kubaho twiteguye kuzahabwa ubugingo buhoraho. Yego, dukeneye gukora cyane kugira ngo tubone ibidutunga twebwe n’imiryango yacu; kandi iyo tugize umugisha wo kubona ubutunzi, kuwishimira ntabwo ari bibi, igihe tutabaye abanyamururumba kandi tukibuka gufasha abakene. Buri gihe tugomba guhora twibuka ko ibyo turundanya byose ari iby’igihe gito, karidi ko turamutse tutabaye maso, bishobora kubangamira ibya mwuka.

`Uramutse uzi ko Yesu azagaruka mu myaka 10 cyangwa 5 cyangwa 3 iri imbere, ni mu buhe buryo wahindura imibereho yawe?`
