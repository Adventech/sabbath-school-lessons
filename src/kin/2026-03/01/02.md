---
title:  Pawulo, Uwahamagariwe Kuba Intumwa ya Yesu nk'Uko Imana Yabishatse
date:   28/06/2026
---

Pawulo atangira urwandiko yandikiye Abanyekorinto yigaragaza ubwe nk'intumwa ya Yesu, yahamagawe "nk'uko Imana yashatse" (1 Abakorinto 1:1; gereranya n'urwa 2 Abakorinto 1:1). Icyizere cye cyerekeranye n'uwo yari we mw'isano yari afitanye na Yesu cyari gikomeye ku buryo, usibye ahantu hake, aya ariyo magambo yatangizaga inzandiko ze zose.

`Soma urwa 1 Abakorinto 1:1 n' Abaroma 1:1. Ni ibihe bintu bibiri byerekeranye n'umurimo wa Pawulo bishimangirwa mur'iyi mirongo? (soma no mu Abagalatiya 1:1).`

Pawulo avuga ko umuhamagaro we no kuba intumwa kwe bisohoza ubushake bw'Imana. Yizera adashidikanya ko umuhamagaro we utavuye ku bantu, ko ahubwo uva ku Mana (Abagalatiya 1:1). Pawulo yahamagawe n'Imana akiri mu nda ya nyina nka Yeremiya (Yeremiya 1:5) nk'igikorwa Imana yamukoreye ku buntu (Abagalatiya 1:15), kandi cyamubereyeho kugira ngo yamamaze inkuru nziza ya Kristo mu Banyamahanga.

Mu rwandiko rwa 1 Abakorinto 15:8, Pawulo ubwe yishyira mu bo Kristo yabonekeye nyuma y'uko azuka (1 Abakorinto 15:5-7). Mu mirongo mike ikurikiraho, avuga ko uguhamagarwa kwe ngo abe intumwa kwaturutse mur'uko guhura na Yesu kwe (1 Abakorinto 15:9-11). Izina ry'icyubahiro "intumwa ya Yesu" rikubiyemo ibintu byinshi. Mbere na mbere. ryumvikanisha igitekerezo cy'umuntu woherejwe na Yesu. Nyamara kandi, Pawulo akoresha iyo mvugo ashaka kwigaragaza ubwe nk'umugaragu wa Kristo (Abaroma 1:1, Tito 1:1, Abagalatiya 1:10), akaba n'umubwiriza n'umwigisha (1 Timoteyo 2:7, 2 Timoteyo 1:11). Yaba abwiriza cyangwa yigisha, Kristo ni we buri gihe ashyira imbere. Muri make, Pawulo ni intumwa ya Yesu.

Yesu ntiyari izingiro ry'umurimo wo kuba intumwa kwa Pawulo wibandagaho gusa: yari n'izingiro ry'imibereho ya Pawulo. Ibyo Pawulo yatekerezaga hamwe n'ibyiyumvo bye byabaga byuzuyemo Yesu. Igihamya cy'uko kuri ni uko yerekeza kuri Yesu inshuro nyinshi mu gice gitangira urwandiko rwa mbere yandikiye Abakorinto hamwe n'igice cyo gushimira (inshuro icyenda mu mirongo icyenda). Pawulo yakundaga Yesu cyane ku buryo atashoboraga kureka gutekereza no kuvuga ibimwerekeyeho. Yifuzaga gusangira Yesu n'abo yari ashinzwe kuyobora, kugira ngo na bo bagire imibereho ishingiye kuri Kristo. Mu gihe yahamagarirwaga kuba intumwa, na bo bari barahamagariwe kuba abigishwa b'indahemuka ba Yesu mu nshingano iyo ari yo yose Umwami yabahamagariye.

`Pawulo yari yarahamagariwe kuba intumwa. Ni uwuhe muhamagaro wawe, kandi ubwirwa ni iki ko ariwo muhamagaro wawe? Niba utekereza ko nta muhamagaro ufite. kuki utasanga Imana ubu maze ukayisaba kukwereka umurimo yaguhitiyemo gukora?`
