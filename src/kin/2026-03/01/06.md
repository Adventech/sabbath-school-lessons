---
title:  Inzandiko Pawulo Yandikiye Abakorinto
date:   02/07/2026
---

`Soma urwa 1 Abakorinto 1:11-13; 1 Abakorinto 4:14; 1 Abakorinto 5:11; 1 Abakorinto 7:1; n'urwa 1 Abakorinto 14:37, 40. Soma kandi urwa 2 Abakorinto 1:12, 2 Abakorinto 2:9; 2 Abakorinto 11:3, n'urwa 2 Abakorinto 13:10. Ni mu buhe buryo iyi mirongo yose idufasha gusobanukirwa impamvu Pawulo yandikiye izi nzandiko Abakorinto?`

Pawulo yari muri Efeso ubwo yandikaga urwandiko rwa 1 rw'Abakorinto (1 Abakorinto 16:5-9). Abagize umuryango wa Kilowe wagiye kumureba umushyira raporo imubwira yuko ibintu bitagendaga neza i Korinto (1 Abakorinto 1:11). Mu rwa 1 Abakorinto 1-6, Pawulo yari ahugiye mu gukemura ibyo bibazo yabwiwe na Kilowe. Ibibazo byari bikubiyemo kwirema ibice, kwiyandarika mu by'imibonano mpuzabitsina, gucirana imanza, n'ubusambanyi bwo kwicuruza. Na none kandi, Pawulo yabonye urwandiko rwavugaga ku bibazo byihariye (1 Abakorinto 7:1). Igisubizo yatanze kur'ibyo bibazo gihera mu gice cya 7 kigakomeza kuvugwaho n'ahakurikiyeho. Ibyo bibazo byajyanaga no gushyingiranwa, gutandukana kw'abashakanye, kudashaka, ibyokurya byaterekerejwe ibigirwamana, imyitwarire mu materaniro yo kuramya, gukoresha impano z'umwuka, no kudasobanukirwa iby'umuzuko. Itorero ry'i Korinto ryari ryuzuyemo ibibazo kandi ritarakura. Ahari itorero ryawe ry'ibanze rifite ibibazo byinshi. Nyamara mu itorero ry'i Korinto byari umwaku.

Urwandiko rwa mbere Pawulo yandikiye Abakorinto ruratureba natwe mur'iki gihe. Nubundi se, twebwe ntidufite ibibazo nk'ibyo ku rwego runaka duhanganye na byo mu matorero menshi yacu mur'iyi minsi? Uru rwandiko rufite byinshi rutubwira. Ni "rumwe mu nzandiko zihugura kandi zifite imbaraga kuruta izindi mu nzandiko ze." - Ellen G. White, _Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa_, p. 185 (2010).

Pawulo ashobora kuba yarandikiye Abakorinto inzandiko eshatu cyangwa enye (gereranya n'ibivugwa mu rwa 2 Abakorinto 10:9). Yanditse urwandiko rubanza mbere y'urwandiko rwa 1 Abakorinto (1 Abakorinto 5:9), ariko rwarazimiye. Mbere y'uko yandika urwandiko rwa kabiri yandikiye Abakorinto, yabanje kwandika urwandiko intiti zikunze kwita "urwandiko rukomeye." (2 Abakorinto 2:3, 4, 9; 2 Abakorinto 7:8) ariko na rwo rwaje kuzimira. Hari bamwe batekereza ko yerekezaga ku rwandiko rwa 1 Abakorinto, cyangwa ko urwo rwandiko igice cyarwo gisigaye cyakomerejwe mu rwandiko rwa 2 Abakorinto.

Kuva mu rwandiko rwa 2 Abakorinto, tubona ko abari bagize itorero ry'i Korinto bagizweho ingaruka n'umuco w'aho bari batuye. Bahaga agaciro ibintu nk'amarushanwa, ubutegetsi, n'ubutunzi, byose bikaba ari ibintu bishobora no kuba ikibazo mu itorero ryacu muri iki gihe. Ibinyuranye n'ibyo, Pawulo yashatse kurema umuco ushingiye kuri Kristo, aribwo buryo bwo kubona isi binyuze mu ndorerwamo y'ubutumwa bwiza. Mbega ukuntu ari iby'ingenzi ko natwe tubona isi turimo muri iki gihe dukurikije ubutumwa bwiza.

`Ongera usome urwa 2 Abakorinto 2:4. Aya magambo atubwira iki cyerekeranye n'uburyo Pawulo yitaga kuri aba bantu? Ufite urukundo rungana iki mu mutima wawe ufitiye abandi?`
