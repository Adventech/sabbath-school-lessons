---
title:  Kuva muri Atene Ajya i Korinto
date:   29/06/2026
---

`Soma Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 17:16-34. Pawulo yari hehe mbere yo kujya i Korinto, kandi yahakoraga iki?`

Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 17:16-34 hagaragaza Pawulo abwiriza Abanyetenayi mbere y'uko ajya i Korinto. Mu bigaragara, nti yari yarateganyije gusura Atene mur'uwo mwanya, ariko yagiyeyo afashijwe na bamwe mu ncuti kubera ko yari yarwanyijwe i Beroya (Ibyakozwe 17:13-15).

Abari baherekeje Pawulo ajya muri Atenayi (Atene) bagarutse i Beroya yabategetse kubwira Timoteyo na Sila kumusangayo vuba cyane (Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 17:15). Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 17:16- 34 hagaragaza icyo Pawulo yarimo gukora mu gihe yari abategereje. Yavugaga ubutumwa bwa Yesu mu isinagogi, mu isoko, no muri Ayeropago. Ntiyashoboraga rwose guhagarika kuvuga Yesu no kubura gukoresha akanya abonye kose ngo abikore.

`Soma Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 18:1-11. Ni iki Pawulo yakoze ageze i Korinto no mu gihe cyose yamaze mur'uwo mugi?`

Pawulo yagiye i Korinto mu gihe cy'urugendo rwe rwa kabiri rw'ivugabutumwa. Luka atubwira ko Pawulo yahamaze igihe cy'umwaka umwe n'igice.

Nk'uko yabimenyereye, Pawulo yatangiye umurimo w'ubutumwa bwe ajya mu isinagogi (Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 18:4-6). Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 17:1, 2 havuga ko ako kari akamenyero ke. Yakurikizaga ingamba yitwa "ubwa - mbere - Abayuda" (Abaroma 1:16; Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 13:46), nk'uko Yesu yari yarabitegetse intumwa Ze (soma Ibyakozwe 1:8).

Ubwo Sila na Timoteyo basangaga Pawulo i Korinto, yarimo "ahatwa n'ijambo ry'Imana. ahamiriza Abayuda yuko Yesu ari we Kristo" (Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 18:5). Mu gihe yari i Korinto, yari ahuze "yigisha ijambo ry'Imana muri bo" (Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 18:11). Ni mur'uyu mujyo kandi, yavuze aya magambo akomeye ko yari "yaragambiriye kutagira ikindi amenyesha" Abakorinto "keretse Yesu Kristo, ni We Yesu Kristo wabambwe" (1 Abakorinto 2:2).

`Ni irthe somo dushobora kuvana ku murimo w'ivugabutumwa Pawulo yakoreye muri Atene n'i Korinto? Ni iki kibangamira umurimo w'ivugabutumwa mu mijyi yacu no mu miryango migari muri iki gihe? Ni mu buhe buryo dushobora kwamamaza ubutumwa bwiza ahantu nk'aho baruhije ariko tukagera ku ntego? Ni izihe ngamba twakwigira mu buryo Pawulo yakoraga umurimo we?`
