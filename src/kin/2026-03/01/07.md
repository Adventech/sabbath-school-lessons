---
title:  Ibindi byo Kwigwa no Kuzirikanwa
date:   03/07/2026
---

Soma igitabo cya Ellen G. White cyitwa _Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa_, igice cya 24 cyitwa "Korinto," p. 152.

"Igihe Pawulo yabwirizaga ubutumwa bwiza i Korinto, Intumwa yakoresheje uburyo butandukanye n'ubwo yakoresheje ari Atene. ... Yiyemeje kutababwira amagambo yo kubemeza no kujya impaka na bo ndetse no 'kutagira ikindi amenyesha' Abanyakorinto, 'keretse Yesu Kristo, ari we Yesu Kristo wabambwe.' " - Ellen G. White, _Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa_, p. 152 (2010).

"Pawulo yari afite urugero agomba kugeraho," ariko "yashidikanyaga ku bwenge bwo kubaka urusengero akoresheje ibikoresho yahasanze. Yabonaga ko i Korinto ari ahantu ho gukemangwaho cyane huzuye ibibazo, maze yiyemeza kuhava ....

"Mu gihe yatekerezaga ibyo kuva mur'uwo mugi akajya gukomereza umurimo ahandi hantu hashobora gutanga umusaruro, ... Umwami Imana yaramubonekeye mu iyerekwa rya nijoro, aramubwira ati, 'Ntutinye, ahubwo uvuge, ... kuko mfite abantu benshi mur'uyu mugi.' Pawulo yasobanukiwe ko iri ari itegeko rimusaba kuguma i Korinto, kandi ko icyo ari icyemezo cy'uko Uwiteka azongera umusaruro w'imbuto zabibwe ... haje gutangizwa Itorero rinini ryari munsi y'ibendera rya Yesu Kristo." - Ellen G. White, _Sketches from the Life of Paul_, pp. 106, 107.

"Ibyanditswe bivuga ko Pawulo yamaze umwaka n'amezi atandatu akora umurimo we i Korinto. Ariko kandi, umuhati we ntiwagarukiye mur'uwo mugi gusa .... Yashyize icyicaro gikuru cy'umurimo we i Korinto. ... Bityo, haza kuvuka amatorero menshi ... Kuba Pawulo atari ari mu matorero yari ashinzwe kwitaho, byaterwaga rimwe na rimwe n'uko yabisimbuzaga kuboherereza ubutumwa bw'itumanaho rinyuze mu nzandiko zifite uburemere kandi zifite imbaraga, zakirwaga akenshi nk'ijambo ry'Imana. ... Izo nzandiko zasomerwaga mu nsengero zose. Ellen G. White, _Sketches from the Life of Paul_, p. 109.

**IBIBAZO:**

`Pawulo yari azi neza adashidikanya ko ari intumwa ya Yesu, kandi ko uyu muhamagaro we wavaga ku Mana. Kuki ari ingenzi ko tumenya abo turi bo n'umuhamagaro wacu uwo ariwo?`

`Mu gihe runaka, Pawulo yumvise asa nk'uwareka umurimo we wo kubwiriza ubutumwa i Korinto ndetse akava muri uwo mugi. Ni iki cyamuteye guhindura icyo gitekerezo yagize? Ni buryo ki ibi natwe bibasha kudufasha igihe dusa nk'abumva twareka umurimo wo kubwiriza ubutumwa? Mbese ntihariho igihe natwe ibi bitugeraho?`

`Abizera b'itorero rya Korinto bumvaga bakururwa bikomeye n'imbaraga y'umuco wari ubakikije. Ibi natwe niko bitumereye mur'iki gihe. Ni buryo ki twaba mu isi (Yohana 17:11, 15) kandi ntidukururwe n' "ikiri mu isi cyose - ari irari ry'umubiri, ari n'irari ry'amaso, cyangwa kwibona ku by'ubugingo" (1 Yohana 2:16)? Ni ibihe bintu bindi bikurura itorero ryacu mu buryo bubi bitewe n'umuco udukikije?`
