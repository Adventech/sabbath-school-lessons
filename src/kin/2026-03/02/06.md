---
title:  Kristo, Imbaraga z'Imana n'Ubwenge Bwayo
date:   09/07/2026
---

Mu 1 Abakorinto 1:19, 20, 30, ndetse n'uwa 31, Pawulo avuga ibijyanye n'uko ubwenge bw'Imana butandukanye n'ubw'abantu mu buryo butangaje, bityo bukaba budashobora gukorana. Nimwite k'uko Pawulo atanga ubwenge nko kuburwanya, ahubwo ubwenge yanga ni bwa bundi bugerageza guhangana n'Imana. Ubwenge bwa muntu ntibushobora gukura umuntu mu bubata bw'icyaha. Kristo wenyine, Ubwenge bw'Imana, ni We ushobora gukora uyu murimo. Reba imbonerahamwe iri hepfo.

|Ariko kuri twebwe abakizwa| [ijambo ry'umusaraba] ni imbaraga z'Imana | 1 Abakorinto 1:18 |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Ariko ku bahamagawe | Kristo [ni we] mbaraga z'Imana | 1 Abakorinto 1:24 |

Mu 1 Abakorinto 1:18 no mu 1 Abakorinto 1:24 hombi hagaragaza ko Kristo ari imbaraga z'Imana, mu cyerekezo cy'uko afite imbaraga zo gukiza abantu akabakura mu byaha byabo. Mu by'ukuri, "Imana yishimiye gukirisha abayizera ubupfu bw'ibibwirizwa" (1 Abakorinto 1:21). Iri tsinda ry'amagambo ngo "twebwe abakizwa" (1 Abakorinto 1:18), "abayizera" (1 Abakorinto 1:21), n' "abahamagawe" (1 Abakorinto 1:24) yose arengurira ku itsinda rimwe, bakaba ari ba bantu bafite imibereho y'agakiza ku bwo kwizera. "Ubutumwa bwiza ... ni imbaraga y'Imana ihesha uwizera wese gukizwa (Abaroma 1:16).

Kristo si imbaraga gusa ahubwo ni n'ubwenge bw'Imana. Ibi bisobanuye ko binyuze muri we, Imana yahanganye n'ikibazo cy'icyaha kandi iragikemura, ikibazo imbaraga za muntu zitari zishoboye gukemura. Ubwenge bw'iyi si ntibushobora gutuma abantu bamenya Imana (1 Abakorinto 1:21). Mu bundi buryo, ni ukuvuga ko binyuze muri Kristo tuba abanyabwenge ku birebana n'agakiza (2 Timoteyo 3:15).

`Soma 1 Abakorinto 1:24-29. Ita kuri aya magambo "ubupfu," "intege nke," "imbaraga," n' "ubwenge." Ni iki ari gushaka kuvuga?`

Mu gusoma 1 Abakorinto 1:24-29, umuntu akwiriye no kwita ku magambo "ubupfu" (cyangwa "ubupfapfa") ndetse n' "intege nke." Ingingo irimo ni uko ubwenge bw'umuntu bushobora kumva ko ubutumwa bw'umusaraba ari ubupfu ndetse n'intege nke. Nubwo bimeze bityo, "Ubupfu bw'Imana burusha abantu ubwenge, kandi intege nke z'Imana zirusha abantu imbaraga" (1 Abakorinto 1:25). Ibi ntibisobanuye ko Imana ari inyantege nke cyangwa ngo ibe umunyabwenge buke, ahubwo ni imvugo yerekana ko imbaraga z'Imana n'ubwenge bwayo biruta cyane ikintu cyose gikomoka ku muntu.

`Nimutinde kuri aya magambo ngo "ab'ubwenge bw'abantu bahamagawe atari benshi: n'imfura zahamagawe atari nyinshi" (1 Abakorinto 1:26). Uyu murongo udufitiye butumwa ki?`
