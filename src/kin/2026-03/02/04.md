---
title:  Imbaraga ku Bakizwa
date:   07/07/2026
---

Ubutumwa bwo mu 1 Abakorinto 1:18 burasobanutse neza ku buryo ntawe utakumva icyo buvuga. Igisobanuro cy'Umusaraba gishingira ku buryo umuntu awubona. Ni ubupfu ku bashaka kwigomeka ku Mana, ariko ni imbaraga ku bifuza agakiza kayo.

`Soma mu Abakolosayi 1:20 na 1 Petero 2:24. Ni iki Yesu yaturonkeye ku musaraba?`

Nk'uko tumaze kubibona, mu kubwiriza ubutumwa bwiza, umubwiriza agomba kwirinda gukoresha "amágambo y'ubwenge bw'abantu, kugira ngo urupfu rwa Kristo ku Musaraba rutaba impfabusa" (1 Abakorinto 1:17) BII. Mu mucyo w'ibivugwa mu 1 Abakorinto 1:17, biroroshye kumva impamvu ikinyuranyo cy'ubupfu ari imbaraga y'Imana, atari ubwenge bw'abantu (1 Abakorinto 1:18). Umusaraba, ikinyuranyo cy'ubwenge bw'umuntu, uhishura uburyo ubwenge bw'umuntu ari ubupfu.

Umurongo wo mu 1 Abakorinto 1:18 mu rurimi rw'Ikigiriki ushobora gusomwa muri ubu buryo: "Ku barimbuka", bishatse kuvuga ko babona ingaruka z'ibikorwa byabo. Uwo murongo ushobora kongera gusomwa muri ubu buryo: "Ku barimbaka, ubutumwa bw'umusaraba ni ubupfu kubirimbura ubwabo." Inshinga y'Ikigiriki _apollymi_ ("kurimbuka") ishobora no gusobanura "kurimbura" (Yohana 10:10). Muby'ukuri, _apollymi_ isobanurwa "kurimbura" ni ivugwa mu 1 Abakorinto 1:19.

Ni iki ubona ahangaha? Pawulo yatanze urufatiro rwo muri Bibiliya muri iyo mvugo yo mu murongo wa 18, yerekeye abantu barimbuka, yifashishijwe mu murongo wa 19, amagambo y'Imana yo muri Yesaya 29:14. Mu murongo wa 19, Imana niyo nyiri gikorwa cyo kurimbura, bisa n'ibivuguruza kwirimbura k'umuntu ubwe byari byavuzwe mbere. Nyamara, nta kuvuguruzanya guhari. Igitekerezo n'icy'uko, Imana izarimbura uwirimbuye na mbere hose.

Ibihabanye n'ibyo byo kurimbuka, interuro "kuri twe abakijijwe" (1 Abakorinto 1:18), igaragaza ko agakiza kava ku Mana honyine. Pawulo avuga ko twakijijwe; ntabwo twikiza ubwacu. Muby'ukuri, ngicyo icyo tudashobora kwikorera ubwacu. Agakiza kacu gafite aho gaturuka. Mu gihe kurimbuka ari twe tubyizanira. Agakiza turagahabwa, ni impano y'ubuntu ku banyabyaha. Nk'uko bisobanurwa mu 1 Abakorinto 1:21, ni Imana ikiza abizeye. Ubupfu muri ubwo buryo, ni igikorwa cyo kwanga icyo Imana yahereye ubuntu inyokomuntu binyuze mu musaraba wa Kristo (1 Abakorinto 1:30), bityo umuntu akizanira kurimbuka.

`"Kuko ibihembo by'ibyaha ari urupfu, ariko impano y'Imana ni ubugingo buhoraho muri Yesu Kristo Umwami wacu" (Abaroma 6:23). Ni mu buhe buryo uyu murongo wongera kuvuga icyo Pawulo yavugaga mu 1 Abakorinto 1:18, 19?`
