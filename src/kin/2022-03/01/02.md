title:  'UMUYOBOZI MU RUGENDO: UMWUNGERI'
date:   26/06/2022

Uwiteka ni we mwungeri wanjye ; sinzakena " ( Zaburi 23 : 1 ) . 

Abana bamwe basabwe gushushanya Imana . Nta n'umwe usigaye , buri wese muri abo bana yashushanyije igishushanyo gifite umutima . Igihe babazwaga impamvu icyo gishushanyo bagihaye umutima , basubirije icyarimwe ko Imana ari urukundo . Igisubizo cyari cyo nta kindi warenzaho . 

Biroroshye kugira igitekerezo cyiza cyerekeye Imana n'imigambi yayo igihe ibintu byose bigenda neza . Ariko uko tugenda dukura mu myaka , ubuzima bugakomera kandi bugasa nk'aho bugoranye , uburyo tubonamo Imana burahinduka . Imana yo ntihinduka ( Abaheburayo 13 : 8 , Yakobo 1:17 ) , ahubwo ni twe duhinduka . 

Kubera imibereho y'ubushumba y'abantu bo mu bihe bya kera , muri Zaburi ya 23 , umunyazaburi yakoresheje ishusho y'umwungeri asobanura uburyo Imana itwitaho . Ikimenyetso cy'umwungeri cyakoreshejwe kigaragaza Imana mu Isezerano Rishya n'Isezerano rya Kera . Ni ishusho nziza kandi idahinduka . Mbere y'uko turebera hamwe ibyanditswe muri Zaburi 23 , mureke tubanze turebe uburyo abanditsi ba Bibiliya batandukanye bumva umurimo n'imico . by'Umwungeri muri Bibiliya yose .
