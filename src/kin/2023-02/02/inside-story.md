####  700 Abahoze bigometse barabatijwe
 
_Byanditswe na Andrew McChesney_
 
Abanyafilipine batewe ubwoba n’inyeshyamba ku kirwa cya Mindoro imyaka 52. Isambu n'amafaranga byahawe inyeshyamba mu rwego rwo gushaka amahoro, ariko nta kintu na kimwe cyakoraga. Inzira isa naho itagira iherezo y'ibitero n'ibitero byahitanye abantu 40.000.
 
Muri 2017, Radiyo y'isi y'Abadiventisti (AWR) yatangiye gutangaza i Mindoro mu rwego rwo gutangiza itorero ry'isi ry'Abadiventisti b'umunsi wa Karindwi uzwi ku izina ry'ivugabutumwa rya TMI. TMI isobanura Uruhare rwabanyamuryango bose, gahunda ishishikariza buri torero kuzana umuntu kuri Yesu. AWR yakodesheje umwanya kuri radiyo zaho, kandi abayoboke b'itorero baho bitabira gutanga Bibiliya no gutumira abaturanyi mu nama z'ivugabutumwa. Abantu bagera ku 1400 barabatijwe mu nama, kandi ibiganiro birakomeza.
 
Muri 2019, inyeshyamba zihishe mu misozi itoshye, y'icyatsi ya Mindoro itangira kumva AWR. Igihe COVID-19 yazengurukaga isi mu 2020, abatari bake muri bo bahisemo kwiyegurira Yesu.
 
Umuyobozi w'inyeshyamba Ka Martin ntiyashoboraga kumva ibibaye, atangira kumva AWR.
 
Perezida wa AWR, Duane McKey, yagize ati: "Yari yihishe mu ishyamba, areba kandi agerageza kumenya impamvu abarwanyi be bamusize." “Yatangiye kumva radiyo.”
 
Martin yagize uruhare mu rupfu rw’abantu benshi, barimo abasirikare 21 yateguye igihe baryamye barasa umwe mu mbunda zabo bwite. Ariko igihe yumvaga AWR, yahisemo kandi guha Yesu umutima we.
 
Igice cyamennye amaraso yamateka ya Filipine cyegereje igihe abagera ku 700 bahoze bigometse, barimo Martin numugore we, batangaga intwaro zabo hanyuma bakabatizwa mu nama y’ivugabutumwa iyobowe na AWR. Muri rusange, abantu barenga 60.000 barabatijwe mu nama “Isi Yanyuma Yanyuma” muri Filipine hashize umwaka.
 
McKey yagize ati: "Ibyo amasasu adashobora gukora, Imana yarabikoze."
 
Guverinoma ya Filipine yahaye imbabazi abahoze ari inyeshyamba. AWR ikorana na guverinoma n’umuryango utegamiye kuri Leta, umunyamuryango wa ASI Farm Stew, kugira ngo bafashe abahoze ari inyeshyamba kwibeshaho binyuze mu buhinzi.
 
_ "Ntabwo tuzahagarika ibiganiro bya AWR", McKey, anaba umufasha wa perezida w’inama rusange akaba ashinzwe uruhare rw’abanyamuryango bose. “Ubu amatorero yaho arayobora ibiganiro, kandi turatanga inyigisho. Abaturage bakora ibi. Uru nurugero rwiza rwuruhare rwabanyamuryango bose. "_
