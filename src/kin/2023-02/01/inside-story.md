### Iruka Usanga Yesu
_Byateguwe na Alicia Marie Harding_

Amashuri yo murugo, gahunda ikora neza, inzu isukuye, ifunguro ryiza murugo. Ibi bintu nibyiza, kandi ndabishishikariye nkumubyeyi wumumisiyonari wumunyamerika urera abana bane b'abamisiyonari muri Zambiya. Ariko ibi bintu nabyo ni ibikoresho bidufasha kubaha Yesu. Niba ibikoresho bigeze mu nzira ya Yesu, dukeneye kwiruka kuri We.

Hari nko mu ma saa kumi za mugitondo Twari hagati yishuri ryo murugo, kandi nanjye narimo nkora byinshi hamwe no kumesa no gutegura ifunguro rya sasita. Noneho umwana umwe yakubise undi kubera gutera urusaku rwinshi. Amosozi yatangiye gutemba mugihe umwana atabashaga kumenya ikibazo cyimibare ye, maze havuka impaka hagati yabavandimwe babiri bashimangira kwishakira inzira. Kwiheba kwanjye kwari kwinshi kuko nagombaga gukomeza gusubiramo amabwiriza kumwana utitayeho.

Icyo gihe, nari nzi ko mfite amahitamo abiri, nashoboraga guha umubiri wanjye kandi nijwi rikaze rigorora abantu bose. Cyangwa nshobora kurwanya impengamiro zanjye kandi nijwi ryiza, ryuzuye ubutumire twese dujyane ibibazo byacu kuri Yesu. Ni iki twungutse mu kazi no ku ishuri niba Yesu atari mu mitima yacu?

Kumwenyura, Nahamagaye buri mwana mwizina. Nabwiye umukobwa wanjye w'imyaka 11 nti: “Shayla. Navuze nti: “Wesley,” mpindukirira umuhungu wanjye w'imyaka icyenda. Nabwiye umukobwa wanjye w'imyaka irindwi n'umuhungu wanjye w'imyaka itatu ati: “Sienna na Winston. “Tugiye kugeza ibibazo byacu kuri Yesu kandi tureke adufashe kubikemura.”

Twapfukamye munsi yigiti cyicucu tubwira Yesu ibibazo byacu. Twasomye muri Bibiliya ivuga uburyo Yesu yatuje umuyaga. Twashimye Yesu n'indirimbo. Hanyuma twasangiye guhoberana, twongera gutangira umunsi wose - hamwe na Yesu. Na none.

Gusubira mu nzu, umwuka wa buri mwana waragabanutse. Bimaze kwinjira, buri mwana yateze amatwi yitonze amabwiriza yanjye. Hariho ubushake bwo gukemura ibyo mutumvikanaho muburyo bwiyubashye bwibanda kubandi, bitandukanye cyane numwuka wo kwibandaho mbere. Twibukijwe ko Yesu yari hafi kandi ukuhaba kwe kwari guha agaciro kuruta urutonde rwose rwo gukora.

Ellen White yaranditse ati: “Ababyeyi barira umurima w'ubumisiyonari bafite umwe mu ntoki zabo. . . . Ntabwo roho zabana be bwite zifite agaciro nkubugingo bwabanyamahanga? Ni ubuhe bwitonzi n'ubwuzu agomba kureba ibitekerezo byabo bikura kandi agahuza Imana n'ibitekerezo byabo byose! Ninde ushobora kubikora nk'umubyeyi wuje urukundo, wubaha Imana? ” (_Adventist Home_, p. 245).

Umubyeyi urenze kuyobora urugo. Nijyanye no kuyobora abana bawe bato kuri Yesu.

_Murakoze ku maturo yanyu yunganira abamisiyoneri kwisi._
