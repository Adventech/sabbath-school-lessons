####  Igice cya I: Incamake
 
Inyigisho yacu muri iki cyumweru yibanze ku mpaka zikomeye hagati yicyiza n'ikibi kuva ibinyejana byinshi. Ibyahishuwe 12 herekana ibice bine bikomeye muri aya makimbirane. Nubugarariji bwa Lusiferi mwijuru, kugerageza kurimbura uruhinja Yesu, igitero yagabye kubantu b'Imana mugihe cyo hagati, nigitero cya nyuma yibasiye abantu basigaye b'Imana muminsi yanyuma. Muri buri makimbirane, Kristo yahishuwe nk'Umwami wacu watsinze, Umucunguzi wacu watsinze, n'Umutsinzi ukomeye.
 
Igitekerezo cy'ingenzi mu isomo ryacu muri iki cyumweru ni uko, nubwo Satani yagabye ibitero bikaze ku bwoko bw'Imana no kugerageza kubatsemba, Yesu aratsinda, Satani aratsindwa. Iki gitekerezo ni ingenzi mu kudutegurira gusobanukirwa ubutumwa bwanyuma bwIbyahishuwe, dusanga mu Byahishuwe 14: 6-12. Nubwo ubwoko bw'Imana buzahura no gukandamizwa, gutotezwa, no gufungwa, ndetse no kwamagana ubukungu n’itegeko ry’urupfu, bafite ibyiringiro byuzuye ko, ku musaraba, Yesu yatsinze ubutware n’ububasha bw’ikuzimu. Satani ni umwanzi watsinzwe. Yesu umwe utarigeze atsindwa kurugamba na satani ntazatsindwa urugamba rwa nyuma. Yesu azabona ubwoko bwe kubutsinzi mumirwano yanyuma yisi.
 
Hariho ibihe bitoroshye. Ubwoko bw'Imana buzahura nikigeragezo gikomeye muminsi yanyuma yamateka yisi, ariko dushobora guhangana nibigeragezo byacu ejo hazaza twizeye cyane ko muri Yesu, binyuze muri Yesu, kandi kubwa Yesu, natwe dushobora gutsinda.
 
####  Igice cya II: Inynganizi
 
Ibyahishuwe 12 hashobora kwitwa hinge igitabo cyose cy'Ibyahishuwe. Igice cya 12 kiri mu mutima wigitabo cya nyuma cya Bibiliya hamwe ninzibacyuho kuva mubyagiye mbere byose bizaza. Igice cya 12 gihuza ibice byabanjirije Ibyahishuwe hamwe n'ibice biri imbere. Ibyahishuwe 1 bitangirana nishusho yicyubahiro ya Kristo nkumuremyi wacu, Umucunguzi, Umutambyi Mukuru, numwami uza. Noneho igitabo cy'Ibyahishuwe gikomeje kwerekana ibintu bitatu bikurikiranye - amatorero arindwi, kashe ndwi, n'inzamba ndwi. Buri kimwe muri ibyo bikurikiranye kirangirira ku ntsinzi ya Kristo n'itorero rye.
 
Intego yibi bice byambere ni uguhishura neza uburyo Kristo yarangije gukuramo uburyo Satani yagerageje kurimbura ubwoko bwImana no kwerekana intsinzi yanyuma ya Kristo Yesu mumirwano yisi hagati yicyiza n'ikibi. Guhishurwa kwa Yesu Kristo muri buri gice kiratanga inzira mu Byahishuwe 12:17 kugirango Yesu ahishurwe mu bwoko bwe. Tuganisha kuri uku guhishurwa, muri buri ruhererekane rukurikiranye rwa barindwi, tubona ko hariho abo bizera bizerwa "batsinze," abatemera imbaraga zikandamiza ibibi, abayoboke ba Kristo, hamwe nabasenga hamwe abizerwa bo mu bihe byose imbere y'intebe ye (Ibyah 7: 9-12).
 
Mu ncuro ya nyuma y'uruhererekane rw'abantu barindwi, umumarayika wa karindwi avuza impanda ye, avuga n'ijwi rirenga ati: '' Ingoma z'iyi si zahindutse ubwami bw'Umwami wacu na Kristo we, kandi azategeka ubuziraherezo n'iteka ryose! ' ”(Ibyah. 11:15). Ibice 11 byambere byIbyahishuwe biduha ibyiringiro byuko Kristo watsinze imbaraga zumuriro utazima kuva kera azatsinda amakimbirane yanyuma yisi. Nubwo ukuri gukandagirwa, ubwoko bw'Imana bwatotejwe, ndetse n’ibihumbi n’ibihumbi bahowe Imana, Satani ntabwo yigeze abasha gukuraho ukuri kw Imana cyangwa kurimbura ubwoko bwayo burundu.
 
Buji y'ukuri irashobora kuba yazimye, ariko ntabwo yigeze izimya. Buri gihe habaye umucyo mu mwijima. Amaherezo, isi yose izamurikirwa n'icyubahiro cy'Imana (Ibyah 18: 1). Umwanditsi w’umunyamerika James Russell Lowell abivuga neza mu gisigo “The Present Crisis”:
 
Ukuri iteka ryose kuri scafold,
 
Ibihe byose ku ntebe y'ubwami -
 
Nyamara iyo scafold ihindagura ejo hazaza,
 
Kandi, inyuma yijimye itazwi,
 
Hagarara Imana mu gicucu,
 
Kugumya kuba maso hejuru ye.
 
** Umurongo w'ingenzi (Igice cya 1): Ibyahishuwe 12: 11 **
 
Nubwo Ibyahishuwe 12 byuzuyemo ibisobanuro, imirongo ibiri irasobanutse cyane kandi ikwiye kwitabwaho bidasanzwe. Umurongo wa mbere ni Ibyahishuwe 12:11: “'Baramutsinda n'amaraso ya Ntama n'ijambo ry'ubuhamya bwabo, kandi ntibakunda ubuzima bwabo kugeza gupfa'”. Ijambo "gutsinda" risobanura gutsinda, gutsinda, no gutsinda. Ikoreshwa inshuro 17 mu gitabo cy'Ibyahishuwe. Ubwoko bw'Imana bwatsinze. Ntibatsinzwe.
 
Ikibazo ni iki - dushobora gutsinda gute? Bishoboka bite gutsinda intsinzi y'ibishuko bibi? Ibyahishuwe 12:11 hasubiza iki kibazo atangaza ati: '' Bamutsinze amaraso ya Ntama. ' ”Imvugo“ 'n'amaraso' ”irashobora kandi guhindurwa ngo“ kubera amaraso ”cyangwa“ hashingiwe ku maraso. ” Mu magambo y'indirimbo imwe ishaje, “Ibyiringiro byanjye nta kindi bishingiye ku maraso ya Yesu no gukiranuka.”
 
Ntabwo dutsinzwe n'ubushake bwacu. Shitani ni umwanzi w'amayeri. Izi ingingo zacu zintege nke. Izi aho twaguye mbere. Izi ingingo zacu zoroshye. Ibyiringiro byacu bishingiye kuri Kristo no gukiranuka kwe. Hano hari amagambo abiri akomeye atanga ibisobanuro ku Byahishuwe 12:11 na Ellen G. White muri SDA Bible Commentary, vol. 7, p. 974:
 
“Abantu bose bazashobora gutsinda. Reka duharanire cyane kugera ku gipimo cyashyizweho imbere yacu. Kristo azi intege nke zacu, kandi kuri We dushobora kujya buri munsi kubufasha. Ntabwo ari ngombwa ko tubona imbaraga ukwezi kumwe. Tugomba gutsinda umunsi ku wundi (MS 28, 1886). ”
 
Ni nkuko twizera Yesu byimazeyo kandi tukamugana buri munsi kugirango adufashe dushobora gutsinda. Ntabwo Satani azi intege nke zacu gusa, ahubwo Yesu arabizi. Kubw'ubuntu bwe n'imbaraga zayo, Yesu adukiza icyaha no gufata icyaha. Binyuze mu maraso ye, twakuwe mu gucirwaho iteka kw'icyaha kandi twibohoye ubutware bwayo. Igitekerezo cya kabiri cya Ellen G. White gisobanura neza iyi ngingo: “Turi abatsinze dufasha abandi gutsinda, n'amaraso ya Ntama n'ijambo ry'ubuhamya bwacu. Gukurikiza amategeko y'Imana bizaduha muri twe umwuka wo kumvira, kandi umurimo ukomoka ku mwuka nk'uwo, Imana irashobora kubyemera (Ibaruwa 236, 1908). ”
 
Intangiriro yicyaha nukwikunda. Iyo tugeze kumusaraba, ubuntu bwa Kristo buraduhindura. Urukundo rwe rudutera gukorera no guha abandi umugisha. “Ijambo ry'ubuhamya bwacu” ryerekeza ku buhamya bwacu. Ntabwo ubuhamya bwacu buha abandi umugisha gusa, ahubwo natwe ubwacu turahirwa iyo dukorera. Imbaraga za Kristo zo kunesha imigezi iva mwijuru ijya kubantu bizera Yesu byimazeyo, bacunguwe nubuntu bwayo, kandi batanga ubuzima bwabo kumurimo we.
 
** Umurongo w'ingenzi (Igice cya 2): Ibyahishuwe 12: 17 **
 
Umurongo wanyuma w'Ibyahishuwe 12 ni umwe mu mirongo y'ingenzi muri Bibiliya yose. Ikiyoka (Satani) kirakajwe n'abantu b'indahemuka b'Imana basigaye kandi barwana n'uyu mutwe w'imperuka, “bakurikiza amategeko y'Imana kandi bafite ubuhamya bwa Yesu Kristo” (Ibyah 12:17). Mu Byahishuwe 13, satani arahamagarira abo bafatanije bombi kumufasha mu ntambara ya nyuma yisi. Abafatanyabikorwa bagereranywa nk'inyamaswa ivuye mu nyanja (Ibyah 13: 1-10) n'inyamaswa yo mu gihugu, izwi kandi ku muhanuzi w'ikinyoma (Ibyah 13: 11-18; gereranya Ibyah 16:13, Ibyah. 19:20, Ibyah 20:10).
 
Abadiventisti b'umunsi wa karindwi bumva ko izo nyamaswa zombi zerekana abapapa (imbaraga za leta ya kiliziya gatolika ya Roma) hamwe na Amerika. Byongeye kandi, bihujwe cyane nubupfumu na Satani kuburyo ikiyoka ari ikimenyetso kuri bombi. Abifashijwemo n’ubwo bumwe butatu - ikiyoka (ubupfumu), inyamaswa iva mu nyanja (Papa), n’inyamaswa yo mu gihugu, izwi kandi ku muhanuzi w'ikinyoma (Abaporotesitanti b'abahakanyi bayobowe na Leta zunze ubumwe za Amerika) —Satani azarwana n'abantu basigaye b'Imana.
 
Ijambo "abasigaye" rikoreshwa muri Bibiliya mu gusobanura ubwoko bw'Imana bwizerwa. Isezerano rya Kera rikoresha ijambo "abasigaye," cyangwa inkomoko yaryo, byibuze mu buryo butatu. Icya mbere, hariho igitekerezo cyo kubungabunga. Abagize umuryango wa Yakobo barokowe n'inzara ikabije kubera ko Yozefu yatabaye. Itangiriro 45: 7 havuga abo bagize umuryango nkurubyaro rwa Yakobo cyangwa mubyukuri, "abasigaye." Hano hibandwa ku kuba abakomoka kuri Yakobo barinzwe. Mu buryo nk'ubwo, ibisigisigi by'igihe cy'Imana birinze ibitero by'ikiyoka na bagenzi be, inyamaswa n'umuhanuzi w'ikinyoma. Mu buryo nk'ubwo Imana yohereje Yozefu muri Egiputa ngo '' urinde urubyaro rwawe ku isi, kandi ukize ubuzima bwawe kubwo gutabarwa gukomeye '' (Itang 45: 7),
 
Icya kabiri, mu 1 Abami 19:14, 18, ijambo "abasigaye" rikoreshwa mu gusobanura "ibisigaye." Eliya atakambira Uwiteka, ati: '' Jyenyine nsigaye '' (1 Abami 19:14).
 
Kandi Imana irasubiza iti: "'Nabitse muri Isiraheli ibihumbi birindwi, amavi yose atapfukamiye Baali'" (1 Abami 19:18). Hano ijambo "abasigaye" ryerekana igitekerezo cyabakomeza kuba abizerwa kandi batitanze kuri ruswa ikikije gusenga ibinyoma.
 
Icya gatatu, mu bihe bimwe na bimwe byo mu Isezerano rya Kera, ijambo "abasigaye" rikoreshwa mu gusobanura abahunze iminyago ya Babuloni (Yer. 23: 3, Yer. 31: 7). Gushyira hamwe iyi mirongo bidufasha gukora ishusho irambuye yimigendere yigihe cyanyuma cyImana mu Byahishuwe 12. Muri make, aba bantu basigaye barashobora kumenyekana kubudahemuka kumategeko yose y'Imana. Bahawe kandi "ubuhamya bwa Yesu" (Ibyah 12:17), nk'uko bivugwa mu Byahishuwe 19:10, "'ni umwuka wo guhanura.' ”Usibye kwigaragaza kw'impano y'ubuhanuzi hagati yabo, abasigaye bamenyekana nk'abarinzwe n'Imana nk'indahemuka ryayo, bahunga ruswa y'iyi si, kandi, binyuze mu kwiyemeza Kristo no kumvira. Ijambo rye, ryakuwe mu nyigisho z'ibinyoma za Babuloni yo mu mwuka.
 
####  Igice cya III: Gushira m'Ubuzima Busanzwe
 
Kubitekerezo byawe bwite: Ibyahishuwe 12 bitanga amasomo menshi yingirakamaro mubuzima bwa gikristo bwo mu kinyejana cya makumyabiri na rimwe. Icya mbere, Ibyahishuwe 12 bitwizeza ko, mugihe cyibigeragezo byubuzima, Kristo ahora ahari. Iki gice kitwibutsa ko Satani ari umwanzi watsinzwe. Iraduha ikizere ko mu ntambara zacu zikomeye na Satani, Kristo yamaze gutsinda intsinzi. Gusoma Ibyahishuwe 12 biduha ibyiringiro ko dukorera Imana ikomeye iruta uwo duhanganye.
 
Urugero rw'ibyo Imana yatanze mu bihe by'amakuba tuyisanga mu mirongo ibiri yihariye - Ibyahishuwe 12: 6 n'Ibyahishuwe 12:14. Iyi mirongo yombi ivuga ku gihe kimwe ariko ifite intego zitandukanye. Imirongo y'iyi mirongo ni itotezwa ry'ubwoko bw'Imana mu gihe cyo hagati, kuva ku ya 538 kugeza ku ya 1798. Ibyahishuwe 12: 6 hagira hati: “Hanyuma umugore ahungira mu butayu, aho afite ahantu hateguwe n'Imana, kugira ngo babigereho kumugaburira ngaho iminsi igihumbi magana abiri na mirongo itandatu ”. Ibyahishuwe 12:14 havuga ko umugore, cyangwa itorero, "yagaburiwe" mu butayu. Ukuri gushishikaza ukuri guhoraho muriyi mirongo nuko mugihe cyibigeragezo, amakuba, nigeragezwa, Imana ihora ifite umwanya waduteguriye. Aratugaburira mu bigeragezo. Mubigeragezo byubuzima, Ariho adukomeza, adukomeza, kandi adushyigikire. Mubigeragezo bikomeye byubuzima,
 
Amasomo abiri akomeye kubanyeshuri bawe bakuramo icyumweru cyo kwiga ni:
 
1. Kristo ntabwo yigeze atsindwa na Satani, kandi igihe cyanyuma, ubwoko bwe buzatsinda.
 
2. Ibigeragezo ibyo aribyo byose duhura nabyo, Imana irahari kugirango itware ubwoko bwayo kunesha.
 
**Muganire:**
 
`1. Ni mu buhe buryo Ibyahishuwe 12 byuzuye ibyiringiro kubantu b'Imana mugihe cyimperuka? `
 
`2. Urebye ibyo byiringiro, ni izihe mpamvu tugomba kwishimira ko Kristo yatsinze ubutware n'imbaraga z'ikuzimu?`
