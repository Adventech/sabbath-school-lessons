---
title:  Kwiga Bibiliya Mu Buryo Bwimbitse
date:   28/04/2026
---

`Nubwo atari ngobwa ko uba inzobere kugira ngo ubashe kwiga Bibiliya, ni gute ushobora kwiga Bibiliya mu buryo bwimbitse?`

**Senga**: Ntabwo twaba dukabije turamutse tuvuze ko isengesho ari inkingi ya mwamba mu gihe cyawe cyo kwiga Bibiliya. Ellen G. White avuga ko iyo tugiye kwiga Bibiliya tutaba turi twenyine. Kubwo kurarika Mwuka Wera ngo aze atuyobore, twigizayo ibirangaza byose, kandi umwanzi na we arahunga. “Bibiliya ntikwiriye na rimwe kwigwa hatabayeho gusenga. Umwuka Muziranenge ni we wenyine ubasha kudutera kumva akamaro k’ibyo bintu bitaruhije gusobanuka, cyangwa akaturinda kugoreka ukuri tudasobanukiwe.”- Ellen G. White, _Intambara Ikomeye_, p. 489.

**Soma kandi Wandike**: Bishobora kuvugwa ko itandukaniro ry’ingenzi riri hagati yo gusoma Bibiliya gusa no kuyiga, rishingiye ku kintu kimwe cy’ingenzi: kwandika. Kwandika bidufasha gukoresha intekerezo zacu twitonze, tugatekereza ku ijambo ry’Imana, tukarikoresha mu buryo duhuza ibyo dushobora kubona, ubusobanuro, gushyira mu bikorwa, no kwiyemeza bikaboneka. Binadufasha kandi gukoresha ibitekerezo byacu byari bisanzwe biri ahantu hatatanye-biturutse mu mitwe yacu, bigashyirwa ku makaramu yacu, maze bikaza mu mitima yacu kuri uwo munsi. Dukunze kandi kwibuka ibintu twanditse (Zaburi 119:15,16). Niba udashoboye kwandika, gerageza usome Bibiliya yawe mu ijwi riranguruye (cyangwa utege amatwi abayigusomera), bikurikirwe n’ibitekerezo byawe nk’isengesho ugeza ku Mana.

**Bwira Abandi**: Gira uwo ubwira ibyo wize. Ibi bizatuma ibyo wize bishinga imizi mu mutima wawe kandi bitere abandi umwete.

Toranya igitabo kigufi cyo muri Bibiliya abe ari cyo utangiriraho (nka Yona, Mariko, Abafilipi cyangwa 1 Yohana), maze ugisome witonze. Hano hari uburyo bworoshye ushobora gukoresha ku murongo umwe (uburyo bw’umurongo ku murungo), igitekerezo cyangwa igice cyose:

1. Senga kugira ngo Mwuka Wera ayobore intekerezo zawe kandi yoroshye umutima wawe igihe usoma Bibiliya

2. Toranya umurongo wa Bibiliya cyangwa igitekerezo.

3. Andika icyo gitekerezo cyangwa icyo uzirikana igihe usoma.

4. Ongera usome icyo gitekerezo usenga kandi uce akarongo ku ngingo z’ingenzi.

5. Andika icyo ingingo waciyeho akarongo zikubwira.

6. Sengera izo ngingo kandi usabe zigire icyo zikora ku mubano wawe n’Imana

7. Zirikana uwo ushobora kubwira uyu munsi.

“Igihe cyose ubwoko bw’Imana bukurira mu buntu, buzahora buhabwa ubusobanuro bw’Ijambo ryayo. Buzabona umucyo mushya ndetse n’ubwiza mu kuri kwaryo. Ibi byabaye impamo mu mateka y’itorero mu bihe byose, kandi bizakomeza kuba bityo kugeza ku iherezo.” – Ellen G. White, _Counsels to Writers and Editors_, pp. 38, 39.

`Ni mu buhe buryo wabonye ko ubutumwa buri muri ayo magambo avuzwe haruguru ari ukuri kuri wowe? Soma na Zaburi 119:105.`
