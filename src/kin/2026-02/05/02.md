---
title:  Igihe
date:   26/04/2026
---

Mbese waba warigeze ushyira isaha ku gihe kugira ngo ikubyutse kandi ikwibutse gusoma Bibiliya? Mbese wigeze urwana no kubyuka maze ukareba ku isaha ugatekereza uti: _‘Nsigaje iminota 15 gusa kugira ngo ntangire umunsi wanjye. Reka nsome nihuta!’_ Mbese wigeze usenga isengesho rigufi cyangwa usoma igice [cya Bibiliya] mu buryo bw’umuhango kugira ngo uturishe umutimanama wawe ariko umutima wawe ukaba utanyuzwe n’umusaruro mbere yuko wihutira kujya mu kazi k’uwo munsi?’

“Usoma Ibyanditse byera ahubutse ntagira icyo yunguka cyane. Umuntu yabasha gusoma Bibiliya yose agaheba ibyiza byayo ntafutukirwe ndetse n’ubwiru bwayo bukomeye buyirimo.”- Ellen G. White, _Kugana Yesu_, p. 92.

Mu gihe hari imigisha izanwa no gusoma Bibiliya – kimwe nko kunywa amazi ava mu cyobo kigurumana umuriro (umuntu asoma byinshi kandi vuba) birashoboka ko umuntu yasoma Bibiliya yihuta cyane ariko agahomba byinshi. Imana yaduhaye ijambo ryayo yahumetse kandi rifite agaciro kenshi kugira ngo turusheho kuyimenya (kandi muri urwo rugendo, tubasha no kwisobanukirwa ubwacu). Iyo dufashe umwanya wo kureba imico myiza itarondoreka y’Imana ndetse n’uburyo yagiye ivugana n’abantu mu gihe cy’amateka yahise, dushobora kurushaho kuyikunda. Uburyo yagiye ivugana n’abantu buri hano mu biganza byacu-ariko tugomba gushaka igihe, tugafata umwanya wo kumenya Imana binyuze mu gusoma Ijambo ryayo (Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 17:11). Zirikana ibitekerezo bikurikira:

**Saba Imana gushyira mu mutima wawe icyifuzo kiyerekeyeho.** Saba gusohorezwa amasezerano ari muri Yeremiya 29:13 na Zaburi 37:4. Saba Imana kujya igukangura mugitondo kare mbere y’igihe usanzwe ubyukiraho cyangwa kuguha igihe cyihariye mu munsi wawe wo gukora ibyo.

**Egurira Imana igihe cyawe.** Yego, ufite byinshi byo gukora, kandi hari ibintu byinshi byihutirwa byo gukora. Ariko kumara igihe uri kumwe n’Imana ni ntagereranywa. Jya ahantu hatuje wenyine usome Zaburi ya 46:11. Ririmba amagambo cyangwa usome indirimbo ya 25 iri mu gitabo cy’Indirimbo zo guhimbaza Imana yitwa “Yesu Ndi Uwawe Nyakira.” Tekereza imigabane y’ubuzima bwawe utaregurira Imana maze na yo uyegurire Imana.

**Mara Umwanya uri kumwe n’Imana ndetse n’igihe wumva bitakurimo.** Nk’uko kugira amagara mazima bisaba amahitamo y’umutimanama ndetse n’igenamigambi (imyitozo ngoraramubiri, kurya neza), ni na ko no kugirana umushyikirano n’Imana bisaba icyemezo gifashwe n’umutimanama. Ibuka ko imico mishya ishobora gufata iminsi nibura 21 kugira ngo imenyerwe, kandi ntabwo dushobora na rimwe gutsinda hatari ubufasha bwa Mwuka Wera.

`Ongera usome Yohana 15:1-8. Ni iki Yesu atubwira cyerekeranye no kuguma muri We, kandi ni kuki ibi ari ingenzi cyane ku kwizera kwacu?`
