---
title:  Amagambo Aryohera!
date:   30/04/2026
---

Tekereza ku ifunguro ukunda cyane. Mbese ni ryiza ku buzima bwawe? Ahari ukoresha ubuki nk’indyoshyandyo, cyangwa ushobora no kuba ukoresha ubuki bw’umuti kubw’intego yabwo y’ubuzima. Niba warigeze urya ikinyagu cy’ubuki, uzi ukuntu kiryohera iyo gishongonokera ku rurimi rwawe.

Muri Zaburi 119:103, 104, umunyezaburi yerekana Bibiliya nk’ikinyagu cy’ubuki - imvugoshusho y’ikintu gishimisha: “Amagambo yawe aryohereye ubu bugeni mu nkanka zanjye! Arusha ubuki kuryohera mu kanwa kanjye. Amategeko wigishije ampesha guhitamo.”

`Bisobanuye iki kuba “amategeko wigishije ampesha guhitamo” [“atuma ngira ubushishozi” BII]? (Zaburi 119:104). Kuki iki gitekerezo ari ingenzi mu gufata icyo kwiga Bibiliya itwigisha?`

Yego, amagambo y’Imana aryohera ubugingo bwacu kandi mu buryo butandukanye n’ikindi kintu cyose isi itanga. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibyokurya byinshi, uburyohe bw’ijambo ry’Imana ni ugukira k’ubugingo bwacu ndetse buhindura imico yacu. Niba warigeze ujya kure y’Imana ushobora gupfukama, ukarambura ijambo ryayo, ukanywa amazi y’ubugingo yo yonyine amara inyota.

`Muri Yesaya 55:1-13, umuhanuzi yagura ubutumwa bwavuzwe haruguru. Mara umwanya usoma iki gice maze usubize ibi bibazo:`

- Ni iki Uwiteka aha abamusanga kugira ngo “barye” ijambo rye?

- Ni ubuhe butumire bwayo kuri wowe hano?

- Ni ikihe kibazo ifite?

- Isezerano ryayo ni irihe?

Ijambo ry’Imana rizima kandi rifite imbaraga rifite ubugi buhinguranya imitima, intekerezo ndetse n’ubugingo mu gihe ridutera umwete wo gukurira muri Kristo. Ariko ridukorera ibyo mu gihe dufashe igihe kandi tukagira umwete wo gucukumbura ijambo dufite imyifatire yo kuganduka no kwicisha bugufi kandi dufite ubushake bwo gukurikiza icyo ryigisha.

`Ni ubuhe buryo bugaragara ushobora “gushaka Uwiteka bigishoboka ko abonwa” (Yesaya 55:6)?`
