---
title:  Ibindi byo Kwigwa no Kuzirikanwa
date:   19/06/2026
---

“Icyo umuntu yaba akora cyose, ntawabasha gukunda Imana byimazeyo atarakunda umuvandimwe we urukundo rutikubiraho. Ariko kugerageza gukunda abandi si byo byadushoboza na gato kugira uwo mutima. Igikenewe ni ukugira urukundo rwa Kristo mu mutima. Iyo ubwikanyize buzimiriye muri Kristo, muri ako kanya urukundo ruhita rushibuka.”-Ellen G. White, Imigani ya Kristo, 237. “Abantu bitabira cyane gukora umurimo wabo wo kuzana abantu kuri Yesu Kristo bagakiranuka ni bo barushaho gukura mu by’umwuka no kwitanga.”- Ellen G. White, _Ivugabutumwa_, p. 275.

“Imbaraga zo kurwanya ikibi zituruka mu gukorana umwete bahangana na cyo.” Ellen G. White, _Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa_, p. 69.

“Kugira ngo dusangire umunezero na we—Umunezero wo kuzabona abazakizwa ku bw’igitambo cye—tugomba gufatanya mu murimo we wo gucungurwa kwabo.”- Ellen G. White, _Uwifuzwa Ibihe Byose_, p. 101.

“Abantu banga amahirwe bahabwa yo gukorana na Kristo umurimo, baba banze kwiga mu ishuri rimwe rukumbi ritunganya umuntu rikamuha ibyangombwa byuzuye byo kuzasangira na Kristo mu ikuzo rye.”- Ellen G. White, _Uburezi_, p. 277.

**IBIBAZO:**

`Kuki urukundo ari urufatiro rukomeye kandi rukaba ari ingenzi ku guhamya gutanga umusaruro?`

`Ni ryari wabonye ko ibi ari ukuri; ko kurehereza imitima y’abantu kuri Kristo bifitanye isano n’urugendo ruzima umuntu agendanamo n’Imana?`

`Mbese hari umurongo fatizo cyangwa imyumvire y’ibanze ikenewe kugira ngo umuntu amenyeshe abandi iby’Imana? Niba ari ko bimeze, yaba ari iyihe?`

`Ku byerekeranye no guha abatizera ibyigisho bya Bibiliya, ni hehe watangirira? Ni iki wabanza kwibandaho: kubaha ibyigisho runaka by’idini, cyangwa ni ukubararaikira kumenya Yesu?`

`rimba cyangwa utege amatwi amagambo ari mu ndirimbo ya 68 yitwa “Naguzwe Amaraso y’Intama” (iri mu gitabo cy’Indirimbo Zo Guhimbaza Imana cy’Itorero ry’Abadiventisite b’Umunsi wa Karindwi) maze utekereze uburyo wamamaza ibiyikubiyemo.`

**Inshamake:** Urukundo rw’Imana n’ijambo ryayo rizima kandi rifite imbaraga nibyuzura mu mibereho yacu ya buri munsi, bizadutera kuyikunda no kuyibwira abatuzengurutse. Dukwiriye kuba abanyamasengesho, kandi tugatekereza neza, tukaba abahamya bafite intego, tukizerayuko ijambo ryayo rituruka mu kanwa kayo ritazagaruka ubusa, ahubwo rizasohoza ibyo ishaka kandi ko rizashobora gukora ibyo yaritumye (Yesaya 55:11).
