---
title:  Akuzuye Umutima Gasesekara ku Munwa
date:   14/06/2026
---

**Soma iby’inshingano ikomeye iri muri Matayo 28:18-20. Andika ubutumwa butandukanye bwa Yesu ubwo akoresha ijambo: “bwose, byose” cyangwa “iminsi yose” (rifite ubusobanuro bumwe n’ijambo ry’Ikigiriki, _pas_).**

Yesu yaduhaye inshingano yo kugeza ku batuye isi ubutumwa bwe: “Mugende muhindure abantu bo mu mahanga yose abigishwa.” Inshingano y’Itorero ry’Abadiventisiti b’Umunsi wa Karindwi ni uguhindura abantu abigishwa, maze abo bantu na bo bagahindura abandi. Muri ubwo buryo, twese twamamaza ubutumwa bwiza bw’iteka ryose ndetse n’ubutumwa bw’abamarayika batatu (Ibyahishuwe 14:6-12) kugira ngo bifashe abatuye isi kwitegura kugaruka kwa Yesu kwegereje.

Umuntu wese wakiriye ubugingo bushya muri Kristo ahamagarirwa guhamya Yesu. Ariko inshuro nyinshi, abantu batekereza ku guhamya nk’ikintu badashobora gukora cyangwa badashaka gukora. Ushobora kwitekereza uri kubwiriza ku muhanda cyangwa uri gutanga ibyigisho bya Bibiliya, maze ukazunguza umutwe ukavuga ati: “Oya si jye! Nta kuntu nabikora! Ntabwo ndi intyoza, guhamya ntabwo ari ibintu byanjye.”

Nyamara, guhamya nyakuri akenshi ni umusaruro uva mu kuba warabonye ibyo Imana ikorera mu mibereho yawe, ukabona ibyo Imana ikwigisha uko urushaho gukurira muri Yo, maze ukubibwira abandi. Imana ni nziza, kandi ibyo yadukoreye ni amakuru meza abatuye isi bashobora kumva. Ntidushobora kandi ntidukwiriye guceceka! Yaragucunguye; yaguhamagaye mu izina—Uri uwe. Mbese hari amakuru meza aruta ayo ku muntu uwo ari we wese n’ahantu aho ari ho hose?

Nubwo abigishwa bo mu itorero rya mbere batari barigiye mu mashuri y’Abigisha b’Abayuda cyangwa ngo babe ari intyoza mu mihango y’umuco wabo, dushobora rwose kubigiraho.

`Soma Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 1:8 n’ Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 4:13. Mbese guhamya byari bimeze bite mu itorero rya mbere? Ni izihe mpinduka Petero na Yohana bateje ku babumvize bahamya?`

Petero na Yohana bakomeje kuvuga bati: “Kuko tutabasha kwiyumanganya ngo tureke kuvuga ibyo twabonye kandi twumvise” (Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 4:20). “Babanaga na Yesu” (Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 4:13) kandi bumvaga bahatwa kubwira abandi ibya Yesu. Mwuka Wera yabahaye kuvuga bashize amanga kandi ashyira imbaraga yemeza mu magambo yabo.

`Fata igihe runaka ubungubu usenge. Saba Imana kuguha ubutwari kugira ngo ubashe kuyimenyesha abandi bantu mubana. Yisabe ubwenge bwo kumenya igihe cyo kuvuga ndetse iguhe n’icyo kuvuga. Soma urwa 1 Yohana 4:7-11 maze usenge usaba urukundo nk’urwo.`
