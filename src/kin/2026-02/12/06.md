---
title:  Mugabure
date:   18/06/2026
---

Twese twagiye tugira ibihe byo gucika intege cyangwa ibihe byo kunyeganyega mu rugendo rwacu tugendanamo n’Imana—mu bikombe aho imitima yacu itakiranutse cyangwa aho twabaye turi akazuyaze. Ni iki cyakugaruye mu mushyikirano urambye ufitanye n’Imana?

`Muri Zekariya 10 hagaragaza ubutumwa bwerekeranye n’uburyo Imana igarura ubwoko bwayo kuri Yo. Soma icyo gice nonaha kandi witonze maze wandike ubutumwa bw’ingenzi bukubiyemo.`

Mu buryo bufatika, kumenya uburyo bwo gusabana ndetse no kuganira n’umuntu ukunda waretse umushyikirano yari afitanye n’Imana bishobora kugorana. Ushobora kwibaza uburyo ibintu byaba bidatandukanye maze bikagira umusaruro utandukanye; ushobora kwibaza uburyo bwo kuganira na bo ubu noneho bafite imyumvire itandukanye n’iyo wowe ufite; ushobora kumva ushobewe kandi udafite gifasha ku byerekeranye n’ibyemezo bidashyitse bashobora kuba bagifata. Ibyo bitekerezo buri gihe bizagira ingaruka ku buryo ufatamo uwo ukunda, kandi ku bw’ibyo, ni ingenzi cyane kubaho no kuvuga ibituruka mu gusenderezwa gukomoka mu gihe umarana n’Umukiza wawe.

Ubuhamya bw’imibereho yawe, ibikorwa byawe, amagambo yawe, ndetse n’amasengesho yawe usabira uwo mwashakanye cyangwa umwana wawe wateye Imana umugongo bishobora guhindura imibereho yabo n’ahazaza habo. (Soma Luka 22:31,32 na Yohana 21:15-17 uburyo amasengesho ya Yesu yasengeye Petero yahinduye ahazaza he). Reka agahinda, kubacira urubanza, cyangwa kubaciraho iteka ushobora kwiyumvamo, maze usabe Imana gusimbuza ibyo byiyumvo urukundo rushobora gutangwa na Yo yonyine. Saba Imana kugutwikiriza imico yayo kugira ngo ushobore kuba icyitegererezo cy’imyifatire irangwa n’urukundo no kutikanyiza. Ibuka ko “nta mbaraga n’imwe ihindura ishobora gutoza umutima w’umuntu inganya ububasha n’imbaraga ihindura y’imibereho itarangwamo kwikunda. Icyabasha kurwanirira ubutumwa bwiza kigatsinda ububugisha impaka ni umukristo ukunda kandi agakundwa.”-Ellen G. White, _The Ministry of Healing_, p. 470.

Binyuze mu rugero rwacu rw’imibereho idahindagurika yereka abandi Kristo, abanze Kristo bazabona muri twe ikintu gishobora guturuka ku Mana gusa. Bazabona amahoro arenze ayo umuntu yamenya, urukundo rutagajuka, ndetse n’ibyiringiro bikomeza kubaho no mu bihe bidasanzwe. Urukundo Imana ikunda hamwe n’abacu ntabwo rwigera rugajūka. Uru rukundo duhabwa buri munsi natwe dushobora kuruha abatuzengurutse.

`Abefeso 3:17-19 hadutera umwete wo gukora iki?`
