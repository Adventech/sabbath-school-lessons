---
title:  Kuguma mu Muzabibu
date:   02/04/2026
---

Rimwe na rimwe, kuguma muri Kristo bishobora gusa n'aho ari kimwe mu bintu bigoye cyane kurusha ibindi. Dushobora kuba tuzi ko ari byo dukeneye, ariko imihihibikano y'ubuzima idukururira mu mugezi wayo, kandi byose bikatugora cyane. Gukurikira Imana bishobora gusa n'aho ari umutwaro uremereye cyane kurusha iyindi yose, cyane cyane ku bantu bahora bahanganye n'umuntu ushaka kubahatira gukurikira Yesu. Ubwo bwoko bw’idini bushobora gusa n'aho ari umuruho bitewe n'uko bushingiye ku bikorwa by'inyuma aho gushingira ku biri mu mutima. Nta kintu na kimwe gishobora kugera kure y'ibyo Imana ishaka, ari cyo cy’umushyikirano ushingiye ku rukundo hagati yanyu, aho gushingira ku mategeko gusa; umushyikirano wihitiyemo (ari Yo yabanje kuguhitamo) ushingiye ku rukundo no kwihitiramo ku bushake.

Rimwe na rimwe, dushobora kuba dufitanye isano n'Umuzabibu mu rugero runaka, ariko mu by'ukuri tukaba tutagumye muri wo n’impagarike yacu yose. Dushobora kujya mu rusengero, tugasenga, kandi tugakora ibyo tuzi ko bikwiriye, ariko imbere mu mutima tukumva twacitse intege. Ukuri ni uku: Nta cyo twakora ngo twihatire kuguma muri Yesu, nk'uko ishami ridashobora kwihatira kuguma ku muzabibu. Imana ni yo yabanje kudukunda; ni yo yafashe iya mbere. Buri gihe, uko twakira ibyo Imana yadukoreye mbere y'ibindi byose, ni na ko twitabira ibyo yabanje kudukorera.

Niwitegereza uburyo umuzabibu ukomeza kubaho mu gihe cy'umuhindo, uzabyigiraho isomo ritangaje: imyumba y’uburabyo yo ku mashami igenda ikamukamo amazi maze igatandukana n’imikurire y’igiti kugeza igihe cy’itumba. Iyo ubutaka bushyushye, imizi inywa amazi, hanyuma amakakama (amazi yo mu giti) akazamukira mu giti cy'umuzabibu akajya mu myumba y’uburabyo, maze agatuma habaho gukura. Iyo umuzabibu udafite amakakama, ntushobora gukura.

Amakakama mu muzabibu ameze nka Mwuka Wera mu mibereho yacu. Dushobora kuba tumeze nk'ishami ryumye, ariko iyo duhisemo kumarana igihe n'Imana, Mwuka Wera adusukamo ibisa n’umushongi w’amakakama uturuka ku mizi, maze ukadusubiza ubuzima kugira ngo dutangire gukura. Mu buryo nk'ubwo, tugomba kugira amahitamo tubyitondeye kugira ngo twifuze kuguma muri Yesu, tugomba kandi gusaba Mwuka Wera (amakakama) agatemba mu mibereho yacu.

`Soma muri Luka 11:13 hamwe na Yeremiya 31:3, urwa 1 Yohana 4:19, n’ Abaroma 8:9-11. Ni ubuhe butumwa bw’ingenzi duhabwa muri aya masomo? `

Mu by'ukuri, Mwuka Wera ni We utuma dukura kandi akareba ko twera imbuto kandi ko dufitanye isano n'Umuzabibu. Tugomba gusenga buri munsi dusaba Mwuka Wera, uri hano ku isi hamwe natwe kugira ngo:

- Atubere Umufasha wacu (Yohana 14:16-18).

- Aduhishurire Yesu (Yohana 15:26).

- Atwemeze icyaha (Yohana 16:7, 8).

- Atuyobore mu kuri kose (Yohana 16:13).

`Ongera usome urwo rutonde. Ni gute buri kintu cyose gifitanye isano n'ibyo Mwuka Wera akora kigira ingaruka ku mushyikirano ufitanye n'Imana?`
