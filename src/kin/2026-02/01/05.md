---
title:  "Mugume [Muri Yesu]"
date:   01/04/2026
---

Abigishwa bakurikiye Yesu bamanuka ingazi bava mu cyumba cyo hejuru bajya mu muhanda wari hasi. Mu gihe bagendaga bagana i Getsemani, mu ijoro ry'ingenzi cyane kuruta ayandi yose yabaye mu mateka y'isi, birashoboka ko batigeze bamenya ukuntu amagambo ya nyuma Yesu yababwiriye mu cyumba cyo hejuru yari afite uburemere.

`Ni iki Yesu yavuze muri Yohana 15:1-11? Ibi bisobanuye iki kuri twe?`

Ayo magambo yavuzwe na Yesu ubwe, agaragaza icyo kugirana umushyikirano wa bugufi n'Imana bisobanura. Zirikana ijambo risubirwamo, atari inshuro ebyiri gusa, ahubwo inshuro icumi zose: mugume. Kuguma muri Yesu ni ukubana na We tukomatana na We.

Mu gihe yitegerezaga umusaraba, Yesu ntiyashimangiye gusa akamaro gakomeye ko kuba muri we, ahubwo yanavuze mu buryo bwumvikana kandi bworoheje ikintu cy'ingirakamaro cy'ukuntu imibereho yacu ikwiriye kumera. Ni ukuvuga ko Yesu ari umuzabibu, natwe tukaba amashami. Nk’ingaruka z’uko tuguma muri We (tukomatana na we), imbuto zizakura ku mashami yacu. Ntidushobora kwera imbuto ubwacu. Rimwe na rimwe, dushobora kugaragara nk’abari muri we, ariko ikimenyetso kizagaragazwa n’uko tudafite imbuto, kandi amaherezo amashami yacu azuma. Niba twumye, Nyir’Umuzabibu azakata amashami. Twaba twera imbuto cyangwa tutazera, amashami yacu azahwanyuzwa.

Ariko nanone, twese duhura n'ibibazo n'ibihe bibabaje. Nidukomeza kuguma muri We, ibyo bihe bizatuma twera imbuto nyinshi mu gihe kirekire. Kwera imbuto bigaragaza abo turi bo (abigishwa). Twera imbuto kugira ngo tumuheshe icyubahiro (aho kugira ngo twiheshe icyubahiro). Kuguma muri Yesu bisobanura gukomeza amategeko ye, ari yo agaragaza imico ye myiza irangwa n'urukundo ruzira kwikunda. Kuguma muri Yesu bihesha ibyishimo byinshi. Kuguma muri Yesu bisobanura gukora ibyo adusaba, ari na cyo gisubizo tumugaragariza. “Kuko gukunda Imana ari uku, ari uko twitondera amategeko yayo. Kandi amategeko yayo ntarushya" (1 Yohana 5:3).

Iyo utekereje kuri ibyo, kuguma muri Yesu ni umuti ku mibereho yacu twebwe ab’ i Lawodikiya (Ibyahishuwe 3:20; Yohana  15:4). Ni ryo banga rikomeye ryo kugira imibereho yuzuye kandi ifite ireme hano ku isi no mu gihe cy'iteka ryose; nyamara kandi, mu buryo runaka, biroroshye cyane ko twibagirwa inama ya Yesu.

Mu buryo bwihariye, Yesu abwira buri wese muri twe ati: "Uko Data yankunze, ni ko nanjye nabakunze; nuko rero mugume mu rukundo rwanjye" (Yohana 15:9). Urukundo rwa Yesu ni rwo mugozi uruta iyindi yose rudukururira kuri We, kandi iyo tumenye urwo rukundo, dusunikirwa mu buryo bwimbitse kurugaragariza Imana n'abandi bantu.
