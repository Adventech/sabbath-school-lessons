### Aho icyigisho cy'iki cyumweru gishingiye:
Ibyahishuwe 3:14-22; Ibyahishuwe 4:9-11; Itangiriro 2:7; Itangiriro 3:8-10; Yeremiya 31:3, 4; Yohana 15:1-11; Abaroma 8:9-11.

> <p>Icyo kwibukwa:</p>
> “Uko Data yankunze, ni ko nanjye nabakunze: nuko rero mugume mu rukundo rwanjye” (Yohana 15:9).

Wasobanura ute umushyikirano ufitanye n'Imana muri iki gihe? Waba ari nta makemwa kandi wimbitse? Mbese utanga igihe ugashyiraho n’umuhati muri uwo mushyikirano, wiga Ijambo ryayo ryahumetswe kandi ukaganira na Yo nk’Inshuti yawe? Niba ari ko biri se, ubikora mu gihe kingana iki? Nanone kandi, mbese, wumva uhatiwe kubwira abandi ibyerekeranye n'umushyikirano ufitanye n'Imana bitewe n'uko ari wo mushyikirano uhebuje kuruta indi yose mu mibereho yawe? Cyangwa se umushyikirano ufitanye n'Imana wagiye wangirika uko igihe cyagiye gihita? Ni aho biri, kandi rimwe na rimwe ujya uhora wigenzura, ariko uvugishije ukuri, uwo mushyikirano ntugikomeye nka mbere. Cyangwa se, wenda, waba uri ahantu runaka hagati y'ibyo, nk’ibyo Bibiliya yita "akazuyaze" (Ibyahishuwe 3:16).

Mbese, waba warigeze wibaza niba abamarayika batekereza impamvu tutabaho imibereho irangwa no kuramya Umukiza n'Umucunguzi wacu dufite imitima yahagizwa n’inyota n'intekerezo zifuza cyane kurushaho kwegera Imana buri munsi? Ariko mu by'ukuri, kugirana umushyikirano n'Imana bihindura ibintu byose – haba muri iki gihe ndetse no mu gihe cy'iteka ryose.

Muri iki cyumweru, nimutyo dusuzume uko umushyikirano dufitanye n'Imana umeze muri iki gihe, kandi turebe inama Bibiliya iduha. Mu by'ukuri, ntidushobora kuva aho turi ngo tujye ahantu heza tutabanje kwisuzuma uko turi maze tukumva umuti Yesu aduha.
