---
title:  Umugeni
date:   23/06/2026
---

Ubwo yari yarahungiye ku kirwa cya Patimo, intumwa Yohana yahawe iyerekwa ry’uko bizamera igihe tuzaba twongeye guhuzwa n’Imana ngo tubane by’iteka ryose.

`Soma Ibyahishuwe 21:9-11. Ni ikihe kigereranyo cyatanzwe, kandi utekereza ko ari ukubera iki cyakoreshejwe?`

Umugeni ni mwiza, kandi ku munsi w’ubukwe bwe, buri wese aba ashaka kumureba. Umunsi w’ubukwe ni igihe cy’icyemezo gikomeye cy’ubuzima bushya bwo kubana k’umugeni n’umukwe, kandi ibyo bizaba ukuri ku mushyikirano tuzagirana n’Imana ubwo Yesu azaba agarutse.

Yesu amaze igihe adutegurira aho tuzaba (Yohana 14:1-3), ahantu heza hatangaje cyane kuhasobanura. Mu by’ukuri “imvugo ya mwene muntu ntishobora gusobanura agaciro k’ingororano izahabwa abakiranutsi. Uzayimenya wenyine ni uzayihabwa. Nta bwenge bw’umwana w’umuntu bwabasha gusobanura ubwiza bwa Paradizo y’Imana.”-Ellen G. White, _Intambara Ikomeye_, p. 548.

Nubwo tudashobora gusobanukirwa by’ukuri uko ijuru rishya n’isi nshya bizaba bimeze, Imana yahaye Yohana iyerekwa ry’aho hantu kugira ngo dutegerezanye amatsiko “ubukwe” bugiye kuba bidatinze. Mu by’ukuri, turarikirwa “guhoza umutima ku biri hejuru, aho kuyahoza ku biri mu isi” (Abakolosayi 3:2).

Imana iri gutegura uyu munsi mu buryo bwitondewe, kandi ntabwo ishaka ko ubu “bukwe” buzadutungura (soma Matayo 22:1-14, Matayo 25:1-13).

Abatuye isi n’ijuru ni bo bagize iteraniro rizabona iki gikorwa kiba, kandi turi bamwe mu bantu bagize izingiro ry’iyi nkuru. Tuzifatanya n’“umugeni,” wa murwa Yesu azatujyanamo ubwo azaba agarutse. Igitangaje, ubwoko bw’Imana (abera) na bo bitwa umugeni (soma Ibyahishuwe 19:7), ahari ni ukubera ko bari mu rurembo rwera, Yerusalemu nshya, rumanuka ruva mu ijuru ku Mana, rwiteguwe nk’uko umugeni arimbishirizwa umugabo we” (Ibyahishuwe 21:2).

Ubu busobanuro bushimishije “bw’Ururembo Rwera bwerekana ko hari isano ikomeye hagati y’ubwoko bw’Imana n’umurwa, kubera ko ibyo byombi byitwa “umugeni.” Bibiliya yerekana ubusobanuro burambuye bw’“Umurwa Wera, Yerusalemu Nshya, ari yo murwa mukuru w’ubwami, yitwa “umugeni, umugore w’umwana w’Intama.” -Ellen G. White, _Intambara Ikomeye_, pp. 353-354.

`Soma Ibyahishuwe 21:9-27. Kuki ibyo bikomeye cyane kuri twe kubitekereza ubu? Mbese ni mu buhe buryo dutangira kuzuza mu bitekerezo byacu ibyasezeranywe aha?`
