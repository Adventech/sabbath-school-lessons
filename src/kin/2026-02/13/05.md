---
title:  Kurikira Umwana w’Intama
date:   24/06/2026
---

Mbese hari ubwo wigeze ubazwa ikintu kiruta ibindi byose wifuza kuzabona mu bihe by’iteka ryose? Nubaza abana iki kibazo, bashobora kuzagusubiza bati: “Ndifuza kugenda ku mugongo w’ingwe,” “gutsuka ku ijosi ry’inyamaswa yitwa agasumbashyamba,” cyangwa “kuguruka nkajya mu mibumbe itandukanye.” Uramutse ubajije umuhungu w’ingimbi, ashobora kuvuga ati: “Kutongera gukora umukoro w’ishuri” cyangwa “gutemberera mu ijuru ndi kumwe n’inshuti tutagira ikitwangiza.” Kandi uramutse ubajije itsinda ry’abantu bakuru, bashobora kuvuga bati: “Kuba ahantu hatari uburibwe, umubabaro,”cyangwa urupfu cyangwa bati: “Ni ukongera guhura n’abo twakundaga.”Ibi bisubizo byose ni byiza kandi ni iby’ukuri kandi hari byinshi dufite amatsiko yo kuzabona  mu ijuru rishya no mu isi nshya. Iby’iteka ryose bigurumana mu mitima yacu, kandi tuzi ko hagomba kuba hari ikintu kiziyongera kuri ubu buzima bukaruta rwose ubwo dufite hano muri iki gihe.

`Ni iyihe migisha yindi dushobora gukumbura kuzabona mu bihe by’iteka ryose? Soma Yesaya 25:8; Ibyahishuwe 7:17 n’ Ibyahishuwe 21:4.`

Mu by’ukuri umugisha w’ijuru ukomeye kurusha iyindi yose uzaba uwo kureba Yesu ndetse no kumushimira imbonankubone, tumushimira ibyo yadukoreye kuri iyi si yacumuye. Tuzifuza kumuhundagazaho amashimwe yacu no kuramya kwacu kuko yadukijije, binyuze mu mibabaro ye ku musaraba, akadukiza urupfu rw’iteka ryose.

“Umwana w’intama watambwe akwiriye guhabwa ubutware n’ubutunzi n’ubwenge, n’imbaraga no guhimbazwa n’icyubahiro, n’ishimwe” (Ibyahishuwe 5:12).

Yohana Umubatiza yagaragaje Yesu nk’ “Umwana w’Intama w’Imana” (Yohana 1:36). Babiri mu bigishwa ba Yohana bumvise aya magambo maze bakurikira Yesu (Yohana 1:37), kandi Ibyahishuwe 14:4 hatubwira ko natwe dukwiriye gukora nk’ibyo. “Abo ni bo bakurikira umwana w’intama aho ajya hose” (Ibyahishuwe 14:4). Nyamara, niba dushaka kumukurikira mu ijuru, tugomba kubanza kumukurikira hano ku isi.

Yesu, umwana w’Intama, ni n’Umwungeri wacu, kandi ni We utoyobora mu nzira zacu mu buryo budashobora gukorwa n’undi uwo ari we wese. Ibi biduhamiriza ko nubwo duhanganye n’ingorane zo mu bihe bigoranye twisangamo, Yesu ntazigera areka kutuyobora, kugeza no mu ijuru. Ibyahishuwe 7:17 haragira hati: “Kuko Umwana w’Intama uri hagati y’intebe y’ubwami, azabaragira akabuhira amasōko y’amazi y’ubugingo.” Nk’ubwoko bwe, intama ze, tuzakurikira Yesu mu ijuru, buri gihe duhore dushaka kuba imbere ye. Kimwe mu biranga ubwoko bw’Imana ni uko “Izina rye rizaba ryanditswe mu ruhanga rwazo.” Ni ukuvuga ko, tuzahora ari we dutekereza.

`Fata umwanya uririmbe indirimbo ya 343 mu gitabo cy’indirimbo zo guhimbaza Imana cy’itorero ry’Abadiventisiti b’Umunsi wa Karindwi, ivuga ngo “Shimwa Ntama y’Imana” maze amagambo ayirimo uyagire isengesho ryawe bwite uyu munsi.`
