---
title:  Amaherezo, Tuzamubona Amaso ku Maso
date:   22/06/2026
---

Twaremewe kuba bugufi bw’Imana (Itangiriro 2:7). Uhereye igihe Imana yaremaga inyokomuntu, yatanze icyo ari cyo cyose kugira ngo yongere isubize mu buryo umushyikirano twari dufitanye na Yo (Yohana 3:16).Yashyize iby’iteka ryose mu mitima yacu, ariko abantu ntibashobora gusobanukirwa icyo Imana yakoze kuva mu ntangiriro ukagera ku iherezo (Umubwiriza 3:11).Tugira uruhare mu ntambara ikomeye ibera ahatuzengurutse-ndetse iyo ntambara ibera muri twe ariko akenshi ntabwo dutuza umwanya uhagije ngo dutekereze  ikiguzi gikomeye cyatanzwe ku bwacu kugira ngo twongere kugarurwa mu mushyikirano Imana ishaka ko tugirana na Yo. Kenshi cyane, twisanga twizingiweho n’intambara n’ibigeragezo byo mu isi, maze tukibagirwa ko “iwacu ari mu ijuru, aho dutegereje ko Umukiza azava ari we Mwami Yesu Kristo uzahindura uyu mubiri wo gucishwa bugufi kwacu akawushushanya n'umubiri w'ubwiza bwe, kuko afite imbaraga zo kumubashisha kwigandurira byose’ (Abafilipi 3:20, 21).

Uko isi yihutira gusatira iherezo, tuzi ko umunsi umwe agacu gato kirabura kazagaragara mu ijuru iburasirazuba. Uko kazagenda karushaho kutwegera, ni ko tuzagenda turushaho kubona ko kuri ako gacu hazaba hicaye “usa n’Umwana w’umuntu, wambaye ikamba ry’izahabu ku mutwe kandi afite umuhoro utyaye mu ntoki ze” (Ibyahishuwe 14:14). Yesu azaba ashagawe n’abamarayika ibihumbi byinshi (Matayo 25:31), kandi amaso yose azamubona (Ibyahishuwe 1:7). Ubwo azaba amanuka, tuzumva ijwi rye, guhamagara kw’impanda y’Imana, maze ibituro by’abasinziriye muri Kristo bizakingurwa kuko ari bo bazabanza kuzuka (1 Abatesalonike 4:16). Bazamenya ijwi ry’uzaba ubahamagara (Yohana 5:28).

`Ikizakurikiraho ni iki? Soma 1 Abatesalonike 4:17. Amaherezo, ni iki Pawulo yandika mu Abafilipi 2:10, 11 kizumvikanira mu isanzure?`

Mbega igitekerezo gitangaje kandi cyiza cyane! Umunsi umwe tuzabona Yesu— mu by’ukuri tuzamubona rwose. Tuzumva ijwi rye, kandi tuzatura ko ari Umwami. Uwo twasomye mu nyandiko, uwo twasenze, uwo twabwiye abandi; uwo imitima yacu yakumburaga…tuzamubona rwose amaso ku maso. Dushobora kubyizera rwose tudashidikanya, kuko Imana ari iyo kwiringirwa, kandi amasezerano yayo ni ay’ukuri (Ibyahishuwe 22:6).

Icyo gihe, ubwo impanda zizavuga maze amaso y’abantu bose akabona Yesu, tuzamenya ko kumutegereza byari bifite agaciro. Buri sengesho ryose ryo kwihangana, buri mwanya wose twagize nyambere igihe tumarana na we, buri gihe cyose twamuvugiye dushize amanga, buri kigeragezo cyose—byose bizasozwa no kubona mu maso he (Ibyahishuwe 22:4).
