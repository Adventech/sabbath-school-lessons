---
title:  Ibindi byo Kwigwa no Kuzirikanwa
date:   26/06/2026
---

“Nitutakira iyobokamana rya Kristo binyuze mu kurya Ijambo ry’Imana, ntituzemererwa kwinjira mu murwa w’Imana. Kubera ko twabeshejweho n’ibyokurya byo ku isi, kubera ko twatoje ipfa ryacu iby’isi, ntibyashoboka ko tuba dukwiriye kuba mu bikari byo mu ijuru; ntabwo twashobora kwishimira umwuka mwiza ukwiriye hose mu ijuru. Amajwi y’abamarayika ndetse n’injyana y’inanga zabo ntibyatunyura. Ubwenge bwo mu ijuru bwaba inshoberamahanga ku ntekerezo zacu. Dukeneye kugira inzara n’inyota byo gukiranuka kwa Kristo; dukeneye guhabwa ishusho no gutunganywa n’imbaraga ihindura y’ubuntu bwe, kugira ngo tube dukwiriye kuzaba mu muryango mugari w’abamarayika bo mu ijuru…

“Icyo gihe amahanga nta yandi mategeko azagira uretse amategeko y’ijuru. Bose bazaba umuryango umwe wishimye kandi wunze ubumwe, wambaye umwambaro wo guhimbaza no gushima…inyenyeri zo mu ruturuturu zizaririmbira hamwe, kandi abana b’Imana bazatera hejuru ku bw’ibyishimo, mu gihe Imana na Kristo bazafatanya gutangaza ngo: “Nta cyaha kizabaho ukundi, nta n’urupfu ruzabaho ukundi.”

“Dushaka kugira akamenyero ko kuganira iby’ijuru, ijuru ryiza. Nimuganire ku bugingo buzakomeza kubaho igihe cyose Imana izaba iriho, icyo gihe muzibagirwa ibigeragezo n’ingorane bito muhura na byo. Nimureke imitima yerekezwe ku Mana.”- Ellen G. White, _The Faith I Live By_, p. 363.

**IBIBAZO:**

`Tega amatwi cyangwa usome iby’iyerekwa rya Ellen G. White ubwo yerekwaga ijuru, biboneka mu gitabo cy’Inyandiko z’Ibanze, p.35-43. Ni iki kirushaho kugutangaza muri ibyo?`

`Ni iki muri iki cyigisho cy’iki gihembwe ushaka kwibuka kurusha ibindi kugira ngo ukomeze kugirana n’Imana umushyikirano ufite imbaraga kugeza ubwo uzabona Yesu amaso ku maso?`

`Ni nde mu mibereho yawe ukeneye kumva iby’ibyiringiro by’ijuru? Iyemeze kuzabimugezaho vuba cyane bishoboka. Ibuka: ntabwo ushobora kubagezaho ibyiringiro na we ubwawe udafite.`

**Inshamake:** Ubwo dukomeje guhanga amaso ku ntego, ndasaba Imana ngo “twiringire ibi rwose, yuko iyatangiye umurimo mwiza muri twe izawurangiza kugeza ku munsi wa Yesu Kristo” (Abafilipi1:6). Imana yatangije umushyikirano ifitanye nawe, kandi izawusohoza. Ndasaba ngo dukurire mu rukundo no mu kwizera ubwo dutegereje wa munsi, mu gihe turuhukira mu gukiranuka kwa Kristo duhabwa ku bwo kwizera.
