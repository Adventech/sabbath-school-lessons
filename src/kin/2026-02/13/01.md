### Aho icyigisho cy'iki cyumweru gishingiye:
Zaburi 80; 2 Abatesalonike 4:17; Ibyahishuwe 21:9-27; Yesaya 25:8; Ibyahishuwe 7:17; Ibyahishuwe 21:4; Yohana 6:44.

> <p>Icyo kwibukwa:</p>
> “Bakundwa, ubu turi abana b’Imana ariko uko tuzamera ntikurerekanwa. Icyakora icyo tuzi ni uko Yesu niyerekanwa, tuzasa na we kuko tuzamureba uko ari” (1 Yohana 3:2).

Mbese ni iki ejo hazaza hakuzigamiye? Ni ibiki bizaba mu gihe kiri imbere? Bishobora gusa n’ibihurije hamwe kuba biteye ubwoba, bishimishije, biteye ubwoba kandi ari byiza cyane. Mumenye ko Yesu ari uwo kwiringirwa kandi amagambo ye na yo ni ay’ukuri (Ibyahishuwe 3:14). Nyamara imbere yacu hazakomeza kubaho ibihe by’imivurungano (Matayo 24:21, 22), ariko yasezeranye ko atazigera agusiga cyangwa ngo aguhāne (Abaheburayo13:5). Azakora rwose ibyo yasezeranye gukora—uko yabikoze ni ko azabikora (Abaheburayo 10:23). Kandi “uwihangana akageza imperuka ni we uzakizwa” (Matayo 24:13).

Hatitawe ku minsi dusigaje hano ku isi, dukwiriye guhanga amaso yacu kuri Yesu, tukamutumbira. Buri gihe si ko biba byoroshye mu isi ishaka gutwara intekerezo zacu, ariko dushobora kuvuga nka Dawidi tuti: “Amaso yanjye ahora yerekeye ku Uwiteka, Kuko azakura ibirenge byanjye mu kigoyi” (Zaburi 25:15).

Muri iki cyumweru, nimureke twige ibyerekeranye n ‘ingororano y’ijuru (Matayo 5:12, Ibyahishuwe 22:12), uko ijuru rizaba rimeze; kandi amaherezo, turebere hamwe uburyo bizaba bitangaje kubana n’Uwaturemye, uwadukunze kugeza ubwo adupfira, uwaducunguye akadukura mu byaha byacu, kandi akaba ari hafi kugaruka. Dukwiriye gukomeza kwizera kwacu kugeza icyo gihe.
