---
title:  Kubaho mur’iki Gihe
date:   21/06/2026
---

Iyo turebye ahatuzengurutse, tubona isi yuzuye byinshi n’imibabaro kandi ibimenyetso Yesu yavuze bigenda bisohora imbere y’amaso yacu. Intambara n’impuha z’intambara, ishyanga ritera irindi shyanga, inzara, ibyorezo, ibishyitsi, no gutotozwa (Matayo 24:6-11) ibyo biri kubera ahatuzengurutse kandi bias n’ibirushaho kwiyongera uko ibihe bihita ibindi bikaza. Ni byo koko, turi mu bihe bikomeye—ibihe bidusaba kugirana n’Imana umushyikirano uhoraho.

Tubwirwa ngo: “Iherezo rya byose riri bugufi. Nuko mugire ubwenge mwirinda ibisindisha, mubone uko mugira umwete wo gusenga.” (1 Petero 4:7). Niba ari uko bimeze rero, ndetse uyu munsi bikaba birushaho kwiyongera, ubu ni igihe cyo gukomeza no kongera imbaraga mu mushyikirano wihariye ugirana n’Imana. Kandi hatitawe ku buryo igihe gihita, ubuzima bwa buri wese bumara igihe gito, uko twaba turama kose. “Nimwumve yemwe abavuga muti: ‘Uyu munsi cyangwa ejo tuzajya mu mudugudu w’ibunaka tumareyo umwaka, dutunde tubone indamu nyamara mutazi ibizaba ejo. Mbese ubugingo bwanyu ni iki? Muri igihu kiboneka umwanya muto kigaherako kigatamūka” (Yakobo 4:13, 14). Tuzi uburyo uwo muburo ari ukuri. Mwebwe muri gusoma aya magambo ubu mushobora kuba mutakiriho mbere yuko uyu munsi urangira. Uyu ni umugabane w’ukuri kubabaje ko kuba mu isi yacumuye. None se ubwo si ingenzi kumenya uko umushyikirano dufitanye n’Imana uhagaze ndetse no kubaho buri gihe tuzi neza ko dukeneye Imana n’ubuntu bwayo bukiza?

Zaburi 80 igaragaza irarika rituruka ku Mana. Soma icyo gice kandi by’umwihariko, uzirikane imirongo Zaburi 80:2-4,15-18,19,20 maze ahari ijambo “twe” urisimbuze ijambo “njye.” Hatitawe ku buryo igihe, ahantu ndetse n’icyerekezo iyi Zaburi yanditswemo bitandukanye n’ibyawe, ni mu buhe buryo ushobora kwisanisha n’ibivugwa muri yo?

Twese dukeneye ububyutse mu mibereho yacu. Biroroshye cyane kunyurwa n’uko tubayeho cyangwa tukibagirwa ibyo Imana yadukoreye ndetse n’ibyo idukorera. Kuki umwizera w’indahemuka, nubwo yaba ahanganye n’ingorane, atasenga asaba ikintu nk’iki ngo: “Umurikishe mu maso hawe natwe turakira” (Zaburi 80: 20)? Igihe wemera icyo Yesu yagukoreye, igihe uzi ko ibyaha byawe byababariwe kandi ko wambitswe gukiranuka kwe gutunganye, ubarwaho ku bwo kwizera, uba ushobora kumenya ko wakirijwe muri we.

`Usobanukiwe ute icyo “kumurika”mu maso hawe kw’Imana bivuze, by’umwihariko mu mujyo wo kumenya ko ugukiranuka kwayo konyine ari ko kugukiza?`
