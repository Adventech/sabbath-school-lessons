---
title:  Inyifato mu Gihe cyo Gusenga
date:   04/05/2026
---

Igihe hari ikitagenda neza mu mibereho yacu, abenshi muri twe bahamagara inshuti ya bugufi bakakiganiraho. Iyo dufite amakuru ashimishije, dushaka umuntu tuyabwira. Ibyo dushobora no kubikora ku Mana. “Gusenga ni ukugururira Imana umutima nk’uko twashyikirana n’inshuti magara”—Ellen G. White, _Kugana Yesu_, p.96.

Isengesho ntirituma dukomeza gushyikirana n’Imana gusa, ahubwo rinabwira umwanzi (Satani) uwo twiyeguriye uwo ari we. Igihe dupfukamye mugitondo dusenga, ni nko gutangariza ku mugaragaro imbaraga z’umwijima ko duhisemo Imana uyu munsi. Si ibyo gusa, ahubwo Imana inohereza abamarayika bakaba mu ruhande rwacu igihe dusenga, maze tugakomera kandi tugatwikīrwa ngo umwanzi ukorera mu mwijima atatugeraho (Zaburi 91).

Igikorwa kigaragarira amaso cyo gupfukama tugandukiye Imana cyerekana inyifato yo kwicisha bugufi. Bitandukanye mu buryo runaka no gusenga wicaye ku ntebe cyangwa uryamye ku gitanda, nubwo dushobora gusenga tumeze dutyo. Nyamara igihe dupfukamye imbere y’Imana, tuba twerekana ko twiteguye gukorera Imana n’imitima yacu yose, maze amagambo yacu agahamya ko ari Yo Mwami ukomeye naho twe tukaba abana bayo yaremye.

`Soma imirongo ikurikira yo muri Bibiliya maze wite ku mibereho y’aba bantu bapfukamye igihe babaga basenga: Daniyeli 6:10; Luka 22:41; Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 7:60; Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 9: 40; Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 20:36.`

Gusenga umuntu ahagaze byari imigenzereze rusange mu bihe bya Bibiliya (2 Ngoma 20:5, 6, 13; 1 Samweli 1:26; Yobu 30;20; Luka 18:11). Na none kandi Bibiliya iduha ingero z’abantu basenze bicaye (2 Samweli 7:18). Abandi bikubise hasi bubamye imbere y’Imana, bubika imitwe ku butaka—nubwo muri rusange ubu buryo butakunze guhuzwa no gusenga, ahubwo bwabaga ari ukugaragaza ko ugandukiye ukuruta (1 Abami 1:47; Mariko 14:35).

Mbese inyifato wowe ukunze kugira igihe usenga ni iyihe? Bibiliya ntidusaba gusenga mu buryo runaka bwihariye, nyamara inyifato tugira ni ingenzi, kuko zigaragaza uburyo twubashyemo, ibyiyumvo biri imbere mu mitima yacu, ndetse n’icyifuzo cyacu cyo kwiyegurira Imana rwose. Hari abantu batabasha gupfukama, bityo biragaragara ko icy’ingenzi ari uburyo umutima umeze. Niba ushobora gupfukama ariko ukaba udakunda kubikora, ni ukubera iki utagerageza gupfukama ubutaha maze ukareba impinduka iza mu gihe umarana n’Imana?

`Bibiliya iturarikira “gusenga ubudasiba” (1 Abatesalonike 5;17), bisobanuye gukomeza gusenga (Abakolosayi 4:2) no gushikama (Abaroma 12:12). Muri iki gihe, igihe uhagaze, wicaye, uryamye, cyangwa ugenda, erekeza ibitekerezo byawe ku Mana maze uvugane na Yo nk’inshuti yawe. Tangira nonaha.`
