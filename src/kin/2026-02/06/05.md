---
title:  Mose, Umuyobozi Wubahaga Imana
date:   06/05/2026
---

Nubwo bigaragara ko Henoki yari afitanye n’Imana umushyikirano wa bugufi, dufite amakuru arushijeho kuba menshi ku bijyanye n’umushyikirano Mose yari afitanye n’Imana, ndetse dushobora no gusoma inkuru nyinshi z’ibiganiro Mose yagiye agirana na Yo. Uko turushaho kugendana na Mose mu bihe by’imbaraga no mu bihe by’intege nke byaranze imibereho yoroheje y’uyu muyobozi, tubona kenshi ko umugabane umwe w’ingenzi mu buzima bwe ndetse n’ibanga ryo gutsinda kwe nk’umuyobozi wubahaga Imana, kwari ukuganira n’Imana no kuguma mu mushyikirano yari afitanye na Yo.

`Soma mu Kuva 33: 15-23. Ni iki cyari gikubiye mu kiganiro Mose yagiranye n’Imana  kandi baganiriye mu buhe uryo?`

Ibaze uko kuvugana n’Imana bishobora byari bimeze maze ukumva ijwi ryayo mu buryo bweruye. Ni igitangaza kumva ukuntu Abisirayeli batashatse kuvugana n’Imana ubwabo ahubwo bagasaba Mose ngo avugane na Yo mu cyimbo cyabo (Kuva 20:18-21). Nyamara, Imana yari yarateguriye Mose kuvugana na yo, uhereye igihe yavuganiraga na we mu gihuru cyaka umuriro, kuri uwo musozi. Nubwo dusoma andi masengesho y’umwihariko ya Mose, tubona ko asa n’ukomeza kuba imbere y’Imana, ayisaba kubayobora ndetse akanasabira ubwoko yari ayoboye.

`Inshuro ebyiri, Mose yasabiye abagize umuryango. Mbese byari byagenze bite ngo abasabire, kandi byari kugenda bite iyo ataza gutera intambwe ngo azibe icyo cyuho?`

- **Aroni**: Kuva 32:1-14, 31-34; Gutegeka kwa Kabiri 9:20

- **Miriyamu**: Kubara 12:13

Igitangaje mu buryo bwihariye cyerekeranye no gusabira Miriyamu imbabazi ni uko Mose ari we wari wagiriwe ishyari. Mu buryo bworoshye, Mose yashoboraga kureka Imana igaha Miriyamu na Aroni igihano cyari kibakwiriye. Aho kugenza atyo, yabaye umuntu wihutira kubabarira mushiki we no kumusabira imbabazi ngo akire. Mbega ishusho ifite imbaraga igaragaza Ubuntu bw’Imana bubabarira abanyabyaha igaragara hano mu bikorwa bya Mose!

`Soma Matayo 5:44 n’Abakolosayi 3:13. Ni mu buhe buryo ushobora kwiga gukora ibyo ubwiwe hano? Kuki ari ingenzi ko ubikora?`
