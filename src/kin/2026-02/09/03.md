---
title:  Ibihome mu Mushyikirano Mfitanye n’Imana
date:   25/05/2026
---

Muri Bibiliya hari ubutumwa bwinshi buvuga ibijyanye n’umushyikirano tugirana n’Imana n’inzitizi zitubuza gukurira muri Kristo. Nimureke twite kuri aya magambo Pawulo na Yesu bavuze:

**“Nuko rero uwibwira ko ahagaze yirinde atagwa”** (1 Abakorinto 10:12). Nka Samusoni, kwiyiringira bizakugusha.

**“Ntukavuze ihembe imbere yawe nk’uko indyarya zibigenza…, ngo bashimwe n’abantu”** (Matayo 6:2). Rekeraho kubwira buri wese ukuntu uri mwiza! Ba umuntu wiyoroheje nk’uko Yesu yari yoroheje.

**“Jyeweho ndababwira yuko umuntu wese ureba umugore akamwifuza, aba amaze gusambana na we mu mutima we. Ijisho ryawe ry’iburyo nirigushuka rikakugusha, urinogore urite kure”** (Matayo 5:28, 29). Kora ibishoboka byose kugira ngo ukure irari mu mutima wawe, kuko iyo ari inkomyi mu mushyikirano ugirana n’Imana.

**“Ntimugacire abandi urubanza mu mitima yanyu, kugira ngo namwe mutazarucirwa; kuko urubanza muca ari rwo muzacirwa namwe”** (Matayo 7:1, 2). Rekeraho kunegura abandi no kubacira urubanza. Imana ni Yo mucamanza; yirekere umurimo wayo (1 Abakorinto 4:5).

**“Ariko jyeweho ndababwira nti: mukunde abanzi banyu, [musabire ababavuma, mugirire neza ababanga] musabire [ababagirira nabi n’] ababarenganya”** (Matayo 5:44). Nimurekeraho kwanga abanzi banyu. Igihe utekereza ibibi ku bakugiriye nabi, bihita bizana inkomyi mu mushyikirano ufitanye n’Imana. Ibiri amambu, tangira usabire abanzi bawe, maze urebe ukuntu bidahindura gusa uburyo ugendana n’Imana, ahubwo bizanahindura umushyikirano ufitanye n’abandi.

**“Ariko jyewe ndababwira yuko umuntu wese urakarira mwene se akwiriye guhanwa n’abacamanza”** (Matayo 5:22). Ahari umaze igihe witsindishiriza ku byo gutonganya abantu ba bugufi bwawe. Ni mu buhe buryo uburakari bwawe buzana ibibazo mu mushyikirano ugirana n’Imana, tutaretse n’abo warakariye? Aha ni hamwe mu hantu hatuma dusitara.

`Yesu yatubwiye ibyo dukwiriye gukora igihe ibiganza byacu, ibirenge byacu, ndetse n’amaso yacu bitumye ducumura. Mbese ni iki Yesu yatuburiraga kwirinda? Soma Mariko 9:42-48.`

Guca ukuboko kwawe cyangwa ikirenge cyawe cyangwa kunogora ijisho bitewe n’uko riri kugucumuza, ni ikintu kirenze. Ni ko byari bikwiriye kumera. Ariko uko ni ko Yesu abona mu buryo butajenjetse icyaha ndetse n’ingaruka kigira ku mibereho yacu. Mbese mu buryo butajenjetse wowe uri kugifata ute?
