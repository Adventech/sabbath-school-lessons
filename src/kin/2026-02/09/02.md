---
title:  Ibiringaza n’Ibishuko
date:   24/05/2026
---

`Soma ibishuko Samusoni yahuye na byo mu Abacamanza 14 ndetse n’ Abacamanza 16:1, 4, 16, 17. Nubwo yari yahamagawe n’Imana ku bw’umugambi usobanutse, Samusoni yakoreye Imana ariko akajya anatsindwa n’igishuko. Mbese ingaruka zaje mu buzima bwe zitwigisha iki?`

Intambara ikomeye iriho, kandi twese iratureba. Urugamba rubera mu isanzure rwatangiriye mu ijuru ubu ruri kurwanirwa mu mibereho ya buri wese muri twe.

Satani azi ko agomba gukurura ibintu byose yaronse muri ibi bihe turimo, mbere yuko Yesu agaruka, kugira ngo atubuze kugirana n’Imana umushyikirano. Ahari warangajwe n’ikintu gishobora kuba kitifitemo ububi muri cyo, nyamara kikaba kigutwara igihe kinini cyane ndetse n’imbaraga nyinshi ku buryo gisigira Imana bike cyane. Wenda ni akazi, imbuga nkoranyambaga, guhaha, imikino, cyangwa ibiryo. Nitwirebaho tubyitondeye, tuzabona ko kugira ibyo ukora mu buryo bukabije n’ubusumbane bushobora kugaragara muri ibyo bintu bishobora gutuma Imana n’abandi bantu bahabwa agahe gato cyane. Umwanzi azi intege nke zacu zose n’ubwoko bw’ibintu biturangaza bikatubuza kugira igihe tumara turi kumwe n’Imana. Dukwiriye kwibuka kubanza gushaka Imana (Matayo 6:33) mbere yo kwirukira mu byacu.

Yesu asobanukiwe uko tumeze, nyamara araducyaha ku bw’ibyo turarikira (Ibyahishuwe 3:14-22). Nubwo ari Imana, yari n’umuntu wagiraga umunaniro nk’uko tuwugira (Yohana 4:6). Yari azi imihati y’ubuzima, nk’uko natwe tuyigira, nyamara yajyaga yiyufura akajya ahiherereye agasenga Se (Luka 5:16; Luka 6:12; Mariko 1:35; Matayo 14:23). Yari azi ko kumara igihe ari kumwe na Se byamurutiraga ibintu byose yashoboraga gukora ngo yongere kuronka imbaraga zo guhangana n’ibishuko. Natwe ni bwo buryo buhebuje ubundi bwose twakoresha bukatugeza ku nsinzi.

Samusoni yaraguye kuko yatekerezaga ko afite imbaraga. Yishingikirizaga ku mbaraga ze ngo atsinde ibishuko. Buri munsi, buri wese muri twe asakirana n’intambara zo kurwanya icyaha mu gihe umwanzi w’imitima agerageza guca intege no kurimbura isano tugirana n’Imana. Azi integenke zacu maze akaziheraho kugira ngo acogoze isano dufitanye n’Imana kandi azane igishinja ndetse no kwiyumvamo ko tudakwiriye, ibyo byose akabikora agira ngo atujyane kure y’Imana. Umwanzi akora uko ashoboye ngo ayobye ibitekerezo byacu, intego zacu, ndetse n’ibikorwa byacu kugira ngo abone ahantu runaka yihisha mu mibereho yacu.  Ariko mwibuke ko kwizera kwacu kuzadufasha guhagarara tudatsinzwe, kandi ko kwizera guheshwa no kumva ijambo ry’Imana.

`Ni iki uri kurwana na cyo nonaha? Ni mu buhe buryo ijambo ry’Imana rishobora kugufasha muri uyu mwanya?`
