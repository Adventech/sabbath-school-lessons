---
title:  Imenye Ubwawe
date:   13/04/2026
---

Abagabo babiri bagiye ku rusengero gusenga. Umwe ni umukuru w’itorero wubashywe uhagarara imbere mbere y’uko gahunda z’itorero zitangira. Ahagarara imbere y’iteraniro kugira ngo bamurebe. Asenga aranguruye, ashima Imana kubw’ineza imugirira. Undi mugabo, ni umuntu usanzwe utazwi cyane mu muryango w’abantu, ahagaze inyuma cyane y’urusengero. Amaso ye arebana ibirorirori kubera amarira arimo kubw’uburemere bw’umutwaro w’ibyaha bye. Apfukama inyuma mu mfuruka y’urusengero, yongorera abwira Imana ababaye cyane agira ati: “Mana ngirira imbabazi kuko ndi umunyabyaha!”

`Soma muri Luka 18:9-14. Uratekereza iki kuri abo bagabo babiri? Ni iki Yesu yatekereje? Ni irihe somo ry’ingenzi twese twabigiraho?`

Biratworohera twese kwishyira hejuru. Rimwe na rimwe bihinduka nk’umuco ko abantu bamenya ibyo twagezeho n’uburyo turi beza. Ariko ibyo bintu, ubwabyo, ntacyo bihindura ku buryo tugaragara mu maso y’ijuru. Muby’ukuri, bihabanye cyane n’ibyo dushobora gutekereza, kubera ko “umuntu wese wishyira hejuru azacishwa bugufi; kandi uwicisha bugufi, azashyirwa hejuru” (Luka 18:14). Na Yesu atugira inama yo guhitamo kwicara mu myanya y’inyuma maze ukareka uwagutoye akaba ari we ukuzamura igihe abonako bikwiriye (Luka 14:8-10).

Ubwo bwami bwo kwiyoroshya Yesu atwigisha bunyuranye n’ubwo twe tuba twiteze. “Umuntu wiyiziho ko ari umunyabyaha niwe wenyine Kristo ashobora gukiza.”-Ellen G. White, _Imigani ya Kristo_, p. 90 (2024).

Iyo tumaze kwimenyaho ko turi abanyabyaha kandi ko dukeneye Kristo kurenza ibindi byose, dushobora kuza aho ari dufite ibyiringiro by’uko “nitwatura ibyaha byacu, niyo yo kwizerwa kandi ikiranukira kutubabarira ibyaha byacu, no kutwezaho gukiranirwa kose” (1 Yohana 1:9).

Uko turushaho kwegera Kristo, niko turushaho kwimenyaho ikibi no kumva ko tudatunganye. “Hari uburyo bumwe rukumbi bushobora gutuma umuntu yimenya mu buryo nyakuri. Tugomba kwitegereza Kristo. Kutamumenya nibyo bitera abantu kwishyira hejuru mu byo gukiranuka kwabo.” -Ellen G. White, _Imigani ya Kristo_, p. 91 (2024).

None se, Imana ibona ite abibone (abishyira hejuru)? Mu rwandiko rwa 1 Petero 5:5 hatubwira ko “Imana irwanya abibone, naho abicisha bugufi ibahera ubuntu.” Ibyo birasobanutse rwose.

`Ni ryari uheruka kwiyumvamo ubuntu bw’Imana mu mibereho yawe? (Mu kuri, tugomba kwiyumvamo ubwo buntu buri munsi.) Tugomba no kugaragariza abandi ubwo buntu. Fata igihe cyo kwihererana n’Imana mu masengesho, uyisabe kugucisha bugufi, ube munsi y’ukuboko kwayo gukomeye, kugira ngo Yo ubwayo izakuzamure igihe cyayo yagennye kigeze.`
