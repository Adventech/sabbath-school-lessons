---
title:  Mose, Umugaragu Wicisha Bugufi
date:   14/04/2026
---

Ibyumba binini by’ingoro z’ubwami bwa Egiputa byabaga birimbishijwe n’imirimbo y’agaciro kenshi, ku buryo byanezezaga ababirimo bakagubwa neza. “Mose yigishwa ubwenge bwose n’Abanyegiputa, agira imbaraga mu magambo ye no mubyo akora” (Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 7:22). Imibereho yo gukomera, ubukire, no kuba ikirangirire nibyo byari bimutegereje, nyamara Mose yahisemo ikindi kintu gitandukanye n’ibyo. “Nk’umuntu wari warize iby’amateka, ubusizi, ubucurabwenge, ubugaba bw’ingabo, n’umunyamategeko, nta wari uhwanye nawe. Nyamara n’ubwo isi yari imuri imbere, yagize ubutwari bwo kwanga ibyo bamushukishaga by’ubutunzi, gukomera no kwamamara, ‘ahitamo kurenganwa n’ubwoko bw’Imana, aho kwishimira ibinezeza by’akanya gato’”. –Ellen G. White, _Abakurambere n’Abahanuzi_, p. 200 (2016).

`Ni iki Abaheburayo 11:24-26 hatubwira cyerekeye impamvu Mose yahisemo indi nzira no kwicisha bugufi?`

Kwicisha bugufi kwa Mose ko hanyuma kuratangaje, iyo urebye ukuntu yari umuntu ukomeye ndetse n’aho yakomokaga n’imibereho yakuriyemo. Nyamara, binyuze ku gikorwa cyo gukora icyaha mu buryo butunguranye (Kuva 2:12), yatakaje icyizere yari yifitemo no kumva yihagije. Ibyubahiro n’isumbwe yari afite byarayoyotse, asigara ari mu ishuri rizengurutswe n’imisozi miremire, amara imyaka 40 yigishwa n’Imana ibyo yari akeneye kumenya bizamubashisha kuyobora ishyanga ry’Imana arikura mu buretwa bwa Misiri, akarigeza mu Gihugu cy’Isezerano. Imibereho yari agenewe kubaho y’ubutware n’ubukire muri Egiputa byataye agaciro igihe Mose yabigereranyaga n’ubugingo buhoraho. Imana yari yaramuhamagariye izindi nshingano z’umwihariko, maze Mose arumvira, arayikurikira.

Ahari mu mucyo w’iyi ngingo tubasha kubyumva neza iyo dusomye amagambo aboneka mu Kubara 12:3: “Kandi uwo mugabo Mose yari umugwaneza, urusha abantu bo mu isi bose.” Mose, umwe mu bakurambere bakomeye bo muri Bibiliya, yari azwiho kwiyoroshya no kwicisha bugufi. Tekereza uburyo imibereho n’ubuyobozi bye byari kumera iyo aza kuba yaratwawe no kwishyira hejuru kubera ibintu bikomeye byaranze ubuzima bwe: igihuru cyaka umuriro kidakongoka, ibyago bya Egiputa, kwambuka Inyanja Itukura, manu yagwaga ivuye mu ijuru, kuvugana n’Imana imbona nkubone, guhabwa Amategeko Cumi, no kumva amagambo avuye mu kanwa k’Imana hanyuma yo gukubita urutare.

`Tekereza ku byaranze imibereho yawe. Mbese umuntu aramutse asobanuye imico ikuranga, wasangamo “kwicisha bugufi” cyangwa “kwiyoroshya” muri urwo rutonde? Kubera iki waba ubifite cyangwa utabifite? Ukuri ni uko tudashobora kwiyoroshya ubwacu. Icyaha kiri mu bigize imibereho yacu, ariyo mpamvu dukeneye Yesu cyane. Tega amatwi cyangwa usome amagambo agize iyi ndirimbo “295 Mpisemo Umukiza Yesu” , ayo magambo yahuze n’imibereho ya Mose hamwe n’iyawe.`
