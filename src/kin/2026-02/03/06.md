---
title:  Mumuhange Amaso
date:   16/04/2026
---

`Ongera usome muri Luka 22:27. Ni ubuhe butumwa bw’ingenzi bureba abayoboke ba Kristo bose buri ahangaha?`

Mu guhabana by’ihabya kw’icyifuzo cy’abigishwa cyo kuba mukuru n’ibyo bibwiraga ko baruta bagenzi babo, tubona urugero rwa Yesu rw’ukuri rwo kwicisha bugufi aho yavuze aya magambo: “Umukuru ni uwuhe, ni uherezwa, cyangwa ni uhereza? Si uherezwa? Ariko jyewe ndi hagati yanyu, meze nk’uhereza” (Luka 22:27). Yesu, buri munsi yagiraga icyo amarira abakene babaga bamuzengurutse kubera ko yari yuzuye impuhwe kandi akaba yarabonaga iyo mbaga y’abantu nk’intama zitagira umwungeri. Yari azi ko mwenemuntu amukeneye kurenza ikindi kintu cyo mu buzima, nubwo bake aribo basobanukiwe uko kuri. Yesu, wigomwe ijuru kugira ngo apfire inyokomuntu yiringiye ko abo bantu bazamenya icyo gikorwa cy’ubuntu bakemera irarika rye bakagirana umushyikirano nawe.

`Soma mu Abafilipi 2:3-8. Ni iki ibyo bitubwira cyerekeye uburyo tugomba kubaho mu mucyo w’umusaraba?`

Yesu yakoze byose. Yishyuye byose. Iyo dutekereje kubyo yakoze, tubona uko ari koko, n’ubutungane bwe – ntidushobora gutindiganya kubona ukudatungana kwacu, uburyo turi babi, n’uburyo tumukeneye mu mibereho yacu muri iki gihe, kurenza ibindi byose.

Iyo turebye kuri We, ibindi byose (cyane ubwacu n’imyumvire yacu yo kumva ko turi ibitangaza) birayoyoka bikaba ubusabusa. Uwo Yesu ari We, icyo yakoze, n’uburemere bw’urukundo yakunze ibiremwa Bye biduhindukira iby’ibanze n’izingiro rya byose. Nitumuhanga amaso, mu kuri inarijye yacu izayoyoka. Izina rya Yesu, mbega izina ryiza, rifite ububasha. Ni urugero ntagereranwa rwo kwicisha bugufi. Igihe imitima yize kumwakira, igihe dusobanukiwe n’ibyo yadukoreye, igihe twemereye amagambo ye y’ubugingo agashinga imizi mu bwenge bwacu, duhita tumenya uburyo turi abibone kandi turi babi. Niba abigishwa be yitoranyirije, babanye nawe bakamwigiraho, bararwanaga no kumva ko bafite isumbwe, hari icyo barushije abandi, ni mu buhe buryo twakwibeshya ko hari icyo tubarusha. Muby’ukuri, umubano wacu na Yesu ushobora gukura gusa igihe ducishijwe bugufi.

`Gena igihe runaka umarana na Yesu. Fata Bibiliya yawe, ikaramu n’aho kwandika maze urebe ahantu hatuje wicare. Saba Imana koroshya no kuvugana n’umutima wawe. Andukura amagambo ya Zaburi 138, ijambo ku rindi. Mu gihe uri kuyandika, ni irihe jambo ryagukoze ku mutima kurenza ayandi?`
