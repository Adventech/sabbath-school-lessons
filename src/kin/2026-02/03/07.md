---
title:  Ibindi byo Kwigwa no Kuzirikanwa
date:   17/04/2026
---

“Uko turushaho kwegera Yesu ni nako turushaho kubona ugutungana kw’imico ye, ni ko tuzarushaho kubona uburyo ubunyacyaha [bwacu] bukabije kuba bubi, kandi bizatuma turushaho kumva tutagomba kwishyira hejuru. Abazwi ko ari abera mu ijuru ni bo baba aba nyuma mu kwamamaza ubwiza bwabo.”- Ellen G. White, _Imigani ya Kristo_, p. 92 (2024).

“Mbere y’icyubahiro habanza kwicisha bugufi. Kugira ngo umuntu afate umwanya w’imbere mu bantu, ijuru rihitamo umukozi umeze nka Yohana Umubatiza, wemera gufata umwanya uciye bugufi imbere y’Imana. Umwigishwa wa Yesu wemera kumera nk’umwana muto ni we ushoboye kandi ukwiriye mu murimo w’Imana. Abamarayika bo mu ijuru bazakorana n’udaharanira kwikuza, ahubwo agashaka gukiza imitima . . .

“Ariko iyo abantu bishyize hejuru, bakibwira ko bakenewe kugira ngo umugambi wagutse w’Imana ushobore kugerwaho, icyo gihe Imana ibigiza iruhande . . . “Ntabwo gusobanurira abigishwa ibyerekeye imiterere y’ubwami bw’ijuru byari bihagije. Ahubwo icyari gikenewe cyane ni uguhinduka mu mitima yabo kugira ngo bashobore kugira imico ibereye ubwami bw’ijuru. … Korohera abandi, kwiyibagirwa, no kugira urukundo rwiyegurira abandi biboneka ku mwana muto, ni ingeso ijuru riha agaciro. Ni imico ifite agaciro gahanitse. . . . “Umutima ugendera mu kuri kandi wicisha bugufi, ni uw’agaciro kenshi imbere y’Imana. Imana ishyira kashe yayo ku bantu, idakurikije icyubahiro baba bafite, ubutunzi bwabo, cyangwa amashuri bize; ahubwo ikurikiza umushyikirano bafitanye na Kristo.” –Ellen G. White, _Uwifuzwa Ibihe Byose_, pp. 344-345.

**IBIBAZO:**

`Ni ubuhe butumwa amasomo akurikira yongera ku byerekeye kwishyira hejuru no kwicisha bugufi? Matayo 23:12; Zaburi 25:9; Zaburi 149:4; na Yakobo 4:6, 10.`

`Tekereza neza: Ni ryari uheruka “gutekereza ku bikorwa byawe by’ubugiraneza”? Ni mu buhe buryo byagize icyo bihindura ku mibanire yawe n’Imana cyangwa no ku mibanire yawe n’abo wagiriye neza?`

`Mbese ni iki ukeneye guhindura mu mibereho yawe kugira ngo ucishwe bugufi imbere y’Imana kugira ngo uburyo ugendana nayo bigire imbaraga?`

**Incamake**: Ubwibone bushobora kuba inzitizi ikomeye itambamira ugukura k’ubucuti bwawe n’Imana. Niba twumva twihagije ntitumenye ko dukeneye uwo mushyikirano tugirana na Yo, ntabwo tuzawugeraho. Ibiramambu, Yesu ni we Muntu wicishaga bugufi kurenza abandi bose bo ku isi, akanaba urugero ntagereranwa rw’uburyo dukwiriye kugirana umushyikirano ushyitse n’Imana.
