---
title:  Guhimbaza, Kwatura, Ibyifuzo, no Gushima
date:   13/05/2026
---

Mu buryo bumeze nk’ubwo Yesu yatwigishijemo uko dukwiriye gusenga muri Matayo 6:5-15, dushobora gukurikiza uru rugero rworoshye igihe tuje imbere y’Imana twiherereye, turi kumwe n’imiryango yacu, cyangwa tuje nk’itorero, tuzirikana ko isengesho ari ukuvugana n’Imana nk’ubwira inshuti. Inshuro nyinshi cyane, amasengesho yacu aba yuzuyemo ibyo dusaba mu gihe Yesu yatwigishije gusengera byinshi birenze icyo!

`Soma isengesho rya Daniyeli muri Daniyeli 9:4-19 maze urebe imigabane itandukanye y’isengesho rye.`

Ita ku kuntu ushobora gushyira mu masengesho yawe ingingo zikurikira:

- **Guhimbaza**: guhimbaza ni ukuramya kugaragaza Imana iyo ari yo ndetse n’uko imeze.  Soma Zaburi 100, indirimbo nziza yo guhimbaza Imana. Ita ku mazina menshi y’Imana ndetse no ku mico yayo itangaje. Yihimbarize ko ari Umucunguzi wawe, Umukiza wawe, Uguhumuriza, Ugukiza indwara, Umwungeri mwiza, Alufa na Omega, ndetse ni Urutare, tuvuze bike gusa.

- **Kwatura n’imbabazi**: iyo twisanze turi kuvugana n’Imana kandi tuguma muri Yo, ntidushobora kwibuza kureka ikintu cyose kidusubiza inyuma kikadutandukanya na Yo, uko cyaba kimeze kose. Uko turushaho kuba bugufi bwayo, ni ko turushaho kubona ko tudakwiriye kandi ko turi impezamajyo. Ibi bidutera kuyihendahendera kudukuraho ibyaha byacu ngo imico yacu iyishushanye n’ishusho yayo. Niba dushaka ko Imana itubabarira, natwe tugomba kuba twiteguye kubabarira abandi. “Mwaturirane ibyaha byanyu kandi musabirane, kugira ngo mukizwe. Gusenga k’umukiranutsi kugira umumaro mwinshi, iyo asenganye umwete” (Yakobo 5:16).

- **Ibyifuzo [byo gusengerwa]**: ni ibihe bibazo uhanganye na byo? Ahari ni ibyo ufite mu muryango, mu nshuti, ibibazo by’ubuzima, amafaranga, akazi, cyangwa mu masomo. Senga urasa ku ntego muri ibyo bibazo cyangwa kuri abo bantu, maze usabe ko ibyo Imana ishaka ari byo bibaho.

- **Gushima**: Soma Abafilipi 4:6 maze utekereze ku migisha yabonetse mu mibereho yawe. Hari ibintu bikomeye bishobora kuza mu bwenge bwawe; ariko se twavuga iki no kuri bya bintu bitoya dukunze gufata uko twiboneye? Duhora tugirirwa imbabazi n’Imana; nyamara se mbega ukuntu gushima kwacu ari gukeya! Mbega ukuntu guhimbariza Imana ibyo yadukoreye ari gukeya!

`Ni ibihe bintu ukeneye guhimbariza Imana, kuyaturira, kuyisaba, ndetse no kuyishimira? Kuki utabikora nonaha?`
