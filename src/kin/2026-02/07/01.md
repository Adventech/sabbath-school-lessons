### Aho icyigisho cy'iki cyumweru gishingiye:
1 Abami 19:1-18; Matayo 6:5-8; Luka 11:2-4; Matayo 6:5-15; Daniyeli 9:4-19; Abaroma 8:26, 27.

> <p>Icyo kwibukwa:</p>
> “Mwa bantu mwe, mujye muyiringira, Ibyo mu mitima yanyu mubisuke imbere yayo, Imana ni yo buhungiro bwacu” (Zaburi 62:9)

Mbese imibereho yawe yo gusenga imeze ite? Usenga inshuro zingahe? Mbese ushyiramo umwete ungana iki? Mbese uba wiringiye guhabwa ibyo usabye ku ruhe rwego? Mbese usenga buri munsi cyangwa usenga iyo ibintu byakomeye? Mbese amasengesho yawe buri gihe aba ari ayo gusaba ikintu runaka, cyangwa unahimbaza Imana mu masengesho yawe?

Ese na none, ujya wisanga uri gusenga mu gitondo mbere yo kurya ndetse wenda no hagati mu munsi wuzuyemo akazi? Ahari wigeze kuba umwe mu bagize itsinda ry’amasengesho ahoraho cyangwa ukaba waragize imibereho yo gusenga ku isaha iyi n’iyi. Mbese waba warasogongeye ku mbaraga no kumva Imana iri kumwe nawe binyuze mu isengesho rihindura ibintu byose mu mibereho yawe?

Isengesho ni isano ihoraho iba hagati yacu (amashami) na Yesu (Umuzabibu). “Niba dushaka gukura no kwera imbuto, tugomba guhora dukura ibidutunga mu Muzabibu Muzima; kuko dutandukanye na We nta mbaraga twagira”—Ellen G. White, _Inyandiko z’Ibanze_, p. 77. Uyu ni umugisha uturuka mu kuguma mu isengesho. Imana iratwumva, kandi buri gihe isubiza mu gihe cyayo no mu buryo bwayo butunganye—nubwo buri gihe atari ko buba buhuye n’ibyo twari twiteze kubona.

Muri iki cyumweru nimureke twige abandi bantu b’abanyamasengesho baboneka muri Bibiliya kandi twite ku buryo bushyizwe mu bikorwa bwo gushyira imbaraga mu isengesho mu mibereho yacu ya buri munsi.
