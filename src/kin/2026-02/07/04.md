---
title:  Yesu Atwigisha uko Dukwiriye Gusenga
date:   12/05/2026
---

Mu gihe cya Yesu, amasengesho yabaga ari maremare kandi ateguranywe ubushishozi nk’ibintu bigeye kwerekanirwa imbere y’abantu—yakoreshwagamo amagambo asobetse kandi inshuro nyinshi abantu bakaba barayafashe mu mutwe—yahabwaga agaciro cyane. Nta bintu byiza Yesu yabaga afite byo kuvuga kuri ayo masengesho (soma Matayo 6:5-8). Yayagaragaje uko yari ari: asengewe kwigaragaza ko ari abakiranutsi.

Abigishwa babonye Yesu asenga, maze bamenya ko isengesho ryari umugabane w’ingenzi w’imibereho ye (soma Luka 5:16; Luka 6:12; Luka 9:18; Luka 22:41; Luka 24:30; Mariko 1:35; Mariko 6:46). Igihe bitegerezaga Yesu, babonye ko atandukanye n’abayobozi b’idini maze basobanukirwa ko mu isengesho harimo byinshi cyane birenze ibyo bari barigeze kwitaho. Nuko abigishwa basanga Yesu, baramusaba bati: “Databuja, twigishe gusenga” (Luka 11:1).

Yesu yahaye abigishwa be (ndetse natwe) icyitegererezo cy’uko dushobora gusenga mu mvugo yoroheje dukoresha buri munsi. Yatweretse ko amasengesho yacu akwiriye kuba avuzwe mu kuri kandi akomotse mu mitima yacu.

Soma Luka 11:2-4 na Matayo 6:5-15 maze wite ku migaragarire ikurikira y’isengesho Yesu yigishije:

- **Data wa twese uri mu ijuru**: kwemera isano yihariye ufitanye na Data wa twese.

- **Izina ryawe ryubahwe**: kwemera ko ukwera kw’Imana gutuma tuza imbere yayo tuyubashye.

- **Ubwami bwawe buze**: kwifuza ko Imana igaruka kandi Mwuka Wera agatura muri twe kugeza ubwo ibyo bizaba bisohoye.

- **Ibyo ushaka bibeho mu isi nk’uko biba mu ijuru**: kwiyegurira Imana no kuyisaba ngo ibyo ishaka bibe mu mibereho yacu, tukiringira ko izi ibitubereye byiza kuruta ibindi byose, aho gusaba ibyo twishakiye gusa.

- **Uduhe ibyokurya byacu by’uyu munsi**: gusaba ibyo dukeneye kugira ngo tubeho, ari iby’umubiri (ibiryo n’amazi) ndetse n’ibya Mwuka (Yesu n’Ijambo rye).

- **Uduharire imyenda yacu nk’uko natwe twahariye abarimo imyenda yacu**: kwihana, gushaka imbabazi, kandi tukibuka kubabarira abatugiriye nabi tutabihatiwe, mbese nk’uko Imana itubabarira.

- **Ntuduhane mu bitwoshya, ahubwo udukize umubi**: gusaba kurindwa no guhishwa ngo umubi atatugeraho muri iyi si (Zaburi 91).

- **Kuko ubwami n’ubushobozi n’icyubahiro ari ibyawe none n’iteka ryose, Amen**: Kwemera ko abo turi bo bose, ibyo dufite byose, ndetse n’ibyo dukora byose ari iby’Imana.  Ni yo yonyine ikwiriye ikuzo no guhimbazwa (1 Ngoma 29:11).

`Kuki utakwimenyereza gusenga cyane maze ugahindukiza amaso yawe ukareba ku Mana buri gitondo nuko ukavugana n’ugukunda kurusha abandi bose? Ni iki kikubuza kugenza utya ko uzi ko ari byo bidukwiriye? Senga muri uyu mwanya, nk’uko Yesu aturarikira kubigenza.`
