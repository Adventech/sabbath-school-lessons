---
title:  Ibindi byo Kwigwa no Kuzirikanwa
date:   15/05/2026
---

“Iyaba twibwiraga kandi tukaganira ibya Yesu, tukarushaho kwiyibagirwa, ni bwo twasabana na We kurushaho”—Ellen G. White, _Kugana Yesu_, p. 105.

“Nitugundira ibitekerezo byo gushidikanya n’ubwoba tukagerageza no gusobanura ikituyobera cyose, no kumenya impamvu y’amagorwa yose atugeraho, kugira ngo tubone kwizera hanyuma, tuzasanga yuko ibituyobera bizarushaho kugwira cyane, tugahora ku nkeke y’umutima iteka. Ariko nidusanga Imana, tumaze kumenya ko ubwacu tudafite shinge na rugero tugaherako tukayibwira ibyo dukennye twicishije bugufi twizera, iyo Mana ifite ubwenge butagerwa, ibona byose, igatwarisha byose Ijambo ryayo uko ishatse, izumva gutaka kwacu itume umucyo uvira mu mitima yacu.”—Ellen G. White, _Kugana Yesu_, p. 100.

“Umutima ushobora kuzamukira ku mababa yo guhimbaza ukegerezwa ijuru. Imana ihora ishengerezwa indirimbo no gucurangirwa mu bikari byo mu ijuru, natwe iyo tuyishimye tuba twisanishije no kuramya kw’ingabo zo mu ijuru… Nuko rero tujye imbere y’Uwaturemye dufite umunezero n’icyubahiro n’ibyishimo n’ijwi rimusingiza [Yesaya 51:3]”—Ellen G. White, _Kugana Yesu_, p. 106.

**IBIBAZO:**

`Ni iyihe ngingo iri mu magambo yavuzwe haruguru igukora ku mutima kurusha izindi zose? Ni iyihe igukomereye kurusha izindi?`

`Ni ayahe masomo yandi ushobora kwigira mu mibereho yo gusenga y’abandi bantu bavugwa muri Bibiliya? (soma Ezira 10:1; 2 Abami 13:4; Yona 4:2, 3; Habakuki 3:1; 2 Abami 19:14-19; Yeremiya 32:16-25; Nehemiya 1:4-11; 1 Abami 8:22-54, urutonde ni rurerure).`

`Ni uruhe ruhare rwo kwiyiriza ubusa mu gihe usenga?`

`Mbese hari ikintu gishya wakwifuza guhindura cyangwa gushyira mu bikorwa mu mibereho yawe yo gusenga, nk’umusaruro w’ibyo wize muri iki cyumweru? Kuki utagira ibyo uhindura?`

**Inshamake:** Bibiliya itubwira inkuru z’abantu basenze amasengesho afite imbaraga kandi y’igihe kirekire ndetse n’abandi batabikoze. Igihe dushakashaka mu mpapuro zayo, buri gihe tuzabonamo umuntu dushobora kwisanisha na we, tutitaye ku kuntu umushyikirano dufitanye n’Imana umeze. Kandi tuzabona amasezerano menshi azadutera ubutwari kandi akatuyobora mu mibereho yacu yo gushyikirana n’Imana. Gukura mu bya Mwuka gukwiriye kuba intego yacu— amaso yacu tukayahoza kuri Yesu, We rugero rwacu muri byose, Ibanze ryo kwizera kwacu ndetse akaba ari na We ugusohoza.
