---
title:  Intwaro Ikomeye Kurusha Izindi
date:   19/04/2026
---

Mbere yuko ducukumbura tukamenya impamvu Bibiliya ari iy’agaciro n’uburyo twakwimbika muri yo tuyiga, tugomba kugira icyo tumenya: Kimwe mu bitero bikomeye Satani akugabaho ni ukukubuza kumara igihe usabana n’Imana mu Ijambo ryayo. Gutuma abantu bahugirana mu mirimo yabo ibazanira inyungu, kudaha agaciro Ijambo ry’Imana, umunaniro, cyangwa kurishidikanyaho, ni wo mugambi we wa mbere. Azi neza ko igihe dufashe igihe tukamarana n’Imana mu Ijambo ryayo, riduhembura, rikanagaburira imitima yacu, akaba ariyo mpamvu akora uko ashoboye kose ngo atuvutse ibyo bihe by’ingenzi!

Tubwirwa ko, “Satani akoresha ubucakura bwose kugira ngo abuze abantu gusobanukirwa na Bibiliya; kuko ihishura ku mugaragaro ubuhendanyi bwe”. Ellen G. White, _Intambara Ikomeye_, p. 417. Satani azi neza ko Ijambo ry’Imana rifite imbaraga, rikaba rimugira umunyantege nke. Satani azi neza ko gusenga no kwiga Bibiliya arizo ntwaro zikomeye umuntu ashobora kumurwanisha, (soma mu Abefeso 6:17, 18; Abaheburayo 4:12), akaba ariyo mpamvu akora ibishoboka byose ngo abuze abantu gusoma Ijambo ry’Imana no gusenga. Azi neza ko Ijambo ry’Imana rifite imbaraga, kandi ariryo ryatumye ibintu byose bibaho (Zaburi 33:6), rishobora kuzura uwapfuye (Yohana 11:41-44) rikaduha n’imbaraga zo kunesha (Matayo 4:1-11).

Iyo Satani atumye abantu baba kure ya Bibiliya zabo, aba azi neza ko umushyikirano bari bafitanye n’Imana urangiye ndetse n’imibanire yabo myiza na bagenzi babo izamo agatotsi. Urushako rwacu narwo rugira ikibazo, ugasanga umubyeyi abwira nabi abana be, kwihanganirana bikabura, byaba mu nshuti zacu cyangwa mubo dukorana. Usanga abantu bahugiranye, baniwe kandi bafite ibibaremereye, umuntu agasigara ntaho afite ahungira. Kandi igitangaje, ni uko tutajya dutekereza ko ibyo birimo kutubaho. Dutekereza ko nta kibazo dufitanye n’Imana, ariko muby’ukuri, iyo iminsi ihise, ibyumweru bigahita tutabumbuye Bibiliya zacu, tugenda dusubira inyuma buhoro buhoro.

`Ariko nubwo twaba dufite ibituziga byinshi, umushyikirano wacu n’Imana ntukomeze nk’uko bikwiriye, Imana yo ntituvirira, ikomeza kuba hafi yacu nk’uko mu gitabo cy’ Amaganya yaȧYeremiya 3:22, 23 hatubwira. Ni iki ubona muri iyo mirongo, kandi wabigereranya ute n’imibereho yacu ya kimuntu? `

Nk’uwigeze kuba umukerubi utwikira mbere y’uko acumura (Ezekiyeli 28:14-17), Lusiferi yari yarumvise amagambo y’Imana akaba azi n’imbaraga zayo. Ubu noneho asigaye yanga ukuri urunuka, bikaba byumvikana impamvu ibitekerezo byacu bigwa ikinya n’imitima yacu igasuherwa igihe tudahisemo kumva amagambo y’Imana ngo tuyinjize mu buzima bwacu bwa buri munsi.

`Mbese ufite ubushake bungana iki bwo guhora ufite inyota yo kumva amagambo y’Imana? Ni iki igisubizo cyawe kikubwira cyerekeye ibijyanye n’impinduka ugomba gukora niba zikenewe? `
