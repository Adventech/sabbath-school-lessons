---
title:  Imiterere y’Umutima
date:   23/04/2026
---

`Ubushobozi bwacu bwo kwakira amabwiriza avuye mu Ijambo ry’Imana (Yobu 22:22) bushingira ahanini ku miterere y’umutima wacu bumeze nk’igihe twinjiye muri Bibiliya. Ni mu buhe buryo 1 Abakorinto 2:14 hasobanura ibyo?`

Kugira ubushishozi mu by’umwuka bisobanura kugira ubushishozi no gusobanukirwa mu buryo bw’umwuka. Ku bw'ibyo, birumvikana ko umuntu ushyira mu gaciro mu buryo bw'umwuka, ashobora gusobanukirwa Bibiliya mu buryo butandukanye cyane n'uko byagenda ku muntu udashyira mu gaciro mu by'umwuka. Umuntu utekereza ko gusoma Bibiliya ari iby’ubupfapfa, ntazigera ashakisha ukuri mu mpapuro zayo.

`Niyo mpamvu, uburyo dufata Bibiliya n’uburyo tuyisoma ari ibintu by’ingenzi iyo bigeze mu gukuza umushyikirano wacu n’Imana. Pawulo abisobanura ate mu 1 Abatesalonike 2:13?`

Ijambo ry’Imana rigira icyo rikora muri twe iyo twizeye. Iyo ubumbuye Bibiliya wizeye ko hari icyo Imana igiye kukubwira binyuze mu magambo yayo ugiye gusoma, byanze bikunze izagira icyo ikubwira kigira icyo gihindura mu mibereho yawe. Ariko ibyinshi bizashingira ku kwizera ufite n’ibyo witeze gukura muri iryo jambo. Inkuru nziza ni uko, iyo kwizera kwawe ari guke, Imana ishobora kugufasha kugakura (Mariko 9:24), ndetse nubwo kwaba kungana urwara nk’akabuto ka sinapi (Luka 17:6).

Umwe mu migambi y’imena ya Bibiliya ni ukutubwira ukuri kukinjira mu mibereho yacu kugatuma umushyikirano dufitanye n’Imana ukomera. Niba umutima wawe ukinguriwe Mwuka Wera, ukaba wiga Ijambo ry’Imana ufite kwiyoroshya, buri gihe rizagira icyo rihindura mu mibereho yawe, nubwo bitahita bigaragarira amaso, kubera ko uko guhinduka no gukura bigenda gahoro. Ariko iyo dukomeje kwizirika ku cyaha ntiducishwe bugufi uko guhinduka ntako tuzabona. Gusoma Bibiliya bishobora kutugirira akamaro gake cyane. Mwuka Wera adushishikariza kwegera Yesu Kristo. Mbese turashaka gutera intambwe tumusanga? Niba ari uko bimeze, twaba “tubaye abanyabwenge mu gakiza” (2 Timoteyo 3:15), kandi tuzabona ibintu tutigeze tunatekereza.

`Iyo ninjiye muri Bibliya, umutima wanjye n’ubwenge bwanjye biba bimeze bite? Mbese ninjiramo nzanye ibitekerezo byanjye kugira ngo mbyemeze Bibiliya, cyangwa ninjiramo mfite umutima n’ubwenge byiteguye kwakira ibirimo, mfite kwizera nk’uk’umwana muto, nshaka kureba icyo Imana ishaka kumbwira uwo munsi? Ni ukubera iki icyo gisubizo ari ingenzi cyane? `
