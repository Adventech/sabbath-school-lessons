---
title:  Hutuhuti zo mu Buzima
date:   31/05/2026
---

Cyari icyumweru kirimo imirimo myinshi cyane. Nubwo yari azi ko hari byinshi byagombaga gukorwa mbere yuko isabato itangira, ibyihutirwa byasaga n’ibyaburijemo iby’ingenzi, maze izuba rirenga atarabitahura. Abagize umuryango basangiye ifunguro ridasanzwe ryo ku wa gatandatu nimugoroba, kandi basengera hamwe.

Ariko ku isabato mugitondo yabyutse kare, maze ntiyashobora kwibuza kubona ko icyumba cyo kogeramo cyanduye, nuko abanza kugisukura. Hanyuma aza kubona ko n’ umwana we muto w’umuhungu yari yanyaye ku buriri, maze afata amashuka ye ayajugunya mu ibase hamwe n’indi myenda. Igihe yari ari gutegurira umuryango we ifunguro rya mu gitondo, yaje kubona ko nta mugati wo guherekeza ifunguro rya saa sita uhari, maze yotsa umugati ukozwe mu bitoke. Yaje kubona ko ishati umugabo we yagombaga kwambara agiye ku rusengero ikeneye guterwa ipasi, maze ayitera ipasi, azinga n’indi myenda kandi asohora imyanda ayishyira hanze.

Ibyo byaje kumubabaza, aravuga ati: “Uyu munsi ni ku isabato-umunsi nkunda cyane kurusha indi yose! None dore ndi gukora iyi mirimo yose kandi nkemera ko ibyo bintu bimbuza gukora icyo isabato yagenewe-ari cyo kwegera bugufi bw’Imana.”

Hashize umwanya muto, intekerezo ze zatangiye gushyigikira ibyo yakoze- avuga ko ibyo byose byari bikeneye gukorwa. Mbese ibyo byari ukuri? Yaje kumenya ko yakoze nka Marita, “yari yahagaritswe umutima n’imirimo myinshi yo kuzimana” (Luka 10:40), ariko amagambo ya Yesu yirangira mu mutima we: “Uriganyira wirushya muri byinshi, ariko ngombwa ni kimwe, kandi Mariya ahisemo umugabane mwiza atazakwa” (Luka 10:41, 42). Wa mugabane mwiza. Kwicara ku birenge bya Yesu bitewe n’urukundo umukunda-bitari ku isabato gusa, ahubwo buri munsi. Ibyo ntabwo ari byo yari yahisemo muri icyo gitondo. Yakundaga Imana, ariko byari byoroshye kwibagirwa ko Imana yamuhaye isabato nk’impano kugira ngo umushyikirano afitanye na Yo urusheho kugira imbaraga. Amarira yashotse ku matama ubwo yari ahagaze mu gikoni.

Intego y’uru rugero ntabwo ari ugutinda ku cyo dukwiriye gukora ndetse n’icyo tudakwiriye gukora ku Isabato; ahubwo ni ukutwibutsa impamvu ari ngombwa kumenya ibintu bigwabiza cyangwa bikangiza umushyikirano tugirana n’Imana. Iyo imitima yacu ibabazwa n’ibyaha ndetse no gutandukanywa na yo maze tukayiyambaza, Yesu aba ari hafi yacu cyane (Zaburi 53:2). Mu biganza bye byahindanijwe n’amaraso, afashe ikanzu yera. Areba amarira yacu yo kwicuza maze akadukuramo imyenda yacu yanduye. Atwambika ikanzu ye yera yo gukiranuka. Ugutungana kwe gutwikira icyaha cyacu twatuye – mu buryo bwuzuye kandi butunganye. Dushobora kumesera ikanzu yacu mu maraso Ye (Ibyahishuwe 7:14).

`Ni mu buhe buryo muri Yesaya 64:6; Zekariya 3:4 na Yesaya 61:10 hagaragaza uku kuri kw’ingenzi kwerekeranye no gukiranuka kwa Kristo aduha? Kuki buri gihe tugomba gushikama ku byo dusezeranirwa aha ngaha? `
