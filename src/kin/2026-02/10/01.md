### Aho icyigisho cy'iki cyumweru gishingiye:
Yesaya 61:10; Hoseya 6; Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 3:18,19; Kuva 34:1-10; Abaroma 6:23; Matayo 22:1-14.

> <p>Icyo kwibukwa:</p>
> “Ariko nitwatura ibyaha byacu, ni yo yo kwizerwa kandi ikiranukira kutubabarira ibyaha byacu no kutwezaho gukiranirwa kose” (1 Yohana 1:19).

Igihugu cy’Isezerano cyasaga n’ikiri kure cyane y’Abisirayeli bari babambye amahema munsi y’inkingi y’igicu, mu kibaya. Mose yari yarazamutse yinjiramu mwijima w’icuraburindi, wari waratwikiriye impinga y’umusozi mu minsi myinshi yari ishije. Batekereje ko mu by’ukuri umuyobozi wabo yari yarapfuye; niba atari yarishwe n’inzara, yaba yarishwe n’umuriro wari mu mpinga y’uwo musozi. Imbaga yari igizwe n’uruvange rw’abantu benshi (Abisirayeli hamwe n’Abanyegiputa bari bavanyeyo na bo bari mu rugendo rwo Kuvayo) muri bo hari abumvise badatekanye kandi barambiwe, bari biteguye gukomeza urugendo bagana mu gihugu gitemba amata n’ubuki. Nubwo abo bantu ari bo bari bamaze iminsi mike basezeraniye Imana ko bazayumvira, bashakaga ishusho [y’ikigirwamana] bashoboraga kurebesha amaso. Nuko, bateranira ahakikije ihema rya Aroni bamusaba kubaremera ikigirwamana. Kubwo kwishakira umutekano, Aroni yarabyemeye. Mu Kuva 32-34, dusoma uburyo iyi nkuru ibabaje yagenze.

Iyi nkuru ni imwe mu nkuru zo mu Byanditswe Byera itwigisha ibyerekeranye no kwihana ndetse no kubabarirwa, ari na yo nsanganyamatsiko y’icyigisho cy’iki cyumweru. Zirikana insanganyamatsiko y’isomo ry’icyo kwibukwa cy’iki cyumweru igihe wiga ingingo ya buri munsi. Ni koko, dukora ibyaha, ariko Imana ishimwe kubw'urupfu rwa Yesu ku musaraba n'umugambi w'inama y’agakiza, umunyabyaha watura kandi akihana by'ukuri ashobora guhabwa imbabazi.
