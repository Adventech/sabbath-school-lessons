---
title:  Ubuntu Buhagije
date:   03/06/2026
---

Iyo twiyumvamo uburemere bw’icyaha kandi tukemera ko Mwuka Wera atuyobora munsi y’umusaraba, tuba dukwiriye gusaba Imana imbabazi, tuzirikana ko “Uwiteka ari umunyebambe n’umunyambabazi, atinda kurakara, afite kugira neza kwinshi” (Zaburi 103:8). Uyu murongo wavuzwe n’Imana ubwayo (Kuva 34:6) nyuma yuko ubwoko bwayo yatoranyije buyibabaza.

`Soma Kuva 34:1-10. Ni ukuhe kuri kw’ingenzi kuboneka aha ngaha?`

Kuba Uwiteka ari umunyebambe n’umunyembabazi, atinda kurakara kandi afite kugira neza kwinshi ni yo mpamvu Yesu yapfiriye ku musaraba-kugira ngo umushyikirano wacu n’Imana wongera kujya mu buryo.

Iyo dufite ubushake bwo kwemera icyaha cyacu no kukihana; igihe tuvuga tuti: “Mwami ndagarutse…, mbabarira kuko ndi umunyabyaha!” (Luka18:13), wa Yesu wari umaze igihe akorera muri twe kandi udukorera umurimo afatanyije na Mwuka wera na mbere yuko tumutabaza-areba uburemere bw’icyaha maze akubudukuraho. Imitwaro yacu tuyitura i Kaluvari, kandi nta gushidikanya Yesu aba ari hafi yacu cyane igihe tumusanze, ndetse na mbere yaho, aradushaka nk’Umwungeri mwiza agahagarara ku rugi agakomanga (Ibyahishuwe 3:20). Nimureke twe kuguma kure y’umusaraba, turebera Imana kure. Nimureke dusange Yesu ibyaha byacu n’imitwaro yacu abituguranire aduhe gukiranuka kwe (Zekariya 3:4).

**Soma witonze imirongo ikurikira. Andika mu magambo yawe bwite icyo iyo mirongo ikubwira cyerekeranye n’ubuntu Imana ikugirira: kuko ibihembo by’ibyaha ari urupfu, ariko impano y’Imana ni ubugingo buhoraho muri Yesu Kristo Umwami wacu** (Abaroma 6:23).

**“Nyamara aho ibyaha byagwiriye ni ho n’ubuntu bwarushijeho gusaga, kugira ngo nk'uko ibyaha byimitswe n'urupfu, abe ari na ko n'ubuntu bwimikwa no gukiranuka, buduhesha ubugingo buhoraho ku bwa Yesu Kristo Umwami wacu”** (Abaroma 5:20, 21).

**“Ariko Imana yerekanye urukundo rwayo idukunda, ubwo Kristo yadupfiraga tukiri abanyabyaha”** (Abaroma 5:8).
