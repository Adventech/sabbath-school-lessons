---
title:  Kwihana Nyakuri
date:   02/06/2026
---

Isi isanzwe idusukaho ubutumwa bwerekeranye n ‘ubwigenge, kwinezeza ndetse no kuzamuka mu ntera – ibi bikaba bihabanye n’amahame agenga ingoma y’Imana. Igitangaje, amagambo ya mbere dusanga muri Bibiliya yavuzwe na Yohana Umubatiza ndetse na Yesu yari ameze kimwe. Yohana yaravuze ati: “Mwihane kuko ubwami bwo mu ijuru buri hafi” (Matayo 3:1, 2). Yesu yaravuze ati: “Igihe kirasohoye, ubwami bw’Imana buri hafi. Nuko mwihane, mwemere ubutumwa bwiza” (Mariko 1:14,15; soma na Luka 24:46, 47). Yesu na Yohana bararikiye abari babateze amatwi kwihana kuko ubwami bwo mu ijuru bwari hafi. Mbese birashoboka ko ubwo butumwa bwaba ari ingenzi kuri twe muri iki gihe?

`Soma Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 3:18, 19. Kuki kwihana ari ingenzi cyane mu rugendo rwo gukura mu bya Mwuka? Mbese igihe cyo “guhemburwa”ni ikihe?`

Ubugwaneza no kugira neza by’Imana biturehereza kwihana (Abaroma 2:4). Kwihana gukubiyemo intambwe ebyiri: (1) kubabazwa n’ibyaha byacu; no (2) gufata icyemezo utaryarya cyo kureka icyaha. Muri Bibiliya, hafi ya buri gihe kwihana kuba guhujwe no kubabarirwa. Twihana by’ukuri, Imana na yo ikatubabarira. Ibyo biroroshye ( 1 Yohana 1:9, Ibyahishuwe 3:19). “Umwami Imana ntitinza isezerano ryayo, nk’uko bamwe batekereza yuko iritinza. Ahubwo itwihanganira idashaka ko hagira n’umwe urimbuka, ahubwo ishaka ko bose bihana” (2 Petero 3:9). Igihe buri wese ku giti cye ategurira umutima we kugaruka kwa Yesu, Imana iduha igihe cyo gukosora ibitameze neza hagati yacu na Yo.

Yesu yarababajwe, arapfa, kandi arazuka kugira ngo igihe twihannye, ubuntu bwe bubashe gukora igitangaza mu mibereho yacu. Ibitandukanye n’isi, itubwira ko uko turi ntacyo bitwaye, Imana idusaba ko tuyigarukira mu kwihana no kwizera (Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 20:21), ari byo bidushyira mu biganza by’Imana mu buryo bwuzuye kugira ngo Imana ibashe kudukuraho amahage no gutunganya imico yacu kugira ngo duse na Yo, maze tuyihamye (Yohana 15:2, 8). Uko ni ko dukura kandi tukera imbuto binyuze mu gukomeza kwihana (Matayo 3:8).

“Nta kwihana nyakuri kudatera guhinduka. Ubutungane bwa Kristo ntabwo ari umwambaro utwikira icyaha kiticujijwe kandi kitazinutswe; ahubwo ni ihame rigenga ubugingo rigahindura imico kandi rikagenga imyitwarire.”-Ellen G. White, _Uwifuzwa Ibihe Byose_, p. 433.

`Kwihana kutugeza ku bugingo (Ibyakozwe n’Intumwa 11:18) kandi ni umugabane w’ingenzi wo gukurira mu mushyikirano tugirana n’Imana. Mu rugendo rwo kwitanga burundu, kwihana no kwemerera Imana kugukuraho amahage, ni iyihe ntambwe ikugora kurusha izindi zose?`
