---
title:  Ikanzu Ihenze Kurusha Izindi
date:   04/06/2026
---

Akenshi, umwambaro mwiza werekana umukire hakurikijwe ibipimo by’ab’isi. Abantu bamwe baravuga bati: “Nambara muri ubu buryo kugira ngo ngaragaze uwo ndiwe.” Ariko mu ijuru, ibintu byose bizashira uretse umushyikirano wacu wonyine (Matayo 6:19-21). Abo turi bo bakwiriye guhishwa muri Yesu no mu ikanzu ye itunganye yo gukiranuka kwe.

`Soma umugani dusanga muri Matayo 22:1-14 Yesu yavuze kugira ngo asobanure ibyo. Ni ubuhe butumwa ushobora kubona muri uyu mugani?`

Yesu yise umuntu utari wambaye umwambaro w’ubukwe “Mugenzi wanjye,” kandi nubwo uwo muntu ntacyo yamusubije, bagomba kuba bari bafitanye isano. Uwo muntu agomba kuba yari azi ibyerekeranye n’ikanzu ariko yari yahisemo kutayambara. Imico ya Yesu iratunganye kandi ntigira ikizinga, kandi iyo mico arayiduha kugira ngo “[umugeni] yambare umwambaro w’igitare mwiza, urabagirana utanduye” (Ibyahishuwe 19:8), “[itorero] ridafite ikizinga cyangwa umunkanyari cyangwa ikintu cyose gisa gityo, ahubwo ngo ribe iryera ridafite inenge” (Abefeso 5:27).

Umwambaro wera ni “ugukiranuka kwa Kristo, imico ye itagira amakemwa, ni yo abamwakira nk’Umukiza wabo bwite bahabwa kubwo kwizera.”-Ellen G. White, _Imigani ya Kristo_, p.189.

Adamu na Eva bari bambaye ikanzu yera y’ubwiza burabagirana mbere yuko bacumura; nyuma y’uko bakoze icyaha, babonyeko bambaye ubusa (Itangiriro 3:7) maze biremera imyambaro y’ibibabi by’imitini. Nyuma yaho Imana yafashe imyambaro yabo y’ibabi by’imitini iyisimbuza umwambaro ukozwe mu ruhu rw’inyamaswa. Hari igitambo cyantanze umwambaro wabo. Mu buryo bumeze nk’ubwo, twakira igitambo cya Yesu binyuze mu kwakira ikanzu ye yo gukiranuka. “Batewe isoni n’uko bambaye ubusa, ni ko kwiremera ibicocero mu bibabi by’umutini ngo bahishe ubwambure bwabo…. Ntacyo umuntu yakwihangira gishobora gusimbura umwambaro w’ubuziranenge yabuze. Nta mwambaro w’ibibabi by’imitini, nta mwambaro usanzwe w’umuturage wo mu isi ushobora kwambarwa n’abazicarana na Kristo n’abamarayika mu birori by’ubukwe bw’umwana w’Intama. Umwambaro Kristo yatanze ubwe wonyine ni wo wadushoboza kuba abantu bakwiriye guhagarara imbere y’Imana. Uwo mwambaro ari yo kanzu yo gukiranuka kwe, Kristo azawambika umuntu wese wihana ibyaha kandi akizera Imana.” - Ellen G. White, _Imigani ya Kristo_, p.189 190.

`Bitekerezeho: Buri munsi dukwiriye kwambara ikanzu yo gukiranuka ya Yesu. Mbese ibyo bisobanuye iki mu by’ukuri, kandi tubikora dute?`
