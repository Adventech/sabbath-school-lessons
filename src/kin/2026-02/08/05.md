---
title:  Ingero zo Kwizera
date:   20/05/2026
---

Uyu munsi, mara umwanya wiga Abaheburayo 11, igice kivuga ibyo kwizera gukomeye. Bwa mbere gisome uranguruye, udahagarara. Hanyuma ugisome inshuro ya kabiri, maze wandike icyo utekereza ku bibazo bikurikira:

- **Ongera usome umurongo wa 1. Ni iki wiringiye uyu munsi udashobora kurebesha amaso? (Tekereza ku byo ukeneye nonaha ndetse no ku bintu bizahoraho wifuza).**

- **Ni uruhe ruhare kwizera kugira mu buhamya bwawe bwite no mu biganiro byawe?**

- **Ongera usome umurongo wa 3, uvuga ku Mana no ku byaremwe. Kuki mu buryo bwinshi, kubaho kw’Imana Rurema gukwiriye kuba ikintu cyoroshye kurusha ibindi byose gufata ku bwo kwizera?**

- **Soma umurongo wa 6 maze wandike ubutumwa buri muri uyu murongo mu magambo yawe.**

- **Kuva ku murongo wa 7 ukageza ku wa 40 hahishura imibereho y’abantu batandukanye bo muri Bibiliya. Kubera iki kwizera ari izingiro risobanura imbaraga z’umushyikirano abo bantu bari bafitanye n’Imana?**

Kumenya Imana no kugirana na Yo umushyikirano muzima kandi ufite imbaraga bisaba kwizera. Ni mu buhe buryo ushobora gukomeza kwizera kwawe cyangwa ugatera ubutwari umuntu ufite kwizera kujegajega? Hano tuhafite ibitekerezo byagufasha:

**Kwizera guto (kungana n’akabuto ka Sinapi) gufite imbaraga kandi kuraguhagije ngo ukurire mu mushyikirano ugirana n’Imana** (Matayo 17:20). Igihe cyose ufite ubushake bwo gukorana na Yo, Imana izafasha kwizera kwawe gukure.

**Kwizera kuzanwa no kumva Imana ivuganira natwe mu ijambo ryayo, ari ryo Bibiliya** (Abaroma 10:17). Fata icyemezo cyo kwiga Bibiliya buri munsi kandi usenga.

**Saba Imana kongera kwizera kwawe** (Luka 17:5). Nk’uko wa mubyeyi yasanze Yesu afite umwana utewe n’abadayimoni maze agatera hejuru n’amarira menshi ati: “Databuja, ndizeye nkiza kutizera!” (Mariko 9:24), dushobora kwemera ko tubuze kwizera, maze tugasaba Imana ikatwongerera kwizera.

**Kwizera no gushidikanya bishobora kubaho icyarimwe** (Mariko 9:24). Ntukajye kure y’Imana bitewe gusa n’uko ufite ibibazo. Mu by’ukuri, ni ingenzi ko usohoza kwizera kwawe utinya kandi uhinda umushyitsi (Abafilipi 2:12-16) kandi ukagira kwizera kwawe bwite aho gutira kwizera k’undi muntu, nk’uko abakobwa batanu b’abapfu babigerageje (Matayo 25:8).

**Umvira Mwuka Wera kandi usabe kumwongererwa** mu bugingo bwawe.

**Koresha kwizera kwawe**. Ibuka ko kwizera atari ibyiyumvo, ahubwo ni icyemezo cyo kwizera. Ibuka ko no mu gihe cy’umwijima, igihe udashobora kuyibona, Imana iba ihari (2 Abakolosayi 5:7).

Nk’isengesho ryo **gushimira Imana ko ari iyo kwiringirwa**, ita ku magambo y’indirimbo ivuga ngo “Nyir’Ukwiringirwa” [#364].
