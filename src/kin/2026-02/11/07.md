---
title:  Ibindi byo Kwigwa no Kuzirikanwa
date:   12/06/2026
---

Igihe duhura n’ingorane zo mu buzima ni cyo gihe tuba dukeneye cyane gushikama ku Mana. Insanganyamatsiko twarebeye hamwe muri iki gihembwe zose zigira uruhare mu kudufasha gukomeza cyangwa kuvugurura urugendo rukomeye tugendanamo n’Imana. Igihe uhuye n’ingorane, mbese nk’ikibazo cy’ubuzima, nk’ibibazo by’amafaranga, gutandukana n’uwo mwashakanye, urupfu rw’umuntu wawe wa bugufi, cyangwa undi mutwaro ukwambura ibyishimo byawe, ita ku bibazo bikurikira kandi utekereze ibyigisho tumaze kwiga kugeza ubu.

**IBIBAZO:** `Ni mu buhe buryo ingorane wahuye na yo cyangwa uri guhura na yo yagize icyo ihindura ku ishusho wabonagamo Imana? Ni mu buhe buryo ushobora kurushaho kubona neza imico nyakuri y’Imana?`

`Ni ryari uheruka gusenga usaba ko ijwi ry’Imana ryumvikana mu mibereho yawe ryarusha imbaraga ijwi ry’umwanzi? Ibuka ko umujura (Satani) azanwa no kwiba, kwica, no kurimbura, ariko Imana itanga ubugingo buzāse (Yohana 10:10).`

`Mbese umutima wawe uciye bugufi? Mbese wizera ko Imana ikiri Umutware ndetse n’Umuyobozi mu mibereho yawe, nubwo hariho ibirushya? Niba atari ko bimeze, ni mu buhe buryo ushobora kwiga uku kwiringira ukugira neza kw’Imana ndetse n’urukundo igukunda ku giti cyawe?`

`Mbese ukomeza gushikama mu ijambo ry’Imana buri munsi? Saba Imana kubyutsa urukundo rwawe rwa mbere wayikundaga igihe unyura mu bihe by’ingorane.`

`Ni ryari uherutse kugarukira Imana mu isengesho nk’Umufasha wawe n’Umujyanama, ukiringira ko ikomeza isezerano ryayo ko itazigera igusiga cyangwa ngo iguhāne (Abaheburayo 13:5)?`

`Niba kwizera kwawe kudafite imbaraga, senga uti: “Databuja, ndizeye, nkiza kutizera” (Mariko 9:24). Bana n’abantu bashobora kugutera umwete aho kubana n’abantu baguca intege.`

`Isi, si ko buri gihe yita ku bantu b’abanyantege nke, abadasobanukiwe, abakomerekejwe ndetse n’abababaye. Ubutumwa bw’Imana bugira buti: “Kuko aho intege nke ziri, ari ho imbaraga zanjye zuzura” ni ubutumwa buhindura imitima y’abantu mu buryo bukomeye. Tekereza ku muntu ushobora kongera imbaraga uyu munsi ukoresheje ubu butumwa.`

**Inshamake:** Turacyari mu isi y’icyaha yuzuye agahinda n’imibabaro, kandi buri wese muri twe ahura n’ibirushya ku rwego runaka mu mibereho ye, ibintu bishobora gutuma dukemanga urukundo rw’Imana. Iyo dusubije amaso inyuma tukareba uburyo abantu batandukanye bo muri Bibiliya bitwaye mu ngorane zo mu buzima, dushobora kugira ubutwari ko uburyo twitwara muri ibyo bihe bushobora kutwongerera imbaraga mu rugendo tugendanamo n’Imana, idahinduka (Malaki3:6) kandi ifite urukundo rudahindagurika.
