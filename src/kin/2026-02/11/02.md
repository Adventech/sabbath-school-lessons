---
title:  Imiraba yo mu Buzima
date:   07/06/2026
---

Yesu yari yiriwe avugana n’imbaga nini y’abantu ku nkombe ya Galilaya. Amagambo ya Yesu yagombaga kumara igihe kirekire yirangira mu mitima y’abantu nditse bikazageza no iteka ryose.

Ubwo bwari bugorobye, Yesu yavuganye n’abigishwa be abararikira kujyana na we mu rugendo. “Twambuke tujye hakurya” (Mariko 4:35). Yesu yari azi ko umuraba uza kubaho ariko ababwira ko bagomba kugenda uko byamera kose. Yari afite icyigisho cy’ingenzi yashakaga kwigisha abayoboke be ba bugufi. Ushobora kuba uzi ibyakurikiyeho.

`Soma iby’uyu muraba muri Mariko 4:35-41. Ni ibihe byigisho byo kwizera ushobora kwigira hano?`

Ita kuri izi ngingo:

1. Yesu yari asinziriye ku cyari kimeze nk’umusego umwe rukumbi wari mu bwato. Ubwato abarobyi bakoreshaga baroba bwabaga bufite umusego umwe, umusare w’ubwato yicaragaho ibwerekeza. Umuntu wabaga yicaye ibwerekeza ni we wayoboraga ubwato aho babaga bagiye. Hano rero, Yesu yari mu mwanya w’umuyobozi w’ubwato, ariko yari asinziririye aho bwerekerezwa.

2. Abigishwa si ko bose bari bashya mu ngendo zo mu mazi. Petero, Yakobo na Yohana bari abarobyi babifitemo uburambe. Bari bazi neza inyanja ya Galileya kandi bagomba kuba bari bazi uburyo bwo guhangana n’umuraba.

3. Iyi ni yo nkuru yonyine mu Byanditswe byera ivuga ko Yesu yasinziriye. Mu gihe cy’umuraba wari mubi kurusha indi yose mu buzima bwabo, igihe abigishwa bari bafite ubwoba bwinshi kandi batekereza ko bagiye gupfa, Yesu yari asinziririye ibwerekeza.

4. Inyifato y’abigishwa mu gihe cyabo cy’ingorane ni “Ntubyitayeho?” Bakemanze imico ya Yesu n’urukundo yabakundaga. Kenshi cyane, iyi ni yo nyifato natwe tugira igihe tugeze mu bihe birushya.

Hagati rwa gati mu gihe cy’ubwihebe ni bwo dushobora kugerageza kwikiza (nk’uko abigishwa babikoze), cyangwa rimwe na rimwe igihe twiyumvamo uburibwe cyangwa igihombo ni bwo dutangira gukemanga cyangwa gushidikanya urukundo rw’Imana ndetse n’uburyo itwitaho. Twibwira ko yakagombye gukora mu buryo runaka dushingiye ku byo dutekereza kandi tubona mu mirebere ya kimuntu. Ariko, nk’uko byagenze ku bigishwa, mu miraba yo mu buzima ni bwo Imana ishobora gukora ibitangaza bikomeye cyane. Buri gihe Imana irakiranuka, ndetse n’igihe bisa n’aho kuba iri mu byacu ntacyo bivuze kuri twe. Imana iba iri kumwe natwe mu miraba yacu kandi ishobora guturisha umuraba igihe twebwe tutabishoboye.

`Mbese ukunze kwitwara ute iyo uhuye n’umuraba mu mibereho yawe? Ni mu buhe buryo ibihe nk’ibyo bigira icyo bikora ku mushyikirano ufitanye n’Imana? Ni ryari wigeze kubaho ukurikije ibivugwa mu 2 Abakorinto 5:7?`
