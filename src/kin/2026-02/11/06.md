---
title:  Reba Yesu
date:   11/06/2026
---

_Mbese hari ubwo wigeze wifuza kureba Yesu igihe wari ucitse intege? Tekereza uri muri izi nzozi._

“Nasaga n’uwicaye nihebye cyane, nubitse umutwe mu biganza byanjye ntekereza nti: ‘Iyaba Yesu yari ku isi, nahita musanga, nkikubita ku birenge bye maze nkamubwira imibabaro yanjye yose. Ntabwo yantera umugongo, yangirira imbabazi, kandi namukunda ndetse nkamukorera igihe cyose.’ Nuko uwo mwanya urugi rurakinguka, maze umuntu wari ufite igihagararo cyiza kandi asa neza mu maso arinjira. Yandebanye impuhwe maze aravuga ati: ‘Mbese urifuza kureba Yesu? Ari hano kandi ushobora kumureba niba ubishaka. Fata ibyo ufite byose maze unkurikire.

“Aya magambo nayumvanye ibyishimo bitavugwa, maze n’umunezero mwinshi negeranya utuntu duke nari ntunze, imitako yanjye yose nahaga agaciro maze nkurikira uwanyoboraga. Yanjyanye ahantu hahanamye cyane ku ngazi zagaragaraga ko zidakomeye. Ubwo natangiraga kuzamuka ingazi, yampaye amabwiriza ko ngomba gukomeza guhanga amaso yanjye hejuru, bitaba ibyo nashoboraga kugira isereri maze nkagwa. Abantu benshi bazamukaga ako gacuri bajyaga bagwa bataragera hejuru.

“Amaherezo twaje kugera ku ngazi ya nyuma maze duhagarara imbere y’urugi. Aho ni ho uwari unyoboye yantegetse gusiga ibyo nari nitwaje byose. Nabirambitse hasi nishimye; maze akingura urugi arambwira ngo ninjire. Mu kanya gato nari mpagaze imbere ya Yesu. Nta kwibeshya kwariho mu maso he hari heza cyane. Nta wundi washoboraga kugira indoro ye yarabagiranagamo ineza n’igitinyiro. Ubwo yakomezaga kunyitegereza, nahise menya ko azi ibyambayeho byose mu mibereho yanjye ndetse akaba azi n’ibyo ntekereza byose n’ibyo nibwira.

“Nagerageje kwikingiriza mu maso ngo ye gukomeza kunyitegereza kuko numvaga ntashobora kwihanganira ko akomeza kunyitegereza. Nyamara yaranyegereye, aramwenyura maze andambika ikiganza ku mutwe aravuta ati: ‘Ntugire ubwoba.’Ijwi rye ryiza ryumvikanye mu mutima wanjye riwutera umunezero ntigeze ngira na rimwe mu buzima bwanjye. Nagize ibyishimo byinshi ku buryo ntashoboraga kugira icyo mvuga, ariko nasabwe n’ibyishimo bitavugwa maze nikubita imbere ye. Ubwo nari ndambaraye aho natentebutse, neretswe ibintu byiza by’agahebuzo kandi nasaga n’uwageze mu bwiza n’amahoro by’ijuru. Hashize umwanya imbaraga zanjye zaragarutse maze ndabyuka. Yesu yari akimpanze amaso ye yuje urukundo, maze kumwenyura kwe byuzuza umutima wanjye ibyishimo byinshi. Kuba imbere ye byanyujuje kwiyoroshya no kubaha bitavugwa ndetse binyuzuza n’urukundo rutarondoreka……

“Izi nzozi zampaye ibyiringiro….kandi ubwiza no kwiyoroshya biva mu kwiringira Imana byatangiye gusaba umutima wanjye wari mu mwijima.”-Ellen G.White, _Inyandiko z’Ibanze_, p.82-83.

Mu ngorane zo muri ubu buzima, tuba dukeneye guhanga amaso kuri Yesu ndetse n’icyo aduhishurira cyerekeranye n’urukundo Imana idukunda.

`Ni ibihe byiringiro uyu mwanya ushobora gukura mu byanditswe mu Abaroma 8:18, 28?`
