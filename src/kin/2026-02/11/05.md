---
title:  Inzira Ijya Emawusi
date:   10/06/2026
---

Mu byumweru bike byari bishize hari habaye ibintu bikomeye kuri abo bigishwa babiri barimo biyibutsa bimwe muri ibyo bintu hamwe n’ibiganiro bari bumvise. Kwinjira muri Yerusalemu nk’umuneshi ndetse no kweza urusengero. Pasika yabereye mu cyumba cyo hejuru. Amasengesho Yesu yasenze ari i Getsemane. Ubugambanyi bubi bwa Yuda. Gucirwa urubanza, gukwenwa, no gukubitwa. Umubiri wa Yesu washenjaguwe umanitswe ku musaraba ndetse n’amagambo aheruka yavuze mbere yuko umutima uca ubwo ikirere cy’igicamunsi cyahindukaga umwijima. Gutabukamo kwirangira k’umwenda wo mu rusengero. Gukinguka kw’ibituro by’abera. Gukura umubiri wa Yesu ku musaraba mu buryo butuje no kuwushyira mu mva mbere yuko isabato itangira. Nuko urujijo, gucika intege, ndetse n’ibibazo bikaza mu bitekerezo by’abigishwa. Ni mu buhe buryo ibyo byose bari babisobanukiwe nabi?

Abayoboke ba Yesu bari babonye ibyo batari biteze, bacitse intege, kandi bari mu rujijo. Iyi ni yo yari ingorane ikomeye kurusha izindi zose yari ije mu buzima bwabo. Icyo batari babonye ni uko icyo cyari igihe gito gusa mu nkuru ikomeye y’ibihe byose. Ubwo babiri muri bo bari mu nzira ijya Emawusi, Yesu yarababonekeye agendana na bo.

`Soma ikiganiro kiboneka muri Luka 24:13-27 maze utekereze imirebere ibiri itandukanye: abigishwa babiri, na Yesu.`

Ubwo amaso yabo yari ahumutse, abigishwa babiri bihutiye kujya i Yerusalemu kubwira abandi ibyabereye mu nzira (Luka 24:33). Ubwo Yesu yazaga agahagarara hagati muri bo, bagize ubwoba bwinshi. Ita kuri iki kibazo yababajije: “Ikibahagaritse imitima ni iki, kandi ni iki gitumye mwiburanya mu mitima yanyu?” (Luka 24:38).

Ubu ni ubutumwa bwa Yesu no kuri twe uyu munsi. Kenshi cyane, twibagirwa ko Yesu agendana natwe mu bikombe byacu. Inshuro nyinshi ntabwo tumumenya. Kenshi cyane twibagirwa ko hari byinshi byiyongera kuri iyi nkuru. Inshuro nyinshi twumva duhagaritse imitima kandi tukemera ko gushidikanya kuvuka mu mitima yacu, tukibagirwa ko Yesu arindiye ubuzima bwacu mu biganza bye. Kandi kenshi cyane dutekereza ko tuzi neza ibibera mu buzima bwacu kurusha uko Yesu abizi (Luka 24:18).

`Bibiliya itanga inama nziza yerekeranye n’uburyo twebwe nk’Abakristo dushobora kwitwara mu bibazo n’ingorane. Fata umwanya wige iyi mirongo migufi ikurikira: Abaroma 8:28; Abafilipi 4:4-13; Yakobo 1:2-4, 12; na 2 Abakorinto12:9, 10. Nk’umugabane w’icyigwa cyawe, andika ubutumwa butatu bw’ingenzi ushobora kubwira umuntu uhanganye n’ingorane uyu mwanya, uzirikana ibyanditswe mu 2 Abakorinto1:4.`
