---
title:  Kira
date:   08/06/2026
---

Tekereza ikivunge cy’abantu benshi bari ku nkombe y’injanya ya Galilaya. Bari bahereye mu gitondo kare bategereje ko Yesu agaruka, kandi ubwo yavaga mu bwato, babyiganye bamusanga, bamukurikira mu mudugudu wa Kaperinawumu. Uwo mwanya, Yayiro, umutware w’isinagogi, agaragara asaba Yesu kuza gukiza umukobwa we.

Umwe muri iyo mbaga y’abantu ni umugore wari umaze imyaka myinshi arwaye. Yari yaratanze amafaranga ye yose mu baganga ariko “ntibagira icyo bamumarira, ahubwo arushaho kuremba” (Mariko 5:26). Yaje kumva iby’uyu muntu ukomeye w’i Galileya, maze mu byiringiro byari bivutse mu mutima, yegeranya udutege yari afite, ava mu rugo rwe muri icyo gitondo yisunga iyo mbaga y’abantu. Umubyigano w’abantu wendaga kumuheza umwuka ubwo yageragezaga kwegera bugufi bwa Yesu. Nuko muri uko gusunikana no guhirikana, uwo mugore abona Yesu. Yishakashatsemo ubutwari ati: “Ninkora imyenda ye gusa ndakira” (Mariko 5:28).

`Soma Mariko 5:21-34. Ni iki cyabaye, kandi ni iki dushobora kwigiramo?`

Ibi byabaye bigaragaza uburyo Yesu yita ku barwayi n’abigunze kandi akabagirira impuhwe, ndetse n’abazimiriye mu kivunge cy’abantu benshi. Abenshi uwo munsi basunikiwe iruhande rwa Yesu ubwo bahirikwaga n’icyo kivunge cy’abantu, ariko umwe wenyine ni we wari ufite intego yo kugera kuri Yesu kandi akamukoraho kugira ngo ahabwe umugisha yari akeneye cyane. Nyamara kumukoraho kwe si ko kwamukijije; kwizera kwe ni ko kwamukijije (Mariko 5:34). “Umukiza yari afite ubushobozi bwo gutandukanya umukozeho afite kwizera no gupfa gukoraho nk’ikivunge cy’abantu batari bafite icyo bitayeho.” Ellen G. White, _Uwifuzwa Ibihe Byose_, p. 270. Umwambaro wa Yesu nta mbaraga zidasanzwe wari ufite; ahubwo kwizera k’uwo mugore ndetse n’amahitamo ye yo gusanga Yesu kugira go amukoreho ni byo byamukijije.

Uwo mugore wari ufite intege nke, mu mubabaro we no kwiheba, yashoboraga kwigumira mu buriri mu rugo muri icyo gitondo; ariko aho kugenza atyo, yahisemo ku bushake bwe no mu byiringiro kujya gushaka Yesu afite ibyiringiro byo gukira. Kumurebera kure ntabwo byari bihagije; yegeye hafi ye. Muri iki gihe, Yesu aturarikira gukora nk’ibyo uwo mugore yakoze. Aravuga ati: “Mwese abarushye n'abaremerewe, nimuze munsange ndabaruhura. Mwemere kuba abagaragu banjye munyigireho, kuko ndi umugwaneza kandi noroheje mu mutima, namwe muzabona uburuhukiro mu mitima yanyu” (Matayo 11:28, 29).

`Ni mu buhe buryo uyu mugore mu cyifuzo gikomeye yari afite yagaragaje igitekerezo kiboneka mu Abaroma 5:3-5? Mbese ibi byamera bite mu mibereho yawe?`
