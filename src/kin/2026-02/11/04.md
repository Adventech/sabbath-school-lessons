---
title:  Yobu
date:   09/06/2026
---


Iyo dutekereje ku ngorane zivugwa muri Bibiliya, ahari Yobu ni we muntu wa mbere uhita uza mu ntekerezo zacu. Ntabwo yabuze ubutunzi bwe bwose gusa (Yobu 1:14-17), ahubwo yabuze n’abana be (Yobu 1:18,19) ndetse n’ubuzima bwe buhura n’ingorane (Yobu 2:7). Nuko, umugore we agerageza kumwemeza ko agomba kuvuma Imana akipfira (Yobu 2:9).

Nyuma y’igihe runaka, inshuti eshatu zaje gusura Yobu. Zaguye mu kantu zibonye uko asa ku buryo zicaranye na we, zikabura icyo zivuga, zikamara iminsi irindwi (Yobu 2:13). Nyuma yaho, ubwo zagiraga icyo zivuga, zagerageje gutanga impamvu za kimuntu zishobora kuba zaratumye ibyago nk’ibyo bigera kuri Yubu, nyamara mu gukora zityo, zabaga ziri kongera umubabaro we zitabigambiriye. Inshuti eshatu zashyize amakosa kuri Yobu, zivuga ko ashobora kuba afite icyaha yahishe mu mibereho ye agomba kwihana (Yobu 8,11,15), ndetse ziravuga ziti: “Ni ukuri aho ni bwo buturo bw’ukiranirwa, kandi aho ni ho hantu h’utazi Imana” (Yobu 18:21).

`Mbese Yobu yabyifashemo ate? Soma Yobu 19:23-27 na Yobu 23:8-12.` 

Nubwo yari akikijwe n’ibiteye ubwoba, kandi akaba atari asobanukiwe impamvu yabyo, Yobu yakomeje kuba indahemuka. Yakomeje kwiringira Imana. Ntabwo yaherereje icyaha ku Mana cyangwa ngo ayivume. Ibiri amambu, ubwo yasakiranaga n’igishuko cyo guherereza ikosa ku Mana, yaravuze ati: “Navuye mu nda ya mama nambaye ubusa, nzasubira mu nda y’isi nta cyo nambaye. Uwiteka ni we wabimpaye, kandi Uwiteka ni we ubintwaye. Izina ry’Uwiteka rishimwe” (Yobu 1:21).

Natwe turi hagati mu rugamba rumeze nk’urwo. Satani aduteza uburibwe, imibabaro, ibihombo, ndetse n’ibirushya nk’umugabane w’umugambi we wo kugoreka uburyo tubonamo Imana y’urukundo. Mu bihe nk’ibyo, dushobora kwifata mu buryo bumwe muri ubu bubiri: guherereza ikosa ku Mana no kuyihakana cyangwa gushikama kuri Yo n’imbaraga zacu zose. Nubwo urugamba rurushaho gukomera ahatuzengurutse, tugomba kwibuka ko mu mucyo w’iby’iteka ryose, amakuba y’igihe gito ni ibigeragezo bitaramba (2 Abakorinto 4:16-18). Kuri iyo shusho hari byinshi cyane birenze ibyo tubona hano muri iki gihe, kandi kimwe mu bintu bikomerera umwizera ni ukwiringira Imana mu bihe bicuze umwijima. Mu buryo bwinshi, Imana yaduhishuriye ukuri kw’impamo k’urukundo rwayo. Tugomba gushikama kuri uko kuri kw’ingenzi- urukundo rw’Imana-nubwo muri uwo mwanya twaba tutarwumva.

`Niba uri hagati mu bihe bikomeye muri iki gihe, tabaza Imana. Fata Bibiliya yawe ndetse n’ikayi ubane n’Imana uri hanze mu byaremwe. Andika amagambo ari mu Abaroma 5:3-5 maze utekereze ku butumwa butandukanye buri muri iyo mirongo, wizere ko urukundo Imana igukunda n’uburyo ikwitaho ari ibintu byizewe kandi bidahinduka mu buzima bwawe.`
