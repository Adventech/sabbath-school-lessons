---
title:  Ibindi byo Kwigwa no Kuzirikanwa
date:   10/04/2026
---

Imana ihamagarira ubwoko bwayo guhagararira imico yayo, ariko kugira ngo tubigereho, tugomba kuyimenya ubwacu. Uburyo bwiza cyane bwo kuyibona neza, n'ubwo imirebere yacu twebwe abantu b’abanyabyaha akenshi isobanukirwa nabi inzira zayo zera kandi zitunganye, ni ukurondora mu Ijambo ryayo, ari ryo Bibiliya.

“Urukundo rwahererekanyijwe binyuze mu mitima y’abantu kuva ku gisekuru ukagera ku kindi, n’amasoko yose yo kugira neza yafukuwe mu mitima y’abantu, iyo bigereranyijwe n’urukundo rw’Imana rutagajuka, byose bihinduka akagezi ugereranyije n’inyanja itagira inkombe. Nta rurimi rubasha kubisobanura, nta karamu yabasha kurwandika. Mubasha kurutekerezaho buri munsi mu buzima bwanyu, mubasha kurushakashaka mu Byanditswe Byera mwitonze ngo mubashe kurusobanukirwa. Mubasha gukoresha imbaraga zose n’ubushobozi bwose Imana yabahaye ngo musobanukirwe urukundo n’impuhwe za Data wa twese uri mu ijuru: nyamara kandi ntirurondoreka. Mubasha kumara imyaka myinshi mwiga urwo Rukundo, ariko ntimubasha gusobanukirwa ijana ku rindi uburebure n’ubugari, ubujyakuzimu ndetse n’ubuhagarike by’urukundo rw’Imana rwatumye itanga Umwana wayo ngo apfire isi. Igihe kizira iherezo nacyo ubwacyo ntikibasha kuruhishura byuzuye habe na mba. Ariko, buhoro buhoro uko turushaho kwiga Bibiliya no gutekereza ku mibereho ya Kristo n’inama y’Agakiza, izi ngingo z’ingenzi zizarushaho kumvikana mu bwenge bwacu.” – Ellen G. White, _Ibihamya by’Itorero_, umuzingo wa 5, p. 686.

**IBIBAZO:**

`Mu mico y'Imana twasuzumye muri iki cyumweru, ni uwuhe wagufashije cyane kuyisobanukirwa?`

`Ni iyihe mico yindi y'Imana ushobora kwiga, kugira ngo ushimangire umushyikirano ufitanye n'Imana kandi uwukomeze?`

`Somera hamwe n'umwe mu bagize umuryango wawe cyangwa inshuti yawe, kandi mwumve neza amagambo yanditswe mu gice cya 1 cy'igitabo Kugana Yesu, maze muyaganireho. Ni izihe ngingo nshya zerekeranye n’imico (kamere) y'Imana na Yesu iki gice cyaguteye gutekerezaho?`

`Abantu benshi bafite imitekerereze mibi (ipica mbi) y’Imana, ari byo Yesu yaje gukosora. Ni iki wakora kugira ngo ufashe abantu mubana/mubonana ngo bagire ishusho isobanutse neza kandi ihuje n'ukuri y'imico y'Imana?`

`Ongera utekereze ku byo wize mu cyigisho cyo ku wa Kabiri. N'ubwo bigaragara neza ko turi abanyabyaha kandi ko tutari abera, nanone Bibiliya itanga amagambo amwe n'amwe asobanutse neza ku byerekeranye n'ukuntu ubwoko bw'Imana bwaranzwe no kugira imibereho itunganye (yera). Soma mu rwa 1 Petero 1:13-16, Abaroma 6:22 n’ Abaheburayo 12:14. Imana ni iyera kandi iturarikira kuba abera. Ariko se koko, kubaho mu buryo bwera bisobanura iki ?`

**Incamake**: Kuva Imana itangira kurema, yifuzaga ko tugirana na Yo umushyikirano wa bugufi. N'ubwo Satani yibasira cyane cyane ubumenyi dufite ku mico y'Imana, Imana itwiyereka mu buryo busobanutse neza cyane ibinyujije mu Ijambo ryayo no mu mibereho y'Umwana wayo Yesu. Mu by'ukuri, kugira ishusho y'Imana isobanutse neza kandi nziza ni iby'ingenzi niba dushaka kugirana na Yo umushyikirano wimbitse.
