---
title:  Ishusho Igaragara Cyane y’Imana
date:   05/04/2026
---

Bibiliya igaragaza neza uko Imana iteye mu buryo bwumvikana neza kandi buhuje n'ukuri. Bibiliya yose igamije gukuraho umwenda utagaragara uri hagati y’isi yacu y’ibigaragara n'ibitagaragara; kutwereka aho tuvuye n'aho tujya; kandi, amaherezo, ikatwereka utegeka kandi akagenga byose ndetse n’uko ateye.

Kuva mu Itangiriro ukageza mu Byahishuwe, dusoma ibyerekeranye n’Imana imwe nyakuri, yatwimenyekanishije ubwayo binyuze muri Bibiliya no muri Yesu Kristo, Imana yigize umuntu. Dushobora gusoma ibyerekeranye n’Imana Ishoborabyose (Yobu 1:12), Izi byose, kamere yayo yo kumenya ibyaremwe byose (Yesaya 46:9, 10), ubutabera bwayo (Yesaya 30:18), imbabazi zayo (Gutegeka kwa Kabiri 7:9), kugira neza kwayo no kwihangana itugirira (Abaroma 2:4), ubwenge bwayo (1 Abakorinto 2:7), ubuntu bwayo (2 Abakorinto 12:9), kubabarira kwayo (Matayo 6:14), ubushake idufitiye (Yeremiya 29:11), imbaraga zayo zo gutsinda urupfu (Yohana 11:25), ubwami bwayo (Zaburi 47:8), kamere yayo ihoraho (Gutegeka kwa Kabiri 33:27), n’ibindi byinshi biyiranga biduha impamvu nyinshi cyane zo kuyikunda no gukomeza kugirana umushyikirano na Yo. Uko turushaho kumenya Imana n’imiterere yayo, niko tuzarushaho kuyikunda no kwifuza kugirana na Yo umushyikirano ubudatuza.

Lusiferi ni we wabanje gushidikanya ku mico y’Imana. Gushidikanya kwe ku byerekeranye n'uko Imana iteye, amaherezo byatumye habaho intambara ikomeye cyane kurusha izindi zose zabayeho mu mateka y'isi n'ijuru. Kuva icyo gihe, “Satani ahora yiga uko yahoza ubwenge bw’abantu ku bintu bibahuza bigatuma batakira ubumenyi buva ku Mana.” – Ellen G. White, _Ibihamya by’Itorero_, umuzingo wa 5, p. 687. Satani nta cyo bimutwaye ku byerekeranye n'uko tubona Imana (twaba twemera ko hariho Imana iboneka mu bintu byose, twaba tutemera ko ibaho, twaba twemera Imana imwe gusa iri hejuru ya byose, n'ibindi n'ibindi), igihe cyose iyo mitekerereze idashingiye ku kuri.

`Soma Itangiriro 3:1-5. Ni iyihe ntego Satani yari afite igihe yavuganaga na Eva? Ni ikihe kinyoma yabwiye Eva cyerekeranye n'imico y'Imana? `

Mu buryo budashidikanywaho, ubutumwa Satani yahaye Eva bwaragiraga buti: _Imana yaguhishe amabanga. Imana ntiyifuza ko ugira imibereho myiza. Ntushobora kuyiringira_. Ellen White yakomeje asobanura ibi ubwo yagiraga ati, “Kuva mu itangiriro ry’intambara ikomeye, wari umugambi wa Satani ko agaragaza imico y’Imana uko itari kandi atere abantu kwigomeka ku mategeko yayo.” – Ellen G. White, _Abakurambere n’Abahanuzi_, p. 276 (2016).

`Ni buryo ki imico y'Imana igaragazwa mu buryo butari bwo muri iyi si yacu? By’umwihariko, ni buryo ki wowe, rimwe na rimwe, wagiye ugaragariza abandi nabi imico y’Imana? Niba byarakubayeho, ni iki wakora ngo bihinduke ubishobojwe na Mwuka Wera? Ni buryo ki wakwerekana urukundo rw’Imana mu bantu mubana/muturanye?`
