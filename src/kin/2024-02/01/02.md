---
title:  Intambara mu Ijuru
date:   31/03/2024
---

`Soma Ibyabishuwe 12:7-9. Ni iki iri somo ritubwira cyerekeranye n'umudendezo uba mu ijuri ndetse n'inkomoko y'ikibi? Ubwo Lusiferi yigomekaga, ni mu zihe nzira kandi ni ubuhe buryo Imana iba yarakoresheje bwo kubishakira igisubizo?`

Iyi mirongo yerekana intambara y'isanrure iri hagati y'icyiza n'ikibi, Satani n'abamarayika be berwanya Kristo, maze bacibwa mu ijuru. Bisa nk'ibitangaje cyane kumvako ahantu hatunganye nko mu ijuru habaye intambara. Byagenze gute? Mbese Imana y'inyarukundo yaremye umumarayika w'umudayimoni wabaye nyirabayazana w'iyo ntambara? Haba hari icyashe kibi mur'uyu mumarayika cyamuteye kwigomeka? Bibiliya isobanura neza inkomoko y'ikibi. Idukingurira umwenda takabona iby'yvi ntambara iri hagati y'icyiza n’ikibi. 

`Gereranya ibivugwa muri Ezekiyeli 28:12-15 no muri Yesaya 14:12-14. Ni ibiki byari mu ntekerezo (mu mutima) z'uyu mumarayika waremwe witwaga Lusiferi byamuteye kwigomeka?`

Ntabwo Imana yaremye sekibi. Yaremye ikiremwa gifite ubwiza burabagirana cyitwa Lusiferi. Uyu mumarayika waremwe yaremanywe ubutungane. Mu butungane bwe yanahawe umudendezo wo guhitamo -ari ryo hame ry'ingenzi ry'ubuyobozi bw'Imana riyobowe n'urukundo, atari igitugu. Icyaha cyakomotse kuri Lusiferi mu ijuru ubwaho. Nta busobanuro bwumvikana bwagaragaza impamyu uyu mumarayika wari uboneye yaba yaremereye ubwibone n'ishyari kwinjira mu mutima we bigakura kugeza ubwo bibyaye kwigomeka ku Muremyi we.

Lusiferi, ikiremwa, yashakaga kuramywa kugenewe gusa Umuremyi. Yagerageje kwigabiza intebe y'Imana mu kugisha impaka ubutegetsi bw'Imana. Kwigomeka kwe kwaje guteza intambara mu ijuru.

Nubwo Imana yihanganiye Lusiferi igihe, ntiyashoboraga kwemera ko ahindanya ijuru akoresheje ubwigomeke bwe. “Nuko mu ijuru hateranira inama zo gukebura Lusiferi Umwana w'Imana yamweretse ugukomera, ubugwaneza ndetse n'ubutabera bw’Umuremyi kandi anamwereka kamere itunganye kandi idahinduka y'amategeko y'Imana. Imana ubwayo ni yo yari yarashyizeho gahunda ikurikizwa mu ijuru; kandi gucisha ukubiri n'iyo gahunda byatumye Lusiferi asuzugura Umuremyi we maze yizanira kurimbuka. Nyamara imiburo yakomeje gutanganwa urukundo n'imbabazi ariko icyo yakoze ni ukumutera kwinangira, Ellen G. White, _Intambara Ikomeye_, p. 353.

`Ni ayahe masomo tubasha kwigira ku mico y'Imana mu guhangana n'ikibi?`
