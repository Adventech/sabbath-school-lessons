---
title:  Zaburi yo mu Gihe cyo Kwiheba
date:   09/01/2024
---

Gusenga hakoreshejwe amagambo ya Zaburi bikora ikirenze gushoboza abaramya gusenga mu bwisanzure. Zaburi zigenzura ibibabaho hakurikijwe igipimo cyashyizweho n'Imana kandi zikababashisha kubyihanganira binyuze mu gutangiza ibyiringiro no kwizera icyubahiro cy'Imana.

`Soma Zaburi 22. Ni iki dushobora kwigira kuri iyi Zaburi cyerekeranye no kwiringira Imana mu gihe cy'imibabaro?`

Amagambo yo kuganya dusanga muri Zaburi 22:2 ashobora gufasha abantu bababaye kugaragaza agahinda kabo no kugaragaza ko bari bonyine: “Mana yanjye, Mana yanjye, ni iki kikundekesheje, ukaba kure ntuntabare, kure y'amagambo yo kuniha kwanjye.” 

Aya magambo yaje kumenyekana mu Bakristo kuko ameze kimwe n'amagambo Yesu ubwe yavuze igihe yari ku musaraba. Aya magambo atwereka uburyo Zaburi ari izingiro ry'ibyabaye kuri Kristo (Soma Matayo 27:46). Nyamara no mu mibabaro ndetse n'ibigeragezo, aya magambo na yo arakoreshwa: “Nzabwira bene Data izina ryawe, nzagushimira hagati yiteraniro” (Zaburi 22:23).

Mu yandi magambo, nubwo ayo marangamutima ashobora kudahura n'ibibazo by'umwanditsi by'iki gihe, umunyezaburi yari akigaragaza kwizera afite mu Mana kandi akavuga ko, uko byamera kose, azakomeza guhimbaza Imana. Ibi bivuze ko, binyuze mu kudufasha kubona amagambo dukoresha dusenga, Zaburi zitwigisha kurenza amaso ibihe turimo, kandi kubwo kwizera, tugahanga amaso igihe ubwo imibereho yacu izagirwa mishya n'ubuntu bw'Imana.

Gusenga hifashishijwe amagambo ya Zaburi bigeza abaramya ku bya mwuka bishya. Zaburi zituma abaramya bagaragaza amarangamutima yabo n'imyumvire yabo, ariko ntabwo bakomeza kuba aho bari basanzwe bari. Zituma abaramya batura imitwaro yabo, ibibababaza, kwiheba, agahinda no kwiheba bakabitura imbere y'Imana kandi bakayiringira, uko ibibababaho byaba bimeze kose.

Urugendo rwo kuva ku maganya ukagera ku guhimbaza rugaragarira muri Zaburi kandi rugaragaza guhinduka mu bya Mwuka abaramya bagira iyo bakiriye ubuntu bw'Imana n'ihumure babonera mu isengesho.

`Ni mu buhe buryo dushobora kwiga kurenza amaso ibigeragezo by'iki gihe kandi tukizera kugiraneza kw'Imana, mu byo duhura na byo byose uyu munsi? `
