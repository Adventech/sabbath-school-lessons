---
title:  Undememo Umutima Uboneye
date:   12/02/2024
---

`Soma Zaburi 51:2-6. Ni kuki umunyezaburi asaba Imana imbabazi?`

Umwami Dawidi yasutse umutima we imbere y'Umwami Imana, amusaba kubabarirwa ibyaha mu gihe imibereho ye yari mu bihe by'umwijima w'iby'umwuka (2 Samweli 12). Imbabazi ni impano idasanzwe y'ubuntu bw'lmana, igaragazwa n’ “imbabazi zayo nyinshi” (Zaburi 51:1).  Umwami Dawidi asaba Imana kumugirira ibidahwanye nficyaha yakoze (Zaburi 103:10); ahubwo ikamugirira ibihwanye nfimico yubumana bwayo, ari byo mbabazi zayo, gukiranuka kwayo, n'impuhwe zayo (Zaburi 51:3; Kuva 34:6, 7).

`Soma Zaburi 51:6-19. Ni buryo ki imbabazi ’icyaha zigaragazwa hano? Ni uwuhe mugambi w’imbabazi z’Imana?`

Imbabazi_z'Imana zikubiyemo ibirenze gutangaza ku mugaragaro ko umuntu agizwe umwere. Zizana impinduka zimbitse zicengera zikagera mu migabane y’imbere y'umuntu bwite. (Zaburi 51:6, Abaheburayo 4:12). Ziremamo umuntu umutima mushya agahinduka icyaremwe gishya  (Zaburi 51:10, Yohana 3:3-8). Inshinga y'lgiheburayo _bara’_, yasobanuwe ngo “kurema,” yerekana ubushobozi bw'Imana bwo kurema (Itangiriro 1:1). Imana yonyine ni Yo ifite ubushobozi bwa ‘bara’ [bwo kurema]; Imana yonyine ni Yo ifite ubushobozi bwo kuzana impinduka idasubirwaho kandi irambye mu mutima w'umuntu w'ihana (2 Abakorinto 4:6).

Dawidi asaba kwejeshwa ezobu [agati Abayuda bagiraga icyuhagiro] (Abalewi 14:2-8, Zaburi 51:9). Yiyumvamo ko igicumuro cye cyamujyanye kure y’amaso y'Uwiteka, mu buryo bumwe nkubw’uko umubembe yajyanwaga kure y'umuryango yabagamo bitewe n'imibereho yanduye. (Zaburi 51:13). Yatinyaga ko ibitambo bidashobora kumweza rwose bitewe nuko nta gitambo cyashobora guhongerera ibyaha bye by'ubusambanyi n'ubwicanyi yakoze abigambiriye (Kuva 21:14, Abalewi 20:10).

Keretse gusa ubuntu bw’Imana ni bwo bwabashaga kwemera igitambo cy’ “umutima umenetse, ushenjaguwe” wa Dawidi maze bugasubiza muri Dawidi imibereho ihuje w'Imana (Zaburi 51:18, 19), Mu gusaba kwejeshwa ezobu, Dawidi ashaka kugaruka imbere y'amaso y'Imana.

`Niba Imana ishobora kububarira Davidl ibyaha n‘ubusambanyi, ubwihebe, n'ubwicanyi, ni ibihe byiringiro wowe ufite?`
