Soma Zaburi 36:5-15, n'ingingo ivuga iby “Urukundo Imana Ifitiye Abantu”, mu gitabo cyitwa “_Kugana yesu_”, pp-6-16. Ni mu buhe buryo ukuri kugaragaza yuko Imana ari urukundo kudufasha kurushaho gusobanukirwa n'imico y'Imana ndeste nibikorwa byayo muri Zaburi?

Soma Zaburi 36:5-15, n'ingingo ivuga iby “Urukundo Imana Ifitiye Abantu”, mu gitabo cyitwa “_Kugana yesu_”, pp-6-16. Ni mu buhe buryo ukuri kugaragaza yuko Imana ari urukundo kudufasha kurushaho gusobanukirwa n'imico y'Imana ndeste nibikorwa byayo muri Zaburi?

Uko dukomeza kwiga Zaburi, ni ngombwa kwibuka gusoma Zaburi dukurikije imico y'lmana yurukundo n'ubuntu ndetse n'umugambi wayo wo gukiza no gusana isi. “Uko turushaho gushimikira kumenya ingeso z'Imana, no gutekereza umusaraba wa Yesu, ni ko turushaho kumenya impuhwe ze n'ibambe no kubabarirwa n'ukuri no kutabera. Ni na ko turushaho gushishoza mibindi byinshi bigaragaza urukundo rutagira akagero n'ibambe Imana idufitiye, riruta iry'umubyeyi agirira umwana we wararutse. ”_Kugana Yesu_", p. 16. Muri Zaburi, niyo abantu bahuye n'urubanza rw'Imana kubwo kwigomeka kwabo, bakomeza kwiyambaza Imana kuko bazi ko uburakari bw'Imana ari ubwo muri icyo gihe gusa, ariko imbabazi zayo zikaba zihoraho (Zaburi 103:8).

`Kuki gusobanukirwa ukuri n'ubwinshi bw'ibibazo bikomeye ari ingenzi mu kudufasha gusobanukirwa yuko nubwo Imana ari Umwami n'Umutware, hakiri imvururu n'imibabaro mu isi yacu? Kuki impamvu y'intambara ikomeye idufasha cyane?` 

`Ni mu buhe buryo kwizera Imana nk'Umuremyi bikwiriye gutunganya imyumvire yo kwisobanukirwa ubwacu ndetse n'umushyikirano tugirana n'ibindi biremwa? Bigenda bite iyo abantu bateshutse ku kuri (Zaburi 106:35-42).`

`Ibibi byo gusenga ibigirwamana kw'amahanga mu bibe bya Bibiliya byari ibihe (Zaburi 115:4-8)? None se bimeze bite ku bigirwamana by'iki gihe? Kuki ibigirwamana biteza akaga mu rugendo tugendanamo n'Uwiteka?`

`Kuki ubwoko bw'Imana bwari bukwiriye kubaho buzi yuko urubanza rutangirira ku bwoko bwayo? Ni mu buhe burye Imana icira ubwoko bywayo urubanza kandi birangira bite?`
