---
title:  Ubutumwa Bugenewe Lawodokiya
date:   26/03/2026
---

`Soma Abakolosayi 4:16-18; Gereranya n' Abakolosayi 2:1-3. Ugitekereza ku butumwa yesu yahaye Lawodokiya (soma ingingo y'ejo hashize), ni iyihe sano ubona bufitanye zubwahawe Abakolosayi, bwagombaga gusomerwa abo mu itorero rya Lawodokiya ryo mu gihe cya Pawulo?`

Mu mateka y'ubwoko bw Imana mu myaka yatambutse, ibibazo nk'ibyo byagiye byisubiramo. Abahanuzi bagiye bacyaha Isirayeli kubwo gushaka kuramya nk'ab'isi ndetse bakabasaba kwihana mbere yuko amazi arenga inkombe. Yesaya na we yaraganyaga ati: "Dore ye, umurwa wiringirwaga uhindutse maraya"(Yesaya 1:21), maze agasaba abantu kugarukira Imana kugira ngo ibababarire kandi ibeze (Yesaya 1:16-20). Yohana Umubatiza (Matayo 3:2, 8-10) na Yesu (Matayo 4:17, Matayo 12:33-37) bombi bararikiye Isirayeli kwihana ndetse no kwera imbuto zari kwihanganira igerageza ry'urubanza mu minsi iheruka. Intumwa na zo zari zitwaye ubutumwa bumeze nk'ubwo (Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 2:38, Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 3:19, Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 17:30; 2 Abakorinto 7:9, 10).

`Gereranya imirongo ikurikira: Yesaya 60:1-3 n'Ibyahishuwe 18:1-4 ndetse na Yesaya 62:1-5 n' Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 19:7, 8. Ni iyihe sano iri hagati y'ubutumwa bwo muri ibyo bitabo byombi?`

Imana izunga ijuru n'isi. Ariko bitewe n'intambara ikomeye, ibyo bigomba gukorwa mu byiciro:

1. I Kaluvari, Satani yatakaje urukundo rwari rusigaye ibiremwa byo mu ijuru byari bimufitiye (Yohana12:31).

2. Binyuze mu murimo wa Kristo wo guca urubanza mu buturo bwera bwo mu ijuru, ubwoko bw'Imana "buratunganye rwose mu byiza byose, kugira ngo bukore ibyo ishaka" (Abaheburayo 13:21) kandi bukwiriye kujya mu ijuru.

3. Binyuze mu rubanza ruzamara imyaka igihumbi ndetse n'urubanza ruheruka ruzabaho nyuma y'imyaka igihumbi, ibibazo byose bisigaye birakemurwa kandi icyaha ndetse n'abanyabyaha batihannye bizatsembwaho bijugunywe mu njanya yaka umuriro, uzeza isi (Ibyahishuwe 21:8).

4. Icyaha nikimara gushyirwaho iherezo ni bwo ijuru n'isi bizunga ubumwe (Ibyahishuwe 21:3).

`Ni iki wowe ubwawe (utarebye ku bandi) ushobora gukora kugira ngo ukomeze kuba indahemuka ku Mana ndetse no ku kuri yaduhaye? Ni ukuvuga, ni ayahe mahitamo uri kugira agaragaza mu by'ukuri ufite umutima wawe?`
