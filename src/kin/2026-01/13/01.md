---
title:  Guhagarara mu Bushake Bwose bw'Imana
date:   21/03/2026
---

### Aho icyigisho cy'iki cyumweru gishingiye:
Abakolosayi 4:7-18; Abefeso 6:21; Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 15:36-40; 2 Timoteyo 4:10,11; 2 Petero 3:10-14; Yesaya 60:1-3.

> <p>Icyo kwibukwa:</p>
> "Mu bibaho byose muhore mushima, kuko ari byo Imana ibashakaho muri Kristo Yesu." (1 Abatesalonike 5:18).  Uyu mugabane uheruka w'urwandiko Pawulo yandikiye Abakolosayi ugaragaza ko abantu Pawulo yakoranaga na bo umurimo bari barushijeho kuba benshi. Igitabo cy'Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa kimugaragaza abanza gukorana na Barinaba, nyuma yaho akaza gukorana na Sila, maze kigatanga inshamake y'ingendo ze eshatu z'ivugabutumwa.

Muri iki cyumweru tuzarebera hamwe uburyo Pawulo yakoreshaga mu ivugabutumwa, bukubiyemo gukoresha neza igihe ndetse n'umutungo mu kugera ahantu hahurira abantu benshi mu bwami bw'Abaroma ndetse no gutoza abakozi basanzwe batangaga icyizere kugira ngo bagere mu mijyi ndetse n'ibirorero Pawulo atashoboraga kugeramo nka Kolosayi, Lawodokiya ndetse na Hiyerapoli.

Binyuze mu gusura yakoze ku giti cye mu ngendo ze ndetse by'umwihariko igihe yari imbohe, binyuze mu nzandiko ze, Pawulo yahoraga ahuza abantu n'amatorero. Yari asobanukiwe ko insinzi mu murimo wo kubwiriza ubutumwa bwiza yari ishingiye ku gukorera hamwe- kw' Abakristo b'Abayuda ndetse n'Abakristo b'abanyamahanga; abagabo n'abagore; abantu nka Tukiko, Arisitariko, Yusito, Epafura, Luka na Numfa. Igishimishije cyane, tunumva iby'urwandiko yandikiye ab'i Lawodokiya rutagize amahirwe yo kubona. Pawulo ashyira byinshi muri iyi mirongo iheruka, harimo no guhugura umugabo witwa Arukipo. Yakoze ibishoboka byose ngo akomeze amatorero igihe cyose yari akibishoboye.
