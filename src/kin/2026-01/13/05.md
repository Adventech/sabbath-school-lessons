---
title:  Kuba mu Isi Ariko Atari uw'Isi
date:   25/03/2026
---

`Soma Abakolosayi 4:14, 15 na 2 Timoteyo 4:10, 11. Mbese Luka atandukanywa ate na Dema kandi ni ukubera iki?`

Intumwa Yohana iratubwira iti: "Ntimugakunde iby'isi cyangwa ibiri mu isi. Umuntu nakunda iby'isi, gukunda Data wa twese ntikuba kuri muri we" (1 Yohana 2:15). Urukundo Luka yakundaga Yesu ndetse n'ubwami bwe rwatumye akomeza kuba hamwe na Pawulo kugeza ku iherezo uko byamera kose, mu gihe Dema yakundaga iby'isi akabirutisha iby'isi y'ahazaza.

`Soma imirongo ikurikira. Ni iyihe nama ihabwa abategereje kugaruka kwa Yesu?`

`1. Mariko 13:32-37`

`2. Tito 2:11-14`

`3. 2 Petero 3:10-14`

`4. Ibyahishuwe 3:17-21`

Yesu n'intumwa ze, batuburira inshuro nyinshi ngo: "Mube maso," mube muhugutse, kandi muhore mwiteguye igihe Shobuja azagarukira kugira ngo mutazatungurwa. Ikibabaje, nk'uko byagenze ku bigishwa bananiwe kubahiriza itegeko rya Yesu ryo "kuba maso no gusenga"(Mariko 14:38), abantu benshi ntibazakora imyiteguro ikenewe. Ibyo byose bigaruka k'uwo cyangwa icyo dushyizeho imitima yacu, kuko tutabasha gukorera abami babiri.

Mu butumwa bwahawe ab'i Lawodokiya, Yesu uduha itekego risobanutse. Icya mbere, ni ukwihana ibyaha byacu. Icya kabiri, ni uko tugomba gukingurira Yesu imitima yacu kandi tukemara ko ayiyobora, ari byo bidushoboza gusingira icya gatatu cyo kugira "izahabu" yo kwizera ndetse n'urukundo byatunganijwe n'igerageza maze bigatsinda igishuko.

`Ni ibiki Yesu yaba agusaba kwihana mu mibereho yawe? Ni uwuhe mugabane w'ibyo agutegeka waba ukeneye cyane kurusha iyindi?`
