---
title:  Gusabirana
date:   18/03/2026
---

`Soma Abakolosayi 4:2-4. Ni ayahe mahame agenga isengesho ubona muri iyi mirongo? Ni ibihe byifuzo Pawulo yifuza ko bisengerwa?`

Amwe mu magambo y'ingenzi dushobora kubwira umuntu uhanganye n'ibibazo by'uburyo butandukanye, byaba ibyo mu muryango, iby'ubuzima, iby'amafaranga cyangwa ikindi kintu cyose, ni "Ndi kugusengera." Ubu ni uburyo bwatoranyijwe n'ijuru bwo guhuza ndetse no gukorana. "Ni umugabane wa gahunda Imana iduha, nk'igisubizo cy'isengesho ryo kwizera. iyo itajyaga kuduha tudasabye dutyo."-Ellen G. White, _Intambara Ikomeye_, p.431.

Zirikana ubusobanuro bw'isengesho buzana impinduka Pawulo akoresha: "Mukomeze (cyangwa mushimikire) "gusengana umwete", "mube maso" kandi "mushima"- bugaragaza ko ari isengesho ryo kwizera (Abakolosayi 4:2). Adusaba gusenga "iteka" (Abefeso 6:18) kandi "ubudasiba" (1 Abatesalonike 5:17). Igitangaje kurusha ibindi, ni uko nubwo tutazi uko dukwiriye gusenga ... Umwuka ubwe ni we udusabira aniha iminiho itavugwa" (Abaroma 8:26).

`Ongera usome Abakolosayi 4:3. Ni uruhe "rugi rwo kuvuga ijambo" Imana yagukingurira kugira ngo ubwire abandi ibyo kwizera kwawe?`

Mu buryo busobanutse, Pawulo yanasengeye amagambo akwiriye yo kuvuga. Rimwe na rimwe, igihe dusoma inzandiko ze cyangwa imbwirwaruhame ze mu gitabo cy'Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa, dutekereza ko intumwa Pawulo burigiye yabaga ari intyoza, adafite gushidikanya ku byo yabaga akwiriye akwiriye kuvuga. Nyamara hano yasabye abantu ko bamusabira kugira ngo abashe kwamamaza ubutumwa "nk'uko bikwiriye" (Abakolosayi 4:4).Yanakoresheje kandi ijambo ry'Ikigiriki ry'ingenzi cyane (_dei_) mu magambo aheruka uwo murongo, rishobora gusobanurwa nka "nk'uko ngomba kuvuga," rigaragaza uburyo dukeneye Imana mu murimo wo kwamamaza ubutumwa bwiza. Yemeraga akamaro ko kugeza ubutumwa ku bantu bo mu nzego zo hejuru mu bwami bw'Abaroma, harimo n'ab'inzu ya Kayizari.

`"Si ngombwa ko buri gihe musenga mupfukamye. Nimugire akamenyero ko kuvugana n'Umukiza igihe muri mwenyine, igihe mugenda mu nzira, n'igihe mukora imirimo yanyu ya buri munsi. Nimureke umutima ukomeze kuzamuka mu isengesho rya bucece musaba ubufasha, umucyo, imbaraga, ndetse n'ubwenge. Uko muhumetse mujye musenga." Ellen, G. White, _The Ministry of Healing_, pp. 510, 511.`
