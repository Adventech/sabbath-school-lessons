---
title:  Kugendera mu Bwenge
date:   19/03/2026
---

Ni ukuhe kuri kurusha ukundi kose kuba ingenzi twebwe, nk'Abakristo, dushobora kumenya? Birumvikana ko ari uko Yesu Kristo yapfiriye ibyaha byacu kandi ko, binyuze mu kumwizera, dushobora kugira ubugingo bw'iteka. Uku ni ukuri twebwe ubwacu tutabashaga gutahura. Ahubwo kwari ukuri twagombaga kubwirwa cyangwa guhishurirwa. Kandi koko twaraguhishuriwe-mu ijambo ry'Imana.

Hari ukuri kwinshi, ubumenyi n'ubwenge tuba tutaramenye iyo Imana itabiduhishurira mu ijambo ryayo. Ariko ubu bumenyi n'ubu bwenge ntabwo twabihawe nk'ubumenyi gusa, mbese nk'ikintu tugomba kumenya gusa. Ibiri amambu, tugomba kubaho tugaragaza uko kuri, ubwo bumenyi ndetse n'ubwo bwenge mu mibereho yacu.

`Soma Abakolosayi 4:5, 6. Ni mu bihe bihe Pawulo agaragaza ko dukeneye by'umwihariko "kugendera mu bwenge"? Kuki ibyo ari ngombwa?`

Ikibabaje, nk'Abakristo, rimwe na rimwe nta kindi tuba turi cyo uretse Abakristo gusa! Kandi, nk'uko Pawulo abigaragaza (asubira mu byanditswe muri Yesaya 52:5), Isirayeli na yo yabaye igisitaza ku batizera: "Izina ry'Imana ritukwa mu bapagani ku bwanyu," (Abaroma 2:24). Ibyo dukorera abandi, by'umwihariko abo tudahuje kwizera, tugomba kubyitondera cyane (soma Tito 2:5, 2 Petero 2:2). Urugo rwa Gikristo, iteraniro ry'urubyiruko rugamije gusenga mu cyimbo cyo gukora ibibi, ibikorwa byoroheje by'ubugwaneza, umwuka wo gutuza no kwihangana, bisobanuye byinshi ku batwitegereza kugira ngo barebe niba ibyo tuvuga ko twizera ari iby'ukuri cyangwa atari byo.

Mu Abakolosayi 4:6, Pawulo yibanda by'umwihariko ku magambo tuvuga: "Ijambo ryanyu rifatanye iteka n'ubuntu bw'Imana." Ikirenze ku kugaragaza ubugwaneza n'ikinyabupfura, amagambo tuvuga akwiriye kuba afatanye n'ubuntu bw'Imana ku bw'imbaraga ya Mwuka Wera.

"Risize umunyu." Bihabanye n'imvugo y'ab'isi y'amagambo "asize umunyu," amagambo yacu akwiriye kuba aboneye kandi ashimisha abo tuyabwira.

"Kugira ngo mumenye uko mukwiriye gusubiza umuntu wese." Umwuka Wera ni we wenyine ushobora kuduha amagambo akwiriye mu gihe gikwiriye ku mpamvu ikwiriye kandi agategurira imitima y'abumva kwakira ubutumwa tugomba kubabwira (Aha nanone ijambo _dei[nk'uko nkwiriye kuvuga]_ rirakoreshwa-soma ibivugwa ku magambo ari mu Abakolosayi 4:4 mu ngingo y'ejo hashize).

`Tekereza ku magambo yawe, ibikorwa byawe, ndetse n'uburyo ugenda imbere y'abandi. Ni ubuhe butumwa uri gutanga ku bijyanye no kwizera kwawe ndetse n'icyo kuba Umukristo bisobanuye?`
