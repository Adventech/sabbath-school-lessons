---
title:  Isano Ishingiye ku Murimo
date:   17/03/2026
---

`Soma Abakolosayi 3:22-25 n' Abakolosayi 4:1. Ni ayahe mabwiriza ahabwa imbata? Ni he mahame ari hano agomba kugenga isano ishingiye ku murimo muri rusange?`

Muri iki gihe, rimwe na rimwe abantu bavuga ko ububata ari uburyo bwo gutesha agaciro inama zo muri Bibiliya zo mu gihe cyatambutse, niba atari ukwambura icyubahiro Ibyanditswe byera uko byakabaye. Ariko, mu buryo bunyuranye n'ubwo, ibi bivuga bike ku mateka ya Isirayeli yo mu Isezerano rya Kera ndetse n'itorero ryo mu Isezerano Rishya. Abantu baremwe mu ishusho y'Imana, kandi kimwe n'ibindi biremwa by'Imana bifite ubwenge, abantu baremewe kugira umudendezo. Amategeko ya Mose yabuzaga ko hari Umwisirayeli wakomeza kuba mu bubata iteka (Gutegeka kwa kabiri 15:12) kandi yateganyaga imyaka 6 itarenga yo gukora kugira ngo hishyurwe umwenda w'amafaranga (Kuva 21:2-6, Abalewi 25:39-43). Muri Bibiliya, ububata nubwo ari ikintu kitemewe muri iki gihe turimo, ntaho bwari bubi cyane ugereranyije n'ubucakara bwakozwe mu bihugu by'Uburengerazuba bw'isi, bwari ikiboko n'ubugome bukabije byakorerwaga inyokomuntu.

Mu bihe by'Isezerano Rishya, itorero ryagombaga gukora hashingiwe ku mategeko y'Abaroma, yemeraga ko abantu bagira imbata, "Ariko, mu buryo butandukanye n'uko ubucakara bwo muri iki gihe bukora, amategeko y'Abaroma yahaga imbata uburenganzira bwinshi ndetse n'amahirwe, kandi kugerageza gukuraho iyo migenzereze byajyaga gukoma mu nkokora iterambere ry'umurimo w'ivugabutumwa."—Clinton Wahlen, “Culture, Hermeneutics, and Scripture: Discerning What Is Universal,” in Frank M. Hasel, ed., Biblical Hermeneutics: An Adventist Approach (Silver Spring, MD: Biblical Research Institute/Review and Herald Academic, 2020), p. 166.

Mu by'ukuri, mu itorero, mu buryo butandukanye muri rusange n'ibyakorwaga mu bwami bw'Abaroma, inshingano ya mbere y'imbata yabaga iyifite ku Uwiteka. Kandi ba shebuja b'imbata bari bategetswe kubafata uko bikwiriye, "kuko muzi yuko namwe mufite Shobuja uri mu ijuru"(Abakolosayi 4:1). Na none kandi, Pawulo yahaye Filemoni amabwiriza yo kudakomeza gufata Onesimo nk'umugaragu we ahubwo akamufata nka mwene se (Filemoni 16). Mu by'ukuri, mu Isezerano rya Kera no mu Isezerano Rishya, abizera bitwa imbata (cyangwa abagaragu) b'Imana (soma Zaburi 34:22, Luka 17:10; 1 Petero 2:16).

Nubwo tudakunda imico imirongo imwe n'imwe ya Bibiliya yandikiwemo, tugomba kwemera ubutware bw'iyo mirongo ubwayo. Iyo bitabaye ibyo, twebwe ubwacu ndetse n'umuco wacu tuba twishyize hejuru y'Ibyanditswe byera. Uburyo bwiza ni ukureba buri kintu cyose Bibiliya ivuga ku ngingo runaka, mbere yo kugera ku mwanzuro w'icyo Bibiliya tubwira kuri iyo ngingo.

`Tekereza uburyo uyu murongo ushobora gukoreshwa ku isano ugirana n'abandi ishingiye ku murimo. Ni mu buhe buryo amahame awubonekamo ashobora kugufasha wowe nk'umukoresha cyangwa umukozi?`
