---
title:  Ibindi byo Kwigwa no Kuzirikanwa
date:   20/03/2026
---

Buri muntu mu bagize umuryango yari akwiriye kuzirikana ko afite inshingano yihariye yo gukora uruhare rwe atanga umusanzu we mu kwimakaza umutuzo, gahunye mu rugo, no gukora icyatuma ibintu bigenda neza. Ntawe ukwiriye gukora asenya undi. Bose bari bakwiriye gufatanyiriza hamwe gukora umurimo mwiza wo guterana umwete; bakagaragarizanya ineza no kwihanganirana, bagakoresha ijwi riciye bugufi kandi rituje, bakirinda guteza impagarara; kandi buri wese agakora uko ashoboye kose kugira ngo yorohereze nyina imitwaro imuvuna ... Buri wese mu bagize umuryango yari akwiriye gusobanukirwa n'uruhare yitezweho kuzuza afatanyije nabandi. Uhereye ku mwana w'imyaka 6 ukazamura, bose bakwiriye gusobanukirwa ko basabwa kugira uruhare bikorera ku mitwaro y'ubuzima."-Ellen G. White, _Urugo rwa Gikristo_, p.173.

"Niba dushaka kugendera mu mucyo, tugomba kwimika Kristo mu mitima yacu no mu ngo zacu. Urugo rukwiriye kuba nk'uko ijambo ribisobanura. Rukwiriye kuba ijuru rito ryo ku isi, ahantu urukundo rwitorezwa rugashimangirwa aho kuba ahantu rupfukiranwa. Umunezero wacu ushingiye kuri uku kwitoza urukundo, impuhwe, no kugaragarizanya ubwuzu nyakuri hagati yacu.

"Dukwiriye kwiyibagirwa, tugahora dushakisha amahirwe yo kugaragaza kunyurwa kubw'ibyiza abandi batugiriye, nubwo byaba ari mu tuntu duto, ndetse tugashakisha amahirwe twakoresha ngo dushimishe abandi, kandi tworoshye ndetse kuborohereze imibabaro yabo n'imitwaro yabo binyuze mu bikorwa by'urukundo. Urwo rugwiro rutangirira mu miryango yacu, ruraguka rukagera hanze y'imiryango yacu, rugafasha kubaka umunezero w'ubuzima; kandi uko kwirengagiza utwo tuntu duto bituma ubuzima bwuzura ubusharire n'agahinda." -- Ellen, G. White, _Ibihamya by'Itorero_, vol.3 pp. 576, 577.

**IBIBAZO:**

`Ku bashakanye, ni ayahe mahame yabafashije mu mibanire yanyu? Ni iyihe nama wagira abatarashyingirwa, n'uburyo bakwiriye kwitegura ingorane zitajya zibura buri gihe mu mubano w'abashakanye?`

`Ababyeyi benshi bakunda abana babo kandi babitaho barereye abana babo mu miryango myiza ya Gikristo akenshi bakunze guhura n'ingorane ziturutse ku bana bakuru baba baranze gukurikiza kwizera kwabo. Ni iyihe nama ndetse n'ihumure ushobora kubaha? Ni iki byarushaho kuba byiza twirinze kukivuga rwose?`

`Nimuganire ku wundi muburo wo "kugendera mu bwenge." Ibinyuranye n'ibyo se, "kugendera mu bugoryi" nk'ikinyuranyo cy'ubwenge, bisobanuye iki? Ni iki wigiye mu bihe bitandukanye ushobora kuba waragendeye muri kimwe muri ibyo?`
