---
title:  Kureka iby'Isi
date:   09/03/2026
---

Muri iki gihe, tujya twumva intero n'inyikirizo nyinshi: "Guhagarika intambara kureka gutsemba amashyamba!", "kureka gukoresha intwaro za kirimbuzi!" Ariko icyo dushobora kuba tutarumvise ni "ukureka iby'isi!" Ibyo ntabwo bihura n'ibyo abo muri iyi si yacu bishimira. Ibyo ntibivuze ko izindi ntero n'inyikirizo atari ukuri cyangwa ngo ibyo zishyigikiye bibe atari ukuri. Ahubwo abantu ntibareba kure, tuzirikanye uburyo iby'iteka ryose biri hafi. Twari dukwiriye kwibanda ku biri hejuru kandi bizahora biri hejuru.

`Soma Abakolosayi 3:5, 6 (soma n' Abaroma 6:1-7). Mbese twiyumvamo dute ubusobanuro by'icyo gupfa ku narijye no gupfa ku by'isi ari cyo, maze tukaba bazima "ku biri hejuru" (Abakolosayi 3:1)?`

Nubwo mu bya Mwuka twapfanye na Kristo, "ingingo" zacu, ni ukuvuga ibishuko twerekwa n'imibiri yacu ndetse n'imitima yacu, na byo bigomba gupfa.

Ariko dukwiriye kumenya ibintu bibiri bifitanye isano n'iri tegeko. Icya mbere: mu Abakolosayi 3:1, imvugo y'Ikigiriki Pawulo akoresha igaragaza ko mu by'ukuri twazuranywe na Kristo. Icya kabiri: itegeko ryo mu Abakolosayi 3:5 ni umusaruro w'uko kuri ("Nuko rero"). Dushobora gupfa ku by'isi (ubusambanyi, guhumana, irari, ibyifuzo bibi, kwifuza, n'ibindi) gusa kubera ko twazuranywe na Kristo kandi tukaba dufite imibereho ye y'ibya Mwuka ndetse n'imbaraga zo gukura ibyo mu bitekerezo byacu ndetse no mu mibereho yacu.

Igitangaje, ahandi hantu hamwe hagaragara interuro ngufi y'Ikigiriki imeze kimwe n'igaragara mu Abakolosayi 3:6, "umujinya w'Imana" ni mu Abaroma 1:18. Imana irareka abantu bagakurikiza inzira zabo mbi, ariko umujinya wayo na wo uzagera (soma Ibyahishuwe 6:16,17) ku "batumvira" (Abakolosayi 3:6). Mu Abaroma 1:18, Pawulo avuga "ubugome no gukiranirwa" akabigereranya no "guhumana" (akoresheje ijambo ry'Ikigiriki riboneka mu Abakolosayi 3:5) mu buryo buhura neza n'iby'abantu "bakurikiza ibyo imitima yabo irarikiye ngo bonone imibiri yabo" (Abaroma 1:24).

Bonona imibiri yabo bate? Icya mbere: kuko banga kwemera Umuremyi ariko na none bayononesha "ibyifuzo bibi." Kuko bigeza ubwo abagore babo bakoresha imibiri yabo uburyo bunyuranye n'ubwo yaremewe. Kandi n'abagabo ni uko, bareka kugirira abagore ibyo imibiri yabo yaremewe, bashyushywa no kurarikirana. Abagabo bagirirana n'abandi bagabo ibiteye isoni" (Abaroma 1:26, 27).

`Ni mu buhe buryo dukurikiza aya magambo ngo: "mwice ingeso zanyu z'iby'isi" (Abakolosayi 3:5)?`
