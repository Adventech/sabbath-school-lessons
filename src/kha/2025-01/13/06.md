---
title:  Halor Baroh, To Ieit Iwei Ia Iwei Pat
date:   27/03/2025
---

Lada ka jingieit ka long ka jingpyndep ia ka hukum, te ym lah ban sumar ia ka hukum U Blei ha ka jingmut kaba pura tang da kaba kiar na kaba leh ia kiei kiei kiba bakla. Ka hukum jong ka jingieit hi (ba la pynpaw ha ka jingdap jong ka Jingthoh Bakhuid) ym tang ba ka hukum ia ngi ban kiar na kaba sniew hynrei ka hukum ka kyntu ia ngi ban leh ia ki kam kiba pynpaw ia ka jingieit U Blei ha kiwei- ym tang ha kiwei pat ki dkhot balang hynrei ha ka pyrthei ruh. Kumta ka long, kaba donkam eh ia u Khristan bashisha ban sakhi

`Pule Jakob 2:1-9. Kiei ki khubor kiba kongsan kiba la ai ha ngi hangne?`

Hangne, u Jakob u kynthoh jur ia ka jingbymhok ha ka imlang sahlang, da kaba kdew kyrpang ia ka jingbym burom ia kiba duk bad ka jingshah lehbein ha ki katto katne kiba riewspah. Nangta, u khot ia ka jingpyrkhat sha ka hukum jong ka jingieit ia u paramarjan, u ong lada phi pyndep ia kane ka hukum, te “phi leh bha” (Jakob 2:8, NKJV). Kumba ka Ellen G. White ka la pynpaw : “Ka jingieit ia u briew ka long kaba kynja khyndew.

`Pule Ioannis 15:1-11. Kaei ka barieh jong ka jingsan ha ka jingkoit jingkhiah bad ha ka mynsiem?`

Kumba ka Ellen G. White ka la pynpaw : “Ka jingieit ia u briew ka long ka jingpynpaw pyrthei ia ka jingieit U Blei. Dei ban pynrung ia kane ka jingieit, ban pynlong ia ngi ki khun jong kawei ka longiing, ba u Syiem ka burom u la long kawei bad ngi. Bad haba la pyndep ia ki ktien iakhlad jong U, ‘To ieit iwei ia iwei pat, kumba nga la ieit ia phi’ (Ioanis 15:12); haba ngi ieit ia ka pyrthei kumba U la ieit ia ka, te na ka bynta jong ngi ka kamram jong U ka la pyndep. Ngi la biang na ka bynta ka bneng; namar ngi don ia ka bneng ha ki dohnud jong ngi.”—Ellen G. White, Ka jingthrang jong ki rta p. 641.

Haba ngi ieit ia ka pyrthei, kumba U Khrist U la ieit ia ka pyrthei — te ngi long kiba biang na ka bynta ka bneng. Katno ka long ka jingpynpaw kaba donbor ia kaei kaba mut ban long u nongbud jong U Jisu!

U Jisu u hukum ia ki nongbud jong U ban “ ‘ieit iwei ia iwei pat;’ ” kumba “ ‘Nga la ieit ia phi’ ” (Ioannis 13:34, NKJV). U Jisu u pynbna ruh: “ ‘Da kane baroh kin tip ba phi long ki synran jong Nga, lada phi don ka jingieit iwei ia iwei pat’ ” Kat kum, 1 Petros 4:8 ka kyntu ia ki Khristan: “Bad halor kiei kiei baroh to phin iaieit shitrhem paralok hi, naba ‘ka jingieit kan tap ia ki pop kiba bun’ ” (NKJV; peit ruh Heb. 10:24 bad 1 Thes. 3:12).

`To pynleit jingmut kham bun ha ka jingmut ban ieit ia ka pyrthei kumba U Khrist U la ieit ia ka pyrthei. Kumno kane ka lah ban iarap ia ngi ban sngewthuh kham bha ia ka jingmut jong ka jinglong janai jong ki Khristan bad kumno la pynlong ia ngi kiba bit na ka bynta ka jingim bymjukut? Wanrah ia ka jubab jong phi sha ka klas ha ka sngi Sabbaton.`
