---
title:  U Nongshun U La Leh Ia Kane
date:   23/02/2025
---

`Pule Mathaios 13:24-27. Kumno kane ka pharshi ka iarap ia ngi ban sngewthuh ia ka jingsniew ha ka pyrthei jong ngi?`

U Jisu u iathuh ia ka jingiathuhkhana shaphang uwei u trai jaka uba bet tang ia ki symbai kiba bha ha ka lyngkha jong u. Hynrei ki ñuit ki mih hapdeng u kew. Haba ki iohi ia kane, ki shakri jong u trai ki kylli ia u, “Ko kynrad, hato phim shym la bet ia u symbai uba bha ha ka lyngkha jong phi? Kumno te ka don ki ñuit?” (Math.13:27). Kane ka long kumjuh bad ka jingkylli kaba ju kylli bunsien shaphang ka jingeh jong ka jingsniew: Lada U Blei U la thaw ia ka pyrthei baroh kawei kaba bha, balei ba ka don ka jingsniew ha ka?

`Pule Mathaios 13:28-30 ha ka jingshai jong ka jingbatai U Khrist ha u Mathaios 13:37- 40. Kumno kane ka pynshai ruh ia ka Tynrai jong ka jingialeh pyrshah ha ka bneng jong ki bneng?`

U kynrad u jubab ia ka jingkylli jong u shakri jong u: “ ‘ “U briew uba shun u la leh ia kane” ’ ” (Math. 13:28, NW). Hadien U Jisu u pynithuh ia uta “ ‘uba bet ia u symbai babha’ ” kum “ ‘U Khun U Briew,’ ” uba long U Jisu hi (Math. 13:37, NKJV), bad u batai ba “ka lyngkha ka long ka pyrthei” (Math 13:37). 13:38), bad u “ ‘nongshun uba la bet’ ” ïa u ñuit u dei “ ‘u ksuid’ ” (Math. 13:39, NKJV), uba pyni shai ïa ka jingïaleh pyrshah ha ka bneng jong ki bneng hapdeng U Khrist bad u Soitan. Balei don ka jingsniew ha ka pyrthei? Ka jingsniew ka long ka jingmih na u nongshun (u ksuid) uba pyrshah ia u kynrad. “ ‘U briew u ba shun u la leh ia kane’ ” (Math. 13:28).

Hynrei kane ka jubab ka pynmih ia ka jingkylli kaba bud, “ ‘Me mon te ba ngin ialeit thiew noh ia ki?’ ” Ha kiwei pat ki kyntien, balei ym pynkhyllem noh mardor ia ka jingsniew? “ ‘ “Em, ” ’ ” u kynrad u jubab, “ ‘ “lym kumta katba phi dang thiew ia ki ñuit phin rat lem bad ki ia u kew ruh. Ai ba kin san lang haduh ka por ot” ’ ” (Math. 13:29, 30, NW; nujor bad Markos 4:29). Kat kum ka pharshi, U Blei un pynkut noh khatduh ia ka jingsniew, hynrei ka jingpynduh noh ia ka kloi kan wanrah ia ka jingpynjot kaba ym lah ban pynphai kaba pynmynsaw ia kiba bha.

`Kiei ki katto katne ki jingma ha kaba wad ban pynduh jait ia ki ñuit na u kew mynta? Ha kajuh ka por, balei kane kam mut tang ban iehnoh ia ka jingsniew kaba ngi iakynduh?`
