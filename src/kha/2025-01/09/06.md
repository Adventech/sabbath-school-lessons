---
title:  Ka Jinglong jong ka Jingialeh Pyrshah ha ka bneng jong ki bneng
date:   27/02/2025
---

Ngi la iohi katto katne ki bynta kiba hikai ia ka jingialeh pyrshah ha ka bneng jong ki bneng hapdeng U Blei bad u Soitan. Hynrei kumno kum kane ka jingialeh pyrshah ka lah ban long? Kumno uno uno un lah ban pyrshah ia U Blei uba donbor baroh? Lada ka jingialeh pyrshah ha ka bneng jong ki bneng ka long halor ka bor ba pura, kan jin da la kut noh shwa ba kan sdang. Ka dei ban long kaba pher. Hooid, ka Jingthoh Bakhuid ka pynpaw ba ka jingialeh ka long ka jingialeh pyrshah ka long ka jingiakajia halor ka jinglong U Blei — ka jingialeh pyrshah halor ki jingkynnoh pynjah burom ba la pynmih da u ksuid pyrshah ia U Blei, ba (nalor kiwei pat kiei kiei) Um long uba dap da ka jingbha bad uba dap da ka jingieit. Kum kine ki jingdawa ym lah ban jop da ka bor ne ka bor ba shyrkhei hynrei da kaba ianujor ia ki jinglong jong kine ki ar.

“Ha ka jingiadei jong U bad ka pop, U Blei U lah ban pyndonkam tang ia ka hok bad ka jingshisha. U Soitan u lah ban pyndonkam ia kaei kaba U Blei um lah — ka jingpynbyrngia bad ka jingshukor. U la wad ban pynlong lamler ia ka ktien U Blei bad u la pynpaw bakla ia ka jingthmu synshar jong U ha khmat ki angel, da kaba ong ba U Blei um long tang ha kaba buh ia ki ain bad ki kyndon halor ki nongshong bneng; ba ha kaba dawa ia ka jingshah pyndem bad jingsngap jar na ki jingthaw jong U, U la wad tang ia ka jingpynkhraw ia lade. Namar kata dei ban pyni ha khmat ki nongshong bneng, kumjuh ruh ha ki pyrthei baroh, ba ka jingsynshar U Blei ka long kaba hok, ka hukum jong U ka long kaba janai. U Soitan u la pynpaw ba ma u hi u wad ban kyntiew ia ka jingbha jong ka pyrthei. Ka jinglong bashisha jong u nongshim kabu, bad ka jingthmu bashisha jong u, dei ban sngewthuh da baroh. U dei ban ioh por ban pynpaw ia lade da ki kam bymman jong u.” Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 498.

`Pule Ioannis 8:44, 45 ha ka jingpynshai jong ka Jingpynpaw 12:7-9. Kine ki bynta ki pynpaw aiu shaphang ka jinglong jong u ksuid bad ka buit jong u?`

Ka jingthmu jong u ksuid naduh kaba sdang ka long ban pyrshang ban pynlong ia ki jingthaw ban ngeit ba U Blei um long shisha uba hok bad uba ieit bad ba ka hukum jong U ka long kaba pynshitom bein bad kaba pynmong ia ki. Kam long kaba phylla ba U Jisu u kdew ia u ksuid kum “u nonglamler bad u kpa jong ki” jinglamler (Ioannis 8:44, NKJV). Ha ka jingïapher, U Jisu u la wan ban “phla ïa ka jingshisha’ ” (Ioannis 18:37, NASB) bad ban ïaleh pyrshah beit ïa ki jinglamler bad jingkren beiñ jong u Soitan, da kaba jop bad, ha kaba kut, yn pynjot ïa u ksuid bad ïa ka bor jong u (1 Ioannis 3:8, 9). Heb. 2:14).

Ka Jingpynpaw 12:9, 10 ka pynithuh ia u Soitan (1) kum u “bsein temmen” (NKJV), (2) kum uta uba ha ka iingbishar bneng u kynnoh ia ki briew U Blei, bad (3) kum u iakjakor nongsynshar uba shukor ia ka pyrthei. Ka kyntien Grik ba la pynkylla “ksuid” ka mut tang “u nongkren bein,” kaba pyni sa shisien ba ka jinglong jong ka jingialeh ka long halor ki jingngeit, kynthup ia ki jingngeit shaphang ka jinglong U Blei.
