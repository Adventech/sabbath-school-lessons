### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong

Lukas 13:34; Jer. 32:17–20; Heb. 1:3; Deut. 6:4, 5; Eph. 1:9–11; Ioannis 16:33.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Nga la kren ia kine kiei kiei ruh ha phi, ba phin ioh ka jingsuk ha nga: ha kata ka pyrthei. Ha kata ka pyrthei phin ioh ka jynjar, hynrei to long shlur phi, nga la jop ia kata ka pyrthei.” (Ioannis 16:33, NKJV).

Ka jingkhrehlypa ka long ka kyntien ba la pyndonkam ban batai ia ka jingleh jong U Blei ha ka pyrthei. Kumno ngi pyrkhat shaphang ka jingpynkhrehlypa bastad U Blei ka pynlong ia ka jingiapher kaba khraw ha ka rukom pyniadei jong ngi bad U Blei, kumno ngi pyniadei bad kiwei pat, bad kumno ngi pyrkhat shaphang ka jingeh jong ka jingsniew.

Ki Khristan ki bat ia ki jingsngewthuh bapher bapher shaphang ka jingiohilypa U Blei. Don kiba ngeit ba U Blei U pyndonkam ia ka bor jong U ha ka rukom kaba pynbeit ia ki jingjia baroh ban jia kumba ki jia. U la jied wat mano ban ioh jingpynim bad mano ban duh! Ha kane ka jingpyrkhat, ki briew kim long kiba laitluid ban jied da kumwei pat lait na kaei kaba U Blei U la buh. Ha ka jingshisha, ki briew kiba ngeit ha kane ka rukom ki kynthoh ba wat ïa ki jingkwah jong u briew ruh la pynbeit da U Blei.

Ha ka jingïapher, ki sakhi kiba khlaiñ jong ka Baibl ki pyni ba U Blei um shym la buh ne rai ïa kiei kiei baroh kiba jia. Ha ka jaka kata, U ai ia ki briew ia ka mon laitluid, wat haduh katta katta ba ki (bad ki angel) ki lah ban jied ban leh beit pyrshah ia ka mon jong U. Ka histori jong ka Jinghap, jong ka pop bad jong ka jingsniew ka long ka jingpynpaw kaba sngewtynnad bad kaba sngewsih ia ki jingmih na ka jingpyndonkam bakla ia kane ka mon laitluid. Ia ka jingthmu jong ka lad jong ka jingpynim la seng khnang ban pynbeit ia ka jingjynjar kaba la mih na ka jingpyndonkam bakla ia ka mon laitluid.

_*Pule ia kane ka lynnong ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, February 22._
