---
title:  Pantokrator (U Ba Donbor Baroh)
date:   17/02/2025
---

Ha ka Jingthoh Bakhuid baroh kawei, la pynpaw ia ka bor ba phylla jong U Blei. Ka Baibl ka kynthup ia ki jingiathuhkhana bym lah khein shaphang ka jingpyndonkam ia ka bor jong U bad ka jingleh ia ki kambah jong U. Bad wat la katta ruh, wat hapdeng kane, bun kiei kiei kiba jia kiba U Blei Um kwah ban jia.

`Pule ia kine ki dkhot : Jingpynpaw 11:17, Jeremaiah 32:17-20, Loukas 1:37, bad Mathaios 19:26. Peit ruh ia ka Hebru 1:3. Kine ki dkhot ki hikai aiu shaphang ka bor U Blei?`

Kine ki dkhot bad kiwei kiwei ki hikai ba U Blei U long Uba donbor baroh bad U pynneh pynsah ia ka pyrthei da ka bor jong U. Hooid, ka Jingpynpaw ka kdew bunsien ia U Blei kum “U Trai U Blei Uba donbor baroh” (kum ka nuksa, Jingpynpaw 11:17; ianujor bad 2 Kor. 6:18, Jingpynpaw 1:8, Jingpynpaw 16:14, Jingpynpaw 19:15). , Jingpyn. 21:22) bad ka ktien ba la pynkylla “Uba donbor baroh” (pantokrator) ka mut shisha “Uba donbor baroh.” Ka jingshisha ba U Blei U long uba donbor baroh ym tang ba la pynskhem da ki ktien hynrei ka paw ruh ha ki jingjia kiba phylla kiba bun ha kaba U Blei U pyndonkam ia ka bor jong U ban pyllait im ia ki briew jong U lane da kumwei pat da kaba phylla u la ïengpdeng ha ka pyrthei.

Hynrei ban ong ba U Blei U long “Uba donbor baroh” kam mut ba U Blei U lah ban leh ia kano kano ka kam. Ka Jingthoh Bakhuid ka hikai ba don katto katne kiei kiei kiba U Blei um lah ban leh; kum ka nuksa, 2 Timothi 2:13 ka ïathuh, ba U Blei “um lah ban len ia lade ” (NKJV).

Katkum kane, kiba bun ki Khristan ki mynjur ba U Blei U long uba donbor baroh (uba donbor baroh), kaba mut ba U Blei U don ka bor ban leh ia kano kano ka kam kaba khlem don ka jingpyrshah— kata ka long, ia kano kano ka kam kaba lah ban long katkum ka jingmut bad kaba iadei bad ka jinglong U Blei. Ba don kiei kiei kiba ym lah ban long na ka bynta U Blei namar kin don ka jingpyrshah ia kiba la pynpaw ha ka jingduwai U Khrist ha Gethsemane. Katba U Khrist U pynskhem ba “ha U Blei kiei kiei baroh ki long kiba lah” (Math. 19:26), U la duwai ruh ha U Kpa katba ka jingshah sahnarphna ka la jan, “ ‘Ko Kpa jong Nga, lada lah, to ai ba kane ka khuri kan iaid noh na Nga; hynrei ym kumba nga mon, hynrei kumba me mon’ ” (Math. 26:39).

Hooid, U Kpa u don ka bor kaba pura ban pyllait im ia U Khrist na ka jingshah shitom halor ka diengphna, hynrei Um lah ban leh ia kane katba U dang pynim ruh ia ki nongpop. Ka la dei ban long ba un pyllait ne ne ba un ym pyllait, ym baroh ar.

`Ka Jingthoh Bakhuid ka hikai ruh ba U Blei U kwah ban pynim ia baroh (kum ka nuksa, 1 Tim 2:4-6, Titos 2:11, 2 Petros 3:9, Esek 33:11), hynrei ym baroh ki ban ioh jingpynim. Kane ka jingshisha ka hikai aiu shaphang ka jingshisha jong ka mon laitluid bad ki pud jong ka bor U Blei bad ki jingthaw ba la ai ia ka mon laitluid?`
