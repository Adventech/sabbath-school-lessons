---
title:  U Blei U Ieit Ia ka Hok
date:   18/03/2025
---

Ka Jingthoh Bakhuid ka pynbna ba U Blei U ieit ia ka hok bad U isih ia ka jingsniew (kum ka nuksa, Salm 33:5, Isa. 61:8), bad U sngewkhia shibun ia ka jingbymhok, kaba pynmih ia ka jingbitar kaba hok na ka bynta kito baroh kiba la shah lehbein ha ka jingbymhok. Ha baroh kawei ka Testament Barim bad Bathymmai, U Blei U long uba sngewlem eh na ka bynta kiba shah pynrem bad shah pynshitom bein katba U pynpaw ia ka jingbitar kaba hok pyrshah ia ki nongban bein bad ki nongpynshitom bein.

`Pule Salm 82. Kumno kane ka Salm ka pynpaw ia ka jingsngewkhia jong U Blei na ka bynta ka hok ha kane ka pyrthei? Ka lah ban mut aiu ia ngi mynta??`

Kumba bun ki nongthoh ki sngewthuh, kane ka bynta ka pynrem ia baroh ar ki nongsynshar pyrthei kiba donkti ha ka jingbymhok ha ka imlang sahlang bad ka long ruh ka jingkdew shaphang ka por ba U Blei U bishar ia ki nongsynshar ba ha khlieh (ki “blei”) shadien jong ki nongbishar bad ki nongsynshar pyrthei kiba bamsap (ki bor ksuid, ka paw shai). Kham kyrpang, la kylli ia ki nongsynshar, “Haduh katno phin bishar bym hok, bad phin ia shah shiliang ia kiba sniew?” (Salm 82:2, KB).

Shuh shuh, la pynkit ha ki kumne: “To iada ia kiba duk bad kiba khlem kpa; leh hok ia kiba jynjar bad kiba kyrduh. Pyllait ia kiba duk bad kiba kyrduh; to pyllait ia ki na ka kti ki bymman” (Salm 82:3, 4, NKJV). Hangne bad shawei pat, ki nongiathuhlypa jong ka Testament Barim ki buh ia ka jingkhot kaba shai bha na ka bynta ka hok. Kane kam dei ka jingsngewkhia ba shabar jong ka Jingthoh Bakhuid; ka long ka pdeng jong ka khubor jong ki nongiathuhlypa ha baroh kawei ka Testament Barim bad ia kaei kaba U Jisu u la kren haba u don hangne ha ka doh.

Kam dei ka ba rieh ia kaei kaba U Blei U kwah bad dawa na kito ki ban kam ba ki ieit bad kohnguh ia U. U batai shai bha ha u Mikah 6:8 (bad ki don ka jingiadei bad kiwei pat ki dkhot): “U la pyni ha me, ko briew, kaei kaba bha; bad aiu U Trai u dawa na phi hynrei tang ban leh hok, ban ieit ia ka jingisynei, bad ban iaid ha ka jingsngewrit bad U Blei jong phi?” (NKJV)

Ia kane ka jingsngew la pynpaw ha ka Jingthoh Bakhuid baroh kawei. Kum ka nuksa, U Jisu u la ong: “ ‘Da kane baroh kin tip ba phi long ki synran jong Nga, lada phi don ka jingieit iwei ia iwei pat’ ” (Ioannis 13:35, NKJV; ianujor bad 1 Ioannis 4:8–16).

`Kumno ki longiing bad ki balang jong ngi kin long lada ngi pynleit jingmut ha ka Mikah 6:8 bad ngi thmu ban pyntreikam ia ka ha ka ktien bad ka kam? Ha kano kano ka jinglong kaba phi don, kumno lah ban pynpaw kham bha ia ka jingpyndonkam ia kine ki nongrim?`
