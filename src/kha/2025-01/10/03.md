---
title:  U Iakjakor Jong Ka Kitab Jingpynpaw
date:   03/03/2025
---

Ka jingïohi kaba jylliew jong ki nongsynshar ha ki jaka bneng ha ka jingïaleh jong ka pyrthei la pynrung ha ka kitab Jingpynpaw, ha kaba la pyni ïa u ksuid kum “u Iakjakor bakhraw” uba pyrshah ïa U Blei bad “u shukor ïa ka pyrthei baroh kawei” (Jingpynpaw 12:9, NKJV).

`Pule Jingpynpaw 13:1–8. Kaei ba kane ka pynpaw shaphang ka jingpyniar ia ka hima jong U Iakjakor?`

U Iakjakor (Soitan) ym tang ba u ialeh thma pyrshah ia U Blei (Jingpyn. 12:7-9) bad ki shakri jong U (kum ka nuksa, Jpw. 12:1-6), hynrei la pyni ia u kum u nongsynshar uba don shadien jong ki hima pyrthei kiba pynshitom bein ia ki briew jong U Blei lyngba ki arta baroh.

U Iakjakor “u la ai . . . ka bor jong u, ka khet jong u, bad ka bor bah jong u” sha u mrad khlaw uba la kiew noh na ka duriaw (Jpw. 13:1, NKJV; nujor bad ka Jingpynpaw. 13:5; Jingp. 17:13, 14). Ia une u mrad na duriaw la “Te la ai ha u ka shyntur kaba ialap ia kiei kiei kiba khraw, bad ia ki jing-kren beiñ Blei: bad la ai ha u ia ka bor synshar ban sah jin sawphew ar bnai.” (Jingpyn 13:5, NKJV).

Kumta, u Soitan (u iakjakor) u ai ia ka bor bad ka bor synshar ha u mrad khlaw (ka bor niam-saiñ hima sima pyrthei). Ia kane ka bor la pyndonkam khnang ban knieh noh ia ka jingmane kaba dei ia U Blei. U mrad u kren bein ia ka kyrteng U Blei; ka ialeh thma ruh pyrshah, bad wat ka jop ia ki riewkhuid (riewkhuid) jong U Blei, la kumno kumno ha ka por kaba biang. Ia kane ka bor bad ka jingsynshar pyrthei la ai ha u da u iakjakor, u nongsynshar ba shim kabu ia kane ka pyrthei.

Hynrei, ki don ki pud kiba shai halor u Soitan bad ki bor jong u, kumjuh ruh, kynthup ïa ki pud kiba shipor. “Naba kumta to iakmen, ko ki bneng, bad phi kiba shong ha ki. Kordit ia kata ka khyndew, bad ia ka duriaw! naba uta u Ksuid u la leit hiar ha phi, uba don ka jingbitar bakhraw, naba u tip ba u don tang khyndiat por.” Jingpynpaw 12:12

U Soitan “u tip ba u don tang khyndiat por.” (Jingpyn. 12:12, ESV), bad ki jingjia ba la batai ha ka Jingpynpaw ki iaid katkum ki por kiba ia iaid ryngkat bad kiei kiba la iathuhlypa, kiba pyni ia ki pud kiba la buh beit (peit Jingpyn. 12:14, Jingpyn. 13:5) kiba ka jingsynshar jong kine ki bor basniew.

Hooid, ka jingjopthiaw U Blei ha kaba khatduh. “ ‘Te U Blei un ñiad noh ia ka jingjaw ummat baroh na ki khmat jong ki; bad ka jingiap kan ym long shuh, lymne ka jingsngewsih, lymne ka jingiam, lymne ka jingpang, kan ym long shuh: kita kiei kiei kiba mynshuwa ki la leit noh.” (Jingpyn 21:4, NKJV).

`Katno ka long kaba eh ia ngi ban iohi ia ka mynta, ha kaba kut ka jingbha kan jop junom ia ka jingsniew. Balei ka long kaba donkam haduh katta katta ba ngin ym klet ia kane ka jingkular kaba phylla?`
