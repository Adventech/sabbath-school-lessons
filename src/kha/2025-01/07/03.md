---
title:  Ki Don Bun kiei kiei Kiba Ngi Ngim Tip
date:   10/02/2025
---

Ka jingkut jong ka histori kan wan ryngkat bad ka jingjop jong ka jingieit halor ka jingsniew. Hynrei, hapdeng kata, bun ki jingkylli kiba pynkhuslai ki dang sah. Kumno ngin pyrkhat bad kren shaphang ka jingeh jong ka jingsniew ha ka rukom kaba lah ban iarap?

`Pule Job 38:1–12. Kumno U Blei Ka Jingjubab ia u Job ka pynshai ia ka jingeh jong ka jingbymman? Haduh katno ngi tip bad ngim tip shaphang kaei kaba lah ban jia sha lyndet jong ki jingjia?`

Ha ka jingiathuhkhana, u Job u la shah shitom shibun bad u la pynmih hi shibun ki jingkylli balei ba ka jingsniew bad jingjynjar ka la jia ha u. U la pan ia ka jingsngap na U Blei khnang ban wad ia ki jubab ia ki jingkylli jong u, khlem da tip ba kham bun kiei kiei ki dang jia shadien, ha ka ïingbishar bneng (peit Job 1, 2).

Ka jingkynthoh U Blei ia u Job ka long kaba phylla. Kham kyrpang, “U Trai u la phai ktien ha U Job napdeng ka erïong, bad u la ong, Uei une uba pyndum ia ka jingpyrkhat da ki ktien khlem ka jingtip?” (Job 38:1, 2, NKJV). Kawei ka jingpynkylla ktien ka buh kumne: “Balei phi kren bun haba phi tip tang khyndiat eh?” (Job 38:2, K.B.). Bad, U Blei u bynrap ha u Job 38:4, “ ‘Hangno me la don mynba nga la buh ia ki nongrim jong ka pyrthei? To iathuh ia Nga, lada phi don ka jingsngewthuh’ ”

`Pule Job 42:3. Kumno ka jingkynthoh jong u Job ka pynshai ia kaei kaba ngi dei ban ithuh shaphang ïa la ka jong ka kyrdan?`

Da ki jubab jong U ia u Job, U Blei u la pynshai ha u Job ba ki don shibun kiei kiei kiba u Job um tip bad um sngewthuh. Kum u Job, ngi ruh, ngi dei ban ithuh da ka jingsngewrit ba ki don shibun kiei kiei kiba jia ha ka pyrthei, bad shadien jong ki, kiba ngim tip ei ei shaphang jong ki. Ka jingshisha ba ngi lah ban tip ia ki jubab jong ki jingkylli jong ngi kam mut ba ym don ki jubab kiba bha ne ba ha kawei ka sngi baroh kin ym lah ban pynbeit. Haduh kata ka por, ngi donkam ban shaniah ha ka jingbha jong U Blei, kaba la pynpaw ha ngi ha kiba bun ki liang.

`Pyrkhat katno ngi tip khyndiat shaphang kano kano ka kam. Balei, te, ngi dei ban nang ban im bad ki jingkylli ki bym don jubab shaphang ki sobjek kiba eh tam: ka jingsniew bad ka jingjynjar?`
