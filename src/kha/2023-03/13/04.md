---
title:  'Ki Juti: Ka Balang Ka Jingialeh Ka Jingsuk'
date:   19/09/2023
---

U shipai Rom, katba u pynkhreh na ka bynta ka thma, un teh skhem ïa la ki juti shipai jong u. Ka pohjat jong ka juti ka dei ban long kaba skhem bad kaba bun syrtap, ba la pynskhemm da ki prek, kaba ïarap ïa u shipai ba u lah ban skhem ha ka khyndew bad ba u lah ban “Ïeng”  (Eph 6:11, 13, 14). U Paul u batai shaphang ka jingphong kjat jong u shipai kaba u shu sot na ka kitab u Isaiah 52:7, kaba ïathuh ï aka jinglehkmen ha kaba u nongwan rah khubor u wanlam ïaka khubor jong ka jingjop jong u Yahweh na ka bynta ki briew jong u. (Isaiah 52:8-10) bad ka jingsuk ka synshar mynta. “ sa type Isaiah 52:7

`Peit biang ïa ki phra sien kiba u Paul u thoh shaphang ka jingsuk ha ka shithi kiba ngeit ha Ephesos. Balei u pyndonkam ïa kine ki dur ki dar jong ka kam shipai haba u don ka jingsngewtynnad shaphang ka jingsuk? Eph 1:2; 2:14, 15, 17; 4:3; 6: 15,23.`

U Paul u shadkmen ha lor ka jingsuk kum ka jingtrei U Khrist, “Ka Jingsuk Jong Ngi” Uta Uba ïalap shaphang ka jingsuk “Sha kito kiba jngai bad ka jingsuk ïa kito kiba don hajan” (Eph 2:14-17), da kaba u khring lang ïa ki Jiw bad ïa ki Jentil sha kawei ka longïing jong ki jaitbynriew bathymmai (Eph 2:15) da kaba ïai pynsah im ïa ki jingïathuhkhan jong ka Gospel kaba U Khrist U la wan ban pynim bad ï aka kam jong ka jingpynlong jong U ï aka jingsuk, da kaba peit shadien ï aka jingjop kaba U la pyndep bad da kaba khmih ruh shakhmat ï aka jingjop da ka jingrisa na ka bynta ka lawei, kiba ngeit ki dei ban pynskhem ïa la ki kjat ba ki lah ban ïeng kiba khlaiñ na ka bynta ka jingïaleh. Kum u nongrah khubor babha ha ka kitab u Isaiah 52:7 kiba ngeit kum ki nongrah khubor kiba pynbna ï aka jingjop U Khrist bad ï aka jingsuk jong U.

U Paul, hynrei um shym la thrang ban khot ïa ngi ban rah ïa ki atiar thma pyrshah ïa ki nongshun jogn ngi. Dei na ka ta ka daw ba u la batai hangne ba kiba ngeit ki dei ban pynbna “Ïaka Gospel jong ka jingsuk,” (Eph 6:15). Em ruh, kam shym la dei kajingkwah jong u ba kiba ngeit kin ïeng ïaleh ha ka jingïadei bad kiwei, namar ba u la ïai ban halor ka jingïalong shitylli, da ka jingkren ai mynsiem jong u, da ka jingling dohnud. (Peit ruh Eph 4:25 – 5: 2). Ka balang ka dei ban “ïaleh ïa ka jingsuk” da kaba u pyndonkam ï aka Gospel kum ka atiar jong u khristan layngba ki jinglong (jong ka jingsngewrit, kajingïaishah slem, ka jingmap, bad kiwei kiwei.) bad u dei ruh ban ïai dwai bad ban mane. Kum kita ki rukom leh ki dei ïa kiba u la kdew kiba long ruh ka plan jong U Blei ban pynlong shitylli ïa kiei kiei baroh ha U Khrist. (Eph 1:9,10)

`Kumno kine ki jingsot kyntien ki lah ban ïarap ïa ngi ha ka jingïadei bad ki jingmutdur jong u kiba kiba-ngeit ki dei ban don kum ki shipai? “U Blei U khot ïa ngi ban phong ïa ki jingkieng atiar. Ngim kwah ïa kita ki jingkieng jong u Saul hynrei ïa ki jingkieng atiar jong U Blei baroh. Te kumta ngi lah ban leit  shaphrang ryngkat bad ka dohnud kum ka jong U Khrist kaba dap da ka jingjai jai, kajingsngewlem, bad ka jingieit. – Ellen G White, (Australasian) Union Conference Record, July 28, 1899.`
