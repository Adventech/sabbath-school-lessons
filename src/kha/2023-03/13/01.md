### Dkhot Baibyl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Eph. 6:10–20, 1 Pet. 4:1, 1 Pet. 5:8, Isa. 59:17, Isa. 52:8–10, 1 Thess. 5:16–18.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> Nalor baroh putet phin da shim ia ka stieh ka jingngeit da kaba phi lah ban pynlip ia ki sum bameh ding baroh ki jong u byman.  To shim ruh ia ka jingda khlieh ka jingpynim bad ka waitlam U Mynsiem kaba long ka ktien U Blei kein.  (Ephesos 6:16,17).

Ka jingthoh ba pawnam jong u John Bunyan, The Pilgrim’s Progress, (Ka JIngïaid Lynti U Pilgrim),  kaba u la thoh katba u dang don ha byndi, ïa u khristan la ïalam lynti sha ka khet syiem ba la buh ïa ki atiar ïaleh bad ïa u la pyni “ïa baroh ki tiar ïaleh bad ki jingbuh jingsat, kiba ki u trai jong ki u la pynbiang na ka bynta ki pilgrim, kum ka waitlam, ka stieh, ka jingïada khlieh, ka jingda shadem, bad ki jingdwai baroh bad ki juti ki bym ju man rim. Bad la don hangta baroh khoit ki jingdeng jingkup kiba la biang ïa baroh ki briew na ka bynta ka jingshakri ha ka kam U Kynrad, katba don ki khlur hangtei ha bneng.” Ha shuwa ba u khristan un mih, ïa u la ïalam biang sha ka jaka buh ki tiar ïaleh, “ki la shim ïa ki bad ki la phong naduh khlieh haduh kjat bad kita ki la lah ban ïada khlem kano kano ka jingpynma, ïoh lehse, u lah ban ïashem ïa kano kano ka jingïaleh ha ka lynti ïaid jong u.”

Ki jingthoh jong u Bunyan ha u snem 1678 ki pynkynmaw ïa kaei ba la thoh 1600 snem mynshuwa da u apostol Paul, ha ka shithi jong u sha kiba ngeit ha Ephesos, ïa kiba u la thoh katba u dang don ha byndi. Ha kane ka jingthoh u la mutdur ïaka kynhun shipai kaba long ka balang kaba la leit rung sha ka jaka buh atiar ïaleh jong U Blei kiba biang na ka bynta kano kano ka jingïada bakynja-mynsiem, ka jingbatai ktien ha ka ktien Greek na ka bynta kajingïada na khlieh sha kjat, ki atiar ïaleh jong U Blei ki ïa biang thik na ka bynta uno uno u shipai kiba u dei ban deng kum shibynta na ki atiar ïada ha ka kynhun shipai jong U kiba da u nar isbad kiba u dei ban phong naduh na khlieh haduh ha kjat. Katba ki leit ban ïaleh ï aka jingsuk ha ka kyrteng jong U.

_*Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, September 23_
