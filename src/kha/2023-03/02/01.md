### Dkhot Baibyl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Eph. 1:3–14; Eph. 2:6; Eph. 3:10; Kol. 1:13, 14; Deut. 9:29.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> Long ba la kyrkhu ia U Blei bad U Kpa U Jisu Khrist uba U Trai jong ngi, uba la kyrkhu ia ngi da ki jingkyrkhu bakynja mynsiem baroh ha ki jaka bneng ha U Khrist”  Ephesos 1:3

Hadien arphew san snem ba u Neil Armstrong u la long u briew uba nyngkong ban iaid halor u bnai, u thoh ia ka shithi ai khublei ia ka kynhun kaba la saiñdur ia ki jainkup ban phong ha haw haw, ka Extravehicular Mobility Unit (EMU), kaba la long u mawjam ha ka histori. Da kaba khot ia ka, “ka liengsuin ba shah shon dur bun tan ha ka histori” bad sin ia ka ba ka lah ban buhrieh ia “ki nongshong ba briew” na kaba iohi, u Armstrong u ai khublei ia “ka EMU Gang” ha ka Johnson Space Center na ka bynta ka ka jainkup “ kaba khlaiñ, kaba lah ban shaniah, kaba sngewsuk” kaba iada ia ka jingim jong u, daba kaba u phah ruh, “ ia ka jingaikhublei bad jingiaroh na ka bynta ki 25 snem.”

U Paul u sdang ia ka shithi sha ki nong Ephesos da ki kyntien ai khublei kaba sngewtynnad, da kaba iaroh ia u Blei na ka bynta ki jingkyrkhu kiba u la theh, ki jingkyrkhu kiba donkam ia ka jingim jong ki riewngeit kumba ka long ia ka jainkup haw haw ia uba iaid halor u bnai. U Paul u iasaid ba u Blei u trei na ka bynta kine ki jingkyrkhu kiba donkam naduh “shwa ka jingpynlong ia ka pyrthei” bad iaroh ia u Blei na ka bynta ka jingtrei ka jong U lyngba ki yrta na ka bynta ki riewngeit.

Ka jingsdang jong u Paul hangne ka pynlong ia ki nong Ephesos kiba kyrpang ha kaban iarap kumno ban mane ia u Blei bad iaroh ia u Blei na ka bynta ki jingkyrkhu kiba bun ba U la ai. Pule ia kane ka lynnong ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, July 8.

_*Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, July 8_
