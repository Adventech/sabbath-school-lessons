---
title:  Khatduh Khatwai, Markhmat
date:   22/06/2026
---

La thaw ia ngi ban long hajan U Blei (Jenesis 2:7). Naduh ka por ba u la thaw ia u bynriew, U Blei u la ai lut ha kaban pynbha pat ia ka jingiadei jong ngi bad U (John 3:16). U buh ia ka bymjukut ha ka dohnud jong ngi, pynban u bynriew um lah ban thew ia kaei kaba U Blei u la leh na kaba sdang sha kaba kut (Eklesiastis 3:11). Ngi long shi bynta na ka jingialeh bakhraw kaba khih bitar sawdong jong ngi—wat ia ngi ruh—pynban, bunsien, ngim ju sangeh khyndiat ban pyrkhat ia ka dor bakhraw ia kaei kaba la ai khnang ba ia ngi yn pynbha sha ka jingiadei kaba U Blei u la thmu ia ngi ban don bad u. Bunsien, ngi lap ialade ba la khum ha ki jingialeh bad jingtynjuh ka pyrthei, da kaba klet ba “ka jinglong traishnong jong ngi ka long ha bneng; nangta ruh ngi ia ap khmihlynti ia U Nongpynim, U Jisu Khrist uba U Trai; uban thaw thymmai ia ka met ka jinglong rangli jong ngi, ia kaban pynlong ia ka shidur bad ka met ka burom jong u” (Philipi 3:20, 21).

Katba ka pyrthei ka ngam sha kaba kut jong ka, ngi tip u lyoh baiong barit un mih paw ha ka sahit bneng ka mihngi. Katba u dang jan nang jan, ngin iohi ha uta u lyoh u shong “uba kum U Khun u briew, uba don halor ka khlieh jong u ka pansngiat ksiar, bad ha ka kti jong u ka rashi banep” (Jingpynpaw 14:14). Ia U Jisu yn synran lang da ki hajar bad ki hajar ki angel (Mathaios 25:31), bad ka khmat baroh kan iohi ia U (Jingpynpaw 1:7). Katba u nang hiar, ngin iohsngew ia ka jingpyrta jong U, ka jingput turoi U Blei, bad ki jingtep jong kito kiba la iohthiah ha U Khrist kin plie katba ki nang mih (1 Thessaloni 4:16). Kin tip ia ka sur jong Uta uba la khot ia ki (Ioannis 5:28).

`Kaei kaban jia hadien? Pule 1 Thessaloni 4:17. Khatduh khatwai, kaei kaba u Paul u la thoh ha ka Philipi 2:10, 11 kan sawa lyngba ka bneng salonsar.`

Katno ka long ka jingpyrkhat bakhraw ba phylla! Ha kawei ka sngi ngi iohi ia U Jisu—ban iohi shisha ia U. Ngin iohsngew ia ka sur jong u, bad ngin phla ba u long U Trai. Uta uba ngi la ju pule, sha uba ngi duwai, ia uba ngi la iakren bad kiwei; Uta uba ka dohnud jong ngi ka thrang . . . ngi iohi shisha ia u markhmat. Ngi lah ban sngewtikna ia kane, namar U Blei u long uba iaineh, bad ki jingkular jong u ki long kiba shisha (Jingpynpaw 22:6).

Ha kata ka khyllipmat ba ka turoi kan sawa bad ba baroh ka khmat ka iohi ia U Jisu, ngin tip ba ka la long ka jingap kaba shongdor. Baroh ki jingduwai ba minot, man ki khyllipmat ba ngi buh ia u ha khmat, man ka por ba ngi kren shlur shaphang jong u, man ki jingtynjuh—kin poi ha kaba kut ha ka jingiohi ia ka khmat jong U (Jingpynpaw 22:4).
