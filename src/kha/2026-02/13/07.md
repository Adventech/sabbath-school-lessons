---
title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat shuh shuh
date:   26/06/2026
---

“Lada ngim ioh pdiang ia ka jingngeit U Khrist da kaba bam na ka ktien jong u, ngin ym don hok ban iohrung sha ka nongbah U Blei. Haba ngi la im ha ki jingbam pyrthei, haba la hikai ia ki jingmad jong ngi ban ieit ia ki jong ka pyrthei, ngin ym long kiba bit na ka bynta ka phyllaw ka bneng; ngin ym lah ban iaroh ia ka jinglong jingman bakhuid jong ka bneng. Ka sur jong ki angel bad ka jingtem jong ki duitara kin ym pynhun ia ngi. Ka jingstad jong ka bneng kan long ka jingbymshai ha ka jingmut jong ngi. Ngi dei ban thrang bad ban sliang ia ka hok U Khrist; ia ngi dei pynwandur da ka bor jingktah ka jingpykylla ka jingaiei jong u, khnang ba ngin long kiba bit ia ka imlang sah lang ki angel ha bneng. . .”

“Kumta ki jaitbynriew kin ym don shuh kino kino ki hukum palat ban ia ki hukum ka bneng. Baroh kin long kiba kmen, ka longing ba tylli, ba la pynkup da ka jainkup ka jingiaroh bad ka jingainguh. . . . Ha ki jingiohi jong ngi, ki khlur step kin ia rwai lang, bad ki khun U Blei kin pyrta ia ka jingkmen, katba U Blei bad U Khrist kin ia snoh haba pynbna, ‘Kan ym don shuh ka pop, bad kan ym don shuh ka jingiap.’

“Ngi kwah ban ioh ia kane ka jingmlien ban kren ia ka bneng, ka bneng ba itynnad. Iakren ia kata ka jingim kaban iai bteng katba im U Blei, bad phin klet ia ki jingtynjuh bad jingjynjar. Ai ba ka jingpyrkhat kan long kaba la khring sha U Blei.”—Ellen G. White, The Faith I Live By, sla. 363.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Lada ioh lad, pule lane sngap ia ka jingthoh ka Ellen G. White ia ka jingthuhpaw ia ka bneng kaba lah ban shem ha ka kot Early Writings, sla. 14-20. Kaei kaba kren jur eh ha phi ha kane ka jingbatai?`

`Kaei ka bynta jong ka jingiapule ha kane ka kwater kaba phi kwah eh ban iai kynmaw khnang ban pynneh ia ka jingiadei ba khlain jong phi bad U Blei haduh ba phin iohi ia U Jisu markhmat?`

`Mano ha ka jingim jong phi kiba donkam ban iohsngew ia ka jingkyrmen jong ka bneng? rai kut ban iasam bad ki kham kloi. Kynmaw: ba phin ym lah ban iasam ia ki ia ka jingkyrmen kaba phi phim don ha la marwei.`
