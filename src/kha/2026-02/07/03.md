---
title:  Haba Ki Jingduwai Ki I Kumba Ym Shem La Jubab
date:   11/05/2026
---

`Lehse phi la duwai shaphang kaei kaei kaba la slem bha, lehse la bun ki snem, bad ka sngew kumba U Blei um shym la iohsngew ia ki jingduwai jong phi. Ka Baibl ka iathuh ia ngi ban “pan, te yn ai ha phi” (Math. 7:7), “lada ngi pan ei ei katba kum ka mon jong U” (1 Ioannis 5:14) Kaei ka jingsngewthuh jong phi shaphang kine ki jjingkular?`

Ka Hannah ka long ka nuksa jong ki kynthei kaba aiti lut kaba la duwai na ka bynta kaei kaei kaba kyrpang bha (1 Sam. 1:10-17). Ha kaba nyngkong ka la long kumba U Blei um shym la jubab ia ki jingduwai jong ka, hynrei ka la iaipyneh, bad U Blei u la jubab, ha ka por kaba janai jong U bad kat kum ka mon jong U. Teng teng ka jingap ka pynjylliew ia ka jingiaid jong ngi bad U Blei, katba ngi nang ban shaniah shuh shuh ha U.

Salm 62:8 ka ong, “To shaniah ha U ha ki por baroh phi ki briew, to theh noh ia la ki dohnud ha khmat jong U, U Blei U long ka jaka rieh ia ngi”. Jingshaniah. Hato ngi shaniah ba U tip shisha ia kaei kaba bha tam, wat haba ngim iohi ia ka jingjubab mardor ia ki jingduwai jong ngi? Hato ngi shaniah ba khatduh khatwai Un sa jubab ha ka por bad ka rukom kaba janai jong U?

Teng teng ki jingduwai jong ngi kim lah ban ioh jubab kloi katba ngi kwah ne ha ka rukom kaba ngi kyrmen ba kin long. Kaei ka jingsneng kaba ka Baibl ka ai ha ngi shaphang kane?

- Wad ia ka mon U Blei, ym ia la ka jong (Math. 6:10; 1 Ioan. 5:14, 15).
- Pyrkhat ia ki jingthmu jong phi (Prob. 16:2, Jakob 4:3).
- Pyrkhat lada phi don ne em ka pop kaba phi sngewtynnad ban leh (Salm 66:18, 1 Pet 3:12, Prob 15:29).
- To iaineh ha U Blei bad ha ka Ktien jong U (Ioannis 15:7). • Dei ban don ka jingngeit haba duwai (Heb 11:6, Jakob 1:6, Markos 11:24, Math 21:22).
- Pyrkhat ia ka jinglong jong ka dohnud jong phi (ba sngewrit ne ba sngew sarong) (Jakob 4:6, 7). Pet. 5:6).
- To iaishah (1 Thes. 5:17, 18).
- Map ia kiwei (Mark. 11:25, 26).
- Khatduh eh, U Blei U iohi ia ka dur kaba heh bad U tip ia kaei kaba bha tam na ka bynta jong ngi (Rom 8:28, Eph. 3:20, Jer. 29:11-13). Teng teng ka jubab jong U ka i kumba ka long tang na ka bynta u Paul: “ ‘Ka jingaiei jong nga ka biang na ka bynta jong phi’ ” (2 Kor. 12:9).

Ka jingshisha kaba kongsan kaba pynbeit ia ka jingkynthoh jong ngi ia kaei kaba i kumba ki jingduwai ki bym shem la jubab ka long ka dur jong ngi shaphang U Blei. Lada ngi iohi ia U Blei kum uba jngai bad uba khlem jingsngewkhia, ka jingiadei jong ngi bad U ka nang tlot. Ha kum kine ki por, wad ha ka Baibl na ka bynta ki sakhi jong ka jingieit bad jingsumar jong U ia phi, bad duwai ba ka dur ba la pynkylla khongpong jong phi shaphang jong U kan kham shai.
