title:  Kiwei Pat Ki Jingkylli Shaphang Ka Jingduwai
date:   14/05/2026
---

**Balei ban duwai haba U Blei u la tip lypa?** Balei ngi dei ban duwai haba U Blei u long uba tip lut? Ka Ellen G. White ka batai ia ka kumne: “Ym ba ka long kaba donkam khnang ban pynpaw ha U Blei ia kaei kaba ngi long, hynrei khnang ban pynlah ia ngi ban pdiang ia U. Ka jingduwai kam pynhiar ia U Blei sha ngi, . hynrei u pynkiew ia ngi sha U.” — Ki Step sha U Khrist, sla 93.Hooid, U Blei U tip ia ki jingkwah bad ki jingdonkam jong ngi, bad U pule ia kawei pa kawei ka jingthmu jong ka dohnud jong ngi. Hynrei, ka jingduwai ka long kaba bha ia ngi. Ka khot ia ngi ban sangeh hapdeng ka jingkhihwin ha ka jingim jong ngi, ban sangeh bad pdiang ba U Blei U long uba donbor halor kiei kiei baroh, bad ban buh ia lade ha ki kjat jong U. Lah ruh ban plie ia ki lad na ka bynta U Blei ban leh haba ngi khot ia U ban leh kumta. U Mynsiem Bakhuid u iasaid na ka bynta jong ngi haba ngim nang ban duwai kumba ngi dei ban duwai (Rom 8:26, 27).

**Balei ban duwai haba baroh ki long kiba bha?** Ka jingsngewrit bad ka jingsarong (peit ia ka Jinghikai 3) ka lah ban long kawei na ki jingkhang kiba khraw tam ia ka jingim jong ka jingduwai kaba khlain. Lada ngi shu sngewthuh haduh katno ngi donkam ia U Blei, ngin sa wan sha U man ka por! Lada ki angel kiba janai ki mane bad mane ia U, balei ngi, kum ki briew kiba pop, pyrkhat ba ngi’n donkam kham duna? Ki Mathaios 5:6 bad Isaiah 44:3 ki ong aiu shaphang kane ka jingshisha?

**Kaei ka bynta jong ka jingngeit ha ka jingduwai?** Pule Hebru 11:6 bad pyrkhat kine ki kyntien: “Ka jingduwai bad ka jingngeit ki iadei bha, bad ki donkam ban pule lang ia ki baroh ar. Ha ka jingduwai jong ka jingngeit ka don ka jingstad blei; ka long ka science kaba uwei pa uwei uba kwah ban pynlong ia ka kam jingim jong u kaba jop u dei ban sngewthuh. U Khrist u ong, ‘Kaei kaei ruh kaba phi kwah, haba phi duwai, ngeit ba phin ioh pdiang ia ki, bad phin ioh ia ki shisha.’ Markos 11:24. U ka pynshai ba ka jingkyrpad jong ngi ka dei ban long kat kum ka mon U Blei; ngi dei ban pan ia kiei kiei kiba U la kular, bad kaei kaei kaba ngi ioh ka dei ban . la pyndonkam ha kaba leh ia ka mon jong U. Ki kyndon ba la pyndep, ka jingkular ka long kaba khlem jingartatien. . . . Ngim donkam ban wad ïa ki sakhi kiba paw shabar jong ka jingkyrkhu.”—Ellen . G. White, Ka Jinghikai, sla 257, 258.

**Bad mano ba dei ban duwai?** Ngi dei ban duwai kham bunsien ia ka lade (tang U Blei bad ma phi), namar ka jingduwai bad ka jingpule Baibl ki long u thied snam jong ka jingim jong ka jingiadei jong phi bad U Blei. Pynbeit kyrpang ia ka por ban wad ia ka dohnud jong phi katba phi kren bad sngap ia U Blei (Math 6:6). Ngi dei ruh ban duwai lem bad kiba ha iing ha sem jong ngi lane ha ki kynhun kiba rit (Ki Kam 12:12), namar ha kaba ar ne lai ngut ki ialum, U Blei U don hangta (Math. 18:20). Khatduh eh, ngi dei ban duwai ryngkat bad ka balang jong ngi . ki jaidbynriew (Jakob 5:13-16). Baroh lai jait ki jingduwai ki long kiba kongsan .

**Kumno ngi dei ban sngap?** Ka jingduwai ka long kham palat ban ia kaba shu kren bad U Blei; ngi ruh donkam ban ailad ia U ban “pynkhuid” ia ngi bad ban kren ha ka jingim jong ngi. Kaba shai bad ka lad kaba shngain tam ban leh ia kane ka long ban pule ia ka Baibl bad pyniasoh lang ha ryngkat ka jingduwai. Ka jingpule Baibl ha ka por duwai step me jan mied jong phi. To husiar ban pynthylli ia ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong phi ne ka jingsngap ia la ki jong ki jingmut jingpyrkhat ban ia kaba wad bniah ia ka Baibl.

`Kaei kaba phi shem ba ka long kaba eh tam na ki mat ba la kdew haneng?`
