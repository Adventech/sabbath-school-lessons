---
title:  U Jisu U Hikai Ia Ngi Kumno Ban Duwai
date:   12/05/2026
---

Ha ka por U Jisu, ki jingduwai kiba jrong bad kiba la shna bha kum ka jingpyni—da kaba pyndonkam da ki kyntien kiba jylliew bad bunsien la buh jingkynmaw—ki la long kiba la niewkor bha. U Jisu um don ei ei kaba bha ban ong shaphang kine ki jait jingduwai (peit Math. 6:5-8). U la pynpaw ia ki ba kin long kumno ki long: ki jingpynpaw pyrthei jong ka “jinglong riewblei.”

Ki synran ki la iohi ia U Jisu ba u duwai, bad ki la tip ba ka jingduwai ka long ka bynta kaba donkam eh ha ka jingim jong U (peit Loukas 5:16, Loukas 6:12, Loukas 9:18, Loukas 22:41, Loukas 24:30, Markos 1:35, Markos 6:46). Katba ki dang peit ia U Jisu, ki la iohi ia ka jingiapher na ki nongialam niam bad ki la sngewthuh ba ka don shibun kiei kiei ha ka jingduwai ban ia kaba ki la ju pyrkhat. Kumta, ki synran ki la wan sha U Jisu, da kaba ong, “ ‘Ko Trai, to hikai ia ngi ban duwai’ ” (Loukas 11:1).

U Jisu u la pyni nuksa ia ki synran jong U (bad ia ngi) ba ngi lah ban duwai wat ha ki kyntien kaba ngi ju pyndonkam man ka sngi. U la pyni ia ngi ba ki jingduwai jong ngi ki dei ban long kiba naduh bad naduh ki dohnud jong ngi. Pule ia u Loukas 11:2-4 bad Mathaios 6:5-15 bad peit ia kine harum ki bynta jong ka jingduwai kaba U Jisu u la hikai:

- Ko Kpa jong ngi uba ha bneng: kaba pdiang ia ka jingiadei shimet jong phi bad U Kpa jong baroh.
- Long ba khuid ka kyrteng jong Me: ka jingithuh ia ka jingkhuid U Blei ka pynlong ia ngi ban wan da ka burom bad jingburom.
- Kan wan ka hima jong me: ka jingkwah ia ka jingwan phai U Blei bad ka jingshong sah jong U Mynsiem Bakhuid haduh ba Un da leh kumta.
- To long ka mon jong Me ha ka khyndew kumba ha bneng: ka jingaiti bad da kaba duwai ba ka mon U Blei kan long ha ka jingim jong ngi, da kaba shaniah ba U tip bha, ban ia kaba shu duwai na ka bynta kaei kaba ngi kwah.
- To ai ha ngi mynta ka sngi ia ka jingbam jong ngi kaba hala ka sngi: da kaba pan ia kaei kaba ngi donkam ban im, . ha ka met (ka bam bad ka um) bad ha ka mynsiem (U Jisu bad ka Ktien ba im jong U).
- To map ruh ia ki jingpalat jong ngi kumba ngi map ia kiba leh lehsniew ia ngi: ka jingkylla kaba mut, ka jingwad jingmap, bad ka jingkynmaw ban map ia kito kiba la pynmong ia ngi katba U Blei U map ia ngi.
- Wat ialam ia ngi sha ka jingpynshoi, hynrei to pyllait ia ngi na kaba sniew: da kaba pan ia ka jingiada bad ka jaka rieh na ki jingsniew ha kane ka pyrthei (Salm 91).
- Naba jong Me long ka hima, ka bor bad ka burom ruh hala ka yrta Amen: ka jingpdiang ba baroh ngi long, baroh ngi don, bad baroh ngi leh . ka jong U Blei. Tang ma u uba dei hok ia ka burom bad jingiaroh jong ngi (1 Khron. 29:11).

Balei phim duwai kham bun bad pynphai ia la ka khmat sha U Blei man la ka step ban iakren bad Uta Uba ieit ia phi kham bun ban ia kiwei pat? Kaei kaba khanglad ia phi ban leh ia kane kumba phi tip ba phi dei ban leh? Duwai mynta hi, kumba U Jisu u la khot ia ngi.
