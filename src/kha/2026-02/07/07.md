title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat shuh shuh
date:   15/05/2026
---

Lada ngi pyrkhat bad kren kham bun shaphang U Jisu, bad . kham duna ialade, ngi dei ban ioh kham bun shibun ia ka jingdon ryngkat jong U.”—Ellen G. White, 1985. Ki step sha U Khrist, p. 102.

“Lada ngi pan jingmut bad ki jingartatien bad ki jingtieng jong ngi, ne ngi pyrshang ban pynbeit ia kiei kiei baroh kiba ngim lah ban iohi shai, shuwa ba ngin don ka jingngeit, kin pynroi bad pynngam jylliew tang ha ki jingsngewthuh bakla.

Hynrei lada ngi wan sha U Blei, da kaba sngew ba ngi donkam jingiarap bad shaniah lut, kumba ngi long, bad ha ka jingsngewrit, ka jingngeit kaba shaniah lut ka pynpaw ia ka jingkwah jong ngi ha Uta Uba ka jingtip jong U ka long kaba khlem kut,. Uba iohi lut ia kiei kiei baroh ha jingpynlong, bad uba synshar ia kiei kiei baroh da ka mon bad ka ktien jong U, U balah bad un sngap ia ka jingkyrpad jong ngi, bad un ai ia ka jingshai ban shai ha ki dohnud jong ngi.”— Mawjam sha U Khrist, ki sla 96, 97.

“Ka mynsiem ka lah ban kiew kham jan sha bneng halor ki thapniang jong ka jingiaroh. Ia U Blei la mane da ka jingrwai bad jingtem ha ki iingbishar ha bneng, bad kumba ngi pynpaw ka jingsngewnguh jong ngi, ngi ia jan bad ka rukom jingmane jong ki paitbneng. To ngin wan da ka jingdonburom bad ka jingkmen ha khmat U Nongthaw jong ngi, da ka ‘jingainguh, bad ka sur jingrwai.’ Isaiah 51:3.”—Mawjam sha U Khrist sla 104.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Kaei ka jingpyrkhat ha ki jingkynthoh haneng kaba pynshlur bha ia phi? Kaei ka jingpyrkhat kaba phin hap ban pynlong ia ka jingialeh?`

`Kiei kiwei pat ki jinghikai kiba ngi lah ban ioh na ka jingim jong ka jingduwai jong kiwei pat ha ka Baibl? (Peit Esra 10:1; 2 Syiem 13:4; Ioan. 4:2, 3; Hab. 3:1; Ki Syiem 19:14-19; Jer. 32:16-25; Neh. 1:4-11; Ki Syiem 8:22-54; list tang katto katne.)`

`Ka treikam aiu jong ka jingshah jingit ryngkat bad ka jingduwai?`

`Hato don kaei kaei kaba thymmai kaba phi kwah ban pynkylla ne pyntreikam ha ka jingim jong ka jingduwai jong phi kum ka jingmih na ka jinghikai jong kane ka taiew? Balei ym pynlong ia ki jingkylla?`
