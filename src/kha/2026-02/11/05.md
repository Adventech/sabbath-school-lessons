---
title:  Ha Ka Lynti Sha Emmaus
date:   10/06/2026
---

Ka la long katto katne taiew kaba la pynkhih jingmut palat na ka bynta ki arngut ki synran katba ki dang ialeh ha la ki jingmut ia ki katto katne ki jingjia bad ki jingiakren kiba ki la mad. Ka jingrung da ka jingjop sha Jerusalem bad ka jingpynkhuid ia ka templ. Ka Niam ka Jinglait ha ka kamra kaba haneng. Ki jingduwai U Jisu ha Gethsemane. Ka jingshetkylla kaba sniew jong u Judas. Ka jingbishar, ka jingshah lehbein, ka jingshah shoh. Ka met ba la mong jong U Jisu kaba la sdien ha ka diengphna bad ki ktien ba khatduh jong U shuwa ba Un pynhiar mynsiem khatduh katba ka bneng mynstep ka la kylla dum. Ka jingsawa kaba jur jong ka jingkad jong ka jaiñ ha ka templ. Ki jingtep ba pra jong kiba hok. Da kaba pynhiar ia ka met U Jisu na ka diengphna bad buh ia U ha ka jingtep shuwa ka Sabbaton. Bad nangta ka jingklumar jingmut, jingduh jingkyrmen bad ki jingkylli ha ka jingmut jong ki synran. Kumno ki la ioh ia ka kaba bakla haduh katne?

Ki nongbud jong U Jisu ki la sngewsih, ki la duh mynsiem bad ki la sngewthuh bakla. Kane ka la long ka jingkhanglad kaba khraw tam ha ka jingim jong ki. Kaei kaba kim shym la iohi ka long ba kane ka long tang ka khyllipmat ha ka jingiathuhkhana kaba khraw tam jong ki por baroh. Katba arngut na ki ki dang iaid ha ka lynti sha Emmaus, U Jisu u la paw bad u la iaid ryngkat bad ki.

`Pule ia ka jingiakren ha u Luakas 24:13-27. Bad pyrkhat shaphang ki ar tylli ki jingiohi bapher bapher: jong ki arngut ki nongbud, bad U Jisu.`

Shisien ba ki khmat jong ki ki la plie, ki arngut ki nongbud ki la leit kloi sha Jerusalem ban iathuh ia kiei kiei kiba la jia ha ki ha lynti (Loukas. 24:33– 35). Katba U Jisu u dang wan bad u la ieng hapdeng jong ki, ki la sngewsheptieng. Peit bha ia ki jingkylli jong U ia ki: “Balei phi khuslai, bad balei ki jingartatien ki mih ha ki dohnud jong phi?” (Loukas 24:38, NKJV).

Kane ka dei ruh ka khubor U Jisu na ka bynta jong ngi mynta. Bunsien eh, ngi klet ba U iaid hajan jong ngi ha ki lhuh jong ka jingim jong ngi. Bunsien ngim ithuh ia U. Bunsien ngi klet ba don shibun kiei kiei ha ka jingiathuhkhana. Bunsien ngi sngew jynjar bad ngi ailad ia ki jingartatien ban mih ha ki dohnud jong ngi, da kaba klet ba U Jisu U bat skhem ia ka jingim jong ngi ha ki kti jong U. Bad bunsien ngi pyrkhat ba ngi tip kham bha ban ia U Jisu shaphang kaei kaba jia shisha ha ka jingim jong ngi (Loukas 24:18).

`Ka Baibl ka ai kum kine ki jingai jingmut kiba bha shaphang kumno ngi kum ki Khristan ngi lah ban leh ia ki jingeh bad ki jingkhanglad. Pyndonkam khyndiat por ban pule ia kine ki bynta kiba lyngkot: Rom. 8:28; U Philipi. 4:4-13; Jakob 1:2-4, 12; bad 2 Korinth. 12:9, 10. Kum shi bynta jong ka jingpule jong phi, thoh lai tylli ki khubor ba kongsan kiba phi lah ban ïasam bad no re bad no uba ïakynduh ïa ki jingkhanglad mynta. To buh ha ka jingmut ia ka 2 Korinth 1:4.`
