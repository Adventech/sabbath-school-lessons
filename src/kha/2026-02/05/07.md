---
title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat shuh shuh
date:   01/05/2026
---

Ka jingthmu jong ka jingpule Baibl ka long ban tip ia U Blei bad ban nangroi ha ka jingiadei jong phi bad U, namar kane ka long kata ka jingim bymjukut-ban long junom bad U Blei uba ngi ieit (Ioannis 5:39, Ioannis 17:3).

Ka bynta jong kano kano ka jingiadei ka long ka jingiateh ba markylliang. Ngi pule ha ka Jingpynpaw 3:20 ba U Jisu U wad ban don ia kane ka jingiadei bad ngi, hynrei ngi dei ban sngewthuh ba, kum ki jingthaw ba la thaw, ngi lah ban nang kham bun shaphang U Nongthaw jong ngi man ka por. Kum u nongtih uba tih ia ki mawkordor, ngi dei ban wad barabor ha ka Baibl. Ka don shi bun kaba ngi dei ban ioh, wat la katno sien ngi la pule ia ki katto katne ki jingiathuhkhana ne ki bynta jong ka Baibl. “La ka long kumno kumno ruh ka jingkiew ha ka bor pyrkhat jong u briew, wat ai ba wat tang khyndiat ba un pyrkhat ba ym donkam ban wad bniah bad iai bteng ia ki Jingthoh Bakhuid ban ioh ia ka jingshai kaba kham khraw. Kum ka jaitbynriew, la khot ia ngi marwei marwei ban long ki nongpule jong ka jingiathuhlypa.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 41.

Ngim dei ruh ban pyrshang ban pynlong ia ka Baibl ban iadei bad ki jingmut ne jingpyrkhat briew jong ngi hi. “Kumno ngin wad bniah ia ki Jingthoh Bakhuid? Hato ngin pynkhih ia ki jingbit jong ka jinghikai jong ngi kawei hadien kawei pat, bad nangta ngin pyrshang ban pynlong ia ka Jingthoh Bakhuid baroh ban iadei bad ki jingmut ba la pynskhem jong ngi? lane ngin shim ia ki jingmut bad ki jingpyrkhat jong ngi sha ki Jingthoh Bakhuid, bad thew ia ki jingmut jong ngi ha man la ki liang da ki Jingthoh Bakhuid kiba sngewthuh ia ka jingshisha? Bun kiba pule bad wat kiba hikai ia ka Baibl, kim sngewthuh ia ka jingshisha kaba kordor kaba ki hikai ne pule. . . . Bun ki ai ia ki kyntien jong ka Jingthoh Bakhuid ia ka jingmut kaba iadei bad ki jingmut jong ki hi.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 36.6.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Kaei ka jinglong kaba phi ju wan sha ka Baibl? Hato don kaei kaei kaba donkam ban pynkynriah? Balei ka jinglong jong ka jingsngewrit bad ka jingaiti lut ha ka Ktien ka long kaba kongsan haduh katta katta?`

`Hato ki don ki jingmut kiba la pynskhem kiba phi lah ban donkam ban buh sharud ban ailad ia ka Jingthoh Bakhuid ban kren hi dalade? Lada ka long kumta, kumno phi lah ban sdang ban duwai shaphang jong ki mynta hi?`

`Kumno ka jinglong tynrai ka lah ban long ka jingkhanglad ha ka jingiadei kaba shabar jong u briew bad U Blei? Kata, kumno ka jingkwah ban wad ia kaei kaei kaba thymmai bad kaba wanrah ia ki jingmut kiba thymmai ka lah ban ialam bakla ia u briew, khamtam lada ki leh ia kata na ka bynta ki jingthmu ba shimet?`
