---
title:  Ka Jingpule Bniah Ia Ka Baibl
date:   28/04/2026
---

Wat lada phim donkam ban long u riewstad ban pule ia ka Baibl, kumno phin pule ia ka Baibl da kaba ngam jylliew?

**Duwai:** Ka long ka bym lah ban pynpaw palat ia ka jingdonkam jong ka jingduwai kum ka jingbuh pynkut ia (bad ban buh jingkynmaw ia baroh kawei) ka por pule Baibl jong phi. Ka Ellen G. White ka iathuh ia ngi ba haba ngi wan ban pule ia ka Baibl, ngim long marwei. Da kaba khot ia U Mynsiem Bakhuid ban long u nongialam jong ngi, ngi kyntait ia kiwei pat ki jingpynthut baroh, bad u nongshun u phet noh. “Ym dei ban pule ia ka Baibl lano lano ruh khlem jingduwai. Tang U Mynsiem Bakhuid U lah ban pynlong ia ngi ban sngew ia ka jingdonkam jong kita kiei kiei kiba suk ban sngewthuh, lane ban khanglad ia ngi ban ialeh pyrshah ia ki jingshisha kiba eh ban sngewthuh.”—The Great Controversy, pp. 599, 600.

**Pule bad Thoh:** Lah ban ong ba ka jingiapher kaba kongsan hapdeng tang kaba shu pule ia ka Baibl bad ka jingpule ia ka ka wan sha kawei ka jingleh kaba kongsan: kata ka long ban thoh. Ka jingthoh ka iarap ia ngi ban pynjai jai ia ki jingpyrkhat jong ngi, ban pyrkhat ia ka Ktien U Blei, bad ka trei lyngba jong ka ha ka ha ka rukong jong ka jingpeit bniah, ka jingbatai, ka jingpyndonkam bad ka jingaiti ka lah ban jia. Ka iarap ruh ia ki jingmut jong ngi kiba her kylleng ban trei ha lade hi — na ka khlieh jong ngi, sha ka jingthoh jong ngi, bad nangta sha ki dohnud jong ngi na ka bynta kane ka sngi. Ngi kham sngewtynnad ruh ban kynmaw ia kaei kaei kaba ngi la buh jingthoh da kaba thoh (Salm 119:15, 16). Lada phim lah ban thoh ia ki jingpyrkhat jong phi, pyrshang ban pule ia ka Baibl da kaba jam (lane sngap ia ka), bud sa ki jingpyrkhat jong phi kum ka jingduwai sha U Blei.

**Iasam:** Iathuh iano re iano ia kaei kaba phi la pule bad nang. Kane kan pynskhem ia ka ha ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong phi bad kan pynshlur ia uwei ruh.

Jied ia ka kitab kaba lyngkot jong ka Baibl ban sdang (kum u Jonah, Markos, Philipi, ne 1 Ioannis), bad suki suki phin shem ia ka lynti lyngba jong ka. Hangne ka don ka rukom kaba suk kaba phi lah ban pyndonkam ha kawei ka dkhot (ka rukom ban pule kawei dkhot- hadien kawei ka dkhot), kawei ka bynta, lane baroh kawei ka lynnong:

1. Duwai ba U Mynsiem Bakhuid Un ialam ia ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong phi bad un pynjem ia ka dohnud jong phi katba phi dang pule.
2. Jied ia kawei ka dkhot ne ka paragraph jong ka Baibl.
3. Thoh ia ka shi bynta ha ka kot lyngkdop ne ki bynta jong ka paragraph kiba sngewtynnad katba phi dang pule.
4. Da ka jingduwai pule biang ia ka bynta bad ruid laiñ ki jingmut ba kongsan.
5. Thoh ia kaei kaba phi la ruid laiñ bad ia ka jingmut ki pynsngew ia phi.
6. Duwai halor kine ki jingmut bad kumno ki ktah ia ka jingiadei jong phi bad U Blei.
7. Pyrkhat ba bad no ba phi lah ban iasam ia kane mynta ka sngi.

“Man la ka por ba ki briew U Blei ki nangroi ha ka jingaiei, kin nang ioh ia ka jingsngewthuh kaba kham shai shaphang ka ktien jong U. Kin sa iohi ia ka jingshai bad jingitynnad bathymmai ha ki jingshisha bakhuid jong ka. Kane ka la long kaba shisha ha ka histori jong ka balang ha ki arta baroh, bad kumta kan iai bteng haduh kaba kut.”— Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, pp. 38, 39.

`Ha kino ki rukom phi la shem ba ka khubor ha ka jingkynthoh haneng ka long kaba shisha na ka bynta jong phi? Peit ruh ia ka Salm 119:105.`
