---
title:  Ka Por
date:   26/04/2026
---

Hato phi la ju buh ia ka alarm jong phi kham kloi khyndiat ban ia kaba ju long khnang ban kyndit bad pule ia ka Baibl jong phi? Hato phi la ju ialeh tyngeh ban tan ia lade na jingthiah bad nangta phi iai peit ia ka baje bad phi pyrkhat, nga don 15 minit shuwa ba ngan trei ban sdang ia ka sngi. Nga kham bha ban kyrkieh! Hato phi la ju iaid lyngba ki jingpyrkhat ban don ia ka jingduwai kaba lyngkot ne ban peit kyrkieh ia kawei ka lynnong, tang ban shem ha ka jingsngew jong phi ba phi la pyndep hynrei ka dohnud jong phi kam sngewhun ia ka jingmih ha shuwa ba phin iaid ia ka sngi?

“Ka don tang khyndiat eh ka jingmyntoi kaba ioh na kaba pule kyrkieh ia ki Jingthoh Bakhuid Uwei u lah ban pule ia ka Baibl baroh kawei bad pynban um lah ban iohi ia ka jingitynnad jong ka ne ban sngewthuh ia ka jingmut kaba jylliew bad kaba rieh jong ka.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, p. 90.

Wat la ki don shisha ki jingkyrkhu kiba wan na ka jingpule ia ka Baibl — kum ba dih um na ka machine pynlipding kaba khlaiñ bor (ka ba bun bad ba stet) —ngi lah ban pule kyrkieh ia ka Baibl bad duh shibun kiei kiei. U Blei u la ai ha ngi ia ka Ktien ba la pyrsad mynsiem bad ka Ktien ba kordor jong U khnang ba ngin nang ban tip shuh shuh shaphang jong U (bad, ha ka kane ka rukom, ban tip shuh shuh shaphang ia lade ruh). Haba ngi pynlut por ban iohi ia ka bym lah ban batai, ia ka jinglong ba itynnad jong U Blei bad ki rukom ba U la pyniadei bad ka jinglong briew ha ka histori baroh kawei, ngi lah tang ban kham ieit shuh shuh ia U. Ka jingbuh jingkynmaw jong ki jingiadei jong U bad u bynriew ka don ha ki kti jong ngi — hynrei ngi dei ban don ia ka por, bad shim por, ban tip ia U Blei lyngba ka jingpule ia ka Ktien jong U (Ki Kam 17:11). Pyrkhat ia kine ki jingai jingmut harum:

Kyrpad ia U Blei ban buh ia ka jingthrang ha ka dohnud jong phi na ka bynta jong U. Kam kynti ia ki jingkular ha u Jeremaiah 29:13 bad Salm 37:4. Khot ia U ban pynkyndit bynriew ia phi kham kloi ban ia kaba ju long lane ban pyllait katto katne ka por ha la ka sngi jong phi ban pule ia ka ktien jong u.

Aiti ia ka por jong phi ha U Blei. Hooid, phi la bunkam, bad ki don shibun kiei kiei kiba kyrkieh ban leh. Hynrei ka por bad U Blei ka long kaba kordor. Leit sha ka jaka kaba kynjah marwei bad pule ia ka Salm 46:10. Pule ia ki kyntien ne rwai ia U Blei ia ka jingrwai “Nga Aiti lut.” Pyrkhat ia ki bynta jong ka jingim jong phi kiba phim shym la aiti bad ai ia ki ha U Blei.

Pynlut ia ka por bad U Blei wat haba phim sngew kwah. Da kaba donkam ia ka jingjied kaba sngewthuh bad ka jingthmu ban trei ban long kiba koit ba khiah (kilan met, bam bha), ka donkam ia ka jingjied kaba sngewthuh ban don ka jingiadei kaba jan bad U Blei. Kynmaw ba ki jingmlien kiba thymmai ki lah ban shim por ym duna ia ka 21 sngi ban pynlong, bad ngim lah ban jop khlem ka jingiarap jong U Mynsiem Bakhuid.

`Pule biang ia ka Ioannis 15:1-8. Kaei kaba U Jisu u iathuh ia ngi shaphang ka jingiai sah ha U, bad balei kane ka long kaba kongsan ha ka jingngeit jong ngi?`
