---
title:  Ka Jingtp Bad Ka Jingleh
date:   28/05/2026
---

Ha ka Jingiathuhkhana halor u Lum, U Jisu u kren shibun shaphang ki jingiadei — bad U bad iwei ia iwei pat. U ong ia kaei kaei kaba pynsngewsih shibun ha kaba kut jong ka khubor jong U:

“Ym baroh kiba ong ha Nga, “A Trai, A Trai,” ki ban rung sha ka hima ki bneng, hynrei uta uba leh ia ka mon U Kpa jong Nga uba ha bneng” (Math. 7:21).

U Jisu u batai ba don ki ban khot ia U bad kin tip shaphang jong U khlem da tip shisha ia U. Hooid, ka jingwad ia ka jingtip ka long kaba kongsan, bad ka Baibl ka iathuh ia ngi ba ki briew jong U Blei ki lah ban shah pynjot namar ka jingbym tip ia U Blei bad namar ba ki la kyntait ia ka jingtip jong ki ia U (Hos. 4:1, 6, 10). Ngim dei ban pynduna ia ka jingdonkam jong ka jingshisha jong ka Baibl kaba bym ju kylla. Hynrei lada kata ka jingtip kam pynkylla ia ngi bad kam pynjylliew ia ka jingaiti bad ka jingiaid jong ngi bad U Blei, kam myntoi ei ei.

“Bad Kane te ka long ka jingim bymjukut, ba kin ioh ithuh ia me U Blei uba shisha tang ma me hi, bad ia U Jisu Khrist, ia uba me la phah. (Ioannis 17:3)”. U Jisu u la pynpaw ba ka jingdonkam shwa ban rung sha bneng ka long ban leh ia ka mon U Blei bad kaba kut eh ban tip ia U Blei-- naba ngim lah ban leh ïa ka mon jong U lada ngim tip ïa U. Kane ka long ka bynta kaba kongsan tam bad ka jingïakyrmen. Kane ka long ka bynta kaba kongsan tam bad ka jingïakyrmen. Lada ki khun jong phi ki ong ba ki ieit ïa phi bad ki ju leh ïa kaba phi pan, ki kam jong ki, ki pynpaw ïa ka jingïar jong ka jingieit bad ka burom jong ki ïa phi. Ha kajuh ka rukom, haba ngi ieit ia U Blei, ngin kwah ban leh ia ka mon jong U, ngi tip ba ym don kaei kaei kaba bha ban leh ban palat ïa kata. Ka jingjubab ka jong ngi ïa U bad ka jingkohnguh jong ngi ïa U—ka dei ban mih na ka jingieit—kaba pynpaw ïa ka jinglong shisha jong ka jingïadei jong ngi bad U.

`U Jisu u la pynkut ïa ka jingiathuhkhana halor u Lum U Jisu u la ai ïa ka jingkhot kaba shai bad kaba kongsan eh sha ki nongshah sngap. Kaei kata ka jingkhot? Pule Mathaios 7:24–29`

Haba ngi iohsngew shisha ia ki khubor U Jisu, ngim lah ban lait na ka jingpynsngewthuh bad ka jingpynkylla ïa ngi. Hynrei, nyngkong eh, ki shkor jong ngi ki dei ban plie bad ki dohnud jong ngi ki dei ban pdiang sngewbha, khnang ba ka jingthmu ban im ha ka lieng kaba iadei kaba jan bad U Blei kan lah ban shah thoh ha ka jingim ka jong ngi kaba ha man la ka sngi, ia ka jingim jong ngi lah ban tei halor u Mawsiang bad ka jingthmu ba janai jong U Blei na ka bynta jong ngi.

Kane ka jingthmu jong ka jingiadei kaba jan kam dei kaba la buhrieh. Hynrei La pynpaw ia ka ha ki sla jong ka Ktien ba la pyrsad mynsiem da U Blei, bad U la ai ia ka ha uwei pa uwei u briew. Ka long ka jingjied shimet jong uwei pa uwei ban pdiang ia ka da ka jingngeit, ban pdiang ia ka hok ba janai jong U Khrist, bad nangta ban im katkum kata ka hok.
