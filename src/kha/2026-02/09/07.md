---
title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat shuh shuh
date:   29/05/2026
---

Kam dei ka jingphylla ïa ngi ba ka jingïakren shaphang ka aiñ ka lah ban jingïasngewthuh bakla bad jingïapynthohbria, namar ba ka jingïaleh kaba khatduh jong u Soitan pyrshah ïa U Blei ka la long halor ka aiñ jong U.

Ha ki sngi jong U Jisu, don kiba pyrkhat ba U la wan ban pynduh noh ïa ka aiñ, hynrei kane kam long satia ka jingshisha. U Jisu u la pynshai paidbah ïa ka aiñ bad ïa ka jinglong ba itynnad jong U Blei, bad U la wan ban pyndep ïa ka aiñ (Mathaios 5:17, 18) ban pyni ha ngi ïa ka jinglong jong U Blei.

“Tang lada ka jingburom ïa Ka Ktien Bakhuid jong U Blei ka la long kaba la sumar bad ka la neh ha ki dohnud jong ki briew, kin lah ban kyrmen ban pyndep ïa ka jingthmu ba kynja-blei. Ka jingñiewburom ïa ka aiñ jong U Blei ka la ai bor ïa ki Israel ha ka por synshar u Dabid bad ha ki snem ba sdang jong ka jingsynshar u Solomon; lyngba ka jingngeit ha ka Ktien kaba im la pynlong ïa ka jingpynthymmai ha ki sngi jong u Elija bad u Josia. Bad dei ha kine kijuh ki Jingthoh Bakhuid jong ka jingshisha, ka pateng jong ki Israel, ba u Jeremaiah u la kyrpad ha ki jingtrei jong u ban wanrah ka jingpynthymmai.”—Ellen G. White, Prophets and Kings, sla. 465, 466.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Kumno ka kolshor paidbah (popular culture) ka peit ïa ka pop? Kumno ka balang jong ngi ka dei ban jubab?`

`Mynno phi la ïohi da lade kumno ka pop ka pynjot ïa ka jingïadei bad U Blei bad ruh bad kiwei pat ki briew?`

`Ha ka jingim jong phi, ka jingkohnguh ïa ka aiñ jong U Blei ka long kaba suk ne kaba eh? Kiei ki daw kiba la pynlong ïa kata?`

`Kumno ma ngi ki Seventh-day Adventists, kiba ka kyrteng hi ka pyni ba ngi shim khia bha ïa ka aiñ, ngi lah ban kiar na ka jingma jong ka legalism—kata ka jingïaroh ïa ka jingkohnguh aiñ jong lade kum ka lad jingïapynim? (Pyrkhat shisien: Ha ka sngi ka jingbishar, hano phin shaniah haba baroh ki pop jong phi kin wan shakhmat U Blei uba khuid bad uba janai eh? Ha ka jingkohnguh aiñ jong phi? Lane ha ka jinghok ba janai jong U Jisu kaba U la ai ha ka jaka jong phi?)`

`Kumno ka jingtip (lane ka jingbym don jingtip) ka lah ban ktah ïa ka jingïadei jong u briew bad U Blei? (Pule Proberbs 24:3, 13, 14.)`
