---
title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat shuh shuh
date:   03/04/2026
---

Ha shuwa ban kha ïa ngi, U Blei U la ieit ïa ngi; U la don ka jingthmu ba ngin ithuh ïa ngi bad ban tip ïa U. U wad ïa ngi, kum u nongaplangbrot uba bha, bad u khot sngewbha ïa ngi ban ïaineh ha U man ka sngi. Ngi shu donkam tang ban shu jied ban jubab ïa ka jingkhot jong ngi, bad ban kylliang ïa ka jinglong jot jong ngi bad ka jinglong Lodikaia na ka bynta ka jingai sngewbha kiba bha jong ngi.(peit-Jingpynpaw. 3:18, 19).

Kum ka jingsan ba suki jong ki tnat u dieng waiñ, ka jingïadei jong ngi bad U Blei ka lah ban long kaba suki, ne ka lah ban long kaba stet bad kaba khlaiñ bha kum ha ka por ba ïoh u slap shibun.Kamp her katno kloi ne suki kata ka jingsan jong ngi bad ka jingpynmih soh jong ngi ha ka jingim, hynrei kaei kaba donkam eh ka long kata ka jingdonkam ba man la ka sngi jong ngi ïa ki “Jor tynrai” jong U Mynsiem Bakhuid, kaba pynthikna ba ngi don ka jingïadei kaba jan bad U Jisu.

“Ka jingïaineh ha U Khrist ka mut ka jingïoh na U Mynsiem jong U, ka jingim kabym tyngkai kaba la aiti lut ha ka jingshakri ka ka kam jong U.Ka lad jong ka jingïaphylliew jingmut ka dei ban ïaidon hapdeng u briew bad U Blei jong u. kumba ka tnat ka kjit barobor ïa ki jor tynrai na u dieng waiñ uba im, kumta ngi ruh ngi dei ban snoh ha ka jingïadei bad U Jisu, ban pdiang na U da ka jingngeit bad ka bor ka jinglong ba janai ka jong U hi.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 676.

“Kumno ba u tnat uba tyrkhong u lah ban long shitylli bad u dieng waiñ? Kumno u lah ban long u nongïashim bynta lang na ka jingim jong u dieng waiñ uba im?Tang da ka jingpynïasnoh ha u dieng waiñ, da ka jingwanrah ka jingïadei kaba bha eh kaba lah ban pynlong kaba shisha.U ksai bad u ksai u thied bad u thied, u tnat u snoh skhem ha u dieng waiñ uba ai jingim ba u tnat u lah ban long shitylli bad u dieng waiñ bad ba u lah ban sei soh kiba syrïiem bad ki sohwaiñ ba shisha.”—Ellen G. White, Manuscript 67, 1897

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Puson ïa la ka jingim jong phi.Hato phi lah ban kynmaw, kaei kaba la khring ïaphi ban don ka jingim kum ka lodikaia ha ka jingim bakynja-mynsiem jong phi? Bad ka jingjia aïu pat kaba khring biang ïa phi ban jan sha U Blei?`

`Ka Ellen G. White ka kren shaphang “Ka jingïaipdiang ïa U Mynsiem Bakhuid.” Katno sien phi ïai-duwai na ka bynta U Mynsiem Bakhuid? Ka jingkylla aïu kaba lah ban jia lada phi ïoh diang ïa U Mynsiem Bakhuid man la ka sngi?`

`Ka jingkylla aïu ka ba lah ban jia ha ka balang lada ngi duwai shirhem barabor na ka bynta U Mynsiem Bakhuid?`

`Kren da kaba shai watla ka pang mynsiem ruh ruh, kumno ka jingïadei jong phi bad U Blei.Ka rai aïu kaba phi lah ban pynlong khnang ba phi lah ban don ka jingïadei kaba jan bad U kaba U kwah hynrei kaba phi la khang lad?`
