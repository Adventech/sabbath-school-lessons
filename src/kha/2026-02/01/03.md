---
title:  Ka Jingsneng, Kylla Kaba-Mut, Ka Bainong
date:   30/03/2026
---

“Kat ïa kiba Nga ieit, nga sneng bad Nga sympat; to sngewshitrhem namar kata, bad to kylla kaba mut.”Jingpynpaw 3:19. “ ‘ Namar kata, bad to kylla kaba mut.”. Ym don uwei ruh na ngi uba kren hok uba lah ban ong ba U Jisu Um pyrkhat shaphang jong ngi wat tang shi khyllipmat ruh na ka bynta ka lawei jong ngi. Kan jyn-da-la long kaba suk ïa U Jisu ban kyntait ïa u khun bynriew ban ïa kaba U la jied ban ïaid ha ka lynti kaba jynjar hangne ha pyrthei.Ka dei tang na kawei ka daw kata ka long ba U ieit eh ïa ngi bad u sneng ïa ngi ha kane ka jinglong kaba kum kane. U kwah ïa ka jingïadei kaba kham khlaiñ bad kaba kham jylliew bad ngi.Um sngew tynnad bad kane ka jingïadei kaba hateng hateng kaba ngi don mynta, ban ong ‘Ngan leit sha U lada nga sngew don kam”.

U Jisu U sneng ïa ngi na ka bynt ka jingbha jong ngi hi. U ong ïa ngi ban kylla kaba mut. Hynrei ngin ym lah ban kylla kaba mut lymda ngi sngewthuh ba ka don kaei kaei kabym beit. Pynban, U la ïathuh ha ngi kaei ka jingeh ba ngi don: ngi pyrkhat ba ngi long kiba riewspah, hynrei ha ka jingshisha ngi long kiba jynjar sat, kiba duk, kiba matlah, bad kiba lyngkhuid” (Jingpynpaw. 3:17).

`Pule Jingpynpaw 3:20. Ïa ngi la kular aïu hangne?Hynrei ngin leh kumno ban ïoh ïa kata ka jingkular?`

Kane ka dei ka dur kaba phylla haki kyntien. U Syiem jong ka bneng U kwah ban shong bam lang bad phi, bad nga. U thrang ïa ka jingïadei kaba jan ban ïakren ïakhana shaphang ka jingbam ba bang. U kwah ïa ka jingïadei kaba jan, kaba neh, bad U Jisu U khot sngewbha ïa ngi ban don bad U.

U Jisu U ïeng da kaba ïaishah bad ap bad U tied mian mian ha ka jingkhang jong ka dohnud jong ngi. Lehse phi la ïohi ïa ka jingdro dur ïa kane ha ki kot khynnah—U Nongpynim Uba jrong, Uba i jemnud bad U tied jingkhang. Um tied eh ban rung jubor shapoh ban kren ïa U. Um knieh ïa ka por bad pynwit ha ka jingbun-kam jong phi.Ka por ka long kaba lyngkot; kumta, lada phi ïohsngew ïa U, plié ka jingkhang.Ba U lah ban rung ha ka jingim jong phi.

Kane ka pharshi ka pyni kaba kumno ka jingïadei kaba U Blei U kwah ban don bad ngi iwei pa aiwei. Hynrei ha kawei ka sngi ynda phi la kynduh ïa U Jisu markhmat, haba phin sab ret ïa ka pansngiat ha ki kjat jong U ha ka pyndonburom ryngkat bad ki millian kiwei pat ban mane ïa U Nongthaw(Jingpynpaw. 4:9–11, Jingpynpaw. 5:11–14), haba phin pyrkhat pat ïa ki por kiba phi dang don ha kane ka pyrthei baroh ki jingrh kiba phi la ïashem kin paw kum kibym don ei-ei ruh em—hato phin pyrkhat ba phi la babe ïa ka por kaba phi la pynlut hangne ha pyrthei bad U Jisu?

`Mynta hi U Jisu U dang tied ha ka jingkhang ka dohnud jong phi, U dang khot ïa phi, watla phim pat lah ban pynlong ka jingrai ban plié ïa ka dohnud jong phi na ka bynta jong U. Kumno ha kaba peit sha ka diengphna shaphang ka mut aïu ba phin pynlong kata ka jingjied?`
