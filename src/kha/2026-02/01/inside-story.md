### Ka Kam business Ka Ba Long Ka Jingkyrkhu

U Kavono Kivatsi Samwele u ju sngewtynnad ban tem drums (ksing) ha shnong jong u ha Democratic Republic jong ka Congo. Ka la long ka jingim kaba bha, ban tem drums katba ka lok jong u ka rwai, katba u ap ban long u rangbah shnong.

Hynrei katba u Kavono, uba dang ha ki pdeng 20 snem, u la ïoh ka buit.U la pyrkhat ba kan bha ba uhi un thoh ki jingrwai ba ka long ka lah ban rwai katba ma u pat un tem da ki ksing. Ban leh ïa kata, u donkam ban nangthoh bad pule. U la ïohi ïa ka skul seventh-day Adventist kaba don ha jan ka shnong jong u, kumta u la ïohrung ha ka klass one.

Ha skul u la nag ban pule bad ban thoh, bad u la tip ruh shaphang U Blei Uba ha bneng. Katba u dang pule hangta u la sngew ïa ka jingkhot U Blei ban long u pastor. Kumta u la ieh noh kata ka jingpyrkhat ban long rangbah shnong bad u la jied noh ban long u pastor seventh-day Adventist. U la don ruh u khun uba kyrteng u Kasereka Maghulu Kavatsi.

Kum u khynnah, u Kasereka u ju bud ïa u kpa ha ki jingleit trei jong u kum u pastor, u ruh u sngew ïa ka jingkhot ba u dei ban shim bynta ha ka kam mission. Hynrei ka talent jong u pat ka dei ha ka kam bisnes, te kumta u la sdang ban trei bisnes, bad u la seng kawei ka farm, u la pynïar ïa ka bad ka jingtong dohkha, u la plié ruh ka dukan die jaiñ. Kata ka kam jong u ka laroi stet eh bad ka la heh bha.

U Kasereka um ju klet ïa ki jingshisha jong ka Baibl kaba u la nang na u kpa jong u, bad u la pynphai kaba shiphew. Hadien ba u la shongkurim, kaba shiphew kaba nyngkong kum ki shilok ka la poi haduh US$10,000 (kumba 8,50,000 tyngka). Ka lok jong u Kasereka, ka khun jong uwei u evangelist, ka la ong ha u ba ki dei ruh ban ai ka jingainguh kaba shiphewna ka shispah ba ki ïoh. U Kasereka u la ju pyrkhat kumno un ai ka jingainguh kaba biang, ka lok jong u ka la pynngeit ïa u kumta. Kine arngut ki la ai kum kaba shiphew hi ha ka jingainguh.Hadien kata, ki la ïaileh kumta kaba shiphew bad ka jingainguh ka long katjuh, bad kim ju duna ei ei ruh. “Haba phi kyrshan ïa ka balang, phi ïoh biang ki jingkyrkyrkhu,” la ong u Kasereka.

Da ka jingwad lad ban leh la ka bynta na ka bynta ban pynphriang ïa ka khubor U Jisu bad ka jingwan noh shen jong U u la ïasam ka kotThe Great Controversy Ka Jingïaleh Bakhraw sha kiba trei bisnes lang bad u bad sha ki nongtrei sorkar ruh. U don ruh ka jaka ri khunswet. U kyrshan ïa ka jingpule jong ki ruh, haduh ba kin da dep master degrees bad doctorates.

Katba u wad lad ban ïarap ïa kiwei, ki jingkyrkhu ki wan phai sha u hi. U la ïarap palat 100 ngut ki khynnah ban dep pule haduh ban da graduate, hynrei kim ïoh kam namar ha ka ri jong ki duna eh ki kam ki jam ïa kiba la dep pule. Kumta, u Kasereka u la khot ïa ki ban trei bad u hi, bad ka jingtbit jong ki ka la pynïar shu shuh bad ka la long ka jingkyrkhu ha ka kam binesse jong u.

“Phi lah ban ai tam sha U Blei ban ïa kaba U ai sha phi,” u la ong.

Shibynta na ka jingkynshew ba ngin lum ha ka khatlai sabbaton jong kane ka quarter, kaba ngi tip kum ka Quarterly Mission Project Offering,kan leit ban kyrshan ïa ka kam ha Democratic Republic of Congo bad sha kiwei de ki jaka ha East-Central Africa Division. Khublei shibun ïa ka jingai bakhraw bad ba bun jong phi ban ïarap ha kaba pynbna ïa ka jingwan shen U Jisu.
