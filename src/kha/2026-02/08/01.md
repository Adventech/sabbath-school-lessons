### Ki Jingpule Na Ka Bynta Kane Ka Taïew
Markos 8:11,12; Matt.15:21-28; Loukas 7:1-10; Ephasos 2:8; Hebru 11; Jingpynpaw 14:12

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Te ka jingngeit ka long kata ka jingsngew skhem ia kita kiei kiei ia kiba ngi kyrmen, kata ka jingtynjuh ia kita kiei kiei kibym iohi.” Hebru 11:1.

Shisien don kiba ong, “Ka jingngeit ka long kum ka Wifi. Ka long kaba ym lah ban iohi, hynrei ka don ka bor ban pyniasoh ïa phi bad kaei kaba phi donkam.” Khlem artatien: khlem ka jingngeit, kan ym don ka jingiadei bad U Blei.

Kumno ka jingngeit jong phi mynta? Hato ka jingngeit jong phi ha U Blei ka la ju khih? Lehse phi la mad ïa kaei kaei kaba la pynjynjar ïa phi haduh ba phim nang shuh kumno ban iaid shakhmat bad ka jingiadei jong phi bad U Blei. Lane hato ka jingngeit jong phi ka long kum ka jingkiw kaba san na u tnat uba jyrngam sha u soh uba rit uba khatduh khatwai u plie sha u syntiew uba shlur bad uba itynnad bad uba pyndap ïa ka kamra da ka jingiwbih kaba ym lah ban klet? Namar shisha, “ka jingngeit ka pyni ïa ka jingshisha jong kaei kaba ngi kyrmen; ka long ka jingpynshisha ïa kiei kiei kiba ngim lah ban iohi” (Hebru 11:1). Kam dei kaei kaei kaba ngi lah ban pynmih hi, namar “U Blei u la ai ha uwei pa uwei ïa ka jingthew jong ka jingngeit” (Rom 12:3). Ka jingngeit ka dei ka jingai jong U Blei (Eph.2:8,9), bad wat ha kata ka por ruh, ka jingngeit jong ngi ha U Blei ka long kaba lah tang namar kaei kaba U Blei u la dep ban leh ha ngi bad na ka bynta jong ngi.

Ha kane ka taiew to ngin wad bniah ïa ka phang jong ka Jingngeit: kaei ban leh bad ka Jingartatien bad Ka Jingbymngeit; kumno, kat kum ka jingong U Jisu, ka jingngeit kaba khlain ka long; bad kaei ka jingmut ban don “Ka Jingngeit U Jisu,”

_Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, May 23_
