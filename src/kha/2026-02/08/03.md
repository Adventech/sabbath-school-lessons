---
title:  U Jisu U Ïohi Ïa Ka Jingngeit Jong Ngi
date:   18/05/2026
---

`Ïanujor kumno U Jisu U batai ïa ka jingngeit jong ki synran jong U ha u Markos 4:40 bad ka jingngeit jong ka kynthei ha u Mathaios 15:21-28.`

Tang namar ba ngi khot ïa lade ki nongbud U Jisu kam mut ba ka jingngeit jong ngi ka long kaba khlain. Ka ka jingshisha, don ki briew kiba kam ba ki ngeit, hynrei U Jisu u la sngewthuh ïa kaei kaba don shisha ha ki dohnud jong ki (Ioannis 2:23-25)

`Pule Loakas 7:1-10. Kaei kaba phi nang shaphang ka jingngeit na kane ka jingiathuhkhana?`

Ha u Markos 9, ngi pule shaphang uwei u briew uba la wan sha U Jisu khnang ba U Jisu un beh noh ïa u ksuid na u khun jong u, hynrei uba lah tang ban lum ïa ka jingngeit kaba biang ban ong, “Ko Trai, nga ngeit; iarap ïa ka jingbymngeit jong nga!” (Markos 9:24)

Ha kawei pa kawei na kine ki jingiakynduh, U Jisu u iohi ïa ka jingngeit jong ki briew, ne ka jingduna jong ka, bad u leh ïa ki kambah kum ka jingmih na kata ka jingngeit ne khnang ban pynskhem ïa ka.

Wat la U Mynsiem Bakhuid U pynlong ïa ngi ban ngeit, hynrei u nongshun jong ki mynsiem u kwah ban gin artatien ne kyntait ïa ka jingdonkti U Blei ha ka jingim jong ngi, “Ka jingbymngeit kaba la pynsah ha ka mynsiem ka don ka bor kaba pynkhih. Ki symbai jong ka jinfartatien kiba ki la bet kin pynmih ïa ka jingot jong ki hynrei ki dei ban iai bteng ban tih ïa kawei pa kawei ka tynrai jong ka jingbymngeit. Haba la tan noh ïa kine ki jingthung ba don bih, ki sangeh ban san namar ka jingduna jingbam ha ka ktien bad ki kam. Ka dohnud ka dei bandon ia u jingthung jong ka jingngeit.bad ka jingieit ha ka khyndew jong ka dohnud kaba la shong khet hangta.” – Ellen G. White, Ka Jingngeit bad ki Kam, sla. 17.

Haba ngi don ka jingartatien shaphang U Blei, ka jinglong jong U, ne ka ktien jong U, ngi dei ban leh aiu ïa ki? U Blei um iehnoh ne iaid lait ïa ka jingmut briew namar U lathaw ïa ngi ha ka dur jong U bad U khot ïa ngi ban iakren bad U, kumba U leh bad u Abraham, u Moses bad u Job. U Blei u khot ïa ngi ban nang ban trei hapoh ki rukom pyrkhat kiba heh bad ki bym kut jong U, wat la ha kawei ka por ngi dei ban aiti noh ïa kaei kaba ngim sngewthuh pura.

`Pyrkhat ïa ki daw baroh kiba thikna kiba phi don na ka bynta ka jingngeit. Ha kajuh ka por, ha kano ka por ba ka jingmutdur ka sangeh bad ka jingngeit, ka jingngeit kaba skhem bad kaba don jingmut, ka donkam ban pyndonkam?`
