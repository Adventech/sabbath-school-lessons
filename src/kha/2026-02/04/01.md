### Ki Jingpule Na Ka Bynta Kane Ka Taïew
Jingrwai Sngewsynei. 3:22, 23; 2 Timothi. 3:15–17; Ioanis 17:17; Ephesos. 1:13; Slam. 119:11; 1 Kotinth. 2:14.

> <p>Dkhot Kynmaw</p>
> “Namar ka ktien U Blei ka long kaba im, bad kaba don bor, bad kaba kham nep ban ia kano kano ka waitlam arliang syrti, bad kaba pei haduh kaba pynkhlad ia ka jingim bad ia ka mynsiem, ia ki mat bad ia ka khlein shyieng ruh, bad kaba bishar ia ki jingmut bad ia ki jingthmu ka dohnud.” (Hebru 4:12,).

Ka Baibl. Khlem artatien phi don kawei ka kopi ne lehse bun tylli. Lyngba ka histori baroh kawei, ia kane ka kitab ba kordor la ju kopi ha kaba rieh, la pynrung tuh, bad la khang pyrshah. Ka dei ka kot kaba la pynmih bun tam ha ka pyrthei, ha kano kano ka ktien, bad ruh kawei na ki kot kiba rim tam. Don kiba la iap khnang ba yn lah ban pynneh pynsah ia ka Baibl.

Hangno ka Baibl ka don ha ka jingim jong phi? Hato phi pule ia ka ne ka don hajan ka jingthiah jong phi ne ha ka jingbuh kot kaba lang ki pum pum? Hato ka jingim ka long kaba bunkam palat ia phi ban pyllait por ban pule shisha ia ka Ktien U Blei ne phi sngew ba phi la thait palat ban plie ia ki sla jong ka?

Ka Ktien U Blei ka long kaba im bad kaba donbor, bad U Blei U khot ia phi ban shah ia ka ban kren ha ka dohnud jong phi, ban pynshlur ia phi, ban khot bad pynkylla ia phi, bad ban ai ia phi ia ka jingpyniaid bad jingkyrmen.

Ka Baibl kam dei tang ka kot sha phang ka jingstad lane ka jinglum lang ia ki jingiathuhkhana barim. Ha ka jaka kata, ka long ka jingiathuhkhana kaba itynnad bad kaba jylliew shaphang kumno U Nongthaw jong ka pyrthei u wad ban pynjan ia ngi sha U. Lada phi don ka jingthrang ban nangsan ha ka jingiadei jong phi bad U Blei, kaba bha tam kaba phi lah ban leh ka long ban aiti ban pynlut ia ka por kaba bha bad U man ka sngi, ban duwai, ban pule ia ka Ktien ba la pyrsad mynsiem jong U, bad ban aiti ia ka mon jong phi ha kaei kaba ka hikai.

_Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, April 25._
