---
title:  Ka Jingthoh Bakhuid, Ka Bor
date:   20/04/2026
---

`Ia ka bor bad ka jingtrei jong ka Baibl la pynbna shai hapoh ki sla jong ka. Pule na 2 Timothi 3:15-17. Pynleit jingmut ia kaei kaba kine ki dkhot ki iathuh ia phi shaphang ka jingtrei jong ka Baibl.`

Haba iadei bad ka jingpule Baibl shimet, ngi dei ban husiar ban ym khmih lynti ba ka Baibl kan shakri ia ki jingthmu ne ki jingpyrkhat jong ngi, ki bym ju long kumjuh bad ka jong U Blei. Kum ka nuksa, ngim dei ban pyndonkam ia ka rukom “khapbrip ia ki khmat bad kdew sha ka dkhot”, namar kane kam dei ka rukom ba U Blei U kwah ban iakren bad ngi lyngba ka Ktien jong U. U Blei um dei u bula ba la shah teh, uba ap ban shakri ia ki jingdonkam bad ka mon jong ngi. Ki lynti bad ki jingpyrkhat jong U ki long kiba kham halor shibun ban ia ki jong ngi (Isaiah. 55:9), bad kumta ngim dei ban pyrshang ban teh lakam ia ki ktien jong U ha ngi. Bad ngim dei ruh ban jied tang ia ki bynta jong ka Baibl kiba ngi sngewtynnad. Ha ka jaka kata, ngi dei ban iohi ia ka Baibl kum ka kynhun baroh kawei ban ia kaba pule ia ki bynta kiba ngi sngew tynnad, kiba la tip bha bad iehnoh ia kiba ialeh pyrshah ne kiba tynjuh. Lada ngi kwah shisha ba U Blei Un kren ha ka jingim jong ngi, ngi dei ban shim ia ka Baibl baroh kawei bad pyndonkam ia ki rukom kiba biang haba ngi iashim bynta ha ka jingpule bniah ia ka Baibl, da kaba shaniah ba U Blei Un pynpaw ia kaei kaba ngi donkam ban iohsngew ha ka por ba ngi donkam ban iohsngew ia ka.

Kumjuh ruh, U Jisu hi U ong ia ngi: “Men ieit ia U Trai U Blei jong me da ka dohnud jong me baroh, da ka mynsiem jong me baroh, bad da ka jingmut jong me baroh’ ” (Mathaios. 22:37,). Kata ka long, ba U Blei um kwah ba ngin iehnoh ia la ka jingmut jingpyrkhat; hynrei, U kwah ban pyntip ia ki jingmut jingpyrkhat jong ngi da ki jingtip bad jingsngewthuh kiba jylliew jong U, kiba la pynpaw, ha kawei ka bynta, lyngba ka Ktien jong U. Ngi lah ban pule bun ki jingiathuhkhana jong ka Baibl ha kaba U Blei u la don ki jingiakren bad ki briew kum u Enokh, u Abraham, u Moses bad u Job, nalor bun ki jingiakren kiba U Jisu u la don bad ki briew. U Blei um iaid lait ia ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong u briew hynrei u khot ia ngi ban pyndem ia ka ha ka Ktien bad jingstad jong U haba ngi “dang trei” ia ka jingpynim jong ngi.

Hynrei ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong u briew ka dang long briew — kaba lah ban bakla bad ban shah shukor. Kam ju long kaba khlem jingbakla. Ka lah ban long ba ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong u briew ban buh noh ia U Blei sha rud ban pyrshang ban pynbeit ia kiei kiei da lade, kaba buh ia lade kum uba iaryngkat bad, ne uba halor, U Blei haba iadei bad ka jingpyrkhat. Ki briew ki lah ban leit sha ka Jingthoh Bakhuid da ka mynsiem kaba sngew sarong bad da ka mynsiem ba wohnia, da kaba pyrkhat ba ki la ju iohsngew ia ka baroh mynshuwa bad ba ym don ei ei kaba thymmai. Dei haba ngi sngew ba ngi long kiba kongsan, kiba sngewskhem, kiba lah sngew biang lade, bad kiba ngim donkam ei ei ba ngi iehnoh ia ka jingiadei jong ngi bad U Blei bad ngi shaniah ha la ka jong ka jingtip kaba duna bad ka jingpyrkhat kaba bakla.
