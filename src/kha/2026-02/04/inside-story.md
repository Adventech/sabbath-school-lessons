### Ka Por Ha Phatok

Da: Ka Carol de Oliveira

Hato phi lah ban mutdur ba la set ia phi ha ka kamra phatok namar ba phi la iasam ia U Jisu? Kane ka long ka jingjia kaba ju jia man ka por na ka bynta ki nongsdang jong ka Global Mishon kiba shakri ha ka ri ba la tap kaba ha ka Southern Asia-Pacific Division. Ia ka ri la khot “ba la tap” ban iada ia ka jingim bad ka jingshakri jong ki nongtrei ba hakhmat eh. Ki nongsdang jong ka Global Mission ki dei ki riewngeit kiba la phah ban sdang ia ki kynhun thymmai jong ki bangeit hapdeng ki kynhun briew ki bym pat poi ka khubor sha ki.

Dang shen, u Chong,* u nongsdang, bad u Peter, u nongpyniaid jong ka ophis jong ka conference, ki la leit sha ka bynta ba shathie jong kane ka ri ban iakynduh bad ki dkhot jong kawei ka balang ba la seng.

Katba u Chong bad u Peter ki dang iabam ja miet bad ki dkhot, ki la iohsngew shaphang lai tylli ki longiing kiba kwah ban aiti ia la ka jingim ha U Jisu. Ynda u Chong u la tip ba ki la pynkhreh ban thang ia ki tiar mane ksuid jong ki, u la rai ban leit jngoh ia ki ha kata ka miet.

Katba u Chong, u Peter bad katto katne ki dkhot balang ki dang leit sha ki ïing jong ki lai tylli ki longïing, uwei u dkhot balang u la ong ba u kwah ba ka longïing jong u kan ïohsngew shaphang U Jisu. Kumta, shwa ba ka kynhun kan poi sha ki longïing, ki la leit sha ïing jong u dkhot ban ïasam ïa ka khubor babha bad kiba haïing hasem jong u.

Khatduh eh, katei ka kynhun ka la poi ha ki iing jong ki lai tylli ki longiing. Ka la janmiet, hynrei ki khmat ba kmen jong ki briew ki la pynkmen ia ka dohnud jong u Chong, bad ka jingkmen jong ki ka la pynkhlaiñ ia ka mynsiem jong u. Katba ki dang ia lum lang sawdong ka ding, u Peter u la pynithuh ia u Chong bad u la iathuh ha ki briew ba u don hangta ban iarap ia ki ban aiti lut ha U Jisu.

Hynrei ki longiing kim shym la donkam ia ka jingiarap jong u Chong. “Ngi long kiba kloi ban aiti ia la ka dohnud ha U Jisu”, la ong u rangbah jong ka iing. “Ngin iaid ha ka jingshai ryngkat bad U Jisu uba ialam, ym ki mynsiem ba kynja pyrthei.”

Katba u Chong u dang ïakren bad ki longïing, uwei u briew u la rung shapoh kamra, da kaba dawa ba u Chong un leit ryngkat bad u sha ïing jong u nongïalam jong ka ta ka jait.

Hadien kata ka janmiet, u Peter bad ki dkhot balang ki la leit phai biang sha ka jaka mane jong ki ban ap khubor shaphang u Chong. Katba nang iaid ki kynta, ki la nang sngewkhia haduh katno ka jingiakynduh bad u nongialam jong kane ka kynhun ka la shim por. Khatduh ki la tip ba u Chong u la shah set ha iing jong u nongialam.

Ha ka por miet, ki dkhot ki la duwai na ka bynta u Chong katba ki nongïalam ki dang pyrshang ban ïakren ban pyllait noh ïa u. Hynrei ym don kaei kaei kaba ki la pyrshang kaba la jop. La rah ia u sha ka jaka set briew ha kaba la set ia u ha ka por ba dang tohkit nyngkong ia katei ka kam.

Da ka jingpynbeit, la pyllait noh ia u Chong hadien saw sngi. Mynta, ka shnong ha kaba u la pynlut por ha phatok ka don palat 100 ngut ki briew kiba la kylla long ki Khristan Seventh-day Adventist! Ha ka jaka ban pynsangeh ia u Chong ban iasam ia U Jisu, ka por ha phatok ka la pynskhem ia ka jingkut jingmut jong u.
