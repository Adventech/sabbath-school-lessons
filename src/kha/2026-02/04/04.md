---
title:  Ka Jingshisha Jong Ka Baibl
date:   21/04/2026
---

Ka jinglong hapdeng ki katto katne ki riewstad Baibl ki bym pyrkhing ha ki snem 1960 ka long ban pynduhnoh ia U Blei na ka phang jong ka theology. Ha u snem 2017, kawei ka jingthoh ha ka sla jong ka kot khubor Time Magazine ka la thoh ïa ka phang “Hato ka Jingshisha ka la ïap?” Ka long kaba sngewtynnad namar kane ka jinglong ka pyni ia ka kyrdan jong ka imlang sahlang jong ngi mynta. Ka jingmut hi jong ka “jingshisha” hi ka la nang sniew haduh katta katta ba ym don ba tip shuh kaei ka jingshisha. Kat kum ka kolshor ba pawnam, ym don u dieng thew, ym don ka nongrim kaba neh beit kaba lah ban shaniah ban shah ia ka jingtynjuh jong ka por. Pynban ia kane, U Jisu u la ong, “ ‘Nga long ka ... jingshisha’ ” (Ioannis 14:6). Ka Ktien jong U ki phla shaphang jong U kum ka jingshisha ha ka jingkhuid kaba pura.

**Pule suki suki ia kine ki dkhot harum, bad nangta sa pule ia ki sa shisien. Kaei kaba phi iohi sha phang kine ki khubor?**

`Ioanis 17:17`

`Proverb 30: 5,6`

`Salm 12:6`

Ka Baibl ka pynbna ba ka jingshisha ba kongsan, U Jisu hi, Um ju kylla (Hebru 13:8). Ha kajuh ka por, katba ngi pule ia ka Ktien U Blei, ka jingsngewthuh jong ngi shaphang U Blei bad ia ka jingshisha jong U ka lah ban nangsan. “Ki don ki par jong ka jingshisha kiba dang dei ban shem da u nongwad ba shitrhem.”—Ka Ellen G. White, Ki Jingphla na ka bynta ka Balang, vol. 5, ka p. 704. Haba kren shaphang ka “jingshisha,” ka Ellen G. White ka ju kdew sha ka jingshisha kumba la ai da U Blei lyngba ka Ktien jong U. Ngi lah ban wad shuh shuh ia ka jingshai ba kham bun ha ka Baibl namar ka Ktien U Blei kam ju pyrshah ia ki jingshisha kiba la leit noh hynrei ha ka jaka kata ka tei halor jong ki.

`Pule 1 Thessaloni 2:13; Salm 33:4, 5; bad Ephesos 1:13. Kaei ka khubor kaba la shem ha kine ki dkhot?`

Ha kaba kut, ka Baibl, bad tang ka Baibl hi, ka dei ban long ka tynrai ba kongsan jong kaei kaba ngi sngewthuh kum ka jingshisha. Kiwei pat ki tyllong baroh dei ban pyrshang bad tynjuh da ka Ktien U Blei. Wat ia kaei kaba ngi pyrkhat ia “ka nongrim” ruh ka donkam ban shah tynjuh da ka Ktien U Blei!

`Don ki briew kiba kwah ban kynthoh ba ym don jingshisha. Balei kata ka jingong ka long kaba pyrshah ia lade? Kata ka long, balei ba ka jingpynlong ia ka jingong ba ym don jingshisha bad ka jingpyrshang ban pynbna ia ka jingshisha, bad kumta ka pynrem ialade?`
