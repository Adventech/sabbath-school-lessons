title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat shuh shuh
date:   24/04/2026
---

“Lada phi pyrkhat ia ki kyntien kiba phi la kren ha kine ki 24 kynta ba la leit noh, kumno phin bishar ia ki? Hato ki long kiba ieit, kiba sbun, kiba kmen, kiba pynkyntiew, kiba sngewsih, kiba thait, kiba khuslai, kiba bitar, kiba kren bein, ne kiba don jingmut sniew? Ka Baibl ka ong, “ ‘Na ka jingdap ka dohnud ka shyntur ka kren’ ” (Mathaios. 12:34,). Haba ngi don ka ba sniew ha ka dohnud jong ngi, ka mih ha ki ktien jong ngi.

Baroh ngi la ju mad ia ka jingsngew bym lar kam, jingsngew thait, ne jingsngew jynjar, bad kata ka jinglong jingman ka pynkylla ia kaei kaba mih na ka shyntur jong ngi (bunsien ki kyntien kiba ngi sngewsih hadien). Kiba iapher, haba ka dohnud jong ngi ka pynmih da ka jingieit ia iano re iano, ka tuid lyngba ki ktien jong ngi.

Ha kajuh ka rukom, ka Ktien U Blei ka kren shaphang ka dohnud bad ki jingthmu jong U ia ngi. Ka long kaba phylla ban pyrkhat ba kine ki kyntien hi, kiba beit na ka dohnud U Blei, ki don ha ka jingpynleit jingmut jong ngi ha ka Baibl. Ka long shisha kaba phylla ban iohi ia ka bor kaba ki ktien U Blei ki la bat lyngba ha ka histori baroh kawei.

“Ka long kawei kaba ngi dei ban niew ia ka Baibl kum ka kot jong ka jinghikai babha shaphang ka akor, ban sngap bha ia kaba iadei bad ka mynsiem lyngba ka por bad ka kyrdan jong ngi ha ka pyrthei; ka long kawei pat ka jinglong ban niew ia ka kumba ka long shisha — ka ktien jong U Blei uba im, ka ktien kaba long ka jingim jong ngi, ka ktien kaba dei ban thaw dur ia ki kam jong ngi bad ia kino kino ki ktien jong ngi, ia kino kino ki ktien jong ngi. Ban bat ia ka ktien U Blei kum kaei kaei kaba duna ban ia kane ka long ban kyntait ia ka. Bad kane ka jingkyntait da kito kiba kam ba ki ngeit ia ka, ka long kaba hakhmat eh napdeng ki daw jong ka jingsngew artatien bad jingbymhok ha ki samla.”—Ellen G. White, Education, p. 260.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Kiei kita baroh ki daw kiba thikna bad kiba don jingmut kiba phi don na ka bynta ka jingngeit jong phi? Lehse ka long kaba kham bun ban ia kaba phi sngewthuh?`

`Kumno phi lah ban pynthikna ba ka jingpule Baibl bad ka jingduwai ki long ka nongrim jong ka jingiadei jong phi bad U Blei? Kumno phi lah ban don ka jingiadei bad U Blei khlem ka jingduwai bad ka jingpule Baibl?`

`Lada don kiba kwah ban don ia ka jingiadei ba kham jylliew jong ki bad U Blei, kumno phin ai jingmut ia ki ban sdang ban pule ia ka Baibl?`

`Kumno phi lah ban im da kawei pa kawei ka ktien kaba mih na ka shyntur U Trai? (Duetteronomi 8:3). Kaei ka ban long shisha ha ka jingim jong phi?`

`Ki dkhot kiba bud ki iathuh aiu ia ngi shaphang ki ktien U Blei? Hebru. 11:3; Salm. 33:6; Mathaios. 11:4, 5; 1 Thessoloni. 4:16; Ephesos. 6:17; Jakob 1:21.`
