---
title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat shuh shuh
date:   10/04/2026
---

“U Blei U khot ïa ki briew ban mihkhmat na ka bynta ka jinglong jong U, hynrei ban leh ïa kane, ngi dei ban ithuh ïa U da lade. Ka lad kaba bha tam ba ngi lah ban ïohi shai ïa U, watla ki khmat bapop ba kynja-doh jong ngi bunsien ki sngewthuh bakla ïa ki jinglong ba hok ba janai jong U,hynrei da kaba wad na ka Ktien jong U ka Baibl ngin shemphang kham bha.

“Baroh ka jingieit bakynja-kpa kaba wan hiar na jrong na kawei ka pateng sha kawei pat ka pateng lyngba ka lad na ka dohnud bakynja-briew, baroh ki thymmei jong ka jingiet thoiñ-thoiñ kiba la plié na ki mynsiem jong ki briew, ki long tang kum ki jingjaw ba rit sha ki duriew ba ïar bad ba jylliew haba ïanujor bad kata ka jingieit ba bymjukut jong U Blei.U thylliej um lah ban batai, u khulom un ym lah ban thoh ne dro. Phi lah lehse ban puson man la ka sngi ha ka jingim jong phi; phi lah ban wad na ki jingthoh bakhuid da kaba ïitbniah eh khnang ba phi lah ban sngewthuh ïa kata; phi lah lehse ban pyndonkam lut ïa ki bor jong phi baroh kiba U Blei U la ai ha phi, da ka jingpyrshang ban shemphang ïa kata ka jingieit bad ka jingleh sbun jong U Kpa ba ha bneng; pynban dang don ka bymjukut sha lyndet kabym lah ban kot. Phi lah lehse ban pule ïa kata ka jingieit da ki bun snem; hynrei da lei lei ruh phin ym lah ban shemphang ïa ka jingjylliew bad ka jingïar jong kata ka jingieit Blei da kaba U la aiti noh la U Khun ban ïap na ka bynta ka pyrthei. Ka bymjukut hi kan ym biang ban pynpaw lut ïa ka. Pynban katba ngi pule ïa ka Baibl bad puson ruh ïa ka jingim jong U Khrist bad ïa ka plan jong ka jingpynim, kine ki mat jingpyrkhat kiba khraw ki lah ban kham pynshai ïa ngi kham bha.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 740.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Katba phi pyrkhat shaphang ka jinglong jong U Blei kiba phi la pule lyngba kane ka taïew, kano na kine kaba ktah jur ïa phi ha ka jingshemphang shaphang U Blei?`

`Kiei de kiwei kiwei ki jinglong jong U Blei kiba phi lah ban pule khnang ban pynkhlaiñ ñ aka jingïadei jong phi bad U?`

`Ryngkat bad ki paralok ne kiba haïing jong phi ïa pule lang ïa ka kot Ki Mawjam sha U Khrist ka lynnong kaba nyngkong, bad sa ïa kren lang. kaei ka jingïohi ba thymmai shaphang ka jinglong U Blei bad ka jinglong jong U Jisu kaba phi lah ban shem ha kane ka lynnong?`

`Kiba bun na ki briew ki don ka jingsngewthuh kaba bakla shaphang U Blei, kaba U Jisu U la wan ban pynbeit. Kaei ka ba phi lah ban leh ban pynshai ïa ki, ba kin ïoh ka jingasngewthuh kaba kham shai shaphang ka jinglong jong U Blei ha kito kiba phi don ka bor ktah`

`Pyrkhat biang kaei kaba phi la sngewthuh na ka jingpule jong phi kaba phi ïoh na ka bynta jong ka sngi ba ar. Watla ngi long kiba pop bad kibym khuid, bad ka Baibl ruh ka kren shai ba ki briew jong U Blei ki dei ba im ka jingim kaba khuid. Pule 1 Petros 1:13–16, Rom 6:22, and Hebru 12:14. U Blei U long Uba khuid U khot ruh ïa ngi ban long kiba khuid (hok) kamut aïu shisha ban im ka jingim kaba khuid (hok)?`
