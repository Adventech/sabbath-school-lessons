---
title:  Immanuel, U Blei Lem Bad Ngi
date:   09/04/2026
---

Lada phi kwah ban ïasam sha kiwei kibym pat long Khristan shaphang ka jinglong jong U Blei katkum ba phi shem ha ka Baibl sha kano ka bynta phin peit?

Ka jubab ba bha tam ka dei sha U Jisu.Ka Baibl ong ba U Jisu ym tang ba U phalang ïa ka jinglong U Blei hynrei U pynpaw ïa U Blei. Ki don shibun eh ki jingthoh kiba pynshai halor kane ka mat, hynrei kaba kham shai ngi lah ban shem haIoannis 14:9. Hangne U Jisu U ong, “ ‘Uta uba la ïohi ïa Nga u la ïohi ïa U Kpa’ ”. Khnang ba ngi lah ban tip Uta U Kpa U long Uba kumno,ngi dei ban peit sha U Jisu—Ki jingkren jong U, Ki kam jong U, bad ka rukom im jong U, bad ka jingieit bakhraw jong U ïa ki briew kumba U la pynpaw ha ka jingïap bad ka jingmihjpat jong U.

Ka jingieit bad ka jingsumar sukher jong U Kpa la pynpaw shynna ha U Khun jong U, U Jisu.Ka jingitynnad jong ka Baibl ka long kane ba U Blei U la ai ha ngi saw tylli ki mat kiba khlaiñ shaphang ka jingim jong U Jisu kumta ba ngi lah ban ïoh ka dur ba kham shai shaphang jong U. Ha ka Gospel katkum u Matthaios (ba la thoh da u Jiw, na ka bynta ki Jiw), ngi ïohi ïa U Jisu kum Uta U Messiah ba la ap la slem Uba la pyndep ïa kaba la kular. Ha ka Gospel katkum u Mark, ngi ïohi ïa U Jisu Uba im bad Uba trei ha ka jingshakri bad ka jinglen lade. Uba ju pyrkhat barobor na ka bynta ka jingbha jong kiwei bad Uba kloi ban leh ïa ka mon U Kpa jong U. Ha ka Gospel katkum uLoukas, ngi lah ban pule shaphang U Jisu kumno U sngew, da ka jinglong briew ryngkat ka jingsngewsynei, bgi lah ban pule ïa kane ka jingbuhkynmaw ban ïoh ka jingsngewskhem ba ïa kaei kaba ngi pule ka long kaba shisha.(Loukas 1:3, 4). Ha ka Gospel katkum u Ioannis, ngi lah ban ïohi ïa U Khun jong U Blei ha ka jingwan longbriew jong U bad ban ngeit ruh ïa kaei kaba U ong ba ka long kaba shisha ba U long kumta,kumta ba ka jingim bakymja-mynsiem jong ngi ruh kan im. Watla baroh ki saw Gospel ki balai ïa ka juh ka mat, “Hynrei kim batai pat ha ka juh ka rukom. Uwei pa uwei u nongthoh u don ka jingïashem la ka jong ka jong, bad kane ka jingïapher ka ïarap ban kham pynjylliew ïa ka jingshemphang jong ngi kaban pynbiang katkum ka jingïapher ki rukom pyrkhat pyrdaiñ.”—Ellen G. White, Manuscript 105, 1900. Kano na kine ki Gospel phi dang shudep ban pule kham bniah?

`Ha Mathios 1:23, La ai da ka kyrteng kaba thikna ïa U Jisu. Balei ba kane ka la long kaba kyrpang ha ka jingsngewthuh jong ngi shaphang ka jinglong jong U Blei? Pule Mathaios 28:20, da peit bha ïa ka bynta kaba khatduh jong ka dkhot. Ïanujor ïa kine ki artylli ki dkhot. Phi ïohi aïu?`

Nga dang shu ktah tang na sla ïa kane ka mat kaba khraw, ka jinglong jong U Blei. U Blei U long Uba kham khraw bad Uba ngim lah ban sngewthuh janai shaphang jong U, bad ngin sa nang kham sngewthuh shuh shuh shaphang jong U lyngba ki arta bymjukut.

`U Blei U donhok ban ïoh ka jingïaroh jong ngi ïa kaei ba U la leh na ka bynta jong ngi bad U dang ïaileh ruh.Shim khyndiat na ka por jong phi mynta ban ïaroh pyndonburom ïa U ha ka jingduwai na ka bynta kaei ba U long. Long kiba kren shai kumba ka Baibl ka pynpaw shaphang jong U ha phi. (kum ka nuksa, “Nga ainguh ïa Me Ah Trai U Blei, na ka bynta ka jingleh _____, kumba Me la kren ha nga ha _____.”)`
