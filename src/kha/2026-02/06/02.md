---
title:  U Daniel Ba Ïaineh
date:   03/05/2026
---

U Daniel u long uwei na ki riewshlur ba khraw jong ka Baibl. Ngi tip ia ka jingiathuhkhana kaba nyngkong ruh (peit Daniel 1): “U Daniel u la thmu ha la ka dohnud ba un ym pyntngit ia lade da ka bynta na ki jingbam ba bang jong u syiem” (Daniel. 1:8, NKJV). Kumjuh ruh, ha u Daniel bad ki lai ngut ki paralok jong u, “U Blei u la ai ha ki ka jingtip bad ka jingstad ha ki kot baroh bad ha ka jingstad, bad u Daniel u la don ka jingshemphang ha ki jingithuhpaw bad ki jingphohsniew baroh” (Daniel. 1:17, NKJV). Ka Baibl ka batai ia u Daniel kum uba stad (Daniel. 1:20; Daniel. 2:14, 21, 23, 48) namar ba U Mynsiem U Blei U la don ha u (Daniel. 4:9, 18; Daniel. 5:14; Daniel. 6:3), bad la ieit eh ia u da ka Bneng (Daniel. 9:1,2). Kine ki long katto katne ki nongbatai shaphang u briew uba don ka jingiadei kaba skhem bad kaba neh bad U Blei.

Ha u Daniel 2, haba u syiem Nebukhadnessar u la pynmih ia ka hukum pyniap ia baroh ki riewstad ha Babilon, u Daniel u la pan ia ka jingisynei jong U Blei shaphang ka jingmaham jong ka jingphohsniew jong u syiem (Daniel. 2:18). Haba U Blei u la pynpaw ia ka jingphohsniew jong u syiem ha u Daniel, u la duwai mardor.

`Pule Danial 2:20–23. Balei U Danial u duwai, bad kaei kaba ngi lah ban nang na kane ka jingduwai?`

Katba ki snem ki nang iaid bad ki syiem ki la mih bad kyllon, u Daniel u dang long u nongai jingmut ia ki syiem bad la batai ia u kum uba pawnam “namar ba la don ka mynsiem kaba bha eh ha u; bad u syiem u la pyrkhat ban buh ia u halor ka hima baroh.” (Daniel. 6:3, NKJV). “Namarba u la long u bangeit, ym shym la shem ia ka jingbakla lane ka jingduna ha u.” (Daniel. 6:4, NKJV). Wat hapdeng ka jingsngewsih kaba jur bad ka jingthmu sniew (Daniel. 6:5-9) na ki paralok jong u, u Daniel u la iaisah bad khlem tieng ha ka jingim jingduwai jong u.

`Pule Danial 6:10–11. Kumno kine ki dkhot ki iathuh ia ngi sha phang u Danial?`

Haba u la ia kynduh ia ki jingeh, U Danial u la duwai. Wat la ka jingbyrngem ka long pyrshah ia ka jingim jong u, hynrei u la long uba iaineh bad uba iai shah ha ka jingduwai (lai sien ha ka shi sngi, kumba u la long ka rukom jong u), bad ka balah ban tip lupa (ha ka jingkhang iit ka ba la plie lai sien ha ka shi sngi kat ba u la duwai da kaba phai sha phang Jerusalem). Ka jingduwai jong u ka long ka jingleh kaba ha kamet (u la dem khohsiew) bad u la pynleit jingmut ha ka jingainguh bad ka jingkyrpad.

`Ha ka jingshai jong ka jingiathuhkhana kum kane, ki jingaidaw jong ngi ki long kiba tlot shisha lada ngim duwai?`
