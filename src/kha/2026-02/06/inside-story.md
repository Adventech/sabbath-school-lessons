### Ka Jingia-kylliang Ia Ka Shuka Bad Ki Patlun

U Pastor u la iathuh ha u Rompas, u khynnah Maasai ha Kenya, ba ki Seventh-day Adventist ki dei ki nongmane ksuid. U la ong ruh ba ki Adventist ki rung iingmane dadien ha ka sngi Saitjain bad ki mane khlem da phong jain.

Ynda u la dap 16 snem, u Rompas u la rai ban wad bniah la u pastor u la iathuh ia ka jingshisha. Ha ka step sngi Saitjain, u la iaid kjat na iing na shnong jong u sha kawei ka iingmane Adventist kaba jngai katto katne mail. U la rieh ha ki lum hajan ka iingmane bad u la peitkai.

Ym slem, u briew uba nyngkong u la poi. Uta u briew um shym la iaid dadien, bad um shym la lyngkhuid. U Rompas u la sngewtynnat ba uta u briew u phong da ka suit bad ka tie kaba itynnad bha. U khynnah u la kwah eh ba un don ka suit bad ka tie. U la lyngkhuid shiteng, u phong tang ka shuka tynrai jong ki Maasai, ka jain kaba saw bad ka jingthoh rong ïong.

Nangta la wan poi u pastor bad kiwei pat ki parabangeit. Ki ruh kim ju rung dadien shapoh iingmane, bad ki la riam bha.

Nangta ka kynhun choir jong ka balang ka la sdang ban rwai. Katba ki kyntien jong ka jingrwai “Oh Sngi Basuk” ki nang rung ha ki shkor jong u Rompas, um lah ban ialeh pyrshah. Da kaba iaid sha iingmane, u la shong ha ka lain kaba shadien bad u la sngap da ka jingsngewtynnat kaba khraw.

Hadien ka jingialap, uwei u samla doh lieh u la wan sha u. U la long u mishoneri na ri America uba um pat ju iohi mynno mynno ruh bad um pat ju iohi shuh. Uta u briew u la kren da ka jingiarap jong u nongpynkylla ktien. “Kine ki dei ki patlun jong phi,” u ong bad ai ha u Rompas ar tylli ki patlun kaba don bun ki pla.

U Rompas u la sngewtynnad! U khlem pat ju don ka patlun. U la phong ia ki bad u la tar ia ka shuka jong u ban shna panpoh ban pynskhem ia ki sha jrong.

Haba la wan phai sha la iing, ki hynmen bad ki para jong u kiba 82 ngut ki la lyngngoh ban iohi ia u khynnah Maasai ba u phong patlun. Ki la ker sawdong ia u bad ki la kylli, “Kaei kaba la jia?”

U Rompas u la buh ia ka patlun kaba kordor hapoh ka jingthiah jong u ha kata ka miet. U la phong biang ia ki tang ynda u la leit phai sha jingiaseng ha ka Sabbaton kaba bud. Ka jingdeng ia ka patlun ka khring ia ka jingmut jong ki khynnah bad ki kynthei jong ka shnong jong u man la ka Sabbaton. Don napdeng jong ki kiba la sdang ban bud ia u sha jingiaseng.

Katto katne bnai ki la iaid, bad u Rompas bad ka kmie jong u ki la aiti ia la ka dohnud ha U Jisu ha ka jingpynbaptis ha kajuh ka sngi. Ki arngut ki dkhot ba nyngkong jong ka longiing kaba don haduh 100 ngut ki briew ki la kylla long ki Seventh-day Adventist. U Rompas u la sngew laitluid ha ka sien kaba nyngkong ha ka jingim jong u. U Jisu u la ong ha ki synran jong U, “Bad phin tip ia ka jingshisha, bad kata ka jingshisha kan pyllaitluid ia phi” (Ioannis 8:32, NKJV).

“Ka jingshisha ka la pyllaitluid ia nga”, la ong u Rompas ha ka jingiakren.

Ka bynta jong kane ka jingkynshew khatlai Sabbaton, kaba la tip ruh kum ka jingkynshew Projek Mishon man la ka kwatar, kan kyrshan ïa ki projek ha Kenya bad shawei pat ha ka bynta jong ka East-Central Africa Division.
