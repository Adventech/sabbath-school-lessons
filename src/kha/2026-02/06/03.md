---
title:  Ka Rukom Ban Jingduwai
date:   04/05/2026
---

Haba don kaei kaei kaba bakla ha ka jingim jong ngi, bunsien ngi khot ia u paralok ba jan ban ia kren shaphang kata. Haba ngi don ka khubor babha, ngi wad ia iano re iano ban iasam ia ka. Ngi lah ban leh kumjuh bad U Blei. “Ka jingduwai ka long ka jingplie ia ka dohnud sha U Blei kum sha u paralok.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ p. 93.

Ka jingduwai kam pynlong tang ia ngi ban iadei bad U Blei hynrei ka iathuh ia u ksuid ba ngi long ki jongno. Haba ngi dem khohsiew ban duwai mynstep, ka long kum ka jingpynbna met ia ki bor jong ka jingdum ba ngi jied ia U Blei ha kane ka sngi. Ym tang katta, hynrei U Blei U phah ia ki angel sha ka liang jong ngi haba ngi duwai, bad la pynskhem bad la tap ia ngi na u nongshun jong ka jingdum (Salm 91).

Ka jingleh ha kamet da kaba dem khohsiew ha kaba pyndem ka pyni ia ka jinglong jong ka jingsngew rit. Ka long kaba ia pher na kaba shong ha ka shuki ne kaba thiah ha ka jingthiah katba ngi dang duwai, wat la ngi lah ban duwai ha kine ki rukom ruh. Hynrei haba ngi dem khohsiew ha khmat U Blei, ngi pyni ba ngi long kiba kloi ban shakri ia U Blei da ka dohnud baroh, bad ki ktien jong ngi ki pynbna ba U long uba donbor bad ba ngi long tang ki khun ba la thaw jong U.

`Pule ia kine ki dkhot Baibl bad pyrkhat ia ka jiingim jong kine ki briew shimet kiba dem khohsiew ha kaba duwai: Danial. 6:10, Laukas 22:41, Ki Kam 7:60, Ki Kam 9:40, Ki Kam 20:36.`

Ka jingduwai ha ba ieng ka long ka rukom kaba ju leh ha ki por jong ka Baibl (2 Khronikl. 20:5, 6, 13; 1 Samual. 1:26; Job 30:20; Loukas. 18:11, 13). Ka Baibl ka iasam ruh ia ki nuksa jong ki briew kiba shong haba ki duwai (2 Slamual. 7:18). Kiwei pat ki dem pylup ha khmat U Blei, da kaba pyndem ia la ka khmat ha ka khyndew— wat la kane ka rukom ka kham duna haka jingiadei bad ka jingduwai, hynrei ka long ka jingpyndem ha khmat uba kham halor (1 Ki Syiem 1:47, Markos 14:35).

Kaei ka rukom leh jong phi haba phi duwai? Ka Baibl kam dawa ia ngi ban duwai ha kano kano ka rukom shong ne ieng, hynrei ki rukom leh ki long kiba kongsan, namar ki pynpaw ia ka jingburom jong ngi, ia ka jingsngew ba shapoh jong ngi, bad ia ka jingkwah jong ngi ban aiti noh ha U Blei. Don ki briew ki bym lah ban dem khohsiew; kumta, ha kaba kut, ka dei ka jinglong jong ka dohnud kaba kongsan tam. Lada phi lah ban dem khohsiew hynrei bunsien phim ju leh kumta, balei phim pyrshang ban dem khohsiew ha kawei ka por ha ba phi duwai bad peit kumno ka ktah ia ka por jong phi bad U Blei?

`Ka Baibl ka khot ia ngi ban “duwai khlem da pud” (1 Thes. 5:17), kaba mut ka jingiaineh (Kol. 4:2) bad ka jingiaishah (Rom 12:12). Mynta ka sngi, katba phi dang ieng, shong, thiah, ne iaid, pynphai ia ki jingmut jingpyrkhat jong phi sha U Blei bad kren bad U kum u Paralok jong phi. Sdang mynta hi.`
