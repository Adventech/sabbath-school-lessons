---
title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat shuh shuh
date:   08/05/2026
---

Ha kaba kut, ngi dei ban duwai namar ngi ieit eh ia U Blei, bad ngim lah khlem da iasam ia kiei kiei baroh ha ka jingim jong ngi bad U: ki jingkmen bad ki “jingjop” jong ka jingim jong ngi, ki jingkit bad ki jingkhuslai jong ngi, ki jingkyrpad bad ki jingdonkam ba man ka sngi jong ngi. “Ngi lah ban bat kham hajan sha U Blei ba ha man la ka jingtynjuh bym shym khmih lynti ki jingpyrkhat jong ngi kin phai sha U kumba u syntiew u phai sha ka sngi. To buh ia ki jingkwah jong phi, ki jingkmen jong phi, ki jingsngewsih jong phi, ki jingkhuslai jong phi bad ki jingtieng jong phi ha khmat U Blei. Phi lah ban pynkhia ia U; phim lah ban pynsngewsih ia U. Uta uba khein ia ki shniuh khlieh jong phi um long uba khlem pyrkhat ia ki jingdonkam jong ki khun jong U. . . . Ka dohnud jingieit jong u ka shah ktah ha ki jingsngewsih jong ngi bad wat ha ki jingkren jong ngi shaphang jong ki. Shim sha U ia kiei kiei baroh kiba pynkulmar ia ka jingmut jingpyrkhat. Ym don kaei kaei kaba khraw palat ia U ban kit, namar U bat ia ki pyrthei, U synshar ia ki kam baroh jong ka pyrthei. Ym don kaei kaei kaba ha kano kano ka rukom kaba iadei bad ka jingsuk jong ngi kaba rit palat ia U ban ym iohi. Ym don ka lynnong ha ka jingshem jong ngi kaba dum palat ia U ban pule; ym don ka jingkynrum kynram kaba eh palat ia U ban pynshai. Ym don ka jingjynjar kaba lah ban jia ia uba rit tam na ki khun jong U, ym don ka jingkhuslai kaba pynshitom ia ka mynsiem, ym don ka jingkmen kaba pynkmen, ym don ka jingduwai kaba shisha kaba lait na ki rmiang shyntur, ia kaba U Kpa jong ngi uba ha bneng um ju peit bniah, lane ha kaba Um shimkhia mardor. . . . Ki jingiadei hapdeng U Blei bad kawei pa kawei ka mynsiem ki long kiba kyrpang bad kiba dap kumba ym don kawei pat ka mynsiem ha ka pyrthei ban iasam ia ka jingsumar jong U, ym don kawei pat ka mynsiem na ka bynta kaba U la aiti ia U Khun baieit jong U.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, sla 99, 100.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Phi lah batai ia ka jingduwai kum kaba sngewtynnad ne kaba ban-khia? Kaei kaba ka la ai ia ka ha ka jingpeit jong phi?`

`Ki don shibun ki khubor kiba sngewthuh jingmut ha ka jingkynthoh haneng. Kaei ka jingpyrkhat kaba kham sngewtynnad ia phi?`

`Na ki lai ngut ki briew jong ka Baibl kiba la pule ha kane ka taiew (U Daniel, u Enokh bad u Moses), ka jingim jingduwai jongno phi iadei bha bad balei?`
