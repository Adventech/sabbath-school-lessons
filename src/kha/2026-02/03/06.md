`Pule “Loukas 22:27 sa shisien. Kaei ka khubor ba kongsan na ka bynta baroh ki nongbud U Khrist hangne?`

Ha ka jingïapher kaba khraw na ka jingkwah jong ki synran ban long kiba kham halor bad ka jingngeit jong ki ba ki kham bha ban ïa u wei pat, ngi ïohi ïa U Jisu — ka nuksa ba khatduh eh jong ka jinglong ba sngewrit. U Jisu, U la ong, “Hynrei nga long hapdeng jong phi kum Uta Uba shakri” (Loukas. 22:27,). U Jisu, uba man la ka sngi u la ai sha kiba donkam kiba don sawdong jong U namar ba U la dap da ka jingsngewlem bad U la iohi ia ki paidbah kum ki langbrot khlem nongap. U tip ba ka jaitbynriew ka donkam ia U kham palat ban ia kiei kiei baroh ha ka jingim, wat la tang khyndiat eh kiba sngewthuh ia kane ka jingshisha da kaba suk. U Jisu, uba la iehnoh ia ka bneng ban iap na ka bynta ka jaitbynriew da ka jingkyrmen ba kin sngewthuh ia ka jingleh jong ka jingaiei jong U bad kin jubab ia ka jingkhot jong U ban don ka jingiadei bad U.

`Pule Philipi 2:3-8. Kine ki dkhot ki iathuh aiu ia ngi shaphang kumno ngi dei ban im ha ka jingshai jong ka Diengphna?`

U Jisu u la leh ia kata baroh. U la shah ia baroh. Haba ngi sangeh slem bha ban iohi ia U — da kaba shisha bad kaba jingkhuid sngur — ngim lah khlem da sngewthuh ia ka jinglong bymkhuid jong ngi, ia ka jinglong jakhlia jong ngi, bad ia ka jingdonkam kaba jur jong ngi ia U ha ka jingim jong ngi mynta.

Haba ngi peit sha U, ia kiwei pat kiei kiei baroh (khamtam eh ia ngi bad ia ka jingkhraw kaba ngi sngewthuh hi) ki kylla long ki bym don jingmut satia. Uei U Jisu, kaei kaba U la leh, bad katno U ieit ia ki jingthaw jong U ka kylla long ha khmat bad pdeng. Ka lade da shisha kan sa jah noh haba ngi peit sha U.

U Jisu. Ka kyrteng kaba sngewtynnad, ka kyrteng kaba donbor. U long ka dak jong ka jinglong ba sngewrit. Haba ki dohnud ba plie jong ngi ki tip shaphang jong U, haba ngi sngewthuh ia kaei kaba U la leh na ka bynta jong ngi, bad haba ngi ailad ia ki ktien jingim jong U ban rung shapoh ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong ngi, ngi sngewthuh haduh katno ngi long kiba sngew sarong bad kiba tngit shisha. Lada ki synran jong U hi, kiba la im bad kiba la nang na U, ki la ialeh pyrshah ia ka jingsarong, ngim lah ban pynbiej ia lade ba ngim long kiba pher ei ei. Khatduh eh, ngi lah ban san ha ka jingiadei jong ngi bad U Jisu tang haba ngi long kiba sngewrit.

`Pynlut kham bun ka por bad U mynta. Shim ia ka Baibl jong phi, u khulom, bad ka kot lyngkdop ne kano kano ka kot bad wad ia ka jaka kaba jar jar — lehse wat shabar ruh. Khot ia U Blei ban pynjem bad kren ia ka dohnud jong phi. Thoh ia ka Salm 138, kawei ka kyntien hadien kawei pat. Katba phi dang thoh, kiei ki kyntien kiba ieng kyrpang eh na ka bynta ha phi?`
