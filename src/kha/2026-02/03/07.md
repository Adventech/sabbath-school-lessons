title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat shuh shuh
date:   17/04/2026
---

“Katba ngi nang jan sha U Jisu bad ngi nang sngewthuh shai ia ka jinglong khuid jong ka jinglong jong U, katta ngin nang sngewthuh shai ia ka jinglong pop kaba palat bad katta ngin kham duna ka jingsngew ba ngi kwah ban pynkhraw ia lade. Kito kiba ka bneng ka ithuh kum ki riewkhuid ki long kiba khatduh eh ban pyni ia la ka jong ka jingbha.”—Ellens O. Christb, White. 160.

“Ha shuwa ka burom ka don ka jingsngewrit. Ban pyndap ia ka jaka kaba jrong ha khmat ki briew, ka Bneng ka jied ia u nongtrei uba, kum u Ioannis Nongpynbaptis, u shim ia ka jaka kaba hapoh ha khmat U Blei. U synran uba syriem eh ia u khynnah dei u nong trei uba tbit tam ha ka jingtrei na ka bynta U Blei. Ki angel jong ka bneng ki lah ban iatreilang bad uta uba iaiwad, ym ban kyntiew ia lade, hynrei ban pynim ia ki mynsiem.

“Haba ki briew ki kyntiew ia ki hi, ki da sngew ba ki long ka donkam eh na ka bynta ka jingjop jong ka jingthmu bakhraw jong U Blei, te U Trai u pynlong ba ia ki yn kyntait sha rud….

“Kam shym la biang ia ki synran jong U Jisu ban shah hikai shaphang ka jinglong jong ka hima jong U. Kaei kaba ki donkam ka long ka ia ka dohnud kaba kylla ka ban wallam ia ki sha ka jinglong shisur bad ki ain tynrai jong ka… Ka jinglong ba synlar, ka jingklet ia ka lade, bad ka jingieit kaba shaniah skhem jong u khynnah rit ki long ki jinglong kiba ka bneng ka ñiewkor eh kine ki dei ki jinglong jong ka jingkhraw kaba shisha.

“Ka mynsiem kaba sngur, kaba sngewsih ia ka pop, ka long kaba kordor ha khmat U Blei. U buh ia la ka jong ka shap jong U ki briew, ym dei kat kum ka kyrdan jong ki, ym kat kum ka spah jong ki, ym dei ka jingkhraw jingstad jong ki, hynrei da ka jinglong shitylli jong ki bad U Khrist.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, pp. 436, 437.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Kiei ki jingshemphang ba kham bniah kiba kine ki dkhot harum ki don shaphang ka jingsarong bad jinglong ba sngewrit: Mathaios 23:12; Salm 25:9; Salm 149:4; bad Jakob 4:6, 10?`

`Pyrkhat bha: kaei ka sien kaba khatduh “phi pynpaw ia la ka jong ka jingbha”? Kumno kane ka ktah ia ka jingiadei jong phi bad U Blei ne kito kiba phi la ia pynpaw ia ka ha khmat ki?`

`Kaei kaba phi lah ban donkam ban pynkylla ha ka jingim jong phi ban pynrit ia lade ha khmat U Blei khnang ban pynskhem ia ka jingiaid jong phi bad U?`
