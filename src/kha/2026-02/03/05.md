---
title:  Ka Jingleh Bymman Kaba Khraw Tam
date:   15/04/2026
---

Pyrkhat ba phi long ki synran jong U Jisu. Phi iaid ryngkat bad U, phi bam ryngkat bad U, phi thiah hajan jong U, bad phi ioh jinghikai na U katba U pynkylla ia ki jingim bym lah ñiew, kynthup ia ka jong phi. Ki briew ki pyrta hadien jong U, bad phi sngewthuh haduh katno ka long kaba kyrpang ba U la jied ia phi ban long uwei na ki 12 ngut kiba jan tam bad U. Nangta phi sdang ban pyrkhat: Mano ba long shisha uba khraw tam na ki synran baroh?

`Ha u Loukas 22:24-27, pule ia ka jubab U Jisu ia ka jingiakajia jong ki synran shaphang kaei ka jingmut jong ka jingkhraw. Kaei ka jingong kaba ktah ia ka dohnud jong ka khubor U Jisu ha kine ki dkhot?`

Don u ban pyrkhat ba hadien kane ka por baroh kaba ki la iajan bad U Jisu, kane ka jait jingiatai kan long kaba khatduh eh ha ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong ki. Hynrei kata kam dei kaei ka ba la jia.

Ha ka jaka ba kine ki briew kin sngewhun ia la ka jingkhot, ka jingsarong ka la kiew ha ki dohnud jong ki haduh ba uwei pa uwei u pyrkhat ba u kham bha ban ia kiwei pat. Ka long kaba suk ban ailad ia kum kine ki jingpyrkhat ban synshar ia ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong ngi. Hynrei la iathuh ia ngi ba “ym don kaei kaei kaba pynsngewsih ia U Blei lane kaba pynma ia ka mynsiem briew kum ka jingsarong bad jingsngew biang lade. Na ki pop baroh ka long kaba khlem jingkyrmen tam, kaba ym lah ban pynkoit.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 154.

Kane ka long kaba khia bha ia ngi. Ka jingsarong jong ngi ka pynsngewsih ia U Blei kham palat ban ia kiwei pat kiei kiei, bad ka dei ka jinglong kaba eh ban jop namar bunsien ngim ju iohi ia kaei ba ka long. Ha ka jinglong jong ngi kaba la sngew biang ia lade, ngi jied ban ym bishar bniah ia lade, namar shisha ka jingsarong ka long ka syiem. Ngi donkam ban sangeh, ban wad bniah ialade, bad ban pan ba U Blei Un plie ia ki khmat jong ngi sha ka jinglong bashisha jong ngi, namar ka jingsarong ka lah ban long ka daw kaba nyngkong eh kaba khanglad ia ngi ban don ka jingiadei kaba jan bad U mynta.

`Lada phi sngewthuh ba tang U Blei U lah ban leh ia ka kam ban pynduh ia ka jingsarong bad ka jingkhwan lade na ka mynsiem jong phi, sangeh bad duwai ia kane ka jingduwai mynta hi: “Ko Trai, shim ia ka dohnud jong nga; namar ngam lah ban ai ia ka. Ka long ka jong Me. To sumar ia ka, namar ngam lah ban sumar ia ka na ka bynta jong Me. Pynim ia nga, ha ka jinglong bat lot, ha ka jingbym long kum U Khrist. Pynlwet bad thaw dur ia nga pynkiew ia nga sha ka jinglong kaba khuid bad kaba sngur, ha kaba ka jingdap jong ka jingieit jong Me ka lah ban tuid lyngba ka mynsiem jong nga.”—Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 159.`
