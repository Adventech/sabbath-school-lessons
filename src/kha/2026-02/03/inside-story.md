### “Ngi Kwah Ban Iarap”

Ka kmie bad lai ngut ki khun kynthei kiba rit ka la ïaid haka lynti sha ïing ha Iolanda ha Belo Jaridin, ka nongbah Brazil kaba don 80,000 ngut ki briew.

Ka Iolanda ka iohi ia ki ba ki wan. Ka la ieng ha ka jingkhang, ka ai ia u khaw bad u rymbai ha uwei u briew uba kam tip uba la wan sangeh ban pan jingbam. Ka iing jong ka ka don ha ka surok kaba bun briew, bad ki briew ki ju wan sha ka jingkhang ban pan jingiarap. Ka la thikna ba ki nongwan kai kiba wan shajan ruh ki donkam ia ka kti, kumta ka la ap hadien ba u nongwei u la leit noh.

Ynda ka kmie bad ki khynnah kynthei ki la poi ha ka jingkhang, ki khmat jong ka Iolanda ki la phai ha ki kjat jong ki khynnah.

“Balei ki khun jong phi ki iaid kjat suda?” ka la kylli.

Ka kmie ka la batai ba ki juti jong ka khun jong ka kaba dang 8 snem ka rta ki la dkut, kumta ka la bthah ia ki khun kynthei jong ka kiba dang saw bad hynriew snem ban weng noh ia ki juti jong ki khnang ba ka para kaba kham heh jong ki kan ym sngew lehrain.

“Ngan sa ioh da kiwei ki sandal bad katto katne ki jingbam,” la ong ka Iolanda.

Ka la rung noh shapoh iing bad ka la wan phai hadien khyndiat por ryngkat bad ki sandal na ka bynta ka khynnah kynthei kaba dang 8 snem bad ka jingbam ba jem, ki biskit bad ka um.

Ki khynnah kynthei kiba rit ki la rkhie da ka jingkmen. “Ngi lah ban khot ia phi I mei-rad?” iwei i la kylli.

Ka kmie ka la lyngngoh ia ka jingsbun jong ka Iolanda.

“Balei phi leh kumne?” ka la kylli.

“Nga dei ka Khristan na ka Balang Seventh-day Adventist, bad ngi sngewtynnad ban iarap ia ki briew,” la ong ka Iolanda. “Nga thain jain na ka bynta ki khynnah, bad ki dkhot balang ki wanrah shibun ki jingai sngewbha sha nga. Kumta, nga don shibun ki sandal bad ki jain.”

“Nga kwah ban long shi bynta jong kane ka balang,” la ong ka kmie. “Nga kwah ban pule Baibl bad phi.”

Hadien shisnem, ka kmie ka la shah pynbaptis bad ka la rung sha ka Balang Adventist.

Ka Iolanda Xavier, ka mei ieit kaba la 86 snem ka rta, ka ngeit ba ym don kaei kaei kaba kham kongsan ban ia kaba kohnguh ia ka hukum U Jisu, “Namar kata to khie da leit noh bad pynlong synran ia ki jaitbynriew baroh, da kaba pynbaptis ia ki ha ka kyrteng U Kpa bad U Khun bad U Mynsiem Bakhuid, da kaba hikai ia ki ban sumar ia kiei kiei baroh kiba nga la hukum ia phi’ ( Mathaios 28:19, 20;)

“Ka mishon ka long kaba kongsan shisha”, ka la ong. “Baroh na ngi la kha na U Blei ban long ki mishoneri.”

“Ngi ngeit ba U Blei u la phah ia ki klew.” Ka la ong. “U Blei da shisha u ieit bad u sumar iala ki jong ki khun bad u lah ban pynlyngngoh iangi da ki jingpyllait kiba phylla na ki jingjynjar jongngi.”
