---
title:  U Moses, U Shakri Ba Sngewrit
date:   14/04/2026
---

Ki hall kiba heh jong ka iing syiem Egypt ki la sngew sarong ia ka jingriewspah, ka jingsngewbha bad ka jingsuk. “La hikai ia u Moses ha ka jingstad baroh ki nong Egypt, bad u la long uba donbor ha ki ktien bad ha ki kam” (Ki Kam 7:22,). Ka jingim kaba im ha ka bor, ka jingpawnam ki long ki jong u na ka bynta ban don ia ki; hynrei, u Moses u la jied ia kaei kaei kaba pher bak-ly-bak. “Kum u nongthoh histori, u myllung, u riewstad, u heh shipai, bad u nongthaw ain, u ieng kum uba khraw-batir. Hynrei ryngkat bad ka pyrthei kaba don ha khmat jong u, u la don ka bor akor ban kyntait ia ki jingkyrmen ba pynsngewbha jong ka spah bad ka jingkhraw bad ka nam, ‘u da jied kham bha ban shah shitom ryngkat bad ki briew jong U Blei, ban ia kaba pynthikna ia ka aiom jong ka pop, Ellen G. White. Ki Kpa tymmen bad ki Nongiathuhlypa, p. 246.

`Pule Kaei ba Ki Hebru 11:24–26 ka ïathuh aiu ïa ngi shaphang balei ba u Moses u la jied ïa ka lynti kaba pher bad pynrit ïa lade?`

Ka jinglong ba sngewrit jong u Moses hadien ka long kaba phylla, haba pyrkhat ia u briew uba donbor u long bad na kano ka jaka u la wan. Hynrei lyngba ka jingleh pop kaba sngewthuh bakla (Eksod. 2:12), u la duh noh ia ka jingshaniah bad jinglah da lade. Da ki lum kum ki kynroh jong ka kamra klas jong u bad ia ka jingsarong kaba la buh noh sha rud, haduh 40 snem la hikai ia u Moses da U Blei ia kaei kaba u donkam ban tip ban ialam ia ka jaitbynriew na ka jinglong mraw sha ka Ri Jingkular. Ka bor bad ka spah jong kaei kaba lah ban long kawei pat ka jingim ha Egypt ka la jah noh ha ka jingmut haba u Moses u la pyrkhat ia ka bymjukut. U Blei u la khot ia u da kaba kyrpang bha, bad u Moses u la bud.

Lehse kaba kham kongsan ha ka jingshai jong kane ka phang, Jingkhein 12:3 ka iathuh ia ngi: “Te uta u briew u Moses u la long uba sngewrit shibun, kham palat ban ia ki briew baroh kiba don ha ka pyrthei”. U Moses, uwei na ki kpa tymmen ba khraw jong ka Baibl, la tip ia u na ka bynta ka jinglong ba sngewrit, ka jinglong jemnud jong u. Pyrkhat katno ka jingim bad ka jinglong nongialam jong u kan jin da la long kaba pher lada ka jingsarong ka la rung ha kawei pa kawei na kine ki jingjia kiba khraw ha ka jingim jong u: ka law bniap ka ba ing, ki khlam ha Ijipt, ka jingleit lyngba ia ka Duriaw Basaw, ka manna kaba hap na bneng, ka jingiakren beit bad U Blei, ka jingpdiang ia ki Shiphew Hukum, bad ka jingsngap ia ki ktien U Blei hadien ba u la shoh ia u mawsiang.

`Pyrkhat biang ia ka jingim jong phi. Lada don kiba batai ia ka jingim jong phi, hato kin kynthup ia ka “jingsngewrit” ne “ka jingjemnud” ha ka thup jong ki kyntien pynpaw? Balei ne balei ym dei? Ka jingshisha ka long ba ngim lah ban long kiba sngewrit ha poh bad ha lade. Ka pop ka dei ka bynta jong ka jingim jong ngi, dei na kata ka daw ba ngi donkam eh ia U Jisu. Sngap, pule ne rwai ia ki kyntien ha ka jingrwai “Nga kham mon ia U Jisu,” bad pyrkhat ia kine ki kyntien ha kaba iadei bad ka jingim u Moses bad ki jong phi.`
