---
title:  Ka Jingpule Ïa Ki Dak Bad Ki Dur Pharshi Ha Ka Syrngiew Kat Kum Ka Baibl
date:   30/11/2025
---

`Pule ïa kine ki dkhot harum kiba kdew sha ki syrngiew bad pyrshang ban batai kaei ba ki dur pharshi ha ka syrngiew ka long? Rom. 5:14, 1 Korinth 10:1–13, Hebru 8:5, and Hebru 9:23.`

Kine ki bynta jong ka Baibl ki pyndonkam ïa ka kyntien “Syrngiew” (Greek typos) lane “Ka Syrngiew ka phalang” (Greek antitypos) ban kdew ïa ka rukom ba u nongthoh jong ka Testament Bathymmai u batai ïa ka jingïadei hapdeng ka jingthoh ne jingjia jong ka Testament Barim bad ka jingmut jong ka ha ka por jong u hi ne ha ka lawei.

Ka jingpule shaphang ka dur pharshi ha ka syrngiew ka long ka jingbatai kyrpang ïa ki briew, ki jingjia, ne ki tnat treikam kiba pyni lypa ïa U Jisu ne kiwei pat ki jingshisha kiba don ha ka gospel. Ka syrngiew ka ïadei bad ka met ba ka syrngiew ka phalang kum ka dur kaba ïohi lypa kaba pynpaw ïa ka dur tynrai, wat lada kaba hadien, ka dur kaba la ïohi ba ka syrngiew ka phalang, kaba pyndep pura ïa ka jingthmu jong ka syrngiew. Kumta, ka syrngiew kat kum ka baibl la pynwandur kum ka jingthmu ba kynja Blei kaba la don lypa, ne ha ka jingmut jingpyrkhat, ha ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong U Blei, bad ka trei ban pynwan dur ïa kata ka met ha kaba ka syrngiew ka phalang sha ka lawei.

Ka long kaba kongsan ban sngewthuh ba ki nongthoh jong ka Testament Bathymmai kim shym la ai jingmut lypa ïa ki katto katne ki jingthoh jong ka Testament Barim khnang ban pynpaw ïa ka jingmut. Ka syrngiew jong ka Testament Barim barabor la ju pynskhem ha ki jingthoh jong ka jingïathuhlypa shuwa ba kan ïoh ïa ka jingpyndep ïa kata ka syrngiew ha ka Testament Bathymmai.

**Peit kumno u Dabid u paw ha ka Testament Barim bad nangta kumno la pyni dur lypa ïa u ha ka Testament Bathymmai. Kiei ki jinghikai kiba ngi lah ban ïoh shaphang kumno ki dur pharshi ha ka syrngiew ka treikam na kane ka nuksa?**

`a. U David (Ki Salm. 22:1, 14–18)`

`b. U David uba Thymmai (Jeremïah 23:5; Isaïah 9:5, 6; Isaïah. 11:1–5)`

`c. Ka jingpyndep ïa ka syrngiew U David (Ïoannis 19:24)`

Da kaba peit ïa kine ki dkhot, ngi shem ba ka Testament Barim da lade hi ka ai ïa ka mat ban ithuh bad pyndonkam ïa ki Syrngiew ha ki Jingthoh Bakhuid. Kata, ki nongthoh jong ka Testament Bathymmai, kiba ka Jingthoh Bakhuid jong ki ka long ka Testament Barim, la pynkhih da U Mynsiem Bakhuid ban pyndonkam ïa ki syrngiew na ka Testament Barim ban pynpaw ïa ka “jingshisha kaba mynta” (2 Pet. 1:12), khamtam eh shaphang U Jisu bad ka jingshakri jong U.
