---
title:  Ki Duwan Jong Ka Jingpynthymmai
date:   11/11/2025
---

`Kaei ka jingpynshlur jong u Joshua ban tei ïa ka duwan ïa U Trai? Pule Joshua. 8:30, 31; ïanujor bad Deuteronomi. 11:26-30, Deuteronomi. 27:2-10.`

Ha ka por jong ki kpa tymmen, ki duwan ki pyni ïa ka lynti jong ka jingïaid pilkrim jong ki bad ki kylla long ki jingpyni kiba lah ban ïohi ïa ka jingkam kynti jong ki ïa ka ri, kaba la kular da U Blei. Mynta, da kaba pynïeng ïa ka duwan, ki Israel ki la sakhi ïa ka jingpyndep ïa ki jingkular ba la ai ha ki kpa tymmen jong ki. Ha kane ka khep, ka jingtei ïa ka duwan ka long ka jingpyndep beit ïa ki jingbthah ba la ai da u Moses (Deuteronomi 11: 26-30, Deuteronomi 27: 2-10).

U Joshua 8:30–35 ka don ka bynta kaba khraw ha kaba pynwan dur ïa ka khubor Theology baroh kawei jong ka kitab. Da kaba pynïadei ïa kawei na ki khana kiba shyrkhei tam, kiba shyrkhei tam (ka thma) bad kaei kaei kaba pher bak-ly-bak — ka jingjia jong ka jingpynskhem biang ïa ka jutang (ka jingmane) — u Joshua u ïalam biang ïa ngi sha kawei na ki phang pdeng ba kongsan tam jong ka jingpule ia U Blei kaba la sdang ha ka kitab kaba sdang: U Joshua u don ka jingbthah ban ïalam ïa ki Israel sha ka jingim jong ka jingkohnguh ïa ka jutang (Joshua:1: 7). Kane ka long ruh ka jingpyni jong u Joshua ha kaba kut jong ka kitab (Joshua 24).

Wat la ka thma bad ka jingjop ka long kaba kongsan, hynrei ka don kaei kaei kaba kham kongsan shuh shuh: ka jinglong ba ïaineh ha ki jingdawa jong ka hukum U Blei. Ka jingjop ka long tang kawei ka sienjam ha ka jingpyndep ïa ka jingthmu U Blei na ka bynta ki Israel bad ka jingpynthymmai ïa ka jaitbynriew baroh kawei. Ka jinglong ba ïaineh ha ki hukum jong ka Torah ka pynlong ïa ka jingkylli kaba kut ha ka jinglong jong ka longbriew manbriew. U Joshua u thoh ïa ka kopi jong ka hukum ha ki maw kiba heh bad kiba la astor, kiba pher na ki maw jong ka duwan (ïa nujor bad Deuteronomi. 27:2-8). Kumta, ki maw, kiba lehse ki don ïa ki Shiphew Hukum, ki la pynlong ïa ka jingkynmaw kaba kyrpang hajan ka duwan, kaba pynkynmaw beit ïa ki Israel ïa ki lad ki kabu bad ki kamram kiba la kdew ha ka jutang.

U Joshua u long ka syrngiew ïa u Jehoshua (Jisu) jong ka Jutang Bathymmai, uba ka kamram jong u ka long, nalor kiwei pat kiei kiei, ban ïalam biang ïa ki briew sha ka kohnguh ïa U Blei. Khnang ban pyndep ïa kane ka thong, U la dei ban shim ïa ka jingïaleh bad ki bor jong ka jingsniew. Ka thong ba khatduh jong u ka long ban pyndep ïa ki jingdawa jong ka jutang na ka bynta jong ngi: “Namar ki jingkular U Blei katba don, ki long ha u kaba hooid, bad ha u kaba amen, ïa kaban pyndon burom ïa U Blei da ngi.” (2 Kor. 1:20, NKJV).

`Kiei ki jingleh ba kynja mynsiem kiba ngi lah ban leh mynta kiba lah ban don ïa kijuh ki kam kum ka jingtei ïa ka duwan kaba la don ha ki por mynhyndai?`
