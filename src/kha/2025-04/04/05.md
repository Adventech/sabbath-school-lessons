---
title:  U Trai Un ïaleh Na Ka Bynta Jong Phi
date:   22/10/2025
---

`Pule kat kum Eksodos 14:13, 14, 25, kaei ka jinglong tynrai jong U Blei bad ka jinthmu kaba bha tam ka jong U shaphang ka jingïadon bynta jong ki Israel ha ka thma?`

Ha kata ka khyllipmat jong ka jingeh, haba ïa ki briew jong U Blei ki Israel ki la I kutlad, “U Moses u la jubab ïa ki briew, ‘Wat tïeng To ïeng skhem bad phin sa ïohi ïa ka jingpyllait kaba U Trai un wanrah ha phi mynta ka sngi. Ki nong Ijipt kiba phi ïohi mynta phin ym ïohi shuh lano lano ruh. U Trai un ïaleh na ka bynta jong phi; phi donkam tang ban sngap jar” (Eksodos. 14:13, 14). Kat kum ka jingïathuhkhana jong ka baibl, wat ki nong Ijipt hi la sngewthuh ïa kata ka jingshisha: “To ngin phet noh na khmat ki Israel, namar ka U Trai u ïaleh na ka bynta jong ki pyrshah ïa ki nong Ijipt” (Eksodos. 14:25).

Ka jingïarap phylla jong u blei na ka bynta ki Israel kiba la ïa kynduh ïa ka jingkutlad, ïa ki briew ki bym shym la shah hikai ha ka ka jingtbit shipai, ka kylla long ka rukom. Ka Eksodos ka la long ka nuksa, na ka bynta ka jingdon bynta ka jong U Blei na ka bynta ki Israel. Hangne, ym tang ka long ka thma ba la ïaleh da U Trai, hynrei la dawa ïa ki Israel ba kin ym ïaleh (Eksodos 14:14). U Blei u long u nongïaleh; ka jingsdang ka long ka jong U. U pynlong ïa ka buit, U batai ïa ki lad, bad ïarap ïa ki ban poi sha ka thong. Lada U Trai kam ïaleh na ka bynta ki Israel, kim don lad ban jop.

Ka Ellen G. White ka batai ïa kane kum ka jingpynpaw ïa ka jingshisha ba U Blei. “Um shym la thmu ba kin ïoh ïa ka ri ka jingkular da ka thma, hynrei lyngba ka jingpyndem bad ka jingkohnguh ïa ki hukum jong u.”—Ellen .G. White, Signs of the Times, September 2, 1880. Kum ha ka jingpyllait im ïa ki khun isreal na Ijipt, U Blei U ïaleh ïa ki thma na ka bynta jong ki. Kaba ki hap ban leh ka long tang ban ïeng bad sakhi ïa Ka jingwan jingïarap kaba khraw jong U.

Ka histori ka pyni ba man la ka por ba ki Israel ki don ka jingshaniah kaba skhem ha U Blei, kim donkam ban ïaleh (peit 2 Syiem 19, 2 Khronikl 32, Isaïah 37). Ha ka jingthmu ba bha tam jong U Blei, ki Israel kim donkam satia ban ïaleh na ka bynta lade. Ka la long ka jingmih na ka jingbymngeit jong ki, kaba la pynpaw hadien ka jingmih noh, ba U Blei u la shah ïa ki ban don bynta ha ka thma, ba long pyrshah ïa ki nong Kanaan. Ha kajuh ka rukom, kim donkam ban rah wat tang kawei ka waitlam pyrshah ïa ki nong Ijipt ha ka por jong ka jingmih jong kin a ijipt; kan ym shym la long satia kaba donkam ïa ki ban ïaleh ha kaba jop ïa ka Kanaan (Deuteronomi. 7:17-19).

`Lada ki khun Israel kim shym la khñium pyrshah ïa U Trai. Um shym la shah ïa ki nongshun jong ki ban ïaleh thma bad ki.”—Ka Ellen G. White, The Story of Redemption, sla 134. Kumno, Hato ki jingkhñium, ki lah ban ktah ïa ka jingim jong ngi mynta?`
