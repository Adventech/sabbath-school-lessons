---
title:  La pyndep Bha
date:   25/12/2025
---

`Pule ïa ki kyntien jingpynkut jong ka kitab jong u Joshua ba la thoh da u nongthoh ba la pyrsad mynsiem? (Joshua. 24:29–33). Kumno kine ki kyntien ym ba ki phai dien pat sha ka jingim u Joshua hynrei kumjuh ruh ka khmih sha ka lawei?`

Ha ka bakut jong ka kitab ka ïathuh shaphang ka jingïap jong u Joshua bad u Eleasar, u rangbah lyngdoh u wanrah ïa ka kitab u Joshua sha ka jingkut kaba pynsngewsih. Da kaba ïathuhkhana lang ïa ka jingtep ïa u Joshua, ïa ka jingtep ïa u Eleasar bad ïa ka jingtep ïa ki shyïeng jong u Joseph, u nongthoh u la pynlong ïa ka jingïapher hapdeng ka jingim shabar ka ri bad ka jingsdang jong ka jingim ha ka ri. Ym donkam shuh ban ïaid wir. Ki basah ba kynja pyrthei jong ki nongïalam kim donkam ban rah ryngkat bad ki. Ki kpa tymmen ki tep ïa kiba ha ïing ha sem jong ki ha ka krem (Jenesis 23:13, 19; Jenesis 25:9, 10), ha ka jaka ba la thied ha Shekhem (Jenesis 33:19). Mynta ka jaitbynriew ka tep ïa ki nongïalam jong ka ha ka jaka jong ka jingïoh-kynti jong ki hi, kumta ka don ka jingsngew ba ki long kiba la shong neh. Ki jingkular ba la ai ha ki kpa tymmen ki la pyndep. Ka jinglong bashisha jong U Yahweh la pynlong ïa ka jingïathuhkhana kaba pynïasoh ïa ki khun ki ksiew jong ki Israel bad ka mynta bad ka lawei jong ka.

Katba ki bynta kiba khatduh jong ka kitab ki pynïadei ïa ka jingïathuhkhana baroh kawei bad ka jingïathuhkhana kiba kham khraw ha ki por ba la leit noh, ki plie ïa ka lad ruh na ka bynta ka lawei. U Archbishop barim jong ka Canterbury Lord George Cary, ha ka jingkren ba kongsan ba la ai ha ka Balang Holy Trinity ha Shrewsbury, u la pynbna ba ka Balang Anglican ka la “sah sa shi pateng bynriew ban duh jait noh.”

Ha ka jingshisha, ka balang ka long barabor ka jngai shi pateng bynriew na ka jingduh jait, bad kumta ka la long bad ki briew jong U Blei ha ka Testament Barim. Ka lynnong kaba khraw ha ka histori jong ki Israel ka poi sha kaba kut. Ka lawei jong ka, ka shong ha kano ka jait jubab kan ai ïa ki jingkylli kiba bun kiba ïadei bad ka lawei. Hato ki Israel kin long kiba ïaineh ha U Trai? Hato kin lah ban bteng ïa ka kam kaba khlem pat lah dep ban bat ïa ka ri baroh kawei? Hato kin lah ban bat skhem ïa U Trai bad ban ym shah teh ha ka jingmane bleithaw? Ka pateng kaba hapoh u Joshua ka la long kaba ïaineh ha U Trai, hynrei hato ka pateng kaba hadien kan bat ïa kajuh ka jingpynïaid ba kynja mynsiem kaba la bud dien da u nongïalam bakhraw jong ka? Kawei pa kawei ka pateng kaba bud jong ki briew U Blei, kiba pule ïa ka kitab U Joshua, ka dei ban ïakynduh ïa kine ki juh ki jingkylli. Ka jingjop jong ki ka shong ha ka jinglong jong ki jubab kiba ki ai ha ka jingim jong ki kaba man ka sngi bad kumno ki ïadei bad ki jingshisha kiba ki la ïoh pateng.

`U Joshua, kum u Paul, “u la ïaleh ïa ka jingïaleh kaba bha” (2 Tim. 4:7, NKJV). Kaei ka kyndon jong ka jingjop jong u Joshua? Kiei ki rai kiba phi donkam ban shim mynta ka sngi khnang ban pyndep da kajuh ka jingpynthikna jong ka jingpynim?`
