### Ïathuh ïa Ngi Shaphang Ka Baibl

_Da: Andrew McChesney_

Kawei ka kynthei kam kwah jingïarap ei ei haba u Kim Sun u la poi ha ïing jong ka ha ka por ba la buh beit.

"Balei?" la kylli u Sun, u nong South Korea uba shakri kum u mishoneri ha ka shnong ba kyndong jong ka ha Philippines. “Nga la leh ei ei kaba bakla?”

“Em, em,” la jubab kata ka kynthei. “Nga kwah ba phin shongthait noh. Phin sa ïoh shuh shuh ki briew ban ïarap kylleng ka dong mynta ka janmiet. Hangne, bam katto katne ki jingbam bad pynjahthait."

U Sun u la shah ktah ha ka jingsngewkhia jong ka kynthei. U la ïarap ïa ki briew khlem da siew ei ei naduh ba u la poi ha katei ka shnong lai bnai mynshuwa. Ha ka jaka ban ïathuh ha ki briew ba u long u mishoneri, u Sun u la wad ban long u paralok jong ki bad ban pyni ïa ka jingieit U Blei. Lai bnai kynthih, ki nongshong shnong ki la pdiang ïa ka jingïarap jong u khlem da pyrkhat ei ei shaphang jong u. Hynrei mynta, kane ka kynthei ka la pynpaw ïa ka jingsumar ïa ka jingbha jingmiat jong u. U la sngewthuh ba u la ïoh ïa u paralok bashisha uba nyngkong eh.

Hynrei kam shym la long kaba khatduh. Ha kajuh ka por, bun ki nongshong shnong ki la sdang ban niew ïa u Sun kum u paralok jong ki, bad ki la kyrpad ïa u da ki jingkylli.

“Balei phi ïarap ïa ngi khlem jingsiew?” la ong uwei.

"Phi dei naei?" la ong uwei pat.

U Sun u la jubab ba u wan na ka 1000 Missionary Movement.

“Nga dei u mishoneri,” u la ong. “Nga kwah ba phin ithuh ïa U Jisu Khrist, kumta nga la shakri ïa phi.”

Ki nongshong shnong ki la lyngngoh ban ïohsngew ba u Sun u dei u mishoneri bad ki la ong, “Lada phi dei u mishoneri, te balei phim ïathuh ïa ngi shaphang ka Baibl?”

“Phi kwah ban pule ïa ka Baibl?” U Sun u la ong.

“Hooid! Hooid!” ki la jubab.

Ym slem, ym don ba kwah shuh ïa ka jingïarap jong u Sun. Baroh ki la pule ïa ka Baibl bad ka Sngi. Khyndiat ngut ki briew kim shym la pan jinghikai Baibl, hynrei ki la mynjur haba ka Sun ka la khot ïa ki. Hadien lai bnai ka jingtrei laitluid, kumno kin kyntait?

U Sun u la kynmaw ïa ka jingbakla kaba u la leh bad ki kmie ki kpa jong u. Kum u Adventist bathymmai, u la wad ban pynsngewthuh ïa la ki kmie ki kpa ban pdiang ïa ki jingngeit jong u haduh san snem bad u la rem shisha. Mynta, u la pyni ïa kawei pa kawei ka jingshisha jong ka Baibl kum ka jingpynpaw ïa ka jingieit U Blei.

U la ïathuh ïa ka jingïathuhkhana jong ka Jenesis shaphang ka jingpynlong. “Kane ka long ka jingieit U Blei ïa phi,” u la ong. U la pule ïa ki pharshi shaphang u nongsamarïa babha bad u khun sarong awrïa. “Kane ka long ka jingieit U Blei ïa phi,” u la ong. U la kren shaphang U Jisu uba la ïap halor ka diengphna. “Kane ka long ka jingieit U Blei ïa phi,” u la ong.

U Kim Sun u dei u nongïarap jong ka seng 1000 Missionary Movement, kaba ïa ka jaka treikam jong ka ha Silang, Philippines, la tei da ka jingïarap jong ka jingkynshew khatlai Sabbaton jong u snem 1996. Pule shuh shuh ha ka taïew ban wan.
