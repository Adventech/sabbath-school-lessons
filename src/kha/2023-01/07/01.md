### Dkhot Baibyl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Lukas 4:16–19; Isa. 62:1, 2; Deut. 15:11; Math. 19:16–22; Lukas 19:1–10; Job 29:12–16.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet:</p>
> “’ ‘ Ynda kumta U syiem un ong ha kito ha ka kamon jong U, “To wan ïa kiba la kyrkhu jong U Kpa jong nga, to ïoh ïa ka hima ïa kaba la pynkhreh keiñ ïa phi naduh myndanglong ka pyrthei” ‘ “ (Math. 25:40).

Ka Baibyl bunsien ka ju kren shaphang ki nongwei(teng teng ngi khot ki sohshnong), ki bakhlem-kpa, bad ki riewkynthei. Dei kum kine keiñ kiba u Jisu u kdew kum “ ‘ “uba rit tam na kine ki para jong Nga” ‘ “ (Math. 25:40).

Kumno keiñ ngin ithuh ïa kine ki briew mynta? Ki nongwei jong ka Baibyl kidei kito ki briew kiba la iehnoh ia ka shong jong ki, lehse na ka daw ka thma ka jingïapthem. Ha kine ki sngi mynta ngi lah ban khot ba ki dei ki nongphetwir na ki daw bym lah lait bym dei ba ki da jied hi ban phet.

Ki bakhlem-kpa ki dei ki khun kiba la duh noh ïa ki kpa ha ka thma, ka jingjia ryngkhat, ne jingpang. Ïa kane ka kynhun ruh lah ban kynthup lang ïa kito kiba ki kpa kiba don ha phatok lane kibym donlang hi re. Katno ka long ka jaka treikam baïar bha ba la pynpyiar hangne.

Ki riewkynthei ki dei kito kiba long na kajuh ka daw kum ki bakhlem-kpa, kiba la duh noh ïa ki kurim jong ki. Kiba bun hi ki dei ka longïing tang ka kmie marwei bad kiba lah ban pyndonkam ïa ka jingïarap ba ai ka balang.

Katba ngin ïapeit ïa kane mynta ka taïew, namar ba ngi long ki nongpynïaid ïa ka kam U Blei, kaba ïarap kam dei ka jingjied. Ka dei kaba bud ïa ka nuksa bad kaba kohnguh ïa ki hukum jong U. 

_* Pynklhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, Rymphang 18_
