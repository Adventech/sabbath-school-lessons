Pule Ellen G. White, “‘It Is Finished,’” pp. 758–764; “‘The Lord Is Risen,’” pp. 779–787; and “‘Why Weepest Thou?’” pp. 788–794, in The Desire of Ages. See also, Clifford Goldstein, Risen: Finding Hope in the Empty Tomb (Nampa, ID: Pacific Press, 2020).

“U Pilat u la thrang ban pyllait ȉa U Jisu. Hynrei u la ȉohi shai ba ym lah ban leh ȉa kane. Bad ban dang ȉoh bat ȉa ka kyrdan jong ka burom jong u. ban ȉa ka ba duh noh ȉa ka bor ba kynja pyrthei, u la jied ban kñia ȉa ka jingim kaba lui lui. Katno ngut mynta. Tang ban lait na ka jingduhnong lane na ka jingshitom saja, ha ka juh ka por ki pynkheiñ noh ia ki ia ki aiñ tynrai babha. Ka jingȉatiplem bad ka kam ram ki kdew sha kawei pat ka lynti. bad ka jingpynbiang ȉa lade ka kdew sha ka wai pat. Ka lat lat ka tuid ka thom ha ka liang kaba bakla, bad ȉa uta uba pynȉadei bad ka liang bad ȉa uta uba pynȉadei bad ka jingsniew la rong noh sha ka jingdum tliew tliew, jong ka jinglong bala rem ha ka pop”—The Desire of Ages, p. 738.

“U Khrist um shem la aiti noh ȉa ka jingim jong U haduh ba u la pyndep ȉa ka kam na ka bynta kaba U la wan ban leh, bad ha ka jingdkut mynsiem artat, U la pyrtajam, “la pyndet” Ioannis19:30 ȉa ka thma la jopthiaw. Ka kti ka mon ka jong U bad ka kti kaba khuid jong U ka la pynȉoh ha u ȉa ka jingjopthiaw. Kum u nongjop u la pynȉeng ȉa ka lama ha ki jaka ba jrong bad ba bymjukut. Hato ym shem la don ka jingkmen ha pdeng ki Angel? Baroh ka bneng ka la shad kmen ha ka jingjopthiaw jong U Nongpynim. Ia u Soitan la jop, bad u la tip ba u la duhnoh ȉa ka hima jong u”.

Ha ki angel bad ha ki pyrthei ki bym shem la hap sha ka pop ka jingpyrta, “La pyndep”. Ka la don ka jingmut ka ba jylliew, ka la dei na ka bynta jong ki ruh kum juh na ka bynta jong ngi ba ȉa ka kam bakhraw jong ka jingsiewspah la pyndep. Ma ki lem bad ngi, ngi ȉa sam lang ȉa u soh jong ka jingjopthiaw jong U Khrist”. —The Desire of Ages, p. 758.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ia Puson**:

`Kiei ki rukom shim rai kiba lah ban ȉarap ȉa phi ban kiar na kaba leh ȉa ka jait jingbakla kaba u Pilat u la leh?`

`Balei ba U Jisu u hap ban ȉap ha ka jaka jong ngi? Balei ba U la dei ban long u Nongmih bujli jong ngi? Balei ka jingȉap jong U ka long kaba donkam lada ngi kwah ban ȉoh ȉa ka jingpynim? Kiei ki bynta jong ka Jingthoh Bakhuid kiba kyrshan ȉa ka jubab jong phi?`

`Kaei ka jingȉadei hapdeng ki sakhi jong ka Jingthoh Bakhuid bad ki sakhi jong ka histori haba ȉadei bad ka jingngeit ha ka jingmihpat U Jisu? Kata, kaei ka jingpynshisha ha histori kaba pynskhem da kaba khlaiñ ȉa ka jingmihpat U Jisu?`

`Pyrkhat shaphang 1 Korinth 15: 12-20. umno u briew un pynsngewthuh ȉa ka jingmut ba, khlem ka jingmihpat U Khrist, “kita kiba la ȉohthiah ha U Khrist ki la jot noh” (1 Kor. 15:18, NKJV), lada kito kiba “la iohthiah ha U Khrist” ki leit noh shisyndon sha bneng? Kumno ki ktien jong u Paul hangne ki pynskhem ȉa ka jingshisha ba kiba ȉap ki ȉohthiah haduh ka jingmihpat ha ka jingwan pat U Khrist?`
