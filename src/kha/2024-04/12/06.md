---
title:  U Jisu Bad Ka Mary
date:   19/12/2024
---

`Pule Ioannis 20:11-13. Kaei kaba la jia hangne kaba pyni balei ba ka Maria Magdalena kam pat sngewthuh ȉa ka jingmut jong ka jingtep kaba thylli?`

Ka jingkdew kaba khadduh shaphang ka Mari ha ka jingthoh shuwa kane kawei ka long ka jingȉathuh jong ka ha u Petros bad u Ioannis shaphang ka jingtep kaba thylli (Ioannis 20:2). Ki la mareh sha kata ka jingtep, bad ka la wan phai hangta hadien khyndiat por. Hadien ba u Peter bad u Ioannis ki la leit jurip ȉa ka jingtep, ki la mih noh na ka. Hynrei ka Mari ka la wan phai bad, da kaba ȉam, ka la sahkut hangta. Khlem artatien ka la leh ȉa ka jingiam kaba khraw ha kine ki khyndiat sngi ba la leit noh. Bad mynta— kane ruh kumjuh? Da kaba sngap jar, ka la peit shapoh.

Ha ka jinglyngngoh jong ka, arngut ki angel kiba phong jaiñ lieh ki don ha ka jingtep, ki shong ha ka jaka ba la buh ia ka met U Jisu. Ki la kylli ia ka, “‘Ko briew, balei pha ȉam?’” (Ioannis 20:13, NKJV). Ka jubab kaba sngewsih jong ka ka long ba ki la shim noh ia U Trai jong ka, bad kam tip shano ki la buh ȉa U.

`Pule Ioannis 20:14-18. Kaei kaba la pynkylla lut ȉa kiei kiei baroh na ka bynta ka Maria?`

Lyngba ki khmat kiba laang ummat, ka Mary ka la phai bad ka la ȉohi ȉa uwei uba ȉeng shadien jong ka. Ha ki kyntien kiba ȉadei dur bad ki angel, u Nongwei u kylli, “‘‘Ko briew, balei pha ȉam? Iano pha wad?’” (Ioannis 20:15, NKJV). Ka pyrkhat ba ka ȉakren bad uta u nongsumar kper, bad ka pan jingȉarap na u ban lap ȉa ka met U Jisu.

U Nongwei u ong tang kawei ka ktien, “Ka Maria.” Ka la long ka jingpynpaw kaba tang kawei ka ktien kaba la pynkylla ia ka pyrthei. Kynsan kynsan, ka Mari kaba la lyngngoh ka ithuh ba U Jisu uba la mihpat u kren bad ka bad ka ithuh ȉa U. U Jisu u pynskhem ba kan ym bat ȉa U, namar ba U dei ban kiew sha U Kpa jong U. Hynrei ka kam jong ka ka long ban leit iathuh ha ki synran ba U dang kiew “‘sha U Kpa jong Nga bad U Kpa jong phi, bad sha U Blei jong Nga bad U Blei jong phi’” (Ioannis 20:17, NKJV). Ka Maria ka la pyndep ȉa ka kamram jong ka. Ka la ȉathuh ha ki synran ba ka la ȉohi ȉa U Trai bad ka la ȉathuh ruh ȉa kiwei pat ki jingtip baroh kiba U la ȉasam bad ka (Ioannis 20:18).

`Pule 1 Korinth 15:12-20. Katkum u Paul, ka jingngeit Khristan jong ngi kan myntoi aiu lada U Khrist um shym la mihpat na kiba ȉap?`
