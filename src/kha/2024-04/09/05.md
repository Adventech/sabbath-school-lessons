---
title:  Ka Jingkyntait Ȉa Ka Thymmei Jong Ka Jingim
date:   27/11/2024
---

Katto katne ki jingjia kiba sngewsih kiba la buh jingkynmaw ha baroh ka Jingthoh kaba la paw ha ka Gospel jong u Ioannis. “Bad kata ka jingshai ka shai ha kaba dum; hynrei kata kaba dum kam pdiang ȉa ka … U la long ha ka pyrthei, bad ka pyrthei ka la long da u, pynban ka pyrthei kam shym la ithuh ȉa u. 11 U la wan sha kiei kiei la ki jong, bad kita kiba dei la ki jong kim shym la pdiang ȉa u.” (Ioannis 1:5, 10, 11, NKJV). Ȉa U “NGA LONG” la kyntait da kiba bun ki briew jong U hi.

Khlem sngew phylla ba u Paul u la maham “Namar kata wat bred noh phi ȉa kata ka jingshlur jong phi,” (Heb. 10:35, NKJV). Ȉa kaba ngi la ȉohi na ka por sha ka por, ȉa U Khrist la kyntait namar ba ki briew kim pdiang ȉa ki ktien jong U.

Ka seng pyrkhat pyrdaiñ kat kum ka to kato kapor sha phang ka long briew man briew ka sdang da ka jingartatien. Ki briew kim shaniah ȉa kaei kaei kat ban pynthikna kaei ka jingshisha. Tang kata kaba neh hadien ka jingtynjuhȉit la pdiang ȉa ka kum ka jingtip kaba lah ban shaniah, kaei kaei kaba uno uno u lah ban pynshong nongrim ȉa la ka jingngeit. Don ruh katto katne kiba pyndon kam da ka juh ka rukom ha ka jingȉadei bad ka Baibl, da kaba buh ȉa kiei kiei baroh ha ka jingartatien na ka liang ka science, history, ka jingsngew, ka sengpyrkhat, na ki sabut ba la lum na mynbarim, bad na ka mariang khnang ba ki lah ban pynshongnia kaei ka jingshisha na ka Baibl. Kae ka rukom wat bniah ka sdang da ka jingpynshong nongrim ha ka jingartatien lyngba kiba bun ki thymmei jingthoh. U Khrist ula kylli, “Hynrei ynda wan U Khun u briew, un shem ne em ȉa ka jingngeit ha ka pyrthei?” (Luke 18:8).”—E. Edward Zinke and Roland Hegstad, The Certainty of the Second Coming (Hagerstown, MD: Review and Herald Publishing Association, 2000), p. 96.

`Pule Jingkheiñ 13:23–33.kaei ka ba pynlong ȉa kine ki jingȉapher ha pdeng kine ki ar tylli ki jingȉathuh jong ki nongsyntiat kiba la wanrah sha phang ka ri Canaan?`

Ka pop jong ki Hebru mynba ki la don ha Kadesh Barnea ka long ban artatien ȉa ka Ktien jong U Blei. U Blei u la ong ȉa kiba kin leit ban shim ȉa ka ta ka ri. Ki khat ar ngut ki nongsyntiat ki la leit sha Canaan ban syntiat ȉa ka ta ka ri. Ki la wan phai bad ki artylli ki jingiathuh. Ki ba bun ki ai ȉa ka jingiathuh kaba pynduh mynsiem. Ki don ki ratbah ha ka ri, ki nongbah ba la ker kynroh, ki tiar ȉaleh kiba ngim ju ȉohi mynshuwa, bad ki shipai kiba la ai jinghikai bha. Kaba ȉapher ka long, ba ngi la long ki mraw ha ka ri Egypt bad khyndiat ki shipai ki bym pat ju ialeh thma. Ki shiphew ngut ki nongsyntiat ki la ong em, ki la pynshong nongrim halor ki sakhi ba la pynpsaphriang da kiei kiei kiba kham khia jong ki jingsngewthuh briew. Ki ar ngut ki nongsyntiat ki la ong hooid, da kaba ki pynshong nongrim ha ka jingngeit jong ki ba la pynpsaphriang da kiei kiei kiba kham khia da ka bor jong ka Ktien jong U Blei.

`Kumno ngin kiar na kaba pynlong ka juh ka jait jingbakla kaba ngi pynlong hangne? Bad kymta, kumno ngin kiar na kaba ngi shu antad, da kaba jingleh bieij hynrei da kaba ngeit ba ngi leh ia ka mon ka jong U Blei bad, namar kata ngim lah ban bakla?`
