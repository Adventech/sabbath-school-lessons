---
title:  Ka Jingshai Bad Ka Jingdum
date:   24/12/2024
---

`Pule Ioannis 1:4-10; Ioannis 3:19-21; Ioannis 5:35; Ioannis 8:12; Ioannis 9:5; Ioannis 11:9, 10; bad Ioannis 12:35. Kaei ka jingȉapher kaba khraw kaba don hangne, bad balei ba kane ka jingȉapher ka long kaba kongsan bha ban sngewthuh ȉa ka jingshisha?`

Ka pyrthei ka don ha ka jingdum; ka ȉaidlait na ka jingshai bad kam lah, da lade hi, ban shem ia ka lynti sha U Blei bashisha, U Blei shimet jong ka Jingthaw, jingpynpaw bad Jingpynim.

“Ka longbriew manbriew, hi, kam lah satia ban poi sha ka jingtip ȉa ka jinglong blei. ‘Ka long kaba jrong kum ka bneng; kaei kaba lah ban leh? kham jylliew ban ȉa ka dujok; kaei kaba me lah ban tip?’ Job 11:8. Tang ka mynsiem jong ka jingpynlong khun kaba lah ban pynpaw ha ngi ȉa kiei kiei kiba jylliew jong U Blei, kiba ‘ka khmat kam shym la ȉohi, lymne ka shkor kam shym la rung ha ka dohnud u briew.’ ‘U Blei u la pynpaw ȉa ki ha ngi da U Mynsiem jong U.’ Ka Ellen G. White, Ka jingthrang jong ki rta, p. 412.

Tang U Jisu Khrist “Uba la phla ȉa U,” U Kpa (loan. 1:18, NKJV). Ka verb Grik ka long exēgeomai, kaba mut “ban batai,” “pynshai” ne “pynpaw.” U Ioannis u pyni ȉa U Jisu kum U Nonglam khubor uba na bneng, Uta Uba batai ȉa kaei ka jingmut ban tip ȉa U Blei. Tang lyngba U Jisu ngi lah ban ithuh shisha ȉa U Blei

`Pule Ioannis 8:42-44. Kumno u Jisu u batai ȉa ka nongrim kaba bakla ha kaba ki nongȉalam niam Israel ki la pynshong nongrim ȉa ka jingngeit jong ki?`

Kito ki bym ȉeng ha ka jingshisha ki kren na la ka jong ka bor. Ki “ȉohi” ȉa ka jingmut jong ka jingthoh tang na ka jingpeit jong u briew. Ha ka jingïapher, ngi dei ban pdiang ba U Khrist U long ka jingshai jong ka pyrthei bad bud ïa U ha ka jingbatai jong ngi ïa ka Ktien jong U. Ha ka jingïapher, u ksuid u kren na “la ka jong ka bor” (Ioannis 8:44, NKJV). Lada ngim husiar bad ngim aiti ha ka jingngeit bad jingkohnguh ȉa U Blei, ngi don ha ka jingma ban leh ȉa kajuh ka kam: ban pule ȉa ka jingthoh kaba pynshong nongrim tang ha la ki jong ki jingkwah, jingkwah bad ki jingpeit jong ngi, kaba kham suk shibun ban leh ban ȉa kaba ngi lah ban leh da pyrkhat.

`Kumno phi jubab ȉa ki jingshisha kiba “ȉeng ha ki kjat jong phi,” pyrshah ȉa kumno phi dei ban jubab ȉa kita ki jingshisha?`
