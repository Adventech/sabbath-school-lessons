### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Ioannis 8:54–58, Jenesis. 12:3, Rom. 4:1–5, Ioannis 12:1–8, Ioannis 19:4–22, Ioannis 20:19–31, Danial 2, Danial 7.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “U Jisu u ȉathuh ha u, Naba me la ȉohi ȉa nga, ko Thomas, me la ngeit? Suk te kita kibym shym la ȉohi, bad kiba ngeit pynban.” Ioannis 20:29

Lyngba ka Gospel jong u baroh, u Ioannis u don ȉa bun jait ki briew – ha kiba bun jait ki tynrai jinglong, ki jingngeit, bad ki jingȉashem – baroh ki ȉa phla sha U Jisu udei uei. “Ha khmih u khunlangbrot jong U Blei” (Ioannis 1:36, NKJV). “Ngi la shem ȉa U Messiah” (Ioannis 1:41) “Ngi la shem ȉa u ba u Moses … u la thoh” (Ioannis 1:45.) “Ko Rabbi, me me long U Khun U Blei, me me long U Syiem jong U Israel!”” (Ioannis 1:49) “Une u long ne em uta U Khrist?” (Ioannis 4:29.) “Naba ngi la ȉohsngew ma ngi hi ȉa u, bad ngi ȉatip ba une u long shisha U Khrist U Nongpynim jong ka pyrthei.” (Ioannis 4:42.) “Ko Trai, dei hano pat ngin ȉaleit? me me don ki ktien ka jingim bymjukut,” (Ioannis 6:68.) “Nga la ngeit ba me me long U Khrist, U Khun U Blei, uban sa wan sha ka pyrthei.” (Ioannis 11:27.) “Ba ȉa kaba nga ju long uba matlah, mynta nga ȉoh imat.” (Ioannis 9:25.) “Ha khmih, U Syiem jong phi!” (Ioannis 19:14.) “Namar nga ngam shem ka jingpalat satia ha u.” (Ioannis 19: 6.) “U Trai jong nga, bad U Blei jong nga!” (Ioannis 20: 28.) .

Kiei ki katto katne kine ki briew, bad balei ki la ȉa leh bad ban phala ba ki ithuh ȉa U Jisu.

_* Pule ia kane lynnong, ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, August 16_
