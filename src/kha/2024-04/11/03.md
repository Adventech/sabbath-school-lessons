---
title:  U Jisu Bad U Kpa
date:   09/12/2024
---

Ia ngi la thaw da U Blei na ka bynta ka jingȉadei jong U bad ki (Gen. 1:26, 27). Pynban, namar jong ka pop, ka jingȉadei la pynȉa jngai bad pynpra. Ngi ȉohi ȉa kane ka jingktah jong kane ka jingpra jinngpait ha ka jingȉathuh khana ka Kper Eden.

`Read Jenesis 3:7–9. Kumno kane ka pynpaw ia ka jingpynȉakhlad kaba ka pop ka la pynlong, bad ka mut aȉu? ba U Blei uba wad ȉa ki, ym ma ki?`

Ka jingthmu jong U Blei ka long ban ai ȉa ka jingkhiah sha ki bynriew baroh ȉa kaba la pynȉakhlad kaba la pynlong da ka pop, wat la baroh ki bynriew kim pdiang ȉa kaei ba la tyrwa.

Ban pyndep ȉa ka jingpynbha pat ȉa kane ka jingȉadei, u wei U dkhot jong ka jinglong Blei un wan long briew. Kumta, U Ktien u la long doh bad u la shong ha pdeng jong ngi, U pynpaw ȉa ka burom jong U BLei (Ioannis 1:14–18). Kum ka jingmih, ba u bynriew u la ȉoh pdiang ȉa ka jingdap jong U bad ka jingaiei. Kane kadei kaba U Jisu u la wan ban ȉasam, ban pynpaw ȉa ka burom jong U Blei ba ka jingȉadei kaba la pynkheiñ da ka pop yn lah ban pynthymmai, la ka long tang ha kito baroh kiba mon ban pdiang da ka jingngeit ȉa kaei ba la tyrwa ha ki ha U Khrist Jisu.

`Kaei ka jingkyrmen ba phylla la ȉohi na ka bynta jong ngi ha kine ki dkhot? Ioannis 1:1, 2; Ioannis 5:16–18; Ioannis 6:69; Ioannis 10:10, 30; Ioannis 20:28.`

“Ha U Khrist don ka jingim, ka ba khlem pan kylliang, kaba khlem hiar pateng”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 530. Pynban, ka jinglong briew jong U Khun uba la “pynrit ȉa lade”” (Phil. 2:7, RSV) ha ka ban pyntreikam ia ka hok jong U, U Khrist, U kren ȉa ka jingdon jong U ha ka pyrthei kum u briew, kaba kdew sha ka jingdon ka jong U ȉa ka jingim kum ka jingai sngewbha na U Blei. “Ka jinglong Blei jong U Khrist ka dei ka jingpynskhem ȉa ka jingim bymjukut jong u ba ngeit” —The Desire of Ages, p. 530.

Ia U Blei ym shym la ithuh da u bynriew (Ioannis 17:25). Kumta, U la phah ȉa la U Khun ba marwei (Ioannis 9:4, Ioannis 16:5) ha kaba ȉa U Kpa yn ithuh.

`Ha kaba ȉadei bad kane ka jingthoh, ki bneng jong ki bneng , u bym ngeit blei u thoh, “ha ka jingdum jong ngi, ha baroh jong kane ka jingȉar, ym don jingai dak kaba lah ban wan ka jingȉarap ȉa ngi nangno nangno ban pynim ȉa ngi na ngi hi” kumno ka Baibl ka hikai ȉa ngi, kaba pyni ba une u briew u la bakla?`
