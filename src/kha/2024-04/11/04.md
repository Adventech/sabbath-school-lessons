---
title:  Ban Ithuh Ia U Khun Ka Long Ban Ithuh Ia U Kpa
date:   10/12/2024
---

Ha baroh ka Gospel jong u Ioannis, u apostol u batai kumno ba U Jisu, U Khun, U trei ȉa ki kam ki ba kdew sha U Kpa. U Jisu U Pynshai U Kpa u long uei bad pynpaw kaei ka jingȉadei jong U ka long sha ka pyrthei. Kine baroh ha ka jingkhmihthuh jong u Ioannis 1:18, ka ba ong ban pynlong ban ȉthuh ȉa U Kpa. (ha ka Greek exēgeomai: ban batai, ban pynshai, ban pynpaw). Na kapor sha kapor U Jisu U la leh ȉa kane. Ka kyntien Kpa (patēr) ka paw 136 sien ha u Ioannis bad 18 sien ha lynnong 1-3 Ioannis, ka kham palat ban ȉa ka shi bynta na ka lai bynta ȉa baroh ba la pyndon kam ha ka Testement Bathymmai. Ka jingkren ai khublei khatduh ka long ka ba bynta ka ba khraw ha ka Gospel ha kaba U Jisu u la pynithuh ȉa U Kpa.

U Jisu U long u nongmih bujli jong U kpa ha pyrthei, bad U la wan ban im, ha ka jinglong doh kum u briew, kaba long ka mon jong U kpa. Ha ka jingshisha, U Jisu U ong ba ha kiei kiei baroh u wad ban leh ȉa ka mon ka jong U Kpa, bad ym ka jong U hi (Ioannis 5:30). Ha kaba nyngkong ka ȉ kum ba ka jingkynthoh ka pynlyngngoh, hynrei ka pyni kumno U Jisu u la pyndem lut, kum u briew, sha U Kpa.

U Jisu u la ong ruh, ba ȉa U la phah da U Kpa ban pyndep ȉa ka kam jong U – ka kam ka jingpynim ȉa u bynriew – bad ka ta ka long ba U Kpa da lade hi u la long U sakhi sha ka kam ka jong U (Ioannis 5:36–38).

U Jisu u la pynbna ba U Kpa u la phah ȉa U ban shakri ba lyngba jong u hi marwei ha kaba ki bynriew ki lah ban wan sha U Kpa (Ioannis 6:40, 44). U Kpa u kwah ba ki briew kin ȉoh ȉa ka jingim bymjukut kaba shem ha U Jisu, uba la kular ban pynmihpat ȉa ki ha ka jingmihpat.

`Kaei ba kine ki dkhot ha rum ki lah ban hikai ȉa ngi sha phang ka jingȉadei ha pdeng U Jisu bad U Kpa? Ioannis 7:16; Ioannis 8:38; Ioannis 14:10, 23; Ioannis 15:1, 9, 10; Ioannis 16:27, 28; Ioannis 17:3.`

U Jisu u kam shaphang ka jingȉadei jong U sha U Kpa ka long kaba phylla. U pynbna ba baroh ki jinghikai jong U ki long ki jinghikai jong U Kpa; kita baroh kiba u la ong, kiba U Hi da lade U la ȉoh sngew na U Kpa; ba kiba ngeit ha U ka long kum juh kumba la ngeit ha U Kpa; ba kiba ki ktien jong U bad ka jingtrei jong U baroh ki long ki jong U Kpa; bad kiba U bad U Kpa ki ȉa tylli ha ka jingieit bad ka jingtrei na ka bynta ka jingpynim ȉa u bynriew. Ka la long ka jingphla ba donbor sha ka jingȉajan jong U Jisu sha U Kpa uba ha bneng!

`Kumno ka jingim jong phi kan kylla ha ka jingpyrkhat bad ha ki kam kin long ȉa ka ban pynpaw ȉa ka mon jong U Blei ha ka jingim jong phi? Kata ka long, kumno ngi lah ban im khambha ȉa kaei kaba ngi la tip na U Jisu ka ba long ka mon ka jong U Blei ha ka jingim jong ngi?`
