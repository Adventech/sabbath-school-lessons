---
title:  Ka Jingduwai Jong U Jisu
date:   12/12/2024
---

Ioannis 17 ka long ba teng teng la khot ka jingduwai jong u rangbah lyngdoh U Jisu, ka pynkut ȉa ka jingȉakren ai khublei. U Jisu U la wan sha kane ka pyrthei ba kumta ȉa u bynriew lah ban pynbha pat, ha kaba khatduh, sha ka jinglong ba janai ha ka jingȉadei bad U Blei. Da ka jingngeit bad ka jingshaniah U la Pyndep ȉa ki dak kiba U Blei u la ai ha U ban leh. Ha ki ktien bad ha ki kam, U la pynpaw ȉa U Blei sha ki briew.

U Jisu shen un sa iehnoh ȉa kane ka pyrthei. U la thrang ban ȉasam ȉa ka jingieit jong U sha ki synran jong U. U kwah ȉa ki ban sngewthuh ȉa ka jingȉadei ba jan ha pdeng jong U, U Kpa, bad U Mynsiem Bakhuid. Bad U kwah ban wanlam ȉa ki sha kata ka juh ka jingȉadei jong ki bad U Blei uba U Kpa bad U Mynsiem kaba U hi da ladei u la don.

`Pule Ioannis 17:1–26. Kiei ki kyntien lane ki jingong ha kane ka lynnong kiba batai ȉa ka jingthrang jong U Jisu na ka bynta ka jingȉadei ba jan jong ka jingieit ha pdeng jong U, U Kpa, bad ki synran jong U?`

Kiba bun ki pule ȉa u Ioannis 17 ki da mut ba tang ȉa kiei kiei kiba dei ia ka jingȉatylli bad ka jingieit. Khlem da jingkylli, ba ka jingthmu jong U Blei ban pynbha ȉa ngi bad ka jingȉadei jong ngi shimet bad U bad ki briew baroh. Hynrei katba dang pule bha ka ȉathuh ȉa kaba kham donkam ba palat ha ka jingȉadei ha pdeng ka jingieit bad ka jingshisha.

“Kane te ka long ka jingim bymjukut, ba kin ioh ithuh ia me U Blei uba shisha tang ma me hi,” (Ioannis 17:3, NKJV), ym dei U Blei, U ba ngi pyrkhat U dei. “Nga la pynpaw ia ka kyrteng jong me ha kita ki briew kiba me la ai ha nga … : bad ki tip shisha ba nga la mih noh na me,” (Ioannis 17:6, 8, NRSV). “To pynkyntang ia ki da ka jingshisha; ka ktien jong me ka long ka jingshisha.” (Ioannis 17:17, NKJV).

U Khrist u la wan ban pynpaw ia U Kpa. Kane ka jingpynpaw ka la long kaba don kam namar jong kiba bun ki jingsngewthuh bakla sha phang U Blei. ka Gospel jong U Ioannis ka pyni ba U Jisu u shim khia ȉa kane ka Mission. U la pyndonkam kaba dei biang ȉa ka Ktien jong U BLei bad ki jingtrei. Lada ka jingshisha kam long kaba donkam, balei ngi hap ban leit sha khmat?

U Jisu U la im ka jingim jong ki jingjynjar kiba khraw wat haduh ka jingshah kyntait da ka bor niam. U la shah ȉa ka jinglong ba ȉa pher na ki briew bad wat, ha kita ki por, na la ki jong ki synran jong U hi. U wei na ki synran u la die noh ȉa U, bad uwei u la len noh ȉa U lai sien. Bad U la ȉaid lyngba ki jingtynjuh kiba ȉai bteng bad U la ȉap halor ka diengphna ha ki kti jong jong kiba U la wan ȉa ka ban pynim.

`Kumno ngi lah ban pynshai kham bha ȉa ka jingieit jong U Blei, kum kata ka jingdon ha pdeng U Jisu bad U Kpa, ha ka jingim jong phi?`
