Pule Ellen G. White, “‘Let Not Your Heart Be Troubled,’” pp. 662–680, in The Desire of Ages; “Additional Note on [Ioannis] Chapter 1,” The SDA Bible Commentary, vol. 5, pp. 911–919..

Ha ka jingpynshong dor U Jisu u long uei, Te U nongbishar ba ȉaleh pyrshah ȉa U, u la thew da ka jingthew ba kynja briew “kum ka rukom ka doh” (Ioannis 8:15, NKJV). Kane lehse ka kham sniew ban ȉa ka jingbishar “da ka jingbishar tang na sla” (Ioannis 7:24, NIV). Hangne ki pynȉadei tang da ki kyndon jong ka doh, jong ka jinglong ba la hap jong u bynriew ha ka pyrthei kaba la hap, khlem ka jingsngew jong ka jingpyniaid jong U Mynsiem (Peit Ioannis 3:3–7). Ki la ȉohi ȉa U ha ka “doh”, kum ba ka long, hynrei khlem pyrkhat ha ka ba u lah ban dei U Ktien uba long doh (Ioannis 1:14). Ban khmih ȉa U Khrist da ka ba buh kyndon ban thew ȉa U da ka jingkhmih ba kynja pyrthei (2 Korinth. 5:16).

“Ia U Nongpyntngen la khot, ‘U Mynsiem jong ka jingshisha’ ka jingtrei jong u ka long ban batai kaei ka jingshisha bad ban kyrshan ȉa ka. Nyngkong eh U shong hapoh ka dohnud kum U Mynsiem ka jingshisha, bad kumne u kylla long U Nongpyntngen. Ka don ka jingtngen bad ka jingsuk ha ka jinghsihsha, hynrei yn ym shem ȉa ka jingsuk lane ka jingtngen kaba shisha ha ka jinglamler. Dei lyngba ka jinghikai ba bakla bad ȉa ka jinghikai pateng ba u Soitan u ȉoh ȉa ka bor jong u halor jong ka jingmut. Da kaba ȉalam ȉa ki briew sha ki kyrdan kiba lamler, u pynsniewdur ȉa ka jinglong. Lyngba ki jingthoh jong U Mynsiem Bakhuid U kren ha ka jingmut, bad u shon jur ȉa ka jingshisha ha ka dohnud. Kumne u thad rong ȉa ka jingbakla, bad u beh lane weng noh ȉa ka na ka mynsiem. Dei da U Mynsiem jong ka jingshisha, uba trei lyngba ka ktien ka jong U Blei, ba U Khrist u pyndem bad pynwan ȉa ki briew kiba la jied jong U sha lade hi”. — The Desire of Ages, p. 671. Khasi sla 903

**Ki Jingkylli ban ia Puson**:

`Ka jingim kaba bymjukut ka long ban tip ȉa U Blei (Ioannis 17:3). Ka mut aiu ban tip ne ithuh ȉa U Blei, ban ȉa pyrshah ȉa kaba shu tip katto katne ki jingshisha shaphang jong U, kata ka long, U long U ba donbor ne U ba ieit ne U Blei jong ka jingbishar hok? Lada don kiba kylli ȉa phi, “hato phi ithuh ȉa U Blei?” kumno phin ong? Kumno phin buh ȉa U Jisu ha ka jubab jong phi?`

`Ha ka jingpynmlien, ba man ka sngi, kaei kaba mut da ki ktien jong U Jisu “ki ktien ki long ka jingshisha” (Ioannis 17:17)?`

`Ka jingduwai U Jisu, “Ngam duwai ba men shim noh ȉa ki na kata ka pyrthei, hynrei ba men sumar ȉa ki na u bymman.” (Ioannis 17:15, NKJV). Kumno ki jingjied jong ngi ka ktah bad kumno kane ka jingduwai ka lah ban long ka jubab ha ka jingim jong ngi?`
