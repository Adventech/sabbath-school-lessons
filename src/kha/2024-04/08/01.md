### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Ioannis 5:17, 20, 36–40, 46, 47; Ioannis 13:18; Ioannis 17:12; Jer. 2:13; Zech. 9:9; Ioannis 8:12–30.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Hynrei ma nga, nga don ka jingphla kaba kham khraw la ka jong U Ioannis; namar kita ki jingleh ia kiba U Kpa u la aiti ha nga ba ngan ioh pyndep ia ki, kita kijuh hi ki jingleh kiba nga leh ki iaphla shaphang jong nga, ba uta U Kpa u la phah ia nga.” (Ioannis 5:36, NKJV).

Ngi lah ban ȉohi bunsien bunsien ha ka kitab jong u Ioannis bad ia kiei kiei baroh kiba U Jisu u ong bad u leh ha kaba pynpaw ia kata, hooid, Messiah (hammashiach), U Khrist, u la wan sha Israel. Bad u la wan, ha ka jingshisha, kum uwei na ki, kum u Jiw ba la kha ha Betlehem, kumba ka jingthoh ka la iathuhlupa.

Pynban, kumba u Ioannis u la thoh, “U la long ha ka pyrthei, bad ka pyrthei ka la long da u, pynban ka pyrthei kam shym la ithuh ia u”. (Ioannis1:10, NKJV).

U la long ha ka pyrthei, bad ka pyrthei ka la long da u, pynban ka pyrthei kam shym la ithuh ia u? kata ka long ka jingthoh kaba la pynlyngngoh. Bad, ngi lah ban iohi ha u Ioannis bad ha kiwei pat ki Gospel, kiba bun ki briew kim shem la ithuh ȉa U wat la ki la dei ban tip ia U. Kyrpang eh namar ia kiei kiei baroh kiba U la leh bad ong. Bad, kham bun ruh. Namar ki jingthoh ka Testement Barim ki kdew sha U.

Ha kane ka Taiew ngin ia khmih ha kiba bun ki lad lynti kiba u Ioannis u pynpaw Ia U Jisu kum U Messiah, bad kunjuh ruh, bad kum juh ruh, ngin ȉa peit ba balei ki katto katne ki briew ki iai bteng ban kyntait ia U-- nalor kata ki daw kiba donbor kiba pynskhem ȉa U ba U long U Khrist.

Kaei ba ngin nang na kane ka jingbakla?

_*Pule ia kane ka lynnong ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, November 23._
