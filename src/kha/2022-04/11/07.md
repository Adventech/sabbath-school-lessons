Pule Ellen G. White, “Dealing With False Science, Cults, Isms, and Secret Societies,” pp. 602–609, in Evangelism; and “Spiritism,” pp. 86–93, in Confrontation.

La mih ka jingstad briew kaba ka thaw ia ka bor stad kaban iarap ia ngi ban iakren bad kiba la iap “lyngba ki text, ki phone bad ki phone videos ba bun briew.”  Ban khot ia uba la iap PMP (Postmaterial persons), kane ka website ka hikai ba haba u briew u la iap u shu hap ban iaid lyngba ka haw haw sha ka bymjukut hynrei ki dang bat ia kata ka jingtip, ia ka dur bad ia ki jingthmu kiba ki don ha ka met kaba dang im.  Hynrei kaba kham kongsan, ka long ba ka phone ki mynsiem ka ong ba ka iaid lyngba ki lai tylli ki bynta, ka jingstad kaban shah ia ka jingiakren hapdeng u briew uba dang im bad uba la iap.

Ka bynta kaba nyngkong, ka long ban ia tek bad ban type bad ki bahaing hasem, bad ki paralok  ruh da kito kiba tip bad ba nang ia kane.  Ka bynta kaba ar ka long ban pynlah ia ka jingiakren hapdeng uba im bad kiba ieit ba thoin kiba laiap hynrei kiba im bymjukut ha kawei pat ka pyrthei.  Ka bynta kaba lai ka ong ba kan plie ia la lad ban iohsngew lane ban iohi ia kiei kiba kito ki briew kiba iashem.

Khamtam kaba sngewtieng ka long kumno ki test la ka kito kiba la iap kiba ia kren ki dei shisha ne em.  Kum ka nuksa, kane ka sites ka ong “ki kpa ki kmie kiba la iam kthang kin kylli ia kine ki jingkylli ia u khun ne ka khun kiba la kylla ia ka pyrthei jong ki:  phi la don ksew uba kyrteng u snoopy haba phi dang long khynnah?  Ngi la ai ne em ia phi ia ka tari ba rit ha ka sngi kha kaba shiphew jong phi?  Oh sngewtynnad ban pule ia kane ka jingmaham na ka shakri jong U Blei:  “ ki mynsiem ksuid ki paw teng teng ha u briew ha ka dur jong kiba la iap jong ki bahaing ne ki paralok kiba iathuh ia ki jingjia kiba ki la leh ha ka por ba ki dang im”  Ellen G. White, Patriarch and Prophets, p. 684.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ia Puson**

`Da kaba pyndonkam ia ka daw jong ka dustur imlang sahlang jong ngi, bun ki khristan ki pdiang lut ia kiei kiei baroh kiba ki media ki la tyrwa.  Kano na ki jinghikai jong ka Baibl kaban ialam ia ka jingiadei jong ngi bad kita ki media, khamtam haba ki kren ia kiei kiei kiba pyrshah ia ka ktien kiba shukor bad kiba bakla?`

`Kumno ngin iarap ia kiwei ban jop ia u soitan bad ki jingshukor jong u ha ki sngi kiba kut khlem da shah khring ia lade ha ki bor pynshoi jong kito ki juh ki jingshukor?`

`Bun ki khristan ki la iohi ia ki jingiathuhkhanna shaphang u Samuel uba la shah khot na ka jingiap kum ka nuksa jingkyrshan jong ka Baibl ba kiba iap ki dang im.  Kaei ba kane ka jingiathuhkhanna ka hikai ia ngi shaphang ba balei ba ngim lah ban shaniah tang ha kawei ka dkhot ne ka jingiathuhkhanna ban seng ia ka jinghikai Baibl jong ngi, hynrei ha ka jaka kata, ngi dei ban khmih ia baroh kiba ka Baibl ka kren shaphang kata ka subjek?`
