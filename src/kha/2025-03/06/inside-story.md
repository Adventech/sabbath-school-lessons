### Ka Mission Ba Kyrpang: Ka Hospital Kum Ka Lyngkha Trei Kam

U Dimitry Bagal, u long u missionary uba shong ba sah ha ri Germany, u la shemia ka

lyngkha mission kaba khlem poi pyrkhatkatba u dang shah sumar ialade naka jingpang cancer (bam-pong) hynrei ki la lap pynban ia lai tylli ki benign tumour. U la ïohi bad kynduh ïa shibun ki briewna ki bynta bapher bapher jong ka imlang sahlang –ki riewpyrthei, katto katne ki briew kiba wan na ki jakahakaba kajingngeit Khristan kam long shuh ka jaid jingngeit kaba ha khmateh, ki don sa katto katne na kipat kiba wan na kiwei kijaidjingngeit jong kane ka pyrthei–kine kiba u la kynduhbaroh ki donshibun ki jingkylli shaphang U Blei.

Uwei na kinongpang ula ïoh hynriew sien ka Chemotherapy bad hadien kata ula leit shah sumar ha ka Hospital kaba don ha nongbah Munich.Hynrei, haba ka cancer ka la khiebiang hadien hynriew bnai, u doktoru khlem sngew myntoi shuh ban sumar biang sa shisien. Uta u briew um don shuh ka jingmut kaba shaibad umlah shuh hi ruh ban ithuh ïa la ka tngajong u. Kata ka tngs jong uta u nongpang ka lathrangeh bandon hajan jong u ha hospital(Palliative Care Unit) lyngba ki sngi kiba khatduh jong u.Hapdeng kata ka jingiashem, u Dimitry u la ai ha ka ïa kawei ka kot lyngkdopkaba kren shaohang U Blei bad ka jingpang, nangta pat U Dimitry u la dwai bad kita ki shikurim. Kane ka jingdwai ka laktah jur ia ka kurim uta u briew, ba ka la pynlong ruh ïa ka ba kan jaw ummat bad ïam.

Kham hadien ha kata kasngi hi, u Dimitry u la ai ïakata ka briew artylliki kot, “Ka Jingïaleh Bakhraw” bad “Ka Jingthrang Ki Arta”, u la ong ha ka, ba kitaki kot kila pyntngen shibun ïa u bad kiba bun kiba la pule ia kiki la ïoh jingmyntoi shibun. Kata ka briew ka la kwahbhaïa kita ki kot -hynrei ymban shu shim ei. Ka la ai ha u 14 Euro(Pisa jinka European Union kabaïa rynkat15USDollar), ka la ai ruh da kipisa khutia suda.U Dimitry u sngewskhembha bad ba kin sa ïashem biang, lada ym dei ha kane ka pyrthei, hynrei ha ka pyrthei kabathymmai.

U Dimitry u la kynduh ruh ïa uwei u samla uba la thiah hospitsl na ka daw ka jingpang ka bymlah ban shem.Une u briew, uba na ri Iran, na ka daw jong kane ka jingpang u la peppule noh ha ka jaka pule kaba ha ri Britain, bad u lah poipat sha Germanyban ïoh jingsumarba biang. Hynrei, ki doktor,hadien ba la leh lut ki test, kimlah shem satia ïa kata ka jingpang.Namar une u samla umlahshuhban ïaid, u Dimitry u la pynshong ïa u ha ka wheel chair bad u la ïalamïa u kylleng ka hospital la jan man la ka sngi.Watla une u samla um shem la long u Khristan, pynban u la shah ba u Dimitry un dwai ha U Jisu na ka bynta jong u.Te, katba u Dimitry u la dwai ïa u man la ka sngi u la sdang koit pynban.Sa tang kattokatne por u la sdang ban ïeng bad ïaid malu mala lyngba ka jingkyrshan-ïarap.U Dimitry bad uta u samla ki ju ïakren ha ka ktien German, hynrei, namar ba u la pule ha ri Britain, u Dimitry u la ai ki kot ha ka ktien Phareng,kaba kyrteng“The Great Controversy”.Ïa katto kattne bnai,u Dimitry u dang ïai dwaisah na ka bynta uta u briew, bad u la kynmaw ia ki kyntien kiba ong, “Hynrei tokynmaw, naba phim tip lemne phim lah thew,ia ka jingmihkaba mihlyngba ka jingpyrshang da ka jingngeit” ((Ellen G. White, Cilpiteur Ministry, Sla, 114).

Ha kita ki artaiew eiei u Dimitry u la aipalat 20 tylli ki kot “The Great Controversy” (‘Ka Jingialeh Bakhraw”), badkiwei kiwei ki kotnalor kata. U la ai ruh kibusiness card kiba kynthupïa ki QR Code jong kane ka kot, khnang ki briew kiba pule kinlah ban downloadlymgba kiphome bad computer.U la buh ruh kumba10tylli ha ka jaka buh kotruhjong kata ka hospital, khnang ki briew kin ïoh pule.

“Shibun ki symbai la dep ban bet bad nga ngeitbad dwaikin sa speh bad mih soh”,ong u Dimitry.Shuh shuh u bteng da kaba ong, “Ngi trei lyngbaka jimgïalam U Blei, ngin buh haU hi ba Un khmih halorkajingmihjongki soh babha”.

Kane ka jingïathuhkhana ka ïathuh ïa ngi shaphang kathup kaba ar jong kathong missionka Balang Seventh-day Adventist, “Nga Ngan Leit”.Kane ka ïadei bad “Ka Jingpynkhlaiñbad ka jingpynroiïa ki kam kner sha kiwei…hapdengki jaidbynriew kiba ngim patju kynjoh, ryngkat ruh sha ki jaid niambym dei Khristan.
