---
title:  Ka Jingpynthymmai Ka Ïaibteng
date:  20/06/2023
---

U Blei u la sei ki briew ha ki sngi ba khadduh ban ieng ha ki tyrpeng jong ki nongpynthymmainiam jong ki por ba la leit bad ka Baibl kum ka jingphla jingngeit kaba tang maka hi, U Khrist

bad tang ma u hi kum u tynrai ka jingpynim, U Mynsiem Bakhuid uba tang ma u hi ka tynraijong ka bor, bad ka jingwan phai U Trai jong ngi kum ka jingpynjanai ia ki jingkyrmen jong kibaroh. Ki jingshisha kiba la buhrieh slem da ka jingdum jong ka jingbakla bad jinghikaipateng, kaba kynthup ia ka Sabbaton ba shisha ka Baibl, yn ialap sha ka pyrthei shwa kajingwan U Trai.

Ki khubor jong ki lai angel ki pynkha ia ka jingpynkhih ha kine ki sngi khadduh ban pyndep iaka jingpynthymmai bad ban iashim bynta bad U Khrist ban pyndep ia ka kam jong u hangneha pyrthei. Ki jingiathuhlypa bakhraw jong ka kitab ba khadduh ka Baibl ka pynpaw ia kajingpynkhih ba kynja blei ha lawei ka mih na ka jingduh jingkyrmen ban ialap ia ka khubor bakhadduh U Blei sha ka pyrthei. Jingpynpaw 14 ka batai shaphang ka balang shityllup pyrtheibad ka khubor babha jong ka kospel bymjukut.

Ia ki lai angel jong ka Jingpynpaw 14 la iasoh da u angel uba saw ha ka Jingpynpaw 18. Uneu angel u ai bor ia ka jingpynbna ia ki lai angel khnang ba yn “pynshai ia ka khyndew da kaburom jong u” (Jingpynpaw 18:1 ). Ka pdeng jong ka lynnong ba 18 ka dei ha ki jingjia bakhraw kiban ialam sha kaba kut ka histori u bynriew bad ka jingjop ba khadduh jong ka Gospel.

`Pule Jingpynpaw 18:1. Kiei ki lai tylli kiei kiei ba u Ioannis u iathuh ia ngi shaphang une uangel? (peit Habbakuk 2:14).`

U angel uba wan hiar na ka jingdon ka burom U Blei ha ka khet bneng jong ka jaka kyntangla phah ia u ban pynbna ia ka khubor jingisnei ba khadduh jong U Blei bad ban maham ia ki nongshong shnong jong ka pyrthei ia kaei kaban wan ha ka pyrthei.

Ka dkhot ka ong ba uta u angel u wan da “ka bor bakhraw.” Ka ktien Grik ha ka testamentba thymmai ia ka kyntien “bor” kadeiexousia . U Jisu u pyndonkam ia kane ka kyntien ha ka Gospel u Mathaios ha ka jingiahap sur ka jingpha jong u ia ki synran jong u. Ha u Mathaios10:1, U Jisu u ai ia ki synran jong u ia ka “bor” halor ki mynsiem bymkhuid bad ki bor jong u bymman. U phah ia ki bad ka bor ba kynja blei ban long nongjop ha ka thma hapdeng kaba bha bad kaba sinew. Ha u Mathaios 28:18, 19, sa shisien u phah ia ki, hynrei hangen da “ka bor baroh” ha ka bneng bad ha ka khyndew ban leit bad “pynlong synran ia ki bynriew baroh.”

`Ha kaba khadduh, kumno ki daw ha ki sngi ba khadduh ki wan ha kabor ? Ka bor jongno ngibud: ka jong U Blei, ka jong ngi, ka jong u mrad khlaw, ne jong kiwei pat? Ka bor jongno phi bud mynta?`
