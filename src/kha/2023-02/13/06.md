---
title:  U Khun Langbrot, U Khun Langbrot Ba La Pynïap
date:  22/06/2023
---

Ki don shibun ki dur pharshi ha ka Jingpynpaw, ki dur pharshi ka Baibl kiba donkam; kata ka long, u iakjakor ha bneng (Jingpynpaw 12:3, 4, 7 ), ki angel kiba her ha sahit bneng (Jingpynpaw 14:6 ), ka briew kaba shong ha u mrad khlaw uba saw hain (Jingpynpaw 17:3 ),bad kumta ter ter. Ki don ha ka Ktien U Blei; U Mynsiem Bakhuid u la ai mynseim ha u

Ioannis ban buh ia ki hangto, bad ki don ia ka bynta ba donkam ha kaban pynpaw ia kajingshisha sha kito kiba pule ia kine ki ktien kane ka kitab, namar kumba ka la ong, “Suk utauba pule, bad kita kiba iohsngew ia ki ktien jong kane ka jingiathuhlypa, bad kiba sumar iakita kiei kiei ruh ia kiba la thoh ha ka: namar kata ka ïa ka la jan” (Jingpynpaw 1:3 ).

Ka don sa kawei ka dur pharshi kaba iai paw lyngba baroh kawei ka kitab Jingpynpaw. Kaeikata ka dur, bad ka ieng pli ia kaei?

`Pule Jingpynpaw 5:6,8,12; Jingpynpaw 7:17; Jingpynpaw 14:1; Jingpynpaw 15:3;Jingpynpaw 19:7; Jingpynpaw 21:22, 23; bad jingpynpaw 22:1, 3. Kaei ka jingmut jong kadur pharshi u khun langbrot, bad balei ka paw bunsien eh ha ka kitab Jingpynpaw?`

Ho,oid, kumba ki kyntien sdang jong kane ka kitab ki ong, kane ka dei ka jingpynpaw “U JisuKhrist.” Bad ym tang ba u dei U Khun langbrot hynrei u dei ruh u Langbrot “ba la pyniap” (Jingpynpaw 5:6, 12; 13:8). Kata ka dei, U Jisu Khris tba la sahnarphna. Kane ka dei ka pdeng,ym tang ka jong ka Baibl hynrei ia ka kitab Jingpynpaw bad jong ki khubor ki lai angel. Ngimlah ban long kiba iaineh sha kaba la khot ia ngi, ngin ym lah ban leh ia ka kam kaba U Blei ula pynmih ia kane ka balang ban leh, lymda ngi don ia U khun langbrot, U Khun langbrot ba la pyniap, U Jisu ba la sahnarphna, ka jingknia ia ki pop jong ngi, kum ka mat pdeng jong kajingialap jong ngi.

Ngi dei ban buh da thmu ia U khun langbrot ba la pyniap hapdeng eh jong ki jinghikai shongtynrai bad ki kam jingtrei ka balang bad hapdeng jong kawei pa kawei ka khubor kaba ngiialap, kino kino ki jingthoh ba ngi thoh, kawei pa kawei ka jingduwai ba ngi pynlong, kanokano ka jingrwai ba ngi rwai, kano kano ka jingpule Baibl ngi pynlong, bad ha kiei kiei barohkiba ngi leh. Ai ba ka jingieit ba la pynpaw da U Khun langbrot ha ka diengphna kan pynkyllaia rukom jingiadei jong ngi iwei ia iwei bad ban pynkhih ia ngi ban long kiba suid sinew shaka pyrtehi.”— Ángel Manuel Rodríguez, “The Closing of the Cosmic Conflict: Role of theThree Angels’ Messages,” unpublished manuscript, sla. 70.

Kata ka mut ba, wat hapdeng ki dur pharshi jong u mrad khlaw ba ishyrkhei, ia u iakjakor ba pynkhih thma, ki khlam, ki jingpynshitom beiñ, bad ka dak u mrad khlaw, ka iaisah ha khmatbad hapdeng eh U Khun langbrot, U Khun langbrot ba la pyniap. Bad tang mna u hi, bad kaeikaba u la leh ia ngi, kaba u leh mynta, bad kaba un sa leh ha shwa kin wai kiei kiei braoh—U long, khadduh eh, kaei kaba ki khubor ki lai angel ki long.

`Balei ba ka long kaba donkam ban buh ia U Khun langbrot ba la pyniap ha pdeng jong kikhubor jong ngi ym tang ban ialam ia kiwei sha U hynrei na ka bynta ka jingim ba kynjamynsiem kaba shimet ka jong phi?`
