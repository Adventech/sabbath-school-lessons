### Dkhot Baibyl ban Pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
1 Petros 4:12-19,  1Petros 5:8-11,  Roman 1:21-32,  Jeremiah 9:7-16,  2 Korinth 12:7-10.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Ko hep baieit, to wat sngewphylla phi ia kata ka jingtynjuh bameh ding kaba ha phi, kaba jia ia kaban tynjuh ia phi, kumba da jia kaba khyllah ha phi:  hynrei kat kumba phi ioh bynta lem na kita ki jingshah shitom U Khrist, to kmen noh: ba ha kata ka jingpynpaw ia ka burom jong u phi ruh phin ioh kmen da ka jingkmen kaba khraw.” 1 Petros 4:12,13.

U Khiew sain u ieng ia (1) u khiew ba ju pyndonkam ban pynum ia ki mar kiba donkam ka jingkhluid kaba jur. (2) Ka jingtynjuh bashyrkhei  (3) Ka jaka ha kaba ka bor bajur ka shat ne ka ban khnang ban pynkylla dur ne thaw thymmai.

Kine ki jingbatai ki iarap ia ngi ba ngin iohi kham bha ia kaei kaba jia ia ka jingim bakynja mynsiem jong ngi. Ha kane ka taiew ngin ia peit ia katto katne ki jingjia kiba ngi lah ban iakynduh kynsan bad bad da ki jingbankhia bad ki jingtynjuh  jong ki, ki lah ban pynkylla , tei ne pynbha ia ka jinglong jong ngi. Kane ka lah ban iarap ia ngi ban sngewthuh ia kaei ba U Blei u leh ha ka jingim jong ngi, khnang ba haba ngi hap ban rung ha u khiew sain, ngin nang kumno ban leh.

**Jingkylli**: Kiei ki daw jong ki jingeh kiba ngi iakynduh ha ka jingim jong ngi?

_* Pynkhreh ia kane ka lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, July 9._
