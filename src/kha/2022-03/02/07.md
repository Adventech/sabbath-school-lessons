“Uta uba pule ia ka dohnud briew u tip kham bha ban ia kaba ki tip ma ki ia lade. Uiohi ba don kiba don ka bor bad ka jingtlot ba lada pyniaid ha ka rukom ba dei, lah ban pyndonkam ia ki ha ka kam jong U. Hapoh ka jingsumar jong U, U ialam ia kine ki briew sha ki kyrdan bapher bad ki jaka bapher khnang ba kin shem  ia ki jingduna ha ka jinglong jong ki kaba kim shym la tip da lade. U ailad ia ki ba kin lah ban pynbeit ia kine ki jingduna, bad ban pynbit ia lade ha ka kam jong U. Bunsien U shah ia ka ding jong ka jingshitom ba kan phrang ia ki khnang ba ia ki yn pynkhuid.” E G White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 471.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ia Puson**

`Ka lah ban long kaba eh bha katba ngi ot ia ki jingmih na ka pop. Ngi kylli, “hato ngi dang lah ban ia suk bad U Blei sa shisien pat?”  Kumno U Blei u kular ban pynshlur ia ngi ban iaitur shaphrang ha kum kine ki por bad ban ym man thait noh? (Pule Rom. 5:1-11).`

`Lada phi tip iano re iano ba iaid lyngba ia u “khiew sain” mynta hi, hato ka donkam ne kam donkam ban tip ia ka daw kaba wallam ia kane jingjia? Kumno phin leh ia u ne ia ka, ha ka jingshitom, kam pher kaei daw kaba la wallam ia kane ka jingjia?`

`Kum ka klass, buh ia ka por ba phin ia leit sha hospital ne shano shano ha kaba phi lah ban iarap, pyntngen, bad pynkmen ia kito kiba , na kano re kano ka daw, ki don ha u khiew sain ka jingtynjuh.`
