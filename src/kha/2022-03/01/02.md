> <p></p>
> “U Trai U long U Nongap langbrot jong nga, ngan ym kyrduh” (Salm 23:1).

La ong ia ki khynnah ban dro ka dur U Blei. Baroh  ki la dro ia ka dur bad ia ka dohnud. Ynda la kylli balei, ki ia ong ba U Blei u long U baieit.

Ka long ka basuk ban  pyrkhat shaphang U Blei haba kiei kiei baroh ki iaid beit. Hynrei katba ngi nangheh nangsan, ka jingim ruh ka nang kham eh bad kyllain, kumta ka jingpyrkhat jong ngi ia U Blei ruh ka kylla. U Blei te um ju kylla (Hebru 13:8, Jakob 1:17), hynrei ngi te ngi kylla.

Namar ba ki briew ha ka por jong ka testament barim ki ju long ki nongsharai jing ri, la pyndonkam ia ka dur jong u nongap langbrot ha ki Salm ban batai ba kumno U Blei u sumar ia ngi.  Ha ka testament barim bad bathymmai u nongap langbrot u thew ia U Blei . Ha shuwa ba ngin pule ia ki Salm 23, to ngin ia peit shuwa kumno ki nongthoh ka Baibl ki sngewthuh ia ka kam bad ka jinglong jong u Nongap langbrot lyngba ka Baibl baroh kawei.

**Phi sngewthuh aiu shaphang u nongap langbrot na kine ki dkhot harum?**

`Jeremiah 23:3,4`

`Esakial 34:12`

`Ioanis 10:14-16`

`1 Petros 2:25`
