Hapoh u Khiew Sain

### Dkhot Baibyl ban Pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Esekiel 24:15-27, Eksodos 32:1-14, Mathaios 5:43-48,  1Petros 2:18-25, Salm 62:1-8.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
>  “Suk kiba jemnud: naba kin iaioh ia ka khyndew.” (Mathaios 5:5).

Ngim ju kham iohsngew ba pyndonkam ia ka ktien jemnud , lehse tang haba ngi pule shaphang u Moses ne ia ki jingkren halor u lum. Kam long kaba eh ban tip balei. Ia ka jingjemnud la batai ba ka long ka jingiaishah lyngba ki jingeh khlem da sngewmong. Teng teng ka Baibl ka pyndonkam ia kane ka ktien da ka jingsngewrit.  Ka jingjemnud bad ka jingsngewrit kam long ka jinglong kaba ka pyrthei ka niewkor ne kwah.

Hynrei ka jingjemnud bad ka jingsngewrit , ka long ka atiar bakhlain bor U Jisu bad ki nongbud jong U. Hynrei ym kut tang hangta: Ka jingjemnud ka mynsiem ka lah ban long ka atiar bakhlain ha ki kti jong kito kiba don hapdeng ka jingpang bad jingshitom. Haoid, u khiew sain u long ka jaka ha kaba yn nang ia ka jingjemnud ka mynsiem, namar ba lyngba ka jingjemnud bad jingsngewrit jong ngi hapdeng ki jingjynjar ba ngi lah ban long ki sakhi badonbor.

**Shisien Peit ia ka Taiew**: Kaei ka jingiadei hapdeng ka jingjemnud bad ka jingshitom? Kumno ba ka jingjemnud ka lah ban long ka jingkhlain, ym ka jingtlot ia u Khristan?

_* Pynkhreh ia kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, August 3_
