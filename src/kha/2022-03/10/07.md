Pule Ellen G. White, “The Importance of Seeking True Knowledge,” pp. 453, 454, in The Ministry of Healing; “The Sermon on the Mount,” pp. 298–314, in The Desire of Ages; “The Worker and His Qualifications,” p. 630, in Evangelism.

“Ia ki jingeh kiba ngi hap iakynduh lah ban pyntlot bor da kata ka jingjemnud kaba la buhrieh ha U Khrist. Lada ngi don ka jingsngewrit U Kynrad jong ngi, ngin jop ia ki jingshahkren bein, ki jingshah kynnoh, ki jingpynwit, kiba ngi iakynduh man la ka por, bad kin jah noh na kaba kah dum ia ka mynsiem. Ka bor ba paw khmat eh ha ka jinglong badonburom u Khristan dei ka jingsynshar ia lade. Uta u bym lah ban ieng da ka mynsiem bajai-jai bad bashaniah ia ki jingshah lehbein bad jingshahkren bein u tuh noh na U Blei ia ka hok jong U ban pynpaw lyngba jong u ia ka jinglong janai ka jinglong jong U. Ka jingsngewrit ka dohnud ka long ka bor kaba ai jingjop ia ki nongbud U Khrist; ka long ka dak jong ka jingiasoh jong ki bad ka bneng.” E G White, The Desire of Ages, p. 301.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ia Puson**

`Kumno ba ka jingsngewrit ka pynlah ia ngi ban jop ia ki jingmong bad jingwit baroh?`

`Kat kum ka jinglong ki khasi- pnar, kumno ngi ia sngew ia ka jingsngewrit bad jingjemnud? Ngi burom, ibein, niewpoh, ne kumno?`

`Balei ba bunsien ia ka jingjemnud bad ia ka jingsngewrit ngi pyniaryngkat bad ka jingtlot?`

`Ngi iohi kumno ba u Dabid u wad ia U Blei kum ka jaka rieh. Hynrei kumno kata ka trei kam? Kumno ia kata ka jakarieh la pynpaw? Kumno ba ngi kum ka balang, ngi lah ban long ka jaka rieh ia kito kiba donkam ia ka? Ka balang jong phi ka long ka jaka rieh kaba kumno?  Kumno ngin iarap ban pynlong ia ka balang jong ngi ka jaka rieh ia kito kiba donkam ia ka?`
