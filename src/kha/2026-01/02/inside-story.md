### Ki Nongrah Khubor Jong U Blei

Da: Andrew McChesney

U Oscar u la ieng beit, u da duwai, katba lai tylli ki ksew kiba wiar bad kiba byrngem ki ker sawdong ia u shabar kawei ka iing ha Kenya. U trai iing uba la pyllait ia ki ksew katba u Oscar u dang wan shajan ka iing, u la wan shajan. "Phi dei uei?" u la kylli.

“Nga long u nonglam khubor jong U Blei,” la ong u Oscar.

“Ka khubor aiu U Blei U la phah ia phi?” u briew u la ong.

“Ka dei ka khubor jong U Blei,” la ong u Oscar. “Nga lah ban rung?”

Uta u briew u la khot noh ia ki ksew bad u la teh ia ki.

U peit ia u Oscar da ka jingkhmih lynti hadien ba ki la rung shapoh iing. “To ngin ia duwai,” la ong u Oscar. U briew um shym la khapbrip ia ki khmat jong u. U Oscar u la duwai bad nangta u la sdang ban iakren shaphang ki san tylli ki kitab banyngkong jong ka Baibl, kata ka Pentateuch.

Uta u briew um dei u Khristan. U dei na ka niam pyrthei kaba khraw, kaba ym dei ka niam Khristan, hynrei u tip ia kita ki kitab jong ka hukum. U la kylli bun ki jingkylli, bad kita ki arngut ki la ialong paralok. Mynta ka sngi, une u rangbah bad ka tnga jong u ki dei ki Seventh Day Adventist.

Ha kawei pat ka por, uwei u trai ïing u la pynpaw ïa ka jingsngewtynnad haba u Oscar u la tyrwa ïa ki jinghikai Baibl. U la phah noh ia ki lai ngut ki khun jong u, kiba dang 6, 8 bad 13 snem ka rta, katba ki dang leit noh, u Oscar u la iohi ba ki la iaid dkoh namar ka jingpang. “To ai ba ki khynnnah kin wan shane khnang ba ngin duwai,” u la ong. Nangta u la iohi ba ki khynnah ki don ia ki chiggers, ki khniang kiba rit kiba don ki khniang kiba im halor ne hapoh ka sniehdoh bad kiba pynlong ia ka pynpang.

U Oscar u la duwai na ka bynta ki khynnah bad u la leit ban thied dawai.

Ka longiing ka la pyrkhat ba ki chiggers ki dei na ka jingleh jadu. Hynrei ynda u Oscar u la wan phai, u la iarap ban pynkhuid ia ka iing ka sem bad ki jain thiah jong ki. U la bthah ia ki kmie ki kpa ban pynsleh ia ki khynnah, bad nangta u la pynbiang ia ki dawai. Ki khynnah ki la khuid. U Oscar u la duwai biang na ka bynta ki khynnah, bad u la pule ia ka Baibl bad ki kmie ki kpa. Hadien kane ka longïing ka la kylla Adventist bad ki la die jaka ban tei ïa ka ïingmane Adventist kaba thymmai.

Ha kaba iasam ia ka gospel, u Oscar u leit ruh na kawei ka jingiaseng sha kawei pat ha ki robibar. Ha Kenya, ka dei ka rukom ban ailad ia ki nongwan jngohkai ban ai khublei ia ki dkhot balang. Hynrei shisien, uwei u nongialam balang u la khot ia u Oscar sha ka jingiaseng bad nangta ki la kyntait ban ailad ia u ban kren haba ki la tip ba u dei u Adventist. U Oscar u la sah na ka bynta ka jingiaseng. Katba u dang leit noh, u la ithuh katto katne ngut ki dkhot kum ki para marjan jong u, bad u la leit ban khublei ia ki.

“Ka Baibl ka long kum ka duriaw bah, bad kane kam dei ka por ban ap ia u pastor ban wan hikai ia phi”, u la ong. “Nga don ki jinghikai kiba phi lah ban pule hi.”

Hynñiew ngut ki briew ki la pdiang ia ki jinghikai Baibl jong ka Voice of Prophecy Discovery Bible lessons on the spot. Kiwei pat ki la husiar hynrei hadien ki la mynjur ban shim ia ki jinghikai. Mynta, 43 ngut ki briew na ka balang ki pule ia ka Baibl bad u Oscar. “Kam pher na ka bynta ka jingiaseng ha kaba ym shah ia nga ban kren,” u la ong da kaba phuh samrkhie.

U Oscar u la trei kum u nongsdang jong ka Global Mission haduh 10 snem, u la ialam ia ki 120 ngut ki briew sha ka jingpynbaptis bad thung ar tylli ki Balang. U kyrmen ban plie shen ia ka Balang kaba lai. Pule kham bniah shaphang ki nongsdang jong ka Global Mission ha https://bit.ly/GMPioneers.
