---
title:  Ban Pyniasuk Ia Kiei Kiei Baroh
date:   19/02/2026
---

`Pule Kolossai 1:19, 20. Kaei ka jingpynïasuk kaba wan lyngba ka diengphna, bad haduh katno ka long kaba donkam eh?`

U Paul u pyndonkam da ki kyniten kiba sngewtynnad ha ka ktien Greek ban batai shai shaphang U Jisu, da kaba kdew sha U Kpa, ïa Uba la kynthoh ha Kolossai 1:12. Ka dei ka jinglong Blei kaba pura kaba la pynsngewbha ha U Kpa ba kan shongtylli ha U Jisu (nujorKolossai 2:9). Kaei ka “Jingdap pura”? UIoannisu kdew biang sha ka burom jong U Kpa, kaba “Dap da ka jingaiei bad ka jingshisha”.(Ioannis 1:14).

Hynrei, haba la pynshongnongrim ha kane ka jingong, ka “Jingdap Baroh” ka kynthup shibun kiei-kiei. Kaba kynthup ïa ka Jinglong-Blei ba bynjukut ba ïaidon khlem kano kano ka thymmei, Uba don ka bor ban thaw bad ban ïaithaw thymmai. Kaba kongsan tam na baroh, ka pynpaw ruh ïa ka jingstad da kaba U lah ruh ban jop ïa ka pop bad ka jingïap lyngba ka lad kaba ym i-lah ngeit kata ka dei da ka----diengphna.Kumta, U pynkylla ïa kata kaei kaei kaba long ka jinglehraiñ sha ka jingphla jong ka jingieit ba bymjukut jong U habaroh ki jingthaw. “Naba U Blei U la ieit katta katta ïa ka pyrthei katba U la aiti noh ïa la U Khun ba la kha marwei, ba uei uei ruh uba ngeit ha U, un ym jot shuh, hynrei un ïoh ka jingim bymjukut.” (Ioannis 3:16).

Don tang kawei ka lad ba ïa ka pop lah ban jop sha ki bynjukut, bad baroh kiei-kiei lah ban pynïasuk ïa kiba dei ban pynïasuk, ïa ka lah ban kren lyngkot kata ka dei ka jingieit jong U Blei ïa ka bneng jong ki bneng, bad U la ieit eh ïa ngi haduh ba U la kheiñduh lyngba ka jingïap U Jisu halor ka diengphna. Ha ka ktien Greek ïa ka kyntien “Pyrthei” la khot ka kosmos, kaba mut kaba kynthup lut ïa ka bneng jong ki bneng. U Paul haba u kdew ha ka jingïadei bad kane ka jingpynpaw jong ka jingbud ïa U Jisu Khrist refers to this universal demonstration in the context of following Christ: “Naba la pynlong ïa ngi ka jingthadrong ïa ka pyrthei, [kosmos, universe] ïa ki angel ruh, bad ïa ki briew.” (1 Korinth 4:9).

“Ka bneng ka khmih da ka jingsngewsih bad ka jinglyngngoh haba ïa U Jisu la sdieng halor ka diengphna. . . . da ka jingim jong ka jingïaleh-pyrshah, u Soitan bad baroh ki nongbud jong u bad baroh kiba la pynïasoh bad u, ki la buh ïalade ban ym ïahap sur shuh bad U Blei, bad ka jingdon jong U kan long ha ki kum ka ding ba bam duh. Ka burom jong Uta Uba long ka jingieit kan pynjot ïa ki.Ha kaba sdang jong ka jingïaleh bakhraw, ki angel kim sngewthuh shaphang kane. . . .

“Hynrei kan ym long kumta ha kaba kut jong ka jingïalehpyrshah kaba khraw.Kumta, ynda haba la pyndep janai ïa ka jingsiewspah, ïa ka jinglong jong U Blei yn sa pynpaw shai sha baroh ki jingthaw ba don ka pyrkhat-pyrdaiñ. . . . “

“Kumta ki angel baroh kin sa lehkmen haba kin peit ha ka diengphna U Nongpynim….U Khrist hi Un sa ïohi ïa ka jingmih jong ka jingkñia kheiñduh kaba U la shah ha Kalbari. Bad sha baroh kaba U la khmih lynti mynba U la pyrta halor ka diengphna “La pyndep” ”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, pp. 760, 764. 66
