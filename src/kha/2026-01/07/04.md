---
title:  To Kmen Ha U Trai Barabor
date:   10/02/2026
---

`Pule Philipi 4:4-7. Kumno ngi dei ban mad ia ka “jingsuk U Blei”?`

Hadien ba u la ktah, sa shisien, ia ka jingdonkam jong ka jingiatylli (Phil. 4:1-3), u Paul u iaid sha kawei pat ka phang: ka jingkmen ha U Trai (Phil. 4:4-7). Katno sien phi la shah pynshitom ha kiei kiei kiba ha kaba kut ki jah khlem jingpyrshang katba ki paw? Na ka daw kaba biang, U Jisu u la pynksan bunsien ba ngim dei ban khuslai (peit Math. 6:25 -34, Math. 10:19), bad u Petros u pynkynmaw ia ngi ba ngi lah ban bret ia ki jingkhuslai ne jingkhuslai jong ngi baroh ha U Trai, “naba U sumar ia phi” (1 Pet. 5:7). Ha ka jingshisha, ki jingeh kiba nangjur ha kylleng ka pyrthei ki dei ban ai mynsiem ia ngi da ka jingkyrmen ba ka jingwan U Trai ka la jan (nujor Math. 24:33, Louk 21:28, Jakob 5:8).

Ka dawai pynjem ia ka jingkhuslai ha kiei kiei baroh, kynthup ha man la ka khyllipmat, ka long da kaba phah ia ka jingduwai jong ka jingngeit (Phil. 4:6, 7). Ka paw shai, ba ngi dei ban ngeit bad leh ha ka jingduwai jong ngi kum kaba la jubab wat shuwa ba ngin iohi ia ka jingpynurlong jong ka, namar ngi dei ban duwai “da ka jingsngewnguh.” Kumjuh ruh la bynrap ia ka kyntien “jingkyrpad” (Grik: deēsis), kaba pyni ia ki por jong ka jingeh kaba palat bad kaba kyrkieh (peit, kum ka nuksa, Loukas 1:13, Phil. 1:19, 1 Tim. 5:5, Jakob 5:16). Ki jingduwai jong ngi ki dang long “ki jingkyrpad,” hynrei ngi lah ban tip ba la pdiang ia ki jingkyrpad jong ngi katba ngi dang pan “katkum ka mon jong U” (1 Ioannis 5:14,). Nangta ngi lah ban shongthait bad ban ioh ia ka jingsuk, da kaba tip ba baroh ki ki jingkyrpad ki don ha ki kti U Blei.

`Kumno ki bynta kiba bud ki pynroi ia ka jingsngewthuh jong ngi shaphang ka jingsuk U Blei? Salm. 29:11, Isa. 9:6, Loukas 2:14, Ioannis 14:27, 1 Kor. 14:33.`

Ka jingsuk U Blei ka long kaei kaei kaba ka pyrthei kam lah ban ai satia, namar ka jingsuk U Blei ka wan na ka jingsngewskhem ba ngi ioh ia ka jingai jong ka jingim bymjukut lyngba U Jisu U Nongpynim jong ngi (Rom 5:1, Rom 6:23). Kane ka jingsuk ka ktah ia kawei pa kawei ka bynta jong ka jingim bad “ka palat ia ka jingshemphang baroh” (Phil. 4:7. Ym lah ban shim ia ka tang da ka jingmut jingpyrkhat, kumba ka kyntien Grik nous (jingmut) ba la pyndonkam hangne ka pyni.

`Kumno phin batai ha uwei pat kaei ka jingmut ban mad “ka jingsuk U Blei”?`
