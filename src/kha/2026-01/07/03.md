`Pule Philipi 3:20, 21. Kumno u Paul u batai shai ba kumno u “u nongshong shnong” Khristan u long?`

Ym kum ki nongshun jong ka Diengphna, kiba “buh ia la ka jingmut ha kiei kiei kiba kynja pyrthei” bad kim don blei uba kham khraw ban ia ka kpoh jong ki (Phil. 3:19), ka jinglong nongshong shnong Khristan ka long ha bneng, bad u nongsynshar jong ngi u long U Jisu Khrist hi. Ban ban bha ia ka jingmut, u Paul u pynpaw ia ka jingdonkam ia “kine ki met kiba ka jinglong rangli jong ngi” (Phil. 3:21), kiba la shah ktah ha ka jingpang, jingsniew bad jingiap, ban pynkylla dur ban long kum ka met jingmihpat ba donburom jong U Khrist.

**Kumno ki bynta kiba bud ki batai ia ka jinglong ba la pyndonburom?**

`U Job 19:25-27`

`U Loukas 24:39`

`1 Kor. 15:42-44`

`1 Kor. 15:50-54`

`Kol 3:4`

Ha kaba kut, lyngba U Jisu yn pynjot noh ïa ka jingïap, “ka nongshun bakhadduh” (1 Kor. 15:26). Bad kata ka long ka jingkyrmen kaba khraw tam jong ngi, ka jingkular kaba khatduh eh kaba la ai ha ngi ha U Jisu — ym tang ka jingkut jong ka jingiap hynrei ka met kaba thymmai pura, wat ka “met badonburom” (Phil. 3:21), .

Ha kawei ka kot shaphang kumno ban shem ia ka “jingpynim” khlem U Blei, kaba la kynthoh, da kaba kham bieit, ba ka jingjop ia ka jingtieng ia ka jingiap ka long ka “jingpynim,” u nongthoh . U Luc Ferry u kam shisha ba ka niam Khristan “ka pynlah ia ngi ym tang ban iaid lait na ka jingtieng ia ka jingiap, hynrei ban jop ruh ia ka jingiap hi. Bad da kaba leh kumta ha ki kyndon jong ka jinglong shimet, ban ia ka jingbym paw kyrteng ne khate, ka long kumba ka long tang ma ka kathymmei kaba ai ia ka jingjop kaba thikna shisha jong ka jinglong shimet . ka jingbymïap halor ka jinglong jong ngi kum ki briew kiba ïap.”—Ferry, Ka History Lyngkot jong ka 1914. Ka Jingpyrkhat (New York: Ka jingpynmih ha ka kot lyngkdop), p. 90. Kaba thikna ka jingpdiang, kaba wan na u nongngeit Blei.

Kumta, na ka bynta u Paul, ka jinglong trai shnong bneng jong ngi ka kynthup ia ka jingkular jong ka . ka jingmihpat bad ka jingim bymjukut ha ka jinglong kaba thymmai pura kaba ngi lah ban mutdur tang khyndiat eh mynta.

`Balei ka jingkular jong ka jingim bymjukut ka long kaba kongsan haduh katta katta ia baroh kiba ngi ngeit? Kaei kaba kane ka pyrthei ka lah ban ai kaba donkam ban pynduh noh ia kaei U Khrist u tyrwa ia ngi?`
