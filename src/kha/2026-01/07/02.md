---
title:  Ki Nongbuh Nongmuna Ba Bha
date:   08/02/2026
---

Baroh ngi la ju shem, ha kano kano ka por, ia ki briew kiba ngi sngewtynnad bad kwah ban pyni nuksa. Na ka bynta ki khynnah, ka long kaba kongsan khamtam eh ba kin don ia ki nongbuh Nongmuna kiba babha. Ka jingmut kaba bha ka long ba u kpa bad ka kmie jong ki. Katba ki nangsan, kin sa shem ia kiwei pat ki nongbuh nuksa, lehse kiba iadei bad ka kam ba ki la jied ne haba ki pule ia ka jingim ki jong ki. Ki lah ruh ban tip kumno ki briew bapher bapher jong ka Baibl ki ïaleh pyrshah ïa ki jingeh bad ïanujor ia ki sha la ki jong ki jingiashem ha ka jingim.

Kaba sngewsih ka long ba ha ki lad pathai khubor mynta, ki nongbuh nuksa kiba sniew ki bun bha. Ia ngi la thombor da ki jingpynbyrngia—ki khana kiba batai bniah ia ki jingeh kiba shyrkhei bad ki jingim ba la pynkulmar jong ki riew pawnam. Ki nongpule jong u Paul ha Philipi, wat la katta ruh. Hooid kim shem don ka jingiadei bad ka internet, watla katta ruh ki la iakynduh ia kijuh ki jingeh.

Ka jingshisha ka long ba ka pyrthei kaba u Paul u im ka long kaba sniew haduh katta katta, kaba khlem akor bad kaba kaba sniew, kumba long ka jong ngi mynta. Ka la don naduh mynshuwa—bad kan don beit, ha haduh kaba kut— kiba bun ki jingsniew iaid kylleng. Ka jingkylli na ka bynta jong ngi ka long: Kumno ngin jubab ia ka?

`Pule Philipi 3:17-19. Kumno la batai ia ki Nongmuna kiba bha bad kiba sniew ha kane ka bynta? Kiei ki mat ba la ai ban pyniapher hapdeng jong ki?`

Ngim dei ban klet ia ka jingieit jong u Paul ia kito kiba um ia mynjur lang— u iam na ka bynta jong ki! Peit ruh ba um shym la khot ia ki ki nongshun jong u hynrei “ki nongshun ka diengphna U Khrist” (Phil. 3:18). U Paul u la ithuh ba ki mat kiba kham heh shibun ki la don ha ka jingma, kata, kumno ka Diengphna ka pyndkut ia ki jingkhang lad bad ka buh ia ngi baroh ha kajuh ka kyrdan, kum ki nongpop kiba donkam ia U Nongpynim (peit Eph. 2:11-14).

Kumjuh ruh, ban ym iehnoh, kumno u Paul u kyntu ia ki nong Philipi ban pynleit jingmut ha ki nuksa kiba bha, ym ha ki nuksa kiba sniew; ban peit bniah ia kito kiba ka rukom im jong ki ka iasyriem shibun bad ka jong u. Kaba sngewtynnad ka long ba u Paul u pyndonkam kum kita ki ktien ha kaba maham ia ki nong Rom ban “kynmaw ia kita kiba pynlong ia ki jingiapait bad ki jingpynthut, kiba pyrshah ia ka jinghikai ia kaba phi la hikai, bad to kiar na ki” (Rom 16:17). Ia ki nongleh thok ha Rome la batai kum kito kiba “ym shakri ia U Trai jong ngi U Jisu Khrist hynrei ia la ka jong ka kpoh” (Rom 16:18).

`Wat la, shisha, U Jisu u long tang ma u hi uba janai, hynrei ki don kiwei pat kiba, la kumno kumno ha ki katto katne ki bynta, ki lah ban long ki Nongmuna kiba bha. Ha kajuh ka por, kaei ka jait Jingbuh nongmuna ba bha kaba phi pyni ha kiwei pat?`
