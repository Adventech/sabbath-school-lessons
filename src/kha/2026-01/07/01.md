### Ki Dkhot Pule Na Ka Bynta Ka Jingïapule Mynta Ka Taïew
Philipi. 3:17–4:23, 1 Korinth. 15:42–44, Ioannis 14:27, Salm. 119:165, Job 1:21, 1 Timothi. 6:7.

> <p>Dkhot spel-lyndet</p>
> “Wat khuslai ia kaei kaei ruh; hynrei ha kaei kaei baroh da ka jingduwai bad ka jingkyrpad lem bad ka jingainguh, To phin pyntip ia ki jingpan jong phi sha U Blei.” (Philipi 4:6)

Ka jinghikai jong kane ka taiew ka pynkut ia ka jingpule jong ngi ia ki Philipi, bad ka long ba la pyndap da ki jinghikai bad ki jingkren kiba kordor na ka bynta ka jingim kaba man ka sngi. Ka long kumba bun na ki jinglong akor kiba bha kiba la ïalam ïa ka jingim u Paul la shem ha ki dkhot ba khatduh jong ka shithi. Ka jingiasyriem bad ki jinghikai U Jisu, kiba pynleit jingmut ha ka jinglong ba shapoh, kaei kaba u Paul u iasam bad ngi ki don ki jingmaham ban im ka jingim Khristan kaba kmen.

Wat haba kiei kiei kim iaid kumba ngi kwah, kaba jia kham bunsien ban ia kaba ngi kwah, ngim donkam ban khuslai ne duh mynsiem. Ha ka jaka kata, ki don ki nongrim ki ban iarap ia ngi ban shem ia ka bor ba shapoh ban ialeh ia ki jingeh kiba ka jingim ka wanrah, bad kumta ngi lah mad ia ka jingsuk kaba skhem bad kaba neh kaba tang U Blei U lah ban ai. Ka mynta bad ka lawei ki don ha ki kti jong U, bad Un pynbiang ia kiei kiei baroh ngi donkam.

Kaba kongsan eh, ngim donkam ban buh ia ka jingkyrmen jong ngi ha ki rukom jong ka sorkar pyrthei, kiba pynduh jingkyrmen ia ngi man ka por. Kum ki Khristan, ngi long ki nongshong shnong jong ka hima bneng

U Blei. Bad ryngkat bad kata ka jinglong nongshong shnong ka wan ki jingmyntoi, ki jingmyntoi kiba phylla. Bad ki jingkitkhlieh ruh..

_Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, February 14._
