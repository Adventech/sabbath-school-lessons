---
title:  Tang Kane Ka Wei –Tip Ia U Khrist
date:   05/02/2026
---

`Pule Philipi 3:10–16., kiei ki katto katne ki mat ba kongsan ki ba U Paul u pynpaw ha kine ki dkhot?`

Hooid ym don kaei kaei kaba kham kongsan ban ia kaba tip ia U Khrist, kaba pynthikna ha kaba kut ba Un ithuh ia ngi bad un ithuh ia ngi ha khmat U Kpa (peit Mathaios. 7:21-23; Mathaios. 10:32, 33). Kumno ngi tip ia U? Lyngba ka Ktien ba la thoh jong U — da kaba pule ia ka bad im ia ka. Ngim lah ban ithuh ia U markhmat kumba la leh ki synran. Hynrei kaba sngewtynnad ka long ba wat hapdeng kata ka jingtip, ki dang shah rem ban sngewthuh ia ki ktien jong U, kaba pynpaw ia ka jingdonkam jong ngi ia U Mynsiem Bakhuid ban ialam ia ngi (peit Ioannis 16:13). Katba ngi nang tip ia U, katta ngi nang jan sha U, namar ngi mad ia “ka bor jong ka jingmihpat jong U” (Philipi. 3:10), kaba kyntiew ia ngi sha ka “jingthymmai jong ka jingim” (Rom 6:4, NKJV).

Kawei pat ka lad ba ngi wan kham jan sha U Jisu ka long lyngba “ka jingiasyllok ha ki jingshah shitom jong U” (Philipi. 3:10, NKJV). Kawei pa kawei ka jingtynjuh kaba ngi iakynduh, kawei pa kawei ka jingjia kaba sngewsih kaba ngi kit, ka iarap ia ngi ban tip bad sngewnguh kham bun ia kaei kaba U Jisu u la iaid lyngba na ka bynta jong ngi, bad ruh ban sngewthuh kham shai ia U bad ia ka mon jong U.

Ka lad kaba lai kaba ngi wan kham jan ka long da kaba ngi beh shaphrang “sha ka thong” (Phil. 3:14, NKJV). Kaei kata ka thong? Ka pynkylla ia ka ktien ba la pyndonkam tang hangne ha ka Testement Bathymmai (skopos). Ka thew ia u lain ba kut jong ka jingiakop bad ia ka khusnam ba la ai ia u nongjop. U Paul u khot ia ka “ka khusnam ka jingkhot sha jrong U Blei ha U Khrist Jisu” (Phil. 3:14, NKJV). Kumba U Khrist, lyngba ka jingiap bad jingmihpat jong U, U la kiew sha jrong sha bneng, U Blei U khot ia ngi ban ioh ia kajuh ka bainong ba kynja bneng — ka jingim bymjukut.

Ka paw shai ba ngim pat poi sha kata haduh mynta. Ngin ym long kiba janai ha ka jingmut kaba pura tad haduh ba ka “met ka jinglong rangli” jong ngi kan da kylla “ia ka ban pynlong shi dur bad ka met ka burom jong U” (Philipi. 3:21, NKJV). Hynrei da kaba tip ia U bad khot ia ka jingdon jong U ha ka jingim jong ngi man la ka sngi, ngi iaid shaphrang sha ka thong ban long kum U Jisu ha ki liang baroh kiba lah mynta. Kane ka dei ka “kawei kaei kaei” kaba u Paul u la pynleit jingmut, ruh. Kumjuh kum ka jingmareh (peit 1 Kor 9:24-27), ngim ju pyrkhat shano ngi la leit ne mano ba bud ia ngi. Ka jingpyrkhat kaba marwei jong ngi ka long shaphang kiei kiei kiba don ha khmat — kata ka khusnam bneng kaba ap ia ngi. Ka dur hangne ka long kaba shai bha: ka jingpyrkhat jong u nongmareh ia ka thong, kaba pynkhih ia kawei pa kawei ka doh ksah bad kaba sngap jar shakhmat ban poi sha u lain ba kut.

`Balei, ha ka jingiaid jong phi bad U Trai, ka long kaba kongsan haduh katta katta ban ym peit shuh shadien, la kumno kumno shadien sha ki pop bad ki jingbymlah jong phi, hynrei ha ka jaka kata ban peit sha khmat ia kaei kaba la kular ia phi mynta hi ha U Khrist?`
