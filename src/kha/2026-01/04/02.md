---
title:  Ka Jingbym Ïatylli Ha Philipi
date:   18/01/2026
---

`Pule Philipi 2:1-3. Kiei ki jingjia kiba i kumba ki la pynlong ia ka jingbym iatylli hapoh ka balang? Kaei kaba u Paul u ai jingmut kum ka pynkhiah?`

Ka la dei ban long ka jingduh jingkyrmen kaba khraw ia u Paul ban iohi ia ka balang kaba u la seng bad kaba u ieit eh ba la pynkhih da ka jingialeh pyrshah bad ba la bam duh da ka jingialeh pyrshah. U pyndonkam da ka ktien kaba khlain bha ban batai ia ki jingeh. “Ka jingkwah burom” ka pynkylla ia ka kyntien (Grik, eritheia), ba la pyndonkam kham mynshuwa ha Philipi 1:17 (ESV) ban kdew ia ki nongialeh pyrshah jong u Paul ha Rom kiba kwah ban kyntiew ia lade ban ia kaba kyntiew ia ka kam U Khrist.

“Ka jingkwah burom” ka long napdeng ki kam jong ka doh (Kalatia. 5:20, NKJV), bad kumba u Jakob u kdew, “Namar ha kaba don ka jingpihuin bad ka jingpyneh sat, hangta don ka jingkhihwin bad ka jingleh basniew baroh.” (Jakob 3:16, NKJV). Ia ka kyntien Grik na ka bynta “ka jingsngew sarong thala” la pyndonkam tang hangne ha ka Testament Bathymmai, hynrei ka paw ha ki kot kiba shabar jong ka Baibl ha ka jingmut jong ka jingsngew sarong, ka jingsngew sarong kaba ym don ei ei kaba thylli, bad ka jingdon ia ka jingsngew ba la pynkhraw ia lade. U Paul u pyndonkam ia ka ktien kaba iadei bha bad kane ha kaba u sneng ia ki Nong Galatia: “Wat ai te ba ngin long kiba sarong thala, kiba pynbitar paralok, da kaba bishni iwei ia iwei pat” (Gal. 5:26, NKJV).

Kynmaw ia ki lad jong ka jingpynkhiah ba u Paul u la buh na ka bynta kine ki jingeh:

1. Ka jingpyntngen ha U Khrist. U Paul un iaid shakhmat ban pyndonkam ia ka nuksa jong U Khrist hi kum ka jingpynshlur kaba khlain.
2. Ka jingpyntngen jong ka jingieit. U Jisu u pynpaw ia ka jingieit ba kynja Blei bad u hukum ia ngi ba ngin “ieit iwei ia iwei pat kumba nga la ieit ia phi” (loanis. 15:12).
3. Ka jingiasyllok U Mynsiem. Ka jingdon ryngkat U Mynsiem Bakhuid ka thaw ia ka jingiadei Khristan kaba jan kum kata kaba la jyllei ha ka balang kaba nyngkong (Ki Kam 2:42; nujor 2 Korinth. 13:14).
4. Ka jingieit (ne jingsngew lem). Ngi iohi ia kane ka jinglong ba kynja Blei kaba la paw bunsien ha ka jingim U Khrist (peit Mathaios. 9:36, Mathaios. 20:34, bad Markos. 1:41) bad la batai ia ka ha ki pharshi jong u nong Samaria babha (Lukas. 10:33) bad u khun sarong awria (Lukas. 15:20).
5. Ka jingisynei. Ia kane ka jinglong, kaba la pyni nuksa da U Jisu, dei ban iohi ruh ha ka jingim jong ki nongbud jong U (Lukas. 6:36).
6. Kiba ialong kajuh ka jingmut, ki badon kajuh ka jingieit, ki ba don kawei ka mynsiem hi, kiba don kawei ka jingmut. Kaei ka jinglong! Ka long kaba eh shisha ban mutdur kumno u Paul u lah ban ban jur ia ka jingdonkam jong ka jingiatylli. Kumba u Paul u kdew, ia ka jingmut kaba ngi dei ban don “kaba la long ruh ha U Khrist Jisu” (Philipi 2:5).
