---
title:  Ka Jinglong Ba La Tynjuh
date:   28/01/2026
---

La kdew ia ka bynta jong u Timothi kum u nongiaphah lang ia kane ka shithi (Philipi. 1:1). Mynta u Paul u sdang ban batai bniah haduh katno u Timothi u long uba kordor kum uwei na ki paralok nongtrei jong u. La batai ia u kum u Evangelist (2 Timothi. 4:5) ia uba u Paul u la phah sha Makedonia (1 Thes. 3:2; nujor ki Kam 18:5, Kam 19:22) bad, ha ki katto katne ki khep, sha Korinth (1 Kor. 4:17, 1 Korinth. 16:10). Mynshuwa u la trei bad u Paul bad u Silas ha Korinth (1 Thessoloni. 1:1, 2 Thessoloni. 1:1) bad hadien ha Ephesos (1 Timothi. 1:2, 3; nujor bad ki Kam 19:22). U Paul u batai ia u Timothi kum uba “long kajuh ka jingmut” (Philipi. 2:20, NW). Ka kyntien Grik (lit. “uba iaryngkat ha ka mynsiem”) ka pyni ba u la long kum u Paul ha kiba bun ki liang, kynthup ia ka jingaiti jong u ha U Khrist, ka jingpyrshang ba shlur jong u ban pynphriang ia ka gospel, bad ka jingsngewkhia jong u kyrpang ia ki nong Philipi.

`Balei phi tharai ba u Paul u kren bha bad slem bha shaphang u Timothi hangne (peit Philipi. 2:19-23)? Kaei pat kaba u Paul u ong shaphang jong u (peit 1 Korinth. 4:17, 2 Timothi. 1:5)?`

Kawei pat ka jinglong jong u Timothi kaba la kdew da u Paul ka long ka “jinglong ba la Tynjuh” jong u (Philipi. 2:22, NW). Ka ktien Grik ka batai ia u briew uba la shah tynjuh bha da ki jingtynjuh (Rom 5:4) bad uba ka jinglong bad ka jingshakri jong u ka la paw ba ka long kaba shisha (2 Korinth. 2:9, 2 Korinth. 9:13). U Paul u tip ba kane ka long kaba shisha shaphang u Timothi namar u la iohi ba la pynpaw lyngba ki jingjia kiba bun ba ki la iatrei lang ha kaba pynphriang ia ka gospel.

Dei ki jingiashem kiba eh jong ka jingim kiba tynjuh ia ka mynsiem jong ngi bad kiba pyni ia ngi kaei kaba ngi don na shapoh ka dohnud. Ka Ellen G. White ka buh ia ka kumne: “Ka jingim ka long ka jinghikai... Kin don ki jingpynkhih ban tynjuh ia ka jingbitar; bad dei da kaba iakynduh ia kine ha ka mynsiem kaba bha ba ki jingaiei Khristan ki nangroi. Lada ia ki jingpynmynsaw bad ki jingkren bein la shah da ka jingjemnud, lada ia ki kyntien pynjah burom la jubab da ki jubab kiba jem, ia ki jingleh kiba sniew la siewkylliang da ka jingsbun, kine ki long ki sakhi kiba U Mynsiem jong U Khrist u shong ha ka dohnud.” — Ka Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 344.. Ka iathuh shuh shuh ba lada “ki jingeh bad ki jingpynsngewsih kiba la khot ia ngi ban shah” ki long “kiba la shah shitom bha, ki pynroi ia ka jinglong kum U Khrist ha ka jinglong bad ki pyniapher ia u Khristan na u briew na ka jinglong jong ka pyrthei.”— Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, ka p. 344.

`Pyrkhat ia ki jingpynkhih, ki jingeh bad ki jingpynsngewsih kiba phi la iakynduh dang shen. Hato kine ki long kiba la “shah da ka jingjemnud” bad “ka jingshah shitom”? Kaei kaba phi lah ban leh ban pynlah ia kine ki jingiashem ban iarap ban pynlong ia phi uba kham sngur?`
