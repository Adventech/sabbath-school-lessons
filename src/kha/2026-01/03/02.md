---
title:  Ia U Khrist La Pynkhraw
date:   11/01/2026
---

`Pule Philipi 1:19 – 20. Kaei kaba I kum ka jingkhmih lynti jong U Paul kum kajingmih na kane ka jingtynjuh? Kaei kaba u pynpaw ba ka long kaba khamdonkam ban ia kaba shah pyllait?`

Watla U Paul um dei u riew sniew, kane kam shym la dei ka sien kabanyngkong ba u la shah set byndi, bad um long u nongwei na ka jingshah pynshitom. Sha ki nong Korinth, u batai shai ia ka jingshah shitom haduh kata ka por: “ha ki ïing byndi kham bunsien, ha ki jingiap bunsien. Na ki Jiw san sien nga la ioh sawphew nawei thri. Lai sien la shoh ia nga da ki dieng sympat, shi sien la lynthem maw ia nga, lai sien nga la shah paitlieng, shimiet shisngi nga la don ha ka duriaw bajylliew: ha ki jingleit bun sien, ha ki jingma na ki wah, ha ki jingma na ki nonglute, ha ki jingma na la ki para jaid, ha ki jingma na ki Jentil, ha ki jingma ha ka nongbah, ha ki jingma ka khlaw, ha ki jingma ha ka duriaw, ha ki jingma hapdeng ki lok baarsap: ha ka jingtrei minot bad ka jingtrei shitom, ha ki jingbymiohthiah bunsien, ha ka jingthngan bad ka jingsliang, ha ki jingshah jingit bunsien, ha ka pjah bad ka jinglyngkhuid,” (2 Korinth 11:23 – 27).

Hynrei ioh ngi pyrkhat ba kine ki jingjynjar ki long kiba hakhmat eh ha ka jingmut jong u, u Paul u la kren shuh shuh, “nalor kita kiei kiei ruh kiba nabar. Don ruh kaba ban ia nga ha la ka sngi, ka jingsumar ia ki balang baroh kein” (2 Korinth 11:28).

`Pule 1 Korinth 4:14 – 16; 1 Thesaloni 2:10, 11; Kalatia 4:19; bad Philemon10. Ka jingiadei aiu kaba u Paul u don bad ki Balang kiba u la seng bad kibriew kiba u la jop sha u Khrist?`

Kum U Jisu u bym shym la tyngkai ia kaei kaei khnang ban pynim iangi, U Paul u sngewbha ban “pynlut bad shah pynlut noh khoit” na ka bynta jong ki parabangeit (2 Korinth 12:15). Hynrei ka long markhongpong, katba ki jingleh jong ubriew ki iasyriem ia kita ki jong U Khrist, katta kan nang duna ka jingiet bad ka jingiaroh na ki katto katne. “Bad baroh kiba mon ban imriewblei ha U Khrist Jisu kin shah lehbein, (2 Timothi 3:12). Hynrei ki khristan kiba iaineh ki iaisah, lehse ha ka rukom kaba donbor ban pyndonburom ia U Blei bad ban pynpaw ia ka jingshisha jong ka Kospel (ianujor ba Philipi 1:7). “Ka jingiaishah bad ka jingkmen jong u Paul lyngba ka por kaba jrong jong ka jingshah set byndi bym shong Nongrim, ka jingshlur bad ka jingngeit, ki la long ka jingialap kaba iai bteng.” Ellen G. White, Ki Kam Jong Ki Apostol, sla, 464.

`Peit kumno phi im bad phi leh ia ki briew, khamtam ia ki briew ki bym leh bha iaki. kaba kumno Ka jingsakhi shaphang U Jisu ba ngi pynpaw?`
