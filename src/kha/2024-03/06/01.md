### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Markos 7, Isaiah 29:13, Eksodos 20:12, Markos 8:11-21.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Ym don ei ei ruh shabar u briew, kaba leit hapoh jong u, ba lah ban pyntngit ia u: hynrei kiei kiei ruh kiba mih noh na u briew, kita kein ki long kiba pyntngit ia u briew’ ” (Markos 7:15).

Ka jingpule mynta ka taiew kadei na u Markos 7 bad shiteng na ka bynta kaba nyngkong ka lynnong 8. Ha kaba sdang u Markos 7, U Jisu u pynkhih ia ka jingiakren da ka jingkyntait ia ki jinghikai pateng. La katta ruh, U leh ia kane ha kata ka rukom kaba kyrshan bha ia kaei kaba don jingiadei bad ka jingim Khristan mynta ki sngi.

U Jisu kumta u iathuh ia ka jingkren kaba wanrah ia ka jingplie ia ka jingkhang sha ka jingsngewthuh shaphang ka jingngeit.

Haba U la leit sha Tyros bad Sidon u la iakynduh bad kawei ka briew kaba tang ma ka hi ka briew kaba jop ia ka jingiapyni nia bad U Jisu. Ka jingiakynduh jong U bad ka ka long ka bym ju kham jia koit, bad ha kane ka jingiakynduh don katto katne ki jingkren ba rieh kaba kane ka briew ka pynpaw. Bad namar ka jingngeit jong ka, U Jisu u la ai ia ka jingkyrpad jong ka.

Ha u Markos 7, ryngkat bad kiwei ki kam jingpynkoit, ka pynpaw ia ka jingshisha ba donkam—kampher katno khraw ka long ka kambah, kita marwei kin ym lah ban plie ia ki dohnud sha ka jingshisha. Haba khadduh hi, kaei ka jingmyntoi jong ki kambah ia ki nongialam niam kiba la kut jingmut ban kyntait ia U Jisu?

Ha u Markos 8, ka jingiapule ka i ia ka jingdonkam jong u kpu kum ka dur pharshi jong ki jinghikai bad ki jinghikai pateng. Kine ki khana ki don ki jinghikai bakhraw shaphang ka jingmut bad ka jingleh jong ka jingim riewblei.

_*Pule ia kane ka lynnong ban Pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, 10._
