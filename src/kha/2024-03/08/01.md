### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Markos 10; Jenesis. 1:27; Jenesis. 2:24; Gal. 4:1, 2; Rom. 6:1–11; Isa. 11:1–16.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Naba shisha U Khun u briew um shym la wan ban ioh shakri, hynrei ban shakri, bad ban ai la ka jingim ban long ka jingsiewspah na ka bynta kiba bun” (Markos 10:45, NKJV).

Ha kane ka taiew ngin ia peit Markos 10, ban pyndep ia kane ka bynta ba kyrpang ha kaba U Jisu u la hikai ia ki synran jong U ha ka ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka diengphna. La kumba shiteng jong kane ka lynnong ka dei tang shaphang ki synran hi, bad kiba sah ki kren sha phang ki jingmih kiba donkam na ka bynta ki synran kiba la iathuh lyngba ka jingiohi jong kiwei kiba la ia shem bad U Jisu. Ki Pharisi ki la wan ban ia tainia bad U Jisu halor ka jingia pyllait Tnga. Ki kmie ki kpa ki wanrah ia ki khun sha U Jisu ba un jingkyrkhu. U samla uba kylli shaphang ka jingim ba bymjukut, bad u briew u ba matlah u pan ia ka jingiohi.

Kane ka lynnong jong U Markos ka don ia ka jinghikai ba donkam shaphang kumno ban bud ia U Jisu, kyrpang eh kumba la iathuh ban im hangne bad mynta: ka shongkha, ki khun, kumno ban ia thuh ba ka spah bad ka bainong bad ka dor haba bud ia U Jisu. Ha kaba pynkhiah ia u ba matlah u ba ar (Markos 10:46–52; ia nujor bad Markos 8:22–26), kaba ai ia ka ba kut jong kane ka bynta jong ka lynnong (Mark 8:22–10:52) bad ka nuksa kaba itynnad ba kumno kaba bud ia U Jisu ka don ka dor.

Ryngkat kane ka lynnong ka pynkhreh ia ki nongbud jong U Jisu – la ka long ki synran 2000 snem mynshuwa lane ki synran ha kane ka spah snem kaba ar phew wei – bad ki jingeh kiba wan ha jong long synran.

_*Pule ia kane ka lynnong ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, August 24_
