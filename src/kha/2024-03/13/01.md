### Ki Dkhot Baibl Ban Pynkhrah ia ka Lynnong
Markos 15:42–47, Markos 16, Kol. 2:10–12, 1 Kor. 15:1–8, Dan. 9:24–27, Ioannis 20:11–18.

> <p>Dkhot Spel lyndet</p>
> “U te u la ong ha ki, Wat sheptieng: phi wad ia U Jisu uba na Nasaret, ia uba la sahnarphna? u la mihpat, um don hangne: ha khmih ka jaka ha kaba ki ia iabuh ia u ” (Markos 16:6, NKJV).

Ka jingshah sahnarphna jong U Jisu ka pynduh ia ka jingkyrmen bad ka jingngeit jong ki synran jong U. Ka la long ka taiew kaba dum ia ki, katba ki dang ia khuslai ia ka jingiap jong U Kynrad hynrei ki la ia sheptieng ruh na ka bynta ka jingim jong ki ruh. (Ioannis 20:19).

Ha u Markos 16, ka lynnong kaba khatduh ha kane ka Gospel, ngin ia peit kaei kaba bud ia ka jingiap jong U.

Nyngkong, ngin ia peit ia ka por jong ka jingmih pat jong U Jisu bad balei ba ki kynthei ki wan sha ka jingtep ha ka sngi Robibar dang step. Ki Adventist ki don ia kaei re kaei kaba ki la phai noh na ka step jong ka jingmihpat namar ba ki la pyndonkam bakla ban kyrshan ia ka jinglong kyntang jong ka robibar. Ngin ia peit ba kumno ngin ia leh kmen ha ka robibar ka jingmihpat, wat la ki theology ba bakla ki la don, sngewsih ba ka la mih na ka.

Ka ba-ar, kane ka lynnong ka batai ia ka dkhot kaba nyngkong u Markos 16, ka pyniadei ia kane ka dkhot sha ka mat pdeng ka ba la iaid lyngba ia ka kitab baroh kawei. Ka jingiapule ha ka sngi ba ar bad ka sngi ba san ngin ia peit ia kine ki jingpyrkhat.

Ba-lai, ka sngi ba saw bad ka sngi ba san kan peit bniah baroh ka lynnong jong u Markos 16 bad ngin ia pyrkhat I ka mission ba la buh ha khmat jong ngi. Kane ka jingiapule ka pynkut bad ka jingiakob ia ki nongpule ia ka kitab u Markos ban shim ia ka Gospel sha ka pyrthei baroh ka wei.

Third, Wednesday and Thursday will examine the rest of Mark 16 and consider the mission it sets before us. This study will close with a challenge to the reader of Mark to take the gospel throughout the world.

_*Pule ia kane ka lynnong ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, September 28._
