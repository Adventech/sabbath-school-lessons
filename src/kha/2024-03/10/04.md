---
title:  Ka Jingijli Bapynduh
date:   03/09/2024
---

`Pule Markos 13:14–18. Kiei ki dak kiba U Jisu u ai haba khmih thuh ia kine ki kyntien “Ka Jingijli bapynduh” bad ka thew ne kdew iano?`

U Jisu U Kdew sha ka mat ba kongsan shaphang ka jingkyllon jong ka Jerusamlem ha u Markos 13:14. U kdew ruh sha “Ka Jingijli bapynduh”. U Trai u ong ba ki nongpule ki dei ban sngewthuh. Da kine ki kyntien, U Jisu u pyni ia ki synran sha ka kitab jong u Danial. Kine ki kyntien ki paw tang ha u Daniel 9:27, Daniel 11:31, bad Daniel 12:11, kaba don jingiasyriem ha u Daniel 8:13.

`Pule Daniel 9:26, 27. U ei “U Ta uba la sleh” bad u ei “U ta u syiem u ban wan”?`

“Uta uba la sleh”ha u Danial 9:26, ha ka ktien Hebru “Masiah” ha ka English, “Messiah”. Ha ka jingpule bha ia u Danial 9: 24-27, ka long kaba shai ba une uba la sleh ka thew sha ka jingwan ka jong U jisu Khrist.

Hynrei uei “uta u syiem uban wan” u ban wan rah ia ka jingpynjot ia ka nongbah Jerusalem? Ia ka nongbah la pynjot da u nongialam shipai Rom u Titus. Hato ka long kaba dei, ba u dei “uta u syiem uban wan” kaba kdew ha u Daniael 9: 26-27. Kine ki ar ngut ki don ka jingiadei namar ka lynti kaba U Messiah u la leh ka kren ia ka jingjot jong ka nongbah.

Kaei kane, “Ka Jingijli bapynduh” kaba U Jisu U Kdew sha u Danial ka kren? Kaba sngewsih, kaba bun ki nongwat bniah ki ngeit ba kane ka Jingijli ka kdew sha phang ka jingpyntngit u Antiochus Epiphanes’s ia ka templ ha ka spah snem kaba ar B.C. Kata kam dei. U Jisu u batai “Ka Jingijli bapynduh” kaei re kaei kaba la jia ha dien ka por jong U hangne, ka long kaba eh ban kdew sha ka ba jia ar spah snem shuwa ka jingtrei ka jong U Khrist ha pyrthei.

Ha ka jingshsha, ka jingijli ka kdew sha ka jingthmu jong ka Rom nongmane bleithaw ban pynieng ia ka lama Israel haka por ba ki leit hiar thma ia ka Jerusalem ha ka bakut jong ka snem 60 A.D. kane ka long ka dak na ka bynta ki Khristan ba kin phet iap, bad ki la phet.

Kumba U Jisu u la iathuhlupa, ka Jerusalem ka la kyllon. Kumno ngi dei ban nang ban shaniah ha U bad ha baroh ki jingiathulupa jong ka Baibl?
