---
title:  Ka Daw Jong ki Pharshi
date:   23/07/2024
---

`Pule Markos 4:10–12. Balei U Jisu u hikai ha ki pharshi?`

Ka jingpule kaba tang na jrong ia kine ki dkhot ka ai ia ka jingshon jingmut kaba U Jisu u hikai ha ki pharshi ka buh ia kiba na shabar ha ka jingdum. Kum kita ki jingiohi kim ia hap ha ka jingleh ka jong U Jisu ha u Markos. Ha u Mark 3:5, 6, U Jisu u la sngewsih da ka jingeh dohnud jong ki nongialam niam. Mark 3: 22-30, U Jisu u shim khia ia ka jingiatai nia jong ki nongthoh bad la batai bniah balei ba ki long kiba bakla. In Mark 12:1–12, ki nongialam niam ki sngewthuh ba ka ka pharshi ka jong U Jisu sha phang ki nongshong wai ka dei sha phang jong ki. Ha ka jingshisha ka dei ka jingmaham sha phang ki jingpyrkhat pyrshah kaba ialam sha phang jong U bad ka bainong kaba sniew ka ban wan. Lada ym shem la sngewlem na ka bynta jong ki, un ym jin da la maham ia ki. Ka jingmih na ki kyntien ki jong U Jisu ha u Markos 4 ka don kam ia ka jingkhmih ka ba jan ban ithuh kaei ka jingmut jong u. U Jisu u shim kyllum na u Isaiah 6: 9,10.

`Pule Isaiah 6:1–13. Kaei kaba jia ia u Isaiah hangne, bad kaei ka khubor kaba la ai ha u ban rah sha ki Israel?`

Ha ka jingithuhpaw u Isaiah u la iohi ia U Blei ha ka Temple bad la pyndap da ka burom ka jong U Blei bad ia ka jingjakhlia jong u. U Blei u la pynkhuid ia u bad u la ai hukum ia u bad ka khubor kaba pynkyndit ia u. Kum ia u Markos, I kumba kam don ka jingiadei kaba iaid ryngkat bad ka jong u Isaiah ha kaba la don ka jingsuk ia ki briew ki jong U Blei.

Ha Isaiah 6 ka khubor ka long ban pynkyndit ia ki briew ha kaba peit khnang ban kin phai na ka lynti ba sniew jong ki. Ha u Markos ban sngewthuh ia ki kyntien ki jong U Jisu la shem ha u Markos 3:35. Ban sngewthuh ia ki kyntien bad ki jinghikai U Jisu, ki dei ban leh ia ka mon ka jong U Blei (Markos 3:35). Kane ka ialam ia uta u brie sha ka longing ka jong U Jisu. Kito kiba la kut jingmut ba pyniaid U Jisu da u ksuid kin ym sngap ia U.

Ka jingthmu jong U Jisu ba u la shim na u Isaiah 6 kam dei ba U Blei u buh noh ia ki briew sha bar hynrei namar ki jingpyrkhat kiba la don ha ki bad ka jingeh ka dohnud ka la khang lad ia ki ban pdiang ia ka jingpynim ka jingshisha.

Kane ka jingshisha ka kham palat ia ka jingmut jong ka pharshi jong u nongbet. Uwei pa uwei u jied ka khyndew ka ba kumno un long. Baroh ki ia jied da lade la kin jied ne em ia U Jisu. Ha kaba kut, ngi jied hi ma ngi.
