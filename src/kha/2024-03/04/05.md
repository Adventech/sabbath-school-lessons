---
title:  Ka Sharak Bad Ka Shang ba Thew
date:   24/07/2024
---

`Pule Markos 4:21–23. Kaei ka jingpynleit jingmut ba kyrpang jong U Jisu ha ka pharshi jong ka sharak?`

Ki ȉing ki long shi bynta ha ka pyrthei ha ki sngi ki jong U Jisu ki ia pher ha ka jingheh bad ka jingtei, baroh ki shong ha ka jaka bad ka spah. Ki iing ki ia bud ia ka jong ki Greek ban tei saw dong ka kper bad ka jingiapher ha ki kyrdan jingitynnad. Lane U Jisu u kren sha phang ki iing kiba rit jong ki nongrep. Ka iing kaba heh ne kaba rit, ka tynrai ka long kane; ha kawei ka sngi ia ka jingshisha sha phang U Jisu yn da pynpaw.

U Jisu u kylli ia ki ar tylli ki Jingkylli ha U Markos 4:21. Ka ba nyngkong la khmih lynti ia ka jubab ka bym sngew tynnad - Ki ju wallam ia ka sharak, ba yn buh hapoh ka shang, lane hapoh ka jingthiah’ kadei kumta?” Ka jingkylli kaba ar la khmih lynti ia ka jubab kaba sngew tynnad – “la wanrah ba yn buh ha ka dieng pynieng sharak, ka dei kumta?” U Jisu u la wanrah ia kane ka mat kaba kham pher ban kham pynsngewthuh. Ki sharak ki dei ban ai ka jingshai, lym kumta kin duhnoh ia ka jingmut jong ka. Markos 4:22 ka pynshai ia ka pharshi da ka ba kdew ia ka jingmut ba rieh ia kaba la pynpaw paitbah. Ia kano kano ka email lane ka computer ia kaba la shah plie tuh ngi dei ban sngewthuh ba ia kaba rieh jong ki la pynpaw paitbah! Hynrei kaei kaba U Jisu u kren ka dei ia ka Gospel.

`Pule Markos 4:24, 25. Kaei ka jinghikai ba U Jisu u ai da ka pharshi jong ka shang ba thew?`

Ha kiba bun ki bynta jong ka pyrthei, ia ki mar kiba thymmai ba la pynmih la ju die ha iew die lang. Ki nongdie ki don ia ki tiar jingthew ba ki thew ia ki mar kiba ki die. Ka long kaba ju leh paitbah jong ki nongdie ban pyntam sa khyndiat sha ki nongthied ban iarap ia ki nongthied ba kin sngew ba ia ki la leh ia kaba dei. U Jisu u la shim ba kumno u nong die babha u leh ia ki nong thied ban iohi ia ka jingshisha. Bad lade iwei I pynpaw bad bud ia ka jingshai , u ne ka kin ioh kham bun shuh shuh. Hynrei lada u ne ka ki kyntait ia ka jingshai, wat ia kaei kaba ki don mynshuwa yn shim noh ia ki.

`Kumno ngi lah ban sngewthuh khambha ia ki ain kiba kumno phi pyndon kam ban thew yn thew pat ia phi? Pyrkhat ia kane ha baroh kaba iadei bad kiwei?`
