Read Ellen G. White, “Lebi-Matthaios,” pp. 272–280; “Ka Sabbaton,” pp. 281–289, ha Ka jingthrang jong ki arta.

“ Haba la kylli: “ ka bit ne -em ban pynkhiah ha ka sngi Sabbaton?” U Jisu U la jubab, “ U no U briew uba na phi, uba don kawei ka langbrot, lada kata ka hap ruh ha ka thliew ha ka sngi Sabbaton, un ym shim bad rah ia ka? U briew te katno u kham kor ia ka langbrot? Kumta ka bit ban leh bha ha ka sngi Sabbaton.” Mathaios 12:1 0-12.

“ kine ki nongsyntiat kim shym la nud ban jubab ia U Khrist ha khmat uta u paid byllien, namar ki la sheptieng ioh ki pynngat ia lade ha ka jingeh. Ki la tip ba U la kren ia ka jingshisha. Ban ia ka ba pynkhein ia ki jinghikai pateng jong ki. Kin ieh ia u briew ban shah jynjar, katba kin pyllait ia u mrad jong ki namar kan long ka jingduhnong ia u trai jong uta u mrad iada ieh ba un iap. Kum ne la kham niew kor bad kham sumar ieit ia u mrad uba khlem jingtip -jing shem phang ei-ei ban ia u briew, ia uba la thaw ha ka dur jong u Blei hi. Kane ka pyni ia ka rukom trei jong baroh ki niam kiba bakla. Ki mih tynrai na ka jingkwah jong u briew ban kyntiew ia lade kham shaneng ia U Blei, hynrei ki long pynban kiba pynpoh ia u briew kham hapoh ia u mrad. Kawei-pa-kawei ka niam kaba ialeh thma pyrshah ia ka jingsynshar jong U Blei ka lute na u briew ia ka burom kaba long ka jong u ha ka jingpynlong, bad ia kaba yn sa pynioh pat sha u ha U Khrist. Kawei-pa-kawei ka niam kaba bakla ka hikai ia kito kiba pyniasoh sha ka ban long ki bym salia ei-ei ia ka jingdonkam jong ka jingim briew, ia ka jing shah shitom, bad ia ki hok jong ka long briew -man briew . Ka Gospel ka khien kordor eh ia ka kynja bynriew kum kata kaba la thied da ka snam jong U Khrist, bad ka hikai ban niew kor ia ki jingkyrduh bad ki jingshem lanot jong u briew . U Trai U ong” Ngan kham pynkordor ia u briew ban ia ka ksiar banylla ka Ophir.” Isaiah 13:1 2.” – Ellen G. White , Ka Jingthrang ki Yrta, sla 364 ,365 .

**Ki Jingkylli ban ia Puson**:

`Kaei ka buit trei kamne ka rukom trei kamkaba iarap ia phi bad ia ka balang jong phi ban long kiba kloi ban iarap jar jar ia ki briew ki ba shitomkumuba iap shiliang ha u Markos 2?`

`Pyrkhat shaphang kumno la pynmatlah da ka jingisih, ki jingiathuhkhana pateng,ki jinghikai tynrai, bad ka niam ba ki nongialamniamkiba kyntait ia u Jisu ki la kylla long kiba eh wat ki kambah jong U kim pli shuh ia ki jingmut jingpyrkhat jong ki sha U. Kumno ngin lah kumki paitbah ban phikir ba kiei kiei kiba syriemia kine kin ymjia ha ngi?`

`Kumno ka balang jong phi ka lah ban long “ ka iing ka sem” ia kito kiba, ki ba ha iing ha semjong ki, ki la kyntait ia ki na ka daw jong ka jingngeit jong ki?`

`Pynshong nia ne iakren kham bniah halor ka jingkylli shaphang “ ki pop ki bymlah map” ha ki klass jong phi, iakren bniah ruh ka mut aiu bad kumno ngin tikna ba ngim palat ia ka.`
