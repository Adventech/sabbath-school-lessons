### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Markos 2 :1-3,6, Mikah 6:6-8, 1 Samuel 2 1:1-6, Markos 3:20-35, Loukas 14 :16.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Te u la ong ha ki la thaw ia ka sabbaton na ka bynta u briew, ymu briew na ka bynta ka Sabbaton : ba kumta te U Khun u briew u long U Trai ka sabbaton ruh” (Markos 2:27,28.)

Ha ka Gospel u Markos 2:1-3,6, d on san tylli ki jingiathuhkhana kib a pynpaw ia ki jinghikai U Jisu kiba long pyrshah ia ki jinghikai jong ki nongialam niam . Kine ki jingiathuhkana ki don ia ka rukom iathuh kaba kyrpang , ia kaba ki don ka jingiadei ne ka jingiasnoh bad kaba don ha shwa lyngba ki subjek kiba iasyriem. K a jing iathuhkhana kaba khatduh ka iaid pyllun nangta ka iasnoh biang bad kata kaba nyngkong.

Kawei pa kawei na kine ki jingiathuhkhana ki pynpaw ia ki mat kiba iathuh ba U Jisu u de i uei, kum ba la pynshai haka gospel u Markos 2:10,17,20,28. K a jingp ule jo ng ngi haka Sngi Nyng kong, Sngi Ba Ar bad Sngi Ba Lai kan tih jylliew ia ki jingmut jong kitei ki jingiathuhkhana bad ia ki jingbatai U Jisu ia ki.

Markos 3:20-35 ka dei ka subjek ia kaba ngin ia pule haka sngi kaba saw bad kaba san. Kaei kab a ngin iohi hang ne ka d ei ka nuksa jong ka jingstad ki nong thoh gospel ki pyndonkam ia kaba la khot “ ki jingiathuhkhana ba la ia khleh” (sandwich stories) kane ka rukom iathuh ka paw kum ba hynriew sien ha u Markos. Ha man la kawei pa kawei ka jingjia don katto kattne ki mat kiba kdew ia ka jinglong U Jisu bad ka kam ram jong U kum u Messaiah, lane ka jinglong jong ki nong bud jong U , kane ka dei ka pdeng ban pyrkhat.

_*Pule ia kane kane ka lynnong, ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, July 20_
