`Pule Mark 2:1-12. Kaei kaba uba iap shilliang u khmih lynti haba la ialam ia u sha u Jisu, bad kaei kaba ula ioh pdiang?`

Hadien ba ki la tar ne kad ia ka tnum iing, ki la pynhiar ia u ha ka jaka kab a U Jisu u don, Markos 2 :5 ka kdew ba u Jisu u iohi ia ka jingngeit jong ki, kumno lah ban iohi ia ka jingngeit? Kumba ia ka jingieit, lah ban iohi lyngba ki kam , kum ta ka jingleh kine ki paralok jong uba iap shilliang ruh ka pyni.

Ka jingdonkam kaba kyrkih jong une u briew ka dei ia ka met. Hynrei, ynda u wan poi ha U Jisu, ki kyntien banyngkong ba U Jisu u kre ]n ka kde ]w sha ka jingmap ia ki pop. U ne u briew um shem kren wat shi kyntien ha baroh kane ka jingjia. Pynb an, ki dei ki nong ialam niam kiba wohdaw (ha ki jingmut/jingpyrkhat) ia kaei kaba u dang shuh dep kren. Ki niew ia ki kyntien jong u Jisu kum ka jing kren bein Blei, bad ba shim ia ka bor kaba de i tang ka jong u Blei.

U Jisu u ia kynduh ne iatai nia ia ki ba wat daw ia u haka buit jong ki hi, da kaba u pyndonkam da ka rukom hikai tynrai ki nonghikai ba khraw ki Jiw , haba ki iatai nia kaba khot “ na kiba rit sha kiba khraw” ka dei kaei kaei kaba khraw ban ong ba ia ki pop jong u/ka

briew la map; ka dei da kum wei pat ban pynlah ia u briew uba iap shilliang ban iaid pat. Lada u Jisu U lah ban pyniaid pat ia u briew da ka bor ka jong u Blei, te ka jingkam jong u ban map ia ki pop ka shong dor ban ioh iaka jingpynskhem .

`Pule Mikah 6:6-8. Kumno kine ki dkhot ki batai ia kaei kaba jia hapdeng u Jisu bad ki nongialam niam?`

Kine ki nongialam niam ki la long kiba m atlah ia kiei kie i kaba donkam shisha: ka jingbishar hok, ka jingaiei, bad ka jingiaid rit hakhmat U Blei. Ki jingmut jingpyrkhat jong ki la ker kut tang ban iada ia ka rukom sngew thuh jong ki shaphang U Blei, ia ki la pynmatlah ban iohi ia ka jingtrei U Blei ha pyrshah ki khmat jong ki. Kam don kano kano ka dak kaba pyni ba kine ki briew ki la kylla ha ki jingmut jingpyrkhat jong ki shaphang u Jisu wat lada ula pyni ha ki

shibun ki dak ki shin ba kin tip ba u wan na u Blei, ym dei tang a U shah ia ki ban tip, ba U lah wat ban pule ia ki jingmut jingpyrkhat jong ki (kam dei ka kam kaba suk) hynrei U la p ynkhiah ruh ia kiba iap shilliang ha khmat jong ki hi ba kin ym lah ban len.

`Kumno ngi lah ban sumar ban lait na ka juh ka jingriam ba kine ki briew ki la hap: ka jingshongshit jong ki ha ka rukom ka niam jong ki, haduh ba ki la kylla matlah ia kaei kaba donkam shisha haka balang kaba shisha.`
