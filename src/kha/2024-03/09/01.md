### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Markos 11:1; 1 ki syiem 1:32 – 48; Sekharaiah 9:9, 10; Isaiah 56:7; Jeremiah 7:11; Markos 12:1 – 34.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Haba phi da ieng kyrpad ruh, to map, lada phi bat sngewsih iano iano ruh, ba U Kpa jong phi ruh uba ha bneng un map ia phi ia ki ryngkang jong phi” Markos 11:25.

Ki san tylli ki jingialeh kawei hadien kawei, hapdeng jong U Khrist bad ki nongialam niam kiba la buh jingkynmaw ha u Markos 2 bad 3 lynnong (peit ha ka lynnong kaba 3). Ha ka jingiapule ha kane ka taiew, haba u Jisu u poi ha Jerusalem, u la don ia ki hynriew tylli ki jingialeh bad ki nongialam niam. Ki ar bynta jong ki jingialeh ki long kum ka jingkut jong ka jingtrei kam jong u ha pyrthei. Kawei pa kawei ka bynta ka iadei bad ki mat kiba donkam ha ka jingim Khristan. Ki jingbthah jong u Jisu, wat ha ki khep jong ki jingiapyrshah kum kine, ki iarap ban ia lam ia kiba ngeit ha baroh arliang, ha ki bynta tynrai jong ka jingngeit bad ha ki bynta kiba jia shisha ka jingiashem ba man la ka sngi.

Ki nongialam balang ki wan ban iania pyrshah, ban pynkulmar jingmut, ban jop ia u Jisu, hynrei kim ju jop. Shi bynta jong ka jingiapule ha kane ka taiew kan kynthup ia ka jingiakhmih bniah ia kaei kaba pynlong ia ki briew ba kin iap yrshah ia U Blei bad ban ia pyrkhat kumno ki khristan ki lah ban leh ban pynduh ia ka jinglong jong ka jingmutdurlypa, bad ban kren sha ki dohnud jong kito kiba kyntait ia ka jingkhot U Mynsiem.

Ha u Markos 11, ka kam jong U Jisu kan long ha Jerusalem na ka bynta ka niam ka jinglait (lber haduh u iaiong). Markos 11 – 16 ka kynthup kham bun khyndiat ban ia ka shi taiew; ka por jong ka jingjia ka la kham suki. Ki shiphew tylli ki lynnong kiba shakhmat ki kren ia ki jingjia ha ki lai snem shiteng. Kane ka jingsuki ka kdew sha ki jingdonkam jong kine ki jingjia ha kaba kut.

_*Pule ia kane ka lynnong ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, August 31._
