Pule ia ka kot, “Ministry,” sla. 426–431ba thoh daka Ellen G.White, bad “uei uba kham khraw?” sla 577-583, haka kot “Ka Jingthrang Jong Ki Yrta.”

“Ha shuwa ka burom ka don ka jingsngewrit. Ban pyndap ia ka jaka ba hajrong ha khmat ki briew. ka Bneng ka jied ia u nongtrei uba, kum u loannis Nongpynbaptis. u shim ia ka jaka kaba hapoh ha khmat jong U Blei. U synran uba syriem eh ia u khynnah u dei u nongtrei uba tbit tam ha ka jingtrei naka bynta U Blei. Ki angel jong ka bneng ki lah ban iatrei lang bad uta uba iaiwad. ym ban kyntiew ia lade. hynrei ban pynim ia ki mynsiem.” Ellen G. White, “Ka Jingthrang Jong Ki Yrta.” sla. 577.

“Ia kata baroh kaba la ai ha ngi kum ka kabu halor kiwei pat.---la ka dei ka jingnang-jingstad bad kaakor ba-itynnad, ka jinglong badonburom, ka jinghikai Khristan, ka jingiashem bakynja-niam,---ngi kitram ha kito kiba kham duna ban ia ngi; bad. katba ka shong ha ka bor jong ngi. ngi dei ban shakri ia ki. Lada ngi long kiba khlain, ngi dei ban kyrshan ia ki kti jong kito kiba tlot. Ki angel jong ka burom. kiba khmih barobor ia ka khmat jong U Kpa Uba ha bneng, ki shadkmen ban shakri ia ki barit. Ki mynsiem kiba khynniuh. kiba don shibun ki jingsniew ha ka jinglong jong ki, ki dei kita ia kiba la sumar kyrpang da ki. Ki angel ki iai don barobor ha ka jaka ba la donkam eh ia ka jingiarap jong ki, ki don bad kito kiba don ka thma kaba shyrkhei eh ban ialeh, bad kiba don ha ka jinglong-jingman kaba duh jingkyrmen tam. Te ha kum kane ka jingshakri ki nongbud bashisha jong U Khrist kin iatrei-lang.” Ellen G. White, “Ka Jingthrang Jong Ki Yrta.” sla. 582-583

**Ki Jingkylli ban ia Puson**:

`Pule biang ia Markos 8:27-29. Bad pykhat katno sien phi la ju phla shaphang u Jisu sha kiwei?`

`Kaei ka jinghikai ba ngi ioh hapdeng ka jingiashem ryngkat bad u khrist kaba halor u lum bad sha trai jong u lum ha kaba iadei ban shakri ia ka jingdonkam jong kiwei?`

`Haka klas, iakren ia ka jubab haba ia dei bad ka jingkylli shaphang uei uba kham khraw jong ka sngi kaba saw. Kaei phi lap ia ka jingiapher hapdeng ka jingbatai jong ka pyrthei bad ka jong U Blei? Iathuh katto katne ngut ki briew haka pyrthei kaba ki briew ki ñiewkor bad lehse U Blei um ñiewkor? Ka jingiapher, lehse mano kita kiba ha kane ka pyrthei ki kyntiat hynrei ha khmat U Blei ki long kiba la pdianghun? Kaei ba kane ka pyrthei ka iathuh ia ngi, ka long kaba jyrthiaw bad kaba kyllaiñ haduh kata kata.`

`Kaei ka jinghikai ba phi ioh ha kaba iadei ban shim khia ia ka pop, kumba u Jisu u la ong, ka bha ba phin long kiba don jingdkoh haka met ban ia kaba leh pop?`
