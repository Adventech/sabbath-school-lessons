---
title:  U Lum bah Bad U Paidbah
date:   13/08/2024
---

`Pule Markos9:1–13. Kaei kaba u U Petros, U Jakob, U loannis, bad ki iohi ha kawei ka miet bad u Jisu?`

Ha u Markos 9:1, U jisu u la iathuhlypa ba don kiba na kine kiba ieng hangne, kiban ym mad ia ka jingiaptadynda ki la iohi ia ka hima U Blei ba ka la wan da ka bor. La pynurlong ia kane ka jingiathuhlypa tang hadien katto katne sngi ynda U Jisu u shim lem ia U Petros, Jakob, bad ia U loannis, bad u leit lam ia ki sha u lum hajrong kyrpang hi; bad hangta la pynkylladur ia u ha khmat jong ki ha ka burom ba kynja bneng.

U Elias lem bad U Moses, kila mih paw bad ki da iakren bad U Jisu. U loukas u buh jingthoh ba ki ia kren shaphang ka jingleit noh jong u(Greek exodos), ia kaba un sa pyndep ha Jerusalem (Loukas9:30,31).namarkata ia kane ka bynta la iasnoh ryngkat bad ka jingiap u Jisu ia ban sa jia da ia kaba yn sahnarpna ia u (ianujor bad Markos 9:9).

Te haba ki dang hiar na uta u lumki la kylli ia u, ki da ong, Balei ba ki ong ki nongthoh ba ka dei ia U Elias ban wan shuwa. Lehse ia ki jingmut jong ki la teh ba u Elijah un mih paw ha khmat u Messiah (ianujor bad Malakhi 4:5,6).U Jisu u la jubab ba u Elijah u lah wan, kaba ieng ia u ioannis nongpynbaptist. Kumba ki la pyniap ia u ioannis kumta ruh u Jisu un iap ha ki kti jong ki, hynrei bad hadien lai sngi un mihpat.

Hadien kata miet, ha trai jong uta u lum pat ka dap da ki jingkulmar (piet Markos 9:14–29).ki khyndai ngut ki synran ki ia kynduh bad u khynnah ba shong ksuid bad kim lah ban pynkhiah ia u. ynda u Jisu u poi ha kata ka jaka, baroh ki la phet sha u ban peit ia u. kane ka jingiathuhkhana ka pynpaw ia ka bor jong u ksuid halorune u khynnah. Bad la ikumba u Jisu u shim por ban kyllibniah shaphang une u khynnah ba shong ksuid. Te kumne kumne ruh haba u kpa uta u khynnah, u la ong “hynrei lada me lah ei ei ruh, to isynei ia ngi. bad to iarap ia ngi.”(Markos 9:22).

U Jisu shisyndon u bat ia ki kyntien kaba pyni ba u artatien. Bad U Jisu u la pynphai ktien bad lehse da kaba ong “me mut aiu haba me ong, Lada me lah ban ngeit?(Markos9:23). Bad kynsan kum u tied u pyrthat ha sahit bneng, u kpa u sngewthuh ba ym dei tang u khun ba don ia ki jingeh hynrei ma u ruh u don ka jingeh ka jingbym ngiet, bad ka lah ban long dei na kane daw ba u khun jong u, um ioh jingpynkiah. Une u kpa bym don jingkyrmen u aiti ialade ha ka jingisynei u Jisu ryngkat bad ki kyntien basah jingkynmaw “Ah Trai; to iarap ia ka jingbymngeit jong nga.” (Markos 9:24). U Jisu u la pynkhiah ia une u khynnah.

`Ha kano ka por ne ka jingjia, ba phi la iam jam bad phi la ong Nga ngeit. Ah Trai; to iarap ia ka jingbymngeit jong nga? Kaei ka jinghikai phi ioh lyngba kane ka jingjia?`
