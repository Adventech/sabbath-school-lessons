`Pule Mark 1:14, 15. Kiei ki lai bynta jong ka khubor ka Gospel kiba U Jisu u la pynbna?`

U Markos kyllum lang hangne kaba suk bad kaba beit ka khubor ka jong U Jisu. Don lai tylli ki nuksa ba la ai harum.

| Ka kyrdan | Jingpyndep |
| --- | --- |
| Ka por jong ka jingiathuhlupa | Ka por jong jong ka jingpyndep. |
| Ka jingkular jong ka Jutang | Ka hima U Blei ka la jan. |
| Khot sha ka jinglong synran | Kylla kabamut bad ngeit ia ka Gospel. |

Ka por jong ka jingiathuhlupa ha kaba U Jisu ula kdew ka long 70 taiew jong ka jingiathuhlupa jong u Danial 9: 24-27. Ia kane ka jingiathuhlupa la pyndep ha ka jingpynbaptis jong U Jisu, ha kaba la pynsleh ia U da U Mynsiem Bakhuis ha ka jingsdang jong ka kam shakri jong U (Acts 10:38). Ka jingiathuhlupa ba phylla jong ka 70 taiew la pyni ha ka dur ha rum:

![Chart](https://sabbath-school-resources-assets.adventech.io/kha/ss/2024-03/01/chart.png)

Ha kane ka jingiathuhlupa, ka shi sngi ka iengpli ia ka shi snem (Num. 14:34, Ezek. 4:6). Kane ka jingiathuhlupa kala sdang ha ka 457 B.C. ia kaba la pynmih ia ka hukum da Artaxerxes, u syiem jong ka Persia, kaba pyndep ia ka jingtei pat ia ka Jerusalem (peit Ezra 7).

Hynriew phew khyndai taiew jong ka jingiathuhlupa ka poi sha duh ka 27 A.D. ha ka por ba ia U Jisu la pynbaptis bad la pynsleh da U Mynsiem Bakhuid ha ka jingsdang jong ka jingshakri jong U. Ia ka jingsahnarphna jong kan jia hadien lai snem shiteng.

Khatduh eh, ka jingpyndep ia ka hynniew phew taiew kan poi sha duh ka 34 A.D. ha kaba ia u Stephan la lynthem maw bad ia ka khubor jong ka Gospel ka la sdang ban leit sha ki Jentil bad kum juh ruh sha ki Jiw.
