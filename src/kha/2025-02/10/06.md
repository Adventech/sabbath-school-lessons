---
title:  Ka Jingbishar Ha Shuwa Ka Jingwan
date:   05/06/2025
---

Ka jingbatai shaphang ka jingbishar kaba la shem ha Daniel 7 ka ai ia ngi ia ka jingiohi ba luk-liak kaba shalynded jong ka pyrda — sha ka lad ba la pynkhih da ka jingtieng burom ban iohi kumno U Blei U pynbeit ia ka jingeh jong ka pop katba U dang siewspah ia kito kiba kwah ban im ha kajutang jong ka jingiadei bad U.

`Pule ia ka jingbatai shaphang ka jingbishar tohkit bniah ba la ai ha Daniel 7:9, 10, 13, 14, 22, 26, bad 27. Kaei ka jingthmu ba kongsan ha ka jingbishar? Kaei ka rai ba la pynmih ha kaba kut jong ka jingpynlong? Kane ka iathuh aiu ia ngi shaphang ka jingthmu jong ka jingpynim?.`

Ki don khyndiat ki jingkylli ba ia ka jaitbynriew la bishar da U Blei. Ka Kitab Eklesiastis 12:14 ka pynthikna ia ngi ba “U Blei un wallam ia ka jingleh baroh sha ka jingbishar” (NKJV), bad u Paul u pynkynmaw ia ngi ba ngim donkam ban bishar iwei ia iwei pat, namar “ngi baroh ngin ieng ha khmat ka khet bishar U Khrist” (Rom 14:10). U Blei, hooid, um donkam ia ki jingthoh ban tip mano ba la pynim ne ba la jah, hynrei ki angel — kiba la bishar bniah ia ka jingthmu jong ka jingpynim baroh shilynter (1 Pet. 1:12) — kin don ki jingkylli da shisha. Ki la long ki sakhi ia ka jingleh pyrshah jong u Soitan bad ki la iohi ia ka shi bynta na ka lai bynta jong ki angel bneng ba la beh noh na bneng (Jingpynpaw 12:4)—bad mynta U Blei U wanrah ia ngi ha khmat jong U. U Blei u plie ia ki kitab bad u ailad ia ki riewkhuid ban iohi ia kiei kiei baroh.

Ka jingiathuhkhana shaphang ka jingkyrpad jong u Abraham na ka bynta ka Sodom bad Gomorrah — ka jait jingbishar — ka ai ia ngi katto katne ka jingshemphang kaba kongsan shaphang ka jingbishar. Ki pop jong ka Sodom ki paw shai ba la tohkit bniah; U Blei u kdew ba ka jingsniew jong kata ka shnong ka la ai nam ia ka haduh ba ka jingpyrta pyrshah ia ka ka la long kaba khraw (Jen. 18:20). Hynrei ym dei tang ka Sodom bad Gomorrah kiba la wad bniah shuwa ban pynjot ia ki. U Blei u la plie ruh ia ka jingkhang ia u Abraham ban pule bniah la U Blei U leh hok ne em ha ka rai jong U ban pynduh jait ia kiba sniew.

Katba, mano ba paw hapdeng ka jingbishar ba kynja bneng, ha u Daniel 7, hynrei “U Khun u briew,” U Jisu (Dan. 7:13; peit ruh Math. 20:28), uba ka jingmih jong u ka long tang ka daw balei ba kane ka jingbishar ka long kaba “long na ka bynta ki riewkhuid jong Uba ha khlieh tam eh” (NK2V,7). Ka hok ba janai jong U marwei ka ioh ia ki briew jong U lyngba ka jingbishar.

Pyrkhat ba phi ieng ha ka jingbishar ryngkat bad ki jingmaham jong phi baroh kiba la pynpaw ha khmat U Blei Bakhuid jong ngi. Kaei ka jingkyrmen kaba tang kawei jong phi ha kata ka por jong ka jingbishar? (Peit ia ka jingpule jong ka sngi Palei.)
