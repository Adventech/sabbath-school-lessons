### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong

Jer. 29:23, 24; Ps. 139:1–6; Dan. 12:4; Rev. 22:10; 2Tim. 3:15–17; Heb. 4:12

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Hynrei to ai ba uta uba sngew sarong un sngew sarong ha kane, ba u sngewthuh bad u ithuh ia Nga, ba Nga long U Trai, uba pyndonkam ia ka jingieit, ka jingbishar bad ka hok ha ka pyrthei. Namar ha kine nga sngewbha,’ ong U Trai” (Jeremaiah 9:24).”

Kumba long bad kiba bun kiei kiei baroh ha ka Jingthoh Bakhuid, ki Khristan kim ia mynjur lang shaphang ka jingiathuhlypa, kaba bunsien ka pynngeit ia kiwei pat ba ka jingiathuhlypa jong ka Baibl ka long ka jingpynlut por. Hadien baroh, lada ki Khristan ki ialeh pyrshah ia kawei pa kawei ka Ka jingtip kaba rit bha (prophetic jot bad title) katno ka lah ban long kaba biang? Kaba sngewsih ka long ba bun ki bangeit ruh ki sdang ban pyrkhat ba katto katne ki kot jong ka Baibl, kum ka kitap Jingpynpaw, ki long ki bym lah ban sngewthuh. Ha ka jaka ban pule ia ki, ki kiar na ki, teng teng da ka jingpynshlur jong u pastor, uba don ka jingmut kaba bha uba pyrkhat ba ka jingpule ia ka jingiathuhlypa ka wanrah kham bun ki jingeh ban ia kaba ka pynbeit.

Kam shym la long kumta man ka por. Ha ki spah snem ba nyngkong jong ka histori Khristan, kiba bun ki Khristan ki sngewtynnad shibun ia ka jingiathuhlypa jong ka Baibl, bad ka la don ka jingiasngewthuh jingmut kaba phylla shaphang kiei ki khubor ba kongsan jong ki jingiathuhlypa. Kane ka long kumno ba U Blei U la thmu ba kan long: “Mynta nga kyrpad ia phi, ko para bangeit, ha ka kyrteng U Jisu Khrist uba U Trai jong ngi, ba phin ia kren kumjuh, bad ba kan ym don ka jingiapher hapdeng jong phi, hynrei ba phin long kiba janai. la pyniasoh lang ha kajuh ka jingmut bad ha kajuh ka jingbishar” (1 Kor. 1:10,). Ha kane ka taiew, ngin wad bniah ia ki katto katne ki nongrim kiba ai ia ka jingsngewthuh kaba iadei bad kaba lah ban shaniah shaphang ka jingiathuhlypa..

_*Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, April 5._
