---
title:  Ka Jingpyrkhat Shuh Shuh
date:   18/04/2025
---

Pule Ioannis 2:1-11, Mathaios 22:1-14, 2 Korinth 11:1-5, bad Mathaios 25:1-13.

Shisien ba phi la sngewthuh haduh katno ki jingshiaha ki don ha ka Baibl kaba baroh, ban iarap ia phi ban pyniar ia ka jingsngewthuh jong phi shaphang ka jingiathuhlypa, ka lah ban long kaba pynshoi ban pyndonkam palat ia ka. Ha ki spah snem baroh, don ki Khristan kiba la pynksan ia ka jingpyndonkam ia ki dak bad ki dur ba la shem ha ki jingiathuhkhana jong ka Baibl haduh ba ki la sdang ban niew ia ka jingiathuhkhana histori kum ka khanatang. Katba ki syrtap jong ka jingmut ki dei ban shem ha man la ki jaka ha ka Jingthoh Bakhuid, ngi dei ban kynmaw barabor ba U Blei U don ka lad ban shim ia ki jingjia bashisha kiba kynthup ia ki briew bashisha bad pyndonkam ia ki ban hikai ia ngi ia kiei kiei shaphang ki jingiadei jong U ha ka lawei bad ka balang.

Ka jingkhawai shongkurim ha Kana, kum ka nuksa, ka lah ban ai jingmut shaphang ka jingpyni nuksa jong ka jingshongkurim kaba la pyndonkam da ka jingiathuhlypa, hynrei ka jingkhawai shongkurim ka long ka jingjia kaba shisha. “Ka ktien U Khrist ka la pynbiang ia ka jingpynbiang kaba biang na ka bynta ka jingkhawai. Kumta ka long kaba bun ka jingpynbiang jong ka jingaiei jong U ban pyndam noh ia ki jingbymhok jong ki briew, bad ban pynthymmai bad pynsah ia ka mynsiem.”—. Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 149.

Lane kumba ka thoh hangne: “Shisha U Trai U Blei un ym leh ei ei, khlem da pynpaw ia la ka jingthmu ha la ki shakri ki nongiathuhlypa.’ Katba ‘kiei kiei kiba rieh ki long ki jong U Trai U Blei jong ngi,’ ‘Kita kiei kiei ruh kiba rieh ki dei ki jong U Trai uba U Blei jong ngi: hynrei kita kiei kiei ruh ia kiba la pynpaw ki dei ki jong ngi bad jong ki khun jong ngi ha la ka rta,’ Amos 3:7; Deuteronomi 29:29. U Blei u la ai ia kine kiei kiei ha ngi, bad ka jingkyrkhu jong U kan don ha ka jingpule da ka jingburom bad jingduwai ia ki jingthoh bakhuid jong ki nongiathuhlypa.”— The Desire of Ages, p. 234.

**Ki Jingkylli Ban Ia Kren Lang:**

`Pule Ioannis 2:1-11. Ki don bun ki bynta ha ka Gospel u Ioannis kiba pynpaw lypa ia ka jingtrei jong U Khrist ha ka lawei, bad ki nongbatai ki la iohi ia ka rukom ka jingthmu bha ha kaba u Ioannis u pynphai ia ki nongpule jong u sha shakhmat sha ka Diengphna. Hangno phi iohi ba la pyni ia ki bynta bapher bapher jong ka jinglong bad ka hima jong U Khrist ha ka lawei jong ka hima ha kane ka jingiathuhkhana? Ka hikai aiu ia ngi shaphang ka jingthmu jong ka jingpynim ne shaphang ka jingkhawai shongkurim kaba khatduh eh kaba la kular ha U Khun U Blei?`

`Ka pharshi jong ki shiphew ngut ki theisotti ka long kaba la tip bha bad kaba la ieit bha da ki nongpule Baibl. Kane ka jingiathuhkhana kaba dankam ka hikai aiu ia ngi shaphang ki jingjia ha ki sngi kiba khatduh? Hato ka long kaba donkam ba u nongleit kurim u la pynslem? Kiei ki jinghikai kiba kane ka pharshi ka lah ban hikai shaphang ka jingiadei jong ngi shimet shimet bad U Khrist pyrshah ia ka rukom ba U Khrist U iadei bad ka balang ha ka rukom kaba iatreilang?`

`Pyrkhat ia ki rukom jong ka jingleh bakla kiba la rung ha ka niam Khristan na shabar ka jingngeit. Nalor kaba paw shai, ka Sngi Robibar Sunday, kaba pyrshah ia ka Sabbaton jong ka Baibl, kiei kiwei pat ki jingngeit lamler kiba la wan rung? Kumno ki la wan rung, bad kaei kaba ngi lah ban leh ym tang ban iada ialade na ki hynrei ban iarap ia kiwei pat ban iohi ia kaei kaba ki long shisha? Ha kino ki rukom ki khubor jong ki lai angel ki long ka jingpyrshang ban leh, ban iarap ia ki briew ban iohi ia ki jingngeit ba lamler kiba la rung ha ka jingngeit?`
