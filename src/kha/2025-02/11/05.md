---
title:  U Haman Bad U Soitan
date:   11/06/2025
---

Ha ka jingiathuhkhana shaphang ka Esther, ngi iakynduh ia u Haman uba thrang ia ka kyrdan bad ka bor. La ai ha u ka kyrdan kaba khraw ha ka hima, halor kiwei pat ki syiem baroh (Esther 3:1).

Lada ngi pule Esekiel 28:11-15 bad Isaiah 14:12-15 shaphang u Lucifer, ngi lah ban shem katto katne ki jingiasyriem bad u Haman, u nongshun ba sniew ia ki briew ba la jied jong U Blei, uba kyntait ban dem khrup ha ka jinglong ba halor jong U Blei. Ki jingthmu ba jylliew jong u Soitan ki paw ha ka jingiathuhkhana shaphang ka jingpynshoi U Khrist, ha kaba u ialam ia U Jisu sha ka jaka kaba jrong ban pyni ia U ia ki hima jong ka pyrthei (Math 4:8-11). U Khrist, kumba ngi la iohi, U la wan ban siewspah ia ka pyrthei bad ban kam ia ka kum ka jong U — bad U la leh ia kata kum uwei na ngi. U Jisu u long u nongsiewspah uba iadei kur, bad ka dor kaba U la siew ban siewspah ia ka pyrthei ka la long kaba khraw palat.

Ha ka kitab Jingpynpaw, ngi iohi ba ka jingkwah brai jong u Soitan ia ka bor bad ka jingmane ka wanrah ia kane ka pyrthei sha ka jingeh kaba khatduh. Ki jingpynshoi jong u ki pynsngewthuh ia ka pyrthei ban “lyngngoh” bad ban bud ia u mrad khlaw (Jingpyn 13:3, 4, NKJV), lait noh tang ki jingpynkylla kiba pawnam kiba kyntait ban mane ia u. Ha kine ki briew, u pyndonkam da ka bor.

U Haman u iohi ba u Mordekai — uwei na ki briew ba la jied jong U Blei — un ym pynslem ia u ne pdiang ia kaei kaba u ngeit ba ka long ka hok jong u ban “mane.” U la “dap da ka jingbitar” (Esther 3:5, 6), bad u la kut jingmut ban pynduh jait lut ia ki briew jong u Mordekai na khmat jong ka pyrthei.

`Pule Esther 3:1-14, Jingpynpaw 12:14-17, bad Jingpynpaw 13:15. Kiei ki jingiasyriem kiba phi shem hapdeng kine ki dkhot? Kumno ka jingbatai jong u Ioannis shaphang ka balang ba sah jong U Blei ka long kum ka jingbatai jong u Haman shaphang ki briew jong U Blei?`

U ksuid u la kam ia kane ka pyrthei, hynrei ka jingdon jong ki briew kiba iaineh hok ha U Blei — kiba sumar ia ki hukum jong U — ka pynrem ia ka jingdawa jong u ba u long uba khraw bor tam. “Ynda ka Sabbaton kan kylla long ka mat ba kyrpang jong ka jingïakynad ha kylleng ka niam Khristan, ka jingkyntait ba bteng jong ka kynhun barit ban kohnguh ïa ka jingdawa jong ki paidbah kan pynlong ïa ki ki briew jong ka jingpynshitom beiñ ha ka pyrthei baroh kawei.”—Ellen G. White, Signs of the Times, 22 tarik u February 1910.

`Pyrkhat ia kiei kiei kiba “rit” kiba tynjuh ia ka jingngeit jong phi mynta. Lada phi pyndem ha ki, ia kiei kiei kiba “rit”, kumno phin leh ynda la wan ka jingtynjuh kaba khraw?`
