### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong

Isaiah 40:7,8; Jenisis 22:1-13; Ioannis 3:16; Jingpynpaw 5:5-10; 1 Korinth 15:15-19; Jingpynpaw 12:1-9.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Ha kaba lashai u Ioannis u iohi ia U Jisu ba u wan ha u, bad u ong, hakhmih, U khun lyngbrot jong U Blei, uba kit noh ia ka pop Ka jong ka pyrthei!” (Ioannis 1:29, NKJV).

Kawei naka ka jingeh kaba kongsan ha ka jingbatai ia ki jingiathuhlypa jong ka Baibl, kum ia ka kitab Jingpynpaw, ka long ba ki bakla ban ithuh ia ki tynrai mynnor jong ka Jingpynpaw. U nongthoh u pyrkhat ia ka jingtip ia ka kitab kaba rim bad pyndonkam ia ki jingbatai kaba ki nongsngap ki sngewthuh bha. Katba wat bniah ia baroh ka Baibl ia ki jingthoh kiba syrim ia ki dkhot kiba phi pule ha ka kitab Jingpynpaw kiba donkam, don ruh ki dkhot tynrai kiba iarap ban wan rah ka jingsngewthuh ia ka kitab khambha ban ia ka jinglah jong kiwei ki dkhot. Kane ka long ka jingshisha ha kaba iadei bad ka kitab Jenesis, kaba la siang ia ka lynti ha kaba ka pyrthei ka la hiar ka jingkulmar jong ka pop. La jan baroh ki mat kiba khongsan kiba kren ha ka kitab Jingpynpaw ki paw – ha ki katto katne ki bynta- haka ba sdang ki lynnong jong ka Bible.

Ha kane ka taiew, ngin ia pule ia ki katto katne ki mat kiba khraw kiba long pdeng jong ka kitab Jingpynpaw. Don shibun, te kumta ngin jied ia ki katto katne ban pyni ka nuksa ia baroh ki mat kiba donkam ban sngewthuh ia ki nongrim kiba hyndai kiba shadien ka kitab Jingpynpaw kiba iarap ia ki nongpule(khynnah skul)ban iohi ki jingkulmar kiba ym lah ban niew shuh ha ki dkhot, ha kaba manla kawei pa kawei na kine ki dkhot ki wan rah ia ki jinghikai kiba donkam shaphang ka jinglong jong ki Briew, U Blei, bad ka jingeh ki jingialeh kiba la ban khia ia ka pyrthei solonsar, bad kumta ha ka jingim jong ngi ruh kumjuh..

_*Pule pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, April 12._
