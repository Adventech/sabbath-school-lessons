---
title:  Ka Kam Jong Ka Jingkylla Kaba Mut
date:   23/06/2025
---

U Jonah u don ka khubor kaba kyrpang bha na ka bynta ki briew jong ka Ninebeh. “Bad u Jonah u la sdang ban rung shapoh ka nongbah ha ka jingleit jong u ha ka sngi kaba nyngkong. Nangta u la pyrta bad u la ong, ‘Sa sawphew sngi, yn pynjot noh ia ka Ninebeh!’” (Ioan. 3:4, NKJV). Ka long kumba ka shai bha: ka jaka ka la shah pynjot. Hadien baroh, hato kata kam dei ka ktien kaba la kren beit na u nongiathuhlypa jong U Trai?

Hynrei, kaei kaba la jia ia ka Ninebeh?

`Pule Jonah 3:5-10. Balei kane ka jingiathuhlypa kam shym la urlong?`

Hooid, ka nongbah baroh kawei ka la kylla bamut, bad la pynsangeh ia ka jingpynjot ba la iathuhlypa, la long tang shipor. “La pynsangeh ia ka jingpynjot ia ki, la pynkhraw bad pyndonburom ia U Blei jong ki Israel ha kylleng ka pyrthei jong ki nongwei, bad la burom ia ka hukum jong U. Ym tang hadien bun snem ba ka Ninebeh ka la shah pynjot ha ki jaitbynriew ba marjan namar ba ki la klet ia U Blei bad lyngba ka jingsarong kaba sngew sarong.” —Ellen G. White, Ki Nongiathuhlypa bad ki Syiem, p. 271.

Hato ngi lah ban khmih lynti ia kaei kaei kaba kum kane ha ki sngi kiba khatduh, ryngkat bad ka khubor kaba khatduh sha ka pyrthei kaba la hap noh? Hooid— bad em. Kata ka mut, kin don, ha kylleng ka pyrthei, bun ki briew kiba sngap ia ka jingkhot, “To iamih noh na ka, u paidlang jong nga, ba phin ym iashang lem bad kita ki pop jong ka, bad ba phin ym iapdiang na kita ki jingiapthem jong ka.” (Jingpynpaw 18:4, NKJV). Ha kylleng ka pyrthei, ki briew kin ieng ha la ka jong ka nongrim bad, da kaba pyrshah ia u mrad khlaw, kin “sumar ia ki hukum U Blei, bad ia ka jingngeit U Jisu” (Jingpyn 14:12). Kine ki briew, kum kito ha Ninebeh, kin lait na ka jingbishar kaba hap halor kiba la sakma.

Katto katne ki jingiathuhlypa, kum ka jingpynbna U Blei ba yn pynjot ia ka Ninebeh, ki long ki kyndon. (Ka Ninebeh kan sa shah pynjot lymda ki briew kin phai noh na ka jingsniew jong ki [peit Jeremiah. 18:7–10].) Hynrei, don ki jingiathuhlypa ki bym wan ryngkat bad kine ki jingmaham. kiba Yn sa pyndep, wat la ki briew ki jubab kumno kumno ruh. Ki jingiathuhlypa shaphang U Messaiah, bad ka jingwan kaba nyngkong bad kaba arsien jong U Khrist, ka dak jong u mrad khlaw, ka jingtheh noh ia ki khlam, ka jingpynshitom beiñ ha ka sngi ba khatduh — kine kim long kiba don kyndon; kin sa jia khlem da khein ia kaei kaba ki briew ki leh. Kaei kaba ki briew ki leh, bad ia ki jingjied kiba ki la pynlong, yn sa rai ha kano ka liang kin don ha ki jingjia ba khatduh, kiba la iathuhlypa da ki nongiathuhlypa, ki nang jia.

`Kiei ki jingjied kiba phi pynlong mynta kiba lah ban iarap ba phin pynlong ia ka jingjied ynda la pynmih ia ka rai ban mane ia U Blei ne ia ka dur blei ka ban wan ha ka pyrthei?`
