### Dkhot Baibl ban pynkhreh ia ka Lynnong
Exodos 25:8, 9, 40; Hebru 8:1–6; Lebitikos. 16:21, 29–34; Lebitikos 23:26–32; Hebru 9:23–28; Daniel 7:9, 10.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Ngi don U Rangbah lyngdoh kat uta uba la shong sha ka kti kamon ka khet, Uba khraw tam ha ki bneng, U Shakri ka jaka kyntang bad kata ka tabernakl kaba shisha ia kaba U Trai u la pynieng ym u briew” (Hebru 8:1,2).

Khyndiat por hadien ka sngi jong ka jingduh jingkyrmen kaba khraw ha ka 22 tarik u October 1844, katto katne ngut na ki nongbud u Miller ki la shem ia ka jingsngewthuh ba ka 2300 sngi jong ka jingiathuhlypa kam don jingiadei bad ka jingwan arsien jong U Jisu hynrei ka iadei bad ka kam jong U Khrist ha ka jaka kyntang ha bneng.

Ka jingpynkhuid ia ka jaka kyntang ha bneng ka long ka jingpypndep ia ka jingpynkhuid ia ka jaka kyntang kaba ha pyrthei. Ban sngewthuh khambha ia kane ka jingshisha kaba donkam, peit ia ka jingiadei hapdeng Daniel 7 bad Daniel 8:

Kine ki jingiadei ki pyni ia ka jinglong jong ka jingpynkhuid jong ka jaka kyntang, ka jingbishar shuwa ka jingwan arsien. Ha kane ka taiew ngin ia peit bniah ia ka jingshakri jong U Khrist ha ka bneng.

_Pule ia ka lynnong mynta ka taiew ia kaba la pynshong nongrim ha ka lynnong 22 – 24 bad ruh 28 jong Ka Jingialeh Bakhraw, ban pynkhreh na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, May 25_
