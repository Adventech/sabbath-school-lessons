---
title:  “Shibun Ha Kane Ka Nongbah”
date:   01/07/2026
---

`Pule Ki Kam 18:4–8. Kaei ka jingmih na ka jingialap jong u Paul?`

Ka jingtrei u Paul hapdeng ki Jiw ha Korinth kam shym la sei soh kumba u kwah. U la hap ban iakynduh katto katne ka jingshun bad jingisih. Ka Baibl ka ong ba “Te haba ki la ieng ialeh pyrshah bad kren beiñ” (Ki Kam 18:6,). Haba ka jingmut jong ka verb Greek _blasphēmeō_ (“ban kren beiñ”) ka long u bynriew, kaba mut “ban kren beiñ” ne “ban pynjah burom.” Ha kiwei pat ki kyntien, ki la thmu ban pynsniew ia ka nam jong u Paul bad ban khanglad ia u ban jop ha ki kam mishoneri jong u.

Kyrpang eh, ka jingtrei jong u Paul ha ka synagoge ha Korinth kam shym la long lehnohei. Halor baroh, U Blei U la long uba peit ia kane ka mishon. U la kular, “[Ka ktien jong nga] kan ym wan phai thylli sha nga” (Isaiah. 55:11). Katto katne ki Jiw kim shym la khmih lynti ba u Krispos, u nongsynshar jong ka synagoge, bad ka ïing jong u baroh kawei kin pdiang ïa U Jisu kum U Messaiah bad kin shah pynbaptis (Ki Kam 18:8). Nalor kata, “bun na ki nong Korinth, haba ki la iohsngew, ki la ngeit bad la pynbaptis ia ki” (Ki Kam 18:8), ka lah ban long ruh namar ka bor ktah jong u Krispos.

`Pule Ki Kam 18:9, 10. Kaei kaba ngi lah ban shim jingmut shaphang ki jingsngew u Paul ha ka jingiashem jong ka jingeh ha Korinth? Kumno U Blei U la pynshlur ia la u shakri?`

Hadien ba u Paul u la mih noh na ka synagog, u la ioh ia ka jingiashem kaba la ai mynsiem ia u. U Khrist hi u la paw ha u mynmiet ha ka jingithuhpaw, da ki kyntien kiba pynkynmaw ha u Isaiah 41:10: “To wat sheptieng me, naba nga long lem bad me”. Hooid, u Paul u kam ba u la don ha Korinth “ha ka jingtlot, ha ka jingsheptieng, bad ha ka jingkhynñiuh” (1 Korinth. 2:3). U la hap ban mih noh na Berea sha Athen namar ka jingpyrshah kaba jur. Kum ban shu ong, u la pyrkhat ba un hap ban mih noh na Korinth na kajuh ka daw: ka jingpyrshah kaba khraw. Hynrei kata kan ym jia ha kane ka kynti. U Jisu u la ong ha u: “ Naba nga don shibun ki briew ha kane ka nongbah’ ” (Ki Kam 18:10,). Bad U Paul u la long u atiar jong U ban rah ia ka khubor jong ka jingpynim sha ki.

`Pule Isaiah 41:10. Kiei ki jingkular ba phylla ba U Blei U ai ha phi ha kane ka dkhot? Kaei ka jingkyrmen kaba ki ai ha phi mynta ha ka jingim jong phi?`
