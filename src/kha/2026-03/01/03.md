---
title:  Na Athen Sha Korinth
date:   29/06/2026
---

`Pule Ki Kam 17:16-34. Hangno u Paul u don ha shuwa ba un leit sha Korinth, bad u leh aiu hangta?`

Ki Kam 17:16–34 ki batai ia ka jingialap u Paul ha ki nong Athen shuwa ba un leit sha Korinth. Khamtam, um shym la thmu ban leit sha Athen ha kata ka por, hynrei u la leit shata da ka jingiarap jong ki katto katne ki paralok namar ka jingpyrshah kaba u la iakynduh ha Beria (Ki Kam 17:13-15).

Kito kiba la leit ryngkat bad u Paul sha Athen ki la leit phai sha Beria ryngkat bad ka hukum ba u Timothi bad u Silas kin iasoh bad u kloi katba lah (Ki Kam 17:15). Ki Kam 17:16–34 ka dei shaphang kaei kaba u Paul u leh katba u dang ap ia ki. U kren shaphang U Jisu ha synagog, ha iew bad ha Ariopagos. U shisha um lah ban sangeh ban kren shaphang U Jisu bad u la shim kabu ia man la ka lad ban leh ia kata.

`Pule ia ki Kam 18:1-11. U Paul u leh aiu ynda u la poi ha Korinth bad ia baroh ka jingsah jong u ha kata ka nongbah?`

U Paul u la leit sha Korinth ha ka jingleit mishoneri kaba ar jong u. U Loukas u pyntip ba u Paul u la sah hangta shisnem shiteng.

Kumba ju long, u Paul u sdang ia ka kam mishoneri jong u na ka synagog (Ki Kam 18:4-6). Ka Kam 17:1, 2 ka kdew ba kane ka dei ka dustur jong u. U la bud ia ka buit trei kam “sha u Jiw nyngkong” (Rom 1:16, Kam 13:46), kumba U Jisu u la hukum ia ki apostol jong U (peit Kam 1:8).

Haba khatduh u Silas bad u Timothi ki la iasoh bad u ha Korinth, u “la pynlut por ha ka ktien, u da pynshisha ha ki Jiw ba U Khrist U long U Jisu” (Ki Kam 18:5,). Ha ka por ba u don ha Korinth, u la bunkam “ha kaba hikai ia ka ktien U Blei” (Ki Kam 18:11). Dei ha kane ka jinglong ruh, ba u la pynpaw ia kita ki kyntien ba pawnam ba u la “kut jingmut ban ym tip ei ei hapdeng” ki nong Korinth “lait noh tang U Jisu Khrist bad ia U ba la sahnarphna” (1 Kor. 2:2).

`Ngi lah ban ioh jinghikai aiu na ka kam mishoneri jong u Paul ha Athen bad Korinth? Kaei kaba pynlong ia ka jingiasam ia ka gospel kaba eh ha ki nongbah bad ki shnong jong ngi mynta? Kumno ngin lah ban iasam ia ka gospel da ka jingjop ha kine ki jaka kiba eh? Kiei ki buit kiba ngi lah ban ioh jinghikai na ka jingtrei mishoneri jong u Paul shaphang kumno ban leh bha ia kata?`
