Pule Ellen G. White, “Corinth,” pp. 243–254, in The Acts of the Apostles.

“Ha kaba ialap ia ka gospel ha Korinth, u apostol u la bud ia ka lynti kaba pher na kata kaba la buh dak ia ki jingtrei shitom jong u ha Athen... U la kut jingmut ban kiar na ki jingiatainia bad jingiakren kiba jylliew, bad ‘ban ym tip ei ei’ hapdeng ki nong Korinth ‘lait noh ia U Jisu Khrist, bad ia U ba la sahnarphna.’ ” — Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 244.

“U Paul u la don ka jingthew jong ka jingjop,” hynrei u la “artatien ia ka jingstad ban tei ia ka balang na ki tiar kiba u la shem hangta. U la shim ia ka Korinth kum ka lyngkha trei kaba don jingkylli shibun, bad u la kut jingmut ban iehnoh ia ka... .

“Katba u dang pyrkhat ban mih noh na ka nongbah sha ka lyngkha kaba kham don jingkyrmen, ... U Trai u la paw ha u ha ka jingphohsniew mynmiet, bad u la ong, ‘Wat tieng, hynrei to kren, ... namar nga don shibun ki briew ha kane ka nongbah.’ U Paul u la sngewthuh ia kane kum ka hukum ban pynroi bad ai jingkular ia u Trai ban sah ha Korinth. Bad u la pyntikna ba U Trai un ai jingroi ia u symbai ba la bet . . ka balang kaba heh la pynrung kyrteng hapoh ka lama U Jisu Khrist.”—Ellen G. White, Sketches From the Life of Paul.

“La buh jingkynmaw ba u Paul u la trei shisnem hynriew bnai ha Korinth. Hynrei ki jingpyrshang jong u kim shym la long tang ha kata ka nongbah... U la pynlong ia ka Korinth kum ka jaka treikam jong u... Kumta u la pynmih katto katne tylli ki balang ….. ka jingbym don jong u Paul ha ki blang ha ka jingsumar jong u la pynbiang da ki jingpyntip kiba khia bad kiba donbor, kiba la pdiang kyllum kum ka ktien U Blei la pule ha ki balang.”—Ellen G. White, Sketches From the Life of Paul . 109.

**Ki Jingkylli Ban Ïa Kren Lang:**

`U Paul u la ngeit skhem ba u long u apostol U Jisu bad ba kane ka jingkhot ka long na U Blei. Balei ka long kaba donkam eh ban tip ba ngi long kiei bad kaei ka jingkhot jong ngi?`

`Katto katne por, u Paul u la sngew kum ban iehnoh ia ka kam mishoneri jong u ha Korinth bad ban mih noh na kata ka nongbah. Kaei kaba la pynlong ia u ban kylla kaba mut? Kumno kane ka lah ban iarap ia ngi haba ngi sngew kwah ban iehnoh ia ka kam mishoneri? Hynrei, hato ka lah ban don ka por kaba ngi dei ban leh kumta??`

`Ki dkhot balang ha Korinth ki la shah ktah shibun ha ka dustur kaba don sawdong jong ki. Kane ka long ruh ka jingshisha kaba shai bha hapdeng jong ngi mynta. Kumno ngi lah ban don ha ka pyrthei (Ioannis 17:11, 15) bad ban ym shah pynkhih ha kaei kaba “don ha ka pyrthei — ki jingkwah brai jong ka doh bad ki jingkwah brai jong ki khmat bad ka jingsarong jong ka jingim” (1 Ioannis 2:16, ESV)? Kiei kiwei pat ki lad kiba ka balang jong ngi ka shah ktah sniew ha ka dustur kaba don sawdong?`
