---
title:  Ka Jingïasyllok Jong U Mynsiem Bakhuid
date:   23/09/2026
---

Ka jingaiei U Jisu kam pynpaw tang ia ka jingieit U Blei ia ngi hynrei ka wanrah ruh ia ka jingiasyllok jong U Mynsiem kum ka jingktah shuh shuh jong kata ka jingieit. Ha kajuh ka por, ka jingiasyllok ka don la ka tynrai ha ka jingieit U Blei. Bad kata ka long namar khlem ka jingieit, ym don jingiasyllok. Kumba u Paul u thoh: “Namar kata lada don kano kano ka jingpyntngen ha U Khrist, lada don kano kano ka jingpynshngaiñ ka jingïaieit, lada don kano kano ka jingiasyllok U Mynsiem, lada don kano kano ka jingisynei, to phin pyndap ia ka jingkmen jong nga, ba phin ia long kajuh ka jingmut, kiba don kajuh ka jingïaieit, kiba shi mynsiem hi, kiba ïa dei jingmut” (Phillipi 2: 1, 2).

Don ki briew kiba ong ba U Mynsiem Bakhuid u long tang ka bor ne ka bor jingktah, hynrei kata kam lah ban long kaba shisha. Nalor baroh, balei ba u Paul un kdew ia ki ar ki person — U Kpa bad U Khun — ryngkat bad ka “bor” ha ka jinglong lai jong ka jinglong Blei? Kata kan ym don jingmut ei ei. Kumba la pyni ia U Kpa bad ia U Khun ha ka jingiadei jong ki (2 Korinth. 1:3, 2 Korinth. 11:31), ka jingiadei U Mynsiem bad ki briew ka ialam ia ngi sha ka jingpynkut nia ba U long ruh u Person (Rom 8:15, 16; peit ruh Ioannis 14:16, 17, Ioannis 15:26;

Ia ka kyntien “ka jingiasyllok U Mynsiem” (Phillipi. 2:1) lah ban sngewthuh ha ki ar rukom. Ka lah ban mut ka jingiasyllok iwei ia iwei pat kaba la ai da U Mynsiem, lane ka jingiasyllok bad U Mynsiem hi. Katto katne ngut ki nongpynkylla ktien ia ka Baibl ki kynthoh ba kine ki jingsngew kim iadei paralok. Nalor baroh, ka jingiasyllok iwei ia iwei pat ka long ka jingmih na ka jingiasyllok bad U Mynsiem.

`Pule 1 Korinth 2:10, 11; 1 Korinth 3:16; 1Korinth 12:11; bad 2 Korinth 3:6, 17. U Paul u la hikai aiu ia ki nong Korinth shaphang U Mynsiem?`

U Paul u don shibun ban ong shaphang ka jingtrei U Mynsiem. Ha 1 bad 2 Korinth, don palat sawphew tylli ki jingkdew shaphang U Mynsiem Bakhuid. U Mynsiem Bakhuid U kyntiew ia ka jingtei ia ka balang (1 Korinth. 14:12), U aibor ban pynlah ia ki briew na ka bynta ka mishon (1 Korinth. 2:4, 5), U pynpaw ia kiei kiei kiba jylliew jong U Blei ha ngi (1 Korinth. 2:10, 11), bad U hikai ia ki ha ngi (1 Korinth. 2:13), U shongsah ha ngi (1 Korinth. 3 :16. 1 Korinth 6: 19), ka jingtrei bad U Khrist na ka bynta ka jingpynksan jong ngi (1 Korinth. 6:11), ka ai ia ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem sha ka balang (1 Korinth. 12-14), ka Shonshap ia ngi na ka bynta ka jingpynim (2 Korinth. 1:22), ka shon shap ia ka hukum ha ki dohnud briew (2 Korinth. 3:3), bad ka ai ia ka jingim kaba thymmai ha U Khrist (2 Korinth. 3:6), bad ka pyllaitluid na ka pop (2 Korinth 3:17) . Dashisha, ngim lah ban im khlem U Mynsiem Bakhuid.

`Balei ruh ba ka jingsngewthuh ia ka jinglong blei jong U Mynsiem Bakhuid ka long kaba kongsan ban sngewthuh pura ia ka jingieit U Blei ia ngi?`
