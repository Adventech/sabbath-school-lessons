---
title:  Ka Jingaiei U Jisu
date:   20/09/2026
---

Ka long kaba pynkhih mynsiem ba ha kaba kut jong ka 2 Korinth, ngi iohi ia ka jingkdew shaphang ka jingaiei U Jisu, kumba long ha ka jingsdang (2 Korinth. 1:2, 2 Korinth. 13:14). U Paul u sdang bad pynkut ia kane ka shithi da kaba kdew shaphang ka jingaiei jong U. Kumba ngi la iohi ha kaba sdang jong kane ka quarter, um lah ban sangeh ban pyrkhat bad kren shaphang U Jisu.

“Namar phi ithuh ia ka jingaiei U Jisu Khrist uba U Trai jong ngi, ba la U la long uba riewspah, pynban U la long uba duk na ka bynta jong phi, khnang ban pynriewspah ia phi da kata ka jingduk jong U” (2 Korinth. 8:9).

Katno ka long kaba sngewtynnad bad kaba ym lah ban ialeh pyrshah ia ka jingaiei U Jisu! U la iehnoh ia ka spah jong ka jinglong bymjukut jong U ha bneng ban long uba duk. U la iaid ha ki surok kiba dap da u pum-pum jong ka Galilaia hyndai. “U la pynrit ia lade da kaba u la long uba kohnguh haduh ka jingiap, wat ka jingiap halor ka diengphna” (Phillipi. 2:8). U la leh kumta khnang ba ngin long kiba “riewspah,” kata, ba ngin ioh lad ban don ryngkat bad U ha bneng. Ka long kaba eh ia ngi, kiba la tip tang ia ka pyrthei jong ka pop, ka jingiap bad ka jingjynjar wat ban sdang ban sngewthuh ia kaei kaba mut ia U Jisu ban mih noh na ki khet na bneng khnang ban wan shane bad ban aiti ia la ka jingim na ka bynta jong ngi.

`Pule Rom 16:20, Kalatia 6: 18, Phillipi 4:23 bad 1 Thessoloni 5:28. Kaei ka jinghikai kaba kongsan kaba phi lah ban iohi ha kine ki dkhot?`

U Paul u kdew bunsien bha ia ka jingaiei U Jisu ha ki shithi jong u. Katto katne ki mat ba kongsan ki kynthup, “Ka jingai ba khlem jingsiew da ka jingaiei U . . . U Jisu Khrist ki la roi kyrhai sha kita kiba bun” (Rom 5:15). Kito kiba ioh ia kane ka jingaiei kaba bun kin “synshar ha ka jingim da U Jisu” (Rom 5:17). Kumba jia ha ka 2 Korinth, u Paul ruh u sdang bad pynkut ia kiwei pat ki shithi kiba kdew ia ka jingaiei U Jisu (Rom 1:7, Rom 16:20, 1 Korinth 1:3, 1 Korinth 16:23, Galatia 1:3, Galatia 6:18, Phillipi 1:2, Phillipi 4:23). Kane ka phang ka la bat ia ki jingpyrkhat jong u, bad u kwah ruh ia ka ban pyndap ia ka jingmut jingpyrkhat jong ki nong Korinth.

Kane ka la long ka jingkwah jong u ia ki balang baroh. Peit kaei kaba u ong ha ki nong Ephesos: “Ka jingaiei ha baroh kiba ieit ia U Trai jong ngi U Jisu Khrist da ka jingieit bymjukut” (Ephesos. 6:24). Hato un kwah ba ngin ieit ia U Jisu kham duna ban ia kata, ka jingieit bym ju iap? Em ym kumta. Nalor baroh, ka jingkwah jong u ka long ba ka jingaiei U Jisu kan poi sha “ki ba bun ki briew” (2 Korinth. 4:15) bad ban long kaba biang na ka bynta jong ki kumba ka la long na ka bynta jong u (2 Korinth. 12:9).

`Pyrkhat ia ka jingaiei U Blei ia phi. Kaei kaba phi dei hok ban ioh na ka bynta ki jait jingleh kiba phi la kren bad leh? Hynrei, kaei kaba ka jingaiei U Blei ka ai ha phi ha ka jaka jong ka?`
