### Ka Jingsdang ha u Bar

_By Rick Kajiura_

Ki briew ha shnong ki la rkhie beiñ. “Kine ki Khristan ki dei kiba la shah pynbiej ban pyrkhat ba ngin mane ia U Blei ha ka ïing kaba don ia ka jinglong ia ka jingshah kynthoh ba sniew ha kip or kiba mynshuwa,” ki la pynbna.

U Leenus, u nongsdang jong ka Global Mission (GM) ha Kenya, u la don ka jaka kaba ym lah ban long ban pynroi ia ka balang— ka kamra kaba rit kaba ju long ka jaka dih kyiad. Ka jingshakri jong u kum u nongsdang GM ruh ka la don ka jingsdang kaba ym lah ban mutdur. U la long u Pastor jong ka jingngeit kaba pher, hynrei u dang sdang ia ka kynhun kaba thymmai jong ki bangeit Seventh-day Adventist.

U Leenus u la kylla long u Adventist hadien ba u la leit sha ki jingiaseng evangelis bad pule ia ki jinghikai Baibl jong ka Sur Jingiathuhlypa (VOP). “Nga lap ba ka don ka jingshisha ha ki”, u la ong. U la thmu ban iasam ia kata ka jingshisha bad ki dkhot barim jong u tang shu dep pynbaptis ia u.

U Leenus u la pynrung kyrteng 360 ngut ki briew ha ka jinghikai Baibl VOP. U la leit na kawei ka iing sha kawei pat, u da jubab ia ki jingkylli haduh ba uwei pa uwei na ki un da pyndep ia ka jinghikai. Ha ka jingkmen jong u, 15 ngut na ki ki la pan ban shah pynbaptis.

Ki nongialam jong ka Conference ki la pynlong ia ki jingpynbaptis bad ki la ong ia u Leenus ba un ialam ia ka kynhun langbrot kaba thymmai. Hynrei ki la kylla ha ka jingmut. Ki la sngewtynnad haduh katta katta ia ka jinglah jong u Leenus ban kot sha kiwei pat haduh ba ki la pynlong ia u u nongsdang jong ka GM. “Ki la ong ia nga ban leit shapoh bad ban wad shuh shuh ia ki briew”, la ong u Leenus.

Haba u Leenus u la poi ha ka shnong thymmai, ym shym la don ki Adventist. U la leit sha kawei ka jingkhang sha kawei ka jingkhang, u da iasam ia ka jingngeit Adventist. Hadien ka jingpyrshang kaba jur, uwei u briew u la pdiang ia ka khubor. “Nga la ong ia une u briew, ‘To bat ia ka kti jong nga, bad ryngkat ngin kor sha kiwei pat.”

U Leenus u la pynlong ia ki jingiaseng ialap, bad katto katne ngut ki dkhot jong ka imlang sahlang ki la iadon lang. Haba ki la kylli, “Hangno ka ïing mane jong phi” u Leenus u la hap ban iathuh ba ki iakynduh ha kawei ka dukan.

“Em, em!” ki la ong, haba ki la tip ba kata ka dukan ka la long mynshuwa ka jaka dih kyiad. “Ngim lah ban mane ia U Blei hangta. Kata kam lah ban long.”

U Leenus u la shah pynshoi ban sngew ba u la duh mynsiem, hynrei shen u la sngewthuh ba U Blei U don ka jingthmu. “U Blei U kwah ba ngin sdang ha ka dukan dih kyiad khnang ba Un lah ban pyndonkam ia ka bor bad ki briew jong U ban kyrshan ia ngi.”

U Leenus u la duwai ban don ka Balang bad, khatduh, U Blei u la iarap ia u ban ioh ia ka jaka bad ki briew ban iarap ban tei ia ka jingtei. Mynta, 10 ngut ki dkhot ki iakynduh man la ka taiew ha ka sngi Sabbaton ha ka ïingmane kaba thymmai. Don kiba wan na ki jaka kiba jngai haduh 12 kilomitar (7.5 mail). U Leenus u thmu ban pynlong ia ki jingiaseng ialap ha ka kata ka shnong. U ngeit ba ka jingdon ia ka ïingmane kaba thymmai ka la iarap ia ki briew ban sngew ba U Blei u don ryngkat bad ka jingthung balang. “Ki lah ban iohi ba U Blei U leh ei ei na ka bynta jong ngi. U pyniar ia ngi bad iarap ia ngi ban gin nangsan.”

Sngewbha duwai na ka bynta ki nongsdang jong ka Global Mission kiba, kum u Leenus, ki la pdiang ia ka jingeh ban thung ia ki Balang hapdeng ki kynhun briew ki bym pat poi ha kylleng ka pyrthei. ban tip kham bniah shaphang ki nongsdang jong ka Global Mission: peit ha ka https://bit.ly/GMPioneers.
