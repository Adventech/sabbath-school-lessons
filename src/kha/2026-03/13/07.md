Pule Ellen G. White, “ ‘Let Not Your Heart Be Troubled,’ ” pp. 662–680, in The Desire of Ages.

“Ka jingaiei U Jisu Khrist marwei ka lah ban pynkylla ia ka dohnud maw sha ka dohnud doh, bad pynlong im ia ka ha U Blei... Ki briew kim don bor ban pynksan ia ka mynsiem, ban pynkhuid ia ka dohnud. Ka jingpang ba kynja mynsiem kam lah ban pynkoit lymda lyngba ka bor jong u Nongpynkhiah bakhraw. Ka jingai ka ba khrawtam jong ka bneng, wat U Khun ba marwei jong U Kpa, Uba dap shoiñ da ka jingaiei bad ka jingshisha, kaba long tang ma ka marwei kaba lah ban siewspah ia kiba la jah noh.”—Ellen G. White, ha ka kot The Signs of the Times, May 2, 1892.

“ ‘U Blei u long ka jingieit.’ 1 Ioannis 4:16. Ka jinglong jong U, ka hukum jong U, ka long ka jingieit. Kaba la ju long; kan iai long. ‘Uta uba ha jrong bad uba ha jrong tam eh uba shong ha ka bymjukut,’ uba ‘ki lynti jong u ki long bymjukut,’ um kylla. Ha U ‘ym don ka jingkylla, lymne ka syrngiew jong ka jingkylla’. Isaiah 57:15; U Habakkuk 3:6; Jakob 1:17. Kawei pa kawei ka jingpynpaw ia ka bor jong ka jingthaw jingpynlong ka long ka jingpynpaw ia ka jingieit bymjukut. Ka jinglong ba khraw bor jong U Blei ka kynthup ia ka jingdap jong ka jingkyrkhu ia ki jingthaw baroh kiba la thaw. . . . Ka histori jong ka jingialeh bakhraw hapdeng kaba bha bad kaba sniew, naduh ka por ba ka la sdang nyngkong eh ha bneng haduh ka jingwengnoh kaba khatduh ia ka jingïaleh pyrshah bad ka jingpynduh pyndam noh syndon ia ka pop, ka long ruh ka jingpyni ia ka jingieit bym ju kylla jong U Blei.” —Ellen G. White, Ki Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 33.

“Ngi donkam ban sngewthuh ba U Mynsiem Bakhuid ... U long u person kumba U Blei U long u person ... U Mynsiem Bakhuid U don ka jinglong main long rynïeng, lym kumta Um lah ban sakhi ia ki mynsiem jong ngi bad da ki mynsiem jong ngi ba ngi long ki khun U Blei. U dei ruh ban long u person uba kynja Blei, lym kumta um lah ban wad ia ka barieh kiba la buhrieh ha ka jingmut jong U Blei.” Ellen G. White, The Faith I Live By, p. 52.

“Ki don lai ki person kiba im jong ki lai kiba ha bneng; ha ka kyrteng jong kine ki lai tylli kiba don ia ka bor ba khraw — U Kpa, U Khun bad U Mynsiem Bakhuid — la pynbaptis ia kito kiba pdiang ia U Khrist da ka jingngeit ba im, bad kine ki bor kin iatreilang bad ki ba kohnguh jong ka bneng ha ka jingpyrshang jong ki ban im ia ka jingim bathymmai ha U Khrist.”— Ellen G. White, Evangelism, p. 615.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Kawei ka jingrwai Khristan kaba pawnam kaba la ai kyrteng “Amazing Grace.” Kaei kaba phylla shaphang ka jingaiei U Jisu?`

`Ka jingpynpaw kaba itynnad ia ka jingieit U Blei lah ban shem ha ka pharshi jong u khun sarong awria. Kumno ngi tip ba u kpa ha kane ka pharshi u dei u kpa uba ieit?`

`Kumno ki kynhun balang ki lah ban pyni ba ka “jingiasyllok jong U Mynsiem” ka don ka jingshisha hangta?`
