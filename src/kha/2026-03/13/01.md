### Ki Jingpule Na Ka Bynta Kane Ka Taïew
2 Cor. 8:9; Rom. 16:20; 1 John 4:8–11; 2 Cor. 13:11; Phil. 2:1, 2; Gal. 4:4–6.

> <p>Dkhot Spel Lyndet</p>
> “Ka Jingaiei U Jisu Khrist Uba U Trai bad ka jingieit U Blei bad ka jingiasyllok U Mynsiem Bakhuid to kin long lem bad phi baroh.” (2 Korinth 13:14).

U Paul u pynkut ia ka 2 Korinth da kaba ban jur biang ia ki bynta kiba kongsan kiba la kren ha ki shithi jong u. U leh ia kane lyngba ki san tylli ki jingbthah kiba donkam (2 Korinth. 13:11).

Ka jingbthah kaba nyngkong, “To kmen”, ka pynkynmaw ia ki dkhot kiba kham mynshuwa ha ki shithi.

Ka jingbthah kaba ar, “To ialeh ban ioh ia ka jingpynthymmai kaba pura”, ka pynkylla ia kawei ka kyntien ha ka ktien Grik (_katartizō_), kaba paw hangne bad ha 1 Korinth 1:10.

Kaba lai, “To ai mynsiem iwei ia iwei pat”, ka wanrah biang ia 2 Korinth 1:3-7. Ba U Paul u sdang bad pynkut ia ka shithi kaba ar jong u da kaba ai mynsiem. Ngi ioh ia ka jingai-mynsiem jong U Blei khnang ban ngin ai mynsiem ia kiwei pat (2 Korinth. 1:4, 6).

Ka jingbthah kaba saw bad kaba san, “To phin ia mut ia ka juh, to phin ia shongsuk” (2 Korinth. 13:11), ka long ka jingkhot sha ka jingiatylli. Kane ka jinglong jong ka jingkmen, ka jingpynkylla dur, ka jingai mynsiem, ka jingiatylli bad ka jingsuk ka long ka jinglong na ka bynta ka jingdon ryngkat U Blei, “U Blei jong ka jingieit bad ka jingsuk” (2 Kor. 13:11). Ka mih na ka jingtrei jong ka jinglong lai jong U Blei ha ka dohnud briew (2 Korinth. 13:14).

Ka jingaiei, ka jingieit bad ka jingiasyllok ki mih na ka jingtrei jong ka jinglong lai U Blei na ka bynta jong ngi. Kine ki lai tylli ki jinglong Khristan ki kyntiew ia ka jinglong kaba la pynpaw da ka jingdon ryngkat U Blei.

_Pynkhreh ïa kane ka lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton September 26._
