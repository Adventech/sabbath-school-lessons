---
title:  Ka Jingshongkha Bad Ka Jinglong Marwei
date:   23/07/2026
---

U Paul u pynskhem ba ka met “ka long ka templ U Mynsiem Bakhuid” (1 Korinth. 6:19,) ka paw ha kane ka dkhot ia ka jingmaham pyrshah ia ka jinglyngkar jong ka shongkha shongman. Ban long ka templ jong U Mynsiem ka long tang kane ka lad ban im ka jingim kaba khuid. Ka balang ka long ka kynhun Khristan kaba pyniapher ia lade na ka sawdong sawkun jong ka imlang ka sahlang jong ka. Ka dei ka jingdon ryngkat U Mynsiem Bakhuid kaba pynlong kaba lah.

`Pule 1 Korinth 6:19-7:9. Kumno kane ka bynta ka pynshai ba kumno u briew u lah ban pyntreikam ia ka hukum ban “kiar noh na ka jinglyngkar jong ka shongkha shongman” (1 Korinth. 6:18,)?`

Ki don ki jinghikai kiba kongsan shaphang ka jingshongkha ha 1 Korinth 7. Lada ngi shim kyllum, lah ban phiah ia kane ka lynnong ha ki ar bynta: (1) ki jingbthah shaphang ka jingshongkha (1 Korinth 7:1-24) bad (2) ki jingbthah shaphang ka jinglong marwei (1 Korinth 7:25-40). Ka Kitab 1 Korinth 7 ka iarap ia ngi ban sngewthuh ba ka jingkren shaphang ka jingshongkha ka long kaba kongsan bad kaba donkam.

Hynrei haba ngi pule ia ka 1 Korinth 7, ngi dei ban kynmaw ba u Paul u jubab ia ki jingkylli kiba kyrpang kiba iadei bad ki mat ha ka balang Korinth. Lym kumta, katto katne ki jingkren ki lah ban ialam sha ka jingmut ba u don ka jingpyrkhat kaba pynhiar dor shaphang ka jingshongkha, ha kaba kam long kumta (1 Tim 4:1-3, 1 Tim 5:14; ruh Heb 13:4).

Kaba phylla ka long ba ia ka hukum “phet ïap noh na ka jingleh awria” ha 1 Korinth 6:18 () la buh hapoh braket da ka jingmut ban iasoh bad U Khrist (1 Korinth. 6:17) bad ban long ka tempi jong U Mynsiem (1 Korinth. 6:19). Hato don daka wei pat ka lad kaba kham bha ban phet ïap na ka jingleh awria lane ka jinglyngkar ha ka shongkha shongman? Dashisha kam dei kumta.

Kumjuh ruh, U Blei u la thaw ia ka jingshongkha, hynrei ka dei ban sngewbha tang ha ka jingshongkha. Ka jingshongkha ka long ka kabu jong kito kiba don ha ka jingshongkha shynrang bad kynthei, tang ma ka ka jait shongkha kaba la mynjur ha ka Baibl.

Haba ong “phet ïap noh na ka jingleh awria,” U Paul u lah ban don ha ka jingmut jong ka jingiathuhkhana shaphang u Joseph (Jenesis. 39:6-18). Ka Baibl ka ong ba shuwa ka jingkwah brai jong ka tnga jong u Potiphar, u Joseph “u la phet noh na iing” (Jenesis. 39:18). Ia kane la kdew ym duna ia ka saw sien ha ka Jenesis 39:6-18. Ka Baibl kam shem la kren da kaba beit, hynrei la pynpaw ba u Joseph u ap ban iathiahlok tang ha ka shongkha shongman (Jenesis. 41:45). U long u briew uba la dap da U Mynsiem Bakhuid (Jenesis. 41:38) bad uba kwah ban leh ia kaba dei ha khmat U Blei.

`Kumno ngi kum ka balang ngin iada ia lade na ki jingiaid bakla na ka lynti kaba dei ha ka shongkha shongman ba lyngkar kiba synshar ia ka dustur?`
