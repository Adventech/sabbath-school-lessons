---
title:  Ka Jingbym Ïahap Hapdeng Ka Jingngeit Bad Ka Jingleh
date:   19/07/2026
---

Ha ka histori Khristan baroh kawei, ki riewstad niam, ki pastor bad ki riewngeit ki la pule bniah ia ka Testament Bathymmai khnang ban tip kumno ka balang ka dei ban long. Ngi sngewphylla, kum ka nuksa, ia ka balang jong ki Kam ki Apostol. Hynrei ngi duh noh kloi ia kawei ka bynta kaba kongsan: ki briew ki don ki jingeh. Ka long kumba ngi lah ruh ban pule ia ka Testament Bathymmai ban iohi aiu ka Baibl ka ong shaphang kumno ka Balang kam dei ban long. Ki shithi jong u Paul sha ki nong Korinth ki long ka jingsdang kaba bha.

`Pule 1 Korinth 5: 1-13 Kaei ka jinglong kaba la pynthut ha ka jingngeit kaba u Paul u batai ha kine ki dkhot, bad balei ka long kaba pynsngewsih pynthut haduh katta katta?`

Ka jingong “ka tnga u kpa jong u” (1 Korinth. 5:1) ka pyni ba u Paul u thew ia ka jingiadei kaba iadei bad ka jingshong sang hapdeng u briew bad ka meinah jong u. Ia kane ka jinglong la iathuh lehse “da kiba ha iing jong ka Kloi” (1 Korinth. 1:11,). La ñiew ia ka jingshong sang kum ka pop kaba shyrkhei haduh katta katta ba ym shym la shah ia ka wat hapdeng ki nongmane bleithaw ki jentil” (1 Korinth. 5:1,). Bad wat la katta ruh, ka dang jia mynta ha ka balang Khristan kaba nyngkong? Ki kyntien jong u Paul ha 1 Korinth 5:1, 2 ki pyni ba u sngewlyngngoh ia ka khubor ba uwei u dkhot balang u leh ia kane.

Hynrei kane ka jinglong kaba sniew ka nang kham sniew. U Paul u kham sngewlyngngoh haba u sngewthuh ba, ban ia kaba sngewsih ia ka jinglong jingman, ki nong Korinth ki la sngew sarong wat ia lade namar ba ki la shah ia kum kane ka pop (1 Korinth. 5:1, 2,). Kumta, u thmu ban pynbeit ym tang ia u briew uba khlem akor hynrei ia ka balang ruh namar ka jingbym iahap jingmut kaba paw hapdeng ka jingngeit bad ka jingleh. Ha ka jingshisha, u Paul u pynshai beit ba ka jinglong ba sngewrit jong ka balang ia uta u briew uba la leh sang ka la dawa ia ka jingpynbeit. Hynrei ban sngew sarong ia kum kane ka jingleh sang, bad wat ban sngew sarong ia ka (1 Kor. 5:2, 6)! Kane ka la long kaba palat ia u Paul ban shah. Kaei kaba la bakla bad kine ki briew?

Ngim don jingbatai balei ba ka balang ha Korinth ka la shah ia u briew uba leh ia ka kam kaba iadei bad ka jingshong sang. Lehse u long u dkhot uba riewspah na uba ka balang ka ioh jingmyntoi? Lane, lehse, namar ba “kiei kiei baroh ki long kiba la shah da ka aiñ” (1 Kor. 6:12), kim shym la shim ia ka kum ka rukom kaba ki dei ban don. Ngim tip ia kata.

La ki daw kiba shisha ki long kumno kumno ruh, ki la kylla matlah ia ka jingpynkheiñ kaba paw pyrthei ia ki Jingthoh Bakhuid (Leb. 18:7, 8). Bad ki la sngew sarong wat ia kata.

`Ïa kiei kiei kiba la pynrem shai ha ka Jingthoh Bakhuid kiba ngi kum ka balang ngi don ha ka jingma ban shah, baroh ha ka kyrteng jong ka “jingieit” bad “jingpdiang”?`
