---
title:  Ka Jingiada ïa ka Jinglong Jong Ka Balang
date:   21/07/2026
---

Ha 1 Korinth 6: 1-11. U Paul u iai bteng ia ka jingkren jong u ba kumno ki Khristan ki dei ban kdew ia kine ki mat jingjia ha kaba iadei bad ki briew ha ka balang.

`Pule 1 Korinth 5: 12, 13 bad 1 Korinth 6: 1-11 Kaei kaba u Paul u pyrshang ban hikai ia ki Nong Korinth bad ia ngi?`

Ka kyntien Grik _pragma_ ha 1 Korinth 6:1, kaba la pynkylla kum “ka jingpyrkhat” ha ka Baibl, ka dei ka kyntien kyllum kaba mut “kaei kaei.” Hangne, ka thew ia ka kam aiñ. Ka long kaba donkam ban kynmaw ba 1 Korinth 6:1–11 kam kdew shaphang ka kam runar. Ia ka bor jong ki iingbishar paidbah na ka bynta ki kam runar la pynskhem ha ki Rom 13:1-5. U Paul u kren shaphang ka jingjia jong ki kam awria hadien ka jong ka jingshong sang, kumba u Moses u la leh ha ka Deuteronomi 22:22-24. Kane ka pyni haduh katno ka rukom leh jong u Paul ia ki jingeh ha ka balang ka long kaba la pynshong nongrim ha ki Jingthoh Bakhuid.

Ka jingshisha ba ia ka jingjia ha ka 1 Korinth 6:1-11 la buh ha ki braket da ki bynta kiba iadei bad ka jingleh awria (1 Korinth 5, 1 Korinth. 6:12- 20) ka lah ban pyni ba ka “mat” ha 1 Korinth 6:1 ka iadei bad ka jingleh awria. Ngim tip thikna kaei ka jingjia, la ka dei ka jingeh kaba rit ki bym dei ki kam niam, kum ka jingïakynad halor ka mar ka mata, ne ka jingeh ha ka jingïadei para briew.

La ka long kumno kumno ruh ka _pragma_ ha kaba kut, u Paul um sngewbha ban iohi ba ki dkhot balang ki rah ia ka sha ka iingbishar paidbah. Hato kim lah, kum ki para Khristan, ban iatreilang hapdeng jong ki, ban ia kaba wanrah ia ka mat ha khmat “ki bymhok” (1 Korinth. 6:1,)?

Ka lah ruh ban long, kumba don kiba pyrkhat, ba ki nongmudui jong 1 Korinth 6:1 ki long u kpa bad u khun jong 1 Korinth 5:1. Ha kano kano ka rukom, kam donkam ban sngewthuh ia ka mat ban sngewthuh ia ka jingmut. U Paul u la sngewkhia ia ka jinglong jong ka balang kum ka kynhun Khristan kumba la iohi da ki briew kiba nabar. Ki Khristan kim dei ban pynpaw ia kiei kiei kiba jakhlia ha ki jaka paidbah (1 Kor. 6:6). Bad kim dei ruh ban pyndonkam da ki lad pyrthei ban bishar ia ki kam kiba hapoh. Ha ka pyrthei Roman, ki briew kiba kham heh ha ka spah ne ha ka kam sain hima sima ki kham sngewtynnad ban shah jied ha ka iingbishar. Pynban, ki Khristan ki dei ban leh ia ka jingbishar kaba kum U Khrist bad ban pyniapher ia lade na ki jinglong pyrthei.

`Pyrkhat ia ka jingthoh jong u Paul ia ki jingsniew ha 1 Korinth 5:10, 11 bad 1 Korinth 6:9, 10. Balei u jer kyrteng ia ki pop shongkha shongman ryngkat bad kiwei pat ki pop kum ka jingmane bleithaw, ka jingtuh, ka jingkwah brai bad ka jinglute?`
