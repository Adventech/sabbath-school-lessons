### Ki Jingpule Na Ka Bynta Kane Ka Taïew
1 Korinth. 5:1-13, 2 Korinth. 2:5-10, 1 Korinth. 6:1-13, 1 Thessoloni. 4:1-8, 1 Korinth. 6:19-7:9.

> <p>Dkhot Kynmaw</p>
> “Hato phim tip ba kata ka met jong phi ka long ka iingshong U Mynsiem Bakhuid uba hapoh jong phi, ia uba phi la ioh na U Blei, bad ba phim long la ki jong hi? Namar la thied noh ia phi da ka dor bah: te namar kata to pyndonburom ia U Blei ha ka met, had ha ka mynsiem jong phi, kiba long ki jongU Blei. ” (1 Korinth 6:19,20,).

Ka jabieng jong ngi ka long kum ki sponge: kaei kaei kaba la wanrah ha ki, lyngba ki jingsngew jong ngi, ka sah ha ki. Ngi lah ban nym sngewthuh ia kiba bun kiei kiei kiba wan rung ha poh ka jabieng (ngin ym lah ban pyrkhat shai lada ngi kynmaw ia baroh), hynrei ki don baroh hangta, bad, ha ki katno katne ki bynta, ka ktah ia kaei kaba ngi pyrkhat, ka jingsngew bad leh.

Dei na kata ka daw ba ka long kaba suk bha ia ngi, wat kum ki Khristan, ban shah ktah ha kiei kiei kiba sniew baroh kiba don sawdong jong ngi. Ka balang Khristan, naduh kaba sdang, ka la ialeh pyrshah ia kane ka jingeh. Na-ei, kum ka nuksa, ka wan ka jingrakhe ia ka sngi Robibar ka wan? Hato ka balang ka shu tan ne wanrah ia ka na ka suiñ? Hooid da shisha. Ka wan na ka dustur kaba don sawdong jong ka.

Bad ngi lah ban iohi ia kane ka nongrim ba la paw hangne ha Korinth. Hadien ka jingkyrpad pyrshah ia ka jinglong ba iapait (1 Korinth 1-4), mynta u Paul u phai sha ki mat kiba iadei bad ka jingleh awria, ki jingmudui, ka jingkhaii met, ka jingshongkha bad ka jinglong marwei (1 Korinth 5-7). Ki jinglong jong ka pyrthei ki la ktah shibun ia ki. Ka jingiapait ka jingiaprat ia kaba la pyni ha 1 Korinth 1–4 ka la plie lad ïa ka jinglong babha ba la pynrem ha ki lynnong kiba hadien. Kumno u Paul u wad ban pynbeit ia kane ka pop ha ka balang, bad kiei ki jinghikai kiba ngi lah ban shim na kaei kaba u la thoh?

_Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, July 25._
