Pule Ellen G. White, “A Message of Warning and Entreaty,” pp. 298–308, ha The Acts of the Apostles.

Kaba sngewtynnad ka long ba ha ka thup jong ki jingsniew ha 1 Korinth 5:10, 11 bad 1 Korinth 6:9, 10, la buh kyrteng ia ka jingmane bleithaw bad ka jingdih kyiad ryngkat bad ka jingleh awria ne jingklim. Kumba u Paul u pynkynmaw ha 1 Korinth 10:7 (ianujor bad ka Eksodos 32:1-6), ki jingkhawai mane bleithaw la ju pynpaw da ka jingbam bad jingdih palat, kaba plie lad ia ka jingleh awria (1 Kor 10:8). Ka Ellen G. White ka ong kumne:

“Kam long ka bym lah ia uno uno ban ioh ia ka jingkyrkhu jong ka jingpynkhuid katba ki dang long kiba khwan lade bad kiba bam lalot... Ka bor jong ka riti synshar briew ban pyrshah ia ki jingshah lehbeiñ ba la buh halor jong ka ka long kaba phylla, hynrei ki jingmlien bakla kiba iai bteng ha ka bam bad jingdih palat kin pyntlot ia kawei pa kawei ka jingtrei jong ka met. Ha ka jingpynhun ia ka jingkwah bad jingkwah awria kaba la pynkylla khongpong wata ia kiba kam khristan la pyndkoh ia ka jingtrei kam jong ka bad pynduna ia ka bor met, bor pyrkhat bad bor jinglong ka-akor.”— The Sanctified Life, sla 25, 26 .

“Haba uno uno u long uba thylli pura ia ka lade, haba la beh noh ia uwei pa uwei u blei ba bakla na ka mynsiem, ia ka jinglong thylli la pyndap da ka jingtuid jong U Mynsiem U Khrist. Uba kum uta u don ka jingngeit kaba trei da ka jingieit bad kaba pynkhuid ia ka mynsiem na baroh ki jingtngit ha ka akor bad ka mynsiem.”—Ellen G. White, The Home Missionary1, Nohprah89 .

“U Blei U wad ban kyntiew ia ngi sha ka kyrdan ba jrong, ba khuid, ba kynja bneng jong U. Na ka bynta kane U Mynsiem jong U U ialeh bad ngi... Ki jinglong ba kynja met jong ngi, lymda la pynbeit da U Mynsiem Bakhuid U Blei, ki don ha ki ia ki symbai jong ka jingiap ha ka mynsiem.”—Ellen G. White, Manuscript 18, 12 .

**Ki Jingkylli Ban Ïa Kren Lang:**

`Bun ki bangeit ha Korinth ki la pynpaw ia ka jingkwah ban ioh ia ka jingmynjur jong ka kolshor. Balei kata ka long kaba ma haduh katta katta ia ka jinglong Khristan? Ngi lah ban leh aiu ban iada na ka jingleh ia kajuh ka jingbakla?`

`Ka jingkylli lyngkot jong u Paul “Hato phim tip ba ka met jong phi ka long ka templ U Mynsiem Bakhuid?” (1 Korinth. 6:19) ka pynkut ia ka thup jong ki hynrñiew tylli ki jingkylli ha 1 Korinth 5, 6 ba la pynrung da ka rukom ki rul ba tikna “Hato phim tip?” (1 Korinth. 5:6; 1 Korinth. 6:2, 3, 9, 15, 16, 19). Baroh ki dawa ia ka jubab kaba pynskhem bad kaba la banjur — ia kaei kaei kum “Hooid phi leh.” Kumno kine ki jingkylli ki iarap ia ngi ban sngewthuh ia ki jingsngewkhia jong u Paul shaphang ka balang? Balei ngi ruh, ngi dei ban sngewkhia ia kine ki mat mynta?`

`Ka jingshongkurim ka dei na U Blei (Jenesis. 1:27, 28; Jenesis. 2:18-24) bad kaba dei ban burom ia ka (Hebru. 13:4). Ha ka juh ka por ki ba bun ki shim ba ka long kaba la rim, kumno ngin pyni sha ka pyrthei ba ka jingshongkha ka long shisha ka jingai na U Blei, kaba wan beit na ka Eden hi?`
