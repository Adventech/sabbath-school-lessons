---
title:  Ka Dawai Pyrshah Ia Ka Jingimlyngkar Ha Ka Shongkha Shongman
date:   22/07/2026
---

`Pule 1 Thessaloni 4:1-8. Kane ka bynta ka ong aiu shaphang ka jingiadei hapdeng ka jingpynkhuid bad ka jingkiar na ka na ka jingimlyngkar ha ka shongkha shongman?`

Wat la u Paul u la thoh sha uwei pat ha ki jingthoh haneng, hynrei ia ka nongrim lah ban pyndonkam ha baroh sawdong, ha baroh ki Khristan, wat la katta ruh, ka ialam sha ka jingkylli: Kaei kaba la jia ha Korinth? Balei kine ki jingeh baroh?

Katto katne ha Korinth kumba ka paw ki ngeit ba namar ba ka gospel ka la pyllaitluid ia ki, la shah ia ki ban leh ia kano kano ka kam. Ki la kynthoh ba kumba la thaw ia ka kpoh na ka bynta ka jingbam, kumta ruh la thaw ia ka met na ka bynta ka jingshongkha, bad ka jingshongkha na ka bynta ka met (1 Korinth. 6:13). U Paul u jubab ba kane ka long ka jingbatai kaba bakla ia ka jinglaitluid jong ki Khristan. Ka jingbymlong hok ha ki kam shongkha shongman ka long ka bym iahap bad ka jinglong Khristan bad ka long ka jingpyndonkam bakla ia ka jinglaitluid ba la ai ha u briew lyngba ka gospel (Rom 8:2, Galatia. 5:13). La pyllait ia ngi na ka pop, ym shym la pyllait “laitluid” ban leh ia ka (Rom 8:2; Rom 6:18, 22). Ha ka jingshisha, “ka met ka long ... na ka bynta U Trai, bad U Trai na ka bynta ka met” (1 Korinth. 6:13). Ngi long ki jong U Khrist (1 Korinth. 6:15), bad kaei ba ngi long, ka dei ban ktah ia kaei kaba ngi leh. Kawei ka jinglong ka iadei bym lah ban pyniakhlad bad kawei pat. Ia kane la pyni ha 1 Korinth 6 ha ki lai tylli ki rukom bapher bapher.

Nyngkong eh, la ithuh ia ngi kum kiba la sait, la pynkhuid bad la pynksan “ha ka kyrteng U Trai Jisu bad da U Mynsiem U Blei jong ngi” (1 Korinth. 6:11,). Ki pop ba la kdew ha 1 Korinth 6:9, 10, kumjuh ruh ka jingimlyngkar ha ka shongkha shongman ba la pynrem ha 1 Korinth 6:12–20, kim don jaka ha ka jingim jong kito kiba la shah sait, pynkhuid bad pynksan.

Kaba ar, ngi long ki dkhot jong U Khrist (1 Korinth. 6:15). Kane ka mut ba ngi dei ban ialong kawei bad U Khrist (1 Korinth. 6:17). Ka jingleh awria ka long ka jingpynkhein ia kata ka jingiasoh (1 Korinth. 6:13, 15). Uno uno uba iasoh bad uwei u briew ha ka jingiadei kaba shabar jong ka shongkha shongman u kylla long “kawei ka met” bad u (1 Korinth. 6:16). Ka jingiasoh bad U Khrist lyngba U Mynsiem ka dei ban pynbeit ia ka akor Khristan ha ki kam shongkha shongman.

Kaba lai, ka met jong ngi ka long “ki Temple U Mynsiem Bakhuid” (1 Korinth. 6:19, 20,). Tang kawei ka lad ban im ka jingim kaba khuid ryngkat bad ka jinglong ba janai ha ki kam shongkha shongman ka long da kaba don ka jingiadei kaba jan bad U Khrist lyngba U Mynsiem Bakhuid. Ha kawei pat ka jaka, u Paul u kdew shaphang ka jingshem jong ka jinglong ka templ jong U Mynsiem ha kaba iadei bad ka jingai ia ka met “kum ka jingknia kaba im, kaba khuid bad kaba pdiang sngewbha ha U Blei” (Rom 12:1,).

`Pyrkhat ia ka jingjot kaba ki pop shongkha shongman ki la wanrah halor ka jinglong briew. Kane ka dei ban iathuh aiu ia ngi shaphang katno kane ka mat ka dei ban long kaba khia ia u Khristan?`
