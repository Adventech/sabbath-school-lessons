### Ki Jingpule Na Ka Bynta Kane Ka Taïew
1 Korinth 12, Ephesos. 4:11-13, 1 Korinth 13, 1 Petros. 4:8-11, 1 Korinth. 14:27, Amos 3:7.

> <p>Dkhot Pule-lyndet</p>
> “To iabeh phi sha kane ka jingieit: la kumta ruh to da kwah hir hir ruh ia kita ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem, hynrei khamtam ba phin ioh iathuhlypa” ( 1 Korinth 14:1,).

Kum ka met u briew, ka balang ka long kawei hynrei ka don bun ki dkhot, uwei pa uwei u don la ki jong ki bynta, ki kam bad ki jingai. Lada pyntreikam da ka jingieit, kine ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem ki kyntiew ia ka jingsngew iatylli kaba pynpaw ia ka jinglong jong U Blei lai ha U Wei.

Ha kane ka taiew ngin sa bishar bniah ia ka 1 Korinth 12–14 bad ka jinghikai jong ka shaphang ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem. Kane ka bynta ka long ka bynta kaba kham heh khyndiat, ha kaba u Paul u kren shaphang ka jinglong Khristan kaba la khmih lynti ha kaba la buh da ki niam (1 Korinth 11–14). Ka jingsngewkhia ba kongsan jong u Paul ka iadei bad ka jingeh jong ki jingiaseng bym ryntih. Ka jubab jong u ia kane ka jingeh ka long ba ka balang ka long ka met kaba ki dkhot jong ka ki don ki kam bapher bapher kiba iarap ha “ka jingtei ia ka met U Khrist” (Eph. 4:12, NKJV). Ha ka jingkren lyngkot, U Blei u la ai ha ka balang ia ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem khnang ban kyntiew ia ka jingiatylli lyngba ka jingpher.

Hooid, u Paul u dang don ha ka jingmut jong u ia ka jingeh jong ka jingiapher kynhun kaba la kren ha ki saw tylli ki lynnong ba nyngkong jong 1 Korinth, ha kaba ka jubab ia ka jingbym iasngewthuh jingmut hapdeng ki dkhot balang ka long ka jingiatylli ha U Khrist. Mynta, u pynpaw ia kata ka jingmut da kaba pyni ia ka jingsngewthuh jong u shaphang ka bynta jong ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem. Kat kum ka jingong u Paul, ka jingiatylli ha U Khrist bad U Mynsiem ka long tang kawei ka lad ban kiar na ki jingiapher jingmut.

_Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, August 8._
