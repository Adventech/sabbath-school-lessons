---
title:  Ki Jingïapher Jong Ki Jingai
date:   02/08/2026
---

Ha 1 Korinth 12:1, u Paul u pynrung ia ka phang kaba thymmai da ka rukom kaba tikna “mynta shaphang.” Ki riewstad ki iakren hato la u kren shaphang “ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem” ne “ki briew ba kynja mynsiem” namar ka kyntien Grik _tōn pneumatikōn_ ka ailad ia baroh ar ki jingbatai. “Ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem” kat kum ka jingshai ba la ai ka 1 Korinth 12:4, ha kaba u Paul u kdew shai shaphang ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem. Ha 1 Korinth 12:2, 3, u Paul u pyni ba ka jingai kaba nyngkong eh jong U Mynsiem ka long ka jingphla shlur ba U Jisu U long U Trai. Ha ki por jong ka Testament Bathymmai, ban ong ba U Jisu U long U Trai ka long kum ban ong ba U Kaisar um dei (Ki Kam 17:7; ruh Ioannis 19:12, 15). Ia kane la iohi kum ka jingleh pyrshah ia ka bor syiem bad, namarkata, la pynshitom da ka jingiap.

U Jisu bad u Paul ki la pynksan ba ka jingngeit ha U Blei — wat hapdeng ka jingshah pynshitom beiñ bad ka jingma jong ka jingiap — ka long ka jingai jong U Mynsiem. Ha ka jingshisha, ka jingngeit ka long ka jingai kaba kongsan tam. Kam long kaba phylla ba ka jingngeit ka long kaba nyngkong eh ha ka thup ha 1 Korinth 13:13. Ba ka jingngeit ka long ka jingai ba kynja mynsiem ka paw shai na ka jingong jong u Paul ha 1 Korinth 12:9. Hynrei ki don bun kiwei pat ki jingai. Ka jingshisha ba U Mynsiem Bakhuid U sam ia ki jait jingai bapher bapher “ha uwei pa uwei marwei marwei katba U mon” (1 Korinth. 12:11,) ka pynshisha ba baroh ki long kiba donkam.

`Pule Korinth 12:1–6. Kaei ka jingshon jur jong kine ki dkhot?`

Ka jingkren pat sa shisien ia ki “ba ïapher” ka pynksan ia ka jingbun ki jingai. Ia kaei kaba u Paul u khot “ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem” ha 1 Korinth 12:1 la pynbha ha ki dkhot 4–6, lyngba ki lai tylli ki bynta bapher bapher: “ki jingai” (_charisma_), “ki kam shakri” ne “ki jingshakri” (_diakonia_), bad “ki kam” (_energēma_). Wat la kine ki kyntien ki mut ïa kiei kiei kiba ïa pher, hynrei ka long kaba donkam ban ym pyniapher palat hapdeng jong ki namar ka jingiasyriem jong ka kine ki dkhot. Uwei u dei ruh ban pyrkhat ba ia ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem la shna khnang ban kyntiew ia ka jingiatylli kaba la pynshong nongrim ha ka jinglong lai jong U Blei (peit ruh Ephesos. 4:8-11). Katba U Mynsiem U ai ki jingai ha kiba ngeit, dei U Blei Uba ai bor ia ki ban shakri ia U Khrist ha ka imlang sahlang jong kiba ngeit (1 Korinth. 12:5, 6). Ia uwei pa uwei u bangeit la ai ia ki jingai marwei marwei (1 Korinth. 12:11), hynrei baroh ki dei ban pynmyntoi ia ka imlang sahlang jong ki bangeit baroh.

`Peit biang ia ka banjur jong u Paul ia ka jingiatylli. Balei kane ka long kaba kongsan ha ka balang?`
