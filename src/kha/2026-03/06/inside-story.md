### U Davis u Indians: Bynta II

_By Michael W. Campbell_

U Davis u khlem lah ban thoh hi dalade ia ka jingthoh ba khatduh jong u ha ka diary, u Davis u la bthah ia ka ha u nongialam jong u uba la thoh ia ka na ka bynta jong u: “U Blei u la kyrkhu khamtam ha kane ka jingleit. Nga la ioh ia ka mishon kaba pura ha wah Paruima, kawei pat ha lum Tulameng, bad nangta ngi la poi sha u lum Roraima. Ia kane ka steshon la seng ha ka 25 tarik u Iaiong, 1911, ka mishon kaba nyngkong eh jong ki Seventh Day Adventist ha kane ka thain. Ha ka jingthiah iap jong u, u Davis u la kular ba uwei u briew un wan hikai ia ki briew. U la iap ha ka 31 tarik u Jylliew, 1911, na ka jingpang blackwater fever bad la tep ia u da u Jeremaiah uba long u nongpeit ha u lum Roraima. Ka Carrie kan sa leit phai noh sha la iing ha ri America.

Hadien ka jingiap jong u Davis, ki nong Indians ki ia lum lang man la ka Sngi Sabbaton hajan ka jingtep jong u ban rwai. Wat hapdeng ki ba bun ki “jingkyrpad” na uwei u nonghikai, ka la long 14 snem shuwa ba ki mishoneri Adventist kin bud dien ia ka kam jong u Davis.

La kumba ha ka snem 1918, uwei u nongwad bniah u la lap ia ka kynhun ki Indians kiba rwai “Ym don paralok kum u Jisu uba rit” ha ka ktien phareng. U la shem ba ka long kaba phylla, ba ki la wanrah ba ka jingktah jong u Hynmen Davis bad ki la pynlong ia ka jingiaseng.

Ha ka snem 1922, hadien palat shisnem ka jingap, u Rangbah Jeremiah u la iais kjat saw taiew sha ka ophis jong ka Adventist mission ha Georgetown ban kyrpad ban ai ia u nonghikai. Ha ka snem 1924, ka komiti phiah ka la rai ba kim dei ban ap sa shisnem wat la la pynduna ia ka mang tyngka. U W. E. Baxter bad u C. B. Sutton ki la leit jngoh ia u lum Roraima ha ka snem kaba bud. Ki la poi ha ka jingtep jong u Davis ha ka 25 tarik u Lber, 1925, hadien ka jingleit kaba jngai ban shem ia ki Indian Davis. U Syiem Jeremaiah bad u khun jong u ki la wanrah ia ki ia ka shi song ki kot. Ha ka la don ka shithi na u Davis kaba la thoh ha ka 17 tarik u Jylliew, 1911, kaba pynshisha ba 130 ngut ki briew ha u lum Roraima ki la “pynbna da kaba sngewrit ia la ka jingthmu ban im shisha bad iaineh” ha “ka Gospel U Khrist bad ki jinghikai bad ki nongrim jong ka balang Adventist.” Ka jingleit shaduh ka mishon ka la pynlong ia baroh arngut u Baxter bad u Sutton ban ioh ia ka jingpang malaria ne khishoh.

Ha u bnai August 1927, da ka jingainguh kaba kyrpang, u Arthur bad ka Elizabeth Cotts ki la lah ban bteng ia ka kam ha u lum Roraima kaba la sdang bun snem mynshuwa.

Ka jingiap jong u Davis “ka la pynkhih ia ki Adventist” bad ka la long kum “ka jingpynkynmaw kaba iai bteng … ba ki dei ban pyndep ia ka kam jong ka gospel wat ha ki jaka ba jngai.”

Mynta ka sngi ia ka Hospital Adventist kaba don 54 jingthiah ha Georgetown, Guyana, la ai kyrteng kum ka burom ia u Davis. Kumjuh ruh ha ka snem 1956, ka Davis Indian Training School kaba don ha Paruima ha Kamarang ka la long ka bynta jong ka Mount Roraima Mission. Ha kaba sdang jong ki snem 1980, la iathuh ba ki don palat 1,500 ngut ki Adventist hapdeng ki “Davis Indians.”

Ia kane ka jingiathuhkhana la shim na ka jingthoh shaphang ka jingim jong u Michael Campbell ha ka Encyclopedia online jong ki Seventh-Sngi Adventist. Ngi khot sngewbha ia phi ban pule na ka https://encyclopedia.adventist.org ban ioh tip shuh shuh ia ki jingiathuhkhana shaphang ki mishoneri Adventist.
