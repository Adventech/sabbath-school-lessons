---
title:  Ka Lad Kaba Kham Bha
date:   04/08/2026
---

“Ka jingieit kam dei kawei na ka jingai hapdeng kiba bun. Ka long ka lad lyngba kaba ki jingai baroh ki poi sha ka jingthmu ba kongsan jong ki.”—U Carl P. Cosaert, “1 Korinth,” Andrews Bible Commentary: New Testament. 1643.

`Pule 1 Korinth 13: 1-7. Kaei ka bynta jong ka jingieit katba kum ka jingai ba kynja mynsiem?`

1 Korinth 13 ka hikai ba tang lyngba ka jingieit lah ban pyndonkam hok ia ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem. U Paul u sdang ia ka 1 Korinth 13 da kaba kdew sha ki jingai ba la kren ha 1 Korinth 12, tang ban ban jur ia ka jingbym don dor jong ki lada ym pynkhih da ka jingieit. Kumta, ka jingtip (1 Korinth. 12:8) bad ka jingngeit (1 Korinth. 12:9), wat ka jingngeit “ka ba lah ban pynkynriah ia ki lum” (1 Korinth. 13:2), kim long ei ei khlem ka jingieit (1 Korinth. 13:2). Khlem ka jingieit, ka jinglah ban kren da kiwei pat ki thylliej (1 Korinth. 12:10, 28, 30) la pynduna noh tang kum “ka wiang rnong kaba sawa lane kum ka shangkuriaw bariew shaw shaw” (1 Korinth. 13:1). Kumjuh ruh wat ka jingai kaba kongsan jong ka jingiathuhlypa kam long ei ei khlem ka jingieit (1 Korinth. 13:2).

Ha 1 Korinth 13:4–7, u Paul u pynleit jingmut ha kaei ka jingieit bad kaei kaba kam dei ka jingieit; khamtam eh, kaei kaba ka jingieit ka leh bad kaei kaba kam leh. Ki verb kiba u la jied ban pynpaw ia ka jingieit ki pyni ba ka jingieit kam dei eh kaei kaei kaba ngi sngew hynrei ka long kaei kaei kaba ngi leh. Kumta, u Paul u kdew ba ka jingieit (1) ka pyni ia ka jingiaishah; (2) pyni ia ka jingsbun; (3) ka lehkmen ha ka jingshisha; (4) ka shah ia kiei kiei baroh; (5) ngeit ia kiei kiei baroh; (6) kyrmen ia kiei kiei baroh; bad (7) ka iaishah jem ia kiei kiei baroh. Pynban, ka jingieit (1) kam ju bishni; (2) kam ju leh sarong ia lade; (3) kam ju pynmeng mynsiem da ka jingsngew sarong; (4) kam ju leh khlem akor; (5) kam ju wad tang ia kiei kiei la ka jong [hok]; (6) kam ju shait khih bitar; (7) kam ju buh jingkynmaw ia ki jingbakla; bad (8) kam kmen ha ka jingbymhok.

Kane ka jingkhein lang ia ki 15 tylli ki verb ka ai ka jingpyniaid kaba skhem na ka bynta ka jinglong kaba dei ha ka jingpynmlien ia ki jingai. Kaba kham kyrpang eh ka long ba kane ka jingiakren ka long ha ka jinglong bashisha jong ka jingieit ka don hapdeng 1 Korinth 12 bad 14, ha kaba u Paul u la leh bad ka jingia-kajia ha kaba iadei bad ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem. Hooid, ka jingieit ka long u shabi ban pyndonkam hok ia ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem. Ia ka jingieit ruh la buh marjan bad ka jingngeit bad ka jingkyrmen, “hynrei kaba kham khraw na kine ka long ka jingieit” (1 Korinth. 13:13).

`Balei ka jingieit ka long kaba kongsan ha ka jingngeit jong ngi? Kaei ka lad kaba kham bha ban mad ia ka jingshisha jong ka jingieit U Blei ban ia kaba wad da ka ba duwai dem diap ban pynpaw ia kata ka jingieit ha kiwei pat?`
