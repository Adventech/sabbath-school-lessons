---
title:  Ka Jingiatylli Lyngba Ka Jingïapher
date:   03/08/2026
---

Ka ktien jong ka jinglong kawei kaba la pynpaw ha 1 Korinth 12:4–6 (“ujuh U Mynsiem,” “ujuh U Trai,” bad “ujuh U Blei”) la pynpaw ha kiwei pat ki bynta jong ka 1 Korinth 12. Ia kane la pynshisha da ka jingpyndonkam jong u Paul ia ki kyntien kum “kawei bad u juh U Mynsiem” (1 Korinth. 12:11). 12:12), “kawei ka met” (1 Korinth. 12:12, 13, 20), “uwei u Mynsiem” (1 Korinth. 12:13 [arsien]), bad “kajuh ka jingsumar iwei ia iwei pat” (1 Korinth. 12:25, NKJV).

Ha ryngkat bad ka jingmut jong ka jinglong kawei, u Paul u pynpaw ia ka jingïapher jong ki dkhot ha ka met U Khrist lyngba ki jingkren kum “shibun ki dkhot” (1 Korinth. 12:12, 20), “la pynbaptis ia ngi baroh” (1 Korinth. 12:13), “ym tang kawei ka dkhot hynrei bun” (1 Korinth.12). (1 Kor. 12:17), “kawei pa kawei na ki” (1 Korinth. 12:18), “ki dkhot jong ka met” (1 Korinth. 12:22, 23), bad “ki dkhot baroh” (1 Korinth. 12:26). Kane ka jingban jur ia baroh ar ka jingiatylli bad ka jingbym iadei ka juh ka pyni ba ki jingai ba kynja mynsiem ki thmu ban kyntiew ia ka jingiatylli lyngba ka jingiapher.

Kane ka jingiatylli hapdeng ka jingpher ka dei ban pynpaw ia ka jinglong U Blei. U Kpa u long uwei, U Khun u long uwei pat, bad U Mynsiem Bakhuid uwei pat. Ki lai ki pynneh ia la ki jinglong marwei katba ki iatrei lang ban tei ia ka balang bad ban pynkupbor ia ka na ka bynta ka mishon (1 Korinth. 12:4-6, Ephesos. 4:11-13).

`Pule 1 Korinth. 12:12-31 Balei ka jingiapyn-syriem ia ka met bad ki bynta kiba bun jong ka kiba biang kaba ïadei ban pyni ia ka balang bad ki dkhot jong ka?`

Kawei ka mat pdeng jong ka jingpyrkhat ha 1 Korinth 12 ka long ba wat la ki dkhot jong ka met ki long kiba pher iwei na iwei (1 Korinth. 12:15- 20), hynrei baroh ki shaniah iwei ïa iwei pat (1 Korinth. 12:21-26). Ki kjat ki shaniah ha ki khmat ban iohi shano ki dei ban iaid; ha kajuh ka por, ki khmat kim lah ban ktah ia kaei kaei— tang ki kti kiba lah. Kumjuh ruh, ka jingmut ba don ki dkhot kiba kham tlot (1 Korinth. 12:22) lane kiba kham duna ka burom (1 Korinth. 12:23) ban ia kiwei pat kiba long tang ka jingpynitynnad dur, namar baroh ki long kiba donkam (1 Korinth. 12:22).

Kaba sngewsih ka long ba hapeng ki Nong Korinth ka la don ka dem kamon ban khein kor ia ki katto katne ki jingai da kaba iehnoh ia kiwei pat. Ban pynbeit ia ki nong Korinth na kaba leh ia kane ka jingbakla, u Paul u la khot ia ka jingpyrkhat jong ki sha ka jingieit, kaba long “ka lynti kaba kham bha” (1 Korinth. 12:31). Ha kiwei pat ki kyntien, wat la ka jingai ka long kumno kumno ruh, lada pyntreikam da ka jingstad bad da ka jingieit, ka long kaba pynsngewbha ia U Blei.

`Peit ia ki thup jong ki jingai ha 1 Korinth 12:8-10, 28; Rom 12:6-8; bad Ephesos 4:11. Kaei ka jingai jong phi? Kumno phi lah ban pyndonkam ia ka ban tei ia ka met U Khrist?`
