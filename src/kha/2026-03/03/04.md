---
title:  Ka Jingstad Bad Ka Jinglong Rangbah
date:   14/07/2026
---

Ha ka jingïalang, ki kynhun baiapait ki mih na ka jingïohi kaba jrong palat ïa ki nongïalam briew. Kane ka long kaba ma shibun ia ka jingiatylli jong ka balang bad ia ka jingkoit jingkhiah ba kynja mynsiem jong ki dkhot, namar ka jingpyrkhat kaba bakla ia ka jingshakri Khristan ka lah ban ialam ia ka balang ban ai jingmut palat ia ki katto katne ki nongialam, baroh ha ka jingma ia kiwei pat. Ka jingmih na kum kane ka jingleh ka long ka jinglong jong ka jingiakob, kaba lah ban pyniakhlad pyniapait ia ka balang. Kaba kham palat ia kata, lada ngi kheiñ ia ki nongialam briew kum ki pdeng jong ka jinglong Khristan jong ngi, ngi buh jingma ban pynkynriah ia U Khrist na ka kyrdan kaba dei ha ka jingim jong ngi.

`Pule 1 Korinth 3: 1-4. Kumno u Paul u batai hangne ia ka jingbym pat long rangbah jong ka jinglong ba kynja mynsiem jong ki nong Korinth?`

U Paul u pynshai ba ka jinglong rangbah ba kynja mynsiem ka ialam ia u bangeit ban sngewnguh ia ka jingstad U Blei (1 Korinth. 2:6, 7), kaba la pynsngew ha ngi lyngba U Mynsiem (1 Korinth. 2:13), bad kaba iapher na ka jingstad jong kane ka arta (1 Korinth. 2:6), ka jingstad briew (1 Korinth. 2:13). Ia ka jingstad U Blei la pynpaw ha ka diengphna U Khrist (1 Korinth. 2:1-4). Kaba kham thikna, ia ka jingstad U Blei la pynpaw ha ka jingshah shitom, jingiap bad jingmihpat U Khrist. Kumta, shuwa ban sdang biang ia ka jingkyrpad jong u ia ka jingiatylli (1 Korinth. 3:1-17), u Paul u kwah ba ki nongpule jong u kin pdiang ia ka jingdonkam ia ka jingstad bad jinglong rangbah bashisha ha U Khrist.

Ki Khristan kiba stad bad kiba la san ki long ki briew kiba kynja mynsiem, ym kiba kynja doh, kim long kum ki khunlung (1 Korinth. 3:1). Ki ianujor ia kiei kiei kiba kynja mynsiem bad kiei kiei kiba kynja mynsiem, namar “ia kiei kiei kiba kynja mynsiem … la bishar bniah da kaba kynja mynsiem” (1 Korinth. 2:13, 14,). Ki Khristan kiba stad bad kiba la san bha ki bam da ka jingbam kaba eh, ym da ka dud (1 Korinth. 3:2; nujor bad ki Hebru. 5:12). U bangeit “Namar uno uno ba ju bam tang ka dud u long ubym shemphang ia ka ktien ka hok: namar u long u khunlung. Hynrei ka jingbam kaba eh ka long ia ki rangbah, kiba namar ka jingmlien ki don la ki jingsngewthuh ba la pynmlien ban bishar ia kaba bha ruh bad ia kaba sniew.” (Hebru. 5:13, 14). Ki Khristan kiba stad bad kiba la san kim ju ong, “Nga long u jong u Paul” lane “Nga long u jong u Apollos” (1 Korinth. 3:4), kaba thew ia ki briew bapher bapher.

Hadien baroh, kine ki briew ki long, kum ma ki, “ki para nongtrei U Blei” (1 Korinth. 3:9). Ngi, kum ka balang, ngi long ka lyngkha, ka jingtei bad ka templ jong U Blei (1 Korinth. 3:9, 16, 17). Baroh ngi long ki jong U Blei lyngba U Khrist (1 Korinth. 3:11).

`Kaei ka jingshem jong phi haba phi la shah pynduh jingkyrmen ha uwei uba phi la sngewtynnad bad ieit? Lada phi la iakynduh ia kane ka jingjia, kiei ki jinghikai kiba phi la ioh na ka?`
