Pule Ellen G. White, “The Training of the Twelve,” pp. 17–24, in The Acts of the Apostles.

“Ka jinglong kawei bad ka jingiatylli jong ki briew kiba sah jong U Blei kiba ngeit ia ka jingshisha kiba rah ia ka jingngeit kaba khlain sha ka pyrthei ba ki don ia ka jingshisha, bad ki long ki briew kiba kyrpang, kiba la jied jong U Blei. Kane ka jinglong kawei bad ka jingiatylli ka pynkulmar ia u nongshun, bad u la kut jingmut ba kan ym don shuh. Ka jingshisha kaba mynta, kaba la ngeit ha ka dohnud bad ki pynlong ka nuksa ha ka jingim jong ki, ka pynlong ruh ia ki briew jong U Blei kawei bad la ai iaki ka bor ktah kaba donbor.”—Ka Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 1, ka p. 327.

“U Blei u ialam ia ka jaitbynriew ban ieng ha ka jingiatylli kaba janai halor ka rynsan jong ka jingshisha bymjukut. U Khrist U la aiti ia lade ha ka pyrthei khnang ba Un lah ban ‘pynkhuid ha lade ia ka jaitbynriew kaba kyrpang, kaba Shitrhem ia ki kam babha.’ Ia kane ka rukom pynkhuid la thmu ban pynkhuid ia ka balang na baroh ki jingbymhok bad ki jingbym iasngewthuh jingmut ha ki. shapoh, bad pynleit jingmut ia ki bor jong ki ha ka kam kaba khraw kaba don ha khmat jong ki U Blei u thmu ba ki briew jong U baroh kin wan sha ka jinglong kawei jong ka jingngeit. Ka jingduwai U Khrist ha shuwa ban sahnarphna ia U ka long ba ki synran jong U kin long kawei, kumba U long kawei bad U Kpa, ba ka pyrthei kan ngeit ba U Kpa U la phah ia U. Kane ka jingduwai kaba ktah bad kaba phylla tam ka poi shaduh ki arta baroh, wat haduh kine ki sngi jong ngi; namar ki ktien jong U ki long: ‘Ngam duwai tang na ka bynta kine, hynrei na ka bynta kita ruh ki ban ngeit ha Nga da ka ktien jong ki.’ ”—— Ka Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 4, ka p. 17.

**Ki Jingkylli ban ïa Kren Lang:**

`Sha jan kaba kut jong ka jingshakri jong U ha ka pyrthei, U Jisu U la duwai ia ka jingiatylli, “ba kin long kawei baroh ... khnang ba ka pyrthei kan tip ba Me la phah ia Nga” (Ioannis 17:21–23,). Balei ka jinglong kawei ha U Khrist ka long ka jingïakren kaba khlaiñ na ka bynta ka jingshisha ba U Blei U la phah ïa la U Khun ban pynim ïa ka pyrthei? Haba iadei bad kane, balei ba ka jingbym don jingiatylli ka long ka jingkhanglad ia ka mishon jong ka balang??`

`Pule 1 Korinth 4:9–13 bad pyrkhat bha kumno la pyni ia ki apostol ha kane ka bynta. Kumno kane ka jingpyni ia ki apostol ka iapher na ki jinglong nongialam ba la khein kor ha ka pyrthei jong ngi? Kane ka bynta ka hikai aiu ia ngi shaphang katno ki jinglong jong U Blei bad ki jinglong jong kane ka pyrthei ki lah ban iapher?`

`Ha ka shithi 1 Korinth 4:16, u Paul u kyntu ia ki nong Korinth ban pyrthuh bud ia u. Hato phin kloi ban pyrthuh bud kum ki nongïalam briew? Kumno ka jingbud nuksa ia u nongialam ka lah ban iapher na ka jingpynkhraw ia uta u nongialam ia ka bym dei, wat la ka long kaba ma?`
