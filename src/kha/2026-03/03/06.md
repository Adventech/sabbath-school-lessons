---
title:  Ka Rukom Im Kaba Pynpaw Ia Ka Diengphna
date:   16/07/2026
---

Ka jingshisha ba ngim dei ban thaw ia ka jingiaprat, khamtam ha sawdong ki nongialam briew, kam mut ba ngim dei ban kyrshan ia ki nongialam jong ngi. Ngi dei ban sngewnguh bad iarap ia kito kiba ialam ha ka kam balang. U Blei u bthah ia ki briew ban leh ia ka kam shakri jong U ha ka pyrthei. Ki nongialam Balang kiba pyni ia ka rukom im kaba pynpaw ia ki jingpyndem ba la phalang da ka Diengphna ki long kiba dei hok ban sngew bad ban bud.

Bad kane ka long kumta namar tang ka Diengphna kaba don ka bor ban pynphai kylla ia kano kano ka rukom synshar kaba pyndonkam bakla ha ka liang ka jingpyndem ha ka Ktien U Blei. Ki nongialam kiba kum U Khrist ki pynshong nongrim ia ka jingjop jong ka jingshakri jong ki tang ha U Blei. Ha ka jingshakri jong U ha ka pyrthei, wat U Jisu kum u briew u la ai ka burom ha U Blei (Ioannis 17:4).

Kat kum ka jingong u Paul, ka jingshakri Khristan kaba iaineh ka dei ban long kaba la pynshong nongrim ha kaei kaba ngi lah ban khot ka theology jong ka Diengphna. Ka Diengphna ka long ka jingpynpaw jong U Blei ia ka jingstad bad ka bor jong U ban pynim. Ha kajuh ka por, ka pyni ruh ia ka jingstad briew kum ka jingbieit. Ha 1 Korinth 4:1–13, u Paul u pynshai bha kaei kaba kum kata ka theology jong ka Diengphna ka long. Nyngkong eh, u pyni ba dei U Blei uba buh ia ka nongrim jong ka jinglong nongialam Khristan (1 Korinth. 4:1-5). Kaba ar, u kdew sha ka jingshisha ba ka jingjynjar ka long ka dak jong ka jingshakri Khristan kaba shisha (1 Kor. 4:9, 11-13). Kane ka mat kaba ar ka dei shisha ban nang pynjanai shuh shuh.

`Pule 2 Korinth 11:23-28 bad Kolosai 1:24. Kaei ba kane ka hikai shaphang kaei ka jingmut ban shah shitom na ka bynta U Khrist?`

Ki nongialam Khristan ki bud ia ki dienjat U Jisu da kaba ki kloi ban shah shitom na ka bynta ki parabangeit shynrang bad kynthei, bad wat lada donkam ban iap na ka bynta ka jingshakri jong ki. U Paul u kdew ia lade bad ia u Apollos kum “ki briew kiba la rai pynrem sha ka jingiap” (1 Korinth. 4:9,). La pyni ia ki kum kiba iakynduh ia ka jingduna bam bad um, kumjuh ruh ba ki long “ki ba lyngkhuid, bad ki ba la shah shoh beiñ, bad ki bym don iing kaba tikna ban shong” (1 Korinth. 4:11). Nalor kata, la kren beiñ ruh ia ki, la pynshitom beiñ ia ki, la pynjah burom ia ki, bad “la pynlong ia ki kum ka jakhlia jong ka pyrthei, ka jakhlia jong kiei kiei baroh haduh mynta” (1 Korinth. 4:12, 13,). Shuh shuh, da kaba kdew da kaba lehbeiñ ia ki nong Korinth kum kiba riewspah, ki syiem, kiba stad bad kiba donburom (1 Kor. 4:8, 10), u Paul u pyni ba ka jingsarong kam dei ban don jaka ha ka jinglong nongialam Khristan bashisha, namar ka long ka tynrai jong ka jingiapait ha ka balang (1 Korinth. 4:6).

`Haduh katno phi la shah shitom na ka bynta U Khrist, la ka long kaei kaei ka bynta jong phi ha ka balang? Kiei ki jinghikai kiba lah ban shem na ka jubab jong phi?`
