---
title:  Ka Jingeh Jong Ki Kynhun Ha Ka Balang
date:   12/07/2026
---

Ka jingkyrpad jong u Paul ba “bad ba kin ym long ki jingiaprat hapdeng jong phi; hynrei ba phin long ia kiba la pynjanai lang ha kajuh ka jingmut bad ha kajuh ka jingiapyrkhat.” (1 Kor. 1:10,) ka jingpyni ha ki saw tylli ki lynnong ba nyngkong jong ka 1 Korinth. Ha ka jingshisha, kiba bun ki riewstad ki mynjur ba ka jingiatylli ka long ka phang kaba kynthup lut ia kiei kiei baroh kaba pyniasoh lang ia baroh ki bynta jong ka shithi.

`Pule 1 Korinth 1:12-17. Kumno kine ki dkhot ka iarap ia ngi ban sngewthuh haduh katno ka long ka bym shongnia ban thaw ia ki kynhuh sawdong jong ki nongialam ha ka balang? Kaei ka jingpynbeit jong U Paul?`

U Paul u pyndonkam da ki kyntien kiba khlain ban pyni ia ka jingbym don jingiatylli hapdeng ki dkhot balang ha Korinth. U pyndonkam ia ki kyntien Greek schisma (“ka jingïapher,” 1 Korinth. 1:10,) bad eris (“ka jingïaleh,” 1 Korinth. 1:11,). Ia ka kyrteng schisma (kumjuh ruh ia ka verb schizō, “ban pyniakhlad”) la pyndonkam shawei pat ha ka Testament Bathymmai ban batai ia ki jingiapher jingmut kiba pynmih ia ki kynhun. Ha ka jingshisha, ka noun eris (“ka jingialeh”) ka ju paw ha ki thup jong ki jingsniew kiba ym dei ban leh da ki Khristan.

`Pule Rom 1:29, Rom 13:13, 1 Korinth 3:3, 2 Korinth 12:20, bad Galatia 5:20. Kiei kiwei pat ki pop kiba la kdew ryngkat bad ka eris (“ka jingïaleh,” “ka jingïakynad”)? Kane ka iathuh aiu ia ngi shaphang katno ka long kaba sniew?`

Ki jingbym iasngewthuh jingmut ha ka balang Korinth ki la wan shabar— wat ha ka dur jong ki jingmudui pyrshah iwei ia iwei pat (1 Korinth. 6:1-3). “ia kaban pynsngew lehraiñ ia phi nga kren ia kane keiñ,” u Paul u la ong ha ki (1 Korinth. 6:5), shaphang kine ki jingiamudui hapdeng ki dkhot balang. Ha ka jingshisha, kim shym la buh sharud ia ki jingiapher jingmut jong ki wat haba ki rakhe ia ka jingkhawai U Trai (1 Kor. 11:17-22).

Ka jingeh jong ka jingbym don jingiatylli hapdeng ki dkhot balang ka long kaba shyrkhei haduh katta katta, bad u Paul u sngewkhia haduh katta katta ia kane, ba kane ka long ka mat kaba nyngkong eh kaba u kren ha kane ka shithi sha ki nong Korinth.

`Pule biang ia ka 1 Korinth 1:12-27. Nangta pyrkhat kumno kane ka bynta ka iarap ia ngi ban sngewthuh balei ba ki jingiaprat (jingiapait) ki long kiba ma haduh katta katta ia ka jingiatylli jong ka balang. Kaei kaba ka balang jong phi ka lah ban leh khnang ban lait na kane ka jingeh?`
