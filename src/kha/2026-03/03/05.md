---
title:  Ka Jinglong Shakri Kum U Khrist
date:   15/07/2026
---

Ha 1 Korinth 3:1–4, u Paul u ai jingmut ba ki kynhun ba iapait ki mih na ka jingbym san ha ka jinglong mynsiem. Hynrei shuwa ban kren ia kane ka phang, u pynskhem, “Ngi don ka jingmut U Khrist” (1 Korinth. 2:16). Kane ka kyntien ka lah ban thew ia ka rukom pyrkhat bad rukom leh jong U Khrist. Ha kiwei pat ki kyntien, u bangeit u don “ka jingmut U Khrist” haba u pyrkhat bad leh kum U Khrist. Ban pyntreikam ia kane ka jingmut jingpyrkhat ha kiei kiei baroh jong ka jingim kam long kaba suk hato kam long kumta? Ha ka pyrthei Greek bad Roman, ka la don ka jingiakhun kaba jur hapdeng ki riew pawkhmat ha ka sain pyrthei, ki riewstad, ki riewpyrkhat bad ki nongialam niam. Ka jingkwah eh ban ioh jingmynjur ha ka dustur jong ka imlang ka sahlang, kumba ka paw ka la pynlong ia ka balang Korinth ban bud ia ki kyndon pyrthei. Kane ka lah ban long ka jingma ia ka balang mynta ruh.

`Pule Philipi 2:5-8. Kumno kane ka dkhot ka iarap ia ngi ban sngewthuh ia ka jingong “Ka Jingmut jong U Khrist” 1 Korinth 2:16?`

Kumba long ha Korinth, ki jingiapher jingmut ruh ki la nang jia ha ka balang Philipi (Philipi. 2:1-4), lehse ha ka jinglong kaba kham rit. Ka Philipi 2:1–8 ka hikai ia ngi ba ka jinglong shakri kum U Khrist ka donkam ban iap ia lade bad ia ki jingkwah brai, bad ban wad ha ka jaka kata ban kyrkhu ia kiwei pat halor jong ngi, kumba U Jisu u la leh.

Ka jinglong shakri kaba kum U Khrist ka long kaba u Paul u mut da ka kyntien “ki shakri U Khrist” (1 Korinth. 4:1). Kane ka kyntien ka lah ban pynpaw ia ka jingmut ba ki shakri ia U Khrist kum ki nongiarap ne ki nongtrei hapoh jong ki. Ka paw shai ba ka jingpyrkhat kaba dei shaphang ki nongialam briew ka long kaba la pynshong nongrim ha ka nuksa jong ka jinglong nongialam jong U Khrist. La pyni shuh shuh ia ki shakri kum ki “Sordar” (1 Korinth. 4:1, 2). U Sordar u dei u briew uba la shaniah ban pyniaid ia ki jingdon jingem jong uwei pat. Bad kaei kaei kaba ngi don, baroh ka long ka jong U Khrist la kumno kumno.

`Da ka jingduwai to pyrkhat ia ka khubor jong ka Philipi 2:5-8. Kumno ngi sngewthuh ia kaei kaba kane ka iathuh ia ngi shaphang ka jingieit U Blei kaba u la kheiñduh ia lade na ka bynta jong ngi? Balei ba ngi donkam ban iap ia lade ha ka rukom ba, ha la ka jong ka pyrthei, ngin lah ban kohnguh ia kane ka jingieit?`
