---
title:  Ka Jingpynshong Nongrim Ha U Jisu
date:   13/07/2026
---

`Pule 1 Korinth 1:10. Kumno phi pyrkhat ba U Paul u mut da kaba “ba phin long ia kiba ia tylli lang ha kajuh ka jingmut bad ha kajuh ka jingiapyrkhat”?`

Ka jingthaw ia ki kynhun baiapait ka la pynlong hangne ia ka jingkyntait ia ka jinglong bashisha jong phi (uwei pa uwei) ha U Khrist (1 Korinth. 1:10). U Blei u la khot ia ngi “Sha ka jingiasyllok bad U Khun jong u, U Jisu Khrist uba U Trai jong ngi” (1 Korinth. 1:9,). U Trai jong ngi u long U Khrist, bad ngi dei ban long kiba la pynshong nongrim ha U. Kumta, ka jubab ia ki jingkylli rhetoric “Hato U Khrist U la phiah? Hato la sahnarphna ia U Paul na ka bynta jong phi? Lane la pynbaptis ia phi ha ka kyrteng U Paul?” (1 Korinth. 1:13) ka long ka jingpynsngew “Em!” U Khrist um shym la pyniakhlad. Dei U Khrist ia uba la sahnarphna na ka bynta jong ngi. La pynbaptis ia ngi “ha ka kyrteng U Kpa bad U Khun bad U Mynsiem Bakhuid” (Mathaios. 28:19,).

U Paul u kdew ba ngi long “ka met U Khrist bad uwei pa uwei u dkhot jong ka” (1 Korinth. 12:27; la pynskhem). Katba ka met ka don bun ki bynta — kawei pa kawei bad la ka kamram — ka dang long kawei ka met. Khnang ba ka met kan treikam bha, kawei pa kawei ka bynta ka hap ban trei ia la ka kam kat kum ka jinglah jong ka. Kane ka jingianujor ka pyni ba u Paul u wad ia ka jingiatylli, ym ia ka jingïalong kajuh kajuh. U wad ia ka jingiatylli ha ka jingpher. Kham palat ia kata, u wad ia ka jingiatylli wat hapdeng ka jingpher.

Hynrei baroh ki jingpyrkhat bad ki jingmut ki dei ban pyndem ha U Khrist, U Trai jong ngi. Ka jingshisha ba U Khrist U long U Trai jong ngi ka long ka jingmut kaba kongsan haduh katta katta ia u Paul haduh ba u pyndonkam ia ka bunsien, ha ka jingsdang jong ka 1 Korinth (1 Kor. 1:2, 7, 8, 9, 10). Kumta, shuwa ba u Paul un kren shaphang ka mat jong ki kynhun bad ki nongialam briew, u pynksan nyngkong ba baroh ngi don ia U Jisu kum U Trai jong ngi. Ka balang kam dei kaba la pynshong nongrim ha ki nongialam briew. Ki Khristan ki pynshong nongrim ha U Jisu.

Ka jingpynksan ia ka jinglong Trai U Jisu ha ki dkhot ba nyngkong jong ka 1 Korinth ka iarap ia ngi ban sngewthuh ia kaei kaba u Paul u mut da ki kyntien: “To iatylli lang ha kajuh ka jingmut bad kajuh ka jingbishar” (1 Kor. 1:10,). Ka kyntien Greek kaba la pynkylla kum “ka jingïatylli” ka wan na ka verb _katartizō_, kaba mut ba dei ban pynphai biang ïa kaei kaei sha ka jinglong kaba dei jong ka. Haba la thaw ia ki kynhun sawdong ki nongialam briew, ki jingiadei hapoh ka balang ki dei ban wanphai biang sha ka jinglong kaba dei jong ki, bad kata ka lah ban jia lyngba ka jingiatylli ha U Khrist bad ka jingiap ia lade kaba ka wanrah.

`Ha kine ki khyndiat phew snem ba la leit, katto katne ki bynta jong ka Balang Seventh-day Adventist ki la pynksan ia ki jingpule Baibl ha ki kynhun barit. Kaei ka jingiapher hapdeng ki kynhun baiapait bad ki kynhun kiba rit? Kumno ngin husiar ba ki kynhun kiba rit kin ym kylla long ki kynhun baiapait?`
