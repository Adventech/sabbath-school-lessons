---
title:  Ka Dur U Jisu
date:   12/08/2026
---

Katba ngi pule 1 Korinth 13:4–7, Ngi laha ban sngew dukkha haba ngi pyrkhat īa kane, ba ngim kot ha ka jingpynpaw īa ka jingieit. Ka i ba u Paul u don ha ka jingmut shaphang U Jisu haba u thoh īa 1 Korinth 13. Hooid shisha tang U Khrist hi Uba lah ban pynpaw īa ka jingieit kaba shisha. Kumta u Paul u pyni īa ka jingieit ba shisha tang ha ka dur U Jisu.

`Pule Ioannis 13:1, 34; Ioannis 15:9, 12; 1 Timothi 1:14; 2 Timoth 1:7, 13; 1 Ioannis 3:16; and 1 Ioannis 4:7–12, 19–21. Ngi lah ban īoh jinghikai aīu na kine ki dkhot?`

U Blei U long ka jingieit(1 Ioannis 4:8).U la ieit katta katta katba U la aiti noh īa U Khun ba la kha marwei (Ioannis 3:16). U Jisu U dei ka jingpynpaw ba dap pura jong kata ka jingieit(Heb. 1:3). Lada ngi kwah ban tip kumno ka jingieit ka pynpaw īalade, ngi dei ban peit sha U Jisu. Lada ngi peit bniah ha ka jingpynpaw īa U Jiau ha ka Testament Bathymmai, ngin īohi da kaba shai īa kita baroh kiba la thoh ha 1 Korinth 13 ki don ha U Jisu.

**U Jisu U Shah slem.** “La kumta ruh na ka bynta kane nga la īoh ka jingisynei, ba ha nga uba kongsan. U Jisu Khrist un īoh pyni shynna īa ka jingshahslem jong U baroh [makrothymia]” (1 Tim. 1:16).

**U Jisu Uba Sbun.** Ka Baibl ka ong ba“U Trai U long Uba sbun” (1 Pet. 2:3).Ka Kyntien “Trai”ha kane bynta ka kdew sha U Jisu.Ka Kyntien “Sbun” ha ka Greek chrēstos, Ka wan na ka juh ka thymmei kum ka verb chrēsteuomai (“Ban pyni jingsbun”) ha 1 Korinth 13:4.

**U Jisu U lehkmen ha ka jingshisha.** U Jisu U shem jingkmen haba U leh īa ka mon U Kpa bad U īoh ka jingieit jong U (Ioannis 15:9–11, Ioannis 17:12–14).

**U Jisu U shah/īa kiei kiei baroh.** Hebru 12:2, 3 ka ong ba U Jisu “U shah īa ka diengphna. . . . [U] shah īa ki jinglehbor jong ki nongpop”. Ym pat ju don u briew uba la shah kat U Jisu(Philippi 2:8). U la leh īa kane na ka bynta ka jingkmen ba la buh hakhmat jong U!

**U Jisu U Ngeit īa kiei kiei baroh.** Haba u Ananias u la artatien shaphang ka jingkylla kabamut u Paul (Ki Kam 9:13, 14), U Jisu U la jubab, “U long u atiar ba la jied ha Nga” (Ki Kam 9:15). U Jisu U īohi īa ki briew ym kumba ki long hynrei kum kiban sa long lyngba ka bor jong U.

`Kiei dei kiwei ki rukom ba U Jisu U la pyni ha ngi kaba kumno ka jingieit kaba shisha ka dei ban long?`
