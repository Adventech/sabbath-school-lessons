---
title:  U Khrist U Soh Nyngkong
date:   18/08/2026
---

Lada U Jisu Um shym Um shym la im pat, kano kano ka jingkhmih lynti ïa ka lawei kan long ka jingpyrkhat ramïa(1 Korinth 15:12–19). “Hynrei ïa U Khrist la pynmih pat na kiba ïap” (1 Korinth 15:20).Ka jingmih pat jong U ka dei ka jingjia ba shisha ha ka histori. Kumta ka jingmih kan long bad ngi da thikna ba kito baroh kiba ïap ha U Khrist kin sa mih pat ha ka jingwan jong U(1 Korinth 15:20–23).

`Pule 1 Korinth 15:20–23. Ka mut aïu ban ong U Jisu “U Soh Nyngkong”?`

Ka jingwai noh kane ka pyrthei kan sdang da ka jingpynmih pat ïa kiba ïap da ka met ba shisha jong kito kiba la ïap ha U Khrist(1 Korinth 15:22, 23; Rev. 20:5, 6). Kum u Adam ba khatduh, U Khrist Un aiti ïa ka hima sha U Kpa kum U Nongsynshar jong ka pyrthei hapoh jong U(1 Korinth 15:25–28). U Khrist Un pynrit ïalade ha U Blei (1 Korinth 15:28) ngi dei ban sngewthuh ha ka jingïadei kumno ïa u Adam bad U Khrist ki ïadei uwei bad uwei pat. Kum U Adam ba khatduh ha ka jingthmu jong ka jingpynim(1 Korinth 15:45), U Jisu U la aiti ïalade ha ka mon jong U Kpa, kaba u Adam ba nyngkong u la rem ban leh.

Ha 1 Korinth 15:29–34, u Paul u khyllie pat ïa ka jingpyrkhat ba ka long ka jingbieit ban len ïa ka jingmih pat jong U Khrist. U pyndonkam da ka jingpynbaptist kum ka dak ban pyni ïa ka jinglong shitylli kiba ngeit bad U Khrist lyngba ka jingïap bad ka jingmih pat jong U(Rom. 6:3, 4; Col. 2:12); Kan ym don jingmut ei ei ban len ïa ka jingshisha ba ka don ka jingmih pat. Ka long kaei kaei kaba eh pynban ban pyrkhat, kadei kaba u Paul u mut ban ong da ka jingpyninia ha “Ka jingpynbaptis na ka bynta kiba ïap” (1 Korinth 15:29).

“Ki don kiwei de ki jingai jingmut, hynrei kan kham biang ban pyni ba haba don kiba la shim ka rai ban shah pynbaptist kumta ba ki lah ban ïa shem biang bad kiba ieit kiba la ïap ha ka jingmih pat. Ka lah ruh ban dei ba ka jingthrang jong ki ban shah pynbaptist ka dei ka jingaijubab jong ki na ka bynta ka jingim babha kaba long kum ka nuksa jong kito kiba la ïap ha U Khrist. Ha kane ka khep ka mut ba kim shim jingpynbaptist ha ka jaka jong kiba la ïap hynrei na ka bynta kiba la ïap.”—Carl P. Cosaert, “1 Korinth,” Andrews Bible Commentary: New Testament (Berrien Springs, MI: Andrews University Press, 2022), p. 1652.

Kaba ar, ban pha la ka jingim kan long kaba khlem jingmut ei ei lada ym don ka jingmih pat(1 Korinth 15:30–32). Kan jyn-da-la kham bha pynban ban leh kmen ha ki jingsngewbha thala jong kane ka pyrthei(1 Korinth 15:32).

`Puson ha ki kyntien jong u Paul ha 2 Timothy 1:12.Kumno u lah ban sngewthikna ïa ka lawei? Hato ngi pat kumno?`
