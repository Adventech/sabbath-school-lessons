### Ki Jingpule Na Ka Bynta Kane Ka Taïew
1 Korinth 15; Loukas 24:44–47; Jingpynpaw. 20:5, 6; Kolosai 2:12; 2 Timothi 1:12; 1 Thessaloni 4:13–17.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Hynrei lada ïa U Khrist ym shym la pynmihpat, te kumta ka jingïalap jong ngi ka long kaba lehnohei, …..ka jingngeit jong phi ka long kaba thala: phi dang sah ha ki pop jong phi.!” (1 Korinth 15:14–17).

Katno ka long kaba phylla eh, wat ha ka por jong u, u Paul u hap ban ïaleh bad kito kiba kyntait ïa ka jingmih jong kiba la ïap.Namar ha kito hi kip r ki briew ki la ïohi kumno ka jingïap ka la leh ha ka met u briew. Ki la tip bha ba ki met ïap ki kylla um, bad ki tyrkhong kum u pum pum, bad hadien kata kim long ei ei shuh. Bad ki tip ruh ba naduh mynshuwa ki briew ki la ïap. Bad ki tip ruh ba shibun ki briew ki la ïap wat ha shuwa ba ki hi kin don.

Ka jingmih pat jong kiba la ïap kam dei kaba lah ban pdiang da ki briew ha kito ki por bad kam long kaba suk ba ki briew kin ngeit ha kine ki sngi ruh, na ka rukom sngewthuh jong ki briew. Bad kata ka dei ka jingeh kaba u Paul u kren hangne.

Bad ka long kaba donkam eh. Lada ïa U Jisu ym sha la pynmih pat, Um lah ban dei kumb aU la ong ba U dei, ka diengphna kam don ka bor, ha ka pop jong ngi bad kata ka mut ym pat shym la siew la ka. Bad ngin long kiba la duh jingkyrmen. Hynrei ïa U Trai jong ngi la pynmih pat, U la kiew ruh sha bneng, bad Un sa wan biang ban ïalam ïa ngi sha ïing!

Ha kane la taïew ngin peit bniah ïa kane ka lynnong 1 Korinth 15 bad ka jinghikai shaphang ka jingmih-pat jong U Khrist. Ba la ker da ka jinghikai ki nongmane bleithaw, don na pdeng ki nong Korinth kiba ong ba ym don jingmihpat. Ban jubab ïa kane U Paul u pynthikna shaphang ka jingmih pat jong U Khrist kaba long tang kane ka jingkyrmen jong ngi.

_Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, August 22._
