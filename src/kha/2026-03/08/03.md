---
title:  Ka Jingmih Pat U Jisu, Tang Ma Ka Ka Jingkyrmen Jong Ngi!
date:   17/08/2026
---

Ha 1 Korinth 15:9–19, u Paul u batai katno ka long kaba ma haduh katta kum ka jingmih ban len ïa ka jingmih pat U Jisu. Khlem ka jingmih pat, ki bangeitkim don ka jingkyrmen ha ka mynta, bad ha ka lawei ruh.

`Pule 1 Korinth 15:9–19. Kaei kaba ngi lah ban duh lada ïa U Khrist ym shym la pynmih pat?`

Kiba bun hi na ki nongmane bleithaw kim ngeit ha ka jingmih pat, khamtam ha ki nong Greek jong ka mynnor, ha ka jingngeit jong ki ba ka met bad ka mynsiem ki dei ki ar bynta kiba don la ka jong ka jong (ha ka jingïap ka mynsiem ka mih noh bad ka her katsha ba ka mon ban leit). U Paul u sdang da ka jingong ha 1 Korinth 15:12–19 da ka jingkylli kaba pynlyngngoh bad kaba jylliew jong u: “Hato kumno ba kiba hapdeng jong phi ki ïa ong, ba ym don ka jingmihpat kiba ïap?” (1 Korinth 15:12). Ha u Paul, ban ym ngeit ha ka jingmih pat ka long kaei kaei kaba um lah ban mutdur, khamtam haba la don bun ki sakhi kiba da ïohi da la ki khmat(1 Korinth 15:5–8). Hynrei kham sniew shuh shuh, khlem ka jingmih pat, kla jingngeit jong ki kan shong nongrim ha ka jingshukor, bad ki dang don hi hapoh ki pop jong ki.

U ong, ha ka jingshisha, lada ym don jingmihpat jong kiba ïap(1) ïa U Khrist ym shym la pynmih pat(1 Korinth 15:13, 16); (2) ka jingïalap jong ngi ka long kaba suda(1 Korinth 15:14); (3) ka jingngeit jong ngi ruh ka long kaba suda(1 Korinth 15:14); (4) ngi long ki sakhi lamler(1 Korinth 15:15); (5) ka jingngeit jong ngi ka long lehnohei(1 Korinth 15:17); (6) ngi dang don ha ka pop(1 Korinth 15:17); bad ka long kaba shai, (7) kito kiba la ïap ki la jot noh(1 Korinth 15:18).

Khlem ka jingmih pat, ka jingïalap bad ka jingngeit ka long kaba thala(1 Korinth 15:14).Ka ktien Greek ka buh da kane ka kyntien “Suda\Thylli” ka dei _kenos_. Kane ka dei ka jingpynkylla ktien kaba dei bha, hynrei ka la kham ïar pat. Bad kito kiba thoh kot ki ju ïania lada kane ka kyntien _kenos_ ka mut Thylli” ha ka jingmut ba kam don ka jingshisha (kumta, Kam dei ka jingshisha”) ne ka jingbymdon ka jingmih (kumta, “Khlem ka jingshisha ne ka jingmih”) ne ka jingbymdon ka jingthmu (Kumta, “Kaba khlem ka jingthmu,” “Kaba thala”).

Watla ka jingmut kaba kumno kumno, ha ka jingphriang ka jinghikai ba ym don ka jingmih pat, ïa ka jingngeit la pyni kum kaba thala, na ka kyntien ha ka ktien Greek _mataios_ (1 Korinth 15:17). Watla ka mataios kam da ïapher than na ka kyntien _kenos_, ka jingmut ka long lada U Jisu U khlem im pat, ka jingngeit ka long kaba thala, tang ka jingngeit bieit, namar ba ïa ki pop jong ngi ym shym la map(1 Korinth 15:17). Ngi jyn-da-la long ki sakhi lamler, kiba shukor bad shah shukor (1 Korinth 15:15).

`Kumno uno uno u lah ban sngewthuh ïa 1 Korinth 15 lada kiba ïap ki la her sha bneng (ne sha dujok) tang mar shu ïap? Balei patba ka jingsngewthuh ba kiba ïap ki ïohthiahka long kawei na ki jinghikai kiba donkam eh?`
