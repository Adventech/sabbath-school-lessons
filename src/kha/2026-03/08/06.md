---
title:  Ka Jingjop Ba Khatduh Halor Ka Jingïap
date:   20/08/2026
---

Katba kane ka pyrthei ka nang jan sha kaba kut, ka bynta na ki khubor jong ki lai angel ka batai shaphang ki briew jong U Blei kita kiba sumar ïa ki hukum U Blei bad kiba don ïa ka jingngeit jong U Jisu.

`Pule1 Korinth 15:54–57. Kine ki kyntien ki kren aïu shaphang ka jingjop halor ka jingïap?`

U Paul u sdang ïa ka paragraph ba khatduh 1 Korinth 15 da ka jingong kaba ktik jingmut: “Ka doh bad ka snam kim lah ban ïoh ïa ka hima U Blei” (1 Korinth 15:50). Kiba bun na ki nongule Baibl ki pyndonkam ïa kane ka jingpynbna ba u Paul u ïada ïa ka jingdon khlem ka met ha bneng. Hynrei kane ka jingong ka pynpaw da kumwei pat. Ka jingpynïaryngkat jong 1 Korinth 15:50 ka ai ka jingmut ba “Ka doh bad ka snem” ka don ka jingïadei bad ka ïa ka “Jingpyut,” kum ka “Hima U Blei” ka don ka jingïadei bad ka “Jingbym pyut.” Kumba ka long ha 1Korinth 15:42–49, hangne, kumba ka long, u Paul u nujor ïa ka met kaba mynta (ne ka met ïap) bad ka met kaban sa mih pat. Ïa ka met ba la tep kan kup ïa ka jingjot bad ka jingïap, katba ka met ba la mih pat kan kup ïa ka jingbymïap bad ka bym pyut shuh (1 Korinth 15:50, 53, 54). Ka jingong ba synlar, u Paul u mut ban ong ba kane ka met ka dei ban ïaid lyngba ïa ka jngkylla kaba khraw khnang ba kan lah ban don ha bneng.

Tang ynda la weng noh ia ka jinglong pop jong ngi (1 Korinth 15:54) bad ngi iaid lyngba ka jingshem jong ka jingpyndonburom (1 Korinth 15:51-53, 1 Thessoloni. 4:13-17) yn sa pynbna “ ‘La nguit noh ia ka jingiap ha ka jingjop’ ” (1 Korinth 15:54) la pyndep. Kumta, yn rwai ia kane ka jingrwai da kaba shlur: “Ah ka jingiap, hangno ka jingjop thiaw jong pha, ko Hades, hangno ka sni jong pha?’ ” (1 Korinth 15:55). Kane baroh kan jia ha ka jingwan arsien U Khrist (1 Korinth 15:51, 52).

Pyrkhat shaphang kane: ngi khapbrip ia ki khmat jong ngi ha ka jingiap, bad kaba bud kaba ngin sa mad ka long ka jingwan arsien U Jisu, ynda Un pynmihpat ia ngi na ka jingiap. Kam pher ha kano kano ka por ba u bangeit u la iap, wat da ki hajar snem mynshuwa, “ha kaba khyllipmat, ha ka jingkhapbrip ka khmat,” yn pynim ia ki “ha ka turoi kaba khadduh: Namar ka turoi kan sawa, ynda kumta ia kiba iap yn pynmihpat ki bym pyut, bad ia ngi yn pynkylla” (1 Korinth 15:52).

`Mano bym shym la sngewsih haduh katno ka jingim ka iaid stet na ngi? Ha ka jingshem jong ngi hi, kumta ka jingwan arsien U Jisu kan long kaba kloi. Lehse ka jingpyrkhat kaba nyngkong eh jong ngi ha ka jingwan phai jong U kan long “Wow, Trai, ka jingwan jong Me shisha ka la long kloi!” Kumno kane ka jingmut ka iarap ia ngi ban kham sngewthuh ia kaei kaba la iohi kum “ka jingpynslem”?`
