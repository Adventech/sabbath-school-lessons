Pule Ellen G. White, “God’s People Delivered,” pp. 635–652, ha The Great Controversy.

“Ka jinglong Blei jong U Khrist ka long ka jingpynthikna jong u bangeit ia ka jingim bymjukut. ‘Uta uba ngeit ha Nga,’ ong U Jisu, ‘wat la u la iap, un im: bad uno uno uba im bad uba ngeit ha Nga un ym iap lano lano ruh. Hato phi ngeit ia kane? U Khrist hangne u khmih sha khmat ia ka por jong ka jingwan arsien jong U. Nangta ia kiba iap kiba hok yn pynmihpat khlem da pyut, bad ia kiba hok kiba dang im yn pynkynriah noh sha bneng khlem da iohi ia ka jingiap. Ka kambah kaba U Khrist U la thmu ban leh, ha kaba pynmihpat ia u Lasaros na ka jingiap, kan pyni ia ka jingmihpat jong kiba iap kiba hok baroh.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 530.

“ïa ka pyrthei la pynkhih jur katba ka sur U Khun U Blei ka la khot ia ki riewkhuid kiba la iohthiah. Ki la jubab ia ka jingkhot bad ki la mih noh da kaba kup da ka donburom kaba jingbymjukut, ki da pyrta, ‘Ka jingjop, ka jingjop, halor ka jingiap bad ka jingtep! Ah jingiap, hangno ka sni jong pha? Ko jingtep, hangno ka jingjop thiaw jong pha?’ (Peit 1 Korinth 15:55.) Nangta ki riewkhuid kiba im bad kiba la mihpat ki la pynkiew ia la ka sur ha ka jingpyrta kaba la rah ia ka jingpyrta jong ka jingjop. Kito ki met kiba la don shapoh jingtep kiba buh dak da ki jong ka jingpang bad jingiap ki la mih ha ka jingkoit jingkhiah bad jingkhlain bym lah iap shuh.

Ki riewkhuid kiba im ia ki la pynkylla ha ka khyllipmat, ha ka jingkhapbrip jong ka khmat, bad ki la kiew ryngkat bad kiba la mihpat, bad ryngkat ki la iashem ia la U Trai ha suinbneng. Oh, katno ka jingialang kaba donburom! Ki paralok kiba ka jingiap ki ba la pyniakhlad ia ki la iasoh lang, kin ym iakhlad shuh.”—Ka Ellen G. White, The Story of Redemption, sla 411, 412.

**Ki Jingkylli Ban Ïa Kren Lang:**

`Pyrkhat shaphang kito kiba la long ki nongsakhi ia ka jingmihpat U Khrist (Ki Kam 1:22, Ki Kam 2:32, Ki Kam 3:15, Ki Kam 4:33, Ki Kam 5:30-32). Kumno ma ngi, kumba ar hajar snem hadien kata ka jingjia, ban long “ki nongsakhi” ia ka jingmihpat jong U?`

`Ka jingmihpat U Khrist ka long ka bynta ba kongsan jong ka khubor gospel (1 Korinth 15:1-4). Khlem ka Jingmihpat, ka jingpynbna ia ka jingiap U Khrist kan ym don jingmut (1 Korinth 15:14). Ka jingiap U Khrist hi kan long kaba khlem jingmut. Balei? Kaei ka jubab jong phi ia kane, ka ong aiu shaphang ka bor jong ka jingmihpat U Khrist??`

`Pyrkhat khyndiat shaphang ka jingong kaba pynsngewtynnat jong u Paul “Lada kiba iap kim mihpat, ‘To ngin iabam iadih, namar lashai ngin iap!’ ” (1 Korinth 15:32, NKJV). Kaei ka jingmut?`

`Ha klas, kren shaphang ka jinglong jong kiba iap. Balei ka 1 Korinth 15 kam don jingmut lada, ha ka jingiap, ia kiba la pynim la rah noh mardor sha bneng?`
