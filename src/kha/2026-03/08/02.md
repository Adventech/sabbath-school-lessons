---
title:  Ka Jingpynbna Ïa Ka Jingmih Pat U Khrist
date:   16/08/2026
---

U Paul u ha kaba sdang 1 Korinth 15 da ka jingshonjur ha ka Gospel.U kren shaphang ka Gospel: (1) ïa kaba la ïalap ha; (2) kaba ki la pdiang; (3) ha kaba ki ïeng skhem (4) ha kaba ïa ki la pynim(1 Korinth 15:1, 2). Kane ka jingsdang ka pynkhreh ïa ki nongpule na ka bynta kaei kaban sa wan kham hadien kaba pyni katno ka long kaba donkam kane ka khubor jong ka jingmih pat U Khrist ha ka jingïadei bad ka jingpynim jong ngi. (peit ruh Rom 10:9, 10). Ka jingmih pat jong U ka long ka bynta ba kongsan eh jong ka Gospel bad ban kyntait ïa ka ka long marpyrshah ïa ka jingngeit ha U Khrist.

`Pule 1 Korinth 15:1–4, Loukas 24:44–47, bad Rom 1:1–4. Kine ki jingthoh ki don ka jingïadei aïu hapdeng jong ki?`

Ha 1 Korinth 15:1–4, uno uno u lah ban shem ïa ka jingkren lyngkot jong u Paul. Lada kane ka jing-ong “Katkum ki jingthoh” ka mut ïa ki jingkren ha ka Testament Barim ne ïa ka Testament Barim hi baroh kawei kata kam pher.Ka jingïap bad ka jingmih pat jong U Jisu ka la pyndep ïa ki jingkular jong U Blei kiba la pynpaw ha ka Testament Barim.

`Pule 1 Korinth 15:2, 11. Balei ba ha kane ka dkhot ka kren shaphang kaba ngeit bad kaba ïalap ki dei ban ïa ïaid lang? ki don ka jingïadei aïu hapdeng kine ar?`

Baroh kiba pynbna ba U Jisu U la mihpat ki dei ruh ban ngeit ba ka jingmih pat ka dei ka jingjia kaba shisha kaba la jia ha ka histori. Ha kane ka khep, 1 Korinth 15:5–8 Ka leh ka kam kaba donkam eh ha ka Testament Bathymmai. Kane ka jingthoh ka ai ha ngi ïa ka jingpynshisha kaba beit bad kaba shai ba U Jisu U la mih pat bad la ïohi da bun ki briew hadien ba U la mih pat, bad kiba bun na ki ki dang im ha ka por ba u Paul u la thoh ïa kane ka shithi(1 Korinth 15:6).

Kum ka jingsdang, u Paul u ong, Let bad kylli hi da lade kaei ba ki la ïohi. Kata ka dei ka jingsngewskhem jong u ha ka jingïadei bad ka jingshisha jong ka jingmih pat U Jisu.

Kine ki briew ki dei ki sakhi ba ïohi da la ki jong ki khmat. Ki long kumta kumba U Jisu U la ong ba ki dei ban long, kita ki dei ki, “Ki sakhi jong kine kiei kiei” (Loukas 24:48).

`Ka daw aïu ba ngi don ban ngeit ha ka jingmih pat U Khrist? Shuh shuh,sa kiei de ki wei pat, kiba kynja-pyrthei ne ki bakynja-mynsiem, hato ngi lah ban ngeit watla ngim ïohi da lade?`
