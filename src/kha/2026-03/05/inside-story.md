### U Davis u Indians: Bynta I

La kha ïa u Ovid Elbert Davis ha Michigan ha ka 3 tarik u Risaw, 1868. Ha ka snem 1902, u la ïoh mynsiem na ka jingai jingmut jong ka Ellen White ban long u mishoneri, kumta u la pyndep ïa ka jingpule ia ki course jong ka jingshakri bad u la pdiang ïa ka jingkhot ban trei hapdeng ki trai ri trai muluk jong ka Alaska bad nangta sha British Columbia.

Ha ka 19 tarik u January, 1906, ka Dorbar bah ka la vote ban phah ia u Davis sha British Guiana (Guiana), ha kaba dang shen la sdang ia ka kam Adventist. U la shongkurim ia ka Carrie Rosley ha u bnai April, bad kine ki arngut ki la leit sha ka lyngkha mishon thymmai jong ki ha ka taiew kaba bud.

U Davis u la sam ia ki kot ha ki ar snem ba nyngkong jong u ha British Guiana. Nangta, ha ka snem 1910, u la iathuh ba u la pynlong ia ka Balang thymmai kaba jngai 160 mer sharum jong ka wah Barama. Kan sa kylla long ka Mishon Rio Paruime. Dei ruh ha ka snem 1910 ba u Davis u la ioh ia ka jingkyrpad na ki jaitbynriew kiba don sha kyndong eh ki bym pat ju leit jngoh da ki doh lieh, kiba kyrpad ban hikai ia ki ia ka khubor jong ka jingpynim.

Kine ki jaitbynriew ki la tip shaphang ka jingtrei jong ki Adventist da kaba iakynduh bad ki trai ri trai muluk ha ka mishon ha Tapagruma Creek. Ki la don ki khubor ba uwei u nongialam uba la tymmen u ba la wan jngoh ia u da u “jingthaw ba phyrnai” uba la hikai ia ki shaphang ka Jingthaw jingpynlong, ka jingrung jong ka pop, ka jingiathuhkhana shaphang U Nongsiewspah ba la kular, bad ka jingwan arsien U Khrist. U la pyni ruh kumno ban mane blei ha ka sngi Sabbaton kaba hynñiew bad ban im ka jingim kaba koit ba khiah. U la iathuh ia u ba uwei u briew uba don ka kot kaba iong un wan ban hikai shuh shuh ia ki.

Ka jingleit ka la shim por ia u Davis ar bnai. Ha kawei ka kaiphod, u la pynpaw ba u la iaid 29 sngi da ka lieng bad sa 10 sngi lyngba ka khlaw kaba rben.

U Davis u la hikai ia ka Ktien U Blei ha ki jaitbynriew ba shong ha khlaw da ka “kot rong ïong” jong u bad u la iathuh ia ka jingseng ia lai tylli ki jaka shong mishon hapdeng jong ki. U la kdew ba 187 ngut ki briew “ki la shim ia ka rai ban sumar ia ki hukum U Blei bad ia ka jingngeit U Jisu.” U la tei lai tylli ki iingmane bad u la sdang ban hikai ia ki bangeit thymmai “halor ki mat jong ka jingngeit jong ngi.” U la hikai ruh ia ki ban rwai ia ka jingrwai ha ka ktien phareng, “Ym don paralok kum u Jisu uba jemnud.”

U Davis u la wan phai biang ha ka sien kaba ar ha ka snem 1911. U la ud ia ka jingpangshit bad jingshitom ha u klongsnam bad mar tang shu mih noh nangta. Ka jingthoh kaba khatduh ha ka diary jong u kaba u la lah ban thoh da la ka jong ka kti ka long “Sngi monday, 17 tarik u Jylliew— La pynlut ia ka sngi ha kaba hikai shuh shuh bad ai kyrteng ia ki briew.” Ka jingai kyrteng ia ki briew ka kdew ia ka jingkwah jong ki nongkylla niam thymmai ia ka kyrteng kaba thymmai kaba pyni ia ka jingkut jingmut jong ki ban bud ia U Blei jong u Khristan bad ban nang ia ki lynti jong U.

_Ia kane ka jingiathuhkhana la shim na ka jingthoh shaphang ka jingim jong u Michael Campbell ha ka Encyclopedia online jong ki Seventh-Sngi Adventist. Ngi khot sngewbha ia phi ban leit peit ia ka https://encyclopedia.adventist.org ban ioh mad shuh shuh ia ki jingiathuhkhana shaphang ki mishoneri Adventist. Pule ia kaba sah jong kane ka jingiathuhkhana ha ka taiew ban wan._
