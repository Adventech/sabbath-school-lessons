### Ki Jingpule Na Ka Bynta Kane Ka Taïew
1 Korinth 8; Ki Kam 15:20; 1 Korinth. 9:1-6; 1 Korinth. 10:5-22; Deuteronomi. 6:4, 5; Markos 12:28-31.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Namar kata lada phi bam ne lada phi dih, ne lada phi leh ei ei ruh, to leh phi ia kiei kiei baroh ia kaban pyndon burom ia U Blei.” (1Korinth 10: 31).

Nyngkong Korinth 8–10 ka wanrah ia ka jingiakren shaphang ka jinglong kynthei ne shynrang (ha ki lynnong 5-6) sha ka jingkut ha kajuh ka por ba ka pynrung ia ki jubab jong u Paul ia ki jingkylli ba kyrpang ba la kylli lyngba ka shithi (1 Korinth. 7:1) na ki nong Korinth. Kine ki jubab kin synshar ia ka bynta ka jingpynkynmaw jong ka 1 Korinth.

Ka jinglong ba kylla jong ka 1 Korinth 7 ka pyni ba ka jingleh klim awria (lynnong 5–7) bad ka jingmane bleithaw (lynnong 8–10) ki long ki phang kiba don ka jingiadei. Hooid, la ju kdew lang ia ki ha ka Testament Bathymmai (peit Ki Kam 15:20, 29; Ki Kam 21:25; 1 Korinth 6:9; Ephesos 5:5; Kolosai 3:5; Jingpynpaw 21:8; Jingpynpaw 22:15).

Ha ka jinglang, katba ha 1 Korinth 5–7 u Paul u ïakren shaphang ka jingeh jong jingklim awria, ha 1 Korinth 8–10 ka jingsngewkhia kaba kongsan eh jong u ka long shaphang ka jingmane bleithaw. U pynpaw ba ki Khristan ki dei ban phet noh na kine baroh ar (1 Korinth. 6:18, 1 Korinth. 10:14).

Ha ka taiew ba la dep, ngi la iohi ba da kaba long ka templ jong U Mynsiem Bakhuid (1 Korinth. 6:19, 20), lah ban phet krad na ka jingleh awria. Ha kane ka taiew, ngin iohi ba u briew u lah ban phet noh na ka jingmane bleithaw da kaba leh “baroh na ka bynta ka burom U Blei” (1 Korinth. 10:31,).

_Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, August 1._
