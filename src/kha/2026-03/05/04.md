---
title:  Ka Jinghikai Na Ki Por Kiba La Leit
date:   28/07/2026
---

Hadien ba u la ai ka nuksa ia ka jinglen ialade na ka jingïashem jong u hi, u Paul u phai kham kyrpang sha ka mat jong ka jingmane bleithaw. Ha kawei ka rukom, ka 1 Korinth 10 ka pynpaw ia ka jingmut jong ka 1 Korinth 9:27, ha kaba u Paul u kynthoh ba u pyndonkam ia ka jingpynbeit ia lade khnang ba un ym long uba la shah kyntait. U kwah ba ki nong Korinth kin bud ia ka nuksa jong u, hynrei U Jisu U long ka nuksa kaba bha tam (1 Korinth. 11:1).

`Pule 1 Korinth 10:7-11. Kaei ka pop kaba ki khun Israel ki la leh ha ka rikhlaw, bad balei ia ka kabu la ai ha ki ki la pynlong ia ki pop jong ki kiba kham sneiw shuh shuh?`

Ha 1 Korinth 10:1–5, u Paul u kdew shaphang ka histori jong ki briew U Blei ha ka ri khlaw. Ka jingkdew shaphang u lyoh bad ka duriaw ka wanrah ia ka jingialam jong U Blei, ka jingdon bad jingiada jong U Blei ha ka jingmut jingpyrkhat. Ha ka jingshisha, ka jingbam bad ka jingdih ki ieng na ka bynta ka jingpynbiang U Blei. U Paul u kdew ia ka jingshem jong ki Israel ha u lyoh bad ka duriaw kum ka jingpynbaptis, kaba iadei dur bad ka jingpynbaptis Khristan. Kumjuh ruh, da kaba kdew shaphang ka jingbam bad jingdih, u Paul u kdew sha ka jingkhawai U Trai.

Ha kiwei pat ki kyntien, ka 1 Korinth 10 ka hikai ba, ha ka jingmut, ki Khristan ki im ia kijuh ki jingjia kumba la im ki Israel. Hynrei u Paul u pynkynmaw ia ka histori jong ki Israel namar um kwah ba kata ka histori kan iai jia biang. Wat la ki Israel ki don ki kabu baroh, hynrei bun na ki briew pynban ki kwah ia kiei kiei kiba sniew (1 Korinth. 10:6), kum ka jingmane bleithaw (1 Korinth. 10:7) bad ka jingklim (1 Korinth. 10:8). Kumta kam long kaba phylla ba “U Blei um shym la sngewbha ia kiba bun na ki” (1 Korinth. 10:5).

Ka long kaba suk ban kdew kti ia ki Israel hyndai bad ong ba ki la leh ia ki pop kiba khraw. Hynrei u Paul u kynthoh ba ki Khristan ki long kiba lah ban leh ia ki pop kiba kum kita wat la ki don ka kabu kaba khraw ban tip ia ka histori U Khrist. Kane ka paw shai ha ka jingmaham: “Naba kumta uta uba mut ba u ieng, to un sumar ioh un kyllon” (1 Korinth. 10:12). Ka kyntien “uba pyrkhat” ka pyni ba don ha ka balang ki bym sngewthuh ba ki don ha ka jingma ban hap ha kita ki pop. Hato ngi iaid ia kajuh ka jingma mynta?

“Naba kumta uta uba mut ba u ieng, to un sumar ioh un kyllon.” Mano napdeng jong ngi ki bym shym la mad ia ka jingshisha jong kata ka jingmaham?

`Ka Baibl ka ong ba U Blei un ym shah ia ngi ban shah pynshoi palat ia kaba ngi lah ban shah, “hynrei lem bad kata ka jingpynshoi Un thaw ruh ia ka lad ban lait” (1 Korinth. 10:13). Haba kumta, balei ngi dang shem ba ka long kaba suk ban ngop ha ka pop?`
