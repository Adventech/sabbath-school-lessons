---
title:  Ka Jingtip Pyrshah Ia Ka Jingieit
date:   26/07/2026
---

`Pule 1 Korinth 8:1-13. Balei u Paul u pyniapher ia ka jingtip bad ka jingieit, bad kaei ka jingmut hangne? Kaei ka jingmut kaba u pynpaw?`

U Paul u pyndonkam ia ka phang jong ka jingbam ba la ai sha ki bleithaw khnang ban kren ia ka jingeh ki kaba kham jylliew: ka jingbym don jingieit ia kiwei pat (1 Korinth 8). Ka mat shaphang ka jingbam ba la ainguh ha ki bleithaw ka la phiah ia ka balang Korinth ha ki ar kynhun. Don kiba ngeit ba ka jingtip jong ki shaphang ka jingbymdon kiwei pat ki blei ka ai hok ia ki ban bam ia kano kano ka jingbam (1 Korinth. 8:4). Ia kine la kdew kum ki “ba khlain” (1 Korinth. 4:10). Ia kito kiba pyrshah ia kane ka jinglong la khot ki “ba tlot” (1 Korinth. 8:9-12). U Paul u pyndonkam ia kum kane ka jingthoh namar ba kim shym la jop ia ki katto katne ki jingngeit biej kiba la pyni ia ka jingshem jong ki ha ka rukom riewblei kaba mynshuwa. Haba ki iohi ia kiba “khlain” ba ki bam ia ki jingbam ba la knia sha ki bleithaw, ki lah ban pynkut nia ba ka niam Khristan bad ka jingmane bleithaw ki iadei dur. Kumta, u Paul um kwah ba kiba “khlain” kin long ka jingpynthut ia kiba tlot.

Ka Baibl ka iohi ia ka jingleh ban bam ia ki jingbam ba la knia ha ki bleithaw ha ka rukom kaba sniew bha (Ki Kam 15:20, 29; Ki Kam 21:25; ianujor bad Jingpynpaw 2:14, 20). Hynrei u Paul um shym la pynmih ia ki jingkren kiba jylliew kumba iohi ha kine ki bynta. Kane ka long namar ba ka jingsngewkhia ba kongsan jong u ka iadei bad ka jingbym iatylli kaba ka jingpyndonkam bakla ia ka jingnang jingstad ka lah ban wanrah. U Paul um shym la kynthoh ia ka jingtip kum kaba sniew ha lade hi; ha ka jaka kata, u pyrshah ia ka jait jingtip kaba ialam sha ka jingsngew sarong bad jingiapher ha ka balang. Ka jingtip khlem ka jingieit kam long satia ka jingtip kaba shisha (1 Korinth. 8:2). Ka jingtip kaba shisha ka mih tang haba u briew u ieit ia U Blei bad la ithuh ia u da U (1 Korinth. 8:3).

Da kaba sot ia ka Deuteronomi 6:4, u Paul u pyni ba kiba ngeit ki dei ban tip ba don tang uwei U Blei (1 Korinth. 8:4-6). Kaba sngewtynnad ka long ba u bud ia kajuh ka jingmut kaba uwei u iohi ha ka Deuteronomi 6:4, 5, ha kaba ia ka jingong ba U Blei jong ngi u long uwei la bud da ka hukum “Men ieit ia U Trai U Blei jong me”. Na ka bynta u Paul bad u Moses baroh arngut, ka jingtip khlem ka jingieit ka long kaba khlem dor.

Da kaba sngewskhem ha ka jingtip jong ki, ki “ba khlain” ki ngeit ba ka jingbam ia ki jingbam ba la knia ha ki bleithaw kam don jingma. Kumba ngin sa iohi ha ka sngi Thohdieng bad sngi Thohdieng, u Paul u la ailad ia ki ia kata ka hok hapoh ki katto katne ki kyndon. Hynrei lada kata ka la kylla long ka jingthut ia kiba “tlot” (1 Korinth. 8:9), dei ban kiar na ka. Ki Khristan ki dei ban pynmlien ia ka jinglen ia lade namar ka jingieit ia U Khrist bad ia kiwei pat.

`U Paul u iatainia ba, Khlem ka jingieit, ka jingtip ka lah ban long kaei kaei kaba sniew (1 Korinth 8). Ha kine ki jinglong ba ka jingtip khlem ka jingieit ka lah ban long shiahs kaba sniew?`
