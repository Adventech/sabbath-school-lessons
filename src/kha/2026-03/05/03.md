---
title:  Ka Jingieit Bym Pyrkhat Shuh ïalade
date:   27/07/2026
---

`Pule 1 Korinth 9:1-6. Kumno kane ka bynta ka ai ka nuksa kaba lah ban leh ia kaei kaba mut ban leh ia ka jinglen ia lade kaba la pynkhih da ka jingieit?`

Ha ka jingiohi kaba nyngkong, ka I kumba u Paul u ïada ïa ka jinglong apostol jong u, ha 1 Korinth 9, kam don jingïadei ei ei bad ka jingïakren kaba mynshuwa shaphang ka jingtip pyrshah ïa ka jingieit. Hynrei ym lah ban klet ba ia ka Baibl ym shym la thoh tynrai ha ki lynnong. Kaei kaba u Paul u hikai ha ka 1 Korinth 9 kam shym la pyniakhlad ia ka jingiadei na ka jingthoh kaba la dep. Hooid, ka 1 Korinth 9 ka ai ka nuksa kaba treikam jong ka jingieit ba khlem pyrkhat shuh ia ka lade na ka bynta U Khrist bad ia ki parabangeit. Na ka bynta ka jingieit, u Paul u iehnoh ia ki katto katne ki hok.

**“Ban bam bad ban dih”** (1 Korinth. 9:4,). Hangne ka bam ka dih ka pyni ia ka jingiarap pisa ha ka jingkren kyllum. Kum u apostol, u Paul u don ka hok ban ioh jingkyrshan ha ka liang ka met na kito kiba u shakri. Kiwei pat ki nongialam niam ha ka por jong u ki ju leh kumjuh. Hynrei um shym la leh kumta; ha ka jaka kata, u la kyrshan ia lade da kaba shna ia ki iingjain (Ki Kam 18:3).

**“Ban shim ryngkat bad ka tnga kaba ngeit”** (1 Korinth. 9:5). La shah ia u apostol uba la shongkurim ban leit mishoneri ryngkat bad la ka tnga da ka jinglut jong ka balang. Ki nuksa jong ki lok mishoneri ki kynthup ia ka Priskila bad u Akwila (Rom 16:3) bad u Andronikos bad ka Junia (Rom 16:7). Hynrei u Paul um shym la shongkurim (1 Korinth. 7:8). U lah ban shongkurim bad nangta u ioh jingmyntoi na ka hok ban don ryngkat bad ka tnga, da ka jingkyrshan pisa na ka bynta baroh arngut.

**“Ban kiar na kaba trei” na ka bynta ban im** (1 Korinth. 9:6). U Paul bad u Barnabas ki don ka hok ban ioh bainong na ka bynta ka kam mishoneri jong ki (1 Korinth. 9:4-6). U Paul u la shna ia ki ïingjaiñ ban im (Ki Kam 18:3), hynrei ngim tip kaei ka kam jong u Barnabas. Ngi tip ba u long uba sngewrit shibun (Ki Kam 4:36, 37), bad kumta, u la kloi ban kyrshan ia lade.

Ha 1 Korinth 9:7-11, u Paul u pynpaw ia ka jingmut jong ka 1 Korinth 9:6 khnang ban pyni ba ka long kaba dei ia u bad u Barnabas ban ioh ia ka jingdonkam jong ka jingim na ka balang (1 Korinth 9:11, 12). U Trai hi u la hukum, “Kita kiba ialap ia ka Gospel ki dei ban im na ka Gospel” (1 Korinth. 9:14; ia nujor bad 1 Timothy. 5:18). Wat la katta ruh, u Paul u ong, “Ngim shym la pyndonkam ia kane ka hok” (1 Korinth. 9:12). Kumta, u Paul u pyni ia lade kum ka nuksa jong ka jinglen ia lade (1 Korinth. 9:1-18) bad u kynthoh ba kane ka ai jingmyntoi ia ka jingialap ia ka gospel ha Korinth (1 Korinth. 9:19-23).

`Kiei kita kiei kiei kiba, wat la ki dei lehse kiba dei ia phi ban ioh, phi lah ban dang kham bha ban iehnoh khnang ban long u nongsakhi uba kham tbit na ka bynta U Trai?`
