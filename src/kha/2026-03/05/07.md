Pule Ellen G. White, “Ka Jingmane bleithaw ha Sinai,” pp. 315–330, in Patriarchs and Prophets.

Katno ka jingbha kan long lada ngi pyndonkam hok ia ki jingiadei jong ngi iwei ia iwei pat! Uwei pa uwei uba la ioh pdiang na ki jingmyntoi jong ka bneng u don hapoh ka jingkitkhlieh ban pynshai katto katne ia ka lynti jong kiwei pat. . . . Te baroh kito kiba ieit shisha ia U Blei kin sangeh noh ia ka jingmane bleithaw jong ki ialade.”—Ellen G. White, ha ka kot khubor The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, Nohprah 18, 1884, p. 730.

“U Paul u la kyntu ia ki para bangeit jong u ban kylli ia lade kaei ka jingktah kaba ki ktien bad ki kam jong ki kin don ha kiwei pat bad ban ym leh ei ei, wat la ka long kaba khlem pop ha lade, kaba lah ban i kumba ka ai jingbit ia ka jingmane bleithaw ne kaba pynsngewsih ia ki jingsngew jong kito kiba lah ban long kiba tlot ha ka jingngeit. ‘Namar kata la phi bam ne phi dih, lane phi leh aiu ruh, to leh ia kiei kiei baroh na ka bynta ka burom U Blei. . . .’

“Ki ktien jingmaham jong u apostol ia ka balang ha Korinth ki long kiba iadei bad ki por baroh bad ki iadei khamtam eh bad ki sngi jong ngi. Da ka jingmane bleithaw u la mut ym tang ia ka jingmane bleithaw, hynrei ia ka jingshakri ia lade, ka jingieit ia ka jingsuk, ka jingpynhun ia ka jingkwah bad ka jingkwah brai.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostol .316, 317.

“Lada phi iohi ba da kaba leh ia ki katto katne kiei kiei kiba phi don ka hok kaba janai ban leh, phi khanglad ia ka jingkiew shaphrang jong ka kam U Blei, to kiar na kaba leh ia kita kiei kiei, wat leh ei ei kaba khang ia ka jingmut jong kiwei pyrshah ia ka jingshisha... Kiei kiei baroh ki lah ban long kiba dei, hynrei kiei kiei baroh kim long kiba myntoi.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 9, p. 215.

**Ki Jingkylli Ban Ïa Kren Lang:**

`Katkum ka jingong u Paul, ka jinglong jong u Khristan uba la san la rangbah teng teng ka lah ban khanglad ia ka jingroi jong u Khristan u bym pat san. Pyrkhat ia ki jingjia ha kaba kane ka lah ban jia. Balei ba ka nongrim ban ieit ia U Blei halor baroh, bad ia kiwei pat kumba ia lade, ka long tang ka lad ban ialeh pyrshah ia kane ka jingeh?`

`Kiei ki katto katne ki bleithaw kiba wat ki Khristan ruh ki lah ban kut noh ha kaba mane ia ki, lada kim husiar bha? Kiei ki katto katne kiei kiei kiba bha kiba ngi lah ban pynkylla long ki bleithaw? Bad ruh, kumno phin tip lada kaei kaei kaba phi da ri da sumar bha shibun eh ka la kylla long ka bleithaw?`

`U Paul u ong ba u la pynshitom ia la ka met bad u la pyndem ia ka khnang ba un ym kylla long u bym bitdor haba u ialap ia ka gospel (1 Korinth. 9:27). Da kaba pynshong nongrim ha ka jingpule jong kane ka taiew, pyrkhat ia kaei kaba lah ban pynduh ia u briew kum u nongialap ia ka gospel.`

`Ha ka shithi 1 Korinth 10, u Paul u kren shaphang ki jingma jong ka jingmane bleithaw bad u ong, “To phet noh na ka jingmane bleithaw” (1 Korinth. 10:14). Balei ka jingmane bleithaw ka long kaba sniew?`
