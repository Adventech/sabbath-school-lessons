---
title:  Ka Jingjop Ia Ka Jingmane Bleithaw
date:   30/07/2026
---

Ha 1 Korinth 8:1–3, u Paul u kynthoh ba ka jingieit ka sumar ia u briew na ka jingmane bleithaw. Ia kane ka jingiakren la bteng biang bad la nang pynpaw shuh shuh ha 1 Korinth 10:23-11:1. Ha 1 Korinth 8:3, u kren shaphang ka jingieit jong ngi ia U Blei. U ong, “Wat ai ba uwei pa uwei un wad ia la ka jong, hynrei uwei pa uwei ia ka jingbha jong uwei pat” (1 Korinth. 10:24). Kane ka dei ka jingieit ia kiwei pat.

`Pule Markos 10: 17-22 bad Markos 12: 28-31. Kaei ba kine ki ar bynta ki don ia ka juh ka jingiadei, bad kumno ki don ka jingiadei bad ka jingjia ha 1 Korinth 10?`

U Paul u leh ha 1 Korinth 10 thik kumba U Jisu u la leh ha Markos 12:28-31; kata, u pyniasoh lang ia ki ar tylli ki hukum kiba khraw jong ka hukum: ka jingieit ia U Blei halor kiei kiei baroh, bad ka jingieit ia kiwei pat. Ha ka jingiathuhkhana shaphang u nongsynshar samla uba riewspah (Mark. 10:17-22), U Jisu u pyniasoh lang ia kine ki ar jait ki jingieit, bad u leh kumta da kaba kdew mar kylliang sha ka Deuteronomi 6:4 (peit Markos 10:18) bad ka bynta kaba ar jong ka Shiphew Hukum (peit Markos 10:19). Ka jingeh jong uta u samla riewspah ka long ba u kham ieit ia la ki jingdon jingem ban ia kaba u ieit ia U Blei bad ia ki para marjan jong u (Markos 10:22). U la khein kor ia ka spah jong u ha ka pyrthei ban ia ki spah ha bneng. U khein kor ia ka pisa jong u ban ia kiba duk (Markos 10:21). U la long u nongmane bleithaw.

Da kaba bud ia ki jinghikai U Jisu, u Paul u ai jingmut ba ia ka nongrim ban ieit ia U Blei halor jong baroh bad ia u paramarjan kum ia lade dei ban pyntreikam ha ki jinglong ba la antad ba u kdew ha 1 Korinth 10:27, 28. Kane ka mut ba wat kiei kiei kiba long katkum ka ain ki lah ban ym long kiba iarap ne pynroi namar ki lah ban pynsngewsih iano iano (1 Korinth 10:23). Ia kane ka nongrim la pynwandur bha ha ki kyntien “To leh ia kiei kiei baroh na ka bynta ka burom U Blei” (1 Korinth. 10:31). Da kaba ong ba baroh dei ban leh na ka bynta ka burom U Blei, u Paul u pyni ba ka jingmane bleithaw ka lah ban pynpaw ia lade ha ki dur kiba pher ba pher, namar kano kano kaba shim noh ia ka burom kaba dei tang ka jong U Blei ka long ka dur jong ka jingmane bleithaw (Isaiah. 42:8).

Ki kyntien jong u Paul ha 1 Korinth 10:31-11:1 ki long kum ka jingpynkut ia ki lynnong 8-10. U pynshai ba um shym la wad ia ka jingmyntoi jong u hi, “hynrei ia ka jong kiba bun, ba kin ioh jingpynim” (1 Korinth. 10:33). Kumta u la pyrthuh bud ia U Khrist (1 Korinth. 11:1).

`Kumno phi lah ban nang ban ieit kham bha ia la u paramarjan kumba ieit ia lade?`
