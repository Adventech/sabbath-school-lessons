---
title:  Ka Gospel Jong Ka Diengphna
date:   05/07/2026
---

U Paul u ong ba ka khubor jong ka diengphna ka long ka bor jong U Blei sha ngi. Kam shem long ka ba phylla ba “U “Jisu Khrist bad ka jingsahnarphna jong U” ka long ka pdeng jong ka jingialap jong U.

`Pule 1 Korinth 1:17–31. Kaei ka mat ba kongsan kaba u Paul u pynlong hangne?`

Ha 1 Korinth 1:18-31, u Paul u kren shaphang ka jingiapher hapdeng ka jingbieit jong u briew bad ka jingstad Blei. Ka Diengphna ka don ka bor ban pyni ia kaba sniew tam jong u briew bad kaba bha tam jong U Blei. Ia kane ka bynta jong ka 1 Korinth la pynrung da ka jingong ha ka 1 Korinth 1:17. Namar ba ka diengphna U Khrist kam dei ban long kaba thylli na ka bor jong ka (1 Korinth. 1:17), ka khubor jong ka Diengphna ka dei ban bat ia ka jaka pdeng jong ka jingialap jong ngi (peit ruh 1 Korinth. 2:2).

U Paul u ong ba la phah ia u ym ban pynbaptis, hynrei ban ialap ia ka gospel jong ka Diengphna. Kane ka jingong ka donkam ar tylli ki jingpeit bniah kiba kongsan. Nyngkong, ka verb Greek ba la pynkylla kum “ban phah” ka long _apostellō_, kaba mih na kajuh ka tynrai bad ka kyntien “apostol.” Kumta, ka kam apostol ba kongsan jong u Paul ka long ka jingpynbna ia ka gospel. Kaba ar, ki ktien jong u Paul shaphang ka jingpynbaptis kim mut ba ka jingpynbaptis kam long kaba kongsan, lane lym kumta kam long kaba kongsan kum ka jingialap. U la long, ha ka jaka kata, uba sneng ia kito kiba la leh ia ka jingleh kaba khraw shaphang mano ba long kiba leh ia ka jingpynbaptis pyrshah ia Uta Uwei, U Jisu, ha uba la pynbaptis ia ki.

Da “ka jingstad ki ktien” (1 Korinth. 1:17), u Paul um mut ban ong ba ki jingkren kiba stad ki long kiba sniew ha lade hi. Ka jingmut ka long ba ka jingstad briew kam dei ban pyndum ia ka khubor jong ka Diengphna. Kane ka kyntien ka thew ia ka jingkren beiñ jong ki Greek bad ki Roman. Ha Athen, u Paul u la pyndonkam ia ka jingmutdur, ka saian bad ka jingmut jingpyrkhat, hynrei kane ka la pynmih tang khyndiat ki soh. Kumta, “u la rai ban bud ia kawei pat ka plan jong ka jingtrei ha Korinth ha ka jingpyrshang jong u ban ioh ia ka jingpyrkhat jong ki bym pyrkhat bad ki bym don ka jingiapher. U la kut jingmut ban kiar na ki jingiania bad ki jingiatainia, bad ‘ban ym tip ei ei’ hapdeng ki nong Korinth ‘lait noh ia U Jisu Khrist, bad ia U ba la sahnarphna.’ ” — The Acles of A. 244.

`Ha kiei ki rukom ba ki jingiakren kiba bniah ki lah ban pyndum ia ka khubor jong ka Diengphna? Balei ka jingpynbna ia U Jisu Khrist bad ia U ba la sahnarphna ka la pynmih kham bun ki soh ha Korinth ban ia ka jingstad kaba hikai ban batai, ka saian bad ka jingstad ki la pynmih ha Athen? Hynrei hato ki lah ban don ki por ba ka jinghikai ban batai, ka jingstad ka jingtip bad ka saian ki lah ban iarap ha kaba pynbna ia ka gospel?`
