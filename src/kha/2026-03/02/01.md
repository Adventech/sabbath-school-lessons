### Ki Jingpule Na Ka Bynta Kane Ka Taïew
1 Korinth. 1:17-31, Kolosai 1:20, 1 Petros. 2:24, Ki Kam 13:16-47, 1 Korinth. 2:1-5.

> <p>Dkhot Spel-lyndet</p>
> “Namar kata ka ktien kaba shaphang ka diengphna ka long shisha ha kita kiba la jot noh ka jingbieit, hynrei ha ngi kiba ioh lait-im ka long ka bor U Blei.” (1 Korinth. 1:18).

U Cicero, u nongthoh bad nongkren ka Roman ba mane bleithaw, u la ong ia ki briew ka Roman ba kim dei ban pyrkhat wat tang shaphang ka diengphna, kumta ka long kaba la isih kum ka jingiap. Wat la u Cicero u la iap kumba shiteng spah snem shuwa ban kha ia U Jisu, hynrei ka jingong jong u ka pyni ia ka jingkren beiñ kaba ki nong Rom ki la bat ia ka diengphna. Ka la long kaba sniew haduh katta katta ba kim dei ban pyrkhat wat tang ia kata ruh.

Ha ka jingïapher, u Paul un thoh kumne: “Ka khubor jong ka diengphna ... ka long ka bor U Blei” (1 Korinth. 1:18,). Na ka bynta u Paul, ka Diengphna ka long ka atiar jong ka jingpyniasuk hapdeng U Blei bad u briew (Ephesos. 2:16, Kolosai. 1:20), ka dak kaba khraw tam jong ka jinglong ba sngewrit jong U Jisu (Philipi. 2:8), bad ka jaka ha kaba la siew ia ka ram kaba khraw jong ngi (Kolosai. 2:14).

Ka Diengphna ka long ka jubab jong u Paul ia ki jingeh ha Korinth. Phi donkam ban leit sha jngai than ha ka 1 Korinth ban sngewthuh ba u sngewkhia shibun ia kawei ka mat kaba kongsan: ki jingiaphiah ha ka balang. U Paul u la sngewlyngngoh haduh katta katta ba tang hadien ki jingkhublei (1 Korinth. 1:1-3) bad ka bynta jong ka jingainguh (1 Korinth. 1:4-9), kane ka dei ka phang kaba nyngkong eh kaba u kren (1 Korinth. 1:10-17). Ha kane ka taiew, ngin phai sha ka khubor ba donbor jong ka Diengphna kum ka jubab ia kane ka jingeh bad ia kiwei pat ki mat ha Korinth.

_Pynkhreh ïa kane ka Lynnong na ka bynta ka Sabbaton, July 11._
