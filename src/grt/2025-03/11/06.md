`Reo. 32:30–32 rangko poraibo. Papirangna mol⸳molchake bi⸳anio Mose badita chel⸳e re⸳angaha?`

Uamangni bobil dake chakatani a⸳sel, namen kenbegnigipa obosta Israelrangni tambuona re⸳baaha, bang⸳a manderang siatako man⸳tokaha. (Reo. 32:28). Aro salgipino, Mose manderangna indake aganprakataha, “Na⸳simang dal⸳begipa papko dakaha, aro da⸳o anga Jihovaona doanggen: haida anga na⸳simangni papna nangrimaniko dakna man⸳gen” (Reo. 32:30).

“Mose Isolona re⸳angtaiaha aro aganha, ‘Aiao, ia manderang dal⸳begipa papko dakaha, aro uamang an⸳tangmangna sonani miterangko dakaha. Indiba da⸳o na⸳a uamangni papko watnakode, aro watjaode, anga mol⸳mola, nang⸳ni segimin ki⸳taponiko angko kimitbo’ ” (Reo. 32:31, 32).

Aiao inmanabee Mose Kristoni bakkon dakaha! Uni papirangna mol⸳molchakani a⸳sel aro uamangna an⸳tangni janggikoba on⸳kangna skani a⸳sel, ua rongtalen Kristoko teng⸳chake mesokaha jekon Ua an⸳ching sakantina dakaha. Mairongpilgipa niamko pegipa manderangna dakmesokani! Ua Gitelna chu⸳gimik on⸳kanganiko aro manderangna on⸳kangpile ka⸳saaniko dake mesokaha. Re⸳ongkata ki⸳tap ua somoi chachao Mose badita ru⸳ute a⸳brio Isol baksa dongaha ukosan mesokaijaha, indiba Deuteronomy ki⸳tap parakaniko dakaha je, ua Sinaio Gitelni mikkango sal 40 naba ong⸳aha. (Deut. 9:18 ko nibo).

Reo. 32:32 o, “Kema ka⸳a” ingipa kattako ia verboniko ra⸳aha, “ripea” ba “chila,” ian Isa. 53:4 o agangipa gita ong∙a, jekon iano an⸳ching indake Jisuni gimin poraia: “Ua an⸳chingni dukrangko chilaha.” Ia apsan verbkon indake pe⸳e agannaba man⸳gen—“Kema ka⸳a” ine Re⸳o. 32:32 o, agangipa aro “chilaha” ine Isa. 53:4 o, agangiparanga ong∙a.

Mairongpilgipa janggi jokatna kam ka⸳ani bidingo aro kema ka⸳ani gimin aro baditan Isolna uara dam rakbee an⸳chingko jokatna kam ka⸳aha uko mesokani ong⸱aha.

Beben iano Mose Isolko manderangni papko “ripechina” mol⸳molenga, jekon Ua hajal bilsirangni ja∙mano chisolsako dakaha. Reo. 32:32 o, uan iako mesokaha je, chu⸳onggijaniko on⸳dapanikosan mesokaijaha indiba sakgipinni palo on⸳chakgipakoba mesoka: Uan Isol An⸳tangmanchakon mesokaha.

Ua katta iako mesokaha je, maikai kema ka⸳ania re⸳baachim, uko mesokaha. Isol Kristoo an⸳chingni paprangna atchiaha je rama gita mangmangsan an⸳ching kema ka⸳aniko man⸳gen. Jokatna kam ka⸳aniko baditan Isolnade namen dam rakbegipa gita ong⸱ahachim uko mesokani ong⸳aha.

`Mose Isolko manderangni paprangko ripechina mol⸳molaha, aro bon⸳kamao Ua ukon Jisuchi chu∙sokataha. Maikai ia aiao inmangipa bebe ong⸱aniko an⸳chingni gisikrangna skie ra⸳na man⸳gen? Maikai uara Isolni ga⸳akgimin mandeni jatna ka⸳saaniko mesokaha?`
