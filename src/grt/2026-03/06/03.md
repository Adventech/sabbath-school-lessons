1 Korinthirangna 12:4–6 o saksan ong‧aniko aganaha (“Gisik saksan,” “Gitel saksan,” aro “Isol saksan”), uarangko 1 Korinthirangna 12-ni bangki bakrango talatskaa. Paulni jakkalgipa kattarangchi uko sakki man‧aha jekai “saksan aro apsan Gisik” (1 Kor. 12:11), “be‧en an‧san ong‧a” (1 Kor. 12:12), “be‧en an‧san” (1 Kor. 12:12, 13, 20), “Gisik saksan” (1 Kor. 12:13 [changgni]), “tong‧sa tong‧gipinna apsan dake sintegen” (1 Kor. 12:25).

Nangrimani baksana, Paul Kristoni be‧eno dingtang dingtang bakrang donganikoba agana jekai “bakrang bang‧a” (1 Kor. 12:12, 20), “pilakan Gisik saksano be‧en an‧sanna baptize ka‧ako man‧aha” (1 Kor. 12:13), “be‧en bak sasan ong‧ja indiba bang‧a” (1 Kor. 12:14), “be‧en gimik” (1 Kor. 12:17), “be‧enni bakrangko, uarangoni tong‧prakko” (1 Kor. 12:18), “be‧enni bakrang” (1 Kor. 12:22, 23), “pilak bakrang” (1 Kor. 12:26). Ia nangrimani aro dingtang dingtang ong‧aniko ga‧chate agananian indake mesoka je gisikni on‧aniranga dingtang dingtang ong‧anichi nangrimaniko ong‧atna miksonggipa ong‧a.

Dingtang dingtang ong∙ani gisepo ia nangrimania Isolni cholonko teng∙chakna nangchongmota. Pagipara saksa bimang ong∙a, Depanteara sakgipin ong∙a aro Gisik Rongtalgipaba sakgipin ong∙a. Mondoliko rikna aro uni kamna uko bilakatna gita damsan kam ka∙engon, Sakgittaman uamangni sakprakprak ong∙aniko name rakkia (1 Kor. 12:4–6, Eph. 4:11–13).

`1 Korinthirangna 12:12–31-ko poraibo. Maini gimin be∙enko uni bang∙a bakrang baksa toe agananiara mondoli aro uni memborrangko mesokna meligipa ong∙a?`

Uaranga bachi re∙na nanggen uko nikna gita ja∙paranga mikronrango pangchaka, unikode, mikronranga mamungkoba dangtapna man∙ja—jakrangsan dangtapna man∙aia. Aroba, mitam bakranga ge∙gipin bakrangna bate bilgribata (1 Kor. 12:22) ba mandera∙gijani ong∙bata (1 Kor. 12:23) ine aganania chanchianisan ong∙aia, maina pilakan nangchongmotgipa ong∙a (1 Kor. 12:22).

Rasonggrie, Korinthirangni gisepo gipin on∙anirangna bate mitam on∙anirangko gamchatbate ra∙na skani gnangchim. Korinthirangko ia guale dakaoniko champengna gita, Paul uamangko ka∙saanina bimchipna aganaha, jean “nambata rama” (1 Kor. 12:31) ong∙a. Minggipin kattarango aganode, jedakgipa on∙aniba ong∙china, uko gisik gnange aro ka∙saani gnang jakkalode, uan Isolna namnikani ong∙a.
