---
title:  “ ‘NginguJosefa Umfowenu’ ”
date:  16/06/2022
---

`Funda: uGenesisi 45. Zifundo zini zothando, ukholo, nethemba ezitholakala kulendaba na?`

Kungalomzuzu, ngenkathi u-Judah ekhuluma “ ‘ngobubi’ ” obabuzowela phezu kuka ’avi, “ ‘ubaba wami’ ” (Gen. 44:34), lapho uJosefa “akhala” (Gen. 45:1) futhi “wazazisa” kubafowabo. Lesisisho, esivama ukusetshenziswa ekuzembuleni kukaNkulunkulu (Eksod. 6:3, Ezek. 20:9), sincoma ukuthi nguNkulunkulu futhi owayezembula lapha, ngokunjalo. Okungukuthi, uJehovah wayebonise ukuthi ukuhlinzeka Kwakhe kuyabusa, nangaphandle kwamaphutha abantu.

Abafowabo bakaJosefa abayikholwa lento abayizwayo nabayibonayo. Ngalokho, uJosefa kumele ephindile, “ ‘NginguJosefa umfowenu’ ” (Gen. 45:4, NKJV), kungokwesibili kuphela, lapho bezwisisa kahle amagama “ ‘lowo enamthengisela eGibhithe’ ” (Gen. 45:4, NKJV), bekholwa khona.

UJosefa uyamemezela: “ ‘uNkulunkulu wangithuma’ ” (Gen. 45:5, NKJV). Lokhu kubhekisa kuNkulunkulu kunezinhloso ezimbili. Kusebenza hhayi ekuqinisekiseni kuphela Abafowabo ngokuthi uJosefa akanamagqubu ngakubo; kungukuvuma okukhulu kokholo, futhi isisho sethemba, ngoba lokho abakwenza Kwakudingekile ukuze kube “ ‘nenkululeko enkulu’ ” kanye nokusinda “ ‘kwesizukulwane’ ” (Gen. 45:7).

UJosefa abe esebagqugquzela Abafowabo ukuba baye kuyise ukuza bamlungiselele ukuza eGibhithe. Uhambisa amazwi akhe ngamagama aqondile mayelana nendawo “abazohlala,” kuyo, okuyi-Goshen, eyaziwa ngokunotha kwamadlelo ayo, “ ‘iyinhle kakhulu ezweni’ ” (Gen. 45:18, 20, NKJV). Unakekela futhi into yokuhamba; izinqola ziyahlinzekwa, okuzogcina kuvumise uJakobe nalokho ababenamava ako (Gen. 45:27). UJakobe Uthatha lesisibonakaliso njengobufakazi bokuthi uJosefa uyaphila, futhi lokhu kwanele kuye ukuba aphile futhi (compare with Gen. 37:35, Gen. 44:29).

Izinto zinhle manje. Amadodana angu-12 kaJakobe ayaphila. UJakobe manje ubizwa ngo “Israyeli” (Gen. 45:28), futhi ukuhlinzeka kukaNkulunkulu kubonakala ngendlela enamandla.

`Yebo, uJosefa uyabonga kubafowabo. Kwakufanele akwazi. Sifunda kanjani, nokho, ukubonga kulabo ukusiphatha kabi kwabo kungabi nemiphumela emihle njengalokhu kwabanjalo kuJosefa na?`
