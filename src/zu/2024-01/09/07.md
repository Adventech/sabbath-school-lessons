---

Read Ellen G. White, “God With Us,” pp. 19–26, in The Desire of Ages.

Ukuba yimikhuleko kaKristu nokuba yimikhuleko mayelana noKristu, amaHubo anikeza isambulo esisodwa somuntu uKristu kanye nomsebenzi wokuhlenga njengaLowo ongu “Nkulunkulu unathi” (Mat. 1:23). UJesu ungu “Nkulunkulu unathi” emikhulekweni yokulwa nokulahlwa kanye nokuhlupheka. Ungu “Nkulunkulu unathi” ekukhaleleni ubulungiswa nokuhlengwa. UJesu ungu “Nkulunkulu unathi” ngokungasishiyi ekulahlekeni kwethu nasekupheleni kwethemba kodwa ekusiboniseni indlela yokholo lunqobile. Waba ngathi umPristi neNkosi yaphakade ukusisindisa ekushabalaleni kwaphakade kwesono. KuKristu, Inkosi ephelele yobuDavide, zonke izithembiso ezinzima zikaNkulunkulu zosindiso zithola ukugcwaliseka (2 Kor. 1:20).

U-Ellen G. White uchaza ubunye bukaKristu nesintu: “Ngobuntu Bakhe, uKristu wathinta isintu; ngobunkulunkulu Bakhe, ubamba isihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu. NjengeNdodana yomuntu, Yena wasinikeza isibonelo sokulalela; njengeNdodana kaNkulunkulu, unikeza thina amandla okulalela. KwakunguKristu owathi ehlokozini Entabeni i-Horeb ekhuluma noMose, ‘NGINGUYE ENGINGUYE. . . . Uyosho kanje kubantwana bakaIsrayeli, UNGINGUYE ungithumile kinina.’ Eksod. 3:14. Lokhu kwakuyisifungo sokuhlengwa kukaIsrayeli. Ngakhoke ngenkathi Yena ‘engumfanekiso womuntu,’ wazibiza yena ngo NGINGUYE. Ingane yase-Bethlehem, uMsindisi omnene nothobekile, inguNkulunkulu ‘ebonakala enyameni.’ 1 Tim. 3:16. Futhi kithina uthi: ‘NGINGUYE uMalusi Olungileyo.’ ‘NGINGUYE Usinkwa sokuphila.’ ‘NGINGUYE uNdlela, uQiniso, noKuphila.’ ‘Onke amandla anikezwe Mina ezulwini nasemhlabeni.’ Johane 10:11; 6:51; 14:6; Mat. 28:18. NGINGUYE isiqiniseko sazo zonke izithembiso. NGINGUYE; ungesabi.”—The Desire of Ages, pp. 24, 25.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`UNkulunkulu ukubonise kanjani ukuthembeka Kwakhe okungazamazamiswa esivumelwaneni Sakhe nakuba abantu Bakhe bengathembekile na? Siqiniseko sini lokho okusinikeza abantwana bakaNkulunkulu abanomshikashika na?`

`Ubupristi obubodwa nobukhulu bukaKristu ngokohlelo luka-Melchizedek busiqinisa kanjani isiqiniseko sosindiso lwabantu bakaNkulunkulu na?`

`OAmavangeli aveza ukuthi izithembiso eziningi zikaMesiya kumaHubo zagcwaliseka kuJesu Kristu. Lokhu kukubonisa kanjani ubuqiniso beZwi likaNkulunkulu na? Kungani kumele simelana nawo wonke umuzwa ovama ukuthena ithemba lethu eZwini likaNkulunkulu na?`

`Iyiphi induduzo enkulu esingayithola kumazwi kaKristu, “ ‘Onke amandla anikeze we mina ezulwini nasemhlabeni’ ” (Matt. 28:18) na? Sisisebenzisa kanjani lesisithembiso kumava ethu na?`
