### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Hub. 18:3–18, Hub. 41:1–3, Dut. 15:7–11, iHubo 82, iHubo 96:6–10, Ps. 99:1–4, Rom. 8:34.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Ngenxa yokubandezeka kwabahluphekileyo nangokububula kwabampofu ngizakuvuka kalokhu,’ usho uJehova, ‘ngimbeke ekusindisweni akulangazelelayo’ ” (IHubo 12:5, NKJV).

Isikhathi sethu akusona kuphela isikhathi kuso ububi, okungemthetho, kanye nembandezelo okubhoke kuso. Abahlabeleli babephila ezikhathini ezinjalo, nabo. Ngalokho, noma yini abayishoyo, amaHubo aphinde abe yizikhalazo zikaNkulunkulu ngodlame nembandezelo emhlabeni, ezweni lethu, kanye nesikhalazo sabahlabeleli, futhi.

Yebo, uJehovah ungobekezelayo futhu uyalubamba ulaka Lwakhe ekubekezeleni Kwakhe okukhulu, engafuni namunye abhubhe kodwa baphenduke futhi baguqule izindlela zabo (2 Pet. 3:9-15). Futhi nakuba isikhathi sikaNkulunkulu esifanele sokungenelela singahambisani njalo nokulindela kwesintu, usuku lokwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu luyeza (Hub. 96:13, Hub. 98:9). Sidinga nje ukuthembela kuYena, nasezithembisweni Zakhe, kuze kufike lolosuku. NguMdali kuphela, osihlalo sobukhosi Sakhe simi ekulungeni nasemthethweni (Hub. 89:14, Hub. 97:2), ongahlinzeka, ngokwahlulela Kwakhe okwengamele, ukuzinza nokuphumelela ezweni. Izigaba ezimbili zokwahlulela zifaka ukuhlengwa kwabacindezelwe kanye nokubhujiswa kwababi (Hub. 7:6−17).

Yilokhu esikuthenjisiwe, futhi yilokhu, nembala, okuyothi ngelinye ilanga— kepha ngesikhathi sikaNkulunkulu, hhayi esethu, kufike, iphuzu eligcizelelwa ngumhlabeleli.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha,uNhlolanja 10._
