---
title:  Phakamisani Izandla Zenu Endaweni Engcwele
date:  17/03/2024
---

`Funda iHubo 134. Ukhonza kwenziwa kuphi lapha na? Yini umphumela wokukhonza uJehovah na?`

IHubo 134 libuyekeza isibusiso sompristi uAroni kuNumeri 6:24-26 (Hub. 67:1) futhi ligqamisa isibusiso njengesimiso nomphumela wobudlelwane phakathi kukaNkulunkulu noIsrayeli. Abantu babusisa uNkulunkulu endaweni engcwele, uNkulunkulu abasise abantu Bakhe eZiyoni. Izibusiso zidlulela kukho konke okuphilayo ngoba uJehovah unguMdali wezulu nomhlaba. Ukubalulwa kweZiyoni njengendawo yezibusiso ezikhethekileyo kugqamisa isibopho sesivumelwano sikaJehovah nabantu Bakhe. Ngalokho kuphakathi kwesivumelwano somusa lapho uIsrayeli esebenzisa ilungelo lokubusisa uJehovah nelokubusiswa nguYena.

`Funda iHubo 18:1; iHubo 36:1, iHubo 113:1; iHubo 134:1, 2; neHubo 135:1, 2. Abakhonzayo bavezwa kanjani lapha na?`

AmaHubo avama ukuveza abakhonzayo njengezinceku zikaJehovah. “Abema ebusuku endlini kaJehovah” (Hub. 134:1, NKJV) kungenzeka kubhekiswa kumqaphi wasebusuku wabaLevi (1 Kron. 9:23-27) noma ekudumiseni okwakwenziwa kuNkulunkulu emini nasebusuku (1 Kron. 9:33).

Ngoba abakwaIsrayeli babekhonza uNkulunkulu ongabonwa, owangemelwe yinoma yiluphi uhlobo lomfanekiso, indawo engcwele yayisebenza ukuveza inkazimula kaJehovah nokunikeza indawo ephephile kwizoni ukuba zibhekane neNkosi yazo engcwele.

Lokhu kunqwamana kuqalwa nguJehovah ngoKwakhe futhi kulawulwa yimithetho nezimiso Zakhe.

“Sondelani kuyo, iyitshe eliphilileyo, elinqatshwe ngabantu, kepha kuNkulunkulu likhethekile, liyigugu, nakhiwe njengamatshe aphilileyo, nibe yindlu yokomoya, ubupristi obungcwele, ninikele ngemihlatshelo yokomoya eyamukelekayo kuNkulunkulu ngaye uJesu Kristu” (1 Pet. 2:4, 5, NKJV). Esikubona lapha, emazwini kaPetro, yisisho seTestamente Elisha semiqondo efanayo eyethulwe kulamahubo, leyo yabantu bakaNkulunkulu, manje ubupristi obungcwele, benikela udumo nokubonga eNkosini yabo uJesu Kristu, uMdali noMhlengi wabo, ngakho konke okuhle abenzele kona.

`Njengamakholwa eTestamente Elisha, nathi sinendima yobupristi ngokuthi sibizelwe ukuhambisa izindaba ezilungileyo zevangeli ezweni. Yiziphi izindlela okuyizona ezifanele zokukwenza lokhu na?`
