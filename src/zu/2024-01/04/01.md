### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Hub. 139:1–18; iHubo 121; Hub. 17:8; Mat. 23:37; 1 Kor. 10:1–4; Heb. 4:15, 16.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Abalungileyo bayakhala, futhi uJehova uyezwa; abakhulule kuzo zonke izinhlupheko zabo” (iHubo 34:17, NKJV).

Ngokuphinda Phinda, amaHubo agqamisa iqiniso lokuthi iNkosi uSomandla, eyadala futhi isekela izwe lonke, iyazembula Yona njengoNkulunkulu woqobo osungula futhi asekele ubudlelwane nabantu Bakhe.

UNkulunkulu usondele kubantu Bakhe nakwindalo Yakhe, ezulwini nasemhlabeni (Hub. 73:23, 25). Nakuba Yena “emise isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi ezulwini” (Hub. 103:19, NKJV) futhi “ehamba ngamafu” (Hub. 68:4, NKJV), Yena futhi “usondele kulabo abambizayo, kubo bonke abambiza ngeqiniso” (Hub. 145:18, NKJV). Ngokungaguqkiyo amaHubo aphakamisa iqiniso lokuthi iNkosi inguNkulunkulu ophilayo, owenzele labo ababiza Yena (Hub. 55:16-22). AmaHubo abalulekile ngoba aqhubukushwa ngu, futhi ebhekise kuNkulunkulu ophilayo, ozwayo nophendula imikhuleko.

Kumele sikhumbule ukuthi impendulo efanele ekusondeleni kweNkosi iqukethe impilo yokholo kuYona, kanye neyokulalela imithetho Yayo. Akukho ngaphandle kwalolukholo kanye nokulalela okuyokwamukeleka kuYe, njengalokhu kwembulwa ngumlando kaIsrayeli.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uMasingana 27._
