### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
iHubo 8; iHubo 100; iHubo 97; iHubo 75; Ps. 105:7−10; Gal. 3:26–29; Hub. 25:10.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “UJehova uyinkosi,wembethe ubukhosi; uJehova wembethe, ubhince amandla.Nezwe limi liqinile, lingazanyazanyiswa” (IHubo 93:1, NIV).

AmaHubo ngokungaphazamiseki aphakamisa inkolo eyisisekelo ngokubusa phezu kwakho konke kukaNkulunkulu. UJehovah wadafuthi wondla lokho akudalile. UyiNkosi yamakhosi yomhlaba wonke, futhi ubusa izwe ngobungiliswa nangokulunga. Imithetho Yakhe nezimiso ilungile futhi iletha ukuphila kulabo abayigcinayo. UJehovah unguMahluleli olungileyo oqinisekisa ukuthi izwe liqhubeka nokuhleleka, futhi lokho ukwenza ngokuvuza abalungileyo kanye nokujezisa ababi, kodwa ngesikhathi Sakhe, hhayi esethu.

Isivumelwano sikaNkulunkulu noIsrayeli sidlala indima ekhethekile ekuvikeleni izwe ngoba sazisa ngosindiso lweNkosi. INkosi yathathela kuyo uIsrayeli njengegugu Layo, yenza uIsrayeli, kuzo zonke izizwe, abantu Bayo. INkosi ithembekile esivumelwaneni Sayo futhi iyaqhubeka nokunakekela umhlambi Wayo ngaphandle kokungathembeki kwawo futhi, ngezinye izikhathi, nokuhlubuka ngamabomu.

Ukubusa kweNkosi okwengamele konke ngalokho kwenza izwe lizinze futhi livikeleke. Abahlabeleli bafuna umfundi aqonde leliqiniso eliyisisekelo. Ngalendlela yokubuka izwe njengesiqondiso sabo, abahlabeleli bafuna ukuthuthuka kanye nokukhonza uNkulunkulu bengaphazamisiwe.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uMasingana 20._
