### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Hub. 79:5–13, Hub. 88:3–12, Hub. 69:1–3, Hub. 22:1, iHubo 77, Hub. 73:1–20, 1 Pet. 1:17.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Siyakuhlabelela kanjani ihubo likaJehova kwelinye izwe na?” (IHubo 137:4).

Akudingekele ukuba size sijule Encwadini yamaHubo ukuze sithole ukuthi amaHubo ashiwo emhlabeni ongaphelele, onesono, ububi, ukuhlupheka, kanye nokufa. Indalo ezinzile ephethwe yiNkosi yakho konke kanye nemithetho Yakhe elunguleyo njalo isengozini yobubi. Njengalokhu isono singcolisa izwe kakhulu, umhlaba usube “yizwe lokuhamba” kakhulu kubantu bakaNkulunkulu. Leliqiniso lidala inkinga kumhlabeleli: Iphilwa kanjani impilo yokholo ezweni lokuhamba na?

Njangalokhu sesibonile, abahlabeleli bayakuvuma kubusa okwengamele kukaNkulunkulu kanye namandla, kanye nokwahlulela Kwakhe okulungileyo. Bayazi ukuthi uNkulunkulu uyinqaba nosizo lwangunaphakade ngezikhathi zokuhlupheka. Ngalesisizathu, abahlabeleli bayakhathezeka ngezinye izikhathi (ngubani ongakhathazeki na?) ngokubukeka sengathi uNkulunkulu akekho kanye nokudlondlobala kobubi phambi kweNkosi enkulu nelungileyo. Isimo esiyindida samaHubo njengemikhuleko sibonakaliswa ekuphenduleni kwabahlabeleli ekubonakaleni sengathi uNkulunkulu uthule. Ngamanye amazwi, abahlabeleli baphendula ukubonakala sengathi uNkulunkulu uthule, nasebukhoneni Bakhe.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNhlolanja 3._
