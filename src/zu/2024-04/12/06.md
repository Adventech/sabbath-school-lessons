---
title:  UJesu noMariya
date:   19/12/2024
---

`Funda kuJohane 20:11-13. Kwenzekani lapha etshengisa isizathu sikaMariya Magdalena sokungaqondi ukuthi lisho ukuthini ithuna elingenalutho?`

Ukugagulwa kwegama likaMariya kokugcina okwandulela lokhu, kulapho etshela uPetru noJohane ngethuna elingenalutho (Johane 20:2). Bagijimela ethuneni, yena wabuyela futhi kamuva. Ngemuva kokuthi uPetru noJohane sebelihlolile ithuna, bahamba. Kodwa uMariya wabuyela, ekhala enjalo, walinda lapho. Ngaphandle kokungabaza, wayesekhale kakhulu kulezo zinsuku ezimbalwa ezedlule, manjeke sekwenzeka lokhu futhi? Wagoba wabheka ngaphakathi.

Wamangaliswa ukubona izingelosi ezimbili ezembethe okumhlophe, ziphakathi ethuneni, zihlezi lapho kwakukade kulele khona umzimba kaJesu. Zambuza, zathi: “ ‘Mame, ukhalelani?’ “ (Johane 20:13). Waphendula kabuhlungu esho ukuthi ithathiwe iNkosi yakhe, akazi ukuthi ibekwe-phi.

`Funda kuJohane 20:14-18. Yini eyaguqula zonke izinto kuMariya?`

Ngamehlo agcwele izinyembezi, uMariya waphenduka, wabona umuntu emi ngemuva kwakhe. Ngamazwi afana nalawo ezingelosi, lomuntu wabuza wathi: “ ‘Mame, ukhalelani? Ufuna bani na?’ “ (Johane 20:15). Yena ucabanga ukuthi ukhuluma nomsebenzi wasengadini, wamcela ukuthi amsize amfunise umzimba kaJesu.

Lomuntu wakhuluma izwi elilodwa-nje, wathi: “Mariya.” Kwakuyisambulo selizwi elilodwa-nje elaguqula umhlaba. Ngokushesha, uMariya owayesamangele, wabona ukuthi ujesu lona okhuluma naye, wamamukela. UJesu wamtshela ukuthi angamlibazisi njengoba kufanele enyukele kuYise. Kodwa umsebenzi wakhe ukuhamba atshele abafundi ukuthi wenyukela “ ‘kuBaba noYihlo, kuNkulunkulu waMi, noNkulunkulu wenu,’ “ (Johane 20:17). UMariya wakwenza lokho ayekuthunyiwe. Watshela abafundi ukuthi uyibonile iNkosi, washo nakho konke eyayimtshele khona (Johane 20:18).
