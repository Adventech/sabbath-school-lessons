---
title:  IParadesi Enemiphefumulo Engenamizimba
date:  30/11/2022
---

Nakuba amaProtestanti engasamukeli isihlanzo, iningi nokho likholwa ukuthi Imiphefumulo yabalungileyo abafileyo kumanje ijabule eparadesi phambi kukaNkulunkulu. Abanye bathi leyo “miphefumulo” yimimoya Engenamizimba; abanye bakholwa ukuthi yimiphefumulo Engenamizimba kodwa eyembozwe ngumzimba wezikamoya wenkazimulo.

Noma ngabe yisiphi isimo okuthiwa abafileyo baphila kuso, lezizimfundiso zicekela phansi imfundiso yebhayibheli yovuko lokugcina kanye nokwahlulelwa kwabafileyo. Kungani kunovuko kanye nokwahlulela (Samb. 20:12-14) uma Imiphefumulo yabalungileyo ivele isiseparadesi na?

`Funda Izenzo 2:29, 34, 35 kanye no-1 Korinte 15:16–18. Lezizahluko zikhanyisa kanjani ngesimo sabafile kanye nalabo abalinde Uvuko na? `

IBhayibheli lifundisa ukuthi bonke abantu abasezulwini bayakhona bephila, njengo-Enoch (Gen. 5:24) no-Elijah (2 Amakhosi 2:9-11), noma bavuswa kwabafileyo, njengoMose (Juda 9) kanye nalabo abavuswa noKristu (Mat. 27:51-53).

Njengalokhu sesibonile, ukubhekisa kwimiphefumulo “engaphansi kwealtare” ekhalela uNkulunkulu ifuna impindiselo (Samb. 6:9-11) yisingathekiso sokufuna ubulungiswa futhi asiqinisi imfundiso yokungafi komphefumulo. Kungenjalo, lababantu abezwakali engathi bajabulela umvuzo wabo waphakade. Empeleni, ithuna yindawo yokuphumula kwabafileyo, abalinde uvuko lokugcina, lapho impilo yabo iyobuyiselwa. Abafileyo, nabo abafileyo abalungileyo, abayona Imiphefumulo Engenamizimba entanta ezulwini, elinde ukuhlanganiswa nemizimba yayo ovukweni lokugcina.

Futhi, yini okungase uPawule abe ukhuluma ngayo ku-1 Korinte 15:18, NKJV, uma ethi uma bekungekho ukuvuswa kwabafileyo, ngalokho, “labo abalele kuKristu babhubhile.” Bangabhubha kanjani uma sebesenjabulweni yasezulwini futhi belapho selokhu bafa na? Imfundiso engumongo weTestamente Elisha, ukuvuka kwabafileyo lapho uKrsitu ebuya, yenziwa ize yilemfundiso yamanga yokuthi abalungileyo abafile bakhuphukela ezulwini ngemuva kokufa kwabo. Nakuba kunjalo, sikuzwa Lokhu sonke isikhathi, ikakhulu emingcwabeni.

`Yiziphi izindlela ongasiza ngazo abantu baqonde ukuthi lomqondo wokuthi abafileyo balele emhlabathini ngempela “yizindaba ezinhle,” ngokuthi ngeqiniso baphumule futhi abazi nhlungu nakuhlupheka na?`
