---
title:  Abangcwele Esihlanzweni
date:  29/11/2022
---

Ibandla lamaKatolika lithi abafileyo abangafanelekanga ukuba sesihogweni kodwa abangakalungeli ukuba separadesi bangahlanzwa izono zabo Esihlanzweni bese bekhuphukela eparadesi. Ukuhlupheka kwabo Esihlanzweni kungaciphiswa ngemikhuleko nokuhlawula kwezihlobo.

_Ikhathekizimu yebandla lamaKatolika isobala_ ngesihlanzo: “Bonke abafela emuseni nasebunganini bukaNkulunkulu, kodwa bengahlanzisisiwe, nembala baqinisekisiwe ngosindiso lwabo lwaphakade; kodwa ngemuva kokufa badlula ekuhlanzweni, ukuze bafinyelele ebungcweleni obudingekile ukuba bangene enjabulweni yasezulwini.”— Catechism of the Catholic Church, (New York: Doubleday, 1995) p. 291. Ithi, futhi, ukuhlupheka kwabo kungancishiswa yimikhuleko yezihlobo zabo kanye nezinye izenzo ezenzelwa abafileyo. “Ibandla futhi likhuthaza ukunikelwa kwezipho, izinxephezelo, kanye nemisebenzi yokuhlawula ukuba yenzelwe abafileyo.”— Catechism of the Catholic Church, p. 291.

`Funda uMshumayeli 9:10, Hezekeli 18:20-22, namaHeberu 9:27. Lezizindimana ziyiphikisa kanjani imfundiso yesihlanzo na?`

Imfundiso yesihlanzo ihlanganisa umqondo wobuhedeni wesihogo esivuthayo kanye nomkhuba wobuhedeni wokukhulekela abafileyo. Lemfundiso ayamukelekile kulabo abakholelwa ezimfundisweni zeBhayibheli (1) zokuthi abafileyo bahlala emathuneni abo bephumule bengezwa lutho (Mshum. 9:10); (2) ukuthi ukulunga komuntu owileyo ngeke kwadluliselwa komunye umuntu (Hezek. 18:20-22); (3) ukuthi nguJesu Kristu Umlamuleli wethu (1 Tim. 2:5); (4) ukuthi ukufa kulandelwa ngukwahlulela kokugcina, lingasekho ithuba lokuguquka ezonweni zalokhu kuphila (Heb. 9:27).

Futhi okunye okunqala kakhulu yindlela lemfundiso yesihlanzo emelene neBhayibheli ehlanekezela ngayo isimilo sikaNkulunkulu. Nembala, “Umsebenzi kaSathane selokhu awa ngowokuhumusha ngokungeyiko uBaba wethu osezulwini. Wancoma imfundiso yokungafi komphefumulo. . . . Umqondo wesihogo esisha ingunaphakade wakhiwa nguSathane; isihlanzo sasungulwa nguye. Lezizimfundiso zenza isimilo sikaNkulunkulu sibe ngamanga, ukuze abonakale njengonesihluku, impindiselo, uzwilakhe, futhi engenayo intethelelo.”—Ellen G. White, Manuscript 51 (Dec. 10), 1890. Kunokuba abalele befile, belindele ukubuya kukaKristu, lombono uthi basesihlanzweni, bahlupheka khona kuze kube khona othile okwazi ukubakhipha khona.

`Lamanga anjengesihlanzo noma ukuhlupheka kwaphakade asifundisani ngokubaluleka kwemfundiso na? Kungani lokho esikukholwayo kubalulekile, hhayi nje ukuthi sikholwa kubani na?`
