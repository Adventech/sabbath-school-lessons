### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Mat. 25:31–46; Dan. 7:9–14; 1 Kor. 6:2, 3; 2 Pet. 2:4–6; Mal. 4:1; Samb. 21:8.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ngokuba thina sonke simelwe ukubonakaliswa phambi kwesihlalo sokwahlulela sikaKristu, ukuze yilowo nalowo amukele njengalokho akwenzileyo esesemzimbeni, noma okuhle noma okubi” (2 Korinte 5:10, NIV).

Uma Izibhalo zisobala ngokukodwa, yiqiniso lokwahlulelwa. UNkulunkulu uyokwahlulela umhlaba. Imibhalo, kwiTestamente Elidala kanye Nelisha, miningi futhi isobala. Ubulungiswa obungekho lapha namanje buyofika ngelinye ilanga.

IBhayibheli lithi uNkulunkulu “ ‘unolwazi oluphelelelyo’ ” (Job 37:16, NIV) futhi “wazi konke” (1 Johane 3:20, NIV), kuhlangene nezinhloso zethu ezifihlakele (Mshum. 12:14, Jer. 17:10). Singacashela bonke kanye nakho konke okunye, kodwa akukho okusithekile kuNkulunkulu.

Leliqiniso ligudla ukuthi Yena akadinge kwahlulela ukwazi impilo Yomuntu ngamunye. Izahlulelo zikaNkulunkulu, nembala, zingukubonelela kwezulu, okwenzelwa izidalwa Zakhe, ezisezulwini kanye nasemhlabeni. Loluhlelo ngelomlando wezwe lonke ngoba uLusifa waqala Ukuhlubuka Kwakhe ezulwini wayesekudlulisela Emhlabeni (Samb. 12:7-9).

Kuleliviki sizobuka Uhlelo Lokwahlulela lwesikhathi Sokugcina kanye nezigaba zako ezintathu: ukwahlulela okwandulela ukuza kukaJesu, ukwahlulela kweminyaka eyinkulungwane, kanye nesigwebo esikhulu. Lonke Uhlelo lugcina ngokuvumelana nabalungileyo kanye nokufa kwesibili kwababi.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Disemba 24._
