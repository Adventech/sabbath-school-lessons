Funda u-Ellen G. White, “The Temptation and Fall,” pp. 52–62 and “The Plan of Redemption,” pp. 63-70, in Patriarchs and Prophets; “The Knowledge of Good and Evil,” pp. 23–27, in Education.

Eminyakeni engekude, Ucwaningo lwenziwe ngalokho okubizwa Ngamava ka Cishe Ngafa (Near Death Experiences, NDEs). Okwenzekayo ngukuthi abantu “bayafa,” ngokuthi Izinhliziyo zaba ziyeka ukushaya, futhi bayeke ukuphefumula. Kodwa, bese bebuyela empilweni, kodwa nezindaba ezimangalisayo zokuntanta komunye umkhakha Wokuphila kanye nokuhlangana nesidalwa sokukhanya. Abanye baze bakhulume ngokuhlangana nezihlobo ezafa kudala. Abantu abaningi, nawo AmaKristu, abangaqondi ngeqiniso lokufa, bakholwa ngukuthi lezizindaba zingubufakazi obandile bokungafi komphefumulo. Nakuba kunjalo (futhi lokhu kumele kube yisixwayiso esisobala kunazo zonke sokuthi kukhona okushaya amanzi), iningi eliba nalamava libanga ngelithi izidalwa zokukamoya abahlangane nazo kulama-NDEs zibanikeze amazwi enduduzo, Izisho ezimtoti ngothando, ukuthula, kanye nokulunga. Kepha abezwanga lutho ngosindiso kuKristu, lutho mayelana nesono, futhi lutho mayelana nokwahlulela. Nakuba bethola ukunambitha impilo yobuKristu ngale kokufa, Akumele yini nga bathole ijuphana lezimfundiso sisekelo zobuKristu kulokho na? Kodwa, lokho abakufundisiwe kuzwakala njengemfundiso ye-New Age, okuchaza isizathu sokuthi, Ezikhathini eziningi, babuya bengasatshekele ebuKristwini njengalokhu bebenjalo ngaphambi kokuba “bafe.” Futhi, kungani kungekho maKristu, aqinisekile ngokuthi ama-NDEs awo abengukubona okungakabonwa kwezulu lobuKristu, engakaze athole imfundiso yobuKristu ngenkathi elapho, ngokumelene nomthamo womizwa ye-New Age na? Impendulo ngukuthi babekhohliswa yilowo owakhohlisa uEva eEdeni, futhi ngamanga afanayo. (Buka isifundo 11).
