### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
2 Pet. 3:13; Samb. 21:3, 22; 1 Johane 3:2, 3; 1 Pet. 1:22; Isa. 25:8; Samb. 22:3–5.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Wayesethi ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi: ‘Bheka, ngenza konke kube kusha.’ Wayesethi kimina: ‘Loba, ngokuba lawa mazwi athembekile, aqinisile’” (Sambulo 21:5, NKJV).

Imibhalo Engcwele isinikeza ithemba: “Kepha ngokwesithembiso Sakhe sibheke amazulu amasha nomhlaba omusha, okukhona kuwo ukulunga” (2 Pet. 3:13, NASB).

Kwabanye, nokho, isithembiso “sezulu Elisha nomhlaba omusha” (Samb. 21:1) kubukeka kuyinganekwane, izindaba ezixoxwa yilabo ababusayo ababenethemba ababesebenzisa ithemba lempilo ngale kokufa ukusiza ekulawuleni izixuku. Umqondo kungukuthi: Nakuba kulukhuni kuwe njengamanje, ngelinye ilanga uyoba nomvuzo ezulwini, noma okunjalo.

Futhi nakuba abanye abantu basebenzise Ithemba lekusasa elethulwa yiBhayibheli ngaleyondlela, ukuhlukumeza kwabo akuguquli iqiniso lezithembiso esinazo mayelana namazulu amasha nomhlaba omusha.

Ezinsukwini zokugcina, abaklolodayo bayolihleka usulu Ithemba Lethu elibusisiweyo (2 Pet. 3:3-7). Kodwa ukukloloda kwabo, njengalokhu kubikezelwe ngaphambili, kungabonakala njengobufakazi bokuthi lokho okushiwo yiBhayibheli kuyiqiniso, ngokuba bakloloda njengalokhu kuprofethe iBhayibheli.

Kuleliviki sizozindla ngesithembiso sezulu Elisha nomhlaba omusha, kuhlangene nethempeli lasezulwini, ubukhona bakaNkulunkulu, ukuphela kokufa nezinyembezi—futhi, ekugcineni, Ukunqoba okukhulu kothando lukaNkulunkulu.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Disemba 31._
