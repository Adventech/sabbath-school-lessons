---
title:  Uvuko LukaMose
date:  23/10/2022
---

`Funda uJuda 9 no Luka 9:28-36. Yibuphi ubufakazi obuthola kulemibhalo yokuvuka ngokwenyama KukaMose na?`

Abanye Abadala bebandla lamaGriski e-Alexandria bathi, ngenkathi uMose efa, oMose ababili babonakala: omunye ephila ngokomoya, omunye efile ngokwenyama; omunye uMose enyukela ezulwini nezingelosi, omunye engcwatshiwe emhlabeni (buka kwi-Origen, Homilies on Joshua 2.1; Clement of Alexandria, Stromata 6.15.) Lokhu kwehlukanisa phakathi kokukhuphuka komphefumulo kanye nokungcwatshwa komzimba kungenza umqondo kulabo abakholelwa emqondweni wamaGriki wokungafi komphefumulo, kodwa lomqondo awukho eBhayibhelini. UJuda 9 uqinisa imfundiso yezibhalo yokuvuka komzimba kaMose, ngoba umbango wawu “mayelana nomzimba kaMose” hhayi ngomphefumulo ophilayo.

UDuteronomi 34:5–7 usitshela ukuthi uMose wafa eseneminyaka engu-120 ubudala, futhi uJehovah wamngcwaba endaweni efihlakele esigodini ezweni lwakwaMowabi. Kodwa uMose akazange asale isikhathi eside ethuneni. “UKristu ngoKwakhe, kanye nezingelosi ezazingcwabe uMose, behla besuka ezulwini ukuzovusa ongcwele oleleyo. . . . Ngokokuqala uKristu wayezinika ukuphila kofileyo. Lapho Inkosi yokuphila kanye nezikhazimulayo ziya ethuneni, uSathane wethukela ubukhosi bakhe. . . . UKristu akazange achithe isikhathi ebanga noSathane. . . . Kepha uKristu wadlulisela konke kuYise, ethi, ‘UJehovah akukhuze.’ Juda 9. . . . Uvuko lwaqinisekiswa ingunaphakade. USathane wephucwa ayekubambile; abalungileyo befile babeyophila futhi.”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, pp. 478, 479.

Ubufakazi obuqandua ikhanda bokuvuka KukaMose butholakala ekuguqukeni kwesimo. Lapho uMose waqhamuka kanye nomprofethi uEliya, owayehlwithelwe ezulwini ngaphandle kokuba afe (2 Amakhosi 2:1–11). UMose noEliya baze baxoxisana noJesu (buka uLuka 9:28–36). “Bheka, kwakhuluma naye amadoda amabili ayengoMose no-Eliya; abonakala ngenkazimulo, akhuluma ngokufa kwakhe abemelwe ukukufeza eJerusalema” (Luka 9:30, 31, NKJV). Ukuvela KukaMose, ubufakazi bokunqoba okuzayo kukaKristu phezu kwesono nokufa, kubonakaliswa lapha ngendlela engenakungatshazwa. KwakunguMose noEliya, hhayi “imimoya” yabo (nembala, uEliya wayengazange afe), eyaqhamuka kuJesu lapho.
