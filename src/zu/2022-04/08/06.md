date:  17/11/2022
---

`Funda u-1 Korinte 15:51–55. Iyiphi “imfihlo” (1 Kor. 15:51) echazwa nguPawule na?`

Abanye abashumayeli abadumile bancoma ukuthi “lemfihlo” (1 Kor. 15:51) nguku “hlwithelwa ezulwini okufihlakele” kwebandla, okumele kwenzeke eminyakeni eyisikhombisa ngaphambi kokuza kwesibili okukhazimulayo kukaKristu. Kulokhu “kuhlwithelwa ezulwini okuyimfihlo” amaKristu athembekileyo ngokuphazima, ngokuthula nangokunyenya, ahlwithelwa ezulwini ngenkathi bonke abanye besele bezibuza ukuthi kwenzekeni kubona. Abantu Bangase bazithole ngokuphazima sebesemotweni engenamshayeli, ngoba umshayeli ehlwithelwe ezulwini, futhi okusele nje “yizingubo zabo zokugqoka.” Uhlelo oluyizigaba ezingu-16 oluthengwa kunazozonke i-Left Behind, yaba ngama-movie ammane, yayithuthukisa lemfundiso yamanga, iyiveza kwizigidigidi zabantu.

Nembala, akukho mbhalo WeBhayibheli ovumelana nalokho kwehlukanisa phakathi kokuhlwithelwa ezulwini kanye Nokuza Kwesibili. “Imfihlakalo” uPawule abhekisa kuyo ngukuguqulwa kwabalungileyo abaphilayo ukuba bahlanganyele nabalungileyo abavusiwe ekuzeni kwesibili kukaKristu. Yikho ke “ukuhlwithwa.” Akukho “kuhlwithwa okuyimfihlo” ngoba Ukuza Kwesibili kuyobonakala kubo bonke abaphilayo (Samb. 1:7), futhi kokubili ukuvuswa kwabafileyo kanye nokuguquka kwabaphilayo kuyokwenzeka ekukhaleni kwecilongo ekubuyeni kukaKristu (1 Kor. 15:51, 52).

Ukuza kwesibili kukaKristu kuyodala ukunqwamana okumanagalisayo. Abaphilayo abalungileyo bayaguqulwa “ngesikhashanyana, ngokuphazima kweso” (1 Kor. 15:52). Ngezwi likaNkulunkulu, bayakhazimuliswa; manje benziwa abangafiyo futhi kanye nabangwele abavusiweyo bahlwithelwa phezulu ukuhlangabeza iNkosi yabo emoyeni. izingelosi. “ ‘ ziqoqe abakhethiweyo bayo emimoyeni yomine, kusukela emkhawulweni omunye wezulu kuze kube komunye’ ” (Matt. 24:31, NKJV).

“Izingane esincane zithwalwa yizingelosi ziye ezingalweni zomama bazo. Abangani okukade behlukaniswa ngukufa Bayahlangana, bengasayikuphinde bahlukane, futhi ngezingoma zenjabulo bakhuphukele ndawonye Edolobheni likaNkulunkulu.”— Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 645.

`Lesi yisithembiso esimangalisa kakhulu, into eyehluke kakhulu kunoma yini esesike saba namava ayo kangangokuba kulukhuni ukuyibamba. Kodwa cabanga ngobubanzi bezulu, kanye nobunkumbinkumbi bempilo lapha. Indalo ngokwayo ifakaza ngamandla amangalisayo kaNkulunkulu. Konke lokhu kusifundisani mayelana namandla kaNkulunkulu okuguqula abaphilayo kanye nokuvusa abafileyo ekuzeni kwesibili kukaJesu na?`
