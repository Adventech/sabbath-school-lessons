---
title:  “Abafileyo Abazi Lutho”
date:  12/10/2022
---

`Funda uJobe 3:11–13; Ihubo 115:17; Ihubo 146:4; noMshumayeli 9:5, 10. Yini esingayifunda kulezizahluko ngesimo sabantu ekufeni na?`

Abanye abaphuwula ngeBhayibheli baphikisana ngokuthi lezizahluko (Jobe 3:11–13; Ihubo 115:17; Ihubo 146:4; noMshumayeli 9:5, 10), ezibhalwe ngolimi lwenkondlo, azikwazi ukusetshenziswa ekuchazeni isimo sabantu ekufeni. Kuyiqiniso ukuthi ngezinye izikhathi izinkondlo zingaba nokufithiza futhi kube lula ukungaziqondi, kodwa lokho akunjalo ngalezizindimana. Ulimi lwazo lusobala, futhi nemiqondo yazo ihambisana ngokuphelele nezimfundiso jikelele zeTestamente Elidala ngalesisihloko.

Okokuqala, kuJobe 3, inzalabantu inengwa ngukuzalwa kwayo, ngenxa yokuhlupheka. (Ngezikhathi ezibucayi kakhulu, ngubani ongakaze afise sengathi ngabe akazalwanga na?) Uyabona ukuthi ukube wayefe ezalwa, wayezoba ngolele futhi ephumule (Jobe 3:11, 13).

Ihubo 115 lichaza indawo lapho abafileyo begcinwe khona njengendawo yokuthula, ngoba “abafileyo abamdumisi uJehovah” (Hub. 115:17, NKJV). Lokhu akuzwakali neze sengathi abafileyo, abathembekile (nababongayo) abafileyo, basezulwini bakhonza uNkulunkulu.

NgokweHubo 146, ukusebenza kwengqondo komuntu kuyayeka ngokufa: “Umoya wakhe uyaphuma, yena abuyele emhlabathini wakhe; ngalona lolo suku amacebo akhe ayaphela” (Hub. 146:4, NASB). Lokhu kuyisichasiso esiphelele seBhayibheli salokhu okwenzeka ekufeni.

Futhi uMshumayeli 9 uthayisela ngokuthi “abafileyo abazi lutho” nokuthi ethuneni “akukho msebenzi, namcabango, nakwazi, nakuhlakanipha” (Mshum. 9:5, 10, NKJV). Lemibhalo iqinisa imfundiso yezibhalo yokuthi abafileyo abaphili.

Imfundiso yezibhalo yokungaphili ekufeni Akumele ivuse ukwesaba kumaKristu. Okokuqala, Asikho isihogo esivutha ingunaphakade noma indawo yokulinda kwalabo abafa bengasindisiwe. Okwesibili, kukhona umvuzo omangalisayo olindele labo abafele kuKristu. Akumangalisi ke ukuthi “kokholwayo, ukufa kuyinto encane. . . . KumKristu, ukufa kungukulala, isikhathi sokuthula nobumnyama. Impilo icashiswe noKristu kuNkulunkulu, futhi ‘ngenkathi uKristu, ongukuphila kwethu, eqhamuka, nani niyakuqhamuka Naye enkazimulweni.’ Johane 8:51, 52; Kol. 3:4.”— Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 787.

`Cabanga ngabafele kuKristu. Bavala amehlo abo ekufeni, noma besethuneni iminyaka engu-1500 noma izinyanga ezingu-5, konke kuyafana kubona. Okulandelayo abakwaziyo ngukubuya kukaKristu. Singaphikisana kanjani ke ngokuthi abafileyo basendaweni engcono kuneyabaphilayo na?`
