### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 Korinte 12, Efes. 4:11–13, 1 Korinte 13, 1 Pet. 4:8–11, 1 Kor. 14:27, Amose 3:7.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Jongani uthando, futhi nizondelele iziphiwo zomoya, ikakhulu ukuba niprofethe” (1 Korinte 14:1, ESV).

Njengomzimba womuntu, nebandla lingumzimba owodwa onamalunga amaningi, ilunga ngalinye lenza okwehlukile, imisebenzi, kanye neziphiwo. Uma leziphiwo zisetshenziswa ngothando, leziphiwo zikamoya ziqhakambisa umuzwa wokusebenza ngokubambisana nokwembula isimilo sikaNkulunkulu oziqu zintathu.

Kuleliviki sizobheka incwadi yama- 1 Korinte 12-14 bese sibheka izimfundiso eziphathelene neziphiwo zikamoya. Lengxenye yokufundisa ithe ukuba nkudlwana lapho uPawulu ekhuluma khona ngendlela okulindeleke aziphathe ngayo umuntu ongumKrestu ezintweni eziphathelene nokukholwa (1 Korinte 11-14). Into eyabe ikhathaza kakhulu uPawulu kwakuyinto eyayihlangene nokuhlangana okungahlelekile. Impendulo yakhe kulenkinga kwakungukuthi ibandla elinamalunga enza imisebenzi eyahlukene nokuyiyona enomthelela “ekuphelelisweni komsebenzi wokukhonza, kwakhiwe umzimba kaKristu” (Efesu 4:12). Ngokufinqiwe, uNkulunkulu wanikeza ibandla iziphiwo zikamoya ukuze lizokwazi ukuqhakambisa ubunye ngabantu abehlukene.

Ngokuqinisekile, uPawulu emqondweni wakhe ucabanga ukuthi izinkinga okukhulunywe ngazo ezahlukweni ezine zokuqala KwabaseKorinte bokuqala, lapho kuvela khona impendulo yokungavumelani phakathi kwamalunga ebandla ngokuba nokubumbana kuKristu. Njengamanje usekhulisa lomqondo ngokuthi aveze indlela aqonda ngayo ukusebenza kweziphiwo zikamoya. Ngokusho kukaPawulu, ubumbano kuKristu kanye noMoya yiyona kuphela indlela yokuvikela ukwehlukana.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNcwaba 8._
