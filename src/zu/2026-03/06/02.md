---
title:  Izinhlobo Ezahlukene Zeziphiwo
date:   02/08/2026
---

UPawulu usethulela isihloko esisha ku-1 Korinte 12:1, lapho kunesethulo sokuthi “mayelana.” Abafundi beBhayibheli bayaphikisana ngokuthi kungabe wayekhuluma “ngeziphiwo zikamoya” noma “omoya babantu” ngoba ngolwimi lwesiGreki kukhona kokubili. “Iziphiwo zikamoya” zibekwe kahle ku-1 Korinte 12:4, lapho uPawulu ebeka ngokusobala iziphiwo zikamoya. Ku-1 Korinte 12:2, 3, UPawulu uveza ukuthi isiphiwo sokuqala sikamoya singukuvuma ngokusobala ukuthi uJesu uyiNkosi (1 Korinte 12:2, 3). EThestamenteni elisha, ukuvuma ukuthi uJesu uyiNkosi lokho kusuke kusho ukuthi uKesari akayona iNkosi (Izenzo 17:7; naku Johane 19:12, 15). Lokhu kwakuthathwa njengokuvukela amandla ombuso, ngakho-ke, kwakulindelwe ukuba abulawe.

UJesu benoPawulu bagcizelela ukuthi ukukholwa kuNkulunkulu-noma ngabe ubhekene nokushushiswa kanye nokuthuswe ngokubulawa- kuyisipho sikaMoya. Okuseqinisweni, ukukholwa yisona sibusiso esibalulekile. Akumangalisi ukuthola ukuthi, ukukholwa yikhona okuqalwa ngakho ku-1 Korinte 13:13. Ukuthi ukukholwa kuyisipho sikamoya kuyinkulumo esobala kaPawulu ku-1 Korinte 12:9. Noma kunjalo, ziningi ezinye izipho ezikhona. Iqiniso ngelokuthi uMoya oNgcwele usabalalisa iziphiwo ezahlukene ukuze “yilowo nalowo ngokwakhe njengokuthanda kwakhe” (1 Korinte 12:11) nokungubufakazi bokuthi bonke abantu bayadingeka.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 12:1-6. Kungabe kugcizelelwa ukuthini kulamavesi na?`

Ukuphindwaphindwa “kweziphiwo ezahlukene” kugcizelela ukubakhona kweziphiwo eziningi ezahlukene. Lokhu uPawulu akubiza ngokuthi “iziphiwo zikaMoya” kunezinxa ezintathu ezihlukene: “iziphiwo” noma “izinkonzo” kanye “nemisebenzi.” Yize lamagama esho izinto eziningi ezahlukene, kubalulekile ukuthi sibone kunomehluko omncane phakathi kwawo ngenxa yokuhambisana kwamagama kulamavesi. Okunye futhi kufuneka sinake ukuthi iziphiwo zikamoya zibekelwe ukutshala umoya wobumbano osuselwa ekubumbaneni kwesimilo sikaNkulunkulu (Funda futhi ku-Efesu 4:8-11). Njengoba uMoya uphana ngeziphiwo kubazalwane abakholwayo, nguNkulunkulu obanika amandla okusebenzela uKristu emphakathini wabantu abakholwayo (1 Korinte 12:5, 6). Umzalwane ngamunye unesiphiwo esiqondene naye (1 Korinte 12:11), kodwa zonke leziphiwo kufuneka zibe yinzuzo kumphakathi wabazalwane bebonke.
