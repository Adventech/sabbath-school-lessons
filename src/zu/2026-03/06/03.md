---
title:  Ubumbano Kubantu Abehlukene
date:   03/08/2026
---

Inkulumo yokuba munye eyethulwe ku-1 Korinte 12:4-6 (“kepha nguye lowo Moya” “Kepha yiyo leyo Nkosi” futhi “Kepha nguye lowo Nkulunkulu”) yindlela yokukhuluma elokhu isetshenziswa kusona sonke isahluko ku-1 Korinte 12. Lobu ngubufakazi bokusebenzisa lendlela yokukhuluma efana nokuthi “lowo Moya munye” (1 Korinte 12:11), “njengomzimba munye” (1 Korinte 12:12), “sibe mzimba munye” (1 Korinte 12:12, 13, 20), “ngoMoya munye” (1 Korinte 12:13 [kabili]), “izitho zonke zinakekelane ngokufanayo” (1 Korinte 12:25).

Okuhambisana nomqondo wokubumbana, uPawulu ugqamisa ukwehlukana kwamalunga omzimba kaKristu ngokuthi “nakuba ziziningi, zingumzimba munye” (1 Korinte 12:12, 20), “thina sonke sabhapathizwa” (1 Korinte 12:13), “umzimba awusiso isitho sinye kodwa eziningi” (1 Korinte 12:14), “umzimba wonke” (1 Korinte 12:17), “yileso naleso emzimbeni” (1 Korinte 12:18), “izitho zomzimba” (1 Korinte 12:22, 23), “izitho zonke” (1 Korinte 12:26). Lokhu kugcizelelwa kobudlelwano kanye nokuhlukana kwabantu kukhombisa ukuthi iziphiwo zikamoya zihlose ukuqhakambisa ubumbano kubantu abahlukene.

Lokhu kubumbana yize kungabantu abehlukene kudingeka kukhombise isimilo sikaNkulunkulu. UBaba ungumuntu oyedwa, iNdodana ingomunye umuntu, kanti noMoya oNgcwele ungomunye umuntu. Bobathathu bayiziqu ezahlukene kodwa bayasebenzisana ukukhombisa ibandla kanye namandla okusebenzisana (1 Kor. 12:4–6, Efes. 4:11–13).

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 12:12-31. Kungani ukubalwa kwamalunga omzimba ngezigaba ezahlukene kuyinto efanelekile ukumela ibandla kanye namalunga alo na?`

Umqondo obalulekile ku-1 Korinte 12 wukuthi yize amalunga omzimba ehlukene ngobunjalo bawo (1 Korinte 12:15-20), kodwa wonke ayasebenzisana (1 Korinte 12:21-26). Izinyawo zithembele emehlweni ukuze zibone lapho kufanele zinyathele khona; ngokufanayo, amehlo awakwazi ukuthinta luthi- yizandla ezikwazi ukuthinta. Okunye ngukuthi, ukuqonda ukuthi amanye amalunga abuthakathaka (1 Korinte 12:22) noma abukeka edelelekile kunamanye (1 Korinte 12:23) kwamanye amalunga kuyinto engukucabanga kwabantu, ngoba bonke abantu babalulekile (1 Kor. 12:22).

Ngeshwa, kwakuvamisile kubantu baseKorinte ukuthi babone ezinye iziphiwo zibalulekile kunezinye. Ukuvimbela ukuba abantu baseKorinte bangaphinde benze iphutha elifana naleli, uPawulu ubavula amehlo okuba bafunde ukuthanda ngoba “senginikhombisa indlela eyona iyinhle kakhulu” (1 Korinte 12:31). Ngamanye amazwi, noma ngabe unasiphi isiphiwo, uma sisetshenziswa kahle futhi sisetshenziswa ngothando, siyinto encomekayo kuNkulunkulu.

`Bheka uhlu lweziphiwo ku-1 Korinte 12:8-10, 28; Roma 12:6-8; kanye noFiliphu 4:11. Kungabe siyini isiphiwo sakho na? Ungasisebenzisa kanjani ukuqhakambisa umzimba kaKristu na?`
