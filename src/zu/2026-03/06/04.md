---
title:  “Indlela Engcono Kakhulu”
date:   04/08/2026
---

“Uthando alukho nje ohlwini lweziphiwo eziningi. Luyindlela esetshenziswa yizona zonke iziphiwo ukuze ziphumelele ukufezekisa ezikuhlosile.”—Carl P. Cosaert, “1 Corinthians,” Andrews Bible Commentary: New Testament (Berrien Springs, MI: Andrews University Press, 2022), p. 1643.

`Funda ku-Korinte 13:1-7 kanye 1 Petro 4:8-11. Kungabe uyini umsebenzi wothando uma sibheka ukusebenza kweziphiwo zikamoya na?`

KwabaseKorinte bokuqala isahluko 13 sifundiswa ukuthi kungothando kuphela lapho iziphiwo zikamoya ezingasetshenziswa ngokuyikho. UPawulu uqala lesahluko sika-1 Korinte 13 ngokuthi akhulume ngeziphiwo ezishiwo ku- 1 Korinte 12, lapho ephawula ngokungakwazi ukuveza ukuthi ziqhutshwa ngukuba nothando. Noma ngabe kungulwazi (1 Korinte 12:8) nokukholwa (1 Korinte 12:9), noma ngukukholwa “okungangokuba kungagudluza izintaba” (1 Korinte 13:2), kepha uma kungenalo uthando, akuyinto yalutho (1 Korinte 13:2). Ngaphandle kothando, noma ungakwazi ukukhuluma izilimi (1 Korinte 12:10, 28, 30) kuphenduka kube yinto efana “nethusi elikhencezayo nensimbi encencethayo” (1 Korinte 13:1). Ngokufanayo, noma ungaba nesiphiwo esibalulekile sokuprofetha, nalokho akulutho ngaphandle kothando (1 Kor. 13:2).

Ku-1 Korinte 13:4-7, uPawulu usegxila ekuchazeni uthando ukuthi luyini nokuthi yini olungeyona; ekakhulu, lokhu okwenziwa wuthando nalokhu elingakwenzi. Amagama ayizenzo awasebenzisile ukuchaza abonisa ukuthi uthando alusiwona umuzwa njengento esingakwazi ukuzivocavoca sigcine siyizuzile. Ngakho-ke uPawulu uveza ukuthi uthando (1) luyabekezela; (2) lumnene; (3) luthokozela iqiniso (4) lubekezelela izinto zonke; (5) lukholwa yizinto zonke; (6) luthemba izinto zonke; (7) lukhuthazelela izinto zonke. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, uthando (1) luyabekezela; (2) aluzikhukhumezi; (3) aluzigabisi; (4) aluziphathi ngokungafanele; (5) aluzifuneli okwalo; (6) alucunuki; (7) alunagqubu; (8) aluthokozi ngokungalungile.

Konke lokhu okuyizenzo eziyishumi nanhlanu kusinika iseluleko esiqinile sokuziphatha ngokuyikho uma sisebenzisa iziphiwo. Okubalulekile, lokhu kukhuluma okuveza ubunjalo bangempela bothando kutholakala kukhulunywa ngakho kuzahluko eziphakathi ku- 1 Korinte 12 kuya ku- 1 Korinte 14, nokulapho uPawulu ekhuluma ngezinto ezishayisana neziphiwo zikamoya. Kuliqiniso ukuthi, uthando yiwona khiye wokusebenzisa ngokuyikho iziphiwo zikamoya. Uthando luphinde luqondaniswe nokukholwa kanye nokuthemba, “kepha okukhulu kulokhu luthando” (1 Kor. 13:13, NKJV).

`Kungani uthando lubaluleke kangaka ekukholweni kwethu na? Kungabe iyiphi indlela engcono yokuzuza amava othando lukaNkulunkulu ngaphandle kokuthandaza ngempela sicele ukwembula uthando lwaKhe nakwabanye abantu na?`
