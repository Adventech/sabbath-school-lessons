---
title:  Uthando Lokuzidela
date:   27/07/2026
---

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 9:1-6. Kungabe lamavesi asitshengisa kanjani izibonelo zokuthi ukuzidela ngothando kusho ukuthini na?`

Uma uqala ukubheka, kubonakala engazathi uPawulu uvikela ukuba ngumphostoli kwakhe, ku- 1 Korinte 9, akuhlangene nalokhu obekukade kushiwo esikhathi esingaphambili okuphathelene nolwazi luqophisana nothando. Kodwa akulula ukuba umuntu akhohlwe ukuthi iBhayibheli kalizange liqale ukubhalwa kusetshenziswa izahluko. Lokhu okufundiswa nguPawulu ku-1 Korinte 9 akusiyona into engahlangene nalokhu okukhulunywe ngaphambili. Kuliqiniso ukuthi ku-1 Korinte 9 sitshengiswa izibonelo eziphathekayo zokuba nothando lokuzidela kukaKristu ezinikela ngenxa yabazalwane. Ukuze ezokwazi ukugcizelela ngothando, uPawulu ubeka eceleni amanye amalungelos.

**“Ukudla nokuphuza”** (1 Kor. 9:4, NKJV). Kulelivesi ukudla kanye nokuphuza kumele ukusizwa ngezimali ngendlela ejwayelekile. Njengomphostoli, uPawulu wayenelungelo lokwamukela ukondliwa ngezinto ezithile zivela kubazalwane ayebashumayeza. Abanye abaholi bevangeli ngesikhathi sokushumayela kwakhe babekwenza lokhu. Kodwa yena wayengondliwa ngalutho; okunalokho, wayezondla ngokuthi athunge amatende (Izenzo 18:3).

**“Ukuthatha unkosikazi okholwayo”** (1 Kor. 9:5, NKJV). Umphostoli oshadile wayevumelekile ukuhamba eshumayela ehambisana nonkosikazi wakhe bekhokhelwa yibandla. Isibonelo zabavangeli ababekwenza lokhu kwabe kungu-Priska kanye no-Akwila (Rom. 16:3), no-Anidroniku noJunika (Rom. 16:7). Kodwa uPawulu wayengaganiwe (1 Kor. 7:8). Wayengaganwa futhi azuze kwilungelo lokuhambisana nonkosikazi wakhe, bekwenza lokhu bekhokhelwa yibandla bobabili.

**“Ukuyeka ukusebenza” ukuze uphile** (1 Kor. 9:6, NKJV). UPawulu kanye noBarnaba babenelungelo lokuthola umholo ngomsebenzi wokushumayela ababewenza (1 Kor. 9:4–6). UPawulu wayeziphilisa ngokuthunga amatende (Izenzo 18:3), kodwa asazi ukuthi uBarnaba wayeziphilisa ngokwenzani. Esikwaziyo ngukuthi wayengumuntu ophanayo (Izenzo 4:36, 37), futhi kungakho wayekwazi nokuzondla.

Ku-1 Korinte 9:7-11, uPawulu usenokucabanga okutholakala kuka-1 korinte 9:6 ukuze ezokwazi ukukhombisa ukwenziwa kobulungiswa nakuye beno-Barnaba ukuba nabo baholelwe yibandla imali yokuziphilisa (1 Korinte 9:11, 12). Inkosi uqobo yakhipha umyalelo othi “abamemezela ivangeli baphile ngevangeli” (1 Korinte 9:14; uqhathanise no-Thimothewu 5:18). Noma kunjalo, uPawulu uthi, “Nokho asilisebenzisanga lelolungelo” (1 Korinte 9:12). Ngakho-ke uPawulu uziveza njengomuntu oyisibonelo sokuzidela (1 Korinte 9:1-18) bese ekhuluma ukuthi konke lokhu kuyinzuzo ekushumayeleni ivangeli eKorinte (1 Kor. 9:19–23).

`Kungabe yiziphi izinto, yize kungabonakala kuyizinto ezifanele ukuza kuwe, kodwa ukhetha ukuhlukana nazo ukuze zizokuba nomthelela omkhulu wokufakaza ngoNkulunkulu na?`
