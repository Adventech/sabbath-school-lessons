---
title:  Ukunqoba Ukukhonza Izithombe
date:   30/07/2026
---

Ku-1 Korinte 8:1-3. UPawulu uqhakambisa ukuthi uthando luvikela umuntu ekukhonzeni izithombe. Lokhu kukhuluma kuqala ukuzwakala kuphinde kuxoxwe kabanzi ngakho encwadini ka-1 Korinte 10:23-11:1. Encwadini Ka-1 Korinte 8:3, kukhulunywa ngokuthanda kwethu uNkulunkulu. Uphawula ukuthi, “Akungabi bikho ozifunela okwakhe, kodwa akube yilowo nalowo afunele omunye” (1 Korinte 10:24). Lokhu kungukuthanda abanye abantu.

`Funda ku-Marko 10:17-22 kanye naku-Marku 12:28-31. Kungabe yini efanayo kulamavesi womabili, nokuthi athintana kanjani nesimo esitholakala ku- 1 Korinte 10 na?`

Akwenzayo uPawulu ku-1 Korinte 10 kuyinto efanayo neyenziwa nguJesu ku-Marko 12:28-31; nokuyilapho ahlanganisa khona imithetho emibili emikhulu: wothanda uNkulunkulu ukwedlula konke, uphinde uthande abanye abantu. Endabeni yensizwa esencane eyabe inothile (Marko 10:17-22), uJesu uhlanganisa lezinhlobo ezimbili zothando, ekwenza lokhu ngokuthi akhulume ngalokhu okushiwo ku-Duteronomi 6:4 (funda ku-Marko 10:18) kanye nohlu lwesibili lwemithetho elishumi (funda 10:19). Inkinga yalendoda eyabe icebile ngukuthi yayithanda kakhulu umcebo eyayinawo ukwedlula ukuthanda uNkulunkulu kanye nomakhelwane wakhe (Marko 10:22). Wabona kungcono ukuthanda ukuceba kwalapha emhlabeni ukwedlula umcebo wasezulwini. Wakhetha ukuthanda imali ukwedlula ukuba nothando kubantu abahluphekayo (Marko 10:21). Wayengumuntu onesithixo asikhonzile.

Uma silandela lokhu okwafundiswa nguJesu, uPawulu ukhuluma ngenkambiso yokuthanda uNkulunkulu ukwedlula zonke izinto kanye nokuthanda umakhelwane njengoba uzithanda wena ngokuthi afanise lokhu nento efanele ukwenziwa nakhuluma ngayo ku-1 Korinte 10:27, 28. Lokhu kusho ukuthi noma ngabe izinto zisemthethweni kungenzeka zingabi wusizo noma kube yinto eyakhayo ngoba kungenzeka kubekhona ukuhlukumezeka kukanembeza womunye umuntu (1 Korinte 10:23). Lenkambiso ibekwe kahle lapho kuthiwa “konke kwenzeleni udumo lukaNkulunkulu” (1 Korinte 10:31). Ngokukhuluma athi konke kufuneka kulethe udumo kuNkulunkulu, uPawulu usitshela ukuthi ukukhonza izithombe kungaqhamuka ngezindlela eziningi, njengoba noma ngabe yini eyedlula udumo olufanele lubhekiswe kuNkulunkulu kuphela kuyindlela ethile yokukhonza izithombe (Isa. 42:8).

Amazwi kaPawulu ku-1 Korinte 10:31-11:1 aqhamuka eyisiphetho sezahluko eziku- 1 Korinte 8-10. Ukubeka kucace ukuthi akafuni kuzuze yena kodwa, “ukusiza abaningi, ukuze basindiswe”(1 Korinte 10:33). Yiyona ndlela ayisebenzisa ukulingisa uKristu (1 Korinte 11:1).

`Singakufunda kanjani ukuthanda umakhelwane njengoba sizithanda thina na?`
