---
title:  Isixwayiso Ngokukhonza Izithombe
date:   29/07/2026
---

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 10:5-22. Kungani kudingeka siqhelelane nokukhonza izithombe na?`

Ku-1 Korinte 10:14-22 UPawulu uqhubeka nodaba lokudla okwenzelwe izithombe. Ukwenzela izithombe ukudla kungabonakala kuyinto engajwayelekile emasikweni amaningi abantu esikhathini sanamuhla, kodwa lokhu kwakuyinto eyayivamisile ukwenzeka ezikhathini zakudala zeBhayibhali. Kwakuthi lapho kuhlatshelwa izithixo kumathempeli abahedeni, ingxenye ethile yesilwane esihlatshiwe yayinikezwa umpristsi ophethe umcimbi womhlatshelo, nowayephinde ayithengise lenyama. Kwakwenzeka kutholakale enye yalenyama isithengiswa ezimakethe zomphakathi. Ngenxa yokuthi lenyama yayingabekiswanga ukuze yehluke kwezinye izinyama ezithengiswa ezimakethe, kwakungenzeka namaKrestu azithole esethenga lenyama bengazi ukuthi lenyama yayingumnikelo wezithixo. Umyalezo ovela kumphostoli wawuthi: inyama efana nalena ingathengwa ngokukhululeka nangabantu abangamaKrestu.

Noma kunjalo, kungenzeka ukuthi inyama eyanikelwa ethempeleni njengomhlatshelo ingadliwa ngabantu abangamaKrestu emakhaya abo (1 Korinte 8:1-13), kodwa umkhuba wokujwayela ukuya emathempelini abahedeni bese niba yingxenye yemikhosi abayenzayo kwabe kuyinto engavumelekile kumaKrestu. Indlela okwakwenzeka ngayo kwakucacile: AmaKrestu avunyelwe ukudla ekhaya ukudla okwenzelwe izithombe ngoba into eyithombe esinguNkulunkulu ayikho (1 Korinte 8:4); yize kunjalo, amaKrestu akufanele abe mdibi ndawonye emicimbini nabahedeni ngoba lokhu sekuyefana nokudumisa amadimoni (1 Korinte 10:20, 21). Ukuba yingxenye emcimbini nabahedeni behlabela amadimoni (1 Korinte 10:20), nokuyinto efanayo nokuba yingxenye nabantu abasesidlweni seNkosi okungathi bandawonye noKristu (1 Korinte 10:16).

Ngakho-ke uPawulu uthi, “Ningesiphuze isitsha seNkosi kanye nesitsha samadimoni” (1 Kor. 10:21, NKJV). Njengoba noJesu wathi: “Akakho ongakhonza amakhosi amabili” (Mat. 6:24, NKJV).

UPawulu usifundisa ukuthi sizinikele ngokuphelele kuNkulunkulu. Uzama ukuthi ukukhonza izithombe kubangela ukuthi “Inkosi ibe nolaka” (1 Korinte 10:22). Ukuze lokhu kungenzeki, uPawulu encwadini ka-1 Korinte 8:4-6, usinika umthetho olwisana nokukhonza izithombe ngokuthi akhulume ngalokhu okwashiwo kokuthi: “Yizwa, Israyeli: uJehova uNkulunkulu wethu, uJehova, munye; wothanda uJehova uNkulunkulu wakho ngayo yonke inhliziyo yakho, nangawo wonke umphefumulo wakho, nangawo onke amandla akho” (Duteronomi 6:4, 5). Kulomqondo wokuthi sithande uNkulunkulu ukwedlula zonke izinto, noJesu wathasisela ngokuthi, “Wothanda umakhelwane wakho njengalokhu uzithanda wena” (Marko 12:31, NKJV; buka no Lev. 19:18).

`Isithixo akudingeki kube yisithombe esakhiwe ngetshe. Singakwazi ukwenza isithixo nangezinye izinto. Kungabe yiziphi lezithixo, uma kungukuthi zikhona, okudingeka siqhelelane nazo ekuphileni kwethu na?`
