### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 Korinte 8; Izenzo 15:20; 1 Kor. 9:1–6; 1 Kor. 10:5–22; Dut. 6:4, 5; Marko 12:28–31.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ngakho-ke noma nidla, noma niphuza, noma nenzani, konke kwenzeleni udumo lukaNkulunkulu” (1 Korinte 10:31, NKJV).

AbaseKorinte Bokuqala 8–10 uletha ukuxoxisana mayelana nokwenza ngokobulili ( ku-1 Korinte 5-6) kulethwa isiphetho, ngesikhathi esifanayo kuphinde kwethulwe izimpendulo zikaPawulu emibuzweni ethile eyayibhalwe encwadini evela kubantu baseKorinte (1 Korinte 7:1). Lezimpendulo sizolokhu sikhuluma kabanzi ngazo encwadini yamaKorinte okuqala.

Indlela osekwehluke ngayo ukukhuluma okutholakala ku- 1 Korinte 7 kuveza ukuthi ubufebe obuku (izahluko 5-7) kanye nokukhonzwa kwezithombe okutholakala ku-(izahluko 8-10) kuzihloko ezihambisanayo. Kuliqiniso ukuthi, zilokhu ziqhamuka ndawonye eThestamenteni Elisha (bheka Izenzo 15:20, 29; Izenzo 21:25; 1 Kor. 6:9; Efes. 5:5; Kol. 3:5; Samb. 21:8; Samb. 22:15).

Ngokuvamile, njengoba kuzahluko eziku- 1 Korinte 5-7 uPawulu yilokhu ekhuluma ngezinkinga zobufebe, Kuzahluko ezikhu- 1 Korinte 8-10 into emkhathaza kakhulu yinkinga yokukhonzwa kwezithombe. UPawulu eseveza ukuthi amaKrestu kudingeka akubalekele lokhu kokubili (1 Kor. 6:18, 1 Kor. 10:14).

Ngeviki eledlule sibonile ukuthi ngokuba yithempeli likaMoya oNgcwele (1 Korinte 6:19, 20), wonke umuntu angabubalekela ubufebe. Kuleliviki, sizokwazi ukubona ukuthi singakwazi ukubalekela ukukhonza izithombe ngokuthi “Konke sikwenzele udumo lukaNkulunkulu” (1 Kor. 10:31, NKJV).

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNcwaba 1._
