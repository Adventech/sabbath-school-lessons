Ellen G. White, “A Liberal Church,” pp. 335–345, in The Acts of the Apostles.

“Labo abanezinhliziyo ezigcwele uthando lukaKristu, bazolandela isibonelo kuYe owathi ngenxa yethu waba mpofu, kwathi ebuphofini bakhe benziwa abantu abacebileyo. Imali, nesikhathi, kuyakwazi ukuba nomthelela kuzona zonke iziphiwo abazithole zivela ezandleni zikaNkulunkulu, bazobona ukubaluleka kwazo njengendlela yokwazi ukwandisa umsebenzi wevangeli. Ngakho-ke lalinjalo ibandla elalikhona ekuqaleni; Uma kungukuthi ibandla elikhona manje selibonwa ngokuba ngamandla kaMoya oNgcwele ngokuthi amalunga alo athathe uthando abanalo ezintweni zalomhlaba, futhi bazimisele ukwenza ukuzinikela kwabo bazuze abanye abantu ngokuzwa ivangeli, nokuyothi iqiniso eliyomenyezelwa likwazi ukuba nomthelela onamandla kubantu abalalele.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 71.

“UNkulunkulu akadingi iminikelo yethu. Angeke sikwazi ukumenza acebe ngezipho esimnika zona. Umhlabeleli uthi: ‘Konke kuvela kuYe, futhi ngalokhu okungokwakhe sikunikeze Yena.’ Noma kunjalo, uNkulunkulu uyasivumela ukuba sikhombise ukumbonga ngomusa waKhe asiphe wona ngokuthi ngemizamo yokuzinikela kwethu sikwazi ukudlulisela okufanayo nakwabanye abantu. Ukwenza kanjena yiyona ndlela kuphela esingaba nayo yokukhombisa ukubonga kanye nokuthanda uNkulunkulu. Ayikho enye into asinike yona.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels on Stewardship, pp. 18, 19.

“Sasisikhulu kangakanani isipho sikaNkulunkulu asenzela abantu, futhi angakwenza kanjani uNkulunkulu wethu lokhu! Ngokukhululeka okungedlulwe yilutho Yena wakwazi ukusipha, ukuze ezokwazi ukusindisa izigidi zamadodana abantu abenze bakwazi ukubona inhloso yaKhe baphinde bedlulise uthando lwakhe. Uzokwazi yini wena, ngezipho kanye neminikelo onayo, ukukhombisa umcabango wokuthi akukho okuhle ongamnika khona uNkulunkulu “onikele ngendodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa ukuba yilowo nalowo okholwa yiyo angabhubhi, kodwa abe nokuphla okuphakade na?”—Ellen G. White, in The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, May 15, 1900.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Gxila ku-2 Korinte 8:9. Kungabe isibonelo sikaJesu sibaluleke kangaka uma kukhulunywa ngobunceku na?`

`U-Johane 3:16 ukhuluma ulwimi lwasezulwini lokunikela. Funda ku-Johane 15:13; Efesu 5:2, 25; Galathiya 2:19, 20; kanye 1 Johane 3:16. Kungabe lamavesi kanye nelika Johane 3:16 aphethe muphi umyalezo ofanayo, nokuthi yimuphi umyalezo esingawuthatha kulamavesi na?`

`Ekufundeni kwakho okugxile ku- 2 Korinte 8-9, kungabe yini ozizuzele yona mayelana nokwazi ukupha na?`

`Ngaphezulu kokwazi ukunikela ngendlela ehlelekile, yimiphi eminye iminikelo ongayenza ukuzama ukulingisa isibonelo sokuzinikela kukaJesu na?`
