### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
2 Korinte 8–9; Johane 3:16; Johane 17:5; Luka 9:58; Samb. 13:8; Rom. 12:8; Rom. 15:26, 27.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ngokuba niyawazi umusa weNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu ukuthi, nakuba ecebile, waba mpofu ngenxa yenu, ukuze ngobumpofu bakhe nicebe nina” (2 Korinte 8:9, NKJV).

AbaseKorinte besibili isahluko 8 kanye no-9 zombili lezizahluko zikhombisa ukuthi uPawulu wanika abantu baseKorinte ithuba lokusiza abafowabo nodadewabo ababehlala eJudiya. Lezahluko zikhombisa ukuthi ukupha kuyithuba esilinikwe nguNkulunkulu, ukuze sizokwazi ukulingisa isimilo sikakristu sokuzidela. Ulwimi lwasezulwini lukhulunywa ngokuthi umuntu abe ngophanayo. Bhekisisa lamagama athathekisayo, “Ngokuba uNkulunkulu walithanda izwe kangaka, waze wanikela ngeNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa” (Johane 3:16).

Okunye okubalulekile, ivesi lika-Johane 3:16 liyibeka ngokusobala inhloso kaNkulunkulu yokunikela ngoJesu, ukuze kuthi “yilowo nalowo okholwa yiyo angabhubhi, kodwa abe nokuphila okuphakade.” Ubunceku kanye Nomsebenzi wokuvangela kuyahambisana kulelivesi. Angeke ukwazi ukukwehlukanisa kuhle kwezinhlangothi ezimbili zohlamvu lwemali. Akumangalisi njengoba uPawulu ezibona Yena kanye nasebenza nabo “beyizikhonzi zikaKrestu nabaphathi bezimfihlakalo” (1 Korinte 4:1). Ngakho-ke nathi singabaphathi ngendlela efanayo.

Kuleliviki sizobheka lesisihloko sokuba zinceku zokuphatha kanye nomsebenzi wokuvangela ukuthi konke lokhu kungumfuziselo walokhu okwakwenziwa nguJesu. Okuseqinisweni, ubunceku kanye nomsebenzi wokuvangela yizinto ezimbili ezingahlukaniseki. Ubunceku bubhekelela isimo sezimali ebandleni kanti abantu bayithuluzi elisetshenziswayo ukwenza umsebenzi kaNkulunkulu.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uMandulo 12._
