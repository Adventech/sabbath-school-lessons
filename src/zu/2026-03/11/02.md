---
title:  UJesu Oyisibonelo
date:   06/09/2026
---

Okuqukethwe kwizahluko eziku-2 Korinte 8 no-9 kumayelana noPawulu egqugquzela amalunga aseKorinte ukuthi baqedele ukunikelela amabandla ahluphekile aseJudiya. Kungenzeka ukuthi base beqalile ukwenza lokhu (2 Korinte 8:10, 11; 2 Korinte 9:5; kanye naku-1 Korinte 16:1-4), kodwa izinkinga zobudlelwano phakathi kwabo kanye noPawulu benza izinto zangahamba kahle. Emuva kokusonjululwa kwezinkinga (2 Korinte 1-7), uPawulu usebuyela emuva ukuqedela umsebenzi osuqaliwe (2 Korinte 8-9).

Ekuqaleni, uPawulu wancenga abantu ngokubakhombisa isibonelo sabantu base-Makadoniya (2 Korinte 8:1-7), ukuthi ebumpofwini obukhulu ababekukho lokho kakuzange kubavimbele “ukuba bavame kakhulu ekuphaneni okupheleleyo” (2 Korinte 8:2). Yebo, ubumpofu nokuphana kungahambisana. Noma kunjalo, lento eyathokozelwa kangaka yokuphana kwabantu base-Makadoniya kuyisibonelo sokuphana esenziwa nguJesu ngokuthi azinikele yena ngenxa yobubi bethu (2 Korinte 8:8-15).

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 8:9. Kungabe lelivesi lisitshela ukuthini ngokuba yisibonelo kukaJesu na?`

Okushiwo nguPawulu ku-2 Korinte 8:9 kungenye yezinto ezimangazayo, nezinamandla, ziphinde zibe yingxenye yeBhayibheli lonke. UPawulu usitshela ngendaba yokushumayela kukaJesu, kodwa ekwenza lokhu ngokusebenzisa amazwi ayinzuzo enkulu. Kuningi okushiwoyo lapha okuphathelene nevangeli. Lapha kukhulunywa ngendaba yokusindiswa, kodwa kuvela kuphela evesini lokuqala.

Okuthathekisa kakhulu ngukuthi, lendaba ixoxwa ngokusebenzisa ulwimi lwezomnotho. Yebo uJesu wayecebile. Ngokuba ngumuntu ocebile kushiwo isimo sokuba khona kwaKhe esesezulwini (Johane 17:5). Wakhetha ukuba mpofu ngokudela inkazimulo yasezulwini wakhetha ukuza kulomhlaba wokuba lusizi. Waba ngumuntu ohluphekile impela (Luka 9:58). Yize wayelingana noNkulunkulu, “kepha wazidela ethabatha isimo senceku, enziwe ngomfanekiso wabantu” (Filiphi 2:7). “wathi efunyenwe enjengomuntu ngesimilo wazithobisa, elalela kwaze kwaba sekufeni, yebo, ekufeni kwesiphambano” (Filiphi 2 :8).

UJesu wanikela ngokuphila kwaKhe ukuze thina sikwazi ukuphila ingunaphakade kunye naYe. Ukuzinikela kwakhe kwabe kunenhloso: yokusindiswa kwethu. Ubunceku kanye nomsebenzi wokuvangela kuyahambisana. KwabaseKorinte Besibili izahluko 8 kuya 9 sitshelwa ngendaba yokunikela okuthile ngemali, kodwa lendaba isuselwa ekwenzeni kukaJesu. Kuleliviki sizobheka inkambiso yeBhayibheli uma kwenziwa iminikelo yokupha esuselwa ekuzinikeleni kukaKristu.

`Ake sigxile ekuzalweni, nesempilweni, nasekufeni, kanye nasekuvusweni kukaJesu. Uma usubona ukuthi konke lokhu kwenzelwa wena ukuze ube nethemba lento engale kokuphila okubuhlungu kwalapha emhlabeni, ngabe kufanele wenzeni ngalokhu na?`
