---
title:  Isimo Sokucabanga
date:   09/09/2026
---

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 8:1-5. Kungabe yiziphi izizathu ezabangela ukuba abantu baseMakadoniya bathande ukunikela ngokukhululeka okungaka na?`

Ukuba nomqondo olungile wabantu baseMakedoniya singakubona ngezindlela ezihlukene.

Okokuqala, **bapha ngenjabulo enkulu** (2 Kor. 8:2). UPawulu uthi abantu baseMakedoniya uthi “ukuthokoza kwabo okuvamileyo nobumpofu babo obukhulu kwenza ukuba bavame kakhulu ekuphaneni okupheleleyo” (2 Korinte 8:2). Kamuva wakhuluma ukuthi, “uNkulunkulu uyamthanda onikelayo ethokoza” (2 Korinte 9:7). Igama lokunikela “ngentokozo” livela kuphela kulendawo uma ufunda lonke iThestamente Elisha. Igama elifana naleli liyavela kwezinye izindawo: “owabayo, enze ngobuqotho, oholayo, ngenkuthalo, ohawukelayo, ngentokozo” (Roma 12:8). Lamagama acishe afane futhi asho into eyodwa kwesinye isikhathi, aphinde aqhamuke nakwezinye izincwadi ezikhuluma ngeBhayibheli, zikusho ngomqondo wokwethaba nokujabula. Evesini eliKu-2 Korinte 9:7, ukuba ngumuntu ophana ngentokozo kusho ukupha ngaphandle kokungabaza.

Okwesibili, **banikela ngokupheleleyo** (2 Korinte 8:2). Ngaphambi kokuba uPawulu akhulume ngokupha okwenziwa ngabantu baseMakadoniya, waqala ngokuphawula ngezinga “elikhulu lobumpofu” abakulona. Igama lokupha “ngokuphelele” livela kabili kuzona zombili izahluko eziku- 2 Korinte 8 kanye no-9. uPawulu uthi, “ukuze ezintweni zonke nicetshiswe kukho ukuphana konke” (2 Korinte 9:11), okusho ukuthi siphiwe ukuze nathi sizokwazi ukupha. Kwenye indawo elandelayo, ukhuluma ukuthi “ngenxa yokulalela kokuvuma kwenu” (2 Korinte 9:13). Lelivesi lichaza ukuthi ngokuvuma ukunikela sisuke sivuma ivangeli likaKristu.

Okwesithathu, **banikela “njengamandla abo”** (2 Korinte 8:3). Lokhu kusho ukuthi banikela ngokuzithandela. Lokhu kuba yinto enhle kakhulu uma kungukuthi umuntu uyasibona isidingo sokuba anikele kulokhu okuningi akutholile, ngoba lakho ababenakho kwabe kukuncane kakhulu. UPawulu usebenzisa umqondo ofanayo ukuchaza ngokuzinikela kukaThithu ukuvakashela abantu baseKorinte. Uthi uThithu waziyela ngokuthanda kwakhe eKorinte (2 Korinte 8:17).

Okwesine, **banikela ngomqondo wokuthi ukunikela kwabe kuyithuba abalinikeziwe** (2 Korinte 8:4). Isimo sokucabanga kwakuyinto eyabe izwakala ezicelweni zabantu baseMakadoniya neyenza bakwazi ukuqoqa umnikelo. “basinxusa kakhulu becela umusa wokuhlanganyela ekukhonzeni abangcwele” (2 Korinte 8:4).

Okokugcina, **baba yingxenye ekunikeleni njengokukhombisa ukuzinikela ngokuphelele** (2 Korinte 8:5). UPawulu uthi, “bazinikela bona uqobo kuqala eNkosini nakithi futhi ngentando kaNkulunkulu” (2 Korinte 8:5). Ukuzinikela komuntu eNkosini kuletha umphumela wokwazi ukuzinikela nakwabanye abantu. Abantu baseMakedoniya bakhulisa umsebenzi wabo wokuvangela kwaze kwedlula ukusiza kwabo ngezimali. Ukwenza ngalendlela kungukupha kuphinde kube ngukuzinikela okungagcini ngokuletha imali kuphela.
