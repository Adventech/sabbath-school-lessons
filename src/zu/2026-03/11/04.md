date:   08/09/2026

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 9:7. Kungabe lelivesi lithini mayelana nokupha na?`

Isinqumo sikaNkulunkulu sokusindisa umhlaba kuyinto eyaqala ukubakhona nangaphambi kokuba umhlaba uwele esonweni. Ukufika kukaJesu ukuzosifela kwabe kuyingxenye yaloluhlelo (Isambulo 13:8). UNkulunkulu akazange azumeke ukwenza lokhu. Wayekuhlelile ukuzinikela ngokuthi anikele ngoJesu. Ezahlukweni eziku -2 Korinte 8 kanye no-9, ukuhlela yilona hlelo olubalulekile lwenkambiso yevangeli oluphathelene nesenzo sokunikela. Lokhu sikubona ngezindlela ezimbili:

Eyokuqala, **ukuhlela kufaka izinqumo zangaphambilini.** UPawulu ukhuluma ukuthi “yilowo nalowo anikele njengalokho azikhethele khona enhliziyweni” (2 Korinte 9:7). Igama elithi “ukuzikhethela” kuyigama eliyisenzo. Lesi senzo sisho ukwenza umcimbi othile. Isiqalo sesenzo sikhomba into ebikade ihleliwe “ngaphambili” kodwa kulelivesi kusho “ukuthatha isinqumo.” Ngakho-ke lokhu kuchaza ngesinqumo esathathwa ngaphambili. Okunye, ngokuqala umusho ngokuthi “yilowo nalowo,” kulokhu uPawulu uzama ukuthi imali ezonikelwa angeke kube yimali elinganayo kubantu bonke. Ayekushoyo lapha ngukuthi umuntu wonikela ngalokho asenqume ukukukhipha, kodwa kuyofuneka bakwenze lokhu ngokucophelela okukhulu. Kufuneka banikele ngalokhu abakholwa ngukuthi kufanele kube yimali engakanani abanganikela ngayo.

Eyesibili, **ukuhlela kufaka nesimiso sokulinganisa.** UPawulu ubika ukuthi abantu baseMakadoniya “banikela njengamandla abo” (2 Korinte 8:3). Okunye okulandelayo, usebenzisa inqubo efanayo yokupha ngokwamandla omuntu, nakhetha ukuyisebenzisa nakubantu abangamakrestu. Wabagqugquzela ukuba baqedele lokho abazinikela ukukwenza, wabavuselela ekubeni baqedele uhlelo abaluqalile ngokuthi basebenzise konke abanakho (2 Korinte 8:11). Usephetha lomcabango ngokuthi umnikelo unikelwe “njengalokho umuntu enakho, kungenjengalokho angenakho” (2 Korinte 8:12). Kanti ngakolunye uhlangothi, iBhayibheli liveza umqondo wokunikela njengalokho umuntu anakho ngokusebenzisa umgomo wokweshumi, okungukuthi, okuyishumi ekhulwini (10%), futhi kuseyinto efanayo nangesikhathi sokuletha umnikelo. “Yilowo nalowo anikele njengalokho azikhethele khona enhliziyweni” (2 Korinte 9:7) ngokuthi asebenzise umgomo wokunikela ngangokuzuza komuntu. Ngamanye amazwi, umuntu ngamunye uyazikhethela ukuthi uzonikela kangakanani okuya ngokuthi uzuze kangakanani bese ekhetha ukuthi uzonikela ngamalini. Umuntu ngamunye kufuneka anikele ngangokuba enakho. Konke lokhu angeke kwenzeka ngaphandle kokuthi kubekhona ukuhlela.

`Kungabe uthembeke kangakanani kokweshumi nasekunikeleni kwakho, noma ngabe umpofu kangakanani noma uceba kangakanani na? Yiziphi izaba ozibekayo okuzokwenza unganikeli kanti wena uyazi ukuthi ungakwazi ukwenza kangcono kunalokhu okwenzayo na?`
