---
title:  Ubunye
date:   10/09/2026
---

Sibonile ukuthi uPawulu ubelokhu egqugquzela ubumbano kumalunga ebandla laseKorinte ukuba bazibandakanye ekuqoqeni umnikelo wokusiza amabandla ahluphekile aseJudiya. Phakathi kokunye ayekuhlosile kwabe kungukufaka umoya wokuba munye. Ufuna bazibandakanye, babe yingxenye yomsebenzi wokuvangela. Wayefuna ukukhombisa amabandla abantu bezizwe ukuthi nabo babe yingxenye yomndeni kaNkulunkulu ngendlela efanayo nokuba yingxenya yabantu abakholwayo abangamaJuda aseJerusalema. Lokhu kusho ukuthi, lababantu obekungabantu ababekade bengezwani nabo, manje sebengabazalwane ndawonye kunye nabo njengensali kaNkulunkulu yabantu abasanda kuphenduka. UPawulu wayefisa ukubona umndeni wonke wamaKrestu, okwabe kungamaJuda kanye nabezizwe, bebumbene ngendlela enamandla ezokuba ngubufakazi nasebandleni ezizukulwaneni ezisazolandela.

UThithu kanye nabazalwane ababili adumisa nabo banikwa umsebenzi wokuphatha iminikelo. UNkulunkulu wabeka enhliziyweni kaThithu umsebenzi wokunakekela ibandla (2 Korinte 8:16). Esebenzisa amabandla uNkulunkulu waphinde wakhetha abanye abazalwane ababili (2 Korinte 8:18-23). Labazalwane babebizwa ngokuthi “bayizithunywa zamabandla, bayinkazimulo kaKristu” (2 Korinte 8:23). Noma ngabe “bayinkazimulo kaKristu” njengoba bechazwa labazalwane abathembekile njengoba babechazwa ngamabandla uqobo lwawo, konke lokho akusho lutho. Ukukhipha umnikelo kwabe kuyiyonanto ekhombisa ukuthembeka kuKristu, oyinhloko yebandla (Efesu 4:15).

KwabaseKorinte Besibili izahluko 8-9 zikhombisa ukuthi iminikelo kufuneka inikezwe abantu abakhethwe nguNkulunkulu okungabantu abazobe bekhethwe ngamabandla. Ukukhuluma okuthi “onke amabandla” (2 Korinte 8:18), “ukhethiwe futhi yiwo amabandla”(2 Korinte 19), kuphinde kuthiwe “bayizithunywa zamabandla” (2 Korinte 8:23) konke lokhu kungenzeka ukuthi kuhlongoza ukuthi: Ngakho- ke ukuzincoma okulandelayo akumangalisi uma kuthi; “Ngakho manibokalise kubo phambi kwamabandla ubufakazi bothando lwenu” (2 Korinte 8:24).

Ukuletha umnikelo ebandleni kuyithuluzi elukhethwe nguNkulunkulu lapha emhlabeni- neliphakamisa ukuba munye futhi ngesikhathi esifanayo, kungumphumela wokukhombisa ukubumbana (2 Korinte 8:13, 14). Imali ingadala ukuba abantu babe nokuzwana okukhulu. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, uma abantu bengaboni ngaso linye ekuletheni inkazimulo kuNkulunkulu, imali ingaphinde idale uqhekeko.

`Kungabe incwadi yamaRoma 15:26, 27 iveza kanjani isifiso sokuba nobumbano lapha na?`

Ekugcineni, uPawulu usekhetha ukuqoqwa komnikelo njengenkonzo noma umsebenzi wokuvangela, njengokwenza komusa, nanjengesibusiso, nanjengokwenza kokudumisa, futhi kuphinde kube yinto yobudlelwano. Kungabe konke lokhu kungumphumela wokunikela na? Ake ucabange ngalokhu.

`Kungabe indlela esinikela ngayo emabandleni kanye nasemsebenzini wokuvangela emazweni aphesheya, izikhathi eziningi okungamazwe aqhele kakhulu, kungabe lokhu kusizaphi ukuletha ubumbano ebandleni elisemhlabeni wonke na?`
