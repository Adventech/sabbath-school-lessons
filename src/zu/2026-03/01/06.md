---
title:  Izincwadi ZikaPawulu Ezibhekiswe KwabaseKorinte
date:   02/07/2026
---

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 1:11-13; 1 Korinte 4:14; 1 Korinte 5:11; 1 Korinte 7:1; kanye 1 Korinte 14:37, 40. Funda futhi naku-2 Korinte 1:12, 2 Korinte 2:9; 2 Korinte 11:3; kanye 2 Korinte 13:10. Kungabe lamavesi asisiza kanjani ukuba siqonde ukuthi kungabe uPawulu wazibhalelani lezincwadi ebhalela abase-Korinte na?`

UPawule wabe ese-Efesu ngesikhathi ebhala incwadi yabaseKorinte bokuqala (1 Korinte 16:5-9). Umndeni ka-Klowe waya kuye uphethe umbiko wokuthi izinto kazihambi kahle emuva eKorinte (1 Korinte 1:11). Ezahlukweni zabase- 1 Korinte 1 – 6, uPawule uxazulula inkinga efike no-Klowe. Inkinga yayifaka phakathi ukwehlukana, ukungaziphathi kahle kwezocansi, nokumangalelana, kanye nokuthengisa ngomzimba. UPawule waphinde wathola incwadi eyabe iqukethe imibuzo ethile (1 Korinte 7:1). Impendulo yakhe itholakala igcwele kuzahluko eziqala kusahluko 7 kuya phambili. Imibuzo yayimayelana nokushada, kanye nokuhlukanisa umshado, nokungabi sothandweni kanye nokungashadi, nokudla ukudla okulungiselwe izithixo, nangendlela yokuziphatha uma kudunyiswa, nokusetshenzisweni kweziphiwo zikamoya, kanye nokungaqondi kahle ngokuvuka kwabafileyo. Ibandla lase-Korinte lalihlupha kakhulu futhi lingavuthiwe kahle. Kungenzeka ukuthi ibandla lakho linezinkinga eziningi. Noma kunjalo, kodwa ibandla laseKorinte lalinezinkinga ezinkulu kakhulu.

Incwadi yokuqala kaPawule ebhekiswe eKorinte kuyincwadi ebalulekile nangalesisikhathi esiphila kusona nathi. Okusempeleni, nathi ngakolunye uhlangothi kungabe sibhekene nesimo esifanayo ezinkonzweni zethu esikuzo namuhla na? Lencwadi isho okukhulu nakithi namuhla. “Lencwadi ingenye yezincwadi ezinothile, nekwazi ukufundisa kakhulu, iphinde ikhulume ngokunamandla kunezinye izincwadi azibhalile.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 301.

Kungenzeka ukuthi kukhona ezinye izincwadi ezimbili noma ezintathu azibhalile azibhekisa kwabase-Korinte (qhathanisa lokhu no-2 Korinte 10:9). Wake wabhala enye incwadi ngaphambili encwadini yama-1 korinte (1 Korinte 5:9), kodwa yalahleka leyoncwadi. Ngaphambi kokubhala incwedi ye- 2 Korinte, wabhala enye incwadi abantu abakhuthalele ukufunda abayibiza ngokuthi “yincwadi enokulwa” (2 Korinte 2:3, 4, 9; 2 Korinte 7:8), nayo futhi lencwadi yalahleka. Abanye bacabanga ukuthi lokhu okusencwadini yamaKorinte Okuqala, noma lencwadi yaphinde yagcinwa esahlukweni sama-Korinte Esibili.

Uma ufunda usuka kuma-Korinte Esibili, siphawula ukuthi amalunga ebandla lase-Korinte ayesengenywe yimikhuba yesiko elalenziwa endaweni. Babekubona lokhu njengento yokuziqhathanisa nabanye abantu, nokuba nombango wamandla, kanye nokubanga umcebo, nokuyizinto ezingaba yinselelo nasemabandleni esikuwona namuhla. Kungakho uPawulu wazama ukwenza usiko oluzogxila kuKristu, nokuyindlela yokubheka umhlaba ngendlela yokukholwa yivangeli. Lokhu kuyinto esemqoka nakithi ukuba sibheke okwenzeka emhlabeni ngeso lokukholwa yivangeli.

`Phinda ufunde ku-2 Korinte 2:4. Kungabe lokhu kusitshela ukuthini thina mayelana nokuthi uPawulu wayenakekela kanjani abanye abantu na? Uma siqhathanisa nathi, kungabe ziphole kangakanani izinhliziyo zethu kwabanye abantu na?`
