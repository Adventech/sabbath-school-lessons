Ellen G. White, “Corinth,” pp. 243–254, ku-The Acts of the Apostles.

“Mayelana nokushumayela ivangeli eKorinte, umphostoli uPawulu walandela indlela eyehlukile kunaleyo ayehlose ukuyenza e-Athene. . . . Wanquma ukungangeni ezindabeni lapho kuphikiswana noma kuxoxiswana, futhi “ezenza umuntu ongazi luthu oluphathelene nabase-Korinte ‘ehlenga uJesu Kristu, nokunguye owabethelwa, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 244.

“UPawulu waba nempumelelo enkulu,” kodwa wagcwala ukungabaza ekwakheni indlu yokukhonzela, eyakhiwa ngezinto ezitholakale kulendawo. Wayebona iKorinte kuyindawo okunemibuzo eminingi ngendlela ababenza ngayo uma kusetshenzwa, futhi ezimisele ukuyishiya lendawo. . . .

“Ngesikhathi esacabanga ukushiya lelidolobha aye kwezinye izindawo ezikhombisa ukubangcono, . . . uNkulunkulu wabonakala kuyena embonweni ebusuku wathi “Ungesabi, ukukhuluma, . . . ngokuba ngeneningi labantu kulelidolobha.’ Pawulu, waba nakuqonda ukuthi lona ngumyalelo omtshela ukuthi angasuki e-Korinte futhi waba nesiqiniseko sokuthi uNkulunkulu uzokhulisa imbewu esitshaliwe. . . . Ibandla elikhulu labakhona ngaphansi kophawu lukaJesu Kristu.”—Ellen G. White, Sketches from the Life of Paul, pp. 106, 107.

“Kunemibiko ekhuluma ukuthi uPawulu wahlala unyaka nezinyanga eziyisithupha e-Korinte. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, ukusebenza kwakhe kwakungagxilile kulelidolobha kuphela. . . . IKorinte walenza ikomkhulu lakhe. . . . Kunamabandla ayidlanzana aqubuka. . . . Ukungabibikho kuka-Pawulu kulamabandla awanakekelayo ayethola ukunakekelwa ngokuthi kubekhona ukuxhumana okunamandla nokulusizo, nokuxhumana okwakuvela ezwini likaNkulunkulu. . . . Lemibiko yayifundwa emabandleni.”—Ellen G. White, Sketches from the Life of Paul, p. 109.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`UPawulu wayenesiqiniseko sokuthi ungumphostoli kaJesu, nokuthi ukubizwa kwakhe kwabe kuvela kuNkulunkulu. Kungani kubalulekile ukuba sazi ukuthi singobani nokuthi sibizelwe ukwenzani na?`

`Okwesikhashana, uPawulu wazizwa ephelelwa ngamandla emsebenzini wakhe wokushumayela eKorinte nokwenza acabange ukushiya lelidolobha. Kungabe yini eyamenza washintsha ukucabanga kwakhe na? Kungabe lokhu kungasisiza kanjani uma sizizwa siphelelwa ngamandla emsebenzini wokushumayela na? Kungabe kungenzeka kube khona isidingo, yize, ngesinye isikhathi kufuneka sivume na?`

`Amalunga ebandla laseKorinte izikhathi eziningi ayevame ukwamukela isimo somthelela wesiko-mpilo walapho behlala khona. Isimo esinjena siyinto ekhona nakithina namuhla. Singenza kanjani ukuba sibe sezweni (Johane 17:11, 15) siphinde singathikamezwa ngumthelela wakuba “konke okusezweni, inkanuko yenyama, nenkanuko yamehlo, nokuzigabisa ngalokhu kuphila” (1 Johane 2:16) na? Yiziphi ezinye izindlela ibandla lethu elithola ngazo imithelela engalungile elethwa usiko-mpilo olusizungezile na?`
