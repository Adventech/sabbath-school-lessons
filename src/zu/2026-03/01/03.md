---
title:  Ukusuka E-Athene Kuyiwa E-Korinte
date:   29/06/2026
---

`Funda ku-Izenzo 17:16-34. Wayekuphi uPawule ngaphambi kokuba aye e-Korinte, futhi wafika wenzani uma esefikile lapho na?`

Izenzo 17:16-34 zikhuluma ngoPawulu eshumayela e-Athene ngaphambi kokuba aphikelele e-Korinte. Mhlawumbe, kwakungenzeka ukuthi wayengakuhlelile ukuvakashela e-Athene ngalesisikhathi, kodwa wazibona eseya khona ngenxa yokusizwa ngabangani bakhe ngoba kwabakhona ukulwisana naye ahlangabezana nakho ese-Bereya (Izenzo 17:13-15).

Labo ababehamba noPawulu ukuya e-Athene babuyela e-Bereya njengokuyaleza kuka-Thimothi no-Sila ukuba bahlanganyele kunye naye ngokushesha okukhulu (Izenzo 17:15). Izenzo 17:16-34 lapha kukhulunywa ngalokhu uPawulu ayekwenza ngesikhathi esabalindile. Wayeshumayela ivangeli likaJesu emasinagogeni, nasezimakethe, kanye nase-Areyophagu. Wayengakwazi ukuthula angasho lutho ngoJesu, kungakho athatha lonke ithuba ayenalo ukukushumayela ivangeli.

`Funda ku-Izenzo 18:1-11. Kungabe wenzani uPawulu ngesikhathi efika eKorinte kanye nangaso sonke isikhathi sokuhlala kwakhe kulelidolobha na`

UPawule waya eKorinte ngesikhathi esehamba okwesibili ukuyoshumayela. ULuka usitshela ukuthi uPawulu wahlala kulendawo isikhathi esingangonyaka nezinyanga eziyisithupha.

Njengokujwayelekile, uPawulu uqalisa ukushumayela ivangeli emasinagogeni (Izenzo 18:4-6). Izenzo 17:1, 2 kuthiwa lokhu ayekwenza kwabe kungumkhuba wakhe. Walandela umkhuba wokuthi ashumayele “kumaJuda kuqala” (Roma 1:16, Izenzo 13:46), enza njengoba uJesu wayeyalile abafundi baKhe (funda Izenzo 1:8).

Kuthe ngasekugcineni wahlangana no-Sila kanye no-Thimothewu e-Korinte “uPawulu wakhathalela izwi, wafakaza kubaJuda ukuthi uJesu unguKristu” (Izenzo 18:5). Ngesikhathi esahlezi e-Korinte, wayelokhu ematasa “efundisa izwi likaNkulunkulu phakathi kwabo” (Izenzo 8:11). Kukulomqondo lapho akhuluma khona amagama adume ngawo athi: “Ngokuba nganquma ukuthi angiyikwazi okunye phakathi kwenu, kuphela uJesu Kristu, lowo obethelwe esiphambanweni” (1 Korinte 2:2).

`Yikuphi esingakufunda emsebenzi wokushumayela kukaPawulu e-Athene kanye nase-Korinte mayelana nokusebenzisa lonke ithuba umuntu alitholayo ukushumayela ivangeli na? Ake ucabange ngamathuba owatholayo okwabelana nabanye abantu ngoJesu nokuthi uwasebenzisa kanjani.`
