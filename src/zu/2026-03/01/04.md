---
title:  Idolobha Lase-Korinte
date:   30/06/2026
---

`Funda ku-Izenzo 18:1-3, 1 Korinte 5:9-11, kanye 1 Korinte 8:4. Yikuphi esingafunda mayelana nezomnotho, nendlela yokuphatha, kanye nempilo yokukholwa kubantu base-Korinte na?`

I-Korinte kwabe kuyindawo ebalulekile emhlabeni esikhathini sasendulo, lendawo yabe yaziwa ngokuba nempumelelo kwezomnotho. Lelidolobha labhujiswa ngamaRoma ngonyaka ka-146 B.C. laphinde lakhiwa kabusha ngu julius Ceasar , elivuselela njengendawo eyingxenye yaseRoma, ekwenza lokhu ngonyaka ka-44 B.C. Kulendawo yase-Korinte etholakala eRoma osekukhulunywa ngayo eThestamenteni eLisha. Ngesikhathi sokuphila kuka-Pawulu, iKorinte kwabe kuyindawo elwisana ne-Athene futhi kukuningi okuhle ababekade bebadlula ngakho. I-Korinte labe linamachweba amabili abalulekile nokuyiwona ayesetshenziswa kakhulu ukuhwebelana futhi enza kubelula ukukhulisa umnotho walendawo.

Okuseqinisweni, uPawule wakhetha lendawo ngoba yabe ibalulekile futhi kungcono ukusebenza kuyo ngoba yayisendaweni ekahle. “Kwavuleleka ithuba lokuba kwandiswe ivangeli. Uma lingakwazi ukugxila e-Korinte, lizokwazi ukwanda kalula lifinyelele nakwezinye izindawo emhlabeni.”—Ellen G. White, Sketches From the Life of Paul, p. 99.

Okunye futhi, ukuchuma kwezomnotho endaweni yase-Korinte kuzokwenza kubelula ku-Pawule ukuba akwazi ukuzondla ngokuthi akhiqize amathende amaningi azowathengisa elokhu ematasa eshumayela ivangeli kulelidolobha elikhulukazi. (Izenzo 18:2, 3). Okusobala ngukuthi, umsebenzsi wokushumayela edolobheni elikhulu nelibonakala ligcwele umnotho akusilona idolobha elingenazo izinselelo. I-Korinte yayidume ngokudunyiswa konkulunkulu abazinhlobo eziningi bekwenza lokhu ngokungenamahloni. (1 Korinte 8:5), lokhu kubonakala ngokuba nezakhiwo zalezithixo ezifana no-Apollo, no-Athena, no-Aphrodite, baphinde badumise nezithixo zase-Gibhithe ezifana no-Sarapis kanye no-Isis.

Okunye okwakuthasisela kulokhu kudideka kokudumisa, i-Korinte yayiphinde yaziwe ngokuhlobonga ngokungenamahloni. U-Strabo, owabe engumuntu owazi kabanzi ngezifundo zokusekelwa kwezwe (geographer) futhi azi kabanzi ngezomlando, waphawula ngokubakhona kwenkulungwane yabantu abangcwele abathengisa ngomzimba bekwenza lokhu ngoba bedumisa isithixo sowesifazane u-Aphrodite ethempelini lase-Korinte. Yize abantu abaningi abafunda imibhalo bekusola lokhu futhi baphinde baxhumanise lokhu kukhuluma njengemfundiso eyize nelwisana nendawo yase- Korinte, Ukudumisa ngokuphinga kwabe kuyinto evamile esikhathini sasendulo. Ukungabi nasimilo kwabe kuyinto eyinkinga endaweni yase-Korinte, nokuyinto eyabe ifana nalokhu okwenzeka nakwezinye izindawo. Ukukhonza izithixo kanye nokuba nesimilo esibi kwabe kuyindlela yokuphila yansukuzonke, futhi lokhu kuziphatha kabi okudabukisayo kuyinto echazwe kabanzi esahlukweni sokuqala nesahluko sesibili KwabaseKorinte.

`Ekushumayeleni kwakhe endaweni yase-Korinte, uPawulu wayebhekena nenkinga yokukhonzwa kwezithixo kanye nongabi nasimili komphakathi. Kungabe yiziphi izinselelo ezitholakala kusikompilo lwanamuhla nokwenza kube lukhuni ukushumayela ivangeli na? Singakunqoba kanjani lokhu na? Kungabe ungakanani umehluko, uma ukhona, kulokho okwakukhona e-Korinte kanye nalokhu okusemadolobheni esikuwo manje na?`
