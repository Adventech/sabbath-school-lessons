`Funda Izenzo 18:4-8. Kungabe waba yini umphumela wokushumayela kuka-Pawulu na?`

Ukusebenza kukaPawule phakathi kwamaJuda ase-Korinte akuzange kube ngumsebenzi onezithelo ezinhle njengoba ayefisa. Kwadingeka abhekane nezimo ezazilwisana naye waphinde wazondwa. IBhayibheli lithi “kwathi benqaba, behlambalaza”(Izenzo 18:6). Uma into ithola (“ukuhlambalazwa”) kubantu kusho “Ukuphoxeka” noma “Ukukhuluma kabi ngomuntu.” Ngamanye amazwi, babeqonde ukupenda kabi udumo lukaPawule bese bemvimbela ukuba angatholi ukuphumelela emizamweni yakhe yokushumayela.

Ngenhlanhla, ukusebenza kukaPawule esinagogeni lase-Korinte akubanga yinto eyize. Okuseqinisweni, uNkulunkulu nguyena owaqondisa ukuvangela kwakhe. Wathembisa ngokuthi: “Izwi lami eliphuma emlonyeni wami: aliyikubuyela ze kimi” (Isaya 55:11). Abanye abaJuda babengalindele ukuthi u-Krispu, owabe engumphathi wesinagoge, endawonye nomndeni wakhe, bamukela uJesu njengo-Mesiya baphinde baBhapathizwa (Izenzo 18:8). Akugcinanga ngabo bodwa, kodwa “nabaningi baseKorinte balalela, bakholwa, babhapathizwa” (Izenzo 18:8), kungenzeka ukuthi kwaba nomthelela lokhu ngenxa yokuphenduka kuka-Krispu.

`Funda ku-Izenzo 18:9, 10. Yikuphi esingakuzuza mayelana nemizwa kaPawulu ngesikhathi ebhekene nezinselelo ezithile e-Korinte na? Kungabe uNkulunkulu wagqugquzela kanjani isisebenzi sakhe na?`

Emuva kokuba esesukile esinagogeni uPawule, wazuza amava amenza waba nomfutho omkhulu. UKristu uqobo lwaKhe waziveza phambi kwakhe ebusuku embonweni, wakhuluma lamazwi: “Ungesabi, ngokuba mina nginawe” (Isaya 41:10). Ngempela, uPawule uyavuma ukuthi ngesikhathi ese-Korinte “Mina ngangikini ngobuthakathaka, nangokwesaba, nangokuthuthumela okukhulu” (1 KwabaseKorinte 2:3). Kwadingeka asuke endaweni yase-Bereya aphikelele e-Athene ngenxa yokuphikiswa okukhulu ayebhekene nakho. Kubonakala engazathi wakucabanga futhi ukusuka kulendawo yase-Korinte ngenxa yesizathu esifanayo: sokuphikiswa kakhulu. Kodwa kulokhu akuzange kwenzeke kanjalo. UJesu wathi kuye: “Ngokuba mina nginawe, akakho ozakukusukela, akulimaze, ngokuba nginabantu abaningi kulomuzi” (Izenzo 18:10). UPawulu wabe eyithuluzi lokuletha izindaba zokusindiswa kulabantu abakulendawo.

`Funda ku-Isaya 41:10. Kungabe yiziphi izithembiso esizinikiwe kulelivesi na? Kungani lokhu kungathinta indlela ophila ngayo yansuku zonke na?`
