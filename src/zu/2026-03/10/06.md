---
title:  Induduzo NeNjabulo
date:   03/09/2026
---

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 7. Kungabe wazizwa kanjani uPawulu emva kokuzwa ukuthi abazalwane baseKorinte sebephendukile na?`

Kungabe lungakanani uthando olugobhoza kumazwi athi “nisezinhliziyweni zethu” (2 Korinte 7:3; bheka futhi naku-2 Korinte 6:11). Kuyisifiso sakhe esikhulu sokuba uthando lwakhe luphendukezelwe, uPawulu uphinde akhuluma ukuthi, “sivuleleni indawo ezinhliziyweni zenu” (2 Korinte 7:2). Ukukhuluma kokuthi “ezinhliziyweni zenu” kuthola ukusekelwa yilokhu okuzoshiwo ngokulandelayo.

Ngempela abantu baseKorinte bazivula izinhliziyo zabo bevulela uPawulu kanye nabantu abasebenzisana nabo. Kungakho ivesi elingelesine seliveza inhlokomo enkulu yokujabula. Indlela uPawulu akhombisa ngayo imizwa yokwamukela lokhu ngalesisikhathi nokwenza asho lokhu: “Ngigcwele induduzo, nginentokozo enkulu kakhulu phezu kosizi lonke esinalo” (2 Korinte 7:4). UPawulu ugcwele induduzo kanye nokujabula. Kungabe ingakanani induduzo kanye nokujabula okungalethwa ngamabandla esikuwona akwazi ukududuza abefundisi babo ngokuthi bazinikele ngokuthembeka kuKristu.

Ku-2 Korinte 7:5-16, uPawulu uqhubekela phambi ukuchaza isizathu sokududuzeka kanye nokujabula. Lezinto zombili yizona ezigqamile kulamavesi. Isenzo “sokududuza” noma ibizo “induduzo” konke lokhu kuvela kanye kanye kuze kube kasikhombisa ku-2 Korinte 7. Lengxenye yalencwadi iphetha ngendlela efanayo, yokuthi, kube nokududuzeka okukhulu kuNkulunkulu (2 Korinte 1:3-7). Induduzo kaPawulu eku-2 Korinte 7 iqhamuka ekukhululekeni kobunzima abuzwile ngoba incwadi eyayihlaba kaPawulu yakhipha umphumela abefisa ukuwuthola.

Yize kungukuthi lokhu kukhululeka kwafika ngenxa yombiko omuhle olethwe nguThithu, kodwa ekugcineni, uNkulunkulu nguyena owalethe umuzwa wokududuzeka kuPawulu (2 Korinte 7:6). Ngempela uNkulunkulu “UnguNkulunkulu wenduduzo yonke, osiduduza osizini lwethu lonke “ (2 Korinte 1:3, 4).

Okuthathekisayo kulokhu ngukuthi, yize uPawulu “egcwele induduzo,” waze “wathokoza kakhulu” (2 Korinte 7:4, 7, 13). Yize incwadi yakhe eyayizwakala ibuhlungu yadala ukuphatheka kabi kwabantu, lokhu kuphatheka kabi kwenzeka ngokwentando kaNkulunkulu ukuze kuzokuba nomphumela wokuphenduka (2 Korinte 7:9-11). Ukudabukiswa kwabantu baseKorinte kwabe kuyintando kaNkulunkulu (2 Korinte 7:11), njengoba “ukudabuka okuya ngokukaNkulunkulu kuveza ukuphenduka” (2 Korinte 7:10). Yini enye engenza kubekhona ukujabula enhliziyweni yomvangeli weqiniso likaNkulunkulu na?

`Usuke waba namava okudabuka kukaNkulunkulu empilweni yakho na? Wazi ngani ukuthi ukudabuka owaba nakho kwabe kuyintando kaNkulunkulu eyabe ikuholela ekubeni uphenduke na?`
