---
title:  Umsebenzi Wevangele LikaKristu Ogxile Ekubuyisaneni
date:   01/09/2026
---

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 5:11-15. Kungabe lamavesi akhombisa kanjani umsebenzi wokuvangela kukaPawulu ukuthi kugxile esisekelweni esinguKristu na?`

UPawulu wayazi ukuthi kufuneka akhombise ngomsebenzi wakhe wokushumayela kuKristu (2 Korinte 5:10). “Ngakho lokhu sikwazi ukwesaba iNkosi, siyabancenga abantu ngevangeli likaKristu” (2 Korinte 5:11). Lokhu kwesaba kuyindlela yokukhombisa ukuhlonipha kanye nokuzinikela kuKristu ngalokho -ke konke lokhu kuhlangene nothando uPawulu ayethanda ngayo uKristu futhi enekuthemba lokuthi naye uzoluthola loluthado. EThestamenteni Elidala, ukusaba uNkulunkulu kusho ukuhamba ezindleleni zakhe. Kusho ukumthanda, uphinde umsebenzele uNkulunkulu ngehliziyo kanye nomphefumulo wakho wonke (Dut. 10:12).

Ukushumayela kukaPawulu kwakungeyona indlela yokuzibonakalisa kodwa kwakugqamisa uKristu. Akazange azichome yena. Isizathu sokuba azishaye isifuba kwabe kunguKristu (2 Korinte 12:9). Wathi “Kepha kimi makungabikho nakunye ukuzibonga, kuphela ngesiphambano seNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu” (Galathiya 6:14). Ngakho–ke ithuba elalinikezwe abantu baseKorinte lokuba nokubonga ngaye (2 Korinte 5:12) nokusho ukuzigqaja ngevangeli lokushumayela kwakhe okugxile kuKristu nokuyinto ephambanayo nokushumayela ivangeli lokuzicabangela elalenziwa ngabantu ababephikisana naye.

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 5:16-21; Kolose 1:19-23; kanye Efesu 2:13-16. Kungabe uPawulu wayesho ukuthini uma ekhuluma “ngenkonzo yokubuyisana” na?`

UKristu ungumshumayeli wevangeli lokubuyisana ngokuyikho. Njengoba “naye ngoKristu, wasinika inkonzo yokubuyisana” (2 Korinte 5:18). Umqondo wokubuyisana uhlala uvela njalonjalo kuwona wonke amavesi aku-2 Korinte 5:16-21. Lokhu kuyisihloko esibalulekile kuPawulu, futhi kudingeka kube yinto ebalulekile nakithi.

UNkulunkulu wenze kwabakhona ukubuyisana phakathi kwabantu kunye naYe ngokubakhona kokufa kwentethelelo okwenziwa yiNdodana yaKhe. Bonke labo abathola ukubuyisana noNkulunkulu sebezidalwa ezintsha (2 Korinte 5:17). Njengamanje, sebefanele ukudlulisa “lomyalezo wokubuyisana” bawuse phambili ngokuthi bamemezele ivangeli likaKristu (2 Korinte 5:19). “Bheka singamanxusa kaKristu ngokungathi uNkulunkulu unxusa ngathi” (2 Korinte 5:20).

`Ake ucabange ngalokhu uKristu akwenzele khona. Cabanga ngokuba necala, nesono, kanye nokugxekwa obekuzokuba ngokwakho kodwa ngenxa yalokhu akwenzele khona esiphambanweni akusenjalo. Kungabe lesisigameko sikuthinta kanjani ekuxhumaneni nabanye abantu, ekakhulu labo abangamazi uNkulunkulu na?`
