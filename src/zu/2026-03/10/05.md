---
title:  Ukubizelwa Ebungcweleni
date:   02/09/2026
---

Ku-2 Korinte 6:3-10, uPawulu uyaqhubeka nokuqguqguzela abantu baseKorinte ukuba babe nokubuyisana noNkulunkulu. Yena usengumuntu omele uhide lwezinkinga kanye nokunqoba okwehlukene okusho ukuthi ukuba ngumlandeli kaKristu kanye nokuba yisisebenzi sikaNkulunkulu kusho ukuthini. Ngokufinqiwe, usebala izimo eziningi zokubhekana nobunzima (2 Korinte 6:4, 5), nokuziphatha kahle (2 Korinte 6:6), nokuyikhono lokwenza umsebenzi wokuvangela (2 Korinte 6:8-10). Emuva kokufundisa amalunga ebandla laseKorinte ukuthi babuyisane noNkulunkulu, uPawulu usenza ubizo olubhekiswe kubona lokuba baphile impilo engcwele, baphinde benze lokhu ngokuthi behlukane nokuba yisibonelo esibi sabantu abangakholwayo futhi bahlukane nezinto ezingahlanzekile (2 Korinte 6:14-17).

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 6:11-7:1. Ngokwamavesi atholakala lapha, kungabe ukuphila impilo engcwele kuyinto ebonakala kanjani na?`

Kulamavesi uPawulu ungcizelela ngokubaluleka kokuzwana nokuthandana ebandleni (2 Korinte 6:11-13). Ubufakazi bokuthi abantu sebebuyisene noNkulunkulu kuba ngukuthi bona bebodwa bafune ukubuyisana. Kuliqiniso ukuthi, baphenduka babe yilokhu ababeyikona, kokuba yizithunywa zokubuyisana lapha ezweni.

Okunye, ubizo lokuba ngcwele lunikeziwe ngokuthi kubhekwe izinto eziyisithupha ezilandelayo; (1) “Maningaboshelwa ejokeni linye nabangakholwayo” (2 Korinte 6:14); (2) “Ngakho-ke phumani phakathi kwabo” (2 Korinte 6:17); (3) “ ‘Nahlukane nabo’ ”(2 Korinte 6:17); (4) “ ‘ningathinti okungcolileyo’ “ (2 Korinte 6:17); “masizihlambulule ekugcoleni konke kwenyama nokomoya,” (2 Korinte 7:1);” ‘siphelelise ubungcwele ngokumesaba uNkulunkulu’ “ (2 Korinte 7:1); Zonke leziphakamiso zikhombisa ukuthi uNkulunkulu oNgcwele ufuna impilo eNgcwele kanye nokuphila okungahlangene nokukhonza izithombe.

Ngakolunye uhlangothi, lamavesi aphinde alethe izithembiso eziyisikhombisa ezitshengisa umsebenzi okufuneka wenziwe yibandla lamaKrestu eliyithempeli elingcwele: (1) “ ‘Ngiyakuhlala phakathi kwabo’ “; (2) “ ‘ngihambe phakathi kwabo’ “; (3) “ ‘ngiyakuba nguNkulunkulu wabo’ “; (4) “ ‘ bona babe ngabantu bami’ “; (5) “ ‘khona ngiyakunamukela’ “; (6) “ ‘ngibe nguyihlo kini’ “; (7) “ ‘nina nibe ngamadodana namadodakazi kimi’ “ (2 Korinte 6:16, 17, 18).

Esikuphawulayo ngukuthi izithembiso ezine eziku- 2 Korinte 6:16 zingumgogodla wezinto ezigcwaliseka ku- 2 Korinte 6:17 (bhekisisa igama elithi “ngakho-ke” elisekuqaleni ku-2 Korinte 6:17). Lokhu kukhombisa ukuthi ukubangcwele akuwona umphumela wento eyenziwe ngumuntu kodwa kungumphumela wokusebenza kukaMoya oNgcwele enhliziyweni yomuntu. Yize ubungcwele kungumphumela ovela kuNkulunkulu, abantu abakholwayo kudingeka kubekhona abakwenzayo baphinde bangabi ngabantu abakhonza izithombe futhi bangabandakanyeki ekwenzeni izinto ezingcolile.
