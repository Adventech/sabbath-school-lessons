---
title:  Intethelelo Nokuqinisekisa Kabusha Uthando
date:   26/08/2026
---

Kunokuba kuvakashelwe abantu baseKorinte okwesibili, uPawulu, emuva kokubuya kwakhe Efesu, wathumela lokhu osekwaziwa ngokuthi “yincwadi ehlabayo” (Buka u-2 Korinte 2:3, 4; 2 Korinte 7:8, 9).

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 7:5-13. Kungabe wabayini umphumela walokhu ayebabhalele khona, nokuthi uPawulu wawuthatha kanjani umphumela wencwadi abekade eyibhalile na?`

UPawulu noThithu bahlangana kamuva eMakedoniya, nokuyilapho uPawulu ezwa izindaba ezimnandi zokuthi amagama akhe aqinile awakhuluma aseveze umphumela omuhle, nokwaba yinto eyaletha ukujabula enhliziyweni yomphostoli. Uma kungukuthi esikhathini sangaphambili, abanye abantu babezibeke ethubeni lokulwisana noPawulu, kodwa manje ibandla selihambisana naYe. Lokhu kukhomba ukubaluleka kokwesekela umholi. Njengamalunga ebandla, singakwazi ukwenza ubelula umsebenzi wabaholi kunalesimi okuyiso njengamanje.

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 2:5-11. Kungabe yimuphi umqondo obalulekile okukhulunywa ngawo lapha na?`

Lamavesi akhuluma ngecala lokuqondisa izigwegwe ebandleni. Abafundi beBhayibheli bayaphikisana ngokuthi kungabe umsolwa lapha kwakungumuntu wesilisa ku- 1 Korinte 5:1-5 noma kwakungomunye umuntu, umuntu okwazi ukuheha abanye abantu ebandleni ezokwazi ukugxeka uPawulu ngokuthi wayengasho into eyodwa futhi wayengenalo uzwelo kubona ngazo zonke izinqumo azenzile esekuhambeni. Okufundiwe kulamavesi kubonakala kusengathi kuhambisana nombono wesibili. Noma ngabe yikuphi, into ebalulekile kulezimfundiso ezikulamavesi ngukuthi ibandla kudingeka limqondise kanjani umuntu owonile.

Lamavesi afundisa ukuthi inhloso yokuqondisa okwenziwa yibandla kusuke kuqondwe ukulungisa isimo sibuyele endaweni yaso ngokuthi sixolelwe isoni kuphinde kuqinisekiswe ukuba nothando kumuntu owonileyo (2 Korinte 2:6-8, 10). Lamavesi aphinde aphawule ukuthi ukuqondisa kwebandla kungenzeka kube yinto ebuhlungu, kodwa kuyadingeka kwenziwe. Lokhu kusho ukuthi, noma ngabe bebenenhloso enhle kanganani, futhi befuna ukuba ngabantu “abanomusa” amanye amabandla okungenzeka angabhekani noma alwisane nento ecacile noma alwisane nesono esaziwayo nasemphakathini. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, abanye abantu bangakhombisa ukuba lukhuni, bangabi nalo uxolo, bakhombise ukulwa. Yisono okufuneka kulwiswane naso, konke lokhu kwenziwe ngothando. Ngakho-ke, uPawulu uxwayisa ibandla ukuba liqinisekise ukuba nothando kumuntu owenze isono. (2 Korinte 2:8) ngoba naye uqobo wayelithanda ibandla (2 Korinte 2:4)!

`Ibandla laseKorinte lalingaba nothando kumuntu owonile (2 Kor. 2:8) ngoba nalo uqobo lalingumphumela wothando lukaNkulunkulu ngothando abaluthola kuPawulu. Kungabe lokhu kusifundisa ukuthini mayelana nothando?`
