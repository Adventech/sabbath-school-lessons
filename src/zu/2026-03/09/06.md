---
title:  Ukunqoba KuKristu
date:   27/08/2026
---

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 2:12, 13. Wayakuphi uPawulu emva kokuba ebhale “incwadi ehlabayo” eya kubona na? Wifike wenzani lapho na?`

Inhliziyo kaPawulu yayingaphathekile kahle ngesikhathi elindele uThithu (2 Korinte 7:5, 6). Yize kwakungumuntu ongaphathekile kahle, wayengeke ahlale angakhulumi ngoJesu (2 Korinte 2:12). Wayemthanda kakhulu uJesu. Ngalesosikhathi, wayengawazi umphumela wencwadi yakhe. Wayelangazelela ukubonana noThithu ukuze ezozwa ngendlela abaphatheke ngayo abantu baseKorinte.

Umsebenzi kaPawulu ngesikhathi ese-Trowa waba yimpumelelo, kodwa “wayengeke ahlale kuleya ndawo isikhathi eside. ‘Ukunakekelwa kwawo wonke amabandla,’ ekakhulu ibandla laseKorinte, kwakungumthwalo owawusinda kakhulu emahlombe akhe. Wayenethemba lokuhlangana noThithu ngesikhathi eseTrowa nokuyilapho ayezozwa ngaye ukuthi bawezwa kanjani amagama eziyalo kanye nokukhuza akuthumela kubazalwane baseKorinte ukuthi bakwamukela kanjani, kodwa okukhulu wayehleli enokukhathazeka. ‘Angizange ngibe nokuthula emphefumulweni wami,’ ebhala ngomuzwa wakhe, ‘njengoba engamtholi uThithu ongumfowabo endaweni yaseTrowa.’ Wakhetha ukusuka eTrowa wawelela ngaphesheya eMakedoniya, kwathi ngesikhathi eseFiliphu, wahlangana noThimothewu.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 323.

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 2 :14-17. Kungabe kwenzekani kuPawulu ngesikhathi edibana noThithu eMakedoniya waphinda wezwa ngezindaba ezimnandi zendlela abantu baseKorinte abamukela ngayo incwadi yakhe na?`

Ngenhlokomo yokujabula, uPawulu wavuseleleka kusiqiniseko sokuthi “uNkulunkulu osihola njalo odwendweni lokunqoba kuKristu” (2 Korinte 2 :14). Lena yinkulumo emnandi kakhulu! Inhliziyo egcwaliswe ngukubakhona kukaKristu kwenaba “okungathi yiphunga lokwazi kwakhe ezindaweni zonke” (2 Korinte 2:14).

UPawulu unokujabula kuKristu ngoba incwadi yakhe eyayibuhlungu yaletha izithelo ayefisa ukuzivuna (2 Korinte 7:5-9). Lokhu ngukunqoba okukhulu. Ngesikhathi esifanayo, ku-2 Korinte 2:17, useqinisekisa ngobuqotho bakhe ekubeni ngumphostoli kaKristu (2 Korinte 2:17; 2 Korinte 1:12). Ngokusho kwalamavesi, ahlukanisa isisebenzi esikholiwe sikaKristu amqhelelanisa nabefundisi bamanga njengoba sibona ukuthi labantu bakamuva yize bephethe ivangeli ukuthola inzuzo, laba abashiwo ekuqaleni bashumayela Izwi likaNkulunkulu ngoba bemthanda ngezinhliziyo zabo zonke uKristu.
