`Funda 2 Korinte 1:3-7. Kungabe yisiphi isizathu esenza uPawulu abe nomoya wokuletha ukubonga lapha na?`

Ukuletha ukubonga kukaPawulu kugxile enduduzweni enikwa nguNkulunkulu kubantu abasekuhluphekeni. Kulelivesi, igama eliyisenzo sokududuza kanye nebizo lokududuza kuqhamuka kundawonye izikhathi ezilishumi. Lokhu kumele ingxenye engeyesithathu kuwona wonke amagama aku-2 Korinte 29. UNkulunkulu uvezwa njengo-“Yise wobubele, uNkulunkulu wenduduzo yonke, osiduduza osizini lwethu lonke” (2 Kor. 1:3, 4, NKJV).

Induduzo umuntu ayithola kuNkulunkulu akuyona into okufuneka azigcinele yona ibe ngeyakhe kuphela (2 Korinte 1:4, 5). Yinhliziyo edabukile kuphela ethola ukwamukela induduzo kaNkulunkulu ekwaziyo ukudlulisa induduzo eyitholile ukududuza nabanye abakhathazekile.

UPawulu wakwazi ukududuza abanye ngoba naye uqobo, ekuhluphekeni kwakhe, wathola ukududuzwa nguNkulunkulu. “Kepha noma sikhathazeka, kungenxa yokududuzwa kwenu okusebenza ukukhuthazela ezinhluphekweni zona lezi esihlupheka ngazo nathi” (2 Korinte 1 :6). Lokhu kuluthando!

`Kungabe uPawulu ukukhombiselani ukubonga ku- 2 Korinte 1:8-11 na?`

UPawulu ukhuluma ngezinkathazo “ezingaphezu kokucabanga, ezedlula amandla ethu” nokwamenza yena kanye nalabo ayesebenzisana nabo bafikelwe ngukusaba kokuthi ukuphela kwesikhathi sekubafikele (2 Korinte 1:8). Ngalesisikhathi, babecabanga ngokuvuka mhla wokuphela nokwabe kungukuphela kwethemba ababanalo. Noma kunjalo, uNkulunkulu wabahlangula, isimo ababebhekene naso sashintsha (2 Korinte 1:10). Ukusabela ukuthi bazokufa (2 Korinte 1:8), bafikelwa yithemba lokuthi uNkulunkulu uzobahlangula futhi (2 Korinte 1:10). Ukunqoba kukaNkulunkulu esikhathini esedlule kusinika isibindi sokuthemba ukuthi usazokwenza okufanayo nasesikhathini esisazofika. UNkulunkulu usebenzisa izinkathazo ukusifundisa ukuba simthembe Yena. Ubunzima bungasiholela ekukhuleni kwezikamoya, okungenani kuze kufinyelele ezingeni elizosenza sisondele kakhulu kuNkulunkulu. UPawulu ngokuletha ukubonga kwakhe ukhombisa ukuba namandla kwemikhuleko yokungenelela ngesikhathi kuthandazelwa abanye abantu kanye nokubonga esikukhombisayo njengoba uNkulunkulu esihlangulile (2 Korinte 1 :11).
