---
title:  UNkulunkulu Wothando
date:   22/09/2026
---

Endulo kumhlaba owabe unabantu abangabahedeni, ababengakholwa wukuthi onkulunkulu babebathanda abantu. Okunalokho, onkulunkulu babenobubi futhi benolaka, baphinde bafune ukujatshuliswa. Umqondo wokuba noNkulunkulu wothando, njengoba sibona eBhayibhelini, kwakuyinto entsha. Njengoba ukukhuluma okufana nalokhu kwakuyisimanga ngesikhathi sokuphila kwakhe, uPawulu wakwazi ukuveza uNkulunkulu wethu njengo-“Nkulunkulu wothando nokuthula” (2 Korinte 13:11).

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 13:11. Ungalithola kanjani ithemba kulokhu okushiwo lapha na? Ungenza kanjani ukuzizwela amava ashiwo kulelivesi na?`

Inkulumo yokuthi: “uNkulunkulu wothando nokuthula” ingahunyushwa ngezindlela ezimbili ezahlukene: Ngakolunye uhlangothi, uNkulunkulu ubukwa njengomuntu ongumsuka wothando nokuthula. Kanti ngakolunye uhlangothi, isimilo sikaNkulunkulu siwuthando nokuthula. Noma kunjalo, akudingeki ukuthi umuntu akhethe okukodwa kulokhu okubili. Njengoba uthando nokuthula kuyiyona ngqikithi yesimilo sikaNkulunkulu, kungakho esipha uthando nokuthula.

Kwenye indawo uPawulu ukhuluma ngoNkulunkulu njengo-“Nkulunkulu wokubekezela nowenduduzo” (Roma 15:5); “Nkulunkulu wethemba” (Roma 15:13); “Nkulunkulu wokuthula” (Roma 15:33; Roma 16:20; 1 Korinte 14:33; Filiphu 4:9; 1 Thesalonika 5:23). “uYise wobubele” (2 Korinte 1:3), nokuthi “uNkulunkulu wenduduzo” (2 Korinte 1:3). UNkulunkulu ungumsuka wazo zonke lezibusiso. Usinika zona zonke ekwenza ngoba esithanda ngothando olungapheliyo.

Okunye futhi ngukuthi, yize kunokukhuluma kokuthi “uNkulunkulu wokuthula” nokuyinto ejwayeleke ukubonakala eBhayibhelini, okuyinkulumo ethi “uNkulunkulu wothando” iqhamuka lapha kuphela (2 Korinte 13:11), ngakho-ke, kudingeka siyibuke njengento eyingxenye eyehlukile.

Abahumushi abaningi baphawule ukuthi, ukukhuluma kukaPawulu ngoNkulunkulu wothando ekhuluma ngakho kumavesi ambalwa ngaphambi kokuba agoqe inkulumo yakhe ngokwenza isibusiso sokuvala esiku- 2 Korinte 13:14 kungenzeka ukuthi lokhu kwakuveza indlela ayemcabanga ngayo uNkulunkulu ukuthi wabe eziqu zintathu. “Yize esebenzisa igama lokuthi ‘Nkulunkulu’ njengomunye kwabathathu, ukuqonda kwakhe ngoJesu kanye noMoya oNgcwele kwezinye izincwadi kugxile ekutheni sibone leligama lichaza uNkulunkulu oyedwa nokunguNkulunkulu owaziwa yibandla lokuqala elalimazi njengoNkulunkulu oziqu zintathu. Kwaze kwadlula ikhulu leminyaka ngaphambi kokuba abafundi beBhayibheli baqala khona ukusebenzisa amagama athi ‘oziqu zintathu’ befinqa lokhu akhuluma ngakho uPawulu.”—Tom Wright, Paul for Everyone: 2 Corinthians (London: Society for Promoting Christian Knowledge, 2004), p. 148.

Sikholelwa kuNkulunkulu oyedwa, lapho kuhlangene iziqu ezintathu, uNkulunkulu ophila ingunaphakade kubudlelwano bothando. LoNkulunkulu oziqu zintathu uyasithanda futhi usimemela ukuba sithandane ngendlela ezokhombisa ukuthandana okufana nokuthandana kwabo bobathathu.
