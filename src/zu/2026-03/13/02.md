---
title:  Umusa KaJesu
date:   20/09/2026
---

Kuyavuselela ukubona ukuthi ekugcineni kwesahluko sika –2 Korinte, sithola kukhulunywa ngomusa kaJesu, njengoba kwenzekile uma kuvulwa lesisahluko (2 Korinte 1:2; 2 Korinte 13:14). UPawulu uqalisa aphinde aphethe incwadi yakhe ngokuthi akhulume ngomusa kaJesu. Njengoba sikwazile ukubona ngesikhathi siqala ukufunda ekuqaleni kwaleNyanga-ntathu, uPawulu akakwazanga ukuhlala engacabangi noma akhulume ngoJesu.

“Ngokuba niyawazi umusa weNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu ukuthi, nakuba ecebile, waba mpofu ngenxa yenu, ukuze ngobumpofu bakhe nicebe nina” (2 Korinte 8:9).

Umusa kaJesu esiwuthokozela kangaka nokungamelwe wenqatshwe. Washiya ubucebi ayehlala kubona ezulwini ingunaphakade wakhetha ukuba ngumuntu ohluphekayo. Wahamba emigwaqeni yobhuqu endaweni eyabe ikhona endulo yaseGalile. “Wazithobisa, elalela kwaze kwaba sekufeni, yebo ekufeni kwesiphambano” (Filiphu 2:8). Konke lokhu wakwenza ukuze nathi sizokwazi ukuba “ngabacebileyo,” siphinde sikwazi ukuthola ithuba lokuba sibe sezulwini. Kulikhuni kithina esinolwazi lwalomhlaba wesono, nokufa kanye nokuhlupheka, ukuba sibe nokuqonda ukuthi ngabe kusho ukuthini ukushiya kukaJesu emazulwini khona ezokwazi ukuba nathi lapha emhlabeni aphinde anikele ngokuphila kwaKhe ngenxa yethu.

Funda KwabaseRoma 16:20; Galathiya 6:8; Filiphu 4;23; kanye no- 1 Thesalonika

`5:28.Kungabe yikuphi okubalulekile esikufundayo kulamavesi na?`

UPawulu ezincwadini zakhe, izikhathi eziningi ukhuluma ngomusa kaJesu. Okunye okuhle esingakucaphuna ngukuthi, “kwavama kwabaningi umusa nesipho sikaNkulunkulu ngomusa womuntu munye, uJesu Kristu”(Roma 5:15). Bonke abathe bawuzuza lomusa ongaka “bayakubusa ekuphileni ngalowo omunye, uJesu Kristu” (Roma 5:17). Njengoba bekulokhu kuvela ku-2 Korinte, uPawulu ubelokhu evamisile ukuqala aphinde aphethe ezinye izincwadi zakhe ngokuthi akhulume ngomusa kaJesu (Roma 1:7; Roma 16:20; 1 Korinte 1:3; 1 korinte 16:23; Galathiya 1:3; Galathiya 6:18; Filiphu 1:2; Filiphu 4:23). Lesi sihloko sigcwele kakhulu emqondweni wakhe, futhi wayefisa ukuthi ukucabanga okunjena kuphinde kugcwalise ukucabanga kwabantu baseKorinte.

Lokhu kwabe kuyisifiso sakhe, efisela ukuba kwenzeke kuwona wonke amabandla. Bhekisisa lokhu akusho ebandleni lase-Efesu: “Umusa mawube nabo bonke abathanda iNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu ngokunganqamukiyo” (Efesu 6:24). Kungenzeka yini afisele nathi ukuba sithande uJesu ngezinga elingaphansi kwaleli, okuwuthando olunganqamukiyo na? Okusempeleni, isifiso sakhe kwabe kungukuthi umusa kaJesu ufinyelele “kubo abaningi” (2 Korinte 4:15) uphinde umusa kaJesu wanele kubantu bonke njengoba wawenele nakuyena (2 Korinte 12:9).

`Ake ucabange ngomusa kaNkulunkulu ofikele wena ngendlela efana nalena. Kungabe kukufanele yini ukuwuthola phezu kwezinto ozikhulumile kanye nalezo ozenzile na? Noma kunjalo, kungabe yini onikwa khona ngumusa kaNkulunkulu na?`
