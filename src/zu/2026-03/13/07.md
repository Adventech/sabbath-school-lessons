Ellen G. White, “ ‘Let Not Your Heart Be Troubled,’ ” pp. 662–680, in The Desire of Ages.

“Umusa kaJesu Kristu uwodwa angeke ukwazi ukuguqula inhliziyo elukhuni okwetshe uyenze inhliziyo yenyama, noma uphinde ukwazi ukuyenza ibe yinto ephilayo kuNkulunkulu. . . . Abantu kabanalo ilungelo lokulungisa imiphefumulo, bangcwelise nenhliziyo yomphefumulo. Ukugula kwesimilo angeke kwelashwe noma kusindiswe ngamandla okusebenza kukadokotela omkhulu. Isipho esikhulu sasezulwini, nokuyisipho seNdodana ezelwe yodwa kaBaba osezulwini, ogcwele umusa neqiniso, nguyena kuphela ongakwazi ukuhlanza umphefumulo olahlekile.”—Ellen G. White, in The Signs of the Times, May 2, 1892.

“ ‘UNkulunkulu uluthando.’ 1 Johane 4:16. Isimilo, nomthetho wakhe kuwuthando. Bekulokhu kuyinto enjalo; futhi kuyohlala kuyinto enjalo. ‘Ophakamileyo nophezulu, ohlala kuze kube phakade,’ ‘ondlela zakhe zimi ingunaphakade,’ ‘okungekho kuye ukuguquka nasithunzi sokuphenduka’ (Isaya 57:15; Habakuki 3:6; Jakobe 1:17). Konke esikubonayo kwamandla akhe okudala kuyindlela yokukhombisa uthando lwaphakade. Ubukhosi bukaNkulunkulu bufaka phakathi ukugcwala kwezibusiso kuyona yonke into edaliweyo. Umlando wokuphikisana okukhulu phakathi kokwenza okuhle nokwenza kabi, kusukela ngesikhathi kuqala kwenzeka ezulwini kuze kufike esikhathini lapho kuyonqontshwa khona konke okuphathelene nokuvukelwa kombuso kaNkulunkulu nesikhathi lapho isono siyoshatshalaliswa singaphinde sibe khona, konke lokhu kungubufakazi bothando lukaNkulunkulu olungaguquki.”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 33.

“Kudingeka sikwazi ukubona ukuthi uMoya oNgcwele . . . ungumuntu okhona njengoba kukhona uNkulunkulu. . . . UMoya oNgcwele unobuntu obuthile futhi angeke ukwazi ukufakaza ngesimo sezikamoya bomunye umuntu futhi ngalomoya esinawo thina sesingabantwana bakaNkulunkulu. UMoya oNgcwele unguNkulunkulu naye, ngaphandle kwalokho, ubengeke akwazi ukuhlola izimfihlo ezihlifakele ezisemqondweni kaNkulunkulu.”—Ellen G. White, The Faith I Live By, p. 52.

“Kukhona abantu abaphilayo abathathu abayiziqu ezintathu zasezulwini; egameni laleziqu ezinamandla ezintathu- uYise, nendodana, kanye noMoya oNgcwele- bonke abantu abamukela uKristu ngokuphila ngokukholwa bathola ukubhapathizwa, futhi lamandla azobambisana nezinye izidalwa ezinokuhlonipha kwasezulwini emizamweni yabo yokuphila impilo entsha kuKristu.”—Ellen G. White, Evangelism, p. 615.
