---
title:  Ubudlelwane BukaMoya ONgcwele
date:   23/09/2026
---

Umusa kaJesu awembulanga kuphela uthando lukaNkulunkulu kithina, kodwa ulethile nokuhlangana kukaMoya ukuze sizoluzwa kangcono uthando lukaNkulunkulu. Ngesikhathi esifanayo, lobudlelwano bunomsuka osothandweni lukaNkulunkulu. Njengoba uPawulu ebhale ukuthi: “Ngakho-ke uma kukhona induduzo kuKristu, uma kukhona ukududuzeka kothando, uma kukhona inhlangano kaMoya, uma kukhona isisa nesihawu, gcwalisani ukuthokoza kwami ukuba nibe-nomqondo munye, ninothando lunye, nibe nhliziyonye, niqonde into inye” (Filiphu 2:1, 2).

Abanye abantu bathi uMoya oNgcwele uyinto enika amandla noma umthelela othile, kodwa lokhu akulona iqiniso. Okusempeleni, kungani kudingeka uPawulu akhulume ngabantu ababili – uYise neNdodana – abaxhube ndawonye “namandla” uma kuvezwa uNkulunkulu oziqu zintathu na? Ukukhuluma okunjena angeke kuwenze umqondo. Njengoba uYise neNdodana bevezwe njengabantu abehlukene kulobudlelwano (2 Korinte 1:3; 2 Korinte 11:31), ukubakhona kokuhlangana noMoya oNgcwele nalababantu kusiholela ekutheni sibone ukuthi naYe ungumuntu ozimele (Roma 8:15, 16; funda futhi naku-Johane 14:16, 17, 26; Johane 15: 26).

Inkulumo ethi “inhlangano kaMoya” (Filiphu 2:1) singayiqonda ngezindlela ezimbili ezahlukene. Kungasho ukuhlangana nomunye umuntu ngenxa yokusebenza kukaMoya oNgcwele, noma ukuhlangana noMoya oNgcwele uqobo lwawo. Abahlaziyi beBhayibheli abehlukene banokuphikisana kokuthi lemiqondo emibili ayehlukene kangako. Okuseqinisweni, ukwazi ukuhlangana nomunye umuntu kusuke kungumphumela wobudlelwano eninabo noMoya oNgcwele.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 2:10, 11; 1 Korinte 3:16; 1 Korinte 12:11; kanye 2 Korinte 3:6, 17. Kungabe uPawulu ubafundisa ukuthini abantu baseKorinte mayelana noMoya oNgcwele na?`

Kuningi afuna ukukusho uPawulu okuphathelene nokusebenza kukaMoya oNgcwele. Ku- 1 kanye -2 KwabaseKorinte, kunokukhuluma okungaphezulu kwamashumi amene lapho kuthintwa khona udaba lukaMoya oNgcwele. UMoya oNgcwele uqhakambisa ukwakheka kwebandla (1 Korinte 14:12), nokwenza abantu balungele ukwenza umsebenzi wevangeli (1 Korinte 2:4, 5), wembula izimfihlakalo ezijulile zikaNkulunkulu kithina (1 Korinte 2:10, 11) uphinde ubafundise ngokuhlakanipha kwenzelwa thina (1 Korinte 2:13), uhlale kithina (1 Korinte 3:16, 1 Korinte 6:19), usebenzisana noKristu ukwenzela ukuba silungisiswe (1 Korinte 6:11), unika iziphiwo zikaMoya ebandleni (1 Korinte 12-14), usibeka uphawu lokusindiswa (2 Korinte 3:3) uqopha imithetho yakhe ezinhliziyweni zabantu (2 Korinte 3:3), usipha ukuphila okusha kukaKristu (2 korinte 3:6) usipha ukukhululeka esonweni (2 Korinte 3:17). Nembala, asikwazi ukuphila ngaphandle kukaMoya oNgcwele.

`Kungani kuyinto ebelulekile ukuqonda ngobuNkulunkulu bukaMoya noNgcwele nokuyinto esemqoka ukuze sizokwamukela ngokugcwele uthando lukaNkulunkulu olubekelwe thina na?`
