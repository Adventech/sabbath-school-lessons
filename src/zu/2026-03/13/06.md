---
title:  UNkulunkulu Wethu Oziqu Zintathu
date:   24/09/2026
---

Uma sifunda ku- 2 Korinte 13:14, umuntu angacabanga ukuthi uKristu nguyena ongumsusa womusa, uNkulunkulu ungumsusa wothando, uMoya oNgcwele ungumsusa wobudlelwano, kodwa akukho esingathi kuqheleli eqinisweni kulokhu kucabanga.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 1:3, 4, 9; 1 Korinte 10:16; 2 Korinte 1:2, 12; Roma 8:35; Roma 15:30; Galathiya 2:20; kanye naku-EFesu 3:19. Kungabe lamavesi akhuluma ukuthini ngomusa, nothando, kanye nobudlelwano kwamalunga amathathu eziqu ezintathu zikaNkulunkulu na?`

UBaba, neNdodana, kanye noMoya oNgcwele bayasebenzisana ekusindisweni kwethu. Umusa, uthando, nokuhlanganyela akuveli esiqwini esisoda kodwa kuvela kuzona zontathu iziqu. Noma kunjalo, isiqu ngasinye sinomsebenzi waso kumlando ophathelene nokusindiswa. UPawulu uyakubona lokhu aphinde agcizelele ukufundisa ngalokhu ezincwadini zakhe azibhalile. Isibonelo, uhlelo lokusindiswa kukhulunywa ngalo ngendlela enenzuzo emangalisayo emazwini atholakala ku- Galathiya 4:4-6, nokuyilapho kukhona ukusebenzisana kwazo zonke iziqu zikaNkulunkulu. UNkulunkulu uYise wathuma uJesu, okuhlongoza ukuthi uBaba nguyena ongumsunguli waloluhlelo (Galathiya 4:4). Indodana yazalwa ngowesifazane (Galathiya 4:4), nokuyinto efaniswa nokuguquka abe ngumuntu nokuyinto eyabe ikhomba ukugcwaliseka kwesithembiso esashiwo endulo (Genesise 3:15). Kwase kuthi uMoya oNgcwele wagunyaza ubunjalo bethu bokuba ngabantwana bakaNkulunkulu (Galathiya 4:6).

Kukhona ezinye izinto ezimbalwa zeziqu ezintathu okukhulunywa ngazo ezincwadini zikaPawulu. Zontathu iziqu ziyasebenzisana, ukulekelela ibandla ukuba likwazi ukwenza umsebenzi wokuvangela (1 Korinte 12:4-6), okusinika ukuqina kwezikamoya (Efesu 3:14-19), kuqhakambise ubumbano phakathi kwamalunga ebandla, ubumbano olufuze ubunye nolukhombisa ubudlelwano obukhona kuziqu ezintathu zikaNkulunkulu (Efesu 4:4-6). Nokuqonda kukaPawulu, akugcini nje ngokuthi uNkulunkulu uziqu zintathu, kodwa labantu abathathu abehlukene bobuNkulunkulu bayasebenzisana ekusindisweni kwethu (Efesu 1:3, 13, 14). KwabaseFesu, uPawulu uyaqhubeka ngokukhuluma ukuthi kudingeka sigcwaliswe ngokugcwala kukaNkulunkulu (Efesu 3:19), nesekwazini iNdodana (Efesu 4:13), siphinde sigcwaliswe ngoMoya oNgcwele (Efesu 5:18).

Uthe uma esevala ukuxhumana kwakhe nabantu baseKorinte (2 Korinte 13:14), uPawulu wayengeke akwazi ukuqedeka ngaphandle kokukhuluma kangcono ngokuthi – afake isithembiso sabahlonishwa abathathu abasezweni lonke, okuyiziqu ezintathu zasezulwini, abahlala kunye nathi njengamanje kanye naseminyekeni eminingi esazofika.

`Kungabe ukuhlangana phakathi kwamalunga ebandla kuveza ukuxhumana okuhle noNkulunkulu na?`
