---
title:  Ukuvikela Ubunjalo BeBandla
date:   21/07/2026
---

Ku-1 Korinte 6:1-11, uPawulu uyaqhubeka nokukhuluma ngokuthi kudingeka amaKrestu aziqaphele kanjani izindaba eziphathelene nokuziphatha kwabantu ebandleni.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 5:3, 12, 13 no-1 Korinte 6:1-13. Kungabe uPawulu wayezama ukubafundisa ukuthini abantu baseKorinte kanye nathi na?`

Igama eliku-1 Korinte 6:1 elithi “indaba” nokungaphinde kusho “into.” Kulelivesi kukhulunywa ngodaba lwasezinkantolo. Kusemqoka ukukhumbula ukuthi ku-1 Korinte 6:1-11 akukhulunywa ngamacala obugebengu. Iziphathimandla zezinkantolo zomphakathi nokuyizinkantolo ezidingida izindaba zomthetho kuyakhulunywa nangazo ku-Roma 13:1-5. UPawulu kulamavesi ukhuluma ngamacala asezinkantolo emuva kokubakhona kwamacala okuphinga, nokuyinto efana nalokhu okwenziwa uMose okutholakala ku- Deteronomi 22:22-24. Lokhu kuyindlela akhombisa ngayo uPawulu ukuthi wayexazulula izinkinga zebandla ngokuthi asebenzise imiBhalo.

Ukukhuluma ngodaba olutholakala ku-1 Korinte 6:1-11 luthola ukusekelwa nangamavesi akhuluma ngezenzo zokuphinga ezikulamavesi alandelayo; (1 Korinte 5; kanye 1 Korinte 6: 12-20) lokhu kungahlongoza ukuthi “indaba” eku- 1 Korinte

6:1 ithathwa njengodaba lokuhlobonga. Kasikho isiqiniseko sokuthi kungabe kwakuyiluphi loludaba, noma kwabe kuyinkinga yombango, njengokubanga indawo, noma kuyinkinga ephathelene nokuphinga.

Noma ngabe kwakuyiluphi udaba, ekugcineni uPawulu wayengakujabuleli ukubona amalunga ebandla ehambisa loludaba ezinkantolo zomphakathi. Kungenzeke yini, njengabazalwane nodade abangamaKrestu, bakwazi ukuxazulula loludaba bebodwa, kunokuba bahambise loludaba ngaphambi kwabantu “abangalungile”(1 Korinte 6:1) na?

Kungenzeka futhi, njengoba abanye behlawumbisela ukuthi, abamangali abathintekayo ku- 1 Korinte 6:1 kungaba ngubaba kanye nendodana abatholakala ku-1 Korinte 5:1. Noma ngabe kungayiphi indlela, akubalulekile ukwazi ukuthi kwakuyiluphi udaba ukuze umuntu ezokwazi ukuqonda kabanzi ngalenkinga. UPawulu wayekhathazeke ngobunjalo bebandla njengoba umphakathi ongamaKrestu uthola ukubukwa ngabantu bangaphandle. AmaKrestu akufuneki aneke iwashingi engcolile esidlangalaleni (1 Korinte 6:6). Futhi akudingeki basebenzise izindlela zokuxhumana zasemphakathini bahlulele izindaba ezenzeka ngaphakathi ebandleni. Emhlabeni waseRoma, abantu ababephezulu kwezomnotho noma kwezombusazwe kwakungathi bayavunwa yizinkantolo. Ngokufanayo, abantu abangamaKrestu kufuneka behlulele njengamaKrestu futhi benze ngendlela ehlukile kunalokhu okwenziwa emphakathini.

`Ake ucabange ngezenzo zobufebe ezakhulunywa ngu-Pawulu ku-1 Korinte 5:10, 11 kanye naku-1 Korinte 6:9,10. Kungani ekhuluma kabanzi ngezono zobufebe aphinde afake ndawonye nezono zokukhonza izithombe, nobusela, nokuba nomhobholo, kanye nempango na?`
