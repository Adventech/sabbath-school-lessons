---
title:  Umshado Nokuba Wedwa Ungashadile
date:   23/07/2026
---

Ukuvumelana kukaPawulu nokuthi imizimba yethu “iyithempeli likaMoya oNgcwele” (1 Korinte 6:19) kuvela kuyisixwayiso esilwisana nobufebe. Ukuba yithempeli likaMoya oNgcwele kungukuphela kwendlela umuntu angaphila ngayo impilo engcwele. Ibandla lingumphakathi ongamaKrestu nokuyindlela elehluka ngayo ibandla kumphakathi obazungezile. Ngukubakhona kukaMoya oNgcwele ongenza lokhu kwenzeke.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 6:19-7:9. Kungabe lamavesi aveza kuphi ukukhanyiseleka ekutheni umuntu angenza kanjani “ukubalekela ubufebe” (1 Kor. 6:18, ESV) na?`

Kukhona izifundo eziphathelene nokuziphatha kwezobulili ezibalulekile encwadini ka- 1 Korinte 7. Ngokusho okukhethekile, lesisahluko singahlukaniswa izigaba ezimbili: (1) yiziyalo eziphathelene nokuganana (1 Korinte 7:1-24) kanye (2) neziyalo eziphatheleni nokungaganani zokuhlala wedwa (1 Korinte 7:25-40). AbaseKorinte Bokuqala isahluko 7 lencwadi ingasisiza ukwazi ukuqonda kabanzi uma kukhulunywa ngezinto ezibalulekile neziyisidingo zokuziphatha ngokobulili.

Noma kunjalo, uma ufunda ku- 1 Korinte 7, kufuneka sikhumbule ukuthi uPawulu wayephendula imibuzo eyayiphathelene nezinkinga ezazitholakala eKorinte. Ngaphandle kwalokhu, okunye okushiwo nguPawulu kungaletha umcabango wokuthi wayebukela phansi ukubakhona komshado, nokuyinto engesikhona lokho (1 Tim. 4:1–3, 1 Tim. 5:14; no Heb. 13:4).

Okusemqoka, lomthetho wokuthi “balekelani ubufebe” otholakala ku-1 Korinte 6:18 okakeleziwe, uvela njengomqondo wokwazi ukuzihlanganisa noKristu (1 Korinte 6:19). Kungabe ikhona yini enye indlela engcono yokubalekela ubufebe na? okuseqinsweni ayikho.

Nakhu okunye, uNkulunkulu wadala ukwenzeka kocansi, kodwa wakwenza lokhu ngenhloso yokuba kujatshuliswane emshadweni kuphela. Ukwenziwa kocansi kuyilungelo lokuhlangana komuntu wesilisa nowesifazane emshadweni, nokuyiyona mvume etholakala eBhayibhelini.

Uma kukhulunywa ukuthi, “balekelani ubufebe,” kungenzeka ukuthi uPawulu wayecabanga indaba kaJosefa (Genesise 39:6-18). IBhayibheli likhuluma ukuthi, ngaphambi kokuba umka-Potifa azisondeze, “uJosefa wabalekela phandle” (Genesise 39:18). Ukukhuluma okufana nalokhu kwashiwo kwaze kwedlula izikhathi ezine ku-Genesise 39:6-18. IBhayibheli kalikubeki ngembaba ukuthi lokhu kwakusho ukuthi uJosefa wayelinde ukwenza ucansi uma eseshadile ( Genesise 41:38) futhi wayefuna ukwenza into elungile phambi kukaNkulunkulu.

`Thina njengebandla, singazivikela kanjani kulokhu kukhuluma okulihlazo kobufebe obubhidlangile osikweni lomphakathi esiphila kuwo na?`
