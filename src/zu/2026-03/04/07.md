Ellen G. White, “A Message of Warning and Entreaty,” pp. 298–308, in The Acts of the Apostles.

Okuthathekisayo ngukuthi, ohlwini lwezixwayiso eziku- 1 Korinte 5:10, 11 kanye naku-1 Korinte 6:9, 10, kukhona ukukhonza izithombe kanye nokudakwa kubalwa ndawonye nokufeba. Njengoba asho uPawulu ku-1 Korinte 10:7 (uqhathanise no-Eksodusi 32:1-6), amadili enzelwa izithixo kwakuyinto ebonakala ngokuthi kudliwe kuphinde kuphuzwe kakhulu, nokuyinto eyayivula umnyango wokwenziwa ubufebe (1 Korinte 10:8). U-Ellen G. White uthi:

“Kuyinto elikhuluni kunoma ngubani ukujabulela isibusiso sokungcweliswa kube kungumuntu ogcwele umoya wokuzicabangela kanye nokuminza. . . . Amandla omthetho-sisekelo wabantu ovimbela ukuxhashazwa yimithetho emangalisayo, kodwa yilokhu kuqhubeka ukwenziwa kwemikhuba emibi ehambisana nokuminza kanye nokuphuza ngokweqile nokuyinto ephazamisa ukusebenza komzimba wonke. Ukujabulela ukuhala kokukhohlakala nemizwa engabambeki sekuyinto esihlasele nabantu abazibiza ngamaKrestu nokuphazamisa ukusebenza komzimba, nengqondo kanye namandla okuziphatha kahle.”—Ellen G. White, The Sanctified Life, pp. 25, 26.

“Uma umuntu esezidelile ngokwanele, lapho zonke izithixo zamanga sezilahlwe kude nomphefumulo, isikhala sithola ukuvaleka ngokuthi kugeleze uMoya kaKristu. Umuntu onjalo unokukholwa okusebenza ngokuba nothando futhi lokhu kuhlanza umphefumulo kukona konke okuncolisa isimilo kanye nokuphila kukamoya.”—Ellen G. White, The Home Missionary, November, 1889.

“UNkulunkulu ufuna ukusikhuphula sifinyelele ezingeni laKhe lasezulwini eliphakeme, nelimsulwa. Ukuze kuzokwazi ukwenzeka uMoya waKhe uyasincenga njalo-njalo. . . . ebuthakathakeni bethu bemvelo, uma kungazange kulungiswe nguMoya oNgcwele kaNkulunkulu, lokhu kungaba nembewu yokufa kwesimilo.”—Ellen G. White, Manuscript 12, 1888.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Amakholwa amaningi aseKorinte ayelangazelela ukwamukeleka osikweni. Kungani isifiso esifana nalesi siyingozi kumuntu ongumKrestu na? Singenza kanjani ukuvimbela ukwenza iphutha elifanayo na?`

`Umbuzo kaPawulu olokhu uphindwaphindwa othi “Kepha kanazi yini ukuthi umzimba wenu uyithempeli likaMoya oNgcwele na?” (1 Kor. 6:19, NKJV) ovala uchungechunge lwemibuzo etholakala ku- 1 Korinte 5-6 eyethulwe ngombuzo othi “Anazi yini ukuthi?” (1 Kor. 5:6; 1 Kor. 6:2, 3, 9, 15, 16, 19). Yonke lemibuzo yayifuna kuvunywe ngempendulo ezwakalayo yokuthi “yebo siyazi.” Kungabe lemibuzo isisiza kuphi thina ukuze siqonde kabanzi ngokukhathazeka kukaPawulu ngebandla na? Kungani kufuneka nathi sikhombise ukukhathazeka ngalokhu esikhathini samanje na?`

`Umshado uvela kuNkulunkulu (Gen. 1:27, 28; Gen. 2:18-24)futhi kufuneka uhlonishwe (Heb. 13:4). Esikhathini lapho abantu abaningi bewubona uyinto eyimfashini endala, singenza kanjani ukukhombisa umhlaba ukuthi umshado ngempela uyisipho esivela kuNkulunkulu, ukusukela ensimini yase-Edeni uqobo na?`
