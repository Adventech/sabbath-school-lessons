### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 Kor. 5:1–13, 2 Kor 2:5–10, 1 Kor. 6:1–13, 1 Thes. 4:1–8, 1 Kor. 6:19–7:9.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kepha anazi yini ukuthi umzimba wenu uyithempeli likaMoya oNgcwele okinina, enimamukele kuNkulunkulu nokuthi anisibo abenu na? Ngokuba nathengwa ngenani elikhulu. Ngakho-ke mbongeni uNkulunkulu emzimbeni wenu” (1 Korinte 6:19, 20, NIV).

Ubuchopho esinabo bufana nesiponji: Noma ngabe yini elethwa kubona, iza ngemizwa, ehlala kubo. Kungenzeka singezwa ukuthi yini engenayo (asikwazi ukucabanga uma singakwazi ukukhumbula konke), kodwa kukhona ebuchosheni, futhi ngandlela thizeni, kuyaba nomthelela ekucabangeni, nasemizweni kanye nasekwenzeni kwethu.

Kungakho kuyinto elula kithi, noma ngabe singamaKrestu, ukuba sichaphazeleke kuzona zonke izinto ezimbi ezenzeka kithina. Ibandla lamaKrestu, kusuka liqala, belilokhu linokukhathazeka ngenxa yezinkinga. Isibonelo, kungabe kwaqalaphi ukugcinwa kosuku lwangeSonto na? Kungabe ibandla lamane lazenzela lakwenza lokhu likususela emoyeni na? Cha akunjalo. Konke lokhu kusuka osikweni esiphila kulo.

Nakho lokhu siyakwazi ukukubona kwenzeka nasebandleni lase-Korinte. Emva kokuxwayisa abantu ngokuba ngamaqembuqembu (1 Korinte 1-4), uPawule useqala ukukhuluma ngesono sokuphinga, nokumangalelana, nokudayisa ngomzimba, nangomshado, kanye nokuphila wedwa (1 Korinte 5-7). Amazinga okuphila ayekhona emhlabeni abanomthelela omkhulu kakhulu. Ubuqembuqembu obuveziwe encwadini yama-1 Korinte 1-4 kwavula umnyango ophikisana nokuziphatha kahle esikuthola kuzahluko ezilandelayo. Kungabe uPawulu wayezama kanjani ukulwisana nalesono ebandleni na? Yiziphi izifundo esingazithatha kulokhu akubhalile na?

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNtulikazi 25._
