---
title:  Ikhambi Elilwisana Nobufebe
date:   22/07/2026
---

`Funda ku-1 Thesalonika 4:1-8. Kungabe lamavesi athini mayelana nokuxhumana phakathi kokungcweliswa kanye nokungazihlanganisi nezenzo zobufebe na?`

Yize uPawulu wayebhalela umuntu othile kulamavesi esiwafundile, inkambiso ingasentshenziswa kuyona yonke indawo, nakuwona wonke amaKrestu.

Yize kunjalo, lokhu kuholela kulombuzo: Kungabe kwakwenzekani eKorinte na? Kungani kunezinkinga ezingaka na?

Abanye abantu baseKorinte kungenzeka babekholwa wukuthi njengoba ivangeli selibakhululile, ngakho sebevumelekile ukwenza noma ngabe yini. Bazivikela ngokuthi njengoba isisu senzelwa ukudla, ngendlela efanayo nomzimba udalelwe ukuzijabulisa ngezocansi, futhi umzimba ungowobufebe (1 Korinte 6:13). UPawulu uphikisana nalokhu ngokuthi akusiyona indlela yokukhululeka komuntu ongumKrestu. Ukungabi nasimilo ezintweni eziphathelene nokuphinga akuhambisani nokujabula komuntu ongumKrestu futhi kungasetshenziswa ngendlela engafanele inkululeko enikezwe abantu yivangeli (Roma 8:2; Galathiya 5:13). Sikhululiwe ekwenzeni isono, kasizange “sikhululwe” ekubeni sone (Roma 8:2; Roma 6:18; 22). Okuseqinisweni, “umzimba ungoweNkosi, neNkosi ingeyomzimba” (1 Korinte 6:13). SingabakaKristu (1 Korinte 6:15), ngakho-ke kudingeka sibe ngumthelela omuhle kulokhu esikwenzayo. Okunye kuyavumelana nokunye. Lokhu kuvezwe ngezindlela ezintathu ezahlukene ku-1 Korinte 6.

Okokuqala, sibonwa njengabantu **abahlanziwe, bangcweliswa, baphinde balungisiswa** “ngegama leNkosi uJesu Kristu nangoMoya kaNkulunkulu wethu” (1 Korinte 6:11). Izono ezibaliwe ku- 1 Korinte 6:9,10, kanye nezenzo zobufebe okuphikiswana nazo ku- 1 Korinte 6:12-20, azinandawo kubantu asebephila impilo yokuhlanzwa, bangcweliswa baphinde balungisiswa.

Okwesibili, **singamalunga kaKristu** (1 Korinte 6:15). Lokhu kusho ukuthi kudingeka sibemunye noKristu (1 Korinte 6;17). Ubufebe bungukwephula ubudlelwano esinabo noKristu (1 Korinte 6:13, 15). Noma ngubani owenza ubufebe nomunye umuntu ngaphandle komshado uphenduka abe “mzimba munye” nalowo muntu aphinga naye (1 Korinte 6;16). Ukuba munye noKristu ngoMoya kudingeka kube ngumhlahla-ndlela womuntu ezintweni eziphathelene nokuziphatha ngokobulili.

Okwesithathu, **imizimba yethu iyithempeli likaMoya oNgcwele** (1 Korinte 6:19, 20). Indlela okunguphela kwayo yokuphila impilo yokuziphatha kahle kwezobulili ngukuba umuntu abe nokuxhumana okuyikho noKristu ngoMoya oNgcwele. Kwenye indawo uPawulu uveza ukuthi amava okuba yithempeli likaMoya oNgcwele kusho “ukwenza umzimba ube ngumnikelo ophilileyo, nongcwele,nothandeka kuNkulunkulu” (Rom. 12:1, ESV).

`Ake ucabange ngomonakalo owenziwe yisono sobufebe esibulethe phezu kwabantu. Ngakube lokhu kusitshela ukuthini ngokubaluleka kwaloluludaba kubantu abangamaKrestu na?`
