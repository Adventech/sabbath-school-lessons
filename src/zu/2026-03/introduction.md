### Ubunjalo Bempilo YomKristu Nokufakaza

Ukubhalwa kwezincwadi kungumkhuba omdala obulokhu wenzeka, nokuyinto engazange iyekwe ukwenziwa. Into eyenzekile ngukuthi kushintshwe indlela izincwadi ezibhalwa ngayo. Kuliqiniso ukuthi esikufunda ezinkundleni zokuxhumana sekufundwa kakhulu ukudlula lokho okubhalwe kumaphepha-ndaba. Noma kunjalo, ubunjalo bokubhalwa kwama-email kanye nokunye okutholakala emishinini yezokuxhumana kusenza umsebenzi ofanayo: Kuhlanganisa abantu ngokuthi bakwazi ukwabelana ngolwazi nangemizwa kanye nalokhu abasuke bekucabanga.

Kungani abantu bebhala izincwadi na? Impendulo engaba khona eyokuthi kusuke kukhona into ethile abasuke befuna ukuyisho. Lokhu kuseyinto efanayo nalokhu okwenziwa ngumphostoli uPawula. Yize kwabe kukuningi ayefisa ukukusho, wayengakwazi ukuthola njalo ithuba lokusho into ebhekene ngqo nabantu afisa ukukhulumisana nabo.

Ngakho-ke, wakhetha ukubhala izincwadi – ezifana nezincwadi zaseKhorinte, eziqukethe amaqiniso athile natholakala embhalweni. Phakathi kwalamaqiniso kukhona nalokhu: “Ngokuba nganquma ukuthi angikwazi okunye phakathi kwenu, kuphela uJesu Kristu, lowo obethelwe esiphambanweni” (1 Korinte 2:2). “Ngokuba niyawazi umusa weNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu ukuthi, nakuba ecebile, waba mpofu ngenxa yenu, ukuze ngobumpofu bakhe nicebe nina” (2 Korinte 8:9). Nakhu okunye okungumculo othathekisayo wothando otholakala ku-1 Korinte 13 na?

Ngakolunye uhlangothi, noma ngubani ofunda izincwadi ezibhalwe nguPawula ezibhekiswe kwabaseKhorinte uzokuba nokudideka okukhulu, kungekhona ukuthi kwakungenxa yezinkinga ezinkulu ezazitholakala ebandleni laseKhorinte – njengesono sokungaziphathi kahle esimilwenikodwa kwabe kukhona nokunye okuncane okwakutholakala nokwabe kungumphumela wokungezwani kwabazalwane bebandla. Uma unokucabanga kokuthi ibandla lakho linezinselelo ezithile, ake ulungele ukubona izinkinga zokulwisana uPawule abhekana nazo ebandleni laseKorinte. Mhlawumbe izinkinga ezikhona ebandleni lakho azibabazeki kangako. Okukhulu okungenzeka ngukuthi, uzokwazi ukubona ukuthi izigameko ezazenzeka eKhorinte kwabe kuzinkinga ezinkulu kakhulu.

Njengoba izinkinga ezazenzeka eKhorinte zingaletha ukukhathazeka, izincwadi ezazibhalelwe abaseKhorinte zingakwazi ukwenza sizindle kabanzi ngazo, kungekhona ukuthi zabe zikhuluma ngezinkinga ezinkulu, kodwa sizobona nedlela uPawula akwazi ukubhekana nazo ngayo. Ngokuthi agcizelele kumalunga ebandla ukuba akwazi ukuzihlola, abheke indlela aziphethe ngayo, kanye nesimo sokuphila esibazungezileyo uma siqhathaniswa nokukhanya kwevangeli likaJesu Kristu, kumyalezo ophakamisa ivangeli lesiphambano. Uma sisebenzisa amazwi akhulunywa nguPawula uqobo lwakhe kwenye indawo, noma yiliphi izinga lokuphila elingekho ezingeni lalokhu okufunywa ngumyalezo wevangeli kufuneka kubhekwe njengento “eqalekisiweyo” (Galathiya 1:8, 9).

Ngesikhathi sokuphila kukaPawula, iKhorinte yabe idume ngokuceba kanye nokuhwebelana okunamandla, konke lokhu kubangelwa wukuba nechweba, namabhilidi ayimidwebo emihle, nokwakhiwa kwemikhumbi, kanye nokunye okuningi okwakhiwe ngobumba. Lelidolobha labe libaluleke ngokuba yisizinda sokuhwebelana. Noma kunjalo, lendawo yayiphinde yaziwe ngokukhohlakala kokungaziphathi kahle, nangokungahambisi kahle ezintweni eziphathelene zevangeli, kanye nezindawo ezazibekelwe ukukhoza izithixo ezahlukene.

Ngokujwayelekile, impilo yansuku zonke yaseKhorinte yabe inezimpawu zokukhohlakala kokukhonza izithixo. Lomlando kanye nesiko lokuphila kwabo kuyasisisiza ukwenza sibe nokuqonda ngalokhu okwakukhathaza kakhulu uPawula nokwakuphathelene namaKrestu ayephila kulelidolobha kanye, nomphumela wokuxhumana kwakhe kunye nabo.

Kulonyanga Ntathu esikuwo, sizobheka kabanzi izincwadi ezabhalwa nguPawula ebhalela abaseKhorinte. Kulezizahluko ezimbili ezithathekisayo eziseThestamenteni Elisha, umphostoli uPawula usilethela umyalezo wevangeli ngento ebalulekile ebufakazini kanye nasekuphileni komKrestu, nokuyisibuko lapho konke okwenzekeyo kuyothola khona ukwahlulelwa. Noma ngabe izinselelo zingakanani kumuntu ngamunye noma ebandleni lilonke libhekana kanjani nezinkinga kuloluhambo lokuya ezulwini – impendulo kukho konke ukusebenza kwethu okungaqondakali sisebenzela uKristu kuyinto efanayo kuwona wonke amaKristu: “Kuphela uJesu Kristu, lowo obethelweyo” (1 Korinte 2:2).

UJesu uyeza masinyane. Lesi yisikhathi sokuba sibemunye kuKristu, ukuze sizokwazi ukuvulela uMoya oNgcwele usebenze, siyeke indlela ebesenza ngayo ngaphambili, ngoba sisebenzisa ngokuyikho iziphiwo zikamoya, futhi siphinde sibe namava aphathekayo eNkosi yethu evusiweyo. Lesi sekuyisikhathi sokufakaza ngokuyikho njengofakazi bamaKrestu, sibandakanyeke ebuncekwini kanye nasevangelini, sikwazi ukulwa impi kamoya silwisana nabefundisa amanga, siphinde sikhule emseni, nasothandweni kanye nasebudlelwaneni. Lesi sekuyisikhathi sokubambelela kakhulu siphinde sithembeke kumyalezo wesiPhambano, njengoba incwadi kaPawula ebhalela abaseKhorinte isifundisa nathi ukuba senze ngendlela efanayo.
