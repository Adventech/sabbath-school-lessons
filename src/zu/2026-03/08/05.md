---
title:  Umzimba Ovusiweyo
date:   19/08/2026
---

Ku-1 Korinte 15:35-39, UPawulu esekhuluma kabanzi ngomzimba ovusiwe. Uqala ukukhuluma ngaloludaba ngokuthi ethule imibuzo emibili: “ ‘Abafileyo bavuswa kanjani na?’ ‘Beza benamzimba muni na?’ ” (1 Kor. 15:35, NKJV). Lemibuzo ithola ukuphendulwa ku-1 Korinte 15:36-47.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 15: 36-41. Kungabe lamavesi ayayiphendula yini imibuzo eku-1 Korinte 15:35 na?`

UPawulu uzama ukuchazela abafundi ukuthi kwenzekani ekuvukeni ngokuthi aqhathanise imifuzuselo yezinto ezintathu. Umfuziselo wokuqala (1 Korinte 15:36-38) uphawula ngokuthi umzimba ufana nembewu okudingeka ife kuqala (noma kuphele ukuba yimbewu kwayo) ukuze kuzokwenzeka isimangaliso sokuba lembuwu iphenduke isithombo. Lukhu kufundisa kuyinto esobala: ukuvuswa kuyisimanga sikaNkulunkulu. Okwesibili, umfuziselo womzimba (1 Korinte 15:39, 40) kuqhakambisa ukuthi, kulomhlaba, uNkulunkulu unikezele ngemizimba eyehlukene ezilwaneni kanye nakubantu ukuba ikwazi ukuphila kumhlaba esuke iphila kuwo. Ngokufanayo, nemizimba yethu iyofaneleka ukuba sendaweni entsha yasezulwini. Lomqondo uphinde uqhubeke ngokwenza umfuziselo womzima onenkazimulo (1 Korinte 15:40, 41), okugcizelela ukuthi inkazimulo yemizimba evusiwe iyedlula kude le imizimba ebikade ikhona, imizimba enobuthakathaka bokuwa yalapha emhlabeni.

Lomqondo ungaphinde ubonwe ngokuthi kuvezwe izimo ezine ezingefani zemizimba yethu yalapha emhlabeni esinayo njengamanje, kanye nemizimba esiyokuba nayo sesivusiwe. Imizimba yangaphambili iyimizimba yasemhlabeni, efayo, nebuthakathaka, kanye neyemvelo. Okuphikisana nalokhu, imizimba yakamuva yasezulwini, yimizimba engafiyo, nenamandla, kanti funti ingekamoya (1 Korinte 15:40-44). Lokhu akusho ukuthi akukho okuhlanganisa lemizimba yomibili. UPawulu usebenzise igama “lomzimba” kuyona yonke imizimba eyancwatshwa kanye nemizimba evusiwe okukhomba ukuxhumana kokuphila. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, ukuqhathaniswa okushiwo ngenhla kuveza isimo esingasaqhubeki, Imizimba yethu emisha (masimbonge uNkulunkulu) angeke isafana nemizimba yethu efayo esinayo njengamanje.

UPawulu akalifanisi igama “likamoya” elifanisa nokubakhona kwento engenamsebenzi. Kwenye indawo ukhuluma ukuthi uJesu “oyoguqula isimo somzimba wethu wokuthotshiswa ufane nomzimba wakhe wenkazimulo” (Filiphu 3:21). Siyokuba nemizimba yangempela, kodwa lemizimba yethu angeke iguge noma ibole. Ngoba konke esikwaziyo njengamanje ngukubola, nokugula, kanye nokufa, futhi kulikhuni ukucabanga ngempilo engenazo lezizinto, kodwa yilokhu esithenjiswe khona ngoJesu.

`Kungabe isiqiniseko sokuthi imizimba yethu iyoguqulwa ibe yimizimba engenasici kungasisiza yini ukuba siqine uma sibheka ubuthakathaka bemizimba yethu namuhla na?`
