### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 Korinte 15; Luka 24:44–47; Samb. 20:5, 6; Kol. 2:12; 2 Tim. 1:12; 1 Thes. 4:13–17.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kepha uma uKristu engavuswanga kwabafileyo, ukushumayela kwethu kuyize, nokukholwa kwenu kuyize. . . . kepha uma uKristu engavuswanga, ukukholwa kwenu akusizi lutho, nisesezonweni zenu!” (1 Korinte 15:14–17, NKJV).

Kuyamangalisa kakhulu ukuthi, ngisho nangesikhathi sokuphila kukaPawulu, kwadingeka abhekane nalabo ababephika ukuvuswa kwabantu abafileyo. Okusempeleni, ngaleso sikhathi abantu babebheka ukuthi ukufa kwenzani emzimbeni womuntu. Babazi ukuthi isidumbu siphenduka uketshezi, bese siyoma siphenduke inhlabathi, bese lokhu kugcina kungelutho. Futhi babazi ukuthi sekunesikhathi abantu befile. Okuseqinisweni, abantu abaningi bathola ukuba ngabantu abafileyo ukwedlula isikhathi abaphila ngaso.

Ukuvuswa kwabantu abafileyo kwabonakala kuyinto engekho esikhathini sasemandulo ukwedlula isikhathi samanje, nokuyindlela ababebona ngayo abantu. Futhi lokhu kwakuwudaba uPawulu ayezama ukukhuluma ngalo.

Futhi lokhu kwakubucayi, Uma uJesu engavuswanga, lokho kusho ukuthi wayengesiyena lomuntu ayethi unguyena, isiphambano sasingasho lutho, futhi nezono zethu kusho ukuthi azizange zihlawulelwe. Lokhu kusho ukuthi sasala singabantu abangenathemba. Ngenxa yokuthi iNkosi yethu yavuswa, yenyukela ezulwini, futhi izophinde ibuye ukusithatha ukuya ekhaya!

Kuleliviki sizogxila ku-1 KwabaseKorinte 15 bese sibheka izimfundiso ezikhuluma ngokuvuswa kukaKristu. Ngenxa yomthelela wendlela abahedeni ababebona ngayo izinto, abanye abantu baseKorinte babekhuluma ukuthi akukho ukuvuswa kwabantu abafileyo. Ephendula lokhu okwakushiwo, uPawulu useqinisekisa ngokuvuswa kukaKristu njengokuphela kwethemba esinalo lokusindiswa.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNcwaba 22._
