Ellen G. White, “God’s People Delivered,” pp. 635–652, in The Great Controversy.

“UbuNkulunkulu bukaKristu buyisiqinisekiso kumuntu okholwayo kokuphila okuphakade. ‘Lowo okholwa yimi,’ kwasho uJesu, ‘noma efile, uyophinde aphile futhi: futhi noma ngubani ophila ekholwa kimi akasoze afa. Kungabe uyakukholwa lokhu na?’ Lapha uKristu usebheka phambili esikhathi sokubuya kwakhe okwesibili. Lapho abafe belungile bayovuswa bengenakho ukubola, bese kuthi abalungile abaphilayo baguqulwe basiwe ezulwini ngaphandle kokubona ukufa. Isimangaliso uKristu ayezosenza, sokuvusa u-Lazaro kwabafileyo, kwabe kuyisimangaliso esasizomela ukuvuswa kwabo bonke abantu abafe belungile.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 530.

“Umhlaba waba nokuzamazama okukhulu ngesikhathi izwi leNdodana kaNkulunkulu lizwakala libiza bonke abangcwele abaleleyo. Baphendula kulokho kubizwa ngokuthi bavuke sebenemizimba yenkazimulo engafiyo, bekhala bethi, ‘Ukufa kugingiwe kwaba ngukunqoba; Kufa, kuphi ukunqoba kwakho na? Kufa luphi udosi lwakho na?’ (1 Korinte 15:55). Abangcwele abayobe bephila kanye nalabo abavusiwe bayophakamisa abaphimbo abo bememeza ngokunqoba kwabo. Leyomizimba eyathi yangena ethuneni kuyimizimba egcwele izifo nefayo, yaphuma sekuyimizimba esinokuphila okuphakade nenomfutho. Abangcwele abayobe bephila bayoguqulwa ngokushesha, ngokuphazima kweso, babuthwe ndawonye nabavusiwe, bendawonye bayohlangabeza iNkosi emoyeno. Yebo, lokhu kungukuhlangana kwenkazimulo. Abangani abathathwe ngukufa bathola ukwehlukana bathola ukuhlanganiswa, bengasophinde behlukane futhi.”—Ellen G. White, The Story of Redemption, pp. 411, 412.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Cabanga ngabantu ababengofakazi bokuzibonela ngokuvuswa kukaKristu (Izenzo 1:22, Izenzo 2:32, Izenzo 3:15, Izenzo 4:33, Izenzo 5:30–32). Singenza kanjani thina, emuva kweminyaka ezinkulungwane ezimbili kwenzeka lesi sigameko, sibe “ngofakazi” bokuvuswa kukaKristu na?`

`Ukuvuswa kukaKristu yiyona ngqikithi yomyalezo wevangeli (1 Kor. 15:1–4). Ngaphandle kokuvuswa, ukukhuluma ngokufa kukaKristu bekuzokuba yinto engenamsebenzi (1 Kor. 15:14). Ukufa kukaKristu nakho bekuzokuba yinto engenamsebenzi. Ngasiphi isizathu na? Impendulo yakho ithini ngamandla Ovuko LukaKristu na?`

`Ake ucabanga ngalokhu okuhlaba umxhwele okwashiwo nguPawulu kokuthi “uma abafileyo bengavuki, masidle, siphuze, ngokuba ngomuso sizakufa!’ ” (1 Kor. 15:32, NKJV). Kuthiwani lapha na?`

`Eklasini, akenikhulume ngesimo sabantu abafile. Kungani ku-1 Korinte 15 kungawenzi umqondo uma, ekufeni, abantu abalungile bathola ukusindiswa ngaso leso sikhathi basiwe ezulwini na?`
