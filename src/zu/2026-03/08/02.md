---
title:  Ukumemezela Ngokuvuswa KukaKristu
date:   16/08/2026
---

Ku-1 Korinte 15 uPawulu uqala ngokuthi agxile evangelini. Ukhuluma ngevangeli: (1) alishumayela kubantu baseKorinte; (2) nokungabantu abalamukela; (3) bema ngalo; baphinde (4) bathola ukusindiswa ngalo (1 Korinte 15:1, 2). Indlela okuvulwe ngayo kulungiselelwa umfundi ozofunda isahluko esilandelayo nesikhuluma ngokubaluleka kokuvuka kukakristu osindisweni lwethu (bheka ku-Roma 10:9, 10). Ukuvuswa kukaJesu kungumyalezo obalulekile wevangeli lokuthi ukufa kuphikisana nokukholwa esinakho ngoKristu.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 15:1-4; Luka 24:44-47; kanye KwabaseRoma 1:1-4. Kungabe lamavesi aqukethe muphi umyalezo ofanayo na?`

Ku-1 Korinte 15:1-4, umuntu angakwazi ukuthola umyalezo osongiwe kaPawulu. Noma ngabe kuyisiqubulo sokuthi “NgokwemiBhalo” nokuyinto ebhekiswe engxenyeni ethile eThestamenteni Elidala noma lilonke iThestemente Elidala lokho kakusho lutho. Ukufa kukaJesu nokuvuswa kwakhe kwagcwalisekisa izithembiso zikaNkulunkulu ezitholakala eThestamenteni Elidala.

`Funda ku- 1 Korinte 15:2, 11. Kungani lamavesi efaka ulwazi lokukholwa kanye nokushumayela kuqhamuke ndawonye na? Kungabe kukhona yini ukuhlobana phakathi kwakho kokubili na?`

Bonke abamemezela ngokuthi uKristu uvusiwe kudingeka baqale ngokuthi bakholwe ukuthi ukuvuswa kwakhe kuyisigameko esingumlando. Kulenkulumo eku- 1 Korinte 15:5-8 yendlalela indima ebalulekile eThestamenteni Elisha. Lamavesi anikeza ubufakazi obuqinile bokuthi uKristu wabonwa ngabantu abaningi emuva kokuba esevusiwe, iningi labo okwakungabantu ababesaphila ngesikhathi uPawulu ebhala lencwadi (1 Kor. 15:6).

Okuseqinisweni, uPawulu uzama ukuthi, hambani nizibuzele ngokwenu ukuthi yini abayibonile. Yiyona ndlela akhombisa ukuthemba ngayo ngokuvuswa kukaKristu. Lababantu babengofakazi abazibonela. Bangabantu uJesu akhuluma ngabo ukuthi: “Nina-ke ningofakazi balezi zinto” (Luka 24:48).

`Yiziphi izizathu esinazo ezenza sikholwe ngokuvuswa kukaKristu na? Okunye futhi, Kungabe yiziphi ezinye izinto, okukhulunywa ngazo noma eziyimfihlo, esizikholwayo noma ngabe kasizange sizibone ngokwethu na?`
