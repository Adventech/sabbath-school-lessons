---
title:  UKristu, Ulibo Lokuqala
date:   18/08/2026
---

Uma uKristu wayengaphili,konke okwakulindelwe ngekusasa bekuzokuba yinto edumazayo (1 Korinte 15:12-19). “Kepha kalokhu uKristu uvusiwe kwabafileyo” (1 Korinte 15:20). Ukuvuka kwakhe sekuyisigameko esingumlando. Ngakho-ke singaba nesiqiniseko sokuthi bonke abathe bafa kuKristi bazovuswa ekubuyeni kwaKhe (1 Kor. 15:20–23).

`Funda ku-Korinte 15:20-23. Kungabe kusho ukuthini ukuthi uJesu “ungulibo lokuqala” na?`

Isiphetho salokhu kuphila okwenyanyekayo njengamanje kuyophetha ngokuthi kuvuswe imizimba yalabo abafile befela kuKristu (1 Korinte 15:24; Isambulo 20:5, 6). Njengo-Adamu wokudala, uKristu uzobuyisela umbuso kaNkulunkulu endaweni yawo ngokuthi aphindisele ukubuswa komhlaba kuYena (1 Korinte 15:25-28). Ngokuzithoba kukaKristu ngaphansi kukaNkulunkulu (1 Korinte 15:28) kudingeka sikuqonde lokhu ngesimo esifanayo sokuhlobana ngokwenza phakathi kuka-Adamu noKristu. Njengoba u-Adamu wokugcina wohlelo losindiso (1 Korinte 15:45), onguJesu wazinikela ngokuphelele ekwenzeni intando kaBaba, nokuyinto ehluleka ukuyenza u-Adamu wokuqala.

Ku-1 Korinte 15:29-34, uPawulu useqala ukuveza umcabango wakhe omayelana nobuphukuphuku bokuphika ukuvuswa kukaKristu. Uqala ngokuthi asebenzise isibonelo sombhapathizo ngoba ungumfuziselo wokuhlangana komuntu okholwayo ngesikhathi ezihlanganisa noKristu ekufeni nasekuvukeni kwaKhe (Roma 6:3,4; Kolose 2:12); akuwenzi umqondo ukuphika ukwenzeka kokuvuka. Into elikhuni ukuyicabanga, ngenye indlela, yilokhu uPawulu akuchaza ngesibonelo sokuthi “pho bayakwenza njani ababhapathizelwa abafileyo” (1 Kor. 15:29).

“Kuningi okuhlongozwayo ukuchaza lenkulumo, kodwa kungcono sihumushe lenkulumo ngokuthi sibheke isinqumo esenziwa ngabanye abazobhapathizwa ukuze bezokwazi ukuzihlanganisa nezihlobo zabo ezithandiweyo esezafa uma sezivuswa. Futhi kungenzeka ukuthi isizathu sokwenza isinqumo sokubhapathizwa bekuyindlela yokubonela empilweni yabantu abathe bafa befela kuKristu. Lengcazelo ingabhekiswa kubantu abangazange babhaphathizwe endaweni yokufa kodwa ababhapathizwe ngenxa yabantu abafile.”—Carl P. Cosaert, “1 Corinthians,” Andrews Bible Commentary: New Testament (Berrien Springs, MI: Andrews University Press, 2022), p. 1652.

Okwesibili, ukuzifaka engozini yokufa kungaba yinto engenamsebenzi uma kungekho ukuvuswa kwabantu abafileyo (1 Korinte 15:30-32). Okunalokho, kungaba ngcono ukujabulela ukuphila ebumnandini balomhlaba (1 Korinte 15:32).
