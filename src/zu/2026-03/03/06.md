---
title:  Ukuphila Okubonakalisa Isiphambano
date:   16/07/2026
---

Iqiniso lokuthi akudingeki sibe ngamaqembuqembu angezwani, ekakhulu okuxhomeke kubaholi babantu, lokhu kakusho ukuthi akudingeki sisekele abaholi bethu. Kuyadingeka sikuthokozele lokhu abasenzela khona siphinde sisizane nabaholi emsebenzini webandla. UNkulunkulu wayalela abantu ukuba benze umsebenzi wokuvangela emhlabeni. Abaholi abakhombisa ukwenza lokhu bakhombisa ukuzinikela ekwenzeni lokhu okumelwe yisiphambano futhi labaholi kudingeka sibalalele siphinde sibalandele.

Konke lokhu sizokwenza ngoba yisiPhambano kuphela esinamandla okujikisa noma yikuphi ukusetshenziswa okungalungile, nokuyinto ezogqamisa ukuzinikela ezwini likaNkulunkulu. Abaholi abanoKristu bavamise ukuqhakambisa impumelelo yokuvangela kwabo bayibhekise kuNkulunkulu kuphela. Ekushumayeleni kukaJesu eselapha emhlabeni, naYe wabhekisa inkazimulo kuNkulunkulu (Johane 17:4).

Ngokusho kukaPawule, ubufakazi bamaKristu athembekile kudingeka kusekelwe evangelini lesiPhambano. IsiPhambano sembula ukuhlakanipha kanye namandla kaNkulunkulu asindisayo. Ngesikhathi esifanayo, isiPhambano siveza ukuhlakanipha kwabantu njengento engubuwula. Encwadini ka-1 Korinte 4:1-13, uPawule ukubeka kucace ukuthi ukushumayela ivangeli lesiPhambano kuyinto eyisimo esinjani. Okukuqala, uveza ukuthi nguNkulunkulu obekela amaKristu izinga lokuba ngabaphathi abasesimweni esinjani (1 Korinte 4:1-5). Okwesibili, Ubakhombisa isimo sokuthi ukuhlupheka kuluphawu lwabashumayeli abangamaKristu angempela (1 Korinte 4:9; 11-13). Iphuzu lesibili kudingeka sigxile kakhulu kulo.

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 11:23-28 no Kolose 1:24. Kungabe lokhu kusifundisa ukuthini mayelana nengcazelo yokuhlupheka ngenxa kaKristu na?`

Abaholi abangamaKrestu alandela ezinyathelweni zikaJesu ngokuthi bavume ukuhlupheka ngenxa kadade nomzalwane, futhi noba ngabe lokhu kungaholela ekubeni babulawe ngenxa yevangeli abaliphetheyo. UPawulu wakhombisa isibonelo ngaye kanye no-Apolisi wathi; “banjengabanqunyelwe ukufa”(1 Korinte 4:9). Bavezwe njengabantu abangenakho ukudla namanzi, futhi bafana nabantu “abahambaze, nabamuhluzwa, beyimihambima” (1 Korinte 4:11). Okunye ababhekana nakho, “siyakhandleka, siyethukwa, siyazingelwa siphinde senziwe kungathi siyinsila yezwe” (1 Korinte 4:12, 13). Okunye futhi, baphinde bathola ukubhinqwa njengabantu baseKorinte abacebile, nabangamakhosi, nezihlakaniphi kanye nabantu abakhethekile (1 Korinte 4:8, 10), uPawulu uveza ukuthi ukuzikhukhumeza akudingeki kunikwe indawo ebuholini bangempela bobuKrestu, ngoba ukwenza lokhu kuyisihlava esitshala ukuhlukana ebandleni (1 Korinte 4:6).

`Kungabe uhlupheke kangakanani ngenxa kaKristu, kunoma yikuphi okwenzayo ebandleni na? Yisiphi isifundo esingasithola empendulweni yakho na?`
