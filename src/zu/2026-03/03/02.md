---
title:  Inkinga Yamaqembu Ebandleni
date:   12/07/2026
---

Isixwayiso sikaPawule sokuthi “kungabikho ukwahlukana phakathi kwenu, kodwa nipheleliswe emqondweni munye nasekuboneni kunye” (1 Korinte 1:10), yiyonanto egqamile kuzona zozine izahluko zokuqala kwabaseKorinte bokuqala. Okuseqinisweni, abafundi abaningi beBhayibheli bayavumelana ngokuthi ubumbano yisona sihloko esenza kube nobumbano kuzona zonke izinto ezitholakala zindawonye kulencwadi.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 1:12-17. Kungabe lamavesi asisiza kanjani ukwazi ukuqonda ngokuthi kuyinto embi kangakanani ukudala amaqoqwana angezwani kubaholi bendawo na? Kungabe yisiphi isisombululo sikaPawule kulenkinga na?`

UPawule usebenzisa amazwi aqosheme ukukhombisa ukungezwani kumalunga ebandla laseKorinte. Usebenzisa igama “lokwahlukana” (1 Korinte 1:10) “nokuphikisana” (1 Korinte 1:11). Igama lokungezwani lisetshenziswa nakwezinye izindawo eThestamenteni Elisha ukuchaza ukwahlukana kwemibono okungaholela ekuxabaneni. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, igama elithi (ukuphikisana) izikhathi eziningi litholakala ohlwini lwezinto ezimbi okungafanele zenziwe ngabantu abangamaKrestu.

`Funda KwabaseRoma 1:29; Roma 13:13; 1 Korinte 3:3; 2 Korinte 12:20 kanye naku-Galathiya 5:20. Kungabe yiziphi ezinye izono ezitholakala ohlwini (“lokuphikisana,” “lokulwisana”) na? Kungabe lokhu kusho ukuthini kithina mayelana nokuthi kuyinto embi kanganani na?`

Ukungezawni ebandleni laseKorinte kwavela obala- ngesikhathi kukhona ukumangalelana kwabazalwane ezinkantolo (1 Korinte 6:1-3). UPawule wabatshela ukuthi: “Ngisho lokhu ukuba nibe namahloni,”(1 Korinte 6:5), nokuyinto eyabe iphathelene nokumangalelana ezinkantolo phakathi kwamalunga ebandla. Okuseqinisweni, kabazange bakubeke eceleni ukungezwani kwabo ngisho nangesikhathi bejabulela ukuba sesesidlweni seNkosi (1 Kor. 11:17–22).

Inkinga yokungabi nalo ubumbano phakathi kwamalunga ebandla kwabe kuyinto eyethusayo, kungakho uPawulu wayenokukhathazeka ngalokhu, wabona ukuthi lokhu kuyinkinga yokuqala okufuneka ayixazulule kulencwadi ayibhekise kubantu baseKorinte.

`Funda futhi ku-1 Korinte 1:12-27. Bese uyazindla ngokuthi kungabe lamavesi asisiza kanjani ukuqonda ngobungozi bokuba namaqembu angezwani nokuyinto embi ekwenzeni ubumbano ebandleni. Lingenza kanjani ibandla lakho ukuvimbela inkinga efana nalena ukuba ingenzeki na?`
