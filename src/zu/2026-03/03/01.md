### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 Kor. 1:12–17, Rom. 1:29, 1 Kor. 1:10, 1 Kor. 3:1–4, Filip. 2:5–8, 2 Kor. 11:23–28, Kol. 1:24.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kepha ngiyanincenga, bazalwane, ngegama leNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu ukuba nikhulume zwilinye nonke, kungabikho ukwahlukana phakathi kwenu, kodwa nipheleliswe emqondweni munye nasekuboneni kunye” (1 Korinte 1:10).

Labo abanaka impilo yasendle bayazi ukuthi ezinye izilwane ziphila ngamaqoqwana, nemihlambi, noma zihamba ndawonye, zikwenza lokhu ngamaqoqwana amakhulu ngokuhlukana. Kusukela ezimpisini kuze kufile kofishi bamahlengethwa, kuze kuye nasemaviyweni ezintuthwane, zonke lezilwane zithanda ukuhlala ndawonye. Izimfene ezinkulu ezaziwa ngokuhlala ndawonye zikhombisa ukuzwana okukhulu, nazo kwesinye isikhathi zihamba ngamaqoqwana ezimfene ezingu 15 kuya ku-150. Noma kunjalo, lobu budlelwano abuhlali buhamba kahle, kwesinye isikhathi lezilwane zinokulwa zindawonye.

Nabantu kwesinye isikhathi benza into efanayo; okusho ukuthi, abagcini ngokukhombisa ukuthi baphila ndawonye, kodwa kwesinye isikhathi bayalwa behlezi ndawonye kulawo maqoqwana. Lokhu kuyinto efanayo nasezinkonzweni esikhonza kuzo! Kubakhona ukungezwani, izikhathi eziningi kubakhona ukubanga ubuholi. Kwesinye isikhathi, ukungezwani kubangwa ngukuthi kubekhona ukungavumelani kwabantu bebodwa.

Ngabe usuke wakubona lokhu ebandleni lakho na?

Uma kunjalo, kusho ukuthi uyasiqonda isimo uPawulu ayebhekene naso eKorinte. Kuleliviki sizoke sibheke encwadini yama-1 Korinte 1-4, lapho umphostoli uPawulu ayebhekene nenkinga yokulwisana okwakusebandleni nokuthi yini okwakudingeka bayenze ukunqoba lokhu kulwisana, ngokuthi babemunye kuKristu.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNtulikazi 18._
