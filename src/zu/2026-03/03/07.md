Ellen G. White, “The Training of the Twelve,” pp. 17–24, in The Acts of the Apostles.

“Ukuba munye nokuba nobumbano kubantu abayinsali ekholelwa eqinisweni likaNkulunkulu labobantu basuke beletha ubufakazi obunamandla emhlabeni bokuthi baphethe iqiniso, futhi bangabantu abehlukile, njengabantu abakhethiwe bakaNkulunkulu. Ubunye kanye nobumbano kuxoshela kude isitha, futhi uyaphikelela ekutheni akusoze kwenzeka kubekhona ukungezwani. Iqiniso elikhona njengamanje, nokuyinto abayikholwayo ehliziyweni nephinde ibe ngubufakazi ekuphila, kwenza babe ngabantwana bakaNkulunkulu, bese beba nomthelela onamandla ekuphileni.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 1, p. 327.

“UNkulunkulu uholela abantu ukuba bamele ubumbano kusisekelo seqiniso laphakade. Ukristu wazinikela emhlabeni ukuze ezokwazi ‘ukuhlanza abantu ukuze bezokuba ngabantu abehlukile abangasohlangothini lwakhe, begcwele ukwenza imisebenzi emihle.’ Lokhu kuhlanzwa okwenzekayo kuhlose ukususa ebandleni konke ukungalungi kanye nomoya wokungezwani nokulwisana, ukuze bezokwakheka esikhundleni sokucekelana phansi, bese begxila kakhulu ekwenzeni umsebenzi omkhulu obekwe phambi kwabo. UNkulunkulu uhlela ukuba abantu bakhe beze kuye benokubumbano kokukholwa. Umthandazo owenziwa nguJesu ngaphambi kokuba abethelwe esiphambanweni kwabe kungukuthi abafundi bakhe bangaba munye, njengoba naye wayemunye noBaba omthumileyo. Lomthandazo omuhle kangaka nokwazi ukuthinta abantu nokwazi ukufinyelela nasezikulwaneni ezalandela eminyakani eminingi eseyadlula, nokungumthandazo obalulekile nanamuhla; ngoba amazwi awakhuluma ayethi: “Angikhulekeli laba kuphela, kodwa nalabo abazokholwa kiMi ngamazwi abazowasho.’”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 4, p. 17.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Ngaphambi kokuphela komsebenzi ayewenza lapha emhlabeni, uJesu wathandazela ukuba kube nobumbano, “ukuba nabo babe munye . . . ukuze izwe lazi ukuba wena ungithumile” (Johane 17:21–23, NASB). Kungani ubumbano kuKristu kuyinto engamandla ekukhulumeni iqiniso uNkulunkulu athume iNdodana yaKhe ukuba izosindisa lelizwe na? Uma seyamanisa nalokhu obekushiwo, kungani ukungabibikho kobumbano kuyisithiyo ekusebenzeni kwebandla na?`

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 4:9-13 bese unaka kakhulu ukuthi abaphostoli babebukwa kanjani kulamavesi. Kungabe indlela ababebukwa ngayo abaphostoli iyaphikisana yini nesimo sokuba ngumholi kulomhlaba esiphila kuwo na? Kungabe lamavesi asifundisa ukuthini mayelana nokuthi lehluke kanjani izinga elibekwe nguNkulunkulu kanye nezinga elibekwe yilizwe na?`

`Encwadini ka-1 Korinte 4:16, uPawulu ugqugquzela abantu base-Korinte ukuba balingise yena. Kungabe ungazimisela yini ukulingisa ubuholi babantu na? Kungabe ukulingisa umholi kwehlukile yini nokumphakamisa lowomholi kube kungafanele futhi kwenzeka ngendlela enobungozi na?`
