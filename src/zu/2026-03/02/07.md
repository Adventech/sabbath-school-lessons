Ellen G. White, “Calvary,” pp. 741–757, in The Desire of Ages.

“Emqondweni wabantu abaningi abaphilayo namuhla, isiphambano saseKhalivari sizungezwe wukukhumbula okungcwele. Imihlangano engcwele yeyafaniswa nesimo sombona wokubethelwa kukaKristu. Kodwa ngesikhathi sokuphila kukaPawule isiphambano sasithathwa njengemizwa yencindezi kanye nokwesaba. Ukubambelela njengoMsindisi wabantu owakwazi ukubhekana nokufa kwesiphambano, lokhu kuzozenzakalela uzizwe ulahliwe futhi ungafunwa muntu.

“UPawulu wayazi ukuthi umyalezo wakhe bazowuthatha kanjani abantu abangamaJuda kanye namaGriki aseKorinte. . . . KumaJuda ayezolalela lomyalezo iningi labo lalizothukutheliswa yilomyalezo uPawulu ayezowumemezela. KumaGreki kuhlawumbiselwa ukuthi amazwi kaPawulu ayezokuba ngamazwi angamukeleki. Babezombuka njengomuntu onomqondo ongaphilile kahle ngokuthi uzama ukubakhombisa isiphambano ngoba lokhu kungeke kwenzeke kwenyuse izinga lokuphila noma kulethe ukusindiswa kwabantu.

“Kodwa kuPawulu, isiphambano sabe siyinto ebaluleke kakhulu. Selokhu abanjwa emsebenzini wakhe ayewenza wokushushisa abantu ababe ngabalandeli balowo owabethelwa waseNazaretha. Akazange esaguquka ekuletheni inkazimulo esiphambanweni. Ngalesosikhathi wathola ukumbulelwa uthando lukaNkulunkulu olungapheliyo, njengoba kwembulwa ngokufa kukaKristu; futhi kwaphinde kwambuleka uguquko oluthile empilweni yakhe, nokwashintsha zonke izinhlelo nalokhu ayefisa ukukwenza kwahambisana nokwasezulwini. Wazazela ngamava abanawo ukuthi uma isoni samukela uthando lukaBaba, njengoba kwembulwa ngokunikelwa ngeNdodasna yaKhe, wase ezinikela ekwenzeni intando kaNkulunkulu, lokhu kwenza kubekhona uguquko enhliziyweni kaPawulu, ukusuka lapho uKristu waba yikho konke kukhona konke okwenzekayo.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, pp. 245, 246.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Esensimini yase-Getshemane, uJesu wathi, “Baba, uma kungenzeka, akudlule kimi lesi sitsha” (Mat. 26:39, NKJV). Kungabe lomthandazo usho ukuthini mayelena nobukhulu benani uJesu alikhokha ngesikhathi esesiphambanweni na?`

`UPawule uthi, “Ngokuba ubuwula bukaNkulunkulu buhlakaniphilekunabantu” (1 Kor. 1:25, NKJV). Kungabe kuyinto enjani ukuhlakanipha kukaNkulunkulu nokwehluke kakhulu ekuhlakanipheni komuntu na?`

`Umyalezo wokubethelwa kukaKristu wabe uyisithiyo esithile kubaJuda kuphinde kube ngubuphukuphuku kumaGreki. Kungabe yiziphi izihloko esingashumayela ngazo namuhla nezingaletha umphumela ofanayo noshiwo ngenhla kubantu abaphilayo kulesisikhathi esikusona, nokuthi kungani kuzokuba nalomphumela na?`

`Pawulu uthi “Umuntu wemvelo kakwamukeli okukaMoya, sichazaokukamoya ngokomoya” (1 Kor. 2:14, NKJV). Ngakho-ke, singakhuluma kanjami ngoJesu kubantu nokungenza zithinteke izinhliziyo zabo na? Noma mhlawumbe, ukwenza kwethu kukwazi ukufinyelela kubona na?`
