### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 Kor. 1:17–31, Kol. 1:20, 1 Pet. 2:24, Izenzo 13:16–47, 1 Kor. 2:1–5.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ngokuba izwi lesiphambano kwababhubhayo lingubuwula, kepha kithina esisindiswayo lingamandla kaNkulunkulu” (1 Korinte 1:18, NIV).

USisero, owayengumbhali kanye nembongi engumhedeni yase-Roma, watshela abantu base-Roma ukuthi umqondo wokwazi ngesiphambano bawubuke njengendlela yokuzihlukumeza, baqhelelane nokucabanga ngaso. Yize u-Sisero wafa eminyakeni engu-50 ngaphambi kokuba uJesu azalwe, lokhu akukhuluma kwakuveza ukucabanga kwabantu base-Roma ngesiphambano. Indlela ababesibuka ngayo isiphambano yabe iyimbi kakhulu ngalendlela yokuthi babengafisi nokucabanga ngaso.

Ngokuphambene nalokhu, uPawule wabhala ukuthi: “Ngokuba izwi lesiphambano . . . lingamandla kaNkulunkulu” (1 Kor. 1:18, NIV). KuPawule, Isiphambano siyithuluzi lokubuyisana phakathi kukaNkulunkulu nabantu (Efesu 2:16; Kolose 1:20), nokuyisibonakaliso esikhulu kakhulu sokutshengisa ukuzithoba kukaJesu (Efesu 2:8), futhi kuphinde kube yindawo lapho kwahlawulelwa khona amacala ethu (Kolose 2:14).

Isiphambano siyimpendulu kaPawulu ezinkingeni zase-Korinte. Akudingeki sibheke kude uma sifunda incwadi ye 1-korinte, sizokwazi ukubona ukuthi uPawulu wayekhathazekile ngezinto ezisemqoka ezaziyimbangela yokungezwani ebandleni. UPawulu waba nokudideka emuva kokuba ebabingelele (1 Korinte 1:1-3) waphinde wadideka emuva kwenkonzo yokubonga (1 Korinte 1:4-9), konke lokhu kwaba yinto yokuqala akhuluma ngayo (1 Korinte 1:10-17). Kuleliviki sizobheka umyalezo onamandla wesiphambano njengempendulo eyaletha isixazululo kulenkinga kanye nezinye izinkinga e-Korinte.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNtulikazi 11._
