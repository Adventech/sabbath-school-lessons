---
title:  Ivangeli LeSiphambano
date:   05/07/2026
---

UPawulu uthi umyalezo wesiphambano ungamandla kaNkulunkulu kithina. Akumangalisi ukuthi “uJesu Kristu, lowo obethelwe esiphambanweni,” (1 Korinte 2:2) iyona ngqikithi yokushumayela kwakhe.

`Funda 1 Korinte 1 :17-31. Yikuphi lokhu okubalulekile uPawulu akhuluma ngakho lapha na?`

Ku-1 Korinte 1;17-31, uPawulu uqhathanisa ubuphukuphuku bokucabanga kwabantu ebuqhathanisa nokuhlakanipha kukaNkulunkulu. Isiphambano sinamandla okuveza ubuthakathaka obukhulu bomuntu siphinde siveze ubuhle bukaNkulunkulu. Lendawana eku- 1 Korinte yethulwe ngamagama atholakala ku-1 Korinte 1:17. “Ngokuba isiphambano sikaKristu akufanele senziwe ize,” umyalezo wevangeli lesiphambano kufuneka kube yikhona esikwaziyo ekushumayeleni kwethu (funda futhi 1-Korinte 2:2).

UPawule uthi wayengathunyelwe ukuba azobhapathiza, kodwa wayethunyelwe ukushumayela ivangeli lesiphambano. Lomyalezo udinga sibheke izinto ezimbili ezibalulekile. Okokuqala, uthi “ukuthunywa” nokuyigama elifanayo nalelo lokuba ngumphostoli. Ngakho-ke umsebenzi omkhulu kamphostoli uPawulu kwabe kungukushumayela ivangeli. Okwesibili, ukukhuluma kuka-Pawulu ngokubhapathiza kwakungasho ukuthi ukubhapathiza akubalulekile noma akusiyona into esemqoka ukwedlula ukushumayela, kodwa okunalokho, wakhombisa ukugcona labo ababebona kubalulekile ukuthi abantu babhapathizwe nokungahambisani nalokhu okusemqoka, okunguJesu, nokuyikho okuyimbangela yokuba babhapathizwe.

Ngoku “hlakanipha kwamazwi” (1 Korinte 1 :17), UPawule akakhulumi ngokuthi ukuba ligagu lokukhuluma kuyinto embi. Kodwa wayezama ukuveza ukuthi ukucabanga komuntu akufanele kuphazamise umyalezo wesiphambano. Ngesikhathi ese-Athene uPawulu, wazama ukusebenzisa ukukhuluma okunomqondo, nokukhuluma kobucwepheshe bokuhlaziya, nobugagu bokubeka kahle izinto. Konke lokhu kwaba nezithelo ezincane kakhulu. Ngakho-ke “wakhetha ukulandela olunye uhlelo lokusebenza e-Korinte, ezama ngokusemandleni ukuba enze abantu banake lokhu akushoyo bangabi nokucabanga okwehlukile. “Wakhetha ukunganaki ukuphikisana nabantu futhi akangavuma ukulokhu ebalisa, kodwa wakhetha ‘ukuzenza umuntu ongazi luthi’ ngokwenzeka kuma-Korinte. Waqoka ukuhlenga uJesu Kristu, nokunguYe owabethelwa esiphambanweni.’ ”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 244.

`Kungabe iyiphi indlela lapho ukukhuluma okuningi kungaphazamisa umyalezi wesiphambano na? Kungani ukufakaza ngoJesu Kristu nokunguYe owabethelwa nokuyinto eyenza kube nezithelo eziningi e-Korinte ukwedlula ukucabanga okuphusile, nokuhlaziya ngokwesayensi, kanye nokukhuluma kobugagu okwenzeka e-Athene na? Kungenzeka yini ukuthi kubekhona izikhathi lapho ukucabanga okuphusile, nobugagu bokukhuluma kanye nokuhlakaziya izinto ngobuchwepheshe kukwazi ukusiza ekumemezeleni ivangeli na?`
