---
title:  Ubuwula Kulabo Ababhubhayo
date:   06/07/2026
---

Uma kuqhathaniswa ubuwula bokucabanga kwabantu buqhathaniswa nokuhlakanipha kukaNkulunkulu, uPawulu ukubeka athi “Ngokuba izwi lesiphambano kwababhubhayo lingubuwula” (1 Korinte 1:18). Lokhu kungokokuqala ezintweni eziyisithupha aziphawulayo uma ekhuluma ngobuwula ku-1 Korinte 1:18-31.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 1:20, 21, 23, 25, no-27. Kungabe lishiwo kanjani igama lobuwula nokuyinto engasisiza ukuba siqonde ukuthi wayethini uPawule ngomyalezo wesiPhambano athi singubuwula kulabo ababhubhayo na?`

Igama lobuwula livela izikhathi ezinhlanu eThestamenteni Elisha, konke lokhu kutholakala e-Thestamenteni elisha (1-Korinte 1 18, 21, 23; 1-Korinte 2:14; 1-Korinte 3:19). Lokhu kanye namanye amagama avela ndawonye izikhathi eziningi eThestamenteni Elisha, futhi ingxenye enkulu yalokhu kukhuluma kwashiwo kubuphostoli bukaPawule. EThestamenteni Elisha lamagama avamile ukuqhamuka endawonye etholakala KwabaseKorinte bokuqala.

Ubuwula uPawule akhuluma ngabo ku-1-Korinte 1:18, 23 abuhlangene kangako nokungabikho kokuhlakanipha kwengqondo kodwa kushiwo indlela yokuziphatha kwabantu kanye nendlela abazindla ngayo, nokushoda ngokuthatha izinqumo ngokuyikho, kuphinde kudale ukulwisana noNkulunkulu. Lokhu kuyincazelo enhle eyenza akhulume kabanzi ngalesisihloko kuyona yonke incwadi yama -Korinte okuqala.

Ake ucabange ngesimo ayekusona uPawule ngesikhathi ekulelidolobha. Ufikela endaweni eyabe izigabisa ngalokhu ababethi kungulwazi kanye nokuhlakanipha kuhlangene nesiko elingaqondakali. Ngenxa yalokhu kucabanga, uPawulu usekhuluma ngomJuda wase-Galile, uJesu waseNazaretha, owabethelwa esiphambanweni ebethelwa ngamaRoma, waphinde wavuswa kwabafileyo-kwenzeka konke lokhu ukuze angagcini ngokuhlawulela izono zabo kuphela, kodwa ehlawulela izono zelizwe lonke. Kungabe lomuntu wayekuqondile lokhu na? Kungabe wayedlalisa ubani ngalokhu na? Lokhu kwabe kungesiyo into engukukhuluma kobuciko futhi kungeyona into yokubhekwa ngobuciko bokukhuluma; kubonakala kufana nokuphambana umqondo, nokuyinto engasile, nokuyinto umuntu ohlakaniphile nofundile kubantu baseKorinte angayithatha njengento ebalulekile.

Njengoba lomyalezo kaPawule wawuzwakala ungubuwula kubuhedeni, kubantu abaningi abangamaJuda lomyalezo wesiphambano wezwakala ungubuwula obedlulele. Kungabe abantu abangamaJuda babelindele uMesiya ozobulawa ngamaRoma na? UMesiya ababemlindele kwakufanele anqobe umbuso wamaRoma, angabi ngumuntu obethelwa ngamaRoma.

Ngakho-ke, kusuka ekuqaleni, uPawulu wayekhuluma okuningi okwakumcindezela endaweni yase-korinte. Noma kunjalo, yize kunalezinkinga, imiphefumulo eminingi yamaJuda kanye neyabezizwe- yathola ukuzuzwa evangelini.

Kungabe yini umyalezo kulokhu na?

Noma ngabe kuwuhlobo luni lokuphikiswa esihlangabezana nakho, uNkulunkulu uhlala enabantu abazimisele ukuzwa iqiniso. Kudingeka sibe ngabantu abakulungele ukusentshenziswa nguNkulunkulu ukuze sizokwazi ukufinyelela kubantu noma ngabe bakuyiphi indawo, ngisho nasezindaweni ezibonakala zizimbi kakhulu, noma izindawo ezimbi ukwedlula indawo yase-Korinte.
