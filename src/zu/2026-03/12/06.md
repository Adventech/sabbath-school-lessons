---
title:  Ubizo Kubantu Abangaphendukanga
date:   17/09/2026
---

Ku-2 Korinte 12:14-13:10, kulamavesi uPawulu utshela ibandla ngokuvakasha kwakhe okwesithathu (2 Korinte 12:14, 2 Korinte 13:1). Usekhombisile ukuthi akamncinyane uma eziqhathanisa nabaphostoli bamanga futhi unokuzithemba ukubuya eKorinte okwesithathu njengoba ezophinde azame ukubuyisa amalunga ebandla angaphendukile. Okuseqiniseni, lokhu kwabe kuyinto yokuqala esemqoka yokuvakasha kwakhe. Konke uPawulu akwenzayo nalokhu akukhulumile kwakuhlose ukuba kwakhiwe ibandla ngakho (2 Korinte 12:19).

`Funda ku- 2 Korinte 12:20, 21. Yiziphi izono ezazilimaza isimo sezikamoya ebandleni laseKorinte na?`

Uhla lwezono olutholakala ku-2 Korinte 12: 20, 21 kuseyizono ezifanayo nalezo ezitholakala nakwezinye izincwadi ezabhalwa nguPawulu (Roma 1:29-31; Galathiya 5:19-21). Izono ezimbili zokuqala zitholakala ku- 1 Korinte 3:3, nokuyilapho uPawulu ekhuluma ngokuba nomhawu kanye nokuphikisana okwatholakala kudlangile kumalunga ebandla laseKorinte. UPawulu unokwesabela ukuthi izinto kungenzeka zingabi nomehluko nangesikhathi esebuya ukubavakashela okwesithathu. Ukhuluma ukuthi, “ ‘ngokuba nginovalo lokuthi ekufikeni kwami mhlawumbe ngizakunifumana ningenjengalokhu engikuthandayo nami ngifunyanwe yinina nginjengalokho eningakuthandiyo” (2 Korinte 12:20). Lokhu kusho ukuthi esikhundleni sokuba kubhekwane nobubi “ngobumnene nangokuthobeka kukaKristu” (2 Korinte 10:1), wakhetha ukuthi njengamanje “alungele ukujezisa ukungalaleli konke” (2 Korinte 10:6).

Into eyabe imkhathaza kakhulu kwabe kungukuthi amanye amalunga ayephatheka ezenzweni “zokungcola, nobufebe, namanyala” futhi bengaphendukanga (2 Korinte 12:21). Futhi kwabe kuyizono ezazifana nalezi ezabe ziyimbangela yokungezwani ebandleni.

Okulandelayo, uPawulu usegxila ezintweni okufuneka zenziwe yibandla ukuze lizokwazi ukunqanda ukwenziwa kwalobubi (2 Korinte 13:1-4). Ukuba buthakathaka akusona isizathu zokuphila impilo yokwenza ububi. Kukhona amandla angatholakala ukusiza abantu abafisa ukuphila impilo yokunqoba (2 Korinte 13:4). Isizathu sokuba kubekhona abantu abathile eKorinte abaphila impilo yobufebe kungubufakazi bokuthi amandla kaNkulunkulu ayengekho ezimpilweni zabo. UPawulu wayefisa ukuba baphenduke ukuze bezothola amandla azobaholela ekulaleleni. Ukubanquma ebandleni kwakuyisinyathelo sokugcina ayengasithatha. Wakhuluma ukuthi, “siyakhuleka kuNkulunkulu ukuba ningenzi lutho olubi, kepha ukuba nina nikwenze okuhle. Sikhulekela nalokhu, ukupheleliswa kwenu” (2 Korinte 13:7). Umthandazo omuhle kangaka! Uyabacela ukuba bazihlole ukuze bezokwazi ukuzibona besekukholweni.

`Funda ku- 2 korinte 13:5. Kusho ukuthini ukuba sekukholweni na? Ungazazi kanjani ukuthi esekukholweni na?`
