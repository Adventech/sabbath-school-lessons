Ellen G. White, “The Laodicean Church,” p. 125, in The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, September 30, 1873.

“UNkulunkulu uhola abantu baKhe ekubeni bangaphindi benza ububi kanye namaphutha abawenzile esikhathini esedlule. Bekulokhu kwanda ukubakhona kwabefundisi bamanga abalokhu bekhuthaza ukwenziwa kwezimfundiso zobubi kanye nokwenza izinto ezingcolile, futhi besebenzela izinkambiso ezingamanga kuyona yonke indawo ekhona, besebenza ngendlela yokukhwabanisa, benza ngakho konke okusemandleni ukukhohlisa, uma kwenzeka, bakhohlise nabantu abakhethiweyo.”—Ellen G. White, in The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, January 7, 1904.

“UNkulunkulu uhlela ukuthi imibono yethu ikwazi ukuvivinywa, ukuze sizokwazi ukubona isidingo sokuhlolisisa zonke izinto ezikhona kubhekwe ukuthi zisekukholweni noma kazikho ekukholweni. Abantu abaningi abakholelwa eqinisweni sebehlezi ngokukhululeka, bekhuluma ukuthi, “ngicebile, futhi nginakho konke, futhi kangidingi lutho.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 36.

“Abantu abathokozela ukukholwa okuphambukile, uma sekubekwa obala iqiniso, kuphinde kube ngukuthi sebeletha izimfundiso zabo ezwini likaNkulunkulu, baphinde bangalifundi izwi likaNkulunkulu ngokukhanyiseleka ngezimfundiso zabo, ukuze bezokwazi ukubona ukuthi ukucabanga kwabo kulungile, angeke bazithole behamba ebumnyameni futhi bengakwazi ukubona, noma bathokozele ukuphambuka. Abantu abaningi banika amagama aseMbhalweni ingcazelo ehambisana nalokhu abasuke bekucabanga, bese bezithola sebephambuka baphinde bakhohlise nabanye abantu ngalokhu abakwenzile kokuhlanekezela izwi likaNkulunkulu. Njengoba sesizimisele ukufunda izwi likaNkulunkulu, kufuneka sikwenze lokho ngenhliziyo ethobekile. Konke ukuzicabangela, nakho konke ukuthanda ukubona umsuka wento, kudingeka kubekwe eceleni. Imibono ebikade siyithokozela akudingeki ukuba ithathwe njengento engenakwenzeka.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, pp. 36, 37.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Funda futhi ku-2 Korinte 10:1-6. Kungabe yiliphi isu likaPawulu lokubhekana “nezimpi” zikamoya ukusimamisa iqiniso likaNkulunkulu, kuphinde kube ngukuthi, singakusebenzisa kanjani lokhu ekulweni izimpi zikamoya esibhekene nazo na?`

`IBhayibheli lithi ngaphambi kokufika komuhla wokuphela, baningi abefundisi bamanga abazozama ukuheha abantu ukuba baqhelelane neqiniso. Lingenza kanjani ibandla lakho ukuvimbela amalunga alo ekubeni badukiswe ngabefundisi bamanga okungenzeka ukuthi bakhona nasebandleni lakho na? Kungani lokhu kuyinto ebalulekile ukuze kugcwaliseke umsebenzi wokuvangela kwebandla na?`

`Kungani uPawulu akuthola kubalulekile ukuthi azibonge ngohla olude lokuhlupheka kwakhe (2 Kor. 11:16–33) na? Okunye futhi, kusho ukuthini “ukuzibonga eNkosini” na?`

`Kungani kubalulekile ukuba amalunga ebandla akwazi ukuzihlola ukuze ezokwazi ukubona ukuthi asesokholweni (2 Kor. 13:5) na? Kungabe kwenza muphi umehluko lokhu na?`
