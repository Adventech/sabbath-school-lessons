---
title:  Ukuvezwa Kwabafundisi Bamanga
date:   15/09/2026
---

IThestamente Elisha ligcwele izixwayiso eziningi ezilwisana nabefundisi bamanga emphakathini wamaKrestu. UJesu uqobo lwaKhe wabaxwayisa abafundi bakhe ngento efana nalena (Mathewu 7:15-20). Nabaphostoli babenza abantu ukuba banake lobubi (Gal. 1:6–9, 1 Tim. 6:3–5, 2 Pet. 2:1–3).

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 11: 1-15. Kungabe uPawulu uyamukela kanjani inselelo yokubhekana nabefundisi bamanga na?`

UPawulu wakubeka kwaba sobala ukusebenza kwabefundisa bamanga. Ngesikhathi esifanayo, wakukhombisa ukuthi umsebenzi wakhe wokuvangela wakhelwe kusisekelo esinguKristu. Wafanisa ibandla laseKorinte nomakoti waphinde wazibona yena enjengobaba, obhekene nomthwalo wokuletha umlobokazi kuKristu (2 Korinte 11:2). Konke lokhu ukwenza ngoba elithanda ibandla (2 Korinte 11:11). Yingakho wayengathandi ukuba ngumthwalo ebandleni ngokuthi akhokhelwe ngomsebenzi ayewenza, yize wayenelungelo lokucela ukondliwa yibandla (2 Korinte 11:7-12).

Ngakolunye uhlangothi, “abaphostoli abaphambili kakhulu” (lapha kwakushiwo abafundisi bamanga nokuyinto eyabe iyindida) babefaniswa nenyoka eyakhohlisa u-Eva (2 Korinte 11:3). Njengoba kwenza uSathane ensimini yase-Edeni, abafundisi bamanga endaweni yaseKorinte bavezwa njengabantu abangabakhohlisi kanye nabakhohlakele (2 Korinte 11:3, 4). Into eyabe ikhathaza kakhulu uPawulu kwabe kungukuthi labafundisi bangadukisa abantu baseKorinte ekukhonzeni kanye nasekuthembekeni kwabo kuKristu.

Izitha zazishumayela ivangeli elehlukile kunaleli elalishunyayelwa nguPawulu. Beshumayela omunye uJesu kanye nelinye ivangeli elehlukile (2 Korinte 11:4). Lokhu kubonisa ukuthi akusibona bonke abantu abashumayela ngoJesu onguNkulunkulu nokuyilona thuluzi okufuneka sishumayele sisebenzisa lona. Ngenxa yalokhu, uJesu uqobo lwaKhe wathi, “ ‘Asibo bonke abashoyo kimi ukuthi: Nkosi, Nkosi, abayakungena embusweni wezulu’ “ (Mathewu 7:21). Encwadini yamaGalathiya 1:6-9, uPawulu uthi noma ngubani oshumayela ivangeli elihlukile lowomuntu usuke ezilethela isiqalekiso kuye, kodwa abanye abantu baseKorinte bakubekezelela lokhu kuphambuka.

UPawulu wabeka obala abaphostoli bamanga ngokubabiza ngokuthi “izisebenzi ezikhohlisayo, beziguqula abaphostoli bakaKristu” (2 Korinte 11:13). Bazifihla ngokuthi bazibize ngabaphostoli bakaKristu, njengoba kwenza “uSathane uqobo eziguqula ingelosi yokukhanya.’ ‘uma nezikhonzi zakhe ziziguqula izikhonzi zokulunga’ “ (2 Korinte 11:14, 15). Lesi yisimo esidabukisayo: abantu abathi basebenzela uKristu kodwa besebenza njengezithunywa zikaSathane. UPawulu usephetha ngalokhu akucabangayo kokuthi “ezikuphela kwazo kuyakubanjengokwemisebenzi yazo” (2 Korinte 11:15).

Bhekisisa indlela alwisana ngayo nokuphambuka okwenzeka ebandleni! Kungabe kufuneka kusitshele ukuthini lokhu na?
