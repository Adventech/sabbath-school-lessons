---
title:  Impi Kamoya
date:   13/09/2026
---

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 10:1-10. Ukuba mnene kukaPawulu ekulwisaneni nabantu baseKorinte ngesinye isikhathi kwakudidaniswa nokuba ntekenteke. Yimaphi amazwi noma ukukhuluma okukhona kulamavesi okwakuveza ukuba nesibindi kukaPawulu ekulwisaneni nenkinga yokuba khona kwabefundisi bamanga eKorinte na?`

UPawulu uqala isahluko esiku-2 Korinte 10 njengento ethinta yena: “Kepha mina Pawulu uqobo ngiyanincenga” (2 Korinte 10:1). Lokhu kukhombisa indlela ayekhathazeke ngayo uPawulu ngabefundisi bamanga ababengenisile ebandleni. Amazwi akhe aku- 2 Korinte 10:1 abekwe ngendlela enokudida ngesikhathi ephendula ukuhlatshwa kwakhe ngabantu ababephikisana naye ngokuthi akhulume ngokubethusa uma ebabhalela izincwadi eqhelile kubona nokwaveza ukuba namahloni kwakhe uma eqondisa abantu kunokuba ababuke ubuso nobuso (2 Korinte 10:10, 11). Wabaphendula ngokuthi, lokhu okubonakala engazathi ngubuthakathaka kufuneka kubhekwe njengethuluzi elinamandla lobumnene nokuba njengoKristu owabe emnene.

Abefundisi bamanga kufuneka kubhekwane nabo ngokuzethemba nokungenamahloni (2 Korinte 10:2) kokwa konke lokhu kuhambisane nokuba mnene kukaKristu (2 Korinte 10:1). UJesu wake wathi, “ngokuba ngimnene, ngithobile ngenhliziyo” (Mathewu 11:29). Noma kunjalo, uJesu wabhekana kanzima nabantu ababethengisa nababethenga ethempelini wagumbuqela amatafula waphinde wababiza ngabaphangi (Mathewu 21:12, 13). Waphinde wabiza abaFarisi ngabazenzisi ebafanisa namaliba acakiweyo abonakala emahle ngaphandle (Mathewu 23:23-27). Ngendlela efanayo nekaJesu, uPawulu uyazi ukuthi sisempini kamoya efuna sisebenzise izikhali zonke zikaNkulunkulu (Efesu 6:12-17).

Indlela akhuluma ngayo uPawulu ku-2 Korinte 10 izwakala inokulwa ngenxa yokuthi izimpilo zabantu zisebucayini (2 Korinte 10:3-6). Lena akusiyona impi yokulwisana kwabantu kodwa kuyimpi kaNkulunkulu yokuba kuzuzwe abantu bazuzelwe uKristu. Kulokhu kushayisana, kokukhuluma okungelona iqiniso nemibono yokuziphakamisa kufuneka kubhekwane nakho kuphinde kushatshalaliswe, ngokuthi kubhekwe okufunwa yizwi likaNkulunkulu, ukuze “namacebo onke siwathumbele ukumlalela uKristu” (2 Korinte 10:5).

Kulempi kamoya, uPawulu udlala indima yokugunyazwa nguKristu. Leligunya lihlose ukwakha, aliqondile ukubhidliza (2 Korinte 10:8). Kuyinto elula ukuthi abaholi bebandla bazitshele ukuthi lokhu abakwenzayo igunya balinikwe nguNkulunkulu. Noma kunjalo, kufuneka bakhumbule ukuthi igunya abanalo balinikezwe nguKristu, futhi njengoba enza naYe, kudingeka bakhombise ukuba mnene baphinde babe nezinhliziyo ezithobekile. Ilungelo uPawulu akhuluma ngalo, nokuyigunya alinikwe nguKristu kungenxa yokukhathazeka kwakhe kokuthi amaKrestu alalela abantu abangalungile, okungadala ingozi yokulahlekelwa ngukuthembeka kwabo kuKristu.

`Singenza kanjani ukuthi sikhombise ukuba mnene siphinde sikhombise ukuba nesibindi uma sibhekene nokulwisana nabefundisi bamanga na? Kungani kufanele sibe nakho kokubili na?`
