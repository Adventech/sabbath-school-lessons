### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
2 Kor. 10:1–17; Jer. 9:24; 2 Kor. 11:1–15, 22–28; 2 Kor. 12:20, 21; 2 Kor. 13:5.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ngokuba izikhali zethu zokulwa kasizo ezenyama, kodwa ngaye uNkulunkulu zinamandla okubhidliza izinqaba” (2 Korinte 10:4, ESV).

Kungathi uPawulu ubengakabhekani nezinkinga ezinkulu ngokwanele, njengoba sekunenye inkinga okufuneka abhekane nayo futhi: abefundisi bamanga abasebandleni. Okubi kakhulu ngalokhu, ngukuthi labefundisi base behehe amanye amalunga ebandla laseKorinte. Lokulwa uPawulu usekubiza ngokuthi yimpi kamoya.

Kungabe ukukhuluma okufana nalokhu sekuyihaba na? Cha, akusilona okwangempela. UPawulu wayazi ukuthi lababantu kabalwisani naye nje kuphela, kodwa sebelwisana noKristu. UPawulu wayengeyena umholi wokudlala uma kubhekwa indlela ayebhekisisa ngayo lokhu ayaziwa ngakho kufaniswe nokuthi usevumelana nokungalungi ngokusebenzisa amandla negunya analo phezu kwabantu abaphetheyo. Wayazi ukuthi umyalezo athunywe ukuba awushumayele kwabe kungumyalezo wokukhetha phakathi kokufa nokuphila, nokwabe kuphathelene nomphumela wokuphila kwaphakade. Futhi wayazi ukuthi wayethunywe nguNkulunkulu uqobo lwaKhe. “UPawulu, obizelwe ngentando kaNkulunkulu ukuba abe ngumphostoli kaJesu Kristu” (1 Korinte 1:1).

Uma kuziwa ekufundiseni izimfundiso zamanga, ibandla kufanele kubekhona elikwenzayo, likwenze ngokukhombisa uthando kodwa lingagudluki eqinisweni, linamathele kulokhu okushiwo yimiBhalo. Umyalezo wevangeli kudingeka ulondolozwe, ungonakali kodwa ugcine umsulwa, ukuze sizokwazi ukunika imiphefumulo ithemba lokuphila kwaphakade.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uMandulo 19._
