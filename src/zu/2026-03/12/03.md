---
title:  Ukuzigqaja ENkosini
date:   14/09/2026
---

Izolo sibone uPawulu kanye nalabo asebenzisana nabo begqamisa umsebenzi wokuvangela njengempi kamoya, bekwazi ukulwa ngokusebenzisa izikhali zikaNkulunkulu. Namuhla sizobheka ukuthi abefundisi bamanga basebenza ngokwemigomo efunwa ngabantu. Banokuzibonga ngokungafanele. Ukulwisana nalokhu kwenza uPawulu uzibonga eNkosini kuphela. Njengoba wabhala ukuthi: “Kepha ozibongayo makazibonge eNkosini” (2 Korinte 10:17).

`Funda ku-2 Korinte 10:13-17. Kungabe umoya wokuncintiselana wenza muphi umonakalo ekushumayelweni kwevangali na?`

Indlela yokuzibonga kukaPawulu kwahlaba umhlwele abahumushi abaningi bezincwadi zokukholwa ababelokhu bevela emakhulwini yeminyaka edlulile. Noma kunjalo, ukuzibonga kwabe kuyinto ejwayelekile esikhathini sasendulo kuphinde kulawulwe yizigodi zomphakathi ukuvimbela ukuphoxana okungase kwenzeke ebantwini. UPawulu wayenolwazi ngalemihlangano yezigodi, futhi ehambisana nayo. Okunye okwakwenzeka, uPawulu wakubeka kwacaca ukuthi ukuzibonga kwakhe kwehlukile kunalokhu okwakwenziwa ngabefundisi bamanga. Wazibonga eNkosini (2 Korinte 10:17). Nasi isicaphuno esise-Thestamente Elidala: “ ‘Kepha ozibongayo makazibonge ngalokhu ukuthi uyangiqonda, angazi ukuthi nginguJehova owenza umusa, nokwahlulela nokulunga emhlabeni’ ”(Jeremiya 9:24). Lelivesi eliyisicaphuno esivela encwadini kaJeremiya, kulona uPawulu usikhombisa ukuthi lenkulumo igxile othandweni, nasekwenzeni ubulungisa, kanye nokulunga kukaKristu.

Ngamanye amazwi, ukuzibonga kukaPawulu kugxile kulokhu okuzuzwe nguNkulunkulu ngoKristu. Ngakho-ke, ukuzibonga kwakhe kusekwelwe kulokhu okushiwo yiBhayibheli futhi yingakho kungeke kuphathe kabi omunye umuntu. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, izimbangi zakhe zazithola zikwesinye isimo sokuthanda ukuncintisana ngokuthi bazilinganise ngokuzifanisa ngendlela abanye abantu abazincomayo abenza ngayo. Ukwenza ngalendlela kungukwenza kobuphukuphuku (2 Korinte 10:12).

Ku-2 Korinte 10:14-16, uPawulu ukhuluma ukuthi ukushumayela ivangeli yiyonanto ebalulekile ekushumayeleni kwakhe, nokuyinto abeyenza eKorinte kanye nakwezinye izindawo ezingaphandle kwaseKorinte. Uthando lukaPawulu ayenalo ngoJesu kwamenza akhulume ngaso sonke isikhathi izindaba ezinhle zosindiso, esizithola ekufeni nasekuvusweni kukaKristu.

Uma siqhathanisa abefundisi bamanga ababekhona eKorinte abagcwele umoya wokuzibonga, kanti ngakolunye uhlangothi, uPawulu wayebongwa futhi eqokwe nanguNkulunkulu (2 Korinte 10:12, 18). “UPawulu, obizelwe ngentando kaNkulunkulu ukuba abe ngumphostoli kaJesu Kristu” (1 Korinte 1:1). UPawulu ukulwile ukulwa okuhle kwaze kwaba sekugcineni kokuphila kwakhe (2 Tim. 4:7).

`Phinda ufunde futhi ku-2 Korinte 10:12-18. Kungenzeka kanjani ukuthi abaholi bebandla, noma amalunga ebandla, akwazi ukuvimbela umoya wokuncintisana na? Kulula kangakanani ukuzithola usungene ezintweni ezingenamsebenzi na?`
