---
title:  Ukuba Semqoka Kothando
date:   09/08/2026
---

Ngeviki eledlule sithinte indikimba yothando, njengoba iveziwe ku-1 Korinte 13. Manje sekufuneka senabe ngokuthi sibheke ngokujulile amagama ashiwo nguPawulu.

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 13. Goqa konke esitshelwa khona ngothando.`

UPawulu akasho ukuthi izilimi (1 Korinte 13:1), ukuprofetha, nokuqonda, nokwazi, nokukholwa (1 Korinte 13:2), kanye nesihe (1 Korinte 13:3) konke lokhu kuyize leze. Kuyize uma kungukuthi akwenziwa ngabantu beqhutshwa wuthando.

Uhlobo lothando uPawulu akhuluma ngalo asitshelwanga ngalo emishweni efana nalena, “ngithanda amajikijolo” noma “ngithanda abangani bami” noma ngithanda umlingani wami nezingane zathu.” Okunye futhi akakhulumi ngothando esivame ukulubona emidlalweni yakomabona-kude. Akusilona uthando lokuphakamisa imizwa, yize lamavesi evamise ukufundwa ezinkonzweni zemishado.

Loluthando akufuneki silunciphise lulingane nothando lwemizwa, noma ukuba nomusa kubantu, noma ukuba nesimilo esihle, noma ukuba nobubele. Noma kunjalo, wonke lamagama enza uthando lube sezingeni eliphansi noma eliphezulu. Okuseqinisweni, uthando encwadini yama-1 Korinte 13 luyisimo sezikamoyalusinika umfutho wokuba sibe nemizwa yokuthanda, umuzwa wokuphana, sibe nesimilo esihle nobubele. Kuyindlela yokuzinikela ngokuphelele kulokhu esisuke sikwenza, nesinemizwa ngakho, kanye nokucabanga kwethu okuncike kuKristu sicabangele nomakhelwana bethu.

`Funda ku-Mathewu 24:12. Yisiphi isixwayiso uJesu asinika sona lapha na?`

Yingakho uthando luyinto ebalulekile neyisidingo. Ngamandla kaKristu, angeke sivumele uthando luphole emakhaya ethu, nasemabandleni kanye nakomakhelwana. Sinesibonelo esinguKristu esesiphambanweni, efela thina. Ngabe iyiphi enye indlela engcono nenamandla okukhombisa loluthando okungenzeka ibe khona na? Yize singeke sikwazi ukukhombisa ngezinga elilinganayo loluthando, ngomusa kaNkulunkulu singakwazi ukuzama ukuluveza ngendlela esiphila ngayo nganoma iyiphi indlela esingakwazi ukuyenza.

`Kungabe yiziphi izikhathi lapho ngempela kudingeka sikwazi ukukhombisa loluthando ukuze kukwazi ukuthinta omunye umuntu oludingayo ukwedlula konke okunye okungahle kubekhona na?`
