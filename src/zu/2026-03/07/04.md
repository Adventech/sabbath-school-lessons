---
title:  Lokho Uthando Olungakwenzi
date:   11/08/2026
---

`Funda ku-1 Korinte 13:4-7. Kungani uPawulu ekhuluma izinto ezimbi kunokuba akhulume ngezinto ezinhle zobunjalo bothando na?`

Izolo sigxile ezintweni eziyisikhombisa ezenziwa wuthando; namuhla sizobheka ezintweni eziyisishiyagalombili ezingenziwa wuthando. Uthando. . .

**1. Alunamhawu (_zeloō_).** Umhawu ungasetshenziswa ngendlela enhle “njengokuzondelela iziphiwo ezinkulu” (1 Korinte 12:31), “nizondelele iziphiwo zikamoya” (1 Korinte 14:1), kanye “nokuzondelela ukuprofetha” (1 Korinte 14:39). Yize encwadini ye-Izenzo 7:9, kubekwa ngendlela engeyinhle. Kulungile ukuhawukela iziphiwo zikamoya, kodwa ungabi nomhawu ngabantu abaphumelelayo. Ngoba lokhu kudala ukwahlukana (1 Kor. 3:3).

**2. Aluzigabisi (_perpereuomai_).** Ukwenza kokuzigabisa kuletha umqondo wokuba nenkani uphinde ufise ukutuswa ngabanye abantu. Noma kunjalo, uthando alugxilile ekuzicabangeleni ngaleyondlela. Lokhu kucaciswe kahle kulokhu okulandelayo.

**3. Aluzikhukhumezi (_physioō_).** Isenzo sokuzikhukhumeza siyavela ku-1 Korinte 8:1 nokuyilapho uPawulu eveza ukukhuluma okumangalisayo kokuthi: “Ukwazi kuyazikhukhumeza, kepha uthando luyakha.” Lapha kukhulunywa ngomuntu ozibona ebalulekile.

**4. Aluziphathi ngokungafanele (_aschēmoneo_).** Uthando lokuziphatha ngokungafanele lungaba nezinto eziningi elizishoyo. Ngokujwayelekile, yize kusho ukwenza okuphambene emphakathini nasekuziphatheni ngendlela engalungile, nehlazisayo, nengathandeki, kanye nokungenzi kahle. Kungenzeka uPawulu uqonde ukukhuluma ngokungaziphathi kahle kokuba nenkani nokuba luhlaza komuntu ozibona “eqinile ekukholweni” ekwenza kumzalwane “obuthakathaka” ebandleni laseKorinte (1 Kor. 4:10, 1 Korinte 8).

**5. Aluzifuneli (_zēteo_) okwalo.** Lokhu kuyefana nalokhu okushiwo nguPawulu ku-1 Korinte 10:24: “Akungabikho ozifunela okwakhe, kodwa akube yilowo nalowo afunele omunye.” Uthando luyayekelela amalungelo alo ukuze kuzuze abanye (funda isifundo 5). Ukufuna ukwenza okuzonikeza amalungelo abanye abantu, ukuze wonke umuntu ezohlomula.

**6. Alucunuki kalula (_paroxynō_).** Isenzo sokucanuka kusuke kubhekiswe kumuzwa ongaphakathi kumuntu wokushesha ukucasuka. Lokhu kusho ukuthi uthando alunayo inhliziyo encane yokucasuka kalula noma yokuba ngumuntu oshesha ukuzwela.

**7. Alunagqubu (_logizomai_).** Isenzo sokuba negqubu kuyisenzo esinomqondo wokubekelela, okusho ukuthi uthando alulokhu lubala okubi okwenziwa kuyena. Ngamanye amazwi, uthando lusho ukuxolela.

**8. Aluthokozi (_chairō_) ngokungalungile.** Uthando alugcini ngokungabekeleli okubi okwenziwe ngomunye umuntu kodwa alikuthokozeli okubi okwenziwayo. Uma sithanda abanye abantu ngokweqiniso, asikuthokozeli ukubabona benza amaphutha kodwa, okunalokho, sizama ukubasiza.
