---
title:  Ukholo, Ithemba, NoThando
date:   13/08/2026
---

Kuze kube manje, sesifunde ukuthi uthando luyabekezela, lumnene, luyathokoza, lumelana nobunzima, luyakholwa, luyathemba, luyakhuthazela (noma luyabekezela) ngoba uJesu uyikho konke lokhu okubaliwe. Uma sibona lezimo kuJesu, isinyathelo esilandelayo okufuneka sisithathe ngukuba sifuze Yena. Lokhu kwakuyisifiso sikaPawulu efisela abantu baseKorinte. Noma Kunjalo, uma singasusa lokhu “elingakwenzi uthando” ezintweni eziphikisana nalokhu okwenziwa wuthando, “sithola incazelo enhle yokuziphatha kwabantu baseKorinte emabandleni abo: Ukuba nomona, nokuzigabisa, nokuba nenkani, nokuba luhlaza, nokuzicabangela, nokucanuka kalula, kanye nokulokhu ubheka amaphutha enziwa ngabanye abantu. UPawulu uhlanganisa izenzo azisebenzisile ezama ukuchaza isimo saseKorinte.”— Verlyn D. Verbrugge, “1 Corinthians,” in The Expositor’s Bible Commentary: Romans–Galatians, revised edition (Grand Rapids, MI: Zondervan, 2008), p. 372.

Kuningi okwakufuneka bekufundile abantu baseKorinte. Nathi ngokunjalo. Emuva kokuba sesichazile ukuthi uthando lwenzani kanye nalokhu elingakwenziyo, uPawulu usephetha lokhu akhuluma ngakho ngokuthi agcizelele isimo sangunaphakade sothando ukuze ezovuselela indlela yokusebenzisa uthando lwangempela.

Ngolunye usuku, iziprofetho aziseyukudingeka; siyokhuluma kuphela ulwimi olulodwa; Ukwazi kwabantu okungaphelele, kudedele indawo ngokwazi kabusha ngoNkulunkulu (1 Korinte 13:12). Iziphiwo zikaMoya ziyophela uma inhloso yokuba khona kwazo sekufinyelele esikhathini sokuba igcwaliseke (1 Korinte 13:10). “Uthando alusoze lwaphela!” (1 Kor. 13:8, NLT).

Ngokufanayo, uma uKristu esebuya, ukukholwa kuzoqhelela isimo sokuzibonela (2 Korinte 5:7), kanti futhi konke lokhu ebesikade sikulangazelela siyokubona kwenzeka ngempela (Roma 8:24). Okunye okubaluleke kakhulu, uthando luyobekezela njengophawu lwesimilo sikaNkulunkulu wethu oziqu zintathu. Yize kunomqondo wokuthi ukukholwa kanye nokuthemba kuyobakhona kuze kube phakade. Ukukholwa njengento engumuzwa wokusindiswa (Roma 4:3), kanye nokwethemba okuyinto esifisayo nesilindele ukuyamukela kanye nokwazi esiyokuthola emhlabeni omusha, konke lokhu kuyokuba ngamava abasindisiwe ingunaphakade. Ngokufanayo, uthando lukaNkulunkulu, luyohlala ingunaphakade.

Kungekudala, sizokwazi ukubona ubuso bukaNkulunkulu (1 Korinte 13:12). Kuze kufike lolosuku, kudingeka ukuphila kwethu sikuhlanganise nalezi zimiso ezintathu: ukukholwa, ukwethemba, kanye nothando. Lezinto zontanthu zimele ukugcwaliseka kokuphila komKrestu ephila ngoMoya. Kungakho kwakulokhu kushiwo lokhu kubantu abangamaKrestu (Roma 5:1-5; Galathiya 5:5, 6; Efesu 1:15, 18; Efesu 4:1-5). Noma kunjalo, uthando yilona olukhulu kakhulu; empeleni, yiyonanto okungukuphela kwayo yokuchaza ubunjalo bukaNkulunkulu uqobo lwaKhe (1 Johane 4:8).

`Hlala ekukhulumeni kokuthi “uNkulunkulu uluthando.” Kungabe yikuphi okufuneka sikuqonde kahle ukuze sazi ukuthi kusho ukuthini lokhu na? Yize sinokuqonda okungaphelele ngalokhu, kungani lokhu kukhuluma kuzindaba ezimnandi kithina na?`
