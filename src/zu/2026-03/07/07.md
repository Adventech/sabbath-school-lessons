Ellen G. White, “The Need of Love,” pp. 545, 546, Review and Herald, August 28, 1888.

“Noma ngabe ufunde kangakanani, umuntu onhliziyo yakhe ingagcwalisiwe wuthando lukaNkulunkulu kanye nokuthanda abantu, lowo muntu akasiyena umlandeli kaKristu. Noma ngabe unokukholwa okukhulu futhi unamandla okwenza izimangaliso, kodwa engenalo uthando ukukholwa kwakhe kuyize leze. Angakhombisa ukukhululeka okukhulu; kodwa uma kungukuthi unezinye izinhloso ezingaphandle kothando lwangempela, ebeka konke anakho ukuze abele abampofu, ukwenza kwakhe angeke kumenze abukeke kahle phambi kukaNkulunkulu. Ngomfutho anawo wokwazi ukubhekana nokufa, ebulawela ukukholwa, uma kungukuthi ukwenza kwakhe akuqhutshwa wuthando, uNkulunkulu uzombona njengomuntu onomfutho wokukhohlisa noma umzenzisi osemfuthweni.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, pp. 318, 319.

“Sinokushumayela okuningi. Into edingeka kakhulu . . . wukuba nothando lwemiphefumulo ebhubhayo, uthando oluvela kuzivunguvungu ezinothile zombuso kaNkulunkulu. UbuKrestu bangempela bendlala uthando kuwona wonke umzimba. Luthinta yonke indawo ebalulekile, umqondo, nenhliziyo, nesandla esisizayo, nezinyawo, wenze abantu bakwazi ukuma bazinze lapha uNkulunkulu efuna beme khona, ukuze bangahambi emizeleni engalungile ngezinyawo zabo, nokungenza umhambi obuthakatha aphume endleleni. Uthando oluvutha lomlilo obhubhisayo lukaKristu emiphefumulweni ebhubhayo luba ngukuphila kwalolonke uhlelo lobuKristu.”—Ellen G. White, Lift Him Up, p. 134.

“Uthando olugeleza enhliziyweni kaKristu yilona kuphela olukwazi ukuphilisa. Kumuntu okugobhoza kuyena uthando, noma ngabe ngamanzi amancane asesihlahleni noma kungakhona igazi emzimbeni, konke lokhu kungawuphilisa umphefumulo olimele.”—Ellen G. White, Education, p. 114.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Uma ucabanga ngohlu lukaPawulu lwezimiso ezilungile zothando luqukethe konke na? Uma kungenjalo, ngabe yikuphi okunye ongakwengeza kuloluhlu na?`

`Ucabanga ukuthi wayethini uPawulu ngalomthetho wokuthi : “Jongani uthando” (1 Kor. 14:1, NKJV) na? Kungabe lokhu kuhlangene ngani nalokhu akusho ku-1 Korinte 13:4-7 na?`

`Yisiphi isimilo sothando osidinga kakhulu ukuze uzokwazi ukulusebenzisa empilweni yakho yansuku zonke na? Yiziphi izimilo zothando obona sengathi ziyadingeka kakhulu ebandleni lakho na? Okunye futhi, kungani uPawulu eqhathanisa uthando neziphiwo zokuprofetha, nezolimi, kanye nokwazi (1 Korinte 13:8) na?`

`UPawulu uveza ukuthi uthando yisona sisombululo sokugcina nokwenza kungabibikho ubumbano kumalunga ebandla laseKorinte. Ngabe yini isizathu na? Kungabe lokhu kuwathinta kanjani amabandla esikuwona esikhathini sanamuhla na?`
