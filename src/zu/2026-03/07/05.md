---
title:  Umfanekiso KaJesu
date:   12/08/2026
---

Uma sifunda ku-1 Korinte 13:4-7, sizizwa sinokukhathazeka kokuthi, ngokufishane noma ngokubanzi, siyashoda ukukhombisa zonke lezizimo zothando. Kungenzeka ukuthi, uPawulu wayenomfanekiso kaJesu emqondweni wakhe ngesikhathi ebhala isahluko esiku-1 Korinte 13. Ngokuqinisekileyo, nguKristu kuphela okwazile ukuveza zonke lezimiso zothando. Ngakho-ke ekugcineni, uPawulu usesivezela umfanekiso wothando ukuthi luyisithombe sikaJesu.

`Funda ku-Johane 13:1, 34; Johane 15:9, 12; 1 Thimothewu 1:14; 2 Thimothewu 1:7, 13; 1 Johane 4:7-12, 19-21. Yini esingayifunda ngothando kulamavesi na?`

UNkulunkulu uluthando (1 Johane 4:8). Uthanda ngalendlela yokuthi wakwazi ukunikela ngeNdodana yakhe eyodwa (Johane 3:16). UJesu ugcwele isimo esimbula ngokuphelele loluthando (Hebheru 1:3). Uma sifuna ukwazi ukuthi uthando luzembula kanjani kufuneka sibhekisise uJesu. Uma sibhekisisa ngokujulile umfanekiso kaJesu eThestamenteni Elisha, sizokwazi ukubona ukuthi konke okuhle kothando oluku-1 Korinte 13 kubonakala kahle kuYe.

**UJesu uyabekezela.** “Kepha ngahawukelwa ngalokhu ukuze uJesu Kristu abonakalise kimi engingesikhulu konke ukubekezela kwakhe, ngibe yisibonelo kwabakholwa nguye, baze babe nokuphila okuphakade [_makrothymia_]” (1 Tim. 1:16, NIV).

**UJesu umnene.** IBhayibheli lithi “iNkosi imnene” (1 Petro 2:3). Igama elithi “iNkosi” kulelivesi lisho uJesu. Igama elithi “umnene” livela egameni lesenzo sobumnene okusho, ( “uthando lumnene”) ku-1 Korinte 13:4.

**UJesu ujabulela iqiniso.** UJesu wezwa ejabule ngesikhathi elandela ukwenza intando kaBaba wazizwa enothando lukaBaba kuYe (Johane 15:9–11, Johane 17:12–14).

**UJesu ubekezelela izinto zonke.** Incwadi yama-Hebheru 12: 2, 3 ikhuluma ukuthi uJesu “wathwala isiphambano. . . . [Yena] wabekezelela inkohlakalo yezoni ngakuYe” (NKJV). Akekho noyedwa owakhombisa ukuzithoba ukwedlula okukaJesu (Filiphu 2:8). Konke wakwenza ngenxa yentokozo eyabe ibekiwe ngaphambi kwaKhe!

**UJesu ukholwa izinto zonke.** Ngesikhathi u-Ananiya ebuza ngokuphenduka kwangempela kukaPawulu (Izenzo 9:13, 14), uJesu waphendula ngokuthi, : “Lo uyisitsha sami esikhethiweyo sokuphatha igama lami” (Izenzo 9:15). UJesu ubona abantu ngendlela abayiyona njengoba bezophenduka babe ngabanye abantu ngenxa yamandla aKhe.

`Kungabe yiziphi ezinye izindlela uJesu azivezayo kithina zokuthi uthando lwangempela luyini na?`
