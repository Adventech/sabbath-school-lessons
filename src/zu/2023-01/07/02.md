---
title:  Impilo Nokusebenza KukaJesu
date:  12/02/2023
---

Esanda kuqala ukungena emsebenzini waKhe uJesu, waya eNazaretha, isifunda saseGalile. Kwakuyidolobha lakubo leli, kanti abantu bendawo base bezwile ngomsebenzi waKhe nezimangaliso. Njengokwesiko laKhe-ke, uJesu waya enkonzweni esinagogeni ngeSabatha.  Nakuba uJesu wayengesiye uRabi ophethe, umholi wamnika incwadi kaIsaya, wamcela ukuthi afunde umbhalo. UJesu wafunda kuIsaya 61:1, 2.

`Funda kuLuka 4:16-19 uqhathanise noIsaya 61:1, 2 (bheka noLuka 7:1923). Ucabanga ukuthi kungani uJesu akhetha lezindimana? Kungashiwo kanjani ukuthi lezindimana ezikuIsaya ziprofetha ngoMesiya?  Yini ezayiveza ngomsebenzi kaMesiya?`

Ngenxa yokuthi abaholi bezenkolo babengazinakanga iziprofetho ezikhuluma ngoMesiya ohluphekayo, bahlanekezela lezo ezikhomba enkazimulweni yokubuya kwaKhe (okuyinto efanele ukusikhumbuza thina ngokubaluleka kokuqondisisa isiprofetho), abantu abaningi babekholelwa ephutheni lokuthi umsebenzi kaMesiya kwaku ukukhulula uIsrayeli ejokeni labacindezeli bakhe, amaRoma. Kwabethusa kakhulu ukuzwa ukuthi kanti uhlelo lokusebenza kukaJesu luthathelwa kuIsaya 61:1, 2.

Abampofu babebukelwa phansi yilabo abaphezulu, njengabaqoqi bentela, osomabhizinisi, nawomakhelwane babo uqobo.  Kwakunomcabango othi ukuhlupheka yisiqalekiso sikaNkulunkulu, nokuthi isimo sabo sokuhlupheka sidalwe yibo ngokwabo.  Njengoba-ke babenalomqondo onjalo, bambalwa abantu ababezihlupha ngabampofu nobumpofu babo.

Kodwa uthando lukaJesu ngabampofu kwaku ubufakazi obukhulu bokuthi unguMesiya, njengoba sibona indlela uJesu aphendula ngayo umbuzo kaJohane uMbhabhadisi efuna ukwazi ukuthi unguYe ngempela yini uMesiya (bheka kuMath. 11:1-6). Njengabafundi bakaJesu, uJohane uMbhabhadisi wayengawuqondisisi umbuso kaKrestu. Wayelindele ukuthi uJesu uzothatha isihlalo sobukhosi sikaDavide; kwathi ngokuqhubeka kwesikhathi, uMsindisi engathathi ubukhosi, uJohane wadideka, wakhathazeka.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 215.

`“Ukukhonza okumhlophe okungenabala phambi kukaNkulunkulu uBaba yilokhu, ukuhambela izintandane nabafelokazi osizini lwabo , nokuzigcina kungabi yicala lokwezwe” (Jakobe 1:27). Ingasisiza kanjani lendimana ukunaka izinto ezimqoka enkolweni yethu na?`
