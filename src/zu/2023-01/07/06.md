date:  16/02/2023

`Funda kuJobe 1:8.  UJobe uchazwa kanjani nguNkulunkulu uqobo na? `

Into enhle kakhulu leyo, ukuthi noNkulunkulu uqobo lwaKhe abize uJobe ngokuthi “akanasici, uphelele” (Jobe 1:8), wayephelele ngendlela yokuthi akekho omunye emhlabeni ngaleso sikhathi owayefana naye. Futhi, amazwi kaNkulunkulu ngokwaKhe lawa, njengoba enjalo.

Nangemuva kokuba uJobe wayevelelwe yinhlekelele emva kwenye, uNkulunkulu waphinda lokho ayekade ekushilo ngoJobe, ukuthi akekho omunye emhlabeni ofana naye, umuntu ophelele nongenasici.  Kwase kwenezelwa okunye. UJobe wayelokhu enakho konke okuncomekayo, ‘noma ungivusele ukumchitha engenacala” (Jobe 2:3).

Nakuba sibona isithombe esithathekisayo sokuphelela kukaJobe nobuqotho bakhe ngokuthi angavumi ukudedela uNkulunkulu phezu kwakho konke okwakwenzeka naphezu kokuchukuluzwa yinkosikazi yakhe ithi: “Usabambelela ekupheleleni kwakho? Thuka uNkulunkulu, ufe kube kanye!” (Jobe 2:9), incwadi iveza olunye uhlangothi lwempilo kaJobe ngaphambi kwezigigaba.

`Funda kuJobe 29:12-16. Sitshengiswani lapho olusinika ukuqonda kangcono ngemfihlo yesimilo sikaJobe?`

Mhlawumbe okusivula amehlo kangcono lapha amazwi kaJobe athi: “Ngaphenya icala engingalazanga” (Jobe 29:16, NKJV). Ngamanye amazwi, uJobe akavelanganje walinda, ukuthi kufike isinxibi esinikizelayo sizocela usizo. Kunalokho, uJobe wathatha izinyathelo zokufuna abadinga usizo, wabasiza.

U-Ellen G. White wabeka kanje: “Ungabalindi abampofu ukuthi badonsele amehlo akho ezidingweni zabo.  Yenza njengoJobe. Lokho ayengakwazi, wakuphenya. Thatha uhambo lokuhlola, uthole izidingo, uthole ukuthi zingahlangatshezwa kanjani.”—Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 151.  Leli iqophelo lokuphathwa kwezimali nobunceku ngempahla kaNkulunkulu elingavamile ukubonwa ebantwaneni bakaNkulunkulu namhlanje.
