Funda umbhalo ka Ellen G. White owenabe kakhulu ngokweshumi kumqingo 9 we Testimonies for the Church, pp. 245-252. Funda isigaba III seCounsels on Stewardship, pp. 65-107.

“Ukuba konke okweshumi kwabazalwane bethu bekungageleza kungene esikhwameni sikaNkulunkulu njengoba kufanele kube njalo, bekungatholakala izibusiso ezingenza kuphinda-phindeke kashumi izipho neminikelo yokwenza umsebenzi ongcwele, kanjalo-ke umhosha ophakathi kukaNkulunkulu nabantu bewuzohlala uvulekile.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 4, p. 474. Ayamangalisa lamazwi.  Ukuba sonke besithembekile ekubuyiseni okweshumi, uNkulunkulu ubezosibusisa ngemali yokukhulisa iminikelo yethu ngamaphesenti angu 1 000.

Esahlukweni sesithathu sikaMalaki, sithola isivumelwano/isibopho esenziwa nguNkulunkulu nabantu. Lapha uNkulunkulu uchaza indima azoyenza yokunikeza ngezipho ezinkulu kulabo abazobuyela kuYena ngokwethembeka kokweshumi neminikelo.”—Ellen G. White, Review and Herald, December 17, 1901.

“Bonke abakhumbule ukuthi izimfuno zikaNkulunkulu kithina zedlula zonke ezinye.  Usipha ngokukhululeka. Isivumelwano asenza nabantu sithi okweshumi kwempahla yaKhe akubuyiselwe kuNkulunkulu. UNkulunkulu uphathise izinceku zaKhe ingcebo yaKhe, kodwa mayelana nokweshumi uthi: Okwami.  Ngesilinganiso uNkulunkulu anike ngaso abantu impahla yaKhe, ngokunjalo abantu ababuyisele kuNkulunkulu okweshumi ngokwethembeka kukho konke abanakho. Lomehluko wenziwa nguJesu Krestu uqobo.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 6, p. 384.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`Gxila kulomqondo othi umkhuba wokukhipha okweshumi awuqalanga kuIsrayeli wasendulo.  Leliqiniso lisisiza kanjani ukuqonda ukuthi lomyalo ulokhu uqhubeke njalo?`

`Eklasini, xoxani ngombuzo osekugcineni kwesigaba sangolwesiBili. Cabanga ukuthi bekungenzekani ukuba abantu bebengakhetha ukuthumela okweshumi kwabo kwenye indawo. Bekuzokwenzekani ebandleni lethu? Belisazokuba khona kodwa lona ibandla? Yini iphutha lomqondo othi: Okweshumi kwami kuncane uma kuqhathaniswa nakho konke okunye, akwenzi mehluko? Bekuzokwenzekani ukuba wonke umuntu ubecabanga kanjalo?`

`Abelana nabanye ngalokho okufundile wakuzwa ngokubuyisa okweshumi. Yini ongayifundisa abanye ngalomkhuba?`
