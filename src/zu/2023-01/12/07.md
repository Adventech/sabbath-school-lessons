Lapha sizobona umfanekiso odwetshwe ngamazwi, webandla elethembekile ngokuba yizinceku ngezimali zomsebenzi kaNkulunkulu emhlabeni.

**Umbono Wamabandla AmaSeventh-day Adventist Ngobunceku Emhlabeni Wonke**

Yisikhathi esizayo; abefundisi nabaholi emabandleni baphumelele ukwenza isimo sobunceku ebandleni. Bafundisile, baqeqesha, besekela, bakhuthaza ibandla ngokuba yizinceku ngokweBhayibheli.

Abantu basebenzisa izimiso zobunceku ezimpilweni zabo. Bayakhula ngokuphana, kukhona abongela izimo ezingalindelekile, futhi bayaphuma ezikweletini.

Indlela abaphila ngayo itshengisa ukuzithiba, ukuzikhuza, nokwaneliseka. Imali ayisancintisani noNkulunkulu, futhi bayakhula ebudlelwaneni babo noNkulunkulu uMdali.

YiSabatha ekuseni, abazalwane bayafika enkonzweni. Babonakalisa ukuthula ebusweni—awekho amaxhala ngezimali, kukhona ukwaneliseka nokubonga.

Imibango yezimali phakathi kwabaganene ayisekho. Bangena enkonzweni belindele ubukhona bukaNkulunkulu nomsebenzi waKhe kubona.

Imisebenzi yebandla yesekelwa ngokuphelele ngezimali, kanti novangelo luyaphila. Kutshengiswa uthando lukaKrestu ngazo zonke izindlela eziphathekayo kulabo abaswele.

Kubekelwe eceleni izimali zokwenza imisebenzi eyesekela uvangelo, kanti lokhu kwenziwa ngendlela enhle kakhulu.

Umbuzo obhekene nathi sonke nanku: “UNkulunkulu uthi asenzeni ngempahla asiphathise yona, noma ngabe iyini?”

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`Eklasini, xoxani ngokuthi sizizwa kanjani izimfundiso zeBhayibheli ezicace kamhlophe: ukusindiswa ngokukholwa, nomvuzo otholakala ngemisebenzi. Senza kanjani ukuthi lemiqondo emibili izwane?`

`Ukuthi aseneliswe ngalokho esinakho manje, kwehluke ngani ekuthini asingazithuthukisi ngasezimalini? Sisho kanjani ukuthi lemiqondo ayishayisani?`

`Akungatshazwa ukuthi iphakade lisilindele. Yiziphi izinqumo esizenzayo manje, nalezo “ezincane”, eziyokusho ukuthi iphakade lethu siyolichithaphi?`
