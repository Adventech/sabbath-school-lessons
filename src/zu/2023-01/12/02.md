---
title:  Umvuzo Wokwethembeka
date:  19/03/2023
---

`Funda kumaHeberu 11:6. Isitshelani lendimana? Sifanele sisabele kanjani kulokho ekushoyo? Bheka neSambulo 22:12, Isaya 40:10, noIsaya 62:11. Zisifundisani zonke lezindimana? `

Umvuzo kaNkulunkulu ebantwaneni baKhe wehlukile kweminye, futhi njengazo zonke izinto zikaMoya, wedlula ukuqonda kwethu kwasemhlabeni.  “Ulimi lwabantu alukwazi ukuchaza umvuzo wabalungileyo. Uyokwaziwa kuphela yilabo abawubonayo. Ayikho ingqondo yasemhlabeni engaqonda inkazimulo yeParadesi likaNkulunkulu.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 674.

UJesu waphetha izibusiso, okuyisiqalo sentshumayelo yasentabeni, ngalamazwi: “Nibusisiwe, nxa benithuka, benizingela, bekhuluma konke okubi ngani, beqamba amanga ngenxa yaMi. Jabulani, nithokoze, ngokuba umvuzo wenu mkhulu ezulwini; ngokuba kanjalo babazingela abaprofethi abananduleleyo” (Math. 5:11). Eseklelise abantu bokukholwa kumaHeberu 11, umbhali uqala isahluko esilandelayo ngokuchaza ukuthi kungani uJesu wakhetha ukufa esiphambanweni.

“Ngakho-ke nathi, lokhu sinefu elingaka lawofakazi elisihaqileyo, masilahle konke okusindayo nesono esithandela kangaka kithi, sigijime ngokubekezela kulokhu kuncintisana esikumiselweyo, sibhekile kuJesu, uMqalisi noMphelelisi wokukholwa kwethu, owathi esikhundleni sentokozo ayibekelweyo wathwala isiphambano engalinaki ihlazo, wahlala ngakwesokunene sesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu” (Heb. 12:1, 2).

Kodwa ukuphiwa umvuzo ngenxa yokwethembeka, akusiyo into efanayo nokuzama ukusindiswa ngemisebenzi. Ubani phakathi kwethu, noma phakathi kwabantu abaseBhayibhelini, owayenemisebenzi emihle ngokwanele phambi kukaNkulunkulu? Akukho noyedwa. Singena lapho-ke isiphambano. Ukuba sasingazisindisa ngemisebenzi yethu, uJesu wayengeke aye esiphambanweni. Kunalokho, sisindiswa ngomusa. “Kepha uma kuya ngomusa, akusayi ngemisebenzi; uma kungenjalo, umusa awuseyiwo umusa. Kepha uma kungemisebenzi, akusewona umusa; kungenjalo umsebenzi akusewona umsebenzi” (Rom. 11:6, NKJV). Kunalokho, imivuzo ukubonakaliswa kwalokho uNkulunkulu akwenzile kithina, enzela thina.

`Siwuzwa kanjani umehluko phakathi kokusindiswa ngomusa, nomvuzo otholakala ngemisebenzi?  Woza nempendulo yakho eklasini ngeSabatha.`
