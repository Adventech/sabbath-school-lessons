---
title:  Izinqumo Ezimbi ZikaLoti
date:  07/02/2023
---

Ngesikhathi uAbrahama eshiya izwe lakubo elalela ubizo lukaNkulunkulu, indodana yomfowabo, uLoti, yakhetha ukuhamba naye kulolo hambo. UGenesise 13 usitshela ukuthi uNkulunkulu wabusisa uAbrama kangangoba waceba kakhulu ngezinkomo (okuyinto okulinganiswa ngayo umnotho womuntu kulelo lizwe), nesiliva, negolide,  (Genesise 13:2). ULoti naye wayefuyile, enemihlambi namatende (Genesise 13:5). Baceba ngendlela yokuthi indawo yayingenele ukuthwala imfuyo yabo bobabili. Ukuze kugwemeke ingxabano phakathi kwabelusi babo, uAbrama wanika uLoti ithuba lokukhetha ukuthi ufuna ukuhlala-phi.  ULoti kwakufanele anike uAbrama, umuntu omdala, kukhethe yena kuqala, futhi uLoti wayecebe ngenxa yokuthi wayenokuxhumana noAbrama.  Kodwa, akatshengisanga ukubonga, kwathi ngokuzicabangela, wakhetha izwe ayecabanga ukuthi yilona elihle kakhulu elingatholakala.

`Funda kuGenesise 13:10-12. Yiziphi izizathu ezenza uLoti akhethe ngalendlela?`

Nakuba uLoti wasenza kalula-nje isinqumo sokuyohlala edolobheni, izinto azimhambelanga kahle lapho, kwathi lapho uAbrama ezwa ngokwenzekile kuLoti, akashongo ukuthi: “Uvuna okutshalile, Loti.” Kunalokho, weza ukuzomlekelela (Genesise 14).

Ngesinye isikhathi emizamweni yethu yokuzuza umnotho, kukhona izifundo esingazifundi. ULoti waphindela eSodoma!  Kodwa uNkulunkulu ngomusa waKhe omkhulu, wathumela izithunywa ukuyoxwayisa uLoti nomndeni wakhe, ukuze bazi ngokubhujiswa okuzayo kweSodoma neGomora.

`Funda kuGenesise 18:20-33. UNkulunkulu wathi kuAbrahama siyini isizathu sokuza kwaKhe  emhlabeni na? Waphendula wathini uAbrahama uma ezwa ukuthi uNkulunkulu uceba ukubhubhisa leyomizi ekhohlakele? `

Ngenxa yokukhathazeka kukaAbrahama ngoLoti nomndeni wakhe, wancenga uNkulunkulu ukuthi angayibhubhisi leyomizi uma kutholakala abantu abalungile kuyona. Waqala ngesibalo sabangu 50, wehla waze wafika kwabangu 10. Njengokwesimilo saKhe sothando, uNkulunkulu akayekanga ukutshengisa umusa kwaze kwayeka yena uAbrahama wayeka ukucela! UNkulunkulu nezingelosi ezimbili yibona abakhulula uLoti nenkosikazi yakhe, namadodakazi akhe amabili, mathupha. Kodwa inkosikazi yakhe yabheka emuva, yaphenduka isiduli sikasawoti.  ULoti wangena eSodom eyisicebi, waphuma esengenalutho. Sidinga ukuqaphela uma senza izinqumo esizenzayo, kakhulukazi singacabangi kuphela ngenzuzo yesikhashana uma kuqhathaniswa nazo zonke izinto (bheka uMarko 8:36, 37).
