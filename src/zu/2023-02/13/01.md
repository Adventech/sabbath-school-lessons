### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 Thes. 5:1–6; Johane 8:32; Samb. 18:1; Samb. 4:11; Eksod. 33:18, 19; Samb. 5:6, 12; Samb. 13:8.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Emva kwalokho ngabona enye ingelosi yehla ezulwini, inamandla amakhulu, nomhlaba wakhanyiswa ngokukhazimula kwayo” (Sambulo 18:1, NKJV).

Kungekudala, izigameko zokugcina zizokwenzeka. Ukuthi nini, nokuthi kanjani—asitsheliwe. Kodwa esikutsheliwe kwanele. Umthetho othile ophoqa ukugcinwa kweSonto, ngokumelene nokugcina umthetho wesine, uzokwenzeka. ISambulo sembulile kithina udaba olubucayi, abalingiswa abasemqoka kulo, futhi ngokubanzi, sisitshelile ngalokho okuzokwenzeka, ngokumelene nokukhonza Lowo “ ‘owenza izulu nomhlaba, ulwandle nemithomba yamanzi’ ” (Samb. 14:7, NKJV), abantu—bonke “omagama abo engalotshiwe Encwadini yoKuphila yeWundlu elahlatshwa kusukela ekusekweni kwezwe” (Samb. 13:8, NKJV)—bayokhonza isilo nomfanekiso waso. Ngamanye amazwi, bonke labo abakhethe ukulandela iNkosi uJesu Kristu futhi balalela imithetho Yakhe—amagama abo ahlezi encwadini yokuphila. Kungcono kakhulu ukuba sohleni lwemibhalo kaNkulunkulu kunokuba semibikweni yabantu.

UNkulunkulu uphakamise Ibandla lama-Seventh-day Adventist ukushumayela lombiko Ezweni. Ngalokho, siyadinga, ngokwethu, ukuphendukela eqinisweni linjengoba linjalo kuJesu, nokuba siguqulwe senziwe basha “ngevangeli laphakade” leSambulo 14:6, eligxile ekufeni kukaKristu efela thina, isiqiniseko sosindiso lwethu kuYena.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNhlangulana 24._
