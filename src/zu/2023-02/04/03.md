---
title:  Ukwesaba Nokulalela UNkulunkulu
date:  17/04/2023
---

Yikuphi okunye iBhayibheli elisifundisa kona mayelana nokuthi kuchaza ukuthini ukwesaba uNkulunkulu na?

`Funda uDuteronomi 6:2; iHubo 119:73, 74; no Mshumayeli 12:13, 14. Lezizindimana zembulani ngomphumela “wokwesaba uNkulunkulu” na?`

Lezizindimana zembula ukuxhumana phakathi kokwesaba uNkulunkulu kanye nokugcina imithetho Yakhe. Ukwesaba uNkulunkulu kungumoya wokuhlonipha oholela thina elulaleleni. Ubizo oluphuthumayo lwezulu olokuthi labo abasindiswe ngomusa balalele imithetho kaNkulunkulu (Efesu 2:8-10). Umusa awusikhululi ekulaleleni imithetho kaNkulunkulu. Ivangeli lisikhulula ecaleni lomthetho, hhayi kumthwalo wokuwulalela.

Umusa usikhulula kuphela ecaleni lokwedlulile, kodwa usinikeze amandla okuphila izimpilo zobunkulunkulu manje. Umpostoli uPawule umemezela ukuthi “samukele Umusa kanye nobupostoli bokulalela okholweni phakathi kwezizwe zonke” (Rom. 1:5, NKJV).

Kukhona abanye abanomqondo ongajwayelekile wokuthi usindiso ngomusa ngendlela ethile luchitha umthetho kaNkulunkulu noma kunciphisa isidingo sokulalela. Bakholwa ukuthi inkulumo emayelana nokulalela ingukuhambisa ngokomthetho (legalism). Bamemezele ukuthi, Engikufunayo nguJesu kuphela. Umbuzo uthi, “Muphi uJesu na?” UJesu wokuzakhela thina, noma uJesu weZibhalo na? UKristu weZibhalo akasiholeli ekudicileleni phansi umthetho Wakhe, oyisibonakaliso sesimilo Sakhe. UKristu weZibhalo akasiholeli ekunciphiseni izimfundiso zeBhayibheli, ezembula ngokusobala ukuthi ungubani Yena kanye necebo Lakhe ngalomhlaba. UKristu weZibhalo akasiholeli ekwehliseni izifumfundiso Zakhe zibe sezingeni lezisho zenkolo ezingadingekile. UJesu yiqiniso elenziwe umuntu. Uyizimfundiso eziphilwayo.

Ubizo lokugcina lweSambulo lusibiza ngokholo kuJesu ukuba samukele ukuphelela kwakho konke akunikezayo. Lusibizela ukuba “sesabe uNkulunkulu,” okubonakala ngokholo emandleni Akhe okusindisa asinikeza amandla okuphila izimpilo zobunkulunkulu nezilalelayo.

`Amazwi kaJesu lapha—" ‘Futhi ningabesabi labo ababulala umzimba kodwa bengakwazi ukubulala umphefumulo. Kunalokho mesabeni Yena okwazi ukubhubhisa umphefumulo nomzimba esihogweni’ ” (Matt. 10:28, NKJV)— asisiza kanjani ekuqondeni okuchazwa kungukumesaba uNkulunkulu na?`
