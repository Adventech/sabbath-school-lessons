### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Samb. 12:6, 14; Dan. 7:25; 2 Thes. 2:3, 4; Dut. 6:8; Dut. 11:18; Eksod. 20:8–11.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ngase ngibona enye ingelosi yenyuka empumalanga, inophawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo, yamemeza ngezwi elikhulu kuzo izingelosi ezine ezanikwa ukuba ziwone umhlaba nolwandle, 3 yathi: “Ningoni umhlaba, nolwandle, nemithi, size sizibeke uphawu izinceku zikaNkulunkulu wethu emabunzini azo’ ” (Revelation 7:2, 3, NKJV).

Kwikhulunyaka leshumi nanhlanu, izigodi zase-Piedemont, phezulu kuma-Alps asenyakatho ne-Italy, zaziyikhaya lama-Waldenses, abantu ababezimisele ukuhlala bethembekile ekuqondeni kwabo kweBhayibheli. Ngenxa yokuthembeka kwabo okuqinile kuKristu, bashushiswe ngokwesabekayo. Ngo-a.d. 1488, ama-Waldenses Esigodini sase-Loyse abulawa ngesihluku yiBandla laseRoma ngenxa yokholo lwabo.

Elinye igagasi lentshushiso leza ngekhulumnyaka leshumi nesikhombisa, ngenkathi i-duke yase-Savoy ithumela amabutho angu-8,000 esifundeni sawo futhi yaphoqa abantu ukuba bahlalise amabutho akhe. Lokhu bakwenza njengokwesicelo, kodwa kwakuyiqhinga lokuthi amabutho athole kalula abazobabulala. Ngo-April 24, 1655, ngo-4:00 A.M., uphawu lwanikezwa lokubulala. Kulokhu ababulawa babalelwa ngale kuka-4,000.

Ngeshwa, umlando uyaziphinda. Isiprofetho “sophawu lwesilo” simayelana nokuxhumana kokugcina eketangeni ekingenabunkulunkulu lokushushisa kwezenkolo okubuyele emuva isikhathi eside. Njengakwizintshushiso ezedlule, kuhlelwe ukuphoqa bonke abantu ukuba bahambisane nezinkolelo ezithile kanye nohlelo lokukhonza olubekiwe. Njengokuvama, nokho, uNkulunkulu uyoba nabantu abangeke bavume.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNhlangulana 17._
