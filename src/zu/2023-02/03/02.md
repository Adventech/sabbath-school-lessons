---
title:  Incwadi YeThemba Egcwele Umusa
date:  09/04/2023
---

Lapho abantu abaningi becabanga ngencwadi yokugcina yeBhayibheli, iSambulo, abacabangi ngomusa kaNkulunkulu. Lapho bebuka umbiko kaNkulunkulu wesikhathi Sokugcina, imicabango yabo ivama ukuphendukela ngokushesha ezilweni ezesabekayo, izimpawu ezifihlakele, kanye nemifanekiso engajwayelekile. Incwadi yeSambulo isabisa abantu abaningi ibe iqinisa abaningi futhi, okuyishwa ngoba, nembala, Umusa kuyo uyisidadada futhi igcwele Ithemba. Okungukuthi, naphakathi kwezilo ezesabekayo kanye nezixwayiso zezintshushiso kanye nezikhathi zobunzima, uNkulunkulu usasinikeza izizathu zokuthokoza osindisweni Lwakhe.

`Funda iSambulo 1:1-3 neSambulo 14:6. Lezizindimana zisitshelani ndawonye hhayi nje kuphela ngencwadi yeSambulo kepha nange“vangeli Laphakade,” na? `

ISambulo simayelana noJesu. Ngumbiko Wakhe kubantu Bakhe futhi uthinta ngokukhethekile ibandla Lakhe ezinsukwini zokugcina. Ngumbiko ogcwele Umusa wethemba Lethu lesikhathi Sokugcina. Kuyo yonke Incwadi, uKristu uchazwa njengeWundlu elabulawa, futhi nesibusiso sithenjisiwe kulabo abafunda, baqonde, futhi benze ngokwamaqiniso ambuliwe.

NgokweSambulo 1:5, 6, uJesu nguYena “owasithanda futhi wasihlanza ezonweni zethu ngegazi Lakhe, futhi usenza amakhosi nabapristi kuNkulunkulu noBaba Wakhe” (NKJV). KuKristu sithethelelwe. Umusa uthethelela ingemuva Lethu, uqinise inamuhla, futhi uhlinzeke ithemba ngekusasa lethu. Okungukuthi, kuKristu sihlengwa enhlawulweni nakumandla esono, futhi ngelinye ilanga maduzane sizokhululwa ebukhoneni besono. Lona ngumbiko wencwadi yokugcina yeBhayibheli, iSambulo.

Futhi ngumbiko ophuthumayo, okokuqala uvezwa njengengelosi indiza ngokushesha emkhathini iphethe “ivangeli laphakade.”

Ivangeli na? Usindiso ngokholo kuKristu na? Ukufa KukaKristu kokubuyisana nathi na? Isithembiso sokuphila okuphakade, hhayi ngenxa yalokho esingakwenza kepha ngenxa yalokho uKristu asenzele kona na? Konke Lokhu kusekuqaleni kwimibiko yezingelosi ezintathu na? Nje!

Akumangalisi, ke, ukuthi yimibiko egcwele Umusa yethemba kanye nesithembiso kithina njengezidalwa ezilimele nezihluphekayo.

`Nakuba kulula ukugxila ezilweni nasezixwayisweni zezinsuku zokugcina, njengalokhu kuvezwa kwiSambulo, singafunda kanjani ukulinganisa lokhu nalokho, nembala, okungumbiko osemqoka kunayo yonke yeSambulo: ukufa kukaKrisut kokuzinikela esikhundleni sethu na?`
