---
title:  'Umhlaba: Ishashalazi LoMsebenzi'
date:  12/10/2023
---

`Funda iSambulo 7:9, 10. Lombhalo uvezani ngokusabalala komsebenzi kaNkulunkulu ezweni na?`

Isifundo saleliviki sikhulume ngokuhlosile ngemibhalo emibili yomsebenzi egcizelela ukuba ngumongo wokwenza abafundi Ekuthunyweni Okukhulu kanye nombiko wevangeli laphakade. Ngokuthathekisayo, yomibili imibhalo inokuxhumana: “ikuphi” lomsebenzi. Ifundeka kanje: “Ngakhoke hambani nenze abafundi ezizweni zonke” (Mat. 28:19, NKJV), “labo abahlala emhlabeni—zonke izizwe, nemindeni, nezilimi, nabantu” (Samb. 14:6, NKJV).

Ngamanye amazwi, ivangeli likaKristu kumele lifinyelele kuzo zonke izigaba, zonke izizwe, zonke izilimi, kanye nabantu. Ithonya levangeli kumele lihlanganise abasindisiwe ebuzalwaneni obukhulu. Sinesibonelo esisodwa sokuba sisilinganise, leso nguKristu. Uma samukela iqiniso njengalokhu likuJesu, ukucwasana ngokobuzwe kanye nomona kuyoshabalala, futhi umoya weqiniso uyohlanganisa izinhliziyo zethu zibe munye.

Ngenkathi uJesu ethi, “ ‘Niyoba ngofakazi Bami’ ” (Zenzo 1:8, NRSV), wayenezifunda ezintathu emqondweni:

Isifunda 1: “ ‘Niyoba ngofakazi Bami eJerusalema.’ ” Ngalesosikhathi, abafundi Bakhe babeseduzane neJerusalema. Ngalokho, uJesu wayethi, “Qala ukwabelana ngamava akho noNkulunkulu nabantu abasondelene nawe.” Umsebenzi uqala ekhaya, ngomndeni, ngomakhelwane, ngabangani. Lena yindawo ebaluleke kunazo zonke yomsebenzi.

Isifunda 2: Ube eseqhubeka: “ ‘EJudiya lonke naseSamariya.’ ” Umsebenzi wethu ufaka labo ngendlela ethile abasondele kithina kodwa kusenjalo bekude nathi. Kuleliqembu kunabantu okungabe bakhuluma ulimi lwethu—abantu osiko labo lifana nelethu, kodwa bengahlali noma bengabelani ngeqiniso esinalo. Lokho kwengezela indawo yokusebenza.

Isifunda 3: Ngale kwalokhu, uKristu uthi: “ ‘kuze kube segcineni komhlaba’ ” (NKJV). Umsebenzi kaNkulunkulu usibizela ekufinyeleleni kubantu ezindaweni zonke, ezizweni, kwizilimi, nakwizinhlanga. Lena yindawo yokugcina yomsebenzi.

`Inselelo: Khulekela, nsuku zonke kuleliviki, umphakathi ophila kuwo. UNkulunkulu ukubeke lapho ngesizathu.`

`Inselelo Ethe Xaxa: Hlola ngokwehlukana kwabantu endaweni okuyo (hlobo luni lwabantu ophila phakathi kwabo)—izinhlanga nenkolo, abadala, abasha, abampofu, abacebile, izilimi ezikhulunywayo, njalo, njalo. Cela uNkulunkulu akubonise indlela ongaba ngayo ngumgudu wothando Lwakhe kubo.`
