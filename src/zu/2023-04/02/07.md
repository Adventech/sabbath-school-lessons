---
title:  Ukucabanga Kabanzi
date:  13/10/2023
---

Kube ngubufakazi ezizweni zonke. “Amazwi oMsindisi, ‘Ningukukhanya kwezwe,’ akhomba eqinisweni lokuthi unikele kubalandeli Bakhe umsebenzi kazwe lonke. Njengoba imisebe yelanga ingena kumagumbi akude omhlaba, kanjalo uNkulunkulu uhlele ukuthi ukukhanya kwevangeli kuyofinyelela kuwo wonke umphefumulo emhlabeni. Uma ibandla likaKristu beligcwalisa inhloso yeNkosi, ukukhanya bekuyofinyelela kubo bonke abahleli ebumnyameni nasesigodini sethunzi lokufa; kunokuba abuthane ndawonye futhi agweme umthwalo nokuthwala isiphambano, amalungu ebandla ebeyosabala kuzo zonke izizwe, avumele ukukhanya kukaKristu kukhanye kuwo, esebenza njengalokho asebenzela ukusindiswa kwemiphefumulo, futhi ‘lelivangeli lombuso’ beliyofinyelela ezweni lonke ngokuphuthuma.

“Emazweni onke ubizo lwaseMasedoniya luyezwakala, ‘Wozani nizosisiza.’ UNkulunkulu uvule amasimu phambi kwethu. Izithunywa zezulu zibambisane nabantu. UNkulunkulu uhamba phambi kwethu, futhi amandla ezulu asebenzisana nemizamo yabantu. Ayimpuputhe ngempela amehlo angakuboni ukusebenza kweNkosi, zivalekile izindlebe ezingaluzwa ubizo loMalusi weqiniso ezimvini zakhe. Abanye baluzwile ubizo lukaNkulunkulu, futhi basabela. Yonke inhliziyo engcwalisiwe mayisabele manje, ngokufuna ukumemezela umbiko onikeza ukuphila. Uma amadoda nabesifazane ngokuzithoba nokuthembeka bengathatha umsebenzi wabo abawunikezwa nguNkulunkulu, amandla ezulu ayobonakala ekuguqukeleni eqinisweni kwabaningi. Iyomangalisa imiphumela yemizamo yabo.”—Ellen G. White, The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, November 14, 1912.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`Ukukholeka kwethonya lebandla emphakathini kudalwa kakhulu yizinga thina—umzimba kaKristu—esibonakalisa ngayo ezimpilweni zethu uthando lukaKristu ekufezeni umsebenzi Wakhe. Wena, qobo lwakho, usabela kanjani kulenselelo na?`

`Ucabanga ukuthi omakhelwane balo abangewona ama-Adventist balibona futhi beliqonda kanjani ibandla lakho na? Wazi kanjani na? Uma indlela ababona ngayo iyinhle, yini ongayenza ukuyiqinisa na? Uma ingeyinhle, yini ongayenza ukuyiguqula na?`

`Kungani ukugcina “ivangeli laphakade” njengomongo wokusebenza emhlabeni kubaluleke kangaka na? Yiliphi ithemba elikhulu esingalethula kunoma ngubani, noma yikuphi, elingazinzile ethembeni elikhulu esinalo ngenxa yevangeli, izindaba ezilungileyo zalokho uJesu asenzele kona esiphambanweni na?`
