### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 Amakhosi 11:1–6; Mat. 4:23–25; Mat. 15:22–28; Marko 7:24–30; Izenzo 10:34, 35; Mat. 8:10.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Khona uJesu waphendula, wathi kuye: ‘O mame, kukhulu ukukholwa kwakho; makube kuwe njengokuthanda kwakho.’ Indodakazi yakhe yasinda kusukela kuleso sikhathi” (Mathewu 15:28, NKJV).

K usukela ekuqaleni, uNkulunkulu onothando wafuna abantwana Bakhe abalahlekileyo (Gen. 3:9); futhi, kuze kube namuhla, loNkulunkulu onothando usafuna ukufinyelela abalahlekileyo (buka iSamb. 14:6-12), kuhlangane nalabo abalahlekile emadolobheni. Ngo-2018, i-United Nations yashicilela imiphumela yophando lwayo olukhulu, eyayithi u-55 percent wabantu abahlala emhlabeni bahlala emadolobheni futhi lokhu kuzokhula (uma isikhathi siqhubeka) kuye ku-68 oercent ngo-2050. Akukhu esingakwenza: kumele sivangelile kulabo abasemadolobheni.

Kepha abantu abaningi bakaNkulunkulu benza njengoJona enza ngenkathi ebizelwa ukuyofakaza edolobheni: noma kungasiphi isizathu, bayawubalekela lomsebenzi. “Ngokuba konke okwalotshwa ngaphambili kwalotshelwa ukufundiswa kwethu, ukuze ngokubekezela nangenduduzo yemibhalo sibe nethemba” (Rom. 15:4, NKJV). Lokhu kufaka nalokho okwalotshwa ngoJona.

Ngenkathi elapha, uJesu akasebenzanga kuphela kulabo ababesemadolobheni akwaIsrayeli kepha kulabo bezifunda zangaphandle ngokunjalo; okungukuthi, kulabo abangebona besizwe samaJuda nabantu abakhethiweyo.

Kuleliviki sizofunda indaba yeBhayibheli ngomsebenzi kaKristu eTire naseSidoni futhi sitomule izifundo ezisebenza ezimpilweni zethu namuhla.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Disemba 16._
