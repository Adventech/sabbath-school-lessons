### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Izenzo 17; 1 Kor. 2:2; Rom. 1:18–25.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘UNkulunkulu owenza umhlaba nakho konke okukuwo, yena oyiNkosi yezulu nomhlaba, akahlali emathempelini enziwe ngezandla’ ” (Izenzo 17:24, NIV).

Efanekisa lokho uPawulu akwenza eAthene, uLuka wabhala: “Ngakho-ke wakhuluma esinagogeni* kubaJuda nakuwo amakholwa nasesigcawini imihla ngemihla kubo ababesuke bekhona” (Izenzo 17:17, NKJV).

Ngokwemvelo, uPawulu wayeyoba okhululekile ukusebenza phakathi kwamaJuda, abenyama negazi lakhe. Kodwa uPawulu wanqaba ukwaneliswa ngukusebenza kuphela phakathi kwabantu bakhe. Wayebizelwe ukufinyelela nakwabanye futhi.

Noma uPawulu wayengasebenza kuphela nabeZizwe “abasaba uNkulunkulu,” ondlela ababuka ngayo izwe yayiguquke kakhulu. Babenesisekelo sezibhalo uPawulu ayengakhela phezu kwaso, nakuba babesadinga ukwazi uNkulunkulu “ababemesaba”—uJesu uMesiya.

Kodwa, qha bo. EseseAthene, idolobha elaziwa ngobuciko balo, uPawulu wafuna ukufinyelela kulabo bantu ngokunjalo. Iningi labo lalinezimvelaphi ezehlukene kakhulu futhi nokubuka kwabo izwe kwehluke kakhulu kunokwamaHeberu nomlando wawo ongcwele, owawenza isisekelo sokholo uPawulu ayefuna ukulufundisa abaseAthene.

UPawulu wenza kanjani ukufuna ukufinyelela kulababantu, futhi yini esingayifunda kwimizamo yakhe na?

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Desemba 9._
