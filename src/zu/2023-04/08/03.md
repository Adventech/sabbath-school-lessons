**Izindaba ezilandelayo zisitshelani ngokusebenzela abasweleyo na?**

`Johane 5:1–9`

`Marko 1:23–28`

U-Ellen G. White usinikeza uhlelo lwezigaba ezinhlanu zendlela kaJesu yokusebenza, ikakhulu kulabo abasweleyo: “Indlela kaKristu kuphela enganikeza impumelelo yangempela ekufinyelele kubantu. UMsindisi waba phakathi kwabantu njengofisa okuhle kubo. Wazwelana nabo, wasebenzela izidingo zabo, futhi wazuza ukumethemba kwabo. Wayebayaleza ‘Landelani Mina.’ ”—The Ministry of Healing, p. 143.

Okokuqala, kumele sibephakathi kwabadinga usizo, sichithe isikhathi sokwazana nabo, futhi siqonde ngezidingo zabo ngenhloso yokwenza okulungileyo kubo. Buka lokho uJesu akwenza koshwabeneyo echibini. UJesu wayelapho, phakathi “kwabagulayo abaningi kakhulu, izimpumputhe, nezinyonga, nabashwabeneyo” (Johane 5:3, NKJV).

Okwesibili, sidinga ukubonisa uzwelo. Lokhu kungaba yinselele kwezinye izikhathi ngenxa yokungathembi, nokuthi ngezinye izikhathi abantu basebenzisa ubumnene njengendlela yokuzuza ithemba lothie okuthi kamuva babahlukumeze. Noma kunjalo, uNkulunkulu usibizela ekuboniseni uzwelo ngaphandle kokulindela imbuyiselo.

Isinyathelo sesithathu ngesokuxazulula izidingo zabo. Lokhu kufaka okungaphezulu kwamazwi. Kuthatha isenzo ukusebenzela izidingo zomngani noma isihambi. UJesu wakhuluma noshwabeneyo, wabuza ayekufuna, wayesemenzela isimangaliso. Endabeni yendoda eyayi “nomoya abangcolileyo,” uJesu walawula isimo, enzela indoda edingile lokho eyayingakwazi ukuzenzela kona.

Isinyathelo sesine ngesokuzuza ithemba labo. Lapho sisebenzela abantu, lapho sibasiza, bazofunda ukusithemba nangalokho esikusho kubona, futhi uma sikhuluma nabo ngoJesu, bayovuleleka ekulaleleni. UJesu wayengafuni nje ukubaphulukisa enyameni; wayefuna babenokuphila okuphakade kuYena (buka uJohane 10:10).

Isinyathelo sokugcina ngesokubaholela kuJesu, isenzo esidinga ukholo kuwena nakulowo omsizayo.

`Asikwazi ukwenza izimangaliso uJesu ayezenza. Kodwa yiziphi izindlela esingakwazi ukusebenzela ngazo labo abasweleyo na?`
