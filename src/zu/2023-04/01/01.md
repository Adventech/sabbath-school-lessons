### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Gen. 3:9–15; Gen. 28:15; Ekod. 29:43, 45; Mat.1:18–23; Johane 1:14–18; Johane 3:16; Johane 14:1–3.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kepha uJehova uNkulunkulu wambiza u-Adamu, wathi kuye: ‘Uphi na?’” (Genesisi 3:9, NKJV).

Umsebenzi uthola umsuka wawo nenhloso kuNkulunkulu kuphela. Lomsebenzi awuqalanaga ngobizo luka-Abram (Gen. 12:1-4) noma ngoFuduko (Eksod. 12:31-42). Awuqalanga nangokufika kukaJesu Kristu emhlabeni (Mat. 1:18-25) noma ngohambo lovangelo lukaPawule (Zenzo 13:4-14:26). Lomsebenzi waqala ngoNkulunkulu Uqobo, ngenkathi edala izwe lonke futhi kamuva edala isintu (Gen. 1:26, 27).

Ezibhalweni, sibona uNkulunkulu ngenhloso efinyelela futhi efuna ukuba nabantwana Bakhe. Kusukela ekuqaleni, Yena wasungula ubudlelwane noAdamu noEva. Nasekungeneni kwesono, Yena waqhubeka nomsebenzi Wakhe, kodwa manje sewungukusungula kabusha ubudlelwane Bakhe nesintu. Ekugcineni, umsebenzi kaNkulunkulu uyofezeka Sambulo 21-22), okungakho kumele sikhuthazele emsebenzini wokumemezela ivangeli laphakade emhlabeni wonke (Samb. 14:6, 7).

Isisekelo sanoma yimiphi umsebenzi, ngakhoke, kumele sigxile kubudlelwane noMdali kanye nokuqondisisa uhlobo lomsebenzi Wakhe nesimo sawo. Kodwa ngaphambi kokuba siqonde umsebenzi kaNkulunkulu, kusemqoka ukuba siqonde uNkulunkulu womsebenzi.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Oktoba 7._
