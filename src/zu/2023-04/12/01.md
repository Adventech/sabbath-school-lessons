### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Dan. 1:1–12; Dan. 6:1–9; Esther 2:1–10, 20; Esther 3:1–15; Esther 4:1–14; Esther 9:1–12.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Ngiyakukubeka ube ngukukhanya kwabezizwe, ube yinsindiso yami kuze kube semkhawulweni womhlaba’ ” (Isa. 49:6, NRSV).

Enye yezindaba ekhuthaza kakhulu eBhayibhelini, indaba “yomsebenzi oxuba amasikompilo,” itholaka encwadini ka-Esteri. Kuningi osekubhaliwe eminyakeni eminingi mayelena nalencwadi, kuze kube namuhla iningi lamajuda ligubha umkhosi we-Purin ngokugxile ku-Esteri 9:26-31. U-Esteri nomalume wakhe babengamanJuda ahlala kwikomkhulu loMbuso wase-Persia, i-Susa. Ngesizathu esithile, ngokungafani namanye amaJuda ayebuyele kwelakwaJudah, bona, kanye nabanye, basala ezweni lokuthunjwa kwabo.

Khona, ngohla lokuhlinzeka, u-Esteri uba yindlovukazi. “Inkosi yamthanda u-Esteri phezu kwabo bonke abesifazane, wafumana umusa nesihe emehlweni ayo kunazo zonke izintombi, wamfaka umqhele wobukhosi, wamenza inkosikazi esikhundleni sikaVashiti” (Esther 2:17, NKJV).

Kukulendima lapho u-Esteri, nakuba wayenqikaza, akwazi ukudlala indima enkulu emlandweni weBhayibheli. Ngendlela yayo eyodwa, lendaba ibonisa indlela abantu bakaNkulunkulu, nasezindweni zokuhamba, abangaba ngofakazi beqiniso.

Lokho okuvunywa yisikhathi sakho, funda incwadi ka-Esteri kulesisifundo saleliviki.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Disemba 23._
