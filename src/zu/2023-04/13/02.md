---
title:  'ISambulo: Umsebenzi KaNkulunkulu Wosuku Lokugcina'
date:  24/12/2023
---

Imishu evula iSambulo ikhomba kumfundi ukuthi lencwadi igxile emsebenzi kaNkulunkulu.

`Funda iSambulo 1:1-7. Kungaziphi izindlela obona ngazo ubufakazi bokuthi iSambulo sigxile emsebenzini kaNkulunkulu ezikhathini zokugcina na?`

Ngemuva kokwembula ezindimaneni zokuqala ukuthi uJesu ngomthombo nokugxila kweSambulo, iSambulo 1:2, 5 sibhekisa kumalungu onke amathathu obuNkulunkulu, asebenza ngobunye ekusindiseni abantu. UBaba ungowaphakade owayekhona futhi ukhona futhi uyobakhona. UMoya oNgcwele, osebenza ngamandla phakathi kwamabandla ekhulu-nyaka lokuqala, uyabalulwa. Bese uJohane ekhumbula isimo sikaJesu Kristu—“ufakazi othembekileyo,” “izibulo labafileyo” (Samb. 1:5, NRSV), ophethe ubunikazi obusemthemthweni bomhlaba. Imizamo kaSathane yokusebenzisa lomhlaba ukusungula umbuso wakhe ishabalele. Ngaphezu kokunqoba uSathane, igazi loMdali wethu lihlanza icala nehlazo lethu.

`Funda iSambulo 1:6 no 1 Petro 2:9. Izihloko zabasindisweyo ziphawulani kulezizindimana na? `

Ukuqonda komsebenzi kaNkulunkulu akukona nje ukudonsela abantu ababhubhayo ekuphepheni. Usundiso lukaNkulunkulu lunikeza isimo esisha nesiphakeme, ngoba umfanekiso kaNkulunkulu ubuyiselwe kithina. Abahlengiweyo baba ngabasebukhosini ngoba sihlobene ngegazi neNkosi yezwe lonke ngegazi likaJesu. Manje, njengamalungu omndeni wasebukhosini, sihlanganyela emsebenzini wasebukhosini ekusindiseni abanye abantu. Lokhu kusenza abapristi! Lokhu kusenza abapristi! UNkulunkulu wayefake ebandleni Lakhe “umbuso” futhi ilungu ngalinye “umpristi.” Ukuba yilungu lombuso kungukuba ngumpristi

KwiSambulo 1:7, sithola ukuphuthuma komsebenzi: uJesu uyeza, futhi izizwe ziyolila ngoba zilahliwe. UNkulunkulu ulangazelela labo abaqhelile kuYena. Incwadi yeSambulo ivula, ke, ngomsebenzi kaNkulunkulu kubantu.

`Asidaliwe nje kuphela nguNkulunkulu, kodwa sihlengiwe nguYena, futhi ngentengo emangalisayo. Kungani lokhu kumele kusinikeze ithemba elingaka, noma ngabe sinjani isimo njengamanje na?`
