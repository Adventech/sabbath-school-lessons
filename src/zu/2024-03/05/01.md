### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Marko 4:35–41, Hub. 104:1–9, Marko 5:1–43, Num. 27:17.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kepha uJesu akamvumelanga, kepha wathi kuye, ‘Hamba uye ekhaya kwabakini, ubabikele ukuthi kungakanani iNkosi ekwenzele khona, yakuhawukela’ ” (Marko 5:19, NKJV).

UKusebenza kukaJesu kwakugxile kakhulu eGalile, ikakhulu ngaseChibini laseGalile, ichibi elilinganiselwa ku-21 km ubude kanye no-13 km ububanzi. Liyisiziba esikhulu kunawo onke esifundeni futhi sasingumongo wokuphila kubantu abahlala ngakuso.

UMarko 4 uphetha ngoJesu nabafundi Bakhe bewela Ichibi laseGalile. Kuqubuka isiphepho uJesu asithulise ngokukhuluma kumoya namagagasi. UMarko 6 uphetha ngombono ofanayo, kodwa kulokhu ngoJesu ehamba phezu kwamanzi eya kubafundi Bakhe emkhumbini. Phakathi kwalemibono phezu kwamanzi ziningi izimangaliso zikaJesu ezenziwa emhlabeni, kanye nomsebenzi wokuqala wabafundi Bakhe. Lezizindaba ziyisihloko sesifundo saleliviki.

Uphawu olwengamele lezizindaba ezinkulu ngukuvumela ofundayo ukuba abone ukuthi uJesu ungubani. NguYena okwazi ukuthulisa isiphepho, aphulukise onamadimoni, aphulukise owesifazane omane wathinta izingubo Zakhe, avuse intomabazane efileyo, ashumayele edolobheni lakubbo, athume abafundi Bakhe ukuyovangela, ondle abangu-5,000 ngezinkwa nezinhlanzi ezimbalwa, ahambe phezu kwamanzi—kubonakalisa amandla amakhulu asondeza abafundi ekuqondeni ukuthi Yena uyiNdodana kaNkulunkulu.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNcwaba 3._
