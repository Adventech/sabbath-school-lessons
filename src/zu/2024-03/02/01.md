### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Marko 1:16–45, Johane 1:29–42, Marko 5:41, Luka 6:12, Levitikus 13.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “UJesu wayesethi kubo, ‘Ngilandeleni; ngiyakunenza nibe ngabadobi babantu’ ” (Marko 1:17, NKJV).

IVangeli ngalinye lethula ukuqala kokusebenza kukaJesu ngendlela ethile. UMathewu wethula uJesu engobiza abafundi futhi eshumayela Intshumayelo Entabeni.

ULuka uxoxa indaba yentshumayelo yokuqala kaJesu ngeSabatha kwisinagoge eNazaretha. UJohane ukhumbula ubizo lwabanye abafundi bokuqala kanye nomshado eKana, lapho uJesu enza khona isibonakaliso Sakhe sokuqala. Ivangeli likaMarko libuyekeza ukubizwa kwabafundi abane futhi lichaze iSabatha e-Capernaum nalokho okwalandela.

Kuleli “Sabatha noJesu” ekuqaleni kukaMarko linikeza umfundi umuzwa wokuthi uJesu ungubani. Kuso sonke isigaba sesifundo saleliviki kumazwi ambalwa Akhe alotshiwe: ukubizela kubufundi, ukukhuza amadimoni, uhlelo lokuvakashela ezinye izindawo, kanye nokuphulukiswa konochoko ngomyalezo wokuhamba aziveze kumpristi ukuze ahlanzeke. Ukugcizelela kusezenzweni, ikakhulu ukuphulukiswa kwabantu. Umlobi wevangeli uthanda ukusebenzisa igama masinyane ukubonisa ukushesha komsebenzi kaJesu.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabath, UNtulikazi 13._
