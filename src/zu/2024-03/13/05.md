---
title:  Ukubonakala KuMariya Nakwabanye
date:  25/09/2024
---

`Funda uMarko 16:9-20. Yini ethayiselwa yilezizindimana endabeni yovuko na?`

Cishe wonke uMarko 16:9-20 unokuhambaisana neminye imibhalo kwiTestamente Elisha—uMariya Magdalene ethuneni ebona uJesu (Mat. 28:1, 9, 10; Johane 20:11-18; qhathanisa noLuka 8:2); amadoda amabili abona Yena emaqeleni (Luka 24:13-35); abayishumi nanye bayathunywa (Mat. 28:16-20, Luka 24:36-49, Johane 20:19-23).

Umuntu wokuqala ukubona uJesu ephila nguMariya Magdalene (Johane 20:1118). Abanye besifazne bambona, ngokunjalo (Mat. 28:8-10). Kuyaphawuleka ukuthi abantu bokuqala ukubona iNkosi evukileyo kwakungabesifazane. Ngoba abesifazane esikhathini sasemandulo babengenalo izinga eliphakeme lokuba ngofakazi, uma indaba yabo yayingamanga, kwakungalindeleka kakhulu ukuba babize amagama abesilisa njengofakazi bokuqala. Kodwa akubona abaselisa, hhayi abaYishumi Nambili, kodwa ngowesifazane. Uyahamba uyotshela abafundi izindaba ezinhle, kodwa ngokungethusi, ababukholwa ubufakazi bakhe, futhi kungezeka kwakubukeka kungakholakali, futhi, ngeshwa, ngoba uMariya engumuntu wesifazane.

Abakhulumela indaba yovuko lukaJesu basebenzise leliqiniso, lokuthi kube ngabesifazane abakuqala ukubona uJesu, njengobufakazi obunamandla beqiniso balendaba.

`Yini eyenzeka kuMarko 16:14 engenzi mqondo uma lendaba ? Ingamanga na?`

Nembala, uma babeqamba amanga ngalendaba, kungani babengazenza babukeke kabi kangaka na? UJesu kwafuneka ebathethisele “ubukhuni bezinhliziyo” zabo. Imibiko yamaVangeli, ukusukela ekuboshweni kuye ekubonakaleni Kwakhe emuva kovuko, iveza abalandeli bakaJesu ngendlela engeyinhle: bayabaleka, bayaphika, abakholwa, njalo-njalo. Lokhu akwenzi mqondo uma indaba ingamanga.

Ngokwehlukile, isibindi sabo sakamuva nokumemezela kwabo okungemanqikanqika ngoKristu ovukileyo, kanye nethemba okulinikeza wonke umuntu, kuveza ubufakazi obunamandla ngokuqiniseka kokubanga kwabo.

`Singazivikela kanjani ekuweleni ogibeni lwezikamoya lokungabaza nokungakholwa na? Kungani kumele sizixhumanise noKristu ovukileyo nsuku zonke na?`
