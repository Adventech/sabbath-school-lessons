---
title:  Ngenakukhohlala
date:  08/09/2024
---

`Funda uMarko 14:1-11. Yiziphi izindaba ezimbili ezixhumene lapha, futhi zihambisana kanjani na?`

UMarko 14:1 ukhomba ukuthi kwasekusele izinsuku ezimbili kube yiPhasika. Lomhlangano kungenzeka wabakhona ebusuku ngoLwesibili noma ngoLwesithathu lwangaleloviki. Abaholi bezenkolo banecebo kanye nesikhathi. Abakudingayo nje kuphela yindlela yokufeza imigomo yabo. Lokhu kuzoqhamuka endaweni engalindelekile.

Lesisahluko siyindaba yesihlanu kweziyisemishi kuMarko (buka isifundo 3). Indaba yogibe ngakuJesu ixhumana nendaba yowesifazane ogcoba ikhanda likaJesu ngamakha ayigugu. Abalingiswa ababili abahambisanayo benza izinto eziphikisanayo.

Lowesifazane olapha akembuliwe nguMarko. Isipho sakhe esimangalisayo kuJesu simelene nenkohlakalo kaJudasi ekukhapheleni iNkosi yakhe. Owesifazane akabiziwe ngegama; uJudas ubizwa ngomunye wabaYishumi Nambili. Ixabiso lesipho sakhe liyashiwo; inani lakhe (Judas) liyisithembiso semali nje.

Asinikezwa isizathu esiqondile sokuthi kungani yena (owesifazane) enza lokhu akwenzayo, kodwa izivakashi esidlweni ziyanengwa ngalokho ezikuthatha njengokuchitha imali enkulu elingana neholo lonyaka ekutheleni amakha kuJesu. UJesu, kodwa, ungenelela ekuvikeleni owesifazane ngokuthi lokho akwenzile kuyofakwa ekumemezelweni kwevangeli kuwo wonke umhlaba njengesikhumbuzo kuyena. Ngokungenakukhohlakala. Nembala, onke amaVangeli omane abika indaba yakhe ngezindlela eziningi, mhlawumbe ngenxa yamazwi kaJesu ekhumbuzisa ngesenzo sakhe.

Ukukhaphela kukaJudasi ngeke kwakhohlakala nako. UMarko ugudla ukuthi isisusa sakhe kwakungumhobholo. Ivangeli likaJohane likubeka obala (Johane 12:46).

UMarko uqukethe ukudlala ngegama “okulungile” ukuze abonise ukuthi izisusa ezimbili ezahlukene, noma ugibe, ziyenzeka kulendaba. UJesu uthi isenzo sowesifazane “silungile/sihle” kuMarko 14:6. Yena uthi ningakwenza njalo “okuhle” kwabampofu (Marko 14:7) futhi kuMarko 14:9, athi isenzo sakhe siyingxenye “yeZindaba Ezilungileyo/ivangeli.” KuMarko 14:11, uJudasi ufuna “ithuba elilungile” (ISV) lokukhaphela uJesu. Lokhu kudlala ngamagama kuncoma ukuthi ugibe lwabantu lokubulala uMesiya luzoba yingxenye yendaba yevangeli ngoba ifeza intando kaNkulunkulu ekunikeleni ngeNdodana Yakhe ukusindisa isintu
