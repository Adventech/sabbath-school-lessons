### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Marko 14, Johane 12:4–6, Rom. 8:28, Eksod. 24:8, Jer. 31:31–34, Zak. 13:7.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Wayesethi,‘Aba, Baba, konke kungenzeka kuwe; ake ususe kimi lesi sitsha, kepha kungabi njengokuba ngithanda mina, kodwa njengokuba uthanda wena’ ” (Marko 14:36, NKJV).

Izahluko 14 kuye ku-16 kuMarko zaziwa njenge—“The Passion Narrative” ngoba zichaza ukuhlupheka, ukufa, nokuvuka kukaJesu. Njengoba kuphawuliwe esifundweni 9, izahluko eziyisithupha zokugcina zikaMarko zibhekana neviki elilodwa kuphela. Iningi lezigameko kuMarko 14—16 zenzeka ngoLwesine nangoLwesihlanu lwe-Passion Week. Ukufa kukaJesu kuzokwenzeka ngoLwesihlanu, futhi ukuvuka Kwakhe ngeSonto.

Isifundo saleliviki sigxila kuMarko 14, siqala indaba eyisemishi lesihlanu, ehlanganisa izenzo ezimbili eziphikisanayo mayelana noJesu. Lokhu kulandelwa yiSidlo Sokugcina, kulandela umshikashika Wakhe e-Gethsemane. Lapho uyaboshwa futhi ayiswe phambi kwabaholi ukuyoquliswa. Umbono wecala uxhumana nokuphika kukaPetro ephika uJesu, okwenza indaba yesithupha neyokugcina kulezi eziyisemishi kuMarko. Futhi, izenzo ezimbili eziphikisanayo ziyenzeka, kodwa isibhinqo, ziqinisa iqiniso elifanayo.

Kuyo yonke lendaba, kunezindaba ezimbili ezimelene ezihambisayo. Ngendlela eqondile, uMarko ubeka phambi komfundi izinhlelo ezingqubuzanayo ngenkathi embula ukunqoba kukaJesu.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UMandulo 14._
