---
title:  Gethsemane
date:  10/09/2024
---

`Funda uMarko 14:32-42. Yini ayikhuleka uJesu e-Gethsemane, futhi umkhuleko waphendulwa kanjani na?`

Beshiya idolobha elibiyelwe laseJerusalema lapho bedle khona iPhasika, uJesu nabafundi Bakhe bawela i-Kidron Valley beya engadini kumehlelo weNtaba yemiNquma. Igama i-Gethsemane lichaza isikhama minquma, okuncoma ukuthi lapho kwakunemboni eyenza amafutha eminquma. Ukuthi kukuphi akwaziwa kahle ngoba amaRoma anquma yonke imithi Entaba yeMinquma ngenkathi ehlasela, ngo-A.D. 70.

Lapho uJesu engena engadini, ushiya abafundi Bakhe lapho aqhubekele phambili noPetro, Jakobe, noJohane. Kodwa abeseshiya laba abathathu futhi aqhubekele phambili yedwa. Lomgamu ukhombisa ukuthi uJesu uyangokuba yedwa lapho ebhekana nokuhlupheka Kwakhe okuzayo.

UJesu ukhulekela ukuba indebe yokuhlupheka isuswe, kodwa kuphela uma kuyintando kaNkulunkulu (Marko 14:36). Usebenzisa igama lesi-Aramaic Abba, uMarko alihumusha ngokuthi “Baba.” Lelilgama akulona elisetshenziswa yingane ukubiza uyise, lelo lithi abi (bheka u-Raymond E. Brown, _The Death of the Messiah_, Anchor Bible Reference Library [New York: Doubleday, 1994], vol. 1, pp. 172, 173). Kepha, ukusetshenziswa kwegama Abba, Baba, kuqukethe ukuba ngumndeni osondelene, okungafanele kuthathwe kancane.

Akukhulekayo uJesu ngukususwa kwendebe yokuhlupheka. Kodwa uzinikela entandweni kaNkulunkulu (qhathanisa noMkhuleko weNkosi, Mat. 6:10). Kuba sobala kuyo yonke i-Passion Narrative ukuthi impendulo kaNkulunkulu kumkhuleko kaJesu ngu—“Cha.” Ngeke Yena ayisuse indebe yokuhlupheka, ngoba ngalawo mava usindiso lunikezwa izwe.

Lapho ubhekana nobunzima, kuyakhuthaza ukuba nabangani abakuphasayo. KwabaseFilipi 4:13, uPawule ukhuluma ngokwenza zonke izinto ngaLowo omnikeza amandla. Iningi liyakhohlwa nguFilipi 4:14, lapho umpostoli evula, “Nakuba kunjalo.” Ufundeka kanje: “Nakuba kunjalo, kwabakuhle kini ukwabalena ngokuhlupheka kwami” (ISV). Yilokhu uJesu ayekufisa e-Gethsemane. Izikhathi ezintathu weza ukuzofuna induduzo kubafundi Bakhe. Izikhathi ezintathu babelele. Ekugcineni, Yena uyabavusa ukuba bahambe Naye ukuyobekana necala. Yena ukulungele; bona cha.
