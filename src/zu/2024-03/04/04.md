---
title:  Isizathu Semifanekiso
date:  23/07/2024
---

`Funda uMarko 4:10-12. Kungani uJesu afundisa ngemifanekiso na?`

Ukufunda okukha phezulu kwalezizindimana kunikeza umqondo wokuthi uJesu wafundisa ngemifanekiso ukuze abangaphandle bangazi lutho. Kodwa lokhu akuhambisani nezenzo zikaJesu kwenye indawo kuMarko. KuMarko 3:5, 6, uJesu udatshukiswa ngukuba lukhuni kwezinhliziyo zabaholi benkolo. KuMarko 3:22-30, uJesu uthathela phezulu izinkulumo zababhali futhi achaze kabanzi ukuthi kungani besephutheni. KuMarko 12:1-12 abaholi benkolo bayaqonda ukuthi umfanekiso kaJesu wabaqashi ungabo. Empeleni uyisixwayiso somphumela wozungu lwabo ngaYe kanye nemiphumela emibi ezolandela. Uma wayengenandaba nabo, Yena wayengeke abaxwayise. Ngenxa yalokho, amazwi kaJesu lapha kuMarko 4 adinga ukubukisiswa ukuze sibone iphuzu Lakhe. UJesu uphinda uIsaya 6:9, 10.

`Funda uIsaya 6:1-13. Kwenzekani kuIsaya lapha, futhi yimuphi umbiko awunikezwa ukuba awudlulisele kuIsrayeli na?`

UIsaya ubona umbono kaNkulunkulu esethempelini futhi wengamelwa yinkazimulo kaNkulunkulu kanye nokungcola kwakhe. UNkulunkulu uyamhlanza futhi amthume nombiko owethusayo. NjengoMarko, kuzwakala sengathi awuhambisani nawo wonke uIsaya lapho kunokududuza okuningi kwabantu bakaNkulunkulu.

KuIsaya 6 umbiko unenhloso yokwethusa abantu nokubaqwashisa ukuze baguquke ezindleleni zabo zobubi. KuMarko ukhiye wokuqonda amazwi kaJesu utholakala kuMarko 3:35. Ukuqonda amazwi kaJesu nezimfundiso, umuntu kumele enze intando kaNkulunkulu (Marko 3:35). Lokhu kuletha umuntu emndenini kaJesu. Labo asebenqumile ukuthi uJesu uphethwe ngamadimoni ngeke balalela.

Okuyikona uJesu akusho ngokucaphuna kuIsaya 6 akukona ukuthi uNkulunkulu akafuni abantu bangene, kodwa ngukuthi yimicabango yabo kanye nobukhuni benhliziyo okuvimbela bona ekwamukeleni iqiniso elisindisayo.

Leliqiniso lingumqondo owengamele womfanekiso womhlwanyeli. Umuntu ngamunye ukhetha uhlobo lomhlabathi azoba yilo. Bonke bazinqumela ngokwabo ukuthi bazozinikela kuJesu noma cha. Ekugcineni, ngamunye siyakhetha.
