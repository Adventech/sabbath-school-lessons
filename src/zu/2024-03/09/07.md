Funda u-Ellen G. White, “A Doomed People,” “The Temple Cleansed Again,” and “Controversy,” in _The Desire of Ages_, pp. 580–609.

“Isenzo sikaKristu ekuqalekiseni umuthi owawudalwe ngamandla Akhe kuyisixwayiso kuwo wonke amabandla nakuwo wonke amaKristu. Akekho ongaphila umthetho kaNkulunkulu ngaphandle kokusebenzela abanaye. Kodwa baningi abangayiphili impilo kaKristu yomusa, nesihe. Abanye abazicabanga bengamaKristu amahle kakhulu abakuqondi okufaka ukusebenzela uNkulunkulu. Bahlela futhi befundele ukuzithokozisa. Benza ngokumayelana nomina kuphela. Isikhathi sisemqoka kubona kuphela uma bengaqoqela kubona. Kuzo zonke izigaba zokuphila lokhu kungumgomo wabo. Hhayi ngokwabanye kodwa ngokwabo bayasebenza. UNkulunkulu wabadalela ukuba baphile ezweni lapho ukuzidela kumele kwenziwe. Wabahlelela ukuba basiza abaphila nabo ngazo zonke izindlela. Kodwa umina mukhulu kangangokuba abakuboni okunye. Abaxhumene nabantu. Labo abaphilela umina bafana nomuthi womkhiwane, owanikeza wonke umfanekiso kodwa ungenazo izithelo. Bagcina izimo zokukhonza, kodwa bengaphendukile noma bengakholwa. ngokusho bahlonipha umthetho kaNkulunkulu, kodwa basilele ngokulalela. Bayasho, kodwa abenzi. Kulesisigwebo kumuthi womkhiwane uKristu ubonakalisa ukunengekeka phambi kwamehlo ukuzenzisa okuyiko. Umemezela ukuthi isoni esingafihli sinecala elincane kunalowo ozibiza ngokhonza uNkulunkulu, kodwa engatheli izithelo zodumo Lwakhe.”—Ellen G. White, _The Desire of Ages_, p. 584.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`Zindla ngencazelo yokuhlanzwa kwethempeli nguKristu. Lesisimiso singasebenza kanjani ebandleni lethu namuhla na? Lokhu kuhlanzwa kumele kwenzeke kanjani na?`

`Kuwo wonke amaVangeli, ngokuphindiwe, uJesu ubhekisa Emibhalweni nangendlela okumele igcwaliseke ngayo. Lokhu kusitshela ini ngendlela engumongo ngayo empilweni yokukholwa na? Kungani kumele sichithe ngokuqinile yonke imizamo yokwehlisa igunya leMibhalo, ikakhulu umqondo wokuthi Imibhalo iyimiqondo nje yabantu ngoNkulunkulu, ukuthi uNkulunkulu ungubani, nokuthi usebenza kanjani na?`

`Ukuphi umugqa ofanele phakathi kwebandla nombuso na? Imfundiso kaJesu kuMarko12:13-17 iyihola kanjani lengxoxo na?`

`Funda izindimana ezikhuluma ngovuko. Kungani lemfundiso ingumongo wenkolo yethu, ikakhulu uma ucabanga ngesimo sabafileyo na?`
