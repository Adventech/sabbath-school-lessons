### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki Marko 11; 1 Amakhosi 132–48; Zak.9:9, 10; Isa. 56:7; Jer. 7:11; Marko 12:1–34.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Futhu njalo uma nima nikhuleka, uma ninkuthile ngakomunye, mxoleleni, ukuze uBaba wenu osezulwini ezinixolele nani amaphutha enu’ ” (Marko 11:25, NKJV).

Uhide lwezimpikiswano ezinhlanu phakathi kukaJesu nabaholi bezenkolo lubhalwe kuMarko 2 no 3 (buka isifundo 3). Esifundweni saleliviki, ngenkathi uJesu efika eJerusalema, uba nohide lwezimpikiswano eziyisithupha nabaholi bezenkolo. Ezimbili zalezizimpikiswano ziba ngezivula futhi zivale umsebenzi Wakhe emhlabeni. Iqoqwana ngalinye libhekana nezinto ezibalulekile empilweni yomKristu. Imfundiso kaJesu, nakuzo lezizimo zempikiswano eshubile, isiza amakholwa kwizisekelo zenkolo kanye nakwizinto eziphathekayo zamava ansuku zonke.

Abaholi bezenkolo beza ukuzolwa, baphazamise, futhi banqobe uJesu, kodwa abaphumeleli. Ingxenye yesifundo saleliviki izofaka ukuhlaziya ukuthi empeleni yini ebangela abantu baphikisane noNkulunkulu futhi nokubuka lokho amaKristu angakwenza ukugqabula lolubandlulo futhi akhulume nezinhliziyo zalabo abamelene nobizo lukaMoya.

KuMarko 11 umsebenzi kaJesu uzoba seJerusalema ngePhasika (uNdasauMbasa). UMarko 11-16 ukhuluma ngokungaphezulu nje kweviki; isikhathi sendaba sihamba kancane kakhulu. Izahluko ezingu-10 zokuqala zibhekane neminyaka elinganiselwa kwemithathu nengxenye. Lokhu kuncipha kwesivinini kukhomba ukubaluleka kwalezizinto ezenzeka ngasekupheleni.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNcwaba 31._
