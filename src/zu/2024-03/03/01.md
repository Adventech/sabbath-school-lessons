### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Marko 2:1–3:6, Mika 6:6–8, 1 Sam. 21:1–6, Marko 3:20–35, Luka 12:53, Luka 14:26.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Wayesethi kubo, ‘ISabatha lenzelwa umuntu, umuntu akenzelwanga iSabatha. Ngokunjalo iNdodana yomuntu iyinkosi neyeSabatha’ ” (Marko 2:27, 28, NKJV).

Mark 2:1–3:6 uqukethe izindaba ezinhlanu ezibonisa ukufundisa kukaJesu ngokwehlukile kunokufundisa kwabaholi bezenkolo. Lezizindaba zilandela indlela eqondile lapho indaba ngayinye elandelayo ixhumana naleyo eyandulelayo ngesihloko esihambisanayo. Indaba yokugcina ibuyekeza futhi ixhumane neyokuqala.

Ngayinye yalezizindaba ibonisa izigaba ezimbili zokuthi uJesu ungubani, njengalokhu kubonakaliswa yizisho kuMarko 2:10, 17, 20, 28. Izifundo zangeSonto, uMsombuluko, ngangoLwesibili zizogxila kabanzi ngencazelo yalemibiko nezisho zikaKristu kuyona.

UMarko 3:20-35 yisihloko sokufunda ngoLwesithathi nangoLwesine.

Esizokubona, futhi, yisibonelo sobuciko umlobi weVangeli abusebenziyo obubizwa “Ngamasemishi Ezindaba.” Lendlela yokuxoxa iqhamuka okungenani izikhathi eziyisithupha kuMarko. Esigabeni ngasinye okuthile okubalulekile ngesimo sikaJesu kanye nendima Yakhe njengoMesiya, noma isimo sobufundi, kugxilwa kukho.

Kuleliviki, sizofunda eminye imibiko mayelana noJesu futhi sibone esingakufunda kuyo.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNtulikazi 20._
