`Funda uMarko 3:20-35. Yikuphi ukuxhumana okubonayo phakathi kwezindaba ezimbili ezithungelene kulesisahluko na?`

Lesisahluko singesokuqala “Sendaba Eyisemishi” kuMarko, lapho enye indaba iqalwa bese iphazamiswa ngenye indaba, bese eyokuqala iqedelwe kamuva.

Indaba yokuqala imayelana nezihlobo zikaJesu ziphuma ukuyolawula Yena ngoba zicabanga ukuthi Yena usangene ekhanda (Marko 3:21). Indaba ephakathi imayelana nababhali baseJerusalema bebeka uJesu icala lokusebenzisana namadimoni. (Ukugxila kwethu namuhla kukwindaba ephakathi ekuMarko 3:22-30.)

KuMarko 3:22, ababhali baletha icala lokuthi amandla kaJesu okuphulukisa aqhamuka kuSathane. UJesu uphendula kuqala ngombuzo owengamelayo, “Usathane angamxosha kanjani uSathane na?”Akwenzi mqondo ukuthi uSathane azilwise. UJesu aqhubeke nokukhuluma ngokwehlukana phakathi kombuso, indlu, kanye noSathane uqobo, ebonisa ukungaphusi lokukwahlukana okungaba yikho empumelelweni yabo. Kodwa ke iNkosi iguqula amatafula futhi ikhulume ngokubopha isidlakela ukuze uphange umuzi waso. Kulesisibonelo sokugcina uJesu yisela elingena endlina kaSathane abophe inkosi yobumnyama ukuze akhulule abathunjiweyo.

`Funda uMarko 3:28-30. Yisiphi isono esingexolelwe, futhi lokho kuchaza ukuthini na?`

Isono esingexoleleke yisono ngakuMoya Ongcwele, ukubiza ukusebenza kukaMoya ngomsebenzi wedimoni. Qaphela ukuthi kuMarko 3:30 isizathu sokuthi uJesu asho lokhu kuMarko 3:28, 29, ngukuthi ababhali bathi Yena unomoya wedimoni, ngenkathi eqinisweni Yena enoMoya Ongcwele. Uma ubiza umsebenzi kaMoya Ongcwele ngomsebenzi wedimoni, ngeke wena wawulalela uMoya Ongcwele ngoba akekho umuntu ophusile ofuna ukulandela ukuhola kukaSathane.

`Kungani ukusaba ukuthi kungenzeka ube wenze “isono esingexolelwe” kwembula ukuthi awukwenzanga lokho na? Kungani lokho kwesaba kungubufakazi bokuthi awukwenzanga lokho na?`
