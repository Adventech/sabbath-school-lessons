---
title:  Amaviki Angu-70 Nezinsuku Ezingu-2,300
date:  15/10/2019
---

Igama “anqunyelwe” elitholakala kuDaniyeli 9:24, “Amaviki angamasg=humi ayisikhombisa anqunyelwe,” ngokuqondile lichaza ukuthi, “Amaviki angamashumi ayisikhombisa anqamuliwe.” Nakuba leligama elihumushwe “anqunyelwe” lingasetshenziswa kwenye indawo eBheyibhelini, liyatholakala ezibhalweni zesiJuda, futhi lichaza “ukunqamula” kokuthile okude. Njengoba uDaniyeli 8 ethula isiprofetho sezinsuku ezingu-2,300, osiqalo saso singanikeziwe kuDaniyeli 8, kungumqondo ukuthi lapho isahluko esilandelayo (Daniyeli 9) sikhuluma ngemiyaka engu-490 “enqunyelwe” noma “enqamuliwe,” “inganqamulwa” kuphela eminyakeni engu-2,300 yesiprofetho ebalulwe esahlukweni esandulelayo. Nembala, lesisikhathi “singanqamulwa” kuphi ngaphandle kwesinye, futhi eside, isiprofetho.

`Funda uDaniyeli 8. Iyiphi ingxenye yombono engazange ichazwe (buka ikakhulu Dan. 8:14, 26, 27) na?`

Ziningi isizathu zokuba isiprofetho samaviki angu-70 sikaDaniyeli9:24-27 kanye nesezinsuku ezingu-2,300 sikaDaniyeli 8:14 zibendawonye: (1) zombili yiziprofetho zesikhathi; (2) amatemu aqondile “umbono” kanye “nokuqonda” ayazihlanganisa (buka Dan. 8:26, 27 no 9:23); (3) ukuhumushwa kwazo zombili kwanikezwa ngu-Gabriel (buka Dan. 8:16 no 9:21); (4) ingxenye kuphela engahumushwanga kuDaniyeli 8 imayelana Nezinsuku ezingu-2,300 kuDaniyeli 8:14; (5) UDaniyeli 8 uqukethe umbono kanye nokuhumushwa okungaphele kwawo, ngenkathi uDaniyeli 9 enencazeli kuphela, kulokho isihumuhusho saleyongxenye kaDaniyeli 8 engahumushiwe—okuyisiprofetho sezinsuku ezingu-2,300 sikaDaniyeli 8:14, ingxenye uDaniyeli angayiqondanga (buka Dan. 8:27).

Ulwazi esilunikezwa ku-Ezra lugcwalisa izikhala zaleziziprofetho ezisencwadini kaDaniyeli, okungukuthi, kuqala kuphi ukubala emlandweni ngaleziziprofetho mayelana nomsebenzi kaKristu ngathi.
