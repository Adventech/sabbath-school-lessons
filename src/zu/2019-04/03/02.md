---
title:  Ukubizwa Kuka-Ezra No-Nehemiah
date:  13/10/2019
---

Singathi u-Ezra wakhethelwa izizathi ezahlukene: (1) wayevuma ukuhamba; (2) wayengumoli; futhi (3) wayengumbhali onekhono futhi enguthisha. Zikhona nezinye isizathu ezijwayelekile esingazithola futhi. Kodwa kunendimana eyodwa mhlawumbe ekuzeva kangcono ukuthi Kungani u-Ezra anikezwa lomsebenzi.

`U-Ezra 7:10 uthini mayelana no-Ezra na? U-Ezra “Wayeyilingiselele” noma “eyibeke” kanjani inhliziyo yakhe “ekufuneni umthetho kaJehova” nokuwenza na?`

Igama elimele “ukulungela” noma “Ukubeka” ngu-kun ngesiHeberu. Leligama lingahumushwa ngokuthi “ukulungela, Ukubeka, ukuqina, ukusungula ngokuqinile, ukuzinza noma ukubambisisa.” Ngakhoke, incazelo yangempela yalesisisho ibonaka engathi isho ukuthi u-Ezra wgxilisa inhliziyo yakhe noma wamisela inhliziyo yakhe ekufuneni uNkulunkulu. Emuva kokufika eJerusalema, u-Ezra waba yisibonelo aokuzinikela kuNkulunkulu, futhi wafndisa Izwi likaNkulunkulu eJerusalema iminyaka engu-13. Kusenokuba kwabonakala kuye sengathi awukho umehluko owenzekayo kuleminyaka engu13, kodwa, emuva kokuqeda ukwaka izindonga, abantu babiza imbizo—bengaphoqiwe, kodwa ngoba befuna. Izwi likaNkulunkulu ababekade belizwa ku-Ezra laligxilile.

`Kungani u-Nehemiah akhethwa na? Funda Nehemiah 1:1–11.`

U-Nehemiah wayenenhliziyo eyayithanda uNkulunkulu nabantu. Wayekhatahzekile ngenkathi ethola ukuthi umsebenzi eJerusalema wawumile. U-Nehemiah wayenentshisekelo yomsebebzi, futhi njengo-Ezra, wazinikela kulomsebenzi. UNkulunkulu wayiphendula imikhuleko nezifiso zabo. Ngezinye izikhathi sithola umqondo wokuthi uma sithanda okuthile akukwazi ukuvela kuNkulunkulu, ngoba uNkulunkulu uzosinikeza imisebenzi elukhuni kuphela esingase singafuni ukuyenza. Kodwa uma sihamba noNkulunkulu, izifiso zokwenza okuthile esikuthandayo zivama ukuqhamuka kuNkulunkulu. UNkulunkulu ufuna sibenentshisekelo ngalokho esmenzela kona.

`Yiziphi izindlela osube namava eqiniso lokuthi uNkulunkulu ukubizela ukuba umenzele izinto ozithandayo na?`
