### Kuleli viki funda ku
Neh. 12:27–47; 1 Chron. 25:6–8; 1 John 1:7–9; John 1:29, 36; 1 Cor. 5:7; Heb. 9:1–11.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Baphendulana ngokumdumisa nangokumbonga uJehova, bathi: ‘Ngokuba muhle, ngokuba umusa wakhe umi phakade phezu kuka-Israyeli’ ” (Ezra 3:11, NKJV).

Indimana Yekhanda yaleliviki inikeza ukhasha emikhubeni yamaHeberu yokukhonza kanye nangendlela ukubonga uNkulunkulu kwakuphuphumele ekudumiseni Yena. Ngo-515 B.C. bagubha ukunikelwa kwethempeli elisha (Ezra 615-18) kwathi futhi, eminyakeni engu-60 emuva kwalokho, abantu bagubha ukunikelwa kodonga olusha lweJerusalema (Neh. 6:15–7:3; 12:27 kuye phambili). Emuva kokubala izizukulwane ku-Nehemiah 11 no 12, Umbhali uphendukela esikhathini lapho bebungaza khona udonga lwedolobha. Kwakuwusiko esizweni ukunikela izinto kuNkulunkulu: ithempeli, udonga lwedolobha, noma izindlu kanye nezakhiwo zomphakathi. Lokhu kunikela kwakuhlelwa kahle futhi kuhambisane nokucula, izingoma, ukudla, imihlatshelo, ukujabula, ukuthokoza, kanye nokuhlanzwa kwabantu. UDavide wasungula lomkhuba wokwenza umhlatshelo ngesikhathi sokunikela, kwathi kamuva abaholi bakwaIsrayeli balandela Isibonelo sakhe, kusukela kuSolomoni ngenkathi eletha umphongolo ethempelini (1 Amakhosi 8:5). Kuleliviki sizobuka indlela ababekhonza ngayo uJehovah ngalesosikhathi futhi sibuke izinto thina, esikhonza uJehovah owayekhonzwa yibo, esingazisebenzisa kithina.
