---
title:  Ukuphendula Kwabantu
date:  06/11/2019
---

Lapho u-Ezra evula Izwi likaNkulunkulu, i-Torah yesiHeberu, abantu bayasukuma. Ngaphambi kokuba u-Ezra afunde, ubusisa uNkulunkulu. Ngemuva kokuba efundile, abantu bayaphendula ngo-“Amen, Amen!” (Neh. 8:5, 6; NKJV) ngazwi linye ngenkathi bephakamisela izandla zabo ezulwini. Bakhothamisa amakhanda abo futhi badumise bekhotheme.

`Funda u-Nehemiah 8:9–12. Kungani abaholi betshela abantu ukuba “bangalili noma bangakhali” na?`

“Ngakhoke, eminyakeni yakamuva, ngenkathi umthetho kaNkulunkulu ufundwa eJerusalema kubathunjwa ababebuye eBhabheli, futhi nabantu balila ngenxa yeziphembeko zabo, amazwi amahle akhulunywa: “Ningakhali.... Hambani nidle okunonileyo, niphuze okumnandi, nithumele izabelo kwabangalungiselwanga lutho, ngokuba lolu suku lungcwele kuJehova; ningadabuki, ngokuba injabulo kaJehova iyinqaba yenu.’ Nehemiah 8:9, 10.”—Ellen G. White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 281. Njengoba abantu babelalele amazwi kaNkulunkulu, bahlabeka ngezono zabo futhi baqala ukulila. Lapho uNkulunkulu ezembula kithina futhi siqale sibone ukuthi uNkulunkulu ugcwele uthando, nokulunga, nomusa, kanye ngokwethembeka, ukusilela kanye nokwehluleka kwethu ukuba yilokho okumele sibe yiko kuba sobala. Ukubona ubungcwele bukaNkulunkulu ngeZwi Lakhe kusenza sibone ububi bethu ngeso elisha. Lokhu kubona kwenza abantu bakwaIsrayeli balile futhi bakhale, kepha kwakungamele badabuke, “ngokuba injabulo kaJehova iyinqaba yenu” (Neh. 8:10). Nagamanye amazwi, nakuba behlulekile, babengathembela emandleni kaNkulunkulu. Lolu kwakuwusuku olukhethekileyo, usuku olungcwele, Umkhosi wamaCilongo (Rosh Hashanah), okungalo ukukhala ngezikhawu ezimfushane kwamacilongo kwakuphawula ukubaluleka kokulungiselela “inhliziyo” ukwahlulela KukaNkulunkulu (Usuku lokuBuyisana, olwagujwa Ngosuku lweshumi lwenyanga ka-Tishri). Ukushaywa kwamacilongo kwakuphawula ubizo lokuma phambi KukaNkulunkulu uphenduke. Ngenxa yokuthi usuku lwaluhlelelwe ukukhumbuza abantu ukuba babuyele kuNkulunkulu, ukulila nokukhala kuyaqondakala. Kodwa abaholi bakhumbuza abantu ukuthi lapho bephendukile, uNkulunkulu wayebazwile, futhi ngalokho kwakuyisikhathi sokujabula entethelelweni kaNkulunkulu.

`Lokhu kumele kusitsheleni ngobubi besono ngokuthi saphanyeka uJesu esiphambanweni njengendlela okungukuphela kwayo yokuxazulula inkinga yesono nokusinika ithemba na?`
