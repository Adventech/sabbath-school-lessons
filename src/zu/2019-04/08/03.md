---
title:  Izivumelwano Emlandweni
date:  18/11/2019
---

Ngemuva kukazamcolo, uNkulunkulu waqala phansi, manje noNowa kanye nabantu abalandela kamuva. Nangabo, futhi, wafuna ubudlelwane, futhi okwakungumongo walobobudlelwane kwakungumqondo wesivumelwano. IBhayibheli libalula izivumelwano eziyisikhombisa ezinkulu uNkulunkulu azenza nabantu:

- 1st Isivumelwano – Adam (Genesis 1–3)
- 2nd Isivumelwano – Noah (Genesis 6–9)
- 3rd Isivumelwano – Abraham (Genesis 12:1–3)
- 4th Isivumelwano – Moses kanye nesizwe sakwaIsrayeli (esaziwa njengeSivumelwano saseSinayi noma sikaMose; Exodus 19–24)
- 5th Isivumelwano – Phineas (Num. 25:10–13)
- 6th Isivumelwano – David (2 Sam. 7:5–16)
- 7th Isivumelwano – New Covenant (Jer. 31:31–34)

`Funda izahluko ezilandelayo. Zichaza ukuthini “ngesivumelwano saphakade” na? (Gen. 9:16, 17:7, Isa. 55:3, Heb. 13:20).`

IBhayibheli lisebenzisa itemu “isivumelwano saphakade” izikhathi eziyishumi nesithupha. Kuzo, eziyishumi nantathu zazibhekiswe kwizivumelwano noAbrahama, noIsrayeli eSinayi, kanye noDavide. Ngasinye kulezizivumelwano ezingenhla, nakuba zizodwa, zazinophawu “lwesivumelwano saphakade.” Njengevangeli laphakade limemezelwa okokuqala kuGenesisi 3:15, kodwa limbuleka ngokuqhubekayo kulo lonke iBhayibheli, kuyafana nakwisivumelwano saphakade. Isivumelwano ngasinye esilandelayo sandisa futhi sidephise ukuqonda kwethu ngesivumelwano saphakade sothando, esembulwa kakhulu ngokuphelele ecebeni losindiso. Isivumelwano Esisha neSidala, njengalokhu zivame ukwehlukaniswa, ziqukethe izinto ezifanayo.

1. Ukungcwaliswa: “Ngizofaka umthetho Wami ezingqondweni zabo, futhi ngiwulobe ezinhliziyweni” (Jer. 31:33, NKJV; qhathanisa no Heb. 8:10).
2. Ukubuyisana: “Ngizoba nguNkulunkulu wabo, futhi bayoba ngabantu Bami” (Jer. 31:33, NKJV; Heb. 8:10).
3. Ukuthunywa: “Abasayikufundisana, kube yilowo nalowo umakhelwane wakhe, kube yilowo nalowo umfowabo, ngokuthi: ‘Yazi uJehova,’ ngokuba bonke bayakungazi, kusukela kwabancane kuya kwabakhulu babo” (Jer. 31:34, NKJV; Heb. 8:11).
4. Ukulungisiswa: “ngokuba ngiyakuthethelela ububi babo, nesono sabo angisayikusikhumbula” (Jer. 31:34, NKJV; Heb. 8:12).
