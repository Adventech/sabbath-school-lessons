### Kuleli viki funda ku
Neh. 10:1–29, Gen. 4:8–19, Heb. 13:20, Joshua 24, Neh. 10:30–39, Heb. 8:1–7.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “ ‘Ngenxa yakho konke lokho senza isivumelwano esiqinisileyo, sasiloba; kwanamatheliswa ngophawu yizikhulu zethu, namaLevi ethu, nabapristi bethu.’ . . .  asinakuyishiya indlu kaNkulunkulu wethu” (Nehemiah 9:38; 10:39, NKJV).

Lichaza ukuthi iBhayibheli lapho likhuluma “ngesivumelwano” na” Incazelo elula kunazo zonke zaloluhlobo lwesivumelwano sezibhalo ngukuthi; ngukusungula ngokusemthethweni ubudlelwane phakathi kukaNkulunkulu nabantu Bakhe. NguNkulunkulu ethi, “Ningabantu Bami, futhi nginguNkulunkulu wenu.” Ngale kwalokho, singathola ukusetshenziswa kwezivumelwano ezilotshiwe phakathi kwabanye abantu emhlabeni wasendulo, imvamisa phakathi kwabaholi nabakhonza ngaphansi kwabo. Lezizivumelwano zasungulwa ngoba zazinenzuzo nhlangothi zombili. Umholi wayenakekela abantu, kuthi abantu bakhokhe intela. Kepha kuNkulunkulu, isivumelwano sasehlukile. UNkulunkulu wayengazuzi lutho kuso, kepha wayethembisa ukwethembeka kuso, nakuba abantu bengathambekile. Nembala, izibusiso neziqalekiso ezihambisana nesivumelwano zazenza kwenzeke kwabakwaIsrayeli ukuba bazi uma izinto ezimbi ziqala ukwenzeka, ukuba babephule isivumelwano. Kuleliviki, sizobuka isivumelwano abakwaIsrayeli abasivuselela noNkulunkulu, ku-Nehemiah 10, futhi sixoxisane ngolwazi olujwayelekile mayelana nomlando kanye nokubaluleka kokwenza isivumelwano eBhayibhelini.
