---
title:  Ithonya Labaholi
date:  22/12/2019
---

Kulo lonke iBhayibheli, singathola izibonelo zobuholi, obuhle nobubi, futhi ngezinye izikhathi ingxubevange yabo bobubili. Abaholi ababi, ngezinye izikhathi, benze izinto ezinhle, ngenkathi, kwezinye izikhathi, abaholi abahle benze izinto Ezimbi. Nembala, bonke abaholi bangabantu, njengathi, bayakwazi ukubabahle nokubababi, ukwenza okuhle nokubi. Ngubani ongakaze abe namava aleliqiniso empilweni yakhe na? Inkinga, nokho, ngukuthi ngenkathi wena ungumholi, unethonya elikhulu, lobuhle noma lobubi. Kubi vele ukuba nethonya elibi ekhaya noma emsebenzini, noma ngabe yikuphi lapho ukhona. Kepha uma usesikhundleni sobuholi, noma kukwezikamoya, kwezepolitiki, noma kokubili, Ithonya liphinda phindeka kaningi. Kubaluleke kakhulu ke, kunoma iyiphi indima, kepha ngokukhethekile njengomholi, ukuba uveze izimiso nezimfundiso zeMibhalo.
