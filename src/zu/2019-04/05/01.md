### Kuleli viki funda ku
Neh. 5:1–5; Exod. 21:2–7; Micah 6:8; Neh. 5:7–12; Deut. 23:21–23; Neh. 5:14–19.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “ ‘Ake nibuyisele namhlanje amasimu abo, nezivini zabo, neminqumo yabo, nezindlu zabo, nenxenye yekhulu yemali, neyamabele, neyewayini, neyamafutha enikubiza kubo kube yinzalo’ ” (Nehemiah 5:11, NKJV).

Kuze kuba namuhla, isintu sinenkinga ngombuzo womnotho, ububha, kanye nomsansa phakathi kwabacebile kanye nabampofu kanye nangalokho okungenziwa ngako. Yebo, uJesu wathi “abampofu bayobakhona njalo phakathi kwenu” (Mat. 26:11, NKJV), kodwa lokho akusona izaba zokungenzi lutho ngokubasiza. Kunalokho, Izibhalo ziseluleka ngokuthi sense indima yethu yokusiza. Kungenjalo asikwazi ukuzibiza ngamaKristu. Kuyathathekisa futhi, futhi, ukuthi Naphakathi kwezinhlupheko zababue ekudingisweni ekwakheni kabusha iJerusalema, lendikimba iyaqhamuka, hhayi nje eyobubha kanye nabampofu, kepha naleyo yenkinga enkulu yabacebileyo becindezela abampofu. Lokhu kwakuyinkinga ngaphambi kokudingiswa, futhi namanje, sebebuyele ezweni labo, isaqhamuka.

Kuleliviki sizobona okunye ukuzibonakalisa kwalendikimba endala, nangendlela u-Nehemiah abhekana nayo. Njengoba sizobona, okwakenza lengcindezi ibe yimbi kakhulu ngukuthi yayenzeka “ngokusemthethweni,” isibonelo esinamandla sokuthi kumele siqikelele singavumeli imithetho ibe yisiphetho sendaba kunokuba ibe wusizo ekuvezeni isimilo sikaJesu.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungela iSabatha lika-November 2._
