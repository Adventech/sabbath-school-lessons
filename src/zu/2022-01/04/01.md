### Kuleliviki funda ku
Lev. 25:25-27; Heb. 2:14-16; Heb. 11:24-26; 1 Korinte 15:50; Heb. 5:8, 9; Heb. 12:1-4.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda “Ngakho-ke njengokuba abantwana bahlanganyela igazi nenyama, Naye uqobo wahlanganyela  khona lokho, ukuze ngokufa amchithe obe nawo amandla okufa onguSathane” (Heberu 2:14).</p>
> AmaHeberu 1 akhuluma nguJesu eyiNdodana kaNkulunkulu, umbusi.phezu kwezingelosi, engu “kumenyezela kwenkazimulo yaKhe, efuze Yena (Heb. 1:3). KumaHeberu 2, uJesu uyiNdodana yoMuntu, owenziwa wangaphansana kwezingelosi, nowathatha isimo somuntu nabo bonke ubuthakathaka baso, waze wafika ekufeni (Heb. 2:7).

KumaHeberu 1, uNkulunkulu ukhuluma ngoJesu athi: “UyiNdodana yaMi” (Heb. 1:5). KumaHeberu 2 uJesu ukhuluma ngezingane zabantu athi “abafowethu” (Heb. 2:12).

KumaHeberu 1, uBaba umemezela ubukhosi obungcwele beNdodana (Heb. 1:8-12). KumaHeberu 2, iNdodana iqinisekisa ukwethembeka kwaYo kuYise (Heb. 2:13a).

KumaHeberu 1, uJesu uyiNkosi eNgcwele.  KumaHeberu 2, unguMdali, oweseka konke, futhi eyiNkosi.  KumaHeberu 2, uJesu unguMpristi oMkhulu wasenyameni, onesihe nothembekile.

Ngamafuphi, ukuchazwa kukaJesu njengomfowethu onesihe kubonakala ekuchazweni kweNdodana ngokuthi ingumfanekiso waphakade kaNkulunkulu onguMdali (Heb. 1:1-4).

*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha likaMasingana 22
