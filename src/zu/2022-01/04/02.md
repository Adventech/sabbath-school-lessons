---
title:  UMfowethu NjengoMhlengi
date:  16/01/2022
---

`Funda: kuLevitikusi 25:25-27, 47-49.  Ubani owayengahlenga umuntu olahlekelwe umhlaba wakhe noma inkululeko yakhe ngenxa yobumpofu?`

Umthetho kaMose wawuthi uma umuntu empofu ngendlela yokuthi kwakudinga athengise ngendawo yakhe, noma yena abe yisigqila somunye, ukuze akwazi ukuphila, wayezoyibuyiselwa leyo ndawo noma inkululeko yakhe ngonyaka wamashumi amahlanu, unyaka wejubili.  Unyaka wejubili kwakungunyaka weSabatha “elikhulu” lapho kwakusulwa izikweleti, kubuyiselwe imihlaba, kumenyezelwe inkululeko yezigqila.

Iminyaka engamashumi amahlanu yisikhathi eside sokulinda kodwa. Kungakho-nje umthetho kaMose wabuye wathi isihlobo segazi esisondele singasikhokha isikweleti esisasele, kanjalo-ke sihlenge isihlobo saso sikhululeke masinyane.

Isihlobo esisondele futhi isona esasiqinisekisa ukuthi buyenzeka ubulungiswa uma kunodaba lokubulala.  Sasingumphindiseli wegazi owayezosukela umbulali wesihlobo sakhe, amjezise (Num. 35:9-15).

`Funda: kumaHeberu 2:14-16.  UJesu, nathi, uchazwe kanjani kulezindimana?`

Lezindimana zisichaza njengezigqila zikaSathane, kodwa uJesu njengoMhlengi wethu. Ngokona kukaAdamu, abantu baba ngaphansi kwamandla kaSathane.  Umphumela kwaba ukuthi asisenawo amandla okumelana nesono (Roma. 7:14-24).  Okubi kakhulu kodwa ukuthi kwadingeka kungene isigwebo sokufa ngenxa yokuthi thina singakwazi ukukhokhela ukwephula kwethu umthetho (Roma 6:23). Icala lethu lalingathembisi.

Kodwa uJesu wathatha isimo somuntu, waba yinyama negazi njengathi. Waba yisihlobo sethu segazi, esisondele, wasihlenga. Akabanga namahloni okusibiza “ngabafowabo” (Heb. 2:11).

Okusengathi kuyaziphikisa ukuthi ngokuthatha imvelo yethu asihlenge, uJesu wembula imvelo yaKhe yobuNkulunkulu. ETestamenteni eliDala, uMhlengi weqiniso kaIsrayeli, isihlobo sabo esisondele, nguYahweh (isibonelo, iHubo 19:14, Isaya 41:14, Isaya 43:14, Isaya 44:22, Jer. 31:11, Hoseya 13:14).

`Yiziphi izindlela ongafunda ngazo ukuzizwela ngokujulile lelo qiniso lokuthi uKrestu angasondela eduze kangakanani kuwena? Kungani lawo mava ebaluleke kangako kuwena?`
