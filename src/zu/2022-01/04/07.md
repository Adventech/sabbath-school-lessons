---
title:  Ukujula Nomcabango
date:  21/01/2022
---

AmaHeberu 2:2:13 aqukethe amazwi kaJesu awakhuluma kuYise maqondana nabazalwane baKhe, uthi: “Bheka mina nabantwana uNkulunkulu anginike bona” (Heb. 2:13). UPatrick Gray unombono othi uJesu uchazwa lapha njengomlindi wabafowabo. Uhlelo lwamaRoma okwakuthiwa yi tutela impuberum lwaluthi ngemuva kokushona kukababa wekhaya, indodana endala iyona ezonakekela izingane zakwabo nefa lazo zize zifinyelele ezingeni lokuzimela, ngaleyo ndlela kwenezelwa phezu kokuziphendulela kwemvelo kwendodana endala ukuthi inakekele izingane zakwabo.”—Godly Fear: The Epistle to the Hebrews and Greco-Roman Critiques of Superstition [Atlanta: Society of Biblical Literature, 2003), p. 126. Lokhu kuyachaza ukuthi kungani amaHeberu esibiza njengabafowabo kaJesu, aphinde asibize ngabantwana baKhe. NjengoMnewethu, uJesu unguthisha wethu, umlindi wethu, nomvikeli wethu.

“UKrestu weza emhlabeni, wathatha isimo sabantu, waba nguMmeli wabantu, ukutshengisa kulempikiswano noSathane, ukuthi umuntu, njengoba wayedalwe nguNkulunkulu, uma exhumene noBaba kanye neNdodana, angaba nakho ukulalela yonke imiyalo kaNkulunkulu.”—Ellen G. White, Selected Messages, book 1, p. 253.

“Ngempilo yaKhe nezimfundiso zaKhe, uKrestu usinike isibonelo esiphelele somsebenzi ongenakuzicabangela odabuka kuNkulunkulu. UNkulunkulu akaziphileli.  Ngokudala umhlaba, nokwesekela izinto zonke, ulokhu esebenzela abanye. ‘Wenza ilanga laKhe likhanye phezu kwababi nabahle, athumele imvula kwabalungile nabangalungile.’ Math. 5:45. UNkulunkulu uwunikele eNdodaneni yaKhe lomsebenzi. UJesu wabekwa ukuthi abe yinhloko yabantu, ukuze ngesibonelo saKhe afundise ukuthi kuyini ukusebenzela abanye.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 649.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`➊ 	AmaHeberu asitshela ukuthi uJesu waba umfowethu ukuze asisindise.  Cabanga ukuthi kusho ukuthini lokho ngokwenziwa uNkulunkulu ukuze asisindise. Kungani-ke ukufulathela lokhu okumangalisa kangaka kuyiphutha elidabukisayo?`

`➋ 	Kubaluleke ngani kithina ukuthi uJesu wazalwa “engathengisiwe esonweni” njengathi (Rom. 7:14)?  Cabanga ngoMose, nokuthi kwakubaluleke ngani kumaIsrayeli ukuthi wayengesona isigqila njengabo. Isisiza kanjani indaba kaMose, ngendlela encane, ukuthi siqonde lokho uJesu asenzela kona?`

`➌ 	Ake ugxile phezu kweqhaza lezinhlupheko ezimpilweni zethu. Kungani kungafanele sicabange ukuthi ukuhlupheka kukodwa, kuyinto enhle, yize noma kukhona okuhle okungavela lapho?`
