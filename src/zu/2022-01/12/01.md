### Kuleliviki funda ku
Heb. 12:18-29; Eks. 32:32; Daniyeli 7:9, 10, 13-22; Hagayi 2:6-9, 20-22; iHubo 15:5; iHubo 16:8; Heb. 13:15, 16.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda “Ngakho-ke lokhu samukeliswa umbuso ongenakunyakaziswa, masibonge ngesingamkhonza ngakho uNkulunkulu ngokumthokozisa, nangokumhlonipha, nangokuthuthumela” (Heberu 12:28, NKJV).</p>
> AmaHeberu 12:18-29, izindimana zaleliviki, yisiqongo salencwadi, futhi zigoqa okuyiyona ngqikithi ngokuphinda lomqondo okwakuqalwe ngawo wokuthi: uNkulunkulu ukhulume nathi ngeNdodana yaKhe, sidinga ukuqaphelisisa akushoyo (Heb. 1:1, 2; Heb. 12:25). Ukuchazwa kukaJesu kumaHeberu 12:22-24 kusinika isibonelo sokushiwo yilencwadi ngaYe: uJesu unguMlamuleli wesivumelwano esisha, kanti igazi laKhe linika abakholwayo ukusindiswa.  Inkonzo yaKhe yobupristi nobukhosi ngenxa yethu yisizathu sokuthi senze umgubho. Okokugcina, incwadi yamaHeberu 12:25-29 iphethe isixwayiso sokugcina esithi: ukwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu kuyeza. Kuyoletha incithakalo ezitheni zaKhe, kodwa abantu baKhe liyobavuna icala, banikwe umbuso (Heb. 12:28, 29).

Isiphetho siqinisa ukubaluleka kwalokho okwafezwa nguJesu esiphambanweni, sitshengise abazalwane ukupheleliswa kokunqoba kukaJesu. UPawulu wasebenzisa imifanekiso ethathwe kuDaniyeli 7 ukukhumbuza abafundayo ukuthi uJesu wamukele umbuso kuNkulunkulu, onguMahluleli (Dan. 7:9-14), kanti uzokwabelana ngalowo mbuso nabakholwayo, “abangcwele boPhezukonke,” kube ngowabo kuze kube phakade (Dan. 7:18).

*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha likaNdasa 19.
