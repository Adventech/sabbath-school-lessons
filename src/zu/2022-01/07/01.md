### Kuleliviki funda ku
Heb. 6:4-6, Math. 16:24, Rom. 6:6, Heb. 10:26-29, Heb. 6:9-13, Heb. 6:17-20.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda “Esinalo njengehange lomphefumulo, liqinile, ligxilile, lingena kokungaphakathi kwesihenqo, lapho uJesu eyisigijimi sokusandulela wangena khona, esewaba ngumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokokuma kukaMelkizedeki” (Heberu 6:19, 20, NKJV).</p>
> AmaHeberu 5:11-6:20 athikameza ukuchazwa kwesifundiso ngobupristi bukaJesu asenzela bona. UPawulu ufaka lapho isixwayiso esiqinile ngengozi yokuhlubuka kuKrestu.

Kubonakala ukuthi abantu babesengozini yangempela yokuhlehlela emuva, bazidabukele, bangabi nokukholwa. Umphostoli uPawulu ukhathazekile ngokuthi abafundayo nabalalele kungase kwenzeke ukuthi bafikelwa ubuthongo bomphefumulo ngenxa yezimo ezilukhuni ababebhekene nazo, ngaleyo ndlela abazange bakhule evangelini.

Lokho kungebe yini ingozi kithina sonke; ukuthi sidangale ngenxa yezilingo, size sihlubuke?

Lesixwayiso siqinile kodwa sigcina ngesikhuthazo esimnandi. UPawulu uzwakalisa ukubethemba abafunda lencwadi, aphakamise uJesu okunguYena oqukuthe emzimbeni waKhe isithembiso sikaNkulunkulu esingenakwephulwa. (Heb. 6:9-20). Lokhu kuxwayiswa nokukhuthazwa kuyaphindaphindwa kumaHeberu 10:26-39.

Sizofunda ngalokho kuphindaphindwa kwesixwayiso esiqinile, nenkuthazo evela kuJesu.

*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha likaNhlolanja 12.
