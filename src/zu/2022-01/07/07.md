Funda kuEllen G. White, “John the Beloved,” pp. 539-545, ku The Acts of the Apostles; “Judas,” pp. 716-722, ku The Desire of Ages.

“Impi yokulwisana no ‘mina’ iyona enkulu kunazo zonke ezake zaliwa. Ukudela ‘umina’ unikele konke entandweni kaNkulunkulu, kudinga umzabalazo; kodwa umphefumulo udinga ukuzithoba kuNkulunkulu ngaphambi kokuthi wenziwe musha ebungcweleni.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, p. 43.

“UJohane wayefisa ukuba njengoJesu, futhi ngokusizwa amandla aguqulayo othando lukaKrestu, wagcina emnene, ethobile. “Umina” wafihlwa kuJesu.  Ngaphezu kwabo bonke abazalwane bakhe, uJohane wazinikela kulawo mandla empilo emangalisayo ….

“Kwaku uthando olujulile lukaJohane ngoKrestu olwamholela ekuthini afise ukuba seduze noJesu njalo. UMsindisi wayebathanda bonke abafundi abayishumi nambili, kodwa umoya kaJohane yiwona owawuvuma kakhulu. Wayemncane kunabanye, ngokwethemba okufana nokwengane, wavulela uJesu inhliziyo yakhe.  Ngaleyo ndlela kwaba khona ukuzwana okukhulu phakathi kwakhe noKrestu, ngaye, izimfundiso zoMsindisi ezijulile zadluliselwa ebantwini ….

“Ubuhle bobungcwele obabumguqulile bakhazimula ngenkazimulo kaKrestu ebusweni bakhe. Ngokudumisa nangothando wabheka kuKrestu kwaze kwafika lapho ukufana noKrestu nobudlelwane naYe kwaba izona zinto azifisayo, esimilweni sakhe kwabonakala isimilo seNkosi yakhe.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, pp. 544, 545.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`➊ 	Izimpilo zikaJohane, umfundi othandiweyo, noJudas Iskariyoti, zisinika ukuqhathanisa okuphikisanayo.  Ngesikhathi uJesu ebona uJohane nomfowabo, wababiza ngokuthi  o-Bowanergesi, okusho  ukuthi ‘amadodana okuduma’.  UJohane wayenokusilela kwesimilo okukhulu. NoJudas ngokunjalo, kodwa okukaJudas kwakungekukhulu kunokukaJohane. Kungani uJohane akwazi ukuguqulelwa emfanekisweni kaJesu, kodwa uJudas wenza isono kuMoya oNgcwele?  Kwakuyini umehluko?`

`➋ 	UJesu umema abakholwayo ukuba bathathe iziphambano zabo, bamlandele.  Yini umehluko phakathi kokuthatha isiphambano, nokuvuma ukuphathwa kabi ngabanye abantu?`

`➌ 	Kungani uNkulunkulu efuna sizinikele ngokuphelele izimpilo zethu kuYena?  Kuxhumene kanjani ukuzikhethela nokusindiswa?`
