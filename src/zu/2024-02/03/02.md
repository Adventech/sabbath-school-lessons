---
title:  'Ukuyekethisa: Iqhinga LikaSathane Lesinyelela'
date:  14/04/2024
---

`Qhathanisa uJohane 14:6 noJohane 8:44. Kunokwehluka kuni phakathi kwesimilo sikaJesu nesikaSathane ngokwalezindimana?`

Lokhu akushoyo uJesu kuyiqiniso ngoba ungumqambi weqiniso.  Iqiniso liphuma enhliziyweni kaNkulunkulu onokuhlakanipha konke, uthando lonke, ulwazi lonke. Uyisisekelo salo lonke iqiniso.

Ngakolunye uhlangothi, uSathane ungumqambi-manga, noyise wawo Uzimisele ukusebenzisa amanga, inkohliso, ulwazi oludukisayo, nokuhlanekezela iqiniso ukuze alahlekise abantu bakaNkulunkulu. Wakhohlisa u-Eva e-Edeni ngokuhlanekezela iqiniso, wafaka ukungabaza, waphika ngokusobala lokho okwashiwo nguNkulunkulu. Amazwi kaSathane athi: “aniyikufa nokufa,” (ekhuluma mayelana nokudla isithelo esenqatshelwe), kwakuphikisana-ngqo neZwi likaNkulunkulu. Kuyo yonke iminyaka uSathane ubesebenzisa lobo buqili. Udicilela phansi ukwethenjwa kweZwi likaNkulunkulu, aphikise intando kaNkulunkulu asembulele yona, ahlanekezele iMibhalo, ngezinye izikhathi acaphune eBhayibhelini ngendlela okungesiyo, uma-nje kuzomsebenzela lokho.

`Funda iZaga 23:23, Johane 17:17, noJohane 8:32. Yikuphi okufanayo okubona kulezindimana mayelana neqiniso likaNkulunkulu?  Iyini ingqikithi yesigijimi sazo?`

USathane wayazi kahle ukuthi iMibhalo eNgcwele izokwenza abantu babone izinkohliso zakhe, bamelane namandla akhe. YiZwi elenza ukuthi noMsindisi womhlaba akwazi ukumelana nokuhlasela kwakhe. Ngaso sonke isikhathi egadla uSathane, uKrestu wayesebenzisa ihawu leqiniso laphakade, ethi: ‘Kulotshiwe.’ Ukuze uSathane azenzele akuthandayo ebantwini, amise igunya likaPhapha, kwadingeka ukuthi abagcine bengenalwazi lweMibhalo.  IBhayibheli lalizophakamisa uNkulunkulu, libeke abantu benyama endaweni yabo yangempela; ngakhoke amaqiniso alo angcwele kwakuzofuneka efihliwe, anyathelwe ngezinyawo. Lomqondo wamukelwa yibandla laseRoma.  Amakhulu eminyaka, kwakungavunyelwe ukusabalalisa iBhayibheli.  Abantu babenqatshelwe ukulifunda noma ukuba nalo ezindlini zabo, futhi abapristi nabefundisi babehumusha izimfundiso zalo ngendlela yokwesekela okufunwa yibo. Ngaleyo ndlela, uPhapha wamukelwa cishe emhlabeni wonke njengalowo omele uNkulunkulu emhlabeni, ophiwe igunya phezu kwebandla nombuso.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 51.

`Xoxani ngezindlela uSathane azama ngazo ukuhlanekezela noma ukuphikisa iZwi likaNkulunkulu namhlanje.`
