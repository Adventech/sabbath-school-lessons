---
title:  Imiphumela Yokufa Yobulozi
date:  02/06/2024
---

Inganekwane ethi ukufa ukungena kwelinye izinga lokuphila yesekelwe phezu komqondo othi umphefumulo awufi. Lomqondo wobuhedeni wafohla ebandleni kudala, njengoba lalilokhu ligudluka esisekelweni salo esiyiBhayibheli, lizama ukwenza inkolo izwakale kahle kubo bonke abantu basembusweni waseRoma: “Imfundiso yokuthi umphefumulo awufi enye yalezo zimfundiso zamanga eyathathwa yibandla laseRoma ebuhedenini layifaka enkolweni yobuKrestu.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 549.

> <p></p>
> “Ningabesabi  ababulala umzimba bengenamandla okubulala umphefumulo, kepha kakhulu mesabeni onamandla okubhubhisa nomphefumulo nomzimba esihogweni” (Mathewu 10:28).

`Lendimana iyodwa-nje, isitsheleni ngokuthi umphefumulo awufi?`

UNkulunkulu wabenqabela abantu baKhe ekubeni nesabelo nokuxhumana nabafileyo noma ngayiphi indlela.  Kwakungafuneki babekezelele “abanedlozi, noma abalozi, noma ababhulayo” (Duteronomi 18:11). Abanjalo babekhandwa ngamatshe baze bafe (Levitikusi 20:27). Lesijeziso sizwakala sinzima kakhulu, kodwa sasenzelwe ukuvikela uIsrayeli ekukhonzeni onkulunkulu bamanga. Ukubhula okukaSathane. Kudukisela abantu ekukhonzeni kwamanga, kulingise ubudlelwane beqiniso noNkulunkulu, kodwa ngeke kwanelise izidingo ezijulile zenhliziyo. Ubungoma busembindini becebo likaSathane lokuthumba umhlaba. Kodwa uJesu, ngomusa waKhe namandla aKhe, ukhulula abathunjwa emaketangeni ababophile.

`FundakuMshumayeli 9:5; Jobe 7:7-9; noIsaya 8:19, 20. Lezindimana zisifundisani ngokufa nokuxhumana nabafileyo na?`

Inkolelo ethi abafileyo baya-ngqo ezulwini uma befa, yize noma kungesiyo eyeBhayibheli, njengokugcina iSonto, seyibekhona isikhathi eside, igxilile, ngendlela yokuthi kulukhuni ukuthi abantu bahlukane nayo. Abantu basebenzisa izindimana ezimbalwa, abazifunda ngokungahambisani nokushiwo yisahluko, bezama ukuthalalisa lenkolelo. Kodwa lemfundiso yamanga ayibavikeli ezinkohlisweni uSathane azijikijela kubona, kakhulukazi kulempikiswano yokugcina.

`Kwakuhambela kanjani wena uzama ukuchazela amanye amaKrestu ngesimo sabafileyo na? Uma kukhona owathola kukusebenzela, yini leyo?`
