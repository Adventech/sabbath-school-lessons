---
title:  Izindaba Ezinhle Zendawo Engcwele-ngcwele
date:  22/05/2024
---

`Funda kumaHeberu 4:14-16 namaHeberu 10:19-22.Yisiphi isiqiniseko nesimemo sezulu lezindimana ezisinika wonke umuntu na?`

Iphuzu likaPawulu lapha kumaHeberu lithi “bambisisani,” “wozani ngesibindi,” “ningapheli amandla,” “gxilisani ukukholwa kwenu kuJesu uMpristi Omkhulu wethu.  KuJesu, sithola konke esikudingayo. Ngokukholwa singangena endlini engcwele yasezulwini “ngendlela entsha nephilayo” esayivulelwa nguJesu.

Uma sibuka ngaphakathi, sibona igazi ezimpondweni ze-altare lethusi. Endaweni engcwele, sibona igazi ezimpondweni zegolide ze-altare lempepho. Sibona igazi elifafaziwe esihenqweni esiphambi kwesihlalo somusa.

Igazi likaJesu lihlelemba indlela. Lokho kusinika ithemba, ngoba sinakho ukuphinda sibe munye noNkulunkulu kuphela-nje uma uJesu ethethelela futhi ecima izono zethu. Umusa kaNkulunkulu awunakulinganiswa, kodwa ngendlela efanayo futhi nangobulungisa baKhe. Kanti ubulungisa ngeke bamukele umhlatshela kaKrestu njengesihlengo sokona kwethu ngaphandle kokuthi uJesu aqinisekise ukuthethelela izono zethu okokuqala; okwesibili, azicime.

`Funda iSambulo 11:19. Ngokuya kwempikiswano enkulu, ubaluleke ngani lombono? Utshengisa kanjani ukuxhumana okungehlukaniswe, phakathi komthetho nevangeli na?`

Lapha, ekukhazimuleni okuxhophayo endlini yesihlalo sobukhosi samazulu, esisekelweni sesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, sithola umthetho kaNkulunkulu emphongolweni wesivumelwano. Lapha-ke, endaweni engcwele-ngcwele, ubulungisa bukaNkulunkulu nomusa waKhe kuyabonakala. Alikho igunya lasemhlabeni elingaguqula umthetho kaNkulunkulu ngoba, phakathi kwezinye izizathu, uqhakambiswe emphongolweni wesivumelwano. KumaHeberu 8:10 kuthiwa: “Ngokuba yilesi esiyisivumelwano engiyakusenza nendlu ka-Israyeli emva kwalezo zinsuku, isho iNkosi: ngiyakufaka imithetho yami engqondweni yabo, ngiyilobe ezinhliziyweni zabo, ngibe nguNkulunkulu wabo, bona babe ngabantu baMi.”  Ngokungena ngokukholwa etabernakele lasezulwini, sithola ukuthethelelwa izono ezindala, namandla okuphila impilo yokulalela ngoKrestu, owasifela nobhala umthetho ezinhliziyweni zethu. UJesu usisindisa ngokupheleleyo” (Heb. 7:25). UJesu usisindisa ngokupheleleyo—esijezisweni sesono namandla aso.

`Kungani ukungenelela kukaJesu kuyizindaba ezimnandi kangaka? Uma simi phambi komthetho oyisilinganiso sokulunga, besingaba naliphi ithemba ngaphandle kwevangeli?`
