### Kuleliviki Funda ku
Eks. 25:8, 9, 40; Heb. 8:1-6; Lev. 16:21, 29-34; Lev. 23:26-32; Heb. 9:23-38; Dan. 7:9, 10; Mathewu 25:1-13; Samb. 11:19.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Kepha okuyindikimba kwesikushoyo yilokhu: sinompristi omkhulu onjalo osewahlala ngakwesokunene sesihlalo sobukhosi soMkhulu emazulwini, isikhonzi sokungcwele nesetabernekele lempela elamiswa yiNkosi kungemuntu: (AmaHeberu 8:1, 2).

Masinyane ngemuva kokuphoxeka kuka Mfumfu 22, 1844, abanye abalandeli bakaMiller, ngemuva kokuthandaza nokupheniyisisa, baqala ukubona iphutha labo. Isiprofetho sezinsuku ezingu 2 300 sasingakhulumi ngokubuya kukaJesu njengoba kwakucatshangwa, kodwa sasikhuluma ngomsebenzi kaKrestu endaweni encgcwele yasezulwini, efanekiswe kahle kakhulu encwadini yamaHeberu.

Ukuhlanjululwa kwendawo engcwele ezulwini kwaku ukugcwaliseka kokuhlanjululwa kwendawo engcwele yasemhlabeni, njengokubhalwe kuLevitikusi. Ukuze usiqonde kangcono lesiprofetho esimqoka, bheka okufanayo kuDaniyeli 7 noDaniyeli 8:

| Danyeli 7 | Daniyeli 8 |
| --- | --- |
| IBabiloni | |
| Medo-Peresiya | Medo-Peresiya |
| Grisi | Grisi |
| Roma | Roma |
| Ukwahlulela ezulwini | Ukuhlanjululwa kwendawo engcwele |

Lokhu kuyasiza ukutshengisa isimo sangempela sokuhlanjululwa kwendawo engcwele, okungukwahlulela okwenzeka ngaphambi kokubuya kukaJesu— ukuhlolwa kwezincwadi.  Kulesifundo saleliviki sizohlola iqiniso elimqoka lomsebenzi kaKrestu endlini engcwele yasezulwini.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki, esithathelwe ezahlukweni 22-24 no 28 zencwadi iGreat Controversy, ukulungisela iSabatha likaNhlaba 25._
