“Yayiyize imizamo kaSathane yokuchitha ibandla likaKrestu ngodlame. Impikiswano enkulu abayifela abafundi bakaJesu ayiphelanga ngokufa kwabo. Ukwehlulwa kwabo kwaba ukunqoba kwabo.  Izinceku zikaNkulunkulu zabulawa, kodwa umsebenzi waKhe waqhubekela phambili. Ivangeli laqhubeka lasabalala, nesibalo sabalamukelayo sakhula. Lafinyelela ezindaweni ezazikade zingafinyeleleki, ngisho naseRoma. Omunye umKrestu owayeqophisana nababusi abagqugquzela izintshushiso, wathi: “Niyasibulala, nisihlukumeze, nisilahle ngecala….Ukuswela kwenu ubulungisa, buyafakaza ukuthi simsulwa….futhi nesihluku senu asisizi ngalutho.”  Lokho kwaku yisimemo sokuthi kuhlushwe abanye futhi. ‘Uma nilokhu nisibulala, kulapho izibalo zethu ziya ngokukhula; igazi lamaKrestu liyimbewu.’—Tertullian, Apology, paragraph 50.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, pp. 41, 42.

Ukubonelela kukaNkulunkulu okuyimfihlo, okuvumela abalungileyo ukuthi bahlushwe ababi, kuyabakhathaza abaningi abanokukholwa obubuthakathaka. Abanye bacishe balahle nokwethemba uNkulunkulu ngoba evumela abantu ababi ukuthi baphumelele, kodwa abalungileyo behlushwa yibo. Bayabuza ukuthi kungenzeka kanjani ukuthi olungileyo nonesihawu, onamandla onke, abekezelele isimo sokungabikho kobulungisa nesencindezelo?  Umbuzo esingeke sizihluphe ngawo lo.  UNkulunkulu usinike ubufakazi obanele bothando lwaKhe, akufanele singabaze ukulunga kwaKhe ngoba singakwazi ukuqonda ukusebenza kokuhlakanipha kwaKhe.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 47.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`Kusiza ngani ukuhlushwa? Ucabanga ukuthi yini indaba uNkulunkulu evuma ukuthi abantwana baKhe bahlushwe ngezinye izikhathi?`

`Uma umngani wakho ubengakubuza lombuzo, bewungaphendula uthini: “Uphi uNkulunkulu njengoba ngihlupheka-nje? Uma uyangithanda, kungani ngihlupheka kangaka?”`

`Lingenza kanjani ibandla lakho ukuthi libe elinakayo, elizokuba nomthelela omunhle emphakathini? Xoxani ngezindlela eziphathekayo zokusebenzisa isifundo saleli viki.`
