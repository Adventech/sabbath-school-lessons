---
title:  AmaKrestu Asinda Ngokubonelela kukaNkulunkulu
date:  08/04/2024
---

Umusa kaNkulunkulu, isihawu saKhe, ukubonelela kwaKhe, nokwazi kwaKhe ngaphambili, konke kubonakala ngokusobala ezigamekweni ezaholela ekuchithweni kweJerusalema. UCestius Gallus namabutho aseRoma bahanqa iJerusalema. Ngokungalindelekile, lapho kubonakala sebesethubeni lokuhlasela, bahoxa. Amabutho amaJuda abasukela, athola ukunqoba okukhulu.

Njengoba amaRoma ayebaleka, namaJuda ewasukela, amaKrestu eJerusalema athola ithuba lokubaleka, aya ePerea, ngaphesheya komfula iJordani. “Isibonakaliso sasikade sinikiwe kumaKrestu, manje-ke lavela ithuba labo bonke abafunayo, ukuthi balalele isixwayiso soMsindisi. Konke kwakuhlelwe yizulu ngendlela yokuthi amaKrestu ayengeke avinjwe amaJuda noma amaRoma.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, ikhasi 30.

`Funda kumaHubo 46:1 noIsaya 41:10.  Lezindimana zisitshelani ngokubonelela kukaNkulunkulu ngaphambi kwesikhathi na?`

UNkulunkulu ungaphezu kwanoma yini, futhi ulawula izigameko emhlabeni ukuze kufezeke injongo yaKhe engcwele. Nakuba ngezinye izikhathi uNkulunkulu ewaguqula amacebo aKhe ngokwendlela esenza ngayo izinqumo, icebo laKhe ngalomhlaba liyofezeka. Kuyokuba nezikhathi lapho abantu bakaNkulunkulu beyohlangabezana nobunzima, ukuhlushwa, ukuboshwa, nokufa imbhala, ngenxa yomsebenzi kaKrestu.  Kodwa nangezikhathi ezinzima kakhulu, lapho uSathane etshengisa ulaka olwesabekayo, uNkulunkulu uyalilonda ibandla laKhe, aligcine.

`Funda kumaHeberu 11:35-38 neSambulo 2:10. Yiliphi iqiniso elembulwa yilezi ndimana ngempi yethu namabutho omubi? Kunakuzwana kuni phakathi kwalezindimana nomcabango wokuvikelwa nguNkulunkulu kulombuzo ongasenhla? Ngabe kukhona ukuphikisana yini nomcabango wokuvikelwa nguNkulunkulu nokuthi uNkulunkulu avumele abanyei bahlupheke kabuhlungu, baze bafele inkolo yabo ngenxa kaKrestu na?`

“Yayiyize imizamo kaSathane yokuchitha ibandla likaKrestu ngendluzula. Impikiswano enkulu, abafundi bakaKrestu abayifela, ayiphelanga ngokufa kwalaba abathembekileyo.  Ekwehlulweni kwabo; banqoba.  Izinceku zikaNkulunkulu zabulawa, kodwa umsebenzi waKhe wawulokhu uqhubekela phambili.”– Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 41.

`Kufanele kusitsheleni ukuthi ababhali beBhayibheli, ababebazi ubuhlungu nokuhlupheka, kodwa babephinda-phinda bebhala ngothando lukaNkulunkulu? Singazizwela kanjani ngokwethu lona lolo thando?`
