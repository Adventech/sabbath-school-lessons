---
title:  Ifa Lothando
date:  11/04/2024
---

`Funda kuJohane 13:35 no 1 Johane 4:21. Zisembulelani lezindimana uma sibuka uSathane evukela umbuso kaNkulunkulu kulempikiswano enkulu? Zisitshelani ngomongo wobuKrestu beqiniso?`

Uthando kwakuyisona silinganiso samaKrestu eminyakeni yokuqala yebandla. UTertullan wathi: “Yizenzo zothando kakhulu eziholela abaningi ekuthini basibeke uphawu esaziwa ngalo. Bheka-nje ukuthi bathandana kangakanani.” (Bheka iTertullian’s Apology 39 as translated by S. Thelwall ku https://www.logoslibrary.org/tertullian/apology/39.html.)

Okunye ukubonakala okukhulu kothando lukaNkulunkulu kwakungesikhathi sezigameko ezimbili zobhubhane lwesifo esahlasela ngabo A.D. 160 no A.D. 265. AmaKrestu eza ngaphambili, esiza abagulayo nalabo abagulela ukufa.  Lezifo zabulala izinkulungwane ngezinkulungwane zashiya imizana namadolobha ingenabahlali. Umsebenzi wokusiza ongenakuzicabangela, wothando, nowokuzidela kwamaKrestu waba nomthelela omkhulu ebantwini. Ekuhambeni kwesikhathi, izinkulungwane, ezakhula kwaba amakhulu ezinkulungwane, nezigidi zabantu zombuso wamaRoma zaphendukela kuJesu ngesikhathi salezinhlekelele ezimbili. Uthando, ukuzwelana nabanye, nokuhleleka komsebenzi wokunakekela abagulayo kwaletha ukunconywa kwabazalwane noKrestu abangamaxusa aKhe.

Incwadi kaRodney Stark ethi The Rise and Fall of Christianity yindaba yomlando wakulezinsuku esitshengisa lezigameko ngendlela entsha nengcono. Uchaza indlela umphakathi wamaKrestu ngobhubhane lwesibili (A.D. 260) owavele wafana nabahlengikazi (nurses), bebonelela ngezidingo zokuphila kulabo abagulayo ukuze basimame. “Ngesikhathi sePhasika, uDionysius wabhala kabanzi encoma izenzo zokuhlenga ngobuqhawe zamaKrestu, amaningi nawo alahlekelwa yizimpilo zawo ezama ukusiza abanye.”

“Abaningi babazalwane bethu bamaKrestu batshengisa uthando olukhulu nokwethembeka, bengazicabangeli bona, becabangela abanye-nje.  Bengayinakile ingozi, banakekela abagulayo, bebonelela izidingo zabo, bebasiza ngegama lika kuKrestu, bawushiya lomhlaba kanye nabo benokuthula nentokozo; ngoba babethelelwe abanye ngalesifo, bathola ukugula komakhelwane babo, bakwamukela ngentokozo lobo buhlungu.”—Rodney Stark, The Rise and Fall of Christianity (Princeton, New Jersey: Princeton University Press, 1996), p. 82.

`Yisiphi isifundo esisobala lapha?  Sikufunda kanjani ukufa kukaMina ukuze nathi, sibonakalise lowo moya wokungazicabangeli?  Akulula; kulula, angithi?`
