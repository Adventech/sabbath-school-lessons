### Kuleliviki Funda ku
Efesu 2:1-10; Efesu 5:14; Roma 5:17; Efesu 5:6; 2 Thim. 1:7.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Kepha uNkulunkulu ecebile ngesihawu, ngenxa yothando lwaKhe olukhulu asithanda ngalo sesifile ngeziphambeko, wenza siphile kanye noKrestu—kungomusa nisindisiwe (Efesu 2:4, 5).

Ngomhla ka 14 kuMfumfu ka 1987, uJessica oneziyanga ezingu 18 wayedlala egcekeni lika Anti wakhe ngesikhathi ewela emgodini wepitsi oshona ngezinyathelo ezingo 22.  Leso sigameko sadonsela izintatheli zomhlaba wonke endaweni yaseMidland, eTexas. Izibukeli  zabona uJessica elele, ekhala, ecula, ebiza unina.  Zabukela ngesikhathi abezimo eziphuthumayo befaka umoya ohlanzekile epitsini.

Ekugcineni, ngemuva kwamahora angu 58 ngemuva kokuwa kukaJessica, umhlaba wabuka ngesikhathi uJessica ehlangulwa kulowo mgodi ayebhajwe kuwo ngaphezu kwezinsuku ezimbili.  Isithombe esathathwa nguScott Shaw lapho samzuzela umklomelo we Pulitzer Prize.  Sasitshengisa intambo ebophe umzimba kaJessica owawugoqwe ngamabhandeji.

Ayikho into  ethinta umphefumulo njengomsebenzi wokuhlangula oyimpumelelo.  UPawulu kwabase-Efesu 2:1-10, usitshengisa ukuhlangulwa okukhulukazi emlandweni—umzamo kaNkulunkulu wokuhlenga abantu.  Okumnandi kulendaba ukuthi asizona-nje izibukeli kuhlangulwa omunye umuntu, kodwa singofakazi bokwethu ukuhlangulwa.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha lika uNtulikazi 22._
