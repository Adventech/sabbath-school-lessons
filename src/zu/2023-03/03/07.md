Funda lokhu kuchazwa okubili kokuphakanyiswa kukaKrestu ngokubhala kuka Ellen G. White:

“Izingalo zikaBaba zigone iNdodana yaKhe, nezwi liyamemezela lithi ‘Izingelosi zonke zikaNkulunkulu mazikhuleke kuYe.’ Heberu 1:6.

Ngenjabulo engenakuphinyiselwa, ababusi nemibuso, namandla bayabuvuma ubukhulu beNkosi yokuphila. Izingelosi ziyawa phambi kwaKhe, izulu ligcwala ukukhamuluka kwentokozo ethi: ‘Lifanele iWundlu elahlatshwa ukwamukela amandla, nobucebi, nokuhlakanipha, nodumo, nenkazimulo, nesibusiso.’  ISambulo 5:12.

“Amahubo okunqoba axubana nomculo wezigubhu zezingelosi, izulu lize libonakale lichichima yintokozo nokudumisa. Lunqobile uthando.  Obekulahlekile sekutholakele.  Izulu liyankenteza ngamaphimbo athi: “KuYena ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi, nakulo iWundlu, makube yisibusiso, nodumo nenkazimulo, namandla kuze kube phakade naphakade.’ ISambulo 5:13.”—The Desire of Ages, pp. 834, 835.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`Cabanga ngokuthi “manje” nokuthi “akukenzeki” kokuphakanyiswa kukaJesu. Singasho kanjani ukuthi uJesu “useyiNkosi yakho konke,” namadimoni engaphansi kwaKhe---okusho isikhathi samanje”? Futhi lokhu kubusa okuphelele phezu kwakho konke kukhomba kanjani esikhathini esizayo—“esingakafiki”? (Bheka ku 1 Korinte 15:24-28).`

`Uphila kangakanani ekukhanyeni kombuso kaKrestu phansi kolawulo lwalemibuso eminye, ewile, amandla ayo anciphayo vele? Wazi kanjani ukuthi yikuphi okuyikona-kona, futhi ungawagwema kanjani amandla obubi okuthi noma esanqotshwa-nje, kodwa asekhona emhlabeni wethu?`
