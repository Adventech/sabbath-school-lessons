### Kuleliviki Funda ku
Efesu 5:21-33; Filipi 2:3, 4; Hezekeli 16:1-14; 2 Korinte 11:1-4; Genesise 2:15-25.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Madoda, thandani omkenu, njengalokho noKrestu ulithandile ibandla, wazinikela ngenxa yalo ukuba alingcwelise, alihlambulule ngesigezo samanzi ngezwi, ukuze azimisele ibandla elinenkazimulo, lingenasisihla, nambimbi, nanto enjalo, kodwa libe ngcwele lingenacala” (Efesu 5:25-27).

Kwabase-Efesu 5:21-33, uPawulu wakhela phezu kwephuzu lokuzithoba kwabazalwane omunye komunye (Efesu 5:21); bese eyala amakhosikazi amaKrestu (Efesu 5:22-24); namadoda (Efesu 5:25-32); aphethe ngokucwenga leso siyalo sabo bobabili (Efesu 5:33).

Kulesiyalo, izazi ngeBhayibheli zingezwa uKrestu ovukile ekhuluma ngobudlelwane bethu. Sisethubeni lokukwenza lokho uma sizwisisa kahle abase-Efesu 5:21-6:9 njengendlela kaPawulu yokufundisa lendikimba enkulu yalencwadi, ubunye, kodwa manje eqondise emndenini wobuKrestu. Nakuba-nje esola kakhulu okungahambi kahle empilweni yomuntu omdala (wesono) (bheka Efesu 2:15) okunamathele ebantwini bonkana nokusilela kwendlela ophila ngayo.  Kulezinhlaka, abazalwane batshengisa ukuthi sekuthululwe emhlabeni amandla amasha, uMoya oNgcwele, akhomba phambili ekugcwalisekeni kwecebo likaNkulunkulu ngabantu nomhlaba (Efesu 2:22; Efesu 3:16; Efesu 5:18-21; Efesu 6:17, 18) nendlela entsha ngokukaKrestu (Efesu 4:13, 15, 20-24, 32; Efesu 5:2, 10, 17, 21-33).

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha lika uMandulo 2._
