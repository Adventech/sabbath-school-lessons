### Kuleliviki Funda ku
Efesu 5:1-20; 1 Korinte 5:1-11; Sambulo 16:1-16; Kolose 4:5; iZaga 20:1; iZaga 23:29-35; iZenzo 16:25.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Ngakhoke bhekisisani ukuthi nihamba kanjani, kungabi njengabangahlakaniphile kepha njengabahlakaniphileyo, nithengisise isikhathi, ngokuba izinsuku zimbi.  Ngakho-ke ningabi yiziwula, kodwa qondani okuyintando yeNkosi” Efesu 5:15-17).

Kungekudala kakhulu, kwakuthengiswa endalini ujeke wekristali eUnited Kingdom. Othengisayo wawuchaza ngokuthi ujeke wesiFrench, owakhiwa ngeminyaka yawo 1800, walinganisa intengo yawo ngokuthi amakhulu amabili amadola aseMelika. Abathengi ababili ababephapheme bawubona lojeke njengongavamile, wamaSulumani. Lalithini-ke inani lawo ngempela? Opondo abayizigidi ezinhlanu (izigidi eziyisithupha zamadola nesigamu).  Yini eyenza lowo mthengi ahambe nomtitilizo ongako? Wayazi okuthile okwakungaziwa umthengisi: inani langempela likajeke.

Kwabase-Efesu 5:1-20, uPawulu uqhathanisa okwakuyigugu kwabangakholwayo nalokho okwakuyigugu kwabakholwayo. Abangakholwayo babebona izindaba eziphathelene nobulili (Efesu 5:4), idili lotshwala (Efesu 5:18), nezocansi (Efesu 5:3, 5) kuyizona-zinto eziyigugu empilweni. Abakholwayo kodwa bayazi ukuthi kukhona usuku olukhulu lokwahlulela oluzayo, lapho inani langempela lazo zonke izinto liyokuba sobala (Efesu 5:5, 6). Esikhundleni sokufaka imali yabo emadilini nokudakwa, igugu labo phakathi kokunye, “lisekulungeni naseqinisweni” (Efesu 5:9) likaKrestu. UPawulu, uyabanxusa-ke ukuthi bathathe imititilizo etholakala kuKrestu njengoba bephila (njengathi sonke) emngceleni wezwe laphakade (Efesu 5:15-17).

_*Funda isifundo salelilviki ukulungisela iSabatha lika uNcwaba 26._
