### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
2 Pet. 3:9, Dut. 7:6–9, Rom. 11:22, 1 Johane 4:7–20, Johane 15:12, 1 Johane 3:16.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “UJesu waphendula, wathi kuye: ‘Uma umuntu engithanda, uyakugcina izwi lami; uBaba uyakumthanda; siyakuza kuye, sihlale naye’ ” (Johane 14:23, NKJV).

Baningi abantu abafundiswe ukuthi igama lesiGreki elithi (agape) lisho ukuba nothando okutholakala uma kukhulunywa ngoNkulunkulu, kanti amanye amagama afana no (phileo) akhuluma ngolunye uhlobo lokuthanda olwehluke kakhulu kunalolu luka-agape. Abanye bathi uthando luka (agape) lukhuluma ngothando oluthinta umuntu oyedwa, lungamfaki omunye umuntu othintekayo kuloluthando. Loluthando alamukeli lutho kodwa luyapha kuphela. Loluthando lwehlukile kunothando olukhona kubantu.

Okuseqiniswe ngukuthi, uma ufunda ngokucophelela ngothando lukaNkulunkulu uzothola ukuthi eMibhalweni yonke kuvela ukuthi leliqondo okukhulunywe ngayo , yize sekuyinto ejwayelekile, kunephutha ukuqodwa kwayo. Okokuqala, igama lesiGreki elithi (agape) alikhulumi ngothando lukaNkulunkulu kuphela, kodwa liyafaka nokuthanda komuntu, yize kungaba wuthando olungamile kahle (2 Thimothewu 4:10). Okwesibili, kuyona yonke iMibhalo, maningi amagama asetshenziswayo asho uthando lukaNkulunkulu ngaphandle kwegama elingu(agape). Isibonelo, uJesu wafundisa lokhu, “ngokuba ubaba uqobo uyanithanda (phileo), ngokuba ningithandile (phileo) nina. Lapha, igama lesiGreki lothando u(phileo) lisetshenziswe njengothando olungasetshenziswa ngabantu kuphela kodwa noNkulunkulu uyalusebenzisa ukuthanda abantu. Ngakho-ke, uthando olungu (phileo) alusho kuphela uthando abantu abathanda ngalo olungaphelele, kodwa kuwuthando uNkulunkulu analo naya.

IMibhalo ayifundisi kuphela ukuthi uthando lukaNkulunkulu lunxanye, kodwa kuwuthando lokuthandana ngapha nangapha. Okuyinto eyenza umehluko kuNkulunkulu noma kubantu ngoba uyabuyisela uthando lwakhe kithi nathi silukhombise nakwabanye.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UMasingana 11._
