---
title:  Isitha Sikwenzile Lokhu
date:  23/02/2025
---

`Funda ku-Mathewu 13:24-27. Kungabe lomfanekiso usisiza kanjani ukuba sibe nolwazi lobubi obenzeka ezweni na?`

UJesu uxoxa indaba yomnikazi wensimu owahlwanyela ensimini yakhe. Okubi okwenzeka, ngukuthi kwamila ukhula phakathi kukakolweni. Uthe uma bekubona lokhu, izisebenzi zathi kumnikazi wensimu, “ ‘ “Nkosi, awuhlwanyelanga imbewu enhle ensimini yakho na? Pho, ilutholephi ukhula na?” ’ ” (Mat. 13:27, NKJV). Lokhu kuyefana nombuzo ohlala ubuzwa ngabantu nanamuhla ophethelene nenkinga yokubakhona kobubi: Uma kungukuthi uNkulunkulu wadala umhlaba omuhle, kungani kugcwele ububi obungaka na?

`Funda ku-Mathewu 13:28-30 Uma sibheka incazelo kaKristu ku-Mathewu 13:37-40. Kungabe nalokhu kusikhanyisela ukuthi kuyinto enjani ukulwisana okukhona ezweni na?`

Umnikazi wensimu waphendula umbuzo wezisebenzi ngokuthi: “ ‘ “Ohlwanyela imbewu enhle yiNdodana yomuntu” ’ ” (Matt. 13:28, NKJV). Kamuva uJesu wakhomba umuntu “ ‘ohlwanyela imbewu enhle’ njenge- ‘Ndodana yoMuntu,’ ‘okungu- Jesu uqobo lwaKhe” (Mathewu 13:37). Waphinda wakhomba “isitha esitshale ukhula, okungu-Sathane” (Mathewu 13:39). Konke lokhu kwakuveza ukungezwani okusezweni phakathi kukaKristu kanye noSathane. Kungani kugcwele ububi emhlabeni na? Ububi bungumphumela wokusebenza kwesitha (esingu-Sathane) olokhu ephikisana nomnikazi. “lokhu kwenziwe ngumuntu oyisitha?” (Mathewu 13:28, NKJV).

Lempendulo yenza kubekhona eminye imibuzo efana nalena, “Uthanda ukuba siye ukulubutha na?” ngamanye amazwi, kungani singayi kolusiphuna njengamanje na? Umnikazi wensimu waphendula wathi, “Qha, funa nithi, nxa nibutha ukhula, nisiphule amabele kanye nalo. Yekani kukhule kokubili ndawonye, kuze kuvunwe” (Mathewu 13:29, 30; qhathanisa no- Marko 4:29). Ngokukhuluma komfanekiso, uNkulunkulu uyoqeda nya ngobubi, kodwa ukulisiphuna njengamanje kungabangela ukuthi kwenzeke umonakalo ongeke usalungiseka nozolimaza nalokhu okuhle okukhona.

`Kungabe yiziphi izingozi ezingavela uma kusiphunwa ukhula lususwa kukolweni njengamanje na? Ngesikhathi esifanayo, Kungabe kusho ukuthini ukunganaki ububi okuye kwenzeke sihlane nabo na?`
