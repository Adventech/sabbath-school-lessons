### Funda lokhu kuleliviki
Eksod. 33:15–22; Hoseya 14:1–4; Samb. 4:11; Johane 17:24; Mat. 22:1–14; Johane 10:17, 18.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Ngiyakuphulukisa ukuhlehla kwabo, ngibathande ngesihle, ngokuba intukuthelo yami ibuyile kubo’ ” (Hoseya 14:4, NKJV).

Yize uPetro wamphika kwaze kwaba kathathu, njengoba uJesu wayekade eshilo ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke (Mathewu 26:34), Lokhu kuphikwa kakuzange kube ngukuphela kwendaba. Emva kokuvuka kwakhe, uJesu wambuza uPetro ukuthi: “Kungabe uyangithanda kunalaba na?’ Wathi kuye: ‘Yebo, Nkosi, uyazi wena ukuthi ngiyakuthanda.’ UJesu wathi kuye: ‘Yalusa izimvu zami.’ Wathi kuye ngokwesithathu: ‘Simoni kaJona, uyangithanda na?’ UPetro wadabuka ngokuba wathi kuye ngokwesithathu: ‘Nkosi, wazi konke wena, uyazi ukuthi ngiyakuthanda.’ UJesu wathi kuye: ‘Yipha izimvu zami.’” (Johane 21:15-17). Njengoba uPetro amphika uJesu kathathu, indlela uJesu ambuza ngayo uPetro uma ethi, “uyangithanda na?” UJesu wakwazi ukubuyisa uPetro kwaze kwaba kathathu.

Yize isimo sethu singehluka kuleso uPetro ayekuso, indlela esenza ngayo iyefana. Indlela uJesu abuza ngayo uPetro, kusengukubuza okufanayo uNkulunkulu akubhekise komunye nomunye wethu namuhla noma ngabe ukuyiphi indawo. Umbuzo ulokhu umile uthi: Uyangithanda na?

Konke okuyokwenzeka kuxhomeke empendulweni esizoyiletho kulombuzo.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UMasingana 4._
