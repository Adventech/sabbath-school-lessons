### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Ihubo 33:5; iHubo 85:10; Duteronomi 32:4; Jakobe 1:17; Titu 1:2; Eksodusi 32:14; Mathewu 5:43-46.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Kepha ozibongayo makazibonge ngalokhu ukuthi uyangiqonda, angazi ukuthi nginguJehova owenza umusa nokwahlulela nokulunga emhlabeni, ngokuba ngiyathokoza ngokunjalo,’ usho uJehovah” (Jer. 9:24, NKJV).

EMpumalanga yasemandulo, “onkulunkulu” besizwe babengezona izinto ezishintsha kalula, bengenaso isimilo, kungaqondakali ukusebenza kwabo, kodwa babekwazi ukulawula izigameko ezinzima kakhulu, ezazifana nokunikelwa kwezingane zibe ngumhlatshelo. Noma kunjalo, izinkumbula zabantu ezazibalandela zazingabathembi kangako, kodwa lezindimbane zabantu zazingalokothi ziye kwabanye “onkulunkulu.”

Ngokusho kwencwadi ka-Duteronomi 32:17, “labonkulunkulu” babesebenza beqhutshwa ngamadimoni (funda ngalokhu futhi ku-1 Korinte 10:20, 21). Indlela ababedumisa ngayo yabe ihambisana nokuxhashazwa kwabantu, ibashiye bengabantu abasebumnyameni kwezikamoya nangendlela abaziphatha ngayo.

UNkulunkulu weBhayibheli angeke ehluke kangako kulemimoya yalamadimoni. UJehova ulunge ngokuphelele, nesimilo saKhe asiguquki. Kungesikhona kuphela okokuthi uNkulunkulu uhlala engolungile, nokungasenza sibe nokuthemba kuye kusukela manje kuze kube phakade.

Isimo esiphikisana kakhulu nesonkulunkulu bamanga ababekhona emhlabeni esikhathini sasendulo, kodwa manje “osekungonkulunkulu” besimanjamanje abakhona namuhla, uJehova unokukhathazeka okukhulu ngobubi, nokuhlupheka, nokungabibikho kobulungiswa, nokucindezeleka. Konke lokhu uhlala ekugxeka ngokungenakho ukungabaza. Okukhulu kunakho konke, ngolunye usuku uyoqeda nya ngakho konke lokhu.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNhlolanja 8._
