### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Dan. 10:1–14, Samb. 13:1–8, Jobe 1:1–12, Jobe 2:1–7, Johane 12:31, Johane 14:30, Marko 6:5, Marko 9:29.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Owenza isono ungokaSathane, ngokuba uSathane uyona kwasekuqaleni. INdodana kaNkulunkulu yabonakaliswa ngalokhu, ukuze ichithe imisebenzi kaSathane” (1 Johane 3:8, NKJV).

Ingxoxo enamandla nekwazi ukuveza ubunjalo bokulwisana okukhona ezweni okutholakala ku- 1 AmaKhosi 18:19-40. U-Eliya wayesentabeni yaseKarmeli, lapho uNkulunkulu aveza obalo lokho okwakwaziwa ngokuthi “onkulunklu bezizwe.” Noma kunjalo, kuningi okwabe kufihlakele kungabonakali ngalaba “onkulunkulu” nokuyinto yokuthi labonkulunkulu babezicuthe zamaqaba ezaziwacabanga. Emva kwalaba “onkulunkulu” ababekhonzwa yizizwe ezabe zizungeze u-Israyeli, okusempeleni, kwakukhona nokunye.

“ ‘Babenikelela amadimoni, benganikeli kuNkulunkulu. Babehlabela onkulunkulu ababengabazi, onkulunkulu abasha, abasanda kuqhamuka, nababengasatshwa ngoyihlo’ ” (Dut. 32:17, NKJV). UPawula athayisele ngokuthi “Lokho okwahlatshwa ngabezizwe bakuhlabela amadimoni, kabakuhlabeli uNkulunkulu” (1 Kor. 10:20, NKJV).

Okwakufihlakele emva “kwalabonkulunkulu” bamanga bezizwe, empeleni, kwabe kuzenza amadimoni. Lokhu kusho ukuthi wonke amavesi akhona emBhalweni akhuluma ngezithixo kanye nonkulunkulu bezizwe kwabe kungamavesi aphathelene “nokulwisana okukhona ezweni.”

Ngenxa yalokhu osekushiwo, isihloko sokulwisana okukhona ezweni sizwakala kangcono. Leliqiniso linezinto eziningi okufuneka siziqonde kahle ekukhulu okumayela nobunjalo balokhu kulwisana nokuthi sithola kuphi ukukhanyiseleka mayelana nenkinga yokuba khona kobubi.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNdasa 8._
