---
title:  Ingelosi Ebambezelekile
date:  02/03/2025
---

Njengoba sesibonile, “onkulunkulu” bamanga bezizwe zonke kwabe kungamadimoni ngokuhlifakele. Kwenye indawo siyabubona ubufakazi bokuthi lamadimoni angababusi bomkhathi nokungaba yiwona  alawula ababusi bezwe. Nezingilosi zasezulwini ezazithunywe nguNkulunkulu zingaphikiswa yilamandla alesitha.

`Funda ku-Danyeli 10:1-14, ngokukhethekile, unake amavesi aku-12, 13. Kungabe lamavesi asifundisani nokuyinto ephathelene nokulwa okukhona ezweni lonke na? Kungabe ucabangani ngezingelosi ezithunywe nguNkulunkulu “ezamiswa” izinsuku ezingamashumi amabili nanye na?`

Kungenzeka kanjani ukuthi ingelosi ethunywe ngu-Nkulunkulu ingakwazi “ukumiswa” amaviki amathathu na? Izidalwa ezinamandla amakhulu kangaka, ezazinamandla okwenza lokhu okwakufunwa nguDaniyeli ngaso lesosikhathi – okungukuthi, ukube wayefisa kwenzeke lokho. Uma wayengakwazi ukusebenzisa amandla akhe ekwenzeni lokhu, wayengakwazi ukwenza ingelosi ivele ngaphambi kuka-Daniyeli ngaso lesosikhathi.  Yize isimo sibonakala sinjena, ingelosi eyabe ithunywe nguNkulunkulu “yamiswa yinkosi yombuso wase-Pheshiya” isikhathi esingamaviki amathathu. Ngabe kwenzekani lapha na?

“Kwaze kwaba ngaviki amathathu lapho u-Gabriyeli alwisana namandla obumnyama, ezama ukuphikisana nomthelela wokusebenza kwalamandla emqondweni ka-Cyrus. . . . Okwakungenziwa yizulu likwenzela abantu bakaNkulunkulu kwabe sekwenziwe. Izwi lokunqoba lase lizwakele; Amandla esitha ayesebanjiwe ngesikhathi sokubusa kuka-Cyrus, nezinsuku zokubusa kwendodana yakhe u-Cambyses.”—Ellen G. White, Prophets and Kings, p. 572.

Ukuze lokhu kulwisana kukwazi ukuvela obala, uNkulunkulu kudingeka angawasebenzisi amandla aKhe wonke. Isitha kudingeka sinikwe ithuba lokwenza izinto lisebezisa ilungelo lokuzikhethela ukuze amandla anikezwe sona ngokungenamqondo, kodwa sikwazi ukuvimbeka izimiso ezaziwa yilezitha zombili (esingambulelwanga ukusuka nokuhlala kwalesimo). Kubonakala engazathi kukhona izimiso ezithile kulokhu kulwa okukhona ezweni, nokungukulwa okungaziwa ngisho nayizingelosi zikaNkulunkulu ezisenza ngaphansi kwalamandla, okuzokhulunywa ngakho esifundweni esilandelayo ngaphansi kwesihloko “imithetho yokungena esivumelwaneni.”

Ngamanye amazwi, ukuze sizokwazi ukuqonda kabanzi ngalezimiso angeke kubelukhuni ukwazi kabanzi ngalomqondo esesikhulumile ngawo, wokuthi uNkulunkulu usebenza ngothando kuphela, nokuthi uthando, olungeyona indlela yokuphoqa, luyisisekelo sombuso waKhe. Lomqondo wokuthi uNkulunkulu usebenza kuphela ngokwenkambiso evela othandweni, kungasisiza ukuba siqonde kangcono ngalempikiswani enkulu eqhubekayo.

`Kungabe wuzwa kanjani ngokubekwa kombandlela wokusebenza kuphela ngenkambiso yothando nokungesiyo into ephoqelelayo na? Ngabe ufundeni mayelana nezimiso zokusebenza kwamandla na?`
