Funda ku-Ellen G. White, “Idolatry at Sinai,” pp. 315–330, in Patriarchs and Prophets.

Ukukhuluma okumayelana nesono esenziwa ngesikhathi kwakhiwa ithole legolide, u-Ellen G. White wabhala lokhu okulandelayo: “Abantwana bakwa-Israyeli babekwa icala lokulwisana nomthetho wezwe, benza izinto ezaziphikisana nokwashiwo yiNkosi eyayibanike inqwaba yezibonelelo nabo bazivumela ngokwabo ngokufunga ukuthi bazohlonipha imiyalelo ekhishiwe. Ukuze umbuso kaNkulunkulu uzokwazi ukulandela izimiso zobulungiswa kwadingeka kubhekwe abantu abalahlekisayo ngokuthi bangenzi okuyikho. Nakhona lapho umusa kaNkulunkulu wawulokhu uveziwe. Ngesikhathi eqikelela ukuthi imithetho yaKhe iyagcinwa, bonke abantu banikwa inkululeko yokuzikhethela nethuba lokuphenduka. Abantu abangavunyelwanga yilabo ababelokhu beqhubeka nokulwisana noNkulunkulu.

“Besikhona Isidingo sokuba isono sithole ukujeziswa, nokuzokuba ngubufakazi ezizweni ezibazungezile okokuthu uNkulunkulu akahambisani nokuxutshwa nabanye onkulunkulu. Ngokuthi kube khona ukwahlulela kulabo abatholakale benecala, uMose, njengesithunywa sikaNkulunkulu, kwafuneka akubeke obala okushiwo nguNkulunkulu, alwisane nokwephula umthetho. Ukusukela lapho sebexwayisiwe, ama-Israyeli kwadingeka akugxeke ukukhonzwa kwezithixo zabezizwe ezibazungezile. Izitha zabo zabagcona abantwana bakwa-Israyeli ababephakamisa uJehova njengoNkulunkulu wabo kodwa baphinda badumisa ithole legolide abalenzile endaweni yase-Horeb. Yize kwakuphoqekile ukuba babhekane namaqiniso abuhlungu okuhlubuka kwabo, u-Israyeli wakwazi ukukhomba isimo esibuhlungu sokuphambuka kwabo. Lokhu kwakuzokuba ngubufakazi bokuthi ukona kwabo kakuzange kuthalaliswe kungenziwa lutho ngakho.

“Uthando olumqoka njengokwenziwa kobulungiswa kwaphoqa ukuba kubekhona ukwahlulelwa kwesono ngokuthi kube khona ubuhlungu ababuzwayo. . . . Kwabe kungumusa kaNkulunkulu owabangela ukuthi izinkulungwane zabantu zibhekane nokuzwa ubuhlungu, ukuze kungabibikho Isidingo sokuvakashela ezinye izigidi zabantu ngokubahlululela nabo. Ukuze iningi lizokwazi ukuphepha, kwadingeka ukuba uNkulunkulu ajezise abantu abambalwa.”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, pp. 324, 325.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`Kungani ube nomcabango wokuthi abantu abaningi banokukhathazeka ngolokhu okuthiwa wulaka lukaNkulunkulu na? Yini ekusizayo wena ukuthi ukuqonde kangcona lokhu na?`

`Yiziphi izinkinga ezibakhona uma abantu befuna ukuziphindisela nokuyinto engenzeki kuNkulunkulu ngesikhathi kunesidingo sokuba akwenze na?`

`Kungabe kwakunjani ukwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu ngesikhathi ejezisa u-Israyeli emuva kokuba ulwisane naye ngokuthi uzenzele ithole legolide na? Kungabe lokhu kwakhombisa kanjani ukuba nomusa kubona na? Yiziphi ezinye izibonelo ezikhona emBhalweni ezikhombisa ukwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu njengesenzo sothando na?`
