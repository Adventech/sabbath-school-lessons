### Kuleliviki Funda ku
Roma 15:4, 5; Roma 5:3-5; 1 Samuweli 26; iHubo 37:1-11.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Kepha isithelo sikaMoya........ ukubekezela” (Galathiya 5:22, NKJV).

Ososayensi benza ucwaningo ngezingane ezineminyaka emine namaswidi okuthiwa ama-marshmallows. Yonke ingane yatshelwa ngusosayensi ukuthi izophiwa imarshmallow; kodwa uma ingane ilindile kwaze kwabuya usosayensi lapho eye khona, iyonikwa amabili. Ezinye izingane zawaloshela ama-marshamallow emlonyeni eqeda-nje ukuphuma usosayensi; ezinye zalinda. Wabonakala umehluko.

Ososayensi baziqaphela lezingane zaze zafika eminyakeni yeshumi nantathu. Lezo ezazilindile zabonakala ziqhuba kahle esikoleni, futhi zinokuzethemba ukwedlula lezo ezingalindanga. Kwabonakala ukuthi ukubekezela kuyinkomba yento enkudlwana, into ebalulekile ekwakhekeni kwesimilo somuntu. Akumangalisi-ke ukuthi uNkulunkulu usitshela ukuthi asikhuthalele ukubekezela.

Kuleliviki sizoke sibheke ukuthi yini ingqikithi yeziko elivivinya kunawo wonke: iziko lokulinda.

**Ukubuka Isifundo Seviki**: Kungani ngesinye isikhathi kudingeka ukuthi silindele izinto isikhathi eside kangaka? Singafunda ziphi izifundo ngokubekezela siseseziko?
