`Funda uEksoduse 32:1-14. Sithola uMose edlala yiphi indima lapha?`

Ngemuva kokuthi abantu bekhonze ithole legolide, uNkulunkulu wathi sekwanele, wamemezela ukuthi uzobabhubhisa, enze uMose abe yisizwe esikhulu. Kodwa kunokuba amukele leso sipho sikaNkulunkulu, uMose wancenga uNkulunkulu ukuthi abe nomusa ebantwini baKhe, uNkulunkulu wavuma.

U-Eksoduse 32:1-14 uveza amaphuzu amabili abalulekile. Elokuqala, isinqumo sikaNkulunkulu sokubhubhisa abantu abahlubukile, abusise uMose kwaku ukuvivinywa okukhulu kuMose. UNkulunkulu wayefuna ukuthi uMose atshengise ukuthi unesihawu esingakanani ngalabantu abangalaleli.  UMose wakuphumelela lokho kuvivinywa. NjengoJesu, wacelela izoni umusa. Lokhu kubonakalisa into ethile ethathekisayo: ngesinye isikhathi uNkulunkulu angavuma ukuthi nathi sibhekane nokuphikiswa; angavuma ukuthi sifakwe eziko lokuvivinywa ukuze Yena, thina, namazulu abukelayo, kubonakale ukuthi sinesihawu esingakanani ngalabo abadukile.

`Yiziphi izizathu ezashiwo uMose zokuthi uNkulunkulu angabhubhisi uIsrayeli na?`

Elesibili, lezindimana zitshengisa ukuthi ukuphikiswa nokungalaleli, ubizo lokutshengisa umusa. Umusa udingeka ngalesikhathi abantu bengawufanele. Kodwa ngesikhathi bengawufanele, kulapho nathi singafuni ukubenzela umusa. Kodwa ngesikhathi udadewabo kaMose uMiriyamu emgxeka, wakhala kuNkulunkulu ukuba amphulukise ochokweni (Numeri 12). Ngesikhathi uNkulunkulu ethukuthelele uKhora nabalandeli bakhe, esefuna ukubabhubhisa bonke, uMose wawa ngobuso ebancengela ukuba bangafi. Ngakusasa, uIsrayeli ekhononda ngoMose ngenxa yokufa kwabahlubuki, noNkulunkulu esefuna ukubabulala bonke futhi, uMose wawa ngobuso, wanxusa uAroni ukuthi abenzele umnikelo wokushweleza ngokushesha (Numeri 16). Ekuzithobeni kwakhe, ekungazicabangelini kwakhe, phakathi kwaleliziko lokuvivinywa, uMose wabacelela umusa labo ababengawufanele nakancane.

`Cabanga ngabantu abakuzungezile ocabanga ukuthi abawufanele umusa. Ungenza kanjani ukuthi ngokuzithoba nokungazicabangeli kwakho, ube yisibonakaliso somusa kaNkulunkulu kubona?`
