### UMdali Owabethelwa 

“Konke kwavela ngaye; ngaphandle kwakhe akuvelanga lutho kukho konke okuvelileyo” (Johane 1:3). 

“Konke” kwavela ngaYe uJesu, kodwa, ngokusho kwemiBhalo, “uJesu wakhala” (Johane 11:35). UMdali wakhala? Nangaphezu kwalokho, uJesu “waphikwa waphinde wedelelwa abantu; umuntu wosizi, odeleliwe nowazana nokuhlupheka” (Isaya 53:3). UMdali, umuntu wosizi, odeleliwe waze waphikwa? Wake wakhala wathi “Nkulunkulu waMi, Nkulunkulu waMi, ungishiyeleni? (Matt. 27:46). 

Kwakungenzeka kanjani lokhu? Kungenxa yokuthi uJesu, uMdali wethu, wayebuye abe uMsindisi wethu futhi, ngaleyo ndlela wayengobethelweyo kaNkulunkulu-uMdali owathatha isimo somuntu, wahlupheka ephila impilo yokuhlupheka nokusebenza kanzima, okwagcina ngokuthi aphanyekwe esiphambanweni samaRoma. 

Kanjalo-ke uMdali wethu, esiphila ngaYe, sikhona ngaYe, sihamba ngaYe, siyikho konke ngaYe” (Izenzo 17:28), wahlupheka njengomuntu ngezindlela esingeke thina size sihlupheke ngazo. Singathwala kuphela olwethu usizi, okwethu ukudabuka; esiphambanweni wathwala “ izifo zethu, wetshatha ubuhlungu bethu” (Isaya 43:4)— bonke. Lesi yisenzo esimangalisa kakhulu kuwo wonke umlando womhlaba nezulu. 

Njengoba sesinawo lomlando wokubethelwa kukaNkulunkulu, kulezinyanga ezimbalwa ezilandelayo sizozama ukuqonda kangcono lokho okungaqondakali—ukuhlupheka kwethu, ukuhlupheka kwamaKrestu, kwalabo abanikele ukuphila kwabo kuKrestu. Asisho ukuthi sinazo zonke izimpendulo; esikushoyo ukuthi “uNkulunkulu uluthando” (1 Johane 4:8) nokuthi yize zenzeka lezi zinto, singamethemba uNkulunkulu, sikhule emseni ngenxa yazo noma kubuhlungu kangakanani. 

Kulonyangantathu sizofunda iZwi likaNkulunkulu, sibone ukuthi abanye abantu benyama negazi, nakuba benconywa ngokukholwa, nokho babhekana nokulahlekelwa yithemba, ukukhashelwa, ukujabhiswa, ukulahlekelwa, ukungabikho kobulungiswa, nokuhlukunyezwa

Uma sibuka laba bantu, okwabehlelayo, imizabalazo emiqondweni yethuyabo, nokuvivinywa kokukholwa kwabo (okucishe ebuthuntu, ubuhlungukufane nokwethu), sidinga njalo ukukuqhathanisa obafikela inhliziyonesiphambano. UNkulunkulu wethu unguNkulunkulu ohluphekayo. No Albert Camus, owayengesiye umKrestu oqinile, wabona ukuthi kusho ukuthini ukuhlupheka kukaNkulunkulu esiphambanweni, wathi: “Ubusuku eGolgotha bubaluleke kakhulu emlandweni wabantu ngenxa emathunzini abo, ongcwele washiya amalungelo akhe, waphuza nomthamo wokugcina usizi lokufa.”—Albert Camus, The Rebel (New York: Vintage International, 1991), p. 33. Noma, njengokusho kuka Ellen G. White: “Isiphambano siwukubonakaliswa emiqondweni yethu ebuthuntu, ubuhlungu obafikela inhliziyo kaNkulunkulu kusukela kungena isono emhlabeni.” --Education, p. 263. 

Izifundo zethu akuhlosiwe ngazo ukuvikela uNkulunkulu phakathi kobubi obukhona. Kunalokho, njengoba sesishilo, zizama ukusisiza ukuthi sibhekane nokuhlupheka okungegwemeke kulomhlaba lapho khona isono silula njengokuphefumula. Esizozama ukukutshengisa ukuthi ubuhlungu, ukuhlupheka, nokulahlekelwa akusho ukuthi uNkulunkulu usishiyile; kusho ukuthi nathi thina abakholwayo, siyathinteka kolokho okwenzeka kubo bonke abantu besono. Umehluko ukuthi, ngoJesu kanye nethemba asinika lona, singathola ithemba nesizathu sokuphila lapho kubonakala sengathi akusekho nesizathu sokuphila, nokuthi noma ngandlela thize, singaboni ukuthi singasethemba kanjani isithembiso esithi: “konke kusebenzelana ukulunga kulabo abathanda uNkulunkulu” (Rom. 8:28)—Yena loNkulunkulu owenza zonke izinto, wahlupheka ngazo zonke izinto futhi (kungakho-nje simthanda).
