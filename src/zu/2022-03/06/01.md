### Kuleliviki Funda ku
Johane 16:5-16; Kolose 1:28, 29; 1 Petru 1:13, Math. 5:29; Genesise 32.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Engikusebenzelayo futhi, ngilwa ngokusebenza kwaKhe okusebenza kimi ngamandla” (Kolose 1:29,NIV).

Owesilisa nowesifazane babehlezi ndawonye esiteshini somsakazo.  Indodana yowesifazane yayikade ibulewe eminyakeni engu 20 ngaphambili, Ulaka lwakhe nentukuthelo kwakusavutha namanje. Owesilisa weyehluke kakhulu. Indodakazi yakhe yayibulewe abashokobezi eminyakeni embalwa eyedlule. Yakhuluma ngokuxolela ababulali nendlela uNkulunkulu ayeguqule ngayo ubuhlungu bakhe. Kubuhlungu kunjalo, lendoda yaba isibonelo sendlela uNkulunkulu angaletha ngayo ukwelapheka ngesikhathi okuyisona esimnyama ezimpilweni zethu.

Kwenzeka kanjani ukuthi abantu ababili basabele ngezindlela ezehlukene kangaka? Inguquko yakwamoya yenzeka kanjani empilweni yoMKrestu, okwenza lowo muntu zimkhulise izinhlupheko kunokuba ehlulwe yizo?

**Ukubuka Isifundo Seviki**: Kunaqhaza lini ukuzikhethela kwethu empini yokulwa no “mina” nesono? Singaligwema kanjani iphutha lokuvumela imizwa yethu ibuse izinqumo esizenzayo?  Kungani sidinga ukuthi sikhuthazele, singanikezeli uma siseziko?

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha likaNcwaba 6._
