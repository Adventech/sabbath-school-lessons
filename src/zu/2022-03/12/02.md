---
title:  Ukuzithoba Ukuze Ukhonze
date:  11/09/2022
---

`Funda kwabaseFilipi 2:5-9. Yimuphi umlayezo obalulekile esiwuphathelwe yilezindimana?`

Isiko lesimanje lithi sonke asilwele amalungelo ethu. Konke lokhu kuhle, futhi kumelwe kube njalo.  Kodwa njengoba kwakunjalo kuJesu, intando kaNkulunkulu ingadinga ukuthi sidele amalungelo ethu ukuze sikhonze uBaba ngezindlela ezizokwenzela umbuso kaNkulunkulu umthelela waphakade. Ukudela amalungelo kungaba lukhuni futhi kungabi ntofontofo, kudale isimo seziko.

Bheka ukuthi uJesu wakwenza kanjani lokhu (Filipi 2:5-8). Lezindimana zichaza izinyathelo ezintathu ezathathwa nguJesu ezithoba phansi kwentando kaYise. Kanti ekuqaleni, uPawulu uyasikhumbuza uthi: “Mawube kini lowo mqondo owawukuJesu” (Filipi 2:5, NIV).

Ukuze akwazi ukusisindisa, uJesu wadela ukulingana noYise, weza emhlabeni ngesimo somuntu nokusilela komuntu (Filipi 2:6, 7, NIV). UJesu akezanga njengomuntu omkhulu wenkazimulo, kodwa njengenceku yabanye abantu (Filipi 2:7, NIV).

Njengenceku engumuntu, uJesu akazange aphile impilo yokuthula nende, kodwa “walalela kwaze kwaba sekufeni.”  Kanti futhi akafanga ngendlela ehloniphekile. Cha, “walalela kwaze kwaba sekufeni—ukufa kwesiphambano!” (Filipi 2:8, NIV).

`Iyiphi imikhakha yempilo lapho lesibonelo sikaJesu siyisifundo sethu na? Uma amalungelo nokulingana kwabantu kukuhle, futhi kufanele ukuvikelwa, ungakuchaza kanjani ukuthi ngesinye isikhathi kungadingeka uwadele lawo malungelo?  Manje-ke funda kwabaseFilipi 2:9. Isisiza kanjani lendimana ukuthi sizwisise umqondo wokuzithoba ngaphansi kwentando kaBaba?`

`Thandazela ukuhlakanipha okuvela kuMoya oNgcwele, uzibuze ukuthi: “Yimaphi amalungelo engibambelele kuwo kumanje-nje, malungelo lawo angase abe umgoqo ekuthini ngizithobe phansi kwentando kaJesu ukuthi ngikhonze umndeni wami, ibandla lami, nalabo abangizungezile?  Ngizimisele kangakanani ukubekezelela ukungaphatheki kahle ukuze ngikhonze abanye ngendlela ebonakalayo?”`
