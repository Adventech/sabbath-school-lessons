---
title:  'Ukuchezuka Okungalindelekile 1: Isigodi'
date:  28/06/2022
---

> <p></p>
> “Noma ngihamba esigodini sethunzi lokufa, angiyikwesaba okubi; ngokuba Wena ukimi; intonga yaKho nodondolo lwaKho kuyangithokozisa” (iHubo 23:4, NKJV).

Bekungaba ngcono ukuba izindlela zokulunga bezijikeleza kuphela emadlelweni aluhlaza ogwini lwemifula epholile.  Kodwa uDavide akasiniki lowo mfanekiso. Futhi, kuyo lendlela kukhona nesigodi sethunzi lokufa—okungesiyo indawo esiyifisayo!  Ngezinye izikhathi zonyaka, amawa atholakala kwaIsrayeli aba nezikhukhula eziqhamuka kungalindelekile. Lezindawo zincane ngobubanzi, zijulile, okwenza ukuthi ukukhanya kusitheke. Kanjalo-ke, “ithunzi lokufa” umfuziselo “wethunzi elijule kakhulu” noma “ubumnyama obugubuzele.”

`Cabanga ngezikhathi lapho wawukwesakho “isigodi sethunzi lokufa.” Kwakunjani?  Wawesaba, yize noma wawazi ukuthi ukhona uMalusi?  Yiziphi izindimana zeBhayibheli ezaziyigugu kakhulu kuwena ngaleso sikhathi, ngasizathu sini?`

`Ucabanga ukuthi zagcina kanjani izimvu esigodini? Ucabanga ukuthi izimvu zaziyela lapho, noma mhlawumbe uMalusi uqobo owaziholela lapho?  Sekela impendulo yakho.`

U-Elisabeth Elliot ubhala athi: “Iwundlu elalingazithola lisesigodini sethunzi lokufa lalingathi lidukisiwe. Kwakungadingeka ukuthi linqamule lobo bumnyama ukuze lifunde ukungesabi. Umalusi ulokho enalo.” –– Elizabeth Elliot, Quest for Love (Grand Rapids, MI: Revell Books, 1996), p. 218.

`Wake wazizwa sengathi “uholelwe ophathe” esigodini?  Wasabela kanjani kuNkulunkulu ngaleso sikhathi?  Ucabanga ukuthi kungani uMalusi ezimisele ukwamukela ukusolwa ngokuvuma ukuthi singene esigodini esimnyama na?`
