### Kuleliviki Funda ku
IHubo 23, Roma 23:18-21.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Ubuyisa umphefumulo wami; uyangihola ezindleleni zokulunga ngenxa yegama laKhe” (iHubo 23:3, NKJV).

USophie wencika ngesicabha sasekamelweni lakhe, washibilika waze wahlala phansi. Amehlo ayehlengezela izinyembezi, kungekudala, wayesebubula. “Angakwenza kanjani Alokhu? Angakwenza kanjani-nje?” USophie wayesanda kuthola izindaba ezephula inhliziyo yakhe. Umuntu ayekade emthatha njengomngani, umuntu ayekade emhlonipha futhi emethemba, wayesabalalisa amanga ngaye ukuze adicilele phansi isithunzi sakhe nomsebenzi ayewenza.  Ethatha iBhayibheli lakhe embhedeni, wazithola esefunda amazwi ajwayelekile, athi: “Ubuyisa umphefumulo wami; uyangihola ezindleleni zokulunga ngenxa yegama laKhe. Yebo, noma ngihamba esigodini sethunzi lokufa, angiyikwesaba okubi; ngokuba Wena unami; Intonga yaKho nodondolo lwaKhe luyangiduduza” (iHubo 23:3, 4, NKJV).

“Ngeke. Angiyikholwa lento!” uSophie ekhuluma yedwa. Kodwa iqiniso lalingenakuphikwa.  UMalusi ehutsheni wayelusa izimvu zakhe ezindleleni zokulunga, kodwa zona lezo zindlela zazijikeleza esigodini sethunzi lokufa.  Kungenzeka yini ukuthi nalokhu kuthengiswa ngumngani wakhe, lesigodi esimnyama, kuse ukusebenza kukaNkulunkulu ukumyala endleleni yokulunga?

**Ukubuka Isifundo Seviki**: Yiziphi izikhathi lapho ukhule khona kakhulu enkoloweni—lapho izinto zikuhambela kalula, noma ebunzimeni?

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha lika Ntulikazi 2._
