### Kuleliviki Funda ku
Filipi 4:4-7, Joshuwa 5:13-6:20, iHubo 145, iZenzo 16:16-34, 2 IziKronike 20:1-30.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Thokozani eNkosini njalonjalo; ngiyaphinda ngithi: Thokozani” (KwabaseFilipi 4:4, NKJV).

Kulula ukudazuluka ngenjabulo kuNkulunkulu uma sijabule. Kodwa akunjalo uma izinto zibheda, uma siphansi kwezimo ezimbi kunazo zonke ongazicabanga, uma iziko lokuvivinywa lishisa kakhulu.  Kodwa kungaleso sikhathi-ke lapho sidinga ukudumisa uNkulunkulu ngaphezu kwakuqala, ngoba ukudumisa indlela esisizayo ukukhulisa ukukholwa.

Empeleni ukudumisa kungaguqula nezimo zethu ezimnyama kakhulu, hhayi ukuguqula emaceleni ethu, kodwa ukuguqula thina, nalabo abasizungezile, ngendlela esisiza ukuthi sibhekane nezinselele.

Ukudumisa ukukholwa okusebenzayo. Kungeze kwazifikela kithina, kodwa uma sizifundisa ukudumisa kuze kube into ezifikelayo empilweni yethu, kunamandla okuguqula nokunqoba.

**Ukubuka Isifundo Seviki**: Kuyini ukudumisa? Kungaba kanjani ukudumisa isikhali esinamandla senkolo ngezikhathi zobunzima na? Ukudumisa kungasiguqula kanjani thina kanye nesimo esikuso?

_Funda isifundo salelilviki ukulungisela iSabatha likaNcwaba 27._
