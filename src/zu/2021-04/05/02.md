---
title:  Sokani Izinhliziyo Zenu
date:  24/10/2021
---

UDuteronomi 10, ongukuqhubeka kukaDuteronomi 9, empeleni ngukuqinisekisa kukaNkulunkulu kwesivumelwano ayesenze noIsrayeli. Nembala, okuningi kwencwadi kungukuvuselelwa kwesivumelwano. Okungukuthi, nangemuva kwesono esibi e-Horeb, lapho okwathi uMose esanda kusuka phakathi kwabo isikhashana bawela ekukhonzeni izithixo, uJehovah wayengakaqedi ngabo.

`Funda: uDuteronomi 10:1-11. Kwenzekani lapha okusisiza ukuba siqonde ukuthi uNkulunkulu wabathethelela abantu Bakhe isono sabo futhi waqinisa kabusha isithembiso sesivumelwano ayesenze kubo nakoyise na?`

UMose waphahlaza izibhebhe zoMthetho Olishumi (Dut. 9:17)—Uphawu lwesivumelwano esiphukileyo (Dut. 32:19). “Ukutshengisa ukunengeka kwakhe yicala labo, wajikela phansi zibhebhe zamatshe, futhi zephuka phambi kwabo bonke abantu, ngalokho ephawula ukuthi babephula isivumelwano sabo noNkulunkulu, ngalokho uNkulunkulu wayephule isivumelwano Sakhe nabo.”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 320.

Ngalokho, ukuthi uNkulunkulu watshela uMose kuba aqophe izibhebhe ezintsha “njengezokuqala” futhi abhale kuzo amazwi ayebhalwe kwezokuqala kubonisa ukuthi uNkulunkulu wayebathethelele abantu futhi engaqedile ngabo, nangalesosikhathi.

`Funda: uDuteronomi 10:14-16. UNkulunkulu uthini kubo na? Ichaza ukuthini Imifanekiso uJehovah ayisebenzisa lapha na?`

Kunokuxubana kwemifanekiso lapha: ijwabu, inhliziyo, nentamo. Nakuba kunjalo, iphuzu lisobala. Ukusoka kwakuluphawu lwesivumelwano, kodwa wuphawu nje lwangaphandle. UNkulunkulu wayefuna Izinhliziyo zabo, Okungukuthi, izingqondo zabo, inkanuko yabo, uthando lwabo. Umfanekiso wezintamo ezilukhuni kalula nje wawukhomba enkanini ababenayo ekungafunini kwabo ukulalela uJehovah. Futhi, lapha nakwezinye izindawo, uJehovah wayebatshela ukuba mabayeke ukuthembeka okungazinzile futh bakhonze Yena ngenhliziyo nangomphefumulo wabo wonke.

`Cabanga ngazo zonke izikhathi uJehovah akuthethelele ngazo izono zakho. Lokho kumele kukutsheleni ngomusa Wakhe na?`
