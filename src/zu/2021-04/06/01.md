### Kuleliviki Funda Lokhu
Dut. 4:1–9, Mat. 15:1–9, Num. 25:1–15, 1 Kor. 10:13, Dut. 4:32–35, Mat. 5:13–16.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “ ‘Futhi yisiphi isizwe esikhulu esinezimiso nezahlulelo ezilungile njengawo wonke lo mthetho engiwubeka phambi kwenu namuhla na?’ ” (Duteronomi 4:8, NKJV).

Izahluko ezintathu zokuqala zikaDuteronomi zaziyisifundo Somlando, zikhumbuza abantu ngalokho ababedlule kukho kuze kube yilesisikhathi. Kuthi lapho sifika esahlukweni sesine, isifundo somlando siguqke sibe yintshumayelo. Ukukhumbula kwezigameko kwakungekona nje okwabathanda umlando; kunalokho, kwakunenhloso, kubabonisa amandla kanye nomusa kaNkulunkulu esebenza phakathi kwabantu, futhi nakuba babona, uJehovah wayesazolukhu ehloniphe isivumelwano Sakhe kanye nabo.

Isahluko sesine Siqala ngegama lesiHeberu (isihlanganasino kanye nesandiso), we‘attah, okungahumushwa ngokuthi “Manje futhi” noma “Manje ngalokho.” Babesanda kubuyekeza umlando wabo ongemdala, isikhumbuzo salokho uNkulunkulu ayekwenzile ekubafikiseni kulendawo—ngalokho, noma “manje ngalokho,” kumele benze lokho uNkulunkulu abatshela ukuba bakwenze (Bheka uDut. 10:12) ngokuphendula.

Yingakho isenzo sokuqala esiqhamuka ngemuva kuka “manje ngalokho” singushama’’ isenzo esifana (futhi ngesimo esifanayo njengaleso esisetshenziswa kumkhuleko we-Shema, futhi sichaza “ukuzwa” noma “lalela” noma “Thobela”— isenzo esiphindaphindwa kuwo wonke uDuteronomi.

Ngalokho, Isahluko Siqala: Manje ngalokho, Israyeli, ngenxa yalokho enginenzele kona, kumele nilalele okulendalayo....

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, November 6._
