### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Num. 20:1–13, Dut. 31:2, Dut. 34:4, Dut. 34:1–12, Juda 9, 1 Kor. 15:13–22.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Kepha uMikayeli, ingelosi enkulu, lapho ephikisana noSathane, ekhulumisana naye ngesidumbu sikaMose, akabanga nasibindi sokuletha ukwahlulela kokuhlambalaza, kodwa wathi: “INkosi mayikujezise!’ ” (Juda 9, NKJV).

Njengoba sibonile kuyo yonke lekota, uMose wayengumongo kuDuteronomi. Impilo yakhe, isimilo sakhe, umbiko wakhe ugcwele incwadi. Nakuba, yebo, uDuteronomi emayelana noNkulunkulu nothando Lwakhe ngo ‘am yisra’el, “abantu bakwaIsrayeli,” uNkulunkulu wayevame ukusebenzisa uMose ukwembula lolothando kanye nokukhuluma kubantu Bakhe uIsrayeli. Manje, sesifika ekugcine kwekota, Isiphetho Sokufunda kwethu uDuteronomi, sifika futhi ekupheleni kwempilo kaMose, okungenani impilo yakhe lapha.

Njengalokhu u-Ellen G. White akusho: “UMose wayazi ukuthi wayezofa eyedwa; akukho mngani wasemhlabeni owayezovunyelwa ukumsiza emahoreni akhe okugcina. Kwakunemfihlo kanye nokwesaba ngombono owawuphambi kwakhe, kuwo inhliziyo yakhe yesaba. Isivivinyo esesabekayo kuyo kwakungukwehlukana nabantu ayebanakekele wabathanda—abantu ukuthatheka kanye nempilo yakhe eyayimunye nabo isikhathi eside. Kodwa wayefunde ukuthembela kuNkulunkulu, futhi ngokholo olungabuzi wazinikela yena nabantu bakhe othandweni Lwakhe nesihawu.”— Patriarchs and Prophets, pp. 470, 471.

Njengoba impilo nokus ebenza KukaMose kwembula okuningi ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu, kanjalo, futhi nokufa nokuvuka kwakhe.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, December 25._
