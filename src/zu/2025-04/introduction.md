### Amathuba Esibili: Incwadi KaJoshuwa

Incwadi kaJoshuwa iphawula inguquko esuka ebuholini bukaMose kuye kobukaJoshuwa. Iqala ngendaba kaIsrayeli engena Ezweni leSithembiso bese iphetha ngabo bezinza kulelozwe.

UJoshuwa, nembala, wayenesibonelo okulukhuni ukusilandela. Okungukuthi, ukuthabatha lapho uMose (uMose!) ayeshiye khona. Kodwa leyonselelo yayiyisiqalo nje. UJoshuwa kwakumele enze lokho uMose ayengazange akwenze: ukuthatha isizwe, emuva kweminyake engu-40 ehlane, asiwelise iJordani angene naso eKanana, ngokwesithembiso uNkulunkulu ayesenzE kokhokho eminyakeni edlule.

“UMose inceku yami ufile; ngalokho suka uwele leli Jordani, wena nalaba bantu bonke, niye ezweni engibanika lona abantwana bakwa-Israyeli” (Josh. 1:2, NKJV).

Umongo wayoyonke indaba utholakala lapha, emazwini kaJehovah kuJoshuwa: bona bazongena “ezweni Mina [YHVH] engibanikeza lona.”

Yebo, uJoshuwa akazukukwenza yedwa, kodwa kuphela ngamandla kanye nokuhola kukaJehovah, okwakungabe wafaka abantu ezweni esizukulwaneni esingaphambili ukube babelalele ingxenye yabo yesivumelwano. Ngeshwa, abazange, futhi ngalokho, babhekana nemiphumela yezenzo zabo.

Iqiniso ngukuthi, ngesikhathi seminyaka engu-40, uIsrayeli wayebhekane nohlangothi olungeluhle lwesivumelwano. Ngenxa yokuhlubuka kwabo kuNkulunkulu, isizukulwane sabo esikhulile esasinamava ezimangaliso zoFuduko, ngaphandle kukaJoshuwa no-Caleb, safela ehlane. Ezine kwezinhlanu izincwadi zikaMose zibhekana nalokho okwenzeka kusona ngenkathi sidwanguza ehlane sonke lesosikhathi. Manje, ngaphansi kobuholi bukaJoshuwa, isizukulwane sesibili sibhekana nenselelo yokuthatha izwe.

“UMose wambiza uJoshuwa, wathi kuye emehlweni abantu bonke bakwa-Israyeli, “Qina, ume isibindi, ngokuba uyakuhamba nalaba bantu, uye ezweni uJehova afunga koyise ukubanika lona; wena uyakwenza ukuba balidle ifa lalo. UJehova nguye ohamba phambi kwakho; uyakuba nawe; akayikukuyeka, akushiye; ungesabi, ungabi nangebhe” (Dut. 31:7, 8, NKJV).

Izithembiso zikaNkulunkulu ezanikezwa izinzalamizi kanye nakuMose sezizogcwaliseka. Sizothola ndawonye Kunomoya wokulindela nenjabulo, isiqalo ukuthi, nakuba esisha kubantu, okukade bengenakhaya futhi bengenalutho. UNkulunkulu wethembekile ekubakhululeni ebugqilini, futhi ngokuqinisekile yabhalwa eminyakeni angathenjwa ukuba agcwalise izithembiso Zakhe engaphezumayelana nezwe.

“Inhloso enkulu yencwadi kaJoshuwa ngeyokuchaza ukungena kukaIsrayeli ezweni ezintathu, umhlaba lesithembiso, ukunqotshwa kwezwe, ukwabiwa esiphila kuwokwalo phakathi kwezizwe. Lenhloso iyisisekelo sombiko wencwadi, okungukuthi, ukuthembeka kukaNkulunkulu ekufezeni isithembiso sezwe asenza kuAbrahama. Incwadi igcizelela ngokwezinselelo zawoukuthembeka kukaNkulunkulu ezithembisweni Zakhe zesivumelwano (Josh. 21:43-45).”— Andrews Bible Commentary (Andrews University Press, 2020), p. 365.

Sizothola ndawonye ukuthi, nakuba incwadi kaJoshuwa yabhalwa eminyakeni engaphezu kwezinkulungwane ezintathu, umhlaba esiphila kuwo namuhla awehlukile kunalowo kaJoshuwa ngokwezinselelo zawo kwezikamoya. Singabhekana nezinselelo zohlobo olwehlukile, kepha nakuba kunjalo kunezinselelo, ikakhulu ezikamoya, ezibeka ukuphepha kwethu, ukholo lwethu, engozini kanye nokugcwaliseka komsebenzi uNkulunkulu awubeke phezu kwabantu Bakhe. Isibonelo sikaJoshuwa nembala siyokhuthaza thina ukuba sibange izithembiso zikaNkulunkulu mayelana nezikhathi zethu kanye nokuphumelela ngamandla Akhe, njengalokhu enza.
