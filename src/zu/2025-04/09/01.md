### Funda Lokhu Kulelivik: Gen. 3:17–24; Deut. 6:3; Josh. 13:1–7; Heb. 12:28; Lev. 25:1–5, 8–13; Ezek. 37:14, 25.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Buyelani enqabeni, ziboshwa esezinethemba; namuhla ngiyanitshela ukuthi ngiyakubuyisela kini okuphindwe kabili” (Zakariya 9:12, ESV).

KuJoshuwa 13-21 kukhona uhla olude lwemingcele yezindawo ezahlukene okwakubekwe izimpawu ezazichaza ukuthi lendawo engaka ingeyasiphi isizwe kwa-Israyeli. Kumuntu ofundayo njengamanje, loluhla lwalezindawo lungabonakala kuyinto engenamsebenzi, kodwa lemingcele isekelwe olwazini lwezenkolo olumayelana Nezwe Lesithembiso nokuyinto ebalulekile kithina namuhla. Ngenxa yoluhla lwalemingcele yangempela, uNkulunkulu wayefuna ukufundisa ama-Israyeli ukuthi izwe Lesithembiso kwakungeyona into eliphupho. Lokhu kwabe kuyisithembiso ababenikeziwe ngayo yonke indlela ephathekayo, nokuyinto abangakwazi ukuyilinganisa ubungako bayo. Kodwa kwakufuneka benze lesisithembiso kube yinto yangempela ngokuthi kubekhona abakwenzayo.

Lokho kusho ukuthi, yebo uNkulunkulu wayezobanika izwe njengefa; kwakuzokuba yisipho abaphiwa sona, isipho esasingukugcwaliseka kwesithembiso esenziwa nguNkulunkulu koyise. “Bhekani, ngilibekile izwe phambi kwenu; ngenani nilidle izwe uJehova alifungela oyihlo, o-Abrahama, no-Isaka, noJakobe ukulinika bona nenzalo yabo emva kwabo” (Duteronomi 1:8). Kodwa noma kunjalo, kukhona indima okwakufuneka bayidlale.

Kuleliviki sizobheka ezinye ezinto zokukholwa eziphathelene Nezwe Lesithembiso kanye nezikamoya, izinto ezibalulekile zabantu abathi zonke izithembiso ngezabo njengabo zitholakala kuJesu.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha lika uLwezi 29._
