---
title:  Ukubuyiswa Kwezwe
date:   27/11/2025
---

`Funda ku-Jeremiya 24:6; Jeremiya 31:16; Hezekiya 11:17; Hezekiya 28:25; kanye no-Hezekiya 37:14, 25. Kungabe sasiyini isithembiso sikaNkulunkulu mayelana nokubuya kuka-Israyeli ubuyela Ezweni Lesithembiso nokuthi lokhu kwagcwaliseka kanjani na?`

Ngesikhathi besekudingisweni kwaseBabiloni, abakwa-Israyeli abagcinanga ngokubhekana nesimo esibuhlungu sokungabi nemvelaphi kodwa baphinde bashaywa ngukuthi ubudlelwano ababenabo noNkulunkulu, yize kwakuyinto ephathekayo ngenxa yesithembiso sokuba nezwe, nokuyinto eyabe ingesiwona umbandela, futhi kungagcini lapho, kodwa bakwazi ukulithola izwe. Ngesikhathi u-Israyeli uvuma izono, ukhombisa ukuphenduka, futhi ufuna uNkulunkulu ngezinhliziyo zabo zonke, uNkulunkulu wasigcwalisa isithembiso sakhe, waphinde wabaphindisela ezweni labo nokwaba luphawu lokubuyiselwa kwabo. Lokhu kusho ukuthi, UNkulunkulu wayelokhu enguNkulunkulu wabo, yize babengekho ezweni labo.

Nokho, njengoba isithembiso sika-Israyeli sokuthola izwe kube ngelabo unomphelo kwakuyisithembiso esihambisana nombandela (Duteronomi 28:63, 64; Joshuwa 23:13, 15; 1 AmaKhosi 9:7; 2 AmaKhosi 17:23; Jeremiya 12:10-12), nesithembiso sokuphinde kubuyelwe ezweni labo kuphinde kwenze u-Israyeli athuthuke ngesikhathi ekulendawo emuva kokubuya ekudingisweni. Ngesikhathi esifanayo, abaprofethi abatholakala eTestamenteni Elidala bakhuluma ngokubuyiselwa ezweni okwabe kuzokwenziwa ngubukhosi bukaDavide esikhathini esilandelayo (Isaya 9:6, 7; Zakariya 9:9, 16). Lesi sithembiso sagcwaliseka ngokuphila, nangokufa, kanye nokuvuswa kukaJesu Kristu, okungaye zonke izithembiso ezazinikwe u-Israyeli wasendulo zazizogcwaliseka.

ETestamenteni Elisha, Izwe Lesithembiso akukhulunywa ngalo ngqo, kodwa siyatshelwa ukuthi izithembiso zikaNkulunkulu zigcwalisekile ngoJesu Kristu (2 Korinte 1;20; Roma 15:8). Ngakho-ke, ngenxa yokukhanyiseleka ngoKristu, Izwe selihunyushwe ngenye indlela, manje sekuyinto emele isimo sokubusiswa ngokukamoya lapho uNkulunkulu ezonika abantu baKhe abakholekile njengamanje (Efesu 2:6) nabanokukholeka esikhathini esizayo.

Ukugcwaliseka kokugcina kwesithembiso sikaNkulunkulu sokuphumula, nokuba nenala, nokuphila kahle ezweni kuzokuba yinto eyokwenzeka emhlabeni omusha, sebekhululiwe esonweni kanye nemiphumela yaso. Uma sinalokhu kuqonda njengamaKrestu, ithemba lethu likusithembiso sokubuya kukaKristu bese kuthi emva kweminyaka eyinkulungwane abasindisiweyo besezulwini, kwakhiwe umbuso oyohlala ingunaphakade umiswa emhlabeni owenziwe waba musha. Lokhu kuyokuba ngukugcwaliseka kokugcina kwezithembiso ezenziwa neziphathelene nokuthola izwe.

`Funda ku-Johane 14:1-3; Thithu 2:13; kanye ne-Isambulo 21:1-3. Kungabe yikuphi ukuthemba kokugcina esikuthola kulamavesi, nokuthi kungani ukufa kukaJesu kusinikeza isiqiniseko sokugcwaliseka kwalelithemba na?`
