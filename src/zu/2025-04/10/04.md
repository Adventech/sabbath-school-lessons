Njengoba sathola esifundweni seviki lokuqala, uJoshuwa uvezwa njengoMozisi, okwathi ekuphileni kwesizukulwane sesibili, waphinda izinyathelo ezibalulekile zokuhamba kusukwa eGibhithe. Ngesimo abe eyisona uMozisi, wathola ukuthunywa ngokuthi abonane noNkulunkulu. Ngaphansi kobuholi kwabo bobabili, udumo luka-Israyeli phakathi kwezizwe kwazenza zaba nokusaba. UMozisi wahola u-Israyeli ngesikhathi bewela Ulwandle Olubomvu, kanti uJoshuwa wahola u-Israyeli ngesikhathi bewela iJordani ngendlela emangalisayo. Bobabili labaholi bakhunjuzwa ngokubaluleka kokusoka kanye nokubaluleka kwePhasika. Imana yaqala ukuwa ngesikhathi sikaMozisi, kodwa kwaphela ukuba khona kwemana ngesikhathi sikaJoshuwa. Bobabili bayalelwa ukuthi bakhumule izimbadada zabo. Ukwelula izandla kwabo bobabili kwakusho ukunqoba kuma-Israyeli. UMozisi wakhipha umyalelo wokuba kwehlukaniswe izwe kuphinde kwakhiwe imizi eyizikhungo zokuphepha. UJoshuwa wayigcwalisekisa lemiyalelo. Bobabili benza inkulumo yokuvalelisa esizweni baphinda bavuselela isivumelwano benzela abantu ababephila ngesikhathi beqeda umsebenzi wabo.

`Funda ku-Duteronomi 18:15-19, Duteronomi 34:10-12, Johane 1:21, Izenzo 3:22-26, naku-Izenzo 7:37. Ngubani owagcwalisa isiprofetho sikaMozisi owabe engumprofethi ofana naye na? Kungabe uJoshuwa ungena kanjani kulomfanekiso esinawo na?`

Impilo kaJoshuwa kwaba ngukuphila okugcwalisa isiprofetho esenziwa nguMozisi (Duteronomi 18:15, 18). Noma kunjalo, isiprofetho esenziwa nguMozisi asizange sigcwaliseke ngokuqonda okuphelele. Ukuqonda okuphelele kwalesi siprofetho kwakuzokwenziwa (noma kwakuzogcwaliswa) nguMesiya. Wayemazi uBaba ngokuba seduzane naYe (Johane 1:14, 18); Wabe eliqiniso waphinde wakwazi ukwembula uNkulunkulu ngokuqinisekileyo (Luke 10:22; Johane 14:6; Mathewu 22:16). UNkulunkulu wakwenza ukuthi afake Amazwi aKhe emlonyeni waKhe (Johane 14:24). Ngakho-ke, ukuphila kwabo bobabili uMozisi kanye noJoshuwa baphenduka baba yisithunzi sokufika kukaMesiya, uJesu.

`Kungabe ubaluleke kangakanani uJesu ekuhambeni kwakho noNkulunkulu na? Kungani kufanele uJesu, nalokhu athe wakwenzela khona, kufanele abe yisisekelo samava empilo yakho yonke yobuKrestu na?`
