---
title:  Ukulangazelela Ukubakhona KwaKhe
date:   13/11/2025
---

`Funda ku-Joshuwa 18:1, 2. Kwakwenziwani lapho uJoshuwa ephazamisa uhlelo lokwabelana izwe na?`

Emuva kokuchazelana ngokwabiwa kwezwe okwakwabelwa izinhlanga ezimbili ezinkulu ngasentshonalanga yomfula i-Jordani kanye nengxenye yesizwe sakwa-Manase, lesi siqephu sengxoxo siveza ukuhlangana komhlangano wase-Shilo, lapho izwe lalabelwa izinhlanga ezincane eziyisikhombisa ezabe zisele.

Ukubakhona kwendlu engcwele kaNkulunkulu, “IThabernakele laMi” lokhu kwakumele ukugcwaliseka kwesithembiso sikaNkulunkulu sokuthi uzophila phakathi kwabantu baKhe (Eksodusi 25:8; Levitikusi 26:11, 12), kuphinde kuveze ingqikithi yalokhu okushiwo encwadini: Ukubakhona kukaNkulunkulu phakathi kuka-Israyeli sekwenze kwaba yimpumelelo ukuba bakwazi ukuthola izwe futhi kuzokuba yinto eyohlala ingumthombo wezibusiso ku-Israyeli, kuthi ngabo, kubekhona umthombo wezibusiso emhlabeni wonke (Genesise 12:3). Ukudumisa uNkulunkulu kwaba yiyonanto engumthombo kanye nokwagqama ukwedlula konke ukunqoba kanye nokwabelana izwe abalizuzile. Ukubakhona kwendlu engcwele kaNkulunkulu, nokwakhiwa kwe-Thempeli kamuva, kufuneka kubeyinto eyohlala njalo isiza abantu ukuba bakwazi ukubona ukubakhona kukaNkulunkulu phakathi kwabo, nokwenza kube ngumthwalo wabo ukwazi ukulandela isivumelwano.

Funda ku-Hebheru 6:19, 20; Hebheru 9:11, 12; kanye no-Hebheru 10:19

`23. Singenza kanjani thina njengama-Krestu angenayo indlu engcwele kaNkulunkulu nokuyindlu egcizelela ukubakhona siqu kukaNkulunkulu phakathi kwethu, esikufunda kuJoshuwa na?`

Ukubakhona kwendlu engcwele kaNkulunkulu akudingeki kufike njengento eqhamuka kungazelelwe, ngoba isihloko esikhuluma ngendlu engcwele kaNkulunkulu kuyinto eseyake kwakhulunywa ngayo encwadini kaJoshuwa lapho asebenzisa khona umphongolo wesivumelwano. Lokhu kwabe kuyinto ebalulekile ezintweni ezitholakala egumbini eliNgcwele Ngcwele, futhi kwenza kwabakhona izinhla ezimbili ezikulencwadi: Ukudlula kwabantu abanqobileyo. Njengamanje, ukubekwa kwesakhiwo setabernakele kwakuyinto ababegxile kuyo kakhulu ngesikhathi kwabiwa izwe. UJoshuwa wakhombisa ukuthi impilo ka-Israyeli yabe isekelwe ekubeni kubekhona indlu engcwele kaNkulunkulu, nokwabe kuyisakhiwo sekomkhulu likaJehova.

`Kuphinde kube yinto ebalulekile kithina okokuthi, njengamaKrestu aphila ngesikhathi esingumfuziselo woSuku LokuBuyisana, kudingeka sibhekise amehlo ethu endlini engcwele kaNkulunkulu yasezulwini njengoba silokhu siqhubeka nokubhekana nezinselelo zalokhu kuphila (ukuphila kwamanje) nokuyinto eyinkinga nenyakazisa ukukholwa, nokuthemba, kanye nokwamukela ezikamoya kwethu. Njengoba silokhu siqhubeka nokuthembela ekusebenzeni kukaKristu okwagcwaliseka esiPhambanweni ngesikhathi esendlini engcwele kaNkulunkulu yasezulwini, singaqhubeka nokubheka phambili ngokukholwa, sibheke esikhathini lapho uNkulunkulu ezophinde ahlale phakathi kwabantu baKhe, kodwa kulokhu useyohlala ingunaphakade. (Qhathanisa lokhu naku-Isambulo 21:3.)`
