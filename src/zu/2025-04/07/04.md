---
title:  Ama-Altare Lokuvuselela
date:   11/11/2025
---

`Yini eyagqugquzela uJoshuwa ukuba akhele uJehova i-Altare na? Funda uJosh. 8:30, 31; qhathanisa no Dut. 11:26–30; Dut. 27:2–10.`

Ngesikhathi sokuphila kwezingqalabutho zokukholwa, i-altare laliwuphawu lokuba zihambi futhi kwakuyinto ephathekayo emele ukubanga kwabo izwe, okuyinto ababeyithenjisiwe nguNkulunkulu. Njengamanje, uma bemisa i-altare, ama-Israyeli ayeletha ubufakazi bokugcwaliseka kwesithembiso esabe sinikezwe obaba-mkhulu. Njengamanje, ukwakhiwa kwe-altare kuyinto engukugcwaliseka kwemiyalelo eyanikezwa u-Mose (Dut. 11:26–30, Dut. 27:2–10).

Ku-Joshuwa 8:30-35 kunesimo esibalulekile sokukhombisa ukuthi umbhalo wonke kunomyalezo wokukholwa okulencwadi. Ngokuthi uxhumanise okunye okwabe kuhlasimulisa umzimba, nokuzindaba ezinodlame (lwezimpi) bese ukusuka lapho kuya esimweni esehluke kakhulu- isimo sokuvuma kabusha isivumelwano (okungukudumisa)-uJoshuwa usibuyisela emuva endaweni ebaluleke kakhulu ezintweni eziphathelene nokukholwa nezabhalwa encwadini kaJoshuwa ngasekuqaleni: UJoshuwa unegunya lokuhola u-Israyeli awuse empilweni yokuhlonipha isivumelwano (Joshuwa 1:7). Lokhu yisona sithombe sikaJoshuwa esivelayo ngasekupheleni kwalencwadi (Joshua 24).

Esingeke sikulibale ngukubaluleka kokwenzeka kwempi kanye nokunqoba, nalapho kukhona okuthile okubalulekile kakhulu: ukuthembeka ezintweni ezifunwa ngumthetho kaNkulunkulu. Ukunqoba kwabo kwakuyigxathu elibalulekile ekugcwalisekeni kohlelo lukaNkulunkulu ku-Israyeli futhi kuyindlela yokubuyisela konke kubantu. Ukuthembeka ukugcina imithetho ye-Torah kwakuphendula umbuzo obalulekile omayelana nekusasa lokuphila kwabantu. UJoshuwa waphinde waloba imithetho kaNkulunkulu phezu kwetshe elibanzi neligezekile labamhlophe, nokuyitshe elabe lehlukile kumatshe atholakala e-altare (qhathanisa lokhu naku-Duteronomi 27:2-8). Ngakho-ke, lamatshe, okungenzeka ayequkethe imiThetho Elishumi, aba yingxenye eyehlukile yesikhumbuzo esitholakala kwi-altare, nokuyinto eyeyilokhu ikhumbuza ama-Israyeli ngamathuba amahle kanye nemisebenzi eyashiwo esivumelwaneni.

UJoshuwa waba yibika leTestamente Elisha elino-Jehoshua (uJesu), omsebenzi wakhe, phakathi kokunye okuningi, kwabe kungukuholela abantu ukuba babuyele emuva ukulalela uNkulunkulu. Ukuze bakwazi ukufezekisa lenhloso, kwadingeka uJesu alwisane namandla obubi. Isiphetho salokhu akuhlosile uJesu kwabe kungukugcwaliseka kwalokhu okufunwa yisivumelwano kwenzelwa thina: “Ngokuba noma izithembiso zikaNkulunkulu ziziningi kangakanani, kuyena zinguyebo, ngakho-ke ngaye zingu-Amen futhi, kudunyiswe uNkulunkulu ngathi” (2 Cor. 1:20, NKJV).

`Yiziphi izinto zikamoya esingazenza namanje nezingaba nomsebenzi ofanayo nowokwakha i-altare elabe likhona esikhathini sasendulo na?`
