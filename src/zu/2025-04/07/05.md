title:  Kubhalwe Ematsheni
date:   12/11/2025
---

`Funda ku-Joshuwa 8:32-35. Kungabe yayisho ukuthini lemithetho echazwe emavesini asiwafundile, futhi ngabe lokhu kusho ukuthini kithina namuhla na?`

Intaba yase-Ebali kugcina ukukhulunywa ngayo encwadini ka-Duteronomi (Duteronomi 11:29; Duteronomi 27:4, 13), kuphinde kuvele nasencwadini kaJoshuwa (Joshuwa 8:30, 33). Lokhu kuyahambisana nokushiwo ngeNtaba yaseGerizimu, nokwakuyindawo lapho kwakuzolethwa izibusiso kanye neziqalekiso zesivumelwano okwakufuneka bakhulume ngazo. Ekakhulu, kwakufuneka bakusho ngendlela ka-Duteronomi 11:29 kanye no-Duteronomi 27:4, 13 nokuyindawo lapho kuneziqalekiso. Kulendawo ama-Israyeli kwakufuneka eme ngapha nangapha komphongolo, bekwenza lokhu kukhona nompristi (Joshuwa 8:33). Olunye uquqaba kwakufuneka lume ngaphambi kweNtaba i-Ebali, kanti olunye uquqaba lume ngaphambi kweNtaba i-Gerizimu. Kulendawo babenza umfuziselo wokubakhona kwezindlela ezimbili zokuxhumana nesivumelwano. Iminikelo eyalethwayo yabe ikhomba kuJesu, owazinikela ukutshatha emahlombe aKhe zonke iziqalekiso zesivumelwano, ukuthi abakholwayo kuYe bezokwazi ukujabulela izibusiso zesivumelwano (Gal. 3:13, 2 Kor. 5:21).

`Kungani kwakubalulekile ukuba kuphinde kulotshwe isivumelwano sesikhumbuzo nesasizokuba sobala kubona bonke abantu na? (Bheka ku Dut. 4:31; Dut. 6:12; Dut. 8:11, 14; 2 Amakhosi 17:38; Hub. 78:7.)`

Thina bantu sivamisile ukulibala. Sivame ukuminyanisa ngokudlulele izimfuno ezididanayo esihlangana nazo nsukuzonke zokuphila kwethu, nokuyizinto zesikhashana esikhathini esinaso. Sigcina ngokukhohlwa izinto eziphinde zivele ngezinga elifanayo noma zifike ngamawala. Njalo uma sinenkozo yesidlo, sithola ithuba elingavamile lokuvuselela ubudlelwano bethu noNkulunkulu, siphinde sivuselele nokuzinikela kusivumelwano sethu. Kuzokuba ngcono ukwamukela amathuba afana nalawa singagcini ngokukubuka njengethuba elibekelwe abantu abathile ukuba bazixhumanise kodwa makube yisikhathi sokuxhumana ekuvuseleleni ukuzinikela kwethu kuNkulunkulu. Emhlabeni ogcwele abantu abaziphilela ngabodwana emphakathini, kudingeka sikwazi ukuthola amandla okwazi ukuxhumana nomphakathi esiphila nawo, sikwazi ukwabelana ngalokhu okwenzeka emhlabeni, sabelane ngalokhu okuhle kanye nokukholwa, kanye nokusebenza ndawonye.

`Ukuthola kulula kangakanani, ukuthi esikhathini esigcwele ukuphithizela ekuphileni, ukuthi sikhohlwe nguNkulunkulu bese sizifunela izinto sisebenzisa amakhono kanye namandla ethu na? Kungani kuyinto elula ukwenza lokhu, ekakhulu uma izinto zikuhambela kahle na?`
