---
title:  Ukuphumula, Nokungaphezulu
date:  19/08/2021
---

UNkulunkulu uyazi ukuthi konke lokho kubaleka kumkhathazile uEliya. UNkulunkulu uyazi ukuthi ngaphezu kokukhathala emzimbeni, uEliya ukhathele emphefumulweni nokuthwala umthwalo osindayo kunembeza.  Njengoba uJesu wenza kulendoda efe uhlangothi eminyakeni eminingi ngemuva kwalokhu, uNkulunkulu ucisha bonke ububi obubhaliwe, anike uEliya ukuphumula.  Ekugcineni, usengathola ubuthongo bangempela, aqabuleke.

Singalindela ukuthi ukuphela kwendaba lokhu, kodwa cha, akunjalo. Ukuphumula kukaNkulunkulu akusona isehlakalo esenzeka kanye kuphela. Ukungena ekuphumuleni kukaNkulunkulu kuphathelene nokwelapheka—kancane, kancane, wehlukane nemicabango emibi nemikhuba elimazayo.  UNkulunkulu akaxhamazeli ngokwelapha.

`Funda: u 1 Amakhosi 19:5-8.  Uyaphi manje uEliya, ngasizathu sini?`

Ngemuva kokuphumula, uEliya uyagijima futhi. Kodwa manje uNkulunkulu uyakulungisa ukugijima kwakhe. UNkulunkulu uyazi ukuthi impilo lapha emhlabeni izoletha ukudangala. Uyazi ukuthi sifuna ukugijima, kodwa ufuna ukukuqondisa kwenye indawo ukugijima kwethu. Esikhundleni sawo wonke lamabhaxa alimazayo esiwazamayo, ufuna ukusifundisa ukulalela izwi “ elincane, elihashazayo” (1 Amakhosi 19:12) elizosinika ukuphumula.

UEliya wayengenamandla okuziphakamisa athathe uhambo lokuyohlangana noNkulunkulu. UNkulunkulu umnika amandla alowo mhlangano, athembise ngekusasa elingcono.

Njengoba uEliya ecambalele ngaphansi kwaleso sihlahla sonwele efisa ukufa, wakholwa ukuthi sekuphelile konke okungcono empilweni yakhe.

`Funda: 1 Amakhosi 19:15, 16 no 2 Amakhosi 2:11. Yini eyayisahlalele uEliya?`

UNkulunkulu wayazi ukuthi kuseza izinsuku ezingcono kuEliya.  Kwafika ukwelapheka kumprofethi njengoba wafunda ukulawula impilo yakhe ngokomgqigqo kaNkulunkulu, amukele ukuphumula kukaNkulunkulu.  Kwakusekhona amakhosi adinga ukugcotshwa, kukhethwe nozolandela uEliya.  UNkulunkulu wayesazi ngoElisha, owayefana nendodana kuEliya. UNkulunkulu wayazi ukuthi ngokukholwa, uEliya uyophinda enze umlilo wehle ezulwini (2 Amakhosi 1:10). UEliya wayengeke afe ekhathazekile, elele ngaphansi kwesihlahla sonwele, kodwa inqola yomlilo yayizomyisa ekuphumuleni kwasezulwini.

`Singafundani endabeni kaEliya, ukuthi kungani, noma ngabe sizizwa kabi kanjani, ngamandla kaNkulunkulu sisadinga ukungalahli ithemba?`
