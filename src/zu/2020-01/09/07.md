Njengoba sibona lapha, kukhona okufanayo kulezahluko. Akusikho nje kuphela ukuthi izizwe zitshengiswe zamataniswa, ukwahlulela kuDaniyeli 7—okuvela ngemuva kweminyaka engu 1 260 (A.D. 438-1798 B.C.) yokubusa kweRoma kaPhapha,ihambisana ngqo, nokuhlanzwa kwendawo engcwele, okuvela ngemuva kweRoma nakho ngokusho kuka Daniyeli 8. Ngamafuphi, lokhu kwahlulela okukuDaniyeli 7—ukwahlulela okuholela ekupheleni komhlaba—yinto efanayo nokuhlanzwa kwendlu engcwele kuDaniyeli 8. Lapha sinikwa izithombe ezimbili zento eyodwa, kanti zombili zenzeka ngemuva kweminyaka engu 1 260 yokushushiswa okwenziwa uphondo oluncane.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`1. Loluhla olungenhla lusitshengisa kanjani ukuthi ukuhlanzwa kwendlu engcwele, okuyinto efanayo nokwahlulela kuDaniyeli 7, kwenzeka ngemuva kwesiprofetho seminyaka engu 1 260 ngophondo oluncane, kodwa ngaphambi kokumiswa kombuso kaNkulunkulu ekugcineni ?`

`2. Isiprofetho sikaDaniyeli 8 sifanekisa umlando onodlame nogcwele ububi. Lezilwane (Dan. 8:8-12). Umbuso wophondo oluncane ophamuka ngemuva kwayo, ungumbuso onesihluku noshushisayo (Dan. 8:23-25). Kanjalo, iBhayibheli lapha alizami ukululaza iqiniso lokuhlushwa kulomhlaba. Lokho kufanele kusisize kanjani ukuthi sifunde ukwethemba uNkulunkulu nokulunga kwaKhe phakathi kobubi obusizungezile na?`
