---
title:  UJesu Uyakubona Ukukholwa Kwethu
date:   18/05/2026
---

`Qhathanisa indlela uJesu achaza ngayo ukukholwa kwabafundi baKhe ku-Marko 4:40 ukuqhathanise nokukholwa kowesifazane ku-Mathewu 15:2128.`

Ukuthi singabalandeli bakaJesu, lokho kakusho ukuthi ukukholwa kwethu kuqinile. Okuseqinisweni, abanye abantu bathi bayakholwa, kodwa uJesu waveza okungaphakathi kubona (Johane 2:23-25).

`Funda ku-Luka 7:1-10. Kungabe sifundani ngokukholwa kulendaba ekuleli vesi na?`

Ku-Marko 9, sifunda ngendoda eyeza kuJesu ukuze ezokwazi ukukhipha amadimoni endodaneni yayo, kodwa owakwazi ukulawula ukukholwa kokuthi, “ ‘Nkosi, ngiyakholwa, siza ukungakholwa kwami!’ ” (Marko 9:24, NKJV).

Kukho konke ukuhlangana okwenziwa nguJesu, waphawula ngokukholwa okukubantu, noma ukungakholwa abanakho, wenza izimangaliso ngenxa yokukholwa kwabo ukuze ezokwazi ukuqinisa ukukholwa abanakho.

Ngendlela efanayo, uMoya oNgcwele uyasivuselela ukuba sikholwe, kanti isitha esilwisana nomphefumulo sifaka umoya wokungabaza noma siqhelise ukubakhona kukaNkulunkulu ezimpilweni zethu. “Ukungakholwa nokuyinto engabakhona ngaphakathi kumuntu yiyonto enomthelela wokuba namandla okuthakathi umphefumulo. Imbewu yokuba nokungabaza ebilokhu itshalwa izothela izithelo zokungakholwa kodwa kudingeka abantu baqhubeke nokugubha bakhiphe impande yokungakholwa. Uma seziphumile lezimpande ezinobuthi bokungakholwa, azibe zisakwazi ukukhula ngoba azisatholi ukunakekelwa ngezenzo kanye namagama esiwakhulumayo. Lemiphefumulo kudingeka kuhlume kuyona isithombo esihle sokukholwa kanye nothando kufakwe emphefumulweni wenhliziyo bese wembathiswa ngaphakathi.”—Ellen G. White, Faith and Works, p. 17.

Uma sinokungabaza ngoNkulunkulu, nangesimilo saKhe, noma singabaza nangamazwi aKhe, senzani ngalokhu na? UNkulunkulu akanakho ukunganaki noma akhohlwe ngukucabanga komuntu, ngoba wasidala ngomfanekiso wakhe futhi uyasimema ukuba sixoxisane naYe, nokuyinto efanayo nalokhu akwenza ku-Abrahama, noMozisi, kanye noJobe. UNkulunkulu usimema ukuba sifunde ukusebenza ngaphansi kwendlela yakhe yokucabanga okubanzi, yize kwesinye isikhathi kuyodingeka sinikezele ezintweni esingenakho ukuziqonda ngokwaneleyo.

`Ake ucabange ngakho konke ukucabanga okunomqondo onakho kokukholwa. Ngesikhathi esifanayo, kungabe kusuke sekunjani lapho sekuphela ukukholwa kuphinde kungabi namqondo, kungabe kuseyinto ephathekayo nengukukholwa okwamukelekile, nokudingeka kuphakanyiswe na?`
