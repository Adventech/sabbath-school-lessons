### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Marko 8:11, 12; Mathewu 15:21-28; Luka 7:1-10; Efesu 2:8; Heb. 11; Isambulo 14:12.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kepha ukukholwa kungukuqiniseka ngezinto ezithenjwayo, kuyiqiniso ngezinto ezingabonwayo” (Heberu 11:1, NLT).

Omunye wake wathi, “Ukukholwa kuyefana ne-WiFi, ayibonakali, kodwa amandla ayo ayakwazi ukukuxhumanisa bese ubona lokhu ofuna ukukubona.” Akungabazeki ukuthi: ngaphandle kokukholwa, ngeke kubekhona ubudlelwano noNkulunkulu.

Kungabe ukukholwa kwakho kumi kanjani namuhla na? Kungabe ukukholwa kwakho kuNkulunkulu kuke kwaba ntekenteke na? Kungenzeka ukuthi kukhona into oyizwayo esiyenze wabhekana nezinkinga ngalendlela yokuthi awusazi ukuthi uzokuya kanjani phambili nobudlelwano onabo noNkulunkulu. Okunye futhi, kungabe ukukholwa kwakho sekufana nembali yerozi ekhula esiqwini esiluhlaza kodwa yona ikhule ibe yibhamuza elithile eligcina ngokuthi livuleke bese liba yimbali enhle, enombala ogqamile, likwazi nokugcwalisa indlu yonke ngephunga layo elimnandi nokungelula ukulikhohlwa na? Lokhu kuyefana nento eliqiniso ngezinto esingeke sikwazi ukuzibona (Hebheru 11:1). Lokhu kakufani nento esingakwazi ukuzisungulela yona ngokwethu, ngoba “uNkulunkulu wabela lowo nalowo isilinganiso sokukholwa” (Roma 12:3). Ukukholwa kuyisipho esivela kuNkulunkulu (Efesu 2:8, 9), futhi nangaleso sikhathi, ukukholwa kwethu kuNkulunkulu kubekhona ngenxa yalokhu asakwenza uNkulunkulu enzela thina.

Kuleliviki sizobheka isihloko esiphathelene nokukholwa: Sizokwenza njani ngokungabaza kanye nokungakholwa; ngokusho kukaJesu, kungabe ukukholwa okuqinile kuyinto enjani; nokuthi kusho ukuthini ukuba “nokukholwa kukaJesu.”

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNhlaba 23._
