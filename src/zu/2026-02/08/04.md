---
title:  Ukukholwa Akusiwo Umuzwa
date:   19/05/2026
---

UJesu wathi uma ninokukholwa okuncane njengembewu yesinaphi, niyokwazi ukugudluza izintaba (Mathewu 17:20). Uma usuke wabona imbewu yesinaphi, uzokuba nolwazi lokuthi luyinto encane kangakanani. Yize kungenzeka ube nokholo oluncane kangako, lungakwazi ukwenza umehluko omkhulu. Ngakho-ke ukukholwa kufuneka kube yinto ebalulekile nenamandla neqine ngokwanele ukuthi lukwazi ukwenza into engaphezulu kokwenza komuntu. Yize kunjalo, imbewu yesinaphu iyakwazi ukukhula ibe yisihlahla esikhulu (Mathewu 13:31, 32), ngokufanayo, ukukholwa kwethu kudingeka kukhule kungabi yinto emi ndawonye.

Kuliqiniso ukuthi, okokuqala kudingeka sikulinganise ukukholwa kwethu ukuze sizokwazi ukuba nobudlelwano noNkulunkulu. (Bheka ku-Roma 12:3).

`Kungabe incwadi yabaseFesu 2:8 isitshela yiphi indima edlalwa ngukukholwa ukuze sizokwazi ukusindiswa na? Kungani angeke azisholo umuntu ukuthi, “Anginakho ukukholwa ngoba uNkulunkulu akakanginiki ukukholwa” na?`

Kudingeka sibe nokuqonda ukuthi ukukholwa akusiyona into ephathekayo; kodwa kuyindlela umuntu asabela ngayo eqhutshwa nguMoya oNgcwele. Ukuba nomuzwa akusikho ukukholwa. Ukukholwa kungokwethu ukuba sikusebenzise, kodwa ukuba nomuzwa wokujabula kanye nomuzwa wokwamukela izibusiso esiziphiwa nguNkulunkulu kusuke kunguYe olethe umusa waKhe kuqala, ngokuthi asebenzise uMoya oNgcwele, ukuba ukwazi ukusidonsela kuYe uma simvumela ukuba akwenze lokhu kithi (Jeremiya 31:3). Sithola ukusindiswa ngokukholwa, nokuyindlela esamukela ngayo umusa kaNkulunkulu esiwutholile ngokufa kukaJesu. Sisindisiwe ngoba siyakholwa kuNkulunkulu ngenxa yomusa waKhe. Konke lokhu kusezinhliziyweni zethu ngoba sinobudlelwano naYe.

Okunye yilokhu, kufuneka sikhumbule ukuthi ukukholwa akusiwona umuzwa.“Baningi abangakusebenzisi ukukholwa kwabo nokuyinto eyilungelo kanye nomsebenzi wabo ukuba balusebenzise, kodwa izikhathi eziningi balindela ukuba nomuzwa nokuyinto esiyilethelwa ngukukholwa kuphela. Ukuba nomuzwa akusikhona ukukholwa. Ukukholwa kungokwethu ukuba sikusebenzise, kodwa ukuba nomuzwa wokujabula kanye nomuzwa wokubusiseka, konke lokhu sikuphiwa nguNkulunkulu.”— Early Writings, p. 72.

Abanye abantu bangaba nomuzwa wokuthi kabanakho ukukholwa ngoba kabazizwa besondelene noNkulunkulu noma kabasikho lokhu okufuneka babe yikhona njengamaKristu. Kodwa ukukholwa kuyinto ephethelene nokukholwa kanye nokuthemba uNkulunkulu, singamthembi kuphela ngezikhathi ezimnandi kodwa nangesikhathi sobumnyama kanye nezivunguvungu, noma nangesikhathi lapho ungenakho ukuqonda kahle okwenzekayo empilweni yaKho.

Umuzwa akufuneki siwuvumele ukuba ukhwele phezu kwamava okukholwa kwethu noma kulimaze ubudlelwano esinabo noNkulunkulu. Mhlawumbe ngalesisikhathi esisuke sicabanga ukuthi siqhelelene noNkulunkulu yingesikhathi lapho kudingeka sisebenzise ukukholwa kwethu bese simbiza Yena (njengoba kwenza ubaba ku- Marko 9:24).

`Funda kulamavesi alandelayo eBhayibhelini bese uwathatha njengokukholwa kwakho okuzokusiza kuqinise ubudlelwano bakho noNkulunkulu namhlanje: Heb. 12:1, 2; 2 Kron. 15:7; Rom. 3:23–26; Luka 7:50. Khuluma kakhulu kuzwakale emthandazweni wakho owubhekise kuNkulunkulu.`
