---
title:  Ukucabanga Okubanzi
date:   01/05/2026
---

Inhloso yokuba nesikhathi sokufunda iBhayibheli ngukuthi sifunde ukumazi uNkulunkulu nokuyinto ezokhulisa ubudlelwano bethu noNkulunkulu, ngokuba lokhu kungukuphila okuphakade- nokuyinto edinga sibe kuYena njengoba simthanda (Johane 5:39; Johane 17:3).

Ingxenye yanoma yibuphi ubudlelwano ngukuthi kubekhona ukuxoxisana nindawonye. Encwadini yeSambulo 3:20 sithola ukuthi uJesu ufuna ukuba sixoxisane kunye naYe, sikwenze lokhu sazi ukuthi singabantu abayizidalwa, njengoba singabantu abazidalwa, singalokhu sifunda okuningi ngoMdali wethu. Lokhu kuyefana nomuntu ogubhayo ezimayini elokhu efuna itshe eliyigugu, nathi kuyafuneka siqhubeke njalo silokhu siphenya okushiwo yiBhayibheli. Kuningi esingakuthola, noma ngabe sifunda kangakanani izahluko kanye nezindaba ezixoxwayo eBhayibhelini. Noma ngabe yini eyenziwa ngumuntu ukukhulisa ulwazi, lowomuntu akufuneki nangowodwa umzuzu acabange ukuthi kasikho isidingo sokuqhubeka abhekisise ukuhlola imibhalo ukuze ezokwazi ukuthola ukukhanyiseleka okungcono. Njengabantu, sibiziwe umuntu ngamunye ukuba sibe ngabafundi beziprofetho.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 41.

Akufuneki siphinde sizame ukwenza iBhayibheli likhulume lokhu okufiswa yimibono esinayo noma ukucabanga kwethu. “Kudingeka siyiphenye kanjani imiBhalo na? Kungabe kufuneka siqhube phambili izimfundiso zethu ngokulandela kwazo, bese sizama ukwenza imiBhalo ihambisane nendlela okufuneka ilandelwe yimibono yethu na? Noma kufuneka sithathe lokhu esikucabangayo nemibono yethu siyenze ihambisane nemiBhalo, bese sifaka esilinganisweni lokhu okuyimibono yethu siyifanise namaqiniso akhona emBhalweni na? Abantu abaningi abafunda baphinde bafundise iBhayibheli, abanakho ukuqonda kahle amaqiniso abalulekile ngalokhu abakufundisayo noma abakufundayo. . . Abantu abaningi bavamisile ukufaka amagama abo kulokhu okushiwo yimiBhalo nokuyizinto ezihambisana nemibono yab.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 36.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Kungabe uvame ukuza ngayiphi indlela ekufundeni iBhayibheli na? Kukhona yini into okufuneka uyiguqule na? Kungani ukufunda iBhayibheli unomoya wokuzehlisa nokuzinikela ezwini kuyinto ebalulekile na?`

`Kungabe kukhona yini imibono osuvele unayo nokungaba khona isidingo sokuba uyibeke eceleni ukuze uvumele imiBhalo ikwazi ukuzikhulumela yona ngokwayo na? Uma kunjalo, ungaqala kanjani ukukuthandazela lokhu njengamanje na?`

`Kungenzeka kanjani ukuthatha izinto njengoba zinjalo ukwenze kube ngumgoqo kumuntu ukuba akwazi ukubona ubudlelwane bakhe noNkulunkulu na? Lokhu kusho ukuthi, kungenzeka yini ukuba nesifiso sokuthola ulwazi oluthile olusha bese lokhu kumphushele kude umuntu kumenze aduke kakhulu, ekakhulu s ekwenza lokhu ukuze anelise lokhu okudingwa nguye na?`

**Ukugoqa Isifundo Saleliviki:** Ukuzinika isikhathi sokufunda iBhayibheli lokhu kungumsuka wokwazi ukuba nobudlelwano noNkulunkulu nokuyinto ephilayo nekwazi ukubekezela. Izwi likaNkulunkulu liyakhuluma ezimpilweni zabantu abaphila eminyakeni engamakhulu angamashumi amabili naye (21st Century) ngendlela ezwakalayo, nokuyinto esalokhu ifana nendlela ayekade ekhuluma ngayo emakhulwini eminyaka eseyadlula. Ngendlela efanayo yokwazi ukugcina ubungani obuphilile, kudingeka sifune izindlela ezokwenza sihlale njalo sinokuphila ngokuthandaza okuyikho njengoba sihlezi kuJesu, silokhu sinokuthemba izithembiso esizinikwa yizwi laKhe esithi “ ‘aliyikubuyela ze kimi, lingafezi lokhu engikufunayo, liphumelele kulokhu engilithumele kukho’ ” (Isa. 55:11, NKJV).
