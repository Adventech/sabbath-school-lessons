`UJesu uyisibonelo esihle kuzona zonke izinto, futhi uma kucatshangwa ngomuntu othola isikhathi sokwenza umkhuleko ekwenza yedwa, nalokhu akuhlukile. Kungabe uMarko 1:35 usitshela ukuthini ngesikhathi uJesu ayezinika sona sokuba noNkulunkulu na?`

Yize kuyivesi elilodwa, kuningi esingakufunda kulona ngokuthi sithole ukubonela kuJesu. Ekuseni kakhulu lingakaphumi ilanga, wayephuma (asuke kubo bonke ubuphithiphithi namanangananga okuphila kwansuku zonke), azitholele indawo lapho ezokuba yedwa khona, nokuyindawo ethule ukuze ezokwazi ukuba ndawonye noBaba waKhe. Ungasicabanga isimo esinjena – uJesu, ehlezi ngasolwandle lwaseGalile noma ehlezi eceleni kwentaba, ethandaza futhi enokuxhumana noBaba waKhe ngapha mbi kokuba umhlaba wonke oseduze naye kubekhona okwenzeka kuwo na? Yize lelivesi lisichazela ngokuba matasa kukaJesu ethandaza, kodwa siyakwazi ukubona kahle ukuthi lokhu kwabe kuyinto ehamba phambili ekwenzeni kwaKhe. Akungabazeki ukuthi lesisikhathi yisona esasimnika amandla okubhekana nazo zonke izinkinga azohlangana nazo. Uma kungukuthi uJesu wayekudinga ukuqala usuku ngalendlela, kangakanani kithi ukuba senze okufanayo na?

UNkulunkulu usitshela ukuthi, “ ‘Funani ubuso bami,’ ” futhi unethemba lokhu imendulo yethu izothi “ ‘Ubuso bakho, Jehova, ngiyabufuna’ ” (iHubo 27:8, NKJV).

`Incwadi ka-1 IziKronike 16:11 ithi singabufuna kanjani ubuso bakaNkulunkulu na?`

Kungabe unayo yini indawo okwazi ukuya kuyona njalo ekuseni uthole ithuba lokuba noNkulunkulu na? Mhlawumbe ungazitholela isihlalo esiseduze kwefasitela lakho, noma indawo ethulile engaphandle, noma itafula elisekhishini, lapho uzokwazi ukusondela khona nsuku zonke ukwazi ukuhlala ezinyaweni zikaJesu uthole ukufunda Izwi likaNkulunkulu. Ukuhlala ezinyaweni zikaJesu kungaba yindawo ekahle impela ongaba kuyona (Luka 10:39-42). Ngokuthi kubekhoba umkhuba owenza nsuku zonke wokuthi kubekhona indawo oya kuyona lapho uzochitha isikhathi noNkulunkulu, lokhu ungakwenza zonke izinsuku. Ungavumeli ukuphela amandla uma kwenzekile kwabakhona usuku lapho ungaphumeleli khona, ngoba kuyenzeka kuqhamuke izimo eziphuthumayo, ezizokwenza ungasitholi isikhathi ozokwazi ukusichitha noNkulunkulu. Kungakuhle uzame ukuthi kungadluli izinsuku, noma amaviki, noma izinyanga eziningi ungazange uchithe isikhathi noNkulunkulu. Khumbula ukuthi ukubakhona kobudlelwano bakho noNkulunkulu kuyisinqumo osithatha nsuku zonke, umkhuba ongaphinde uqale ukuwenza futhi, nokuyinto ongayenza nanamuhla uma ukhetha ukukwenza lokho.

`Evikini elidlule, kungabe uzinike isikhathi esingakanani sokuthandaza nokufunda iBhayibheli na? Kungabe impendulo onayo ikutshela ukuthini mayelana nosekufuneka uqale ukukwenza nokuyinto esizohamba phambili ezintweni ozenzayo na?`
