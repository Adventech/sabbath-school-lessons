---
title:  Isibusiso Esiphindaphindiwe
date:   29/04/2026
---

Ziningi izindlela esingafunda ngazo iBhayibheli, ukufunda ngokuthi ufunde ivesi nevesi. (nokuyindlela osekukhulunyiwe ngayo), ukufunda isahluko, ufunde isihloko esithile, ufunde nencazelo yegama, noma ufunde incwadi. Singafunda ngokubheka ukuvumelana kwezinto nangokusebenzisa isichaza-mazwi esikhona seBhayibheli, singalifunda iBhayibheli sikuxube ndawonye nezigameko ezenzeka eminyakeni eyadlula nokuyinto engaletha ulwazi olungezelelwe. Singathatha uhambo lokushaywa ngumoya sibuke imvelo siphinde silalele ukufundelwa iBhayibheli ngabanye abantu befundela thina, noma sihlangane nomngani noma idlanzana elincane sifunde ndawonye.

Ngalendlela siyakwazi ukugcina isimo sokuzwana nabangani bethu ngezindlela eziningi kanye nangamava amasha esihlangana nawo. Kudingeka sigcine njalo ukuxhumana kwethu noNkulunkulu kukusha futhi kuyinto ejabulisayo ngokuthi sisebenzise izindlela eziningi zokufunda iBhayibheli. Kuyohlala njalo kukhona okuningi esingakufunda!

Enye yezinto engasiza ukwenza sihlale sifunda iBhayibheli ngukuthi kubekhona ukwabelana nabanye abantu ngalokho esithe sakuthola. Uma sibachazela ngalokhu esikufundile, sikwazi ukugoqa inkulumo siyenze ibe mfishane, sikuhlaziye lokhu esikufundile bese sikuhlanganisa nalokhu esesikwazi. Konke lokhu kungasiza sikwazi ukuhlala sikhumbula lonke ulwazi esilufundile. Isibusiso esiphindaphindiwe ngukuthi sabelane siphinde sixoxisane nabanye abantu, ukuxoxisana ngezikamoya kuyohlala njalo kuyinselelo kuphinde kukwazi ukuniqinisa nina nobabili. Njalo uma sabelana noma sifundisa abanye kudala isimo sokufunda ngokujulile nokuzokwenza sicabange kahle.

Sizokwazi ukuphinde sibone ukuthi lokhu esikufundayo usuku nosuku akugcini ngokuba umyalezo kaNkulunkulu owenzelwe wena kuphela kodwa lomyalezo wenzelwe nabanye.

`Funda ku-Isaya 50:4. Kungabe lelivesi lisitshela ukuthini thina mayelana nokuba khona kobudlelwano noNkulunkulu nokuthi kungathinta kanjani ubudlelwano bethu nabanye abantu na?`

Ukuba nesikhathi sokufunda iBhayibheli komuntu ngamunye akugcini ngokuqinisa thina kodwa kusisiza ukuba sikwazi nokulekelela abanye ukuba bakwazi ukuhlangana ngolunye usuku. Uma kwenzeka into efana nalena iphenduka ibe yisibusiso esiphindaphindiwe.

Impilo yethu yezikaMoya iyefana nomncintiswano wokugijima. Cela uNkulunkulu ukuba akwazi ukukunika amandla okugijima ungakhathali, ulokhu ugqolozele umklomelo ozowuzuza ekugcineni (Filiphu 3:14). Ungazizwa uphelelwa ngamandla uma kungukuthi ube nakho ukudembesela okwesikhashana, kodwa zama ukwenza izinguquko ezifanele ekuphileni kwakho nokuzokwenza ukwazi ukugcina ubudlelwano bakho noNkulunkulu, usebenzise ikakhulu iBhayibheli kanye nesikhathi sokukhuleka. Ngokweqiniso, lokhu kungukuphila okuphakade, okokuthi sazi uNkulunkulu kusukela namuhla (Johane 17:3). Ukuzimisela kwansuku zonke kokuhlala kuNkulunkulu kanye nasezwini laKhe yikhona okungaguqula izimpilo zethu.

`Funda indimana yenkumbulo yaleliviki bese ubhekisisa ukuthi ngabe isho ukuthini. Kungabe ufundani njengamanje na? Uzimisele ukwabelana nobani lokhu okufundile na?`
