---
title:  Ukufunda IBhayibheli Okujulile
date:   28/04/2026
---

Noma ngabe awusona isifundiswa, ungenza kanjani ukuze ukwazi ukufunda iBhayibheli ngokujulile na?

**Thandaza:** Akulula ukungagcizeleli ukubaluleka komkhuleko njengoba ulokhu ufunda (uphinde ube nezindawo ozibekisayo) eBhayibhelini lakho ngesikhathi ulifunda. U-Ellen G. White usitshela ukuthi uma sizofunda iBhayibheli, sisuke singesodwa. Kudingeka simeme ukubakhona kukaMoya oNgcwele kube yiwona osikhombisayo, bese siphebeza konke okunye okungahle kusiphazamise, nokuzokwenza sibaleke isitha. “Akufuneki uqale ukufunda iBhayibheli ungazange uwenze umkhuleko. UMoya oNgcwele uwodwa ungasenza sibone ukubaluleka kwezinto esithe saziqonda kalula, noma usivimbele ukubhekana namaqiniso okuzoba lukhuni ukuwaqonda.”—The Great Controversy, pp. 599, 600.

**Funda uphinde Ubhale:** Singasho ukuthi umehluko phakathi kokufunda iBhayibheli okujwayelekile, ukuqhathanisa nokulifunda ngenhloso yokulazi, konke lokhu kugcina kufinyelele ekwenzeni into eyodwa: okungukubhala. Ukubhala kuyasiza ukwenza umuntu acabange agxile, abhekisise Izwi likaNkulunkulu, abe nokuzindla ngalokhu akufundayo, akuhlaziye, kubekhona akwenzayo, kube khona nokuzinikela kulokhu okwenzekayo. Lokhu kungasiza ukuqoqa ukucabanga kwethu obekukade kuhlakazekile ukuze kukwazi ukucabanga ngokuyikho, sigcine sikubhale kahle kwahleleka, bese kungena ezinhliyweni zethu ngaso lesosikhathi. Futhi kungenzeka sikhumbule olunye ulwazi esike salubhala phansi (iHubo 119:15,16). Uma kungukuthi awukwazi ukubhala phansi, ake uzame ukufunda ngokuthi ukhulume kuzwakale ngesikhathi ufunda (noma ulalele lokhu okufundwayo), konke lokhu kungakuhle kulandelwe wukucabanga okujulile njengoba ulokhu uqhubeka ukhuleka kuNkulunkulu.

**Yabelana:** Tshela omunye umuntu lokhu okufundile. Lokhu kuzokwenza ukuthi lokhu osukufundile kugxile engqondweni yakho kuphinde kuvuselele omunye umuntu.

Khetha incwadi emfishane ekhona eBhayibhelini uma usaqala ukufunda (njengokufunda incwadi kaJona, noMarko, nabaseFiliphu, noma 1 Johane), uye ngokuya ukhulisa ukufunda kwakho iBhayibheli. Nansi indlela elula ongayisebenzisa yokufunda ivesi elilodwa (wukuba nendlela yokufunda ivesi ngalinye), isihloko esithile noma isahluko sonke:

1. Thandaza ukuze uMoya oNgcwele uzokwazi ukulawula ukucabanga kwakho bese uthambisa inhliziyo yakho njengoba ulokhu ufunda.
2. Zikhethele ivesi noma isahluko.
3. Bhala phansi ebhukwini noma indawo ethile kusahluko osithandile njengoba uqhubeka nokufunda.
4. Phelekezela ngomthandazo kukhona konke okufundayo bese udwebela umqondo othe wawuzuza.
5. Bhala phansi umqondo owutholile nokwazile ukuwudwebela.
6. Konke lokhu okubhale phansi okusemqoka, zama ukubheka ukuthi kubuthinta kanjani ubudlelwano onabo noNkulunkulu.
7. Ake ucabange ukuthi lokhu okufundile ungabelana nobani ngakho namuhla.

“Ngaso sonke isikhathi uma abantwana bakaNkulunkulu beya ngokukhula emseni waKhe, bazokwazi ukuhlala bethola ukuqonda kangcono Izwi laKhe. Bazokwazi ukuba nokukhanyiseleka ngobuhle bamaqiniso aKhe afihlakele. Isimo esifana nalesi besilokhu siliqiniso kuwona wonke umlando webandla owenzeka eminyakeni eseyadlula, futhi lokhu kusazoqhubeka kuze kube sekupheleni.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, pp. 38, 39.

`Kungabe lukusiza kuphi ulwazi oluyisicaphuno esingenhla nokuthi ngabe lokhu kuzwakala kuliqiniso na? Funda futhi naku- ihubo 119:105.`
