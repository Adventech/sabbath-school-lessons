---
title:  Isikhathi
date:   26/04/2026
---

Kungabe usuke walicupha iwashi lakho ukuba likhale ngaphambi kwesikhathi osijwayele ukuze lizokwazi ukukuvusa bese uthola isikhathi sokufunda iBhayibheli lakho na? Usuke waba nobunzima bokuzithola uzihudula wehla embhedeni ubuke isikhathi lapho uzobona ukuthi, unemizuzu eyishumi nanhlanu ngaphambi kokuba uqale imisebenzi yosuku. Kungakuhle ngisheshe! Usuke wazithola usesimweni sokuba wenze umthandazo omfishane noma uphenye isahluko, futhi nonembeza wakho ukujabulela lokhu kodwa inhliziyo yakho inganelisekile ngomphumela ozowuthola ngaphambi kokuba uphuthume ukwenza imisebenzi yosuku na?

“Kungabe kukhona yini okuncane esikuzuzayo uma sifunda imiBhalo sijahile na? Omunye angalifunda lonke iBhayibheli aze ayofika ekugcineni kodwa ehluleke ukubona ubuhle balokhu akufundayo noma angakwazi ukuqonda kahle izinto ezijulile zalokhu okufihlakele kulona.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, p. 90.

Yize kungaba khona izibusiso esizithola ngokuthi sifunde iBhayibheli- okufana nokuphuza kumpompi wesicisha-mlilo (lapho amanzi efika mawala futhi emaningi)- kungenzeka ufunde iBhayibheli ulihwaphuluze ngokushesha kodwa kubekuningi ongeke ukuzwe kahle. UNkulunkulu usinike Izwi laKhe eliphefumulelwe, Izwi eliyigugu, nesizokwazi ukulifunda ukuze sizokuba nokuqonda izinto eziningi ngoNkulunkulu (futhi ngesikhathi esifanayo, siphinde sifunde kabanzi nangathi uqobo). Uma sizinika isikhathi sokwazi ukubona izinto ezingachazeki, kanye nesimilo esihle sikaNkulunkulu, siphinde sibone izindlela akwazi ukuzisebenzisa ukuxhumana nabantu kuwona wonke umlando seloku kwaba khona umhlaba, lokhu kungasenza simthande kakhulu Yena. Indlela uNkulunkulu axhumana ngayo nathi isezandleni zethu- kodwa kudingeka sithole ithuba, sizinike isikhathi sokumazi uNkulunkulu ngokuthi sifunde Izwi laKhe (Izenzo 17:11). Ake ubheke lokhu okuhlongozwe ngezansi:

Cela uNkulunkulu ukuba afake enhliziyweni yakho umuzwa wokumfisa Yena. Sifune lesi sithembiso ku- Jeremiya 29:13 naku iHubo 37:4. Mmeme Yena ukuba asheshe akuvuse ngaphambi kokufika kwesikhathi ojwayele ukuvuka ngaso noma uzibekele isikhathi osukwini lapho uzokwazi ukuthola ithuba lokukhuluma naYe.

Nikela isikhathi sakho kuNkulunkulu. Yebo, kungenzeka ukuthi uxakekile, futhi kunezinto eziningi okufuneka uphuthume ukuzenza. Kodwa ukuthola isikhathi noNkulunkulu kuyinto ebaluleke kakhulu. Hamba uye endaweni ethule lapho uzokuba wedwa khona bese ufunda iHubo 46:10. Funda lamazwi noma ucule leliculo elithi “Nginikela Konke.” Zindla kabanzi ngezinto ongazimisele ukuzidela bese uzenza umnikelo kuNkulunkulu.

Chitha isikhathi noNkulunkulu noma ngabe awuzizwa kahle ukwenze lokhu. Njengoba kufuneka uthathe isinqumo esiqwashileyo nalapho kuzobakhona uhlelo ozolulandela noluzokwenza uphile kahle (njengokuzivocavoca, nokudla kahle), konke lokhu kudinga unembeza ozokwazi ukuzikhethela ukuba nobudlelwano ubuzokwenza usondele kuNkulunkulu. Khumbula ukuthi imikhuba emisha ingamthatha umuntu izinsuku ezingu- 21 ngaphambi kokuba ajwayele, futhi angeke uphumelele ngaphandle kokuthi uthole uMoya oNgcwele ukuba ukusize.

`Funda futhi ku-Johane 15:1-8. Kungabe uJesu usitshela ukuthini mayelana nokuhlala kuYe, futhi kungabe lokhu kubaluleke kangakanani ekukholweni kwethu na?`
