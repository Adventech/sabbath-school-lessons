---
title:  UNkulunkulu UNgcwele
date:   06/04/2026
---

Ukuba ngcwele akulona igama abantu abavame ukulisebenzisa uma bekhuluma nsuku zonke, mhlawumbe ngenxa yokuthi zimbalwa kabi izinto esithi zingcwele ezintweni eziseduzane nathi. ISabatha kungusuku olungcwele, noNkulunkulu naYe ngokunjalo ungcwele. Impilo yansuku zonke esiphila kuyo iyashoda ngokuba ngcwele.

Uma ufunda kabanzi ngezinto eziphathelene nesimilo sikaNkulunkulu, uzothola ukuthi ukuba ngcwele yiyonanto engumsuka nechazayo ukuthi uNkulunkulu uyini. Kungabe lokhu kusho ukuthini na

`Kungabe lamavesi alandelayo amchaza kanjani uNkulunkulu na? Levitikusi 20:26, 1 Samuweli 2:2; Isaya 57:15, naku Hezekiya 38:23.`

Uma iBhayibheli lichaza uNkulunkulu njengento engcwele, lokhu kusho ukuthi leyonto ayinachashaza lobubi kanye nesono. UNkulunkulu muhle ngokuphelele kusuka ekuqaleni kuze kube sekupheleni. Ngenxa yalokhu kucabanga, ubungcwele bukaNkulunkulu buyisisekelo sakho konke okungahle kwaziwe ngaYe.

Isibonelo, uthando lukaNkulunkulu lumsulwa, lunguthando olungcwele – uthando olungenaso isici sokuzicabangela kanye nolungenawo umoya wokuzifunela okwalo. Ukwazi kwakhe zonke izinto kuyinto engcwele yokwazi konke, nokuchaza ukuthi kukhululekile ekwenzeni izinto ezimbi. Ungamethemba yini uNkulunkulu owazi konke uma kungukuthi lowoNkulunkulu akanabo ubungcwele na? Okunalokho, singamethemba, futhi kube kufanele sikwenze lokho, ngoba simesaba loNkulunkulu.

Ukuba ngumnini mandla (okungukuba namandla kakhulu) kungukuba ngumninimandla oNgcwele. Ake ucabange ngoNkulunkulu onguMninimandla onke kodwa ongekho ngcwele. Angaba namandla, angaba ngumcindezeli ononya. NgubuNgcwele bukaNkulunkulu obuvumela buphinde busenze ukuba simthande ngempela, ngoba unguNkulunkulu omuhle kusuka ekuqaleni kuze kube sekugcineni. Kungakho ubungcwele mhlawumbe buyisimilo esibaluleke kakhulu ukuze sizokuba nolwazi ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu. Noma kunjalo, ubungcwele buyisimilo esingaqondisiswa kahle naso.

Ake ucabange ngezimilo zalabantu abalandelayo abenjengo Mozisi, u-Isaya, u-Hezekiya, u-Daniyeli kanye noJohane, abakwazi ukufinyelela lapho kwakukhona uNkulunkulu. Kungabe bakwamukela kanjani lokhu kuhlangana noNkulunkulu na? Bakhumula izimbadada zabo, becashisa ubuso babo, noma bawela phansi kwangazathi bafile. Njengoba singabantu, siyizoni kungakho singekho ngcwele ngalendlela yokuthi angeke sikwazi ukumelana nobukhona bukaNkulunkulu phambi kwethu. Noma ngumuphi umuntu ongakwazi ukubhekana nobuso bukaNkulunkulu angeke aphile. Ngokufanayo, ngesikhathi u-Ellen G. White eba sembonweni, izikhathi eziningi wayekhala athi, “uNgcwele, uNgcwele, uNgcwele,” ngoba yigama elilodwa elalibonakala likwazi ukubamba lokhu ayekubona. Kanjalo, ngokufanayo, izidalwa ezine ezabe ziphila kazizange ziphumule imini noma ubusuku zingazange zilibale ukuthi, “iNgcwele, iNgcwele, iNgcwele, iNkosi uNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke, owayekhona nokhona nozayo” (Isambulo 4:8).

`Ngempela, uNkulunkulu ungcwele ngokuphelele, futhi uma siza kuye kudingeka simbone ngalendlela. Kungabe ukwazi lokhu kukuvuselela kanjani wena na? Kungabe lokhu kukulethela yiphi inselelo mayelana nokuziphatha kwakho na?`
