---
title:  U-Emanuweli, UNkulunkulu Unathi
date:   09/04/2026
---

Uma bekudingeka wabelane nabanye abantu abangewona amaKrestu, bese kudingeke uchaze ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu ususela kulokhu okushiwo yiBhayibheli, kungabe ubuzoqala ngokuthini na?

Impendulo engaba ngcono, ngokujwayelekile, kungaba nguJesu. IBhayibheli lithi uJesu akagcinanga ngokuba yisibuko sokubona uNkulunkulu kodwa waphinde waveza ukuthi uNkulunkulu ungubani. Maningi amavesi aseBhayibhelini akuchazayo lokhu, kodwa ivesi elikuchaza kahle lokhu litholakala ku- Johane 14:9. Kulelivesi uJesu uthi, “Ongibonile mina ubonile uBaba.” Ukuze sazi kabanzi ngoNkulunkulu, kusho ukuthi kufuneka sibheke kuJesu – amazwi awakhulumayo, izenzo zaKhe, indlela enza ngayo, kanye nendlela akhombisa ngayo uthando lwakhe kubantu ngokufa kanye nokuvuka kwaKhe.

Uthando kanye nokunakekela kukaBaba kuyinto evezwe yacaca kahle ngokuthi sibheke iNdodana yaKhe enguJesu. Ubuhle beBhayibheli ngukuthi uNkulunkulu usinike izindlela ezine ezinothile nokuyizo ezikwazi ukuveza impilo kaJesu ezokwenza sikwazi ukuba nesithombe esicacile sokumazi ukuthi ungubani Yena. Encwadini kaMathewu (ebhalwe nguMjuda ebhalela amanye amaJuda), sibona uJesu njengoMesiya obekuyisikhathi eside elindelwe nokwaba ngukugcwaliseka kwalokho okwakuthenjisiwe. Encwadini ka-Marko, sibona uJesu ephila impilo yokuba semsebenzini kanye nokuzinikela kwakhe. Ngaso sonke isikhathi ubelokhu ecabangela abanye abantu aphinde amele lokhu okuyintando kaBaba waKhe. Encwadini ka-Luka, sifunda ngendlela uJesu azizwa ngayo, esesimweni sokuba ngumuntu kanye nozwelo ayenalo ekuhluphekeni kwabantu, futhi singafunda ngalesi sigameko ukuze sibe nesiqiniseko sokuthi lokhu esikufundayo kuliqiniso (Luka 1:3, 4). Encwadini ka-Johane, kulesi sahluko sibona iNdodana kaNkulunkulu egqokiswe ukuba ngumuntu, bese simenywa ukuba sikholwe ngukuthi lokhu uJesu asitshela khona ukuthi uyikho, ukuze kukwazi ukuvuselela ukuphila kwethu kwezikamoya. Yize sikwazi ukuhlola lamavangeli amane elokhu akhuluma ngento eyodwa, “kodwa awavezi into eyodwa ngendlela efanayo. Umbhali ngamunye uveze ngendlela abona ngayo, kanti lokhu kukhuluma ngokuhlukana yikhona okwenza sibe nokwazi okujulile nokulethwa kithini ukuze kukwazi ukwenelisa ukucabanga kwethu okuhlukene.” —Ellen G. White manuscript 105, 1900.

Kungabe yiliphi ivangeli ozinike isikhathi sokulifunda kulezinsuku ezedlule na?

`Ku Mathewu 1:23, kukhona igama elikhethekile elinikezwe uJesu. Kungani lokhu kuyinto ebalulekile ukuze sizokwazi ukuqonda ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu na? Funda ku-Mathewu 28:20, ugxile kakhulu ngasekugcineni kwevesi. Qhathanisa lamavesi amabili. Kungabe yini oyiphawulayo ngalamavesi na?`

Sisathinte phezulu kulesi sihloko esibanzi kangaka, sokwazi ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu. UNkulunkulu mkhulu kakhulu futhi ubukhulu bakhe bungaphezulu kokucabanga kwethu, kungakho siyohlala njalo sifunda ngaYe kuze kube phakade.

`UNkulunkulu kufanele simdumise ngenxa yalokhu ayikona kanye nalokhu athe wasenzela khona ekuphileni kwethu.Ake uthathe isikhathi sakho manje wenze umthandazo wokudumisa uNkulunkulu ngenxa yalokhu ayikona. Zama ukusho izinto iBhayibheli elikutshela ukuthi uNkulunkulu uyizona (Isibonelo “Ngiyabona Nkulunkulu, ngokuba wena unguNkulunkulu o_______, njengoba ungitshela mina e_________.”)`
