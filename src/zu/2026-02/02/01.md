### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Genesise 3:1-5; Levitikusi 20:26; 1 Samuweli 2:2; 1 Johane 4:7-19; Genesise 1;1; Genesise 2:7; Mathewu 1:23; Mathewu 28:20.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Ukuphila okuphakade yilokhu ukuba bazi wena Nkulunkulu wedwa oqinisileyo, nomthumileyo, uJesu Kristu’ ” (Johane 17:3, NKJV).

Ukuba nokuqonda kangcono ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu yisona sisekelo esimqoka ekubeni nobudlelwano obuqinile noNkulunkulu. Kungakho kuleliviki sizobheka ngokucophelela ukuthi ngabe iBhayibheli lithini mayelana nesimilo sikaNkulunkulu, silokhu sibeke emqondweni wethu ukuthi “ngubumnyama bokungaqondisisi kahle ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu nokuyinto esigubazela umhlaba wonke. Abantu balahlekelwa ngulwazi lokuqonda ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu. Besilokhu singaqondakali futhi kunokuhlanekezela ukusiqonda ngokuyikho. Kulesi sikhathi umyalezo ovela kuNkulunkulu kudingeka waziswe, umyalezo oqhamuka kumthelela kanye nasemandleni okusindisa. Isimilo saKhe kudingeka saziwe ngabantu bonke. Kulobu bumnyama obusezweni kudingeka kulethwe ukukhanya kwenkazimulo yaKhe, ukukhanya kokulunga, nomusa, neqiniso laKhe. Umlayezo wokugcina kudingeka usiwe ezweni lonke, lokhu kungukwembulwa kwesimilo saKhe sothando.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 415.

Kubonakala kuyinto elukhuni ukuchaza ngesimilo sikaNkunkulu ngokwanele,ngakho-ke into esinokuyenza ngukuba sibheke okushiwo yiBhayibheli ngaYe. Yize singeke sibe nolwazi, okukhulu kulesi sikhathi, kodwa kuningi esingakuqonda mayelana nesimilo esimangalisayo sikaNkulunkulu, kufuneka sithandaze njengoba silokhu sifunda ngaYe, ukuqonda kanye nothando lwethu ngaYe kuzokuba yinto ejulile, ukuze ekugcineni, sizobona sikudinga ukusondela kakhulu kuYena ukuze sizokwazi ukuveza uthando lwaKhe kanye nesimilo saKhe nakwabanye abantu.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha,UMbasa 11._
