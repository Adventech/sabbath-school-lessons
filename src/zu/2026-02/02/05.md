---
title:  UNkulunkulu Ekudaleni
date:   08/04/2026
---

Kungenzeka ukuthi amagama okuqala eBhayibheli uwazi ngekhanda lapho lithi: “Ekuqaleni uNkulunkulu.” NgesiHebheru, igama elithi uNkulunkulu kuthiwa (Elohim). Yize leligama liphinde lisetshenziswe uma kukhulunywa “ngonkulunkulu” bamanga, kodwa uma lisho uNkulunkulu weqiniso, lisuke lichaza ongusomandla, onguMdali omkhulu kakhulu nowadala konke okukhona ezweni; Lona nguNkulunkulu ongaphezu kokuqonda komuntu nowazi ukulawula konke okukhona ezweni. UnguMdali onamandla ngalendlela yokuthi uma ekhuluma, kukhona into edalekayo ivele ibekhona ngokuthi azebenzise izwi laKhe kuphela.

Kodwa esahlukweni esilandelayo, ku-Genesise 2, lapho kuvela elinye igama likaNkulunkulu elithi: Yahweh. Leligama liyaxhumana negama elithi (Yahweh Elohim), nokusho into eyodwa yokuthi ungu Somandla omkhulu kakhulu; kodwa igama lika-Yahwey kuligama lomuntu abizwa ngalo, nokuyigama likaNkulunkulu weqiniso elivamise ukusetshenziswa uma kugcizelelwa ukuthi uNkulunkulu unguNkulunkulu wesivumelwano, osebudlelwaneni bothando kunye nabantu bakhe abadalileyo.

`Qhathanisa ukuchazwa kukaNkulunkulu okutholakala ku-Genesise 1:1 kanye naku Genesise 2:7. Kungabe yini oyiphawulayo kulamavesi na?`

Encwadini ka-Genesise 2:7 sinesithombe sikaNkulunkulu eguqa ngesikhathi ebumba umuntu wokuqala emenza ngobumba aluthathe emhlabathini, embumba ngezandla zakhe. “UJehova uNkulunkulu wambumba umuntu ngomhlabathi, wamphefumulela emakhaleni akhe umoya wokuphila, kanjalo umuntu waba umphefumulo ophilayo” (Genesise 2:7). Lona nguNkulunkulu ubonakala esondele kakhulu kumuntu ngalendlela yokuthi waze waphefumulela emakhaleni ka-Adamu umphefumulo wokuphila. Leligama lika-Yahweh, liveza isithombe sikaNkulunkulu osondele kakhulu, kodwa uMozisi uwasebenzisa womabili lamaGama futhi ewasebenzisa kuzona zombili izahluko eziseBhayibhelini ukuze ezokwazi ukusichazela ngazo zombili izimilo zikaNkulunkulu.

Okumangalisayo yilokhu! Lapha sibona uNkulunkulu ezehlisa esondela kithi engu Elohim, kwathi ukuqhamuka kwakhe, eba seduzane nathi, wase eba ngu- Yahweh. Kuhle kangakanani ukuba sibone lezinhlangothi zombili eziyisimilo sikaNkulunkulu: Ubukhulu bakhe bokwazi ukulawula zonke izinto bese eba seduzane nathi. Njengoba kwasho uPawula ngesikhathi esentabeni i-Mars yase Athenians wathi: “ ‘ingani kakude kulowo nalowo kithi. Ngokuba siphila, sihamba, sikhona kuye’ ” (Izenzo 17:27, 28, NKJV).

Kubalulekile ukuthi silokhu siqhubeka sifuna isithombe okuyiso sikaNkulunkulu khona sizokwazi ukukhanyiseleka ngalokhu esikutshelwa yiBhayibheli uma likhuluma ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu ukuze buzokhula ubudlelwano bethu noNkulunkulu. Kungakho kubalulekile ukuba sifunde yonke into eshiwo yiBhayibheli kunokuba sigxile kuphela endaweni eyodwa yeBhayibheli. Kuliqiniso ukuthi uma silokhu sifunda kabanzi ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu, sizokuya njalo sikhula ekumthandeni Yena.

`Funda incazelo ka-Elihu ngalokhu okushiwo ngoNkulunkulu ku-Jobe 36:24-33 naku Jobe 38 no 39. Kungabe lezizahluko ozifundile zisikhombisani mayelana noNkulunkulu na?`
