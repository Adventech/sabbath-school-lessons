---
title:  Isithombe Esithe Ukucaca SikaNkulunkulu
date:   05/04/2026
---

IBhayibheli lisinika isithombe sikaNkulunkulu esiliqiniso, nesicacile, kanye nesihlala sinjalo. Lilonke iBhayibheli lizama ukwembula iveyili engemuva nengakaze ibonwe ephakathi komhlaba kanye noNkulunkulu ongabonakali; lilokhu lizama ukusikhombisa lapho sisuka khona liphinde likhombe lapho siphikelele khona; bese kuthi ekugcineni, lisikhombise ukuthi ngubani olawula konke lokhu nokuthi ungumuntu onjani.

Kusuka ku-Genesise kuze kufike ku-Sambulo, silokhu sifunda ngoNkulunkulu oyedwa weqiniso, okwazi ukuzembula kithi ngokuthi simfunde eBhayibhelini siphinde simazi ngoJesu Kristu, onguNkulunkulu ovezwe waba ngumuntu. Singafunda ngokuba ngamandla kukaNkulunkulu (Jobe 1:12); owazi konke, nonolwazi lwakho konke okudaliweyo (Isaya 46:9,10), Onobulungiswa (Isaya 30:18), onomusa (Duteronomi 7:8), onesihawu nogcwele uthando kanye nokwazi ukusibekezelela (Roma 2:4) ogcwele ukwazi (1 Korinte 2:7), nonomusa (2 Korinte 12:9) nonentethelelo (Mathewu 6:14), nosifisela okuhle (Jeremiya 29:11), nonamandla okunqoba ukufa (Johane

11:25) nonobukhosi (Ihubo 47:8), nophila ingunaphakade (Duteronomy 33:27) nokunye okuningi okuyisimilo sakhe nokusinika izizathu eziningi zokuba simthande bese siba nobudlelwano bokuhlala kuYe. Njalo nje uma kukuningi esikwaziyo ngoNkulunkulu kanye nalokhu akuthandayo, sizokuya ngokuya simthanda siphinde sifise nokusondelana naye sibendawonye ebudlelwaneni kunye naYe.

Kwaba nguLusifa owaqala ukuba nokungabaza ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu. Lokhu kungabaza ngobunjalo bukaNkulunkulu kwagcina kuholela ekubeni kubekhona ukulwa okukhulu emlandweni wokusekelwa komhlaba. Kusukela kuleso sikhathi, “kungumsebenzi kaSathane ukuzama ukwenza imiqondo yabantu ihlale icabanga ngezinto ezizobenza bangatholi ukwazi kahle ngoNkulunkulu.” -Ellen G. White, Testimonies for Church, vol. 5, p. 740. USathane akanandaba nokuthi sinomfanekiso onjani ngoNkulunkulu (njengokudunyiswa kwemvelo, ukungakholelwa ekutheni uNkulunkulu ukhona, Ukukholelwa ekubeni khona kwamandla amakhulu, nokunye okuningi), inqobo nje, uma kuzokuba ngumfanekiso ongewona wangempela kaNkulunkulu.

`Funda ku-Genesise 3:1-5. Ngabe kwakuyini inhloso ka-Lusifa ngesikhathi exoxisana no-Eva na? Kungabe yimaphi amanga awaqambayo mayelana nesimilo sikaNkulunkulu na?`

Ekugcineni, umyalezo kaSathane owabe ewubhekise ku-Eva wabe uthi: UNkulunkulu kukhona anifihlela khona nina. UNkulunkulu akafuni kubekhona okungcono enizokuzuza. Angeke nikwazi ukumthemba Yena. U-Ellen White uphinde enabe ngokuthi, “ukuqala kokuphikisana okukhulu bekulokhu kuyinhloso kaSathane yokuveza umfanekiso ongesiwona wesimilo sikaNkulunkulu kanye nokufaka umoya wokulwisana nomthetho waKhe.”—Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 338.

`Kungabe isimilo sikaNkulunkulu sihlanekezelwe sabhediswa kanjani emhlabeni na? Okukhulu kunakho konke, wena wenze kanjani, ngesinye isikhathi, ukuhlanekezela isimilo sikaNkulunkulu kwabanye abantu na?`
