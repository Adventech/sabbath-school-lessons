---
title:  Umgwaqo Oya Emawuse
date:   10/06/2026
---

Kwakuyizigameko ezabe zilandelana nezenzeka esikhathini esingaba ngamaviki amabili lapho abafundi ababili babematasa bebukeza konke okwenzeka ekucabangeni kwabo bekhumbula izigameko ezahlukene kanye nokuxoxisana ababenakho esikhathini esedlule. Baxoxa ngokungena ngenhlokomo eJerusalema kanye nokususwa kwabadayisi ethempelini. Baxoxa nangePhasika elenzeka edlini engaphezulu, Baxoxa ngomthandazo kaJesu eGetshemane. Bekhuluma ngokukhaphelwa okwenziwa ngu-Judas nokwaba yinto embi. Bakhuluma nangokuthethwa kwecala, nokugconwa, kanye nendlela ashaywa ngayo. Bakhuluma nangomzimba kaJesu owawulenga esiphambanweni, nomzimba otubekile kanye namazwi okugcina awakhuluma ngaphambi kokuba kuphume umphefumulo waKhe njengoba kwase kumnyama nasesibhakabhakeni. Bakhuluma nangokudabuka kwekhethenisi lasethempelini okwaba nomsindo. Bakhuluma nangamathuna avuleka abantu abalungile. Nokuthathwa ngobunono komzimba kaJesu ngesikhathi wethulwa esiphambanweni, wabekwa ethuneni ngaphambi kokufika kweSabatha. Nobuphithiphithi obenzeka, nokulahlekelwa yithemba, nemibuzo abafundi bakaJesu ababa nayo emiqondweni yabo. Kungabe yini eyenza bangawuboni wonke lomonakalo na?

Abalandeli bakaJesu babenokujabha, bephelelwe ngamandla, bedidekile. Lesisehlo kwabe kuyisigameko esikhulu nesababuyisela emuva ekuphileni kwabo. Into abangazange bakwazi ukuyibona kwaba ngukuthi, lesisigameko kwabe kuyinto yesikhashana kuyona yonke indaba eyoke yenzeke selokhu kwasekelwa umhlaba. Njengoba lamadoda amabili ayehamba emgwaqeni oya Emawuse, uJesu waqhamuka wase ehambisana nabo.

`Funda ngalokhu kuxoxisana ku-Luka 24:23-27 bese ucabanga ngezinto ezimbili: okungabalandeli ababili, kanye noJesu.`

Kuthe lapho amehlo abo esevulekile, abalandeli ababili bagijimela eJerusalema ukuyokwabelana ngalokhu okwenzekile ngesikhathi besendleni (Luke 24:33, 34). Kuthe uma uJesu esondela esema phakathi kwabo, baba nokwethuka ngenxa yokwesaba. Ake uqaphele imibuzo uJesu ayibhekisa kubona: “Nikhathazekeleni na? Kuvelelani imizindlo ezinhliziyweni zenu na?” (Luka 24:38).

Lona kusengumyalezo kaJesu ofanayo nakithina namuhla. Izikhathi eziningi siyakhohlwa ukuthi uJesu uhamba eduzane kwethu lapho sihlala khona. Izikhathi eziningi asikwazi ukubona ukuthi Yena undawonye nathi. Izikhathi eziningi siyakhohlwa ukuthi kuningi esikwaziyo nesingakhuluma ngakho. Izikhathi eziningi sizizwa sikhathazekile bese sivumela ukungabaza kuvuke ezinhliziyweni zethu, sikhohlwe nawukuthi uJesu ubambe ukuphila kwethu ngendlela ephephile ezandleni zaKhe. Futhi okunye, izikhathi eziningi sicabanga ukuthi sazi kangcono kunoJesu ngalokhu okusuke kwenzeka ezimpilweni zethu (Luka 24:18).

`IBhayibheli lisinika iziyalo ezinhle mayelana nokuthi njengabantu abangama-Kristu kufuneka senze kanjani uma sibhekana nezinselelo kanye nezinkinga ezingasibuyisela emuva. Zinike ithuba ufunde kabanzi ngalamavesi amafishane: Roma 8:28; Filiphu 4:4-13; Jakobe 1:2-4, 12; kanye naku-2 Korinte 12:9, 10. Njengengxenye yokufunda kwakho, bhala phansi imiyalezo ebalulekile emithathu ongakwazi ukwabelana ngayo nomunye umuntu obhekene nezingqinamba ezimbuyisela emuva njengamanje, ulokhu ubeke emqondweni incwadi eku-2 korinte 1:4.`
