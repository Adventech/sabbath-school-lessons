---
title:  Izivunguvungu Zokuphila
date:   07/06/2026
---

UJesu uchithe usuku lonke ekhuluma nezixuku ezinkulu zabantu ezabe zingasosebeni lwaseGalile. Amazwi kaJesu ayelokhu enkenteza isikhathi eside emiqondweni yabantu futhi lokhu kuzoqhubeka kwenzeka kuze kube phakade.

Kwathi lapho selishonile ilanga, uJesu wakhuluma nabafundi baKhe, ebamema ukuba bathathe uhambo kunye naYe. “Asiwele siye ngaphesheya” (Marko 4:35). UJesu wayazi ukuthi kuzokuba khona isivunguvungu kodwa wathi mabaqhuke bahambe yize kuzokuba nalesisimo. Kwakukhona isifundo sokuphila esibalulekile ayefuna ukufundisa ngaso kubalandeli abasondelene naYe. Kungenzeka ukuthi unolwazi lokuthi yini eyalandela.

`Ake uphinde ufunde ngalesi sivunguvungu esiku-Marko 4:35-41. Kungabe yiziphi izifundo ongazithatha kulendaba neziphathelene nokukholwa na?`

Cabangela la maphuzu:

1. UJesu ufikelwa ngubuthongo esemkhunjini lapho kwakungathi kunomqamelo. Imvamisa umkhumbi wokudoba uba nomqamelo owodwa, lapho umshayeli womkhumbi enesihlala ahlala phezu kwaso. Umshayeli ohlezi esihlalweni usuke eshayela umkhumbi uze uyofika lapho uyakhona. Ngakho-ke lapha, uJesu usendaweni efanayo neyomshayeli “womkhumbi,” kodwa usefikelwa ngubuthongo eshayela.
2. Akusibona bonke abafundi ababekhona emkhunjini ababeqala ukuhamba ngomkhumbi. U-Petro, no-Jakobe, kanye no-Johane babekujwayele ukudoba izinhlanzi. Babelwazi njengoba bezazi ulwandle lwase-Galile, ngakho-ke kwakufanele bazi ukuthi bangasivika kanjani isivunguvungu.
3. Kulendaba yisigameko okungukuphela kwaso lapho sitshelwa ukuthi uJesu waphathwa ngubuthongo. Lokhu kwenzeka ngesikhathi kunesivunguvungu esikhulu ngendlela engakaze yenzeke ngayo selokhu baba khona, nangesikhathi abafundi babegcwele ukwesaba becabanga ukuthi bazokufa, kodwa uJesu uselele eshayela umkhumbi.
4. Indlela abenza ngayo abafundi uma sebebhekene nalesisimo esibucayi bathi “awunaki, nakuba sibhubha, na?” Babebuza ngesimilo sikaJesu kanye nangothando lwaKhe kubona. Izikhathi eziningi, nathi yiyona ndlela esenza ngayo uma sibhekene nezikhathi ezinzima.

Phakathi kwesimo sokulahlekelwa yithemba singazama ukuzisindisa thina (njengoba kwenza abafundi), noma kungenzeka ngesikhathi sizwa ubuhlungu noma kuba khona okusilahlekelayo bese siyaqala ukuba nemibuzo noma sikhombise ukungabaza uthando lukaNkulunkulu nokusinakekela kwaKhe. Sifikelwa ngumcabango wokuthi kufuneka kube khona akwenzayo ngendlela esicabanga ngayo ngalokhu esisuke sikubona ngeliso lokuba ngabantu.Kodwa, njengoba kwenzeka kubafundi, kusuke kuyisikhathi sesivunguvungu sokuphila lapho uNkulunkulu engenza khona izimangaliso. UNkulunkulu uhlala ethembekile, nasesimweni lapho sibona sengathi akukho lutho alwenzayo nokuyinto engawenzi umqondo kithina. Usuke ekunye nathi esivunguvungwini esibhekene naso futhi angakwazi ukusidambisa lapho thina singakwazi kwenza lutho.

`Kungabe wenza kanjani uma ubhekene nezivunguvungu zokuphila na? Lamaphuzu osuwabonile anamuphi umthelela ebudlelwaneni bakho noNkulunkulu na? Kungasiphi isikhathi lapho uphila khona ngendlela eshiwo ku-2 Korinte 5:7 na?`
