---
title:  Ukucabanga Okubanzi
date:   12/06/2026
---

Kungesikhathi sokubhekana nezinselelo ekuphileni lapho sidinga kakhulu ukunamathela kuNkulunkulu. Izihloko esithe sazithinta kuwona wonke lonyanga-ntathu konke esikufundile kungasiza ukugcina noma ukuvuselela ukuhamba kwethu noNkulunkulu kuqinile. Uma usesimweni sokubhekana nezigameko ezikuphindisela emuva izinto ezifana nokuba nempilo ebuthakathaka, nokuba nezinkinga zokungabi namali, nokubhidlika komshado, nokufelwa ngumuntu osondelene nawe, nanoma yimuphi omunye umthwalo ungakuphuca ukujabula kwakho, ake ubheke lemibuzo elandelayo bese uqhathanisa nalokhu okufundile esifundweni.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Kungabe izigigaba eziphindisela impilo yakho emuva obhekene nazo noma usuke wabhekana nazo, kuwuphazamise kanjani umfanekiso kaNkulunkulu onawo na? Ungenza kanjani ukubona isimilo sikaNkulunkulu esiyiqiniso, usibone kangcono na?`

`Ugcine nini ukuthandazela ukuzwa izwi likaNkulunkulu ukuba libe yinto eqinile kunalokhu okushiwo yisitha ekuphileni kwakho na? Khumbula ukuthi isela (uSathane) lisuke lize ngokuzokweba, nokubulala kanye nokucekela phansi, kodwa uNkulunkulu unikana ukuphila okuchichimayo (Johane 10:10).`

`Kungabe inhliziyo yakho ithobekile na? Kungabe unokuthemba ukuthi uNkulunkulu usabusa futhi kusenguye ohola ukuphila kwakho, yize ubhekene nobunzima na? Uma kungenjalo, ungenza kanjani ukuze uzokwazi ukufunda ukuthemba okunokuthobeka ekulungeni kanye nasothandweni lukaNkulunkulu na?`

`Kungabe uyakwazi ukuzigcina uqinile ezwini likaNkulunkulu nsuku zonke na? Cela uNkulunkulu aphinde avuselele uthando lwaKhe obunalo ekuqaleni njengoba ulokhu uhamba ezikhathini ezinezinselelo.`

`Ugcine nini ukuphendukela kuNkulunkulu njengoMduduzi kanye noMholi wakho emthandazweni, ulokhu uthemba ukuthi uNkulunkulu usigcinile isithembiso saKhe sokuthi akasoze akuyeka futhi akasoze akushiya (Hebheru 13:5).`

`Uma ukukholwa kwakho kubuthakathaka, thandaza uthi, “Ngiyakholwa, siza ukungakholwa kwami!”(Marko 9:24). Jwayelana nabantu abazokwazi ukukuvuselela kunabantu abazokulahlekisa ithemba.`

`Umhlaba ngaso sonke isikhathi awunandaba nabantu abangelutho, nabangazi lutho, nabalimele, kanye nabantu abanezinhliziyo ezibuhlungu. Umyalezo kaNkulunkulu uthi, “ngesikhathi ungenamandla, yena unamandla” lona ngumyalezo ongakwazi ukuguqula izimpilo zabantu. Awucabange ngomuntu ongamvuselela ngalomyalezo namuhla.`

**Ukugoqa Isifundo Saleliviki:** Sisaphila emhlabeni ogcwele isono nogcwele ubuhlungu kanye nokuhlupheka, futhi omunye nomunye wethu kuyenzeka abhekane nobunzima ekuphileni kwakhe, kwenzeka izinto ezingenza sizibuze ngothando lukaNkulunkulu. Uma sibheka emuva kubantu abahlukene abakhona eBhayibhelini ukuthi benza kanjani ngesikhathi benezinto ezibabuyisela emuva ekuphileni, singakwazi ukuba nesibindi ngesingakwenza uma nathi sufikelwa yizikhathi ezifana nalezi nokuzosiqinisa ohambweni lwethu noNkulunkulu (Malaki 3:6), futhi onothando olungaguquki.
