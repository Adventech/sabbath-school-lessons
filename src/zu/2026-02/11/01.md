### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Marko 4:35-41; Marko 5:21-34; Roma 5:35; Jobe 19:23-27; Jobe 23:8-12; Luke 24:13-27; Roma 8:18, 28.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kepha akusikho lokhu kuphela, kodwa sizibonga nangezinhlupheko ngokwazi ukuthi ukuhlupheka kuveza ukukhuthazela, ukukhuthazela kuveza ukuqiniseka, ukuqiniseka kuveza ithemba; ithemba alijabhisi, ngokuba uthando lukaNkulunkulu lutheliwe ezinhliziyweni zethu ngoMoya oNgcwele esimuphiweyo” (Roma 5:3–5, NKJV).

Ngobunye ubusuku, njengoba ilanga lase liyozilahla kunina, intombazanyana encane yayisohambweni ibheke ekhaya ngesikhathi sekuvuka isivunguvungu kuleyondayo. Lomntwana wazama ukushesha, azi ukuthi indlela asazoyihamba iseyinde. Kwaqala kwawa ithonsi lemvula esihlathini sakhe, kwalandela elinye, kuthe engalindele, wayesemanzi umzimba wonke. Waqala wagijima kwaze kwafika isikhathi lapho evula khona umnyango wakubo, nobaba wakhe wagijimela kuye esuka ewindini elingaphambili. Kuthe ngesikhathi emsonga emahlombe akhe ngengubo, wayibuza intombazanyana ukuthi, “ngiqale ukukubona khona manje usemvuleni. Kungani uqhubeke nokugijima phakathi kombani obaniza kangaka na? Awumanga ngani ubheke phezulu bese uyamamatheka na?”

“Yebo, ngike ngema ngabheka phezulu,” kusho intombazanyana, “ngoba uNkulunkulu ubethatha isithombe sami!”

Kungabe thina siphawula sithini uma izivunguvungu zokuphila zifika phezu kwethu noma kukhona izingqinamba ezisibuyisela emuva kubudlelwano esinabo noNkulunkulu na? Kungabe sibhekisa amakhanda ethu phansi njengoba amathonsi emvula ewela phezu kwethu noma sibheka phezulu, ngoba sinolwazi futhi sethemba ukuthi uNkulunkulu ukhona njengoba sibhekisa ubuso bethu ngakuYe na?

Kuleliviki, sizobheka izindlela esenza ngazo uma izimo zokuphila ziza nezinselelo. Sizobheka ukuthi singasebenzisa kanjani izingqinamba ezisibuyisela emuva ukuba zikwazi ukusiqinisa, futhi zingakwazi ukwenza buthakathaka into yethu ebalulekile yobudlelwane.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNhlangulana 13._
