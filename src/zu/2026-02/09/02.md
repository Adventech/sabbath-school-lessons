---
title:  Ukuphazamiseka kanye Nezilingo
date:   24/05/2026
---

`Funda ngezilingo zika-Samsoni encwadini yabaHluleli 14 naku-AbaHluleli 16:1, 4, 16, 17. Yize kwabe kungumuntu owayebiziwe nguNkulunkulu ukuba enze umsebenzi othile okhethekile, uSamsoni wamsebenzela uNkulunkulu elokhu evumela kuqhubeke ukulingwa kwakhe. Kungabe usifundisani thina umphumela wendaba yokuphila kwakhe na?`

Ukuphikisana okukhulu kuyinto ekhona ngempela, futhi sonke siyabandakanyeka kulempikiswano. Ukulwisana okukhona ezweni lonke nokwaqala ezulwini, kuyinto eqhubeka ukwenzeka ekuphileni komuntu ngamunye.

USathane uyazi ukuthi kufuneka asebenze ngokusemandleni akhe kulesi sikhathi esiphila kusona, ngaphambi kokubuya kukaKristu, ukuze ezokwazi ukuvimba ukubani sibe nobudlelwano obusondele kuNkulunkulu. Mhlawumbe usuke waphazamiseka ngezinye izinto okungenzeka ukuthi kazinabo ububi, kodwa okuyizinto ezithatha isikhathi kanye namandla akho amaningi ngalendlela yokuthi kuncane kakhulu okusele ongakwenzela uNkulunkulu. Mhlawumbe kungenzeka ukuthi kube ngumsebenzi, wukunaka izindaba zomphakathi, ukuyothenga, ezemidlalo, noma ukudla. Uma sibhekisisa ngempela ukuphila kwethu, sizokwazi ukubona ukuthi kunesikhathi esiningi esichithakile kanye nokungalingani kwesikhathi sokwenza lezizinto sashiya isikhathi esincane kakhulu sokusebenzela uNkulunkulu nokusiza abanye abantu. Isitha siyabazi ubuthakathaka bethu siphinde sazi nezinto ezingasiphazamisa ekuchitheni isikhathi noNkulunkulu. Kufuneka sikhumbule ukufuna uNkulunkulu kuqala (Mathewu 6:33) ngaphambi kokuba sigijimele ukwenza imisebenzi yethu yansuku zonke kanye nalokhu okungaqhamuka ekuhambeni kwethu.

UJesu uyasiqonda isimo sethu, kodwa akahambisani nendlela esisizwa ngayo (Isambulo 3:14-22). Yize enguNkulunkulu, wake waba ngumuntu owakuzwa ukukhathala njengoba kwenzeka nakithi (Johane 4:6). Uyabazi ubunzima bokuphila njengoba nathi sibazi, kodwa wakwazi ukuphepha kubo ngokuthi ahambe ayothandaza eyedwa ekhuleka kuBaba waKhe (luka 5:16; Luka 6:12; Marko 1:35; Mathewu 14:23). Wayazi ukuthi isikhathi asichitha noBaba waKhe kwabe kuyinto enhle kakhulu abenokuyenza ukuze ezokwazi ukuthola amandla okulwisana nezilingo. Ukwenza okufana nalokhu kuseyinto enhle nakithi.

USamsoni wawa ngoba wacabanga ukuthi uqine kakhulu. Wathemba ukuba namandla kwakhe ukuthi kuzomenza akwazi ukunqoba izilingo. Nsuku zonke, omunye nomunye wethu ubhekana nokulwisana nesono njengento eyisitha somphefumulo ezama ukusiqeda amandla bese kuphela nobudlelwano esinabo noNkulunkulu. Uyabazi ubuthakathaka bethu bese enaka bona ukuze ezokwazi ukudambisa ubudlelwano esinabo noNkulunkulu bese eletha umuzwa wokuba yizoni kanye nokuzizwa singafanelekile, konke lokhu kuholela ekutheni siqhele kuNkulunkulu. USathane usemsebenzini wokuqhelisa ukucabanga kwethu, nalokhu esihlose ukukwenza, kanye nalokhu esikwenzayo ukuze ezothola ukusibamba kwezinye izinto ezenzekayo ekuphileni kwethu. Kodwa nakhu okufuneka sikukhumbule: Ukukholwa kwethu kuyosisiza, kusenze sikwazi ukuma, futhi ukukholwa sikuthola ngokuzwa Izwi likaNkulunkulu.

`Yini ekukhathazayo njengamanje na? Lingakusiza kanjani Izwi likaNkulunkulu njengamanje na?`
