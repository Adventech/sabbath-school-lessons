---
title:  Ukucabanga Okubanzi
date:   29/05/2026
---

Akudingeki kube yinto esimangazayo ukuthi isihloko esiphathelene nomthetho kube yinkulumo ehlanekezelwe iphinde ingaqondakali, njengoba sazi ukuthi inhloso enkulu kaSathane kwabe kungukulwisana nomthetho waKhe.

Abanye abantu ababephila ngesikhathi sikaJesu elapha emhlabeni babecabanga ukuthi wayezele ukuqeda ngokuba khona komthetho, kodwa ukucabanga okunjalo kwakuqhelile eqinisweni. UJesu waletha ukukhanyiseleka ngomthetho kanye nesimilo esihle sikaNkulunkulu, futhi wayezele ukuzogcwalisa ukugcinwa komthetho (Mathewu 5:17, 18) enzela ukusikhombisa ukuthi uNkulunkulu ungumuntu onjani.

“Njengoba ukuhlonipheka Kwezwi Elingcwele likaNkulunkulu kwakuyinto eyajatshulelwa ezinhliziyweni zabantu, kungabe babezokwazi yini ukufeza izinto ezifunwa nguNkulunkulu. Ukwazisa umthetho kaNkulunkulu kwasiza ukunika u-Israyeli amandla ngesikhathi sokubusa kuka-Davida kwaphinda futhi nangesikhathi sokuqala kokubusa kuka-Solomoni; Kwakungenxa yokukholelwa ezwini eliphilayo lapho kwenzeka imvuselelo ezinsukwini zika-Eliya kanye noJosaya. Futhi kwakuseyiwona lawomaqiniso aseMbhalweni, amaqiniso angumcebo wamagugu ka-Israyeli, uJeremiya wazama ngokusemandleni ukuwavuselela.”—Ellen G. White, Prophets and Kings, pp. 465, 466.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Kungabe isiko elidumile lisibeka ngayiphi indlela isono na? Kufuneka lenzenjani ibandla ukubhekana nalesisimo na?`

`Kungabe uzibonele nini ukuthi isono sibulala ubudlelwano bethu noNkulunkulu kanye nobudlelwano esinabo nabanye abantu na?`

`Kungabe ukuhlonipha umthetho kaNkulunkulu kube yinto elula noma kubeyinto elukhuni empilweni yakho na? Yimaphi amaphuzu abe nomthelela kulokhu na?`

`Thina njengama-Sabatha, esinegama elichaza ukuthi siwazisa kangakanani umthetho, singakugwema kanjani ukubukwa njengabantu abazisa ukugcinwa komthetho, singabantu abancike ekugcineni umthetho -siwugcina ngoba sihlose ukuthola ukusindiswa na? (Yenza inhlolovo yokucabanga: ngosuku lokwahlulela, kungabe uzothembela kuphi njengoba izono ozenzile ziyolethwa ngaphambi kukaNkulunkulu omsulwa nongcwele nongumahluleli na? Ukugcina kwakho umthetho na? Noma Ukulunga okungenasici kukaJesu okusesikhundleni sakho na?)`

`Kungabe ulwazi (noma ukushoda kolwazi) kungathikameza kanjani ubudlelwano bethu noNkulunkulu na? (Funda ku-Izaga 24:3, 13, 14)`

**Ukugoqa Isifundo Saleliviki:** Izimpilo zethu zingcoliswe yisono, nokuyisona esisahlukanisa noNkulunkulu. Noma kunjalo, uNkulunkulu uyasimema ukuba simazi futhi simthande Yena ngokuqonda konke esingaba nakho, nangezinhliziyo zethu, nangomphefumulo wethu kanye namandla ethu. Uma sikwenza lokhu, ngokwemvelo, sizokwazi ukuthanda abanye abantu. Uthando esinalo lukaNkulunkulu kanye nokuthanda abanye abantu kuyinto ekhona emthethweni kaNkulunkulu, esanikezwa wona ukusivikela siphinde sivikele ubudlelwano esinabo noNkulunkulu kanye nalabo abasizungezile. Umthetho kaNkulunkulu ubekwe kahle ukuveza isimilo saKhe, uma siwuqonda umthetho waKhe, ubudlelwano esinabo kuYe buzokhula buye ngokuqina.
