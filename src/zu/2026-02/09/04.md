---
title:  Umthetho
date:   26/05/2026
---

`Ungasichaza kanjani noma ungathi siyini isono kumuntu ongesiyena umKrestu na? Kungabe iBhayibheli lisichaza ngayiphi indlela isono na? Roma 3:20 kanye no- 1 Johane 3:4.`

Isono singukwephula umthetho kaNkulunkulu (1 Johane 3:4), futhi isono singafakwa ndawonye nobunjalo bethu (iHubo 51:5; Jeremiya 17:9). Ngakho-ke ngumthetho okwazi ukusikhanyisela ngokuthi siyini isono ngempela. Umthetho ufana nomuntu efaka izibuko ukuze ezokwazi ukubona kahle lokhu okusizungezile noma esebenzisa isibuko sokuzibuka ukuze ezokwazi ukuzibona ukuthi unjani ngempela. Wenza kube khona ukucaciseleka uphinde ulethe ukuzisola ekuphileni kwethu kanye nangezimilo zethu, futhi ngesikhathi esifanayo, usitshela kabanzi ngesimilo sikaNkulunkulu nokuthi yini ebalulekile kuYe.

Imithetho Elishumi (Ekodusi 20:3-17) yayibhalwe nguNkulunkulu eyibhala ngomunwe waKhe. UJesu wagcizelela ngokubaluleka kwemithetho: “Woyithanda iNkosi, uNkulunkulu wakho ngayo yonke inhliziyo yakho, nangawo wonke umphefumulo wakho, nangayo yonke ingqondo yakho, nangawo onke amandla akho. Owesibili yilo: ‘Womthanda umakhelwane wakho njengalokhu uzithanda wena’” (Marko 12:30, 31). Wathasisela ngokuthi: “Kuleyo miyalo emibili kubambelela umthetho wonke nabaprofethi” (Mathewu 22:40).

Amazwi kaNkulunkulu awabhekisa kuma-Israyeli ngesikhathi eseNtabeni yasesiNayi nokungamazwi asashiwo nakithi namuhla (Hebheru 1:1, 2) asitshela ukuthi umthetho uyinto ephathelene nezinhlobo eziningi zobudlelwane. UNkulunkulu wasinika umthetho njengento yokuvikela ubudlelwano bethu kunye naYe nobudlelwano bethu nabanye abantu. Ngeshwa, uSathane uveze isithombe okungesona sobuhle bomthetho kaNkulunkulu enzela ukuthi siwubone njengento engumthwalo. Ukuqiniswa komthetho kuqophisana nokuba nothando kanye nokukhululeka, konke lokhu kweyamaniswa nomthetho, yize iBhayibheli lisitshela ukuthi, “Ngokuba ukuthanda uNkulunkulu yilokhu ukuba sigcine imiyalo yakhe; nemiyalo yakhe ayinzima” (1 Johane 5:3).

`1. Usebenzisa isilinganiso sokubala kusuka ku 1 kuya ku-5, kungabe liligugu kangakanani Izwi Lokuphila (kanye nomthetho njengengxenye yakho) kimina na?`

`2. Uma ngicabanga ngomthetho ka-Nkulunkulu, kungabe lomthetho uyangivimbela noma uyangiqinisa mina na? Ngingenza kanjani ukuze ngikwazi ukuqonda kangcono umthetho uma ngikhetha isizathu sokuqala na?`

`3. Kungenzekani uma umthetho kaNkulunkulu wokuthanda Yena kanye nabanye abantu walethwa waba yinsika yokuphila kwami, nomndeni wami, kanye nebandla lami na? Yini engaguquka ekuphileni kwami kanye nasebudlelwaneni enginabo na?`
