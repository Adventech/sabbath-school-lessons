---
title:  Umthetho kanye Nevangeli
date:   27/05/2026
---

UJesu uqobo lwaKhe wakuchaza lokhu ngendlela enamandla necacile lapho aveza khona ngobudlelwane anabo nomthetho.

`Ku-Mathewu 5:17, 18, kungabe uJesu wathini lapha ngomthetho na?`

Ngendlela efanayo nalokhu okwenziwa ngumzali uma ebeka imingcele ethile kubantwana bakhe uyakwazi ukuveza ukubaluleka kwalemingcele. Umthetho kaNkulunkulu usitshela kabanzi ngesimilo saKhe kanye nalokhu okubalulekile kuYe. UNkulunkulu wasinika umthetho wakhe ukuze uzokwazi ukuvikela ubudlelwano bethu kuYena nobudlelwano obukhona phakathi komunye nomunye, sibe nolwazi lokuthi umthetho waKhe uzolawula konke okudingekayo ekuphileni kwethu njengoba siya ngokukhula kuYena. Empeleni, ngubani ongakaze abuzwe ubuhlungu obesabekayo obenzekile ngenxa yesono, okungukwephula umthetho, nokuyisigameko esithinte omunye nomunye wethu na?

Ukuthanda uJesu yiyona ngqikithi yomthetho. uJesu wathi, “Uma ningithanda, niyakugcina imiyalo yami” (Johane 14:15). Uma simthanda ngokweqiniso uJesu, sizophoqeka ngokwemvelo ukuba sigcine umthetho waKhe. Uma siwubheka umthetho waKhe, noma kanjani, sizozizwa siphoqelekile ukuba simthande kakhulu uJesu. Okunye okubaluleke kakhulu, kudingeka sibheke esiphambanweni lapho sizobona ukufa kukaKristu efela thina, nokuyinto esiyibona ngamehlo engqondo nokuzokhulisa uthando lwethu kuNkulunkulu.

Kungakho kukhona ukuhambisana phakathi kwevangeli kanye nomthetho. Lokhu kusho ukuthi, noma ngabe sikholelwa kangakanani emthethweni kanye nokubaluleka kokuwugcina, kuyodingeka sihlale sikhumbula ukuthi ngokomthetho, phambi kukaNkulunkulu umthetho wenzelwe ukugxeka kuphela. Umthetho kawusoze umthethelele umuntu, awukwazi nokumngcwelisa, futhi kawuzihlambi nezono. Okunalokho, umthetho yiwona okhomba ukuthi kungani sidinga ukuthethelelwa, kungani sidinga ukwenziwa ngcwele, kungani sidinga ukuhlanzwa izono. Kungakho nje, ukuhambisana nomthetho, nokuyinto eyisisekelo sokuqonda kwethu umthetho, kuyivangeli lokufa kukaKristu efela thina, nokuyinto esiyinikeziwe, kungesona isizathu sokuthi besigcina umthetho, kodwa esikunikezwe ngenxa yokukholwa.

`Funda lamavesi alandelayo: Roma 3:28; Roma 4:13-16; Galathiya 2:16; Galathiya 3:13; Filiphu 3:9. Kungabe lamavesi asifundisa ukuthini nokuyinto ezosisiza, njengabantu abakholelwa ekugcineni umthetho, ukuthi kakufanele sigcine singabantu abazisa ukugcinwa komthetho kakhulu kunazo zonke ezinye izinto na?`
