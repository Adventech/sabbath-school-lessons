title:  Ukucabanga Okubanzi
date:   15/05/2026
---

“Uma sinokucabanga sikhulume kakhulu ngoJesu, sinciphise ukukhuluma ngathi, lokho kuyosenza sibe nobukhona baKhe kakhulu.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, p. 102.

“Uma samukela iziyalo sinokungabaza nokwesaba, noma sizama ukuxazulula zonke izinto ebesingakwazi ukuzibona kahle ngaphambi kokuba sibe nokukholwa, ukudideka ebesinakho kuzojula kwande kakhulu. Kodwa uma sisondela kuNkulunkulu, sizwa silahlekelwe yithemba futhi singakwazi nokuzisiza, njengoba sisesimweni esinjalo ngempela, bese siba nokuzithoba, nokukholwa kokuthemba lapho simtshela ngalokhu esikudingayo, uNkulunkulu onolwazi olungapheliyo, nokwazi ukubona zonke izinto ezidaliweyo, nobusa zonke izinto ngezwi nangokwentando yaKhe, angakwazi uzozilalela zonke izikhalo zethu, bese eletha ukukhanya okuzokhanyisa ezinhliziyweni zethu.”—Steps to Christ, pp. 96, 97.

“Umphefumulo ungakhuphuka ucishe ufike ezulwini usebenzisa izimpiko zokudumisa. UNkulunkulu simkhonza ngezingoma nangokuhlabelela ezinkantolo zaphezulu, futhi njengoba sikhombisa ukubonga kwethu sisuke sizama ngokusemandleni ukumdumisa njengezinkumbi zasezulwini. Masijabule ngesizotha uma sisondela phambi kukaMdali, sigcwele ‘injabulo nentokozo, ukubonga nezwi lokuhlabelela’ Isaya 51:3.”—Steps to Christ. p. 104.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Kungabe yiyiphi inkulumo ekuhlabe umxhwele ecashunwe kusicaphuno esingenhla na? Iyiphi inkulumo esicaphunweni ekulethela inselelo na?`

`Yiziphi ezinye izifundo esingazifunda empilweni yokuthandaza eyabe yenziwa ngabanye abantu abaseBhayibhelini na? (2 AmaKhosi 13:4; Jona 4:2, 3; Habakuki 3:1; 2 AmaKhosi 19:14-19; Jeremiya 32:16-25; Nehemiya 1:4-11; 1 AmaKhosi 8:22-54; lokhu ngamavesi ambalwa esingawasebenzisa.)`

`Ngabe kusizaphi ukuzila okuhambisana nokuthandaza na?`

`Kungabe ikhona yini into entsha ofisa ukuyishintsha noma ofisa ukuyenza empilweni yakho yokuthandaza nozoyenza ngenxa yalokhu osukufunde esifundweni sangalelisonto na? Kungani ungenzi izinguquko na?`

**Ukugoqa Isifundo Saleliviki:** IBhayibheli lisitshela ngabantu ababenomthandazo othathekisayo, ababehlala bephila impilo yokukhuleka kanye nabanye ababengakhuleki. Njengoba silokhu siphenya amakhasi alo, siyohlala njalo sithola umuntu esingenza isibonelo ngaye, nokuyinto engahlangene nobudlelwano esinabo noNkulunkulu. Sizokwazi ukuthola futhi izithembiso eziningi kakhulu ezizokwazi ukusivuselela siphinde sizilawule ngendlela yokuhlala sidumisa. Ukukhula kwezikaMoya kudingeka kube ngumgomo wethu- silokhu sigxilise amehlo ethu kuJesu, uyohlala eyisibonelo kuzona zonke izinto futhi unguMqalisi kanye noMphelelisi wokukholwa kwethu.
