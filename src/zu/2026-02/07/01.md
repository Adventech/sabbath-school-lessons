### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 AmaKhosi 19:1-18; Mathewu 6:5-8; Luka 11:2-4; Mathewu 6:5-15; Daniyeli 9:9-19; Roma 8:26, 27.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Thembani kuye ngezikhathi zonke nina bantu, nithulule inhliziyo yenu phambi kwakhe; uNkulunkulu uyisiphephelo sethu” (iHubo 62:8, NKJV).

Kungabe impilo yakho yokuthandaza injani na? Kungabe uthandaza kangaki na? Ukujwayele kangakanani ukuthandaza na? Usuke ulindele ngokuthokoza okungakanani impendulo na? Kungabe uthandaza nsuku zonke noma uthandaza kuphela uma kukhona isimo esiphuthumayo na? Kungabe imithandazo yakho ihlala ngokucela njalo, noma kungenzeka ukuthi uyamdumisa uNkulunkulu ekuthandazeni kwakho na?

Nakhu okunye, kuyenzeka yini uzithole wenza umthandazo ekuseni ngaphambi kokuba udle kanti futhi kuyenzeka yini uthandaze izikhawu ezithile kulokhu kuxakeka kwakho kwansuku zonke na? Kungenzeka ukuthi ubuyingxenye yabantu abahlangene ukwenza umkhuleko noma usuke waba namava okuthandaza ubusuku nemini. Usuke wawezwa amandla kanye nobukhona bukaNkulunkulu emthandazweni nokuyinto ekwazile ukushintsha zonke izinto empilweni yakho na?

Umthandazo yiwona owenza kubekhona ukuxhumana phakathi kwethu (esingamagatsha) kanye noJesu (ongumvini). “Uma singakwazi ukukhula siphinde sisabalale, kuyodingeka sikwazi ukuqhubeka nokumunca amanzi kanye nokudla sikumunca kuMvini oPhilile; ngoba ekwahlukaneni noMvini kasinawo amandla.”— Ellen G. White, Early Writings, p. 73. Lokhu yisibusiso esisithola ngokuhlala emthandazweni. UNkulunkulu uyasizwa, futhi uhlala njalo ephendula imithandazo ngesikhathi Sakhe nangendlela abona ngayo Yena- yize kungaba yimpendulo esingayilindele.

Kuleliviki sizoke sifunde ngabanye abantu ababethandaza abaseBhayibhelini bese sibheka izindlela eziphathekayo zokuthandaza nezizokwazi ukuqinisa ukuthandaza kwansuku zonke zokuphila kwethu.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNhlaba 16._
