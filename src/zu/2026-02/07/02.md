---
title:  U-Eliya Uthandaza Esimweni Esibucayi
date:   10/05/2026
---

U-Eliya okholekile wayephila ezikhathini ezimbi, lapho iNkosi u-Ahabi enza okuningi “wacunula uJehova, uNkulunkulu ka-Israyeli, kunawo onke amakhosi akwa-Israyeli ayengaphambi kwakhe” (1 AmaKhosi 16:33). Ngokudlulele isimo esisabekayo empilweni yakhe kuyilapho aba nokubhekana nokwenzeka entabeni yase Karmeli (bheka ku- 1 AmaKhosi 18). Kuvuthondaba kwalokhu okukhulunywa ngakho nokuyinto eyesabekayo eshiwo lapha, u-Ahabi kanye nombuso wakhe bazibonela ngamehlo abo iqiniso lokuthi uNkulunkulu uyawuphendula umthandazo. Kulomzuzu ongekhohlakale emlandweni ka-Israyeli, nokwaba yinto eyenza kwaguquka izinto ngokushesha kulendaba, nokwathathekisa ngendlela emangalisayo.

`Funda ku-1 AmaKhosi 19:1-18, esizokuphawula lapho, ngumthandazo ka-Eliya kanye nokuxoxisana kwake noNkulunkulu. Kungabe yini eyabe isenhliziyweni ka-Eliya ngokujabha kwakhe lapha na? Kungabe uNkulunkulu wamphendula kanjani mayelana nalokhu okwenzeka entabeni e-Karmeli na?`

Kusona sonke isigameko sosuku, yize uNkulunkulu ayiphendula yonke imithandazo ka-Eliya, ukungaphatheki kahle kuka-Eliya, nokuphazamiseka kwakhe emqondweni, nokuyinto eyabonakala nasemzimbeni wakhe kwabakhona ushintsho. Yize u-Eliya athola ukunqoba okumangalisayo esizwa nguNkulunkulu ngalolusuku, kodwa wabanakho ukukhathazeka kwesikhashana, nokwamlethela uvalo lokusaba ukuthi uzokufa nokuyinto eyaba ngaphezulu kokukholwa kwakhe kuNkulunkulu. Into emangalisa kakhulu ngalendaba ngukuthi, yize u-Eliya aba nengcindezi yokudangala waphinde walahlekelwa nayithemba, uNkulunkulu wasondela kuyena wakhombisa ukuba nesineke kuye waphinde wamnakekela, ngokuthi amuphe ukudla waphinde wamupha namanzi futhi (1 AmaKhosi 19:5, 6) – wakwenza ngangokuba kwaze kwaphela izinsuku ezingu-40 nobusuku obungu-40 (1 AmaKhosi 19:8). Ngesikhathi esegcina uNkulunkulu wazembula Yena uqobo, lokhu kwakuyisimo esihlukile kunaleso ake abakusona ngaphambili.

Kwesinye isikhathi empilweni yethu, kuyenzeka uNkulunkulu aphendule ngendlela eyehlukile, nenamandla, nokuzindlela esingeke sikwazi ukuzinqaba. Konke lokhu kuqinisa ukukholwa kwethu, bese sizizwa sisondele kakhulu kuNkulunkulu ekuphileni kwethu.

Kwesinye isikhathi siyanyakaziswa sigcine sizinikele ekulingweni, sibe nokucabanga kokuthi kulukhuni kakhulu ukulandela uNkulunkulu nokuba nokholo olungezanyazanyiswe. Sivame ukubheka izimpendulo zikaNkulunkului ngendlela esilindela ukuba asiphendule ngayo, ibe singenalo ulwazi lokuthi imicabango kanye nezindlela zaKhe ziphakeme kakhulu futhi zinokuhlakanipha okwedlula okwethu (Isaya 55:8, 9). Njengoba kufana nokudalwe nguNkulunkulu nokuyinto esingenakho ukuyiqonda, akufuneki sibe nokumangala ngokuthi ziningi izindlela zikaNkulunkulu esingaziqondi kahle.

`UNkulunkulu, omuhle nonguBaba omnene, ukwazi kahle konke esikudingayo. Kungenzeka kanjani ukuthi uhlale ngokwanele umethembe Yena uphinde ugcine amehlo akho egxilile kuYe kukho konke odlula kukhona na? Khuluma naYe ngalokhu njengamanje.`
