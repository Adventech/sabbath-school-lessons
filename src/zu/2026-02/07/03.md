---
title:  Uma Imithandazo Kusengathi Ayiphendulwa
date:   11/05/2026
---

`Kungenzeka ukuthi usuke wathandaza isikhathi eside ngento ethile, mhlawumbe usuthandaze iminyaka, futhi sekubonakala engazathi uNkulunkulu akazange ayizwe imkhuleko yakho. IBhayibheli lisitshela ukuthi “celani, khona nizakuphiwa; funani, khona nizakufumana” (Mat. 7:7), “uma sicela utho ngokwentando yakhe, uyakusizwa” (1 Johane 5:14, NKJV). Kungabe uziqonda kanjani lezizethembiso na?`

U-Hana uyisibonelo sowesifazane owabe ezinikele nowathandazela into ethile ebalulekile (1 Samuweli 1:10-17). Ekuqaleni kwakubonakala engazathi uNkulunkulu akayiphenduli imithandazo yakhe, kodwa waqhubeka nokukhuleka, wagcina ephenduliwe nguNkulunkulu, ngesikhathi athandaza ngaso Yena wakwenza ngokwentando Yakhe. Kwesinye isikhathi ukulinda kuyenza ukuhamba kwethu noNkulunkulu kujule, njengoba siya ngokuya simethemba Yena.

IHubo 62:8 lithi: Thembani kuye ngezikhathi zonke nina bantu, nithulule inhliziyo yenu phambi kwakhe; uNkulunkuku uyisiphephelo sethu” (NKJV). Themba. Kungabe uyethemba ukuthi uNkulunkulu uyakwazi ngempela okukufanele, noma ngabe kasiboni impendulo esheshayo emithandazweni yethu na? Kungabe sinalo ithemba lokuthi ekugcineni uzokwazi ukuphendula ngesikhathi esifaneleyo nangendlela Yakhe na?

Kwesinye isikhathi imithandazo yethu kungenzeka ingaphendulwa ngokuphuthuma njengoba sifuna noma iphendulwe ngendlela esithemba ukuthi izophendulwa ngayo. Kungabe iBhayibheli lisinika siphi siyalo mayelana nalokhu na?

- Funa intando kaNkulunkulu, hhayi eyakho (Mat. 6:10; 1 Johane 5:14, 15).
- Naka lokhu okuhlosile (Izaga 16:2, Jakobe 4:3).
- Ake ucabange ukuthi kungabe kukhona isono osithandayo (Hub. 66:18, 1 Pet. 3:12, Izaga 15:29).
- Hlala kuNkulunkulu naseZwini laKhe (Johane 15:7).
- Yiba nokukholwa uma uthandaza (Heb. 11:6, Jakobe 1:6, Marko 11:24, Mat. 21:22).
- Bheka isimo senhliziyo yakho (ukuthi inokuzithoba noma inokuzazisa) (Jakobe 4:6, 1 Pet. 5:6).
- Bekezela (1 Thes. 5:17, 18).
- Thethelela abanye abantu (Marko 11:25, 26).
- Ekugcineni, uNkulunkulu unomfanekiso obanzi futhi uyakwazi okusifanele thina (Rom. 8:28, Efes. 3:20, Jer. 29:11–13).
- Kwesinye isikhathi impendulo yaKhe ingafana nalokhu okwenzeka ku-Pawula: “Umusa wami ukwanele” (2 Kor. 12:9, NKJV).

Into ebalulekile nezokwazi ukusenza sikwamukele nakanjani lokhu okubanakala kuwukungaphendulwa kwemithandazo nokuzosinika umfanekiso ofanele kaNkulunkulu. Uma sibona uNkulunkulu eqhelile futhi engenandaba ngokwenzekayo, ubudlelwano bethu naYe buzoxega. Ezikhathini ezifana nalezi, funda iBhayiyibheli ubheke ubufakazi bothando nokunakekela akunika khona, bese uthandaza ukuba umfanekiso osunawo ngaYe nokungumfanekiso ongamile kahle, ukuthi awenze kube ngumfanekiso ocacile.
