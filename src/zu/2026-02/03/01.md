### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
1 Johane 2:15-17; Luka 18:9-14; 1 Johane 1:9; Heb. 11:24-26; Luka 22:24-27; Fil. 2:3-8.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Ngokuba oziphakamisayo uzakuthotshiswa; nozithobayo uzakuphakanyiswa’ ” (Luka 14:11, NKJV).

Sonke siyabazi abantu abanomuzwa wokuzibeka phezulu, abantu abacabanga ukuthi kabalenzi iphutha. Mhlawumbe kukhona omaziyo othanda njalo ukuthi kube nguye ophetheyo, ongakuthandi ukushayelwa umthetho noma ongathandi ukugxekwa ngisho nangendlela eyakhayo. Noma kubenomuntu okujwayele ukuhlala exabana nabanye noma ongumpetha ekudicileleni abanye abantu phansi. Lokhu kungasenza sicabange ngabanye abantu, kodwa umbuzo okhona wangempela kungaba ngukuthi: Kungeke yini kube ngomunye wethu na? Uma silokhu sikhomba ngeminwe yethu abanye abantu, silokhu siwuphika umoya wokuziphakamisa okhona ekuphileni kwethu, siyazikhohlisa.

Sizamile ukulwisana nokuzikhukhumeza. Sonke sesike saba nawo umuzwa lapho sifisa ukubona, senza, sikhuluma, noma sibonakale singcono kunabanye abantu abeseduzane nathi ngoba sikholwa ngukuthi thina singcono kunabo, noma ukwenza okucishe kufane nalokhu. Omunye umuntu wake wathi ukuzikhukhumeza kuqhamuka kumuntu ngesikhathi efisa ukuzibonakalisa ukuthi impilo ayiphilayo ibalulekile. Noma kunjalo, kufuneka sihlale sazi ukuthi impilo yethu inokubaluleka ngoba sadalwa ngomfanekiso kaNkulunkulu, futhi singabanye balabo bantu uKristu owabafelayo.

Kuleliviki sizofunda kabanzi ngomthelela ongabakhona kubudlelwano bethu noNkulunkulu kanye nabanye abantu ongadalwa ngukuziphakamisa kwethu. Sizobheka futhi ukuthi kungabe iBhayibheli lisifundisa ukuthini mayelana nokuzithoba phambi kwabanye abantu kanye naphambi kukaNkulunkulu.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UMbasa 18._
