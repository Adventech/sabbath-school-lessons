---
title:  UMose, Inceku Ethobekileyo
date:   14/04/2026
---

Amahholo amakhulu asesigodlweni saseGibhithe ayenokuzigqaja ngokunethezeka, nobumnandi, nokuzizwa ukhulukile uma ukuwo. “ ‘UMose wafundiswa ukuhlakanipha konke kwabaseGibhithe; waba namandla ngamazwi nangemisebenzi yakhe’ ” (Izenzo 7:22, NKJV). Impilo yokuba semandleni, nasemcebweni, nokuba nodumo kwabe kuyinto okwakufuneka azithathele yona, kodwa uMose wakhetha ukwenza okwehlukile. “Njengombhali wezomlando, nomqambi wezinkondlo, nomuntu onekhono lokukhuluma, enguJenene wamabutho kwezempi, engumuntu oshaya imithetho, akukho umuntu owayenjengaye kontanga yakhe. Yize wawuvuleleke kangaka umhlaba phambi kwakhe, wakwazi ukuba namandla okuphila ngokuzithiba ngokuthi enqabe ukuzijabulisa ngobucebi nodumo kanye nobukhulu ayenabo, wakhetha ukuba aphile impilo yokuhlupheka kanye nabantu bakaNkulunkulu, kunokuba ajabulele ukuzijabulisa esonweni okwesikhashana.’”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 246.

`Kungabe isitshela ukuthini incwadi yama-Hebheru 11:24-26 mayelana nokuthi kungani uMose akhetha ukulandela lendlela yokuba azehlise na?`

Ukuzehlisa kwakamuva kukaMose kwaba yinto emangalisayo, uma ubheka isimo sokuba mkhulu ayekusona usiqhathanisa nendawo lapho ayevela khona. Noma kunjalo, ngenxa yokona kwakhe akwenzayo (Eksodusi 2:12), walahlekelwa ngukuzethemba kanye nokwaneliseka ayekade enakho. Ebhekene nezintaba ezabe ziphakeme njengezindonga zekilasi ayekulo kanye nokuziphakamisa akubeka eceleni, uMose wachitha iminyala engu 40 elokhu efundiswa nguNkulunkulu lokhu ayefisa ukuba akwazi okokuthi ahole abantwana bakwa-Israyeli ukubakhipha ebugqilini abase eZweni Lesithembiso. Amandla kanye nobucebi ayenakho, okwabe kuyizinto abekade ezozithokozela ukuba waqhubeka nempilo yokuba seGibhithe wakubona kuyize uma ekuqhathanisa nokuphila okuphakade. UNkulunkulu wambiza ngokukhethekile, noMose walamukela ubizo wamlandela.

Mhlawumbe into ebalulekile mayelana nalesi sihloko kungaba yilokhhu okushiwo ku-Numeri 12:3 lapho kuthiwa: Kodwa lowo muntu, uMose wayemnene kunabo bonke abantu ababesebusweni bomhlaba)” (NKJV). UMose ungomunye wezingqalabutho zokukholwa ezikhona eBhayibhelini, waziwa ngokuzehlisa kwakhe, nendlela ayethobeke ngayo. Ake ucabange ngempilo ayeyiphila kanye nobuholi ayekubona ukuthi kulempilo wakwazi ukuvumela ukuziphakamisa kungangeneleli ukuvimba lezigameko ezathi zenzeka ekuphileni kwakhe: ukubona isihlahla esasivutha, izinhlupho ezenzeka eGibhithe, ukuwela ulwandle olubomvu, nokuwa kwemana ivela ezulwini, nokwazi kwakhe ukukhuluma ebhekene noNkulunkulu ngesikhathi amukela imithetho elishumi, nangesikhathi ezwa izwi likaNkulunkulu emuva kokushaya idwala.

`Ake ubheke emuva ekuphileni kwakho. Uma bekungaba khona umuntu ongakuchaza wena, ubungakwazi yini ukufaka lezivumelwano zezenzo ezingamagama afana “nokuthobeka” noma “ukuba mnene” na? Kungani kungenzeki noma kukwazi ukwenzeka na? Iqiniso lithi angeke sikwazi ukuzehlisa ngokwethu ukwenza. Isono siyingxenye yokuphila kwethu, nokuyinto eyisizathu sokuba simdinge kakhulu uJesu. Lalela noma ufunde lamagama aleliculo elithi, “Ngikhetha ukuba noJesu” bese ubhekisia lamagama alandelayo mayelana nokuphila kukaMozisi kanye nokwakho.`
