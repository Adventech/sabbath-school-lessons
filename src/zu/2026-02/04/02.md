---
title:  Isikhali Esinamandla Kunazo Zonke
date:   19/04/2026
---

Ngaphambi kokuba siphenye ukuthi kungani iBhayibheli kuyincwadi ebaluleke kangaka nokuthi singenza kanjani ukwazi kabanzi ngolwazi oluqukethwe ngamaBhayibheli ethu, kufuneka kubekhona esizokuqonda kulokhu: Enye yezindlela ezinamandla ezingenzeka uma uSathane ekuhlasela ngukuthi akuyekise ukuba nesikhathi osichitha ufunda izwi likaNkulunkulu. Wenza abantu bangasondeli kumaBhayibheli abo ngokuthi basuke bematasa ngamabhizinisi abawenzayo, nangenxa yokunganaki, bedangele, noma benokungabaza, konke lokhu kungamaqhinga okuqala awasebenzisayo uSathane. Wazi kahle ukuthi uma uba nesikhathi osichitha noNkulunkulu ezwini lakhe, lokho kuvuselela ukuphila kwakho bese waneliseka emphefumulweni. Ngakho-ke uzokwenza konke angakwenza ukusivimbela ukufunda izwi laKhe!

Siyatshelwa ukuthi “uSathane wenza ngakho konke ukuvimbela abantu ukuba bat-hole indlela yokwazi iBhayibheli; ngoba likhuluma ngokusobala futhi liyakwazi ukuveza amaqhinga akhe okudukisa.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 593. USathane uyazi ukuthi izwi likaNkulunkulu elinamandla limenza aphelelwe ngamandla. USathane uyazi ukuthi umthandazo kanye nokufunda Izwi likaNkulunkulu, lokhu kokubili kuzikhali ezinamandla umuntu angazisebenzisa ukulwisana naye (Efesu 6:17, 18; Hebheru 4:12), ngakho-ke uSathane wenza ngakho konke anakho ukusivimba ekufundeni Izwi kanye nokuthandaza. Uyazi ukuthi izwi likaNkulunkulu linamandla angagcinanga ngokukhulunywa kwase kudaleka lomhlaba (iHubo 33:6), kodwa lakwazi nokuvusa abafileyo (Johane 11:41-44) futhi lisinika amandla okwazi ukunqoba ukwenza okubi (Mathewu 4:1-11).

Ngokuthi kuvinjelwe abantu ukuba bangalifundi iBhayibheli, uSathane akagcini ngokuthikameza ubudlelwano esinabo noNkulunkulu kodwa kugcina kuthikameza ngisho nobudlelano esinabo nabanye abantu. Imishado yethu isebucayini, sihlukumeza ngisho nabantwana bethu, asikwazi ukubekezelela abangani noma abantu esisebenza nabo. Impilo ibonakala igcwele ubuphithiphithi; sinomuzwa wokucindezeleka nowokusindwa nayizinkinga, kungekho nentuba esingabaleka siphume kuyo. Okumangalisayo, kasiziniki isikhathi esanele sokuthi kesibheke ukuthi kwenzekani. Singaba nomcabango wokuthi sisasondele kuNkulunkulu, kodwa okusempeleni, uma kulokhu kudlula izinsuku namaviki singazange silokothe sivule izwi likaNkulunkulu, siya ngokuya siba buthakathaka nsuku zonke.

`Noma ngabe singaba nezimo ezibheke phansi naphezulu futhi kunobudlelwano obungaqondakali noNkulunkulu, kodwa kuyamangalisa ukuthi uNkulunkulu uhlala esesimweni esisodwa esingajiki, njengoba kushiwo ku- Isililo 3:22, 23. Kungabe uphawula ukuthini kulamavesi, nokuthi ungawaqhathanisa kanjani nesimo sethu njengabantu na?`

Njengoba ikherubhi elizisibekelayo ngaphambi kokuba liwe (Hezekiya 28:14-17), uLusifa walizwa izwi likaNkulunkulu waphinde wawazi amandla amangalisayo alelizwi. Njenganje uyalizonda leliqiniso, ngakho-ke kusobala ukuthi kungani ukucabanga kwethu kuya ngokuya kuba buthuntu nezinhliziyo zethu ziya ngokuya ziphola njengoba singafuni ukukhetha ukuzwa futhi silethe izwi likaNkulunkulu ekuphileni kwethu kwansuku zonke.

`Kungabe unokungabaza noma nokungaqinisiki ekukhonzeni uNkulunkulu ekuphileni kwakho na? Kungabe impendulo yakho ikutshela ukuthi kufuneka wenze ziphi izinguquko, uma kungukuthi kukhona isidingo sazo na?`
