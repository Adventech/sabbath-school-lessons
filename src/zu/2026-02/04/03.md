---
title:  Imibhalo, Eyigunya
date:   20/04/2026
---

`Igunya leBhayibheli nomsebenzi walo kubekwe kahle emakhasini alo. Funda bese ukopisha lokhu okutholakala ku-2 Thimothewu 3:15-17. Qikelela ukuthi lamavesi akutshela okuthile mayelana nomsebenzi weBhayibheli.`

Uma sinaka njengabantu abazifundela iBhayibheli, kufuneka sicophelele ukuthi iBhayibheli kalifezi izinhloso noma indlela abona ngayo umuntu, nokungaba yinto engeke ifane nalokhu okufunwa nguNkulunkulu. Isibonelo, akufuneki silisebenzise ngokuthi “sivale amehlo bese sikhomba ngomunwe ivesi” bese sithi yiyona ndlela yokufunda imiBhalo, ngoba ukwenza okufana nalokhu akusiyona indlela uNkulunkulu afuna sixhumane ngayo nezwi laKhe. UNkulunkulu akasiyena upopayi olengiswe entanjeni, okulindeleke enza lokhu esikufunayo nalokhu esifisa kwenzeke. Izindlela kanye nemicabango yakhe kuphakeme kakhulu kunokucabanga kwethu (Isaya 55:9), ngakho-ke akudingeki sizame ukulawula amazwi aKhe abhekiswe kithi. Futhi kakufuneki sikhethe esikuthandayo sifunde kuphela amavesi aseBhayibhelini esiwezwa ekhuluma kahle kithi. Okunalokho, kufuneka iBhayibheli silibheke ngokuphelela kwalo kunokuba sifunde lapho kulula khona, sifinde amavesi ajwayelekile bese sishiya ngaphandle lawo ashayisana nathi noma ayinselelo kithina. Uma sifisa ngempela uNkulunkulu akhulume nokuphila kwethu, kufuneka iBhayibheli silinake lonke ngokuphelele bese sisebenzisa indlela enomqondo uma silifunda ngokucophelela, sithembe ukuthi uNkulunkulu uzokuba nathi ukwambula lokhu afuna sikuzwe uma kudingeka samukele lokho akushoyo.

Nakhu okunye, uJesu usitshela ukuthi: “ ‘Woyithanda iNkosi uNkulunkulu wakho ngayo yonke inhliziyo yakho, nangawo wonke umphefumulo wakho, nangayo yonke ingqondo yakho’ ” (Mat. 22:37, NKJV). Lokhu kusho ukuthi, uNkulunkulu akafuni singayinaki ingqondo yethu; okunalokho, ufuna ukutshela izingqondo zethu ngamathuba anothile olwazi kanye nokuqonda ngokuyikho, nokuyinto esimbulelwe yona, nokuyingxenye, yalokhu esikuthola eZwini laKhe. Singafunda izinto eziningi ezishiwo yiBhayibheli lapho uNkulunkulu exoxisana nabantu abafana no-Enoki, no-Abrahama, no-Mozisi kanye no-Jobe, nalokhu okwakuthayisela ezintweni eziningi ezazikhulunywa nguJesu ngesikhathi ekhuluma nabantu. UNkulunkulu akwenzeki angakunaki ukucabanga kwabantu kodwa uyasimema ukuba sikwethule ezwini lakhe futhi siba nokuhlakanipha uma “sisebenzela” ukusindiswa kwethu.

Ngebhadi, ukucabanga kwabantu kusalokhu kulingana nezinga abaphila kulonaukucabanga okugcwele inkohliso namaphutha. Akukwazi ukungabi namaphutha. Angeke kwenzeke ukuthi ukucabanga komuntu kubeke uNkulunkulu eceleni lapho ezozama ukwenza izinto ngamandla anawo. Lokhu kufana nokuzibeka ezingeni elilinganayo, noma elingaphezulu kunokuhlakanipha kukaNkulunkulu. Abantu bangayihlola iMibhalo benokucabanga kokuzazisa futhi belifunda ngomoya wokubheka okungalungile, benokucabanga okugcwele ukuthi sebeke bakuzwa lokhu ngaphambili, nokuthi akukho okusha abazotshelwa khona. Uma sizibona sibalulekile, sinokuzithemba, sanelisekile ngalokhu esesikwazi, singadingi lutho okungenza singabunaki ubudlelwane bethu noNkulunkulu silokhu sibambelela olwazini olungaphelele nolugcwele ukucabanga okunamaphutha.
