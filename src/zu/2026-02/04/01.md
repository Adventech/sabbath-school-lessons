### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Isililo 3:22, 23; 2 Thimothewu 3:1517; Johane 17:17; Efesu 1:13; iHubo 119:11; 1 Korinte 2;14.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ngokuba izwi likaNkulunkulu liphilile, linamandla, libukhali kunezinkemba zonke ezisika nhlangothi zombili, lihlaba kuze kwahlukaniswe umphefumulo nomoya, amalunga nomnkatsha, lahlulela imicabango nezizindlo zenhliziyo” (Heberu 4:12, NJKV).

IBhayibheli. Akungabazeki ukuthi unalo elilodwa noma unamaBhayibheli amaningi. Kuwona wonke umlando, lencwadi enhle kangaka ibilokhu yandiswa, nakho lokhu kuyinto eyenziwa ekufihlakaleni, lisatshalaliswe isinyelela, ungavunyelwe umphakathi ukuba ubenalo. IBhayibheli yiyona ncwadi esisatshalaliswe umhlaba wonke, lilokhu libhalwa kunoma yiluphi ulwimi, nokuthi lencwadi yiyona endala kakhulu ukwedlula zonke ezinye izincwadi. Abanye bakhetha ukuba bafe ukuze iBhayibheli lizokwazi ukugcineka, lihlale likhona.

Kungabe likuphi iBhayibheli empilweni yakho na? Kungabe uyalifunda noma lizihlalele eduze kombhede wakho noma lapho kuhlala khona izincwadi esezigcwele izintuli na? Kungabe uxakeke kakhulu ngalendlela yokuthi kawusasitholi isikhathi sokufunda Izwi likaNkulunkulu noma uzizwa ukhathele kakhulu ngangokuba awusakwazi nokuvula amakhasi eBhayibheli na?

Izwi likaNkulunkulu lingukuphila futhi linamandla. UNkulunkulu uyakumema ukuba ulivumele likhulume enhliziyweni yakho, likuvuselele, liphinde likuphonsele inselelo futhi likwazi ukukwenza uphenduke, liphinde likukhombise indlela okufuneka uyilandela futhi likuvuselele ithemba.

IBhayibheli aliyona incwadi yokufundwa ukukhulisa ulwazi nje kuphela noma incwadi eyiqoqo lezindaba ezenzeka kudala. Okunalokho, liyincwadi enhle kakhulu, elandisa kabanzi lapho uMdali wezwe lonke ezama ukusisondeza eduzane naYe. Uma unesifiso sokukhula kubudlelwano bakho noNkulunkulu, into engcona kakhulu ongayenza ngukuthi uzinike isikhathi esanele kanye naYe, ulokhu uthandaza, uphinde ufunde Izwi laKhe eliphefumulelwe, ugcine ngokuthi uzinikele ngokuphelele kulokhu akufundisa khona.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UMbasa 25._
