---
title:  Okushiwo YiBhayibheli
date:   22/04/2026
---

Yini engaba wuguquko emzini wakho uma ukhetha ukuvula iBhayibheli ngesikhathi ubhekana nesimo sokuthatha izinqumo ezinzima, izinqumo eziphathelene nobudlelwano, noma kukhona izinselelo obhekana nazo na? Yini engaguquka emsebenzini owenzayo noma esontweni uma kungukuthi amazwi avela eBhayibhelini aba yisibuko lapho abantu bebuka khona okwenzeka emhlabeni bese bekhetha ukuphila ngalo na?

Ababhali beBhayibheli babazi ukuthi abaluleke kangakanani amazwi aseBhayibhelini. Ayikho enye incwadi engakhuluma empilweni yakho ukwedlula amazwi afana nalawa. Amazwi azihlalele emakhasini eBhayibheli, kodwa angenza kanjani ukuba uwagcine enhliziyweni yakho na?

`Kungabe sithini isaluleko sika-Davide encwadini yaMaHubo 119:11, nokuthi ungenza kanjani ukuba ukwazi ukulandela amazwi ashiwo lapha na? (Funda futhi Heb. 4:12.)`

Enye yezinto ezishiwo yiBhayibheli ngalo okutholakala kuMaHebheru 4:12. Inkemba esika nhlangothi zombili inamandla futhi ibukhali kakhulu, kodwa iBhayibheli lona lingenza into engeke yenziwe yithuluzi emphefumuleni womuntu. IBhayibheli lizichaza ngokuthi liyinto ephilayo. Mhlawumbe ungaba nokumangala ukuthi kungabe lokhu kuyinto enjani, njengoba sazi ukuthi labhalwa eminyakeni ezinkulungwane eyadlula, kodwa uJesu wathi, “ ‘Amazwi engiwakhulume kini angumoya, angukuphila’ ” (Johane 6:63, NKJV). Uma inhliziyo yakho yephukile noma impilo yakho iphithene, uNkulunkulu angakhuluma amazwi aKhe enhliziyweni yakho azokwazi ukuphendula izinto. IThestamente elidala lichaza Izwi likaNkulunkulu njengento esebenzayo futhi kalisiyona into ethule engasho lutho noma into engathi shu (bheka ku- Isaya 55:11). Ngesikhathi uDavida ephawula ngamandla okusebenza kwezwi likaNkulunkulu empilweni yakhe, wabhala ukuthi, “Lokhu kuyinduduzo ekuhluphekeni kwami, ngokuba izwi lakho lingiphilisile” (Hub. 119:50, NKJV).

Mhlawumbe usuke wahlaselwa yindlala enkulu kumava odlule kuwo ekuphileni kwakho, noma mhlawumbe uke wazila noma washintsha indlela odla ngayo. Kungabe kuzwakala kumnandi kangakanani ukudla emva kokulamba odlule kukhona na? Nasesimweni sezinto zikamoyo, iBhayibheli lingukudla emphefumulweni wethu.

Uma umphefumulo wakho uzizwa ungenalutho futhi ulambile, kudingeka uvulele amazwi okuphila. Funda ku-Jeremiya 15:16; 1 Petro 2:2; kanye naku-Mathewu 4:4. Amazwi kaNkulunkulu ezwakala emnandi emqondweni kanye nasenhliziyweni ngesikhathi uwafunda, azokwazi ukusanelisa aphinde asilondoloze njengoba sithenjisiwe.

Amazwi atholakala eBhayibhelini avela kuNkulunkulu uqobo lwaKhe. UNkulunkulu uthumazela amazwi aKhe ukuba eze kithina kanye nakwabanye abantu abafuna ukumthola. Uma siwafunda sinomthandazo, sivule izinhliziyo zethu, amazwi esizowathola lapho angeke abe yize.

`Kungabe yiziphi izingqinamba obhekene nazo njengamanje iBhayibheli elikusizile ukuzixazulula na? Kungani ungeke uvumele ubuwena ukuba bukuvimbe kulokhu elikushoyo nokufuneka ukulandele na?`
