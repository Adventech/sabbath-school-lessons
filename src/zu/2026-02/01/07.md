---
title:  Ukucabanga Okubanzi
date:   03/04/2026
---

Ngaphambi kokuba sizalwe, uNkulunkulu wasithanda; wayenohlelo lokusazi nokuthi nathi kudingeka simazi Yena. Usifuna lapho sikhona, kuhle kukamelusi olungileyo, bese esimema ukuba sihlale kuye nsuku zonke. Sidinga ukuba sikwazi ukuzikhethela ukwamukela ukubizwa nguYe, bese sikwazi ukwabelana naYe ubuze bethu kanye nesimo sethu esifana nebandla lase- Lawodekeya ukuze sithole izipho zakhe ezinhle. (Isambulo 3:18, 19).

Njengokuba kukhula kancane amagatsha esihlahla somvini, ubudlelwano bethu noNkulunkulu kungenzeka bukhule kancane, noma bubonakale buqhakambe ngokushesha ngenxa yokufika kwemvula ebikade idingeka. Noma ngabe kwenzeka ngaliphi izinga ukukhulula kwethu kanye nokuthela kwezithelo ezibonakalayo ekuphileni kwethu, siyakudinga ukulokhu siphuza kumsuka noma kuMoya oNgcwele, ukuze siqiniseke ngokuthi silokhu sisaxhumene noJesu.

“Ukuhlala kuKristu kusho ukuhlala njalo ulokhu uthola uMoya oNgcwele, nokuyimpilo yokuzidela ngokuthi sizinikele ekwenzeni umsebenzi waKhe. Umgudu wokuxhumana kudingeka uhlale uvulekile phakathi komuntu kanye noNkulunkulu. Njengomvini olokhu udonsa ukuphila kumsuka wokuphila kwawo, kanjalo nathi kudingeka sinamathele kuJesu, ukuze sizokwazi ukuthola kuYe ngokukholwa amandla kanye nokupheleliswa kwesimilo saKhe kithina.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 676.

“Kungenzeka kanjani ukuthi igatsha elomile nelingaxhumene nesiqu likwazi ukuba ndawonye nesiqu esingumzali waso na? Kungenzeka kanjani ukuthi ube yingxenye yokuphila unethezeke kumvini onokuphila na? Lokhu kungenzeka ngokuthi unamathele kumvini, ngokuthi ulethwe ukuba usondelane nobudlelwano. Kube nokuxhumana kwegxolo nelinye igxolo, umthambo nomunye umthambo, igatsha libambelela kakhulu esiqwini esiphilayo ukuze ukuphila okukhona kumvini kugcine kuyinto eyodwa nokuphila okusemagatsheni, ukuze igatsha lizokwazi ukuthela izithelo ezitholakala kumvini.”—Ellen G. White, manuscript 67, 1897.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Ake ubheke emuva ekuphileni kwakho. Ungakwazi ukubona isigameko esake sabakhona ekuphileni kwakho nesikwenze waheheka ukuba ufane nesimo sabantu bebandla lase-Lawodekeya kwezikamoya na? Kungabe yiziphi izinto ezikusondeza kuNkulunkulu na?`

`U-Ellen G. White ukhuluma ngokuthi “ukuhlala njalo wamukela uMoya kaNkulunkulu.” Kungabe kukangaki uzithola uthandazela uMoya oNgcwele na? Yini engaguquka uma kungukuthi uyawuthola uMoya oNgcwele nsuku zonke na?`

`Yini engaguquka uma kungukuthi thina, njengebandla, besingakhulekela ukuthola uMoya oNgcwele sikhuleka ngokweqiniso futhi sikwenza njalo lokhu na?`

`Ake uzikhathaze, uzwe nobuhlungu, ukhulumisane nawe ngokweqiniso mayelana nobudlelwano phakathi kwakho noNkulunkulu. Yiziphi izinto unembeza wakho ozidingayo ukuze ukwazi ukusondela kakhulu kuNkulunkulu nokuyinto ayifunayo kodwa wena ulokhu uzindela na?`

**Ukugoqa Isifundo Saleliviki:** Ngaphambi kokuba siqale ukukhula ebudlelwaneni noNkulunkulu, okokuqala kudingeka sizindle ngesimo sobudlelwane esinabo kunye naYe sizibone ukuthi ngabe kuyinto emi kanjani. Uma kungokwase-Lawodekeya noma amagatsha ethu awazitheli izithelo, uJesu unesisombululo esikulungele ukubhekana nesimo sakho kwezikamoya: kudingeka uhlale kuYena.
