---
title:  Amanzi Omuthi
date:   02/04/2026
---

Ukuhlala kuKristu kungaba nomuzwa wokuthi ukwenza lokhu kulukhuni. Kungenzeka sazi ukuthi siyakudinga lokhu, kodwa izimo zokuphila zisidonsela kolunye uhlangothi, bese siba nomuzwa wokuthi lento inzima kakhulu. Ukuba ngumlandeli kaNkulunkulu kungabonakala kungumthwalo onzima kakhulu, ekakhulu kulabo abalokhu bebeleselwa ngabanye abantu bezama ukubenza ukuba bagcine bengabalandeli bakaJesu. Ivangeli elifana naleli lingagcina limenze umuntu azizwe eseyisidina ngoba konke lokhu kumayelana nalokhu esibona kwenziwa kunokuba kube yinto ephathelene nomuzwa okhona ngaphakathi kumuntu. Akukho okubonakala kungenzeke uNkulunkulu lokhu afuna kwenzeke, okufana nokwakheka kobudlelwano obusekelwe esimweni sokuthandana, okungeyona imithetho kuphela, kodwa ubudlelwano bokusondelana kakhulu (nokuyilapho kunguYena okukhethe kuqala) kulokhu kuthandana okuhambisana nelungelo lokukhetha ngokuzithandela.

Kungenzeka kwesinye isikhathi sibe ngabantu abaxhumene nomvini kodwa sibe singaxhumene ngokuphelele nalomvini. Singabonakala siya ezinkonzweni, sithandaza, siphinde senze konke okulungile, kodwa ingaphakathi lethu linomuzwa wokungakwenameli lokhu esikwenzayo. Iqiniso lithi: Angeke sihlale kuJesu ngokwethu njengoba negatsha lingeke likwazi ukuzixhumanisa nesihlahla somvini. UNkulunkulu nguyena osithandile kuqala; nguyena othathe igxathu kuqala ukuba kwenzeke lokhu. Konke okwenziwa yithina sekungukuvuma lokhu uNkulunkulu akwenze kuqala enzela thina.

Uma ubheka ukuthi isihlahla somvini siphila kanjani ngesikhathi sasebusika, uzokwazi ukufunda izinto ezithathekisayo ngalokhu – amaqabunga asemagatsheni agcina esomile futhi engasakwazi ukukhula kuze kufike isikhathi sasentwasahlobo. Uma inhlabathi isifudumala, nezimpande sezikwazi ukudonsa amanzi, bese lokhu okungamanzi omuthi sekukhuphuka kuhamba ngesiqu somvini kugcine kufike kumaqabunga azokwazi ukuqala akhule. Ngaphandle kwamanzi omuthi ukuba ukwazi ukuguduza esiqwini somvini, akukho ukukhula okuzokwenzeka.

Amanzi omuthi esiqwini somvini afana noMoya oNgcwele ekuphileni kwethu. Singabonakala sifana namagatsha abunile, kodwa uma sikhetha ukuchitha isikhathi noNkulunkulu, uMoya oNgcwele uzothululeka phezu kwethu bese lokhu kufana nomsuka oqala ezimpandeni ugcine usenze sibe nokuphila okuzosenza sikhule. Ngendlela efanayo sidinga ukuba nonembeza ozokwazi ukusenza sikhethe ukuhlala kuJesu, kudingeka siphinde sicele uMoya oNgcwele (ongumsuka) ukuba ukwazi ukubakhona ekuphileni kwethu.

`Funda ku-Luka 11:13 kuhambisane naku- Jeremiya 31:3; 1 Johane 4:19; kanye KwabaseRoma 8:9-11. Kungabe ngumuphi umyalezo obalulekile esiwuthola lapha na?`

Okusempeleni, nguMoya oNgcwele owenza ukuba sikwazi ukukhula futhi nosinika isiqiniseko sokuthi Siyathuthuka futhi sibambelele esiqwini soMvini. Kudingeka sicele uMoya oNgcwele nsuku zonke, nokuyinto ekhona ngaphakathi kithi ukuze:

- Usiduduze (Johane 14:16-18).
- Wembule uKristu kithina (Johane 15:26).
- Usiqondise ukuze singoni (Johane 16:7, 8).
- Usiholela kulona lonke iqiniso (Johane 16:13).

`Ake uphinde ulifunde loluhlu olungenhla. Kungenzeka kanjani ukuba iphuzu ngalinye eliyisenzo sikaMoya oNgcwele lingakwazi ukuba nomthelela kubudlelwane bakho noNkulunkulu na?`
