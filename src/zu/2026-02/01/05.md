---
title:  Hlalani
date:   01/04/2026
---

Abafundi bakaJesu bamlandela behla naye besuka egumbini eliphezulu behla ngezitebhisi ezincane, behlela ngezansi beya emgwaqeni. Njengoba babelokhu behamba ndawonye ukuya eGetsemane, ngobusuku obabubalulekile emlandweni wokusekelwa komhlaba, kungenzeka ukuthi babengenalo ulwazi ngamagama okugcina ashiwo nguJesu ewabhekise kubona ngesikhathi besendlini engenhla.

`Kungabe uJesu wathini kuJohane 15:1–11 na?`

Lamagama ashiwo nguJesu uqobo lwaKhe, ayechaza ukuthi ubudlelwano bokusondelana noNkulunkulu buyinto emi kanjani. Ake ubhekisise igama elilokhu liphindwaphindwa, lingaphindwa kabili kodwa liphindwa kashumi: Hlalani. Ukuhlala kuJesu kungukuphila ngokuxhumana kunye naYe.

Ngesikhathi ebhekene nesiphambano, uJesu akagcinanga ngokugcizelela kokubaluleka kokuhlala kuYena, kodwa wakuveza kwaba sobala ukuthi isimo sizobonakala sinjani ezimpilweni zethu.

Okungukuthi: UJesu ungumvini, thina singamagatsha. Umphumela wokuthi sihlale kuYena (njengoba sixhumene naYe), kuveza izithelo emagatsheni. Angeke sikwazi ukwenza kuvele izithelo ngokwethu. Kwesinye isikhathi singabonakala engazathi sihlezi kuyena, kodwa kungabi bikho ubufakazi ngoba kungaveli izithelo, bese kugcina ngokuthi amagatsha angabi nayo impilo, ome. Uma esomile, umlimi wensimu yomvini uzogcina ngokuwaphundla amagatsha omvini. Noma ngabe zikhona noma kazikho izithelo, kuzoqhubeka ukuphundlwa kwamagatsha.

Ngesikhathi esifanayo, sonke sisenkingeni yokubhekana nezinselelo kanye nokuzwa ubuhlungu. Uma sihlala kuYe, lezikhathi zokubhekana nalezinselelo kuzokuba khona izithelo ezizovela ngokuhamba kwesikhathi. Ukuba khona kwezithelo kufakazela ukuthi (singabafundi). Sikwazi ukuthela izithelo senzela Yena (singazenzeli thina) udumo. Ukuhlala kuJesu kusho ukuthi siyayigcina imithetho yaKhe, nokuyinto ebonakala ngokuthi sibe nesimilo sokuziphatha kahle singabi nothando olugcwele umona. Ukuhlala kuJesu kusilethela ukujabula okukhulu. Ukuhlala kuJesu kusho ukwenza lokhu afisa ukuba sikwenze, ukukhombisa ukuthi siyamuzwa ekhuluma nathi. “Ngokuba ukuthanda uNkulunkulu yilokhu ukuba sigcine imiyalo yakhe; nemiyalo yakhe ayinzima” (1 Johane 5:3, NKJV).

Uma ucabanga ngalokhu, ukuhlala kuJesu kungenye yezinto eziyisilapho sokuba nesimo esasikhona ebandleni lase-Lawodesiya (Isambulo 3:20, Johane 15:4). Lokhu kuyimfihlo enkulu yokugcwaliseka kokuba nokuphila okunomqondo emhlabeni kanye nokuphila kwangunaphakade. Ngeshwa, kuyenzeka sikukhohlwe kalula lokhu kuyala kukaJesu.

Ekugcineni, uJesu ukhuluma lokhu komunye nomunye wethu uthi, “ ‘Njengalokhu uBaba engithandile nami nginithandile; hlalani othandweni lwami’ ” (Johane 15:9, NKJV). Uthando lukaJesu yisona sisombululo esisidonsela kuYe, futhi uma silwazi loluthando, sizokujabulela kakhulu ukuthanda uNkulunkulu sikwazi nokuthanda nabanye abantu.
