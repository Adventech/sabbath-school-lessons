---
title:  Ukucabanga Okubanzi
date:   08/05/2026
---

Ekugcineni, kufuneka sikhuleke ngoba simthanda kakhulu uNkulunkulu, futhi ngeke kusisize ukwabelana ngazo zonke izinto noNkulunkulu: Ukujabula esinakho kanye nalokhu “esikuzuzile,” imithwalo nokukhathazeka esinako, izicelo kanye nalokhu esikudingayo. “Singakwazi ukusondelana noNkulunkulu ngalendlela yokuthi kunoma yikuphi ukulingwa esingakulindele akukho okunye esingakucabanga ngaphandle kokuncika kuYe kuhle kwembali igobela ngalapho kunemisebe yelanga, ukujabula kwakho, ubuhlungu okubona, izinkinga obhekana nazo, nokusaba onakho phambi kukaNkulunkulu. Angeke umenze asindwe yilemithwalo uNkulunkulu, angeke kumkhathaze lokhu Yena, lowo okwazi ukubala izinwele ezisekhanda lakho akehluleki kuzidingo zabantwana bakhe. . . . Inhliziyo yaKhe yokuba nothando iba nokuthinteka ngobuhlungu esibhekana nabo kanye nangendlela esiphimisela ngayo lezinhlungu. Akukho okukhulu kakhulu angeke akwazi ukubhekana nakho Yena, ngoba wonke umhlaba usezandleni zaKhe, uyabusa kuzona zonke izinto ezikhona ezweni. Akukho into ekhona ephazamisa ukuthula kwethu angeke ayazi noma ngabe incane kangakanani. Akukho isahluko samava okuphila esike sabhekana nawo nesifana nefu elimnyama angeke akwazi ukusiqonda Yena. Ayikho ingxaki engaba khona elikhuni kakhulu nangeke akwazi ukuyixazulula. Kasikho isigameko esibuhlungu esingehlela abantwana bakhe abangelutho, akukho ukukhathazeka okungahlukumeza umphefumulo, akukho nokujabula, akukho nomthandazo owenziwe ngokuqiniseka ophuma ezindebeni zomlomo, nokungaba yizinto uBaba osezulwini angeke azinake, noma angeke akuthathele izinyathelo ngokushesha. . . . Ukuxhumana okukhona phakathi kukaNkulunkulu nomphefumulo ngamunye kuyinto egqamile nebonakalayo okungazathi akekho omunye umuntu okhona emhlabeni okufuneka amnake, akunawo nowodwa umphefumulo angazange awunikelele iNdodana yaKhe ethandiweyo.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, pp. 99, 100.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Kungenzeka uchaze ukuthandaza njengento enhle noma kuyinto engumthwalo na? Kungabe yini oyizuzile esimweni obona ngaso na?`

`Kunemibono eminingi etholakala kusicaphuno esibhalwe ngenhla. Kungabe yimuphi umbono okuhlabe umxhwele na?`

`Kulezibonelo zabantu abathathu esifundile ngabo kuleliviki (uDaniyeli, u-Enoki, no-Mose), kungabe ngumuphi umuntu futhi kungani ungakwazi ukuzihlanganisa nomunye wabo ngendlela ayekhuleka ngayo na?`

**Ukugoqa Isifundo Saleliviki:** Njengoba besifunda ngokuthandaza kwamaqhawe akhona eBhayibhelini, kungaba yinto elula ukucabanga ukuthi angeke sibe nobudlelwano bokusondela kuNkulunkulu noma sibe nokuzinikela njengalamaqhawe. Kodwa singakwazi ukukwenza lokhu. Njengo-Daniyeli, singakwazi ukubambelela futhi sibe nokukholeka ngokuthi siguqe sithandaze nsuku zonke, yize kungabakhona izimo ezingasivumeli. Ngendlela efanayo neka-Enoke, singakukhetha ukuhamba siphinde sikhulume noNkulunkulu, siphendukele kuYena ngaphambi kokuba senze umsebenzi asibekele ukuba siwenze. Singenza njengo-Mose, ngokuthi sikwazi ukuhola abantu emphakathini uma sikhetha ukuhlala ngaphansi kwethunzi lika-Somandla, onguMholi kanye noMngani wethu.
