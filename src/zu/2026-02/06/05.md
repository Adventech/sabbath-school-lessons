---
title:  UMose, Umholi OnobuNkulunkulu
date:   06/05/2026
---

Yize u-Enoke kwakucacile ukuthi unobudlelwano bokusondelana kakhulu noNkulunkulu, kodwa thina sinokukhanyiseleka ngobudlelwano bukaMose kanye noNkulunkulu futhi singakwazi ukufunda ngezigameko eziningi lapho uMose aba nokuxoxisana kunye naYe. Njengoba sizohambisana noMose ngesikhathi edlula kumaginxi-gonxo okuphila kwakhe kokuba ngomholi ozithobile, sizokwazi ukubona ukuthi izikhathi eziningi kunento eyodwa eyabe ibalulekile ekuphileni nokwabe kuyimfihlo yokuphumelela ayenakho njengomholi owayenobuNkulunkulu nokuyiyona ndlela yokuhlala exhumene aphinde ahlale kubudlelwano noNkulunkulu.

`Funda ku-Eksodusi 33:15-23. Kungabe yiluphi ulwazi oluqukethwe lapha kanye nendlela okuxoxiswana ngayo phakathi kukaMose noNkulunkulu na?`

Ake ucabange ukuthi bekuzokuba yinto enjani ukwazi ukukhuluma noNkulunkulu uphinde ulizwe kahle ngokucacile izwi laKhe. Kuyamangalisa ukuthi u-Israyeli kazange akufune ukuba naloluhlobo lokuxhumana noNkulunkulu lokubonana naYe njengesizwe esikhundleni sokuncenga uMose ukuba ayobakhulumela kuNkulunkulu (Eksodusi 20:18-21). Noma kunjalo, uNkulunkulu wayemlungisile uMose ukuba akulungele ukwenza lomsebenzi, emlungiselela lomsebenzi kusukela mhla kubakhona ukuxoxisana kwabo beseduzane nesihlahla esabe sivutha, konke lokhu kwenzeka kuyona lentaba. Yize sifundile ngeminye imithandazo eyenziwa nguMose, ecela uNkulunkulu amkhombise ukuthi enze kanjani siphinde simuzwe encengela abantu ayebahola.

**Izikhathi ezimbili, uMose wancengela amalunga omndeni wakhe. Kungabe yisiphi isigameko esabe sizungeze ukuncenga kwakhe, futhi kwakuzokwenzekani ukube akazange aqhamuke ukuqeda ukungezwani na?**

`Aaron: Eksodusi 32:1–14, 31–34, Dut. 9:20`

`Miriam: Num. 12:13`

Into emangalisayo mayelana nokubakhona kokuxaxisana noMiriyamu ngukuthi uMose kwakunguye ongumgilwa ngokuthi bamkhombise ukuba nomona obhekiswe kuyena waphinde waphathwa kabi. Kwakulula ukuba ahlehlele emuva bese evumela uNkulunkulu ukuba kube nguyena oziphindiselela kuMiriyamu kanye no-Aroni njengoba lokhu kwakubafanele.

Kunalokho, waba ngowokuqala ukubathethelela waphinde wancenga ukuthi kuphiliswe udadewabo. Lokhu kungumfanekiso obalulekile ukukhombisa indlela yomusa uNkulunkulu athethelela ngayo izoni nokuyinto ebonakalayo kulokhu kwenza kukaMose.

`Funda ku-Mathewu 5:44 naku Kolose 3:13. Ungafunda kanjani ukwenza lokhu okutshelwe lapha na? Kungani kubalulekile ukuba ukwenze lokhu okutshelwe kulamavesi na?`
