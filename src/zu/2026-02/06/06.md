---
title:  UMose Unxusela Isizwe
date:   07/05/2026
---

`Funda ku-Eksodusi 32:31, 32. Kungabe lokhu kusifundisani ngoMose kanye nomthandazo na?`

UMose waba nesibindi sokuncengela abantu bakaNkulunkulu ekwenza lokhu kaningi ningi. UMose waguqukela kuNkulunkulu ngesikhathi abantu bomile befuna ukuphuza amanzi (Eksodusi 15:25; Eksodusi 17:2-6), nangesikhathi abantu belambile (Numeri 11:21, 22), nangesikhathi bengenakho ukwaneliseka (Numeri 11:11-15).

Ngesikhathi u-Israyeli akha ithole legolide emuva kancane kokuba uNkulunkulu eqeda ukwenza isivumelwano nabo, uMose wakhumbula ukuthi, “ ‘Ngokuba ngangesaba intukuthelo nolaka uJehova anithukuthelela ngakho ukuba anibhubhise; kepha uJehova wangizwa nangaleso sikhathi’ ” (Dut. 9:19, NKJV).

Ngesikhathi kubuya izinhloli ukuyohlola Izwe lesiThembiso, uMose wakhumbula ukuthi, “ ‘Ngawela phansi phambi kukaJehova lezo zinsuku ezingamashumi amane engawa ngazo, ngokuba uJehova wayeshilo ukuthi uyakunibhubhisa’ ” (Dut. 9:25, NKJV).

Ngesikhathi isizwe sakwa-Levi sehlukene nezinye izizwe ukuze sizokwazi ukuba sendlini kaNkulunkulu eNgcwele (Sanctuary), uMose wakhumbula ukuthi, “ ‘Ngahlala entabeni njengakuqala izinsuku ezingamashumi amane nobusuku obungamashumi amane; uJehova wangilalela nangaleso sikhathi, akanibhubhisanga’ ” (Dut. 10:10, NKJV). UNkulunkulu wakulalela ukuncenga kukaMose.

Kuningi esingakufunda ngempilo kaMose uma sikhuluma ngokuthandaza kanye nokubambelela ngempela kuNkulunkulu:

- **UMozisi wayenothando olujulile lukaNkulzunkulu futhi enesithombe esicacile sesimilo saKhe.** UNkulunkulu wazichaza kuMose encwadini ka-Eksodusi 34:6: “ ‘NguJehova! NguJehova! NguNkulunkulu ogcwele isihe, onomusa, ophuza ukuthukuthela, novame ubumnene neqiniso’ ” (NKJV).
- **UMose wayenesibindi futhi ekholekile njengoba wakwazi ukubambelela kuNkulunkulu kunamaginxi-ginxi** okuhamba indlela ekhathazayo yokuya Ezweni Lesithembiso. Yize wayekhathazekile njengoba nathi sinokukhathazeka, uMose wawathemba amandla, nokubakhona kanye nobuholi bukaNkulunkulu ekuphileni kwakhe (Eksodusi 33:13).
- **UMose wakhumbuza uNkulunkulu ngesivumelwano asenzileyo (Eksod. 32:13), wafuna kufezwe izithembiso uNkulunkulu azenzile, enzela abantu baKhe (Dut. 7:8), wakhumbula nangokuhola kukaNkulunkulu akwenzile esikhathini esidlulileyo (Dut. 8:2).**
- **UMose wazamukela izimpendulo zikaNkulunkulu zemithandazo yakhe, noma ngabe izismpendulo zazingu-Yebo noma zingu-Qha.** Uma unokusondelana kobudlelwano noNkulunkulu lokho kakusho ukuthi uzokuthola konke okufunayo (Duteronomi 3:23-29), kodwa noma kunjalo, kudingeka siqhubeke nokukhuleka singakhathali (Luke 18:1-8).

`Kungabe ngubani odinga ukuba umkhulekele njengamanje na? Kungabe yini ekuvimbela ukuba umthandazele njengamanje na?`
