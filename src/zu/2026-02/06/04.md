---
title:  U-Enoke Wahamba futhi Waxoxisana
date:   05/05/2026
---

`Funda ku-Genesise 5:22-24. Okuseqinisweni, yini ngempela esiyaziyo ngo-Enoki na?`

IBhayibheli kalikhulumi okuningi ngempilo ka-Enoke, kodwa liyakhuluma ngokuthi wahamba noNkulunkulu iminyaka engu-300 kwaze kwaba uNkulunkulu uyamthatha ukumusa ezulwini. Kuyinto enhle kangakanani yokuthi uma umuntu elokhu ebambelele ekukhulekeni kuNkulunkulu lokho kugcina sekuchaza indlela abephila ngayo!

Iyodwa into okufuneka siyazi ngo-Enoke eyokuthi “ekukhulekeni wayeqinisele njalo” (Roma 12:12), ukuqinisela nokukhula ebudlelwaneni nasekusondeleni kuYe ngokukholwa okungamava ansuku zonke. Umhlaba uya ngokuya ugcwala ububi esikhathini esiphila kusona, ngokufanayo, u-Enoke wayexakekile edumisa uNkulunkulu. Wayengeke aphumelele ukwenza lokhu ngaphandle kokuhlala kuYe.

“Kubuphithiphithi bokuphila kokusebenza kwansuku zonke, u-Enoke waqhubeka nokubambelela ekuxhumaneni kwakhe noNkulunkulu. Uma kuya ngokubanzima enokucindezeleka ngenxa yemisebenzi abe eyenza, kungaleso sikhathi lapho aqhubeka nokubambelela futhi enokuqiniseka ekukhulekeni kwakhe. . . . Emuva kokuphila isikhathi esithile nabantu ayehlala nabo, eqhubeka nokusebenza eba yisibonelo esihle, waqhela endaweni ngokuthi aphume emphakathini, ukuze ezokwazi ukuthola ithuba lokuba yedwa, lapho ezothola ukulamba aphinde omele ukwazi kabanzi nokuyinto ayezoyinikwa nguNkulunkulu kuphela. Ngokuxhumana kwakhe noNkulunkulu, u-Enoki waya ngokuya ekhombisa ukuba nomfanekiso kaNkulunkulu. . . . Ngisho nabantu ababengamazi uNkulunkulu bathinteka ngokumangala ukubona isimo somuntu wasezulwini uma behlangana naye.”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, pp. 86, 87.

UNkulunkulu akadingi ukuphila njengomuntu ohlala yedwa noma umuntu ohlala esigodlweni ngaphansi kwesifungo esithile, nokuyisimo sokuqhela emhlabeni kube sengathi akaphili kulomhlaba. Njengo-Enoki, singakwazi ukuba wusizo ngokuthi sifeze izidingo zokuphila ezikhona eduzane nathi, kodwa sibe sikwazi ukuhamba siphinde sikhulume noNkulunkulu sikwenza lokhu ngesineke, sihlezi kubudlelwano bethu kunye naYe ukuze sizokwazi ukuveza isimilo saKhe esimangalisayo ngendlela esizoziphatha ngayo.

Singathandaza noma ngabe kunini, noma yikuphi. Ayikho indawo ethile emhlabeni lapho uNkulunkulu engakwazi ukubona khona noma indawo lapho angeke akwazi ukusizwa khona. (iHubo 139: 7-12); Uhlala ekulungele ukuzwa ukulila kwezinhliziyo zethu, noma ngabe sikuyiphi indawo (funda ku- Isililo 3:55-57). Noma kunjalo, kukhona okufuneka sikukhulume ngokuthi sithandaze kuzwakale kakhulu esikhundleni sokuba senze umthandazo wokuzindla. Uma senza umthandazo wokuthula, kungenzeka kubekhona ukuphazamiseka noma kubekhona isimo esizokwenza singabe sisaqhubeka nokucabanga noma siqedele umusho ebesiwucabanga, nokuyinto engenza kubenzima ukuhlala sicabanga ngokuphelele. Kodwa uma siphimisela ngokukhuluma kuzwakale, noma ngabe sikhulumela phansi noma sikhuluma ngokujwayelekile, lokhu kwenza sibe nokukhumbula emqondweni wethu ukuthi uNkulunkulu uyisimo esikhona ngempela, nokuthi ukhona ukusilalela, asinike nethuba lokusho okuthile sikhuluma ngaYe.

`Njengoba ulokhu umatasa wenza imisebenzi yakho yosuku, kungabe kukuyiphi indawo noma uzokwenza kanjani lapho uzothola khona ithuba lokusho umthandozo ngokuhleba ngesikhathi uxhumana noJesu na?`
