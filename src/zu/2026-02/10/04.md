---
title:  Ukuphenduka Kwangempela
date:   02/06/2026
---

Umhlaba wamanje wandisa ukukhuluma komyalezo wokuzimela, nokubekezelelana, nokuzithuthukisa - nokuyinto ephambene nesimemo sikaNkulunkulu wokuba zisebenzi nokuba mnene (Mathewu 5:5). Okuthathekisayo ngukuthi, igama lokuqala elibhalwe nguJohane uMbhapathizi kanye noJesu elitholakala eBhayibhelini kwabe kungamazwi afanayo. UJohane wathi “Phendukani, ngokuba umbuso wezulu ususondele!” (Mathewu 3:1, 2). UJesu wathi, “isikhathi sigcwalisekile, umbuso kaNkulunkulu ususondele; phendukani, nikholwe yivangeli” (Marko 1:14, 15; funda futhi naku- Luka 24:46, 47). Bobabili uJesu noJohane bamema abantu ababelalele ukuba baphenduke ngokuba umbuso wezulu ususondele. Kungenzeka yini ukuthi umyalezo ofanayo kusengumyalezo osabalulekile nanamuhla na?

`Funda ku-Izenzo 3: 18, 19. Kungani ukuphenduka kuyinto ebalulekile esimweni sokukhula kwezikamoya na? Kungabe yisiphi isikhathi “sokuphinda uqale kabusha” na?`

Ukulunga nokuba mnene kukaNkulunkulu kusiholela ekuphendukeni (Roma 2:4). Ukuphenduka kufaka phakathi izinyathelo ezimbili: (1) ukuzwa ubuhlungu bangempela ngesono esisenzile; kanye (2) nokuthatha isinqumo sangempela sokushiya isono. EBhayibhelini, ukuphenduka kucishe kusho into efanayo nokuthola intethelelo. Siyaphenduka ngokwangempela bese uNkulunkulu esithethelela. Kulula kanjalo lokhu (1 Johane 1:9; Isambulo 3:19). “Inkosi ayilibali ngesithembiso sayo, njengalokhu abanye bathi kungukulibala, kepha iyanibekezelela, ingathandi ukuba kubhubhe namunye, kodwa ukuba bonke beze ekuphendukeni” (2 Petru 3:9). Njengoba umuntu ngamunye elungisa umphefumulo wakhe ukwamukela ukubuya KwesiBili kukaJesu, uNkulunkulu usasinika ithuba lokuba silungise izinto naYe.

UJesu wezwa ubuhlungu, wasifela, waphinde wavuka ukuze kuthi lapho siphenduka, umusa waKhe ukwazi ukwenza isimangaliso ekuphileni kwethu. Ukukhuluma okuphambene nalokhu kwasemhlabeni kusitshela ukuthi sikahle uma sihlala sinjena, kodwa uNkulunkulu ucela ukuba sibuyele kuYena ngokuthi siphenduke bese siyakholwa (Izenzo 20:21), sizibeke ngokuphelele ezandleni zakhe ukuze ezokwazi ukusiphundla bese esenza sibe bahle esimilweni sethu asenze sifane nesaKhe, sikwazi nokufakaza ngaYe (Johane 15:2, 8). Ngokwenza kanjena siyakwazi ukukhula siphinde sithele izithelo ezinhle ukukhombisa ukuhambisana nokuphenduka (Mathewu 3:18).

“Akukho ukuphenduka kwangempela okungakwazi ukwenza ukuvuseleleka. Ukulunga kukaKristu akusiyona indwangu yokumboza isono esingazange sivunywe sase siyashiywa; ukuphenduka kuyindlela yokuphila ekwazi ukuguqula isimilo bese kulawula indlela esiziphatha ngayo.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, pp. 555, 556.

`Ukuphenduka kuholela ekuphileni (Izenzo 11:18) futhi kuyingxenye ebalulekile yokukhula ebudlelwaneni noNkulunkulu. Esimweni sokuzidela, sibe nokuphenduka, sivumela uNkulunkulu ukuba aphundle impilo yethu, kungabe yisiphi isinyathelo esiyinselelo enkulu esizokuba kuwena na?`
