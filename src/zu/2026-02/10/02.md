---
title:  Ubuphithiphithi Bokuphila
date:   31/05/2026
---

Bekuliviki elinokuxakeka okukhulu. Yize wabe azi ukuthi kuningi okufuneka akwenze ngaphambi kokufika kwe-Sabatha, ukuxakeka kwabonakala kugwinya izinto ezibalulekile, kodwa kwathi ngaphambi kokuba azi, lomuntu wabona selishonile ilanga. Umndeni wadla ndawonye isidlo esikhethekile sakusihlwa ngoLwesihlanu base bethandaza ndawonye.

Kuthe lapho sekufika iSabatha ekuseni washesha ukuvuka, wabona ukuthi indlu yokugezela ayihlanzekile, waphuthuma ukuyesula. Waphinde waphawula ukuthi indodana yakhe imanzise umbhede, ngakho-ke wasusa amashidi asengcolile wawafaka emshinini wokuwasha kanye nezinye izingubo ezingcolile. Ngesikhathi elungiselela umndeni wakhe isidlo sasekuseni, waphawula ukuthi ukudla kwasemini kushoda ngesidlo esidliwa kamuva okuthiwa yidizethi, waphuthuma wabhaka ngokushesha isinkwa sikabhanana. Waphinde wabona ukuthi kudingeka elule ihembe likababa wekhaya azoligqoka esontweni ngensimbi yoku-ayina, wakwenza nalokhu, wase elula nezinye izingubo ukuze ezokwazi ukususa ukushwabana.

Wakhumbula ukuthi namuhla wusuku lweSabatha alithanda kakhulu ukwedlula zonke ezinye izinsuku. Kodwa manje usezithola esenza zonke lezizinto ezidinga ukulungiswa nokwenza azithole ephazamiseka ekwazini ukuthi iSabatha liyini ngempela- okungukusondelana noNkulunkulu.

Okwesikhashana, lokhu ayekucabanga waqala ukukuvikela ngokubona indlela enze ngayo- ukuthi konke lokhu akwenzile bekudinga ukulungiswa. Kungabe ngempela kwakufuneka ekwenzile lokhu akwenza na? Wazibona esenza ngendlela efanayo neyenziwa ngu-Marta, “ekhathazekile ukusebenza okuningi” (Luka 10:40), kodwa amazwi kaJesu ahlala engqondweni yakhe: “Marta, Marta, unakekela, ukhathazeka ngezinto eziningi. Kodwa inye into edingekile; uMariya ukhethile isabelo esihle angayikwaphucwa sona” (Luka 10:41, 42). Lendawana yezwakala kahle. Ukuhlala ezinyaweni zikaJesu, egcwele uthando lwaKhe-engamthandi ngeSabatha kuphela, kodwa emthanda zonke izinsuku. Akazange akhethe lokhu ngokusa kwangalelo langa kuphela.

U-Mariya wayemthanda uNkulunkulu, yize kwakuyinto elula ukulibala ukuthi uJesu umnike iSabatha njengesipho sokuba nesikhathi sokwenza ubudlelwano abanabo bukwazi ukuqina. U-Marta waciphiza izinyembezi emehlweni akhe ngesikhathi emile ezithulele ekhishini.

Inhloso yokuba sibe nesibonelo esifana nalesi, akukhona ukuthi sinake lokhu okufanele noma okungafanele sikwenze ngeSabatha; kodwa okunalokho, lokhu kuyisikhumbuzo sokuthi kungani kubalulekile kithina ukuba sinake izinto ezixegisa noma ezephula ubudlelwano esinabo noNkulunkulu. Uma sizwa sinobuhlungu ezinhliziyweni zethu okubangelwa ngukwenza isono kanye nokuhlukana noNkulunkulu bese sibiza igama laKhe, uJesu useduzane ukusisiza (Ihubo 53:2). Ezandleni zakhe ezigcwele igazi, ubambe indwangu emhlophe. Uyakwazi ukubona izinyembezi zokuphenduka bese esusa izingubo zethu ezingcolile. UJesu uzosisonga kuyona yonke indawo ngengubo yaKhe ecolisakele yokulunga. Ubungcwele baKhe buzomboza ukona kwethu-ngokuphelele futhi kwenzeke ngendlela efanelekile. Sithola ithuba lokugeza izingubo zethu egazini laKhe (Isambulo 7:14).

`Kungabe lamavesi alandelayo ka-Isaya 64:6, no- Zakhariya 3:4, kanye no-Isaya 61:10 asembulela liphi iqiniso elibalulekile mayelana nokulunga kuka-Kristu na? Kungani kufuneka sihlale sibambelele ngokubekezela kusithembiso esisinikeziwe kulamavesi na?`
