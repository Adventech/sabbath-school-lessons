---
title:  Ukucabanga Okubanzi
date:   19/06/2026
---

“Noma ngabe wenza msebenzi muni, akekho umuntu onothando olungenasici kuNkulunkulu ngaphandle kokuthi unothando olungenabo ubugovu okungamenza angakwazi ukusiza umfowabo. Kodwa ngeke sikwazi ukuba nesimo sokuba nalomoya wokuzama ukuthanda abanye abantu. Into edingekayo wuthando lukaKristu ezinhliziyweni zethu. Uma ubunjalo bomuntu buhlanganiswe noKristu, ukuthanda kuyazivelela kuziphumele obala.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 384.

“Bonke labo abaqashiwe ukuba basebenze benza umsebenzi benentshisakalo yokuthembeka befisa ukuzuza imiphefumulo beyizuzela uJesu Kristu, bangabantu abaqeqeshekile kwezikamoya nasekuthandazeni.”—Ellen G. White, Evangelism, p. 356. “Amandla okubalekela ububi atholakala kangcono ngokuthi umuntu asebenze kakhulu.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 105.

“Ukuze ukwazi ukungena ekujabuleni kwaKhe-ujabulela ukubona ukusindiswa kwemiphemulo isindiswa ngumhlatshelo waKhe-kudingeka sibandakanyeke ekwenzeni umsebenzi waKhe ukuze abantu bezothola ukuhlengwa.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 142.

“Labo abangafuni ukwamukela ithuba lokuhlanganyela ndawonye noKristu emsebenzini, basuke benqaba ukuqeqeshwa okungukuphela kwakho okukwazi ukutshala amava okuzibandakanya naYe enkazimulweni yaKhe.”—Ellen G. White, Education, p. 264.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Kungani uthando luyisisekelo luphinde lubaluleke ekwenzeni umsebenzi wokuvangela na?`

`Kunini lapho uthole lokhu kuliqiniso: okokuthi ukuzuza umphefumulo kuhlangene nobunjalo bomuntu kanye nokuhamba kwakhe noNkulunkulu na?`

`Kungabe kukhona umugqa lapho kuqala khona noma lapho kunokuqonda okuthile okufunekayo ukuze umuntu ezokwazi ukwabelana ngoNkulunkulu nabanye abantu na? Uma kukhona, ngabe yini lento edingekayo na?`

`Uma kuziwa ekunikezeni isifundo seBhayibheli kumuntu ongakholwayo, kufuneka uqale kuphi na? Yini okufuneka ugxile kuyona uma usaqala: kungabe wukuletha ubufakazi obesekela izimfundiso ezithile zebandla noma wukumema omunye umuntu ukuba abe nolwazi ngoJesu na?`

`Ake ucule noma ulalele amagama eculo elithi “Ngihlengiwe!” bese ucabanga izindlela wena omemezela ngayo lelivangeli.`

**Ukugoqa Isifundo Saleliviki:** Uma uthando lukaNkulunkulu kanye namazwi aKhe aphilisayo nanamandla ehlala ebakhona ekuphileni kwethu kwansuku zonke, sizozwa siphoqekile ukumthanda siphinde sabelane ngaYe kubantu abasizungezile. Kudingeka sibe ngabantu abathandazayo, nabakwazi ukucabanga futhi siphinde sibenenhloso yokuphuma siyofakaza, sikholwa ukuthi Izwi laKhe, eliphuma emlonyeni waKhe, ngeke libuye lilambatha, kodwa lizokwazi ukufeza ukuncenga kwakhe futhi lizophumelela ekwenzeni umsebenzi eliphumele ukuwenza (Isaya 55:11).
