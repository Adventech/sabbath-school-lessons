### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
Mathewu 28:18-20; 2 Petro 3:18; 1 Petro 3:8-15; Hoseya 7; Zakhariya 10.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Inkosi uJehova inginikile ulwimi lwabafundi ukuba ngazi ukuvuselela oyethileyo ngamazwi ahlumelelayo; ivusa njalo indlebe yami ekuseni imihla ngemihla ukuba ngizwe njengabafundi’ ” (Isaya 50:4, NKJV).

Kwabe kuyiSabatha ekuseni ngesikhathi ematasatasa uMfundisi G. Wayevuke ekuseni kakhulu, elungiselela ukubamba iSikole seSabatha kanye nenkonzo, futhi wayebamba uchungechunge lwezimvuselelo njalo ntambama. Wathatha izikhiye zakhe, waphuthuma emnyango, wase ethatha uhambo.

Wadlula ngemoto yakhe phakathi kokucinana kwezimoto edolobheni, kwamcasula ukubona ukuthi abantu baphume ngobuningi babo ekuseni ngoMgqibelo, nokungaba yinto engamenza abambezeleke ukufika enkonzweni. Kungabe bonke lababantu baphikelele kuphi na? Kuthe enganakile, enye imoto yabonakala isiphambi kwakhe. Wanyathela amabhuleki wase ephakamisa ingqindi yakhe ukukhombisa ukunengeka futhi ethukuthele, ethethisa umshayeli wemoto emphazamisayo.

Ekugcineni, uMfundisi G wagcina efikile enkonzweni. Kuthe ngesikhathi emile, efundisa isifundo, amehlo akhe aqalaza abantu ababekhona ekilasini ayelifundisa, wase ebona ubuso abujwayela: phela kwabe kungumshayeli wemoto emphazamisile emizuzwini engamashumi amabili adlulile.

Kamuva, kuthe uma ilunga lebandla lithula lomshayeli njengomuntu ongeyena umSabatha ongumlindi wokubuya kukaKristu, kodwa uze ngoba evakashela isihlobo sakhe, uMfundisi G wabona futhi ukuthi konke azokusho, kubantu abajwayele kanye nakubantu angabajwayele, kudingeka kube yinto esekelwe othandweni oluvela esimweni sokuhlala ekubudlelwano noNkulunkulu. Angeke wazi ukuthi ukwenza kwakho, ikakhulu njengomuntu olikholwa, kungaba nomthelela onjani kwabanye abantu.

_Funda lesisifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNhlangulana 20._
