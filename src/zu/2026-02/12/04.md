---
title:  Amacebo Okwabelana NgoJesu
date:   16/06/2026
---

Umbuzo obhekiswe komunye nomunye kithina uthi: Kungabe wabelana nobani ngoJesu -kumuntu ohambisa izimpahla, kumabhalalne wesitolo, ngumuntu ombona nsuku zonke uma kwenzeka uthatha uhambo na? UNkulunkulu umema wonke umuntu okholwayo ukuba azosiza ukwenza umsebenzi wakhe, futhi uyathembisa ukuthi uzokunika “ulwimi lwabafundi ukuba ngazi ukuvuselela oyethileyo ngamazwi ahlumelelayo” (Isaya 50:4). Futhi kungumsebenzi wawo wonke amaKrestu ukuba ahlale ekulungele ukuba yisivikelo sokukholwa kanye nokuthemba okukhona kithina (1 Petro 3:15).

`Funda ku-1 Petro 3:8-15. Kungabe izwi likaNkulunkulu lisitshela ukuthini kulamavesi na?`

Lapha ngezansi kunamacebo okudingeka uwagcine emqondweni njengoba ulokhu uhlolisisa ukuthi ungenza kanjani ukwazi ukwabelana ngoJesu nabanye abantu:

- Zama ukwazana nomunye umuntu bese wakha ubuhlobo bobungani ngokuhamba kwesikhathi. Ukufudumala kwakho, nobumnene kanye nentshisakalo yokungazenzisi ongaba nakho kulomuntu (othandekayo) kuzosiza ukumdonsela kuNkulunkulu. (Abanye abantu lobu bungani babubiza ngokuthi “ngubungani bezokuvangela”).
- Thandaza ukuze uMoya oNgcwele uzokwazi ukusebenza enhliziyweni yalomuntu. Thandazela ukuba kubekhona amathuba azovela lapho nizokwazi ukuxhumana kahle nalababantu.
- Funa izindlela zokwemvelo ezizokwenza ukwazi ukukhuluma ngawakho amava ekukholweni noma ubathandazele lababantu okhulumisana nabo. Cela uNkulunkulu akuphe isibindi esihambisana nokuba mnene uma usuqala ukukhuluma nalababantu.
- Thola izindlela zokuxhumanisa lomngani wakho omusha umxhumanise namanye amalunga ebandleni, ukuze ezokwazi ukuba namava okwamukelana nomphakathi wasebandleni lakho. Umphakathi noma iqeqebana elincane labantu elihlangene ngokufunda iBhayibheli lokhu kuyisinyathelo esihle esingalandela.
- Thandazelani izidingo ezikhethekile zomngani wakho noma imibuzo angaba nayo umngani wakho omusha bese ubheka ithuba elingavela lapho uzokwazi ukumkhombisa ukuthi iBhayibheli lingamnikeza induduzo, nesaluleko, kanye nokuqondiswa ekuphileni kwakhe. Ungakwenza lokhu ngokuthi wabelane naye ngesinye sezithembiso eziseBhayibhelini noma ukwazi ukuphendula umbuzo owodwa nokuyiwona ozovulela ukuxoxisana ngezinye izinto ezijulile. Kuthandazele nalokhu futhi.
- Kuzofika isikhathi lapho uzofisa ukubuza umngani wakho ukuthi angathanda yini ukuthatha isinqumo sokwenza okulandelayo (okufana nokufunda iBhayibheli nokungaholela ekubhabhathizweni). Akufanele ukujaha ukufika kulezinqumo futhi kubalekele ukulibaziseka usale ngemuva. Kuthandazela lokhu.
- Konke esikwenzayo kufuneka kuveze ukuthi singabakabani. Indlela esiphatha ngayo abanye abantu ekuphileni kwethu yikhona okukhuluma kakhulu. Njengoba izimilo zethu zilokhu zilungiswa ukuba zigcine ukusenza sifane noKristu (okungukungcweliswa), sizophilela ukuheha bonke abantu ukuba beze kuYe.
