---
title:  Kungaphoqiwe kodwa Kunamandla
date:   15/06/2026
---

Kungabe usuke wazibuza ukuthi uJesu wayenza kanjani ukuthi ahlale ekhuthalele ukusebenza, nokuphilisa, nokududuza, nokushumayela kanye nokufundisa abantu abaningi kangaka nsuku zonke na? Sitshelwa ukuthi “kepha ebona izixuku wabanesihe ngazo, ngokuba zazikhathazekile, zihlakazekile njengezimvu ezingenamalusi” (Mathewu 9:36). Kwabe kuwuthando nokuzwelana kukaJesu nabantu okwamenza akwazi ukwenza umsebenzi waKhe. Ngendlela efanayo, uthando lukaNkulunkulu olungaphakathi kithi kudingeka lusenze sibe nomuzwa wokuba nomthwalo wokuholela abantu kuYe kanye naseqinisweni laKhe (2 Korinte 5:14).

Usuke wazihlupha ukubheka ubuso babantu ongabaziyo besesixukwini, wase uba nomcabango wengunaphakade esingakafiki kulo, bese uyazibuza ukuthi kungabe bayamazi uJesu na? Usuke wabanawo umuzwa wokuthi yini eyodwa engaba wuthando lukaNkulnkulu ongayidlulisa komunye umuntu onesidingo okungumuntu ongamaziyo na? Uthando lukaNkulunkulu lusenza sizizwe sinomthwalo wokuholela abanye abantu kuYena. UJeremiya ukubeka kanjena lokhu, “Izwi lakho elisenhliziyweni yami linjengomlilo oshisayo, ovalelwe emathanjeni ami” (Jeremiya 20:9).

Noma kunjalo, uma sabelana ngoNkulunkulu nabanye abantu akumele sibaphoqe ukuthi bamukele uNkulunkulu noma amaqiniso aseBhayibhelini. Ukujaha umuntu akuhambisani nesimilo sikaNkulunkulu. UNkulunkulu akazange abaphoqe u-Adamu no-Eva ukuba bangasondeli emthini wokwazi okuhle nokubi (Genesise 2:16, 17). UNkulunkulu akazange abaphoqe abantu ukuba bangene emkhunjini ukuze bezophepha kuzamcolo (Genesise 7:1). UNkulunkulu akazange aphoqe ama-Israyeli ukuba ahlale esivumelwaneni saKhe kunye nabo (Duteronomi 4:23-25). Okunalokho, wabanika lokhu ababekudinga (Mathewu 4:23-25) waphinde wabamema ukuba bamlandele. UJesu wayengaphoqi muntu ukuba amlandele noma aphoqelele ngeqiniso laKhe, kodwa akadinwa wukulokhu esincenga (Mathewu 23:37).

Uma sifakaza, indlela esifakaza ngayo kudingeka ngaso sonke isikhathi iveze indlela ayenza ngayo uJesu. U-Ellen G. White uthi, “Akuyona indlela kaNkulunkulu yokuphoqa abantu ukuba bamamukele. Lena yindlela kaSathane, indoda eyaxoshwa ngenxa yomoya wayo, uyathanda njalo ukuphoqa unembeza. Abukho ubufakazi obuqinile bokuthi nathi sinomoya kaSathane ngaphandle kwalokhu okusezinhliziyweni zabantu abafuna ukulimaza baphinde bonakalise labo abangakuthandi ukusebenza kwethu, noma abantu abenza lokhu okuphikisanayo nalokhu esikucabangayo.”—The Desire of Ages, p. 487.

Kufuneka sivumelane nokuthi sisetshenziswe ukwenza umsebenzi kaNkulunkulu. Siphila emhlabeni ongalithandi iqiniso, kodwa ukwazi leliqiniso kudingeka kungasivimbeli ukuba sabelane ngeqiniso sinokucabanga okuphelele,nangendlela ethandekayo. Khumbulani ukuthi izikhathi eziningi ubufakazi esinabo ngathi yibona bufakazi obunesisindo, ikakhulu uma umuntu esaqala ukwenza ubufakazi (Isambulo 12:11).

`Funda ku-2 Petro 3:18. Kungabe yiziphi izindlela ezingasikhulisa emseni nasekwazini na? Kungabe kuyabonakala yini lokhu ngesikhathi uxhumana nabantu abaseduzane nawe na?`
