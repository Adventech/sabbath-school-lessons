---
title:  Babuyiseni
date:   18/06/2026
---

Sonke sike saba buthakathaka noma kasizange siziphathe kahle ekuhambeni kwethu noNkulunkulu-ezigodini lapho izinhliziyo zethu zingazange zithembeke noma lapho sikhombise ukuba luvindi singashisi futhi singabandi nokube yisikhathi eside. Kungabe yini lena ekubuyisele emuva esimweni sokuhlala ebudlelwaneni noNkulunkulu na?

`Incwadi ka-Zakariya 10 sithola imiyalezo emihle lapho uNkulunkulu ebuyisela abantu baKhe kuYena. Fundisisa lesisahluko ungajahile bese ubekisa imiyalezo ebalulekile.`

Okuseqinisweni, ukwazi ukuxhumana uphinde ukhulumisane nomuntu omthandayo osephambukile kubudlelwano noJehova kungaba yisimo esiyinselelo enkulu. Ungafikelwa ngumcabango wokuthi izinto bezingaba ngenye indlela eyehlukile ukuba bekungesinye isimo; ungafikelwa ngumcabango wokuthi ungenza kanjani kulesisimo osekuyisona manje uma kungukuthi banombono owehlukile ngokwenzeka emhlabeni; kungenzeka uzizwe uhlukumezekile futhi kungekho ongakwenza mayelana nezinqumo ezingaphusile abasaqhubeka nokuzithatha. Imicabango efana nalena iyohlala njalo inomthelela esimweni sokuphatha umuntu omthandayo, futhi kungakho kubalulekile ukuphila uphinde ukhulume into esuka kwimpuphumo yokuba nesikhathi osichithe undawonye noMsindisi wakho.

Ubufakazi ngempilo yakho, nangendlela owenza ngayo, nendlela okhuluma ngayo, kanye nemithandazo yakho oyenzela umlingani wakho noma ingane yakho ephambukile yaqhela kuNkulunkulu kungaba yinto engaguqula impilo kanye nekusasa labo. (Funda ku-Luka 22:31, 32 kanye noJohane 21:15-17 ubheke indlela uJesu athandazela ngayo uPetro nokwaba yinto eyashintsha ikusasa lakhe). Hlukana nanoma yikuphi ukuphatheka kabi, nokwahlulela, noma ukugxeka ongahle ubenakho ngabanye abantu, okunalokhu thandaza ucele uNkulunkulu akusize ukuthi imizwa onayo uyishintshe kube yimizwa yokuthanda nokuyinto uKristu ayengasinika yona. Cela uNkulunkulu akumbese ngesimilo saKhe ukuze uzokwazi ukuba nothando, ungabi nomoya wobugovu. Khumbula ukuthi “kawukho omunye umthelela ongakwazi ukuzungeza umphefumulo ukwedlula umthelela wokuba ngumuntu ophila impilo engenabo ubugovu. Ukuqophisana okuqinile kokukhuluma okuhambisana nokuthanda ivangeli lokhu kuyinto ethandekayo nephinde yenze umKretu onothando.”—Ellen G. White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 470.

Ngenxa yokuba nomthelela wokuhlala njalo siphila impilo ekhomba abanye abantu kuKristu, labo abangamfuni uKristu bazobona ngaphakathi kithi into ethile engaba yinto esingayithola kuphela ivela kuNkulunkulu. Bazokwazi ukubona ukuthula okwedlula ukuqonda konke, babone uthando olungasoze lwaphela, kanye nokuthemba okudlula noma ngabe yiziphi izimo. Uthando uNkulunkulu asithanda ngalo kanye nabathandwa bethu alusoze lwazanyazanyiswa. Singakwazi ukwedlulisa loluthando, nathi esiluthola nsuku zonke, silwedlulise kubantu abasizungezile.

`Kungabe incwadi yabase- Efesu 3:17-19 isigqugquzela ukuba senzeni na?`
