---
title:  Umakoti/Umlobokazi
date:   23/06/2026
---

Ngesikhathi esekudingisweni esiqhingini sase-Phathimosi, umfundi ongu-Johane waboniswa umbono wokuthi kuyokuba yisimo esinjani uma sesiphinda sihlanganiswa noNkulunkulu ingunaphakade.

`Funda ku-Isambulo 21:9-11. Kungabe yikuphi ukuqhathanisa esinikezwa khona lapha, nokuthi kungani uma ucabanga kusetshenzisiwe lapha na?`

Umakoti muhle bese kuthi ngosuku lwakhe lomshado bonke abantu banesifiso sokumbona umakoti. Usuku lomshado kusuke kulusuku lokungena empilweni entsha kumakoti kanye nomkhwenyana, futhi lokhu kuyinto ezobe ifana kubudlelwano bethu noNkulunkulu ekubuyeni kwaKhe.

UJesu ubelokhu esilungisela indawo (Johane 14:1-3), okuyindawo enhle ngendlela yokuthi kulukhuni ukuyichaza. Okuseqinisweni, “akulula ukukuchaza ngokwanele uma usebenzisa ulwimi olukhulunywa ngabantu ukuchaza umvuzo oyonikwa abalungileyo. Lokhu kuyokuba yinto eyokwaziwa kuphela yilabo abayokwazi ukukubona. Akukho ukucabanga okwanele okungakwazi ukuyiqonda lenkazimulo yase-Paradise likaNkulunkulu.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 675.

Yize kungeke kubelula ukukuqonda lokhu esiyokubona ezulwini elisha nomhlaba omusha ukuthi kuyobe kuyinto enjani, uNkulunkulu wabonisa uJohane ngalendawo ukuze nathi sibenakho ukulangazelela ukubakhona “emshadweni” osekuseduze wenzeke. Kuliqiniso ukuthi simenyiwe ukuthi “nakani okwaphezulu, ninganaki okusemhlabeni” (Kolose 3:2).

UNkulunkulu ngokucophelela okukhulu ulungiselela ukwenzeka kwalomcimbi, futhi akafuni ukuthi “lomshado” usithole singazelele (Mathewu 22:1-14; Mathewu 25:1-13).

Izwe lonke eligcwele izidalwa eziyowubona lomcimbi uma sewenzeka, kanti yithina esiyobe sizitatanyiswa ezimqoka kulendaba. Sizokwazi ukuba ndawonye “nomakoti,” kulomuzi omkhulu uJesu azosithatha asise kuwo uma esebuya okwesibili. Okumangazayo kulokhu ngukuthi, abantu bakaNkulunkulu (abangcwele) nabo bayobizwa ngokuthi bangabalobokazi (Isambulo 19:7), mhlawumbe ngenxa yokuthi “umuzi ongcwele, iJerusalema elisha, wehla uvela ezulwini kuNkulunkulu, ulungisiwe njengomlobokazi ohlotshiselwe umyeni wakhe” (Isambulo 21:2). Lencazelo enhle kangaka yoMuzi oNgcwele ikhombisa ukuthi sekukhona ukuxhumana okungasophinde kuphele phakathi kwabantu bakaNkulunkulu kanye nalomuzi, ngoba bobabili sebebizwa ngokuthi “bangomakoti.” IBhayibheli lembula incazelo eveza konke okuyobe kuse-“Mzini Ongcwele,” iJerusalema Elisha kanye nalokhu okubizwa ngoMlobokazi, okungumakoti wewundlu.’ ”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 426.
