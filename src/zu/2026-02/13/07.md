---
title:  Ukucabanga Okubanzi
date:   26/06/2026
---

“Uma singamkeli ivangeli likaKristu ngokuthi silokhu sincela ezwini likaNkulunkulu, akusifanele ukubizwa ukuba singene emzini kaNkulunkulu. Uma besilokhu sidla okwasemhlabeni, sifundise imizwa yethu ngokuthi sithande ukudla kwasemhlabeni, angeke sibe ngabantu abafanele ukwamukeleka ekhaya lasezulwini; angeke sikwazi ukuthokozela ubumsulwa, nokugobhoza kwamaza asezulwini. Amaphimbo ezingelosi kanye nomculo wamahabhu azo angeke kusanelise thina. Ukwazi kwasezulwini kuzokuba yinto engaqondakali emiqondweni yethu. Sidinga ukulambela siphinde somele ukulunga kukaKristu; sidinga ukwakhiwa senziwe ngcono ngesimilo esiguqulayo somusa waKhe, ukuze sizokwazi ukufaneleka ukuba semphakathini wezingelosi zasezulwini. . . .

“Okuyolandela ngukuthi, izizwe aziseyukuba nomthetho omunye ngaphandle komthetho wasezulwini. Bonke abantu bayohlala ngokujabula, bengumndeni ohlangene, bagqokiswe izingubo zokudumisa kanye nokubonga. . . . Uma sesibona izinkanyezi zokusa siyohlabelela ndawonye, bese kuthi amadodana kaNkulunkulu ahlokome ngenxa yenjabulo, ngesikhathi uNkulunkulu benoKristu behlangana ukumemezela ukuthi, “akusophinde kubekhona isono, futhi nokufa akuseyukuba khona.’

“Sifisa ukuba nomkhuba wokukhuluma ngezulu, izulu elihle. Sikhulume ngalokho kuphila okuyohlala kukhona njalo inqobo nje uma uNkulunkulu esekhona, bese sikhohlwa yizilingo kanye nobunzima obuncane. Kwangazathi ukucabanga kwethu kungazindla ngoNkulunkulu.”—Ellen G. White, The Faith I Live By, p. 363.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Ake ulalele noma ufunde umbono wezulu obhalwe encwadini ka-Ellen G White, Early Writings, pp. 14-20. Kungabe yini ekuthathekisayo kulencazelo ofunde ngayo na?`

`Ngabe yikuphi ofunde ngakho kulonyanga-ntathu esiwuphethayo ofisa ukuhlala ukukhumbula njalo ukuze uzokwazi ukugcina ubudlelwano bakho noNkulunkulu buqinile kuze kufike isikhathi lapho uyobona uJesu ubuso nobuso na?`

`Ekuphileni kwakho ngabe ngobani abadinga ukuzwa ngethemba lasezulwini na? Zama ukuzinika ithuba lokwabelana nabo ngokushesha okukhulu. Khumbula; ngeke ukwazi ukwabelana nabo ngethemba eliyinto nawe ongenayo.`

**Ukugoqa Isifundo Saleliviki:** Njengoba silokhu sigqolozele kumklomelo, “sethembile ngempela yona lento yokuthi owaqala umsebenzi omuhle kuthina uyakuwufeza kuze kube lusuku lukakristu Jesu” (Filiphu 1:6). NguNkulunkulu owaqala ubudlelwano anabo nawe, futhi nguYena ozoqedela lokhu akuqalile. Kwangathi singakhula othandweni nasekukholweni njengoba silokhu silindele lolosuku, silokhu sihlezi ebudlelwaneni noKristu kuphela, nokuyinto esiyitholile ngokukholwa.
