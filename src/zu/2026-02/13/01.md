### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki
iHubo 80, 1 Thes. 4:17; Samb. 21:9-27; Isa. 25:8; Samb. 7:17; Samb. 21:4; Johane 6:44.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Bathandekayo, manje singabantwana bakaNkulunkulu, kepha akukabonakali esiyakuba yikho. Siyazi ukuthi, nxa ebonakaliswa, siyakuba njengaye, ngokuba siyakumbona njengokuba enjalo” (1 Johane 3:2, NKJV).

Kungabe ikusasa likuphatheleni na? Yini ekulinde ngaphambili na? Kungabonakala kuyinto elukhuni ukuyenza, kubonakale kuthathekisa, kubonakale kwesabeka, noma kuthathekisa konke ngasikhathi sinye. Yazi ukuthi uJesu uthembekile futhi amazwi aKhe aliqiniso (Isambulo 3:14). Kungenzeka kubekhona izikhathi ezinzima kakhulu ezisihlalele (Mathewu 24:21, 22), kodwa Yena uthembisile ukuthi akasoze asiyeka futhi engasoze asishiya (Hebheru 13:5). Uzokwenza lokhu athe uzokwenza-ukwenzile ngaphambili futhi usazoqhubeka akwenze njalo (Hebheru 10:24). “Kodwa okhuthazelayo kuze kube sekupheleni nguyena oyakusindiswa” (Mathewu 24:13).

Ngaphandle kokunaka izinsuku eziseleyo silokhu sisaphila lapha emhlabeni, kudingeka sigxilise amehlo ethu kuJesu, silokhu sigqolozele kuYena. Ukwenza kanjena akusiyona into eyohlala ilula ukuyenza kulomhlaba odinga ukuba sinake okwenzeka kuwona, kodwa kungenzeka njengoba kwenzeka ku-Davide lapho akhuluma wathi, “Amehlo ami akuye uJehova njalo, ngokuba nguyena okhipha izinyawo zami enetheni” (iHubo 25:15).

Kuleliviki, akesifunde ngomvuzo wasezulwini (Mathewu 5:12; Isambulo 22:12), ukuthi izulu liyokuba yinto enjani; bese kuthi ekugcineni, kuyokuba yinto enhle kangakanani lapho siyozithola sindawonye nalowo owasidalayo, owasithanda kwaze kwaba sekufeni, okwazile ukusihlenga ezonweni zethu, futhi ozobuya masinyane. Esikudingayo, ngukuthi sibambelele ekukholweni kuze kufike leso sikhathi.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UNhlangulana 27._
