title:  IHubo 5
date:   21/05/2025
---

`Funda iHubo 5. Kulomsebenzi, uDavide uqhathanisa phakathi kwalabo abalahliweyo kanye nalabo abahlengiweyo. Qhathanisa leliHubo nolwimi lweSambulo 14:1-12. Yikuphi ukufana okutholayo, futhi lokhu kukwazisa kanjani ukuqonda kwakho kokuthi kuchaza ukuthini ukuba yingxenye yensali kaNkulunkulu yesikhathi sokugcina na?`

Kuyafundisa ukuqaphela ukuthi uDavide uphikelela ngokuthi ububi “ngeke bahlala” noNkulunkulu (Hub. 5:4, ESV). Inhloso yetabernakeli kwakungelokuthi uNkulunkulu ahlale phakathi kwabantu Bakhe, futhi okufanayo kuyobanjalo embusweni kaKristu (buka iSamb. 21:3). Labo abazosondela esihlalweni sikaNkulunkulu sobukhosi kumele bahlengwe.

Kubalulekile futhi ukuthi uDavide uchaza isenzo sokukhonza kwiHubo 5:7, okuludaba olungumongo olusengcupheni kwimpikiswano enkulu. Isambulo 13 sibalula “ukukhonza” izikhathi eziyisithupha, futhi Imibiko yezingelosi ezintathu ibizela izwe “ekukhonzeni Yena owenza.” UDavide usitshela ukuthi “wesaba” uNkulunkulu, futhi umbiko wensali ubiza izwe ukuba “ ‘Mesabeni uNkulunkulu, nimnike inkazimulo, ngokuba ihora lokwahlulela kwakhe selifikile’ ” (Sam. 14:7, NKJV)

Qaphela futhi indlela abasindisiweyo beSambulo 14 ukuthi “abanayo inkohliso” (Samb. 14:5, NKJV) olimini lwabo; bangabakhuluma iqiniso, omazwi kanye nezenzo zabo zibonisa isimilo sokulunga sikaNkulunkulu. Ababi, ngokukaDavide, “abanalo iqiniso Olimini lwabo” (Hub. 5:9, ESV).

Kungumbono omangalisayo uJohane awethula kulengxenye esemqoka yeSambulo: aboni bahlanguliwe ekufeni futhi baba nelungelo lokuma ebukhoneni bukaNkulunkulu. Abazange balizuze lelilungelo; linikezwe bona ngenxa yeWundlu likaNkulunkulu—iNdodana yomuntu—imi kanye nabo. Bathethelelwe, bahlengiwe; abasadingekile ukuba bathwale icala labo (qhathanisa neHub. 5:10), ngoba iWundlu likaNkulunkulu libathwalele lona (qhathanisa noIsa. 53:12 no 2 Kor. 5:21).

Lapho nje igama likaNkulunkulu liqoshiwe Enhliziyweni yakho, kubalukhuni ukuthula. Abantu bakaNkulunkulu bethula Ithuba lokugcina lomusa “ngezwi elikhulu” (Samb. 14:7). “Kepha mabajabule bonke abathemba kuwe, bahalalise kuze kube phakade, abathanda igama lakho bethabela kuwe” (Hub. 5:11, NKJV).

`Awucabange umi phambi kukaNkulunkulu ongcwele nopheleleyo ekwahlulelweni, naso sonke isenzo osuke wasenza sisobala ngokugcwele phambi Kwakhe. Lesisibonakaliso sikutshelani ngokudinga kwakho ukulunga kukaKristu na?`
