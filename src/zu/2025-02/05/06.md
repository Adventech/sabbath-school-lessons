---
title:  Profetha Futhi
date:   01/05/2025
---

Ibandla lensali lazalwa Emhlabeni Omusha, lapho khona labo ababefuna inkululeko kwezenkolo babebalekele khona ngesikhathi sonyakakhulu beshumi nesikhombisa noweshumi nesishiyagalombili. Njengoba kwakukhona izingqinamba kwezenkolo nepolitiki kwezinye izindawo, kuyangabazeka ukuthi ukusungulwa kwaloluhlelo kwakuzoshesha noma kube namandla kwenye indawo njengalokhu kwabanjalo ezweni elisha elaba yi-United States.

`Funda iSambulo 10:1-11, esichaza Ukuzalwa kwaloluhlelo. Buka izinto ezithile esesizifundile, njenge“zizwe,” umhlaba, kanye nolwandle. Usebenzisa ukuqikelela okufanele, ukuze ungafundi okuningi kwisahluko, yiziphi izinto ongazithola kulendaba na?`

Ingelosi imemeza ngezwi elikhulu, iningi lezingelosi zeSambulo 14 kanye nengelosi yeSambulo 18 zenzakanjalo. Lona ngumzuzu ophuthuuNhlabao emlandweni ngenkathi umsebenzi webandla lensali usungulwa ngenxa “ ‘yabantu abaningi, izizwe, izilimi, kanye namakhosi’ ” (Samb. 10:11, NKJV).

Ingelosi iphethe “incwadi encane”—okungenzeka kube yincwadi kaDaniyeli (buka uDan. 12:4)—evulwa okokuqala ezizukulwaneni eziningi. Ibeke unyawo olulodwa olwandle olunye emhlabeni. Lokhu kungakhomba emqondweni wokuthi umbiko wengamela konke okusemhlabeni, Emhlabeni Omdala nakoMusha. Kungaba futhi yinkomba yomqondo wokuthi lombiko ngowezizwe zonke: labo abahlala emhlabeni nalabo abahlala olwandle “lwabaFokazi.”

Umhlaba, ekugcineni, uyokhanyiswa ngenkazimulo kaNkulunkulu, futhi imibiko yokugcina yeSambulo 14 ifinyelela kubo bonke. NjengoIsrayeli, umthwalo wethu njengebandla ngowokushuuNhlabaela ivangeli “ ‘ezweni lonke, kube ngubufakazi ezizweni zonke, andukuba kufike ukuphela’ ” (Mat. 24:14, NKJV).

UNkulunkulu ududulela Umlando wesintu esiphethweni sawo esikhulu: ukuphela kwemibuso Yabantu kanye nokubekwa esihlalweni sobukhosi kukaKristu ingunaphakade. Funda uDaniyeli 2:34, 35, 44, 45. IBhayibheli likwenza kube sobala, ngaphandle kokungabaza, ukuthi yonke lemibuso yomhlaba iyoshabalala, ingaphinde ibonakale yona nemilando yayo, futhi endaweni yayo umbuso kaNkulunkulu Waphakade, lapho isono, Ukuhlupheka, ukugula, ububi, nokufa kungasayikuphinde kube khona futhi.

`Buka indlela iziprofetho zikaDaniyeli 2 no 7 ezihlabe esikhonkosini ngayo isibikezelo sokuvuka nokuwa kwalemibuso yezwe. Kungani lokhu kuhlaba esikhonkosini, okumangalisayo uma ucabanga ukuthi uDaniyeli wabhalwa nini, kusisiza ekuthembeni kuYena esithembisweni sombuso wokugcina nowaphakade kaNkulunkulu na?`
