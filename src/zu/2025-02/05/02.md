---
title:  Umthetho Wokuqala
date:   27/04/2025
---

Insimu yase-Edeni yayiyiklasi labantu bokuqala bakaNkulunkulu, indawo lapho ukuxhumana kwabo nendalo kwakuyobafundisa njalo bona nenzalo yabo kabanzi ngoMdali. “Isibili esingcwele sasingesona nje abantwana ngaphansi kokunakekelwa nguNkulunkulu,” kuveza u-Ellen G. White, “kodwa sasingabafundi abafundiswa nguMdali wabo bonke ubuhlakani. . . . Izimfihlo zezwe elingabonakali—‘imisebenzi emangalisayo yaLowo ophelele olwazini’ (Jobe 37:16)—kwakubanikeza umthombo ongenamkhawulo wemfundo nentokozo.”—Patriarchs and Prophets, pp. 50, 51.

`Funda uGenesisi 2:9-17.Wawuthini umthetho wokuqala, ukukhuza, uNkulunkulu awunikeza isintu, futhi kungani wawubaluleke kangaka na?`

Ukusetshenziswa kwesenzo u-tswh, “ukuyaleza,” uNkulunkulu akunikeza abantu kwakukuGenesisi 2:16, umyalezo wokungadli emthini wokwazi okuhle nokubi. Ulwazi oluthile lungavinjelwani na? Akusizi yini njalo ukuba namava kanye nokwazi kabanzi na?

Hhayi ngokweZibhalo: uNkulunkulu wayehlosile Ukufundisa abantu Bakhe ngokuphelele ebe futhi ebavikela ekuhluphekeni okwakungabangwa wulwazi oluthile, njengalokhu kwakuzokwenzeka kamuva ngenkathi abantu beqoka ukuzibusa kunokuba babuswe nguJehovah ngoKwakhe.

Ezinkulungwaneni zeminyaka elandelayo, ngenkathi uIsrayeli efuna inkosi, uNkulunkulu wabendlalela imiphumela (njengalokhu sibonile ngeviki elidlule), futhi wazisa abantu Bakhe ngokuthi isinqumo sokusuka ngaphansi kokubuswa nguYena sasiyoqhubeka kuze kube sekupheleni kwesikhathi. Njengalokhu amakhosi aya ngokuya ekhohlakala, abantu bakaNkulunkulu besivumelwano babanobuzwe, futhi basuka enhlosweni yabo, ngangangokuba Yena wabanikeza ngokwandile lokho ababekufunile: uhulumeni wabantu.

Ukuya encwadini kaDaniyeli ngalomlando emqondweni kuyakhanyisa: ukulandelana kwemibuso okubonakaliswa emibonweni yencwadi ayilona nje icala “labezizwe”—Abafokazi—iyicala futhi lokwehluleka KukaIsrayeli, ukunqaba kwabo ukulandela imi-mitswot (Imithetho) Yakhe. Amakhulu eminyaka yokucindezelwa, kunokuba nenkululeko eyanikezwa okokuqala eEdeni, kwakuyoba yigumbi elisha lokufunda lapho izinhliziyo ezivuuNhlabao zazizobona ukwehluka okugqamile phakathi kwemibuso yalomhlaba kanye nokaNkulunkulu.

`Cabanga ngezinhlobo zolwazi, namanje, obekungcono iningi lethu lingazazi. Lokhu kusisiza kanjani ekuqondeni lokho okwakunqatshiwe eEdeni na?`
