### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki

Gen. 2:9–17; Dan. 2:31–35; Isa. 17:12, 13; Dan. 7:1–3; Rom. 3:10–19; Samb. 12:15, 16; Samb. 10:1–11.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Thulani, niqonde ukuthi mina nginguNkulunkulu; ngiyakuphakama phakathi kwabezizwe, ngiphakame emhlabeni!” (iHubo 46:10, NKJV).

Kuyo yonke iminyaka, abanye abantu bathe uNkulunkulu wakufuna Ukuwa, ukuthi kwakuyinhloso Yakhe ukuthi abantu bawele esonweni nasekufeni, kuthi ngalokho kuholele Yena—ngomuntu uJesu—esiphambanweni. Nembala, kungayiphi enye indlela enamandla Yena ayengabonkalisa ukujula kothando Lwakhe kubantu ngaphandle kokubafela esiphambanweni na? Kafushane, Ukucabanga kuthi, uNkulunkulu wayedinga ukuthi abantu bawe.

Lokhu kuyisimo esesabekayo nesibi sokucabanga. Kwakungeyona inhloso kaNkulunkulu ukuthi uSathane noma abantu bawe. Ukuhlubuka kukaSathane, bese kulandelwa ngokwabantu, kwabayinhlekelele enemiphumela emikhulu, futhi Injabulo Yethu kuYena yayizohlala iphelele ukube abazali bethu bokuqala abazange bawe.

Kuleliviki, sizoqhubeka nokubuka izinkinga ezidalwe nguKuwa kanye nesifiso sombuso wabantu ngokumelene nokubusa kukaNkulunkulu. Lamaqiniso embuleke ngamandla encwadini kaDaniyeli, ebonisa ukuthi uNkulunkulu wayeqinisile ngenkathi exwayisa abantu Bakhe ngalokho okwakuzokwenzeka uma befulathela kuYena futhi bekhetha amakhosi omhlaba. Yilokho ke abakuthola: amakhosi omhlaba, futhi izoni zibusa phezu kwezoni—akukaze kube yinto enhle.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabathsa, UNhlaba 3._
