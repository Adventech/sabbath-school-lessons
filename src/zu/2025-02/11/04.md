---
title:  UBowazi, njengoMhlengi
date:   10/06/2025
---

UBowaiz uba nothando olujulile no-Ruth futhi afise ukumshada, kodwa kukhona isithikamezo esibalulekile: kukhona Isihlobo esisondele kakhulu naso esinelungelo kulowesifazane kanye nomhlaba. Uma sibuka uBowazi njengomele uKristu, lesisimo singembula udaba olusengcupheni kwimpikiswano enkulu. UKristu uyasithanda thina, kodwa kukhona “Isihlobo esisondele kakhulu” naso esinombango: uSathane.

`Izindimana ezilandealyo zembulani uNhlabaelana nombango kaSathane kwisintu na? (Job 1:6–11; Mat. 4:8, 9; Juda 1:9; Luka 22:31).`

Ngenkathi uSathane ebonakala esigungwini sasezulwni, watshela uNkulunkulu ukuthi yena ubekade “ ‘ezula emhlabeni nasekuhambahambeni kuwo’ ” (Jobe 1:7), futhi ngenkathi uNkulunkulu embuza ukuthi wayemqaphele uJobe Olungileyo, uSathane wabanga ngelithi ungowakhe, encoma ukuthi inhliziyo kaJobe kwakungeyona ngempela ekaNkulunkulu. Okungukuthi, yena ngempela ukulandela Wena ngoba Wena umenzela okuhle. Yeka ukuba muhle kuyena bese ubona ngempela ukuthi uJobe ukhonza bani.

KuJuda, sibona ukubhekisa okufushane endabeni eyayaziwa kakhulu kwaIsrayeli: ngemuva kokuba uMose engcwatshwe nguNkulunkulu (Dut. 34:6), wavuswa kamuva. Nakuba singenayo imininingwane, umbhalo ngokwawo, othi baphikisana ngomzimba kaMose, usikisela ekutheni uSathane wayewubanga.

“Okokuqala uKristu wayezonikeza ukuphila kofileyo. NjengeNkosana yokuphila kanye nabakhazimulayo baya Ethuneni, uSathane wasabela ukubusa kwakhe. Yena nezingelosi zakhe ezimbi wema ukuphikisana nokuhlaselwa kwesifunda ayethi kungesakhe. Wazidla ngokuthi inceku kaNkulunkulu yayiyisiboshwa sakhe. Wamemezela ukuthi noMose akakwazanga ukugcina umthetho kaNkulunkulu; ukuthi yena wayezithathele kuye udumo olwalufanele uJehovah—lesosono kanye esibange ukuxoshwa kukaSathane ezulwini—futhi ngokona wayesengaphansi kokubuswa nguSathane.”— Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 478. Ngokusobala, uKristu wakuphikisa ukubanga kukaSathane, futhi uMose wavuswa (buka uMat. 17:30).

Ku-Ruth 4:1-12, uBowazi uya Esangweni lase-Bethlehem—idolobha, kulo uKristu angena umhlaba wethu njengozalo. Abadala baqoqana, futhi ekugcineni kwanikezwana imbadada (uphawu lobunikazi).

Isango lesigodi yilapho izindaba zazinqunywa khona: lokhu kuluphawu lombono wokwahlulela. Kubonisa umbono wokwahlulela kaDaniyeli 7:13, 14, 22, 26, 27. Akumele sigeje lengxenye esemqoka yokwahlulela: ukwahlulela “kuvuna abangcwele” kodwa ngenxa kuphela yokuthi uKristu wayikhokha inhlawulo ngathi, njengalokhu uBowazi enza ngomlobokazi wakhe.
