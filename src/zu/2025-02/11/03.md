---
title:  Ruth no Boaz
date:   09/06/2025
---

UNayomi wacela ukuba igama lakhe liguqulwe libe ngu-Mara, ngenxa yobuhlungu obabehlele impilo yakhe (buka u-Ruth 1:20). Ubudlelwane bethu noMdali wethu bonakele ngokungalungiseki ngenxa yesono, kwasiphoqela ebumpofini kwezikamoya. Ikusasa lethu lifiphele, futhi sichitha izimpilo zethu sikhothoza esingakuthola onqenqemeni lwensimu, siphila ngemvuthuluka yenjabulo esengatholakala emhlabeni ophukileyo. Kodwa konke kuyaguquka ngomzuzu sithola ngokumangalisayo: uNkulunkulu akasilibele.

`Funda u-Ruth 2:5-20. Kungani lokhu kungumzuzu osemqoka kulendaba na? Ucabanga ukuthi kungani ukuthola kukaNayomi ukuthi umsizi wabo ungubani kwaba yizindaba ezinhle na?`

UNayomi wayephucwe konke, futhi kusenokuba wayebuyele ukuzothengisa umhlaba wakhe ukuze akhokhe izikwelethu ngaphambi kokuya kwaMoabi. UNkulunkulu wayenze umhlinzeko kwaIsrayeli wabantu abalahlekelwe yikho konke ukuba babange futhi ifa labo Ezweni leSithembiso: Isihlobo esisondelene sasingabahlengela umhlaba. UBowazi wayengeyena nje umlimi omnene; wayewuzalo. Uma ungakwazi ukukhokha izikweletu zakho futhi ubuyise ingxenye yakho kwifa lakwaIsrayeli, Isihlobo esiseduzane sasingakwenzela lokho: “ ‘Uma umfowenu eba mpofu, athengise ngefa lakhe, kuyakufika isihlobo sakhe, sihlenge lokho umfowabo athengisile ngakho’ ” (Lev. 25:25, NKJV).

Ukuthola ukuthi uBowazi kwakungekona nje ukuthi wayenenhliziyo enhle futhi enobubele kodwa wayeyisihlobo kwakuyizindaba ezinhle kunazo zonke: ubuphofu lamakhosikazi ayezithola kubo kwakungamele bubephakade.

AmaKristu akuqondile lokhu isikathi eside ukuthi uBowazi umele uKristu, ongeyena nje uMdali wethu, kodwa owakhetha ukuba yisihlobo sethu—umuntu wangempela wenyama negazi, esinye sezizathu sokuthi njalo azibize Yena “Indodana yoMuntu” (Mat. 12:8, Marko 8:31, Luke 22:22, Johane 3:14).

Iningi labantu licabanga ngoNkulunkulu enonya: mhlawumbe Yena uzosivumela singene ezulwini uma siphawula zonke izikhala ohleni lokuziphatha kahle, kodwa Yena uzokukwenza lokho ekhononda ngoba singene ngokwemsebenzi ethile. Umfanekiso kaKristu owembulwa kuBowazi uphikisa lemicabango ngokuphelele. UNkulunkulu akasiqapheli njenkuphela; noma simpofu kangakanani kwezikamoya, Yena usifuna njengomlobokazi Wakhe.

`Zama Ukucabanga hhayi nje kuphela ngoMdali eba yingxenye yendalo Yakhe kodwa abe eseyifela. Leliqiniso elimangalisayo kumele liyithinte kanjani indlela esibuka ngayo ubukhona bethu na?`
