---
title:  Ukucabanga Kabanzi
date:   18/04/2025
---

Funda uJohane 2:1–11, Mathewu 22:1–14, 2 Korinte 11:1–5, no Mathewu 25:1–13.

Ngenkathi ubona ubuningi bemininingwane obukhona kulo lonke iBhayibheli ukulekelela ekwandiseni ukuqonda kwakho isiprofetho, kuba nesilingo sokubusebenzisa ngokweqile. Eminyakeni yonke, amanye amaKristu agcizelele izimpawu nemifanekiso etholakala ezindabeni zeBhayibheli aze afinyelela ekuthatheni Umlando njengenganekwane. Ngenkathi imikhakha yezincazelo itholakala kuyo yonke Imibhalo, kumele njalo sikhumbule ukuthi uNkulunkulu unendlela yokuthatha izigameko zangempela ezifaka abantu bangempela futhi azisebenzise Ukufundisa thina izinto uNhlabaelana nokuxhumana Kwakhe nebandla okuzayo.

Umshado waseKana, ngokwesibonelo, unganikeza ukubona Okuthile esingathekisweni somshado esisetshenziswa yisiprofetho, kodwa umshado yisigameko sangempela. “Amazwi kaKristu anikeza umhlinzeko owanele edilini. Mningi kakhulu umhlinzeko womusa Wakhe ukucima Izono zabantu, kanye nokuvuselela nokondla umphefumulo.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 149.

Noma, njengalokhu ebhala: “ ‘UJehovah ngeke enze lutho, kodwa wembula Imfihlo Yakhe ezincekwini Zakhe abaprofethi.’ Kunjalo, ‘lezo zimfihlo zingezikaJehovah uNkulunkulu wethu,’ ‘lezo zinto ezembuliwe zingezethu kanye nabantwana bethu ingunaphakade.’ Amos 3:7; Duteronomi 29:29. UNkulunkulu uzinikezile lezizinto kithina, futhi isibusiso Sakhe siyoba nokufundwa okuthobekile, nokunomkhuleko kwezibhalo zesiprofetho.”—The Desire of Ages, p. 234.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Funda uJohane 2:1-11. Ziningi izinto Evangelini likaJohane ezandulela umsebenzi ozayo kaKristu, futhi abaphawulayo baqaphele indlela yamabomu uJohane abhekisa abafundi bakhe ngayo Esiphambanweni. Uzibona kuphi izigaba ezahlukene zesimilo sikaKristu nombuso ozayo zibonakaliswa kulendaba na? Isifundisani uNhlabaelana necebo losindiso, noma uNhlabaelana nedili lokugcina lomshado elithenjiswe Indodana kaNkulunkulu na?`

`Umfanekiso wezintombi eziyishumi waziwa ngokubanzi futhi uyathandwa ngabafundi beBhayibheli. Lendaba ebalulekile isifundisani uNhlabaelana nezigemeko Zesikhathi sokugcina na? Kubalulekile yini ukuthi umkhwenyana ulibele na? Yiziphi izifundo okungabe lomfanekiso uyazifundisa uNhlabaelana nobudlelwane ngamunye esinabo noKristu ngokumelene nendlela uKristu anobudlelwane ngayo nebandla lonke na?`

`Cabanga ngemikhuba yamanga esingene ebuKristwini iphuma ngaphandle kwenkolo. Ngaphandle kwaleyo esobala, iSonto, ngokumelene neSabatha lebhayibheli, yiziphi ezinye izinkolo zamanga esezingenile na? Zingena kanjani, futhi yini esingayenza hhayi kuphela ekuzivikeleni kuzo kodwa nasekusizeni abanye babone ubunjalo bazo na? Kungaziphi izindlela Imibiko yezingelosi ezintathu ezama ukwenza nje lokho, ukusiza abantu babone izinkolo zamanga esezingene ebuKristwini na?`
