---
title:  UIsaka no Rebekah
date:   16/04/2025
---

Ngenkathi uAbrahama esemdala futhi ngokungangabazeki ecabanga ngezithembiso uNkulunkulu ayezenze kuyena uNhlabaelana nenzalo yakhe (buka uGen. 15:5), wanikeza inceku yakhe endala nethembeke kunazo zonke umsebenzi onzima.

`Funda uGenesisi 24:1-4. Kungani kwakubaluleke kakhulu kuAbrahama ukuthi Indodana yakhe ingaganwa “ ‘ngamadodakazi aseKanana’ ” (Gen. 24:3, NKJV) na?`

Nakuba umyalezo wakhe wawukhetha kangakanani, udaba kuAbrahama lwalungezikamoya, hhayi ubuhlanga; lwalungokwemfundiso, hhayi ubuzwe. UAbrahama wayekwazi kahle ukungcola kwemikhuba yenkolo yabaseKanana, ungasabalulanga ukukhonza kwabo onkulunkulu bamanga, futhi wayazi kahle ukuthi kungaba lula kangakanani ukuthi Indodana yakhe iwele kulemikhuba uma ingaganwa phakathi kwawo.

Nembala, lendaba euNhlabaelana kakhulu noIsrayeli Wasendulo, futhi nangebandla lobuKristu eminyakeni yonke, ibe iyona kuyo abantu bakaNkulunkulu—okwakumele bafakaze emhlabeni—bazithole bebambeka emhlabeni nakwizimfundiso zawo nezinkolo zamanga. Mhlawumbe isibonelo esikhulula kunazo zonke saleliqiniso elidabukisayo kube ngukufakwa kweSonto, Usuku lwabahedeni lwelanga, endaweni YeSabatha lebhayibheli losuku lwesikhombisa, iqiniso eliyodlala indima egqamile ezinsukwini zokugcina.

`Funda uGensisi 24:57-67. Yiziphi izifundo esingazikhothoza uNhlabaelana noKristu nebandla Lakhe kweminye imininingwane esiyithola kulendaba na? Yini ekhona ukuba siyifunde, ngokwesibonelo, uNhlabaelana nesimo sethu esiwileyo ekutheni u-Rebekah wayeyisihlobo esiqhelile kuIsaka na?`

Akungabazeki ukuthi sihlobene noMdali wethu, njengoba ekuqaleni senziwa ngomfanekiso Wakhe. Sehlukanisiwe kuYena yisono, kepha, sisathathwa ngokuthi singumlobokazi kuYena, nakuba ngokukhetha kwethu singenza lomshado ubengoxokozela kakhulu kunokudingekile.

`Yebo, uNkulunkulu uyasithanda thina, umlobokazi Wakhe, ngaphezu kokuba simthanda Yena. Yiziphi izinqumo esingazenza, nekumele sizenze, nsuku zonke ezingaqinisa Ukuthanda kwethu uNkulunkulu na? Kusenjalo, yiziphi izinqumo ezingabulala uthando lwethu na?`
