---
title:  Umfazi Oyisifebe KaHoseya
date:   15/04/2025
---

Isicelo sikaNkulunkulu kumprofethi uHoseya singaba ngezinye ezingavamile kunazo zonke ezake zanikezwa omunye wezinceku Zakhe: shada isifebe ngenhloso! Kodwa uNkulunkulu wayesebenzisa uHoseya ukusiza thina siqonde, ngokokubuka Kwakhe, ubuhlungu besono nokuhlubuka komuntu. Ngothando uNkulunkulu wayekhethe unkosikazi, uIsrayeli, owayemphingela ngokuphindaphinda kepha, ngokumangalisayo, Yena wayemamukela futhi amvuselele.

`Qhathanisa uHoseya 1:2; Hoseya 3:1, Sambulo 17:1, 2; neSambulo 18”1-4. Yibuphi ubufebe obubalulwe lapha na? Yiziphi izifundo ibandla lobuKristu elingazifunda kulendaba KaHoseya na? Yiziphi izindlela ibandla eliphinda ngazo Izono zeTestamente Elidala na?`

IBhayibheli lembula ukuthi amaphutha kaIsrayeli kwiTestamente Elidala ayezophindwa kakhulu yibandla likaNkulunkulu LeTestamente Elisha. Abantu bakaNkulunkulu besivumelwano baya ophathe kakhulu ngaphambi kokudingiswa kwabo, beletha imikhuba yezithixo zezizwe ezibakakile esizweni sikaNkulunkulu sesivumelwano. Kunyakakhulu wesine, ibandla lobuKristu, linomuzwa lwenzuzo, lashela umbusi waseRoma, u-Constantine, lamumema ukuba aqale Ukuxazulula izingxabano zebandla. Esimweni ngasinye, abantu bakaNkulunkulu baphumela ngaphandle kobudlelwane babo Naye ukuze bayothola “izisombululo” zalokho ababekubona kuyinkinga.

Amazwi akhethwa nguNkulunkulu enza kubonakale obala ukuthi akazami nje kuphela ukusibonisa lokho esingakwenzanga kahle kodwa nokuthi kumenze Yena azizwe enjani. Labo abakhashelwe ngumlingani bangaba nemizwa yokuhlukumezeka ukungathembeki kwethu kuKristu okuyivusayo ezinkundleni zasezulwini. Mhalwumbe into emangalisayo endabeni KaHoseya yibanga elihanjwa ngumprofethi ukuhlenga umkakhe ophiyazayo.

Lapho sibona isililo sokugcina kubantu, esibiza abantu bakaNkulunkulu baphume eBhabhiloni, kuyaqapheleka ukuthi Yena ubiza abantu Bakhe, hhayi izihambi. Yena ubazi ngokusondelene. Yena uyabathanda. Futhi lapho izwe lilengela ehoreni lalo elibi kakhulu, Yena usanikeza inani losindiso athe walikhokha ukuze asithenge ngengazi Lakhe. Isiphambano sikaKristu, Ngaphezu kwakho konke, kumele sisibonise ukuqiniseka kukaJehovah ngokufuna ukusindiswa abantu Bakhe abaphiyizayo.

`Yiziphi izindlela namuhla noma yiliphi ibandla, nalo elethu, elingabe lithandana nokuhlobonga kwezikamoya na?`
