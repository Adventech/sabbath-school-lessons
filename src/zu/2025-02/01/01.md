### Funda Lokhu Kuleliviki

Jer. 29:23, 24; Hub. 139:1–6; Dan. 12:4; Samb. 22:10; 2 Tim. 3:15–17; Heb. 4:12.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘kepha ozibongayo makazibonge ngalokhu ukuthi uyangiqonda, angazi ukuthi nginguJehova owenza umusa nokwahlulela nokulunga emhlabeni, ngokuba ngiyathokoza ngokunjalo,” usho uJehova” (Jeremiya 9:24, NKJV).

Njengakho cishe konke okunye Ezibhalweni, amaKristu awavumelani uNhlabaelana nesiprofetho, okuvama ukwenza abanye baqiniseke ngokuthi Isiprofetho SeBhayibheli siyincitha sikhathi. Nembala, uma amaKristu ebanga uhlamvu ngalunye lwesiprofetho, singaba yiqiniso kanjani na? Ngeshwa, amakholwa amaningi aseqala Ukucabanga ukuthi Ezinye izincwadi zeBhayibheli, njengeSambulo, aziqondakali nje kwaphela. Kunokuba bazifunde, bayazigwema, ngezinye izikhathi bekhuthazwa ngumfundisi oqonde okuhle ocabanga ukuthi ukufunda Isiprofetho kudala izinkinga kunokuzixazulula.

Kwakungenjalo ngasosonke isikhathi. Kwiminyakakhulu engu-18 yokuqala yomlando wobuKristu, amaKristu amaningi ayengenankinga ngesiprofetho sebhayibheli, futhi kwakunokuvumelana okumangalisayo uNhlabaelana nemibiko yeziprofetho. Yilokho okwakuhloswe nguNkulunkulu: “Kepha ngiyanincenga, bazalwane, ngegama leNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu ukuba nikhulume zwilinye nonke, kungabikho ukwahlukana phakathi kwenu, kodwa nipheleliswe emqondweni munye nasekuboneni kunye” (1 Kor. 1:10, NKJV).

Kuleliviki, sizohlola ezinye zezimiso eziletha ukuqonda okungaguqukiyo nokwethembekile kwesiprofetho.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, UMbasa 5._
