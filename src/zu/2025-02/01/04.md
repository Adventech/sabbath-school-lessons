title:  Daniyeli—Vala Amazwi
date:   01/04/2025
---

`Funda uDaniyeli 12:4. Yini uJehovah ayitshela uDaniyeli lapha na? (Qhathanisa lokhu neSamb. 22:10.)`

Kuyinto evamile ukuzwa abashuuNhlabaeli besebenzisa uDaniyeli 12:4 ukubikezela ukuqubuka kolwazi kweze-technology nesayensi ngaphambi kokuza kukaKristu. Abaningi futhi basebenzisa lesisahluko ukuchaza ukuthuthuka kwezokuhamba okwenzekile kwinyakakhulu eledlule. Iningi lezincwadi zethu zibuke ngalendlela nazo. Nakuba kuyizihumusho ezinomqondo, kungasho okunye, ngokunjalo.

Funda Indimana futhi. Umyalezo wengelosi kuDaniyeli uqale ngomyalelo “wokuvala amazwi, futhi unamathisele incwadi.” Udaba okukhulunywa ngalo yincwadi kaDaniyeli uqobo. Mhlawumbe, ke, ngabe lololwazi oluzokwanda ngokuphazima ekugcineni kwesikhathi wulwazi lwencwadi kaDaniyeli uqobo na?

Lokhu kwenza Incwadi kaDaniyeli yehluke kwiSambulo, ngokuthi uJohane watshelwa ukuba angayinamathiseli incwadi yakhe (Samb. 22:10). ISambulo kwakumele siqondwe kusukela ekuqaleni, ngoba “ ‘isikhathi sasesifikile.’ ” Ngokwehlukile, uDaniyeli wayezoqondwa kangcono kakhulu esikhathi esithile kwikusasa eliqhelile.

Eminyakeni eminingi, abahlaziyi bamaKristu bazama ukuchaza incwadi kaDaniyeli, futhi abanye baphumelela. Ukuqonda uDaniyeli kwanda ngokushesha, Kepha, ekupheleni kwesiprofetho seminyaka engu-1,260, eyaphela ngo-1798, Ngenkathi abahlaziyi abaningi emhlabeni beqala ukufinyelela esiphethweni sokuthi Okuthile Okukhulu kwakuzokwenzeka ngo-1843. Abagqamile kulabo, nokho, kwakungu-William Miller, okushumyela kwakhe kwavusa i-Great Advent Movement yonyakakhulu luka-19 futhi kwaqala uhla lwezigameko ezazizozala Ibandla “lensali” kanye nokuqonda kahle kwemibiko yezingelosi ezintathu.

Ukuzalwa kohlelo lwethu emhlabeni wonke, ngamanye amazwi, kungukugcwaliseka kwesiprofetho sikaDaniyeli sokuthi “ulwazi luyokwanda” “esikhathini sokuphela.”

Ngokwehlukile , futhi ngaphandle kokwahlulela usindiso lwabantu, cabanga ngo “bumnyama” obengamele ubuKristu obubanzi. Into eyisisekelo njengeSabatha losuku lwesikhombisa, elasekwa eEdeni, ayinakiwe, futhi iyachithwa, ngokwamukela iSonto, Usuku olusekwe ebuhedenini. Noma cabanga ngokungazi kakhulu ngokufa, ngeningi lamaKristu lokukholwa umqondo wobuhedeni wokuthi abafile ngokuphazima bantingela kokunye ukuphila, okuthi kwabanye kuchaze ukusha ingunaphakade Esihogweni.

Ngokwehlukile, kume sibonge kakhulu—futhi sithobeke—ngolwazi lweqiniso.
