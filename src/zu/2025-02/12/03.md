---
title:  Ukukhonza Umfanekiso
date:   16/06/2025
---

Nakuba ukuthembeka u-Nebuchadnezzar, ethathekile ngalokho uDaniyeli ayekwenzile, abanako ekuqaleni kuDaniyeli nakuNkulunkulu wakhe (buka uDaniyeli 2, ikakhulu vs. 46-48), akuzange kuhlale isikhathi.

`Funda uDaniyeli 3:1-12.Yikuphi ukubaluleka esingakuthola ekutheni umfanekiso wawuyigilode wonke nokuthi inkosi yaphoqa ukuthi ukhonzwe na?`

Inkosi yagcizelela ukudelela kwayo umbiko kaNkulunkulu ngokwakha umfanekiso owenziwe ngegolide kuphela. Umbiko? IBabiloni ayisoze yawa, futhi u-Nebuchadnezzar uyohlezi eyinkosi. Futhi noma ngubani onesibindi sokuphikisana nalokho wayezobulawa. Kuba yisikhumbuzo esinamandla sokuthi inkanuko yethu yesintu yokuzilawula ingasenza singaboni ngokushesha iqiniso lwendlela impikiswano enkulu eyothi indlaleke ngayo.

Ngezindlela ezithile, u-Nebuchadnezzar ubonakalisa izimpawu zikaLusifa: wayefuna okungcono, ezibona ebalulekile, futhi enokuzidla ngokwanele ukuba ahlubuke obala egunyeni likaNkulunkulu. Ngezinye izindlela, nembala, kunomehluko ogqamile: u-Nebuchadnezzar wafinyelela ekukholweni kuNkulunkulu weqiniso futhi kungenzeka sohlangana naye embuseni yena aqala ngokulwisana nawo.

`Funda uDaniyeli 3:17, 18, amazwi abafana abathathu bamaHeberu bemelana nenkosi. Lokhu kusifundisani uNhlabaelana nokholo nalokho olungakufuna kithina ngezinye izikhathi na?`

Cabanga Ngendlela okwakungabalula ukuthi Lamadoda amathathu ahlaziye indlela engcono yokuphuma kulesisimo esiyingozi. Empeleni babeyobe bengewona amashanhliziyo, azimisele ngokushiswa ephila ngokukhothama nje kuphela na? Babengembuluze, bakhothamele ukubopha izintambo zezicathulo ngenkathi bekhuleka kuNkulunkulu wabo na? Kwafanelekile ngempela lokho ababebhekene nako na? Nembala, bacabanga kanjalo, nakuba amazwi abo abonisa ukuthi babazi ukuthi ngeke baphunyuke kulokho bephila.

`Singakugwema kanjani ukuzicabangela okukhona okusinikeza amathuba okuyekelela okholweni lwethu na? Lombhalo Uthini ngokubhekise esilingweni esifanayo: “ ‘Lowo othembekile kokuncinyane uthembekile nakokukhulu; ongalungile kokuncinyane kalungile nakokukhulu’ ” (Luka 16:10, NKJV) na?`
