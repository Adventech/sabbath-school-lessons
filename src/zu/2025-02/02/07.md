title:  Ukucabanga Kabanzi
date:   11/04/2025
---

Funda u-Ellen G. White, “The Revelation,” pp. 578–581 in The Acts of the Apostles.

Iningi lezinkolo zezwe zibhekana nemiqondo nje kuphela; ngokumelene kakhulu, imiqondo etholakala kwinkolo yobuKristu isekelwe ngokuqinile ezigamekweni zomlando. IBhayibheli liyindaba kaNkulunkulu exhumana nesintu kuwo wonke Umlando, futhi ngokufunda ngalokho kuxhumana kweminyaka eyizinkulungwane, singafunda okuningi uNhlabaelana nokungaguquki kukaNkulunkulu.

Ngezinye izikhathi, nokho, amaKristu akhononda ngokuthi ayakhathala ngokuzwa izinto ezizodwa. Ngezinye izikhathi, ngenkathi umbiko wethu ohlukile wesiprofetho ushuuNhlabaelwa emphakathini, sicabanga ukuthi sesike sawuzwa futhi akukho okusha esingakufunda.

Ukuthi umbiko wethu awuguquki futhi uyaphikelela, nokho, akuchazi ukuthi ulula noma awunayo Inselelo.

Ngokuphikayo: ngenkathi ufunda ulwazi esilunikeziwe lusuka engqondweni kaNkulunkulu Waphakade, usheshe uthole ukuthi ngeke wafinyelela ekugcineni kwesihloko.

Esinye sezizathu u-Ellen G. White azikhomba njengesizathu sokubhalwa kwencwadi yeSambulo ngesokugxilisa Ibandla lobuKristu embikweni walo womlando wesikhathi sonke. “Abanye babasebenzi abasebasha [ngalesosikhathi]. . . basebekhathale ngamaqiniso aphindaphindwayo. Ekufiseni kwabo ukuthola okusha nokwethusayo bazama ukwethula izigaba ezintsha zezimfundiso.”—The Acts of the Apostles, p. 580. Kangangokuba, akuyona nje incwadi euNhlabaelana nekusasa; iyincwadi futhi yekamuva, ehlelelwe ukusigcina sigxilile okholweni lwethu lomlando ukuze singaholelwa ophathe yizinkanuko zokufuna okwendabuko.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana:**

`Izibhalo azehluleki ukuveza ulwazi olusha kanye nokuqonda okusha. Othile ulinganisa kanjani inkanuko Yokufunda okusha kanye nokubaluleka kokuhlala uzinzile emaqinisweni esesiwanikeziwe na?`

`Kumele ibandla liphendule kanjani ezihumushweni ezintsha zesiprofetho na? Nakuba sazi ukuthi ngasosonke isikhathi kukhona okweningi kokufunda, sibona kanjani ukuthi ukukhanya okusha kudingekile noma kuyinganekwane, noma futhi kuyiphutha na?`

`Empini Yesibili Yomhlaba itilosi kwi-Pacific lalala lifa, lamemeza udokotela, “Ngiyintandane. Lapho ngifa, ngubani oyongikhumbula na?” Udokotela waphendula, “Mina ngiyokukhumbula njalo.” Nakuba ayenenhloso enhle lawomazwi kadokotela, kungekudala naye wayezofa, futhi kanjalo nenkumbulo yomatilosi oyintandane. Lendaba isisiza kanjani ukuba sibone ubuze bayo yonke impilo Yethu uma ukufa, ekugcineni, kunezwi lokugcina na?`
