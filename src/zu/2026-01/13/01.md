### Funda lokhu kuleliviki
Kol. 4:7–18; Efes. 6:21; Izenzo 15:36– 40; 2 Tim. 4:10, 11; 2 Pet. 3:10–14; Isa. 60:1–3.

> <p>Indimana Yenkumbulo</p>
> “kukho konke bongani; ngokuba lokhu kuyintando kaNkulunkulu kuKristu Jesu ngani” (1 Thesalonika 5:18, NKJV).

Lesisigaba sokugcina sabaseKolose sembula ububanzi bohlelokuxhumana lwabantu abasebenzisana noPawule. Izenzo zibonisa yena ehlanganyela noBarnaba okokuqala, bese kuba noSila, bese kuba nokubukezwa kohambo oluthathu lwezovangelo.

Kuleliviki sizobuka ecebeni likaPawule lezovangelo, elalifaka ukusebenzisa ngekhono isikhathi kanye nezinsiza ekufinyeleleni izizinda ezinkulu zoMbuso waseRoma, kanye nokuqeqesha abashumayeli abathembisayo ukuze bafinyelele emadolobheni uPawule ayengezukuwahambela, njengeKolose, Lawodekiya, ne-Hierapolis.

Ngokuvakasha kwakhe uqobo ngesikhathi sohambo lwakhe futhi, nangesikhathi eyisiboshwa, ngezincwadi zakhe, uPawule waxhumanisa ngasosonke isikhathi abantu namabandla. Wabona ukuthi impumelelo yomsebenzi wevangeli yayincike kubo bonke besebenzisana—amaKristu amaJuda nawabeZizwe; amadoda nabesifazani; abantu abanjengo-Tychichus, Aristarchus, Justus, Epaphras, Luka, no-Nympha. Ngokuthathekisayo, sizwa futhi ngencwadi ayibhalela abaseLawodekiya esingagcinelwanga yona. UPawule ufaka okuningi kulezizindimana zokugcina, kuhlangene nokuyala kwakhe othile obizwa ngo-Archippus. Wenza konke anako ukuqinisa amabandla esenako.

_Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, uNdasa 28._
