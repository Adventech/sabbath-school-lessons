`Funda abaseKolose 4:14, 15 no 2 Timothewu 4:10, 11. ULuka wehlukaniswa kanjani no-Demas futhi kungani na?`

Umpostoli uJohane uyasitshela ukuthi, “Ningathandi izwe nezinto ezisezweni. Uma umuntu ethanda izwe, uthando lukaYise alukho kuye” (Johane 2:15). Uthando lukaLuka ngoJesu nombuso Wakhe kwamholela ukuba ame noPawule kuze kube sekugcineni, noma kwenzekani, kodwa u-Demas wathanda lelizwe ukwedlula izwe elizayo.

**Funda izindimana ezilandelayo. Yisiphi iseluleko esinikezwa kulabo abalindele Ukuza Kwesibili na?**

`Marko 13:32–37`

`Titu 2:11–14`

`2 Pet. 3:10–14`

`Samb. 3:17–21`

UJesu nabapostoli baxwayisa njalo ukuba “siqaphele,” silinde, futhi silunge ngaso sonke isikhathi sokuza kweNkosi ukuze singazumeki. Ngeshwa, njengalokhu kwehluleka abafundi ukulandela umyalelo kaJesu woku “linda nikhulule” (Marko 14:38, NKJV), iningi ngeke liwenze amalungiselelo adingekile. Konke lokhu kuma ekutheni yini noma ngubani olawula izinhliziyo zethu, ngoba ngeke sakhonza amakhosi amabili.

Embikweni wabase-Laodicea, uJesu unikeza ikhambi eliqondile. Okokuqala, phendukani ezonweni zenu. Okwesibili, kumele sivulele uJesu izinhliziyo zethu ukuba azilawule, okwenza (okwesithathu) sikwazi ukuzuza “igolide” lokho nothando oluvivinywe nezinhlupheko futhi lwanqoba izilingo.

`Yikuphi okuqondile uJesu angafuna wena uphenduke kukho empilweni yakho na? Iyiphi ingxenye yalelikhambi oyidinga kakhulu na?`
