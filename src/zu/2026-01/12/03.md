---
title:  Abazali Nabantwana
date:   16/03/2026
---

Abantwana banendima ebalulekile ngengengxenye yemboni yomndeni. Badinga ukwazi ukuthi bayathandwa futhi bangamalungu abalulekile omndeni kanye nezakhamuzi zombuso wezulu. Umkhuleko womndeni usemqoka, ulula futhi wenzeka njalo, ekuseni nantambama. Ukusukela eminyakeni yokuqala, abantwana bangaqala ukusiza ngokuhlanza ikhaya nangeminye imithwalo. Okubaluleke kunakho konke, kumele balalele umyalelo kaPawule: “Lalelani abazali benu ezintweni zonke, ngokuba lokho kuyathandeka eNkosini” (Kol. 3:20, NKJV).

**Funda izindimana ezilandelayo. Yiziphi izimiso ezinikezwa lapha zokukhulisa abantwana na?**

`Zaga. 22:6, 15`

`Mat. 19:14`

`Dut. 6:6, 7`

`Zaga. 1:8, 9`

Beqeqeshelwe iNkosi, ngezimiso nangesibonelo, abantwana bayoba yisibusiso emndenini, ebandleni, nangale. Futhi umyalelo kaPawule kubazali, njengomyalelo wakhe kumadoda namakhosikazi, ulingene futhi uyasizana: “Boyise, ningabathukuthelisi abantwana benu ukuba bangadangali” (Kol. 3:21, NKJV). Indlela abazali, ikakhulu ubaba, axhumana ngayo futhi nahlwaya ngayo abantwana ithinta kakhulu indlela abakhula ngayo kwezikamoya.

Ucwaningo luyabonisa, futhi, ukuthi ngenkathi bobabili abazali beya enkonzweni, inani elikhulu labantwana liqhubeka nokuya enkonzweni, ngokumelene nokuya komzali oyedwa. Futhi okumangalisa kakhulu, ukuya enkonzweni okungaphazamiseki kukababa, ngaphezu kukamama, kuchaza inani elikhulu labantwana lihlala enkonzweni ebudaleni. Indima kababa, ke, ekwakhekeni kwabantwana kwezikamoya, ngeke yathathwa kancane. Kusemqoka ukuthi obaba bathathe indima yabo ngokuqinile kakhulu.
