---
title:  Ukugcwala Izulu Emqondweni
date:   08/03/2026
---

`Funda abaseKolose 3:1-4. Yisiphi isimo uPawule athi siyadingeka ukuze thina sigcwale izulu emqondweni na? Ucabanga ukuthi kuchazani lokho na?`

Phezu kwentaba, kuyenzeka ukwazi ukuhlola ukuma komhlaba okuzungezile. Ukusukela emandulo, izintaba zihanjelwe kaningi yilabo abafuna amava asondelene noNkulunkulu (buka iHub. 121:1, 2). Nazo izintaba ezakhiwe ngabantu, ezibizwa ngama-ziggurat, zakhiwe ngabahedeni ngenhloso efanayo—ukuhlangana nonkulunkulu. Ngouthathekisayo, idolobha lase-Ur, kulo okwabizwa u-Abrama ukuba alishiye, laline-ziggurat enkulu eyayibonakala usekude kakhulu. Kodwa ukuguqula ukuphakama kothile ngeke ngokwako kudonsele noma ngubani ezulwini ngokwezikamoya. Imizamo yabantu ngeke yakwazi.

Kunalokho, ngesimangaliso somusa kuphela, lapha sife noKristu futhi savuswa kanye Naye (ngokufanekiswa ngombhabhathizo [Kol. 2:12, 13]), kungenzeka sidonseleke ezulwini.

Qaphela ukuthi ukusukela ekuqaleni kwabaseKolose 3 isigcizelelo esiphindiwe kulokho okuphezulu, okungukuthi, lokho okusezulwini: “lezo zinto ezingaphezulu,” “lapho kukhona uKristu,” “izinto eziphezulu,” “noKristu kuNkulunkulu,” “kanye Naye enkazimulweni (Kol. 3:1-4, NKJV).

Siyavuma, kuningi empilweni yobuKristu okungachazekiyo. Umuntu “angafa” kanjani ngempela futhi “avuswe” ngenkathi, kukho konke ukubonakala, yena useyilowo muntu futhi akanawo amava okuphila nokufa na? Kuningi okungenzi mqondo engqondweni yemvelo, engenalo ithonya likaMoya Ongcwele. Kodwa kulabo abacabanga ngokwezikamoya, ngoba bamukele inhliziyo entsha ethenjiswe nguNkulunkulu, ukufa esonweni kanye nokuvuswa noKristu ngamaqiniso angampela. Njengoba iculo liqinisa: “Buza ngaz’ ngan’Yen’uyaphila? Yen’ uphil’ enhliz’wen’.”

Nakuba kunjalo, uPawule unikeza lemiyalelo ngoba kukhona isidingo esiqhubekayo sokuvuselela lempilo yezikamoya (buka 2 Kor. 4:16). Thina, nembala, singawa futhi silahleke! Futhi asiphephile ekulingweni kulokhu kuphila.

Ngalokho, kumele thina sikhethe nsuku zonke “ukufuna lezozinto ezingaphezulu” (Kol. 3:1). Ukuphila kwethu kwaphakade “kufihlwe noKristu kuNkulunkulu” (Kol. 3:3, NKJV) ngokuphepha, kodwa izibonakaliso zangaphandle zaleyompilo azicashile.

`Ikuphi imicabango yakho: ngokuvamile ngaphezulu noma ngaphansi na? Uma kungaphansi, uyiguqula kanjani na?`
