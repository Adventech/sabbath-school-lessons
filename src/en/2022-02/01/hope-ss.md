### Outline #1 The Creation

*  The God of Creation
	* The inspired testimony of God as Creator is woven throughout the entire Bible, from Genesis to Revelation. Genesis 1:1, Psalm 33:6,9, 95:1-6, 100:3, John 1:1-3, Revelation 4:11, 14:7
	* What is the significance of the fact that in the two creation accounts recorded in Genesis, God is presented as transcendent Elohim (Genesis 1:1-2:4) and immanent Yahweh (Genesis 2:4-25)?
	* What is an appropriate response to this profound truth that God is Creator of all things? Psalm 95:1-5, 100:1-3, 139:14

*  The Details of Creation as recorded in Genesis
	* Genesis 1:4,10,12,18,21,25,31 What is the significance of the refrain “it was good” (tov in Hebrew) in the first creation ac count?
	* Genesis 2:4-9, 18,21-25 What new revelations regarding creation do you see in the second creation account?
	* How did Jesus relate to the Genesis account of creation? Matthew 19:4
	* How has the Bible truth of God as our Creator impacted your life?

*  The Creation of Humanity in God’s Image
	* Genesis 1:26-29 How did the creation of humans differ drastically from the creation of other living creatures?
	* What is the significance of the fact that God created man and woman in His image, after His likeness?
	* Where do you see the image of God reflected in the lives of individuals in the Bible? Hebrews 1:1-3, John 14:9. Other Bible characters?

*  The Duties of Humanity
	* Genesis 2:15-17 What expectations did God express to Adam when he was placed in his garden home?
	* How was Adam expected to care for his companion Eve? Genesis 2:24 (see also Ephesians 5:25-29)

*  The Sabbath as a Sacred Reminder of Creation
	* Genesis 2:1-3 How is the Sabbath an important reminder of God as our Creator?
	* Why is it important to remember God always completes the work He starts? Genesis 2:1, Philippians 1:6, Hebrews 7:25, Jude 25
	* Exodus 20:8-11 Why was it necessary for our Creator to remind us about the precious gift of the Sabbath?
	* How have you grown in your appreciation of the Sabbath as a time to worship your Creator?
