#### inQuire

`What does the parable of the wheat and the tares tell us about the origin and presence of evil in the world?`

`What does the parable of the wheat and the tares tell us about the presence and triumph of good in the world?`

`How have you experienced God’s goodness in your life?`

`What changes does God’s goodness bring about in your life?`

`How can we trust God’s goodness even when life is at its lowest point?`

`How can we inspire others to recognize God’s goodness in a world of suffering and pain?`

`Why do you think Jesus calls Himself “the Son of Man”? What is the significance of this title?`

`Why is the Son of Man (Jesus) the only One able to judge people at the end of time?`

`How can we make sure we belong to God and avoid getting rooted out like the tares?`
