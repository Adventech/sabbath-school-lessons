#### inQuire

Discuss the following questions with your Bible study group.

`What experiences has God given you that you have already shared with others to encourage them?`

`What experiences has God given you that you have not shared with others? Why have you not shared?`

`What is the danger of only believing another’s report of a situation?`

`What principles of witnessing and tact can we learn from Nehemiah’s experience?`

`What are principles of biblical leadership for eliciting the people’s support?`

`What are the principles of leadership in handling opposition?`

`What walls in your life need rebuilding?`

`How does one balance the emphasis between the people and the project?`
