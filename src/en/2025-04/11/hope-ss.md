### Part #11 Living In The Land

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing a series of studies on Lessons of Faith from Joshua. Today’s topic: LIVING IN THE LAND.

**Scripture Song:** Be strong and of good courage – Deut. 31:6

- **Working together**
	- What request did the leaders of the tribes of Reuben and Gad make to Moses before the children of Israel crossed the River Jordan? Numbers 32:1-5,16-19 (see also Joshua 12:6)
	- How did Moses respond to their request? Numbers 32:20-22
	- What can we learn from the testimony of Joshua about working together as a community of faith? Joshua 22:1-4
	- What admonition and gift did Joshua share with these Transjordanian tribes? Joshua 22:5-8
	- What lessons can we learn from this story about working together in unity? Colossians 3:23-24, Galatians 5:13-14, 1 Peter 4:10, etc.
- **Conflicts arise**
	- What action of the Transjordanian tribes was a source of conflict with their relatives on the west side of the Jordan? Joshua 22:9-12,13-19
	- Was this “great, impressive altar” on the east side of the River Jordan an act of rebellion or a memorial like the first altar constructed when the children of Israel crossed into Canaan? (see Joshua 4:1-7, 22:21-29)
	- Why do you think the Transjordanian leaders declare “El, Elohim, Yahweh” two times?
	- Why is it important to remember that “He knows!”? 1 Samuel 16:7, Hebrews 4:12-13
	- How is the counsel of the Psalmist helpful when we are falsely accused? Psalm 37:1-6
	- How could this conflict have been avoided? Ephesians 4:1-3, Philippians 2:1-4, John 7:24, 1 Corinthians 4:5, etc.
	- What lessons can we learn from this story for our lives today? Proverbs 15:1, Luke 6:36-38
	- Share a time when a conflict arose in your community of faith that could have been avoided if time had been taken to listen attentively. James 1:19
- **Finding a peaceful resolution**
	- How did Phinehas and the leaders from the west side of the River Jordan respond when they heard the testimony of the Transjordanian leaders? Joshua 22:30-33
	- Why is it important to be honest when we have made a mistake? 1 Peter 5:5b
	- What are your thoughts about the name given to this altar by the Transjordanian leaders? Joshua 22:34
	- What important lesson have you learned from this story of conflict and resolution for your life today?
