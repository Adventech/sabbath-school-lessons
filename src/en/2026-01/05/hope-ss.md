### Shining As Lights In The World

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series on Inspired Letters from Prison. Today’s topic from Philippians: SHINING AS LIGHTS IN THE WORLD.

**Scripture Song:** Rejoice always – 1 Thessalonians 5:16-18, Philippians 4:6

- **Cooperating with God**
	- Philippians 2:12 What faulty conclusion might you come to just reading this verse?
	- Compare the following inspired testimonies by the same author (the Apostle Paul): Ephesians 2:8-9, Romans 3:23-24, 5:8.
	- How does Philippians 2:13 help us to understand the Apostle Paul’s comment recorded in v.12?
	- Why is it vitally important for us allow God to work in us according to His good pleasure?
- **Shining as lights in a dark world**
	- Philippians 2:14 How is it possible to do all things without complaining and disputing?
	- Philippians 2:15 The Apostle Paul described the Philippian culture as a “crooked and perverse generation.” How would a prophet of God describe our culture today?
	- Philippians 2:15-16 How does the work of God in us and through us cause us to shine as lights in this dark world? (see also John 8:12, Matthew 5:14-16)
- **A living sacrifice**
	- Philippians 2:17-18 What does it mean to be poured out as a living sacrifice? (see also 2 Timothy 4:6)
	- Where is the Scriptures can we find an appeal to present our lives as living sacrifices to God? Romans 12:1-2
	- What counsel would you give to someone who says, “I’m afraid to surrender my life completely to God”?
- **Examples of shining lights**
	- Timothy: Philippians 2:19-24 What words of affirmation did the Apostle Paul share about Timothy? (see also Timothy 1:3-7, 1 Timothy 2:15-16)
	- Epaphroditus: Philippians 2:25-30 What can we learn about Epaphroditus from his name and the words of affirmation the Apostle Paul shared about him?
	- Shining lights today: Share a time you saw the light of Jesus shining brightly through a follower of Jesus.
	- Why is it important to offer words affirmation and encouragement to fellow Christians when we see the light of Jesus shining through them?
	- Why is it dangerous to be constantly examining ourselves to see if the light of Jesus is shining brightly through us? Hebrews 12:1-2
	- What important lessons have you learned from our study today?
