### The Preeminence Of Christ

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series on Inspired Letters from Prison. Today we shift our focus to Colossians and THE PREEMINENCE OF CHRIST.

**Scripture Song:** Rejoice always – 1 Thessalonians 5:16-18, Philippians 4:6

- **Paul’s letter to the Colossians**
	- Share some background about Paul’s inspired letter from prison to Christians in Colossae.
	- Who was Epaphras? (Colossians 1:3-8, 4:12)
	- What lessons can we learn from the growth of the Christian community in Colossae?
- **Paul’s prayer for Christians in Colossae**
	- Colossians 1:9-12 What are your thoughts about the Apostle Paul’s prayer at the beginning of his letter?
	- It is a great blessing to have someone praying for you. Who has been a prayer warrior in your life?
- **Paul’s exalted revelation of Jesus Christ**
	- Jesus Christ: Redeemer
		- Colossians 1:13-14 What important revelation about Jesus Christ does the Apostle Paul share at the beginning of his inspired letter from prison to Christians in Colossae?
		- Where else in the Scriptures do we find a clear teaching that salvation comes faith in Jesus and what He has accomplished for us? John 3:16, 1 Peter 2:21-24, etc.)
	- Jesus Christ: Image of the Invisible God
		- Colossians 1:15a What does the expression “image of the invisible God” mean? (Colossians 1:19, Hebrews 1:1-3, John 14:9, 10:30, etc.)
		- Where do you see the image of the invisible God most clearly revealed in the life of Jesus?
	- Jesus Christ: Firstborn over all creation
		- Colossians 1:15b What does the expression “firstborn over all creation” mean?
		- What reasons does the Apostle Paul give for Jesus being called the firstborn over all creation? Colossians 1:16-17 (see also John 1:1-3)
	- Jesus Christ: Head of the church
		- Colossians 1:18 Why is it important to remember that Jesus Christ is head of the church? (see also Colossians 2:10,18-19)
		- Colossians 1:19-22 How did God the Father reconcile all things to Himself through His Son Jesus Christ?
- **Discussion questions**
	- What are your thoughts about the exalted revelation of Christ given by the Apostle Paul in his inspired letter from prison to Christians in Colossae?
	- What is your personal response as you consider all God has done to deliver you from the power of darkness and redeem you? (Colossians 1:13)
