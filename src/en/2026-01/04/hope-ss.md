### Unity In Christ

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series on Inspired Letters from Prison. Today’s topic from Philippians: UNITY IN CHRIST.

**Scripture Song:** Rejoice always – 1 Thessalonians 5:16-18, Philippians 4:6

- **The problem of disunity**
	- Philippians 2:1-3a What were some reasons for disunity among the Christians in Philippi?
	- What were some other causes of disunity in the early Christian church? 1 Corinthians 1:11-13, Luke 22:24, etc.
	- What are some major causes of disunity among Christians today?
	- Share a time you belonged to a group that was experiencing disunity. How did this disunity impact you personally?
	- What counsel would you give to someone who is being negatively impacted by a spirit of disunity?
- **The antidote for disunity**
	- According to the Apostle Paul, what is the antidote for disunity? Philippians 2:3-4
	- Which part of this antidote impresses you the most? (being like-minded, having the same love, etc.)
	- How is it possible for everyone to have the same agape love? Romans 5:5, Luke 11:13
	- How does a spirit of humility and love reflect the mind of Christ? Philippians 2:5
	- Share an example from the New Testament of a follower of Jesus whose life was a revelation of the mind of Christ.
	- How can we have the mind of Christ? 1 Corinthians 2:16, Romans 12:2
	- What changes have you noticed in your life since you have asked God to renew your mind?
- **The exaltation of Jesus**
	- Philippians 2:6-8 Where can we see the greatest demonstration of humility and agape love?
	- What impresses you the most as you reflect of this inspired testimony of Christ’s humility and self-sacrificing love?
	- Who has helped you to appreciate more fully the self-sacrificing love of Jesus?
	- How did God the Father respond to Christ’s demonstration of humility and agape love?
	- How does the exaltation of Jesus bring glory to God the Father? Philippians 2:9-11, John 14:8-11
	- How should we respond to Jesus, considering all He has done for us? John 20:26-28, Revelation 5:11-14, etc.
	- How can we help others to see a clearer revelation of the immeasurable, unfailing love of God?
