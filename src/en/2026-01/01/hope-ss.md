### Persecuted But Not Forsaken

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are beginning a new series on Inspired Letters from Prison: Philippians and Colossians. Today’s topic: PERSECUTED BUT NOT FORSAKEN.

**Scripture Song:** Rejoice always – 1 Thessalonians 5:16-18, Philippians 4:6

- **Paul, a prisoner in Rome**
	- Acts 28:16,30 How long was the Apostle Paul held under house arrest in Rome?
	- What inspired letters did Paul write while imprisoned in Rome? Philippians 1:1-7, Colossians 1:1-2,4:2-3 (these two inspired letters will be the focus of our study in this series)
	- What other inspired letters did Paul write during his first imprisonment in Rome? (Ephesians, Philemon)
	- Why did Paul identify himself as “a prisoner of Jesus Christ” rather than “a prisoner of Rome”? Ephesians 3:1, Philemon v.1
	- How did Paul use his time in prison as “ambassador in chains”? Ephesians 6:20, Philippians 1:12-13
	- What other hardships had Paul experienced that prepared him for this time of imprisonment in Rome? 2 Corinthians 4:7-12
	- What can we learn from Paul about how to face hardship and persecution? 2 Corinthians 6:3-7
	- Why is the promise of the LORD recorded in Hebrews 13:5 meaningful for your life today?
- **Paul’s first visit to Philippi**
	- What miraculous intervention on Paul’s second missionary journey led to his first visit to Philippi? Acts 16:6-12
	- What was special about the city of Philippi?
	- Who did Paul meet shortly after his arrival in Philippi? Acts 16:13-15
	- What suffering did Paul and his companion Silas experience in Philippi? Acts 16:22-23 (see Acts 9:16)
- **Paul and the city of Colossae**
	- When did the Apostle Paul visit Colossae?
	- Where was Colossae located?
	- Who first shared the Gospel message with the citizens of Colossae? Colossians 1:1-8, 4:12-13
	- Where did Epaphras learn about Jesus? Acts 19:1,8-10 (possibly while visiting Ephesus)
- **Inspired letters from prison**
	- What does the word “inspired” mean? 2 Timothy 3:16, 2 Peter 1:20-21, 3:14-15
	- Who delivered Paul’s letter to believers in Philippi? Philippians 2:25, 4:18
	- What do we know about Paul’s two companions who delivered his letter to the believers in Colossae? Colossians 4:7-9 (see Philemon v.16)
	- What are your thoughts about the way Paul begins these letters? Philippians 1:1-3, Colossians 1:1-2
