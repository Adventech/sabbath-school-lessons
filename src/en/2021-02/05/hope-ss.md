### Outline #5	Children of the Promise

*  The LORD is your shield and exceedingly great reward
	* Genesis 15:1 What are some reasons Abram might have found to be fearful?
	* What encouragement is found in the promise “I am your Shield”? See also Psalm 18:30, 84:11
	* Share some other Bible passages that express this same promise in different words. Isaiah 41:10, Revelation 1:17-18, 1 Corinthians 10:13, etc.
	* How do you respond to the precious truth that the LORD is your exceedingly great reward? (see John 14:23)

*  The Messiah promise
	* Genesis 12:1-3, 18:17-18, 22:18 Why does the LORD repeat this promise multiple times?
	* What lesson can we learn from the fact that the LORD repeated the Messiah promise to Jacob?  Genesis 28:11-14
	* How have all nations of the earth been blessed through Abraham’s seed? Matthew 1:1
	* How has Jesus, Son of Abraham, brought blessing to the entire world? John 3:16, Romans 10:13, John 8:12, etc.
	* How have you been blessed since you learned the truth about Jesus and accepted Him as your Savior and Lord?
	* What precious promise did Jesus give to His disciples before He ascended to heaven? John 14:1-3

*  A great and mighty nation
	* Genesis 12:2 Why did the LORD make Abraham’s descendants a great and mighty nation?
	* When was this promise fulfilled? Exodus 1:6-7
	* Sometimes the LORD fulfills His promises at times and in ways we do not expect.  How should we prepare ourselves for such unexpected outcomes? Psalm 27:13-14, Colossians 4:2, etc.
	* What special mission assignment was given to the descendants of Abraham? Exodus 19:5-6, Deuteronomy 4:6-8, Isaiah 60:1-3, etc.
	* As children of the promise (Galatians 3:29), what special mission assignment is given to each one of us? 1 Peter 2:9, Mark 16:15, Matthew 28:19-20

*  A great name
	* Genesis 12:2 How did the LORD fulfill this promise to Abram? Galatians 3:6-9 Hebrews 11:8-10,17-19
	* Who are some other Bible characters whose names became great, not to bring glory to themselves but to bring glory to God? Joseph, Daniel, Esther, etc.
	* How can we avoid a proud spirit if the LORD chooses to bless us and make our name great?
