### Outline #13	The New Covenant Life

*  Experiencing fulness of joy
	* John 15:9-11 What is the desire of Jesus for each one of us as we abide in His love? (see also John 10:10)
	* 1 John 1:1-4 How did the apostle John restate this promise of Jesus in his first epistle?
	* Why does resting in a loving covenant relationship with God generate fullness of joy? (Galatians 5:22)
	* Share a time when you experienced fullness of joy in the LORD.

*  Experiencing freedom from the guilt of sin
	* Romans 8:1 (see also John 8:10-11)
	* How can a holy God declare there is no condemnation to those who have broken His holy law and violated their covenant relationship with Him? 1 Peter 2:21-24, 2 Corinthians 5:21, Romans 3:23-26, Isaiah 53:4-6, etc.
	* 1 John 1:9 What important truth is often missed in this teaching by the apostle John?
	* John 5:24 How do you respond to this amazing declaration of Jesus?
	* What encouragement would you offer to a friend who feels burdened by the guilt of sin?

*  Experiencing and sharing the love of God
	* Ephesians 3:14-21 What impacts you the most as you listen to the apostle Paulâ€™s prayer for you?
	* Romans 5:5 How is the love of God poured into our hearts? (see also 1 John 4:13-16)
	* John 7:37-39 What important truths is Jesus sharing here?
	* Share an example (either from the Bible or your life today) of the love of God flowing through someone to bless others.
	* Share a time when you experienced the love of God in a memorable way.

*  Having the assurance of eternal life
	* John 11:25-26 How can we live even if we die? (see John 6:39-40, John 5:28-29
	* Where else in the Scriptures can we find the promise repeated of a resurrection from the dead? 1 Thessalonians 4:13-18, Revelation 20:6, 2:11
	* How does the experience and assurance of eternal life help us in the trials and challenges of this life?

*  Sharing the Good News
	* Matthew 28:19-20 Why it is impossible to keep the Good News to ourselves if we are abiding in a loving covenant relationship with the LORD?
	* How has your life been blessed as you have shared the Good News about Jesus with others?
