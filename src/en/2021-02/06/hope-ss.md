### Outline #6	Abraham’s Seed

*  A chosen people
	* Deuteronomy 7:6-8 Why did the LORD choose the descendants of Abraham to be His special people?
	* What additional revelation from the LORD is recorded by the prophet Ezekiel? Ezekiel 16:8
	* It would be easy to become proud if the LORD told you He had chosen you “above all the peoples on the face of the earth”. How would you respond?

*  The Promised Land
	* Genesis 12:1, 13:14-17, 15:13-16, 28:13, 50:24
	* When did the LORD intend for the children of Israel to possess the Promised Land? Numbers 13:1-2
	* Why were the Israelites initially unwilling to possess the Promised Land? Numbers 13:31-33, 14:1-2
	* Who was eventually chosen by the LORD to lead the children of Israel into the Promised Land? Joshua 1:1-5 Why Joshua? (See Numbers 14:6-8)
	* What lesson can we learn from this story for our lives today?

*  Israel and the Covenant
	* What word of counsel and warning did Moses give to the children of Israel before his death? Deuteronomy 28:1,15
	* What were the consequences for the children of Israel not keeping their promises as part of their covenant with the LORD? Jeremiah 11:1-8
	* Why did the children of Israel abandon the covenant the LORD had made with their forefathers?

*  The Remnant
	* Just like Joshua and Caleb, there was a remnant who chose to remain faithful to the covenant. What encouragement do you find in the following inspired testimonies from the prophets? Daniel 9:3-4, Zephaniah 3:13-17
	* What biblical characters in the Old Testament inspire you as part of the faithful remnant during times of spiritual apostasy? Elijah, Hannah, etc.
	* What does the apostle John reveal about the faithful remnant in the last days? Revelation 12:17

*  Covenant promises to spiritual Israel
	* Read Galatians 3:6-9,26-29 What important truths is the apostle Paul sharing here?
	* How can we remain faithful to our covenant with Jesus, our Savior and Lord?
	* How do you respond to the promise of Jesus given to those who remain in a loving, obedient relationship with Him? John 10:27-29
