#### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`What excites you when you think about eternity with God?`

`Could God handle the great controversy in a less painful way?`

`Why is it significant that God is love, and how should it impact our spiritual activities and outreach?`

`How can you share our hope with people you know who are lost?`

`What kinds of things do you feel when studying the end of the great controversy?`

`What difference should the Second Coming effect in young people’s lives?`

`What insights do you think the redeemed might gain during the millennial judgment?`

`Why is it essential that the wicked are not only destroyed but also judged?`

`What have you learned about trusting God in the past thirteen lessons?`

`What has your study of the great controversy taught you about God’s love?`
