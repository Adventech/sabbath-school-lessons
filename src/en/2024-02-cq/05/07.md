#### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`What are some ways you see people misstating God’s Word today?`

`What were the steps that led Eve to sin?`

`Was God fair in offering humanity a second chance?`

`How can we learn to seek God when we sin instead of hiding or seeking distractions to numb the guilt?`

`What does enmity with Satan look like in a practical sense?`

`Why is sin fundamentally bad?`

`Where can Satan’s lies from the Garden of Eden (Gen. 1:3–5) be found in today’s world?`

`What are some of Satan’s strategies to lead people into a life of sin?`

`What are some ways to combat Satan’s custom-made temptations?`

`As believers, how can we echo God’s proactive, grace-driven approach to reconciliation in our relationships with others?`
