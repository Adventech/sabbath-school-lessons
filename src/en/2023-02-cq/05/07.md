#### inQuire

`Why is the judgment good news and not bad news?`

`How does the knowledge that “the hour of His judgment has come” impact our daily lives?`

`What do you think Jesus’ exact role in the judgment is? What do you think He will be saying and thinking?`

`How can Christ’s role in the judgement motivate us to be more faithful to Him, knowing that only because of what He did for us can we have the hope of salvation?`

`How does the judgment reveal God’s character to the universe?`

`How does Psalm 51 help shed light on the meaning and purpose of the judgment?`

`What are some simple steps you could take today to start living differently because we know the judgment is real?`
