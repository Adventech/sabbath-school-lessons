#### inQuire

`How is the biblical understanding of Creation different from a deistic understanding of it? Why is this difference so important?`

`Why is being created by God not enough for humanity? Why does humanity also need the promise and fulfillment of redemption?`

`How have you personally experienced God’s love through His creative power working in your life?`

`How does the reality that God created the entire cosmos comfort you? How does it humble you?`

`What does it say about God if He used evolutionary methods such as death, violence, destruction, suffering, and mass extinction to “create” humanity?`

`How does this week’s study impact your private and public worship of God?`
