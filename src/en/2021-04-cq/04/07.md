#### inQuire

`What are the blessings that God has given you, both material and spiritual?`

`How can we manifest in our lives that we serve God alone?`

`What are some practical ways to love God with heart, soul, and strength?`

`What are some ways that we can make sure we see and do everything through the lens of our love for God?`

`In what ways might you pass on this love relationship you have with God to your children/future children, or friends and family?`

`How have you seen love leading to changes in your behaviors?`

`How can we combat legalism in our own lives and in our church?`
