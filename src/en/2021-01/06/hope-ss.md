### Outline #6 Playing God

*  Judgment prophesied against Babylon
	* Isaiah 13:1, 17-22 How was it possible for the Isaiah to make this prophecy long before Babylon had even risen to superpower status and taken the people of Judah into captivity? Isaiah 46:9-10, Amos 3:7
	* Which Bible prophet also predicted the fall of Babylon and recorded the details of Babylon’s fall? Daniel 5:13-17, 25-31
	* 2 Peter 1:19-21 When did you come to the conviction that Bible prophecy was a reliable revelation given by God through His prophets?
	* What prophecy of the Bible has made the greatest impact on your life?

*  Prophetic revelation about Lucifer
	* Isaiah 14:12-15 Why does Isaiah embed a prophetic revelation about Lucifer in the midst of a prophecy about judgment on the enemies of God’s people?
	* Where else in the Scriptures can we find a prophetic revelation about Lucifer? Ezekiel 28:13-15, Revelation 12:7-9
	* How did Jesus describe the character of the Satan? John 8:44
	* Share a time when you experienced an intense attack from Satan.
	* What special gift is given to Christians as a protection against Satan and the kingdom of darkness? Ephesians 6:10-18
	* Why is it dangerous to deny or ignore the reality of evil forces in the world? 1 Peter 5:8

*  Babylon in the prophecies of Revelation
	* Revelation 17:1-6 Why is the persecuting power described in the prophecies of Revelation also given the name Babylon? (see also Daniel 7:21-27)
	* Compare man’s way of salvation described in Genesis 11:1-4 with God’s way of salvation described in Genesis 28:10-17. (see also John 1:49-51 and John 14:6)
	* What passage of Scripture clearly confirms for you God’s way of salvation through faith in Jesus rather than by our own effort? Ephesians 2:8-9, Romans 10:13, etc.
	* What urgent appeal from heaven is given to those who are caught in the confusion of Babylon? Revelation 18:1-4
	* When did you hear the call of God to come out of the confusion of Babylon and fully trust Jesus as your Savior, Lord, and soon-coming King?
