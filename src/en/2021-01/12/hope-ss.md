### Outline #12  Messiah—the Desire of All Nations

*  Finding our way home to God
	* Isaiah 59:1-2 How does our sin affect our relationship with God?
	* Does God separate Himself from us when we sin or do we separate ourselves from Him? Support your answer from Scripture. (Genesis 3:8)
	* According to the teachings of the Bible, what is an appropriate response when we feel our sins have caused a separation between us and God? 1 John 1:9, Psalm 32:1-5, Psalm 51:1-2, 10-12, Jeremiah 31:31-34, Romans 6:23, Romans 10:13, etc.
	* What can we learn from the parable of the lost sheep about how we can find our way home to God? Luke 15:4-6, 20-24 (see also Isaiah 61:10)

*  A manifestation of the glory of the LORD
	* Isaiah 60:1-3	Who is referred to in this prophecy?
	* While Israel as a nation failed to realize God’s plan and purpose due to their rebellion and unbelief, name one Person whose life fulfilled this prophecy in all its fulness. John 1:14 (see also Haggai 2:6-9)
	* Is this prophecy also applicable to each of our lives today? Matthew 5:14-16
	* How is it possible for each one of us to arise and shine, reflecting God’s glory? (see John 8:12)

*  Proclaiming the year of the LORD’s favor
	* Isaiah 61:1-3 Who is referred to in this prophecy? (see Luke 4:18-21)
	* What happened during the acceptable year of the LORD, the year of Jubilee? Leviticus 25:10
	* What is the significance of the year of Jubilee in the plan of salvation? Isaiah 61:10-11, John 8:36
	* What is our greatest debt that has been cancelled?

*  Proclaiming the day of vengeance of our God
	* Isaiah 61:2 When Jesus quoted this prophecy 700 years later, He omitted the phrase “the day of vengeance of our God. (Luke 4:19) Why?
	* When will the day of vengeance of our God take place? 2 Peter 3:10
	* Is this a day of vengeance against sinners or a day of vengeance against sin? 2 Peter 3:9, John 3:16-17
	* How is the vengeance of the LORD a manifestation of His immeasurable, unfailing love?
	* What warning message must be shared while there is still time to repent and turn to God? Isaiah 55:5-7, Revelation 14:6-7, etc.
	* How would you respond to a friend who says, “I want to share God’s warning message, but I am afraid of being rejected or ridiculed”?
