### A Portrait of Love

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing a series on Letters to New Christians. Today’s topic: A PORTRAIT OF LOVE.

**Scripture Song:** It is good to give thanks – Psalm 92:1-4

- The centrality of agapé love
	- What theme do you hear in Paul’s testimony about agapé love to Christians in Corinth? 1 Corinthians 13:1-3
	- How can we be filled with such amazing love? Romans 5:5, Luke 11:13
	- What prophecy regarding agapé love did Jesus give concerning the last days of earth’s history? Matthew 24:12
	- Where do you see a lack of agapé love in the world today?
	- Share a time when you saw the agapé love of God revealed.
- A description of agapé love
	- 1 Corinthians 13:4a,6b-8a What positive aspects of agapé love are highlighted in these verses?
	- Which of these positive aspects of agapé love impacts you the most?
	- How does Paul’s description of agapé love in Galatians 5:22-23 reinforce the testimony found in Romans 5:5?
- What agapé love is not
	- 1Corinthians 13:4b-6a What are some things agapé love does not do?
	- Why doesn’t the Apostle Paul simply focus on the positive aspects of agapé love? 
	- Which of these negative characteristics are most common in our world today?
	- Which of these negative characteristics has been displaced from your life as you have been filled with the agapé love of God?
-  Agapé love revealed in the life of Jesus
	- Where do you see the agapé love of God revealed in the life of Jesus? John 13:1, Mark 10:16, Matthew 14:14, etc.
	- What appeal did Jesus make to His disciples in response to His agapé love? John 13:34, 15:9,12
	- How did the Apostle John respond to this revelation of God’s agapé love? 1 John 3:1,16, 4:12
- The greatest of these is agapé love
	- 1 Corinthians 13:13 How did the Apostle Paul conclude his discussion about agapé love?
	- How important is faith for the Christian? Hebrews 11:6, Ephesians 2:8, 1 John 5:4, etc.
	- How important is hope for the Christian? 1 Thessalonians 4:13, Romans 15:13, etc.
	- Why is agapé love greater than even saving faith or enduring hope? Colossians 3:14, 1 John 4:12, etc.
