### Generosity and Mission

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing a series on Letters to New Christians. Today’s topic: GENEROSITY AND MISSION.

**Scripture Song:** It is good to give thanks – Psalm 92:1-4

- An appeal for generosity
	- What instruction had Paul given in his first letter to Christians in Corinth about a collection for the Christians in Jerusalem? 1 Corinthians 16:1-2
	- How had Christians in Macedonia responded to Paul’s appeal? 2 Corinthians 8:1-4
	- What additional appeal did Paul make in his second letter to Christians in Corinth? 2 Corinthians 9:1-5
	- Share an appeal for generous support that touched your heart.
- A reflection of our generous God
	- According to Paul, how was the generosity of God revealed? 2 Corinthians 8:9
	- How did Paul emphasize the generosity of God in his letter to Christians in Philippi? Philippians 2:5-8
	- What other passages of Scripture emphasize the generosity of God? Lamentations 3:22-24, 1 John 3:1, John 3:16, etc.
	- What are your thoughts about the generous gift of Jesus to rescue us from this sin-ravaged world?
	- When did you accept the generous gift of Jesus as your personal Savior?
- A generous spirit
	- How does a generous spirit go beyond what might be expected? 2 Corinthians 8:5
	- How is the giving of ourselves in addition to material gifts a reflection of the generous spirit of God? (James 1:17, John 3:16, Luke 11:13, etc.)
	- What important principles about giving are found in 2 Corinthians 9:7?
	- Some people may return a tithe of their income and give offerings out of a sense of obligation or fear of negative consequences. (Malachi 3:8-9) What blessing is lost when we give with such an attitude?
	- Why should we avoid comparing our generosity with the generosity of others? 2 Corinthians 8:3,12
	- Share a time when you witnessed a cheerful giver.
	- Why is a generous spirit essential if we are to accomplish the mission of sharing a message of eternal hope through Jesus with the world?
	- Share an example of an early follower of Jesus who gave generously to advance the mission of God.
	- Share a time when you witnessed someone manifesting a generous spirit when sharing the truth about Jesus with others.
	- How is God impressing you to be generous with your financial resources and with your time?
