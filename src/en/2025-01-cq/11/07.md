### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`How did Paul seek to comfort the grieving believers in Thessalonica?`

`What’s the difference between how believers grieve and how unbelievers grieve? (1 Thess. 4:13.)`

`How did Job’s faith endure the devastating loss of his children? (Job 1:18–22.)`

`What was the best thing Job’s friends did for him in his grief? (Job 2:13.)`

`What is the significance of labeling death as an “enemy” (1 Cor. 15:26)?`

`Why did Jesus desire His friends’ support as He entered His darkest hour? (Matt. 26:38.)`

`What has been the most meaningful support that friends have given you during difficult times?`

`What are some ways you can improve in how you comfort grieving people?`

`How can we have a more vivid hope in heaven, where there is no more death? (Rev. 21:4.)`
