#### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`What does the Sabbath symbolize? Explore the idea!`

`What changes have you made for someone you love?`

`How does the Sabbath demonstrate our rest, trust, and reliance on God and His righteousness?`

`How did Jesus redefine the Sabbath from the heavy drudgery it had become? Does it need to be redefined in your life?`

`If you were raised as a Sabbath keeper, what is your earliest Sabbath memory?`

`Did you have positive or negative thoughts regarding the Sabbath as a child? Have those changed over the years? How?`

`When have you been able to share the Sabbath with someone who’s unfamiliar with it? How did that go?`

`What principles guide your decisions about how to keep the Sabbath?`

`What misconceptions about the Sabbath have you encountered, both by those who keep the Sabbath and those who don’t?`

`What’s your favorite part about the Sabbath?`
