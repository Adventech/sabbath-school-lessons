#### inQuire

`Why do you believe God’s law is needed in our culture today?`

`How did God reveal His love and care to His people before giving the law? (Exod. 20:2)`

`Why do you think God saved His people from slavery before stating His requirements?`

`What does it mean that God is a “jealous God” (Exod. 20:5)? What does this tell you about His character and requirements?`

`Why does God connect love for others with love for Him? (1 John 4:20, 21)`

`How did Jesus uphold the importance of the law while freely offering forgiveness?`

`How do you understand the statement that the law is a revelation of God’s character? (See inSight if needed.)`

`What do you think are people’s biggest objections to God’s law? How do you answer those objections?`

`How has God’s law helped you personally?`
