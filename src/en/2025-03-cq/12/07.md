#### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`When have you experienced delay and disappointment in your life? How did it strengthen or weaken you?`

`What lessons can we learn from the Israelites’ story about how to not lose faith and hope while waiting for answers?`

`How did Aaron attempt to make the idol worship more acceptable? (Exod. 32:5)`

`Why was the sin of the golden calf so terrible that God was ready to destroy the whole nation except for Moses? (Exod. 32:10)`

`How do you explain God’s anger and Moses’ anger? (Exod. 32:11, 19.) When is anger appropriate?`

`Is there a situation where you might be willing to die for another person? What kind of person would you be willing to die for?`

`List the differences you see between Aaron’s response and Moses’ response to the rebellion. What lessons do you notice about true leadership?`

`What are some examples of idols that we have today? What are “idols of the heart” (Ezek. 14:3, 4)?`

`How can we have the courage to stand for God when everyone around us is headed in the wrong direction?`
