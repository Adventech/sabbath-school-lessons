#### inQuire

`What do you think drove Pharaoh to continually refuse God’s requests?`

`Have you ever read about the Egyptian gods? Look some of them up on the internet (e.g., www.britannica.com/list/11-egyptian-gods-and-goddesses). Why would God want to attack the gods of another country?`

`Do false miracles like the ones worked by the Egyptian magicians still happen today? Consider Matthew 24:24; Revelation 13:13, 14; 16:14.`

`What do miracles accomplish, and what do they not accomplish?`

`What miracles have you seen?`

`Are miracles important to the Christian experience? Why or why not?`

`How did God show mercy to the Egyptians?`

`What significance do you see in some of Pharaoh’s servants believing and supporting Moses?`

`What was the purpose of the plagues? Why are they such a pivotal story in the Old Testament?`
