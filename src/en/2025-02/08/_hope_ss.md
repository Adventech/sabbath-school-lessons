### Part #8 Prophecy In The Psalms: Part 1

**Scripture Song:** Let the word of Christ – Colossians 3:16

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series of studies on Bible Prophecies and Stories for the Last Days. Today’s topic: **Prophecy In The Psalms: Part 1.**

- **Inspired songs**
	- The book of Psalms is a collection of songs, an ancient songbook. Ephesians 5:19, Colossians 3:16
	- What was the psalmist David’s testimony about the writing of these psalms? 2 Samuel 23:1-2
	- What testimony did the apostles give about the inspiration of the Scriptures, including the psalms? 2 Timothy 3:16-17, 2 Peter 1:19-21

- **The House of the LORD**
	- Psalm 122:1 The House of the LORD is mentioned frequently in the psalms. Why did the psalmist experience joy at the thought of coming to the House of the LORD?
	- Who designed the earthly sanctuary that was constructed in the wilderness during the exodus from Egypt? Exodus 25:40
	- What can we learn from the author of Hebrews about the original sanctuary in heaven? Hebrews 8:1-2
	- When was the movable wilderness tabernacle replaced with a permanent structure? 1 Kings 5:1-5
	- What lessons was God wanting to teach through the sanctuary and its services? Hebrews 9:11-15
	- Why is cleansing from sin an essential preparation for entering the House of the LORD? Psalm 15:1-5, 24:3-4
	- The psalmist David prophesied that he would dwell in the House of the LORD forever. (Psalm 23:6) How could he be certain of that glorious future after all the sins he had committed? Psalm 51:1-12, 5:7
	- Psalm 100:1-6 The psalmist encourages us to enter the House of the LORD with thanksgiving and praise. What reasons do you have to give thanks and praise to the LORD today?

- **Prophecies about Messiah**
	- What evidence is there that Jesus regarded the psalms as an inspired source of truth? Luke 24:44-45, Matthew 22:41-44, etc.
	-  Which psalm of David contains the most prophecies about the Messiah?
		- Psalm 22:1, Mark 15:34
		- Psalm 22:7-8, Matthew 27:39-43
		- Psalm 22:16, John 20:24-27
		- iv) Psalm 22:18, John 19:23-24 v) Psalm 22:19-21, Luke 24:1-8
	- What other psalms contains prophecies about Messiah? Psalm 34:19-20 (John 19:33,36), Psalm 8:1-2 (Matthew 21:15-16), etc.
	- What are your thoughts these Messianic prophecies?
