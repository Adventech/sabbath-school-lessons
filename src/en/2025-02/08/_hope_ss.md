### Part #8 Prophecy In The Psalms: Part 1

**Scripture Song:** Let the word of Christ – Colossians 3:16

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series of studies on Bible Prophecies and Stories for the Last Days. Today’s topic: **Prophecy In The Psalms: Part 1.**

1. Inspired songs
	- a. The book of Psalms is a collection of songs, an ancient songbook. Ephesians 5:19, Colossians 3:16
	- b. What was the psalmist David’s testimony about the writing of these psalms? 2 Samuel 23:1-2
	- c. What testimony did the apostles give about the inspiration of the Scriptures, including the psalms? 2 Timothy 3:16-17, 2 Peter 1:19-21
	
2. The House of the LORD
	- a. Psalm 122:1 The House of the LORD is mentioned frequently in the psalms. Why did the psalmist experience joy at the thought of coming to the House of the LORD?
	- b. Who designed the earthly sanctuary that was constructed in the wilderness during the exodus from Egypt? Exodus 25:40
	- c. What can we learn from the author of Hebrews about the original sanctuary in heaven? Hebrews 8:1-2
	- d. When was the movable wilderness tabernacle replaced with a permanent structure? 1 Kings 5:1-5
	- e. What lessons was God wanting to teach through the sanctuary and its services? Hebrews 9:11-15
	- f. Why is cleansing from sin an essential preparation for entering the House of the LORD? Psalm 15:1-5, 24:3-4
	- g. The psalmist David prophesied that he would dwell in the House of the LORD forever. (Psalm 23:6) How could he be certain of that glorious future after all the sins he had committed? Psalm 51:1-12, 5:7
	- h. Psalm 100:1-6 The psalmist encourages us to enter the House of the LORD with thanksgiving and praise. What reasons do you have to give thanks and praise to the LORD today?

3. Prophecies about Messiah
	- a. What evidence is there that Jesus regarded the psalms as an inspired source of truth? Luke 24:44-45, Matthew 22:41-44, etc.
	- b.  Which psalm of David contains the most prophecies about the Messiah?
		- i. Psalm 22:1, Mark 15:34
		- ii. Psalm 22:7-8, Matthew 27:39-43
		- iii. Psalm 22:16, John 20:24-27
		- iv) Psalm 22:18, John 19:23-24 v) Psalm 22:19-21, Luke 24:1-8
	- c. What other psalms contains prophecies about Messiah? Psalm 34:19-20 (John 19:33,36), Psalm 8:1-2 (Matthew 21:15-16), etc.
	- d. What are your thoughts these Messianic prophecies?
