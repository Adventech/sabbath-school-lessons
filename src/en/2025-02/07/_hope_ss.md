### Part #7 Foundations For Prophecy

**Scripture Song:** Let the word of Christ – Colossians 3:16

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series of studies on Bible Prophecies and Stories for the Last Days. Today’s topic: **Foundations For Prophecy.**

1. The assurance of God’s continued presence
	- a. What two reminders of His love and care did the LORD God provide for our first parents after they sinned and were expelled from the Garden of Eden?
		- i. Genesis 3:21 How did the garments of animal skins remind our first parents both of their sin and of God’s love and care?
		- ii. Genesis 3:24 How did the cherubim symbolize God’s continued presence with His children? (see Exodus 25:18-22, 1 Chronicles 13:6 etc.)
	- b. What precious promise did the LORD give to Joshua and to each one of us? Joshua 1:5, Hebrews 13:5
	- c. How does the promise of the LORD’s presence give us hope and courage?

2. Prepared to prophesy
	- a. Samuel
		- i. How did the LORD prepare young Samuel to prophesy? 1 Samuel 1:27-28, 2:18-19
		- ii. What lessons can we learn from the call of Samuel and Samuel’s response? 1 Samuel 3:1-10
	- b. Isaiah
		- i. Isaiah 6:1-5 What was the prophet Isaiah’s response when he saw the glory of the LORD?
		- ii. How did the LORD prepare Isaiah to prophesy? Isaiah 6:6-7
		- iii. What is the meaning of being touched on the mouth by a hot coal from the altar?
		- iv. After the LORD prepared Isaiah to prophesy, how did he respond to God’s call? Isaiah 6:8
	- c. Jeremiah
		- i. Jeremiah 1:4-10 What lessons can we learn from God’s call to young Jeremiah?
		- ii. Who can relate to Jeremiah’s feelings of inadequacy when asked to speak for God?
	- d. We are not all called to be prophets, but we are all called to prophesy. Isaiah 43:10, Acts 1:8 What can we learn from the experience of others who were called to prophesy?

3. Staying humble as we prophesy
	- a. Why it is important that we stay humble as we prophesy for the LORD? 1 Peter 5:5-6
	- b. What lessons can we learn from the fall of Lucifer, one who had been called to testify of the LORD’s holiness and glory? Isaiah 14:12-14
	- c. According to the Apostle Paul, how did God remind him to stay humble as he prophesied in the power of the Holy Spirit? 2 Corinthians 12:7-9
	- d. What thorn in the flesh helps you to stay humble?
