### Part #9 Prophecy In The Psalms: Part 2

**Scripture Song:** Let the word of Christ – Colossians 3:16

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series of studies on Bible Prophecies and Stories for the Last Days. Today’s topic: **Prophecy In The Psalms: Part 2.**

1. Help and hope in troubled times
	- a. Psalm 46:1-11 How does this psalm, written under the inspiration of the Holy Spirit by the sons of Korah, oﬀer encouragement to our lives today?
	- b. Why do you think the LORD is referred to in this psalm as the God of Jacob?
	- c. What lies does Satan often present to us when we are going through troubled times?
	- d. Share a time when you experienced help and hope in a time of trouble.
	- e. Why is the message of this psalm important as we approach the time of trouble prophesied by the prophet Daniel? Daniel 12:1
	- f. Read the prophecy recorded in Jeremiah 4:23-25. How does the message of hope in Psalm 91:1-2, 3-7, 8-16 help us face the future without fear?
	
2. The assurance of ultimate victory
	- a. Psalm 47:1-9 Why is it important to remember that the LORD reigns over the nations?
	- b. Where else in the Scriptures can we find the assurance that God reigns over the nations? Psalm 97:1-6, 99:1-3, Daniel 4:17, etc.
	- c. If God reigns over the nations, why is there so much evil and turmoil in the world?
	- d. When will all the nations be finally subdued under God’s authority? Daniel 2:44-45

3. The judgment of the wicked
	- a. Psalm 75:1-10 If God desires all people to be saved (2 Peter 3:9), why does He give the wicked a cup of judgment?
	- b. What prophecy did the Apostle John record about this cup of judgment given to the wicked? Revelation 14:8-10
	- c. Based on your study of the Scriptures, what is the answer to the LORD’s question recorded by the prophet Ezekiel in Ezekiel 18:23? (The LORD gives His answer in Ezekiel 18:32)
	
4. Sharing a message of eternal hope
	- a. Psalm 67:1-2 How will the message of salvation be known among all nations?
	- b. Revelation 14:6-7 Who is this first angel recorded in Revelation 14:6 and what message of hope is shared with every nation, kindred, tongue, and people?
	- c. What is the everlasting gospel?
	- d. Why is the sharing of this message of eternal hope so urgent?
	- e. Share a time when God gave you the opportunity to share the everlasting gospel with someone who was looking for hope.
