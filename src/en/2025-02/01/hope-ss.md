### Part #1 Some Principles Of Prophecy

**Scripture Song:** Let the word of Christ – Colossians 3:16

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are beginning a new series of studies on Bible Prophecies and Stories for the Last Days. Today’s topic: **Some Principles Of Prophecy.**

- **God’s challenge in revealing truth**
	- What challenge does God face when seeking to reveal truth to the human family? Isaiah 55:8-9
	- How does the testimony of the Psalmist David reinforce this challenge? Psalm 139:1-6 (see also Psalm 147:5)
	- What was the Apostle Paul’s testimony about the challenge of understanding God’s ways? Romans 11:33, 1 Corinthians 13:12

- **God’s revelation through the prophets**
	- Hebrews 1:1-2, Amos 3:7 What is one important way God reveals truth to the human family?
	- How can we be certain God wants us to understand His revelation through the prophets? Revelation 1:1- 3, Matthew 24:15, etc.
	- What is the role of the Holy Spirit in this process of revelation? 2 Peter 1:20-21, John 16:13

- **How to study God’s revelation in the Bible**
	- What counsel did the Apostle Paul give to Timothy about studying the Word of God? 2 Timothy 3:14-17
	- How did Jesus help His disciples understand God’s revelation in the Scriptures? Luke 24:27,44-45
	- What important principle do these texts reveal about how to study God’s revelation in the Scriptures? Isaiah 28:9-10
	- How can we experience the guidance of the Holy Spirit when studying God’s revelation in the Bible? Luke 11:13
	- Why is the counsel of James so important as we study God’s revelation in the Bible? James 1:5
	- Share a time when you experienced joy as the Holy Spirit helped you to understand God’s revelation in the Bible (particularly the prophecies of Daniel and Revelation).

- **The use of symbols in God’s revelation**
	- What are some symbols used in Bible prophecy? (Daniel 7:2-3, Revelation 1:16, 12:1, etc.)
	- What can we learn from Daniel’s account in Daniel 7:7-8,24-25?
	- Why did God frequently use symbols when revealing truth to the prophets? (see Luke 8:10)

- **Jesus, the Center of God’s revelation**
	- John 5:39-40 What important truth did Jesus reveal about God’s revelation through the prophets?
	- Why is it vitally important to remember that Jesus is the Center of God’s revelation? Hebrews 1:1-3
	- When did you come to realize that Jesus is the Center of God’s revelation to you personally?
