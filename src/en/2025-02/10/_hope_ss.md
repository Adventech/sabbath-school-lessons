### Part #10 Lessons From Noah And Lot

**Scripture Song:** Let the word of Christ – Colossians 3:16

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series of studies on Bible Prophecies and Stories for the Last Days. Today’s topic: **Lessons From Noah And Lot.**

1. Stories recorded for our learning
	- a. 1 Corinthians 10:11-12 What important message is found in this inspired counsel from the Apostle Paul?
	- b.  In addition to providing counsel for those living in the end-time, what else can we discover through the stories in the Bible? Romans 15:4
	- c. Who was blessed to have someone, perhaps a parent or grandparent, who shared Bible stories with you when you were growing up? Which story do you remember most clearly from your childhood?

2. Lessons from the story of Noah
	- a. Genesis 6:1-8,13-14,22 What lessons can we learn from the story of Noah?
	- b. What lessons did Jesus share from the story of Noah for those living in the last days of earth’s history? Matthew 24:36-44
	- c. Noah had to wait many years before the fulfillment of God’s promise. He was mocked and ridiculed. What prophecy did the Apostle Peter give for those living in the last days of earth’s history? 2 Peter 3:1-7
	- d. What lesson impacts you the most from the story of Noah?

3. Lessons from the story of Lot
	- a. What perilous choice did Lot make regarding where to live and raise his family? Genesis 13:10-13
	- b. What additional revelation has been provided for us regarding the sinful condition of Sodom? Ezekiel 16:49,40, Jude 5-8
	- c. Before long, Lot and his family were living in Sodom. How did this evil environment impact his family? Genesis 19:1-14
	- d. What lessons can we learn from the interaction between Abraham and the LORD prior to the destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah? Genesis 18:17-32
	- e. How did the two angels sent to Sodom finally manage to extract Lot, his wife, and two daughters before the destruction of Sodom? Genesis 19:15-16
	- f. Genesis 19:17,26 Why did Lot’s wife disobey the command of the angel and look back?
	- g. How does the judgment of Sodom and Gomorrah foreshadow the final judgment of the whole earth? 2 Peter 2:4-6, 3:9-14
	- h. The Apostle Peter describes Lot as a righteous man, (2 Peter 2:7-8) yet he chose to raise his family in an evil environment. What lessons can we learn from his story?
