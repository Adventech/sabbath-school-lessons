### Part #13 Two Judgment Stories

**Scripture Song:** Let the word of Christ – Colossians 3:16

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are concluding our series of studies on Bible Prophecies and Stories for the Last Days. Today’s topic: **Two Judgment Stories.**

1. God’s judgment against Ninevah
	- a. Review 1 Corinthians 10:11-12 and Romans 15:4.
	- b. Some people view the story of Jonah and the great fish as a fairy tale. How did Jesus regard the story of the prophet Jonah? Matthew 12:38-41
	- c. Jonah 1:1-3 Why was Jonah reluctant to share a warning of impending judgment with the people of Ninevah?
	- d. What lessons can we learn from the events that occurred during Jonah’s voyage to Tarshish? Jonah 1:4-17
	- e. What are your thoughts about Jonah’s prayer in the belly of a great fish and the LORD’s response? Jonah 2:1-10
	- f. How did the people of Ninevah respond to this warning of impending judgment? Jonah 3:1-10
	- g. What attitudes regarding the final judgment are revealed in Jonah 4:1-11 (see also 2 Peter 3:9, Ephesians 2:8-9, etc.)
	- h. What personal lessons do you learn from the story of Jonah that are relevant for your life today?

2. God’s judgment against Babylon
	- a. Read the testimony of King Nebuchadnezzar recorded in Daniel 4:34-37
	- b. Was his grandson Belshazzar aware of the truths revealed to Nebuchadnezzar and his grandfather’s conversion? Daniel 5:18-22
	- c. How did Belshazzar show his contempt for the Most High God? Daniel 5:1-4
	- d. What warning of impending judgment was given to Belshazzar and what does his response reveal about the condition of his heart? Daniel 5:5-9
	- e. Daniel 5:30 Could there have been a diﬀerent ending to Belshazzar’s story?
	- f. What parallels do you see between the judgment against Belshazzar and the events recorded in Revelation 16:12-19?
	- g. What word of hope is found imbedded in this message of judgment? Revelation 16:15
	- h. What garments are necessary for us to face the final judgment without fear? Revelation 7:13-14

3. A message of hope for the last days
	- a. How does the grace manifested by King Cyrus of Persia relate to the grace manifested by King Jesus? 2 Chronicles 36:22-23, Matthew 25:34
	- b. What encouragement do you find in the words of Jesus recorded in John 5:22-24?
	- c. How is it possible to live without fear in these last days of earth’s history? John 16:33, Isaiah 41:10, etc.
