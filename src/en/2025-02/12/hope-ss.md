### Part #12 Lessons From Two Fiery Furnaces

**Scripture Song:** Let the word of Christ – Colossians 3:16

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series of studies on Bible Prophecies and Stories for the Last Days. Today’s topic: **Lessons From Two Fiery Furnaces.**

- **Stories recorded for our learning**
	- Review 1 Corinthians 10:11-12 and Romans 15:4.
	- In this study, we will examine the stories of two fiery furnaces: the experience of Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego, and the experience of early Christians. We’ll discover lessons for our lives in these last days on planet earth.

- **The fiery furnace on the Plain of Dura**
	- Daniel 3:1 How was the construction of a massive golden statue by King Nebuchadnezzar of Babylon a direct act of defiance in response to a revelation given to him by God? (review vision of Daniel 2)
	- Daniel 3:2-5 What command was given by King Nebuchadnezzar and what was the significance of that command?
	- Daniel 3:6 What was the consequence for refusing to obey the king’s demand?
	- Daniel 3:7-18 Contrast the response of Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego with the response of the majority of those gathered on the plain of Dura.
	- Daniel 3:19-25 What important lessons can we learn for these last days of earth’s history from the experience of Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego? (see Luke 16:10, Isaiah 41:10, etc.)
	- How does the experience on the Plain of Dura foreshadow an attempt to force worship in the last days of earth’s history? Revelation 13:11-17
	- How are faithful followers of Jesus described in Revelation 14:12?
	-  Is obeying the commandments of God the way to be saved or evidence that we are His redeemed children? Support your answer from the Scriptures. (Galatians 2:16, Ephesians 2:8-9, John 14:15, Revelation 12:17, etc.)
	- Which commandments of God will be a central issue in the last days of earth’s history? Revelation 14:6-7

- **The experience of early Christians**
	- What prophecies did Jesus give about how His followers would be treated? John 15:20, 16:1-3, etc.
	- Acts 7:59-8:3, 12:1-4 What do these stories reveal about the experience of early Christians?
	- Acts 12:5-17 What lessons can we learn from God’s deliverance of the Apostle Peter?
	- What encouragement do you find in the words of Jesus recorded in Matthew 5:11-12?
	- What other promises in the Bible encourage you as you think of the challenges that lie ahead?
