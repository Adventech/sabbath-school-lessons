### Part #11 Lessons From Ruth And Esther

**Scripture Song:** Let the word of Christ – Colossians 3:16

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series of studies on Bible Prophecies and Stories for the Last Days. Today’s topic: **Lessons From Ruth And Esther.**

1. Stories recorded for our learning
	- a. 1 Corinthians 10:11-12 What important message did we discover in our previous study from the inspired counsel of the Apostle Paul?
	- b. In addition to providing counsel for those living in the end-time, what else can we discover through the stories in the Bible? Romans 15:4
	- c. In this study, we will examine the stories of Ruth and Esther and see what lessons we can learn for these last days on planet earth.

2. The story of Ruth
	- a. Ruth 1:1-5 Can anyone here relate to such intense hardship and overwhelming loss?
	- b. Ruth 1:6-18 What lessons can we learn from the testimony of Ruth when her mother-in-law decided to return to Bethlehem?
	- c. Ruth 2:1-20 How does the conduct of Boaz prefigure the loving care of our Redeemer, Jesus?
	- d. Ruth 3:1-9 How does Ruth’s conduct on the threshing floor relate to our response to the loving care of Jesus? (see Matthew 11:28-30)
	- e. Ruth 4:13-17 What is the joyful ending of Ruth’s story?
	- f. What lessons can we learn from the story of Ruth as we face hardships and trials prior to the glorious coming of Jesus our Redeemer? (John 16:33, etc)

3. The story of Esther
	- a. Can someone summarize for us how a young Jewish orphan girl named Hadassah ended up as the wife of King Ahasuerus of Persia?
	- b. Esther 3:1-6 Who was the villain in this story and how did he exhibit the character of Satan? (see also Matthew 4:8-9, John 8:44, Isaiah 14:12-14)
	- c. Esther 3:8-9 How does this death decree in the days of Queen Esther prefigure the events right before the glorious return of Jesus? (see also Revelation 12:17, 13:11-15)
	- d. How did Queen Esther expose the evil character of Haman? Esther 4:1-16, 5:1-6
	- e. What earnest appeal did Queen Esther make to the King at a second banquet with Haman? Esther 7:1-8
	- f. What lessons can we learn from the final days of Haman’s life? Esther 6:4-12, 7:6-10
	- g. Queen Esther came to a position of leadership “for such a time as this” (Esther 4:16). What is our special work in these last days of earth’s history?
	- h. What is the most important lesson you have learned from the story of Esther?
