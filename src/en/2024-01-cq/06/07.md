### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`What led Pilate to fail in his responsibilities when he agreed to facilitate Jesus’ death?`

`How do Jesus’ trial and crucifixion demonstrate the dangers of blending church and state?`

`What are some of the dangers of blending church and state not mentioned in this lesson?`

`Looking at our complete history, why should Christians be the last people on earth to use the powers of state for religious purposes?`

`Why do you think Christians are so prone to becoming oppressors when we’ve had to fight so many oppressive situations?`

`What details did Jesus include in His warning about future persecution? How many of those details do you think will be relevant for us?`

`How can studying the events leading to Jesus’ death prepare us for the coming challenges and persecution?`

`How can we experience the same kind of transformative encounter with Christ that Paul had? Do we have to wait for Jesus to show up, or is there something we can do?`

`If we never experience a mind-blowing encounter with Jesus like Paul did, how can we know He’s changing us? How can we avoid stagnating in our relationships with Him?`

`How can we have the same serenity and peace Jesus displayed amidst the false accusations and total madness of His trial?`
