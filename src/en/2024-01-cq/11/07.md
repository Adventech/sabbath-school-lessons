### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`Where is Jesus in Revelation 13?`

`Why should young people care about the prophecies in Daniel and Revelation? Are they relevant to our daily lives?`

`In what ways has the church suffered from secular tyranny in this world? Religious?`

`How does the earth beast (Rev. 13:11–18) convince the world to follow its religious agenda?`

`If the beast powers will perform miracles too, how can we tell which are of God and which aren’t?`

`How does the image of the beast prophecy change the way you see current and future events?`
