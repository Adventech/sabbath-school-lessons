### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`How do the seven last plagues liberate God’s people?`

`What kinds of things will we be free from in the new earth?`

`What does the marriage relationship teach us about freedom?`

`What things does God’s law protect us from?`

`Why is it so necessary for our freedom to be guarded by boundaries?`

`How can freedom of choice be dangerous? Why does God give it to us anyway?`

`How has Satan redefined freedom?`

`Why will nobody ever again choose to sin in the new earth?`

`What freedoms did Jesus give up to purchase our freedom?`

`How can we develop deep love and unshakable trust in God today?`
