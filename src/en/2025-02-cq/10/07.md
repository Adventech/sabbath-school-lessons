### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`How does the Bible help us set our minds on heavenly things? (Col. 3:2)`

`How should Scripture guide what and how we pray to God?`

`How can music help Christ’s words speak to people? (Col. 3:16)\`

`How do we put to death the sinful behaviors Paul mentioned in Colossians 3:5–9? How is this related to our altar worship experience with God?`

`What are the most helpful methods of Bible study you’ve discovered?`

`Are prayer and Bible study separate spiritual exercises? Why or why not?`

`What is the difference between Biblical meditation and the meditation of Eastern religions?`

`How can you help Christian friends who are bored with the Bible discover a fresh experience in the Word?`

`How has the Bible changed your life? Feel free to share with the class.`
