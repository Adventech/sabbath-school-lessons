### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`How does the spiritual condition of one’s heart reflect the condition of their worship altar?`

`Why did God select Jeremiah, a young man (Jer. 1:7), to deliver such a powerful call to repentance?`

`What does it mean to worship God with our lips and not with our hearts? (Matt. 15:7–9)`

`How is obedience to God an act of worship? Explain from Matthew 15:7–9 or other texts.`

`When we give God our hearts, what changes will be evident in our lives?`

`What things today draw our hearts away from God and His Word?`

`In what ways does your devotional time with God purify your heart?`

`What’s the Holy Spirit’s role in heart transformation? (Ezek. 36:25–27)`

`How can you resist the urge to spend time on social media instead of in the Word of God?`
