### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`What do you find most rewarding about your daily worship experience? Most challenging?`

`How have you grown spiritually as a result of your daily devotional time with God?`

`How do you keep your devotional time fresh and meaningful?`

`What sacrifice are you making when you come to God each day in worship?`

`What are some of the dangers of worshiping in the same way every day?`

`When was the last time you shared something from your daily time with God with someone else?`

`What significance do you see in the Levites praising the Lord during the morning and evening sacrifices? (1 Chron. 23:30)`

`How much of worship should be a regular discipline, and how much of it should be spontaneous?`

`What can you do if your daily worship time seems stagnant? Get creative!`

`What elements do you see in the Israelites’ daily worship that you can incorporate into your daily worship routine?`
