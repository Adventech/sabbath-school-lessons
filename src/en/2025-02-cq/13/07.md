### inQuire

Share insights from this week’s memory verse and Bible study as well as any discoveries, observations, and questions with your Sabbath School class (or Bible study group). Consider these discussion questions with the rest of the group.

`When in your life have you felt an overwhelming sense of God’s presence?`

`God’s holiness left Isaiah feeling “undone,” which is also translated as “ruined,” “lost,” and “doomed” (Isa. 6:5). How does God’s holiness impact you?`

`Why must we be purified before we’re ready to go on God’s missions?`

`If God knows everything, why did He ask Isaiah, “Whom shall I send?”`

`How does true worship provide a sense of purpose and mission?`

`Describe a time in your life when you had to deliver an unpopular message. What was the experience like?`

`When have you sensed God’s holiness and felt the need for reverence?`

`How does Isaiah’s experience in God’s throne room change your view of personal worship?`

`What guidelines and goals for collective worship do you find in Isaiah 6?`

`What is God’s call to you? What will your response be?`
