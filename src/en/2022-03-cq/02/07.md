#### inQuire

`Where are areas in your life that need more “diligence, honesty, thrift, temperance, and purity”?`

`How have you experienced “spiritual creation” and “spiritual maintenance” in your life?`

`How can the focus of God be kept throughout Sabbath?`

`What are factors that cause us to lose the focus of God on Sabbath?`

`How else are salvational concepts linked with creation and Sabbath?`
