#### inQuire

`How do you see “proper places” in society?`

`How has this idea been abused by those who have been “created”?`

`How does the Creator supersede these abuses in society?`

`How does the Sabbath guard against sin on the personal/individual level as well as the social/corporate level?`

`What is the difference between laws and principles?`

`Why do creatures seek to become the Creator?`

`How do we differentiate social independence from spiritual independence?`

`How do you express your indebtedness to God as your Creator?`
