### Reality Check: Examining Your Spiritual Condition

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are beginning a new series on Growing in your relationship with God. Today’s topic: REALITY CHECK: Examining your spiritual condition.

**Scripture Song:** Seek the LORD – Isaiah 55:6-7

- **The need for self-examination**
	- What appeal did the Apostle Paul make to Christians in Corinth? 2 Corinthians 13:5
	- Why is that appeal so relevant for our lives today?
	- What prayer of the Psalmist David might be helpful as we seek to assess our spiritual condition? Psalm 139:23-24
	- Share a time when you realized your great need for a closer relationship with God. How did you become aware of your great need?
- **Counsel to lukewarm Christians**
	- Revelation 3:14-17 Why is this counsel of the Faithful and True Witness to Christians in Laodicea so relevant for our lives today?
	- What portion of this counsel impacts you the most?
	- What advice does Jesus offer to us? Revelation 3:18-19
	- Where do you see the love of God revealed in this message to lukewarm Christians?
	- What is your response to the invitation of Jesus recorded in Revelation 3:20?
	- How do we open the door of our lives to experience a more intimate relationship with Jesus?
- **The LORD God desires an intimate relationship with us**
	- Genesis 2:7 How does the creation account illustrate our Creator’s desire for an intimate relationship with us?
	- Genesis 3:1-9 How did the entrance of sin affect God’s desire for an intimate relationship with our first parents?
	- Genesis 5:21-24 How can we experience the level of intimacy with God that Enoch experienced?
	- What revelations given to the prophets confirm the LORD’s desire to experience an intimate relationship with us? Exodus 25:8, Jeremiah 31:3, Zephaniah 3:17, etc.
	- Where do you find the greatest revelation of God’s desire for an intimate relationship with us? John 1:1-4, 14,16, 14:23, etc.
	- What stories in the life of Jesus illustrate God’s desire for an intimate relationship with us?
	- How does the teaching of Jesus in John 15:1-11 illustrate God’s desire for an intimate relationship with us?
	- How does the Holy Spirit help us as we seek a more intimate relationship with God? John 14:16-18, 15:26, 16:7,8,13, Romans 8:26, etc.
	- What is your response to the LORD’s invitation to experience a more intimate relationship with Him?
