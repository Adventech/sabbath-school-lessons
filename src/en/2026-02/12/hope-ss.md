### A Message Worth Sharing

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series on Growing in your relationship with God. Today’s topic: A MESSAGE WORTH SHARING.
Intro: When we are growing in our relationship with God, we must share the good news with others.

**Scripture Song:** Seek the LORD – Isaiah 55:6-7

- **Sharing what we have experienced**
	- John 1:35-46 What invitation do you see repeated in this story about Jesus?
	- Mark 5:18-20 What instruction did Jesus give to a man set free from a legion of demons? How much did this man know about Jesus?
	- What can we learn from the instruction of Jesus given to His followers? Mark 16:15, Matthew 28:19
	- What enabled the early Christians to share the good news about Jesus with power? Acts 1:8
	- What can we learn from the story of Philip the deacon about sharing the good news about Jesus with others? Acts 8:26-35
	- Share a time when you sensed the Holy Spirit helping you to share the good news about Jesus with others.
- **Always be ready to share with others**
	- 1 Peter 3:15 What important insights about sharing the good news about Jesus with others can we learn from the appeal of the Apostle Peter?
	- How can we prepare so we are always ready to share the good news about Jesus with others? Acts 4:13, Luke 11:13, 2 Timothy 2:15, etc.
	- What promise did Jesus give to those who would be called to testify before authorities about their faith in God? Matthew 10:18-20
- **Searching for those who have wandered away**
	- What promise did the LORD give regarding those who have wandered away from Him? Jeremiah 31:16-17
	- What is our primary work for those who have wandered away from God? Colossians 4:2
	- What lesson can we learn from the parable of Jesus recorded in Luke 15:1-7?
	- Some have wandered away from God and don’t even know that they are lost. What lesson can we learn from this same parable in Luke 15:8-10?
	- Some people are lost deliberately. What lessons can we learn from the father of the son who deliberately wandered away from home? Luke 15:20
	- What important lessons can we learn from this 3-part parable of Jesus about searching for those who have wandered away from God and welcoming them home?
	- Why is the prayer of the Apostle Paul so relevant for our lives today? Ephesians 3:14-19
