### The Bible: A Guidebook For Life

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series on Growing in your relationship with God. Today’s topic: THE BIBLE: A GUIDEBOOK FOR LIFE.

**Scripture Song:** Seek the LORD – Isaiah 55:6-7

- **Why Satan doesn’t want you to read the Bible**
	- The Bible exposes the true character of Satan. John 8:44, Revelation 12:7-9,12, 1 Peter 5:8, etc.
	- The Bible is a powerful weapon against the enemy. Ephesians 6:17, Matthew 4:1-11
	- The Bible reveals the true character of God. Lamentations 3:22-23, Zephaniah 3:17, John 3:16-17, etc.
- **Why reading the Bible is important for Christians**
	- 2 Timothy 3:15-17 How does reading the Bible help us grow in a relationship with God? (see also Jeremiah 15:16, Psalm 119:105)
	- Why should we ask the Holy Spirit to guide us when we read the Bible? 1 Corinthians 2:13-14, John 16:13
	- Which early Christians set a noble example through a daily reading and study of the Scriptures? Acts 17:10-11
	- What evidence do you see in the Gospels that Jesus spent significant time hiding the Word of God in His heart? Matthew 4:4,7,10, 15:3-4,7-9, 22:36-40, etc.
	- When did you come to the realization that reading the Bible would help you grow in your relationship with God?
	- Today we have many ways to fill our minds with the Word of God. What ways have been most helpful for you? (reading different translations, listening to an audio Bible, a daily Bible reading plan on your phone, etc.)
- **The reliability of the Scriptures**
	- What testimony did Jesus give about the reliability of the Scriptures? John 17:17, 16:13-15, etc.
	- What testimony did the Apostle Peter give about the reliability of the Scriptures? 2 Peter 1:19-21, Acts 1:16, 2:16-21, etc.
	- What testimony did the Apostle Paul give about the reliability of the Scriptures? Acts 28:23, 1 Thessalonians 2:13, etc.
	- What other testimonies in the Scriptures confirm their reliability as the Word of God? 2 Samuel 23:1-3, Psalm 12:6, Proverbs 30:5-6, etc.
- The primary purpose of the Scriptures
	- What is the primary purpose of the Scriptures? John 5:39-40, Luke 24:27
	- Which books of the Bible would you encourage a person to read first? Why?
	- What words of Jesus recorded in the Gospels are especially meaningful to you?
