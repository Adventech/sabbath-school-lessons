### Spiritual Warfare

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series on Growing in your relationship with God. Today’s topic: SPIRITUAL WARFARE.

**Scripture Song:** Seek the LORD – Isaiah 55:6-7

- **Distractions and Temptations**
	- What are some distractions Satan might use to divert your attention from your relationship with God?
	- Share a time when you were distracted from focusing on your relationship with God. How did God draw you back into a relationship with Him?
	- Some distractions are direct temptations to do evil. Matthew 4:1-11, 1 John 2:15-17
	- How do we become aware that we are being tempted to sin? Romans 3:20, Psalm 119:105, 1 John 3:4
	- What is the best course of action when we are tempted to do evil? James 4:7-8, Hebrews 12:1-2
	- What caution did the Apostle Paul give to those who might be careless about the temptations of Satan? 1 Corinthians 10:12
	- If we stumble and fall into sins of thought, word, or deed, what precious promise can we claim from the Word of God? 1 John 1:9
- **Spiritual attacks from the kingdom of darkness**
	- Share a story in the Scriptures where someone came under a spiritual attack from the kingdom of darkness. Mark 5:1-5, Acts 13:4-8, etc.
	- What warning did the Apostle Peter give about spiritual attacks from Satan? 1 Peter 5:8
	- What special resource does God make available to protect us against spiritual attacks from the kingdom of darkness? Ephesians 6:10-18
	- Why is prayer to God also an important resource when we are attacked by the kingdom of darkness?
- **Strongholds of the enemy**
	- Is anyone here aware of strongholds that Satan established in your immediate family or ancestors?
	- How did those strongholds impact your life?
	- What stronghold did Satan establish in the life of Samson? Judges 14:1-3, 16:1,4
	- How was the LORD finally able to break that stronghold of the enemy? Psalm 50:15
	- What message of encouragement did the Apostle Paul give to those who sense the enemy has established strongholds in their lives? 2 Corinthians 10:3-5
	- Are there some strongholds of the enemy that cannot be torn down?
	- What is your testimony about strongholds God has torn down in your life?
