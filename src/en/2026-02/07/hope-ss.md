### Practical Lessons About Prayer

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing our series on Growing in your relationship with God. Today’s topic: PRACTICAL LESSONS ABOUT PRAYER.

**Scripture Song:** Seek the LORD – Isaiah 55:6-7

- **Praying in a crisis**
	- 1 Kings 18:37 What are your thoughts about Elijah’s prayer at a time of crisis for the nation of Israel?
	- How did the LORD God respond to Elijah’s simple prayer and what was the response of the people gathered on Mount Carmel? 1 Kings 18:38
	- How did Elijah pray in a time of personal crisis following the showdown on Mount Carmel? 1 Kings 19:1-4
	- How did the LORD God respond to this prayer by Elijah? 1 Kings 19:5-18
	- What does this story teach us both about prayer and about the character of our prayer-answering God?
- **When prayers seem unanswered**
	- What apparent delayed answer to his prayer did Elijah the prophet experience? 1 Kings 18:41-45
	- Sometimes God’s answer to our prayer is “No”. When did God say “No” to a prayer of Elijah? (1 Kings 19:4)
	- Share a time when you experienced a delayed answer to your prayer or a negative answer from God. What did you learn through that experience?
- **Lessons about prayer from Jesus**
	- What practical instruction did Jesus give regarding prayer? Matthew 6:5-8
	- How did Jesus respond to the request of His disciples who wanted to learn how to pray? Luke 11:1-4
	- The Lord’s Prayer begins with praise. Why is praise to God an important part of our prayer time with God? Psalm 18:3, 100:4, 9:1-2, 92:1-2, etc.
	- What other important elements so you see in the Lord’s Prayer?
	- What can we learn from the words of Jesus recorded in Matthew 6:9?
	- What can we learn about how to pray from the prayers of Jesus? Luke 10:21-22, John 11:41-42, Luke 22:41-42, John 17, Luke 6:12, etc.
- More practical lessons about prayer
	- What lessons about prayer can we learn from the following admonitions of the Apostle Paul? Colossians 4:2, 1 Thessalonians 5:17, Ephesians 6:18
	- What other practical lessons about prayer have you found in the Scriptures? Ezra 10:1, Nehemiah 1:4-11, Daniel 9:3-19, etc.
	- Share a time of prayer that was especially meaningful for you.
	- How might your prayer time with God change in response to our study today?
