---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   03/04/2026
---

Ngaphambi kokuzalwa kwethu, uThixo wasithanda; Yena wayenecebo lokusazi nelokuba nathi simazi Yena. Yena uyasikhangela, njengoMalusi olungileyo, aze asimeme ukuba sihlale kuYe mihla le. Okwethu nje kukukhetha ukusabela kuYe, size emva koko sinanise ngobudwayinge bethu nesimo sethu saseLawodike ngenxa yezipho zaKhe ezilungileyo (bona IsiTyhi. 3:18, 19).

Njengokukhula okucothayo kwamasebe omdiliya, ubudlelane bethu noThixo busengakhula ngokucothayo, okanye busengeza ngesiquphe ngenxa yemvula ebidingeka ngokumandla. Nokuba sesiphi isantya esikhula ngaso kwakunye nobuninzi besiqhamo esiveliswayo ebomini bethu, sidinga imihla ngemihla “incindi,” okanye uMoya oyiNgcwele, ukuqinisekisa ukuba sihleli siqhagamshelekile kuYesu.

“Ukuhlala kuKristu kuthetha ukwamkela okuthe rhoqo koMoya waKhe, ubomi bokuzinikela ngokupheleleyo emsebenzini waKhe. Ijelo lonxibelelwano kufuneka livuleke ngokuthe gqolo phakathi komntu noThixo wakhe. Njengokuba isebe lomdiliya litsala incindi rhoqo kumdiliya ophilileyo, nathi masitshele kuYesu, size samkele kuYe ngokholo amandla nengqibelelo yesimilo saKhe.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 676.

“Eli gatya lomileyo lixhuzulekileyo lingakwazi njani ukuba nto-nye nesikhondo sediliya? Lingenziwa njani ukuba lixhamle ubomi nesondlo somdiliya ophilileyo? Kuphela ngokuhlonyelwa emdiliyeni, ngokuziswa kobona budlelane busondeleyo bunokwenzeka. Umchwe ngomchwe, umthambo ngomthambo, isetyana libambelela nkqi kumdiliya onika ubomi bude ubomi bomdiliya bube bunye nesebe, lize nesebe livelise isiqhamo esifana nomdiliya.”—Ellen G. White, manuscript 67, 1897.

**Imibuzo Yenkumbulo:**

`Khangela ngemva kubomi bakho. Ungakhomba naziphi iziganeko zobomi eziye zakuzolisa kwimeko yobuLawodike basemoyeni? Ziganeko zini ezikusondeze kuThixo?`

`U-Ellen G. White uthetha “ngokwamkela rhoqo uMoya waKhe.” Uwuthandazela kangaphi uMoya oyiNgcwele? Yintoni engatshintsha xa ubuwamkela yonke imihla uMoya oyiNgcwele?`

`Yintoni engatshintsha ukuba thina, njengebandla, besiwuthandazela uMoya oyiNgcwele ngokunyameke ngakumbi nakuthe rhoqo ngakumbi?`

`Yiba phandle, nkqu nakabuhlungu, unyaniseke kuwe malunga nobudlelane bakho noThixo. Zigqibo zini ezisengqondweni ekudingeka uzenze ukuze kubekho ukusondelelana naYe akufunayo Yena kodwa wena okulibazisayo?`

**Isishwankathelo:** Ngaphambi kokuba siqalise ukukhula kubudlelane noThixo, kufuneka kuqala siqale sinqumame ukuqwalasela into obuyiyo ubudlelane esinabo kunye naYe ngoku. Ukuba busabuLawodike okanye ukuba amasebe ethu awondleki, uYesu unesisombululo esigqibeleleyo semeko yethu yasemoyeni: ukuhlala kuYe.
