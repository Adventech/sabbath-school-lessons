---
title:  Uthando Olungunaphakade
date:   31/03/2026
---

Emva kokuzoba yokudangala kwethu, uYesu usixelela ukuba le yinto efanele ukweyiswa. “ ‘Lowo weyisayo, ndiyakumvumela ukuba ahlale nam etroneni, njengokuba nam ndeyisayo, ndaza ndahlala phantsi noBawo etroneni yakhe’ ” (IsiTyhi. 3:21). Kwabanye bethu, isenokuba lidabi elikhulu esiqubisana nalo-ukusuka nje siqonde imeko yethu ebuthathaka, yokungadingi nto; ukwamkela ukukhalima kukaYesu; ukuguquka; kwakunye nokwamkela ingubo kaYesu yobulungisa phezu kwethu ngamehlo abonayo ngokwenene.

Okona kumangalisayo kukuba uYesu uyayiqonda imeko yethu edangeleyo, edikidiki yaye uzibandakanya nathi (hayi ngoba uYesu wakha wadikidiki). Yena uthi, “ ‘Lowo weyisayo . . . njengoko nam ndeyisayo’ ” (IsiTyhi. 3:21) Ngenxa yokuba Yena wafa ukuze asisindise, uYesu usoyisile isono kwakunye nesohlwayo saso. Uyawaqonda amadabi esono esijamelene nawo yaye uthembisa ngokusinceda.

Intlaninge yabantu yaasabela kwisimemo sikaThixo sokuba kubudlelane bomnqophiso kunye naYe. Le yimbali engungqa-phambili okanye umxholo weBhayibhile iphela. Xa sijonga abanye baba bantu, sinokubona ukuba uThixo waanxibelelana nabo ngokwahlukileyo ngamaxesha ohlukeneyo.

**Ezi ngxelo zisifundisa ntoni ngendlela uThixo anxibelelana ngayo nabantu kwiimeko ezohlukileyo?**

`Gen. 2:7, Gen. 3:8–10`

`Gen. 5:24`

`Gen. 6:13`

`Gen. 12:1–4`

`Eksod. 34:29`

Nokuba uThixo esisiqu waahamba nabantwana baKhe okanye wavela wathetha kunye nabo, inyaniso kukuba Yena ubesoloko elangazelela ukuba kufutshane eluntwini. Nokuba ubudlelane bakho noThixo bunjani na namhlanje, uThixo unqwenela ukusondela kuwe. Singafunda ngale ngcamango kuYeremiya 31:3, 4 ngezantsi:

“UYehova ubonakele kum kwakude, wathi, Ndikuthandile ngothando olungunaphakade; ngenxa yoko ndikolulele inceba. Ndiya kubuya ndikwakhe, wakheke.’ ”

Nokuba iyaqala okanye iyaphela imini yakho kanye ngoku, uThixo ufuna wena yaye ulindile, efuna ukukusondeza kufutshane naYe. Yena ufuna ukwakha-okanye akhe kwakho-ubudlelane bakho kunye naYe. Ukuba abenzeki, isiphoso sikwicala lakho, hayi kwelaKhe.

`Zintoni izinto ebomini bakho kanye ngoku ezinokuthi, ukuba azoyiswanga, zibe nako kwaye zithintela ubudlelane bakho noThixo?`
