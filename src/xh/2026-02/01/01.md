### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Isityhi. 3:14–22; Isityhi. 4:9–11; Gen. 2:7; Gen. 3:8–10; Yer. 31:3, 4; Yohane 15:1–11; Roma 8:9–11.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Njengoko uBawo wandithandayo, nam ndanithanda; hlalani ke eluthandweni lwam’ ” (Yohane 15:9).

Ungabuchaza njani ubudlelane bakho noThixo namhlanje? Busemdleni yaye buluqilima? Ingaba uchitha ixesha utyala kobu budlelane, uphengulula iLizwi laKhe eliphefumlelweyo, kwaye uncokola naYe njengoMhlobo? Ukuba kunjalo, lingakanani ixesha olichitha usenza oko?

Kanjalo, unyanzelekile ukuba wabelane nabanye ngobudlelane bakho noThixo kuba bubobona budlelane bumangalisayo ebomini bakho? Okanye, endaweni yalo nto, ubudlelane bakho noThixo buthothile ngokuya lihamba ixesha? Bukhona, ewe, yaye uyabuhlola rhoqo, kodwa xa sithetha inyani, abusekho luqilima konke konke. Okanye, mhlawumbi, usesidikidikini, soko iBhayibhile ikubiza ngokuba “bubudikidiki” (IsiTyhi. 3:16).

Wakhe wazibuza ukuba iingelosi ziyayicinga na eyokuba kutheni singaphili ngokudumisa uMsindisi noMkhululi wethu, ngentliziyo ezilambileyo neengqondo ezizondelele ukuba sikhule ngokusondeleleneyo kuThixo yonke imihla? Kuba eneneni, ubudlelane noThixo butshintsha yonke into-ngokufanayo apha nakunaphakade.

Kule veki, masigqale isimo sangoku sobudlelane bethu noThixo kwakunye nento eliyiyo icebo leBhayibhile kuthi. Eneneni, asinakusuka kwindawo esikuyo sisinge kwindawo engcono de senze uhlolo olunyanisekileyo kwiziqu zethu size siphulaphule isisombululo asichazayo uYesu.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Apreli 4._
