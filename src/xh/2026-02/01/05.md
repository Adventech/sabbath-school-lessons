---
title:  Hlalani
date:   01/04/2026
---

Abafundi bamlandela uYesu ukwehla amanqwanqwa acutheneyo ukusuka kwigumbi eliphezulu ukusinga kwisitalato esisezantsi. Eli xesha behamba kunye ukusinga eGetsemane, koko kwakuzakuba bobunye ubusuku obubalulekileyo kwimbali yehlabathi, mhlawumbi abazange baqonde ngokwenene indlela awayebuhlungu ngayo amanye amazwi okugqibela kaYesu kubo kwiGumbi eliPhezulu.

`Waathini uYesu kuYohane 15:1-11?`

La mazwi, ethethwa nguYesu ngokwaKhe, adandalazisa indlela obuyiyo ubudlelane obuqhogene noThixo. Qaphela igama eliphindiweyo, hayi nje kabini koko izihlandlo ezilishumi: ukuhlala. Ukuhlala kuYesu kukuphila ngokuqhagamshelene kunye naYe.

Eli xesha Yena ejongene noMnqamlezo, uYesu akagxininisi nje kuphela ukubaluleka okukhulu kokukuhlala kuYe, kodwa Yena uchaza ngokuselubala nangokusulungekileyo inkalo eqhelekileyo yendlela obukhangeleka ngayo ebomini bethu.

Eyile: UYesu ungumdiliya, thina singamasebe. Ngenxa yokuhlala (yokuqhagamshelana) kuYesu, isiqhamo sizakukhula kumasebe ethu. Asinakukwazi ukwenza isiqhamo sikhule ngokunokwethu. Ngamanye amaxesha singakhangeleka ngokungathi sihleli, kodwa ubungqina buyakuba kukungabi naziqhamo kwethu, aze kamva amasebe ome. Ukuba somile, uMnini-sidiliya kamva uyakuwasusa amasebe. Nokuba sithwala isiqhamo okanye hayi, amasebe ethu ayakuthenwa.

Kwangaxesha nye, sonke sijamelene nemicela-mngeni kwakunye namaxesha abuhlungu. Ukuba sihleli kuYe, la maxesha ayakuvelisa ngakumbi isiqhamo isithuba eside. Ukuxakatha isiqhamo kuqinisekisa ukuba singobani (abafundi). Sithwala isiqhamo ukuzisa kuYe (hayi kwiziqu zethu) uzuko. Ukuhlala kuYesu kuthetha ukugcina imithetho yaKhe, eyimbonakaliso yesimilo saKhe esihle sothando olungazicingeliyo. Ukuhlala kuYesu kuzisa uvuyo olukhulu. Ukuhlala kuYesu kuthetha ukwenza oko Yena akufunayo kuthi, njengokusabela kuYe. “Kuba ukumthanda uThixo kuko ukuthi, siyigcine imithetho yakhe; ayinzima ke imithetho yakhe” (1 Yohane 5:3).

Ukuba uyacinga ngayo, ukuhlala kuYesu lelinye lamachiza kwimeko yethu yobuLawodike (IsiTyhi. 3:20, Yohane 15:4). Yimfihlo enkulu yobomi obuzalisekileyo nobunentsingiselo emhlabeni nakunaphakade, noko kunjalo ngandlel’ ithile sililibala ngokulula icebiso likaYesu.

Ekugqibeleni, uYesu uthi kumntu ngamnye kuthi, “ ‘Njengoko uBawo wandithandayo, nam ndanithanda; hlalani ke eluthandweni lwam’ ” (Yohane 15:9). Uthando lukaYesu luyeyona ntambo inyanzelayo esitsalela kuYe, kwaye xa silwazi oluthando, siyakuchukumiseka ngokunzulu ukuba sisabele ngothando kuThixo nakwabanye.
