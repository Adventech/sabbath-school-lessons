---
title:  Ukohlwaywa, Inguquko, Umvuzo
date:   30/03/2026
---

“ ‘Mna bonke endibathandayo ndiyabohlwaya, ndibaqeqeshe,’ ” uyasixelela uYesu kwiSiTyhilelo 3:19. “ ‘zondelela ngoko uguquke.’ ” Akukho namnye kuthi, nkqu noba ngumzuzwana, onokuthi ngokukuko uYesu akakhathali ngathi okanye ingomso lethu. Indlela ekwakunokubalula ngayo ukuba uYesu aluncame uluntu aze angayihambi indlela ebuhlungu awayikhethayo kulo mhlaba. Ngokwenene kungenxa yokuba Yena esithanda nzulu kakhulu le nto Yena asikhalimelayo kwimeko esikuyo ngoku. Unqwenela ubudlelane obuluqilima ngakumbi, obunzulu kunye nathi. Akanelisekanga zizimo zethu zamathub’ athile, inkqubo yethu yokuba “Ndiya kuza kuYe xa ndimdinga Yena.”

Endaweni yoko, uYesu usikhalimelela ukulungelwa kwethu. Yena usixelela ukuba siguquke. Kodwa asinakuguquka de siqonde ukuba ukhona undonakele. Noko kunjalo Yena usixelele ncakasana undonakele kuthi: sicinga ukuba sizizityebi, kodwa eneneni “ ‘sizindwayinge, iintsizana, amahlwempu, imfama, yaye sihamba ze’ ” (SiTyhi. 3:17).

`Funda iSiTyhilelo 3:20. Sithenjiswe ntoni apha? Kodwa yintoni ekufuneka siyenzile ukuzuza eso sithembiso?`

Eli lilizwi elingumfanekiso omhle nongaqhelekanga ngeyona ndlela. UThixo welimiweyo unqwenela ukuhlala esidlweni kunye nawe, kunye nam. Ulangazelela ukubandakanyeka okumacala kwakunye nencoko engokutya okukuko. Le yinkcazelo yobudlelane obuqhogeneyo, obukhoyo, kwaye uYesu usimemela ukuba sibe noku kunye naYe.

UYesu umi elinde ngomonde yaye enkqonkqoza kucango lwentliziyo yakho. Mhlawumbi uyibonile imifanekiso yoku kwiincwadi zabantwana-uMsindisi omde, onobabalo, enkqonkqoza ngobunono. Akambonzeleki aze akunyanzele ukuba uncokole kunye naYe. Akanyanzeli kwixesha lakho okanye kubomi bakho obumanxadanxada. Ixesha lifutshane, ngoko ke, ukuba uyamva Yena, vula ucango. Uyakubakho ukuba angene ebomini bakho.

Esi sikweko sidandalazisa uhlobo lobudlelane uYesu afuna ukuba nalo kunye nomntu ngamnye kuthi. Kodwa ngenye imini, xa uhlangana noYesu ubuso ngobuso, xa uphosa isithsaba sakho ezinyaweni zaKhe ukubonga nokudumisa kunye namawaka angamashumi amawaka abanye ukunqula uMdali (IsiTyhi. 4:9–11, IsiTyhi. 5:11–14), xa uzama ukuphosa iingqondo zenu emva kwizilingo zasemhlabeni nize nibone ukuba zifiphale kukungabaluleki-ucinga ukuba ngelo xesha uyakukhe ulikhalele ixesha olichithe noYesu emhlabeni?

`Kanye ngoku, uYesu uyankqonkqoza. Yena uyabiza. Wena, ngoko, kufuneka wenze ukhetho olusezingqondweni lokuvula intliziyo yakho kuYe. Ukuphonononga umnqamlezo kwakunye nokucinga oko ukuthethayo kungakuvuselela njani ukuba wenze olo khetho?`
