---
title:  Imeko Esikuyo
date:   29/03/2026
---

Ukhe uzibuze ukuba uYesu angathini na xa Yena enokuchaza ubudlelane bakho kunye naYe kanye ngoku? Mhlawumbi Yena uyakuthi buluqilima, okanye bukhe baluqilima kwixa elidlulileyo. Ukhe uzibuze ukuba uYesu angathini na xa Yena enokuchaza abantu baKhe kule mihla yokugqibela? KwisiTyhilelo 3:14-22, uYesu eneneni uyabuchaza.

Uqala ngokuthi Yena “ ‘ “ulingqina elithembekileyo, eliyinyaniso, ingqalo yayo indalo kaThixo” ’ ” (IsiTyhi. 3:14). Ingqina elithembekileyo neliyinyaniso alixoki koko lithetha phandle nangokunyanisekileyo.

`Funda kwiSiTyhilelo 3:14-17, apho uYesu adandalazisa imeko yasemoyeni yabantu baKhe namhlanje. Ezi ndima zikuchaza kakuhle njani njengesiqu?`

UYesu uyasixela thina, ziqu zamaKristu aphila kwimihla yokugqibela, ukuba Yena usazi nqo. Asishushu kanjalo asibandi, ngenxa yokuba, kule ndawo yethu sikuyo, asidingi nto kwaphela. Iintsuku neeveki ziyagqitha, yaye sichitha ixesha elincinci noThixo apha naphaya, size sicinge oko kwanele. Kodwa akunjalo. Ndaweni yoko, eneneni Yena simdinga kakhulu ngokumandla ngaphezu kokuba siqonda. Ukuba kuphela besinokuthanda size siphilele uYesu ngokuphelelisileyo okanye siyeke. Oko bekuyakuba ngcono ngokokubona kukaThixo kunokuba dikidiki. UYesu uthi Yena uzakusihlanza siphume emlonyeni waKhe ngenxa yokuba sijavujavu sinjalo. Kodwa ke Yena akakwenzi oku, kwaye Yena uyasicela ukuba sense iziggqibo eziqinisekileyo kanye ngoku.

`Lithini icebiso laKhe kuthi kwiSiTyhilelo 3:18, 19?`

Kumaxesha amandulo, “ukuthenga” into kwakuthetha ukunaniselana okanye ukutshintshiselana ngezinto. Apha, uYesu ununusa ngesisa ukutshintshiselana: ukudangala kwethu ngegolide yaKhe, ngeengubo zaKhe ezimhlophe, kwakunye nangentambiso yaKhe. Yena ufuna ukusenza sityebe emehlweni aKhe; ufuna ukusambathisa ngengubo yaKhe egqibeleleyo yobulungisa; yaye Yena ufuna ukuvula amehlo ethu sibone inyaniso yendlela ubudlelane obukhoyo kunye naYe obungatshintsha yonke into ngokupheleleyo. Yena usinunusa ngako konke oko sikudingayo, ngokukodwa kuba oko sikudingayo, asikwazi kuzibonelela ngokunokwethu. Nguye yedwa onako, kwaye uyakukwenza, oko kuphela xa sinentumekelelo.

`Ukuba uyibona iyinto ebuhlungu ukuzibhenca kwakunye nobume bakho basemoyeni, themba lini obonelelwa ngalo kwezi ndima zanamhlanje?`
