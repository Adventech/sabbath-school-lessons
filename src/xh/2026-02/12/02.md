---
title:  Ngokokuphuphuma
date:   14/06/2026
---

`Funda uMyalelo oMkhulu kuMateyu 28:18-20. Bhala phantsi imiyalezo eyohlukeneyo kaYesu xa Yena esithi, “zonke” okanye “yonk’ imihla” (nto leyo eligama elinye kwisiGrike, upas).`

UYesu wasinika umyalelo wokwabelana ngomyalezo waKhe nehlabathi: “Hambani ngoko niye kuzenza abafundi.” Injongo esemqoka yeBandla laBalindi kukwenza abafundi, abanokuthi kamva nabo benze abanye abafundi. Ngaloo ndlela sonke sivakalisa iindaba ezilungileyo ezingunaphakade kwakunye nezigidimi zeengelosi ezintathu (Isityhi. 14:6–12) ukulungiselela ihlabathi lethu ukubuya kukaYesu okukhawulezileyo.

Wonk’ ubani ofumene ubomi obutsha kuKristu ubizelwa ekungqineni. Noko kunjalo, amaxesha amaninzi, abantu bacinga ngokungqina njengento abangenako ukuyenza okanye abangafuniyo ukuyenza. Unganomfanekiso wakho ushumayela kwikona yesitalato okanye uqhuba isifundo seBhayibhile esimbaxa, ize ngenxa yoko unikine intloko. “Hayi mna! Soze! Andisosithethi; ukungqina asiyonto ndonwaba kuyo.”

Noko kunjalo, ukungqina kokwenyani kusisiphumo nje sokuba lingqina loko ukubonileyo kwinto ayenzayo uThixo ebomini bakho, sokuqaphela oko Yena akufundisa kona lo gama ukhulayo kuYe, ize ke ngokulula wabelane ngamava akho kunye nabanye. UThixo ulunge ngeyona ndlela, kwaye oko Yena asenzele kona zezona ndaba zingcono eli hlabathi elinokuziva. Asinako kwaye masingathi cwaka! Ukukhulule wena; Yena ukubize ngegama wena-ungowaKhe. Zingakhona ezinye iindaba ezingcono zakhe nabani na naphi?

Nangona abafundi bebandla lokuqala babengafundanga okanye bengenabuciko, sisengafunda kubo.

`Funda iZenzo 1:8 neZenzo 4:13. Kwakunjani ukungqina kwibandla lokuqala? Futhe lini uPetros noYohane ababa nalo kwabo babebave bengqina?`

UPetros noYohane baqhubekeka bankqangaza, “ ‘Kuba singenako thina ukuthi singazithethi izinto esazibonayo, esazivayo’ ” (Zenzo 4:20). “babenaye uYesu” (Zenzo 4:13) kwaye babenyazelekile ukuba babelane. UMoya oyiNgcwele wabanika isibindi kwakunye namandla eyisayo kumazwi abo.

`Chitha ixesha emthandazweni kanye ngoku. Cela inkalipho yaxa uThixo ekukhokelela kwabo unokungqina kubo ngesibindi, kwakunye nobulumko bokulazi ixesha lokuthetha nobokuyazi into yokuthetha. Funda ku-1 Yohane 4:7-11 uze uthandazele olu hlobo lothando.`
