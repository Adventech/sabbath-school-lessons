---
title:  Amaqhinga Okwabelana ngoYesu
date:   16/06/2026
---

Umbuzo womntu ngamnye kuthi: Wabelana nabani ngoYesu-unoposi, umabhala wevenkile, umntu ombona mihla le xa uphandle uhamba? UThixo ubiza ikholwa ngalinye ukuba lincedise Yena kulo msebenzi, kwaye Yena uyathembisa ukuba uzakunika “ulwimi lwabafundileyo, ukuze ndikwazi [wena] ukuzimasa otyhafileyo ngelizwi’ ” (Isa. 50:4). Ikwa luxanduva lomKristu ukusoloko elungele ukukhusela (ukuthethelela) ukholo nethemba elingaphakathi kuthi (1 Pet. 3:15).

`Funda ku-1 Petros 3:8-15. Lisixelela ntoni iLizwi likaThixo kwezi ndima?`

Nanga amanye amaqhinga alula ekufuneka uwagcine engqondweni xa uqwalasela indlela yokuba nenjongo ngakumbi ngokwabelana noYesu nabanye:

- Ziqhelanise nomntu uze wakhe ubuhlobo ngokuhamba kwexesha. Ubushushu bakho, ububele, kwakunye nomdla wokwenyani ngakuye (ngokuthandekayo) uyakunceda ekumtsaleleni kuThixo. (Abanye balubiza olu “njengovangelo lobuhlobo.”)
- Thandazela uMoya oyiNgcwele ukuba usebenze entliziyweni yalo mntu. Thandazela amathuba angawo okunxibelelana naye.
- Khangela iindlela eziqhelekileyo zokuthetha ngamava akho okholo okanye umthandazele. Cela uThixo ukuba akunike isibindi kodwa ukuthantamisa kwindlela oza ngayo.
- Fumana iindlela zokuqhagamshelanisa lo mhlobo wakho mtsha kunye nabanye ebandleni lakho, ukuze akwazi ukungcamla ukwangiwa ngabantu bebandla lakho. Iqela lokuncokola okanye elincinci lokufunda iBhayibhile linyathelo elilandelayo elilungileyo.
- Thandazela iintswelo ezithile okanye imibuzo asenokuba nayo lo mhlobo wakho mtsha uze ukhangele ithuba lokumbonisa indlela iBhayihbile ebonelela ngayo ngentuthuzelo, icebiso, nomkhomba-ndlela ebomini bethu. Usengabelana nje ngesithembiso esinye seBhayibhile kuqala okanye uphendule umbuzo omnye, ozakuvula ucango lweengxoxo ezinzulu. Thandazela nezo, kanjalo.
- Kuyakubakho ixesha apho uyakunqwenela ukubuza ukuba ingaba umhlobo wakho lo ungathanda na ukuthatha inyathelo elilandelayo (ukufunda iBhayibhile kwakunye, ekugqibeleni, nokubhaptizwa). Sukuwangxamela la manyathelo kodwa kanjalo sukuhamba ngonyawo lonwabu. Kuthandazele oku.
- Izenzo zethu kufuneka zityhile ukuba singabakabani na. Indlela esibaphatha ngayo abanye ebomini bethu iyakuthetha lukhulu. Njengoko izimilo zethu zibunjwa ngokokufana naYe (ukungcwaliswa), siyakuphilela ukutsalela bonke abantu kuYe.
