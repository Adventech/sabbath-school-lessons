### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Mat. 28:18–20, 2 Pet. 3:18, 1 Pet. 3:8–15, Hoseya 7, Zekariya 10.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘INkosi uYehova indinike ulwimi lwabafundileyo, ukuze ndikwazi ukuzimasa otyhafileyo ngelizwi. Ivusa imiso ngemiso, indivusela indlebe ukuba ndive ngokwabafundileyo’ ” (Isaya 50:4).

Yayi yintsasa exakekileyo yeSabatha kuMfundisi uG. Wayevuke kwangonyezi, elungiselela iSikolo seSabatha kwakunye nentshumayelo, kwaye wayekwakhokela uchungechunge lovangelo emva kwemini. Waanqakula izitshixo zakhe, wabaleka ukuphuma ngomnyango, waza wagqotsa.

Waaqhuba ecanda phakathi kwezithuthi esixekweni, ekhutyekiswa kukuba intlaninge engako yabantu yayiphumile ngentsasa yangoMgqibelo yaye yayizakumenza afike emva kwexesha enkonzweni. Babesiya phi bonke? Emva koko, engacinganga, enye imoto yanqumla phambi kwakhe. Wazibamba iziqhoboshi waza waphakamisa inqindi lakhe ngokuxakwa nokucaphuka, engxolisa umqhubi.

Ekugqibeleni, uMfundisi uG waafika enkonzweni. Uthe xa aphakamisa amehlo ukuba ezakufundisa isifundo, amehlo akhe athi krwaqu iklasi yakhe aza athi ntsho kubuso obuqhelekileyo: umqhubi wesithuthi ebecatshukiswe siso kanye kwimizuzu engama-20 edlulileyo.

Ethubeni, xa ilungu le bandla lamazisayo umqhubi njengomntu ongengoMlindi owayetyelele izizalwane, uMfundisi uG wayiqonda kwakhona indlela unxibelelwano ngalunye, ngokufanayo kwabo sibaziyo nesingabaziyo, ekufuneka luqube ngayo kuthando oluphuma kubudlelane obumileyo kunye noThixo. Ngekhe wazi indlela izenzo zakho, ngokukodwa njengekholwa, ezinokuba nefuthe ngayo kwabanye.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Juni 20._
