---
title:  Mababuyiswe
date:   18/06/2026
---

Sonke sikhe sanezigaba ezibuthathaka nezixengaxengayo kuhambo lwethu noThixo-iintili apho intliziyo yethu ikhe yangathembeki okanye sikhe nje sadikidiki ixesha elide kakhulu. Yintoni le yakubuyisela kubudlelane obumileyo kunye naYe?

`UZekariya 10 uthi thaca imiyalezo eliqela emnandi ngoThixo ebuyisela abantu baKhe kuYe. Funda esi sahluko ngoku ngokucothayo uze uphawule imiyalezo engundoqo.`

Ngendlela ephathekayo, ukwazi indlela yokunxibelelana, nokuncokola, nomntu osenyongweni oshiye ubudlelane neNkosi kunoba ngumngeni. Usengazibuza indlela izinto eziye zohluka ngayo kwisiphumo esohlukileyo; usengazibuza indlela yokuncokola kunye naye kuba ngoku izinto ezibona ngenye indlela; kwaye ungazibona ukhathazekile ungenakuzinceda ngezigqibo ezibi asenokuba kanti uyazenza. Ezi ngcinga ziyakusoloko zinefuthe kwindlela omphatha ngayo lowo usenyongweni kuwe, kwaye kubaluleke kakhulu ngoko ukuphila nokuthetha ngokokuphuphuma kwexesha lakho buqu kunye noMsindisi wakho.

Ubungqina bobomi bakho, izenzo zakho, amazwi akho, kwakunye nokuthandazela kwakho umlingane wakho okanye umntwana oshiye uThixo zinokutshintsha ngokumandla ubomi nengomso labo. (Funda kuLuka 22:31, 32 noYohane 21:15-17 ngendlela imithandazo kaYesu ngoPetros eyalitshintsha ngayo ingomso lakhe.) Nikela lonke usizi, ugwebo, okanye ukugxeka onokubuva ngakuye, uze endaweni yoko ucele uThixo ukuba abeke endaweni yezimvakalelo uthando olunokunikezelwa nguYe kuphela. Cela uThixo ukuba akugqume ngesimilo saKhe ukuze ube ngumzekelo wemo ethandayo, engazicingeliyo. Khumbula ukuba “akukho mpembelelo ingenye enokurhangqa umphefumlo womntu enamandla ngolo hlobo njengempembelelo yobomi obungenamna. Eyona ngxoxo imandla ekumeleni igospile ngumKristu othandayo nothandekayo.”—Ellen G. White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 470.

Ngomzekelo wethu wobomi obungagungqiyo obukhombisa abanye kuKristu, abo bambhebhethileyo uKristu bayakubona kuthi into enokuvela kuThixo kuphela. Bayakubona uxolo ologqitha konke ukuqonda, uthando olungancamiyo, nethemba elikholelwayo kuzo naziphi iimeko. Uthando lukaThixo ngakuthi nangakwabo sibathandayo alukhe luhexe. Sinako ukunikezela oluthando, esiluxhamla mihla le, kwabo basingqongileyo.

`AmaEfese 3: 17-19 asikhuthaza ukuba senze ntoni?`
