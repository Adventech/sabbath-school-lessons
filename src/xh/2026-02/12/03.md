---
title:  Benganyanzelwanga Koko Ngegunya
date:   15/06/2026
---

Wakhe wazibuza ukuba uYesu wayewugcina njani umdla wokusebenza, ukuphilisa, ukuthuthuzela, ukushumayela, nokufundisa inyambalala yabantu umhla nezolo? Siyaxelelwa ukuba “Wathi akuzibona izihlwele, wasikwa yimfesane zizo, ngokuba zibe zityhafile, zichithakele ngokwezimvu ezingenamalusi” (Mat. 9:36). Yayiluthando nemfesane kaYesu ngakuluntu ezaziqhuba ukusebenza kwaKhe. Kwangalendlela inye, uthando lukaThixo kuthi kufuneka lusinyazelele ekuwuveni umthwalo wokukhokelela abantu kuYe nakwinyaniso yaKhe (2 Kor. 5:14).

Wakhe wabujonga ubuso babantu ongabaziyo kwisihlwele waza wagabadela ukucinga ngonaphakade, uzibuza ukuba bayamazi na uYesu? Wakha wazibuza ukuba ingayintoni na ekuphela kwayo kuwe enokuba luthando lukaThixo kuwe ngakumntu ongamaziyo osweleyo? Uthando lukaThixo kuthi luyasinyanzela ukuba siwuve umthwalo wokukhokelela abantu kuYe. UYeremiya waavakalisa oku xa wayesithi, “ ‘Kusuke entliziyweni yam kunge ngumlilo otshayo, uvalelwe emathanjeni am; ndadinwa kukunyamezela, andaba nako’ ” (Yer. 20:9).

Noko kunjalo, xa sisabelana nabanye ngoThixo, masingakhe sizame ukunyanzela umntu ukuba amkele uThixo okanye inyaniso yaKhe eseBhayibhileni. Ukunyanzela kuyakhabana kanye nengxam yesimilo sikaThixo. UThixo akazange anyanzele uAdam noEva ukuba bangasondeli kumthi wokwazi okulungileyo nokubi (Gen. 2:16, 17). Akazange abanyanzele abantu ukuba bangene emkhombeni basindiswe kuNogumbe (Gen. 7:1). Akazange awanyanzele amaSirayeli ukuba ahlale emnqophisweni wawo kunye naYe (Dut. 4:29–31). Endaweni yoko, Yena waahlangabezana nezidingo zabo (Mat. 4:23–25) waza emva koko wabamemela ekuthini bamlandele Yena. UYesu akazange anyanzele nabani na ukuba amlandele okanye inyaniso yaKhe, kodwa Yena akasincami (Mat. 23:37).

Xa singqina, indlela esenza ngayo mayisoloko ibonakalisa indlela yokwenza kaYesu. UEllen G. White uthi, “Asiyonxalenye yenjongo kaKristu ukunyanzela abantu ukuba bamamkele Yena. NguSathana, kwakunye nabantu abalawulwa ngumoya wakhe, abazama ukunyanzela isazela. . . . Akunakubakho obunye ubungqina obuqukumbelayo bokuba sinomoya kaSathana ngaphezu kwesimo sokuhlungisa nokutshabalalisa abo bangawuxabisiyo umsebenzi wethu, okanye abenza ngokuchasene neengcamango zethu.”—The Desire of Ages, p. 487.

Kufuneka sivumele iziqu zethu ukuba zibe ngamajelo omsebenzi kaThixo. Siphila kwihlabathi eliyithiyileyo inyaniso, kodwa loo nyaniso mayingasithinteli ekwabelaneni ngeendlela ezinenkathalo, ezinothando. Khumbula ukuba isoloko ibubungqina bethu siqu obona busindayo, ngokukodwa kwizigaba zokuqala ekungqineni (Isityhi. 12:11).

`Funda ku-2 Petros 3:18. Kungaziphi iindlela okhula ngazo elubabalweni nakulwazi? Oku kubonakala njani ekunxibelelaneni kwakho kunye nabo bakungqongileyo?`
