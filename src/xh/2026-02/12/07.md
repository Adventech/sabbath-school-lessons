---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   19/06/2026
---

“Nokuba uzibiza ngantoni na, akukho mntu unothando olunyulu kuThixo ngaphandle kokuba unothando olungazicingeliyo ngakumzalwane wakhe. Kodwa asinako ukufikelela ekubeni nalo moya ngokuzama ukuthanda abanye. Okudingekayo luthando lukaKristu entliziyweni. Xa umna edityaniswe kuKristu, uthando lugqabhuka ngokuzenzekelayo.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 384.

“Abo basebenza ngokuzimisele kakhulu ekwenzeni ngokunyaniseka okunomdla umsebenzi wabo wokuzuzela uYesu Kristu imiphefumlo, ngabona baphuhle mpela ngokwasemoyeni nangokonqulo.”—Ellen G. White, Evangelism, p. 356.

“Amandla okuchasa ububi azuzeka ngcono ngokusebenza okubukhali.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 105.

“Ukuze singene kuvuyo lwaKhe-uvuyo lokubona imiphefumlo ekhululwe ngenxa yedini laKhe-masithabathe inxaxheba emisebenzini yaKhe yokukhululwa kwabo.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 142.

Abo batyeshela ilungelo lokudlelane noKristu ekusebenzeni, batyeshela uqeqesho ekuphela kwalo olufaka ukulungela ukuthabatha inxaxheba kunye naYe ebuqaqawulini baKhe.”—Ellen G. White, Education, p. 264.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Kutheni le nto uthando lusisiseko yaye lubalulekile kulo naluphi uhlobo lokungqina obusebenzayo?`

`Ukufumene nini oku kuyinyani: ukuba ukuzuza imiphefumlo kwayanyaniswa nohambo lomntu buqu nangokusemdleni noThixo?`

`Ingaba sikhona isiqalelo okanye umqondo osisiseko odingekayo wokwabelana nabanye ngoThixo? Ukuba kunjalo, kungayintoni oko?`

`Xa kufikelelwa ekunikezeleni isifundo seBhayibhile kongakholwayo, ungaqala? Yintoni ingqalelo yakho yokuqala: ukudandalazisa iimfundiso ezithile okanye ukumemela ubani ekwazini uYesu?`

`Cula okanye uphulaphule amazwi kwiculo “Ndikhululwe!” (UKristu Engomeni, no. 68) uze wetyise iindlela oyivakalisa ngazo.`

**Isishwankathelo:** Xa uthando lukaThixo kwakunye neLizwi laKhe eliphilileyo, elinamandla zizalisa ubomi bethu bamihla le, siyakunyanzeleka ukuba sithande yaye sabelane ngaYe kunye nabo basingqongileyo. Kufuneka sikuthandazele, sikucinge, kwaye sibe nenjongo ngokungqina, sikholelwa ukuba iLizwi laKhe, eliphuma emlonyeni waKhe, aliyi kubuyela kuYe lilambatha, koko liyakukuphumeza Yena akholwa kuko, kwaye liyakuphumelela kwizinto alithumelele zona (Isa. 55:11).
