---
title:  IsiBhalo, Igunya
date:   20/04/2026
---

`Igunya nomsebenzi weBhayibhile zibhengezwe ngokucacileyo kumaphepha ayo. Funda ukope u-2 Timoti 3:15-17. Qaphela oko ezi ndima zikuxelela kona ngomsebenzi weBhayibhile.`

Xa sifikelela ekuzifundeleni buqu iBhayibhile, kufuneka silumke singalindeli ukuba iBhayibhile ifezekisa iinjongo zethu okanye iinkalo, ezingasoloko zifana nezo zikaThixo. Umzekelo, masingasebenzisi indlela “yempumputhela,” kuba oko akuyondlela uThixo anqwenela ukunxibelelana nathi ngayo ngeLizwi laKhe. UThixo akasositshozi, esilindele ukwenza iimfuno nentando yethu. Iindlela zaKhe neengcinga ziziphakamele mpela ezethu (Isa. 55:9), kwaye ngenxa yalo nto masingakhe sizame ukulawula amazwi aKhe aza kuthi. Kananjalo akufuneki sikhethe kuphela iindawo zeBhayibhile ezivakala zilungele thina. Endaweni yoko, kufuneka siyibone iBhayibhile njengesambuku esipheleleyo endaweni yokufunda ezi zicatshulwa zilula, ziqhelekileyo size sishiye ezo zinkqangisayo okanye zicela umngeni. Ukuba ngokwenene sifuna uThixo athethe ebomini bethu, kufuneka siyamkele iBhayibhile iphela size sisebenzise iindlela ezifanelekileyo xa sithath’ inxaxheba kufundo lweBhayibhile olunyamekileyo, sithemba ukuba uThixo uyakutyhila kuthi oko kufuneka sikuvile ngexesha ekufuneka sikuve ngalo.

Kanjalo, uYesu ngokwaKhe uyasixelela: “ ‘Uze uyithande iNkosi uThixo wakho ngentliziyo yakho iphela, nangomphefumlo wakho uphela, nangengqondo yakho iphela’ ” (Mat. 22:37). Oko kukuthi, uThixo akafuni singazihoyi iingqondo zethu; ndaweni yoko, Yena ufuna ukuchubela iingqondo zethu ngomthambo omkhulu wolwazi nengqiqo, ezityhilwe, ngenxalenye, ngeLizwi laKhe. Singafunda intlaninge yamabali aseBhayibhileni nalapho uThixo wayeneengxoxo nabantu abafana noEnoki, uAbraham, uMoses, noYobhi, ukongeza kwinqwaba yeencoko uYesu awayenazo kunye nabantu. UThixo akayicezeli ingqiqo yobuntu koko usimemela ekuyinikeleni kwiLizwi nobulumko baKhe xa “sisebenza” usindiso lwethu.

Ingqiqo yobuntu, noko kunjalo, ihleli iyeyobuntu-inganako ukugqwidiza nokukhohlisa. Ayikhe ingabi naziphoso. Kuyenzeka ukuba ingqiqo yobuntu ityhalele uThixo ecaleni ukuzama ukwenza izinto ngokunokwethu, nto leyo ebeka umna alingane, okanye abengentla, kuThixo xa kufikelelwa ekucingeni. Abantu banokuza kwisiBhalo ngomoya okhukhumeleyo nonzima, becinga ukuba bakhe bakuva konke ngaphambili yaye akukho nto intsha. Kuxa siziva sibalulekile, sizithembile, sanelisekile, yaye singaswelanga nto esiye sityeshele ubudlelane bethu noThixo size sayame kulwazi lwethu oluphelelwayo nakwingqiqo enamakhwiniba.
