---
title:  Ubume Bentliziyo
date:   23/04/2026
---

`Isakhono sethu sokwamkela umyalelo ophuma eLizwini likaThixo (Yobhi 22:22) sixhomekeke kakhulu kwinto esiyiyo isimo sentliziyo yethu xa sisiza eBhayibhileni. Ama-1 Korinte 2:14 akuchaza njani oku?`

Ukuba nokucalucalula ngokwakwamoya kuthetha ukuba nengcamango nengqiqo yakwamoya. Iqondeka kakuhle, ke ngoko, ukuba umntu ongqondo ivulekileyo ngokwakwamoya uyakuba nezinto ezahlukileyo azifumbathayo ekufundeni iBhayibhile kunalowo ungqondo ivalekileyo ngokwakwamoya. Umntu ocinga ukuba iBhayibhile le ibubudenge akasoze akhangele nyaniso kumaphepha ayo.

`Ngoko ke, ngokunye isimo sethu ngakwiBhayibhile kwakunye nendlela esisondela ngayo ekufundeni yona zibaluleke ngeyona ndlela ekukhuleni kubudlelane noThixo. UPawulos ukuchaza njani oku ku-1 Tesalonika 2:13?`

ILizwi likaThixo liyasebenza kuthi xa sikholwa. Xa uvula iBhayibhile uze ukholelwe ukuba uThixo unento afuna ukuyithetha kuwe ngamazwi asemaphepheni, Yena ngokwenene uyakuthetha kuwe aze asebenze ebomini bakho. Kodwa okuninzi kuxhomekeke kukholo lwakho kwakunye neminqweno yakho. Iindaba ezimnandi zezokuba ukuba ukholo lwakho luncinane, uThixo angulunceda lukhule (Marko 9:24), nkqu nokuba lisuntswana okwekhozo lemostade (Luke 17:6).

Enye yeenjongo ezitshatsheleyo zeBhayibhile kukuthetha inyanisos ebomini bethu malunga nemeko yobudlelane bethu noThixo kwakunye nendlela esinokubuqinisa ngayo. Ukuba intliziyo yakho ivulekile kuMoya oyiNgcwele, ukuba usondela eLizwini ngentobeko, uyakusoloko usimka utshintshile, nangani omnye enganakusoloko eyiqaphela kwangoko le nto mihla le, kuba inguqu nokukhula okunjalo kusoloko kusanda. Kodwa ukuba sinamathele ekudangaleni kwethu nasesonweni yaye singakulungele ukutshintsha, ukufunda iBhayibhile kunokusinceda kancinane. UMoya oyiNgcwele uyasixhokonxa ukuba sisondele kuYesu Kristu. Siyafuna ukusondela? Ukuba kunjalo, siba “ngabalumkiselwe elusindisweni” (2 Tim. 3:15), kwaye siyakubona izinto esingazanga zathi qathwa kwiingcinga zethu.

`Sithini isimo sentliziyo nengqondo yam xa ndisiza kwiBhayibhile? Ingaba ndiza nje nezimvo zam eBhayibhileni ngomgomo wokuzama ukuthethelela zona, okanye ndiza nengqondo nentliziyo evulekileyo, ndilungele ngokholo olufana nolomntwana ukubona oko uThixo afuna ukundixelela kona namhlanje? Kutheni loo mpendulo ibalulekile nje?`
