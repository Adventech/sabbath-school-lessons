### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Izili. 3:22, 23; 2 Tim. 3:15–17; Yohane 17:17; Efe. 1:13; Ndum. 119:11; 1 Kor. 2:14.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kuba ilizwi likaThixo liphilile, lisebenza, libukhali ngaphezu kwamakrele onke antlangothi mbini; lihlaba liphumele, lide lahlule umphefumlo kwanomoya, amalungu kwanomongo; linokugweba iingcingane nezicamango zentliziyo” (Hebhere 4:12).

IBhayibhile. Akuthandabuzeki unayo enye okanye mhlawumbi ezininzi. Kuyo yonke imbali, le ncwadi yexabiso ikhutshwe ezingontsini, yathutyeleziswa, yabhangiswa. Iyeyona ncwadi ipapashwe ngokugqithisileyo, kulo naluphi ulwimi, yaye kananjalo yenye yezona zindala. Abanye bafile ukuze iBhayibhile ibe nako ukugcinwa.

Iphi iBhayibhile ebomini bakho? Ingaba uyayifunda okanye ihlala ngasebhedini yakho okanye kuvimba wakho weencwadi iqokelela uthuli? Ingaba ubomi bumatasatasa kuwe ukuba ufumane ixesha lokuba ngokwenene ufunde iLizwi likaThixo okanye uziva udinwe uyimfe ukuba unganqika amaphepha ayo?

ILizwi likaThixo liphilile linamandla, yaye uThixo umema wena ukuba ulivumele lithethe nentliziyo yakho, likukhuthaze, likucele umngeni likuguqule, lize likunike nomkhomba-ndlela nethemba.

IBhayibhile asiyoncwadi nje yezemfundo okanye ingqokelela yamabali amadala. Endaweni yoko, yingxelo entle, enzulu yendlela uMdali welimiweyo afuna ngayo ukusisondeza kuYe. Ukuba unolangazelelo lokukhulu kubudlelane bakho noThixo, eyona nto iphucukileyo unokuyenza kukuzibophelela ekuchitheni ixesha elisemgangathweni kunye naYe mihla le uthandaza, ufunda iLizwi laKhe eliphefumlelweyo, kwaye unikele intando yakho koko likufundisayo.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Apreli 25._
