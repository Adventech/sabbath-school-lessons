---
title:  Esona Sixhobo Sinamandla
date:   19/04/2026
---

Ngaphambi kokuba siphonononge isizathu sokuba iBhayibhile ixabiseke kangaka kwakunye nendlela yokwendela nzulu kufundo lwethu lweBhayibhile njengesiqu, kufuneka siqonde nto: Enye yezona ntlaselo zibalulekileyo uSathana anokuzenza kuwe kukuyekisa ukuchitha ixesha noThixo eLizwini laKhe. Ukugcina abantu bechasele kwiiBhayibhile zabo ngenxa yokuba manxadanxada, ukudangala, ukudinwa, okanye intandabuzo liqhinga lakhe elinkqenkqeza phambili. Uyazi ukuba xa sichitha ixesha noThixo eLizwini laKhe, livuselela ubomi bethu yaye londle imiphefumlo yethu, ngoko ke kakade uzakwenza nantoni na ukuthintela oku!

Siyaxelelwa ukuba “uSathana usebenzisa sonke isixhobo esinokufumaneka ukuthintela [abantu] ekuzuzeni ulwazi lweBhayibhile; kuba iintetho zayo ezicace gcaa zityhila amaqhinga akhe.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 593. USathana uyazi ukuba iLizwi likaThixo elinamandla limenza abe liathalala. USathana uyazi ukuba umthandazo nokufunda iBhayibhile zezona zixhobho zinamandla uluntu elinokuzisebenzisa ngakuye (Efe. 6:17, 18; Heb. 4:12), ngenxa yoko yonke into anokuyenza kukusithintela ekufundeni nasekuthandazeni. Uyazi ukuba amazwi kaThixo anamandla kwanokuba awazange athethe nje kuphela kubekho ihlabathi (Ndum. 33:6), koko anako ukuvusa abafileyo (Yohane 11:41–44) kwaye asinike amandla okweyisa (Mat. 4:1–11).

Ngokugcina abantu bakaThixo qelele kwiBhayibhile zabo, uSathana uchaphazela hayi nje kuphela ubudlelane bethu noThixo koko ubudlelane bethu kunye nabanye. Imitshato yethu iyasindeka, siyabangxolisa abantwana bethu, kwaye asinamonde nezihlobo zethu okanye oogxa bethu emsebenzini. Ubomi bukhangeleka bumaxhaphetshu ngeyona ndlela; siziva sinoxinzelelo sinomthwalo, kungekho ndlela yakubhungca. Kwaye okungumnqa, asisoloko sinqumama ngokwaneleyo ukuqonda oko kwenzekayo. Sisenokucinga sikufutshane kuThixo, kodwa eneneni, xa iintsuku neveki zidlula singakhange nakanye silinqike iLizwi likaThixo, siba buthathaka ngakumbi mihla le.

`Nkqu naxa sinobudlelane obehla benyuka nobungazinzanga mpela noThixo, uThixo uthe gqolo ngokumangalisayo, njengoko iZililo 3:22, 23 zisixelela. Uqaphela ntoni kwezi ndima, kwaye zithelekiseka njani kunye neemvelo zethu zobuntu?`

Njengekherubhi egqubuthelayo ngaphambi kokuwa kwakhe (Hezek. 28:14–17), uKhwezi waaweva amazwi kaThixo kwaye wawazi amandla awo angummangaliso. Xa kungoku uyayicekisa le nyaniso, ngoko ke kucacile ukuba kutheni iingqondo zethu zisiba ndindisholo neentliziyo zethu zidakumba xa sikhetha ukungaweva size siwazise amazwi kaThixo kubomi bethu bemihla ngemihla.

`Umpuntsheza okanye awuzinzanga kangakanani kubomi bakho bonqulo? Impendulo yakho Meyiikuxelele ntoni ngokwenza iinguqu ezithile, ukuba kuyadingeka?`
