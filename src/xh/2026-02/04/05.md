---
title:  Amabango EBhayibhile
date:   22/04/2026
---

Yintoni enokutshintsha ekhayeni lakho xa ububhenela eBhayibhileni xa ujamelene nezigqibo ezikhulu, ingxaki kubudlelane, okanye umceli-mngeni? Yintoni enokutshintsha kwindawo osebenza kuyo okanye ibandla xa amazwi aseBhayibhileni ngokwenene athi abeyindlela yokujonga (sisilanga-ilens) abantu abajonga ngaso ihlabathi baze bakhethe ukuphila ngawo?

Ababhali beBhayibhile babeyazi indlela axabiseke ngayo amazwi eBhayibhile. Ayikho enye incwadi enokuthetha nobomi bakho njengoko enokwenza la mazwi. La mazwi anokuhlala ephepheni kwiBhayibhile yakho, kodwa ke ungawagcina njani entliziyweni yakho?

`Lithini icebiso likaDavide kwiNdumiso 119:11, kwaye ungalilandela njani? (Bona kanjalo kumaHeb. 4:12.)`

Elinye lamabango ewenzayo iBhayibhile ngayo buqu lifumaneka kumaHebhere 4:12. Ikrele elintlangothi-mbini linamandla libukhali, kodwa iBhayibhile inokwenza oko izixhobo zobuntu zingenako ukukwenzela umphefumlo womntu. IBhayibhile izichaza ngokwayo njengephilileyo. Mhlawumbu uyazibuza ukuba oku kungenzeka njani, ngenxa yokuba yaabhalwa kumawaka eminyaka eyaadlulayo, kodwa uYesu wathi, “ ‘Amazwi la ndiwathethayo kuni mna angumoya, abubomi’ ” (Yohane 6:63). Ukuba intliziyo yakho yaphukile okanye ubomi bakho buyaphasalaka, uThixo anganako ukuthetha amazwi aKhe kwilizwe okulo aze aziguqule izinto. ITestamente eNdala ikwachaza amazwi kaThixo njengasebenza ngeyona ndlela hayi ame ngxi okanye angakhiyo (bona kuIsa. 55:11). Xa uDavide wayekhangela ngemva kwifuthe lamazwi kaThixo ebomini bakhe, yena waabhala, “Yiyo le intuthuzelo yam ekucinezelekeni kwam, yokuba intetho yakho indiphilisile” (Ndum. 119:50).

Mhlawumbi ukhe wanamava okuthwaxwa yindlala emandla ngathuba lithile ebomini bakho, okanye mhlawumbi ukhe wazila okanye waphila ngcathu. Ingaba ukutya akunancasa imnandi emva kokulamba? Kwimeko yakwamoya, iBhayibhile ikukutya kwimiphefumlo yethu.

Ukuba umphefumlo wakho uligobhoza yaye ulambile, nqika iLizwi eliphilileyo. Funda kuYeremiya 15:16, 1 Peter 2:2, noMateyu 4:4. Amazwi kaThixo anencasa emnandi engqondweni nasentliziyweni, yaye xa siwafunda, ayakusihluthisa aze asixhase njengoko kuthenjisiwe.

Amazwi aseBhayibhileni yethu aphume kuThixo ngokwaKhe. UThixo wawathumelela thina nqo kwakunye nawuphina umntu ongomnye oye wafuna Yena. Xa siwafunda ngentliziyo enomthandazo, evulekileyo, loo mazwi soze abe yilahleko.

`Zeziphi iinzima ezikhoyo ebomini bakho ngoku eziphendulayo iBhayibhile? Kutheni le nto kungafuneki uvumele umna ukuba akuthintele ekulandeleni oko ikuthethayo kuwe?`
