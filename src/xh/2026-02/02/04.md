---
title:  UThixo Uluthando
date:   07/04/2026
---

Uthando mhlawumbi lelona gama liqhelekileyo lisetyenziswa ngamaKristu ukuchaza isimilo sikaThixo. Oku kunokwenzeka ngenxa yentetho yokuzazisa engoThixo ku-1 Yohane 4:8, ethi, “uThixo uluthando.” UYohane akazange athi, “uThixo uyathanda,” koko endaweni yoko, “uThixo uluthando.” Uthando sisimilo saKhe, ingxam kanye yalowo anguye Yena.

Kubantu abaninzi, umfanekiso wabo ngoThixo uvumbuluka kwinkcazelo yabo yobuntu engethado, esoloko itenxile yaye ingagqibelelanga. Endaweni yalo nto, eyona nkcazelo kanye yethu yothando Meyibunjwe bubuyena bukaThixo kwakunye noko akutyhilayo ngaYe kwiLizwi laKhe eliphefumlelweyo.

`Uchaza ntoni kuthi u-1 Yohane 4:7-19 ngothando?`

Uthando lukaThixo lugqibelele, lukhululekile, kwaye lunxulumana ngokunzulu, njengoko kutyhiliwe kwisimemo esiphindaphindiweyo “sokuhlala” kuYe ku-1 Yohane, kuba “sikwazile thina, sakholwa kukusithanda kukaThixo. UThixo uluthando; lowo uhleli eluthandweni uhleli kuye uThixo, noThixo uhleli kuye” (1 Yohane 4:16).

UThixo uluthando, kwaye Yena wasidala ngokomfanekiselo waKhe (Gen. 1:27) ukuba sithande silangazelele uthando. KwisiHebhere, igama elinye elingundoqo lothando nguhesed. Oku kuchaza umnqophiso kaThixo wothando ngakuluntu, elibandakanya iimpawu zentembeko, ukukhusela, ukuzinza, kwakunye nokuthantamisa.

Iilwimi zamandulo zesiHebhere nesiGrike zisebenzisa intlaninge yamagama ohlukeneyo ukuchaza uThixo, amagama ashicilela intsingiselo yokukhanyisa kwiinkalo ezohlukeneyo zesimilo esihle sikaThixo. Nantsi imizekelo nje emibini:

- Adonai: INkosi yabo bonke, elawula ngonaphakade, ngokubhekiselele emnqophisweni (Gen. 15:2, Abagwe. 6:15, Mal. 1:6, Ndum. 97:5).
- Yahweh-Yireh: UYehova wozibonela (Gen. 22:13, 14).

Ekugqibeleni, eyona nkcazelo inkulu yothando lukaThixo ityhilwe ngesipho soNyana waKhe kweli hlabathi (Yohane 3:16) owaafela aboni (Rom. 5:8). UThixo wayenako ukukunkonya oku kuluntu, kodwa noko kunjalo ngenxa yothando lwaKhe olunesisa, olugqibeleleyo, olucingela abanye ngokumandla, uThixo waathumela uYesu emhlabeni ukuze sikwazi ukukhetha ngokukhululekileyo ukusabela kuthando lwaKhe, olutyhilwe kukufa kwaKhe okungenelelayo endaweni yethu. Akanelanga nje uYesu kukuvala umsantsa isono esiwenzileyo phakathi kwethu noThixo (Isa. 59:1, 2), waaphilela ukusibonisa isimilo sikaThixo esigqibeleleyo sothando (Yohane 14:9, Heb. 1:3) kwakunye nokutsalela abantu bonke kuYe (Yohane 12:32).

`Intlaninge yamagama kaThixo ashicilela ubungcwele baKhe nothando kumongo wawo. Funda kuma-1 Korinte 13:4-8, yaye kwimeko nganye, fakela kwigama “uthando” “uThixo.” Oku kuwandisa njani umnqondo wakho ngesimilo sikaThixo? Ukuba ubuzafakela igama lakho apho ithi “uthando,” ibiyakungena kakuhle njani kuwe?`
