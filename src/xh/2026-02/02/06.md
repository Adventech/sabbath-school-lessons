---
title:  Imanuweli, uThixo Unathi
date:   09/04/2026
---

Ukuba ubujonge ukwabelana nomntu ongengomKristu ngenkcazelo eseBhayibhileni malunga nesimilo sikaThixo, ubuzakubhekisa ngaphi?

Eyona mpendulo ingcono, eneneni, iyakuba nguYesu. IBhayibhile ithi uYesu akabonakalalisi kuphela uThixo koko utyhila uThixo. Zininzi izicatshulwa zeBhayibhile ezichaza oku, kodwa eyodwa ekwenzayo oko ngokulula nguYohane 14:9. Apha, uYesu uthi, “ ‘Lowo undibonileyo mna umbonile uBawo.’ ” Ukuze sazi ngcono ngento ayiyo uThixo, kufuneka sijonge kuYesu-amazwi aKhe, izenzo zaKhe, ubume baKhe, kwakunye nothando lwaKhe olukhulu ngakuluntu ekufeni nasekuvekeni kwaKhe.

Uthando nenkathalo kaBawo idandalaziswe kakhulu ngokuselubala kuNyana waKhe, uYesu. Ubuhle beBhayibhile kukuba uThixo usinike iinkalo ezine eziteketeke ngobomi bukaYesu ukuze sibe nomfanekiso opheleleyo walowo anguYe. KuMateyu (obhalwe ngumYuda, ujoliswe kumaYuda), sibona uYesu njengoMesiya obelindwe kwantlandlolo ozalisekise oko kwakuthenjisiwe. KuMarko, sibona uYesu ephila ubomi obumanxadanxada bokusebenza nokuzenz’ idini. Yena wayesoloko ecinga ngabanye yaye esoloko esabela kwintando kaYise waKhe. KuLuka, singafunda ngendlela uYesu aweva ngayo, kubuntu baKhe obugqibeleleyo nemfesane, kwaye sinokuyifunda le ngxelo ukuze sibe nengqiniseko yokuba oko sikufundayo kuyinene (Luka 1:3, 4). KuYohane, sibona uNyana kaThixo oze esenyameni kwaye simenyelwa ekukholelweni ukuba uYesu unguYe lo athi unguYe, ukuze ubomi bethu basemoyeni bube nokuvuselelwa. Nangona zonke eziGospile zine ziphonononga umhlaba omnye, “azivezi izinto phantse ngohlobo olunye. Mbhali ngamnye unamava akhe eyedwa, kwaye okukwahluka kuphangalalisa kwendelise ulwazi oluvezwayo ukufikelela kwizidingo zeengqondo eziyingxubevange.” —Ellen G. White manuscript 105, 1900. Yeyiphi iGospile oyifunde kakhulu kutsha nje?

`KuMateyu 1:23, igama elitsolileyo linikezelwa kuYesu. Kutheni le nto oku kubalulekile ekuqondeni isimilo sikaThixo. Funda kuMateyu 28:20, ugxile kwisigaba sokugqibela sale ndima. Uqaphela ntoni?`

Sichukumise kuphela umphezulu wesi sihloko sikhulu, isimilo sikaThixo. UThixo mkhulu yaye uyamangalisa kakhulu kunokuba sicinga, kwaye siyakufunda unampela ngaYe kunaphakade.

`UThixo ufanelwe kukubonga kwethu ngenxa yalowo anguYe kwakunye noko akwenzileyo Yena nasakwenzayo ebomini bethu. Thatha ixeshana ngoku wenze umthandazo wokubonga uThixo kuba engulo anguYe. Litsolise malunga noko iBhayibhile ikuxelela kona ngoThixo. (Umzekelo, “Enkosi kuWe, Thixo, ngokuba_____, njengoko Wena undixelele ku _____.”)`
