---
title:  UThixo Endalweni
date:   08/04/2026
---

Mhlawumbi uwazi ngentloko amazwi okuqala eBhayibhileni: “Ekuqalekeni uThixo.” KwisiHebhere, igama elimele uThixo apha nguElohim. Nangona eli gama linokusetyenziswa xa uthetha “ngoothixo,” xa lisingisele kulo Thixo umnye unyanisekileyo, lichaza uMdali onobungangamsha, onamandla onke ngokuphathelele kwindalo yonke; uThixo otshatsheleyo ongaphaya kwengqiqo yethu kodwa elawula zonke izinto. Unamandla ngeyona ndlela kangangokuba xa ethetha Yena, into iyadaleka nje ngelizwi laKhe.

Kodwa kwisahluko esilandelayo, uGenesis 2, kuvela igama elahlukileyo ngoThixo: Yahweh. Eli gama liqhagamshela kuElohim (Yahweh Elohim), kwalo Thixo umnye unamandla onke, unobungangamsha; kodwa igama uYahweh elikhethekileyo lalo Thixo umnye uyinyaniso, elisoloko lisetyenziswa ukugxininisa ukuba uThixo unguThixo womnqophiso, ononxulumano oluthandayo nabantu baKhe abadaliweyo.

`Thelekisa iinkcazelo zikaThixo kwiGenesis 1:1 ? nakwiGenesis 2:7. Uqaphela ntoni`

Apha kwiGenesis 2:7, sinokuba nombono kaThixo eguqa ukuze abumbe umntu wokuqala ngothulu lomhlaba ngezandla zaKhe. “UYehova uThixo wambumba umntu ngothuli lwasemhlabeni, wamphefumlela emathatheni akhe impefulelo yobomi; umntu ke waba ngumphefumlo ophilileyo.” Lo nguThixo osondela ngeyona ndlela-osondela ngeyona ndlela kangangokuba Yena aphefumlele emathatheni ka-Adam impefumlo yobomi. Eli gama, uYahweh, lizoba umfanekiso kaThixo osondele ngakumbi, kodwa uMoses usebenzisa omabini la magama kwizahluko ezibini zokuqala zeBhayibhile ukudandalazisa ezi mpawu zimbini ngoThixo kuthi.

Indlela ekhwankqisa ngayo! Sibona apha ukutshatshela kukaThixo kuthi njengoElohim, kwakunye nobukho baKhe, ukusondela kwaKhe kuthi, njengoYahweh. Indlela ekulunge ngayo kuthi ukuba sicinge ngazo zombini ezinkalo zesimilo sikaThixo: ulawulo lwaKhe jikelele kwinto yonke kwakunye nokusondela kwaKhe kuthi. Njengokuba waatshoyo uPawulos kwabaseAthene eAreyopago: “ ‘nakuba ingekude kuthi sonke ngabanye. Kuba kuyo sihleli, siyashukuma, sikho’ ” (Zenzo 17:27, 28).

Kubalulekile ukuba siqhubeke ukufuna umfanekiso ocacileyo, olinganayo kaThixo ngokusekelezelwe koko iBhayibhile isixelela kona ngesimilo sikaThixo ukuze sikhule kubudlelane kunye naYe. Yiyo le nto kubalulekile ukufunda zonke iindawo zeBhayibhile endaweni yokugxila kuphela kwisiqwenga esinye. Ngokwenene, ngokuya sifunda ngesimilo sikaThixo, kokukona siyakufunda ukumthanda Yena.

`Funda xa uElihu edandalazisa ezinye zeempawu zikaThixo kuYobhi 36:24-33 naku Yobhi 37. Emva koko ufunde isibhengezo sikaThixo sokuba namandl’ onke kuYobhi 38 no-39. Ezi zicatshulwa zityhila ntoni kuthi ngoThixo?`
