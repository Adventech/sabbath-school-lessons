---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   10/04/2026
---

UThixo ubiza abantu baKhe ukuba babonise isimilo saKhe kodwa, ukwenza oku, sidinga ukumazi Yena ngokunokwethu. Eyona ndlela ingcono yokumbona ngokucacileyo Yena, nangona amehlo ethu anesono esoloko engaziqondi kakuhle iindlela zaKhe ezingcwele nezigqibeleleyo, kungokuphengulula iLizwi laKhe, iBhayibhile.

“Lonke uthando lobuzali olutyhutyhe ukusuka kwisizukulwana ukuya kwesinye ngejelo leentliziyo zabantu, yonke imithombo yobubele eye yavuleka kwimiphefumlo yabantu, ifane nje nomlanjana omncinci kulwandlekazi olungenamda xa ithelekiswa nothando lukaThixo olungaphele-ndawo, olungapheliyo. Ulwimi alunakuluchaza; usiba alunakuluzoba. Usengacamngca ngalo imihla ngemihla ebomini bakho; usengaziphengulula iziBhalo ngenyameko ukuze uluqonde; usengabiza onke amandla nesakhono uThixo akunike sona, ngelinge lokuqonda uthando nemfesane kaBawo osemazulwini; kodwa noko kunjalo ngapha koko kusekho okungaphelendawo. Usengafunda ngolo thando kangangeenkulungwane; noko kunjalo awunakubuqonda ubude nobubanzi, ubunzulu nokuphakama, kothando lukaThixo ekunikeleni uNyana waKhe ukuba afele ihlabathi. Unaphakade ngokwakhe akanakulutyhila ngokupheleleyo. Kodwa ngokuya sifunda iBhayibhile size sicamngce ngobomi bukaYesu necebo lenkululo, le mixholo itshatsheleyo iyakuya ityhileka kwingqiqo yethu ngakumbi nangakumbi.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 740.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Xa uqwalasela iimpawu zikaThixo ozifundileyo kule veki, loluphi olube nefuthe ngamandla ekuqondeni kwakho uThixo?`

`Zeziphi ezinye iimpawu zikaThixo ongazifunda ukwendelisela nokuqinisa ubudlelane bakho noThixo?`

`Kunye nelungu losapho okanye umhlobo, funda okanye umamele Amanyathelo okuya kuKristu (Steps to Christ), isahluko 1, nize nisixoxe kunye. Mibono mini emitsha engesimilo sikaThixo kunye noYesu esenze esi sahluko ukuba ucinge ngaso?`

`Uninzi lwabantu lunomfanekiso ogqwethekileyo ngoThixo, lowo uYesu weza kuwubuyisela. Ungenza ntoni ukwabelana ngomfanekiso ocacileyo nochanekileyo ngesimilo sikaThixo kwabo bakumgangatho wakho wempembelelo?`

`Qwalasela kwakhona oko ukufunde kwisifundo sangoMvulo. Nangona ngokuselubala sinesono yaye singekho ngcwele, iBhayibhile kanjalo ikhupha iintetho ezicacileyo ezingabantu bakaThixo amabaphile ubomi obungcwele. Funda ku-1 Peter 1:13-16, amaRoma 6:22, namaHebhere 12:14. UThixo ungcwele kwaye uyasimema ukuba sibe ngcwele. Kodwa kuthetha ukuthini ngokwenene ukuphila ubomi obungcwele?`

**Isishwankathelo:** Ukusuka ekuqalekeni kweNdalo, uThixo ubenqwenela ukuba kubudlelane obuqhogeneyo kunye nathi. Nangona umqondo wethu wesimilo saKhe ilichele lohlaselo lukaSathana, uThixo Yena uzityhila kuthi ngokucacileyo ngeLizwi laKhe kwakunye nangobomi boNyana waKhe, uYesu. Ekugqibeleni, ukuba nomfanekiso ocacileyo, omhle kaThixo kubalulekile ukuba sifuna ukwendelisela ubudlelane bethu kunye naYe.
