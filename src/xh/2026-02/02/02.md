---
title:  Umfanekiso Ocace gcaa kaThixo
date:   05/04/2026
---

IBhayibhile ithi thaca owona mfanekiso uyinyani, ucacileyo nothe gqolo ngoThixo. IBhayibhile iphela izama ukutyhila lo mdiyadiya ungabonakaliyo uphakathi kwelizwe lethu elibonakalayo kunye nelo lingabonakaliyo; ukusibonisa apho sivela khona nalapho siya khona; ize, ekugqibeleni, nokusibonisa lowo uselulawulweni kwanokuba unjani Yena.

Ukusuka kwiGenesis ukuya kwiSiTyhilelo, sifunda ngoThixo omnye oyinyaniso, ozazisayo Yena kuthi ngeBhayibhile nangoYesu Kristu, uThixo oze esenyameni. Singafunda ngokuba namandl’ onke kukaThixo (Yob 1:12), ukwazi konke kwaKhe, imvelo yaKhe eyazi kwanto (Isa. 46:9, 10), ubulungisa baKhe (Isa. 30:18), inceba yaKhe (Dut. 7:9), ububele nokuzeka kade umsindo kwaKhe kuthi (Rom. 2:4), ubulumko baKhe (1 Kor. 2:7), ubabalo lwaKhe (2 Kor. 12:9), uxolelo lwaKhe (Mat. 6:14), intando yaKhe ngobomi bethu (Yer. 29:11), amandla aKhe okoyisa ukufa (Yohane 11:25), ukuba ngukumkani kwaKhe (Ndum. 47:8), imvelo yaKhe engunaphakade (Dut. 33:27), kwakunye nentlaninge yeempawu ezisinika intaphane yezizathu zokumthanda kwakunye nokuba nobudlelane obumileyo kunye naYe. Ngokuya sisazi ngoThixo kwakunye nokuba unjani Yena, kokukona siyakumthanda Yena size silangazelele ubudlelane obuqhogeneyo nobumileyo kunye naYe.

Yaba nguKhwezi owaqala wathandabuza isimilo sikaThixo. Iintandabuzo zakhe zokuba ungubani uThixo ekugqibeleni zaakhokelela kwelona dabi likhulu kwimbali yelizwe lethu. Ukususela ngeloo xesha, “kusisifundo sikaSathana esithe gqolo ukugcina iingqondo zabantu zithe phithi zezo zinto ziyakubathintela ekuzuzeni ulwazi ngoThixo.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 740. USathana akayikhathalele eyokuba sinohlobo olunjani lomfanekiso kaThixo (uthixo omnye nendalo, uphiko-uthixo, uthixo ongungantweni, njalo njalo), ukuba nje wona awuchanekanga.

`Funda kwiGenesis 3:1-5. Wawu yintoni umgomo kaSathana kwincoko yakhe noEva? Mposiso zini awazixelela uEva ngesimilo sikaThixo?`

Ekugqibeleni, umyalezo kaSathana kuEva yaba ngulo: uThixo uneemfihlo azigushileyo kuni. UThixo akafuni oko kungcono ngani. Awunako ukumthemba Yena. UEllen White unaba koku xa esithi, “Kwasekusungulweni kwemfazwe enkulu ibe yinjongo kaSathana ukugqwetha isimilo sikaThixo kwakunye nokuxhokonxa uvukelo ngakumthetho waKhe.”—Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 338.

`Isimilo sikaThixo sigqwethwe njani ehlabathini lethu? Okubaluleke ngakumbi, ingaba wena, ngamaxesha athile, usigqwethe njani isimilo saKhe kwabanye?`
