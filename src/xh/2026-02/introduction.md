### Ukukhula Kubudlelane noThixo

Nokuba ukhulele unguMlindi okanye usemtsha kwinkolo; nokuba ufunde intlaninge okanye ezimbalwa kwiziFundo zeBhayibhile zaBantu abaKhulu; kwaye, okokugqibela, nokuba uzifumana uphi ngokwasemoyeni namhlanje, isihloko sendlela yokukhula kubudlelane obunentsingiselo noThixo sibalulekile.

Lo mxholo uchaphazela yonke eminye. Umfanekiso onawo ngoThixo usenokuba kanti urhoqozekile okanye umfiliba; ukuba kunjalo, thandazela ukucacelwa okukhulu lo gama ufunda. Usengazibuza indlela yokuhlaziya ubomi bakho bonqulo (umthandazo nokufunda iBhayibhile), okanye usengaba kanti ucinga ezinye iinkalo ezichaphazela ubudlelane bakho noThixo, ezifana nendima yekratshi nentobeko, ukholo nolwazi, isono nomthetho kaThixo, inguquko noxolelo, indlela yokoyisa iinqaba nezithintelo, kwakunye nendlela yokukhuthaza abanye kuhambo lwabo noThixo.

Ubudlelane bakho noThixo bobona budlane bubaleke bodwa tshu. Sukuzilazila ukubakha, ukubuqinisa, ukubenza bubeluqilima kangangoko unako. Lingoku-hayi ngathuba lithile kwixa elizayo-ixesha lokusebenza obu budlelane, obuzakuchaphazela yonke enye into: umtshato wakho (ukuba uyabandakanyeka) ubuzali bakho (ukuba uyabandakanyeka), izihlobo zakho, izigqibo zakho zezimali, ukonwaba kwakho, amaphupha akho . . . kwakunye, eneneni, nengomso lakho likanaphakade.

Ngenxa yesi sihloko-eso sokuba uThixo ulangazelela ukuba kubudlelane noluntusigubungela yonke iBhayibhile, zininzi iinkalo ezohlukeneyo, amabali, kwakunye nezicatshulwa zeBhayibhile engezikhethiwe ukufundisa esi sihloko sibaluleke kangaka. Ngenxa yobume beSifundo seBhayibhile saBantu aBakhulu, singalandela kuphela inani eliqingqekileyo.

Nokuba ubudlelane bakho noThixo bunjani na namhlanje, ezi zifundo zibhalwe kucingwa wena engqondweni. Ekugqibeleni, umnqweno kukuba ezi zifundo zifutshane, zitsolileyo zingavuselela uthando lwakho nokuzibophelela kuYesu Kristu ekumfuneni kwakho ngokutsha kule kota.

Ngenxa yokuba ubume besi sihloko bungobudlelane, esi Sifundo seBhayibhile saBantu aBakhulu sifundeka ngokwahlukileyo kancinci kunezinye zangaphambili. Izifundo zibhalwe ngohlobo lwesiqu ngakumbi kuba zingoThixo osisiqu onqwenela ukwazi wena buqu.

U-Elleni G. White uthi “ubomi ubuthe gqolo kuKristu ukudandalazisa uhambo ngummangaliso omkhulu.” The Desire of Ages, p. 407. IBhayibhile isebenzisa isifaniso sogqatso ukudandalazisa uhambo lobomi noThixo. Umvuzo wethu sisithsaba esingabuniyo (1 Kor. 9:24, 25) kwakunye nobomi esingabuniyo (1 Kor. obungunaphakade noThixo. Ugqatso lwethu lakwamoya kukubaleka umgama omde, hayi ukugqotsa. Kungakho amaxesha esiyekayo ukubaleka okanye nkqu siwe sicambalale ngobuso. Kuyenzeka oko, kwaye xa kusenzeka, noThixo. sivela siphakame size siqhubekeke ukubheka phambili. Kufuneka sihlale endleleni, naphakathi kwezilingo neenzima ezizayo ngokuqinisekileyo (Heb. 12:4-11). Kwaye asilubaleki sedwa olu gqatso; ezinye iimbaleki ezithanda uYesu neLizwi laKhe zibaleka kunye nathi. Okubaluleka ngakumbi, uYesu waathembisa ngokusinika uMncedi. “ ‘Mna ndiya kucela kuBawo, aze aninike omnye uMthetheleli, ukuze ahlale nani ngonaphakade: uMoya wenyaniso, lowo lingenako ihlabathi ukumamkela, ngokuba lingamboni, lingamazi nokumazi. Nina ke niyamazi; ngokuba uhleli nani, kanjalo uya kuba kuni.’ ” (Yohane 14:16, 17).

Asilubaleki sedwa ugqatso-uMthetheleli akakho kunye nathi nje kuphela koko uhlala kuthi ukuze asomeleze asixhase lo gama sibaleka kwaye sijolise amehlo ethu “kuYesu, imbangi nomgqibelelisi wokholo lwethu” (Heb. 12:2).

Njengokuba ndibhala, ndiyathandaza ukuba uMoya oyiNgcwele asebenze kuthi njengeziqu nanjebandla likazwe-lonke ukusisondeza kuThixo ngaphezu kunakuqala. Kuba ngokuqinisekileyo akunakubakho nto ibaluleke ukogqitha ukuba nobudlelane obuluqilima noThixo.

Ngoko ke, masifunde kunye, ukufunda, ukuthanda, kwakunye nokuhlala kuYe.
