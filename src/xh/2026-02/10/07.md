---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   05/06/2026
---

IBhayibhile isoloko isebenzisa izifaniso zolimo ukuchaza imeko yethu yasemoyeni. UHoseya 10:12 ngumzekelo omnye obamba oko besikushukuxa kule veki:

“Zihlwayeleleni, ukuba kuvele ubulungisa;\
Vunani ngokwenceba;\
Zikhubeleleni umkhubelo,\
Ekubeni ilixesha lokumquqela uYehova,\
ade afike aninisele ubulungisa”.

Siyahlwayela, sivune, siqwabulule umhlaba olukhuni, size sifune uThixo ukuze sisondele kuYe. Umhlaba weentliziyo zethu mawulungiswe ulungele imvula (Moya oyiNgcwele) ukuba iwe. UThixo unokusinika umnqweno wokulungisa umhlaba, kodwa, ekugqibeleni, ubudlelane noThixo yintsebenziswano (bona Fil. 2:12, 13). Kufuneka sijongise iintloko zethu kuYe, siqhagamshelane naYe, size sibambelele kuYe. Emva koko uzakusebenza Yena ukwenza yonke enye into.

Umzekelo omnye omkhulu wento ethethwa kukunamathela kuThixo unokufumaneka kwezi ndima: “Amehlo enu akubonile oko wakwenzayo uYehova kuBhahali-pehore; ngokuba bonke abo babemlandela uBhahali-pehore, uYehova uThixo wakho ubatshabalalisile phakathi kwakho. Ke nina nanamathelayo kuYeova uThixo wenu, niphilile nonke namhla” (Dut. 4:3, 4).

**Imibuzo Yenkumbulo:**

`“Ungasingenisi ekulingweni, sihlangule enkohlakalweni” (Mat. 6:13). UYesu ngokutsolileyo wabafundisa abafundi baKhe ukuba bathandaze ngale ndlela, kodwa ingaba siyawugcina le ngcamango kwimithandazo yethu yamihla le? Uluthandazela ngokuthe gqolo kangakanani ukhuselo ngakwisihendo nesono?`

`Ungasichaza njani isipho sexabiso sengubo kaKristu yobulunigsa kumntu ongengomKristu okanye olikholwa elitsha?`

`Ingubo yobulungisa kaKristu inxibelelana njani nesigidimi somnquba, esingokuxolela nokuhlambulula kukaThixo umoni oguqukayo xa sisonke? Ubuqonda kakuhle kangakanani ubuhle nokutyeba kwesi sigidimi?`

**Isishwankathelo:** Ukuchaza izono zethu ngokusabela kwizixhokosho zikaMoya oyiNgcwele nokunikela umna ngeliguqukayo zizinto ezibalulekileyo ekubeni nobudlelane obuphilileyo noThixo. Ukwazi ukuba sixolelwe sogqunywa ngokupheleleyo yingubo kaYesu yobulungisa ngawona mava aguqulayo emntwini. Asivi nje kuphela ubunzima besono bususiwe, koko sikwava uthando lukaThixo lusirhangqile lo gama sisondezwa kufutshane naYe. Oku kusibophelela kuThixo, kusiqinise ngokwasemoyeni, kuze kusinyanzele ukuba simthande Yena ngesuntswana ngalinye lobuntu bethu.
