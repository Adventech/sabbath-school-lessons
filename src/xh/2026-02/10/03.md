---
title:  Izixhokonxo ZikaMoya OyiNgcwele
date:   01/06/2026
---

Ngethuba awayecinga ngomgama ophakathi kwakhe nenkosikazi yakhe, wayesazi ukuba wenza kakubi. Ebengenabubele kwaye engqwabalala kwaye ethethe izinto aye wazisola ngazo. Noko kunjalo ingcamango yakhe elandelayo waba kukuba _Ebengalifanelanga na, nkqu nokuba kukancinci?_

Ingaba esi sigaba sokucinga siqhelekile kuwe? Kulula ukuphethuka kwisimo sokuzisola uye kukuzigwebela kweengcinga nezenzo. Akusoloko kulula “Ndiyaxolisa . . .” xa sigqwidizile, noko kunjalo oku kusemqoka ngeyona ndlela ekwakheni ngokutsha okanye ekuqiniseni nabuphi ubudlelane.

Kukwanjalo phakathi kwethu noThixo. UMoya oyiNgcwele usoloko uxhokonxa iingqondo zethu kwizono esizenzayo. Iintliziyo zethu ziyachukumiseka ngenxa yezizixhokonxo, kodwa kubalula ukubhekelisela pha ela lizwi lizolile, lincinci lo gama sithethelela unobangela woba senze ngohlobo oluthile. Enye yeendima zikaMoya oyiNgcwele “ ‘kukulohlwaya ihlabathi ngenxa yesono’ ” (Yohane 16:7, 8). Isipho esimangalisa ngolo hlobo esivela kuThixo (Luka 11:13), wendlela esizidinga ngayo ezo zohlwayo ukungciba umgama onokunyubeleza kuhambo lwethu kunye naYe!

`Funda uHoseya 6. Yintoni oyiqaphela ngokutsolileyo apha ngendlela uThixo azichaza ngayo Yena kwisibongozo saKhe senguquko?`

Qwalasela indima kaMoya oyiNgcwele kwisigaba sokuhlonyelwa kwethu kwakhona eMdiliyeni (Yohane 15:4). “Sisoloko sibuhlungu ngenxa yokuba izenzo zethu ezingendawo zizisa imiphumela ejavujavu kwiziqu zethu; kodwa oku akuyiyo inguquko. Ukuzohlwaya kwenene ngenxa yesono sisiphumo sokusebenza kukaMoya oyiNgcwele. UMoya utyhila ukungabi nambulelo kwentliziyo eye yadelela yahlungisa uMsindisi, uze usizise ekuzohlwayeni ezinyaweni zomnqamlezo. Ngesono ngasinye uYesu uhlatywa ngokutsha; . . . sibe buhlungu ngenxa yezono ezizise intuthumbo kuYe. Ukuba buhlungu okunjalo kuyakukhokelela ekushiyweni kwesono.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 300.

Inyaniso kukuba, asinakukhula kubudlelane bethu noThixo xa isono esityunjiweyo nesikokosiweyo simi phakathi kwethu naYe. Asinakugqibelela, kodwa singanakokwaye kufuneka-siguquke kwizono zethu xa uMoya oyiNgcwele esikhumbuza ngazo (Efe. 4:30).

`Ugqibele nini ukuva ukukhalinyelwa okanye ukubizelwa enguqukweni? Usabele njani? Chitha ixesha elithile kanye ngoku ekuthandazeni, ucele uThixo ukuba athambise intliziyo yakho avule nendlebe zakho kwilizwi laKhe eliseLizwini laKhe kule veki?`
