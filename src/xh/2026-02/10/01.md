### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Isa. 61:10; Hoseya 6; Zenzo 3:18, 19; Eksod. 34:1–10; Roma 6:23; Mat. 22:1–14.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ukuba sithi sizivume izono zethu, uthembekile, ulilungisa, ukuba asixolele izono zethu, asihlambulule kuko konke ukungalungisi” (1 Yohane 1:9).

ILizwe lesiThembiso lalikhangeleka likude kakhulu kumaSirayeli, awayehleli ngaphantsi komqulu welifu entlango/ ethafeni. UMoses wayenyukele kubumnyama obushinyeneyo, obabugqume incopho yentaba kwiintsuku eziliqela ngaphambili. Ngokuqinisekileyo inkokeli yabo ibhubhile xa kungoku, bacinga ngolo hlobo, ukuba asiyondlala mhlawumbi ngoko ngumlilo olenyayo encochoyini yayo. Indibandiba ephakathi kwabo yaziva ingenasizinzo namonde, ilungele ukubheka phambili kwilizwe elibaleka amasi nobusi. Nangona aba bantu banye, kwintsuku nje ezimbalwa ezingaphambili, babenze umnqophiso ondilisekileyo kunye noThixo wokuba bazakumthobela Yena, babefuna umfanekiso abanokuwubona. Ngenxa yoko, bahlanganisana ngakwintente ka-Aaron bafuna ukuba abenzele isithixo. Esoyikisela ukhuseleko lwakhe, uAaron waavuma. KwiEksodus 32-34, sifunda indlela eli bali elicombuluka ngayo.

Le ngxelo libali nje elinye elisuka kwisiBhalo elisifundisa ngenguquko noxolelo, umxholo wesifundo sale veki. Gcina umxholo wendima yenkumbulo yale veki engqondweni yakho lo gama utyhutyha nesifundo sosuku ngalunye. Ewe, siyona, kodwa enkosi kuMnqamlezo necebo losindiso, usindiso lusoloko lukhona kumoni ovuma isono noguqukayo.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Juni 6._
