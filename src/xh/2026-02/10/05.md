---
title:  Ubabalo Olwaneleyo
date:   03/06/2026
---

Xa sibuva ubunzima besono sethu size sivumele uMoya oyiNgcwele ukuba usikhokelele ezinyaweni zomnqamlezo, kufuneka sicele uxolelo lukaThixo, sisazi ukuba “Unemfesane, enobabalo uYehova, uzeka kade umsindo, mkhulu ngenceba” (Ndum. 103:8). Kwale ndima inye yaathethwa nguThixo ngokwaKhe (Eksod. 34:6) emva kokuva uhlanga alunyulileyo limhlungisile Yena.

`Funda kwiEksodus 34:1-10. Nyaniso ni ebalulekileyo efumaneka apha?`

Into yokuba uYehova abe nemfesane nobabalo, azeke kade umsidno, abe mkhulu ngenceba ikwa kanjalo sesosizathu sinye esabangela uYesu afe emnqamlezweniukuze ubudlelane bethu noThixo bulungiswe kwakhona.

Kuxa sikulungele ukuqonda nokuvuma isono sethu; xa sisithi, O, Nkosi, ndim kwakhona . . . “ ‘Yiba netarhu kum mna moni!’ ” (Luka 18:13), athi uYesu-obekade esebenza ngaphakathi kuthi nangenxa yethu kunye noMoya oyiNgcwele nkqu nangaphambi kokuba simbize Yena-ubona ubunzima aze abususe kuthi. Imithwalo yethu iyasuswa eKalvari, yaye uYesu ngokuqinisekileyo ukufutshane kakhulu xa sisiya kuYe kwaye, nkqu kwangaphambili, uyasifuna njengoMalusi oLungileyo aze ame emnyango ankqonkqoze (Isityhi. 3:20). Masingahlaleli kude noMnqamlezo, sijonge uThixo sithe qelele. Masibalekele kuYesu size sinanise ngezono nemithwalo yethu ngenxa yobulungisa baKhe (Zek. 3:4).

**Ngokucothayo funda ezindima zilandelayo. Bhala ngawakho amazwi oko zikuxelela kona ngobabalo lukaThixo kuwe:**

“Kuba umvuzo wesono kukufa; ke sona isibabalo sikaThixo bubomi obungunaphakade, kuKristu Yesu iNkosi yethu” (Rom. 6:23).

“Ke apho sandayo isono, lwaphuphuma ngakumbi ubabalo; ukuze njengokuba isono saba nokulawula ngokufa, ngokunjalo nobabalo lube nokulawula ngobulungisa, luse ebomini obungunaphakade, ngaye uYesu Kristu, iNkosi yethu” (Rom. 5:20, 21).

“Ke yena uThixo uqondakalisa okwakhe ukusithanda ngokuthi, sakubon’ ukuba sisengaboni, uKristu asifele” (Rom. 5:8).
