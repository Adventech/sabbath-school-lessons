---
title:  Ingubo Yelona Xabiso Likhulu
date:   04/06/2026
---

Impahla entle isoloko ichaza abo bazizinhanha ngokwemigangatho yehlabathi. Abanye abantu bathi, “Ndinxiba ngolu hlobo ukuvakalisa ukuba ndingubani.” Kodwa ezulwini, yonke into engebubo ubudlelane bethu iyakudaka (Matt. 6:19–21). Ubuqu bethu mabuthandelwe kuYesu nengubo yaKhe yobulungisa egqibeleleyo.

`Funda umzekeliso okuMateyu 22:1-14 owabaliswa nguYesu ukudandalazisa oku. Miyalezo mini onokuyifumana kulo mzekeliso?`

UYesu wabiza indoda eyayingenayo ingubo “mhlobo,” yaye nangona ingazange ibe nampendulo, kumele ukuba babenobudlelane. Indoda leyo makube yayi nolwazi ngengubo kodwa yakhetha ukuba ingayinxibi. Isimilo sikaYesu sigqibelele asinachaphaza, kwaye unikezela oku kuthi “ukuba athiwe wambu ngelinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe” (Isityhi. 19:8), elingenabala, elingenamibimbi, nanye into enjalo” (Efe. 5:27).

Ilinen emhlophe “bubulungisa bukaKristu, isimilo saKhe esingenachaphaza, esiye ngokholo sidluliselwe kubo bonke abamamkelayo Yena njengoMsindisi weziqu zabo.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 310.

UAdam noEva babenxibe ingubo emhlophe yokukhanya okungaqaqambanga ngaphambi kokuba bone; emva kokuba bonile, baziqonda ukuba baze (Gen. 3:7). Emva koko uThixo wabeka endaweni yengubo yamagqabi omkhiwane eyenziwe nguAdam noEva ilaphu lezikhumba zezilwanyana. Kwabakho idini elaavelisa ingubo yabo. Kwangolo hlobo, samkela idini likaYesu ngokwamkela ingubo yaKhe yobulungisa. “Beze benentloni, baazama ukuvala isikhewu seengubo zasezulwini ngokuthunga kunye amagqabi omkhiwane ukuze bazogqume. . . . Akukho nto umntu anokuyiyila ukuthatha indawo yengubo yakhe yobumsulwa aphulukene nayo. Akukho ngubo yagqabi lamkhiwane, akukho mpahla yammi welizwe, inokunxitywa ngabo bahlala noKristu kunye neengelosi kwisidlo somsitho weMvana. Kuphela sisambatho uKristu ngokwaKhe abonelele ngaso esinokusenza sifaneleke ukuba sibonakale phambi koThixo. Esi sambatho, ingubo yobulungisa baKhe, uKristu uyakuyibeka phezu komphefumlo ngamnye oguqukayo, okholwayo.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 311.

`Yetyisa: Kufuneka imihla ngemihla sizambathise ngengubo kaYesu yobulungisa. Oku kuthetha ntoni kanye kanye, kwaye sikwenza njani oku?`
