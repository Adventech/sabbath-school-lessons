---
title:  UYobhi
date:   09/06/2026
---

Xa sicinga ngezithintelo eBhayibhileni, uYobhi mhlawumbi ngumntu othi qatha kuqala engqondweni. Akazange aphulukane nobutyebi bakhe bonke kuphela (Yobhi 1:14–17), koko kanjalo waaphulukana nabantwana bakhe (Yobhi 1:18, 19) nempilo yakhe (Yobhi 2:7). Inkosikazi yakhe emva koko yaazama ukumeyisela ekuthini aqalekise uThixo afe (Yobhi 2:9).

Emva kwethutyana, abahlobo abathathu beza ukuze bahlale noYobhi. Boothuswa ngeyona ndlela yinkangeleko yakhe kangangokuba bahlala naye, bengathethi, iintsuku ezisixhenxe (Yobhi 2:13). Ekugqibeleni, ekuthetheni kwabo, bazama ukunikezela ngezizathu zobuntu zokuba kutheni uYobhi ehlelwe lishwangusha elinjalo, kodwa ekwenzeni oko, boongeza ekubandezelekeni kukaYobhi bengaqondanga. Abahlobo abathathu bamsola, besithi makube unaso isono asigoxileyo ebomini bakhe ekufuneka eguquke kuso (Yobhi 8, 11, 15), de bathi, “ ‘Inene, injalo iminquba yabanobugqwetha, injalo indawo yongamaziyo uThixo’ ” (Yobhi 18:21).

`Waaphendula njani uYobhi? Funda kuYobhi 19:23–27 noYobhi 23:8–12.`

Nokuba zazilusizi kangakanani na iziganeko ezazimngqongile, kwaneyokuba yena wayengaziqondi, uYobhi waahlala ethembekile. Waabambelela nkqi. Akazange amsole uThixo okanye amqalekise. Endaweni yoko, xa kwakukho into ethi makasole uThixo, waavakalisa: “ ‘Ndaphuma ndize esizalweni sikama, ndiyakubuyela khona ndikwaze: uYehova wanika, ikwanguYehova othabathileyo; igama likaYehova malibongwe’ ” (Yobhi 1:21).

Nathi, aba, siphila esizikithini seli dabi. USathana usithwaxa ngentlungu, inkxwaleko, ilahleko, nenzima njengenxalenye yecebo lakhe lokutenxisa umfanekiso esinawo woThixo onothando. Kwixesha elinjalo, singasabela ngenye kwezimbini iindlela: simsole simbukule uThixo okanye sitshele kuYe ngamandla ethu onke. Nangona idabi livutha ngenxa zonke kuthi, kufuneka sikhumbule ukuba, xa sijonge kunaphakade, iinkathazo zethu zexeshana zizimbandezelo zomzuzwana (2 Kor. 4:16– 18). Kuninzi kakhulu okusemfanekisweni kunoko sikubonayo apha ngoku, kwaye omnye weyona micela-mngeni yekholwa kukuthemba uThixo nkqu nangawona maxesha entsunguzi yobumnyama. UThixo, ngeendlela ezininzi, ubutyhilile kuthi ubunyani bothando lwaKhe. Kufuneka sitshele kule nyaniso isemqoka-leyo yothando lukaThixo-nkqu naxa ngokwangoku singayiva.

`Ukuba usesizikithini sexesha elinzima kanye ngoku, balekela kuThixo.Thatha iBhayibhile nencwadi obhala kuyo uze ube kunye noThixo phandle endalweni. Shicilela phantsi amaRoma 5:3-5 uze wetyise imiyalezo eyohlukeneyo ekwesisicatshulwa, ukholelwa ukuba uthando nenkathalo kaThixo ngakuwe yeyona nto iqinisekileyo neyona izinzileyo ebomini bakho.`
