---
title:  Bona UYesu
date:   11/06/2026
---

Wakhe wanqwenela ukuba ubone uYesu xa uziva unxunguphele? Yiba nomfanekiso wakho kweli phupha?

“Yayingathi ndihleli kukuphelelwa lithemba okuxhwalekileyo, ubuso bam buphakathi kwezandla zam, ndicinga oku: Ukuba uYesu ebesemhlabeni, bendizakuya kuYe, ndiziphose ezinyaweni zaKhe, ndize ndimxelele zonke iinkxwaleko zam. Ebengazokundishiya, Yena ebezakuba nenceba kum, yaye kufuneka ndimthande ndimkhonze Yena maxa onke. Kanye ngelo xesha ucango lwavuleka, kwaza kwangena umntu okwisimo nenkangeleko entle. Wandijonga kalusizi waza wathi: ‘Uyafuna ukubona uYesu? Yena ulapha kwaye ungambona Yena ukuba uyanqwenela ukwenza njalo. Thatha yonke into onayo uze undilandele.’

“Ndeva oku ngochulumanco olungathethekiyo, ndaza ndaqokelela ngothakazelelo bonke ubugcwabalalana bezinto endinazo, sonke isihombiso esixabisekileyo, ndaza ndalandela umkhokeli wam. Wandikhokelela kwisinyuko esiphakamileyo nesikhangeleka sibuthathaka. Ndithe xa ndiqalayo ukunyuka amanqwanqwa, wandiyala ukuba ndigcine amehlo am ekhangele phezulu, hleze ndibe nesiyezi ndiwe. Abanye abaninzi ebebenyuka lomnqantsa bawile ngaphambi kokufika encotsheni.

“Ekugqibeleni saafika kwinqwanqwa lokugqibela saza sema phambi kocango. Apha ke umkhokeli wam wandiyalela ukuba ndishiye zonke izinto ebendize nazo. Ngomdla ndazibeka phantsi; waza wavula ucango wathi mandingene. Ngethutyana ndabe sendime phambi koYesu. Kwakungekho kubhidanisa loo nkangeleko intle. Loo mbonakaliso ibengezelayo yesisa nobungangamsha akekho omnye ebenoba nayo. Kusakube ukujonga kwaKhe kuthe ntsho kum, ndaayazi kwangoko ukuba Yena uyazana nemeko nganye yobomi bam kwakunye nazo zonke iingcinga zangaphakathi neemvakalelo.

“Ndazama ukuzifihla ekuqwalaselweni nguYe, ndiziva ndingenako ukumelana namehlo akhe agocagocayo, kodwa Yena wasondela kufutshane ngoncumo, waza, ebeke isandla saKhe phezu kwentloko yam, wathi: “Musa ukoyika.’ Isandi selizwi laKhe elimyoli saazalisa intliziyo yam ngemincili engazanga yayingcamla ngaphambili. Ndachulumance kakhulu ukuba ndithethe ilizwi, kodwa, ndonganyelwe yimincili engenambaliso, ndawa ezinyaweni zaKhe. Lo gama ndandithe tywa apho ngokungenakuncedakala, iziganeko zobuhle nozuko zathi thaa kum, kwaye kwakukhangeleka ngathi ndifile kukhuseleko noxolo lwezulu. Esidaleni amandla am abuya, ndaza ndaphakama. Amehlo anothando kaYesu ayesandithe ntsho, noncumo lwaKhe lwazalisa umphefumlo wam ngovuyo. Ubukho baKhe bandizalisa ngentlonelo engcwele nothando olungenakuxelwa. . . .

“Eli phupha landinika ithemba . . . [kwakunye] nokholo. . . . Baza ubuhle nokusulungeka kokukholosa ngoThixo kwaqalisa ukuthi chapha kumphefumlo wam osebumnyameni.”—Ellen G. White, Early Writings, pp. 79–81.

Phakathi kwezithintelo zobomi, sidinga ukugxila kuYesu kwakunye noko Yena akutyhilayo ngothando lukaThixo kuthi.

`Themba lini onokuzithathela lona buqu, kanye ngoku, koko kubhaliweyo kumaRoma 8:18, 28?`
