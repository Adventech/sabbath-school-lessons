---
title:  Indlela Eya E-Emawusi
date:   10/06/2026
---

Ibe nguphantsi-phezulu weeveki ezimbalwa kubafundi ababini xa babesetyisa ezingqondweni zabo ezinye zeziganeko neencoko abakhe bazingcamla. Ukungena ngoloyiso eYerusalem kwakunye nokuhlanjululwa kwetempile. IPasika kwigumbi eliphezulu. Imithandazo kaYesu eGetsemane. Ukungcatsha kukaYuda okunoqhushululu. Ukuxoxwa kwetyala, ukugculelwa, ukubethwa. Umzimba kaYesu omanxebanxeba ojinga emnqamlezweni kwakunye namazwi aKhe okugqibela ngaphambi kokuba Yena atsale umxhelo wokugqibela ngethuba isibhakabhaka sasemva kwemini sijika sisiba mnyama. Ukukrazuka kwekhuselo letempile okunesankxwe. Amangcwaba avulekileyo amalungisa. Ukuthulwa ngobunono kukaYesu emnqamlezweni kwanokumlalisa engcwabeni ngaphambi kweSabatha. Kwakunye nokudideka, ukutyhafa, nemibuzo esezingqondweni zabafundi. Bawubhude phi umcimbi?

Abafundi bakaYesu baaphoxeka, batyhafa, yaye badideka. Esi yaba sisona sithintelo simandla ebomini babo. Into ababengayiboni kukuba esi yayi sisigaba nje kwibali elitshatsheleyo lamaxesha onke. Ngethuba isibini sabo sasihamba sijongise e-Emawusi, uYesu wabonakala wahamba naso.

`Funda incoko kuLuka 24:13-27 uze ucinge ngezinkalo zimbini zohlukeneyo: abalandeli ababini, kwakunye noYesu.`

Esakube evuliwe amehlob abo, aba balandeli babini bakhawuleza ukusinga eYerusalem ukuya kwabelana ngezinto ezehle endleleni (Luka 24:33, 34). Uthe uYesu esakuza eme phakathi kwabo, banoloyiko. Qaphela imibuzo yaKhe kubo:

“ ‘Yini na ukuba nikhathazeke? Kungani na ke ukuba kunyuke izicamango ezintliziyweni zenu?’ ” (Luka 24:38).

Lo ikwa ngumyalezo kaYesu kuthi namhlanje. Amaxesha amaninzi, siyalibala ukuba uYesu uhamba ecaleni kwethu kwiintili zethu. Amaxesha amaninzi asimqondi Yena. Amaxesha amaninzi siyalibala ukuba kusekuninzi okusebalini. Amaxesha amaninzi siziva sikhathazekile size sivumele amathandabuzo avuke ezintliziyweni zethu, silibale ukuba uYesu ububambe nkqi ubomi bethu ezandleni zaKhe. Kwaye amaxesha amaninzi sicinga ukuba sazi ngcono kunokuba uYesu esazi malunga noko ngokwenene kwenzekayo ebomi bethu (Luka 24:18).

`IBhayibhile inikezela icebiso eliphucuke ngolo hlobo ngendlela thina njengamaKristu esinokusabela ngayo kwimingeni nezithintelo. Thabatha ixesha ufunda ezi zicatshulwa zifutshane: Rom. 8:28; Fil. 4:4–13; Yakobi 1:2–4, 12; naku- 2 Kor. 12:9, 10. Njengenxalenye yokufunda kwakho, bhala phantsi imiyalezo emithathu onokwabelana ngayo nomntu ojamelene nezithintelo kanye ngoku, ugcine engqondweni u-2 Korinte 1:4.`
