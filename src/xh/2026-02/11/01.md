### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Marko 4:35–41; Marko 5:21–34; Roma 5:3–5; Yobhi 19:23–27; Yobhi 23:8–12; Luka 24:13–27; Roma 8:18, 28.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Asikukuphela ke oko; sizingca nangeembandezelo ezi, sisazi ukuba imbandezelo isebenza unyamezelo; unyamezelo ke lusebenza ukucikideka; ukucikideka ke kusebenza ithemba; ithemba ke alidanisi, ngokuba uthando lukaThixo luthululelwe ezintliziyweni zethu ngoMoya oyiNgcwele, esamnikwayo” (Roma 5:3–5).

Ngarhatya luthile, xa ilanga lalisithela elundini, igqiyazana elithile lalisendleleni egodukayo xa kwakuvuthuza uqhwithela olumnyama tsiki. Waaqupha ukuhamba, esazi ukuba kwakusekho umgama ekufuneka ewukhathule. Iqabaza elinye le mvula laawela esidleleni, emva koko yalelinye, kwaza, ngaphambi kokuba aqonde, wamanzi toxo. Waaqalisa ukubaleka de walivula gengelele ucango lakokwabo, utata wakhe engxamile ukuya kuye esuka kwifestile engaphambili. Uthe xa ebhijela ingubo kuye, wambuza, “Ndikubone kanye ngoku, emvuleni. Bekutheni, qho xa kulenyeza umbane, uzuyeke ukubaleka ukuze ukhangele phezulu uncumile?”

“Oo, ndiye ndema ukuze ndikhangele phezulu,” watsho, “ngenxa yokuba uThixo ebethwebula umfanekiso wam!”

Ithini impendulo yakho xa iinqwithelo zobomi zifika okanye xa sinezithintelo ezithile kubudlelane bethu noThixo? Sithokombisa iintloko zethu lo gama imvula igalela emqolo kuthi okanye siyaqwanya, sisazi kwaye sithemba ukuba uThixo ukhona xa sibhekisa ubuso bethu kuYe?

Kule veki, sizakuphononoga ezinye zeempendulo esivamise ukuba nazo xa ubomi bungumcela-mngeni. Sizakuqwalasela indlela esingasebenzisa ngayo izithintelo zobomi ukuqinisa, hayi ukudodobalisa, obona budelane bethu bubalulekileyo.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Juni 13._
