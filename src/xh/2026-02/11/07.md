---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   12/06/2026
---

Kuxa sijamelene nemingeni yobomi esiye sidinge ngokumandla ukutshela kuThixo. Izihloko esizishukuxileyo kuyo yonke le kota zonke zinegalelo ekugcineni okanye ekuhlaziyeni uhambo oluqinileyo noThixo. Xa ujamelene nesithintelo esifana nomngeni wempilo, iinzima zezezimali, ukuqhawuka kumtshato, ukubhubha komntu osenyongweni kuwe, okanye omnye umthwalo okuhlutha uvuyo, qwalasela le mibuzo ilandelayo uze ukhangele emva kwizifundo ezifundiweyo ukuza kuthi gaa ngoku.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Siwuchaphazela njani nokuba sesiphi na isithintelo ojamelene naso, okanye okhe wajamelana naso, umfanekiso onawo ngoThixo? Ungasibona ngokucacileyo njani isimilo sikaThixo sokwenene?`

`Ugqibele nini ukuthandazela ukuba ilizwi likaThixo ebomini bakho libe namandla kunelo lotshaba? Khumbula ukuba isela (uSathana) lizele ukuba libe, libulale, lize litshabalalise, kodwa uThixo unikezela ngobomi obugqithisileyo (Yohane 10:10).`

`Ingaba intliziyo yakho ithobekile? Usathemba ukuba uThixo usenobunganga kwaye ekhokela ebomini bakho, phakathi kobunzima? Ukuba akunjalo, ungakufunda njani ukukholosa okuthobekileyo ekulungeni nothando lukaThixo ngokwakuwe siqu?`

`Ingaba uzigcina uzinzile eLizwini likaThixo yonke imihla? Cela uThixo ukuba akhwezele uthando lwakho lokuqala kuYe lo gama uthubeleza kumaxesha angumngeni.`

`Ugqibele nini ukubhenela kuThixo njengoMthuthuzeli noMcebisi wakho emthandazweni, ukholose ngelithi usigcinile isithembiso saKhe sokuba akasoze ukushiye okanye akulahle (Heb. 13:5)?`

`Ukuba ukholo lwakho lubhetyebhetye, thandaza, “ ‘Ndiyakholwa, Nkosi; siza, ususe ukungakholwa kwam!’ ” (Marko 9:24). Zidibanise nabantu abanokukukhuthaza endaweni yokukutyhafisa.`

`Ihlabathi alisoloko libakhathalele ababuthathaka, abangaziyo, abangxwelerhekileyo, nabatyumkileyo. Umyalezo kaThixo sokuba “Xa ndibuthathaka, kuxa ndinamandla” ngumyalezo onokuguqula ngokumandla ubomi babantu. Cinga ngomntu onokumkhuthaza ngalo myalezo namhlanje.`

**Isishwankathelo:** Sisahlala kwilizwe elinesono elizaliswe yintlungu nokubandezeleka, kwaye ngamnye kuthi ujamelene neenzima ngathuba lithile ebomi bethu, izinto ezinokusenza sithandabuze uthando lukaThixo. Lo gama sijonga emva kwindlela abantu abaliqela abaseBhayibhileni abasabela ngayo kwizithintelo zobomi, sinokukhuthazwa kukuba ukusabela kwethu kumaxesha anjalo kunokuqinisa uhambo lwethu noThixo, ongaguquguqukiyo (Mal. 3:6) nothando lwakhe luhlala luhleli.
