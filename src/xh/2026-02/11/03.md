Khawucinge ngesihlwele sabantu kunxweme lwaseGalili. Bebelindele ukubuya kukaYesu kwangonyezi, kwaye xa esehlika emkhombeni, bayathontelana ukumngqonga Yena, belandela Yena ukusinga kwiphandle laseKapernahum. Ngequbuliso, uYayiro, umphathi wendlu yesikhungu, uthi gqi abongoze uYesu ukuba azokuphilisa intombi yakhe.

Omnye wabantu abakwisihlwele yintokazi ebingaphilanga iqela le minyaka. Yaachitha yonke imali yayo koogqirha, kodwa “ingasizakali lutho, imana ukuba mandundu” (Marko 5:26). Yayivile ngalo Mntu mkhulu waseGalili kwaye, ngethemba elisentliziyweni yayo, yaaqokelela inketshenketshe yamandla eyayinawo ukuba ishiye indlu yayo ngale ntsasa ukuba ithelele kwisihlwele. Ukuxinana kwabantu kuvakala kufuthanisela xa ecothoza ukusondela kuYesu. Yaza emva koko, ngokutyhala nokutyhudisa, yambona Yena. Yazikhuthaza yona buqu: “ ‘Ukuba ndithe ndachukumisa neengubo zakhe ezi, ndosindiswa’ ” (Marko 5:28).

`Funda kuMarko 5:21-34. Kweenzeka ntoni, yaye singafunda ntoni?`

Esi siganeko sibonisa inkathalo nemfesane kaYesu ngakwabagulayo namalolo, kwakunye nabo bavamise ukulahleka kwisihlwele. Uninzi ngala mhla lwaluthe ncaa ecaleni kukaYesu eli xesha lwaluyabula nesihlwele, kodwa mnye kuphela owafikelela ngenjongo ukuba achukumise uYesu ukufumana impiliso ewayeyidinga ngokumandla. Noko kunjalo, yayingeyonkcukumiso yakhe eyamphilisayo; lukholo lwakhe olwamphilisayo (Marko 5:34). “UMsindisi waba nako ukwahlula inkcukumiso yokholo kukugileka njee kwesihlwele esingahoyanga nto. .”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 344. Ingubo kaYesu yayi ngenamandla akhethekileyo; koko, yaba lukholo lwale ntokazi kwakunye nokhetho lwayo lokuba ifikelele kuYe imchukumise olwayiphilisayo.

Loo ntokazi yayi buthathaka, ekubulalekeni nasekubandezelekeni kwayo, yayinako ukuhlala ebhedini ekhaya ngala ntsasa, kodwa endaweni yoko, ngokuzithandela nethemba yaakhangela uYesu ukuhlumisa ubuhlobo baKhe ngethemba lokuphiliswa. Ukumbonela mgama Yena akuzange kwanele; yaasondela yona kuYe.

UYesu usikhwebela ekwenzeni oko namhla. Yena uthi, “ ‘Yizani kum nonke nina nibulalekayo, nisindwayo ngumthwalo, ndoninika ukuphumla. Thabathani idyokhwe yam niyithwale, nifunde kum; ngokuba ndinobulali, ndithobekile ngentliziyo; noyifumanela ukuphumla imiphefumlo yenu’ ” (Mat. 11:28, 29).
