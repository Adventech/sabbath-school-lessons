---
title:  Iinqwithelo Zobomi
date:   07/06/2026
---

UYesu waachitha usuku ethetha nezihlwele zabantu kumanxweme aseGalili.

Amazwi kaYesu ayakuhlokoma ezingqondweni zabantu umlibe nomtyhutyhumezo kanaphakade.

Kuthe kwakuhlwa, uYesu wathetha nabafundi baKhe, ebamemela kuhambo kunye naYe. “ ‘Masiwele siye ngaphesheya’ ” (Marko 4:35). UYesu wayesazi ukuba uqhwithelo luzakuza kodwa waabacebisa ukuba bahambe sekunjalo. Wayenesifundo esibalulekileyo sobomi ewayezakusifundisa abalandeli baKhe abasondeleyo.

Umele kukuyazi okulandelayo okwenzekayo.

`Funda ngolu qhwithelo kwakhona kuMarko 4:35-41. Zifundo zini ezingokholo onokuzifumana apha?`

Qwalasela ezi ngongoma:

1. UYesu ubiwa bubuthongo kwinto enokuba yayi kuphela kwayo engumqamelo emkhombeni. Imikhombe yokuloba yayivamise ukuba nomqamelo omnye, nalapho umqhubi womkhombe, esinqeni somkhombe, wayehlala khona. Umntu osesinqeni somkhombe wayeqhubela umkhombe apho uphela khona. Ngoko ke apha, uYesu ukwisikhundla “somqhubi” womkhombe, kodwa ubiwa bubuthongo esevilini.
2. Ayingabo bonke abafundi ababebatsha kuhambo ngomkhombe. UPetros, uYakobi, noYohane babengabalobi abangamagqala. Babelwazi uLwandle lwaseGalili njengomva wesandla sabo, kwaye babe ngayazi indlela yokuzamazamana noqhwithela.
3. Le kuphela kwengxelo eshicilelweyo yeGospile kaYesu elele. Ngexesha lenye yeenqwithela ezibalaseleyo ebomini babo, xa abafundi benkwantya kwaye becinga ukuba bazakufa, uYesu ulele esinqeni somkhombe.
4. Impendulo yabafundi kwixesha labo lentlekele yethi “Akukhathali na?” Baathandabuza isimilo sikaYesu kwakunye nothando lwaKhe ngakubo. Amaxesha amaninzi, le yimpendulo yethu xa sijamelene namaxesha anzima. Kusesizikithini sokuphelelwa lithemba esiye sizame ukuzisindisa (njengabafundi), okanye ngelinye ixesha kuxa sisiva intlungu okanye ilahleko esiye siqalise ukugocagoca okanye ukuthandabuza uthando lukaThixo nenkathalo kuthi. Sicinga ukuba Yena kufuneka enze ngohlobo oluthile ngokwendlela esicinga nesibona ngayo ngokwenkalo yethu yobuntu. Kodwa, njengakubafundi, kukwiinqwithelo zobomi apho uThixo aye enze imimangaliso emikhulu. UThixo usoloko ethembekile, nkqu naxa inkangeleko yaKhe yokungathi akakhathali ingaqondakali kuthi. Yena ukunye nathi kwiinqwithelo zehu kwaye uyakwazi ukuzolisa uqhwithelo xa thina singakwazi.

`Uvamise ukusabela njani xa ujamelene noqhwithela ebomini bakho? Loo mathuba abuchaphazela njani ubudlelane bakho noThixo? Ukhe wawaphila nini ama-2 Korinte 5:7?`
