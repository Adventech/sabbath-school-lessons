---
title:  Isimo Sokuthandaza
date:   04/05/2026
---

Xa kukho into engahambi kakuhle ebomini bethu, inkoliso yethu itsalela umnxeba umhlobo osenyongweni ukuba ithethe ngayo. Xa sineendaba ezimnandi, siba nomntu wokwabelana naye ngazo. Singenza kwangolohlobo noThixo. “Umthandazo kukuzityand’ igila kuThixo njengakumhlobo.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, p. 93.

Umthandazo awusigcini nje kuphela siqhagamshelene noThixo koko ukwaxelela umtyholi ukuba singabakabani na. xa siguqa ukuba sithandaze kusasa, kukufana nokubhengeza ngokwasemzimbeni kumagunya obumnyama ukuba sikhetha uThixo namhlanje. Akupheli apho, koko uThixo uthumela izithunywa ecaleni kwethu xa sithandaza, size siqiniswe kwaye sigqunywe kutshaba lobumnyama (Ndumiso 91).

Isenzo sasemzimbeni sokuguqa ngolulamo sibonakalisa isimo sokuthobeka. Ngandlel’ ithile sahlukile ekuhlaleni esitulweni okanye sokungqengqa ebhedini xa sithandaza, nangona sinako ukuthandaza ngezindlela, kanjalo. Noko kunjalo, xa siguqa phambi koThixo, intliziyo yethu izinikela ngokukhululeka ngakumbi, njengoko umzimba wethu namazwi ethu ebhengeza ukuba Yena unobungangamsha kwanokuba thina singabantwana nje baKhe abadalileyo.

`Funda ezi zicatshulwa zilandelayo zeBhayibhhile uze ugqale ubomi baba bantu babeguqa xa bethandaza: Dan. 6:10, Luka 22:41, Zenzo 7:60, Zenzo 9:40, Zenzo 20:36.`

Ukuthandaza umile yayi sisenzo esiqhelekileyo ngamaxesha eBhayibhile (2 Zikroni. 20:5, 6, 13; 1 Sam. 1:26; Yobhi 30:20; Luka 18:11). IBhayibhile kanjalo ikwachaza imizekelo yabantu abathi bahlala phantsi xa bathandazayo (2 Sam. 7:18, 2 Kumkani 4:38). Abanye baazandlala phambi koThixo, ubuso babo busemhlabeni-nangona esi simo ngokwesiqhelo sasisayanyaniswa kancinci nomthandazo, koko endaweni yoko ikukululama phambi kwalowo wongamileyo (1 Kumkani 1:47, Marko 14:35).

Sithini isimo sakho sesiqhelo xa uthandaza? IBhayibhile ayifuni sithandaze sikwisimo esikhethekileyo, kodwa ke izimo zibalulekile, njengoko zibonakalisa intlonipho yethu, iimvakalelo zethu zangaphakathi, kwakunye nolangazelelo lwethu lokuzinikela kuThixo. Abanye abantu abakwazi kuguqa, ngoko ke, kakade, yimeko yentliziyo engundoqo. Ukuba unako ukuguqa kodwa uqhele ukungenzi njalo, kutheni ungazami ukuguqa kwixa elizayo uthandaza uze ubone indlela okuchaphazela ngayo ixesha lakho noThixo.

`IBhayibhile iyasimema ukuba “sithandaze singayeki” (1 Tesa. 5:17), ukubonakalisa ukuthi gqolo (Kol. 4:2) kwakunye nokuzingisa (Rom. 12:12). Namhlanje, lo gama umile, uhleli phantsi, ungqengqile, okanye uhamba, bhekisa iingcinga zakho kuThixo uze uthethe naye naYe njengoMhlobo wakho. Qala kanye ngoku.`
