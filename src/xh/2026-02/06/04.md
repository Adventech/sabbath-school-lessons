---
title:  UEnoki Wahamba Wancokola
date:   05/05/2026
---

`Funda iGenesis 5:22-24. Yintoni, ncakasana, esiyaziyo ngoEnoki?`

IBhayibhile ayithi thaca okuninzi ngobomi bukaEnoki, kodwa iyasixelela ukuba waahamba noThixo iminyaka engama-300 wada uThixo wamsa ezulwini. Indlela entle ngayo eyokuba ukuzinikela okuthe gqolo komntu kuThixo kube yinto echaza ubomi bakhe!

Inye into esiyaziyo yeyokuba uEnoch makube “wayezingisa emthandazweni” (Rom. 12:12), exikile yaye ekhula ngokusondela kuThixo ngokholo ngamava akhe emihla ngemihla. Ihlabathi lalisiya liqatsela ebubini ngexesha awayephila ngalo, kwaye uEnoki waaqhubekeka esisinxadanxada ekhonza uThixo, kodwa wayengenako ukwenza oku ngendlela eyiyo engahlelanga kuYe.

“Phakathi kobomi bokusebenza, uEnoki ngokungagungqiyo walugcina unxibelelwano lwakhe noThixo. Ngokuya ikhula nangokuya ixakekisa imisebenzi yakhe, kokukona yayisithi gqolo kwaye inyameka imithandazo yakhe. . . . Emva kokuba phakathi kwabantu ithuba elithile, esebenzela ukubanceda ngokufundisa nomzekelo, wayezikhwebula, ukuze ayokuchitha ixesha bucala, elambele kwaye enxanelwe olo lwazi lungcwele lunokunikezelwa nguThixo kuphela. Enxibelelana ngolo hlobo noThixo, uEnoki waafikelela ngakumbi nangakumbi ekubonakalaliseni umfanekiso ongcwele. . . . Nkqu nabangendawo babebona ngokukhwankqisayo umbhalo wezulu kwimbonakalo yakhe.”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, pp. 86, 87.

UThixo akasibizeli ekupheleni njenge okanye onongendi, sizinxweme ehlabathini de singabi loncedo elizweni. NjengoEnoki, sinokusebenza kwaye sizazi izidingo ezisingqongileyo, kodwa kungokuhamba nokuthetha noThixo kubudlelane obuzinzileyo, obuphilileyo anokuthi Yena abonakalise isimilo saKhe esimangalisayo ngathi.

Sinako ukuthandaza nanini na, naphi na. akukho ndawo ehlabathi uThixo angasiboniyo okanye angasiviyo kuyo (Ndum. 139:7–12); Yena usoloko eziva izililo zeentliziyo zethu, nokuba siphi na (funda Izili. 3:55–57). Noko kunjalo, kukho into ekufuneka ithethiwe ngokuthandaza ngokuvakalayo endaweni yasezingqondweni nje kuphela. Xa sithandaza ngokuthe cwaka, sisengaphazamiseka okanye singalugqibi ukrozo lwengcamango okanye isivakalisi sethu, kwaye kunokuba nzima ukuba iingcinga zethu zihlale zigxilile. Kodwa sithandaza sithetha, nokuba siyasebeza okanye ngelizwi lesiqhelo sisikhumbuzo esi kuthi sokuba uThixo ukho, sokuba Yena uphulaphule, kwanesokuba thina sinento etsolileyo esinokuncokola ngayo kunye naye.

`Xa uqhubekeka nemihla ngemihla yakho, uzakuwusebeza phi okanye njani umthandazo kunxibelelwano noYesu?`
