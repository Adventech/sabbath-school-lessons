---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   08/05/2026
---

Ekugqibeleni, kufuneka sithandaze kuba uThixo simthanda ngeyona ndlela, kwaye asinakuyinceda eyokwabelana ngento yonke esebomini bethu kunye naYe: imivuyo yethu “neempumelelo” zobomi, imithwalo yethu neenkathazo, izicelo zethu kwakunye neentswelo zethu zamihla le. “Sinokusondela kuThixo kangangokuba kuso sonke isilingo esithi gqi ekhwapheni iingcamango zethu ziyakujolisa kuYe ngokuqinisekileyo njengentyantyambo ijongise elangeni. Gcina iintswelo zakho, imivuyo yakho, iintsizi zakho, amaxhala akho, kwakunye nezoyikiso zakho phambi koThixo. Awunakumsinda Yena; awunakumdina Yena. Lowo ubala iinwele zentloko yakho akangongazinakanga iintswelo zabantwana baKhe. . . . Intliziyo yaKhe yothando iyachukumiseka ziintsizi zethu nkqu nokuzichaza kwethu. Yisa kuYe yonke into edida ingqondo. Akukho nto ingumhohoma kuYe ukuba ayithwale, kuba Yena ufunqule amazwe, Yena ulawula kuyo yonke imicimbi yelimiweyo. Akukho nto nangayiphina indlela exhalabisa uxolo lwethu incinci kakhulu ukuba Yena angangayiqapheli. Akukho sahluko kumava ethu esimnyama kangangokuba Yena akangesifundi; akukho kudideka kunzima kakhulu de Yena angabi nakusombulula. Akukho ntlekele inokwehlela noyena umncinane kubantwana baKhe, akukho nkxalabo izungula umphefumlo, akukho vuyo lonwabisa, akukho mthandazo usulungekileyo ugqitha imilebe, angayiqapheliyo uBawo wethu osemazulwini, okanye angabonakalisi mdla ukhawulezileyo Yena kuyo. . . . Amakhonkco aphakathi koThixo nomphefumlo ngamnye ahluke kwaye agcwele ngeyona ndlela ngokungathi awukho omnye umphefumlo osehlabathini onokwabelana nawo ngenkathalo yaKhe, awukho omnye umphefumlo Yena awanikezela uNyana waKhe oyintanda ngenxa yawo.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, pp. 99, 100.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Ungawuchaza umthandazo njengomnandi okanye njengosindayo? Yintoni ebe nenxaxheba kuluvo lwakho?`

`Mininzi ngeyona ndlela imiyalezo enomqondo kwesi sicatshulwa singentla. Yeyiphi ingcamango enkenteza ngokukodwa kuwe?`

`Kwezi ziqu zithathu zeBhayibhile kufundwe kuzo kule veki (Daniyeli, Enoki, noMoses), bobukabani ubomi bomthandazo owoyama kubo kakhulu, kwaye kutheni?`

**Isishwankathelo:** Xa sifunda ngeengxilimbela zomthandazo eziseBhayibhileni, kunokubalula ukucinga ukuba asinakuba nobudlelane obuqhogene ngolo hlobo noThixo okanye sizibophelele ngolo hlobo. Kodwa sinako. NjengoDaniyeli, sinokungagungqi yaye sithembeke ekuguqeni yonke imihla, naphakathi kwenkcaso. NjengoEnoki, singakhetha ukuhamba kwaye sincokole noThixo, sibhenele kuYe ngaphambi kokuba siwenze umsebenzi asibizele ukuba siwenze. NjengoMoses, sinokubakhokela abo bakwinqanaba lempembelelo yethu, sikhungele iintsapho zethu kwakunye nabo bakwimiphakathi yethu xa sikhetha ukuhlala phantsi kwesithunzi sikaSomandla, iNkokeli noMhlobo wethu.
