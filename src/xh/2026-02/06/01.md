### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Dan. 2:20–23; Dan. 6:10, 11; Zenzo 20:36; Gen. 5:22–24; Eksod. 33:15–23; Eksod. 32:31, 32.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ndiyamthanda, ngokuba uYehova eliva ilizwi lam, ukutarhuzisa kwam. Ngokuba endithobele indlebe yakhe, ndiya kumnqula ngemihla yam yonke” (Ndumiso 116:1, 2).

Cinga xa ubuthetha manqaphanqapha nomhlobo osenyongweni okanye iqabane lakho. Kungekudalanga obo budlelane bebuzakuqhawuka, yaye bekuzakubakho ingxaki. Kwangohlobo olunye, umthandazo yinxalenye engundoqo ekubeni nobudlelane obuqhogeneyo noThixo. Sisimbo esisemqoka sokunqula, eso asidingayo umntu ngamnye kuthi nanokusiqinisa. Ukuba asithandazi rhoqo nangokuthe gqolo, sizakubhadula eNkosini kungekudala.

EBhayibhile, sifunda ngobomi babantu abohlukeneyo abaathandaza ngeendlela ezohlukeneyo. Sinobuyel’ emva sikrwaqule kwindlela ukunxibelelana kwabo noThixo ekwaba nefuthe ngayo kubudlelane babo kunye naYe, indlela kwakunye noko sikuthandazela nathi, kwakunye nendlela imithandazo yabo eyatshintsha ngayo ubomi babanye abantu. Yinene: imithandazo yethu ichaphazela hayi nje kuphela thina koko nabanye kanjalo.

NjengeBhayibhile, lo mxholo womthandazo ngokufanayo mkhulu kwaye ubalulekile njengoku ubanzi kakhulu kunoko kunokugqitywa ngeeveki nje ezimbini. Kule veki sizakufunda ezinye ezifundo kwabanye babo kwiBhayibhile bathe bathandaza baza basibonisa indlela umthandazo osemqoka ngayo ekubeni nobudlelena obuluqilima noThixo. Makhe sifunde kwimizekelo yabo.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Meyi 9._
