---
title:  UMoses Uthethelela Uhlanga
date:   07/05/2026
---

`Funda kwiEksodus 32:31, 32. Oku kusifundisa ntoni ngoMoses nomthandazo?`

UMoses ngokungazibandeziyo waathethelela abantu bakaThixo izihlandlo ngezihlandlo. UMoses waabhenela kuThixo xa abantu babenxaniwe (Eksod. 15:25; Eksod. 17:2–6), xa abantu babe lambile (Num. 11:21, 22), naxa babekhathazeke ngokumandla (Num. 11:11–15).

Esakube amaSirayeli enze ithole legolide kanye emva kokuba uThixo enqophisene nabo, waabalula, “Kuba bendinxunguphele ngumsindo nobushushu abenoburhalarhume ngabo uYehova kuni, ukuba anitshabalalise. Ke uYehova wandiva nangeso sihlandlo’ ” (Dut. 9:19).

Ukubuya kweentlola kwiLizwe leDinga, uMoses waabalula, “ ‘Ndawa ke phantsi phambi koYehova, ezo mini zimashumi mane nobusuku obumashumi mane, endawayo phantsi; kuba uYehova wathi uyakunitshabalalisa’ ” (Dut. 9:25).

Xa uLevi wayesahlulwa kwezinye izizwe ukuba asebenze etempileni, uMoses waabalula, “ ‘Ndema ke mna entabeni, njengemihla yokuqala, iimini ezimashumi mane nobusuku obumashumi mane; wandiva uYehova nangeso sihlandlo; uYehova akathanda ukukonakalisa’ ” (Dut. 10:10). UYehova wakuva ukudomboza kukaMoses.

Singafunda lukhulu kubomi bukaMoses xa kufikelelwa kumthandazo nokubambelela nkqi kuThixo:

- **UMoses wayenothando olunzulu ngakuThixo, kwakunye nomfanekiso ocacileyo wesimilo saKhe.** UThixo wazichaza ngokwaKhe kuMoses kwiEksodus 34:6: “ ‘UYehova, uYehova, uThixo onemfesane, onobabalo, ozeka kade umsindo, omninzi ngenceba nenyaniso.’ ”
- **UMoses ngokufanayo wayeqinisekile kwaye ethembekile ngethuba awayebambelele kuThixo kumahla ndenyuka** endlela edinayo esinga kwiLizwe leDinga. Nangona wayesokola njengoko sonke sisenza, uMoses waathemba amandla kaThixo, ubukho, nenkokelo kobakhe ubomi (Eksod. 33:13).
- **UMoses wamkhumbuza uThixo ngomnqophiso waKhe (Eksod. 32:13), wabanga amadinga kaThixo egameni labantu baKhe (Dut. 7:8), yaye wayikhumbula inkokelo kaThixo kwixa elidlulileyo (Dut. 8:2).**
- **UMoses waamkela iimpendulo zikaThixo kwimithandazo yakhe, nokuba yayingu ewe okanye uhayi.** Ukuba kubudlelane obuqhogeneyo noThixo akuthethi ukuba ngokuzenzekelayo sakusoloko sikufumana oko sikufunayo (Dut. 3:23–29), koko kufuneka sithandaze ngokuzingisa sekunjalo (Luka 18:1–8).

`Ngubani odinga imithandazo yakho yentethelelo kanye ngoku? Yintoni ekunqandayo ukuba ungathandazi kanye ngoku?`
