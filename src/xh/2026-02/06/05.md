---
title:  UMoses, Inkokeli Enobuthixo
date:   06/05/2026
---

Nangona uEnoki ngokuselubala wayenobudlelane obuqhogeneyo noThixo, sinomqondo othe xhaxhe ngobudlelane bukaMoses noThixo kwaye singade sifunde neengxelo eziliqela ngeencoko zikaMoses kunye naYe. Xa sidukuda noMoses kumahla-ndenyuka obomi bale nkokeli ithobekileyo, sibona isihlandlo nesihlandlo ukuba esona sigaba sisodwa sibaluleke ngakumbi sobomi bakhe kwanemfihlo yokuphumelela kwakhe njengenkokheli enentlonelo yayi lunxibelelwano lwakhe oluthe gqolo kwanobudlelane obumileyo kunye noThixo.

`Funda kwiEksodus 33:15-23. Uthini umongo kwakunye nohlobo lwencoko kaMoses noThixo?`

Khawufan’ ucinge kwakunokuba njani ukuthetha noThixo uve ilizwi laKhe ngokucace olo hlobo. Ngumnqa eyokuba amaSirayeli engazange alufune wona oluhlobo lokunxibelelana noThixo endaweni yokubongoza uMoses ukuba athethe kunye nawo endaweni kaThixo (Eksod. 20:18–21). Noko kunjalo, uThixo wayesele emlungiselele oku uMoses, ekuqalekeni kokuncokola kwabo kwityholo elivuthayo, kwakule ntaba. Nangona sifunda ngeminye imithandazo kaMoses buqu, siyabona ukuba uphantse ukusoloko ekwindawo enoThixo, ecela inkokhelo yaye ekhunga ngenxa yabantu abakhokelayo.

**Kwizihlandlo ezibini, uMoses ukhungela amalungu osapho. Ziimeko zini ezazijikeleze ukukhunga kwakhe, kwaye ngekwakwenzeke ntoni ukuba wayengezanga angenelele ukuvala ithuba?**

`Aaron: Eksodus 32:1–14, 31–34, Dut. 9:20`

`Miriam: Num. 12:13`

Okumangalisa ngokukodwa ngonxibelelwano noMiriam kukuba uMoses nguye owayengumxhamli wemphatho-mbi yakhe nomona. Ngewayebuye umva ngokulula aze avumele uThixo awise isohlwayo esasifanele uMiriam noArron. Endaweni yoko, waakhawuleza ukuxolela nokukhungela ukuphiliswa kodade wabo. Umbonakaliso onamandla ngolo hlobo wobabalo lukaThixo oluxolelayo kuboni ubonakala apha kwizenzo zikaMoses.
