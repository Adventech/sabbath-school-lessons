---
title:  Iinqaba Kubudlelane Bam NoThixo
date:   25/05/2026
---

Mininzi imiyalezo eseBhayibhileni ethetha ngobudlelane bethu kunye noThixo kwakunye nemiqobo esithintelayo ekukhuleni kuKristu. Gqala la mazwi asuka kuPawulos noYesu:

**“Ngoko ke lowo uba umi, makalumke angawi”** (1 Kor. 10:12). NjengoSamson, ukuxhomekeka kwisiqu sakho kuyakukukhahlela phantsi.

**“ ‘Ungahlokomisi ixilingo phambi kwakho, njengoko benza ngako abahanahanisi . . ., ukuze bazukiswe ngabantu’ ”** (Mat. 6:2). Yeka ukuxelela uwonkewonke indlela ophucuke ngayo! Thobeka, njengokuba noYesu wayethobekile.

**“ ‘Ke mna ndithi kuni, wonke umntu okhangela umntu oyinkazana, ukuba amkhanuke, uselemkrexezile engqondweni yakhe. Ukuba ke iliso lakho lokunene liyakukhubekisa, linyothule ulilahle kuwe’ ”** (Mat. 5:28, 29). Yenza nantoni na ukususa inkanuko entliziyweni yakho, kuba oku kungumqobo kubudlelane bakho noThixo.

**“ ‘Musani ukugweba, ukuze ningagwetywa. Kuba ngogwebo enigweba ngalo, niya kugwetywa kwangalo nani’ ”** (Mat. 7:1, 2). Yeka ukubalasela ekugxekeni nasekugwebeni abanye. NguThixo ongumgwebi, ngoko ke myeke Yena abe nguye (1 Kor. 4:5).

**“ ‘Ke mna ndithi kuni, zithandeni iintshaba zenu, basikeleleni abo baniqalekisayo, benzeleni okulungileyo abo banithiyayo, nibathandazele abo banigxekayo banitshutshise’ ”** (Mat. 5:44). Yeka ukuthiya iintshaba zakho. Xa usiva kakubi ngakwabo bakuphatha kakubi, ngoko nangoko oko kubeka umqobo kubudlelane bakho noThixo. Endaweni yoko, qalisa ukuthandazela iintshaba zakho uze ubone indlela oku ukutshintsha ngayo hayi nje kuphela ukuhamba kwakho noThixo koko nobudlelane bakho kunye nabanye.

**“ ‘Ke mna ndithi kuni, wonke umntu ofumana amqumbele umzalwana wakhe, woba sisisulu somgwebo’ ”** (Mat. 5:22). Mhlawumbi uyasithethelela isizathu sokungxolela abo basondeleyo kuwe. Umsindo wakho ubuchaphazela njani ubudlelane bakho noThixo, kungekathethwa ngabo unomsindo ngakubo? Ezi ziindawo nje ezimbalwa ezisenza sikhubeke.

`UYesu walumkisa ngemasikwenze xa izandla zethu, iinyawo, namehlo esibangela ukuba sone. Yintoni le uYesu wayesilumkisa kuyo? Funda kuMarko 9:42-48.`

Ukunqumla isandla sakho okanye unyawo lwakho okanye ukukrolonqa iliso lakho kuba likubangela wone kubaxekile. Kwakufanele ukuba njalo. Kodwa le yindlela uYesu asibona sinobuzaza ngayo isono kwakunye nefuthe laso ebomini bethu. Wena usithatha ngokunobuzaza kangakanani na?
