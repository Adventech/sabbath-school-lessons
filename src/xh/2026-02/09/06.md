---
title:  Ukwazi Kwakunye Nokwenza
date:   28/05/2026
---

KwiNtshumayelo yaseNtabeni, uYesu uthetha lukhulu ngobudlelane-kunye naYe nagomnye komnye. Uthetha into echukumisa ngeyona ndlela xa esiya ngasemaphethelweni kumyalezo waKhe:

“ ‘Asingabo bonke abathi kum, Nkosi, Nkosi, abaya kungena ebukumkanini bamazulu; ngokwenzayo ukuthanda kukaBawo osemazulwini’ ” (Mat. 7:21).

UYesu utyatyadula ngelithi abanye bayakubiza Yena kwaye bazi ngokucacileyo ngaYe babe ngokwenene bengamazi Yena. Eneneni, ukufuna ulwazi kubalulekile, kwaye iBhayibhile isixelela ukuba abantu bakaThixo banokutshatyalaliswa ngenxa yokuswela ukumazi uThixo nangenxa yokuba bekubukulile ukumazi kwabo Yena (Hos. 4:1, 6, 10). Masingakuthathi buyekeye ukubaluleka kwenyaniso yeBhayibhile engaphelelwayo. Kodwa ukuba olo lwazi alusitshintshi luze lwendelisele ukuzibophelela kwethu kwakunye nohambo lwethu noThixo, aluncedi nto.

“ ‘Bubo ke obu ubomi obungunaphakade, ukuba mabakwazi, wena ukuphela koThixo oyinyaniso, naye uYesu Kristu omthumileyo’ ” (Yohane 17:3). UYesu watsho ukuthi into efunekayo ekungeneni ezulwini kukwenza intando kaThixo ize ekugqibeleni ibe kukwazi uThixo-ngoba asinakwenza intando yaKhe singakhange simazi Yena. Le yimeko egqibayo kwakunnye nomnqweno ofikelelekayo ngeyona ndlela. Ukuba umntwana wakho uthi uyakuthanda abe esoloko esenza oko ukufunayo, isenzo sakhe sityhila ubunzulu bothando lwakhe nentlonipho ngakuwe. Ngohlobo olunye, xa simthanda uThixo, siyakunqwenela ukwenza intando yaKhe, kuba sisazi ukuba akukho nto ingcono esinokuyenza! Ukusabela kwethu kuYe, kwakunye, ekugqibeleni, nentobelo yethu kuYe njengokuphuphuma kothando lwethu, zibonisa isimo esisiso sobudlelane bethu kunye naYe.

`UYesu uqukumbela iNtshumayelo yaseNtabeni ngokushiya abaphulaphuli baKhe ngomcela-mngeni wokugqibela ochukumisayo. Wawusithini? Funda kuMateyu 7:24-29.`

Xa ngokwenene siyiva imiyalezo kaYesu, asinakuyinceda eyokucelwa umngeni neyokutshintshwa. Kodwa, eyokuqala kwinto yonke, iindlebe zethu mazivuleke neentliziyo zethu zamkele, ukuze uqalo lokuphila kubudlelane obuqhogeneyo noThixo lube nokukrolwa kanye emiphefumlweni yethu ngempefumlo nganye esiyithathayo. Ubomi bethu bunokwakhelwa phezu koLwalwa necebo likaThixo eligqibeleleyo ngathi.

Olu qalo lobudlelane obuqhogeneyo aluyomfihlo. Lutyhilwe kumaphepha eLizwi likaThixo eliphefumlelweyo, kwaye Yena olununusa kumntu ngamnye. Lukhetho lomntu ngamnye buqu ukuba alwamkele ngokholo, ukubanga ubulungisa obugqibeleleyo bukaKristu, kwakunye ke nokuphila obo bulungisa.
