### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Abagwebi 14; Marko 9:42–48; Roma 3:20; Mat. 5:17, 18; Roma 3:28; Mat. 7:24–29.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Andiyi kuzilibala naphakade iziyalezo zakho, ngokuba undiphilisile ngazo. Ndingowakho mna, ndisindise, kuba ndicamanga ngeziyalezo zakho” (Ndumiso 119:93, 94).

Ngaphandle kwentandabuzo, isono ngowona mqobo mkhulu kubudlelane obuqhogeneyo noThixo. Isono asisahluli nje kuphela noThixo (Isa. 59:2), koko sikwakhohlisa, sihlungisa, sigqiba, size ekugqibeleni sitshabalalise kwathina. Idabi lethu nesono kwakunye nomna lelona dabi likhulu ekwakha kwafuneka sijamelane nalo, elinemiphumela emikhulu, nkqu engunaphakade.

Abanye bayasibhebhetha isono njengenxalenye yobomi ethe mbende. Kakade, yimvelo yomntu ukuthi gcobho kokuyolisayo. Kodwa ingaba sisithatha buyekeyeke isono kuba uluntu luye alwathukuthezelelwa siso? Sisengachabasa kumxholo wesono, sisoyikisela ukuba sakukhubekisa ubani othile sisichaza ubunjalo baso, kodwa ekugqibeleni, ngokuya sikhetha ukuphila ngokungathukuthezelwa siso, kokukona siyakutyibilika kubudlelane obuphilileyo noThixo.

Ewe, wonke umntu uyona, kwaye iingcinga zethu, iinjongo, izenzo, namazwi ziyabahlungisa abanye, iziqu zethu, kwakunye noThixo. Ekugqibeleni, isono sitshabalalisa ubudlelane bethu noThixo, kodwa uThixo uzityhilile Yena kuthi ngokwazi umthetho waKhe, obengezelisa ukukhanya kwisono esikubomi bethu.

Kule veki masikhe sihlole isizathu sokuba uThixo asinike umthetho waKhe kwanokuba, xa ubani esaphula umthetho kaThixo aze kamva one, yintoni okanye ngubani onokunceda ukubuyisela ubudlelane bakhe noThixo.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Meyi 30._
