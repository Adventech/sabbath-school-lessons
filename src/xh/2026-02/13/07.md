---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   26/06/2026
---

“Ukuba asilwamkeli unqulo lukaKristu ngokuzondla ngeLizwi likaThixo, asoze sakufumana ukungena kumzi kaThixo. Siphilile ngokutya kwasemhlabeni, sifundise imidla yethu kwizinto zomhlaba, asinakuze sizilungele iinkundla zasezulwini; asikwazanga ukuqonda umsinga onyulu, wezulu otyhutyha ezulwini. Amazwi eengelosi kwakunye nomculo weehandi zazo awungeze wasanelisa. Sidinga ukulambela nokunxanelwa ubulungisa bukaKristu; sidinga ukubunjwa nokulungiswa yimpembelelo eguqulayo yobabalo lwaKhe, ukuze silungele ukuhlala nezithunywa zezulu. . . .

“Ngelo xesha iintlanga aziyi kuba namthetho wumbi ingengumthetho wezulu. Bonke bayakuba lusapho olonwabileyo, olumanyeneyo, olwambathiswe iingubo zokudumisa nokubulela. . . . Ngaphaya kombono amakhwezi ayakucula kunye, nonyana bakaThixo bamemelele uvuyo, lo gama uThixo noKristu beyakumanyana ekuvakaliseni, ‘Akusayi kubakho sono, kungasayi kuphinde kubekho kufa.’

“Sifunaukungena kwisimbo sokuncokola ngezulu, izulu elihle. Thetha ngobobomi obuyakuqhubekeka lo gama uThixo esekho, yaye emva koko uyakuzilibala izilingo ezincinci zakho neenzima. Ingqondo mayitsaleleke kuThixo.”—Ellen G. White, The Faith I Live By, p. 363.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Phulaphula okanye ufunde umbono wezulu kaEllen G. White ofumaneka kwiEarly Writings, pp. 14-20. Yintoni ebalasele ngokumandla kuwe kule nkcazelo?`

`Yeyiphi inkalo yezifundo zale kota onqwenela ukuyikhumbula ngakumbi ukuze ugcine ubudlelane bakho noThixo bomelele ude umbone uYesu ubuso ngobuso?`

`Ngubani ebomini bakho odinga ukuva ngethemba lezulu? Zibophelele ekwabelaneni naye ngokukhawuleza. Khumbula: awunakwabelana naye ngethemba wena ongenalo buqu.`

**Isishwankathelo:** Lo gama sithe ntshoo amehlo ethu kumgomo, ngamana “singakholosa ngayo le nto, yokuba lowo wawuqalayo ngaphakathi kwenu umsebenzi olungileyo, wowufeza, ide ibe yiloo mini kaYesu Kristu” (Fili. 1:6). UThixo waasungula ubudlelane anabo Yena kunye nawe, kwaye uyakubugqibezela Yena. Ngamana singakhulu eluthandweni nokholo lo gama sisalinde loo mini, lo gama maxa onke sayame kubudlelane bukaKristu, ubudluliselwa kuthi ngokholo.
