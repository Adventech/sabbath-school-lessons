---
title:  Ukuphila Namhlanje
date:   21/06/2026
---

Xa sikhangele, sibona ihlabathi likhefuzela lincwina, kwaye nemiqondiso uYesu awasixelela ngayo iyenzeka phambi kwamehlo ethu. Iimfazwe namarhe emfazwe, iintlanga zivukelana nezinye iintlanga, iindlala, iindyikityha zokufa, iinyikima, neentshuntshiso (Mat. 24:6–11) ziyenzeka macal’ onke kwaye zikhangeleka ngathi ziya zikhula ngakumbi ngokuya lihamba ixesha. Ewe, siphila kumaxesha anobuzazakumaxesha esidinga ubudlelane obumileyo noThixo.

Siyaxelelwa, “Ke kaloku ukuphela kwezinto zonke kusondele. Yibani nesidima ngoko, nibe nobungcathu, ukuba nithandaze” (1 Pet. 4:7). Ukuba kunjalo ngoko, ngokuthe chatha ngoku nali ixesha lokomeleza nokuqinisa ubudlelane bakho noThixo. Kwaye nokuba ixesha liyakuphela nini, ubomi bethu bufutshane, nokuba siphile ixesha elingakanani na. “Namhla, nokuba kungomso, siya kuya kula mzi, senze khona umnyaka ube mnye, sirhwebe, sizuze; kanti nina aba anikwazi kakuhle okwangomso. Kuba buyintoni na ubomi benu? Bungumphunga obonakala ixesha elincinane, uze ke uthi shwaka” (Yakobi 4:13, 14). Siyayazi indlela esiyinyani ngayo eso silumkiso. Nina bawafundayo la mazwi linokutshona sele ningasekho. Le yinxalenye yenyaniso ebuhlungu yokuphila kwihlabathi eliwileyo. Kubaluleke kangakanani, ngoko, ukuqiniseka ngobudlelane bethu kunye noThixo kwakunye nokusoloko siphila sikuqonda ukumdinga kwethu Yena nobabalo lwaKhe olusindisayo?

`INdumiso 80 ibonelela ngesibongozo esihle kuThixo. Funda esi sahluko uze, ngokukhethekileyo, uqwalasele indima 1-3, 14-17, 18, 19, uze ubeke igama “ndi” apho ithi “si.” Nokuba lahluke kangakanani na ixesha, indawo, nemeko yale ndumiso, kungaziphi indlela onokuthi wena buqu unxulumane nayo?`

Sonke sidinga imvuselelo ebomini bethu. Kulula ukuqhela okanye nkqu ukulibala oko uThixo akwenzileyo nakwenzela kona. Leliphi ikholwa elithembekileyo, nokuba litsala nzima, elingenakuyithandaza into efana nale: “Ukhanyise ubuso bakho, sisindiswe!” (Ndum. 80:19)? Xa usamkela oko uYesu akwenzele kona, xa usazi ukuba izono zakho zixolelwe kwanokuba wombathiswe ngobulungisa baKhe obugqibeleleyo, budluliselwa kuwe ngokholo, ungayazi ukuba usindisiwe ngaYe.

`Uyiqonda njani intsingiselo yokuba uThixo “akhanyise” ubuso baKhe kuwe, ngokukodwa kumba wokwazi ukuba bubulungisa baKhe kuphela obukusindisayo?`
