---
title:  Ekugqibeleni, Ubuso Ngobuso
date:   22/06/2026
---

Sasidalelwe ukuba sibe kufutshane kuThixo (Gen. 2:7). Kususela oko Yena waadala uluntu, uThixo unikezele yonke into ukubuyisa ubudlelane bethu kunye naYe obuqhawukileyo (Yohane 3:16). Yena ubeke unaphakade ezintliziyweni zethu, noko kunjalo abantu abakwazi kuyiqonda into uThixo ayenze ukususela ekuqaleni ukuya ekugqibeleni (Ntshuma. 3:11). Siyinxalenye yemfazwe enkulu eshushu ngenxa zonke kuthi-nkqu nakuthi aba-noko kunjalo, amaxesha amaninzi asinqumami okwethutyana ukujonga ixabiso elikhulu loko kunikezelweyo ukuze thina sibuyiselwe kubudlelane uThixo ancwase ukuba sibe nabo kunye naYe. Amaxesha amaninzi, sithandeleke kumadabi ethu asemhlabeni nezilingo, silibale ukuba “ikowethu lisemazulwini, apho kananjalo silinde khona uMsindisi, iNkosi uYesu Kristu; eya kuwenza kumila kumbi umzimba wokuthobeka kwethu, ukuze wona wenziwe ufane nawo umzimba wozuko lwakhe” (Fili. 3:20, 21).

Xa ihlabathi lijongise ezantsi ukusinga ekupheleni, siyazi ukuba ilifu elincinci elimhlophe ngenye imini liyakuthi gqi kwimpuma yesibhakabhaka. Ngokuya lisondela ngokusondela, siyakubona ukuba kwelo lifu kuhleli “ofana noNyana woMntu, enesithsaba segolide entloko, esandleni sakhe enerhengqa elibukhali” (Isityhi. 14:14). UYesu uyakuphelekwa ngamawaka waka ezithunywa zezulu (Mat. 25:31), yaye onke amehlo ayakumbona Yena (Isityhi. 1:7). Xa Yena esehla, siyakukuva ukudanduluka kwaKhe, ukubiza kwexilongo likaThixo, aze amangcwaba abo balele kuKristu avuleke xa bevuka kuqala (1 Tesa. 4:16). Bayakulazi ilizwi lwaLowo ubabizayo (Yohane 5:28).

`Kuyakwenzeka ntoni emva koko? Funda ku-1 Tesalonike 4:17. Ekugqibeleni, oko abhale ngako uPawulos kwabaseFilipi 2:10, 11 kuyakuhlokoma kwelimiweyo liphela.`

Ingcinga emangalisa ngokumandla kanjalo, etshatsheleyo! Ngenye imini siyakumbona uYesu-ngokwenene, nyanisweni sibone Yena. Siyakuliva ilizwi laKhe, yaye siyakuvuma ukuba Yena uyiNkosi. Lowo sifundileyo ngaye, sithandaze kuye, sithethe ngaye kwabanye; Lowo iintliziyo zethu bezimlangazelela . . . siyakumbona ngokwenene ubuso ngobuso Yena. Sinokungagungqi siqiniseke ngoku, kuba uThixo uthembekile, kwaye nezithembiso zaKhe ziyinene (Isityhi. 22:6).

Ngelo xesha, xa amaxilongo nawo onke amehlo omntu ebona uYesu, siyakukwazi ukuba bekufuneka silindile. Wonke umthandazo ozingisileyo, lonke ixesha esiye sakhokelisa ixesha kunye naYe, lonke ixesha siye sathetha ngokuzimisela ngenxa yaKhe, sonke isilingo-siyakupheliswa kukubona ubuso baKhe (Isityhi. 22:4).
