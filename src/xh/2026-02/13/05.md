Sowukhe wabuzwa ukuba yeyiphi eyona nto ukhangele ngokumandla kuyo kunaphakade? Ukuba ubuza umntwana, usenokuthi, “ukukhwela ingwe,” “Ukwehla kwintamo yendlula-mthi,” ukubhabhela kumazwe ohlukeneyo.” Ukuba ubunokubuza umntwana osafikisayo, usengathi, “Ukungaphindi ndenza umsebenzi wesikolo kwakhona” okanye “ukuhlola izulu nabahlobo bam singakhange sonzakale.” Kwaye ukuba ubunokubuza iqela labantu abakhulu, bebenokuthi, “Ukuba kwindawo engasenantlungu, inkxwaleko, okanye ukufa” okanye “Ukudityaniswa kwakho nabasenyongweni.” Zonke ezi mpendulo zilungile ziyinyani, kwaye kuninzi kakhulu ekunokukhangela phambili kuko kwizulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha. Unaphakade uyavutha ngaphakathi ezintliziyweni zethu, kwaye, ngemvelo, siyazi ukuba ininzi into enokuba sebomini kunalapha ngoku.

`Zeziphi ezinye iintsikelelo esinokukhangela kuzo kunaphakade? Funda kuIsaya 25:8, Isityhilelo 7:17, neSityhilelo 21:4.`

Ngokuqinisekileyo eyona ntsikekelo ibalaseleyo yezulu iyakuba kukubona uYesu ekugqibeleni nokumbulela Yena, buqu, ngenxa yento Yena asenzele yona kwelihlabathi liwileyo. Siyakufuna ukubonisa ukubonga nokunqula Yena ngenxa yokusisindisa, ngokubandezeleka kwaKhe emnqamlezweni, kukufa okungunaphakade. “ ‘Kuyifanele iMvana eyaxhelwayo ukwamkela amandla, nobutyebi, nobulumko, nokomelela, nembeko, nozuko, nokubongwa!’ ” (Rev. 5:12, NKJV).

UYohane umBhaptizi wamazisa uYesu “njengeMvana kaThixo” (Yohane 1:35– 37). Abafundi baalandela ngokusondeleyo kuYe, kwaye neSityhilelo 14:4 sithi nathi siyakwenza oko. “Aba ngabo bayilandelayo iMvana apho isukuba isiya khona” (Isityhi. 14:4). Noko kunjalo, ukuba thina sifuna ukumlandela Yena ezulwini, kufuneka siqale ukumlandela apha emhlabeni Yena.

UYesu, iMvana, ukwanguMalusi wethu, kwaye Yena ukhokela indlela yethu ekungekho wumbi ungenza njalo. Oku kuyaqiniseka kwakhona kuthi lo gama sinkantsa kumaxesha anzima esizibhaqa sikuwo, kodwa uYesu akasoze ayeke ukusikhokela, nkqu nasezulwini. Isityhilelo 7:17 sithi, “ ‘ngokuba iMvana esesazulwini setrone iya kubalusa, ibakhokelele emithonjeni ephilileyo yamanzi.’ ” Njengabantu baKhe, izimvu zaKhe, siyakumlandela uYesu ezulwini, sifune ukuba kwindawo enaYe ngonaphakade. Uphawu olunye oluchaza abantu bakaThixo kukuba “igama lakhe libe semabunzini abo” (Isityhi. 22:4). Oko kukuthi, siyakuhlala ngalo lonke ixesha sicinga ngaYe.
