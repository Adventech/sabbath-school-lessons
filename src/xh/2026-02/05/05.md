---
title:  Intsikelelo Ephindaphindeneyo
date:   29/04/2026
---

Zininzi iindlela esinokuzifunda ngazo iiBhayibhile zethu, ezifana nendlela yendima nganye (esele ikhankanyiwe), ukufunda isahluko, ukufunda umxholo, ukufuna igama, okanye ukufunda incwadi. Sinako ukufunda ngesichazi-ndima kwakunye nesichazi-magama seBhayibhile ngaxa linye nothotho Lwembambano yamaPhakade (Conflict of the Ages) ukongeza umqondo. Sinako ukuthi chu ngenyawo phandle endalweni size simamele iBhayibhile esiyifundelwayo, okanye sihlangane nomhlobo okanye iqela elincinane ukuba sifunde kunye.

Kwangalendlela esiye sigcine ngayo ukuhlobana kwethu kuphilile ngezenzo eziziintlobo nezintsha, kufuneka sigcine idinga lamihla le noThixo lilitsha yaye linika umdla ngokusebenzisa iindlela ezohlukeneyo lo gama sifunda iBhayibhile yethu. Kusoloko kukunininzi emakufundwe!

Into eyakunceda ukugcina ixesha lakho lokufunda iBhayibhile linika umdla kukwabelana nabanye ngoko ukubhaqileyo. Xa sicazulula oko sikufundileyo, ukushwankathela, inkqubo yokubumba ihlanganisa iingcinga zethu. Oku kusinceda ukugcina ulwazi. Intsikelelo ephindaphindeneyo kukuba xa sisabelana sincokola nabanye, incoko yakwamoya iyakusoloko inomcel’ umngeni ize iniqinise nina nobabini. Kusoloko kuxa sisabelana okanye sifundisa abanye okuthi ukufunda okunzulu kwenzeke ezingqondweni zethu.

Kanjalo uyakufikelela ekuboneni ukuba oko ukufundayo mini nganye akungomyalezo wakho nje kuphela koko kukwa ngumyalezo nakwabanye.

`Funda uIsaya 50:4. Le ndima isixelela ntoni ngokuba nobudlelane noThixo kwakunye nendlela obunokuchaphazela ngayo ubudlelane bethu nabanye abantu?`

Ukufunda kwethu iBhayibhile buqu ukuqinisi nje thina koko kusenza sikwazi ukukhuthaza abanye esinokuhlangana nabo ngaloo mini. Kunokuba yintsikelelo entlantlu-mbini.

Ubomi bethu basemoyeni lugqatso. Cela iNkosi ukuba ikugcine ubaleka ugqatso lwakho ngokuthe gqolo, amehlo akho ethe ntsho kumgomo (Fil. 3:14). Sukuziva utyhafile xa ukhe wabuyekelela okwethutyana, koko yenza naziphi iinguqu ebomini bakho ukugcina ubudlelane bakho, bunika umdla, kwaye ngokukodwa ixesha lakho lokufunda iBhayibhile nelokuthandaza. Kuba ngokwenene, bubo obu ubomi obungunaphakade, ukuba simazi uThixo namhlanje (Yohane 17:3). Ukuzibophelela kwethu mihla le ukuba sihlale kuYe nakwiLizwi laKhe kuguqula ubomi.

`Funda indima yenkumbulo yale veki uze ugqale intsingiselo yayo. Ufunda ntoni ngoku? Ungabelana nabani ngoku?`
