---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   01/05/2026
---

Injongo yokufunda iBhayibhile kukwazi uThixo kwanokukhula kubudlelane baKhe kunye naYe, kuba oku yile nto ubomi obungunaphakade buyiyo-ukuba kunye naYe lowo simthandayo (Yohane 5:39, Yohane 17:3).

Inxalenye yabo nabuphi ubudlelane kukuzibophelela okumacala. Sifunda kwiSityhilelo 3:30 ukuba uYesu ufuna ukuba noku kunye nathi, sekunjalo kufuneka siqonde ukuba, njengezidalwa, sinako ukusoloko sifunda ngakumbi ngoMdali wethu. Njengesimba-mgodi sisomba amatye anqabileyo exabiso, kufuneka sisoloko siphengulula iBhayibhile rhoqo. Kusoloko kukuninzi okunokubhikicwa, nokuba zininzi kangakanani na izihlandlo esizifunde ngawo amabali athile okanye izicatshulwa zeBhayibhile. “Nokuba ingayintoni na inkqubela-phambili yengqondo yomntu, makangakhe acinge nangamnye umzuzu ukuba akukho sidingo sokuphengulula iziBhalo ngokucokisekileyo nangokuthe rhoqo ukuze kufumaneke ukukhanya okuthe kratya. Njengabantu, sibiziwe njengeziqu ukuba sibe ngabafundi besiprofeto.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 41.

Kufuneka kanjalo singazameli ukwenza iBhayibhile ingene gingci kwiimbono okanye iingcinga zethu zobuntu. “Singaziphengulula njani iziBhalo? Masibeke emngciphekweni imfundiso yethu enye emva kwenye, size emva sizame ukwenza isiBhalo sikhawulelane neembono zethu ezingagungqiyo? okanye masithathe iingcamango nezimvo zethu sizise kwiziBhalo, size silinganise iimfundiso zethu macal’ onke ngeziBhalo zenyaniso? Intlaninge yabo bafunda de bafundise iBhayibhile, abayiqondi inyaniso exabisekileyo abayifundisayo okanye abayifundayo. . . . Abaninzi banika amazwi esiBhalo intsingiselo ekholisa iimbono zabo.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 36.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Simo sini ovamise ukuza ngaso eBhayibhileni? Ingaba ikhona into na into ekufuneka ishenxile? Kutheni le nto isimo sentobeko nokuzinikela eLizwini zibaluleke kakhulu nje?`

`Ingaba zikhona na iimbono ezingagungqiyo ekudingeka uzikhwelelise bucala ukuvumela isiBhalo ukuba sizithethele ngokunokwaso? Ukuba kunjalo, ungaqalisa njani ukuthandaza ngazo kanye ngoku?`

`Undlela-ntsha angangumqobo njani kubudlelane bangaphandle bomntu noThixo? Oko kukuthi, ulangazelelo lokubhaqa into entsha nephuculiweyo lungamlahlekisa njani umntu, ngokukodwa ukuba oko ukwenzela iinjongo ezinomna phakathi?`

**Isishwankathelo:** Ukuzifundela iBhayibhile buqu kusembindini wokuba nobudlelane obuphilileyo nobomeleleyo noThixo. ILizwi likaThixo lithetha nobomi bethu benkulungwane yamashumi amabini ananye ngeendlela ezibalulekileyo, kwanjengokuba lenzile kwiinkulungwane ezidlulileyo. Ngendlela efanayo yokugcina nabuphi ubuhlobo buphila, kufuneka sikhangele iindlela zokugcina ubomi bethu bonqulo buphilile njengoko sihleli kuYesu, sithembe isithembiso saKhe sokuba iLizwi laKhe “ ‘aliyi kubuyela kum lilambatha, lingakwenzanga endikuthandayo, lingakuphumelelisanga oko ndilithumela kuko’ ” (Isa. 55:11).
