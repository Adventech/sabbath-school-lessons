date:   27/04/2026

`UYesu ungumzekelo ogqibeleleyo ezintweni zonke, kwaye xa kufikelelwa kunqulo lomntu buqu, akho nto yahlukileyo. UMarko 1:35 usixelela ntoni ngexesha likaYesu noThixo?`

Nangona le iyindima nje enye, singafunda okuninzi apha ngomzekelo kaYesu. Kwantlandlolo ngaphambi kokuba ilanga lithi qheke, Yena wahamba (wazisusa Yena kwimaxhaphetshu nentshukumo yobomi bemihla ngemihla) ukuba abe kwindawo ebucala ethe cwaka ukuze Yena akwazi ukuba noYise waKhe. Khawufan’ ucinge eso siganeko-uYesu, ehleli ngakuLwandle lweGalili okanye ngakwicala leliwa, ethandaza encokola noYise waKhe ngaphambi kokuba ihlabathi elimngqongileyo livuke? Nangona le ndima ichaza ukuzibophelela kukaYesu emthandazweni, sibona ngokuselubala ukuba yayi yinto esemqoka kuYe. Akuthandabuzeki ukuba eli xesha yayi lilo elimnika amandla Yena ukuba ajamelane nako konke awakunyamezelayo Yena. Ukuba uYesu waadinga oku ukuba aqale usuku ngalunye, kubekisele phi kuthi?

UThixo usixelela ukuba, “ ‘Funani ubuso bam,’ ” yaye Yena unethemba lokuba impendulo yethu izakuthi “ ‘Ubuso bakho, Yehova, ndiya kubufuna’ ” (Ndum. 27:8).

`Zithini i- 1 Zikronike 16:11 ngendlela emasibufune ngayo ubuso baKhe?`

Unayo indawo ongaya kuyo qho kusasa ukuba ube kunye noThixo? Mhlawumbi ungafumana isitulo ngasefestileni, indawo ezolileyo phandle, okanye nkqu netafile yasekhitshini, apho unokuya khona mihla le ukuba uhlale ezinyaweni zikaYesu ukuba ufunde eLizwini likaThixo. Ukuhlala ezinyaweni zikaYesu yeyona ndawo engcono onokuba kuyo (Luka 10:39–42). Ngokwenza isimbo samihla le sokuya kwindawo ethile ukuya kuchitha ixesha noThixo, uyakuba namathuba amakhulu okubuyela apho mihla le. Sukutyhafa xa uphosa usuku pha naphaya, kuba iingxaki ziyavela, kwaye usenokungakwazi ukuchitha ixesha noThixo. Kodwa zama ukuba ungavumeli uthotho lweentsuku, iiveki, okanye iinyanga ukuba zidlule ungalichithanga ixesha kunye noThixo. Khumbula ukuba, ukuba nobudlelane obumileyo kunye noThixo sisigqibo samihla le, eso onokusiqalisa kwakhona, namhlanje, ukuba ukhetha njalo

`Kule veki idlulileyo, lingakanani ixesha olichithele emthandazweni nasekufundeni iBhayibhile? Impendulo yakho ikuxelela ntoni ngeenguqu ekunokufuneka uzenzile kwizinto ezisemqoka kuwe?`
