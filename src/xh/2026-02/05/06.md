---
title:  ’Kumnandi!
date:   30/04/2026
---

Cinga ngesibiliboco osithandayo. Siyilungele impilo yakho? Mhlawumbi usebenzisa ubusi njengento yokumnandisa, okanye usengasebenzisa nkqu nobusi bakwaManuka ngenxa yamaqithiqithi ayo onyango ababazwayo. Ukuba wakha walizama ikhepheza lobusi, uyakuyazi indlela okumnandi ngayo ukwakhiwa kwayo okuthambileyo xa linyibilikka elulwimini lwakho.

KwiNdumiso 119:103, 104, iBhayibhile izichaza njengekhepheza lobusiisifaniso sobumnandi: “Hayi, ukuba mnandi kwentetho yakho ekhuhlangubeni lam, igqithe nobusi emlonyeni wam! Ngeziyalezo zakho ndinokuqonda.”

`Kuthetha ukuthini ukuba “Ngeziyalezo zakho ndinokuqonda”? (Ndum. 119:104). Kutheni le nto le ngcamango ibalulekile ekuqondeni oko ufundo lweBhayibhile lusenzela kona?`

Ewe, amazwi kaThixo ngokwenene amnandi kwimiphefumlo yethu yaye awafani nayo nayiphi na into engenye ihlabathi eliyinunusayo. Ngokungafaniyo nentlaninge yezibiliboco, ubumnandi beLizwi likaThixo buyaphilisa kwimiphefumlo yethu buze buguqule ubomi kwizimilo zethu. Ukuba ubuchasele kuThixo, usengawa emadolweni, uvule iLizwi laKhe, uze usele kumanzi aphilileyo ekuphela kwawo anokwanelisa.

**KuIsaya 55:1-13, umprofeti uyanaba kumyalezo okhankanywe ngentla. Chitha ixesha elithile ufunda esi sahluko ngoku uze uphendule le mibuzo:**

- Yintoni uYehova ayinika abo beza kuYe, ukuba “badle” kwiLizwi laKhe?
- Sithini isimemo saKhe apha kuwe?
- Uthini umcela-mngeni waKhe?
- Lithini idinga laKhe?

` `

ILizwi likaThixo eliphilileyo nelinamandla ligqobhoza ngqo ezintliziyweni zethu, iingqondo, nemiphefumlo njengoko lisicela umngeni wokukhula kuKristu. Kodwa linokukwenza oku kuthi kuphela xa sithatha ixesha nenzame (kwaye ewe, kuthatha umgudu) ekupicotheni iLizwi sikwisimo solulamo nentobeko kwakunye nentumekelelo yokulandela oko likufundisayo.

`Zeziphi iindlela eziluqilima “onokufuna ngazo uYehova esenokufumaneka” (Isa. 55:6)?`
