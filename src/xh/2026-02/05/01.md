### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Yohane 15:1–8, Marko 1:35, 1 Zikro. 16:11, Ndum. 119:105, Isa. 50:4, Isa 55:1–13.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Liya kuba njalo ilizwi lam eliphuma emlonyeni wam; aliyi kubuyela kum lilambatha, lingakwenzanga endikuthandayo, lingakuphumelelisanga oko ndilithumela kuko’ ” (Isaya 55:11).

Cinga emva xa wawufumana iBhayibhile yakho yokuqala. Mhlawumbi wawungumntwana, kwaye yayinikezelwe kuwe sisizalwene esingumKristu. Okanye mhlawumbi wazithengela yona ungumntu omdala. Nokuba kudala kangakanani na uneBhayibhile yakho (mhlawumbi uneekopi ezingaphezu kwesinye), gqala ixabiso olibeka kule ncwadi. Yenye yezinto onazo ezixabisekileyo, ezithandwayo, okanye uyithatha buyekeyeke into yokuba ube neLizwi eliphilileyo likaThixo ezintupheni zakho? Uyasokola ukuba uthi gqolo ekulifundeni? Wakhe wazibuza: Ndiqale phi? Ndiyifunde njani le Ncwadi ukuze ndikhule ekusondeleni kuThixo?

UMartin Luther wathi, “Kangangeqela le minyaka ndiyifunde kabini ngoku iBhayibhile ngonyaka. Ukuba iBhayibhile ibingumthi omkhulu, onamandla ngendiwafunxe onke amasebe awo, ndinyamekele ukwazi ukuba kukho ntoni khona kwaye iyintoni elizakuza nayo.”

Nokuba ungcamla ixesha eliyimpumelelo, la mihla le lokufunda iBhayibhile, okanye nokuba iBhayibhile yakho ihleli ngungqikana osoloko evaliwe kwishelufu yeencwadi, inyaniso kukuba sonke sinako ukuqalisa amaxesha ofundo lweBhayibhile aluqilima kunye noThixo. Kule veki sizakuqwalasela ezinye zendlela eziphathekayo zokufunda iLizwi likaThixo ngcono.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Meyi 2._
