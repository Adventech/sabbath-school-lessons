---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   15/05/2026
---

“Ukuba besicinga sincokola kakhulu ngoYesu, kube kancinci ngomna, singaba nobukho baKhe ngokuthe kratya mpela (Yakobi 5:13–16). “Ukuba sicebisana neentandabuzo nezoyikiso zethu, okanye sizama ukusombulula yonke into esingenako ukuyibona ngokucacileyo, ngaphambi kokuba sibe nokholo, iinkohla ziyakwanda kwaye zendele kuphela. Kodwa ukuba siza kuThixo, siziva singama-athalala sixhomekekile, njengoko sinjalo ngokwenene, size ngokholo oluthobekileyo, olukholosileyo siyixele iminqweno yethu kuYe lowo olwazi lungaphele ndawo, obona yonke into endalweni, kwaye olawula yonke into ngentando yaKhe nelizwi, Yena unako yaye uyakusihoya isikhalo sakhe, kwaye uyakuvumela izikhanyiso zibengezele ezintliziyweni zethu.”—Steps to Christ, pp. 96, 97. “Umphefumlo unokunyukela kufutshane nezulu ngeempiko zokubonga. UThixo unqulwa ngengoma nomculo kwiinkundla zangasentla, kwaye ngokuya sivakalisa umbulelo wethu sisondela kunqulo lwezihlwela zasezulwini. . . . Masisondele ngovuyo olunentlonelo phambi koMdali wethu, ‘ngemibulelo nezwi lohadi.’ Isaiah 51:3.”—Steps to Christ. p. 104.

**Imibuzo Yenkumbulo:**

`Yeyiphi ingcamango kwizicatshulwa ezingentla ekuvuselela kakhulu? Yeyiphi ingcamango ekucela mngeni kakhulu?`

`Zeziphi ezinye izifundo esinokuzifunda kubomi bomthandazo babanye abaseBhayibhileni? (Bona kuEzra 10:1; 2 Kumkani 13:4; Yona 4:2, 3; Hab. 3:1; 2 Kumkani 19:14–19; Yer. 32:16–25, Neh. 1:4–11, 1 Kumkani 8:22–54, ukukhankanya nje abambalwa.)`

`Yintoni indima yozilo oluhambelana nomthandazo?`

`Ikhona enye into entsha ongathanda ukuyitshintsha okanye uyihlomele kubomi bakho bomthandazo ngenxa yesifundo sale veki? Kutheni ungenzi zinguqu?`

**Isishwankathelo:** IBhayibhile yabelana ngeengxelo zabantu ababenobomi bomthandazo obusemandleni, obumileyo kwakunye nabo bangabanga njalo. Xa siphengulula amaphepha ayo, siyakusoloko sifumana umntu esinokuzayamanisa naye, nokuba buthini na ubume bobudlelane bethu noThixo. Siyakufumana kanjalo intlaninge, ngentlaninge yezithembiso ezizakusikhuthaza zisikhokele kubomi bethu bonqulo. Ukukhula ngokwasemoyeni makube ngumgomo wethu-amehlo ethu ekuYesu, umzekelo ongowona kwizinto zonke kwakunye neMbangi noMgqibelelisi wokholo lwethu.
