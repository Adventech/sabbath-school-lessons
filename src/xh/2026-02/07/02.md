---
title:  UEliya-Ukuthandaza Ngexesha Lentlekele
date:   10/05/2026
---

UEliya othembekileyo wayephila kumaxesha angendawo, uKumkani uAhabhi egabadela “ukumqumbisa uYehova uThixo wakwaSirayeli, ngaphezu kwabo bonke ookumkani bakwaSirayeli ababengaphambi kwakhe (1 Kumkani 16:33). Ngokuqinisekileyo ezona zigaba zivus’ umxhelo ebomini bakhe zeza kunomji waseNtabeni yeKarmele (bona kwi-1 Kumkani 18). Kuvutho-ndaba weli bali limangalisayo, uAhabhi kwakunye nobukumkani bakhe bazibonela ngawabo amehlo inyaniso yokuba uThixo uyawuphendula umthandazo. Esi yaba sisigaba esingalibalekiyo kwimbali kaSirayeli kwaye sisizathu sokuba ukuguquka okukhawulezileyo kweziganeko kweli bali kusifumana singacingelanga.

`Funda ku-1 Kumkani 19:1-18, uphawula ngokutsolileyo imithandazo kaEliya kwakunye nonxibelelwano lukaThixo kunye naye. Yintoni ekwingxam yokudandatheka kukaEliya apha? Impendulo kaThixo yahluke njani koko kwenzekayo kwiNtaba yeKarmele?`

Kubo bonke ububanzi bosuku olunye, nangona uThixo ephendula wonke umthandazo kwimithandazo kaEliya, isimo sasemphefumlweni, engqondweni, nasemzimbeni kumprofeti kungekudala siyatshintsha. Nangona uEliya waxhamla uloyiso olukhulu ngoThixo ngala mhla, wathi yena, kwithutyana lokudinwa, wavumela ukoyika ukufa koyisa ukholo lwakhe kuThixo. Okutsal’ umdla ngeli bali kukuba, nangona uEliya woonganyelwa kukungabi nathemba nokutyhafa, uThixo weza kuye ngokuthantamisa nenkathalo, ebonelela ngokutya nangamanzi kwakhona (1 Kumkani 19:5, 6)—kangangokuba yena waaxhathisa kangangeentsuku ezingama-40 nobusuku obungama-40 (1 Kumkani 19:8). Kwaye ngethuba uThixo ekugqibeleni wazityhilayo ngokwaKhe, kwakungendlela eyahluke mpela kunawayenze ngayo Yena kuqala.

Ngamanye amaxesha ebomini bethu, uThixo uphendula ngeendlela ezithe ngqo kakhulu, ezinamandla, nezingenakuphikwa. Oku kuqinisa ukholo lwethu, size sikuve ukusondela kukaThixo ebomini bethu.

Ngamanye amaxesha siyaxengaxenga size soyiswe sisihendo, sicinge ukuba kunzima ngeyona ndlela ukulandela uThixo ngokholo olungagungqiyo. Sikhangela iimpendulo zikaThixo ngeendlela esilindele ukuba aphendule ngazo Yena, singaqondi ukuba iingcinga neendlela zaKhe ziphakame mpela kwaye zilumkile kunezethu (Isa. 55:8, 9). Kwanjengokuba kukuninzi okusendalweni kaThixo esingakuqondiyo, masingakhwankqiswa yinto yokuba kuninzi kanjalo okukwiindlela zikaThixo esingakuqondiyo.

`UThixo, uBawo wethu olungileyo nonobubele, ukwazi nqo okudingayo. Ungazola kangakanani ngokwaneleyo ukuba ukholose ngaYe yaye uwagcine amehlo akho ethe ntshoo kuYe maxa onke? Thetha kunye naYe malunga noku ngoku.`
