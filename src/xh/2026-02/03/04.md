---
title:  UMoses, Umkhonzi Othobekileyo
date:   14/04/2026
---

Amaholo ambejembeje aseYiputa ayedlisela ngobunewunewu, uyolo, nokonwaba. “ ‘Waqeqeshwa ke uMoses kubo bonke ubulumko baMayiiputa; wayeke eyinkunkqela ngamazwi nangemisebenzi’ ” (Zenzo 7:22). Ubomi bobunganga, ubutyebi, nodumo babu bobakhe ukuba abuxhamle, noko kunjalo uMoses waakhetha into eyahluke mpela. “Njengesazi-mbali, imbongi, incubi-buchopho, injengele yemikhosi, kwakunye nomwisi-mthetho, utshatshele ngokungenantanga. Kodwa nangona ihlabathi laliphambi kwakhe, waba nesibindi sokwala imibono ekhohlisayo yobutyebi nobuhandiba nodumo, ‘enyula kanye ukuba aphathwe kakubi, ndawonye nabantu bakaThixo, kunokuba axhamle izono okomzuzwana.’”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 246.

`AmaHebhere 11:24-26 asixelela ntoni ngesizathu sokuba uMoses akhethe indlela eyahlukileyo aze azithobe yena?`

Intobeko yamva kaMoses iyamangalisa, xa uqwalasela indoda enobunganga awayeyiyo kwakunye nendawo awayesuka kuyo. Noko kunjalo, ngenxa yesenzo sokungxama esinesono (Eksod. 2:12), waaphulukana nokuzithemba kwakhe nokwaneliseka kwakhe. Iintaba ziziindonga zamagumbi akhe okufundela nekratshi litshayelelwe bucala, kangangeminyaka engama-40 uMoses waahlohlwa nguThixo oko wayedinga ukukwazi ukuze akhokele uhlanga oluphuma ebukhobokeni lusinge kwiLizwe leDinga. Amandla nobutyebi kwento engeyibe bobunye ubomi eYiputa zaambatsha ngokwexabiso xa uMoses uqwalasela unaphakade. UThixo waambiza ngokutsole ngeyona ndlela, waza uMoses walandela.

Mhlawumbi okubaluleke ngeyona ndlela xa sijonge lo mxholo, iNumeri 12:3 isixelela ukuba: “(Ke indoda le inguMoses ibilulamile Kunene kunabantu bonke abasemhlabeni).” UMoses, elinye lamanyange atshatsheleyo eBhayibhile, waziwa ngentobeko yakhe, ubulali. Qwalasela indlela obabunokwehluka ngayo ubomi nobunkokheli bakhe xa ikratshi belinyubeleze kuso ngasinye kwezi ziganeko zikhulu ebomi bakhe: ityholo elivuthayo, izibetho zaseYiputa, ukuwela ulwandle olubomvu, ukuwa kwemana isuka ezulwini, ukuthetha ngqo noThixo, ukwamkela iMithetho eliShumi, kwakunye nokuva amazwi kaThixo emva kokuba uMoses ebethe iliwa.

`Jonga ngemva kubomi bakho. Ukuba umntu ebenokuchaza wena, ebenokubandakanya “ukuthobeka” okanye “ubulali” kuluhlu lwezichazi? Ngoba okanye kutheni engenako? Inyaniso kukuba asikwazi kuthobeka thina ngokunokwethu. Isono yinxalenye yobomi bethu, yiyo lo nto siding uYesu ngeyona ndlela. Mamela okanye ufunde amazwi akwiculo “Kungcono ndibe noYesu” uze uqwalasele la mazwi ngokungqamene nobomi bukaMoses kwakunye nobakho.`
