---
title:  Isikhubekiso Esikhulu
date:   15/04/2026
---

Khawufan’ ucinge ungumfundi kaYesu. Uhamba kunye naYe, udla kunye naYe, ulala ecaleni kwaKhe, yaye ufunde kuYe lo gama Yena aguqula intlaninge yobomi, kuquka nobakho. Abantu babhenela kuYe, uze uqonde indlela ekukhetheke ngayo ukuba Yena achonge wena ukuba ube ngomnye kwabali-12 abasondeleyo kuYe. Emva koko uqale uzibuze: Ngubani ngokwenene omkhulu kubafundi?

`KuLuka 22:24-27, funda impendulo kaYesu kwimbambano yabafundi malunga nentsingiselo yobukhulu.Yeyiphi intetho enye exhakamfula intliziyo kumyalezo kaYesu apha?`

Ubani wayenokucinga ukuba emva kwalo lonke eli xesha ndisondele kuYesu, olu hlobo lwengxoxo-mpikiswano lwakuba yinto yokugqibela ezingqondweni zabo. Kodwa oko ayikuko okwenzekayo.

Endaweni yokuba la madoda aneliseke lubizo lwawo, ikratshi laavumbuluka ezintliziyweni zawo lafikelela apho omnye wazibona engcono kunabanye. Kulula ukuvumela ezoo ngcinga ukuba zongame ezingqondweni zethu. Kodwa siyaxelelwa ukuba “akukho nto ihlupha uThixo okanye eyingozi ngeyona ndlela kumphefumlo womntu njengekratshi nokwaneliseka. Kuzo zonke izono sesona singenathemba kakhulu, sesona singanyangekiyo.”—Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 154.

Oku kunobuzaza kuthi. Ikratshi lethu liyamhlungisa uThixo ngaphezu kwayo nayiphi na enye into, kwaye luphawu lwesimilo ekunzima ukuloyisa kuba sivamise ukungaliboni into eliyiyo. Kwimeko yethu yokwaneliseka, sikhetha ukungazibhenci buqu, ngenxa yokuba ikratshi lingukumkani ngokuqinisekileyo. Kufuneka sime, sizibone undonakele buqu, size simcele uThixo asivule amehlo kwimeko yethu yokwenene, kuba ikratshi linokuba sesona sizathu esisenza singabi nabudlelane buqhogeneyo noThixo namhlanje.

`Ukuba uyaqonda ukuba nguThixo yedwa onokwenza umsebenzi wokususa ikratshi nokuzicingela emphefumlweni wakho, nqumama uze uthandaze lo mthandazo kanye ngoku: “Nkosi, thatha intliziyo yam; kuba andikwazi kuyinikezela.Yinto yaKho.Yigcine nyulu, kuba andikwazi kuyigcinela Wena. Ndisindise phakathi kwesiqu sam, isiqu sam esibuthathaka, esingafaniyo noKristu. Ndibumbe, ndilungise, ndiphakamise ndibe kwindawo enyulu nengcwele, apho khona umsinga otyebileyo wothando lwaKho unokuqukuqela kumphefumlo wam.”—Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 159.`
