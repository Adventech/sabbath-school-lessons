title:  Khangela kuYe
date:   16/04/2026
---

`Funda kuLuka 22:27 kwakhona. Uthini umyalezo ongundoqo wabo bonke abalandeli bakaKristu apha?`

Ngokwahluke mpela kumnqweno wabafundi wokuba zizitatanyiswa kwakunye nenkolo yabo yokuba bangcono kunomnye lo, sibona uYesu-owona mzekelo wokuqina wentobeko, UYesu, owathi, “Ke mna phakathi kwenu ndinjengomlungisele’ ” (Luka 22:27). UYesu, owayesithi mihla le anikezele kwabo basweleyo bamngqongileyoYena ngenxa yokuba wayezaliswe yimfesane kwaye nezihlwele wazibona njengeegusha ezingenamalusi. Yena wayesazi ukuba uluntu luyamdinga Yena ngaphezu kwayo yonke enye into ebomini, nangona iliqaqobana elayiqondayo le nyaniso ilula. UYesu, owancama izulu ukuze afele uhlanga loluntu ngethemba lokuba luyakusiqonda isenzo saKhe sobabalo luze lusabele kwisimemo saKhe sokuba nobudlelane kunye naYe.

`Funda kwabaseFilipi 2:3-8. Oku kusixelela ntoni ngendlela emasiphile ngayo xa siqwalasele uMnqamlezo?`

UYesu waakwenza konke. Waathwala konke. Xa sinqumama ithuba elide kakhulu ukuba simbone Yena-ngokwenene nangokunyulu-asikwazi kuzibamba ekuqondeni ukungahlambuluki kwethu, ukungcola kwethu, kwakunye nesidingo sethu esimandla saKhe ebomini bethu.

Xa sijonga kuYe, yonke enye into (ngokukodwa iziqu zethu kwakunye nobukhulu bethu esiziqambele bona) iyambatsha ingabaluleki konke konke. Lowo anguYe uYesu, oko Yena akwenzileyo, kwakunye nendlela ayithanda ngayo indalo yaKhe kuba phambili nasembindini. Umna ngokuqinisekileyo uyakuthi shwaka xa sikhangele kuYe.

UYesu. Igama elimnandi, elinamandla ngolo hlobo. Yena uyingxam yentobeko. Xa iintliziyo zethu ezivulekileyo zifunda ngaYe, xa sikuqonda oko Yena asenzele kona, naxa sivumela amazwi aKhe obomi ukuba athontsize ezingqondweni zethu, siyaziqonda indlela esikhukhumele nesizindwayinge ngayo ngokwenene. Ukuba abafundi baKhe, ababephila nabaafundayo kuYe, babesokoliswa likratshi, asinakuzibhanxa ngelithi sahlukile. Ekugqibeleni, sinokukhula kubudlelane bethu noYesu kuphela xa sithobekile.

`Chitha ixesha elongezelekileyo kunye naYe kanye ngoku. Thatha iBhayibhile yakho, usiba, kwakunye nencwadi yeziganeko okanye iphepha elithile uze ufumane indawo ezolileyo- mhlawumbi nkqu naphandle. Mema uThixo ukuba athambise aze athethe nentliziyo yakho. Bhala iNdumiso 138, igama negama. Lo gama ubhala, ngawaphi amazwi abalasele ngakumbi kuwe?`
