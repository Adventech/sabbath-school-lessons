---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   17/04/2026
---

“Ngokuya sisondela kuYesu yaye kokukona sibona ngokucacileyo ubumsulwa besimilo saKhe, kokukona siyakubuqonda ngokucacileyo ububi obubalaseleyo besono kwaye kokukona kuyakuncipha ukunqwenela ukuphakamisa iziqu zethu. Abo izulu libaqonda njengabo bangcwele ngabokugqibela ukubonakalisa ukulunga kwabo.” — Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 160.

“Ngaphambi kwembeko lululamo. Ukugcwalisa indawo ethe qabavu phambi kwabantu, iZulu likhetha umsebenzi othi, njengoYohane umBhaptizi, athathe indawo ephantsi phambi koThixo. Oyena mfundi ufana nomntwana ngoyena unenkqubela ekusebenzeleni uThixo. Izenzeleleli zezulu zinokusebenzisana naye lowo unqwenela, hayi ukuphakamisa umna, koko ukusindisa imiphefumlo. . . .

“Xa abantu beziphakamisa, beziva ngathi basisidingo sokuphumelelisa icebo likaThixo elikhulu, iNkosi yenza ukuba bathi guu bucala. . .

“Kwakunganele ukuba abafundi bakaYesu bafundiswe ngemo yobukumkani baKhe. Oko babekudinga yayi kukutshintsha kwentliziyo okwakuzakubenza babe kulungelelwano nemimiselo yabo. . . . Ukusulungeka, ukuzilibala, kwakunye nothando oluthembayo lwemveku ezo ziimpawu iZulu elizixabisileyo. Ezi ziimpawu zobukhulu bokwenene. . . .

“Umphefumlo onyanisekileyo, ozohlwayayo uxabisekile emehlweni kaThixo. Ubeka uphawu lwaKhe ebantwini, hayi ngokwenqanaba labo, hayi ngokobutyebi babo, hayi ngokobukhulu bobukrelekrele babo, koko ngokumanyana kwabo kunye noKristu.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, pp. 436, 437

**Imibuzo Yenkumbulo:**

`Zeziphi ezinye iingcamango ezongezelekileyo ezinazo ezindima zilandelayo ngekratshi nentobeko? Mateyu 23:12; Ndumiso 25:9; Ndumiso 149:4; noYakobi 4:6, 10.`

`Cinga ngokunyanisekileyo: Ugqibele nini “ukubonakalisa ukulunga kwakho”? Oku kwaba nefuthe njani kubudlelane bakho noThixo okanye abo wawukubonakalise phambi kwabo?`

`Yintoni ongadinga ukuyitshintsha ebomini bakho ukuze uzithobe phambi koThixo ukuze uqinise uhambo lwakho kunye naYe?`

**Isishwankathelo:** Ikratshi linokuba ngomnye wemiqobo emikhulu kakhulu ekukhuleni kubudlelane noThixo. Ukuba siziva sanelisekile kwaye singakuqondi ukudinga kwethu obu budlelane, ngokuphandle nje asoze sabulandela. Ngokwahlukileyo, uYesu wayengoyena Mntu uthobekileyo emhlabeni kwanowona mzekelo ugqibeleleyo wendlela yokuba nobudlelane obuqhogeneyo noThixo.
