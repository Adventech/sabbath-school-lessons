title:  Sazi Isiqu Sakho
date:   13/04/2026
---

Amadoda amabini aya enkonzweni ukuya kuthandaza. Enye yindod’ enkulu ehlonitshwayo ema ekuqaleni ngaphambi kokuqala kwenkonzo, ngaphambi kwebandla ukuze babenako ukumbona. Iyathandaza ikhwaza, ibulela uThixo ngokulunga kwayo ekucingelayo. Enye indoda, ichwethelwe bucala ekuhlaleni, imi kanye ngemva ebandleni. Amehlo ayo aluzizi ziinyembezi ngenxa yobunzima obusemagxeni wakhe ngenxa yesono. Kwikona esemva ebandleni, uwa emadolweni, esebeza ngenxa yokhathazeko, “Nceda, Nkosi, yiba netarhu kum, mna moni!”

`Funda kuLuka 18:9-14. Ucinga ntoni ngala madoda mabini? Sifundo sini esilapha esibalulekileyo kuthi sonke?`

Kulula kuthi ukuziphakamisa. Ngamanye amaxesha iba yimvelo yethu yesibini ukwazisa abanye ngeempumelelo zethu nendlela thina esiphucuke ngayo. Kodwa ezi zinto, kuzo ngokunokwazo, azenzi mehluko kudumo lwethu ngokwamehlo ezulu. Eneneni, kona kona kuyakhabana noko sinokukucinga, ngenxa yokuba “ ‘wonke umntu oziphakamisayo uyakuthotywa; nozithobayo ke uyakuphakanyiswa’ ” (Luka 18:14). UYesu ukwasicebisa ukuba sithathe isihlalo esiphantsi uze uvumele usotheko akuphakamisa xa yena ebona njalo (Luka 14:8–10). Obu bukumkani buphequkileyo buchasene noko thina sikulindeleyo. “Kuphela ngulo uzazi yena njengomoni anokuthi uYesu amsindise.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 158.

Ekuqaleni siqonde ubume bethu bokuba nesono kwakunye nokudinga kwethu uKristu okumandla, sinako ukuza kuYe sisazi ngengqiniseko ukuba “sithi sizivume izono zethu, uthembekile, ulilungisa, ukuba asixolele izono zethu, asihlambulule kuko konke ukungalungisi” (1 Yohane 1:9).

Ngokuya sisondela kuKristu, kokukona siqonda ukuba nesono kwethu nokungafaneleki. “Kungendlela enye kuphela ekunokuthi ukuzazi buqu kufikelelwe. Kufuneka sibone uKristu. Kukungamazi Yena okubenza abantu babe semanqwanqweni ngokwasebulungiseni babo.”—Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 159.

Ngoko ke, uThixo ucinga ntoni ngabakhukhumali? UPetros wokuqala 5:5 usixelela ukuba “uThixo uyabachasa abanekratshi, abababale ke abazithobileyo.” Ayinakucaca ngaphezu koku.

`Ugqibele nini ukungcamla ubabalo lukaThixo ebomini bakho? (Ngokwenene, kufuneka silungcamle mihla le olu babalo.) Kufuneka kanjalo sibonakalise ubabalo kwabanye. Chitha ixesha emthandazweni kanye ngoku, ucele uThixo akuthobe ngaphantsi kwesandla saKhe esinamandla, ukuze Yena yedwa akuphakamise ngexesha elifanelekileyo.`
