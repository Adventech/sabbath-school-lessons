---
title:  Ukholo Aluyomvakalelo
date:   19/05/2026
---

UYesu wathi ukuba unokholo oluncinane kangangokhozo lwemostade, uyakususa iintaba (Mat. 17:20). Ukuba wakha walubona ukhozo lwemostade, uyayazi indlela oluluncinci ngayo. Noko kunjalo ukuba nolukholo luncinane kungenza inguqu enkulu ngolo hlobo. Makube kanti ukholo, ngoko, lubaluleke ngeyona ndlela kwaye malume namandla luqine ngokwaneleyo ukuze lwenze into engaphezu kobuntu. Noko kunjalo, kwanjengokuba ukhozo lwemostade lunokukhula lube ngumthi omkhulu (Mat. 13:31, 32), ukholo lwethu kufuneka lukhule lungami ngxi.

Ngokwenene, kwasekuqala sidinga umlinganiselo wokholo ukuze sibe nobudlelane noThixo (bona kuRom. 12:3).

`Asixelela ntoni amaEfese 2:8 ngendima yokholo ekusindisweni? Kutheni le nto umntu engenakuthi ngokufanelekileyo, “Andinakholo kuba uThixo akandinikanga naluphi na”?`

Kufuneka siqonde kuqala ukuba ukholo olunto iphathekayo; kukusabela komntu okuxhokonxwe nguMoya oyiNgcwele. UThixo ngumsunguli onobabalo, othi, ngoMoya oyiNgcwele, asitsalele kuYe xa simvumela Yena ukuba enze njalo (Yer. 31:3). Sisindiswe ngokholo, olukukusabela kubabalo lukaThixo esilunikwe ngokufa kukaYesu. Sisindiswe kuba sikholelwa kuThixo ngenxa yobabalo lwaKhe. Oku kungqengqe embindini wokuba nobudlelane kunye naYe.

Okulandelayo, kufuneka sikhumbule ukuba ukholo oluyomvakalelo. “Abaninzi abalusebenzisi ukholo olulilungelo noxanduva lwabo ukuba balusebenzise, besoloko belinde loo mvakalelo enokuziswa lukholo kuphela. Imvakalelo ayilokholo. . . . Ukholo lolwethu ukuba silusebenzise, kodwa imvakalelo enemihlali kwakunye nentsikelelo zezikaThixo ukuba azinikezele.”—Early Writings, p. 72.

Abanye abantu basenokuva ngathi abanalukholo kuba bengazivi besondele kuThixo okanye abayiyo le nto kufanele babe yiyo njengamaKristu. Kodwa ukholo lungokukholelwa nokuthemba uThixo, hayi nje kuphela ngamaxesha alungileyo koko kwisithokothoko okanye kuqhwithela, okanye nkqu xa ungenakukuqonda ngokupheleleyo ukuba kuqhebeka ntoni na ebomini bakho.

Iimvakalelo mazingakhe nakanye zikhonye kumava ethu onqulo okanye kubudlelane bethu noThixo. Xa kanye sicinga ukuba simgama kuThixo ekuye kudingeke ukuba sisebenzise ukholo lwethu size sibize Yena (njengoko wenzayo utata okuMarko 9:24).

`Jonga ezindima zilandelayo zeBhayibhile uze uzibange njengesenzo sokholo sokuqinisa ubudlelane bakho noThixo namhla: Heb. 12:1, 2; 2 Zikro. 15:7; Rom. 3:23–26; Luka 7:50. Zithethe ngokuphandle njengenxalenye yomthandazo wakho kuThixo.`
