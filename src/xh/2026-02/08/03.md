---
title:  UYesu Uyalubona Ukholo Lwethu
date:   18/05/2026
---

`Thelekisa indlela uYesu aluchaza ngayo ukholo lwabafundi baKhe kuMarko 4:40 kwakunye nokholo lwentokazi ekuMateyu 15:21-28.`

Kuba silandela uYesu lo nto ayithethi kuthi ngokuzenzekelayo ukholo lwethu lomelele. Eneneni, abanye abantu babebanga ngelithi bayakholwa, kodwa uYesu wabona oko ngokwenene kwakusezintliziyweni zabo (Yohane 2:23–25).

`Funda kuLuka 7:1-10. Sifunda ntoni ngokholo kule ngxelo?`

KuMarko 9, sifunda ngendoda eyeza kuYesu ukuze uYesu akhuphe umoya ongcolileyo kunyana wayo, kodwa ayakwazi ukuhlanganisa kuphela ukholo olwaneleyo ukuba ithi, “ ‘Ndiyakholwa, Nkosi; siza, ususe ukungakholwa kwam!’ ” (Marko 9:24).

Nganye kwezincoko, uYesu waaqaphela ukholo lwabantu, okanye ukuswela kwabo ngoko, waza wenza imimangaliso ngenxa yolo kholo okanye ukuze aliqinise.

Kwangale ndlela inye athi uMoya oyiNgcwele asixhokonxe ukuba sikholwe, utshaba lwemiphefumlo lufuna sithandabuze okanye sibhebhethe ukubandakanyeka kukaThixo ebomini bethu. “Ukungakholwa okukhuliswa emphefumlweni kunamandla athabathekisayo. Iinkozo zentandabuzo ebebezityala ziyakuvelisa isivuno sazo kodwa kufuneka baqhubeke ukomba ingcambu nganye yokungakholwa. Xa ezi zityalo ziyityhefu zincothulwe, ziyayeka ukukhula ngenxa yokuswela isondlo samazwi okanye izenzo. Umphefumlo mawube nezityalo ezixabisekileyo zokholo nothando ezibekwe kumhlaba osentliziyweni zize zimiliselwe apho.”—Ellen G. White, Faith and Works, p. 17.

Xa sineentandabuzo ngoThixo, isimilo saKhe, okanye iLizwi laKhe, senza ntoni ngazo? UThixo akayibethisi ngoyaba okanye ayiceze ingqiqo yobuntu, kuba Yena wasidala ngokomfanekiselo waKhe yaye usimemela ekuncokoleni kunye naYe, njengoko wenzayo noAbraham, uMoses, noYobhi. UThixo uyasimema ukuba sifunde ukusebenza phakathi kwiindlela zengqiqo yaKhe ezinkulu, ezingaphele ndawo, nkqu nokuba ngamanye amaxesha kufuneka sinikezele koko singakuqondi ngokupheleleyo.

`Cinga ngaso sonke isizathu esiqiqekayo onaso endaweni yokholo. Kwangaxesha linye, kukwesiphi isigaba eye ingqiqo ime luze ukholo, ukholo oluqinileyo nolusezingqondweni, kufuneke ukuba lumiliselwe?`
