title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:   22/05/2026
---

Sigwetyelwa (sixolelwa silungelelaniswe noThixo) ngokholo (Rom. 5:1). Kanjalo emva koko siyangcwaliswa (sinikwa amandla okufana noYesu) ngokholo (Zenzo 26:18). Xa simema uYesu ebomini bethu, kanjalo siba ngabantwana bakaThixo ngokholo (Yohane 1:12). Siphila ngokholo kuNyana kaThixo (Gal. 2:20).

“Akukho nto ikhangeleka inobuathalala kakhulu, kodwa noko kunjalo ngokwenene ingenakweyiswa mpela, ngathi ngumphefumlo okuvayo ukungabinto kwawo uze ungqiyame ngokuphelele kwimifaneleko yoMsindisi. Ngomthandazo, ngokufunda ilizwi laKhe, ngokholo kubukho baKhe obuhleliyo, ubuthathaka babantu bunokuphila ngokunxulumana noKristu, kwaye Yena uyakubabamba ngesandla esingayi kuphuncula.”—Ellen G. White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 182.

“Ukholo lwabo maluqiniswe ngumthandazo omandla nozilo, kwakunye nokuthotywa kwentliziyo. Mabaqongqothwe umna, baze bazaliswe nguMoya namandla kaThixo. Isikhungo esinyamekileyo, esizingisileyo kuThixo ngokholoukholo olukhokelela ekuxhomekekeni okupheleleyo kuThixo, kwakunye nokuzinikela okungenakumbi emsebenzini waKhe-kuphela siso esinokunceda ukuzisa abantu kuncedo loMoya oyiNgcwele kwidabi nezilawuli nawo amagunya, nazo iziphatha-hlabathi zobumnyama balo eli phakade, nayo imikhosi yomoya abakhohlakeleyo kwezasezulwini iindawo.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 431.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Zeziphi iingongoma ezintlanu ezingundoqo ezivezwe kwisicatshulwa esikufutshane ngasentla xa kufikelelwa ekusebenzisaneni noMoya oyiNgcwele ukulwa notshaba?`

`Ndima ni edlalwa lukholo kweli dabi?`

`Ukubona njani oku kanye ngoku ebomini bakho?`

`Funda amaHebhere 10:23. Kutheni le nto kubalulekile ukubambelela nkqi kuvumo lwethu lokholo?`

`Kukangaphi uyicingisisa inyaniso yokuba xa uziva uliathalala, lithuba lakho lokuxhomekeka ngokuphelele ngakumbi kuYesu?`

**Isishwankathelo:** UThixo unika umntu ngamnye umlinganiselo wokholo njengesiseko sobudlelane kunye naYe. NjengeMbangi noMgqibelelisi wokholo lwethu, uYesu waba ngumzekelo wamandla okholo kuthi sonke. Xa ukholo lwethu luluncinane, xa sisiza ngokuntywizisa nokubongoza ngentliziyo enikelweyo, uThixo uyakusebenza imimangaliso ebomini bethu (bona kuYere. 31:2–4, 9, 11, 12). Yena uyakusikhokelela ezindleleni zaKhe ezithe tye ukuze singabi sakhubeka kwakhona, kwaye siyakuba noxolo. UYesu ungumzekelo ogqibeleleyo ezintweni zonke, kwaye ukuba nokholo lwaKhe kuyakusichaza njengabantu baKhe kumaxesha okugqibela.
