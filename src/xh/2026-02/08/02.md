---
title:  Ndinike Umqondiso!
date:   17/05/2026
---

Mhlawumbi ukhe weva kusithiwa, “Ukuba nje ndinokubona uLwandle oluBomvu luvuleka, okanye imanna isemhlabeni, okanye uYesu ephilisa indoda eyimfama, ndingakholwa.” Okanye mhlawumbi ukhe wanazo ezoo ntlobo zeengcinga nawe.

Kwelinye icala, kutheni kuzakuba lula kuthi ukuba nokholo kunoko kwakunjalo kwabo bamaxesha amandulo eBhayibhile? AmaSirayeli ayengenaBhayibhile ipheleleyo, engazange abe nomntyangambo wembali wokukhangela ngemva kuyo, njengoko sisenza. UMoses wayibethelela eyokubaluleka kokukhangela ngemva ukuze kukhunjulwe inkokhelo nokulunga kukaThixo (bona kwiDut. 4:7–10; Dut. 8:2, 3). Ngokungafaniyo namaSirayeli, sineminyaka engama-6,000 yembali yeBhayibhile amasixhamle kuyo (bona kuYohane 20:30, 31).

Isizukulwana ngasinye sifuna umnqondiso, kwaye nesethu asohlukanga. Kodwa imiqondiso ikho macal’ onke kuthi. Ukuba ufunda uMateyu 24, uyakubona indlela ezininzi ngayo ezinto esele zizalisekile nezisazalisekayo ngoku.

`Abantu nkqu nangexesha likaYesu babefuna umqondiso wokuba ngokwenene uYesu nguNyana kaThixo, nangona babefumene intlaninge yemiqondiso. Waasabela njani uYesu? (Bona kuMarko 8:11, 12.)`

Ingaba siyaxambulisana noYesu simlinge Yena njengoko babesenza abaFarisi? Ingaba simenza “[ancwine] kanzulu ngomoya wakhe”(Marko 8:12) ngenxa yokuswela kwethu ukholo, xa kanti Yena sele esinikile konke esidinga ukukukholelwa?

“Le miqondiso asiyilento amaYuda ayayedinga. Akukho bungqina nje bangaphandle obabunokubanceda. Into ayeyidinga yayingekokukhanyiseleka ngokwasengqondweni, koko yayiluhlaziyo lakwamoya.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 406. Kungenzeka ukuba nathi ngokwethu siding uhlaziyo lakwamoya-uhambo loqobo, lokwenene, umzuzu nomzuzu noThixo? Mhlawumbi eneneni asidingi mqondiso, kuba sinenqwaba yolwazi olukwiintupha zethu, ngokukodwa olusuka eziBhayibhileni zethu.

Ngoko ke, endaweni yokwenza uYesu “[ancwine] kanzulu” ngenxa yokungabi nakholo kwethu, ngamana singakhumbula amazwi uYesu awawathetha kuThomas: “ ‘Banoyolo abo bangabonanga, bakholwa noko!’ ” (Yohane 20:29; bona kanjalo kumaHeb. 11:1). UThixo akasibizeli ekubeni nokholo olumfamekileyo-Yena sele esinikile izizathu zokukholwa eziyinqwaba ngeyona ndlela. Kodwa noko kunjalo, nkqu sezikhona zonke ezo zizathu, kusoloko kukho ithuba lentandabuzo. Undoqo kukugxila koko kuqinisa ukholo, hayi koko kuzisa intandabuzo.

`Ngemizuzwana nje engama-60, ungaluchaza njani ukholo lwakho kuThixo? Impendulo yakho ikuxelela ntoni ngohambo lwakho noThixo?`
