### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Marko 8:11, 12; Mat. 15:21–28; Luka 7:1–10; Efe. 2:8; Hebhere 11; Isityhi. 14:12.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ukholo ke kukukholosa ngezinto ezithenjiweyo, kukweyiseka bubukho bezinto ezingabonwayo” (Hebhere 11:1).

UBani wakhe wathi, “Ukholo lufana newayifa. Alubonakali, kodwa inamandla yokukuqhagamshelanisa kule nto uyidingayo.” Akuthandabuzeki: ngaphandle kokholo, bekungasayi kubakho budlelane noThixo.

Lunjani ukholo lwakho namhlanje? Ingaba ukholo lwakho kuThixo lukhe lwahexa? Mhlawumbi ukhe wanamava ento ekucele umngeni de wafikelela kwinqanaba lokuba ungazi mawubheke phambili njani nobudlelane bakho noThixo. Okanye ingaba ukholo lwakho lufana nenyibiba ekhula kwisikhondo esiluhlaza sibe lithupha elincinane eliye kamva libhenqeke libe yintyatyambo eqolileyo, emibalabala ize izalise igumbi ekulo ngevumba layo elingalibalekiyo? Kuba ngokwenene, “ukholo ke kukukholosa ngezinto ezithenjiweyo; kukweyiseka bubukho bezinto ezingabonwayo” (Heb. 11:1). Aluyonto esinokuyivelisa ngokunokwethu, njengoko “uThixo abele ulowo nalowo umlinganiso wokholo” (Rom. 12:3). Ukholo lusisipho sikaThixo (Eph. 2:8, 9), kwaye nkqu nangoko, ukholo lwethu kuThixo lunokubakho kuphela ngenxa yoko uThixo sele kwenza kuthi yaye esenzela kona.

Kule veki makhe sihlole umxholo wokholo: emasikwenze ngentandabuzo nokungakholwa; yintoni, ngokokutsho kukaYesu, olufana nayo ukholo oluqinileyo; kwanokuba kuthetha ukuthini ukuba “nokholo lukaYesu.”

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Meyi 23._
