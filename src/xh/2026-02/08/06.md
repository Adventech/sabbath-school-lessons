---
title:  Ukholo LukaYesu
date:   21/05/2026
---

Xa eli lizwe lisondela ekupheleni, inxalenye yezigidimi zeengelosi ezintathu ichaza abantu bakaThixo begcina imithetho kaThixo yaye benokholo lukaYesu.

`Funda iSityhileyo 14:12. Luthetha ukuthini “ukholo lukaYesu”?`

Ukuba ufunda indlela aBalindi abakuqonde ngayo ukugwetyelwa ngokholo, uyakubona ukuba kwiminyaka yoo1890, ukuqondwa kokholo lukaYesu kwakunye nezigidimi zeengelosi ezintathu zaahlanganiswa ebandleni. Kude kube ngoko, ibandla lalibeke isixa esikhulu sogxininiso lalo kumthetho, kwaye lalidinga ngakumbi ugxininiso kwigospile. UEllen G. White wayishwankathela kakuhle: “Imithetho kaThixo ivakalisiwe, kodwa ukholo lukaYesu Kristu alukavakaliswa ngaBalindi njengolubaluleke ngokufanayo, umthetho negospile zihamba kunye.”—Selected Messages, book 3, p. 172.

Nangona amaHebhere 11 edwelisa amadoda anentlonelo ayenokholo olomeleleyo, akukho bani ukhe wanokholo oluthelekiswa nokholo lukaYesu.

`Funda kuMateyu 26:36-42. Oku kusixelela ntoni ngokholo lukaYesu kwelithuba lisemqoka?`

Xa sinokholo lukaYesu, oku akuthethi nje kuphela ukuba, ngentobelo kuYe neLizwi laKhe siyakulinganisa ukholo awayenalo Yena kuThixo, koko nokuthi siyakuba namava emihla lee asebenzayo naphilayo kunye noYesu. Kukwazi nokwenza kwinto yokuba ngaphandle kokwenza uYesu abe ngumbindi wobomi bethu bemihla ngemihla, asinakuba nabudlelane busindisa noThixo.

Ukuba nokholo lukaYesu kuthetha ukuba noYesu ohleli ngaphakathi kuthi, ize ngalo ndlela ukholo lubesezintliziyweni zethu, kuba uYesu usisiseko sokwenene sokholo lwethu. Ngamanye amaxesha ukholo lwethu lunokuba buthathaka lubebhetyebhetye. Kodwa uYesu ufanelwe (Isityhi. 5:9), kwaye singanalo ukholo lwaKhe, kokubini lubonakaliswe emaveni ethu kwanokuba lubalelwe kuthi, ngesipho saKhe sobabalo kubo bonke abakholwayo.

`Uludinga kangakanani ukholo lukaYesu? Mcele uThixo ngokuthobekileyo ukuba akunike oku uze ubange amaHebhere 11:6 njengomthandazo wakho buqu, usithi, “Nkosi, ngaphandle kokholo akukwazeki kukukholisa Wena. Ndiza kuWe kwaye ndikholelwa ukuba Wena unako, kwanokuba Wena uyakundivuza xa ndikufuna ngenyameko. Ndikwenza oko ngoku.”`
