---
title:  Imizekelo Yokholo
date:   20/05/2026
---

**Chitha ixesha namhlanje ufunda amaHebhere 11, isahluko esikhulu sokholo. Sifunde ngokukhwazayo kuqala, unganqumami. Emva koko sifunde okwesibini, uze ubhale iingcinga zakho kule mibuzo ilandelayo:**

- Funda indima yokuqala. Yintoni oyithembileyo namhlanje ongenako kodwa ukuyibona? (Cinga ngezidingo zangoku kwakunye namaphupha kanaphakade.)
- Ndima ni ukholo oluyidlalayo kubungqina bakho buqu nakwincoko yakho?
- Funda kwakhona indima 3, ngoThixo neNdalo. Kutheni, ngeendlela ezinzi, le nto ubukho bukaThixo onguMdali bufanele ukuba yeyona nto ilula ukuyithatha ngokholo?
- Funda indima 6 uze ubhale umyalezo wale ndima ngawakho amazwi.
- Iindima 7-40 ziqhala ubomi bezithwala-ndwe eziliqela zeBhayibhile. Kutheni le nto ukholo luyinto engundoqo echaza ukuqina kobudlelane babantu kunye noThixo?

` `

Ukwazi uThixo kwakunye nokuba nobudlelane obuphilileyo nobuluqilima kunye naYe kudinga ukholo. Ungaluqinisa njani ukholo lwakho okanye ukhuthaze umntu othile onokholo oluhexayo? Nazi iingcamango ezimbalwa:

**Ukholo oluncinane (njengokhozo lwemostade) lunamandla kwaye kuphela kwento oyidingayo ukukhulisa ubudlelane noThixo** (Mat. 17:20). Lo gama usakulungele ukusebenza kunye naYe, uThixo uyakunceda ukholo lwakho ukuba lukhule.

**Ukholo luza ngokuva uThixo ethetha nathi eLizwini laKhe, iBhayibhile** (Rom. 10:17). Zibophelele ekufundeni iBhayibhile mihla le nasekuthandazeni.

**Cela uThixo ukuba ongeze ukholo lwakho** (Luka 17:5). Kwanjengokuba wenzayo utata oweza kuYesu enomntwana onomoya ongcolileyo owathi “wakhala eneenyembezi, Ndiyakholwa, Nkosi; siza, ususe ukungakholwa kwam!’ ” (Marko 9:24), singakuqonda ukungakholwa kwethu size sicele uThixo asongezelele ukholo.

**Ukholo nentandabuzo zinokuhlalelana** (Marko 9:24). Sukumka kuThixo kuphela kuba unemibuzo. Eneneni, kubalulekile ukusebenzela usindiso lwakho unoloyiko nokungcangcazela (Fil. 2:12–16) kwanokuba “ulubange” ukholo lwakho endaweni yokuluboleka komnye, njengoko sazamayo isihlanu seentombi (Mat. 25:8).

**Sabela kuMoya oyiNgcwele uze ucele isongezelelo saKhe** ebomini bakho.

**Sebenzisa ukholo lwakho.** Khumbula ukuba ukholo aluyomvakalelo, koko lusisigqibo sokukholwa. Khumbula ukuba nkqu nasebumnyameni, xa wena ungenakumbona Yena, uThixo ulapho (2 Kor. 5:7).

**Njengomthandazo** wakho siqu **wokubulela uThixo ngenxa yokuthembeka kwaKhe**, qwalasela amazwi akwiculo “Kukhulu ukuthembeka kwaKho.”
