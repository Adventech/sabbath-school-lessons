Funda: Ellen G. White, “ ‘A Great Gulf Fixed,’ ” pp. 260–271, in Christ’s Object Isifundos; “IKalvari,” amaph. 749–752, ku-Ulangazelelo Lwamaphakade; and “Teachers as Examples of Christian Integrity,” p. 504, in Fundamentals of Christian Education.

“Emzekelisweni wesityebi noLazaro, uKristu ubonisa ukuba kobu bomi abantu basenza ngokwabo isiphelo sobomi babo obungunaphakade. Ngeli xesha lenceba, ubabalo lukaThixo lunikwa yonke imiphefumlo. Ukuba ke abantu badlala ngawo amathuba ngokwenza ukuzithandela, bazinqumla kubomi obungunaphakade. Akukho nceba yimbi baya kube bayinikwe.  Ngokwabo ukukhetha bamilisele umsantsa ongenakudlulwa phakathi kwabo noThixo wabo.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Isifundos, p. 260.

“Xa loo maKristu okuqala ayebalekele ezintabeni nakwiintlango, xa ayeshiywe emihadini ukuba afe yingqele, yindlala, nakukungcungcuthekiswa, xa ukufelukholo kwakubonakala ikuphela kwendlela yokuphuma kuxinzelelo, avuya kuba ayebalelwe kwabo bakufaneleyo ukubulaleka ngenxa kaKristu, owaya bethelelwa ngenxa yawo. Umzekelo wawo ofanelekileyo uya kuba yintuthuzelo nenkuthazo kubantu bakaThixo abaya kuziswa kwixesha lembandezelo engazange yabakho efana nayo.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 213.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxwa**

`Imbono yeBhayibhile epheleleyo engendalo yomntu ingasinceda njani ukuba siziqonde ngcono ezinye iindawo esizifunde kule veki?`

`Camngca ngomahluko phakathi kwenkolo yamafelukholo angamaKristu engenakuxoleleka nenkolo ejikajikayo yesi sizukulwana sethu seli xesha langoku. Ngamanye amazwi, zinto zini ezifanele ukuba kungafelwa zona? Sekunjalo, ukuba ubani unembono yokuba zonke iinyaniso zifanele nje imeko leyo igqubayo, okanye zihambelana nenkcubeko, kungani ukuba ubani abe angafela nayiphi na kuzo? Kwangelo xesha linye, yintoni esingayifunda kwabo babekulungele ukufa ngenxa yeenkolo esikholwayo thina ukuba zaye zibubuxoki?`

`Hlalani ngakumbi kumzekeliso wendoda esisityebi noLazaro. Xa uYesu wayevuswa kwabafileyo, abaninzi bakholelwa kuYe. Kwathi ke, nangona abanye babenabo ubungqina obunye, bona abakholwa. Kusifundisa ntoni oku ngendlela iintliziyo zabantu ezinokuba lukhuni ngayo ngakwinyaniso? Yintoni esingayenza ukuzikhusela kwimeko yobulukhuni obufana nobo?`

`UYesu wathetha ngexesha xa abafileyo baya kuphila: “ ‘abo benza izinto ezilungileyo beze eluvukweni lobomi, nabo ke benza izinto ezimbi, beze eluvukweni lokugwetywa’ ” (Yohane 5:29, NKJV). Ezi ziganeko zibini zahlukene ngeminyaka eliwaka, nangona zivakala ngokungathi zenzeka ngexesha elinye. Kungasinceda njani oku ukuba sikuqonde okuthethwa nguPawulos kuFilipu 1:23?`
