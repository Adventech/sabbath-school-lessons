---
title:  Intsingiselo Yomnqamlezo
date:  03/11/2022
---

`Funda: 1 Korinte 1:18–24. Uthini uPawulos ngomnqamlezo, ewuthelekisa njani “nobulumko behlabathi”? Kutheni, nanamhlanje, “ukuthanda izinto” (ingcamango yokuba konke okubalulekileyo izizinto kuphela, into ethi akukho Thixo okanye indawo apho kukho izinto ngaphaya kwendalo) kuhamba phambili “kubulumko behlabathi,” isisigidimi somnqamlezo esibaluleke kakhulu?`

Umnqamlezo kaKristu ungumongo kanye wembali yosindiso.

“Unaphakade akanakho ukulinganiswa kubunzulu bothando olwatyhilwa eKalvari. Kwakulapho uthando olungenamda lukaKristu lwajongana ubuso nobuso nokuthanda okukokwakhe uSathana okungenamida.”—Stephen N. Haskell, The Cross and Its Shadow (South Lancaster, MA: Bible Training School, 1914), p. v.

Xa uKristu wayezinikela ngokuthobeka ukuba abe yintlawulelo yohlanga loluntu, uSathana, ngomona, wayemgubungela ngobunzima nentlungu. UKristu akazange afe ukufa nje kwendalo, oku lonke uluntu lujongene nako. Wafa ukufa kwesibini, ukuze bonke abamamkelayo bangaze bakufe bona ngokwabo.

Ngokubhekiselele kwintsingiselo yomnqamlezo, iliqela imiba esimele ukuyikhumbula. Kuqala, uMnqamlezo uphezulu kwisityhilelo sobulungisa bukaThixo ngakwisono (Roma 3:21–26). Okwesibini, uMnqamlezo usisityhilelo esiphezulu sothando lukaThixo ngakuboni (Roma 5:8). Okwesithathu, uMnqamlezo ulithende elikhulu lamandla okuqhawula amatyathanga esono (Roma 6:22, 23; 1 Kor. 1:17–24). Okwesine, uMnqamlezo kuphela kwethemba lethu lobomi obungunaphakade (Filipu 3:9–11; Yohane 3:14–16; 1 Yohane 5:11, 12). Kanti ke okwesihlanu, uMnqamlezo ulichiza lokuthintela uvukelo lwexesha elizayo kwindalo yonke (IsiTy. 7:13–17, IsiTy. 22:3).

Ayikho neyodwa kwezi nyaniso zibalulekileyo enakho ukuzuzwa “bubulumko behlabathi.” Ngokuchaseneyo, ngoko, njengangoku, ukushunyayelwa komnqamlezo “bubudenge” kubulumko behlabathi, obukholisa ukungavumi nenyaniso le iphandle ikhoyo: ukho uMdali (bona nakumaRoma 1:18–20).

Igama lesiGrike “ubudenge” lihlangene negama lesiNgesi “isiphukuphuku”; oko kukuthi, ukushunyayelwa komnqamlezo “bubuphukuphuku” ngoko“bulumko behlabathi.” Ubulumko behlabathi abunakho ukumazi uYesu okanye usindiso olusinika ngokufa kwaKhe okumi endaweni “yokwethu” emnqamlezweni.

`Nokuba bulixabiso elithini “ubulumko behlabathi,” kutheni le nto kufuneka singaze sivume ukuba buphazamise oko sikukholelwayo ngoYesu kunye nethemba esinikwa lona ngo“ubudenge besigidimi esishunyayelwayo” (1 Kor. 1:21)?`
