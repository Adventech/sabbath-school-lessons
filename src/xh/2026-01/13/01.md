### Fundela Isifundo Sale Veki
Kol. 4:7–18; Efese 6:21; IZenzo 15:36–40; 2 Tim. 4:10, 11; 2 Pet. 3:10–14; Isa. 60:1–3.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ezintweni zonke bulelani; kuba oko kukuthanda kukaThixo kuKristu Yesu ngani” (1 Tesalonika 5:18).

Le ndawo yokugqibela kwabaseKolose iveza ububanzi boqhagamshelano lwabasebenzi kunye noPawulos. IZenzo zimbonisa ehlangana kuqala noBharnabhas, emva koko noSilas, ize inike umfanekiso weehambo zakhe zovangelo ezintathu.

Kule veki siza kukhangela icebo likaPawulos lokuvangela, elaliquka ukusebenzisa ngendlela enempumelelo ixesha nezixhobo ekufikeleleni kwiindawo ezinkulu zobukumkani baseRoma, nasekuqeqesheni abasebenzi abathembisayo ukuze bafikelele kwizixeko needolophu angazange akwazi uPawulos ukuzihambela, njengaseKolose, eLawodike, naseHierapoli.

Ngokuya kwakhe buqu kwiihambo zakhe, ngakumbi elibanjwa, ngeencwadi zakhe, uPawulos wayesoloko eqhagamshelene nabantu namabandla. Wayeqonda ukuba impumelelo yomsebenzi weendaba ezilungileyo ixhomekeke ekusebenzisaneni, — amaKristu amaYuda naweNtlanga; amadoda nabafazi; abantu abafana noTikiko, Aristaki, Yustus, Epafra, Luka, noNimfa. Ngokumangalisayo, siva ngencwadi awayibhalela eLawodike esingazange thina siyigcinelwe. UPawulos ufaka okuninzi kwezi ndima zokugqibela, kuquka ukukhuthaza kumntu onguAchipo. Wenza konke ukomeleza amabandla esakwazi ukwenza oko.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ulungiselela iSabatha kaMatshi 28._
