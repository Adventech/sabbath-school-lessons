---
title:  Izifundo Ngovangelo
date:   22/03/2026
---

Sifunda okuninzi kuPawulos ngokusasazwa kwevangeli. Iihambo zakhe zikwisithuba seemayile ezingama-13,400. Kuyamangalisa oku, njengoko okuninzi kuko kwakuhanjwa ngeenyawo kuthi, maxa wambi, abe sentolongweni.

UPawulos wachitha ixesha elikhulu kwiindawo zoshishino, njengaseKorinte nase-Efese, apho wayekwazi ukusa isigidimi kwiidolophu ezikude nolwandle. Wayekwabuyela emabandleni awayewamisile ukuze akhuthaze omeleze amakholwa amatsha apho. Xa engakwazi ukuwahambela ngokwakhe, wayethumela iileta. Ngale ndlela, amakholwa ayesazi ukuba uyawakhumbula nokuba unenkathalo ngawo.

`Funda Kolose 4:7–9; uthelekise noEfese 6:21. Uchazwa njani uTikiko, zisithini izizathu azinikayo uPawulos zokumthumela yena no-Onesimo eKolose?`

Ezinye izinto zidluliswa ngcono ngomlomo kunokuthetha. Bekuya kunika umdla ukwazi ukuba zithini iindaba ezathunyelwa ngala madoda mabini eKolose. Ngokwazi injongo kaPawulos yokuba ezi zinto zaziya kunika “intuthuzelo”(NKJV) ne“nkuthazo” kubo (Kol. 4:7–9, ESV), zinokuba zazinazo iinkcukacha ngeemeko zikaPawulos entolongweni. Phofu nakanjani, uqhagamshelano olunjalo lwalubalulekile ekugcineni amaqhina olwalamano asondeleyo ukubugcina bubambene ubunye bamakholwa.

UTikiko, ogama lakhe lithetha ukuba “nethamsanqa,” kucacile ukuba wayesisithunywa esithembekileyo. Njengoko wayechazwa ngoko “mlungiseleli othembekileyo” “nomkhonzi kunye naye,” wayengomnye wesibini esasuka eAsiya esasikhethwe nguPawulos (IZenzo 20:4) ukuba sihambe naye kuhambo lwakhe bephethe umnikelo wamakholwa asweleyo aseYerusalem. Wayekwa noPawulos ekuvalelweni kwakhe kwesibini eRoma, apho wasuka khona ukuya kwakhe e-Efese ukuya kuqinisa umsebenzi khona (2 Tim. 4:12). UPawulos wacinga nangokumthumela kuTito eKrete (Tito 3:12). Wayekhatshwa nguOnesimo, owaguqulwa nguPawulos eRoma (bona Isifundo 1) nochazwa njengo“thembekileyo.”

Kuyabonakala ukuba uPawulos wayekwafuna ukwazi ngeemeko zamakholwa eKolose. Kwakungayi ukuba kubuyiselwe ilizwi kuye ngomntu, xa ingenguye uTikiko ngokwakhe. L, yayiyenye indlela uPawulos awathumela uthando lwakhe nenkathalo yakhe ngamakholwa alapho, nangona engazange awahambele yena ngokwakhe, kwanendlela awawaqinisa ngayo enkolweni ukuze akwazi ukufikelela kwabanye.

`Ezi zinto zikwezi leta ezijongene naye uPawulos zenza njani ukunganeli nje ukusibonisa ubuntu bakhe, kancinane, kodwa nokuqinisekisa ukuxabiseka kolungiselelo lwakhe?`
