“Umntu nje obengacacanga owayengatsho izinto ezinjengezo uYesu wazithethayo wayengayi kuba yititshala enkulu yokufundisa okusesikweni. Wayeya kuba mhlawumbi ligeza—kumgangatho womntu othi uliqanda elibhadlisiweyo—okanye abe ngoNgendawo wesihogo. Ungazikhethela. Mhlawumbi lo mntu waye okanye unguNyana kaThixo: okanye indoda ephambeneyo okanye into engaphezulu. Unakho ukumvalela njengesidenge, ungamtshicela umbulale njengedemon; okanye ungawa ezinyaweni zaKhe umbize ngokuthi yiNkosi noThixo. Sekunjalo, masingenzi nanye into eyamkela inkunkuma ngokuba eyititshala enkulu. Akayiyekelanga kuthi leyo. Waye engajonge ukwenza oko.”—C. S. Lewis, Mere Christianity (New York, Collier Books, 1952), p. 41.

“UYise ukuko konke ukuzala kobuThixo ngokomzimba kwaye akanakubonakala kwiliso lonokufa.

“UNyana ukuko konke ukuzala kobuThixo obuye babonakalaliswa. ULizwi likaThixo limazisile ukuba ‘ungumfanekiselo waKhe ogqibeleleyo.’”—Ellen G. White, Evangelism, p. 614.

“UKristu ungowayekho kwangaphambili, owabakho ngokwaKhe uNyana kaThixo. . . . ekuthetheni ngobukho baKhe kwangaphambili, uKristu unenkumbulo yasemva kwizizukulwana ezinganakumiselwa mhla. Usiqinisekisa ukuba alizange lakho ixesha xa wayengazange abe nobudlelwane obusondeleyo noThixo ongunaphakade. . . .

“Wayelingana noThixo, ukungabi nasiphelo nokuba namandla onke. . . . Ungunaphakade, uNyana obekho ngokwaKhe.”—Ellen G. White, Evangelism, p. 615.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Hlalani ngakumbi kumba wokuba nguThixo ngonaphakade kukaYesu. Cingisisani ngokuthethwa licebo losindiso liphelele nentsingiselo yomnikelo emnqamlezweni ukuba uYesu ube eyenye into engenguye uThixo ongunaphakade, Yena lo Ongazange adalwe kodwa wayehleli ekho kususela ephakadeni eladlulayo, ekhona. Ibaluleke ngantoni loo mfundiso? Eklasini, thethani ngokuba bekuya kuthini ukuba, ngenene, uYesu wayengenguye unaphakade, kodwa ngandlel’ ithile abe Yena ngokwaKhe wayedaliwe. Kwakhona, yintoni elahlekileyo kuloo ndlela yokucinga?`

`Xa sicinga ngoYesu, neendaba ezilungileyo necebo losindiso, kutheni kufuneka siyigcinile imbono yokubandakanyeka kweyunivesi yonke kwakunye nomdla koko uYesu akwenzileyo apha njengenxenye yokucinga kwethu? Kunokuba kuqhubeke ntoni ezingqondweni zabo xa bebona uMdali wabo, uMdali wabo ongunaphakade, ejinga emnqamlezweni? Kukodwa ukuba thina sikhwankqiswe kuko, kodwa iyunivesi engawanga yayimazi kubuqaqawuli baKhe obungunaphakade. Kunokuba kwenzeka ntoni ezingqondweni zabo xa babembona Lowo babemnqula ezulwini esifa emnqamlezweni?`

`Ungathini kumntu ongakholwayo ukuba uBawo noNyana bahlala bekho kunye? Kutheni le nto oku kuyinyaniso ebaluleke kakhulu? Ungachaza njani ukuba alizange lakho ixesha xa uYise wayengenaye uNyana, ngaphandle kwasemnqamlezweni, apho kwabakho “ukunikela kumandla kaThixo” okwexeshana” (Ellen G. White Comments, The SDA Bible Commentary, vol. 7, p. 924)?`
