### Fundela Isifundo Sale Veki
Filipi 1:19–30, 1 Kor. 4:14–16, 2 Kor. 10:3–6, Yohane 17:17–19, Mika 6:8, IZenzo 14:22.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kuba kum mna, ukudla ubomi kunguKristu” (Filipi 1:21).

Ukufa, sihlala sisiva, kuyinxalenye yobomi. Bubuxoki obo. Ukufa kuchasene nobomi, kulutshaba lobomi. Ukufa akudlulanga ekwakhelweni ebomini kubugoxo obakhelwe emotweni. UPawulos ukutsho ngokucinezela ukuxela ukuba uKristu wafa ukuze “ambhangise lowo unamandla okufa, oko kukuthi, uMtyholi lo; abakhulule ebukhobokeni bonke abo babethe ngalo ixesha lokudla kwabo ubomi bazizisulu zokoyika ukufa” (Heb. 2:14, 15).

Nangona wayekulungele ukufa ngenxa kaKristu, uPawulos wayeqinisekile ngesiphelo sobomi bakhe obude. Eyona nto ibaluleke kakhulu kuye okwangoko, yayikuba, ngobomi bakhe okanye ngokufa kwakhe, amzukise uKristu nokushumayela iindaba ezilungileyo kubantu abaninzi kangangoko. Mhlawumbi sisizathu eso sokuba sibe neeleta ezininzi ezinegama lakhe. Ngemibhalo yakhe, siyakwazi ukufikelela kubantu neendawo ezininzi, kuquka nezo yena ngokwakhe angazange azihambele.

Ubomi bufutshane, kwaye kubaluleke kakhulu ukwenzela igalelo elikhulu kakhulu ubukumkani bukaThixo ngeli xesha lale minyaka uThixo asinike yona. Akukho nentwana yelo galelo enento yokwenza nokukhuthaza kwethu “ubunye benkolo.” Njengoko siza kubona ekuqaleni kwale veki, lo mxholo wawungomnye wesizathu esibalulekileyo sokuba uPawulos abhalele abaseFilipi.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ulungiselela iSabatha kaJanuwari 17._
