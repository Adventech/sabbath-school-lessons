### Fundela Isifundo Sale Veki
Kol. 3:1–17, Roma 1:18, Roma 6:1–7, Efese 4:22–24, Dut. 7:6–8, 1 Sam. 16:23.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ke, phezu kwazo zonke ezo zinto, yalekani uthando, oluyintambo yogqibelelo” (Kolose 3:14).

Kuye kukhuthazwe ukuba singabi nengqondo esezulwini kangako; kungenjalo asiyi kuba lulutho emhlabeni. Nangona oko kuyinyaniso ngendlela ethile, kukwakho nembono ebalulekileyo ayibabazayo uPawulos kwabaseKolose 3: ukuba sinengqondo eqonde kakhulu emhlabeni, asiyi kuba lulutho ezulwini eNkosini.

UPawulos utsalela ingqiqo yethu kwimithetho-siseko emininzi ephathekayo yobomi obububo ezalwa lizulu nenokuqondwa kuphela ngabo “bavuswe noKristu” (Kol. 3:1).

Iingcebiso zikaPawulos ziyimithetho-siseko ephantsi kakhulu eya kubukhulisa bonke ubudlelane bethu, kungekho kuphela ngaphakathi ebandleni.

UYesu wathi, “Zithandeni iintshaba zenu, basikeleleni abo baniqalekisayo, benzeleni okulungileyo abo banithiyayo . . . ukuze nibe ngabantwana boYihlo osemazulwini; ngokuba ilanga lakhe elenza liphume phezu kwabakhohlakeleyo nabalungileyo; nemvula eyinisa phezu kwabalungileyo nabangalungisiyo” (Mat. 5:44, 45).

Kuvakala kunzima, akunjalo? Kunjalo, ngokwabantu. Siyakudinga ukufa ngaphambi kokuba siphilele uThixo ngokwenene. Yiyo le nto kufuneka sibe nengqondo ejonge ezulwini ukuba sithembe ukuba lulutho olunokusetyenziswa nguBawo wethu osemazulwini.

Kule veki siza kubona iindlela ekuthi ngazo ukuphila noKristu kube nokwenza umahluko, ngoku nangonaphakade.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ulungiselela iSabatha kaMatshi 14._
