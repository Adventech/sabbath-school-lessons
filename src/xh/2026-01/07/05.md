Uxolo olugqithisele kuko konke ukuqonda “luya kuzigcina iintliziyo neengqiqo zenu kuKristu Yesu” (Filipi 4:7). Ubomi bethu bangaphakathi buyakudinga ukukhuselwa. Okwenza umdla, uFilipi 4:7, ngokuhlangene noxolo lukaThixo, usebenzisa isifaniso somkhosi. Isenzi sesiGrike (phroure) sisetyenziswe ukufanisa nomkhosi wamasoldati ojonge ukukhusela isixeko kuhlaselo (2 Kor. 11:32; thelekisa IZenzo 9:24).

Elinye icala elibaluleke kakhulu kuxolo lwangaphakathi libandakanya ukuhlala ngemvisiswano nentando kaThixo. “Banoxolo olukhulu abawuthandayo umyalelo wakho; akukho sikhubekiso kubo” (Ind. 119:165).

`Funda Filipi 4:8, 9. Zeziphi izenzo eziqinisekileyo ezikhuthazwayo?`

UPawulos uqala uFilipi 4:8,9 ngokuthi “elokugqiba” (GNV) aze enike uluhlu lwesithandathu sezinto ezintle, ezilandelwa sisishwankathelo esifutshane sazo nenkuthazo yokuba kulinganiswe umzekelo wakhe Pawulos. Esi sikhuthazo sixande kwinkcubeko singena kakuhle ncam kwimeko yobuGrike/Roma yaseFilipi, nokucinezela kwaso okumbaxa kokuhle nomzekelo. Into eyenza umdla, phofu, luqwalaselo kokuhle kweziBhalo, okucace kakhulu ngokushiya kukaPawulos okona kuhle kwisiGrike okuzingongoma ezine (ubulumko, ugwebo, ubungcathu, nesibindi).

1. Inene—kungekho ngempazamo, uludwe luqala ngokuhle kwenyaniso okuphambili ngokweBhayibhile, athi uYesu (“Inene, ndithi. . .”) ibe iTestamente eNtsha ikholisa ukucinezela (bona, umzekelo, IZenzo 26:25, Roma 1:18, 1 Kor. 13:6, 2 Kor. 4:2, Efese 4:15, 1 Tim. 3:15, Yakobi 1:18, 1 Pet. 1:22, 1 Yohane 2:21).
2. Isidima—igama lesiGrike libhekisa kokuhle komntu
3. Ukulungisa [ukwenza okulungileyo]—olu phawu lokuhle lucaciswa ngesimilo esilungileyo sikaThixo (thelekisa nokusetyenziswa kwaso kuFilipi 1:7).
4. Ubunyulu—ingcinga nesenzo eziphuma kubulungisa obugwebelayo bukaThixo zamkelwa ngokholo (bona u-1 Yohane 3:3).
5. Ukuthandeka—ubuhle obuthandekayo, obubonakala gabalala kwindalo kaThixo.
6. Okubukekayo—“ububele nomtsalane nobunene” (AMPC).

UPawulos unika ezinye iimpawu ezimbini zokufaneleka, hleze intsingiselo yobuhedeni ifakwe nakokuphi koku kuhle: “ukuba kukho nantoni eyenye etshatsheleyo okanye efanele ukudunyiswa” (Filipi 4:8, NIV), kuza kufuneka sicinge ngazo ezi mpawu zintle zinobungcwele. Ngoko ke, ukususa yonke intandabuzo nokungaqondi okunokubakho, uPawulos uthi masenze oko sikufundileyo, sakwamkela, sakuva, sakubona kumzekelo wakhe (Filipi 4:9).
