`Funda Filipi 3:20, 21. UPawulos ubuchaza ngokucace kangakanani ukuba buyinto enjani ukuba “ngummi” ongumKristu?`

Ngokungafaniyo neentshaba zomnqamlezo, “ezibeka ingqondo yazo kwizinto zasemhlabeni” nezingenaye uThixo omkhulu kunezisu zazo (Filipi 3:19), ubumi bomKristu busezulwini, kwaye umlawuli wethu nguYesu Kristu ngokwaKhe. Ukuyibethelela le ngongoma, uPawulos ubabaza intswelo “yale mizimba ithobekileyo yethu” (Filipi 3:21, NET), ephantsi kwesifo, ukuphela nokufa, ukuba yenziwe ngokutsha ifane nomzimba kaKristu owazukiswayo ekuvukeni.

**Ezi ndawo zilandelayo ziyichaza njani imo ezukisiweyo?**

`Yobhi 19:25–27`

`Luka 24:39`

`1 Kor. 15:42–44`

`1 Kor. 15:50–54`

`Kol. 3:4`

Ekupheleni, ngoYesu, ukufa, “utshaba lokugqibela,” kuya kutshatyalaliswa (1 Kor. 15:26). Lelona themba lethu likhulu elo, uvutho-ndaba lwesithembiso esibe sisinikiwe kuYesu—kungekuphela kokufa kodwa kwanokuba siya kunikwa umzimba omtsha wonke, “umzimba ozukisiweyo” (Filipi 3:21, ESV).

Encwadini engendlela yokufumana “usindiso” ngaphandle kukaThixo, exoxa, ngokobudenge, ukuba ukoyika ukufa asilulo “usindiso,” umbhali uLuc Ferry akavumi ukuba ubuKristu “busenza ukuba singaneli nje ukunyukela ngaphaya kokoyika, kodwa nokoyisa ukufa ngokwako. Ngokwenza njalo, ngokokwazi ubunjani bomntu, kunokuba kubekho ukungaziwa okanye ukungabikho, ingathi yindlela enika uloyiso oluqinisekileyo ngokwenene lokungabi nakufa phezu kwemeko esikuyo njengabanokufa.”—Luc Ferry, A Brief History of Thought (New York: HarperCollins, 2011, Kindle edition), p. 90. Ukuvuma okuqinisekileyo, kuphuma kongakholwa kuThixo.

Kungoko, kuPawulos, ubumi bethu basemazulwini buquka isithembiso sovuko nobomi obungunaphakade kubo bonke ubukho obutsha esingakwaziyo ukubucinga ngoku.

`Kutheni le nto isithembiso sobomi obungunaphakade sibaluleke kangaka kuko konke esikukholelwayo? Ingaba yintoni enokunikwa leli hlabathi ekungafanela ukuba sincame oko uKristu asinika kona ukuba sizuze yona?`
