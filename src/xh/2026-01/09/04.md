---
title:  Icebo LikaThixo Likanaphakade
date:   24/02/2026
---

`Funda Kolose 1:24, 25. Uthini uPawulos ngokuva ubunzima ngenxa kaKristu?`

Nangona uPawulos wayebhalela abaseKolose ngexesha ephantsi kobubanjwa basendlwini eRoma, mhlawumbi okona kuva ubunzima kakhulu baphuma ekungakwazini ukusebenza ngamandla ukuya kwiindawo ngeendawo nakumakhaya ngamakhaya, njengoko wayesenza kwixa elidlulileyo (IZenzo 20:20). Ezi mbandezelo (okanye iinkathazo), awalumkisa ngazo kwangaphambili uKristu (Mat. 24:9, Yohane 16:33), “azinakulinganiswa nobuqaqawuli obu buza kutyhilwa kuthi” (Roma 8:18). Nguwo ke lo umfanekiso omkhulu. Njengoko uPawulos wawabhalelayo amaKristu aseFilipi, ngoku, ke ngoko, kwawaseKolose uvuya ngaphezu kokuva kwakhe ubunzima, okuyinzuzo oko kuwo (Kol. 1:24).

UPawulos unokuba usentolongweni, kodwa “ilizwi likaThixo alibotshiwe” (2 Tim. 2:9). Ngexesha uPawulos waye koku kuvalelwa, incwadi eya kwabaseFilipi, nabase-Efese, nakuFilemon nazo zabhalwa. Emva kokukhululwa kwakhe, uThixo wamphefumlela ukuba abhale iingcebiso ezibalulekileyo ezifunyanwa ku-1 Timoti nakuTito. Emva koko, ngexesha lokuvalelwa kwakhe kokugqibela kwintolongo yaseRoma, wabhala u-2 Timoti. Ngokufutshane, leminyaka yokugqibela yamnika uPawulos ithuba lokubhala indawo ebonakalayo yeTestamente eNtsha, ekunokwenzeka ukuba yaquka amaHebhere.

Icebo likaThixo likanaphakade lalikuqaphele konke oku nangaphezulu. Igama lesiGrike alisebenzisayo uPawulos kwabaseKolose 1:25, Likholisa ukuguqulwa “ubugosa,” nguoikonomia. Kunqongophele ukusetyenziswa kwalo (njengakumzelo, ka-Timoti 1:4), libhekisa “kwindlela kaThixo yokucwangcisa izinto.”—Luke Timothy Yohaneson, The First and Second Letters to Timothy (New York: Doubleday, 2001), p. 164. Oko bekuya kuquka ubupostile bukaPawulos. Kanti ke, ngokubanzi, liquka yonke imisindleko uThixo ayenzileyo kwicebo losindiso. Ulungiselelo lukaPawulos, nabanye abapostile, kanye nabaprofeti beTestamene eNdala (Efese 2:20, Efese 3:5), kuquka noMoses, babelungiselwe “ukuzalisekisa ilizwi likaThixo” (Kol. 1:25), konke ngokuhlangene neli cebo likaThixo.

Nangona siza kuqwalasela ngokusondele ngakumbi kulo mxholo kwisifundo sangomso, kuyanceda ngoku ukuqaphela ukuba uPawulos walwamkela ulungiselelo lwakhe njengentwana encinane yokukhulu kakhulu, okulicebo likaThixo elifikelela kude elaqala asetyenzwa “kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi” (Mat. 13:35, Efese 1:4).

`Cinga ngobakho ubomi. Izigqibo ozenzayo (ezikhulu nezincinci) zingakwenza njani ukufanele ukuba ngaphakathi kwecebo elikhulu likaThixo? Ingaba ngokwenene singazi ukuba isigqibo siqinisekile ukuba “sincinane”? Kungenzeka njani ukuba sibe namajiko-jiko amakhulu aya kuthi acace kamva?`
