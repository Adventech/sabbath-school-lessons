---
title:  Amalalela
date:  15/09/2022
---

Njengoko sibonileyo izolo, ukululamela intando kaThixo kungadeleka xa sixhomekeka kumandla ethu. Kunokwenzeka kanjalo ukuxhomekeka kwizinto ezizezinye ezithatha indawo kaThixo. Xa abanye abantu beziva bexinzelelekile, bahamba bokuthenga into ezakubavisa kamnandi. Xa abanye beziva bengenako, bazungulana nodumo. Xa abanye beneengxaki nabalingane babo, bakhangela omnye ozakubasondeza abavise kamnandi.

Uninzi lwezinto esizisebenzisayo zingaluphozisa uxinzelelo, kodwa aziyisombululi ingxaki okanye zisifundise ukumelana nale meko ngokungcono kwixa elizayo. Kuphela luncedo olungaqhelekanga oluvela kuThixo olunokwenza oko. Ingxaki kukuba amaxesha amaninzi sixhomekeka kwizinto ezithatha indawo kaThixo kunakuThixo ngokunokwaKhe.

Nazi izinto ezintathu ezithatha indawo esinokuzisebenzisa endaweni kaThixo:

1. Ukusebenzisa ingqiqo yobuntu okanye amava adlulileyo xa siding intyilileyo engcwele entsha.

2. Ukunqanda iingxaki ezingqondweni zethu xa siding izisombululo ezingcwele.

3. Ukubaleka inyani nokuphepha uThixo xa siding ukunxibelelana noThixo ukuze sifumane amandla angcwele.

UZekariya uyasinceda ukuba sigxile koko ngokwenene kubalulekileyo xa sihendekela ekusebenziseni izinto ezithatha indawo. Emva kweminyaka emininzi kude, abathinjwa ekugqibeleni babebuyile eBhabheli baza kwangoko baqalisa ukuyakha kwakhona itempile. Kodwa kukho umthamo omandla wenkcaso koku (imvelaphi ethile ingafumaneka ku-Ezra 4-6). Ngoko ke uZekariya weza nolu daba lokukhuthaza kuZerubhabheli, owayekhokela umsebenzi.

`Funda: lo myalezo kuZekariya 4. Uthetha ukuthini uThixo kuZekariya 4:6? Ukugqityezelwa kwephulo lokwakha lungachatshazelwa njani nguMoya oyiNgcwele? Oku kusifundisa ntoni ngobudlelane obuphakathi koMoya oyiNgcwele nezinto eziphathekayo esizenzayo? `

UThixo zange ayithintele inkcaso ngakwitempile okanye asindise uZerubhabheli kuxinzelelo lokujambelana nayo. Kwaye uThixo akazokusoloko esikhusela kwinkcaso. Kodwa xa inkcaso ifika, uThixo usenokuyisebenzisa njengeonti yokusifundisa ukuxhomekeka kuYe.

`Xa uxinzelelo lwengqondo lufika, yintoni into yokuqala oyenzayo? Ukutya? Umabona-kuded? Umthandazo? Ukululamela uThixo? Impendulo yakho ikuxelela ntoni ngawe nezinto ekufuneka uzifundile okanye uzitshintshe?  `
