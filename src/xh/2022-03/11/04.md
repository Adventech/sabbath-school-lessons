---
title:  'UDavide: Umzekeliso Wokulinda'
date:  06/09/2022
---

Ku-1 Samuweli 16:1-13, sibona uDavide inkwenkwana eselula ethanjiswa nguSamuweli njengekumkani. Noko kunjalo, yaba luhambo olude ukusuka emasimini kaYise, uYese, ukusinga etroneni eYerusalem. Akuthandabuzeki, ngamanye amaxesha wayeziva esesizikithini seonti.

Okokuqala, le nkwenkwana ibizwa ukuba idlale umculo ukuthomalalisa umoya odandathekileyo kaSawule (1 Samuel 16). Kamva, iba liqhawe likaSirayeli xa ebulala uGoliyati (1 Samuel 17). Emva koko iba yintlaninge yeminyaka nalapho uDavide abhacela ubomi bakhe. Bobabini uSawule noYonatan unyana wakhe bayazi ukuba uDavide umiselwe ukuba yikumkani ezakulandela (1 Sam. 23:17, 1 Sam. 24:20). Kodwa uDavide akenzi nto ukuqhubela phambili uThixo wakhe-isiphelo asinikiweyo. Eneneni, ukhangeleka esenza okwahlukileyo. Nkqu naxa uSawule wayezama ukumbulala waza uDavide wanqamla isondo lengubo kaSawule, wazisola ukuba ngengakwenzanga oko (1 Sam. 24:5-7). Kwakhona, xa uSawule ezama ukubulala uDavide, uDavide uyala ukubulala uSawule xa ithuba livela (1 Sam. 26:7-11).

`Funda: ku-1 Samuweli 26: 1-11. Kutheni uDavide esala ukubulala uSawule? Mimiselo mini efundiswa koku kuthi ngendlela uThixo aphumeza ngayo amacebo aKhe kubomi bethu?   `

`Ngoku funda ku-1 Samuweli 26:12-25. Ukwala kukaDavide ukubulala uSawule kumchaphazela njani uSawule. Oku kusifundisa ntoni ngamaqithiqithi okulinda uThixo?     `

Sijonge indlela kaDavide yokusinga etroneni xa iyonke, mhlawumbi singayishwankathela ngesivakalisi esifutshane-sukuxhakamfula oko uThixo angekakuniki kona. Izipho zikaThixo zixhanyulwa ngcono ezandleni zaKhe ngexesha laKhe. Oku kunga kungadinga umlibe wexesha lokulinda. Izithombo zembotyi zingakhula poqo ngesibhubhubhu seeyure, lo gama umthi wom-oki uzakuthatha iminyaka. Kodwa ke xa iinqwithela zifika, umthi awuzokusitshulwa neengcambu.

`Cinga ngendlela ekwakunokuba lula ngayo ukuba uDavide athethelela ukubulala kwakhe uSawule. (Kakade, uDavide wayexelelwe ukuba uzakuba setroneni, kwaye uSawule wayekhohlakele kakade.) Noko kunjalo, izenzo zakhe zixela ukholo lokwenene kuThixo. Yintoni ongazithathela yona kulo mzekelo kuyo nantoni na ongaba uyilindile?`
