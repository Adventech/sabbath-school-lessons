### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Rom. 15:4, 5; Rom. 5:3-5; 1 Samuweli 26; Ndum. 37:1-11.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ke sona esoMoya isiqhamo . . . ukuzeka kade umsindo” (Galati 5:22).

Iinzulu-lwazi zenza ufuniselo ngabantwana abaneminyaka emine ubudala nemarshmallow. Mntwana ngamnye waxelelwa yinzulu-lwazi ukuba angazithathela; noko kunjalo, ukuba umtwana ulinde de usonzulu-lwazi wabuya ekusebenzeni, uzakunikwa zibe mbini. Abanye abantwana bayingxala imarshamallow emilonyeni yabo ngoko nangoko esakuhamba usonzulu-lwazi; abanye baalinda. Kwaqatshelwa umohluko.

Oosonzulu-lwazi ngoko balandela ekhondweni laba bantwaba de bayokufika kwiminyaka yokufikisa kwabo. Abo baalindayo babaqhele ngcono, abafundi abangcono, yaye bezithembe ngakumbi kunabo bangazange balinde. Kwabonakala ukuba umonde luphawu lwento enkulu, into ebalulekileyo kwisimilo somntu. Akumangalisi, ngoko, ukuba iNkosi isixelela ukuba masiwuvelise.

Kule veki, sizakukhangele ukuba yintoni enokuba kwezinye zezona onti zivavanya zonke: ionti yokulinda.

**Amagqabantshintshi Eveki**: Kutheni le nto kufuneka silinde umlibe kwizinto? Zifundo zini esinokuzifunda ngomonde lo gama sikwionti?

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Septemba 10._
