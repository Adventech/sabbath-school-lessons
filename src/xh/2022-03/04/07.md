“Ulwakhiwo lwesimilo ngowona msebenzi ubalulekileyo wakha wabekwa emagxeni oluntu; kwaye akukho xesha apho ukufundwa kwawo ngenyameko kwakha kwabaluleka njengangoku. Akho sizukulwana ngaphambili sakha sabizelwa ukuhlangana nemiba ebaluleke ngolo hlobo; akho xesha ngaphambili apho umlisela nomthinjana wakha wajamelana nemingcipheko emikhulu njengale bajamelene nayo namhlanje.”—Ellen G. White, Education, p. 225.

“Kumzekeliso, iintombi eziziyatha zibonakalaliswa njengezicela i-oli, nezisilelayo ukuyifumana ekuyiceleni kwazo. Olu luphawu lwabo bangazilungiselelanga ngokuphuhlisa isimilo ukuba simelane nexesha lentlekele. Kukhangeleka ngathi kufuneka baye kubamelwane babo bathi, Ndinike isimilo sakho, kungenjalo ndakulahleka. Ezo zazilumkile azizange zikwazi ukunikezela i-oli yazo kwizibane ezicimayo zeentombi eziziyatha. Isimilo asosuleli. Asinakuthengwa okanye sithengiswe; kufuneka sizuzwe. INkosi inike wonke ubani ithuba lukuzuza isimilo esinobulungisa kweli xesha lenceba; kodwa ayinikezelanga ngendlela apho esinye isenzeleleli esingumntu singadlulisela komnye isimilo esiphuhliswe ngokuthubeleza kumava anzima, ngokufunda izifundo kuMfundisi omkhulu, ukuze akwazi ukubonakalisa umonde phakathi kwesilingo, aze asebenzise ukholo ancothule iintaba zoko kungenakwenzeka.”—Ellen G. White, The Youth’s Instructor, Jan. 16, 1896.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo**

`➊ Kuthetha ntoni “ukwakha isimilo”? Ungakwenza njani oku? Kuyinto esemqoka kangakanani ukwakhiwa kwesimilo ebomini bakho nentlanganisela yebandla okuyo?`

`➋ Isifundo sangoLwesine sithethe ngendima ebalulekileyo yoluntu ebomini bomKristu. Lisebenza kakuhle njani ibandla ongena kulo njengomzimba kaKristu? Niyibonakalisa kakuhle njani iNkosi njengentlanganisela? Njengeklasi, thethani ngeninokukwenza ukuphucula.`

`➌ Njengeklasi, thethani ngombuzo wokuba kutheni ukwakhiwa kwesimilo kubalulekile nje, nokuba sisindiswa ngokholo kuphela kuYesu. Ukuba ubulungisa baKhe nesimilo saKhe esigqibeleleyo zizinto ezisisindisayo, ngoko ke kutheni kufuneka siphuhlise isimilo?`

`➍ UHelen Keller, owaba sisithulu eyimfama kwasebuncinaneni, wabhala, “Isimilo asinakuphuhliswa ekonwabeni nakwinzolo. Kuphela kusemaveni okulingwa nenkxaleko onokuthi umphefumlo womelezwe, umbono utyhileke, umdla uvuselelwe, nempumelelo iphunyezwe.”—Leadership, vol. 17, no. 4.   Uyangqina? Xoxani ngobudlelwane phakathi kwesimilo, ukuva ubunzima, nemfazwe enkulu.`
