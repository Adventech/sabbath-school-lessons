### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Eksodus 14, Eksod. 15:22-27, Eksod. 17:1-7, Mizekeliso 3, Luka 4:1-13, 1 Pet. 1:6-9.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Enigcoba ngalo, nokuba nithe naba buhlungu umzuzwana ngoku, ukuba nimelwe kuko, phakathi kwezilingo ngezilingo” (1 Petros 1:6).

Emini emaqanda, nasekuveni umculo wamanye amazwi, intaka evalelweyo ayinakuyicula ingoma inkosi yayo efuna ukuyifundisa yona. Ifunda isinqam sokuya, isingcangcazelo sokuya, kodwa hayi uncuthu lwengoma olulodwa okanye olupheleleyo. Kodwa inkosi yayo iyayigquma ikupu le, ize iyibeke apho intake kuloo ngoma inye izakuyicula. Esithokothokweni, iyazama izame kwakhona ukucula loo ngoma de ifundwe, ize idanduluke ngoncuthu lwengoma olugqibeleleyo. Emva koko intake iyakhutshwa, kwaye nanini na emva koko ingayicula loo ngoma ekukhanyeni. UThixo ubaphatha ngaloo ndlela kanye abantwana baKhe. Unengoma azakusifundisa yona, kwaye xa sithe sayifunda kumathunzi eembandezelo singayicula unampela emva koko.”—Ellen G. White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 472.

Phawula ukuba lo uthatha intake ayise kwisithokothoko yinkosi ngokwayo.

Kulula ukuqonda ukuba uSathana ubangela intlungu, kodwa uThixo ngokwaKhe angathabatha inxaxheba ekusikhokeleleni kwii-onti apho singcamla khona ukudideka okanya intlungu?

**Amagqabantshintshi Eveki**: Mizekelo mini onokuyicinga eBhayibhileni nalapho uThixo ngokwaKhe akhokelela abantu kumava aziyo ukuba azakuquka ukubulaleka? Ucinga ukuba yayizeziphi iingoma ezintsha awayefuna bazicule?

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Julayi 16._
