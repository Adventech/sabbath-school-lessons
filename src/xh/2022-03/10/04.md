---
title:  Ukuthanda Abo Basikhubekisayo
date:  30/08/2022
---

Ubani wakha wathi: “Ukuthanda iintshaba zethu, ngoko, akuthethi kuthi kufanele sithande ubumdaka egqumeleleke kubo iperile; koko kuthetha ukuthi sithanda iperile esenkunkumeni. . . . UThixo akasithandi kuba ngemvelo sithandeka. Kodwa siyathandeka kuba Yena uyasithanda.”

`Xa ujonga “iintshaba” zakho, ukholisa ukubona ntoni-iperile okanye ubumdaka ekubo? `

`Funda: kuMateyu 5:43-48. UYesu usibizela ekuthandeni nasekuthandazeleni iintshaba zethu. Mzekelo mni wendalo asinika wona uYesu apho osinceda siqonde isizathu sokuba sizithande iintshaba zethu? Ngongoma ni asifundisa yona?  `

KuMateyu 5:45, uYesu usebenzisa umzekelo kaYihlo waKhe osemazulwini ukubonakalisa indlela amasibaphathe ngayo abo basivisa kabuhlungu, abathi mhlawumbi basifake kolona hlobo luqatseleyo lweonti. UYesu uthi uYise uthumela intsikelelo yemvula ngokufanayo kolungisayo nongalungisiyo; ukuba uThixo unika nkqu nabo bangalunganga imvula, simele ukubaphatha njani ngoko, nathi?

UYesu akazami kuthi masisoloko sinemvakalelo eshushu, engacacanga kuye wonke ubani osihluphayo, nangona oku kunokwenzeka. Okokuqala, ukuthanda iintshaba akwenzelwanga ukuba kube yimvakalelo esinayo ngakuzo koko iizenzo ezithile ngakuzo ezityhila inkathalo nesihoyo.

UYesu uqukumbela esi sicatshulwa ngendima esoloko idal’ingxoxo: “ ‘Ngoko yibani ngabafezekileyo, njengokuba uYihlo osemazulwini efezekile’ ” (Mat. 5:48). Kodwa intsingiselo icace gca kulo mbandela: Abo bantu bafuna ukugqibelela njengoko uThixo egqibelele mababonakalise uthando kwiintshaba zabo kwanjengoko uThixo ebonakalisa uthando kwezaKhe. Ukugqibelela emehlweni kaThixo kukuthanda ikcaso; kwaye ukwenza oku kuthatha ubulali bentliziyo obunikezelwa nguThixo kuphela.

`Ugcine engqongweni ingcaciso yethu yobulalali (ukunyamezela intlungu ngomondo kungekho kucaphuka”), dwelisa iinguqu ekufuneka uzenzile ukuze uvumele iNkosi ikunike uhlobo lobulali bentliziyo oluzakunceda ube nesimo esilungileyo “ngakwiintshaba.”`
