date:  28/08/2022
---

`U-Oswald Chambers utshilo ukuthi kufuneka sibe “sisonka esinkuphukileyo newayini echithekileyo” kwabanye. Ucinga ukuba uthetha ukuthini ngoku?  `

Kuyo yonke iBhayibhile kukho imizekelo yabantu “abaphulwa” ukuze bakhonze abanye. UMoses wabizelwa ekunyamezeleni amaze angenasiphelo entlebendwane nogxeko xa wayekhokela abantu ukusinga kwiLizwe lesiThembiso. UYosefu wabizelwa kuhambo oluquka ukungcatshwa nokubhadla entolongweni xa wayebekwa kwisikhundla sokusebenza eYiputa. Kwimeko ngaye, uThixo waavumela iimeko ukuze ubomi babantu baKhe bube ngamaqonga obabalo nenkathalo yaKhe, hayi nje kuphela kubo koko nangenxa yokulungelwa kwabanye, kanjalo UThixo angasisebenzisa nathi ngalo ndlela. Kulula ukuziva unomsindo okanye ucaphuka kwezoo meko. Kodwa njengoko siphawule izolo, ubulali sisakhono osinikwa nguThixo sokunyamezela ezo zinto zinjalo “ngomonde kungekho kucaphuka.”

`Funda: kuHezekile 24:15-27. Kwenzeka ntoni apha? Kwakutheni ukuze uHezekile afakwe kule onti?  `

KuHezekile 24:24 uThixo uthi, “ ‘ “UHezekile sisimanga kuni; niya kwenza njengako konke akwenzileyo; ekuzeni kwako oko, niya kwazi ukuba ndiyiNkosi uYehova.” ’ ” Ngomzekelo kaHezekile, abantu bakaSirayeli babezakweyiseka yinyaniso yokuba ungubani uThixo, uYehova oyiNgangamsha-kwaye babezakuyibona le nyaniso xa bengcamla ukuzaliseka kwesiprofeto esasifuziselwe bubomi bukaHezekile nenkxwaleko awayejamelene nayo. Ngubani owaziyo ukuba bangaphi abantu abayakumbona “uYehova oyiNgangamsha” ngathi kwiindawo zethu ezaphukileyo, ngokunjalo?

`Kungekudala ubomi ngokwabo buyasaphula sonke. Abe yintoni amava akho ngokwaphuka? Zifundo zini ozifundileyo? Umphefumlo wakho owaphukileyo ungasetyenziswa njani yiNkosi ukunceda abanye abantu?   `
