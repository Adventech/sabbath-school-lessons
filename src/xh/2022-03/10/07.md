Funda Ellen G. White, “The Importance of Seeking True Knowledge,” pp. 453, 454, in The Ministry of Healing; “The Sermon on the Mount,” pp. 298-314, in The Desire of Ages; “The Worker and His Qualifications, p. 630, in Evangelism.

“Iinzima ekufuneka siqubisene nazo zinokucuthwa kakhulu bobobulali obuzifihle kuKristu. Ukuba sinentobeko yeNkosi yethu, siza kunyukela ngaphaya kwezenyeliso, izicukucezo, iingcaphukiso, esisesichengeni sazo mihla le, kwaye ziya kuyeka ukubeka ithinzi emphefumlweni. Obona bungqina buphakamileyo ku… kumnKristu kukuzeyisa. Lowo uthi phantsi kwempatho-mbi okanye inkohlakalo asilele ukubamba umoya ozolileyo nokholosileyo uvimba uThixo ilungelo laKhe lokutyhila kuye ingqibelelo yaKhe yesimilo. Ukuthobeka kwentliziyo ngamandla anikezela uloyiso kubalandeli bakaKristu; luphawu loqhagamshelwano lwabo kunye neenkundla zangasentla.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 301.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo**

`➊ Intobeko isivumela njani ukuba “sinyukele ngaphaya” kwezikhubekiso neengcaphukiso? Ucinga ukuba loluphi uphawu olubalulekileyo lwentobeko olusivumela ukuba senze oku?`

`➋ Kweyakho inkcubeko eyodwa, iimpawu zentobeko nobulali zibonwa njani? Zihlonitshiwe, zidelekile, okanye kwenzeka ni? Zintlobo zini zoxinzelelo ojamelana nazo kwinkcubeko yakho ezisebenza ngokuchaseneyo nokuphuhlisa kwakho ezi mpawu?`

`➌ Ikhona imizekelo emikhulu yobulali nentobekho kubantu abaphila kule mihla? Ukuba kunjalo, ngobani, bazibonakalise njani ezi mpawu, kwaye ungafunda ntoni kubo?`

`➍ Kutheni le nto sisoloko sifanisa ubulali nentobeko nobubhetyebhetye nje?`

`➎  Sibonile indlela uDavide awayifuna ngayo iNkosi njengegwiba. Kodwa kusebenza njani oko? Elo gwiba lisoloko libonakaliswa njani? Ngamanye amazwi, singakwazi njani, njengebandla, ukuba ligwiba kwabo badinga igwiba? Luhlobo luni legwiba elibonelela ngalo ibandla ongena kulo? Ungenza ntoni ukunceda ukuba lenziwe indawo eligwiba kwabo balidingayo?`
