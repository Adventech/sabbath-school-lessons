### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Rom. 8:28-39, Yohane 14:1-14, Efe. 1:18-23, Isa. 40:27-31.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ngokholo wayishiya iYiputa, akawoyika umsindo wokumkani; kuba wakrota, wanga uyambona lowo ungenakubonwa” (Hebhere 11:27).

Inkcazelo yokholo kwincwadi yamaHebhere isoloko ingumcela-mngeni. “Ukholo ke kukukholosa ngezinto ezithenjiweyo; kukweyiseka bubukho bezinto ezingabonwayo” (Heb. 11:1). Singaqiniseka njani ngento esingayiboniyo? Noko kunjalo, oku yile nto kanye uMoses ayibonakalisa kwindima yethu yenkumbulo: “Wazingisa kuba wambona lowo ungenakubonwa” (Heb. 11:27, NIV).

Kucel’umngeni ngakumbi ukuqonda ukuba sibizelwe ekumboneni “lowo ungenakubonwa” hayi nje xa amathuba evuma, kodwa ngokukodwa xa yonke into ingeyiyo. Koku sidinga ukholo, ukholo olufana nolukaKristu ekufuneka lubunjwe yinyaniso engoThixo kunye nobukumkani bukaThixo. Inyaniso engokulunga kukaBawo wethu, amandla kwigama likaYesu, amandla oVuko, kunye nemfesane kaThixo ziinyaniso ezibalulekileyo eziyakusenza sime nkqi xa sikwi onti kwaye sinokuhendelwa ukuba sithandabuze yonke into.

**Amagqabantshintshi Eveki**: Zeziphi iinyaniso ngoThixo ezinokunceda ukusixhasa nkqu nakwezona meko zimandundu?

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Agasti 20._
