### Fundela Isifundo sale Veki

Rute 1:1–5; Rute 2:5–20; Yobhi 1:6–11; Mat. 4:8, 9; Estere 3:1–14; IsiTyh. 12:14–17.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kwathi, ukumkani akumbona uEstere ukumkanikazi emi entendelezweni, wambabala; ukumkani wayolulela kuEstere intonga yegolide ebisesandleni sakhe. Wasondela ke uEstere, waphatha encamini yentonga leyo” (Estere 5:2).

Kule veki siza kuqhubeka nokuhlalutya amabali awayebonisa kwangaphambili iziganeko zemihla yokugqibela. Ngokusebenzia iziganeko ezazingabantu benene nabantu, uThixo usinceda ukuba sizibone izinto ngakwelaKhe icala aze asincede ukuba sizichaze iziprofeto ezilandelayo, ezinikwe ukuba zincede ukunika amandla ukholo lwethu.

Ingqalelo yethu ijika iye kwiintokazi ezibalulekileyo ezimbini ezimabali azo achukumise izizukulwana: uRute noEstere. Omnye wayengumhlolokazi ophucwe konke ofumana ithemba emva kokuhlangana noBhohazi onobubele, umhlanguli osisizalwana sakhe. Umtshato wabo waba libali elithandwa kakhulu ngamaKristu ngenxa yendlela obonakalalisa ngayo ukusithanda kukaKristu. Omnye, yintwazana eyafumana ulwazi ngecebo lokutshabalalisa abantu bayo yaza yazifumana iphoswa kwiqonga eliphezulu kumdlalo otyhilekayo ukubasindisa.

Esiprofetweni, phofu, umfazi ungumfuziselo onamandla webandla likaThixo, ophosa ukukhanya okuninzi kwindlela uThixo ababona ngayo abantu baKhe. Masiqwalasele iingxelo zeBhayibhile yaba bafazi babini, abameko zobomi babo zinikwe ukungafi eLizwini likaThixo, size sizame ukukhupha nasiphi isifundo xa sinakho kumava wabo.
