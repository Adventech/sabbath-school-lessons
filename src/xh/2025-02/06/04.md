---
title:  Imvana YePasika
date:   06/05/2025
---

Incwadi yesiTyhilelo ibhekisa kuYesu “njengeMvana” phantse amaxesha angamashumi amathathu. Kususela kwimihla yasekuqaleni kwicebo losindiso, abantu bakaThixo basebenzisa iimvana njengomfuziselo kaMesiya ozayo. UAbheli wanikela “kumazibulo ezimvu zakhe” (Gen. 4:4), kwathi ngaphambi kokuba uIsrayeli ayishiye iYiputha aye kwilizwe lesithembiso, ayalelwa ukuba ahlangule wonke umntu olizibulo okanye isilwanyana ngemvana emnyaka mnye ubudala (Eksodus 12:5).

`Funda Eksodus 12:1–11; Isaya 53:7, 8; 1 Korinte 5:7; noIsiTyhilelo 5:6. Zisifundisa ntoni ezi ndima ngoYesu njengombingelelo wePasika? Kuthetha ntoni oko kuthi ngabanye?`

Sekuyiminyaka emva kokufa, ukuvuka, nokunyuka kukaKristu, uPetros ethetha ngokwenzekayo, waza wabhala wathi, “Khumbulani ixabiso enakhululwa ngalo kwintlalo ebingento yookhokho benu. Anihlawulelwanga nganto itshitshayo, ifana nesiliva negolide. Nahlawulelwa ngegazi elixabisekileyo leXhwane elinguKrestu ongenasisihla nasiphako” (1 Pet. 1:18, 19, IBHAYIBHILE).

UYesu waphila ubomi obunye bomntu obanelisa ubungcwele bukaThixo; thina sonke sonile, kwaye indlela esibuphila ngayo ubomi bethu besono ngokuphandle isixelela ubuxoki ngendalo yoMenzi wethu.

UYesu, sekunjalo, waba ngu“Adam wokugqibela” (1 Kor. 15:45). Apho sasilela khona, waphila ngokugqibeleleyo. Kubuntu baKhe, wayeyiyo yonke uhlanga loluntu olwalufanele ukuba lube yiyo. Wasibonakalalisa isidima sikaThixo ngokufezekileyo. “Ukuba undibonileyo mna,” watsho kuFilipu, “umbonile uBawo” (Yohane 14:9).

Ngelo xesha linye, uYesu wabethelelwa ngemini yePasika, okwakubonisa ngakumbi ukuba usisiqu seMvana. KuYohane 18:19, 20, uYesu wathi “[u]thetha ngokungafihlisiyo” ngemfundiso yaKhe. Ngendlela ehamba kunye, ngokubhekiselele kwimvana yePasika ekuEksodus 12:5, 6, abantwana bakaIsrayeli baya bayalelwa ukuba bakhethe imvana yePasika, “baligcine” okanye balibeke embonisweni ngeentsuku ezingaphambi kombingelelo. Xa umbingeleli omkhulu wayembuza uYesu ngeemfundiso zaKhe, watsho uYesu ukuthi, Yena ngokwaKhe wabekwa wangumboniso etempileni ukuze wonke umntu amqaphele. Ubomi baKhe, imisebenzi yaKhe, imfundiso yaKhe—zonke zazimveza ukuba ungubani. UyiMvana engenasiphako, eyona ntetho inamandla kakhulu yobulungisa nozuko lukaThixo.

`Zeziphi iindlela esingabonisa ngcono ngazo ukugqibelela kwesimilo sikaYesu kobethu ubomi?`
