### Fundela Isifundo sale Veki

Isa. 1:2–15, Heb. 10:3–10, Eksod. 12:1–11, 1 Kor. 5:7, Hagayi 2:7–9, Isa. 6:1–5, IsiTyh. 4:7–11.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Avuma ingoma entsha, esithi: Ufanele ukuyithabatha incwadi, nokuwavula amatywina ayo; ngokuba wena waxhelwa, wasithenga ukuba sibe ngabakaThixo ngalo igazi lakho, siphuma kuzo zonke izizwe, neelwimi, nabantu, neentlanga” (IsiTyhilelo 5:9).

Wathi xa uYesu esiya ngakuye, uYohane umBhaptizi wadanduluka: “Nantso iMvana kaThixo, ethwala isuse isono sehlabathi!” (Yohane 1:29). Oku yayikukubhekisa okungenayo impazamo kwingcamango yombingelelo wesilwanyana, wonke owawusalatha ekufeni okumelayo endaweni yalo lonke uluntu.

EBhayibhileni, asikwazi ukuwuphepha umxholo wombingelelo wesilwanyana; utyhutyha kuyo yonke njengomsonto obomvu kuwo wonke amaphepha udlala indawo esembindini kumboniso omkhulu kwisiTyhilelo, apho uYohane asiwa kwigumbi letrone kaThixo (IsiTyhilelo 4 nesi-5). Into yokuba uYesu avele kulo mboniso ungundoqo, ebonakala njengemvana exheliweyo (IsiTyh. 5:6), isisitshixo esibalulekileyo ekuqondeni konke okwenzekayo emdlalweni wesiprofeto.

Kule veki siza kuqwalasela eminye imixholo yombingelelo esinika ukuqonda ngoYesu, iMvana exheliweyo, umdlali ophambili kumboniso okwigumbi letrone. Wamkeleke njengofaneleyo, apho kungekho wumbi ufaneleyo, kube ukufanela kwaKhe okumangalisayo kuthetha okuninzi ngoko iNkosi yayikwenza ngohlelo lweenkonzo zemibingelelo. Imveza enguThixo onothando olungenamda owenza umbingelelo wokugqibela, isenzo thina, nabanye abazingqondi beyunivesi, esiya kukhwankqiswa yiyo kuse emaphakadeni.

_*Funda: Isifundo sale Veki Ulungiselela ISabatha kaMeyi 10._
