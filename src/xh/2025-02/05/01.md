### Fundela Isifundo sale Veki

Gen. 2:9–17; Dan. 2:31–35; Isa. 17:12, 13; Dan. 7:1–3; Roma 3:10–19; IsiTyh. 12:15, 16; IsiTyh. 10:1–11.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Yekani, nazi ukuba ndinguThixo mna. Ndiya kuphakanyiswa ezintlangeni, ndiya kuphakanyiswa ehlabathini!” (Indumiso 46:10).

Ekuhambeni kweenkulungwane, abanye abantu baxoxa besithi uThixo wayekufuna ukuWa [ukungena kwesono], kwaye yaye iyinjongo yaKhe ukuba abantu bahle bangene esonweni nasekufeni, ukuze oko kumse Yena—ekumntu onguYesu—emnqamlezweni. Ngapha koko, yeyiphi enye indlela enamandla nezotywe kakuhle ebonisa ubunzulu nokuthanda kwaKhe uluntu kunokubafela emnqamlezweni? Ngokufutshane, ukucinga kuyaqhuba, uThixo wayekudinga ukuwa koluntu.

Leyo yindawo eyoyikekayo nembi ukuba ithethwe. Ayizange ibe yinjongo kaThixo ukuba kuwe kwauSathana okanye uluntu. Uvukelo lukaSathana, emva koko ololuntu, lwaba yintlekele eyaba neziphumo ezikhulu, kwaye uvuyo lwethu kuYe lwaluya kuhlala luphelele ukuba abazali bethu bokuqala babengazange bawe.

Kule veki, siza kuqhubeka siqwalasela iingxaki ezabangwa kukuWa, kunye nolangazelelo lolawulo lomntu ngokuchasene nolawulo lukaThixo. Ezi nyaniso zityhilwe ngamandla encwadini kaDaniyeli, ebonisa ukuba uThixo wayelungisa xa elumkisa abantu baKhe ngokuya kwenzeka xa bemkile kuYe bakhetha ookumkani basemhlabeni endaweni yaKhe. Yeyona nto kanye abayifumanayo: ookumkani basemhlabeni, naboni abagagamela aboni—indibaniso engazange yalunga.

_*Funda: Isifundo sale Veki Ulungiselela ISabatha kaMeyi 3._
