---
title:  Abalawuli BeeNtlanga
date:   23/04/2025
---

Impatho kaThixo uIsrayeli ikunika iimbono ezityebileyo kwindlela aliphatha ngayo ibandla leTestamente eNtsha. Inyaniso yona, ngeendlela ezininzi, isizwe sikaIsrayeli saba ngumboniso wangaphambili weempazamo zebandla. Ayekude kakhulu ekubangeni naluphi uhlobo lokongama kwawo amaIsrayeli akudala, nawo amaKristu ayethabatheka lula, njengoko nangoku kusenjalo, zizihendo ezifanayo.

`Funda Mateyu 20:25–28. Zeziphi iimpazamo awalumkisa ngazo uYesu abafundi baKhe ukuba baziphephe ekumiliseleni umsebenzi webandla lobuKristu?`

UIsrayeli wacela ukumkani womntu, isicelo esakhokelela ekuweni kokuziphatha kwesizwe. Ookumkani babheka phambili ekukhohlakaleni ngakumbi wada uThixo wayivumela iBhabheli ukuba ibathabathe ibase ebukhobokeni abantu baKhe, phofu ngendlela yokubalungisa.

Into efanayo yenzeka kwimbali yebandla lobuKristu. Kwathi nangona babengafanele ukuzimisa njengesizwe seNtlanga, wathi akuthabatha iintambo zolawulo uKonstantina wazibiza ngokuthi ungumKristu, amakholwa aziva ekhululekile: intshutshiso yayiphelile ngoku! Loo nto ngokwayo yayiyintsikelelo, kodwa emva koko kwenzeka ebandleni ukuba bangakwazi ukulinganisa igunya likakumkani ukuba libancede.

Kwabakho iqela leengxabano ezinkulu phakathi kwamaKristu enkulungwane yesine, lathi xa ibandla lizifumana lingakwazi ukuzisombulula, labhenela kukumkani ukuba angenelele. Ukususela apha ukubheka phambili, ubhishopu wanyukela ngasentla ngokubaluleka, apho wakha wayibhishopu eyintloko phakathi kwalingana nabo. Ibandla lacela uburhulumente ukuba bungenelele kwimicimbi yenkolo, baza uburhulumente balubeka unyawo emnyango, emva koko izinto zonakala kakhulu.

NjengoIsrayeli wakudala, ezininzi kwezona zahluko zimnyama embalini yobuKristu zisisiphumo esingqalileyo sokuhlangabezana kwebandla nehlabathi. Wathi uIsrayeli akubuyela kunqulo lwemifanekiso eqingqiweyo baza ookumkani bakhe bonakaliswa kukuba nelithe legunya—kwada kwafikwa apho kunikelwa abantwana kwizithixo—ibandla lacondobala ukwamkela ezininzi kwiindyebo neendlela zobukumkani bobuhedeni kwada kwafika kwindawo apho amakholwa amaninzi athembekileyo abulawa ngenxa yokholo lwawo kuba ayebonwa eyingozi kwisimiso sebandla nesizwe.

`Kweyakho inkcubeko, ekuhlaleni kwakho, zeziphi iindlela apho izihendo ezikwa zinye zinokubeka engozini ukunyaniseka kwenkolo yethu?`
