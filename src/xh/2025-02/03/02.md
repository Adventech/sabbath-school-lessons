---
title:  '[Ukuba] Nyama-nye'
date:   13/04/2025
---

Zimbalwa izifaniso zeBhayibhile ezibethelela ukusondelelana uThixo akulangazelelayo nohlanga loluntu ngaphezu koko komtshato. Olu ufaniso lusetyenziswe rhoqo kwibali leBhayibhile—kwaye livela licace kakhulu kwisiTyhilelo —kuba kunyanzelekile kubafundi beBhayibhile ukuba bakubambe oko uThixo aqhubela kuko xa esebenzisa lona eLizwini.

`Funda Genesis 2:23–25 nomaEfese 5:29–32. Kungaziphi iindlela othi umtshato womntu ubonakalalise iqhina phakathi kukaKristu noluntu?`

Kwesinye isiganeko, xa uYesu wayethetha kubaFarisi, wacaphula ingxelo ekuGenesis yomtshato ka-Adam noEva, nabasuka bakhawuleza banombuzo kuyo abaFarisi, “Phofu, ibiyini na ukuba uMoses awise umthetho wokuthi, makamnike incwadi yokwahlukana, amale?” (Matt. 19:7).

UMoses, phofu, wayethatyathwa njengomprofeti ongumseki wesizwe. Yiba nombono woMbhali wesiseko somtshato ethelekiswa nowaKhe umprofeti. Ubuchule babo babufana nendlela abamphatha ngayo uYesu: bakholisa ukuzama ukubonisa ukuba iimfundiso zaKhe zaziphikisana neziBhalo.

Umtshato othembekileyo wobomi bonke wawungumgangatho owamiswa nguThixo ekusekweni kohlanga loluntu. Uluntu oluwileyo, ngelishwa, lwasonakalisa esi sipho esaye sisisiseko esivela kuThixo.

Mhlawumbi, njengoko kubalulekile okunikwa umtshato sisiBhalo, asikuko ukungqubana kweziganeko ukuba isimiso [somtshato] sisoloko siphantsi kokuhlaselwa okunngamandla. Sikunye neSabatha, sesinye sezipho ezibini esazinikwa e-Eden, kwaye zozibini zenzelwe ukubonisa ulangazelelo lukaThixo lokuba nolwalamano olusondeleyo nendalo yaKhe.

Umtshato, ukwenziwa kwesibini esayameneyo sabantu ababini abangagqibelelanga, uya kusoloko unika isizathu sokungqubana. Umtshato phakathi kwebandla noKristu ukukwenziwa kwesibini koMsindisi ogqibeleleyo nomtshakazi ongagqibelelanga kwaphela. Noko kunjalo, sinakho ukufunda ngothando lukaThixo koko kunikwa ngumtshato olungileyo.

`Yiyo le imithetho-siseko emithathu yomtshato. Kuqala, mxolele otshate naye, nokuba akufanelekanga. Okwesibini, mamkele otshate naye, neziphoso nako konke, njengoko uKristu esamkela thina, neziphoso nako konke. Okwesithathu, njengoko uKristu waxabisa thina ngaphambi kwaKhe, xabisa otshate naye ngaphambi kwakho. Ingenzeka njani le mithetho-siseko yakhelwe phezu kwegospile ukusinceda ukuba singaneli nje ukuqonda indlela uThixo alamana nathi ngayo kodwa nokunceda nawuphi na umtshato?`
