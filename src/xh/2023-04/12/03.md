---
title:  Kwinkundla Yasemzini
date:  18/12/2023
---

Kamva, emva kokuwa kweBhabheli nokuphakama kwaMedi namaPersi, uninzi lwamaYuda lwabuyela kumazwe okhokho bawo. Kodwa ayingawo onke abuyelayo. Amanye ahlala apho ayehlala khona kangangesizukulwana okanye nangaphezulu.

Ngale mvelaphi engqondweni, nesinawo umnconwanana womxholo webali likaEstere. (Estere 1:2). Apha kulapho ibali leBhayibhile lityhileka khona, ubukumkani bamaPersi phantsi kwalo kumkani.

Kwisahluko 1, ukumkani uVashiti uyakhiqwa ngukumkani, nto leyo ekhokelela ekubeni akhangele omnye, ozakungena ezihlangwini zikaVashiti okhiqiweyo. Kukulomxholo apho uEstere nomalume wakhe, uMordekayi, baqala khona ukuvela.

`Funda kuEstere 2:1-9. Ezi ndima zisifundisa ntoni ngemeko kaMordekayi noEstere?`

Kukhangeleka ngathi uMordekayi, njengegosa lasebukhosini, wayehleli esangweni lasebhotwe kwaye wayehlala kwisixeko saseShushan kunye nentombi yakhe, okanye umzala, uEstere. Ngenxa yesikhundla sabo nokuhlala apho babehlala khona, babentyumpantyumpeka kwinkcubeko yamaPersi. Oku kumele ukuba yinxalenye yesizathu sokuba uEstere achongelwe ukuya kuzibonakalalisa kuKumkani: (Estere 2:8).

`Funda kuEstere 2:10, 20. Kwakusenzeka ntoni apha, kwaye kwakutheni ukuze uMordekayi amnike umyalelo onjalo?`

Nangona indima ingatsho ngokutsolileyo ukuba kutheni, akunzima ukuthekelela. Njengabaphambukeli kwinkcubeko nenkolo yasemzini, enokuba nobutshaba, sizakubona, balumka ekutyeni tyum ngosapho nabantu babo.

`Zeziphi iimeko onokucinga ngazo apho kunokuba bubulumko ukuba ungabi phandle ngenkolo yakho? Okanye masingaze sikwenze oko? Ukuba akunjalo, kutheni?`
