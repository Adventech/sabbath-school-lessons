---
title:  'Ukwenza Abafundi: Ingqalelo Yephulo'
date:  09/10/2023
---

`Funda kuMateyu 28:16-20. Ziqalelo zini zobufundi onokuzibona kwesi sicatshulwa?`

UMateyu 28:16-20 uvakalisa umyalelo oseBhayibhileni, ovamise ukuchazwa njengoMyalelo oMkhulu (Mat. 28:18–20), nalapho uYesu ayalela abalandeli baKhe ukuba baye ngaphandle ukuze benze abafundi, bebafundisa ngokwasenkolweni, baze babangenise kubudlelane (bona kuMarko 16:15, 16; Luka 24:44–49; Yohane 20:21–23; Zenzo 1:8).

Iziqalelo ezisisiseko zikaMateyu 28:16-20 zinokubonwa kwiinkalo ezine ezilula: (1) uYesu uyalela abafundi baKhe ukuba baye eGalili ukuze babe kunye naYe (Mat. 28:16, 17), (2) uYesu ufika kubo, avakalise igunya nobungangamsha baKhe (Mat. 28:18), (3) uYesu emva koko uyalela abafundi baKhe kumsebenzi otsolileyo-ukuthi, ukwenza abafundi (Mat. 28:18), (4) uYesu uthembisa ngokuba kunye nabafundi kude kube sekupheleni (Mat. 28:20).

Ukwenza abafundi yinjongo ephambili yoMyalelo oMkhulu, nowona msebenzi ungundoqo kwiphulo. Ngeliphandle, kwimvelaphi yolwimi lwesiGrike, isiqalo sikaMateyu 28:19 sithi, “Xa niyile, ngoko, yenzani abafundi [...]” Lo “ngoko” unikezela isiseko kumyalelo koko sele kuthethiwe (Mat. 28:18): amandla kaYesu, igunya, kunye nobungangamsha-zonke ezi zivela kuloyiso aluzuze ekuvukeni kwaKhe.

Kubalulekile ukuveza ukuba esona senzi sibenzayo esinamandla ayalelayo kuMyalelo oMkhulu “kukwenza abafundi.” Ukufundisa abantu bonke, ukubabhaptiza, nokwabelana ngeemfundiso zikaYesu ehlabathini lonke ziimpawu zesigaba sobufundi. Apha uYesu ngokuphandle uyalela abafundi baKhe kwinjongo enye: ukwenza abafundi. Esi ngokwenene sesinye sezicatshulwa zovangelo ezitshatsheleyo kwisiBhalo sonke. Siphela ngesithembiso sikaYesu sokuba kunye nabalandeli baKhe unampela.

Ngokuqinisekileyo, uMyalelo oMkhulu wawulungiselelwe ukuba ungapheleli nje kubafundi bokuqala ababehlangene kulo meko iyodwa. Babengenakuya “kwiintlanga zonke” ngokunokwabo ukuze bafezekise iphulo elitsha abalinikiweyo lokwenza abafundi. Ngenxa yoko, umyalelo ugabalala ngokobume bawo: wonke umlandeli wokwenene kaYesu Kristu kufuneka abandakanyeke ekwenzeni abafundi. Ukongeza, umyalezo ekufuneka udlulisiwe-igospile engunaphakade kaYesu Kristu-ilungiselelwe ihlabathi lonke, kungekho mida yamhlaba, intlalo, okanye ubuzwe.

`Iphulo “kukwenza abafundi.” Lo myalelo weNkosi uyichaphazela njani indlela ophila nolugiselela ngayo abanye? Ungenza ntoni ukuba ubandakanyeke ngakumbi koko ubizelwe ukuba ukwenze?`
