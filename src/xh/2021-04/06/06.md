---
title:  Bubulumko Benu neNgqondo Yenu
date:  04/11/2021
---

UDuteronomi 4:1–9, njengoko sibonilelyo, yayi yinkcazo hayi nje yenqanaba elilodwa lohlanga kodwa yobizo lwalo lokuvangela, kanjalo. as we saw, was a powerful expression of not merely the nation’s special status but of its missionary calling, as well. Okutyhutyha zonke ezo ndima yingcamango yokuba kufuneka bathobele, balandele, benze oko uYehova abayalela ukuba mabakwenze.

`Funda: kwakhona kwiDuteronomy 4:6.Yintoni ayixelayo ngokutsolileyo uYehova ukuba “bubulumko nengqondo yabo” emehlweni ezo ntlanga?`

Xa ukrwaqula nje kunokuba ngathi le mimiselo namasiko zizo ezazi nobulumko nengqondo. Kodwa asiyonto ethethwa yile ndima leyo. UYehova wabafundisa imimiselo namasiko, ewe; kodwa ubulumko nengqondo yabo zaziphuma ekuyigcineni kwabo, ekuyithobeleni kwabo. Intobelo yabo-leyo yayibubulumko nengqondo yabo.

USirayeli ngewaba neyona nkqubo imangalisayo yomthetho nemiyalelo nemiqathango eyakha yabonwa lihlabathi (eneneni, yenzeka) kodwa kwakunokunceda ntoni konke oko ukuba uSirayeli wayengayilandeli? Ndaweni yoko, ubulumko babo, ingqondo yabo, yavela kanye ekubonakaliseni imithetho kaThixo ebomini babo. Kwakufuneka baphile iinyaniso abanike zona uYehova, yaye oko babenokukwenza kuphela ngokuzithobela. Konke ukukhanya nenyaniso zazingazokubanceda bona okanye abahedeni ababangqongileyo ukuba uSirayeli wayengayiphili loo nyaniso. Yiyo lo nto, ngokuthe rhoqo bebizelwa ukuba bathobele, kuba intobelo yabo kwimimiselo namasiko, hayi imimiselo namasiko ngokunokwayo, yayi yeyona nto ibalulekileyo, ekubeni lingqina ehlabathini.

“Intobelo yabo kumthetho kaThixo yayizakubenza babe yimizekelo emihle yempumelelo phambi kweentlanga zehlabathi. Lowo wayenokubanika ubulumko nesakhono kuwo wonke umsebenzi wobugcisa wayezakuqhubekeka engutitshala wabo, yaye wayezakubaphucula abaphakamise ngenxa yokugcina imithetho yaKhe. Ukuba babethobela, babezakukhuselwa kwizifo ezathwaxa ezinye iintlanga baze basikelelwe ngamandla engqondo. Uzuko lukaThixo, ubungangamsha namandla aKhe, kwakufuneka zityhilwe kuyo yonke impumelelo yabo. Babezakuba bubukumkani bababingeleli namatshawe. UThixo wabathwesa ngayo yonke into enokubenza babe lolona hlanga lukhulu ehlabathini.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Isifundos, p. 288.
