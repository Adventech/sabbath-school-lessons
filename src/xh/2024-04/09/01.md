### Fundela le Veki
Yohane 1:4; Yohane 10:10; Yohane 1:12, 13; Yohane 6:61–68; Num.13:23–33; Matt. 4:1–4.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Ndim indlela, ndim inyaniso, ndim ubomi. Akukho namnye uzayo kuBawo, engezi ngam’” (Yohane 14:6).

AwiVangeli kaYohane, xa uYesu wayebuzwa ukuba ungubani na, waphendula ngesaci esalatha ubuThixo. U“NDINGUYE” wayekukubhekisa, ngokungenako ukuphazanywa, kuYehova ngokwaKhe, owabonakala kuMoses etyholweni elivuthayo. “ ‘NDINGUYE ENDINGUYE,’ ” watsho kuMoses (Eksodus 3:14). KwaYena lo Thixo mnye, u“NDINGUYE” welo xesha “waba yinyama wahlala phakathi kwethu, sabubona ubuqaqawuli baKhe, ubuqaqawuli

kanye bowokuphela kwamzeleyo uYise, ezele lubabalo nenyaniso” (Yohane 1:14).

Umxholo u“NDINGUYE” ukho kuye wonke uYohane. Kule ndima yenkumbulo yale veki loo mxholo: “ ‘Ndim indlela, ndim inyaniso, ndim ubomi’ ” (Yohane 14:6). U“NDIM” ukukuKhanya kwehlabathi, iSonka soBomi, iSango okanye uCango lezimvu, uMalusi Olungileyo, noMdiliya weNyaniso.

Le veki iqhubeka nesityhilelo sikaThixo njengoko sisinikiwe kuYohane. Siza kuphanda nangakumbi ngokupheleleyo elinye icala lezinto, ekuthe kuzo, sele bukho ubungqina ngoYesu njengoMesiya, abanye bamala. Siya kufunda le ngcamango ngenxa yezizathu ezibini: ukuphepha impazamo enye, kodwa ukuphawula indlela esingathi ngayo sikwazi ukufikelela kwabo basengozini yokuyenza loo mpazamo, nabo.
