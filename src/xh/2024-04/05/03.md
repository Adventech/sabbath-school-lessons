title:  Intokazi Equleni
date:   28/10/2024
---

`Funda Yohane 4:7–15. Uyisebenzisa njani le ntlangano u Yesu ukuqala ukungqina kule ntokazi?`

“Intiyo phakathi kwamaYuda namaSamariya yayiyalela le ntokazi ukuba yenze ububele kuYesu; kodwa uMsindisi wayezama ukufumana isitshixo sokufikelela kule ntliziyo, kwaye ngesenzo esiphuma kuthando olungcwele, wacela, akanika, uncedo. Ukunika uncedo kwakunokwaliwa; kodwa ukuthemba kuzala ukuthemba.”—Ellen G. White, Ulangazelelo Lwamaphakade, iph. 184.

Njengoko kwakunjalo ekuhlanganeni kwaKhe noNikodemo, uYesu uyakwazi okusezintliziyweni zabanye. Ukusabela ekumangalisweni kwayo ukuba umYuda acele uncedo olunjalo kumSamariya, uYesu wangena ngqo emxholweni. “ ‘Ukuba ubusazi isipho sikaThixo, nokuba ngubani lo uthi kuwe, Ndiphe ndisele: unge ucele kuye wena, wakupha amanzi aphilileyo’ ” (Yohane 4:10).

Impendulo yentokazi yayifana nekaNikodemo, owabuza, “ ‘Zingathini na ezi zinto ukuba nokubakho?’ ” (Yohane 3:9) ngokokuzalwa ngokutsha. Yabuza, “ ‘Akunanto yakukha, nequla linzulu; uwazuza phi na ke amanzi aphilileyo lawo?’ ” (Yohane 4:11). Kwiimeko zombini, uYesu ubalathisa (omnye, ititshala ebalulekileyo yomYuda; omnye, intokazi yomSamariya yesimilo esixakileyo) kwiinyaniso zasemoyeni zangasentla ngamnye kubo awayekudinga ukuba azive aziqonde. Kwimeko nganye, uYesu, ngokwenene, bobabini wayebaxelela into enye: badinga amava okuguquka.

`Ithini imvelaphi yentetho kaYesu ngokwemvelaphi yeTestamente ngamanzi aphilileyo? (Yer. 2:13, Zek. 14:8).`

Amanzi ayafuneka ebomini; abantu abakwazi ukuphila ngaphandle kwamanzi, kwaye ngoko amanzi akwangumfanekiselo onamandla nofanelekileyo wabo ubomi obungunaphakade. Kungoko, uYesu athi, “ ‘Ke yena othe wasela kuwo amanzi, endiya kumpha wona mna, akasayi kuza anxanwe naphakade; aya kusuka amanzi, endiya kumpha wona, abe ngumthombo kuye wamanzi ampompozela ebomini obungunaphakade’ ” (Yohane 4:14).

`Funda Yohane 7:37, 38. Uthini uYesu kuthi kwezi ndima, kwaye siba nako njani oku akuthembisayo apha?`
