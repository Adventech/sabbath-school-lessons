### Fundela Isifundo sale Veki

Yosh. 21:43–45; 2 Tim. 2:11–13; Yoshuwa 23; IsiTy. 14:10, 19; Dut. 6:5.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Akukho nalinye igama kuzo zonke izithembiso ezilungileyo athe uYehova wazenza nendlu kaIsrayeli elikhe lasilela; onke enzeka” (Yoshuwa 21:45, ESV).

Xa uJohn F. Kennedy wayethetha neMelika ekubekweni kwakhe njengomongameli, ngoJanuwari 20, 1961, intetho yakhe yayingamazwi ali-1,366 kuphela, kodwa ashiya uphawu olungacimiyo kwiingqondo zamaMelika. Njengoko wayekhuthaza ilizwe lakhe ngemithwalo namalungelo abo, wathi: “Ngesazela esilungileyo umvuzo wethu ekuphela kwawo oqinisekileyo, nembali ingumgwebi wethu wokugqibela wezenzo zethu, masibheke phambili silikhokela ilizwe esilithandayo, sicela intsikelelo yaKhe noncedo lwaKhe, kodwa sisazi ukuba apha emhlabeni umsebenzi kaThixo kufuneka ngokwenene ungowethu.”

Xa uYoshuwa, inkokeli egugayo, yayiziva ukuba isondele ekupheleni kobomi bayo, wagqiba ukuba athethe neenkokeli zesizwe namaIsrayeli (Yoshuwa 23 no-24). UYoshuwa 23 ujonge ngakumbi kwixesha elizayo nendlela yokunqula uThixo: uYehova.

Kule veki, siza kufunda kunye intetho yokuqala kaYoshuwa, apho athi tshazi emva kuloyiso lukaIsrayeli, kodwa kwangelo xesha linye elanda indlela yempumelelo kaIsrayeli kwixesha elizayo.

_*Funda Isifundo sale veki ulungiselela iSabatha kaDisemba 20._
