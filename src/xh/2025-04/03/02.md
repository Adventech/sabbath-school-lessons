---
title:  Ukuwela IYordan
date:   12/10/2025
---

`Funda: Yoshuwa 3:1–5 noNumeri 14:41–44. Kwakutheni ukuze uThixo athi kumaIsrayeli kufuneka akulungise ngendlela eyodwa oko kwakuza kwenzeka?`

Kokokuqala oku ityeya yomnqophiso ikhankanywa encwadini kaYoshuwa. Ukuza kufika kule ndawo kwibali leTestamente eNdala, ityeya ivelile kumxholo wengcwele (Eks. 40:21); kuhambo lukaIsrayeli ukusuka eSinayi (Num. 10:33–36); nakumzamo wokuqala ukoyisa iKanan (Num. 14:44). Yayiyeyona mpahla ingcwele engcweleni kaIsrayeli kwaye inezinto ezintathu, iyileyo imele ulwalamano olukhethekileyo noThixo: (1) amacwecwe anemiThetho eliShumi; (2) intonga ka-Aron, umbingeleli omkhulu; (3) isitya semana (Eks. 16:33; Heb. 9:4).

Ityeya nokulungiselela ukuwela iYordan kwawakhumbuza amaIsrayeli ukuba ayengangeni eKanan ngohlobo nexesha lawo. Ukoyisa kwakuya kuba yimpumelelo kuphela xa elandele indlela nexesha likaThixo. UThixo — ochazwa njengosetroneni phezu kweekherubhi ezazigqume ityeya yomnqophiso (Eks. 25:22, Num. 7:89), nazo zonke iintshukumo ezalatha kwiintshukumo zetyeya—ungena eKanan phambi kwamaIsrayeli njengOkhokelayo ekoyiseni.

Isaci esiguqulwe “ngcwalisa” (Yosh. 3:5) okanye “zinikela” (ESV) libhekisa kwinkqubo yohlambululo efana neyayisenziwa ngababingeleli phambi kokuba baqale inkonzo yabo engcweleni (Eks. 28:41, Eks. 29:1) nokwakusenziwa ngabantu ngaphambi kokuba aziTyhile eSinayi (Eks. 19:10, 14). Oku kuzingcwalisa kwakuquka ukubeka bucala isono nokususa konke ukungacoceki kwenkonzo. Umyalelo omnye uyavela kuNumeri 11:18, wayame kummangaliso owawulindelekile kaThixo. Ukulungisa okunjalo kwakufuneka ngaphambi kokuba kuliwe amadabi emfazweni (Dut. 23:14). Ngaphambi kokuba uThixo alwele uIsrayeli edabini, kufuneka babonise ukwayama kuYe nokumthemba njengoMkhokeli wabo.

Ummangaliso wokuwela iYordan wawuza kubonisa uIsrayeli ukuba isithembiso sikaYehova sokugxothela ngaphandle aba bantu basezweni sinokuthenjwa. Lowo unokwenza indlela eyomileyo ewela iYordan unako ukubanika isipho selizwe.

`UThixo akasoloko eyohlula iYordan. Ukungenelela kwaKhe akusoloko kuyinto ephandle. Ucinga ukuba singenza njani ukuphuhlisa ukuhlala silungile kwethu kwasemoyeni ukuze sibe ngamava sikuqonde ukungenelela kukaThixo ngenxa yethu?`
