### Fundela Isifundo sale Veki

Yosh. 5:1–7; Eks. 12:6; 1 Kor. 5:7; Yosh. 8:30–35; Dut. 8:11, 14; Heb. 9:11, 12.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Funani ke kuqala ubukumkani bukaThixo nobulungisa baKhe, zaye zonke ezi zinto ziya kongezwa kuni” (Mateyu 6:33, ESV).

Kule veki siza kuqwalasela eminye imizuzu ngexesha lokuba seZweni leDinga kukaIsrayeli, xa uIsrayeli ezinikela ngokutsha kuYehova, maxa wambi bejongene nengozi ezayo. UYoshuwa wenza isigqibo esibonakala singacingisiswanga sokwalusa amaIsrayeli kummandla wotshaba (Yosh. 5:1–9); ukukhumbula iPasika bejongene nengozi ekufuphi (Yosh. 5:10–12); ukwakha isibingelelo nokunqula uYehova ngexesha uloyiso lushushu (Yosh. 8:30–35); nokumisa umnquba kaYehova ngexesha izizwe ezisixhenxe zingekalizuzi ilifa lazo (Yosh. 18:1, 2).

Kubomi bethu obuxakekileyo, sikholisa ukunika ingqalelo kwizinto ezingxamileyo eziphoswa kuthi bubomi. Amaxesha amaninzi, siyakutyeshela ukusika ixesha elibalulekileyo lokuhlaziya ukuzinikela kwethu kuThixo nokuma sibonise ukuba nombulelo ngoko asenzele kona naqhubeka esenzela yonke imihla. Unqulo lwakusasa nangokuhlwa, nesibingelelo sosapho, zibangathi zikude nomxholo wobomi bethu obugcwele kakhulu, obuqhutywa kukuba nethuba, nobukekelele kwimpumelelo. Kanti ke, ngaphakathi kubunzulu beentliziyo zethu, sonke siyawazi amathuba esiwachitha noThixo nesibathandayo ukuba ayeyona ndyebo ilungileyo egciniweyo yexesha lethu elinomda.

_*Funda: Isifundo sale veki ulungisela iSabatha kaNovemba 15._
