### Amathuba Esibini: Incwadi KaYoshuwa

Incwadi kaYoshuwa iphawula utshintsho ekukhokeleni kukaMoses ukuya kolukaYoshuwa.Iqala ngebali likaIsrayeli engena eZweni leDinga ize iphele ngabo belimile elo lizwe.

UYoshuwa, ngokwenene, wayejonene nomsebenzi onzima ekwakufuneka ewulandele. Kwakufuneka athathe apho wayeshiye khona uMoses, (uMoses!). Phofu, umngeni wawusekuqaleni kuphela. UYoshuwa kwakufuneka enze oko uMoses angazange akwenze: ukuthabatha isizwe, emva kwama-40 eminyaka entlango, awele iYordan angene eKanan, ngokwesithembiso uThixo awasenza kooyise kwiminyaka eyadlulayo.

“UMoses umkhonzi wam ufile; suka ke ngoko, uyiwele iYordan le, wena naba bantu bonke, niye kwelo zwe ndibanika lona oonyana bakaIsrayeli” (Yosh. 1:2).

Isitshixo salo lonke ibali sifumaneka apha, emazwini kaYehova kuYoshuwa: baza kungena “izwe mna [YHVH] Yehova endibanika lona.”

Kunjalo, kodwa uYoshuwa akazi kukwenza yedwa oku, kodwa kuphela ngamandla nenkokelo kaYehova, ongewabazisa kweli lizwe kwisizukulwana esingaphambili ukuba babeyithobele eyabo indawo yomnqophiso. Ngelishwa, abazange, baza bafumana iziphumo ngezenzo zabo.

Eyona nyaniso yeyokuba, ngexesha lama-40 leminyaka ngaphambili, amaIsrayeli ayewujonge umnqophiso kwicala elingalunganga. Ngenxa yovukelo lwawo ngakuThixo, sonke isizukulwana sabantu abadala esayibonayo imimangaliso nezimanga zeMfuduko, satshabalala entlango, kwasala uKalebhi noYoshuwa. Iincwadi zikaMoses ezine kwezintlanu ziphethe oko kwenzekayo kubo xa babebhadula entlango lonke elo xesha. Ngoku, phantsi kwenkokelo kaYoshuwa, isizukulwana sesibini sasikulungele ukumelana nemingeni yokulithatha ilizwe.

“UMoses wambiza ke uYoshuwa, wathi kuye, ‘Yomelela ukhaliphe; kuba wena uya kungena naba bantu kwelo zwe wafungayo kooyise uYehova ukuba wobanika; wena uya kubenza balidle ilifa. UYehova nguYe ohamba phambi kwakho. Uya kuba nawe; akayi kukuyekela, engayi kukushiya; uze ungoyiki ungaqhiphuki mbilini’” (Dut. 31:7, 8, NKJV).

Izithembiso zikaThixo ezanikwa kumanyange nakuMoses sele ziza kuzaliseka. ukuba, nangona

Kukho umoya wokulindela novuyo, isiqalo incwadi kaYoshuwa esitsha sabantu, ekudala bengenawo amakhaya yabhalwa ngaphezu behluthwe konke. UThixo uye wathembeka ukubahlangula ebukhobokeni, kwaye, kunjalo nje, unako ukuthenjwa ukuzizalisa izithembiso zaKhe ngokubhekiselele kwilizwe.

“Injongo ephambili ngencwadi kaYoshuwa namhlanje awahlukanga kukuchaza ukungena kukaIsrayeli ezweni kulowo uYoshuwa lesithembiso, ukoyiswa kwezwe, ukwabelana ngalo ngokwemingeni phakathi kwezizwe [zakwaIsrayeli]. Le njongo isisiseko sesigidimi sencwadi, esithi, ukunyaniseka kukaThixo ekuzalisekiseni isithembiso selizwe esenziwa kuAbraham. Incwadi ibethelela ukunyaniseka kwizithembiso zomnqophiso waKhe (Yosh. 21:43-45).”—Andrews Bible Commentary (Andrews University Press, 2020), p. 365.

Siza kufumana kunye ukuba, nangona incwadi kaYoshuwa yabhalwa ngaphezu kwenkulungwane ezintathu ezadlulayo, umhlaba esiphila kuwo namhlanje awahlukanga kulowo uYoshuwa ngokwemingeni yasemoyeni. Singajongana nemingeni yohlobo olungafaniyo ngemo, kodwa noko kunjalo ikho imingeni, ngokukodwa ekamoya, ethembisa okubi ngenkuseleko yethu, ngokholo lwethu, nokufezekiswa kwesikuthunyiweyo nguThixo kubantu baKhe. Umzekelo kaYoshuwa, ngokuqinisekileyo, uya kusikhuthaza ukuba sizibange izithembiso ezingamaxesha ethu nokuphumelela ngamandla aKhe, njengoko wenza.
