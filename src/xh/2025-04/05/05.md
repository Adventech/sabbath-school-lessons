---
title:  Ukukhetha Okukhululekileyo
date:   29/10/2025
---

`Funda Duteronomi 20:10, 15–18; Duteronomi 13:12–18; anoYoshuwa 10:40. Umthetho wemfazwe nenkqubo [yemfazwe] ngakwidolophu yonqulo lwezithixo, ekuthethwe ngawo kuDuteronomi, usinceda njani ukuba siyiqonde imida yentshabalalo epheleleyo kwimfazwe awayeyilwa amaIsrayeli?`

Indima yesiHebhere isebenzisa isaci esingaqhelekanga ukuchaza ukutshatyalaliswa kwabantu emfazweni: cherem. Esi saci sibhekisa koku“valwe umlomo” “okugwetyiweyo” okanye “okunikelwe ekutshatyalalisweni.” Ixesha elininzi, lithetha ukubekwa kummandla owodwa kaThixo, okupheleleyo nokungenako ukujikwa kwabantu, izilwanyana, okanye izinto ezingaphiliyo, okuthi, kwiimeko ezininzi, ngemfazwe kuquke ukutshatyalaliswa kwazo. Imbono nokwenza icherem njengokutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo kwabantu emfazweni kudinga ukuqondwa ngokwemfazwe kaYehova nemikhosi yomhlaba wonke yobubi, apho isimilo negama laKhe lisemngciphekweni.

Kwakhona, kususela ukuvela kwesono ehlabathini, akakho ongenacala: ungaba ngakuThixo okanye uchasane naYe. Elinye icala likhokelela ebomini, ubomi obungunaphakade, elinye ekufeni, ukufa kwaphakade.

Isenzo sokutshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo sichaza ukulunga kokugweba kukaThixo isono nobubi. Ngokungaqhelekanga, uThixo wathumela ukwenzeka kwenxenye yezigwebo zaKhe kwisizwe saKhe, uIsrayeli wakudala. Ukunikela kwintshabalalo kwakuphantsi kolawulo lwaKhe olungqongqo njengoThixo, luphelela kwixesha elithile lembali, uloyiso, nakwimida ecaciswe kakuhle yommandla womhlaba omiweyo, iKanan yakudala. Njengoko sibonile kwisifundo sayizolo, abo bavalwa umlomo yintshabalalo bathi rhoqo ukuvukela ngakwiinjongo zikaThixo bazidelela, abaze baguquke. Ngoko ke, isigqibo sikaThixo sokubatshabalalisa sasingenakhethe singenabo nobuzwe.

Ngaphezu koko, uIsrayeli wayeya kulindela impatho enye xa begqibe ukuba bamkele indlela yokuphila enye efana naleyo yamaKanan, (thelekisa noDuteronomi 13). Nokuba kubonakala ngokungathi amaqela ayekhethe naliphi icala lemfazwe kaThixo eyayichazwe kwangaphambili, ayenako ukusuka kwelinye icala aye kwelinye (wona amaIsrayeli aza kulifumana ilizwe, amaKanan wona aza kutshatyalaliswa), njengoko siza kubona kwiimeko zooRahabhi, nooAkan, namaGibheyon.

Abantu abazange banikwe ukhuseleko okanye bavalwe umlomo ngamaqhinga. Abo baba nabakuzuzayo kulwalamano noYehova babesenga lahlekwa ngumgangatho wabo owodwa ngenxa yovukelo, bathi abo bavalwe umlomo xa bengazinikela kwigunya likaYehova baphile.

`Ukudelela uThixo kwamaKanan kuthetha ntoni ngokwasemoyeni kwindawo yethu namhlanje? Oko kukuthi, zithini iziphumo zokukhetha kwethu okukhululekileyo kuthi ngokwethu?`
