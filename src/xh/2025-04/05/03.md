---
title:  Umgwebi Owongamileyo
date:   27/10/2025
---

`Funda Genesis 18:25; Indumiso 7:11; Indumiso 50:6; Indumiso 82:1; Indumiso 96:10; no-2 Timoti 4:1, 8. Zithini ezi ndima ngesimilo sokuziphatha kukaThixo? Indawo kaThixo njengomgwebi weyunivesi isinceda njani ukuba siwuqonde umcimbi wemfazwe kaThixo?`

Ubungcwele besimilo sikaThixo buthetha ukuba akakwazi ukusinyamezela isono. Uyanyamezela. Sekunjalo, isono kufuneka sivune isiqhamo saso, esikukufa (Roma 6:23). UYehova wamema imfazwe ngakwisono, nokuba sasiphi, nokuba sasikwaIsrayeli okanye phakathi kwamaKanan. UIsrayeli wayengazange angcwaliswe ngokuba nenxaxheba kwiimfazwe ezingcwele ngaphezu kwezinye izizwe (Dut. 9:4, 5; Dut. 12:29, 30) naxa kwaba yindlela yokusibetha isizwe saKhe asinyulileyo. Umahluko kwabanye abantu beMpuma ekufuphi yakudala, amaIsrayeli afumana ukuhlehliswa kwemfazwe engcwele, xa uThixo engazange awalwele kodwa walwa nawo, evumela iintshaba zabo ukuba zibacinezele (thelekisa noYoshuwa 7).

Iyonke imbono yemfazwe engcwele kufuneka iqondwe njengokwenza kukaThixo njengomgwebi. Xa ibonwa ngale ndlela, iimfazwe zakwaIsrayeli zokoyisa zithabatha imo eyahluke kakhulu. Ngokuchaseneyo neemfazwe zokulawula zokuzanelisa, ezazixhaphake kakhulu kwihlabathi lakudala (nezethu namhlanje), iimfazwe zikaIsrayeli zazingajonge ukuzizuzela bona uzuko kodwa ukumisela ubulungisa noxolo lukaThixo elizweni. Ngoko ke, embindini wokuqonda imbono yemfazwe engcwele kumi umbono wolawulo wolawulo nobungangamsha bukaThixo, obusengozini kumfanekiselo kaThixo njengomlwi, kanye njengoko zinjalo emfanekiselweni kaThixo njengokumkani okanye umgwebi.

UYehova njengomlwi nguYe, lo ungumgwebi, uzinikele ekumiseni, ekuzinziseni, nasekugcineni umgaqo womthetho, osisibonakalaliso sesimilo saKhe. Umfanekiselo kaThixo njengomlwi, ofana nalowo womgwebi nokumkani, uxela ukuba uYehova akasayi kulunyamezela uvukelo ngakucwangco lwaKhe alumiseleyo ngonaphakade. Ngoko ke, omnye angaqinisekisa ukuba umgomo wokusebenza kukaYehova awuzange wayimfazwe wona, okanye uloyiso ngokwalo, kodwa kukumiselwa ngokutsha kobulungisa noxolo. Ekugqibeleni, ukugweba nokuqhuba imfazwe, okanye ukuzisa ubulungisa, kuyinto enye xa uThixo eyintloko yokwenziwayo.

`Cinga ngoThixo njengomgwebi olilungisa ongenakuthengwa nongenakuphenjelelwa ngokuba nekhethe. Uyinxalenye njani yegospile uThixo ongasokuze anyamezele isono umphelo, nengcinezelo, nokubulaleka kwabamsulwa, kunye nokuxhatshazwa kwabacinezelweyo?`
