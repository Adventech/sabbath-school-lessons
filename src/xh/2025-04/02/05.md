---
title:  Okuhle Okuphikisanayo
date:   08/10/2025
---

`Funda Yoshuwa 9:1–20. Zinto zini ezifanayo nezahlukeneyo phakathi kwebali likaRahabhi nelamaGibheyon? Kungani ukuba la mabali abe nentsingiselo?`

Esi sahluko sikaYoshuwa siqala ngokusinika ulwazi lokuba iikumkani ezintlanu zamaKanan ezaziqhele ukulawula izizwana zezixeko zagqiba ekubeni zenze imbumba yokuchasa amaIsrayeli. Ngokungafaniyo, abemi baseGibeya bagqiba ukuba bamisele umnqophiso noIsrayeli.

Ukuze kuqhathwe amaIsrayeli ukuba angene kumnqophiso nawo, amaGibheyon abhenela ukuceba ukuba abe ngabameli belizwe langaphandle. Ngokwe Duteronomi 20:10–18, uThixo wenza umahluko phakathi kwamaKanan nabantu ababehlala ngaphandle kweZwe leDinga.

Igama eliguqulwe “ngobuqhinga” okanye “ngobuqhophololo” linokusetyenziswa ngentsingiselo eyakhayo, libonise ubulumko nokwenza ngobulumko (IMiz. 1:4; IMiz. 8:5, 12), okanye ngendlela echasayo, lithethe injongo yenkohlakalo (Eks. 21:14, 1 Sam. 23:22, Ind. 83:3). Kwimeko yamaGibheyon, okwakuqhuba isenzo sabo senkohliso yayiyinjongo enganeno kokutshabalalisa yokuzigcina.

Intetho yamaGibheyon ifana ngokumangalisayo nekaRahabhi. Zombini zivuma amandla kaThixo kaIsrayeli, zaye zombini zivuma ukuba impumelelo asinto nje eyenziwa ngumntu. Ngokungafaniyo namanye amaKanan, awalivukeli icebo likaYehova lokulinika ilizwe amaIsrayeli, kwaye ayavuma ukuba uYehova ngokwaKhe uyazigxotha ezi zizwe zimke phambi kukaIsrayeli. Iindaba zokuhlangulwa eYiputa, nokoyiswa kweSihon neOgi, zakhuthaza uRahabhi namaGibheyon ukuba afune ukuhlangana namaIsrayeli. Noko kunjalo, endaweni yokwamkela ngokupheleleyo intumekelelo yokuzinikela kuThixo kaIsrayeli, njengoko wenza uRahabhi, amaGibheyon abhenela eqhingeni.

Umthetho kaMoses wayenza imisindleko yokufunda intando kaThixo kwiimeko ezinje ngale (Num. 27:16–21). UYoshuwa wayefanele abuze intando kaYehova akuphephe ukukhohliswa ngamaGibheyon.

Umsebenzi ophambili wenkokeli elawulwa nguThixo, nayo nayiphi inkokeli engumKristu, kukufuna intando kaThixo (1 IziKro. 28:9, 2 IziKro. 15:2, 2 IziKro. 18:4, 2 IziKro. 20:4). Ngokungakukhathaleli oku, amaIsrayeli anyanzeleka ukuba aphule iimeko ezisisiseko sokoyisa ilizwe okanye ukwaphula isifundo esasenziwe egameni likaYehova, esasibophelela ngokufanayo.

`Kukangaphi uzifumana utsala nzima phakathi kwezinto ezimbini zeBhayibhile ezibonakala zilungile kodwa zingathi ziyaphikisana?`
