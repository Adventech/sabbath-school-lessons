### Fundela Isifundo sale Veki

Yosh. 2:1–21, Num. 14:1–12, Heb. 11:31, Eks. 12:13, Yoshuwa 9, Neh. 7:25.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ngokholo uRahabhihi ihenyukazi akatshabalalanga nabo bangevayo, akuba ezamkele iintlola ngoxolo.” (Hebhere 11:31).

“Ndiyenzele ntoni kodwa le nto?” Mhlawumbi sonke sesikhe sawathetha la mazwi. Ngapha koko, imbali ayaneli nje ukuziphinda, kodwa lwenza njalo lonke uluntu jikelele, nathi ngokwethu ngokukodwa. Kukangaphi siphinda phinda iimpazamo ezinye!

UIsrayeli unethuba lesibini lokungena eZweni leDinga, yena ewuthatha lo msebenzi ngondiliseko. Inyathelo lokuqala kukuba abe nokuqonda ngokucacileyo oko babejongene nako. Uthumela iintlola ezimbini ukuba zimphathele ulwazi olubalulekileyo ngelizwe: ukhuselo olucwangcisiweyo, ukulungela komkhosi ukulwa, ubukho bamanzi, nomoya wabantu xa kungena umkhosi ohlaselayo.

Omnye angacinga ukuba isithembiso sikaThixo sokunika uIsrayeli ilizwe sasingafuni nonjani umzamo kwelabo icala. Kanti ke, isiqinisekiso senkxaso kaThixo ayidluli ngaphaya kokufuneka bekwenzile. UIsrayeli umi kumda weKanan okwesibini. Okulindelweyo kwaye kukukhulu, kodwa ngexesha langaphambili uIsrayeli esemdeni ekwanalo msebenzi mnye, isiphumo saba kukusilela kakhulu.

Kule veki siza kuhlalutya amabali amabini angawona anika umdla encwadi kaYoshuwa sifumane ukulungela kwawo ukholo lwethu namhla. Ubabalo lukaThixo lunezinto ezingapheliyo ezinokwenzeka ukuba zisothuse.

_*Funda: Isifundo sale veki ulungiselela iSabatha kaOktobha 11._
