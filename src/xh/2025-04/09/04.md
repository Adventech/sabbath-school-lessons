`Funda Yoshuwa 13:1â€“7. Nangona ilizwe laseKanan lalisisipho esivela kuThixo, yeyiphi eminye yimingeni eyeza nokulithabatha?`

Njengokuba amaIsrayeli ayephila engamakhoboka, ubuchule bomkhosi yayinganele ukuloyisa ilizwe. Kwaneenkosi zobukhoboka bawo, amaYiputa, nemikhosi yawo enobuchule nexhobe yafohlela, ayengakwazi ukulihlala umphelo. AmaYiputa akazange ayoyisa ngokupheleleyo iKanan ngenxa yokungangeneki kwezixeko ezineendonga. Ngoku isizwe esasisa kuba ngamakhoboka kuthiwa masoyise ilizwe iinkosi zawo zangaphambili zingazange ziloyise. Ukuba bangaze balifumane ilizwe, kungaba kungobabalo lukaThixo kuphela, hayi ngeyabo imizamo.

UYoshuwa 13 ukuya ku-21 uphethe ukwabiwa kwelizwe kwiintlanga ezahlukeneyo zikaIsrayeli. Oku kwabiwa akuxeli kuIsrayeli kuphela oko banikwe kona kodwa noku kusajongwe ukuba bahlale kuko kuloo mmandla. AmaIsrayeli angahlala ngokukhuselekileyo elizweni uThixo awabanika njengelifa. Bangabahlali abafanele kakhulu nabasemthethweni belizwe phantsi kokuba lilelikaThixo. Kanti ke, ukuqala kukaThixo kumele kuIsiqingatha sokuqala sencwadi sibonisa indlela uThixo awawanika ngayo ilizwe ngokuthi alihluthe kumaKanan; isiqingatha sesibini sinika ingxelo yendlela uIsrayeli awalithabatha ngayo ilizwe ngokuhlala kulo.

Obu bunzina bokoyisa kubonisa iziqwenga zosindiso. Ngokufana noIsrayeli, asisokuze senze nantoni ukuze sizuze usindiso lwethu (Efese 2:8, 9). Sisipho, njengoko lalinjalo ilizwe ukuba sisipho sikaThixo kuIsrayeli sakhelwe kulwalamano lomnqophiso lwawo naYe. Ngokuqinisekileyo, sasingakhelwanga kokuhle kwawo (bona uDut. 9:5).

Sekunjalo, ukuze amaIsrayeli asonwabele isipho sikaThixo, kwakufuneka enze konke okwakuluxanduva olweza nokuphila elizweni, njengoko nathi kufuneka siphumele kwinkqubo yokungcwaliswa kwethu ngokuthobela okunothando kokufunwa kukuba ngabemi bobukumkani bukaThixo. Nangona ingento inye, ukuhamba kunye phakathi kokunikwa kwabo ilizwe ngobabalo ngokunikwa kwethu usindiso ngobabalo kusondelelene ngokwaneleyo. Sinikwe isipho esimangalisayo, kodwa siyinto enokusilahleka ukuba asilumkanga.

`AmaKristu namhlanje amelana njani nemingeni efanayo naleyo yokumiwa kweZwe leDinga? Bona uFilipi 2:12, Heb. 12:28.`
