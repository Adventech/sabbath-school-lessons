---
title:  Ukukhululeka Ukuba Ukhonze
date:   23/12/2025
---

Njengenkokeli eyinyaniso nethembekileyo, uYoshuwa wayihlonipha intando ekhululekileyo yokuba uIsrayeli angakhonza uYehova ngokuzithandela kunokuba anyanzelwe. Yinto kanye le eyenziwa ukusetyenziswa ngabom kwesenzi “ukukhethwa” (bona uYosh. 24:22). Kwezinye iindawo ubakhar, “ukukhetha,” uchaza ukunyula kukaYehova uIsrayeli (Dut. 7:6, 7; Dut. 10:15; Dut. 14:2). UIsrayeli ukhululekile ukuba athi “hayi” kuYehova emva kokunyulwa nguThixo, kodwa oko bekuya kuba kukusa nobudenge. UIsrayeli angathi “ewe” kuThixo aze aqhubeke aphile, okanye bangabuya umva kuYe baze bayeke ukubakho.

`Wasabela njani uIsrayeli kwisibheno sikaYoshuwa? (Yosh. 24:16–18). Kutheni le nto ucinga ukuba uYoshuwa waphendula njengoko wenza kwimpendulo yabo? (Yosh. 24:19–21).`

Kwimpendulo yabo eyayintle ngokucacileyo, uIsrayeli wazi ukuba uThixo wooyisemkhulu nowooyise ngoku ukwa “nguThixo wethu” (Yosh. 24:17, 18), abanentumekelelo ukumkhonza ngokunyaniseka okujonge calanye. Emva kokunjalo ukuqinisekisa okungenantandabuzo kokunyaniseka kwabo, besingalindela amazwi okuqinisekisa nenkuthazo evela kuYoshuwa. Noko kunjalo, akuzange kube njalo. Impikiswano phakathi kukaYoshuwa nabantu iguquka ngokukhawuleza apho uYoshuwa ebonakala edlala indawo yegqwetha longendawo. Usuka ekuthetheni ngokwazelelela okunobubele kukaThixo kwixesha eladlulayo aye ekuwoyikiseni amaIsrayeli ngomfanekiso kaThixo ekungelula ukumkhonza.

UYoshuwa uyakwazi ukungazinzi kwesizukulwana sokuqala, esathembisa ukumthobela uThixo ngeentetho ezifanayo (Eks. 19:8, Eks. 24:3, Dut. 5:27), ukanti sazilibala izithembiso zaso amazwi abo esesemilebeni yabo (Eksodus 32). Ngoko ke, ngendlela yokuthetha, ufuna ukumenza uIsrayeli ukuba aqaphele izinto eziliqela. _Okokuqala_, isigqibo sokumkhonza uThixo sibaluleke kakhulu. Siya kusibumba isizwe sonke ngokokutyhilelwa nguThixo. Iintsikelelo zokungathobeli kufuneka nazo ziqondwe ngokupheleleyo. Ukuxolelwa kwezono asilolungelo loluntu elinganakususwa kodwa ngummangaliso wobabalo lukaThixo.

_Okwesibini_, isigqibo samaIsrayeli sokumkhonza uThixo kufuneka sibe sesabo, singaphoswa kuwo yinkokeli, noYoshuwa ngokwakhe.

_Okwesithathu_, uIsrayeli kufuneka aqonde ukuba abantu abakwazi ukumkhonza uThixo ngawabo amandla. Ukukhonza uThixo akuzuzwa ngokwayama okwenziwayo kwimiyalelo yomnqophiso kodwa ngolwalamano lomntu nokukhonza uYehova (thelekisa noEks. 20:1, 2; Dut. 5:6, 7).
