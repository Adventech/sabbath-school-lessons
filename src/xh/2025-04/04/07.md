---
title:  Ingcamango Eyongeziweyo
date:   24/10/2025
---

Funda: Ellen G. White, “Ukuwa KweYeriko,” Amaph. 487–493, kuAmanyange Nabaprofeti.

Xa uvukelo luphakama kwiyunivesi ukuchasa uThixo, uThixo mhlawumbi wayeza kuyeka ukuba koko kufunekayo akuko, okungaguqukiyo, nokungunaphakade, akunikezele ubukhokeli bayo yonke iyunivesi kwesine sezidalwa zaKhe ezinovukelo, okanye wayeza kuba nguBawo ongcwele, olilungisa, onothando, nonenceba kubo bonke abakhoyo. IBhayibhile inika umfanekiso wesibini, kwaye kule meko, ukungqubana phakathi kwemikhosi yobubi namandla aKhe kuqinisekile.

Xa amagunya opolitiko okanye awembali yasekuhlaleni emanyene nemikhosi yezwe yesiphithiphithi novukelo athi abonakalalisa ingqondo enokudelela okunye ngakuYehova, Yena, njengeNkosi enobungangamsha yeyunivesi, waya wangenelela. Umxholo kaYehova njengomlwi ukukuboniswa kwangaphambili koloyiso lokugqibela phakathi kokulungileyo nokubi (IsiTy. 20:8–10). Ngapha koko, iimfazwe zikaIsrayeli zikaThixo azaneli kubonisa nje ukwalama imfazwe yezwe lonke njengokwasesipilini, kodwa ziyinxenye yembambano enye, elindele umgwebo kaThixo wexesha lokuphela kummandla wembali yangoku.

“UThixo wayekwenze kwalilungelo noxanduva lwabo ukulingena ilizwe ngexesha elimisiweyo, kodwa ngenxa yokungakhathali kwabo okuneenkani, larhoxiswa elo lungelo. . . . Yayingeyiyo injongo yaKhe ukuba balizuze ilizwe ngemfazwe, kodwa kube ngokuthobela ngqongqo imiyalelo yaKhe.”—Ellen G. White, Amanyange Nabaprofeti, iph. 392.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxwa:**

`Imvelaphi yemfazwe yezwe lonke ikunceda njani ukuqonda ngcono ukukhokela kukaYehova uIsrayeli ukuba aye emfazweni?`

`Eklasini, xoxani iimpendulo zenu kumbuzo wangoMvulo ngokubhekiselele kubunyaniso bembambano enkulu nendlela eyenzeka ngayo emhlabeni wonke osingqongileyo. Ithini indawo yethu kule mbambano, kwaye sikufuna njani ukuyizalisekisa?`

`Singawenza njani umtheho-siseko wokuma silindele uYehova asilwele kubomi bethu basemoyeni?`

`Amaxesha amaninzi, kwiingxoxo zethu nokungavumelani kwethu ebandleni ngamanye amaxesha, siyafuna ukubona ukuba ngubani ongakwicala lethu. Kufuneka siyiguqule njani le ngqondo ngokukaYoshuwa 5:13–15?`
