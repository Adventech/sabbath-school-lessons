---
title:  Okulandele Kakhulu Kokulungileyo
date:   23/10/2025
---

`Funda Eksodus 17:7–13 noYoshuwa 6:15–20. Zinto zini ezifanayo ozifumana kwezi ngxelo zimbini zemfazwe? Zahlukana njani?`

Okokuqala uIsrayeli esilwa emva kweMfuduko kubhalwe kuEksodus 17, apho amaIsrayeli ayezikhusela kuma-Amaleki. UIsrayeli wayewabonile amandla obugorha bukaThixo kuko kokubini ukungcungcuthekiswa kwamaYiputa nokukhokelela amaIsrayeli enkululekweni. Sibonile ukuba icebo likaThixo lokuqala ngoIsrayeli lalingaquki imfazwe ngakwabanye abantu (Eks. 23:28, Eks. 33:2). Noko kunjalo, athi esandula ukukhululwa eYiputa, amaIsrayeli aqala ukukhalaza endleleni (Eks. 17:3), ade abuze ukuba ukho na uThixo phakathi kwawo. Kwakungeli xesha awathi ama-Amaleki eza kulwa noIsrayeli ukuze bafunde ukumthemba uThixo kwakhona.

UThixo uthe engayiyekelelanga imithetho-siseko yaKhe, ahle aze kumgangatho abakuwo abantu baKhe, asoloko ebabizela kwicebo elililo: ukuthembela okupheleleyo nokukhululekileyo ekungeneleleni kukaThixo. Okukuko, umthetho wemfazwe (Duteronomi 20) wanikwa kuphela emva kwama-40 eminyaka yamava asentlango, okwabangelwa kukungakholwa kukaIsrayeli. Iimeko ezintsha zafuna amacebo amatsha, kwaza kwangelo xesha ukuba afune uIsrayeli awatshabalalise kuphele amaKanan (Dut. 20:16–18).

Ngaphandle kobunyaniso bokuba imfazwe yaba yinto efunekayo kuIsrayeli, kwaya kwafuneka kube luvavanyo lokuthembela kwabo kuYehova. UThixo akazange abancame kodwa wabayekela ukuba bawabone amandla aKhe ngokuba babe nokuxhomekeka okupheleleyo kuYe.

Ukuba nenxaxheba kwamaIsrayeli eloyisweni kubonakele ekuphelweni okuzotywe nguYoshuwa esiphelweni sencwadi. Apha, amaKanan kuthiwa ayesilwa namaIsrayeli (Yosh. 24:11). Nangona ukuwa kweendonga kwaba sisiphumo sommangaliso kaThixo, abantu bakwaIsrayeli kwakufuneka bangene nabo edabini bajongane nokuxhathisa okuneenkani kwabemi besixeko.

Ukuba nenxaxheba kukaIsrayeli kwimfazwe yezixhobo kwaba yindlela yokuphuhlisa ukuthembela ngokupheleleyo kuncedo lukaYehova. Kanti ke, uIsrayeli wayekhunjuzwa rhoqo (Yosh. 7:12, 13; Yosh. 10:8) ukuba isiphumo sedabi ngalinye lalilele ezandleni zikaYehova, nokuba indlela ekuphela kwayo ababengenza kubekho impembelelo yesiphumo semfazwe yomkhosi yayingengqondo yabo yokholo, okanye ukungakholwa, ngakwizithembiso zikaYehova. Ukukhetha kwakuko kwabo.
