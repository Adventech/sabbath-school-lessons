---
title:  Umfuziselo noQobo
date:   01/12/2025
---

Abachazi beBhayibhile abakwazi ukuzikhethela ukugqiba ngokuba uyintoni umfuziselo weBhayibhile, okanye ngendlela loo mfuziselo omawuzaliseke ngayo kwiTestamente eNtsha nangapha kwayo. IBhayibhile ngokwayo inika ulawulo oluthile nemithetho-siseko ngendlela yokuwusebenzisa umfuziselo.

Ngokufanayo, iTestamente eNtsha ikuveza ukuzaliseka kuqobo komfuziselo ngezigaba ezithathu: (1) ebomini bukaKristu, (2) kumava ebandla, (3) [nase] kupheleni kwexesha.

Singayifumana le mifuziselo namaqobo kuyo yonke iBhayibhile, kwaye iluncedo kakhulu ekuboniseni abafundi indlela yokuyiqonda iBhayibhile noko kufundiswa liLizwi likaThixo ngoYesu, nosindiso, novutho-ndaba lethemba esinalo.

**Khangela kwimifuziselo yeTestamente eNdala: uIsrayeli, iMfuduko, nengcwele. Elowo nalowo izaliseke njani kwizigaba ezithathu zesiQu [okanye zokwenzeka kwazo]: esobuKristu, esebandla, nesokugqitywa kwezinto?**

1. Israyeli
	- a. Isigaba sobuKristu (Mat. 2:15):
	- b. Isigaba sebandla (Gal. 6:16):
	- c. Isigaba sokugqitywa kwezinto (IsiTy. 7:4-8, 14):
2. IMfuduko
	- a. Isigaba sobuKristu (Mat. 2:19–21):
	- b. Isigaba sebandla (2 Kor. 6:17):
	- c. Isigaba sokugqitywa kwezinto (IsiTy. 18:4):
3. Ingcwele
	- a. Isigaba sobuKristu (Yohane 1:14, Yohane 2:21, Mat. 26:61):
	- b. Isigaba sebandla (1 Cor. 3:16, 17; 2 Cor. 6:16):
	- c. Isigaba sokugqitywa kwezinto (IsiTy. 3:12, IsiTy. 11:19, IsiTy. 21:3, IsiTy. 21:22):

“Njengoko isiBhalo sinombhali omnye wezulu, iindawo ezahlukeneyo zesiBhalo siyahambelana enye nenye. . . . Zonke iimfundiso zeBhayibhile ziya kudibana enye nenye; iinkcazo zezicatshulwa ngazinye ziya kuvumelana ngokupheleleyo noko kufundiswa sisiBhalo kumxholo onikiweyo.”—Handbook of Seventh-day Adventist Theology, p. 65.
