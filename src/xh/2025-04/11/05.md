---
title:  Impendulo Ethambileyo
date:   10/12/2025
---

`Funda uYoshuwa 22:21–29 ngokukhanya kweMizekeliso 15:1. Singafunda ntoni kwimpendulo yezizwe zaseMpuma?`

Impendulo yababetyholwa, eyayingqalile inamandla njengesityholo, iqulathe okubini ngokomxholo nokwakhiwa kwesahluko. Ukuza kufika apha, izizwe azikaziphenduli izityholo kodwa endaweni yoko zaziphulaphule zithule izinto ezazityabekwa ngazo. Ngokobunzima bezityholo, unyamezelo lwazo lungumzekelo njengoko bebonisa intsingiselo eyinyaniso yeqhalo: “Impendulo ethambileyo ibuyisa ubushushu; ilizwi elidubulayo linyusa umsindo” (Imiz. 15:1, NKJV).

Isivakalisi esiqalayo sokuzikhusela silukrozo lwamagama kaThixo abizwa ngawo uThixo kaIsrayeli: Thixo, Thixo Yehova, Yehova (Yosh. 22:22). Siphindwe kabini ukubonisa ukukhula kokunyanzela, njengoko iba sisifungo esinendili ukugxotha iintandabuzo nezityholo zobuxoki eziphantse ukukhokelela kwimfazwe yekhaya kwaIsrayeli. Baqiniseke ngamandla ukuba uThixo wazi ngokupheleleyo kwaye eyiqonda imeko, baye bethemba ukuba aba bathunywa bakhoyo baya kufika kwisigqibo esinye. Izizwe ezibini nesiqingatha ziyakwamkela ukufuneka zibe nokuziphendulela kwazo phambi koYehova ngokubiza Yena ukuba aziphindezele (thelekisa noDut. 18:19, 1 Sam. 20:16) ukuba ngokwenene zinetyala.

Kulandela isityhilelo esimangalisayo, kwelinye icala sibonisa ukungabi nasiseko kwesityholo (isibingelelo asikwazi ukusebenza njengendawo yombingelelo kuphela) kwelinye icala, sityhila eyona njongo iziqhubayo. Ukoyika ukwahlukana kuIsrayeli, kunokukreqa, sesona siseko sesenzo sabo. Ngoko, ukwakhiwa kwesibingelelo akubonisi kukreqa, njengoko bekucingwa. Okona kukuko okuyinyaniso kokuchasene noku kucingwayo: benze ngokoyika uYehova, njengoko zenzayo nezizwe ezazikwiNtshona yeYordan. Isiseko esiyinyaniso sikaIsrayeli bubunye asingomhlaba okanye ububanzi belifa kodwa ukunyaniseka ngokwasemoyeni kokufunwa nguYehova.

Eyona nto kanye eyayihlupha izizwe ezilishumi kwicala lakwiNtshona yomlambo ikwatyhilwe kuvuyo lwazo loqobo xa izizwe ezimsulwa zaseMpuma ziqinisekiswa. Endaweni yokuziva zoyisiwe ngeengxoxo zabazalwana bazo, zibonisa uvuyo loqobo kuba ukukrokra kwazo kufumaneke kungalunganga. Imfazwe yekhaya kuIsrayeli yaphepheka baza ubunye besizwe bagcinwa.

`Uziphatha njani izityholo zobuxoki? Yabelane ngeminye imithetho-siseko ekhokela ingqondo yakho. Ukuzuza inkuthazo, bona Indumiso 37:3–6, 34, 37.`
