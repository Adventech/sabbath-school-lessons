### Fundela Isifundo sale Veki

Yoshuwa 22; Efese 6:7; Yohane 7:24; Numeri 25; Imiz. 15:1; 1 Pet. 3:8, 9.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Impendulo epholileyo idambisa ingqumbo, kodwa ilizwi elibukhali lixhokonxa umsindo.” (Imizekeliso 15:1, NIV).

Ukuhlala nabantu, maxa wambi, kukhokelela kwiingxabano noxinzelelo. Oku kuyinyaniso ngakumbi ekuhlaleni nabantu abanje ngebandla, apho abantu baneemvelaphi nemigangatho eyahlukeneyo—nokuthi mhlawumbi ukhulele kwindawo eyahluke ngokupheleleyo ngeenkcubeko—nihlale nisebenze kunye ngenjongo efanayo.

Kule veki siza kufunda uYoshuwa 22 nomngeni owabakho ngenxa yokungaqondani okukhulu phakathi kwabantu. Ekuqalekeni kwencwadi, uYoshuwa wayalela ezinye izizwe ukuba ziwele iYordan zibe nenxaxheba ekoyiseni, kunye nezizwe ezikwelinye icala leYordan (Yosh. 1:12–18). Ngoku umsebenzi ugqityiwe, zikhululekile ukuba zingabuyela. Noko kunjalo, kwiNtshona yeYordan, zakha isibingelelo esenza kwakho ukukhathazeka kwizizwe ezikwiNtshona Yordan.

Kutheni le nto kuyingozi ukutsibela kwisigqibo esikhawulezileyo ngesimilo sabanye? Singabukhuthaza njani ubunye ebandleni? Kubaluleke ngokuba kutheni ukubugcina engqondweni ububanzi obukhulu bobizo lwethu singabanjwa ziziphazamiso? Le yeminye yemibuzo esiza kuyiphatha kule veki.

_*Funda: Isifundo sale veki ulungiselela iSabatha kaDisemba 13._
