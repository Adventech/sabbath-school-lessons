---
title:  Ingcamango Ngakumbi
date:  16/02/2024
---

Funda uEllen G. White, “The Sinner’s Need of Christ,” pp. 17–22, in Steps to Christ.

KwiNdumiso, amazwi abantu bakaThixo atsho kunye ukuphinda ikhoras “ingunaphakade inceba yakhe” ukubhiyozela uthando lukaThixo olungunaphakade (Ndum. 106:1; Ndum. 107:1; Ndum. 118:1–4, 29; Ndumiso 136). “Ukungambongi uThixo kuyakuthetha ukulibala onke amaqithiqithi aKhe, ukungaxabisi izipho zikaThixo. Kuphela ngabo badumisayo abangalibaliyo. Ukucinga nokuthetha ngoThixo ayikabi kukumdumisa. Ukudumisa kuqala xa ubani evuma ubungangamsha nemisebenzi kaThixo aze asabele ngokubonga ukulunga, inceba, nobulumko baKhe.”—Hans LaRondelle, Deliverance in the Psalms, p. 178.

Ukubaluleka kokuvuma okunendili kwenceba kaThixo engapheliyo kuzuza ukubaluleka nzulu ngakumbi xa sikhumbula ukuba i-khesed kaThixo-oko kukuthi imfesane yaKhe yomnqophiso kunye nokuthembeka-ziqine nkqi yaye zingagungqi phakathi kwesono somntu novukelo ngakuThixo.

“Sonile ngakuYe, yaye asiyifanele imfobe yaKhe; kodwa noko kunjalo ubeke emilebeni yethu esona sibongozo simangalisayo kuzo zonke, ‘Ngenxa yegama lakho, musa ukusigiba; ungayihlazisi itrone yobuqaqawuli bakho. Khumbula, ungawaphuli umnqophiso nathi.’Yer. 14:21. Xa sisiza kuYe sivuma ukungafaneleki kwethu nesono, uzibophelele ngokwaKhe ukusibazela indlebe isikhalo sethu. Uzuko lwetrone yaKhe luba semngciphekweni ngenxa yokuzalisekisa ilizwi laKhe kuthi.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Isifundos, p. 148.

Ukuva ukuba uThixo ube nemfesane ngakuye kukhuthaza umdumisi ukuba athi, “NguYehova umenzi wemisebenzi yobulungisa, nezigwebo ngenxa yabacudisiweyo bonke” (Ndum. 103:6). Ngalo ndlela, injongo yokugqibela yobungqina bomdumisi buqu, kwakunye nokubonga inceba kaThixo ebomini baKhe, kukuqinisekisa kwakhona abanye ngemfesane kaThixo ukuze, nabo, bavule iintliziyo zabo kuThixo baze bazuze ubabalo lwaKhe olusindisayo babonge uThixo (Ndum. 9:11, 12; Ndum. 22:22–27; Ndum. 66:16).

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo**

`Zeziphi izinto eziphathekayo zenyaniso yokuba inceba kaThixo ingunaphakade ngenxa yosindiso lwabantu? Kutheni le nto oku kungathethi kuthi ubani angaqhubekeka esona ngenxa yokuba inceba kaThixo ingunaphakade?`

`Sikuhlanganisa njani ukuxolela kukaThixo izono zethu nengcamango yokugweba kukaThixo isono?`

`Ezi nkcazo zenceba kaThixo kwiTestamente eNtsha zingena njani gingci kwezo zikwiNdumiso (Efe. 2:4, 5; 1 Tim. 1:16; Tito 3:5; Heb. 4:16)`
