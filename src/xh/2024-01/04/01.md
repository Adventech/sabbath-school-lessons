### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Ndum. 139:1–18; Ndumiso 121; Ndum. 17:8; Mat. 23:37; 1 Kor. 10:1–4; Heb. 4:15, 16.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Akhala, weva uYehova; wawahlangula ezimbandezelweni zawo zonke”(Ndumiso 34:17).

Nngokuthe gqolo, iiNdumiso ziveza inyaniso yokuba uYehova onobungangamsha, owadala waxhasa elimiweyo, ukwazityhila njengoThixo osisiqu osungula axhase ubudlelane nabantu baKhe.

UThixo usondele kubantu baKhe nakwindalo yaKhe, ngokufanayo ezulwini nasemhlabeni (Ndum. 73:23, 25). Nangona “eyizinzise emazulwini itrone yakhe (Ndum. 103:19) aze “akhwele ezinkqantosini” (Ndum. 68:4), kanjalo “usondele uYehova kubo bonke abamnqulayo, kubo bonke abamnqulayo ngenyaniso (Ndum. 145:18). IiNdumiso ngokungagungqiyo ziphakamisa inyaniso yokuba uYehova nguThixo ophilileyo, ongenelela endaweni yabo bakhankanya Yena (Ndum. 55:16– 22). IiNdumiso zinentsingiselo ngokutsolileyo kuba zixhokonxwa, yaye zibhekiswa, kuThixo ophilileyo, ova aphendule imithandazo.

Kufuneka sikhumbule ukuba ukusabela okukuko ekusondeleni kukaYehova kubandakanya ubomi bokukholwa kuYe, kunye nobentobelo kwimithetho yaKhe. Akukho nto inganeno kolu kholo nentobelo enokwamkeleka kuYe, njengoko imbali kaSirayeli yaasoloko ityhila.

_*Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungisela iSabatha, Janyuwari 27._
