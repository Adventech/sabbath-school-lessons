### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Ndumiso 23; Yohane 10:11–15; Ndumiso 22; Ndum. 89:27–32; Kol. 1:16; Ndumiso 2; Heb. 7:20–28.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ilitye abalilahlayo abakhi laba yintloko yembombo. Kuvela kuYehova oku, kungumsebenzi obalulekileyo emehlweni ethu” (Ndumiso 118:22, 23).

IiNdumiso zingqina ngobuntu nolungiselelo lukaKristu. Phantse zonke iinkalo zomsebenzi waKhe kwicebo losindiso zibonakala kwiiNdumiso. Ngeendlela ezohlukeneyo, ubomi nomsebenzi kaKristu zifuziselwe zaxelwa ngenxa engaphambili kuzo, amaxesha amaninzi ngokuchaneka okumangalisayo.

Izihloko ezityhilwe kwiiNdumiso ziquka ubuthixo bukaKristu, ubunyana baKhe, intobelo yaKhe, ukuzondelela kwaKhe itempile kaThixo, ukuzazisa kwaKhe njengoMalusi oLungileyo, ukungcatshwa kwaKhe, intlungu yaKhe, ukungophulwa kwamathambo aKhe, ukufa kwaKhe, uvuko, unyuko, ububingeleli, nobukumkani. Konke kupha, njengoko kuxelwe kwangaphambili kwiinkulungwane ezininzi ngaphambi kokuba uYesu eze esenyameni.

Akumangalisi ngoko, umzekelo, xa kuthethwa ngomzekelo waKhe, uYesu wayekhombe emva kwiiNdumiso xa wayethetha nabafundi kwindlela eya eEmawusi (Luka 24:44). Wayefuna bafumane kwiiNdumiso ubungqina bokuba unguBani.

Ezinye iiNdumiso ezinenzaliseko engumfuziselo kuKristu ziquka Ndumiso 24, 45, 72, no-101 (iKumkani noMgwebi osemqoka, 88, no-102 (imithandazo yomkhonzi kaThixo obandezelekileyo.

Kuzo zonke iiNdumiso, ngezililo zabadumisi, imibulelo, imibongo, nokukhalela ubulungisa nohlangulo, sinokuva iintlokoma zokuthandazela kukaKristu ukusindiswa kwehlabathi.

_*Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungisela iSabatha, Matshi 2._
