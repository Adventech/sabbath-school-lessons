### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Ndum. 105:5, Kol. 3:16, Yakobi 5:13, Ndumiso 44, Ndumiso 22, Ndumiso 13, Ndum. 60:1–5.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kwathi esendaweni ethile ethandaza, akupheza, wathi othile wabafundi bakhe kuye, Nkosi, sifundise ukuthandaza, kwanjengoko uYohane wabafundisayo abafundi bakhe’” (Luka 11:1).

Inkolelo yokuba umthandazo ongacwangcwiswanga, ongafundwanga ngowona mthandazo wokwenene ibonakala ixhaphakile kwamanye amaKristu. Noko kunjalo, abafundi bakaYesu bavuzwa ngokumandla xa babecela uYesu ukuba abafundise ukuthandaza. UThixo waabeka incwadi yomthandazo, iiNdumiso, embindini weBhayibhile, hayi nje ukusibonisa indlela ababethandaza ngayo abantu bakaThixo bamandulu koko ukufundisa nathi indlela yokuthandaza namhlanje.

Ukusukela kumaphakade angaphambili, iiNdumiso zibumbe imithandazo yabantu bakaThixo, kuquka nemithandazo kaYesu (1 ZiKroni. 16:7, 9; Neh. 12:8; Mat. 27:46; Efe. 5:19). Kule veki sizakuqwalasela indima edlalwe ziiNdumiso ekuncedeni abantu bakaThixo ukutyhutyhumeza uhambo lwabo lobomi baze bakhule kubudlelane babo noThixo. Kufuneka sikhumbule ukuba iiNdumiso yimithandazo yaye, ngenxa yoko, ayinaxabiso, hayi nje ekukrobiseni kwimfundiso kodwa kanjalo nangeendlela ezikwazi ngayo ukuphucula nokuguqula imithandazo yethu njengeziqu nanjengentlanganisela.

Ukuthandaza iiNdumiso kuncede amakholwa amaninzi ekumiseleni nasekugcineni ubomi bomthandazo obuthe gqolo nobanezayo. Kule veki sizakuqhubeka ukuqwalasela kwiiNdumiso, ngokukodwa kumba wamaxesha apho izinto zingasihambeli kakuhle.

_*Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungisela iSabatha, Janyuwari 13._
