---
title:  Engcweleni Yeengcwele
date:  20/05/2024
---

`Funda: Levitikus 16:21, 29–34; Levitikus 23:26–32; namaHebhere 9:23–28. Kwakutheni ukuze iMini Yocamagushelo ibaluleke kangaka kuIsrayeli wakudala?`

Ababingeleli babelungiselela yonke imihla yomnyaka, kodwa ngemini yoCamagushelo, eyayibizwa ngesiHebhere ngokuthi yiYom Kippur, amehlo kaIsrayeli wonke ayekhangela engcweleni.  ULevitikus 16 no-23 unika imiyalelo ecace gca yeMini yoCamagusho. Zonke izenzo zesiqhelo zaziyekwa. Wonke umntu azile. Ngexesha umbingeleli omkhulu engene phambi kukaThixo ngenxa yabo kwiNgcwele kangcwele, abantu babehlolisisa iintliziyo zabo. Babemfuna uThixo ngokuzithoba nangokuvuma izono okuphuma entliziyweni.

Nawuphi owayenga“cudisekanga” ngeMini yoCamagushelo waye“nqanyulwa,” angabi saba yinxalenye yabantu abanyuliweyo (Lev. 23:27, 29). NgeMini yoCamagushelo, umbingeleli omkhulu wayelithabatha igazi lebhokhwe kaYehova angene nalo engcweleni aze, emva kokuba elifefile phezu kwesihlalo senceba, aliqabe igazi ezimpondweni zesibingelo segolide nesibingelo sobhedu, ehlambulula ingcwele yonke ngokupheleleyo. Aku“gqiba ukucamagushela,”umbingeleli omkhulu wayebeka izandla zakhe phezu kwebhokhwe ephilileyo avume izono zikaIsrayeli. Emva koko yayikhokelelwa entlango ukuba yahlukane nenkampu ngonaphakade kanaphakade (Lev. 16:20-22).

Igazi lalisiwe engcweleni ngexesha leenkonzo zemihla ngemihla, ukubonisa ukubhalwa kwesono (Yer. 17:1) nokuthabatha uxanduva kukaThixo ekulahlweni kwaso okokugqibela. Ngoku, ngeMini yoCamagushelo, sasikhutshwa engcweleni sibekwe phezu kwentloko yebhokhwe uAzazele, omele uSathana nokutyhila umthwalo wakhe wokugqibela ngenxa yengxaki yesono.

Le bhokhwe yayisiwa kude entlango ukuze, ekupheleni kweMini yoCamagushelo, uThixo abe nengcwele ecocekileyo nabantu abacocekileyo. Engcweleni yasezulwini, uKristu ulungiselela thina, wakuqala eNgcweleni, ngoku, eNgcweleni kangcwele kususela ngomnyaka we-1844, ekupheleni kwama-2,300 eentsuku.

Siya kuphumela kulo mgwebo mkhulu ngenxa kaYesu, uMmeli wethu. Ngokutsho kukaEllen G. White, “sigwetyelwa ngobulungisa baKhe, esinganasabelo kubo.”— Ulangazelelo Lwamaphakade, iph. 25. Ngenxa yesiphumo sobu bulungisa, obubalelwa kuthi ngesisa, siyayicinezela imiphefumlo yethu, ukuthi siyasishiya isono. Oko kuthetha ukuba asifikanga ekubamkeleni kamnandi ububi, saye singazithetheleli okanye sitshele kwizono esizithandayo, kodwa sikhula elubabalweni nasekuphileni ubomi bobungcwele.

`Ibaluleke ngantoni iMini yoCamagushelo ebomini bethu namhlanje? Kungani ukuba kufuneke yenze umahluko kwindlela esiphila ngayo?`
