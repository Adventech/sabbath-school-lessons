`Funda: Roma 3:27–31; Roma 6:15–18; noRoma 8:1, 2. Zisifundisa ntoni ezi ndima zingosindiso ngobulungisa bukaKristu kuphela?`

Kukho umoya omtsha obhudlayo ebandleni lobuKristu ngemihla kaLuther. Amashumi amawaka abantu bafundiswa ukuba bakhangele kude kwiziqu zabo ezinesono endaweni yoko bakhangele kuYesu. Ngokuqinisekileyo aba bantu, bekhangele kwiziqu zabo, nokuba babenjani bona, babona kuphela izinto ezibenza balahle ithemba. Leliphi ikholwa namhlanje elingenawo loo mava anjalo? Yiyo le nto, sidinga ukukhangela kuYesu, endaweni yoko.

Ubabalo lukaThixo luyasiguqula. Ngenye imini, uJohn Wesley waya kwintlanganiso yamaMoriva eLondon. UWesley wahlala emangalisiwe xa emamele uLuther esenza isikhokelo sencwadi yamaRoma. Kwaye kukokokuqala ebomini bakhe, waza waqala ukuziqonda iindaba ezilungileyo. Kwabakho into emshukumisayo ngaphakathi, waziva etsalelekile ngendlela engaqhelekanga kulo Kristu owanikela ubomi baKhe ngenxa yakhe. Wadanduluka, “Ndaziva ndithembe uKristu, uKristu kuphela ngosindiso: ndanikwa nesiqinisekiso sokuba uzisusile izono zam, ezam kanye, wandisindisa emthethweni wesono nokufa.”—Yohane Whitehead, The Life of the Revelation. John Wesley, M.A. (London: Stephen Couchman, 1793), iph. 331.

`Funda: 1 Petros 2:2, 2 Petros 3:18, Kolose 1:10, namaEfese 4:18–24. Zithini iinyaniso eziphilileyo ezityhilwa zezi ndawo ngobomi bomKristu?`

AbaHlaziyi balifunda ngendlela ehleliweyo iLizwi ukuze bafumane inyaniso engaphezulu. Benganelisekanga yimeko eyayikho, okanye ngamava kankankanka wenkolo kube kukuncinane okanye kungekho ukukhula, bathi rhoqo ukulangazelela ukumazi ngcono uKristu. AmaKristu amaninzi awayekholelwa eBhayibhileni ngexesha leMiddle Ages ahlawula ixabiso eliphezulu ngendlela eyodwa ngokuzinikela kwabo. Angcungcuthekiswa, avalelwa ezintolongweni, agxothelwa ekubhaceni, abulawa. Iimpahla nemizi yawo yathinjwa, amakhaya wabo atshiswa, imihlaba yagqugqiswa, iintsapho zawo zatshutshiswa. Xa ayegxothwa emakhayeni awo, ayekhangele umzi “ungcibi, umbangi wawo, inguThixo” (Heb. 11:10). Xa aye ngcungcuthekiswa, abasikelela abangcungcuthekisi bawo, athi akuvalelwa emihadini emnyama, izisele ezimanzi, abanga izithembiso zikaThixo zengomso eliqhakazileyo. Nangona imizimba yawo yayivalelwe, ayekhululekile—ekhululekile kuKristu, ekhululekile esenyanisweni yeLizwi laKhe, ekhululekile esethembeni lokubuya kwaKhe kwakamsinyane.
