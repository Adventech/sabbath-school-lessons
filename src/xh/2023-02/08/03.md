---
title:  ISabatha NeNdalo
date:  15/05/2023
---

Kungenxa yokuba umhlaba wethu usidinga ngamandla isigidimi sokuqinisekisa iNdalo okwenza ukuba uThixo asinike iSabatha. Phakathi kweminyaka ye-1800 xa iingcinga zazilibamba ihlabathi lobuchopho ngamandla, uThixo wathumela isigidimi sethemba elingenakukholwa. Sibe sifunda esi sigidimi, sifunyanwa kwisiTyhilelo 14:6, 7.

USathana uwenze onke amacebo okugqwetha ingcamango yeNdalo kuba ecaphukela uYesu engafuni ukuba azuze unqulo olumfaneleyo njengoMdali noMhlanguli wethu. ISabatha ikumbindi wembambano enkulu ngokufaneleka kukaKristu ukuzuza unqulo njengoMdali wethu. Isigidimi semihla yokugqibela siso esibiza lonke uluntu ukuba lubuyele ekunquleni uKristu njengoMdali wezulu nomhlaba. Isiseko salo lonke unqulo yinyaniso yokuba wasidala.

`Funda Genesis 2:1–3, Eksodus 20:8–11, noDuteronomi 5:12–15 ngokuhambelana nesiTyhilelo 14:6, 7. Kumthetho weSabatha sikubona njani ukuhlangana phakathi kweNdalo nokuHlangulwa?`

ISabatha ingumfuziselo ongunaphakade wokufumana kwethu ukuphumla kuYe. ingumqondiso owodwa wokunyaniseka kuMdali (Hez. 20:12, 20). Endaweni yokuba ibe iyinto efuneka ngobuqhinga, ityhila ukuba uphumlo lwenene kubulungisa ngokomthetho lufumaneka kuYe. ISabatha ithetha ngoThixo osizuzele oko sibe singenakho ukuzenzela kona thina ngokwethu.

IsiBhalo sisibizela ukuba siphumle kuthando lwaKhe nenkathalo yaKhe, kwiSabatha nganye. ISabatha imngumfuziselo wophumlo, hayi imisebenzi; wobabalo, hayi ubungqongqo bomthetho, wengqiniseko, hayi ukugwetywa; wokuxhomekeka kuYe, hayi phezu kwethu. ISabatha nganye sivuyiswa kukulunga kwaKhe, simdumise ngosindiso olunokufumaneka kuphela kuKristu.

ISabatha ikwalikhonkco elingunaphakade phakathi kwemfezeko ye-Eden kwixesha eladlulayo nobuqaqawuli bamazulu amatsha nomhlaba omtsha kwixesha elizayo (Isa. 65:17, IsiTyh. 21:1).

ISabatha isibizela ukuba sibuyele kwiingcambu zethu. ilikhonkco kusapho lwethu lwemvelo. ISabatha igcinwe njalo kususela kwaqala ixesha. Iluqhagamshelo olungaqhawukiyo kwixesha lonke ukuya ekudalweni kwethu. Isigcina siqwalasele kwinyaniso enozuko yokuba singabantwana bakaThixo. Isibizela kulwalamano olusondele kakhulu kuYe.

`Urheshwe njani umthetho weSabatha kwisiTyhilelo 14:6, 7, kwaye kubaluleke ngantoni kwisigidimi sethu sexesha lokuphela? (Bona uEks. 20:8–11.)`
