---
title:  Umnyaka We-1844
date:  04/05/2023
---

Iminyaka yokuqala engama-490 yeminyaka yesiprofeto engama-2,300 yamiselwa bucala isizwe sakwaYuda sakudala nokufika kukaMesiya. Inxenye yokugqibela yama-2300 eminyaka inokwenza nabantu bakaThixo, bonke, umYuda neNtlanga, kunye nokuhlanjululwa kwengcwele yasezulwini, kunye nokubuya kwesibini kukaKristu, ekugqibeleni.

Ama-490 eminyaka yokuqala abhekisa ekufikeni kokuqala kukaMesiya aza aphela ngoa.d. 34. Ukuthabatha ama-490 eminyaka kuma-2,300 eminyaka kusishiya ne-1810 leminyaka. Eli bathu lesibini le-1810 leminyaka libhekisa kubantu bakaThixo. Xa siqala kua.d. 34 size songeze i-1810 leminyaka, sifikelela kumnyaka ua.d. 1844.

Ngalo mbono wokuhlanjululwa okanye ukubuyiselwa kwenyaniso engengcwele nexesha lesiphelo lomgwebo wasezulwini, uThixo wenza isibheno saKhe sokugqibela kulo lonke uluntu kwisiTyhilelo 14:6, 7 ukuba basabele kuthando lwaKhe, bamkele ubabalo lwaKhe, baphile ubomi bokumhlonela nokumthobela uThixo.

`Funda Levitikus 16:16. Sasiyintoni isizathu sokuhlanjululwa kwengcwele, kukwasifundisa ntoni thina ngazo Iindaba Ezilungileyo Ezingunaphakade?`

Ngenxa yezono zabantu, ubungwenxa babantu, ingcwele kwakufuneka ihlanjululwe, nto leyo eyayisenzeka kuphela ngegazi lezilwanyana. Kukwanjalo nakuthi. Sidinga uMsindisi, obomi baKhe bufuzisela izilwanyana ezazibulawa ngeMini Yocamagushelo, njengendlela ekuphela kwayo ukuze siphumelele sisindiswe emgwebeni.

`Funda Levitikus 23:26–29. Wayalela ntoni uThixo ukuba abantu baKhe bayenze ngaloo mini yomgwebo, kwaye oko kufanele ukuthini kuthi?`

AmaIsrayeli ayeyi“cikida imiphefumlo yawo.” Esi saci sibonisa ukuba kwakufuneka ezithobile azihlole iintliziyo zawo, azivume izono zawo, aguquke, amcele uThixo ukuba awahlambe njengoko umbingeleli omkhulu wayehlambulula ingcwele yasemhlabeni.

`Izahluko zesiprofeto zikaDaniyeli 7–9 nesiTyhilelo 14 ziqwalasela ngokukodwa kwisibheno esikhawulezileyo selixa lokugweba ukuba kulungiswe. Kususela kowe-1844, sibe siphila kwilixa lomgebo, saye isigidimi sesiTyhilelo sengelosi yokuqala sivakalisa ukuba “Ilixa lokugweba kwaKhe lifikile” (IsiTyh. 14:7). Sikwenza njani namhla “ukuyicikida imiphefumlo yethu”?`
