---
title:  Imbewu Nganye Ikhupha Isivuno
date:  06/04/2023
---

KwisiTyhilelo 14 kukho izivuno ezibini. Isivuno sokutya okuyingqolowa simele amalungisa, kanti esi seediliya simele abangemalungisa okanye abalahliweyo. Zozibini izivuno zivuthwe zagqibelela. Yonke imbewu ehlwayelweyo ivuthwe ngokupheleleyo.

`Funda IsiTyhilelo 14:17—20. Isaci esithi “esixovulelweni esikhulu somsindo kaThixo” sithetha ntoni? Bona IsiTyhilelo 14:10, IsiTyhilelo 15:1, nesiTyhilelo 16:1.`

“Kwaphuma esinye isithunywa sezulu esibingelelweni, sinegunya kuwo umlilo” (IsiTyh. 14:18). Le yingelosi elawula imililo yomgwebo wokugqibela kaThixo. Isivuno sivuthiwe. Isono sifikelele kwimida yaso. Uvukelo ludlule umda wenceba kaThixo. Njengokuba izinto zibe zimbi zingalunganga, ziza kuthi chatha ngaphambi kokuba kudlule konke. UThixo onothando ukwenze konke anokusenzela kona, kuhlaganisa nokuzinikela Yena emnqamlezweni engumbingelelo ngenxa yezono zethu. “Kuba lowo ungazani nasono, wamenza isono ngenxa yethu, ukuze thina sibe bubulungisa bukaThixo kuYe” (2 Kor. 5:21; bona nakuGal. 3:13).

Yintoni enye awayengayenza uThixo ukudlula loo mnqamlezo? Akukho nto ingaphezulu enokwenziwa lubabalo ukuhlangula abo bathi bazingisa ukwala uMoya Oyingcwele.

Nasi isigidimi sesiprofeto esikhawulezileyo sesiTyhilelo 14. Yonke imbewu iye esivunweni. Iingqolowa igqibele ukuvuthwa, neediliya zigqibele ukuvuthwa. Abantu bakaThixo batyhila umfanekiso wobabalo lwaKhe, imfesane, inceba, nothando phambi kweyunivesi. Abantwana bongendawo baveza ukubawa, inkanuko, umona, nentiyo. Isimilo sikaYesu sityhiliwe kwelinye iqela nesimilo sikaSathana sityhiliwe kwelinye.

Iyunivesi iya kubona ebantwini bakaThixo isiTyhilelo sobulungisa, mhlawumbi, ekungekho sizukulwana sakha sabubona ngaphambili. Ukuphikisana nobulungisa bukaKristu obutyhilwe ebantwini baKhe iyunivesi iya kubona iziphumo ezipheleleyo zovukelo ngakuThixo. Inkohlakalo, ububi, nokuchasa umthetho kuya kuba sesichengeni phambi kwabantu neengelosi. Umahluko phakathi kokulungileyo nokubi, okulungileyo nokungalunganga, ukuthobela nokungathobeli, kuya kucaca kuyo yonke iyunivesi, kubantu nakwizithunywa zezulu.

`Ungawuqonda kakuhle kangakanani umahluko phakathi kokulungileyo nokubi? Kubaluleke ngantoni ukuba sikwenze oko? (Bona amaHeb. 5:14.)`
