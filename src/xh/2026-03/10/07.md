Ellen G. White, “The Message Heeded,” pp. 323–334, kwiThe Acts of the Apostles.

Kwiveki ephelileyo, sifunde esi sicatshulwa singentla sikhankanyiweyo kwiThe Acts of the Apostles. Kubalulekile ukusifunda kwakhona. Kwesi isihlandlo, gxila ngakumbi kwiindawo ezisingisele kwincwadi kaPawulos ebuhlungu, iimvakalelo zakhe ekuyibhaleni kwakhe, kwakunye novuyo lwakhe esakufumana iindaba ezimandi ezingenguquko esulungekileyo yabo ibibhalelwa bona. Emva koko, yetyisa ngento esiyixelelwa koku ngokunyaniseka kolungiselelo lukaPawulos kwakunye nezifundo esinokuzihlomela ekusebenzeleni kwethu uKristu.

“Kufuneka sityhile elizweni, kwihlabathi eliwileyo nakumazwe angawanga, ukuba kukho uxolelo kuThixo, ukuba ngothando lukaThixo sinokuxolelaniswa noThixo. Umntu uyaguquka, azohlwaye entliziyweni, akholelwe kuKristu njengedini lakhe elicamagushayo, aze akholelwe ukuba uThixo uxolelanisiwe naye.”—Ellen G. White, Special Testimonies On Education, p. 223.

“Njengebandla, sifumene ukukhanya okukhulu. Oku kukhanya iNkosi ibuphathise thina ukuze kuxhamle kwaye kusikeleleke ihlabathi. Kuthi kunikezelwe ulungiselelo loxolelwaniso. Ngamandla avela ngasentla kufuneka sibabongoze abantu ukuba baxolelaniswe noThixo.”—Ellen G. White, Letter 32, 1903.

Besakuxolelaniswa noThixo, abantu mabafune ubungcwle. Ehlomla kuma-2 Korinte 7:1, uEllen G. White ukrobisa koko kwakuththwa nguPawulos “ngokufeza ubungcwele sisoyika uThixo” (2 Kor. 7:1). Yena uthi uPawulos wayefuna ukunceda amakholwa amatsha “ukuba abe ngamaKristu azimeleyo, akhulayo, omeleleyo elukholweni, anyamekileyo, naphelelisileyo ekuzinikeleni kwawo kuThixo nakumsebenzi wokuqhubela phambili ubukumkani baKhe.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 201.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`UPawulos uthetha ngathi “njengezitya zomdongwe” ezithwele ubutyebi begospile (2 Kor. 4:7). Into yokuba imeko yomntu ibuthathaka, ibhetyebhetye, kwaye izaliswe yimida ingomeleza njani endaweni yokusingele phantsi ukuvakaliswa kwegospile?`

`Kuthetha ukuthini ukuba “sisidalwa esitsha” (2 Kor. 5:17)? Oku kubuchaphazela njani ubomi bethu bemihla ngemihla? UKristu ukwenze njani isidalwa esitsha?`

`Kuma-2 Korinte 6:4, 5, uPawulos ubonelela ngomntyangampo woluhlu lweenzima ngenxa yegospile. Waasabela njani kwiimbandezelo zakhe (bona kuma-2 Kor. 6:6, 7)? Oku kukunceda njani ukuba usabele kwezakho?`

`UPawulos uthelekisa ukuba buhlungu ngokokukaThixo kunye nentlungu yehlabathi (2 Kor. 7:10). Kungaluphi uhlobo okunokuthi ukuba buhlungu bungqamane nenguquko? Ungakuchaza njani ukuba buhlungu ngokokukaThixo xa ukuthelekise nokuba buhlungu ngokwehlabathi?`
