---
title:  Imbandezelo Nozuko
date:   31/08/2026
---

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 4:7-18. Yenza uludwe lweembandezelo zikaPawulos. Wazinyamezela njani iimbandezelo zakhe?`

UJohn Huss, umhlaziyi omkhulu weBohemia yakudala, wakha wathi ngoYesu, “Yena uyiNkosi yehlabathi, size thina sibe ngabantu abadelekileyo-noko kunjalo Yena waabandezeleka! Kutheni, le nto ngoko, singenakubandezeleka kanjalo, ngokukodwa xa ukubandezeleka kuthi ikokwentlambululo?”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 105.

Umpostile uPawulos waabonakalisa kwiinkulungwane ezingaphambili kwa intumekelelo enye yokubulaleka ngenxa kaKristu. Wayesazi ukuba akanto kuphela usisitya sodongwe esophukayo (2 Kor. 4:7). Wayethe gqolo ukuziva exinekile, ethingaza, etshutshiswa, kwaye ekhahlelwa phantsi, kodwa noko kunjalo engatyunyuzwa, engancami, engalityalwa, okanye atshatyalaliswe (2 Kor. 4:7). Wayekulungele ukukuthwala “ngomzimba ukufa kukaKristu, ukuze ubomi bukaKristu” bubonakalaliswe kuye (2 Kor. 4:10, 11).

“Ngokufa kukaYesu,” mhlawumbi uPawulos wayethetha iimbandezelo azikhankanye kwiindima ezingaphambili. Kwelinye icala, kwingcamango ekhawulezileyo, ibinzana “ubomi bukaYesu” kungahle kusingisele ekuhlangulweni ekufeni okanye amandla akwamoya obomi bangoku. Kodwa ke, ekugqibeleni, oku kubhekiselele eluvukweni (2 Kor. 4:12).

Okungumdla, isibini “ukufa nobomi” sivela izihlandlo ezithathu kuma-2 Korinte 4:10-12. Esi sisikhumbuzo sokuba, kweli xesha langoku, ubomi buxutywe nokufa. Noko kunjalo, kuzuko oluzayo, sakungcamla ubomi kungekho kufa (IsiTyhi. 20:14, Isityhi. 21:4).

Okubaluleke ngakumbi, ama-2 Korinte 4:7-18 abonisa ukuba igospile ishunyayelwa ngabantu ababuthathaka ukuze uzuko luye kuThixo kuphela (2 Kor. 4:15). Hayi manqaphanqapha, abahambisa ivangeli bayabandezeleka kwisithuba somsebenzi wabo wokuvangela. Noko kunjalo, ukubandezeleka kwethu apha kukhaphukhaphu yaye kokwethutyana xa buthelekiswa nobuqaqawuli obulinde thina obungunaphakade (2 Kor. 4:17). Ikholwa liphila ngokholo, hayi ngokubona (2 Kor. 4:18, 2 Kor. 5:7).

Eli themba kubomi obuzayo lathimba ingqondo kaPawulos kangangokuba uthe gqolo ukuthetha ngalo xa isicatshulwa siqhubeka (2 Kor. 5:1–10). Ubhekisa kumzimba wakhe ofayo ngesifaniso sendlu yalo mhlaba. Kwelinye icala, “isakhiwo esivela kuThixo” sisifaniso somzimba ovusiweyo (2 Kor. 5:1), ithemba elikhula lamakholwa awo onke amaphakade.

`Kutheni le nto kubalulekile ukuba, phakathi kwako konke esithubeleza kuko ngoku, sisoloko sigcina ithemba lovuko, uvuko lwethu (1 Kor. 15:52), liphambi kwethu?`
