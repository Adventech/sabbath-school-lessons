---
title:  Intuthuzelo Novuyo
date:   03/09/2026
---

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 7. Zithini iimvakalelo zikaPawulos esakuva ukuba ama-Korinte aye aguquka?`

Lungakanani uthando oluphuphuma kula mazwi “Nisezintliziyweni zethu” (2 Kor. 7:3; bona kanjalo kuma-2 Kor. 6:11). Kulangazelelo lwakhe olunzulo lokuba uthando lwakhe luphindiswe, uPawulos kanjalo uthi, “Samkeleni ngeentliziyo zenu” (2 Kor. 7:2). Lo gama ibinzana “ezintliziyweni zenu” lingekho kumbhalo wesiGrike, inkoliso yeenguqulelo zesiNgesi zibonelela ngalo, nto leyo ilungileyo kuba umxholo uyayixhasa lo nto.

Ngokwenene, amaKorinte azivula iintliziyo zawo kuPawulos kunye noogxa bakhe. Yiyo lo nto indima yesine ilumemelelo lovuyo. Amazwi kaPawulos avakalisa ezimnandi ngazo iimvakalelo zakhe kwesi sithuba: “Ndinokungafihlisi okukhulu kuni, ndizingca kakhulu ngani; ndizele yintuthuzelo, ndiphuphuma uvuyo kakhulu kuyo yonke imbandezelo yethu” (2 Kor. 7:4). UPawulos uzaliswe yintuthuzelo novuyo. Intuthuzelo novuyo olungako amabandla ethu anokuzizisa kubalungiseleli bawo ngokuzinikela ngokupheleleyo kuKristu.

Kuma-2 Korinte 7:5-16, uPawulos uchaza ngakumbi isizathu sentuthuzelo yakhe novuyo. Ezi ngcamango zimbini ziyagquba kwesisicatshulwa. Isenzi _uparakaleō_ (“ukuthuthuzela”) okanye isibizo _uparaklēsis_ (“intuthuzelo” zivela kunye zibe sixhenxe xa zizonke kuma-2 Korinte 7. Esi sigaba sencwadi siphela ngendlela esiqale ngayo, oko kukuthi, ngentuthuzelo eninzi kuThixo (2 Kor. 1:3–7). Intuthuzelo kaPawulos kuma-2 Korinte 7 ivela kwisiqabu aye wanaso kuba la ncwadi ibuhlungu ivelise isiphumo ebenenjongo yaso ngayo.

Nangona esi siqabu sisisiphumo sengxelo emnandi kaTixo, ekugqibeleni, uThixo sisenzeleli sentuthuzela awayingcamlayo uPawulos (2 Kor. 7:6). UThixo ngokwenene “nguThixo onako konke ukuthuthuzela, osithuthuzelayo kuyo yonke imbandezelo” (2 Kor. 1:3, 4).

Ngokunika umdla, lo gama uPawulos “ezaliswe yintuthuzelo,” kwayena “uphuphuma uvuyo” (2 Kor. 7:4, 7, 13). Nangona le ncwadi ibuhlungu ibibangele unxunguphalo olukhulu, yayi lunxunguphalo ngokwentando kaThixo ngenjongo yenguquko (2 Kor. 7:9–11). AmaKorinte “aba buhlungu ngendlela enobuThixo (2 Kor. 7:11), intlungu evelisa “inguquko ekhokelela elusindisweni” (2 Kor. 7:10). Yintoni engenye enokuzisa uvuyo ngakumbi kwintliziyo yomlungiseleli onyanisekileyo kaThixo?

`Ukhe wayingcamla intlungu enobuThixo ebomini bakho? Waayazi njani ukuba yayi yintlungu engokwentando kaThixo ukuba ikukhokelele enguqukweni?`
