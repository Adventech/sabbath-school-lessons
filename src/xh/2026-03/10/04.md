---
title:  Ulungiselelo Loxolelaniso Olugxile KuKristu
date:   01/09/2026
---

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 5:11-15. Esi sicatshulwa sibonakalisa njani ukuba ulungiselelo lukaPawulos lwalungoKristu?`

UPawulos wayesazi ukuba umele ukuze anikezele ingxelo yolungiselelo lwakhe kuKristu (2 Kor. 5:10). Yena wayekwazi “ukoyika iNkosi” wazama ukuqononondisa abanye ngegospile kaKristu (2 Kor. 5:11). Olu loyiko yintlonipho nokumangala nguKristu kwaye, yiyo lo nto, idityaniswe nothando lukaPawulos ngakuKristu kwakunye nokuthemba uthando lukaKristu ngaye. KwiTestamente eNdala, ukoyika iNkosi kuthetha ukuhamba ezindleleni zaYo, ukuyithanda Yona, nokuyikhonza Yona ngayo yonke intliziyo yakho nomphefumlo (Dut. 10:12).

Ulungiselelo lukaPawulos alugxilanga kumna koko lugxile kuKristu. Yena akazange azincome. Isizathu sokuqhayisa kwakhe nguKristu (2 Kor. 12:9). Yena wathi, “Mna ke mandingakhe ndiqhayise nganto, ingenguwo umnqamlezo weNkosi yethu uYesu Kristu” (Gal. 6:14). Ngoko ke, ithuba lamaKorinte lokuqhayisa ngaye (2 Kor. 5:12) lithetha ukuzingca ngolungiselelo lwakhe olugxile kuKristu ngokwahlukileyo kulungiselelo olugxile kumna lwabo bamchasayo.

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 5:16-21, Kolose 1:16-21, nakumaEfese 2:13-16. Wayethetha ukuthini uPawulos “ngolungiselelo loxolelaniso”?`

UKristu ngumlungiseleli woxolelaniso ngokugqwesileyo. Kangangokuba, Yena “usinike ulungiselelo loxolelaniso” (2 Kor. 5:18). Ingcamango yoxolelaniso ivela ngokuthe gqolo kuma-2 Korinte 5:16-21. Le yingcamango esemqoka kuPawulos, kwaye kumele ibenjalo nakuthi, aba.

UThixo uluxolelanise naYe uluntu ngokufa koNyana waKhe okucamagushayo. Abo baxolelaniswe noThixo bazizidalwa ezitsha (2 Kor. 5:17). Ngoku, bafanele ukudlulisela esi “sigidimi soxolelaniso” phambili ngokuvakalisa igospile kaKristu (2 Kor. 5:19). Kule meko, “sizizigidimi ke ngoko ngenxa kaKristu, ngokungathi uThixo uniyala ngathi” (2 Kor. 5:20).

`Cinga ngoko uKristu akwenzele kona. Cinga ngobutyala, isono, ukugwetywa ebekuzakuba bobakho kodwa ngenxa yoko Yena akwenzele kona emnqamlezweni. Le nyaniso ifanele ukuyichaphazela njani indlela onxulumana ngayo nabanye, ngokukodwa abo bangayaziyo iNkosi?`
