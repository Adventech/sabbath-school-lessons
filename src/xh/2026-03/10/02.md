---
title:  Iziqhamo Zolungiselelo Lokwenene
date:   30/08/2026
---

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 3:1-9. Kukweyiphi imeko esinokuthi sibe yincwadi kaKristu?`

Iincwadi ezingqinelayo zaziqhelekile kwixesha lamaGrike-Roma. Noko kunjalo, uPawulos wayengazithwali ezoo ncwadi. Amandla aguqulayo kaMoya kubomi bamaKorinte ayebubungqina bolungiselelo lwakhe olunyanisekileyo. Noku kunjalo uPawulos wayeqinisekile kwelokuba ibandla laseKorinte alibangakho ngenxa yokuhlakanipha kwakhe okanye imigudu (2 Kor. 3:4–6). Zange abandakanyeke ekuzinyuseni (2 Kor. 3:5, 1 Kor. 2:2).

UPawulos uthetha ngolungiselelo lwakhe ngokuthi gqaba-gqaba ngeminqophiso emibini: lowo umdala omelwe nguMoses kwakunye nomtsha omelwe nguye noogxa bakhe. Umfundi ongxamileyo angacinga ukuba umnqophiso omdala zange unikezele themba lasindiso, kodwa oku akuyonyani. Usindiso lwalufumaneka koMdala njengakuMnqophiso oMtsha. UMnqophiso oMdala wawuyigospile ngenxa engaphambili. “IsiBhalo, sibona ke ngenxa engaphambili ukuba uThixo uyazigwebela iintlanga ngokwaselukholweni, sazishumayela ngenxa engaphambili iindaba ezilungileyo kuAbraham, sisithi, Ziyakusikelelwa kuwe zonke iintlanga’” (Gal. 3:8).

Kuma-2 Korinte 3:1-4:6, singabona ukuba uMnqophiso oMdala usetyenziselwe ukufuzisela amava okugcina ngqongqo umthetho kwabo babexhomekeke kwimisebenzi yabo yokuthobela ngendlela yokukholisa uThixo. UMnqophiso oMtsha, noko kunjalo, umele amava abo baxhomekeke ngokupheleleyo kubabalo lukaThixo ukuba benze konke oko uThixo athembise ukukwenzela bona nangaphakathi kubo.

UPawulos uthetha ngeentlobo ezimbini zokusabela, kwabakholwayo kwakunye nabangakholwayo, egospileni. Akathethi ngeegospile ezohlukeneyo, enye kwiTestamente eNdala kwakunye nengenye kweNtsha-kuba inye kuphela igospile, enikezelwe nguThixo, “wasibiza ngobizo olungcwele, engenzi ngokwemisebenzi yethu, esenza ngokweyakhe ingqibo, nobabalo, esababalwa ngalo ngoKristu Yesu phambi kwamaxesha aphakade” (2 Tim. 1:9).

Oku ayikokuphika ukuba ama-2 Korinte 2:14-4:6 aqulathe iindawo ezithile ezinembali. Kodwa yena usebenzisa loo mbali ukuzisa ingongoma yokuba abanye phakathi kwabo “bayasindiswa” baze abanye “batshabalale” (2 Kor. 2:15). Ngenxa yendlela yokusabela, leyo yokungakholwa kwakunye nokungaluthembi ulungiselelo lukaMoses, ulungiselelo lwakhe lunokubonwa njengolungiselelo logwebo nokufa. Ngenxa yokuba ibandla eKorinte lakholwayo, ulungiselelo lukaPawulos phakathi kwabo lwabonakala njengolungiselelo lobulungisa, ulungiselelo loMoya onikezela ubomi.

La mava osindiso ebandla laseKorinte abubungqina bolungiselelo lukaPawulos olunyanisekileyo.
