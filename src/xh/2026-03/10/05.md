---
title:  Ukubizelwa Ebungcweleni
date:   02/09/2026
---

Kuma-2 Korinte 6:3-10, uPawulos uthe gqolo ukukhuthaza amaKorinte ukuba axolelaniswe noThixo. Uzisa umtyangampo woluhlu lweenzima neempumelelo ukubonisa ukuba kuthetha ukuthini ukuba ngumlandeli kaKristu kwakunye nomlungiseleli kaThixo. Ngamafutshane, udwelisa iimeko ezinzima (2 Kor. 6:4, 5), iimpawu zesimilo (2 Kor. 6:6), izixhobo zolungiselelo (2 Kor. 6:7), kwakunye namahla-ndenyuka olungiselelo (2 Kor. 6:8–10). Emva kokuyala amalungu aseKorinte ukuba axolelaniswe noThixo, uPawulos uyawabongoza ukuba aphile ubomi obungcwele, kwaye akwenze oko ngokuzahlula kwimpembelelo eyingozi yabo bangakholwayo nakwizinto ezingahlambulukanga (2 Kor. 6:14–17).

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 6:11-7:1. Ngokwesisicatshulwa, bukhangeleka njani ubomi obungcwele?`

UPawulos uyagxininisa kwesi sicatshulwa ukubaluleka kobushushu nothando phakathi ebandleni(1 Kor. 6:11–13). Ubungqina kwinto yokuba abantu baxolelanisiwe noThixo kukuba bazabalazela uxolelaniso omnye komnye. Ngokwenene, bona baba, njengoko kwakunjalo, ngonozakuzaku boxolelaniso olusemhlabeni.

Okulandelayo, isimemelelo sobungcwele sinikezelwe ngokusetyenziswa kwezibongozo ezithandathu, oko kukuthi, (1) “Musani ukunxulumana nabolunye uhlobo, abangakholwayo ke” (2 Kor. 6:14); (2) “ ‘Phumani phakathi kwabo’ ” (2 Kor. 6:17); (3) “ ‘Nizahlule’ ” (2 Kor. 6:17); (4) “ ‘Into engcolileyo ningayichukumisi’ ” (2 Kor. 6:17); (5) “Masizihlambulule kuko konke ukudyobheka kwenyama nokomoya” (2 Kor. 7:1); (6) “Yenzani ubungcwele bugqibelele, nisoyika uThixo (2 Kor. 7:1). Ezi zikhuthazo zibonisa ukuba uThixo ongcwele ufuna ubomi obungcwele nokuzikhwebula kunqulo-zithixo.

Kwelinye icala, isicatshulwa sikwazisa izithembiso ezisixhenxe eziphawula indima yebandla lobuKristu njengetempile engcwele: (1)“ ‘Ndiya kuhlala phakathi kwabo’ ”; (2) “ ‘Ndiya kuhamba phakathi kwabo’ ”; (3) “ ‘Ndibe nguThixo wabo’ ”; (4) “ ‘Babe ngabantu bam bona’ ”; (5) “ ‘Ndandiya kunamkela’ ”; (6) “ ‘Ndibe nguBawo kuni’ ”; (7) “ ‘Nina nibe ngoonyana neentombi kum’ ” (2 Kor. 6:16, 17, 18).

Qaphela ukuba izithembiso ezine kuma-2 Korinte 6:16 zisisiseko semiyalelo emithathu kuma-2 Korinte 6:17 (jonga igama “Kungoko” ekuqalekeni kwama-2 Korinte 6:17). Oku kubonakalisa ukuba ubungcwele abusosiphumo semigudu yomntu koko ngumsebenzi kaMoya oyiNgcwele entliziyweni. Nangona ubungcwele buvela kuThixo, abakholwayo mabenze eyabo indima kwaye balubukule unqulozithixo kunye nasiphi isenzo esingahlambulukanga.

`Izithembiso zikaThixo kuma-2 Korinte 6:16-18 zisixelela ntoni ngento obuyiyo ubungcwele?`
