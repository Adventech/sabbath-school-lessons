---
title:  Uloyiso Lokugqibela Phezu Kokufa
date:   20/08/2026
---

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 15:54-57. Esi sicatshulwa sisixelela ntoni ngoloyiso lwethu lokugqibela phezu kokufa?`

UPawulos uqalisa umhlathi wokugqibela kuma-1 Korinte 15 ngentetho etsal’umdla: “Inyama negazi azinako ukubudla ilifa ubukumkani” (1 Kor. 15:50). Intlaninge yabafundi beBhayibhile isebenzisa esi sibhengezo ukuthi uPawulos ukhusela ukuphila okungabambekiyo emazulwini. Kodwa umxholo ubonisa ngenye indlela. Uthelekiso olukuma-1 Korinte 15:50 luchaza ukuba “inyama negazi” ihambelana “nokonakala,” njengoba “ubukumkani bukaThixo” buhambelana kunye “nokungonakali.” Kwanjengoba kwenzekile kuma-1 Korinte 15:42-49, nalapha kanjalo uPawulos uthelekisa umzimba ngokwangoku (okanye nkqu nesidumbu) kumzimba ovusiweyo. Umzimba ongcwatyiweyo uphawuleka ngokonakala nokufa, lo gama umzimba ovusiweyo uphawuleka ngokungonakali nokungafi (1 Kor. 15:50, 53, 54). Ibekwe ngokulula, uPawulos uthi imizimba yethu idinga ukuphumela kutshintsho olumandla ukuze ikwazi ukulidla ilifa izulu.

Ngamafutshane, uPawulos usebenzisa iingcamango zokonakala nokufa ukusingisela kwimo yethu yesono. Kwimibhalo yobuYuda, “inyama negazi” libinzana elingoluntu oluwileyo, nto leyo esisizathu sokuba imizimba yethu iguqulwe ize igutyulwe konke ukungagqibeleli ekubuyeni kwaKhe.

Kuphela kuxa imvelo yethu yesono isusiwe (1 Kor. 15:54) size sithubeleze kumava okuzukiswa (1 Kor. 15:51–53, 1 Tesa. 4:13–17) esiyakuthi isibhengezo “ ‘Ukufa kufincelwe eloyisweni’ ” (1 Kor. 15:54) sizaliseke. Emva koko, le ngoma inobunganga nendelo iyakuculwa: “ ‘Luphi na, kufa ulwamvilwa lwakho? Luphi na, Hadesi, uloyiso lwakho.’ ” (1 Kor. 15:55). Oku konke kuyakwenzeka ekubuyeni kwesibini kukaKristu (1 Kor. 15:51, 52).

Cinga ngoku: Sivala amehlo ethu ekufeni, ize into elandelayo esizakuyingcamla ibe kukubuya kwesibini kukaYesu, xa esivusa kwelabafileyo. Nokuba ikholwa life nini, nkqu kumawaka eminyaka adlulileyo, “ngesiquphe, ngephanyazo,” bayakwenziwa baphile “ngexilongo lokugqibela.” Kuba liya kulila ixilongo, bavuke abafileyo bengenakonakala, senziwe ngakumbi ke thina” (1 Kor. 15:52).

`Ngubani ongakhange akhalazele indlela ubomi obudlula ngayo ngephanyazo? Kumava ethu, leyo yindlela ekuyakukhangeleka kukhawuleze ngayo ukubuya kwesibini kukaYesu. Mhlawumbi ingcamango yethu yokuqala xa ebuya iyakuba yile “Yhoo, Nkosi, ukubuya kwaKho kukhawulezile!” Le ngcamango isinceda njani ukuba sikwamkele ngcono oko kubonwa ngathi “kukulibala”?`
