---
title:  Ukuvakalisa Uvuko lukaKristu
date:   16/08/2026
---

UPawulos uqala ama-1 Korinte 15 ngokugxila kwigospile. Uthetha ngegospile: (1) awayishumayela kumaKorinte; (2) leyo ayamkelayo (3) leyo bamileyo kuyo (4) leyo basindiswayo kuyo (1 Kor. 15:1, 2). Esi siqalo silungiselela umfundi oko kulandelayo kwisahluko esi size sibonise indlela olubaluleke ngayo uvuko lukaKristu kusindiso lwethu (bona kanjalo Rom. 10:9, 10). Uvuko lwaKhe sesona sigaba sibalulekileyo kumyalezo wegospile kangangokuba ukuluphika kuyakhabana nokukholwa komntu kuKristu.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 15:1-4; Luka 24:44–47; namaRoma 1:1–4. Yintoni efanayo ezinayo ezi zicatshulwa?`

Kuma-1 Korinte 15:1-4, umntu angafumana isishwankathelo somyalezo kaPawulos. Nokuba ibinzana “ngokweziBhalo” lisingisele kwizicatshulwa ezithile zeTestamente eNdala okanye kwiTestamente eNdala iphela lo nto ayinamsebenzi. Ukufa kukaYesu novuko zizalisekisa amadinga kaThixo afumaneka kwiTestamente eNdala.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 15:2, 11. Kutheni le nto ezi ndima zibeka zimelane ezi ngcamango eyokukholwa neyokushumayela? Yintoni unxulumano phakathi kwesi sibini?`

Bonke abo bavakalisa ukuba uKristu uvukile kufuneka baqale bakholwe ukuba uvuko lwaKhe sisiganeko esiyimbali. Kule imeko, ama-1 Korinte 15:5-8 adlala indima ebalulekileyo kwiTestamente eNtsha. Esi sicatshulwa sibonelela ngobungqina obuluqilima besiBhalo bokuba uKristu waabonwa yintlaninge yabantu emva kokuvuka kwaKhe, noninzi lwabo olwalusaphila ngexesha uPawulos awayebhala ngalo le ncwadi (1 Kor. 15:6).

Ngokuphambili, uPawulos uthi, Hambani niyokuzibuzela into abayibonileyo. Yindlela leyo awayeqiniseke ngayo yinyaniso yokuvuka kukaKristu. Aba bantu ngamangqina azibonele gaqa. Babe yilento uYesu wayethe bazakuba yiyo, oko kukuthi, “amangqina ezi zinto” (Luka 24:48).

`Zizathu zini esinazo zokukholelwa kuvuko lukaKristu? Kanjalo, zeziphi ezinye izinto, ezelizwe okanye ezingcwle, esizikholelwayo sibe singazibonanga ngokunokwethu?`
