### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
1 Korinte 15; Luka 24:44–47; IsiTyhi. 20:5, 6; Kol. 2:12; 2 Tim. 1:12; 1 Tesa. 4:13–17.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ukuba ke uKristu akavukanga, koba akunto ukushumayela kwethu, loba luyalambatha nokholo lwenu. . . . Ukuba ke uKristu akavukanga, loba luyinto engeyanto ukholo lwenu, noba nisesezonweni zenu!” (1 Korinte 15:14–17).

Kunikisa umdla ukuba, nkqu ngexesha lakhe, uPawulos kwafuneka ajamelane nabo babephika uvuko lwabafileyo. Kakade, abantu belooxesha bayibona into eyenziwa kukufa emzimbeni womntu. Babeyazi indlela isidumbu esinyibilika ngayo, size emva koko some sibe ngumhlaba, size kamva siphantse singabi yonto. Kwaye babeyazi, kanjalo, ukuba lide ithuba abantu befile. Eneneni, inkoliso yabantu babesele baafayo kudaloo kwangaphambi kokuba baphile.

Uvuko lwabafileyo lwalubonakala lungaseyonto yamkelekileyo kubo ngeloo xesha kunokuba lusenza njalo kuthi ngoku, phantse xa uxonge kwinkalo yobuntu. Kwaye kumele ukuba oko yayingumba uPawulos awayewushukuxa.

Yaye, wawubalulekile, nawo. Ukuba uYesu akavukanga, Yena akanguYe lo wathi unguye Yena, uMnqamlezo awunafuthe, kwaye nezono zethu azihlawulelwanga. Ukuphelelwa lithemba kuphela kwento ngokwenene ebiyakuba kanti sisele nayo. Kodwa iNkosi yethu ivukile, yenyukela ezulwini, kwaye izakubuya ukuba isithathele ekhaya!

Kule veki sizakugxila kuma-1 Korinte 15 kwakunye nemfundiso yawo ngovuko lukaKristu. Bephenjelelwa yimbono yobuhedeni eyayibangqongile, abanye eKorinte babesithi akukho luvuko. Ukuphendula, uPawulos uqinisekisa uvuko lukaKristu njengethemba lethu losindiso ekuphela kwalo.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Agasti 22._
