---
title:  Umzimba Ovusiweyo
date:   19/08/2026
---

Kuma-1 Korinte 15:35-39, uPawulos uguqukela kwingxoxo emfutshane ngomzimba ovusiweyo. Uqalisa eli candela ngokuveza imibuzo emibini: “ ‘Bathini na ukuvuka abafileyo? Beza benamzimba unjani na?’ ” (1 Kor. 15:35). Le mibuzo iphendulwe kuma-1 Korinte 15:36-49.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 15:36-41. Esi sicatshulwa siwuphendula njani umbuzo okuma-1 Korinte 15:35?`

UPawulos usebenzisa izifaniso ezithathu ukunceda abafundi bakhe baqonde into eyenzeka eluvukweni. Isifaniso sokuqala (1 Kor. 15:36–38) siphawula ngelithi umzimba ufana nembewu ekufuneka ife kuqala (okanye iyeke ukuba yimbewu) ukuze ngommangaliso ibe sisityalo. Imfundiso icacile: uvuko ngummangaliso kaThixo. Okwesibini, isifaniso seziqu (1 Kor. 15:39, 40) siphawula ukuba, kweli hlabathi, uThixo waabonelela ngeentlobo ezohlukeneyo zeziqu ezilwanyaneni nasebantwini ezilungele imeko yangoku. Ngokunjalo, imizimba yethu iyakulungela iimeko ezintsha ezulwini. Le ngcamango ihlabela mgama ngesifaniso somzimba onobuqaqawuli (1 Kor. 15:40, 41), esigxininisa ukuba ubuqaqawuli bomzimba ovusiweyo bubalasele ngokugqithisileyo kobo bomzimba wangaphambili, leyo yethu iwileyo yasemhlabeni. .

Le ngcamango kwakhona isenokubonwa kwizithelekiso ezine phakathi komzimba wethu womhlaba apha ngoku, kwakunye nomzimba wethu ovusiweyo. Lo wokuqala ungowomhlaba, uyonakala, ubuthathaka, kwaye ngowemvelo. Kwelinye icala, lo wamva ngowasezulwini, awunakonakala, unamandla, kwaye ngowomoya (1 Kor. 15:40–44). Oku akuthethi kuthi akukho kuqhubekeka phakathi kwesi sibini. Ukusebenzisa kukaPawulos igama lesiGrike _usōma_ (“umzimba”) kuyo yomibini umzimba ongcwatyiweyo nomzimba ovusiweyo kubonisa ukuqhubekeka. Ngokwahlukileyo, ezi zithelekiso zine zingentla zikwabonisa ukungaqhubekeki. Imizimba yethu emitsha (bulela iNkosi) ayizokuba yileyo sinayo ngoku ibolayo.

UPawulos akanxulumanisi igama “yomoya” ekuphileni okungabalulekanga. Kwenye indawo, uthi uYesu “uzakuwenza kumila kumbi umzimba wokuthobeka kwethu, ukuze wona wenziwe ufane nawo umzimba wozuko lwakhe” (Fil. 3:21). Siya kuba nemizimba yokwenene, kodwa ayisayi konakala okanye ibole. Kuba konke esikwaziyo ngoku ikukubola, ukugula, kwakunye nokufa, kunzima ukucinga ngobomi zingekho ezi zinto, kodwa yinto leyo esiyithenjisiweyo ngoYesu.

`Ingqiniseko yokuba imizimba yethu iyakuguqulwa igqibelele isinceda njani ukuba siqine kwizithintelo zethu namhlanje?`
