Ellen G. White, “God’s People Delivered,” pp. 635–652, kwiThe Great Controversy.

“Ubungcwele bukaKristu yingqiniseko yekholwa yobomi obungunaphakade. ‘Lowo ukholwayo kum,’ watsho uYesu, ‘nokuba ubethe wafa, wodla ubomi: bonke abadla ubomi bekholwa kum, abasayi kufa naphakade; uyakholwa na koko?’ UKristu apha ukhangele phambili kwixesha lokubuya kwaKhe kwesibini. Ngelo xesha amalungisa afileyo ayakuvuswa enokungonakali, aze amalungisa aphilayo anyuselwe ezulwini engabonanga kufa. Ummangaliso awayezakuwenza uKristu, ekuvuseni uLazaro, uzakumela ukuvuswa kwawo onke amalungisa afileyo.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 530.

“Umhlaba waadidizela ngokumandla xa ilizwi loNyana kaThixo libiza abangcwele abaleleyo. Baasabela ubizo baza baphuma bembathiswe ukungafi okubengezelayo, bedanduluka, ‘Uloyiso, uloyiso, phezu kokufa nengcwaba! Owu kufa, luphina ulwamvila lwakho?’ (Bona 1 Kor. 15:55.) Emva koko abangcwele abaphilayo kwakunye nabo bavusiweyo baphakamisa amazwi abo ngendanduluko ende yoloyiso. Loo mizimba ibiyehlele engcwabeni ithwele iimpawu zezifo nokufa yaphuma isempilweni engafiyo nasemandleni. Abangcwele abaphilayo baguqulwa ngesiquphe, ngephanyazo, baze baxhwilwe kunye naba bavusiweyo, baze kunye bahlangane neNkosi esibhakabhakeni. Yhoo, hayi intlangano ezuke ngolo hlobo! Abahlobo ebebohlulwe kukufa bahlanganiswa, bengazokube bohlukane.”—Ellen G. White, The Story of Redemption, pp. 411, 412.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Cinga ngabo bazibonela ngawabo amehlo ukuvuka kukaKristu (Zenzo 1:22, Zenzo 2:32, Zenzo 3:15, Zenzo 4:33, Zenzo 5:30–32). Singakwazi njani thina, phantse kwiminyaka engamawaka amabini emva kwesasiganeko, ukuba “ngamangqina” okuvuka kwaKhe?`

`Ukuvuka kukaKristu kungumongo wesigidimi segospile (1 Kor. 15:1– 4). Ngaphandle kovuko, ukuvakaliswa kokufa kukaKristu bekungazoba namsebenzi (1 Kor. 15:14). Ukufa kukaKristu ngokunokwako bekungazoba namsebenzi. Kutheni? Impendulo yakho koku ithini ngamandla okuvuka kukaKristu?`

`Cinga kancinci ngentetho kaPawulos etsal’ umdla “ukuba abafileyo abavuki, ‘Masesisidla, sisela, kuba ngomso siya kufa! ’ ” (1 Kor. 15:32). Ithini ingongoma?`

`Eklasini, thethani ngemeko yabafi. Kutheni ama-1 Korinte 15 engaqondakali kwaphela ukuba kanti, ekufeni, abasindisiweyo ngoko nangoko basiwa ezulwini?`
