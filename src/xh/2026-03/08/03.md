---
title:  UKristu Ovukileyo, Ithemba Lethu Esiqine Ngalo!
date:   17/08/2026
---

Kuma-1 Korinte 15:9-19, uPawulos ucacisa indlela ezimbi nezoyikeka ngayo iziphumo zokuphika uvuko. Ngaphandle kovuko, amakholwa awanathemba kwixesha langoku, ngakumbi mpela kwixa elizayo.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 15:9-19. Yintoni esiphulukana nayo xa uKristu engavukanga?`

Kakade, abahedeni bamandulo babengakholelwa kuvuko, ngokukodwa kwilizwe lamaGrike, ngenkolo yalo yeziqu ezibini (ekufeni umphefumlo unyukela kwindawo eya kuyo imiphefumlo yabafiliye). UPawulos usungula umhlathi kuma-1 Korinte 15:12-19 ngombuzo buciko obonisa ukudideka kwakhe okunzulu: “Intoni? Bakwazi njani abanye benu ukuthi akukho luvuko lwabafileyo?” (1 Kor. 15:12, ukuziguqulela kombhali). KuPawulos, ukungakholelwa kuvuko akukholeleki, ngokukodwa xa babebaninzi kangako abazibonelayo (1 Kor. 15:5–8). Kodwa ngakumbi mpela, ngaphandle kovuko ithemba labo lisekelwe ebuxokini, kwaye basesezonweni zabo.

Uthi, eneneni, ukuba akukho luvuko lwabafileyo, ngoko ke(1) uKristu akavukanga (1 Kor. 15:13, 16); (2) akunto ukushumayela kwethu (1 Kor. 15:14); (3) nokholo lwethu alunto (1 Kor. 15:14); (4) singamangqina axokayo (1 Kor. 15:15); (5) luyinto engento ukholo lwethu (1 Kor. 15:17); (6) sisesezonweni zethu (1 Kor. 15:17); kwaye, ngokuqinisekileyo, (7) abo bafileyo batshabalele (1 Kor. 15:18).

Ngaphandle kovuko, zombini ukushumayela nokholo zililize (1 Kor. 15:14). Igama lesiGrike elichaza igama “akunto” _ngukenos_. Le yinguqulelo eyiyo, kodwa ibanzi ngeyona ndlela. Abapicothi baxoxa ngelokuba _ukenos_ uthetha “akunto” kwimeko yokuswela inyaniso (ngoko, “ukungabi yonyani”) okanye ukuswela iziphumo (ngoko, “ukungabi naziphumo okanye ifuthe”) okanye ukuswela injongo (ngoko, “ukungabi nanjongo,” “ukulambatha”)

Nokuba yeyiphi na intsingiselo etsolileyo, kwimeko apho uvuko lungekhoyo khona, ukholo luchazwa njengolungento, nto leyo isuka _kumataios_ wesiGrike (1 Kor. 15:17). Nangona _umataios_ engahlukanga kangako _kukenos_, ingcamango kukuba ukuba uYesu akaphili, ukholo luyalambatha, ulahlekiso, kuba izono zethu azixolelwanga (1 Kor. 15:17). Siyakuba ngamangqina obuxoki, silahlekisa silahlekiswa (1 Kor. 15:15).

`Umntu uwenza aqondakale njani ama-1 Korinte xa abafileyo benyukela ezulwini (okanye esihogweni) ngoko nangoko? Kutheni, ngoko, ukuqonda ukuba abafileyo balele kuyimfundiso ebaluleke ngolo hlobo?`
