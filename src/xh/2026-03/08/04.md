---
title:  UKristu, Intlahlela
date:   18/08/2026
---

Ukuba uYesu ebengaphilanga, nakuphina ukulindela ungomso bekuzakuba lulahlekiso kuphela (1 Kor. 15:12–19). “UKristu yena kunje uvukile kwabafileyo” (1 Kor. 15:20). Ukuvuka kwaKhe sisiganeko esiyimbali. Ngenxa yoko, sinokuqiniseka ukuba bonke abafele kuKristu bazakuvuswa ekubuyeni kwaKhe (1 Kor. 15:20–23).

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 15:20-23. Kuthetha ukuthini ukuthi uYesu “uyintlahlela”?`

Ukuphela kwexesha langoku lobubi kuyakuphawulwa kukuvuka kwemizimba yabo bafele kuKristu (1 Kor. 15:24; IsiTyhi. 20:5, 6). NjengoAdam wokugqibela, uKristu uyakubuyisela ubukumkani kuBawo ngokuzisa ulawulo lweli hlabathi kuYe kwakhona (1 Kor. 15:25–28). Ukuzithobela kukaKristu ngokwaKhe kuThixo (1 Kor. 15:28) kufuneka kuqondwe ngokwendlela uAdam noKristu abazotywe ngayo ngokobudlelane bomnye komnye. NjengoAdam wokugqibela kwicebo losindiso (1 Kor. 15:45), uYesu uzithobela Yena ngokupheleleyo phantsi kwentando kaBawo, nto leyo uAdam wokuqala awasilelayo ukuyenza.

Kuma-1 Korinte 15:29-34, uPawulos uqala kwakho ingcamango yakhe ngobudenge bokuphika ukuvuka kukaKristu. Usebenzisa umfuziselo wobhaptizo kuba ngokunokwalo luluphawu lokumanyana kwekholwa kunye noKristu ekufeni nasekuvukeni kwaKhe (Rom. 6:3, 4; Kol. 2:12); ayincedi nto eyokuphika ubunyani bovuko. Ekunzima ukuyiqonda, ngoko, yintsingiselo yoko kwakuthethwa nguPawulos ngale ntetho “ababhaptizelwa abafileyo” (1 Kor. 15:29).

“Iingcamango ezohlukeneyo ziveziwe, kodwa kungcono ukutolika intetho njengesingisela kwisigqibo sabanye sokubhaptizwa ukuze bakwazi ukuhlangana kwakhona nababathandayo abangasekhoyo eluvukweni. Kusenokwenzeka kanjalo ukuba isigqibo sokubhaptizwa yayikukusabela kubomi obungumzekelo babo bafele kuKristu. Le imeko iyakusingisela kubantu abangabhaptizwanga esikhundleni sabo bafileyo koko ngenxa yabafileyo.”—Carl P. Cosaert, “1 Corinthians,” Andrews Bible Commentary: New Testament (Berrien Springs, MI: Andrews University Press, 2022), p. 1652.

Okwesibini, ukuba semngciphekweni ngenxa yokufa kuya kuba lilize ukuba bekungekho luvuko (1 Kor. 15:30–32). Bekuyakuba ngcono, endaweni yoko, ukonwabela iziyolo zehlabathi (1 Kor. 15:32).
