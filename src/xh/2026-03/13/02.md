---
title:  Ubabalo LukaYesu
date:   20/09/2026
---

Kuyakhuthaza ukuba ekupheleni kwama-2 Korinte, sibona kusalathwa kubabalo lukaYesu, njengasekuqaleni (2 Kor. 1:2, 2 Kor. 13:14). UPawulos uqala aqukumbele le ncwadi ngokubhekisa kubabalo lwaKhe. Njengoko sibonileyo ekuqalekeni kwale kota, akakwazanga kuyeka ukucinga nokuthetha ngoYesu.

“Kuba niyalwazi ubabalo lweNkosi yethu uYesu Kristu, okokuba yathi, ibisisityebi nje, yaba lihlwempu ngenxa yenu, ukuze nina nibe zizityebi ngobuhlwempu bayo obo” (2 Kor. 8:9).

Indlela oluncomeka nolulangazeleleka ngayo ubabalo lukaYesu. Waashiya ubutyebi bobukho baKhe obungunaphakade ezulwini ukuze abe lihlwempu. Waahamba kwiindlela ezingumqwebedu zeGalili yamandulo. “Wazithoba, waba ngolulamileyo, wada wesa ekufeni, ukufa ke kwasemnqamlezweni (Fil. 2:8). Wakwenza oko ukuze thina sibe “zizityebi,” oko kukuthi, ukuze sibe nethuba lokuba kunye naYe ezulwini. Kunzima kuthi, thina bathe kuphela bazi ihlabathi lesono, nokufa, nembandezelo, ukuba siqalise kwa ukuqonda ukuba kwakuthetha ukuthini kuYesu ukuba ashiye iinkundla zezulu ukuze eze pha aze anikele ubomi baKhe ngenxa yethu.

`Funda kumaRoma 16:20, Galati 6:18, Filipi 4:23, nakuma-1 Tesalonika 5:28. Mfundiso ni ebalulekileyo oyibonayo kwezi zicatshulwa?`

UPawulos ubhekisa kubabalo lukaYesu kaninzi kwiincwadi zakhe. Ezinye zeendyebo ziquka, “Isipho esingobabalo olu lwalo . . . uYesu Kristu, saphuphumela kubo abaninzi aba” (Rom. 5:15). Abo bamkela oku kuphuphuma kobabalo baya “kulawula ebomini ngaye . . . uYesu” (Rom. 5:17). Njengoko kwenzekile kuma-2 Korinte, uPawulos kanjalo uqala aqukumbele ezinye iincwadi ekhankanya ubabalo lukaYesu (Rom. 1:7, Rom. 16:20, 1 Kor. 1:3, 1 Kor. 16:23, Gal. 1:3, Gal. 6:18, Fil. 1:2, Fil. 4:23). Lo mxholo wawugubungele ingcinga yakhe, kwaye, kanjalo, wayefuna izalise ingcinga yamaKorinte.

Lo yayingumnqweno wakhe kuwo onke amabandla. Qaphela oko akuthethayo kumaEfese: “Ubabalo malube nabo bonke abayithandayo iNkosi yethu uYesu ngokungenakonakala” (Efe. 6:24). Ubengasinqwenela ukuba simthande uYesu ngaphantsi kunoko, uthando olungabuniyo? Ngokuqinisekileyo hayi. Kakade, umnqweno wakhe yayi kukuba ubabalo lukaYesu lufikelele “ebantwini ngakumbi nangakumbi abantu” (2 Korinte 4:15) kwaye lubanele nabo kwanjengokuba lwalunjalo nakuye (2 Korinte 12:9)

`Cinga ngobabalo lukaThixo ngakuwe ngolu hlobo. Ufanelwe yintoni ngo kohlobo lwezinto ozithethileyo nozenzileyo? Noko kunjalo, lukubonelela ngantoni ubabalo endaweni yoko?`
