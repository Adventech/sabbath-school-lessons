---
title:  UThixo Wethu Oziqu Zithathu
date:   24/09/2026
---

Xa efunda ama-2 Korinte 13:14, umntu angacinga ukuba uKristu kuphela komthombo wobabalo, uThixo kuphela komthombo wothando, kwanokuba uMoya oyiNgcwele kuphela komthombo wobudlelane, kodwa akukho nto ingenye inokogqitha inyaniso.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 1:3, 4, 9; 1 Korinte 10:16; 2 Korinte 1:2, 12; Roma 8:35; Roma 15:30; Galati 2:20; nakumaEfese 3:19. Ezi zicatshulwa zithini ngobabalo, uthando, nobudlelane ngokuphathelele kula malungu obuThixo amathathu?`

UYise, uNyana, noMoya oyiNgcwele basebenzisana kunye ngenxa yokusindiswa kwethu. Ubabalo, uthando, nobudlelane aziveli komnye kuphela koko kuBo bobathathu. Noko kunjalo, ngamnye kubo unomsebenzi waKhe kwimbali yosindiso. UPawulos uyayiqonda lo nto kwaye ugxininisa le mfundiso kwiincwadi zakhe. Umzekelo, icebo losindiso libaliswa ngendlela emangalisayo kusongiwa amazwi kumaGalati 4:4-6, ngenxaxheba eyohlukeneyo yamalungu amathathu obuThixo. UThixo uYise waathumela uYesu, nto leyo eveza ukuba uYise nguMthombo weloo cebo (Gal. 4:4). UNyana waazalwa ngumfazi (Gal. 4:4), nto leyo ebhekisa ekubeni nguThixo esenyameni kwaye yalatha ekuzalisekisweni kwedinga lamandulo (Gen. 3:15). UNyana wasikhulula waza wasibuyisela kubudlelsne obububo noYise, awaaxoka ngaye uSathana (Gen. 3:5). Kwaye uMoya oyiNgcwele wenza kube semthethweni ukubizwa kwethu njengabantwana

Zikhona ezinye izalathiso zooThixo oziqu zithathu kwiincwadi zikaPawulos. Esi sithathu sisebenza kunye, sisenza ukuba ibandla likwazi ukuvangela (1 Kor. 12:4–6), sisinika amandla akwamoya (Efe. 3:14–19), kwaye sikhuthaza ubunye obunzulu phakathi kwamalungu ebandla, obo bulinganisa ubunye obuphawula kanye ubudlelane bamalungu obuThixo (Efe. 4:4–6). Ekuqondeni kukaPawulos, uThixo akanaziqu zithathu kuphela, koko kanjalo iziQu ezithathu zobuThixo zisebenza kunye ngenxa yosindiso lwethu (Efe. 1:3, 13, 14). KumaEfese, uPawulos ude afikelele ekukhankanyeni ukuba masizaliswe yinzaliseko kaYise (Efe. 3:19), uNyana (Efe. 4:13), noMoya oyiNgcwele (Efe. 5:18).

Ekuqukumbeleni imbalelwano namaKorinte (2 Kor. 13:14), uPawulos akakwazanga kugqiba ngesiphelo esingcono-idinga lokuba aba baHlonipheki bathathu belimiweyo, isiThathu sezulu, siyakuba nathi ngoku nakwiphakade elizayo.

`Ubudlelane phakathi kwamalungu ebandla kumele ukububonakalisa njani ubudlelane obuthandekayo bobuThixo?`
