---
title:  UThixo Wothando
date:   22/09/2026
---

Kwihlabathi lamadulo lobuhedeni, abantu babengakholelwa ukuba oothixo bayabathanda abantu. Endaweni yoko, oothixo babekhohlakele benengcwangu, kwaye kwakufuneka baxoliswe. Ingcamango yoThixo wothando, njengoko sibona eBhayibhileni, yayi yinto entsha. Ikhwankqisa injalo le ntetho ngexesha lakhe, uPawulos ubalula uThixo wethu “njengoThixo wothando noxolo” (2 Kor. 13:11).

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 13:11. Ungalizuza njani ithemba koko kuthethwayo apha? Ungakungcamla ngcono njani oko le ndima ikufundisayo?`

Ibinzana “uThixo wothando noxolo” linokutolikwa ngeendlela ezimbini ezohlukeneyo. Kwicala elingelinye, uThixo ubonwa njengomthombo wothando noxolo. Kwelinye icala, uThixo uphawuleka ngothando noxolo. Akudingeki, ngoko, ukugqiba phakathi kwesi sibini. Kuba uthando noxolo ziimpawo zikaThixo ezikuye, Yena usinika uthando noxolo.

Kwenye indawo, uPawulos walatha kuThixo “njengoThixo wonyamezelo novuselelo” (Rom. 15:5); “wethemba” (Rom. 15:13); “woxolo” (Rom. 15:33, Rom. 16:20, 1 Kor. 14:33, Fil. 4:9, 1 Tesa. 5:23), “uSozimfefe” (2 Kor. 1:3), kwakunye “noThixo onako konke ukuthuthuzela” (2 Kor. 1:3). UThixo ungumthombo wazo zonke ezi ntsikelelo. Yena wasinika zona zonke ngokuqhutywa luthando lwaKhe olungapheliyo.

Kanjalo, nangona ibinzana “uThixo woxolo” ngahlobo luthile liqhelekile eBhayibhileni, ibinzana “uThixo wothando” livela apha kuphela (2 Kor. 13:11), kwaye, yiyo lo nto, lifanelwe yingqwalasela yethu enzulu.

Njengoko abahlalutyi abaninzi beqaphele, ukubhekisa kukaPawulos kuThixo wothando kwiindima ezimbalwa ngaphambi kofefe oluThixo uziqu zithathu kuma-2 Korinte 13:14 kubonisa ukuba ubona uThixo njengeziQu ezithathu. “Nangona esebenzisa igama “uThixo” njengomnye kwabathathu, ukuqonda kwakhe uYesu kwakunye noMoya kwezinye indawo kwiincwadi zakhe . . . kusinyanzela ukuba silibone lonke eli binzana njengelichaza uThixo omnye elathi ibandla lokuqala lafikelela ekumboneni kwizimo ezintathu. Kuyakuba semva kwenkulungwane ngaphambi kokuba abefundisi-buThixo . . . baqalise ukusebenzisa amagama afana ‘nothixo oziqu zithathu’ njengondlela-mfutshane wokuvakalisa oko uPawulos asele ekuthetha.”—Tom Wright, Paul for Everyone: 2 Corinthians (London: Society for Promoting Christian Knowledge, 2004), p. 148.

Sikholelwa kuThixo omnye, ubunye bezique ezithathu eziphila ngonaphakade kubudlelane bothando. Lo Thixo uziqu zithathu uyasithanda kwaye usibizela ekuthandeni omnye nomnye ngendlela ebonakalisa uthando oluphakathi kwaBo.
