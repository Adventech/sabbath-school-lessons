---
title:  Ubudlelane BukaMoya OyiNgcwele
date:   23/09/2026
---

Ubabalo lukaYesu alutyhili nje kuphela uthando lukaThixo kuthi koko lukwazisa ubudlelane boMoya njengesiphumo esingesinye solothando. Kwangaxesha linye, ubudlelane umthombo wabo ukuthando lukaThixo. Kwaye kungenxa yokuba ngaphandle kothando, akukho budlelane. UPawulos ubhala: “Ukuba ke ngoko kukho usizo kuKristu, ukuba kukho ukhuzo lothando, ukuba kukho ubudlelane boMoya, ukuba kukho imfesane neemfefe, luzaliseni uvuyo lwam, ukuze nicinge nto-nye, ninaluthando lunye, ninamphefumlo mnye, nicinga nto-nye” (Fil. 2:1, 2).

Abanye abantu bathi uMoya oyiNgcwele kuphela kwamandla okanye impembelelo, kodwa oko akunakuba yinyani. Ukanti ke, kutheni le nto uPawulos ezakukhankanya iziqu ezibini-uYise noNyana-kunye “namandla” nje kwinkqubo eqingqiweyo yeziqu ezithathu? Lo nto soze yaavakala. Kwanjengokuba uYise noNyana bebonakaliswa kubudlelane beziqu (2 Kor. 1:3, 2 Kor. 11:31), ubudlelane boMoya kunye neziqu bukhokelela ekuqukumbelene ngelithi naYe usisiqu (Rom. 8:15, 16; bona kanjalo kuYohane 14:16, 17, 26; Yohane 15:26).

Ibinzana “ubudlelane boMoya” (Fil. 2:1) linokuqondwa ngeendlela ezimbini. Linokuthetha ubudlelane bomnye nomnye obunikezelwa nguMoya, okanye ubudlelani kunye noMoya ngokwaKhe. Abapicothi beBhayibhile abaliqela baxoxa ngelithi ezinkalo ziyahambelana. Kakade, ubudlelane omnye nomnye kusisiphumo sobudlelane noMoya.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 2:10, 11; 1 Korinte 3:16; 1 Korinte 12:11; nakuma-2 Korinte 3:6, 17. Waafundisa ntoni uPawulos kumaKorinte ngoMoya?`

UPawulos unento eninzi yokuthetha ngomsebenzi woMoya. Kuma-1 naku-2 Korinte, zingaphezu kwamashuma amane izalathiso zikaMoya oyiNgcwele. UMoya oyiNgcwele ukhuthaza ukwakheka kwebandla (1 Kor. 14:12), ulungiselela abantu uvangelo (1 Kor. 2:4, 5), utyhila izinto ezinzulu zikaThixo kuthi (1 Kor. 2:10, 11) aze asifundise zona (1 Kor. 2:13), uhlala kuthi (1 Kor. 3:16, 1 Kor. 6:19), usebenzisana noKritsu ukuze sigwetyelwe (1 Kor. 6:11), unikezela ngezipho zakwamoya ebandleni (1 Korinte 12–14), usitywinela usindiso (2 Kor. 1:22), ubhala umthetho ezintliziyweni zabantu (2 Kor. 3:3), aze anikezele ngobomi obutsha kuKristu (2 Kor. 3:6) kwakunye nenkululeko esonweni (2 Kor. 3:17). Ngokuqinisekileyo, asinakuphila ngaphandle koMoya oyiNgcwele.

`Kutheni, kanjalo, ukuqondwa kobuthixo bukaMoya oyiNgcwele kubalulekile ukuze luqondwe ngokupheleleyo uthando lukaThixo kuthi?`
