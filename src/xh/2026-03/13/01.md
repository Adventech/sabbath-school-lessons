### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
2 Kor. 8:9; Roma 16:20; 1 Yohane 4:8–11; 2 Kor. 13:11; Fil. 2:1, 2; Gal. 4:4–6.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ubabalo lweNkosi uYesu Kristu, nothando lukaThixo, nodlelano loMoya oyiNgcwele, malube nani nonke”. (2 Korinte 13:14).

UPawulos uqukumbela ama-2 Korinte ngokugxiniisa kwakhona amabakala abalulekileyo ashukuxwe kwiincwadi zakhe. Uyenza le nto ngeziyalo ezihlanu (2 Kor. 13:11).

Isiyalo sokuqala, “Vuyani,” ukhumbula iindima zokuqala kwiincwadi.

Isiyalo sesibini, “zilungiseni, nigqibelele,” siguqula igama elinye lesiGrike (_katartizō_), elibonakala apha nakuma-1 Korinte 1:10.

Esesithathu, “Khuthazanani,” siqalisa kwakhona kuma-2 Korinte 1:3-7. UPawulos uqala aze aqukumbele incwadi yakhe yesibini ngenkuthazo. Sixhamla inkuthazo kaThixo ukuze sikhuthaze abanye (2 Kor. 1:4, 6).

Iziyalo zesine nezesihlanu, “Cingani nto-nye, zolani” (2 Kor. 13:11), zizimemelelo zobunye. Esi simo sovuyo, ukubuyiswa, inkuthazo, ubunye, noxolo yimeko yobukho bukaThixo, “uThixo wothando noxolo” (2 Kor. 13:11). Yimiphumela yokusebenza koThixo wethu oziqu zithathu entliziyweni yomntu (2 Kor. 13:14).

Ubabalo, uthando, nobudlelane zingumphumela womsebenzi woThixo oziqu zithathu kuthi. Ezi mpawu zintathu zobuKristu zikhuthaza isipho esiphawulwa bubukho bukaThixo.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Septemba 26._
