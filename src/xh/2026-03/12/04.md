---
title:  Abefundisi Ababubuxoki Bekhonjwa
date:   15/09/2026
---

ITestamente eNtsha iqulathe izilumkiso eziliqela ezichasene nabefundisi ababubuxoki kwiintlangano zobuKristu. UYesu ngokwaKhe wabalumkisa abafundi kwangale nto inye (Mat. 7:15–20). Abapostile nabo batsalela ingqalelo kulo nto (Gal. 1:6–9, 1 Tim. 6:3–5, 2 Pet. 2:1–3).

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 11:1-15. UPawulos uyizoba njani imingeni ajamelene nayo kwababefundisi bobuxoki?`

UPawulos uhluba umsebenzi wababefundisi bobuxoki. Kwangaxesha linye, uyabonisa ukuba ulungiselelo lwakhe lungoKristu. Ufanisa ibandla eliseKorinte nomtshakazi aze yena azichaze njengotata wakhe, onoxanduva lokumnikezela kuKristu (2 Kor. 11:2). Ukwenza oko kuba elithanda ibandla (2 Kor. 11:11). Ngenxa yoko, wayekulungele nkqu nokungabi ngumthwalo kulo ngokwasezimalini, nangona wayenalo ilungelo lokuxhaswa lilo (2 Kor. 11:7–12).

Kwelinye icala, “abona bapostile baziwayo” (oku kungenzeka kusingisele kubefundisi ababubuxoki ngeliphoxayo) bafaniswa nenyoka eyalukuhla uEva (2 Kor. 11:3). NjengoSathana eMyezweni wase-Eden,abefundisi ababubuxoki eKorinte babonakala ngenkohliso nokungcola (2 Kor. 11:3, 4). Ixhala elisemqoka likaPawulos yayikukuba babenokulahlekisa amaKorinte emke kukuzinikela kwawo okusulungekileyo nokuzibophelela kwawo kuKristu.

Oogqada-mbekweni babeshumayela umyalezo owahlukileyo kulowo uPawulos wayewushumayela-uYesu ongomnye kwakunye negospile engenye (2 Kor. 11:4). Oku kubonisa ukuba ayinguye wonke umntu oshumayela uYesu osisixhobo esithunywe nguThixo. Kulo mba, uYesu ngokwaKhe wathi, “ ‘Asingabo bonke abathi kum, Nkosi, Nkosi, abaya kungena ebukumkanini bamazulu; ngokwenzayo ukuthanda kukaBawo osemazulwini’ ” (Mat. 7:21). KumaGalati 1:6-9, uPawulos uthi nabani na oshumayela igospile engenye uzisa isiqalekiso kuye, kodwa ke abanye eKorinte balunyamezela olu hlobo lwemposiso.

UPawulo ubhenca abapostile bobuxoki ngokuthi “ngabasebenzi abakhohlisayo, bezimisa okwabapostile bakaKristu” (2 Kor. 11:13). Bona bazimilisa okwabapostile bakaKristu, kwanjengokuba “uSathana ezimilisa okwesithunywa sokukhanya” yaye “abalungiseleli bakhe [bakaSathana] bazimilise okwabalungiseleli bobulungisa (2 Kor. 11:14, 15). Imeko embi ngolo hlobo: abazibiza njengabalungiseleli bakaKristu basebenze njengabalungiseleli bakaSathana. UPawulos uqukumbela le ngcamango ngokuthi esabo “isiphelo siya kuba ngokwemisebenzi yabo” (2 Kor. 11:15).

Qwalasela indlela asabela ngayo ngokumandla kulahlekiso ebandleni! Kanjalo, oko kumele ukuthini kuthi?
