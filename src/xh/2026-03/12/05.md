---
title:  Iimbandezelo Ngenxa Yegospile
date:   16/09/2026
---

Emva kokubhenca abefundisi ababubuxoki njengezenzeleli zikaSathana (2 Cor. 11:1–15), uPawulos ngoku “udlala” umdlalo wabo ngokuqhayisa kancinci njengoswele ukuqonda (2 Kor. 11:16–21) ukuze amaKorinte abone indlela eyayingenamsebenzi ngayo into yokuphulaphula intetho yabefundisi bobuxoki. Ukuba ama-Korinte ayebathathele phezulu, uPawulos wayefanelwe ukujongelwa phezulwana. Iimbandezelo zakhe ngenxa yegospile ziyabonisa ukuba wayesisicaka esithembekileyo sikaKristu (2 Kor. 11:22, 23).

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 11:22-28. Ngongomani ayivezayo apha?`

Lo gama amagama kaPawulos obuYuda efana nalawo abefundisi bobuxoki (2 Kor. 11:22), ukusebenzela kwakhe uKristu kuyakogqitha okwabo (2 Kor. 11:23). “Bangabalungiseleli bakaKristu na?” uyabuza. Impendulo ithi “Ndingongaphezulu mna.” Imisebenzi yakhe yayigqithisile; ukuba sezintolongweni kwakhe, kwakuxhaphakile; ukubethwa kwakhe,Kwakuqatha ngakumbi.

Kodwa oku ayikuko konke. Uluhlu lwakhe lweembandezelo kanjalo luquka iziganeko ezihlanu zemivumbo engamashumi amathathu anesithoba (2 Kor. 11:24), ukubethwa ngeentonga, ukuxulutywa ngamatye, ukwaphukelwa ngumkhombe, ingozi emanzini anzulu (2 Kor. 11:25), ingozi kwiihambo, ingozi zemilambo, ingozi zezihange, ingozi zamakowethu, ingozi kwabeeNtlanga, ingozi phakathi komzi, ingozi entlango, ingozi elwandle, ingozi phakathi kwabazalwana ababubuxoki (2 Kor. 11:26), imisebenzi enzima, ukubulaleka okudinayo, ukungabi nakulala, ukulamba, ukunxanwa, ukushokoxeka kokutya, ingqele, ubuze (2 Kor. 11:27). Ngokungathi akwanele, kwakusafuneka ejongene nokubandezeleka engqondweni ngenxa yenkxalabo yakhe enzulu ngenxa yamabandla (2 Kor. 11:28).

Kuphela ngumkhonzi wokwenene kaKristu onokukulungela ukubulaleka ngolu hlobo ngenxa yegospile. Ukuba uPawulos ngokwenene wayenokuqhayisa ngeembandezelo zakhe, kuninzi awayenokukuthetha. Noko kunjalo, isigaba esilandelayo sencwadi sibonisa ukuba isizathu sokuqhayisa kwakhe sasisekelezelwe hayi koko yena wayekwenzele uKristu kodwa koko uKristu wayekwenzele yena. UPawulos wayesazi ukuba amandla kaThixo adandalaziswa ngokuphandle kubuthathaka bomntu (2 Kor. 12:9, 10). Ngokumnika uviko enyameni (2 Kor. 12:7), uThixo wayekhusela uPawulos ekuqhayiseni ngeempumelelo zakhe. Oku kwamgcina ethobekile, ebuqonda ubuthathaka bakhe, exhomekeke kumandla angcwele, yaye ekwimeko yokuxhamla ngakumbi kubabalo lukaThixo nenceba.

`Ukhe nawe wabulaleka ngenxa yegospile? Ufunde ntoni kula mava? Indlela uPawulos awaqubisana ngayo neembandezelo zakhe ingakunceda njani ekuqubisaneni nezakho?`
