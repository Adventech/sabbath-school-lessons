---
title:  Isiyalo Kongaguqukanga
date:   17/09/2026
---

Kuma-2 Korinte 12:14-13:10, uPawulos uxelela ibandla ngotyelelo lwakhe lwesithathu (2 Kor. 12:14, 2 Kor. 13:1). Ubonakalisile ukuba akekho ngaphantsi kuye nawuphi kwababapostile bobuxoki kwaye ngoku uqinisekile ngokuza eKorinte kwakhona aze azame ukubuyisela amalungu angaguqukanga. Eneneni, le yayiyenye yeenjongo eziphambili kutyelelo lwakhe. Konke awakwenzayo nawakuthethayo uPawulos kwakulungiselelwe ukwakheka kwebandla (2 Kor. 12:19).

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 12:20, 21. Zeziphi izono ezazibeka emngciphekweni imeko yakwamoya yebandla laseKorinte?`

Uludwe lwezono ezikuma-2 Korinte 12:20, 21 luyafana nezinye ezifumaneka kwezinye iincwadi zikaPawulos (Rom. 1:29–31, Gal. 5:19–21). Izinto zokuqala ezimbini zibonakala kuma-1 Korinte 3:3, apho uPawulos asingisela kumona nembambano phakathi kwamalungu aseKorinte. UPawulos woyikisela ukuba izinto asoze zaahluka kakhulu ngexesha lotyelelo lwakhe lwesibini. Yena uthi, “Kuba ndiyoyika, hleze ndithi ndakufika, ndinifumane ningenjengendikuthandayo.” Kwelinye icala, uthi, “Ninokungandifumani hayi ngokwendlela ebeninqwena ngayo” (2 Kor. 12:20). Oku kuthetha ukuthi endaweni yokuqubisana nabo “ngobulali nokuthantamisa kukaKristu” (2 Kor. 10:1), yena ngoku “uzakulungela ukohlwaya konke ukungathobeli (2 Kor. 10:6).

Eyona nkxalabo yakhe kukuba loo malungu abandakanyeka “ekungcoleni, kumbulo, naseburheletyweni” kunokwenzeka awakaguquki (2 Kor. 12:21). Kwaye zizono ezifana nezi ezibangela iiyantlukwano ebandleni

Okulandelayo, uPawulos ugxila kwindima yokohlwaya kwebandla ukubuyisa abo basesonweni (2 Kor. 13:1–4). Ubuthathaka ayisosizathu sakuzithethelela ngenxa yobomi besono. Kukho amandla afumanekayo kwabo bafuna ukuphila ubomi bokweyisa (2 Kor. 13:4). Into yokuba abanye eKorinte babezibandakanya kwisono sezesondo bubungqina bokuba amandla kaThixo ayengeyonyani ebomi babo. UPawulos wayefuna baguquke baze bangcamle amandla akhokelela kwintobelo. Ukubohlwaya yinto yokugqibela awayenqwenela ukuyenza. Yena uthi, “Ndithandaza ke kuye uThixo, ukuba ningenzi nanye into embi . . ., kodwa nenze into entle nina. . . . Ukubuyiswa kwenu yeyona nto siyithandazelayo” (2 Kor. 13:7–9). Umthandazo omhle ngolo hlobo! Uyabacela ukuba bazigocagoce ukuba ingaba baselukhokweni na.
