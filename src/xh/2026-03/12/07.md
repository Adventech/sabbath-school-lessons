Ellen G. White, “The Laodicean Church,” p. 125, kwiThe Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, Septemba 30, 1873.

“INkosi ibalondolozile abantu bayo ngasekuphindaphindeni iziphoso neempazamo zexa elidlulileyo. Kusoloko kuxhapheke abefundisi ababubuxoki abathe, ngokuxhasa iimfundiso ezibubuxoki nezenzo ezingengcwele, kwaye besebenza ngemimiselo ebubuxoki ngohlobo olulahlekisayo, olugqunyiweyo, olulukuhlayo, abazame ukulahlekisa, ukuba konokwenzeka, abo kanye bonyuliweyo.”—Ellen G. White, in The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, January 7, 1904.

“INkosi imisele ukuba iingcamango zethu zivavanywe, ukuze siyibone imfuneko yokuqwalasela kabukhali iinyaniso eziphilayo sikwazi ukubona ukuba siseselukholweni na okanye akunjalo kusini na. Abaninzi ababanga ukuba bayakholelwa kwinyaniso baye bakholiseke besithi, “ndisisityebi, ndizityebisile, andiswele nto.” Ellen G.White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 36.

“Abantu bahoya iimposiso, elo xesha inyaniso idandalazisiwe; kwaye ukuba bebenokuzizisa iimfundiso zabo elizwini likaThixo, kwaye bangafundi ilizwi likaThixo bejonge kwiimfundiso zabo, ukubona ukuba iingcamango zabo zizizo, bebengasayi kuhamba esithokothokweni nasebumfameni, okanye baxabise ubuxoki. Uninzi lunika amagama esiBhalo intsingiselo elungele iingcamango zabo, baze balahlekise iziqu zabo bakhohlise nabanye ngamagqwidigqwidi abo elizwini likaThixo. Xa siqalisa ukufunda ilizwi likaThixo, kufuneka sikwenze oko ngeentliziyo ezithobekileyo. Konke ukuzicingela, konke ukuthanda imvelaphi, makubekelwe bucala. Iingcamango ekutshelwe kuzo mazingathathwa ngoba azinasiphoso.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, pp. 36, 37.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`IBhayibhile ithi ngaphambi kokufika kwesiphelo, intlanginge yabefundisi ababubuxoki bayakuzama ukurhwebesha abantu basuke enyanisweni. Yintoni enokwenziwa libandla okulo ukuthintela amalungu ekuphenjelelweni ngabafundisi ababubuxoki abasenokuba kanti bakweli bandla ningena kulo? Kutheni le nto oku kubalulekile ekufezekisweni kwephulo lebandla?`

`Kwatheni ukuze uPawulos akubone kuyimfuneko ukuqhayisa ngomntyangampo woluhlu lweembandezelo (2 Kor. 11:16–33)? Kanjalo, kuthetha ukuthini “ukuqhayisa ngeNKosi”?`

`Kutheni le nto kubalulekile kumalungu ebandla ukuba azibhence ukuze abone ukuba aselukholweni na (2 Kor. 13:5)? Mehluko mni owenziwa kuko?`
