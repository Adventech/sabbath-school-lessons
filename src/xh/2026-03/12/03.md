---
title:  Ukuqhayisa ngeNkosi
date:   14/09/2026
---

Izolo, sibonile ukuba uPawulos kwakunye noogxa bakhe balusebenzisa ulungiselelo njengedabi lakwamoya, yaye bakwenza oko besebenzisa izixhobo zikaThixo. Namhlanje, sizakubona ukuba abafundisi ababubuxoki benza ngokwendlela yabantu. Baqhayisa ngokungafanelekanga. Kwelinye icala, uPawulos uqhayisa ngeNkosi. Njengoko yena wabhalayo: “Kodwa ‘oqhayisayo ke, makaqhayise ngeNkosi’ ” (2 Kor. 10:17).

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 10:13-17. Ungakwazi njani umoya wokukhuphisana ukulimaza ukushunyayelwa kwegospile?`

Ukusebenzisa kukaPawulos intetho yokuqhayisa ngesiqu itsale umdla kubatoliki kwiinkulungwane. Noko kunjalo, ukuqhayisa ngesiqu yayisisenzo esiqhelekileyo kwihlabathi lamandulo kwaye kwakulawulwa zizivumelwano zokuhlala ukuphepha ukukhutyekiswa kwabaphulaphuli. UPawulos wayezazi ezo zivumelwano, kwaye wayezilandela. Ukongeza, uPawulos uyenza icace eyokuba indlela yakhe yokuqhayisa iyazahlula kuleyo yabafundisi ababubuxoki. Yena uqhayisa ngeNkosi (2 Kor. 10:17). Esi sisicatshulwa esivela kwiTestamente eNdala: “ ‘Oqhayisayo makaqhayise ngale nto yokuba endiqiqa, endazi mna ukuba ndinguYehova, owenza inceba, nesiko, nobulungisa, ehlabathini’ ” (Yer. 9:24). Ngokucaphula esi sicatshulwa kuYeremiya, uPawulos uyabonisa ukuba nguKristu osesithatheni-uthando lukaKristu, okusesikweni, nobulungisa.

Ngamanye amazwi, ukuqhayisa kukaPawulos kugxile kwizinto ezifezekiswe nguThixo ngoKristu. Ngalo ndlela, ukuqhayisa kwakhe kokweBhayibhile kwaye, yiyo lo nto, kungakhubekisi. Kwelinye icala, abachasi bakhe bafika kwimeko yokukhuphisana ngokuzithelekisa omnye komnye. Obu bubudenge! (2 Kor. 10:12).

Kuma-2 Korinte 10:14-16, uPawulos untluvisa ngelithi ukushumayela igospile yeyona nto isemqoka kulungiselelo lwakhe, ngokufanayo eKorinte nakwimimandla engaphaya kwaseKorinte. Uthando lukaPawulos ngakuYesu lwamkhokelela ekuthetheni rhoqo ngeendaba ezilungileyo zosindiso, ezifumaneka ekufeni nasekuvukeni kukaKristu.

Ngokungafaniyo nabafundisi ababubuxoki eKorinte ababezincoma ngokunokwabo, uPawulos unconywe wangqinwa nguThixo (2 Kor. 10:12, 18). Yena “wabizelwa ebupostileni ngokokuthanda kukaThixo” (1 Kor. 1:1). Yena wathembeka kubizo lwakhe de kwasekupheleni kobomi bakhe (2 Tim. 4:7).

`Funda kwakhona ama-2 Korinte 10:12-18. Zingakwazi njani iinkokeli zebandla, okanye nkqu namalungu, ukuphepha umoya wokhuphiswano? Kutheni le nto kulula kangaka ukubanjwa zizinto ezingenamsebenzi?`
