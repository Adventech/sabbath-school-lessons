---
title:  Idabi Lakwamoya
date:   13/09/2026
---

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 10:1-11. Ubulali bukaPawulos ekuqubisaneni kwakhe namaKorinte ngamanye amaxesha babubhidaniswa nobubhetyebhetye. Ngawaphi amazwi okanye amabinzana kwesi sicatshulwa atyhila isibindi sikaPawulos sokuqubisana nengxaki yabefundisi ababubuxoki eKorinte?`

UPawulos uqala ama-2 Korinte 10 kakhulu ngokobuqu: “Mna Pawulos ngokwam, ndiyaniyala” (2 Kor. 10:1). Oku kubonisa indlela awayexhalabe ngayo uPawulos ngeemfundiso ezibubuxoki ezazithubeleza ebandleni. Amazwi akhe kuma-2 Korinte 10:1 ngokuyimpoxa asingisele kwisimangalo sabachasi bakhe sokuba yena ungumgrogrisa owoyikekayo xa ebhala iincwadi ekude ajike abe ligwala elilusizi xa ejongene nabantu ubuso ngobuso (2 Kor. 10:10, 11). Uphendula ngelithi oko kukhangeleke njengobuthathaka makubonwe njengobulali obumandla kwakunye nokuthantamisa ngokobuKristu.

Abefundisi ababuxoki kufuneka kuqutyiswane nabo ngobugagu nengqiniseko (2 Kor. 10:2) kodwa zidityaniswe nokuthantamisa kukaKristu (2 Kor. 10:1). UYesu wakha wathi, “Ndinobulali, ndithobekile ngentliziyo” (Mat. 11:29). Noko kunjalo, uYesu ngobugagu waqubisana nabananisi-mali etempileni ngokuphequla iitafile zabo ebabiza izihange (Mat. 21:12, 13). Kanjalo waxelela abaFarisi ukuba ngabahanahanisi namangcwaba aqatywe mhlophe ngaphandle kanye ezinkonkqeni (Mat. 23:23–27). NjengoYesu, uPawulos naye uyazi ukuba sikwimfazwe yakwamoya efuna kusetyenziswe sonke isikrweqe sikaThixo (Efe. 6:12–17).

Intetho kaPawulos kuma-2 Korinte 10 yeyasemkhosini ngenxa yokuba ubomi busemngciphekweni (2 Kor. 10:3–6). Le asilongquzulwano nje lwasenyameni koko idabi elingcwele lokuzuzela abantu kuKristu. Kuloo meko, yonke ingxoxo ebubuxoki kwakunye nengcamango ekhukhumeleyo makuqutyiswane nayo kwaye idilizwe, ngokusekelezelwe eLizwini likaThixo, ukuze yonke ingcamango iziswe ekuthinjweni “yenziwe ilulamele uKristu” (2 Kor. 10:5).

Kule mfazwe yakwamoya, uPawulos usebenza kwigunya likaKristu. Eli gunya, noko kunjalo, lijolise ekwakheni, hayi ekuchitheni (2 Kor. 10:8). Kulula kwiinkokeli zakwamoya ukucinezela ukuba zisebenza ngokwegunya likaThixo. Noko kunjalo, kufuneka zikhumbule ukuba igunya lazo linikezelwe nguKristu, yaye, njengaYe, kufuneka zibe nobulali zithobeke entliziyweni. Ibango likaPawulos ligunya lakhe alinikwe nguKristu linento yokwenza nenkxalabo yakhe yokuba amaKorinte aphulaphula abantu abangafanelekanga, baze ngokwenza oko babeke emngciphekweni ukunyaniseka kwabo kuKristu.

`Singakwazi njani ngaxeshanye ukuba nobulali nobugagu xa siqubisana nabefundisi ababubuxoki? Kutheni thina kufuneka sibe zizo zombini?`
