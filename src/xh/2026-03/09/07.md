Ellen G. White, “The Message Heeded,” pp. 323–334, kwiThe Acts of the Apostles.

“Abo bakhe bathwala ezona ntsizi zibuhlungu basoloko ingabona bathwala eyona ntuthuzelo ukusa kwabanye, besizisa ukubengezela kwelanga naphi na apho bahamba khona. Abo banjalo bathwaxiwe bathanjiswa ziimbandezelo; abazange bayilahle ingqiniseko yabo kuThixo xa inkathazo ibibazungula, kodwa banamathela ngokusondeleyo kuthando lwaKhe olukhuselayo. Abo banjalo babubungqina obuphilayo ngokukokosa kukaThixo.”—Ellen G. White, God’s Amazing Grace, p. 122.

Ubomi bobuKristu obuzinikeleyo busoloko buzisa ukukhanya nentuthuzelo noxolo. Bubonakala ngobunyulu, ukuhlakanipha, ukusulungeka, nentsebenzo. Bulawulwa lolothando lungazicingeliyo olungcwalisa impembelelo. Buzaliswe nguKristu, kwaye bushiya umzila wokukhanya naphina apho ahamba khona umntu onabo.”—God’s Amazing Grace, p. 122.

“Umpostile uPawulos kwakuyimfunekho ukukhalimela ububi ebandleni, kodwa akazange aphulukane nokuzilawula ekukhalimeleni isiphoso. Usichaza ngenyameko isizathu sesenzo sakhe. Indlela awasebenza ngayo ngobunono ukuze ashiye ingcamango ethi yena ungumhlobo wabagqwidizayo! Wabenza baqonda ukuba kumenze buhlungu ukubenza kwakhe babe buhlungu. Waashiya ingcamango ezingqondweni zabo yokuba umdla wakhe uhambelana nowabo.”—Ellen G. White Comments, in The SDA Bible Commentary, vol. 6, p. 1094.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Kuma-2 Korinte 2:1-14, uPawulos uqinisekisa ;intembeko yakhe kulungiselelo. Kutheni le nto olu phawu lobulungiseleli lubaluleke ngolo hlobo?`

`Into yokuba uPawulos waatshintsha isicwangciso sakhe sokuhamba isixelela ntoni ngemfuneko yokumvoco (ukuthamba) kulungiselelo lobuKristu? Kutheni le nto kubalulekile ukulungela utshintsho xa kudingeka?`

`UPawulos wayejamelene nokubandezeleka nenkxalabo kulungiselelo lwakhe. Oku ngokucacileyo kubonisa ukuba iinkokeli zebandla zingabantu abasesichengeni soxinzelelo njengabo bonke abantu. Yintoni enokwenziwa ngamalungu ebandla ukuthambisa umsebenzi wabo?`

`UPawulos ubhekisa kukungonwabi kwakhe (2 Kor. 2:13) kanye ngaphambi kokuba akhankanye uloyiso lwakhe kuKristu (2 Kor. 2:14). Wenza njani ukuba athethe ngobuthathaka nokomelela kwakhe ngaxesha linye? Singenza njani?`
