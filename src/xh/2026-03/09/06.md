title:  Uloyiso KuKristu
date:   27/08/2026
---

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 2:12, 13. Waaya phi uPawulos emva kokubabhalela “le ncwadi ibuhlungu?” Weenzani apho?`

Intliziyo kaPawulos yayingonwabanga lo gama elinde uTito (2 Kor. 7:5, 6). Phakathi kokokungonwabi, akazange akwazi ukuyeka ukuthetha ngoYesu (2 Kor. 2:12). Wayemthanda uYesu kakhulu. Wayengxamele ukubona uTito azokuva ngendlela asabele ngayo amaKorinte.

Umsebenzi kaPawulos eTrowa waaba yimpumelelo, kodwa “Inkathalo yawo onke amabandla,’ ngokukodwa eyebandla laseKorinte, yayisinda entliziyweni yakhe. Wayenethemba lokuhlangana noTito eTrowa aze eve kuye ngendlela amkelwe ngayo amazwi engcebiso nawesohlwayo ebewathumele kubazalwana baseKorinte, kodwa koku waaphoxeka. ‘Andibanga nakonwaba emoyeni wam, ngokungamfumani uTito umzalwana wam.’ Ngenxa yoko wayishiya iTrowa wahamba waya eMakedoni, apho, eFilipi waahlangana noTimoti.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 323.

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 2:14-17. Kwaba yintoni ukusabela kukaPawulos ekudibaneni kwakhe noTito eMakedoni nasekuveni impendulo emnandi yamaKorinte?`

Ngokugqabhuka luvuyo, uPawulos uqinisekisa ukuba uThixo “usoloko esikhokelela eloyisweni sikuKristu” (2 Kor. 2:14). Intetho emangalisa ngolo hlobo! Intliziyo ezaliswe bubukho bukaKristu isasaza “ivumba lokwaziwa kwaKhe ndaweni zonke” (2 Kor. 2:14).

UPawulos uvuya kuKristu kuba laa ncwadi ibuhlungu yaaxakatha iziqhamo awayenenjongo zokuzivuna (2 Kor. 7:5–9). Olu luloyiso olukhulu. Noko kunjalo, kuma-2 Korinte 2:17 uPawulos ubethelela ukunyaniseka kwakhe njengompostile kaKristu (2 Kor. 2:17, 2 Kor. 1:12). Ngokwesisicatshulwa, into eyahlula umkhonzi othembekileyo kaKristu kumlungiseleli wobuxoki kukuba lo gama lo wamva ehambisa igospile kuba enyeke inzuzo, lo wokuqala ushumayela iLizwi likaThixo ngothando oluphelelise entliziyweni ngenxa kaKristu.
