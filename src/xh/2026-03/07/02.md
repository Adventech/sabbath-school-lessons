---
title:  Ukubaluleka Kothando
date:   09/08/2026
---

Kwiveki ephelileyo sithethe ngomxholo wothando, njengoko lubonakala kuma-1 Korinte 13. Sidinga ukuphonononga amazwi kaPawulos apha ngokunzulu ngakumbi.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 13. Shwankathela oko akuthethayo ngothando.`

UPawulos akathi iilwimi (1 Kor. 13:1), ukuprofeta, ukuqonda, ulwazi, ukholo (1 Kor. 13:2), nesisa azinamsebenzi (1 Kor. 13:3). Azinamsebenzi kuphela xa zingaqhutywa luthando.

Uhlobo lothando uPawulos athetha ngalo alubonakaliswa kwizivakalisi ezifana nezithi, “Ndiyawathanda amaqunube” okanye “Ndiyabathanda abahlobo bam” okanye nkqu “Ndiyamthanda umlingane wam nabantwana.” Akathethi kanjalo ngohlobo lothando umntu alubona kwiibhanyabhanya. Kwaye hayi, asilothando lwenkanuko, nangona esi sicatshulwa sivamise ukusetyenziswa kwiintshumayelo zemitshato.

Olu thando alukathotyelwa imvakalo yobubele nemfesane, okanye ukulunga, okanye isisa. Noko kunjalo, zonke ziyalubonakalisa kwinqanaba elingezantsi okanye elikhulu. Olu thando sisibabalo esisodwa esithiwa jize kuthi nguMoya. Ngokwenene, uthando kuma-1 Korinte 13 yinkuthazo enikezelwe nguMoya esikhokelela ekuthini senze ngemvakalelo yobubele nemfesane, ukulunga,nesisa. Kukuzibophelela okupheleleyo kwezenzo zethu, iimvakalelo, neengcinga ngakuKristu nabamelwane bethu.

`Funda kuMateyu 24:12. Silumkiso sini uYesu asinika sona apha?`

Yiyo lo nto _agapē_ ebaluleke ngolo hlobo kwaye edingeka. Ngamandla kaKristu, asinako ukuyeka uthando lubande ceke emakhayeni ethu, emabandleni, nasebumelwaneni. Sinomzekelo kaKristu emnqamlezweni, efela thina. Kokuphi okunye ukubonakaliswa okungcono nokunamandla ngakumbi koluhlobo lothando okunokubakhona? Nangona kakade singenakuze silubonakalisa ngokulinganayo olo hlobo lothando, ngobabalo lukaThixo kufuneka sizabalazele ukulutyhila ebomini bethu ukuya kuma kwinqaba esinokufikelela kulo.

`Ngawaphi amanye amaxesha xa, ngokwenene, ukubonakalisa olu hlobo lothando kwakunokushiya umfanekiso obukekayo engqondweni yomntu owayedinga olu hlobo lothando ngaphezu kwayo yonke enye into?`
