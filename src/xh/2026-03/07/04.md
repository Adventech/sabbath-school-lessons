---
title:  Oko Uthando Lungakwenziyo
date:   11/08/2026
---

`Funda kwakhona kuma-1 Korinte 13:4-7. Kutheni le nto uPawulos ekhankanya iimpawu ezimbi zothando endaweni yezintle kuphela?`

Izolo, sigxile kwizinto ezisixhenxe oluzenzayo uthando; namhlanje, sizakuqwalasela izinto ezisibhozo olungazenziyo. Uthando . . .

**1. Alunamona (_zeloō_).** _UZeloō_ unokusetyenziswa kakuhle “njengokulangazelela [_zeloō_] izipho ezisemanqwanqweni (1 Kor. 12:31), ukulangazelela [_zeloō_] izipho zakwamoya (1 Kor. 14:1), kwakunye “nokulangazelela [_zeloō_] ukuprofeta (1 Kor. 14:39). Apha, ngoko, njengakwiZenzo 7:9, kubi. Kulungile ukulangazelela izipho zakwamoya, kodwa hayi ukumonela abantu abanesiphiwo. Oku kwenza iyantlukwano (1 Kor. 3:3).

**2. Aluzigwagwisi (_perpereuomai_).** Isenzi _uperpereuomai_ uveza ingcamango yokukratsha nokunqwenela ukudunyiswa ngabanye. Uthando, ngoko, alujongi kumna ngolo hlobo. Oko kucace ngakumbi koku kulandelayo.

**3. Alukhukhumali (_physioō_).** Isenzi _uphysioō_ sivela kuma-1 Korinte 8:1 kwintetho kaPawulos emangalisayo, “ ‘Ukwazi oku kuyakhukhumalisa, ke lona uthando luyakha.” Isingisele emntwini ozaliswe kukuzingca.

**4. Aluziphathi krwada (_aschēmoneo_).** Isenzi _uaschēmoneo_ sinokuba neentsingisile ezahlukileyo eziliqela. Kakade, ngoko, sithetha ukwenza ngokunxamnye nemigangatho yokuhlala nokuziphatha ngendlela engenambeko, elihlazo, engeyiyo, okanye engafanelekanga. Kungenzeka, uPawulos usingisele kukuziphatha okukhukhumeleyo nokukrwada kwalowo “womeleleyo” ngakuloomalungu “uswele amandla” eKorinte (1 Kor. 4:10, 1 Corinthians 8).

**5. Alufuni (_zēteo_) okukokwalo [_amalungelo_].** Oku kunye noko uPawulos akuthetha kuma-1 Korinte 10:24: “Makungabikho namnye ufuna okukokwakhe ukulungelwa; elowo makafune nokwabanye” (inguqulelo yombhali). Uthando luncama amalungelo walo ngenxa yabanye (bona iSifundo 5). Kwimeko apho wonke ubani efuna amalungelo omnye, bonke bayaxhamla.

**6. Alucaphuki msinya (_paroxynō_).** Isenzi _uparoxynō_ sibonisa imeko yokuxhokonxwa ngaphakathi eyalatha kumntu ocatshukiswa ngokulula abe nomsindo. Oku kuthetha ukuba uthando alunamsind’ usempumlweni okanye ocaphuka msinya.

**7. Alugcini zingxelo (_logizomai_) zeziphoso.** Isenzi _ulogizomai_ sinesimo sengxelo apha, oko kukuthi uthando aluziqwalaseli iziphoso zomnye. Ngamanye amazwi, uthando lukwathetha ukuxolela.

**8. Aluvuyeli (_chairō_) kungalungisi.** Uthando aluyigcini ingxelo yeziphoso zomntu qha koko aluzivuyeli. Xa sibathanda ngokwenene abanye, asizivuyeli iimpazamo zabo koko, sizama ukubanceda.
