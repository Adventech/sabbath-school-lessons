---
title:  Isipho Sobuprofeti
date:   06/08/2026
---

Isipho sokuprofeta sithathe indawo ephambili kwingxoxo kaPawulos ngezipho zakwamoya. Ngokunika umdla, isipho sokuprofeta sivamise ukukhankanywa ngaphambi kwesipho seelwimi (1 Kor. 12:10, 28; 1 Kor. 13:8). Xa isipho seelwimi sikhankanywa kuqala, oku kungokugxininisa ukungabaluleki noko xa sithelekiswe kwisipho sobuprofeti (1 Kor. 14:4, 5, 6, 22).

`Funda amaEfese 4:11-13 nama-1 Korinte 14:3, 4. Ezi zicatshulwa zithini ngenjongo yezipho zakwamoya gabalala kwakunye nesipho sokuprofeta ngokuthe ngqo?`

Isipho sokuprofeta silungiselelwe ukubonelela ukwakhiwa, uyalo, nentuthuzelo (1 Kor. 14:3; thelekisa neZenzo 15:32). Oku kubonisa ukuba ukuprofeta akunanto inkulu ngokuchaza ixa elizayo njengoko kunento yokwenza nendlela yokuphila kwixesha langoku. Isenzi sesiGrike _uprophēteuō_ sinokuthetha enye yezi “ukuchaza into kwangaphambili” okanye “ukuchaza into esikhundleni somnye umntu.” Umzekelo, intsingiselo yokuqala ibonwa kwiZenzo 2:29-31 (thelekisa noAmos 3:7), nalapho ingcamango yokuba uDavide ungumprofeti ichazwa khona “njengokubona ngenxa engaphambili.” Intsingiselo yesibini ibonwa kwiZenzo 15:32, apho uYuda noSilas bachazwa njengabaprofeti. Esabo “isiprofeto,” noko kunjalo, sasibandakanya ukukhuthaza nokomeleza “abazalwana ngamazwi amaninzi.”

Ukusuka kumaEfese 4:11-13, sifunda ukuba izipho zakwamoya aziphelanga ngexesha labapostile. Kufuneka zihlale zikho kude kube sekupheleni (Zenzo 2:39). Noko kunjalo, ukuba ubani uzibiza ngokuba ungumprofeti, lowo kufuneka aphononongwe ngokweziBhalo. Ukuthetha ngokubanzi, imigaqo emine kufuneka yaneliseke. Okokuqala, iziprofeto ziyenzeka (Dut. 18:22; Yer. 28:8, 9). Okwesibini, umyalezo uyangqinelana nowabaprofeti abadlulileyo (Dut. 13:1–3, Isa. 8:20). Okwesithathu, ubomi bemihla ngemihla kubonisa ukuzibophelela kuKristu (1 Yohane 4:1–3). Okwesine, uYesu wathi abaprofeti bobuxoki bokwaziwa ngeziqhamo zabo (Mat. 7:15–20). Oku kukwayinyani kanjalo xa kufikelelwa kubaprofeti benene.

Incwadi yeSiTyhilelo ibonisa ukuba isipho sokuprofeta luphawu olwahlukileyo lwebandla lesisalela (IsiTyhi. 12:17, IsiTyhi. 19:10). NjengaBalindi, sikholelwa ukuba isipho sokuprofeta sathiwa jize uEllen G. White kwaye siyabonakala kwimibhalo yakhe.

`Zithini zonke izizathu esinazo zokukholelwa kwisipho sokuprofeto sikaEllen G. White? Yeyiphi imibuzo esekhona ngendima yakhe negunya?`
