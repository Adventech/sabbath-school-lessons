---
title:  Ubunye Phakathi Kokwahluka
date:   03/08/2026
---

Intetho yobunye evezwe kuma-1 Korinte 12:4-6 (“ikwanguloo Moya mnye,” “ikwayiloo Nkosi inye,” kwakunye “ikwanguloo Thixo mnye”) iphuhliswa kuwo onke ama-1 Korinte 12. Oku kuqinisekiswa kukusebenzisa kukaPawulos amabinzana afana “kwanguloo Moya mnye” (1 Kor. 12:11), “umzimba umnye” (1 Kor. 12:12), “mzimba mnye” (1 Kor. 12:12, 13, 20), “Moya mnye” 1 Kor. 12:13 [izihlandlo ezibini]), “ukunyamekelana kwamalungu ngakunye” (1 Kor. 12:25).

Ngokuhambelana nengcamango yobunye, uPawulos uveza ukwahluka kwamalungu kumzimba kaKristu ngeentetho ezifana “namalungu amaninzi” (1 Kor. 12:12, 20), “thina sonke sabhaptizelwa” (1 Kor. 12:13), “ungelungu linye, ungamaninzi” (1 Kor. 12:14), “umzimba lo uphela” (1 Kor. 12:17), “lalelo nelo” (1 Kor. 12:18), “amalungu omzimba” (1 Kor. 12:22, 23), “amalungu onke” (1 Kor. 12:26). Olu gxininiso ngokufanayo nokwahlukana lubonisa ukuba izipho zakwamoya zinenjongo yokukhuthaza ubunye phakathi kokwahluka.

Obu bunye phakathi kokwahlukana kufuneka bubonakalise isimilo sikaThixo. UBawo sisiqu esinye, uNyana sesinye isiqu, noMoya sesinye. Zontathu zigcine ubuqu bazo lo gama zisebenza kunye ukwakha ibandla kwakunye nokulixhobisela umsebenzi walo (1 Kor. 12:4–6, Efe. 4:11–13).

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 12:12-31. Kutheni le nto ufaniso lo mzimba onamalungu amaninzi lufanelekile ukumela ibandla kwakunye namalungu alo?`

Ingcamango enye ephambili kuma-1 Korinte 12 kukuba nangona amalungu omzimba lilodwa lohluke ngolo hlobo kwelinye (1 Kor. 12:15–20), xa ewonke axhomekeke elinye kwelinye (1 Kor. 12:21–26). Iinyawo zixhomekeke emehlweni ukuze zibone indawo emazihambe kuyo; ngokunjalo, amehlo awanakuchukumisa nto-kuphela zizandla ezinako. Ngokunjalo, ingcamango yokuba amanye amalungu abuthathaka (1 Kor. 12:22) okanye aswele imbeko (1 Kor. 12:23) kunamanye luluvo nje, kuba onke ayadingeka (1 Kor. 12:22).

Ngelishwa, kwakukho isimbo phakathi kwamaKorinte sokuxabisa izipho ezithile ngokungananzi ezinye. Ukuthintela amaKorinte ekwenzeni le mpazamo, uPawulos waabizela ingqalelo yawo kuthando, “oluyeyona ndlela incamisileyo” (1 Kor. 12:31). Ngamanye amazwi, nokuba isipho sesiphina, ukuba sisetyenziswa ngobulumko nothando, siyanelisa kuThixo.

`Jonga izintlu zezipho kuma-1 Korinte 12:8-10; Roma 12:6-8; namaEfese 4:11. Yintoni isipho sakho? Ungasisebenzisa njani ukwakha umzimba kaKristu?`
