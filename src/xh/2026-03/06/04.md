“Uthando alusosipho sinye phakathi kwezinzi. Luyinqwelo ezithi ngayo zonke ezinye izipho zifikelele kwinjongo yazo epheleleyo.”—Carl P. Cosaert, “1 Corinthians,” ˆ: New Testament (Berrien Springs, MI: Andrews University Press, 2022), p. 1643.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 13:1-7 naku-1 Petros 4:8-11. Yintoni indima yothando ngokuphathelele kwizipho zakwamoya?`

Ama-1 Korinte 13 afundisa ukuba kuphela kungothando ekunokuthi izipho zakwamoya zisetyenziswe ngendlela eyiyo. UPawulos uqala ama-1 Korinte 13 ngokubhekisa kwizipho ezikhankanywe kuma-1 Korinte 12, kuphela ukuzama ukucinezela ukungabi naxabiso kwazo xa zingaqhutywa luthando. Ngoko ke, ulwazi (1 Kor. 12:8) nokholo (1 Kor. 12:9), nkqu ukholo “olunokususa iintaba aziyonto ngaphandle kothando (1 Kor. 13:2). Ngaphandle kothando, isakhono sokuthetha ngeelwimi (1 Kor. 12:10, 28, 30) sincitshiswa sifane nje “nexina ehlokomayo okanye icangci elikhenkcezayo” (1 Kor. 13:1).Ngokunjalo, nkqu nesipho esibalulekileyo sokuprofeta asiyonto ngaphandle kothando (1 Kor. 13:2).

Kuma-1 Korinte 13:4-7, uPawulos ugxila kwinto oluyiyo uthando kwaye nento olungeyiyo; ngokukodwa, into oluyenzayo uthando kunye naleyo lungayenziyo. Izenzi azikhethileyo ukuphawula uthando zibonisa ukuba uthando asiyonto ikakhulu esiyivayo njengoko luyinto esiyenzayo. Ngaloo ndlela, uPawulos ukhankanya ukuba uthando (1) lubonakalisa umonde; (2) lubonakalisa ububele; (3) luvuyela inyaniso; (4) luthwala izinto zonke; (5) lukholwa ziinto zonke; (6) luthemba izinto zonke; (7) lunyamezela iinto zonke. Ngokwahlukileyo, uthando (1) alunamona; (2) aluzigwagwisi; (3) alukhukhumaliswa likratshi; (4) aluziphathi krwada; (5) alufuni okukokwalo [amalungelo]; (6) alucaphuki msinya; (7) alugcini ingxelo yeziphoso (8) aluvuyeli ukwenza okungafanelekanga.

Zizonke ezi zenzi zili-15 zibonelela ngesikhokelo esiluqilima sokuziphatha okufanelekileyo ekusetyenzisweni kwezipho. Ngokuphawulekayo, le ngxoxo kwimo eyiyo yothando ilele ngqo phakathi kwama-1 Korinte 12 no-14, nalapho uPawulos ashukuxa imbambano enento yokwenza nezipho zakwamoya. Ngokwenene, uthando lusisitshixo ekusetyenzisweni ngobulumko kwezipho zakwamoya. Uthando lukwabekwa ngokuthelekiswa nokholo nethemba, “eyona inkulu ke kuzo apho luthando” (1 Kor. 13:13).

`Kutheni le nto uthando lubaluleke ngolo hlobo kwinkolo yethu? Yeyiphi indlela engcono yokungcamla ubunyani bothando lukaThixo kunokufuna ngokuthandaza ukubonakalisa olothando kwabanye?`
