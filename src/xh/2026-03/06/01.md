### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
1 Korinte 12, Efe. 4:11–13, 1 Korinte 13, 1 Pet. 4:8–11, 1 Kor. 14:27, Amos 3:7.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Phuthumani uthando olo, nibe kodwa nizizondelela izipho ezizezoMoya, ngokukodwa ke ukuba niprofete” (1 Korinte 14:1).

Njengomzimba womntu, ibandla linye kodwa linamalungu amaninzi, ngalinye linamaxanduva ohlokeneyo, imisebenzi, kwakunye nezipho. Zisetyenziswe ngothando, ezi zipho zakwamoya zikhuthaza ubume bobunye obubonakalisa isimilo sikaThixo oziqu zithathu.

Kule veki sizakuhlola ama-1 Korinte 12-14 kwakunye nemfundiso yawo kwizipho zakwamoya. Esi sigaba yinxalenye yesixa esiphantse sibe sikhulu, nalapho uPawulos ashukuxa nokuziphatha okulindeleke kumKristu kwiimeko zenkolo (1 Korinte 11–14). Eyona nkxalabo iphala phambili kaPawulos inento yokwenza nengxaki yemihlangano enesiphithiphi. Impendulo yakhe kule ngxaki kukuba ibandla lingumzimba omalungu awo anemisebenzi eyohlukeneyo enegalelo “ekwakhiweni komzimba kaKristu” (Efe. 4:12). Ngamafutshane, uThixo waanika ibandla izipho zakwamoya ukuze akhuthaze ubunye phakathi kokwahluka.

Ngokuqinisekeliyo, uPawulos engqondweni usenengxaki yoqhekeko eshukuxwe kwizahluko ezine zokuqala zama-1 Korinte, nalapho impendulo ikukuvumelana okungekhoyo phakathi kwamalungu ebandla ibubunye kuKristu. Ngoku, uphuhlisa loo ngcamango ngokuzisa indlela ayiqonda ngayo indima yezipho zakwamoya. NgokukaPawulos, ubunye kuKristu kwakunye noMoya kuphela kwendlela yokuphepha iyantlukwano.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Agasti 8._
