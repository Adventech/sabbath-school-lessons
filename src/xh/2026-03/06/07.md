Roswell F. Cottrell, “Spiritual Gifts,” pp. 5–16, kuEllen G. White, Spiritual Gifts, vol. 1.

“Abantu mabahambe bayokusebenza, bethembele eNkosini, kwaye Yona iyakuhamba nabo, iqinisekisa yaye iguqula imiphefumlo. Omnye umsebenzi usenokuba sisithethi esicwalileyo, omnye umbhali ocwalileyo, omnye unokuba sisithethi somthandazo osulungekileyo, onyamekileyo, oshushu, omnye isipho sokucula. Omnye unokuba namandla awodwa okucazulula ilizwi likaThixo ngokucacileyo. Kwaye isipho ngasinye kufuneka sibe ngamandla kaThixo ngenxa yokuba Yena esebenzisana nomsebenzi lo. Komnye uThixo unikezela ilizwi lobulumko, komnye ulwazi, komnye ukholo. Kodwa bonke kufuneka basebenze phantsi kwentloko enye. Ukwahluka kwezipho kukhokelela kukwahluka emisebenzini, ‘kodwa ke ikwangulooThixo mnye uzibenzayo izinto zonke kubo bonke’ (1 Kor. 12:6).

“Makungabikho mntu uzidelelayo izipho ezikhangeleka ngathi zincinci. Bonke mabaye emsebenzini. Makungabikho usonga izandla zakhe kwintandabuzo ngenxa yokuba ecinga ukuba akanakwenza misebenzi mikhulu. Yeka ukujonga kumna. Jonga kwinkokeli yakho. Ngobulali, ukusulungeka, nothando yenza oko unako ukwenza.”— Ellen G. White, in The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, April 12, 1906.

“sonke ngabanye siyaludinga uncedo esinokulifumana kwezinye iingqondo. UThixo uyakusebenza kwezinye ingqondo kunakwezethu. Izipho ezohlukeneyo ezinikezelwe kwabo bohlukileyo kufuneka zidityaniselwe ‘ukuze abangcwele basulungekiselwe umsebenzi wolungiselelo, ukwakhiwa ke komzimba kaKristu’ (Efe. 4:12). . . .

“Kuyakusoloko kukho imiqobo phambi kwethu, kodwa kufuneka silandele iNkokeli yethu, size siqubisane neenzima zethu ngokumanyeneyo, sibambene.”— Ellen G. White, The Upward Look, p. 141.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Cinga ngakumbi ngesipho sokuprofeta. Kutheni le nto isiprofeto sibaluleke ngaphezu kweelwimi nje xa zithe azatolikwa? Ukuba kuyadingeka, funda kuma-1 Korinte 14 kwakhona ukuze uzikhumbuze ngeengxoxo zikaPawulos.`

`Eklasini, thethani ngobomi nolungiselelo lukaEllen G.White kwanokuba, kutheni thina, njengebandla, sikholelwa ukuba ngokwenene waabonakalisa isipho sokuprofeta. Ntsikelelo zini eziza ebandleni ngenxa yesisipho? Iyintoni kanjalo imingeni ekwazini indlela engcono yokusebenzisa esi sipho?`

`Cinga ngabantu abathathu nabahlanu abakuthanda ngokwenyani. Wazi njani ukuba uthando lwabo ngakuwe lunyanisekile? Oku kukuxelela ntoni ngesizathu sokuba uPawulos athethe kakhulu ngolo hlobo ngothando kwingxoxo yakhe ngezipho zakwamoya?`

`Nokuba lubaluleke kangakanani na uthando, kutheni le nto oko kungafuneki kube yindlela ekuphela kwayo ekugqibeni ukuba ubani uthetha inyaniso okanye hayi kwaye ufanelwe kukumanyelwa?`
