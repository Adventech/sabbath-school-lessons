---
title:  Iintlobo Zezipho
date:   02/08/2026
---

UPawulos uzisa isihloko esitsha kuma-1 Korinte 12:1, ngendlela eqingqiweyo “ngoku malunga.” Iingcaphephe zixoxa kwelokuba uthetha “ngezipho zomoya” okanye “abantu bakwamoya” kuba ibinzana lesiGrike _utōn pneumatikōn_ liyazivumela zombini ezondlela zokutolikwa. “Izipho zakwamoya” ukhetheka ngenxa yama-1 Korinte 12:4, nalapho uPawulos akhankanya ukuba isipho sokuqala sikaMoya kukuvuma ngokungenazintloni ukuba uYesu uyiNkosi (1 Kor. 12:2, 3). Kumaxesha eTestamente eNtsha, ukuthi uYesu uyiNkosi kukuthi uKesare akayiyo (Zenzo 17:7; kwakhona kuYohane 19:12, 15). Oku kwakubonwa njengovukelo-mbuso ngokuchasene negunya lombuso yaye, yiyo lo nto, isohlwayo yayikukufa.

UYesu noPawulos baagxinisa ukuba ukholo kuThixo-nkqu naxa ujamelene nentshutshiso kwakunye nesigrogriso sokufa-lusisipho sikaMoya. Eneneni, ukholo sesona sipho sokuqala. Ngokukhwankqisayo, ukholo lolokuqala kuluhlu olukuma-1 Korinte 13:13. Elokuba ukholo lusisipho sakwamoya icace gcaa ngentetho kaPawulos kuma-1 Korinte 12:9. Noko kunjalo, zininzi izipho ezizezinye. Into yokuba uMoya oyiNgcwele usaba iintlobo ezahlukeneyo zezipho “elowo ngokwakhe njengoko athandayo” (1 Kor. 12:11) ibonisa ukuba zonke ziyadingeka.

`Funda kumaKorinte 12:1-6. Yintoni ugxininiso kwesi sicatshulwa?`

Ukuphindaphindwa “kweentlobo” kugxininisa ubuninzi bezipho. Oko uPawulos akubiza “izipho zakwamoya” kuma-1 Korinte 12:1 kuphuhla kwiindima 4-6, ngeenkalo ezintathu ezohlukeneyo: “izipho” (_charisma_) “imisebenzi” okanye “izilungiselelo” (_diakonia_), kwakunye “neentsebenzo” (_energēma_). Nangona la magama ethetha izinto ezohlukeneyo, kubalulekile ukukrwela umgca owahlulayo phakathi kwazo ngenxa yofaniso olukwisicatshulwa. Umntu kufuneka aqaphele kanjalo ukuba izipho zakwamoya zicwangciselwe ukukhuthaza ubunye ngelisekelezelwe kwisimilo sikaThixo oziqu zithathu emnye (bona kanjalo kumaEfe. 4:8–11). Lo gama uMoya unikezela izipho kumakholwa, nguThixo owaxhobisayo ukuba asebenzele uKristu kwintlanganisela yamakholwa (1 Kor. 12:5, 6). Kholwa ngalinye labelwa izipho ngokunokwalo kodwa kufuneka zibe luncedo kwintlanganisela yamakholwa iphela.
