---
title:  Ukutshata Nokungatshati
date:   23/07/2026
---

Ukunqonqozisa kukaPawulos ukuba umzimba wethu “uyitempile yoMoya oyiNgcwele” (1 Kor. 6:19) kwenzeka kwimeko yesilumkiso sokuchasa ukuziphatha gwenxa ngakwezesondo. Ukuba yitempile yoMoya kuphela kwendlela yokuphila ubomi obungcwele. Ibandla liyintlanganisela yamaKristu ezahlula kwimeko ejikelezileyo. Bubukho bukaMoya oyiNgcwele okwenza kwenzeke.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 6:19-7:9. Esi sicatshulwa sikhanyisa njani kwindlela ubani anokuwenza ngayo umthetho “wokubaleka umbulo” (1 Kor. 6:18)?`

Kukho izifundo ezibalulekileyo malunga nezesondo kuma-1 Korinte 7. Ngokuthetha okufutshane, esi sahluka sinokwahlulwa sibe zizigaba ezibini: (1) iziyalo eziphathelele emtshatweni (1 Kor. 7:1–24) kwakunye (2) neziyalo eziphathelele kukungatshati (1 Kor. 7:25–40). Okokuqala amaKorinte 7 asincede siqonde ukuba ukuthetha ngomba wezesondo kubalulekile kwaye kuyadingeka.

Noko kunjalo, xa ufunda ama-1 Korinte 7, kufuneka sikhumbule ukuba uPawulos uphendula kwimibuzo etsolileyo enento yokwenza nemiba ekwibandla laseKorinte. Kungenjalo, ezinye iintetho zinokukhokelela kwingcamango ethi umtshato uwusingela phantsi, kube kungenjalo (1 Tim. 4:1–3, 1 Tim. 5:14; kanjalo Heb. 13:4).

Ngokuphawulekayo, umyalelo “wokubaleka umbulo” okuma-1 Korinte 6:18 ubiyelwe yingcamango yokunamathela eNkosini (1 Kor. 6:17) kunye nokuba yitempile yoMoya (1 Kor. 6:17). Ingaba ikhona indlela engcono yokubaleka umbulo? Kakade ayikho.

Kanjalo, uThixo waakudala ukwabelana ngesondo, kodwa lelokonwatyelwa emtshatweni kuphela. Ukwabelana ngesondo yinyhweba kwabo bakumtshato wendoda nomfazi, uhlobo ekuphela kwalo olungcwalisiweyo eBhayibhileni

Xa esithi, “Wubalekeni umbulo,” uPawulos kunokuba wayecinga ngebali likaYosefu (Gen. 39:6–18). IBhayibhile ithi phambi kwezincokoliso zenkanuko zenkosikazi kaPotifaro, uYosefu “wabalekela phandle” (Gen. 39:18). Oku akukhankanywanga hayi ngaphantsi kwezihlandlo ezine kuGenesis 39:6-18. IBhayibhile ayilikhuphi litsole, koko iveziwe eyokuba uYosefu waalinda ukuba abelane ngesondo kuphela emtshatweni (Gen. 41:45). Wayeyindoda ezaliswe nguMoya oyiNgcwele (Gen. 41:38) kwaye wayefuna ukwenza oko kulungileyo emehlweni kaThixo.

`Singakwazi njani, njengebandla, ukukhusela iziqu zethu kwiingcamango ezitenxileyo ngesondo ezongamele inkcubeko?`
