Ellen G. White, “A Message of Warning and Entreaty,” pp. 298–308, kwiThe Acts of the Apostles.

Ngokunika umdla, kudweliso lwezono kuma-1 Korinte 5:10, 11 nakuma-1 Korinte 6:9, 10, unqulo-zithixo kwakunye nokunxila zidweliswe kunye nombulo. Njengoko uPawulos ekhumbula kuma-1 Korinte 10:7 (thelekisa ne-Eksod. 32:1–6), imibhiyozo yonqulo-zithixo yayivamise ukubalulwa ngobudla-kudla nokusela okugqithisileyo, nto ezo zazivula ikroba lokuziphatha gwenxa ngokwezesondo (1 Kor. 10:8). UEllen G. White uthi:

“Akunakwenzeka ukuba nabani na ukuba onwabele intsikelelo yokungcwaliswa lo gama ezicingela kwaye elidla-kudla. . . . Amandla obume bomzimba okuchasa izinto ezingalunganga ezibekwe phezu kwawo angummangaliso, kodwa imikhwa engeyiyo ethe gqolo yobudla-kudla nokusela okugqithisileyo iyakuthibaza konke ukusebenza komzimba. Ekwaneliseni ilithe elitenxileyo nentshisakalo nkqu nabo bazibiza njengamaKristu balimaza indalo ekusebenzeni kwayo baze badodobalise amandla omzimba, ingqondo, kunye nokuziphatha.”—Ellen G. White, The Sanctified Life, pp. 25, 26.

“Xa ubani umna uqongqothiwe kuye, xa wonke uthixo wobuxoki ukhutshiwe emphefumlweni, ubugobhoza bubonelelwa kukungena koMoya kaKristu. Lowo unjalo unokholo olusebenza ngothando luze luhlambulule umphefumlo kuko konke ukudyobheka kokuziphatha nokwakwamoya.”—Ellen G. White, The Home Missionary, November, 1889.

“UThixo unqwenela ukusifunqulela kumgangatho waKhe ophakamileyo, onyulu, wasezulwini. Ngenxa yale njongo uMoya waKhe uthe rhoqo ukuzamana nathi. . . . Izimbo zethu zemvelo, de kube kanti zilungiswe nguMoya oyiNgcwele kaThixo, kuzo ngaphakathi zineembewu zokuziphatha okufileyo ”—Ellen G. White, Manuscript 12, 1888.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Intlaninge yamakholwa aseKorinte ayebonakalisa ulangazelelo lokuzuza ukwamkeleka kwinkcubeko. Kutheni le nto oko kuyingozi ngeyona ndlela ekuzibeni ngobuKristu? Yintoni esinokuyenza uthintela thina ekwenzeni kwaloo mpazamo?`

`Umbuzo buciko kaPawulos “Anazi na, ukuba umzimba wenu uyitempile yoMoya oyiNgcwele?” (1 Kor. 6:19) uqukumbela umtyhutyhumezo wemibuzo esixhenxe kuma-1 Korinte 5–6 eziswe ngale ndlela iqingqiweyo “Anazi na?” (1 Kor. 5:6; 1 Kor. 6:2, 3, 9, 15, 16, 19). Yonke ifuna impendulo enqonqozisayo negxininisayo- into efana “Kakade uyazi.” Le mibuzo isinceda njani ukuba siqonde iinkxalabo zikaPawulos ngebandla? Kutheni le nto, nathi, kufuneka sixhalabe ngezi zinto namhlanje?`

`Umtshato uphum’ ezandleni zikaThixo (Gen. 1:27, 28; Gen. 2:18-24) kwaye kufuneka ubekwe ngendlela (Heb. 13:4). Kwixesha apho intlaninge iwibona njengento ephelelwe lixesha, singalibonisa njani ihlabathi ukuba umtshatho ngokwenene usisipho sikaThixo, uphuma nqo e-Eden ngokwayo?`
