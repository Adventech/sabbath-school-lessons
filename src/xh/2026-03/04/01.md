### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
1 Kor. 5:1–13, 2 Kor 2:5–10, 1 Kor. 6:1–13, 1 Tesa. 4:1–8, 1 Kor. 6:19–7:9.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Anazi na, ukuba umzimba wenu uyitempile yoMoya oyiNgcwele ongaphakathi kwenu, eninaye evela kuThixo; nokuba ke aningabenu? Kuba nathengwa ngexabiso. Mzukiseni ke uThixo ngomzimba wenu” (1 Korinte 6:19, 20).

Iingqondo zethu zifana neemfunxa (iziphontshi): yonke into eziswa kuzo, ngezivo zethu, ihlala kuzo. Sisenokungayiqondi into eninzi engenayo (besingenakukwazi ukucinga kakuhle ukuba besikhumbula yonke into), kodwa yonke ilapha, kwaye ngahlobo luthile ichaphazela oko sikucingayo, sikuvayo, size sikwenze.

Yiyo lo nto kulula kuthi, nkqu nathi njengamaKristu, ukuba sichatshazelwe zizinto zonke ezimbi ezisingqongileyo. Ibandla lobuKristu, kwasekuqalekeni, lisokoliswe yile ngxaki. Kwaavela phi, umzekelo, ukugcinwa kweCawa? Ingaba ibandla lavela nje lakukha emoyeni? Kakade akunjalo. Kwaavela kwinkcubeko eyayingqonge lona.

Kwaye sinako ukuwubona lo mmiselo utyhileka eKorinte. Emva kokubongoza ngokuchasene noqhekeko (1 Korinte 1–4), uPawulos ngoku ubhenela kwimiba enento yokwenza nokungaziphathi kakuhle ngokwezesondo, ukumangalelana, uhenyuzo, umtshato, kwakunye nokungatshati (1 Korinte 5–7). Imigangatho yehlabathi yabachaphazela ngokubanzi. Uqhekeko oluzotywe kuma-1 Korinte 1–4 lwavulela ucango ukuziphatha okuchasiweyo kwizahluko ezilandelayo. Uzama njani uPawulos ukushukuxa esi sono ebandleni, yaye zifundo zini esinokuzithabatha koko akubhalileyo?

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Julayi 25._
