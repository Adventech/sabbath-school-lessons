---
title:  Ukungangqinelani Kwenkolo Nezenzo
date:   19/07/2026
---

Kuyo yonke imbali yobuKristu, abahlalutyi-nkolo, abefundisi, kwakunye nomndilili bayifundile iTestamente eNtsha ukuze babone ukuba lifanele kukukhangeleka njani ibandla. Siyakhwankqiswa umzekelo, kwibandla leZenzo. Kodwa siyakhawuleza ukuphulukana nesiqalelo esinye esibalulekileyo: abantu baneengxaki. Kukhangeleka ngathi singayifunda kanjalo iTestamente eNtsha ukubona into ethethwa yiBhayibhile ngento ibandla emalingakhangeleki liyiyo. Iincwadi zikaPawulos kumaKorinte ziyindawo efanelekileyo yokuqalisa.  

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 5:1-13. Yimeko ni elisikizi ayichazayo uPawulos kwesi sicatshulwa, kwaye kutheni iphazamisa ngaloo ndlela?`

Intetho “umfazi kayise”(1 Kor. 5:1) ibonisa ukuba uPawulos ubhekisa kubudlelane bombulo phakathi kwendoda nomama wayo wesibini. Le meko mhlawumbi yaachazwa “ngabakuloKlowe” (1 Kor. 1:11). Umbulo wawudume njengesono esibi kangangokuba “wawunganyamezeleki nkqu naphakathi kwabahedeni” (1 Kor. 5:1). Noko kunjalo ngoku wenzeka kwibandla lokuqala lobuKristu? Amazwi kaPawulos kuma-1 Korinte 5:1, 2 abonisa ukuba wothukile yingxelo yokuba kubekho ilungu lebandla elenza oku.

Noko kunjalo, le meko imbi iyaqatsela. UPawulos wothuka ngakumbi kukufumanisa ukuba, endaweni yokuba buhlungu ngale meko, amaKorinte ayenebhongo ngeziqu zawo ngokunyamezela isono esinjalo (1 Kor. 5:1, 2). Ngoko ke, ujonge ekulungiseni hayi nje kuphela le ndoda yaphukaneke isimilo koko kanjalo nebandla ngokungangqinelani kwalo okubonakalayo phakathi kokholo nesenzo. Eneneni, uPawulos uyibeka icace gcaa ngokuthe gqolo eyokuba imo yebandla eyekeleleyo ngakule ndoda yenza umbulo yayidinga ukulungiswa. Kodwa ukuzidla ngesiqebheyi esinjalo sezesondo, de uqhayise ngaso (1 Kor. 5:2, 6)? Yayingumhohoma kuPawulos angakuthwala. Yintoni eyayigwenxa ngaba bantu?

Asinangcaciso yesizeka-bani sokuba ibandla laseKorinte liyinyamezele ngolo hlobo le ndoda yenza umbulo. Mhlawumbi yayililungu elisisinana ibandla elalixhamla amaqithiqithi kuyo? Okanye, mhlawumbi, ngenxa yokuba “zonke izinto zivumelekile” (1 Kor. 6:12), abazange bayibone ngendlela ekwakufanele bayibona ngayo? Thina asazi nto.

Nokuba zeziphi izizathu eziyinyani, baamfameka kukunyhashwa okuphandle kweziBhalo (Lev. 18:7, 8). Kwaye babezidla ngako?

`Zeziphi izinto ezikhatywa ngokuphandle kwisiBhalo thina, njengebandla, esisemngciphekweni wokuzinyamezela, zonke phantsi kwegama “lothando” kwakunye “nokwamkeleka”?`
