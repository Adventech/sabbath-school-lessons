---
title:  Ukukhusela Igama leBandla
date:   21/07/2026
---

Kuma-1 Korinte 6:1-11, uPawulos uyaqhubekeka nengxoxo yakhe ngendlela amaKristu amawahlangabezane ngayo nemibandela ebandakanya abantu ebandleni.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 5:3, 12, 13 nakuma-1 Korinte 6:1-13. Yintoni le uPawulos azama ukuyifundisa amaKorinte kunye nathi?`

Igama lesiGrike _upragma_ kuma-1 Korinte 6:1, eliguqulwe “langumba” kwiNguqulelo yeKing James, ligama eliqhelekileyo elithetha “into.” Apha, lisingisele kumba womthetho. Kubalulekile ukukhumbula ukuba ama-1 Korinte 6:1-11 awabhekisi kwityala lolwaphulo-mthetho. Igunya leenkundla zelizwe kwimiba yolwaphulo-mthetho liqinisekiswe kumaRoma 13:1-5. UPawulos ushukuxa umba wokumangalelana kanye emva komba wokuziphatha gwenxa ngokwesondo, kwanjengokuba wenzayo uMoses kuDuteronomi 22:22-24. Oku kubonisa ubungakanani bohlobo uPawulos ajamelana ngayo neengxaki ebandleni obusekelezelwe kwiziBhalo.

Into yokuba lo mbandela ukuma-1 Korinte 6:1-11 ubiyelwe zizicatshulwa ezinento yokwenza nokuziphatha gwenxa ngokwesondo (1 Korinte 5 naku-1 Kor. 6:12–20) isengabonisa ukuba lo “mba” ukuma-1 Korinte 6:1 ubandakanya ukuziphatha gwenxa ngokwesondo, nawo. Ngokuqinisekileyo asinalwazi ukuba ityala lingantoni na, noba ngumba ongephi wobuzwe, njengonomji ongempahla, okanye ingxaki yezesondo.

Nokuba ekugqibeleni yayi yintoni na _ipragma_, uPawulos wayengonwabanga kukubona amalungu ebandla eyisa kwinkundla yelizwe. Babengenako na bona, njengabazalwana noodade abangamaKristu, bayisombulule ngokunokwabo, kunokuba bazise lo mcimbi phambi “kwabangemalungisa” (1 Kor. 6:1)?

Kunokwenzeka kanjalo, njengoko abanye bebanga, ukuba aba bamangaleleneyo kuma-1 Korinte 6:1 ngutata nonyana abakuma-1 Korinte 5:1. Nokuba imeko ithini na, akuyomfuneko ukuqonda umba ukuze uqonde ingongoma. UPawulos wayekhathalele inkangeleko yebandla njengentlanganisela yamaKristu xa lijongwe ngabangaphandle. AmaKristu akufuneki oneke amabibi esidlangalaleni (1 Kor. 6:6). Engenako kananjalo ukusebenzisa iindlela zelizwe ukugweba imiba yangaphakathi. Kwihlabathi lamaRoma, abantu ababekekileyo ngokobutyebi okanye ngokwezikhundla zombangazwe babevamise ukuthethelelwa enkundleni. Ngokwahlukileyo, amaKristu mawenze ugwebo olufana nolukaKristu kwaye azohlule kwimigangatho yelizwe.

`Cinga ngoludwe lukaPawulos lwezono ezikuma-1 Korinte 5:10, 11 naku-1 Korinte 6:9, 10. Kutheni le nto edwelisa izono zesondo kunye nezinye izono ezifana nonqulo-zithixo, ubusela, ukubawa, kwakunye nokuphanga?`
