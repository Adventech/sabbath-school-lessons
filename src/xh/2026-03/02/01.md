### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
1 Kor. 1:17–31, Kol. 1:20, 1 Pet. 2:24, Zenzo 13:16–47, 1 Kor. 2:1–5.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kuba ilizwi lomnqamlezo libubudenge okunene kwabatshabalalayo; kodwa ke kwabasindiswayo, thina aba lingamandla kaThixo” (1 Korinte 1:18).

UCicero, umbhali neciko lomRoma elingumhedeni, wabaxelela abantu baseRoma ukuba bagcine ingcamango yomnqamlezo, njengophawo lwesohlwayo, kude ezingqondweni. Nangona uCicero ebhubhe phantse kwisiqingatha senkulungwane ngaphambi kokuba uYesu azalwe, intetho yakhe ibonakalisa indelelo ayewujonge ngayo umnqamlezo amaRoma. Yayimbi kangangokuba ayengacingi nokucinga ngayo.

Ngokwahlukileyo, uPawulos ubhala: “Ilizwi lomnqamlezo . . . lingamandla kaThixo” (1 Kor. 1:18). KuPawulos, uMnqamlezo sisixhobo soxolelwaniso phakathi koThixo nomntu (Efe. 2:16, Kol. 1:20), uphawu oluveleleyo lokuthobeka kukaYesu (Fil. 2:8), kwakunye nendawo apho ityala lethu elikhulu laahlawulwa khona (Kol. 2:14).

UMnqamlezo uyimpendulo kaPawulos kwiingxaki zaseKorinte. Akudingeki ukuba ungene nzulu kuma-1 Korinte ukuze uqonde ukuba yena uxhalabe ngeyona ndlela ngomba omnye osemqoka: izahlukaniso ebandleni. UPawulos udideke ngeyona ndlela kangangokuba kanye emva kwemibuliso (1 Kor. 1:1–3) kwakunye nesigaba sombulelo (1 Kor. 1:10–17), esi sisihloko sokuqala asishukuxayo. Kule veki, sizakuqwalasela isigidimi esinamandla soMnqamlezo njengempendulo kule ngxaki kwakunye neminye imiba eKorinte.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Julayi 11._
