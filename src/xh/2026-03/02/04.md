---
title:  Lingamandla Kwabo Basindiswayo
date:   07/07/2026
---

Umyalezo okuma-1 Korinte 1:18 ucace gcaa uba ubani angawuphosa umxholo. Intsingiselo yoMnqamlezo ixhomekeke kwindlela ubani ayijonga ngayo. Ibubudenge kwabo bavukela uThixo, kodwa ingamandla kwabo balangazelela usindiso lwaKhe.

`Funda kumaKolose 1:20 no-1 Petros 2:24. Yinto uYesu awayiphumeza ngenxa yethu emnqamlezweni?`

Njengoko sesibonile, ekushumayeleni igospile, umntu kufuneka aphephe “amazwi obulumko obucikozayo, hleze umnqamlezo kaKritsu uhlutywe amandla awo” (1 Kor. 1:17, inguqulelyo ye-ES). Xa ujonge kuma-1 Korinte 1:17, kubalula ukuqonda isizathu sokuba isichasi sobudenge ingamandla kaThixo, hayi ubulumko bobuntu (1 Kor. 1:18). UMnqamlezo, owahluke mpela kubulumko bobuntu, utyhila indlela obubudenge ngayo ubulumko bobuntu.

Indima yesiGrike yama-1 Korinte 1:18 ibonisa ukuba “abo batshabalalayo” bafumana imiphumela yezenzo zabo. Le ndima inokufundeka ngolu hlobo: “Kuba ilizwi lomnqamlezo libubudenge kwabo bazibhangisayo.” Isenzi sesiGrike _apollymi_ “ukutshabalala”) sisenokuthetha “ukuzibhangisa” (Yohane 10:10). Eneneni, _uapollymi_ uguqulelwe “njengokutshabalala” kuma-1 Korinte 1:19

Kwenzeka ntoni apha? UPawulos ubonelela ngesiseko esiseBhayibhileni kule ntetho kwindima ye-18, ngaba bantu batshabalalayo, ngokucaphula kwindima ye-19 amazwi kaThixo kuIsaya 29:14. Kwindima ye-19, uThixo nguye osemva kwentshabalalo, nto leyo ekhangeleka ikhabana nekratshi elizitshabalalisayo elikhankanywe ngaphambili. Noko kunjalo, akukho kukhabana. Ingcamango kukuba uThixo uzakutshabalalisa oko sele kuzitshabalalisa kuqala.

Ngokwahlukileyo kwabo batshabalalayo, ibinzana “kwabo basindiswayo” (1 Kor. 1:18) libonisa ukuba usindiso luvela kuphela kuThixo. UPawulos uthi siyasindiswa; oko kukuthi, asizisindisi ngokwethu. Thina, kakade, asinako. Usindiso lwethu lunomthombo ovela ngaphandle. Lo gama intshabalalo ibangelwa ngumntu siqu, usindiso lunokunikezelwa kuphela, njengesipho sobabalo kuboni. Njengoko kucacileyo kuma-1 Korinte 1:21, nguThixo osindisa abo bakholwayo. Ubudenge, kule imeko, sisenzo sokubukula oko uThixo akunikezela kuluntu ngomnqamlezo kaKristu (1 Kor. 1:30), aze ngalo ndlela azise intshabalalo kwisiqu sakhe.

`“Kuba umvuzo wesono kukufa, ke sona isibabalo sikaThixo bubomi obungunaphakade, kuKristu iNkosi yethu” (Roma 6:23). Kungaziphi iindlela ethi le ndima iphinde oko uPawulos akuthethe kuma-1 Korinte 1:18, 19?`
