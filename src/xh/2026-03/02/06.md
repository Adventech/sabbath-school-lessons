---
title:  UKristu, Amandla noBulumko bukaThixo
date:   09/07/2026
---

Kuma-1 Korinte 1:19, 20, 30, no-31, uPawulos uthetha ngendlela ubulumko bukaThixo nobabantu obahluke ngayo ngokumangalisayo ize, ngalo ndlela, bukhabane ngokulinganayo. Qaphela ukuba uPawulos akabukhabi ngolo hlobo ubulumko, koko ukhaba uhlobo lobulumko bobuntu obuzama ukukhuphisana noThixo. Ubulumko bobuntu abukwazi ukukhupha abantu esonweni. Kuphela nguKristu, obubulumko bukaThixo, onokwenza lo msebenzi. Bona kolu ludwe lungezantsi.

| Kodwa kuthi basindiswayo | [ilizwi loMnqamlezo] lingamandla kaThixo | 1 Kor. 1:18 |
| ke kwabo babiziweyo | uKristu ungamandla kaThixo | 1 Kor. 1:24 |

Zombini ama-1 Korinte 1:18 nama-1 Korinte 1:24 zibonisa ukuba uKristu ungamandla kaThixo, ngokohlobo lokuba Yena unamandla okusindisa abantu ezonweni zabo. Ngokwenene, “kwakholeka kuThixo ukusindisa abo bakholwayo, ngabo ubudenge bokuvakalisa iindaba ezilungileyo” (1 Kor. 1:21). Amabinzana “thina basindiswayo” (1 Kor. 1:18), “abo bakholwayo” (1 Kor. 1:21), kwakunye “nabo babiziweyo (1 Kor. 1:24) asingisele kwiqela elinye, oko kukuthi, abantu abaphila amava osindiso ngokholo. “Iindaba ezilungileyo zikaKristu . . . zingamandla kaThixo okusindisa bonke abakholwayo” (Roma 1:16).

UKristu akangomandla nje kuphela koko ukwabubulumko bukaThixo. Oku kuthetha ukuthi ngaYe uThixo waajamelana wasombulula ingxaki yesono, ingxaki obungenamandla akuyisombulula ubulumko bomntu. Ubulumko belihlabathi abukwazi ukwenza bamazi uThixo (1 Kor. 1:21). Kwelinye icala, ngoKristu sihlakaniphela usindiso (2 Tim. 3:15).

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 1:24-29. Qaphela amagama pha, afana “nobudenge,” “ngenamandla,” “amandla,” kwakunye “nolumkile.” Ngongoma ni ayivezayo?`

Ekufundeni ama-1 Korinte 1:24-29, umntu kufuneka aqaphele nokuba amagama ubudenge (okanye ebubudenge) kwakunye nokungabi namandla (ezingenamandla). Ingongoma kukuba ubulumko bobuntu bunokubona isigidimi soMnqamlezo njengobudenge nokungabi namandla. Noko kunjalo, “into kaThixo ebubudenge ilumkile kunabantu, nento kaThixo engenamandla yomelele kunabantu” (1 Kor. 1:25). Oku akuthethi kuthi uThixo akanamandla okanye usisidenge, koko yinkcazo nje ebonisa indlela amandla nobulumko bukaThixo ezogqitha ngayo nayiphi na into enobuntu.

`Gxila kumazwi “azizininzi izilumko ngokwenyama, ababaninzi abanamandla, akamaninzi amanene, abiziweyo” (1 Kor. 1:26). Myalezo mni esiwunikiweyo pha?`
