---
title:  UMesiya Obethelelwe Emnqamlezweni
date:   08/07/2026
---

UPawulos wabhala ukuba “AmaYuda Acela umqondiso, amaGrike afuna ubulumko” (1 Kor. 1:22). UMnqamlezo-ingcamango kaThixo, uMesiya, obethelelwe emnqamlezweni-yayingengomqondiso amaYuda ayewulindele. Wabe ungelulo nohlobo lobulumko amaGrike ayelufuna. Wawukhabana neminqweno yomntu wonke.

Eneneni, konke amakakwenze umntu kukufunda indlela abasabela ngayo abafundi kwingcamango yokubethelelwa kukaYesu emnqamlezweni (bona kuMarko 8:31, 32; Marko 9:30–32; noMarko 10:32–34) ukuze aqale abone indlela eyayingaqhelekanga, neyayihewulwa ngayo, xa iyonke le ngcamango, ngokukodwa kumaYuda. Njengoko kutshiwo ngaphambili, amaYuda ayelinde ukuba uMesiya oyise amaRoma; oko asikoko kweenzekayo, phantse noko hayi “ngokokoyisa” kwemikhosi yehlabathi.

Kangangeenkulungwane, uMnqamlezo, kumaKristu, ube luphawu lokholo. Kunzima kumaKristu enkulungwane yamashumi mabini ananye ukuqonda indlela eyayiphambene ngayo ingcamango yoThixo obethelelwe emnqamlezweni kwingqondo yenkulungwane yokuqala.

Noko kunjalo, injalo kanye kuba esi yayisisigidimi esothusayo esenza sifanelwe zezona zicamango zethu zinzulu. Umzobo kaMesiya obethelelwe emnqamlezweni wenza icace gcaa elizweni liphela umgama uThixo awayelindele ukuwuhamba ukuze afezekise icebo losindiso. Ingcamango yomnqamlezo ngokwawo, kwakunye neyokufa kweNkosi emnqamlezweni, ikhwankqisa ngokwaneleyo kuthi, boni abasemhlabeni. (Khawucinge, ngoko, into enokuba yayithethwa yiyo kwiziqu ezingenasono ezaziyazi, yaye ziyinqula, iNkosi uYesu emazulwini!)

`Funda kwiZenzo 13:16-47 (ngokukodwa iindima 26, 38, neyama-47). Esi sicatshulwa sisifundisa ntoni ngentsingiselo yoMnqamlezo?`

UPawulos uthi uKristu waamthuma ukuba ashumayele igospile. Ngenxa yoko ke uPawulos ushumayela isigidimi soMesiya obethelelwe emnqamlezweni (1 Kor. 1:23). Uziqala kwakho ezingcamango kuma-1 Korinte 2:1-5. Umpostile lo wayethembakele kumsebenzi uKristu ammisele wona. Ekuvakaliseni igospile, akazange asebenzise “ubugcisa bokuthetha okanye ubulumko” (1 Kor. 2:1); endaweni yoko, waagxila kuphela “kuYesu Kristu naye ebethelelwe emnqamlezweni” (1 Kor. 2:2). Intetho nomyalezo wakhe “kube kungengamazwi oyisa ngabulumko babantu; bekungokuqondakalisa koMoya nokwamandla” (1 Kor. 2:4) kuba, eneneni, “ubulumko babantu” bahluke ngokuselubala “kumandla kaThixo” (2 Kor. 2:5, ESV).

`UMesiya obethelelwe emnqamlezweni yinto eyayingalindelekanga konke konke kumaYuda namaGrike. Oku kusixelela ntoni ngenyaniso yokuba uThixo akasoloko esenza ngendlela thina esilindela ngayo? Kutheni le nto oku kuyingcamango ebalulekileyo ukuba siyibambe, ngokukodwa xa izinto zingahambi ngendlela ebesilindele ngayo?`
