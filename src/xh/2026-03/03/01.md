### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
1 Kor. 1:12–17, Roma 1:29, 1 Kor. 1:10, 1 Kor. 3:1–4, Fil. 2:5–8, 2 Kor. 11:23–28, Kol. 1:24.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ke kaloku, ndiyaniyala, bazalwana, ngalo igama leNkosi yethu, uYesu Kristu, ukuba nithethe nto-nye nonke, nokuba kungabikho zahlukahlukano kuni; ke nibe ngabalungisiweyo, nigqibelele ngqiqweni-nye, nasicamangweni sinye” (1 Korinte 1:10).

Abo bafunda nzulu ngobomi basezindle bayazi ukuba ezinye izidalwa zihlala zingamaqela, ziyimihlambi, zingamagqiza, zonke ngokwemilinganiselo eyohlukeneyo. Ukusuka kwiingcuka (kakade, ukusinga kwihlengesi, nkqu nakumkhosi weembovane, ezi zidalwa zihlala kunye. Iinkawu zaziwa ngokukodwa ngamakhonkco azo okuhlala aluqilima, ngamanye amaxesha ziphile zingamaqela eenkawu ezili-15 ukuya kwezili-150. Noko kunjalo, obu budlelane abusoloko bunoyondelelwano, kwaye ngamanye amaxesha iinkawo zilwa kwazodwa.

Abantu ngandlel’ ithile bakwanjalo nabo; oko kukuthi, abaphili bengamaqela nje kuphela, koko ngamanye amaxesha bayalwa bebodwa bengaloomaqela. Kwakheka amaqela, amaxesha amaninzi ngakwinkokeli ethile enempembelelo. Kwaye, okumandundu ngakumbi, ngamanye amaxesha elinye iqela alivani nabanye. Kwaye oko kuyinene nkqu nasemabandleni ethu!

Wakha wayibona lo nto ebandleni lakho?

Ukuba kunjalo, unawo ngoko umbono womba awayejamelene nawo uPawulos eKorinte. Kule veki sizakuqwalasela kuma-1 Korinte 1-4, apho umpostile uPawulos ajamelene nengxaki yeembambano ebandleni kwakunye nendlela yokuzisombulula, oko kukuthi, ngobunye kuKristu.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Julayi 18._
