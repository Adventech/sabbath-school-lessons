Ellen G. White, “The Training of the Twelve,” pp. 17–24, kwiThe Acts of the Apostles.

“Ubunye nomanyano lwabantu bakaThixo besisalela abakholelwa kwinyaniso luthwele ingcamango emandla esinga ehlabathini yokuba banenyaniso, yaye bangabantu bakaThixo abakhethekileyo, abanyuliweyo. Obu bunye nolumanyano luphazamisa utshaba, kwaye luzixelele ukuba malungabikho. Inyaniso yangoku, isakukholelwa entliziyweni ize ibonakaliswe ebomini, yenza abantu bakaThixo babe banye, kwaye ibanike impembelelo emandla.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 1, p. 327.

“UThixo ukhokela abantu ukuba bame kumanyano olugqibeleleyo phezu komgangatho wenyaniso engunaphakade. UKristu ngokunokwaKhe ehlabathini ukuze Yena ‘azihlambululele abantu abangabakhe, abazondelela imisebenzi emihle.’ Esi sigaba siphuculayo sicwangciselwe ukuhlambulula ibandla kuko konke ukungalungisi nakumoya wokungavisisani nowembabano, ukuze babenokwakha endaweni yokudiliza, baze banikele amandla wabo kumsebenzi omkhulu ophambi kwabo. UThixo ucwangcise ukuba abantu baKhe baze bonke kubunye benkolo. Umthandazo kaKristu kanye ngaphambi kokubethelelwa kwaKhe wawungowokuba abafundi baKhe babe banye kwanjengokuba naYe wayemnye noBawo, ukuze ihlabathi lazi ukuba uBawo umthumile Yena. Lo mthandazo uchukumisayo nomangalisayo ufikelela emaphakadeni, nkqu nakwelixesha lethu; kuba amazwi aKhe ngala: ‘Andiceleli ke aba bodwa, ndicelela nabo baya kukholwa kum ngelizwi labo.’”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 4, p. 17.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`Xa sisondela ngasemaphethelweni olungiselelo lwaKhe, uYesu waathandazela ubunye, “ukuze bonke babe banye . . . ukuze ihlabathi likholwe ukuba wena wandithuma” (Yohane 17:21–23). Kutheni le nto ubunye buyingxoxo emandla emel’ inyaniso yokuba uThixo wamthumela uNyana waKhe ukusindisa ihlabathi? Ngeliqhagamshelaniswe koku, kutheni le nto ukungabikho kobunye kungumqobo kwiphulo lebandla?`

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 4:9-13 uze unikezele ingqwalasela kwindlela abapostile abazotywe ngayo kwesi sicatshulwa. Oku kuzotywa kwabapostile kwahluke njani kunye neempawu zobunkokeli ezixatyisiweyo ehlabathini? Esi sicatshulwa sisifundisa ntoni ngendlela enokwahluka ngayo imigangatho kaThixo kwakunye nemigangatho yehlabathi?`

`Kuma-1 Korinte 4:16, uPawulos ubongoza amaKorinte ukuba alinganise yena. Ungakulungela ukulinganisa iinkokeli ezingabantu? Ukulinganisa inkokeli kungahluka njani ekuphakamiseni ngokungafanelekanga, nkqu nangokuyingozi?`
