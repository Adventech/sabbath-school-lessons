---
title:  Ukumiliselwa kuYesu
date:   13/07/2026
---

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 1:10. Ucinga ukuba wayethetha ukuthini uPawulos “ngokumanyana ngqiqweni-nye, nasicamangweni sinye”?`

Ukuyilwa kwamaqumrhu kwakubandakanya apha ukukhanyela komntu ukuzidibanisa noKristu (1 Kor. 1:10). UThixo wasibizela “ebudlelaneni boNyana wakhe, uYesu Kristu iNkosi yethu” (1 Kor. 1:9). INkosi yethu nguKristu, yaye kufuneka similiselwe kuYe. Ngoko ke, impendulo kwimibuzo buciko “Wahlukahlukene na uKristu? Kwabethelelwa uPawulos yini na emnqamlezweni ngenxa yenu? Nabhaptizelwa egameni likaPawulos kusini na?” (1 Kor. 1:13) “nguHayi” ohlokomayo! UKristu akahlukahlukanga. NguKristu owabethelelwa ngenxa yethu. Saabhaptizelwa “egameni loYise, loNyana, loMoya oyiNgcwele” (Mat. 28:19).

UPawulos ukhankanya ukuba thina “singumzimba kaKristu, namalungu awo” (Mat. 28:19). Nangona umzimba unamalungu amaninzi-ngalinye kuwo linomsebenzi walo-isekwangumzimba omnye. Ukuze umzimba usebenze kakuhle, ilungu ngalinye kufuneka lenze umsebenzi walo ngokwezakhono zalo. Lo mfanekiso ubonisa uPawulos ujonge kubunye, hayi ekufaneni. Ujonge kubunye phakathi kokungafani. Ngaphezu koko, ujonge kubunye nangona singafani.

Noko kunjalo, zonke iingcamango nezimvo mazibe phantsi koKristu, iNkosi yethu. Into yokuba uKristu uyiNkosi yethu yingcamango ebaluleke ngeyona ndlela kuPawulos kangangokuba ubhenela kuyo rhoqo, ekuvuleni ama-1 Korinte (1 Kor. 1:2, 7, 8, 9, 10). Ngoko ke, ngaphambi kokuba uPawulos ajongane nomba wamaqela neenkokeli ezingabantu, uqala agxininise ngelithi sonke sinoYesu oyiNkosi yethu. Ibandla alimiliselwanga kwiinkokeli ezingabantu. AmaKristu amiliselwe kuYesu.

Ugxininiso kubuNkosi bukaYesu kwiindima zokuqala zama-1 Korinte lusinceda siqonde into eyayithethwa nguPawulos ngala mazwi: “Nimanyene ngqiqweni-nye, nasicamangweni sinye” (1 Kor. 1:10). Igama lesiGrike eliguqulwe “njengokumanyana” lisuka kwisenzi _ukatartizō_, elithetha ukuba into izakubuyiselwa kwimeko yayo efanelekileyo. Xa amaqumrhu esakhiwa kujikelezwe iinkokeli ezingabantu, ubudlelane ngaphakathi ebandleni mabubuyiselwe kwimeko yabo efanelekileyo, kwaye oko kunokwenzeka ngokumanyana kuKristu kwakunye nokufa kumna okuhamba nako.

`Kumashumi eminyaka edlulileyo, ezinye iindawo kwiBandla laBalindi zigxininise ukufundwa kweBhayibhile ngamaqela amancinane. Yintoni umahluko phakathi kwezintlu namaqela amancinane. Singakulumkela njani ukuba amaqela amancinane angaguquki abe zizintlu?`
