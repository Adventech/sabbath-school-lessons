---
title:  Ubulumko Nokukhula
date:   14/07/2026
---

Ukanti ke, imikhambathi iqhambuka ngenxa yokujongelwa phezulu kweenkokeli ezingabantu. Oku kusisigrogriso kakhulu kubunye bebandla kunye nokuphila kwamalungu ngokwasemoyeni, kuba imbonakalo egqwethekileyo yolungiselelo lobuKristu inokukhokelela ibandla ekuxabiseni ngokubaxekileyo iinkokeli ezithile, konke oko kube yingozi kwabanye. Imiphumelo yokuziphatha okunjalo ngumoya wokhuphiswano, onokuliqhekeza ibandla. Ngaphezu koko, ukuba iinkokeli ezingabantu siziphatha njengezisemqoka kubuqu bethu bobuKristu, sikumngcipheko wokushenxisa uKristu kwindawo efanelekileyo akuyo ebomini bethu.

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 3:1-4. Ukuchaza njani apha uPawulos ukungakhuli kakuhle ngokwasemoyeni kwamaKorinte?`

UPawulos uyibeka icace gcaa eyokuba ukuvuthwa ngokwakwamoya kukhokelela ikholwa ekuxabiseni ubulumko bukaThixo (1 Kor. 2:6, 7), obudluliselwa kuthi ngoMoya (1 Kor. 2:13), nobukhabanayo nobulumko besisizukulwana (1 Kor. 2:6), ubulumko babantu (1 Kor. 2:13). Obu bulumko bukaThixo butyhilwe eMnqamlezweni kaKristu (1 Kor. 2:1–4). Ngokuchaneke ngakumbi, ubulumko bukaThixo butyhilwe ekubandezelekeni kukaKristu, ukufa, kwakunye novuko. Ngoko ke, ngaphambi aqalise kwakho isibongozo sakhe sobunye uPawulos ufuna abafundi bakhe basivume isidingo sobulumko benene kwandawonye novuthwa kuKristu.

AmaKristu alumkileyo navuthiweyo ngabantu bakwamoya , hayi abenyama, abafani neentsana (1 Kor. 3:1). Bathelekisa izinto zakwamoya nezinto zakwamoya, kuba “izinto zakwaMoya . . . zipicothwa ngokomoya” (1 Kor. 2:13, 14). AmaKristu alumkileyo navuthiweyo atya ukutya okuqinileyo, hayi ubisi (1 Kor. 3:2; thelekisa namaHeb. 5:12). Ikholwa “elisezwa amasi alinamava elizwi lobulungisa, kuba lilusana. Kokwabakhulileyo ke ukudla okuqinileyo, abo bathi, ngenxa yobugcisa, babe nezimvo eziqheliswe ukucalula okuhle kwanokubi (Heb. 5:13, 14). AmaKrisu alumkileyo nakhulileyo awathi, “Mna ndingokaPawulos” okanye “Mna ndingoka-Apolo” (1 Kor. 3:4), ebhekisa kubantu abohlukeneyo.

Kakade, aba bantu, bafana nabo, “oogxa babo emsebenzini kaThixo” (1 Kor. 3:9). Thina, njengebandla, siyintsimi kaThixo, isakhiwo, netempile (1 Kor. 3:9, 16, 17). Sonke singabakaThixo ngoKristu (1 Kor. 3:11).

`Abe yintoni amava akho ngokuphoxeka kakhulu ngumntu obumxabise ngeyona ndlela? Ukuba ube nala mava, zifundo zini ozifundileyo kuwo?`
