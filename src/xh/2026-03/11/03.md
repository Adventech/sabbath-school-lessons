date:   07/09/2026

`FundaKorinte 8:1, 5 naku-2 Korinte 9:7, 9, 13, 15. Uthini umyalezo osemqoka kwezizicatshulwa?`

Intetho yokunikela ityhutyha-tyhutyha ama-2 Korinte 8 no-9: “Ubabalo lukaThixo . . . lunikezelwe” (2 Kor. 8:1); “bazinikela” (2 Kor. 8:5); “Elowo makanike njengoko agqibe ngako entliziyweni . . ., kuba uThixo uthanda umphi ochwayitileyo (2 Kor. 9:7); “Wapha abasweleyo” (2 Kor. 9:9); “Bamzukise uThixo ngenxa . . . isisa sinxaxheba yena” (2 Kor. 9:13); “Makubulelwe ke kuThixo ngenxa yesipho sakhe singenambali!” (2 Kor. 9:15). AmaKorite esibini 8 no-9 aqala aphele ngentetho yokunikela (2 Kor. 8:1 no-2 Kor. 9:15). Kufuneka sizifunde ezi zahluko zibini ngengcamango yokunikela engqondweni. Ziveza ubuncinane izizathu ezine ezimandla zokunikela iminikelo yethu.

**Umbulelo ngenxa yobabalo lukaThixo** (2 Kor. 8:1; 2 Kor. 9:14, 15). AmaKorinte esibini 8-9 aqala ngokubhekisa “kubabalo lukaThixo” (2 Kor. 8:1). Kumgama ongephi, uPawulos uthi, “Kuba niyalwazi ubabalo lweNkosi yethu uYesu Kristu” (2 Kor. 8:9). Ubabalo lukaThixo noKristu luchazwa njengesizathu esisemqoka emntwini sesenzo sokunikela. UThixo wenza okukhulu ngeyona ndlela ngokusinika uKristu. Ngokunikela ngezipho zethu ukuphindisa, samkela ubabalo lukaThixo ebomini bethu.

Njengengcamango yokunikela, igama “ubabalo” (_ucharis_ ngokwesiGrike) likwavela ngokuphindaphindeneyo kuma-2 Korinte 8-9. Kwangokunjalo, igama _ucharis_ livela ekuqaleni nasekuphele kwesi sahluko (2 Kor. 8:1; 2 Kor. 9:14, 15). UPawulos usebenzisa eli gama ngeentsingiselo ezohlukeneyo kwesi sicatshulwa ukugxininisa ukuba ubabalo lukaThixo ebomini bethu lukhokelela kubabalo ngakwabanye kwakunye nasekubuleleni.

**Ulangazelelo lokulandela umzekelo kaYesu** (2 Kor. 8:9). UYesu wayesisityebi waza waba lihlwempu (khumbula ukuba le yimifuziselo yokubakho kwaKhe kwasephakadeni waza weza ebuntwini, ngokwahlukeneyo). Inye kuphela ekweenzeka ngayo oko: Yena waanikela konke. Ngokwakuthi, ngokwabelana ngeminikelo yethu, sibonelela ngeendlela zokuba abanye bamazi uKristu.

**Ulangazelelo lokwabelana ngeentsikelelo zikaThixo** (2 Kor. 9:10, 11). Sinikezela kwabanye kuphela ngenxa yokuba siqale samkela kuThixo. Yena uyasityebisa ukuze sibe nesisa.

**Uthando olusulungekileyo** (2 Kor. 8:8, 24). Ukunikela luvavanyo lothando olusulungekileyo nolokwenyani. Bobona bungqina baneleyo bokuba uthando luhleli entliziyweni yomntu. Ukusebenzisa intetho yesiNgesi, “yokwenza ngaphezu kokuthetha.”

`Unesisa kangakanani? Ujonge eMnqamlezweni, kungakanani okunikelayo xa kuthelekiswa noko unokukunikela?`
