`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 8:1-5. Sesiphi isizathu esinokuba sasingunobangela wentumekelelo yabaseMakedoni ukuba banikele iminikelo yabo ngesoosisa?`

Isimo esihle sabaseMakedoni sinokubonwa ngeendlela eziliqela.

Okokuqala, **baanikela ngovuyo oluphuphumayo** (2 Cor. 8:2). UPawulo uthi abaseMakedoni “ukuphuphuma kovuyo lwabo, nobuhlwempu babo obondeleyo, kwaphuphumela ekutyebiseni ukunyanisa kwabo (2 Kor. 8:2). Kamva ukhankanya ukuba “uThixo uthanda umphi ochwayitileyo” (2 Kor. 9:7). Igama lesiGrike eli chazwa “njengochwayitileyo” livela apha kuphela kwiTestamente eNtsha. Igama elivela kumnombo omnye livela kwenye indawo: “Owenza inceba makayenze inceba echwayitile” (Rom. 12:8). Amagama asuka kulo mnombo wamagama ngamanye amaxesha avela kwimibhalo eyongezelelekileyo yeBhayibhile, ngemo yokuchulumanca nokonwaba. Kuma-2 Korinte 9:7, ukuba ngumphi ochwayitileyo kuthetha ukunikela okungekho mathidala.

Okwesibini, **baanikela ngesisa** (2 Cor. 8:2). Ngaphambi kokuba uPawulos akhankanye isisa sabaseMakhedoni, uqale wabhekisa “kubuhlwempu obuqatseleyo” babo. Igama “isisa” (ngesiGrike, _nguhaplotētos_) livela kabini ngaphezulu kuma-2 Korinte 8 no-9. UPawulos uthi, “Nizakutyetyiswa ngeendlela zonke ukuze nibe nesisa kwiimeko zonke” (2 Kor. 9:11, kugxininisiwe), into ethetha ukuthi siyaphiwa ukuze nathi ngoko siphe. Ukongeza, ukhankanya “isisa segalelo lenu” (2 Kor. 9:13, kugxininisiwe). Kwesi sicatshulwa, ukuba nesisa ekubeni negalelo yindlela yokuvakalisa igospile kaKristu.

Okwesithathu, **baanikela “ngokuqhutywa yintliziyo”** (2 Kor. 8:3). Oku kuthetha ukuthi baanikela ngokuzithandela. Oku kuncomeka ngakubmi xa ubani ebona ukuba abazange banikele bethatha kwintabalala yabo, kuba imithombo yabo yayishokoxeke ngokumandla. UPawulos usebenzisa le ngcamango inye ukuphawula intumekelelo kaTito yokutyelela amaKorinte. Waaya eKorinte ngokuzithandela (2 Kor. 8:17).

Okwesine, baanikela benento ethi ukunikela kuyinyhweba (2 Cor. 8:4). Esi simo siyabonakala kwisicelo sabaseMakedoni sokuthabatha inxaxheba ekuqokeleleni. “Besibongoza kakhulu ngenxa yelungelo lokwabelana ngolulungiselelo” (2 Kor. 8:4).

Okokugqibela, **baathabatha inxaxheba ekuqokeleleni njengesenzo sokuzinikela** (2 Kor. 8:5). UPawulos uthi, “Bathi kuqala bazinikela eNkosini, baza kamva bazinikela nakuthi ngokuthanda kukaThixo” (2 Kor. 8:5). Ukuzinikela eNkosini kukhokelela ekuzinikeleni kwabanye. AbaseMakedoni bakwandisa ukubandakanyeka kwabo kuvangelo bagqithela ngaphaya koncedo lwezezimali. Oko kukuthi, ukunikela kwakunye nokuba nesisa akubophelekanga emalini kuphela.
