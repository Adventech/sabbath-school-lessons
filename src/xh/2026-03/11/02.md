---
title:  Umzekelo kaYesux
date:   06/09/2026
---

Umxholo wama-2 Korinte 8 no-9 unento yokwenza nokukhuthaza kukaPawulos amalungu eKorinte ukuba agqibe unyuso-mali yamabandla akwaYuda ahlelelekileyo. Kuyabonakala ukuba, babesele bezibophelele ekwenzeni oko (2 Kor. 8:10, 11; 2 Kor. 9:5; naku-1 Kor. 16:1–4), kodwa imiba yobudlelane obuphakathi kwabo noPawulos yazenza nzima izinto. Emva kokushukuxa le miba (2 Korinte 1–7), uPawulos ubhenela ekuqukunjelweni kwaloo msebenzi (2 Korinte 8–9).

Ekuqaleni, uPawulos ubhenela kumzekelo wabaseMakedoni (2 Kor. 8:1–7), ababuhlwempu babo buqatsele kungazange kubathintele ekuphuphumeni “kubutyebi besisa” (2 Kor. 8:2). Ewe, ubuhlwempu nesisa zinokuhamba kunye. Noko kunjalo, esi sisa sibukekayo sabaseMakedoni kuphela kukubonakaliswa kwesisa sikaYesu ekuzinikeleni kwaKhe ngenxa yethu (2 Kor. 8:8–15).

`Funda kuma-2 Korinte 8:9. Esi sicatshulwa sisixelela ntoni ngomzekelo kaYesu?`

Intetho kaPawulos kuma-2 Korinte 8:9 yenye yezicatshulwa ezizezona zimangalisayo, zinamandla, nezinzulu kwiBhayibhile iphela. UPawulos ubalisa ibali lephulo likaYesu ngokongiwa okumangalisayo kwamazwi. Ininzi ngeyona ndlela imfundiso apha. Eli libali lenkululo, koko kwindima enye kuphela.

Okutsala umdla ngakumbi kukuba eli bali libaliswa ngentetho yezezimali. Ewe, uYesu wayesisityebi. Ukutyeba kwaKhe kusingisele kubukho baKhe kwangaphambili ezulwini (Yohane 17:5). Waakhetha ukuba lihlwempu ngokuncama ubuqaqawuli bezulu eze kweli hlabathi leentsizi. Waaba lihlwempu poqo (Luka 9:58). Nangona wayelingana noThixo, Yena “wazihluba, Wathabatha ubume bomkhonzi, enziwe wafana nabantu” (Fil. 2:7). “Wathi ngemo efunyenwe enjengomntu, wazithoba, waba ngolulamileyo, wada wesa ekufeni, ukufa ke kwasemnqamlezweni” (Fil. 2:8).

UYesu waanikela ngobomi baKhe ukuze sihlale naYe ngonaphakade. Umnikelo waKhe wawunenjongo: usindiso lwethu. Ubugosa novangelo zihamba kunye. AmaKorinte esibini 8-9 abalisa ibali lomnikelo othile wemali, kodwa eli bali lisekelezelwe kuYesu. Kule veki, sizakubona imimiselo eyimfundiso enxulumene nesenzo sokunikela izipho ezisekelezelwe ekuzinikeleni kukaKristu ngokwaKhe.

`Gxila ekuzalweni, ekuphileni, ekufeni, nasekuvukeni kukaYesu. Xa uqonda ukuba konke oku kwakusenzelwa wena ukuze ube nethemba le nto engaphaya kokukuphila kalusizi apha, kufanele ukuba njani ukusabela kwakho?`
