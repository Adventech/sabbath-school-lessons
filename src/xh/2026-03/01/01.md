### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
1 Kor. 1:1, Gal. 1:1, Zenzo 17:16–34, 1 Kor. 5:9–11, Zenzo 18:4–10, 2 Kor. 2:4.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Yathi ke iNkosi ngombono ebusuku kuPawulos, Musa ukoyika, thetha ungathi tu; ngokuba mna ndinawe, kanjalo akukho namnye uya kukusa sandla, akuphathe kakubi; ngokuba ndinabantu abaninzi kulo mzi’ ” (Zenzo 18:9, 10).

Umvangeli omkhulu wasemaNgesini uWilliam Carey wayedla ngokuthi wayethunga izihlangu ukuhlawula iindleko zakhe, kodwa owona msebenzi wakhe ungundoqo yayi kukuzuza imiphefumlo.

Ngokunjalo, uPawulos wayesebenza njengomenzi-ntente ukuze aphile (Zenzo 18:1–3), kodwa owona msebenzi wakhe wokwenyani, kakade, yayi kukuzuzela abantu kuKristu.

Kule veki, sizakufumana amagqabantshintshi olungiselelo lukaPawulos kwibandla elikwisixeko saseKorinte. Ibandla, njengoko sizakubona, laligutyungelwe ziingxaki, noninzi lwazo lwalungahlukanga koko ibandla lethu lijamelene nako namhlanje, phantse kwiminyaka engamawaka amabini emva koko. Ngokwenene, nabani na okhe wasebuKrestwini nokuba lixesha elingakanani na, okanye obandakanyeke kumsebenzi webandla, angawubuza umbuzo: Ukhe walifumana iqela lamaKristu elingenazo naziphi na ingxaki? Impendulo, kakade, iqinisekile.

UPawulos ujamelene nemicela-mngeni eKorinte kodwa ukwenza oko ngesigidimi soMnqamlezo (1 Kor. 2:2). Ukunyaniseka kwesi sigidimi isekwayindlela yokujamelana nemicela-mngeni esinayo namhlanje. Njengoko sizakubona kule veki nakuyo yonke le kota, isigidimi samaKorinte 1 no-2 sibandakanya nathi aba.

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Julayi 4._
