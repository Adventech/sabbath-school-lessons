---
title:  Ukusuka eAtene Ukusinga eKorinte
date:   29/06/2026
---

`Funda kwiZenzo 17:16-34. Wayephi uPawulos ngaphambi kokuba aye eKorinte, kwaye weenza ntoni apho?`

IZenzo 17:16-34 zidandalazisa ukushumayela kukaPawulos kwabaseAtene ngaphambi kokuba aye eKorinte. Kuyabonakala ukuba, akazange akucebe ukutyelela eAtene iZenzo 17:16–34 ngela xesha, koko waya khona ngoncedo lwezinye izihlobo ngenxa yenkcaso awaqubisana nayo eBhereya (Zenzo 17:13–15).

Abo bahamba noPawulos ukuya eAtene babuyela eBhereya benomyalelo wokuba uTimoti noSilas baye kuye ngokukhawuleza (Zenzo 17:15). IZenzo 17:16-34 zingoko uPawulos akwenzayo lo gama elinde bona. Uthetha ngoYesu endlwini yesikhungo, endaweni yembutho, kwakunye naseAreyopago. Ngokwenene wayengaphezi ukuthetha ngoYesu kwaye wayelisebenzisa lonke ithuba lokwenza oko.

`Funda kwiZenzo 18:1-11. Wenza ntoni uPawulos xa efika eKorinte nangexesha lakhe lonke lokuhlala kwesa sixeko?`

UPawulos waya eKorinte ngexesha lohambo lovangelo lwesibini. ULuka uyasixelela ukuba uPawulos waahlala pha unyaka onesiqingatha.

Njengesiqhelo, uPawulos uqala umsebenzi wakhe wovangelo endlwini yesikhungo (Zenzo 18:4–6). IZenzo 17:1, 2 zikhankanya ukuba eli yayi lisiko lakhe. Waalandela iqhinga elithi “umYuda kuqala” (Rom. 1:16, Zenzo 13:46), kwanjengokuba uYesu wayebayalele abapostile baKhe (bona kwiZenzo 1:8).

Ngethuba ekugqibeleni uSilas noTimoti bafika kuye eKorinte, “wayexinwe lilizwi, engqina kumaYuda ukuba uKritu unguYesu”(Zenzo 18:5). Kwaku kulemeko, kanjalo, apho waavakalisa loo mazwi ayindumasi okuba “ndigqiba kwelokuba ndingazi nto phakathi” kwamaKorinte “ingenguYesu Kristu, naye ke ebethelelwe emnqamlezweni” (1 Kor. 2:2).

`Singafunda ntoni kumsebenzi wovangelo kaPawulos eAtene naseKorinte ngokusebenzisa lonke ithuba ekushumayeleni igospile? Cinga ngamathuba akho okwabelana ngoYesu kwakunye nendlela owasebenzisa ngayo.`
