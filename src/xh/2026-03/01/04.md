---
title:  Isixeko saseKorinte
date:   30/06/2026
---

`Funda kwiZenzo 18:1-3, 1 Korinte 5:9-11 naku-1 Korinte 8:4. Yintoni esinokuyiqonda ngoqoqosho lwaseKorinte, ukuziphatha, kwakunye nobomi bezenkolo?`

IKorinte yayiliziko elibalulekileyo lehlabathi lamandulo, iyindumasi ngoshishino lwayo oluyimpumelelo. Isixeko esi satshatyalaliswa yiRoma ngo146 b.c. saza sakhiwa kwakhona nguJulius Ceasar njengephandle laseRoma ngo-44 b.c. Yile Korinte yaseRoma le ithi gqi kwiTestamente eNtsha. Ngexesha likaPawilos, iKorinte yayi yenye kwabakhuphisana neAtene kwaye yayide yayogqitha kwiinkalo eziliqela. IKorinte yayinamazibuko amabini abalulekileyo nawathi enza ukuba ukurhwebelana kwakunye nokuhluma kushishino lwayo lube lula.

Ngokwenene, uPawulos waakhetha iKorinte ngenxa yokubaluleka kwayo kwakunye nommandla emi kuwo oluncedo. “Ithuba lokusasaza igospile lathi lanikezelwa ngolo hlobo. Ukuba ithe yamiliseleka eKorinte, iyakudluliselwa lula kuzo zonke iindawo zehlabathi.”—Ellen G. White, Sketches From the Life of Paul, p. 99.

Ukongeza, ushishino lwaseKorinte olunempumelelo luzakwenza kube lula kuPawulos ukuba azixhase ngokunokwakhe ngokuvelisa nokuthengisa iintente lo gama enkqangaza igospile kwesa sixeko (Zenzo 18:2, 3). Ngokuqinisekileyo, umsebenzi wovangelo kwisixeko esingubhazabhaza nesinobutyebi awunangabi namicela-mngeni. IKorinte yayiphawuleka ngeyantlukwano eselubala yezonqulo (1 Kor. 8:5), ibonakala ngeziganga zayo eziliqela ezakhelwe ukunik’ imbeko koothixo abafana noApollo, uAthena, uAphrodite, phakathi kwabanye, kwakunye nonqulo loothixo bamaYiputa abafana noSarapis noIsis.

Ukongenza kuloqulukubhode wezonqulo, iKorinte yayikwaziwa ngokuthi gcobhoo kwezesondo. UStrabo, ingcali ngelizwe nesazi-mbali, ukhankanya ukuba ayeli-1,000 amahenyukazi angcwalisiweyo anikelwe kunqulo luka-Aphrodite kwitempile yakhe eseKorinte. Nangona intlaninge yeengcaphephe ikujonga oku ngeliso elikrokrayo zize eli bango zilidibanise nento engeyonyani esuka kuAthene ngokuchasene neKorinte, imibhiyozo yobuhenyu yayiqhelekile kwihlabathi lamandulo. Ukwaphukaneka kwezesondo kwakuyingxaki eKorinte, njengayo nayiphi indawo. Unqulo-zithixo nokwaphukaneka kwezimilo zaziyinxalenye yobomi bemihla ngemihla, kwaye le nyaniso ibuhlungu edandalazisa okukhulu ngomxholo wamaKorinte okuQala kwakunye nawesiBini.

`Kumsebenzi wakhe wovangelo eKorinte, uPawulos waajamelana nomcelimngeni woluntu olukunqulo-zithixo norheletyo. Yeyiphi imiceli-mngeni ekwinkcubeko yanamhlanje enokwenza kubenzima ukushumayela igospile? Singayoyisa njani? Ungakanani umahluko, ukuba ukhona, okhoyo phakathi kweKorinte kwakunye nezixeko zethu namhlanje?`
