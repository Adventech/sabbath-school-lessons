Funda kuEllen G. White, “Corinth,” pp. 243–254, in The Acts of the Apostles.

“Ekushumayeleni igospile eKorinte, umpostile waalandela indlela eyahlukileyo kuleyo yayiphawula imisebenzi yakhe eAtene. . . . Wagqiba ekubeni aphephe iingxoxo neentetho ezihonjisiweyo, kwanokuba ‘angazi nto’ phakathi kwamaKorinte ‘ingenguye uKristu, naYe ebethelelwe emnqamlezweni.’”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 244.

“UPawulos wayenomlinganiselo othile wempumelelo,” kodwa yena “wabuthandabuza ubulumko bokwakha ibandla ngezinto azifumene kwapha. Wayibona iKorinte njengomhlaba wokusebenza okrokrisayo, waza wagqiba ukuba ayishiye. . . .

“Uthe xa esacinga ukushiya isixeko ukuba aye kumhlaba othembisayo, . . . iNkosi yabonakala kuye ngombono wasebusuku, yaza yathi, ‘Musa ukoyika, thetha, . . . ngokuba ndinabantu abaninzi kulo mzi.’ UPawulos wakuqonda oku njengomyalelo wokuba ahlale eKorinte, kwakunye nesiqinisekiso sokuba iNkosi izakongeza kwimbewu etyaliweyo. . . . Ibandla elikhulu laabhaliswa phantsi kwebhanile kaYesu Kristu.”—Ellen G. White, Sketches from the Life of Paul, pp. 106, 107.

“Kushicilelwe ukuba uPawulos waasebenza unyaka oneenyanga ezintandathu eKorinte. Iinzame zakhe, noko kunjalo, zazingabophelelekanga ngokukhethekileyo kwisixeko. . . . Waayenza iKorinte undlu-nkulu wakhe. . . . Amabandla aliqela ngaloo ndlela amiswa. . . . Ukungabikho kukaPawulos kumabandla ononophelo lwakhe kwaabonelelwa ngokuyinxalenye ziimbalelwano ezinzima nezimandla, ezaye zamkelwa gabalala njengelizwi likaThixo. . . . Ezi ncwadi zazifundwa emabandleni.”—Ellen G. White, Sketches from the Life of Paul, p. 109.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo:**

`UPawulos wayeqinisekile ukuba yena ungumpostile kaYesu, kwanokuba ubizo lwakhe luvela kuThixo. Kutheni le nto kubalulekile ukuzazi ukuba singobani kwanokuba luyintoni ubizo lwethu?`

`Okwethutyana, uPawulos waziva ngathi uyancama kumsebenzi wakhe wovangelo eKorinte, aze asishiye isixeko. Yintoni eyamenza atshintshe ingqondo? Oku kungasinceda njani xa siziva ngathi singancama kwiphulo lovangelo? Lingakhona na, ngoko, ixesha lokwenza njalo?`

`Amalungu ebandla eKorinte ngokumandla aphenjelelwa yinkcubeko engqongileyo. Le yinyaniso erhabaxa phakathi kwethu namhlanje. Singakwazi njani ukuba sehlabathini (Yohane 17:11, 15) size singaphennjelelwa koko “kusehlabathini-inkanuko yenyama, nenkanuko yamehlo, kwakunye noqhankqalazo lobubomi (1 Yohane 2:16)? Zeziphi ezinye iindlela ibandla lethu eliphenjelelwa kakubi zizo kwiinkcubeko ezilingqongileyo?`
