---
title:  Ukufunda Kwimbali
date:   28/07/2026
---

Emva kokubonelela ngomzekelo wokuzincama kumava akhe, uPawulos uguqukela ngokuthe poqo ngakumbi kumba wonqulo-zithixo, ngandlel’ ithile, ama-1 Korinte 10 avelisa ingcamango yama-1 Korinte 9:27, nalapho uPawulos ahlomla ngelithi usebenzisa ukuziqoba ukuze angakhiqwa. Unqwenela ukuba amaKorinte alandele umzekelo wakhe, kodwa uYesu ungumzekelo otshatsheleyo (1 Kor. 11:1).

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 10:7-11. Zono zini awazenzayo uSirayeli entlango, kwaye kutheni le nto amalungelo ababewanikiwe esenza izono zabo ziqatsele ngakumbi?`

Kuma-1 Korinte 10:1-5, uPawulos ubhekisa kwimbali yabantu bakaThixo entlango. Ukujolisa kwilifu kwakunye nolwandle kuzisa ukukhokela kukaThixo, ubukho, nokhuseleko engqondweni. Kwelinye icala, ukutya nento eselwayo zimele umsindleko kaThixo. UPawulos ubhekisa kumava kaSirayeli elifini naselwandle njengobhaptizo, ngokuhambelana nobhaptizo lwamaKristu. Kwangokunjalo, ngokubhekisa ekutyeni nakwinto eselwayo, uPawulos ubhekisa kuMthendeleko.

Ngamanye amazwi, ama-1 Korinte 10 afundisa ukuba, ngandlel’ ithile, amaKristu aphila laa mava ayephilwa nguSirayeli. Noko kunjalo, uPawulos ukhumbula imbali kaSirayeli kuba akafuni loo mbali ukuba iziphinde. Phakathi kwawo onke amalungelo uSirayeli awayenawo, intlaninge yabantu yaarhalela izinto ezimbi ezifana nonqulozithixo (1 Kor. 10:6), kwakunye nombulo (1 Kor. 10:8). Yiyo lo nto, ngoko, “uThixo engazange akholiseke ngabona baninzi kubo” (1 Kor. 10:5).

Kulula ukukhomba ngomnwe kuSirayeli wamandulo uze uthi beenza izono ezibi. Noko kunjalo, uPawulos uxoxa ngelithi amaKristu asemngciphekweni wokwenza izono ezifanayo nangona enelungelo elikhulu lokwazi imbali kaKristu. Oku kucace gcaa kwisilumkiso: “Lowo uba umi, makalumke angawi” (1 Kor. 10:12). Ibinzana, “lowo uba” libonisa ukuba abanye ebandleni babengaqondi ukuba basemngciphekweni wokweyela kwezo zono. Ingaba sikulo mngcipheko namhlanje?

“Lowo uba umi, makalumke angawi.” Ngubani phakathi kwethu ongakhange abungcamle ubunyani besosilumkiso?

`IBhayibhile ithi uThixo akasoze asivumele ukuba silingwe ngaphezu koko sinokukuthwala, “owothi, kunye nesilingo eso, avelise ithuba lokuphuma kuso” (1 Kor. 10:13). Kutheni, ngoko, sisakufumana kulula ukuwa esonweni?`
