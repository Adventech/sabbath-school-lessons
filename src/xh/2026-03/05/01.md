### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
1 Korinte 8; Zenzo 15:20; 1 Kor. 9:1–6; 1 Kor. 10:5–22; Dut. 6:4, 5; Marko 12:28–31.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Ke ngoko nokuba niyadla, nokuba niyasela, nokuba nenza yiphi na into, zenzeleni uzuko lukaThixo izinto zonke” (1 Korinte 10:31).

AmaKorinte okuqala 8-19 azisa ingxoxo engezesondo (kwizahluko 5–6) emaphethelweni kwangaxa linye ethi izise ngalo iimpendulo zikaPawulos kwimibuzo ethile ebuzwe ngembalelwano (1 Kor. 7:1) evela kumaKorinte. Ezi mpendulo zizakongamela intsalela yama-1 Korinte.

Imo egqithayo yama-1 Korinte 7 ibonisa ukuba ukuziphatha gwenxa ngokwesondo (izahluko 5–7) kwakunye nonqulo-zithixo (izahluko 8–10) yimixholo ezalanayo. Ngokwenene, zisoloko zikhankanywa kunye kwiTestamente eNtsha (bona kwiZenzo 15:20, 29; Zenzo 21:25; 1 Kor. 6:9; Efe. 5:5; Kol. 3:5; IsiTyhi. 21:8; IsiTyhi. 22:15).

Ngokuthe gabalala, lo gama kuma-1 Korinte 5-7 uPawulos eshukuxa umba wokuziphatha gwenxa ngokwesondo, kuma-1 Korinte 8-10 eyona nkxalabo isemqoka kuye ngumba wonqulo-zithixo. Uyatsho ukuthi amaKristu mawazibaleke zombini (1 Kor. 6:18, 1 Kor. 10:14).

Kwiveki ephelileyo, sibonile ukuba ngokuba yitempile kaMoya oyiNgcwele (1 Kor. 6:19, 20), ubani kufuneka awubaleke umbulo. Kule veki sizakubona ukuba ubani angalubaleka unqulo-zithixo ngokwenzela “konke ukuzukiswa kukaThixo” (1 Kor. 10:31).

_Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Agasti 1._
