title:  Uthando Olungenakratshi
date:   27/07/2026
---

`Funda kuma-Korinte 9:1-6. Esi sicatshulwa sibonelela njani ngomzekelo ophathekayo wentsingiselo yokuzincama usenziwa luthando?`

Xa ukrwaqula, kukhangeleka ngathi ukuthethelela kukaPawulos ubupostile bakhe, kuma-1 Korinte 9, abunanto yakwenza nengxoxo engaphambili engolwazi luthelekiswe nothando. Ubani akakwazi kulibala, ngoko, ukuba iBhayibhile ekuqalekeni yayingabhalwanga yazizahluko. Into ayifundisayo uPawulos kuma-1 Korinte 9 ayiqhalwanga kumsebenzi ongaphambili. Eneneni, ama-1 Korinte 9 abonelela ngomzekelo ophathekayo wothando olungazicingeliyo ngakuKristu nangakubazalwana. Ngenxa yothando, uPawulos ushiya amalungelo athile.

**“Lokudla nokusela”** (1 Kor. 9:4). Apha ukudla nokusela zibhekisa kuncedo lwezezimali gabalala. Njengompostile, uPawulos wayenelungelo lokwamkela inkxaso ngezinto kwabo wayelungiselela bona. Ezinye iinkokeli zenkolo ngexesha lakhe zazidla ngokwenza kanye oko. Kodwa yena akazange; endaweni yoko, wazixhasa ngokwenza iintente (Zenzo 18:3).

**“Ukuphelekwa ngumfazi okholwayo”** (1 Kor. 9:5). Umpostile otshatileyo wayevumelekile ukuba athabathe uhambo lovangelo kunye nenkosikazi yakhe ngeendleko zebandla. Imizekelo wezibini ezivangelayo iquka uPrisila noAkwila (Rom. 16:3), uAndroniko noYuniya (Rom. 16:7). Kodwa ke uPawulos wayengatshatanga (1 Kor. 7:8). Wayenako ukutshata aze axhamle kwilungelo lokuphelekwa yinkosikazi, ngenkxaso yezimali yabo bobabini.

**“Ukungasebenzi” ukuze uphile** (1 Kor. 9:6). UPawulos noBharnabhas babenelungelo lokuzuza umvuzo ngenxa yomsebenzi wabo wovangelo (1 Kor. 9:4– 6). UPawulos wayephila ngokwenza iintente (Acts 18:3), kodwa asinalwazi lokuba wawuyintoni umsebenzi kaBharnabhas. Siyazi ukuba wayenesisa ngeyona ndlela (Zenzo 4:36, 37), yaye ngalo ndlela wayekulungele ukuzixhasa ngokunokwakhe.

Kuma-1 Korinte 9:7-11, uPawulos uvelisa ingcamango yama-Korinte 9:6 ukuze abonise ukuba kuselungelweni ukuba yena noBharnabhas bazuza into yokuphila ebandleni (1 Kor. 9:11, 12). INkosi ngokwaYo yaamisela, “abazazisayo iindaba ezilungileyo, ukuba baphile ngeendaba ezilungileyo ezi (1 Kor. 9:14; thelekisa no-1 Tim. 5:18). Noko kunjalo, uPawulos uthi, “Asalisebenzisa ke elo gunya” (1 Kor. 9:12). Ngaloo ndlela, uPawulos uzizisa buqu njengomzekelo wokuzincama (1 Kor. 9:1–18) aze axoxe ngelithi oku kuyinzuzo ekushunyalweni kwegospile eKorinte (1 Kor. 9:19–23).

`Zintoni izinto, nangona mhlawumbi izezakho, onokuba ngcono xa uzishiyile ukuze ube lingqina leNkosi elisebenza ngokukuko?`
