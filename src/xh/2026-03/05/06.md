---
title:  Ukoyisa Unqulo-zithixo
date:   30/07/2026
---

Kuma-1 Korinte 8:1-3, uPawulos uxoxa ngelithi uthando lumgcina ekhuselekile ubani kunqulo-zithixo. Le ngxoxo iyaqhubeka ize iphinde iveliswe kwakhona kuma-1 Korinte 10:23-11:1. Kuma-1 Korinte 8:3, uthetha ngothando lwethu ngakuThixo. Yena uthi, “Makungabikho namnye ufuna okukokwakhe ukulungelwa; elowo makafune nokomnye” (1 Kor. 10:24). Olu luthando ngakwabanye.

`Funda kuMarko 10:17-22 nakuMarko 12:28-31. Ezi zicatshulwa zibini yintoni ezinayo efanayo, kwaye zingena njani kwimeko ekuma-1 Korinte 10?`

UPawulos wenza kuma-1 Korinte 10 kanye oko uYesu waakwenzayo kuMarko 12:28-31; oko kukuthi, udibanisa kunye imithetho emikhulu emibini yomthetho: ukuthanda uThixo ngaphezu kwako konke, kwakunye nokuthanda abanye. Kwibali lendodana esi sisityebi (Marko 10:17–22), uYesu umanya ezintlobo zimbini zothando, aze akwenze oko ngokujolise ngokwahlukeneyo kuDuteronomi 6:4 (bona kuMarko 10:18) kwakunye nesigaba sesibini soMthetho (bona kuMarko 10:19). Ingxaki yendodana esisityebi kukuba yayithanda izinto enazo ngaphezu kokuba ithanda uThixo kwakunye nabamelwane bakhe (Marko 10:22). Wayexabise ubutyebi bakhe basemhlabeni ngaphezu kobutyebi bezulu. Wayexabise imali yakhe ngaphezu kwamahlwempu (Marko 10:21). Wayengumnquli-zithixo.

Elandela iimfundiso zikaYesu, uPawulos untluvisa ngelithi ummiselo wokuthanda uThixo ngaphezu kwako konke kwakunye nommelwane njengesiqu sakho kufuneka Kusetyenziswe kwezimeko zisezingcingeni azikhankanyayo kuma-1 Korinte 10:27, 28. Oku kuthetha ukuthi nkqu nezinto ezisemthethweni zisenokungabi lUncedo okanye ziphucule kuba zisenokuba sisikhubekiso kwisazela somnye umntu (1 Kor. 10:23). Le mfundiso ihlanganiswe ngobuchule kumazwi ”Zenzeleni uzuko lukaThixo izinto zonke (1 Kor. 10:31). Ngokuthi konke makwenzelwe uzuko lukaThixo, uPawulos ubonisa ukuba unqulo-zithixo lunokuzibonakalisa ngezona ndlela zahlukileyo, kuba nantoni ephanga uzuko ololukaThixo yedwa iluhlobo lonqulo-zithixo (Isa. 42:8).

Amazwi kaPawulos kuma-1 Korinte 10:31-11:1 asebenza njengesishwankathelo sezahluko 8-10. Uyibeka elubala eyokuba yena ebengafuni kulungelwa kwakhe, “koko ndifuna okwabaninzi, ukuze basindiswe” (1 Kor. 10:33). Leyo yindlela awaxelisa ngayo uKristu (1 Kor. 11:1).

`Ungafunda njani ukuthanda ngcono ummelwane wakho njengesiqu sakho?`
