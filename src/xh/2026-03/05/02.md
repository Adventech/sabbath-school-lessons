---
title:  Ulwazi Luthelekiswa Nothando
date:   26/07/2026
---

`Funda kuma-1 Korinte 8:1-13. Kutheni le nto uPawulos ethelekisa ulwazi nothando, kwaye uyintoni umxholo apha? Ngongoma ni ayivezayo?`

UPawulos usebenzisa umxholo wokutya okunikelwe kwizithixo ukuze ashukuxe umba onzulu: ukushokoxeka kothando ngakwabanye (1 Korinte 8). Umbandela wokutya okunikelwe kwizithixo ulahlula ibandla laseKorinte libe ngamaqela amabini. Abanye babekholelwa ukuba ulwazi lwabo ngokungabikho kwabanye oothixo lalubanika ilungelo lokutya nantoni na (1 Kor. 8:4). Aba babalulwa njengabo “bomeleleyo” (1 Kor. 4:10). Abo babeluchasa olu hlobo lokuziphatha babizwa ukuba “baswele amandla” (1 Kor. 8:9–12). UPawulo usebenzisa loo nkcazo ngenxa yokuba abazange boyise iimfundiso ezinenkolelo ezazibonakala kumava abo angaphambili obuhedeni. Xa bebona “abomeleleyo” besitya ukutya okunikelwe kwizithixo, babenokuqukumbela ngelithi ubuKristu nonqulo-zithixo ziyadibana. Ngenxa yoko, uPawulos wayengafuni abo “bomeleleyo” babe ngumqobo kwabo babuthathaka.

IBhayibhile isibona isenzo sokutya ukutya okunikelwe kwizithixo kakubi ngeyona ndlela (Zenzo 15:20, 29; Zenzo 21:25; thelekisa neSiTyhi. 2:14, 20). Noko kunjalo, uPawulos akawisi ntetho zingqongqo njengoko ubani ebona kwezi zicatshulwa. Oku kungenxa yokuba eyona nkxalabo isemqoka kuye inento yokwenza nokungabikho kobunye nto leyo inokubangelwa kukusetyenziswa gwenxa kolwazi. UPawulos akalugxeki ulwazi ngelithi lubububi ngokunokwalo; ndaweni yoko, uchasene nohlobo lolwazi olukhokelela ekukhukhumaleni nakwiyantlukwano ebandleni. Ulwazi olungenathando asilolwazi lokwenene konke konke (1 Kor. 8:2). Ulwazi lokwenene luvela kuphela xa ubani ethanda uThixo kwaye esaziwa nguYe (1 Kor. 8:3).

Ecaphula kuDuteronomi 6:4, uPawulos uyabonisa ukuba amakholwa mawayazi ukuba mnye kuphela uThixo (1 Kor. 8:4–6). Ngokusemdleni, ukwalandela kwale ngcamango inye ubani ayibonayo kuDuteronomi 6:4, 5, nalapho intetho yokuba uThixo wethu mnye ilandelwa ngumthetho “Uze uyithande iNkosi uThixo wakho.” Ngokufanayo kuPawulos nakuMoses, ulwazi olungenathando alunto yanto.

Beqinisekile ngolwazi lwabo, abo “bomeleleyo” babekholelwa ekuthini ukudla ukutya okubingelelwe kwizithixo akunabungozi. Njengoko sizakubona ngoLwesithathu nangoLwesine, uPawulos walamkela kubo elo lungelo phantsi kwemiqathango ethile. Noko kunjalo, ukuba oko kwakuba ngumqobo kwabo “babuthathaka” (1 Kor. 8:9), kufuneka iphetshwe. AmaKristu kumele aziqhelanise nokuzincama ngenxa yokuthanda uKristu nabanye.

`UPawulos uxoxa ngelithi, ngaphandle kothando, ulwazi lunokuba yinto embi (1 Korinte 8). Kukweziphi iimeko olunokuthi ngokwenene ulwazi olungenathando lube lubi?`
