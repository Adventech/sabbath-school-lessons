---
title:  Iiveki ezingama-70 kunye neeNtsuku ezingama-2,300
date:  16/07/2019
---

Igama “bamiselwe” elifumaneka kuDaniyeli 9:24, “Bamiselwe iiveki ezimashumi asixhenxe,” ngokuphandle sithetha ukuba, “Iiveki esingamashumi zinqanyulwe.” Nangona igama eliguqulwe njengoku “miselwa” lingasetyenziswanga kwenye indawo eBhayibhileni, liyafumaneka kwimibhalo yamaYuda, kwaye lithetha “ukunqanyulwa” kwinto ende. Kuba uDaniyeli 8 esizisa isiprofeto seminyaka engama-2,300, nesindawo yaso yokuqala inganikezelwanga kuDaniyeli 8, ngokwengqiqo kuyalandela ukuba xa isahluko esilandelayo (Daniyeli 9) sithetha ngeminyaka engama-490 “emiselweyo” okanye “enqanyulweyo,” “inokunqanyulwa” kuphela kwiminyaka engama-2,300 ekhankanywe kwisahluko esingaphambili. Kakade, yeyiphi indawo esi siprofeto esinokunqanyulwa kuyo ngaphandle kwesinye, neside, isiprofeto sexesha?

`Funda uDaniyeli 8. Sesiphi isigaba esinye sombono owanikezelwayo esingazanga sicaciswe (bona ngokukodwa uDan. 8:14, 26, 27)?`

Zininzi izizathu ezibangela ukuba isiprofeto seeveki ezingama-70 sikaDaniyeli 9:2427 nesiprofeto sokuhlwa kusisa okungama-2,300 sikaDaniyeli 8:24 zihambe kunye: (1) zozibini ziziprofeto zexesha; (2) isigama esisodwa “sombono” “nokuqonda” kuyazidibanisa (bona uDan. 8:26, 27 no-9:23); zozibini izicombululo zeziziprofeto zanikezelwa nguGabriyeli (bona kuDan. 8:16 no-9:21); (4) indawo ekuphela kwayo yombono engachazwanga ngokuhlwa kusisa okungama-2,300 (eguqulwa ngamanye amaxesha ibe “ziintsuku”) kuDaniyeli 9:14; (5) uDaniyeli 8 unombono ize kanjalo abe nokucazululwa okungagqitywanga kwawo, lo gama uDaniyeli 9 enokuconjululwa kuphela, kule imeko ukuconjululwa kwendawo ekuphela kwayo kaDaniyeli 8 engaconjululwangaeyimihla engama-2,300 yesiprofeto sikaDaniyeli 8:14, indawo ekuphela kwayo embonweni angazange ayiqonde uDaniyeli (bona kuDan. 8:27).
