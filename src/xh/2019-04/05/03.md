---
title:  Ngokuchasene nomoya woMthetho
date:  29/07/2019
---

`Funda uNehemiya 5:6–8 (bona ku-Eksod. 21:2–7). Kutheni uNehemiya evutha ngumsindo nje?`

Nokuba kunzima kangakanani na ukubuqonda namhlanje, ubukhoboka babuyinto eqhelekileyo ngokwenkcubeko kwihlabathi lamandulo. Umzali wayesenokuba likhoboka yena gaqa okanye athengise umntwana. Ngokwasekuhlaleni nangokwasemthethweni, abazali babenelungelo lokuthengisa oonyana neentombi zabo. Noko kunjalo, kuba uThixo engonikezela inkululeko, wasilawula esi senzo kwaSirayeli ngokuthi ababoleki-mali mabawakhulule amakhoboka abo qho emva kweminyaka esixhenxe. Ngalo ndlela uThixo wayekhusela abantu ekubeni ngamakhoboka ngonaphakade ekwabonakalisa umnqweno waKhe wokuba abantu baphile ngokukhululekileyo.

Nangona ukubolekisa kwakuvumelekile ngokomthetho, ukubiza inzala kwakungavumelekanga (ngemiqathango yeBhayibhile malunga nokuchasa ukubiza inzala, bona u-Eksod. 22:25–27; Lev. 25:36, 37; Dut. 23:19, 20). Noko kunjalo, inzala ababoleki-mali ababeyibiza yayincinci xa ithelekiswa noko kwakubizwa ziintlanga ezibangqongileyo. Imibhalo yaseMesopotamia yenkulungwane ye-7th ibonisa inzala ye-50 pesenti kwisilivere nee-100 pesenti yokutya okuzinkozo ngonyaka. Ngako oko ke, inzala ye-12 pesenti ngonyaka yayiphantsi xa uyithelekisa kwisenzo samazwe aseMesopotamia. Kodwa jikelele, ngokweLizwi likaThixo, eyona nto ingeyiyo eyayisenziwa ngababoleki-mali yayikukubiza inzala (Neh. 5:10), yaye okunika umdla, kunani ukuba abantu bayikhankanye lo nto kwisikhalazo sabo. Yonke enye into yayiqhelekile ngokwasekuhlaleni kanjalo ikwakhona kumabango omthetho. Ngoko ke kutheni uNehemiya “evutha ngumsindo” nje? Okuphawulekayo, akenzi nto kwangoko, koko usazikisa ukucinga ngalo mcimbi.

Indlela uNehemiya ajongana ngayo nalo mcimbi ngokukhawuleza iyancomeka kakhulu. Akashiyi sikhalazo sodwa ngenxa yokuba ingekokwaphula mthetho okanye samkelekile ngokwasekuhlaleni, nkqu “esithandekayo” xa usithelekisa nezenzo zelizwe. Yayingumoya womthetho lo wawaphulwe kule imeko. Ngokukodwa ngexesha lokutsala nzima kwezoqoqosho, yayiluxanduva lwabantu ukuncedana. UThixo ungakwicala labacinezelekileyo nabasweleyo, yaye kwafuneka athume abaprofeti ukuba bathethe ngokuchasene nobubi nolwaphulo-mthetho olwalusenziwa kumahlwempu.

`Kungaziphi iindlela esinokuthi, singaqondanga, silandele incwadi yomthetho logama sisaphula umoya okuwo? (bona kuMika 6:8.)`
