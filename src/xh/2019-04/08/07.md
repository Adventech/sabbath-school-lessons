Read Ellen G. White, “Consecration,” pp. 43–48, in Steps to Christ. “Ulungiselelo lomnquba wasemhlabeni lwalunamacandelo amabini; ababingeleli babelungiselela imihla ngemihla kwindawo engcwele, logama Kanye ngomnyaka umbingeleli omkhulu wayesenza umsebenzi owodwa wokucamagusha kwingwele kangcwele, ukuze kuhlanjululwe umnquba. Imihla ngemihla umoni oguqukayo wayesizisa umnikelo wakhe kumnyango womnquba aze, abeke izandla zakhe phezu kwentloko yexhoba, azivume izono zakhe, ngalo ndlela ngokomfuziselo azisuse kuye azidlulisele kwidini elimsulwa. Emva koko isilwanyana sasibulawa. ‘Kungaphalalanga gazi,’ utsho umpostile, akubikho kuxolelwa. ‘Kuba umphefumlo wenyama usegazini wona’ Levitikus 17:11. Ngale nkonzo isono, ngegazi, sasidluliselwa ngokomfuziselo kumnquba. Kwezinye imeko igazi lalingasiwa kwingcwele; koko inyama kwakufuneka ityiwe ngababingeleli, njengoko uMoses wayebayalele oonyana baka-Aron, esithi, ‘uThixo uninike lona ukuze nibuthwale ubugwenxa bebandla.’ Levitikus 10:17.  Zombini ezinkonzo ngokufanayo zazifuzisela ukudluliswa kwesono ukusuka kulo uzohlwayayo ukuya emnqubeni. .”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 418.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo**

`1. Cinga ngezithembiso ozenzileyo ozaphulileyo, nokuba wawunenjongo enyaniseke nenyameke kangakanani ukuba uzigcine. Ufunde ntoni kulo mava enokuthi, mhlawubi, ikuncede ikugcine ekwenzeni impazamo enjalo kwakhona?`

`2. Umnqophiso kukumiselwa ngokusemthethweni kobudlelane. Siyawaphula esinawo noThixo, kodwa usoloko ethembekile kwelaKhe icala, nokuba asinjalo kwelethu. Oku kukuqonda ukulunga nokuthembeka kukaThixo kungalitsalela njani uluntu kubudlelane obusondeleyo kunye naYe buze, ngalo ndlela, busindece siphile ngendlela ekufuneka siphile ngayo?`

`3. Cinga ngezihlandlo othe owathembakala ngazo kuThixo nezithembiso esinazo phantsi “komnqophiso omtsha” jonga Luka 22:20, Heb. 8:13, 9:15).  Kutheni, kubaluleke kangaka ukuqonda icebo losindiso, nesithembiso sokuxolelwa esinaso ngenxa yedini likaYesu, ogazi laKhe lasitywinela “umnqophiso omtsha?”`
