### Fundela Isifundo Sale Veki
Neh. 8:1–8, Dut. 31:9–13, Mat. 17:5, Zenzo 8:26–38, Neh. 8:9–12, Lev. 23:39–43.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Balesa encwadini, emyalelweni kaThixo, ngokucacileyo, beqononondisa; bakuqonda ke ukulesa oku” (Nehemiah 8:8).

Udonga lwaseYerusalem lwagqitywa. Ekubekweni kwamasango, amaSirayeli, phantsi kolawulo lukaNehemiya, awugqiba ngolo hlobo owona msebenzi ungundoqo. Ukugqitywa kodonga, iintlanga ezibangqongileyo zoyika zaqonda ukuba oku “kuvele ku . . . Thixo” (Neh. 6:16). Iintshaba zaqonda ukuba uThixo kaSirayeli ukho kuba, nangona amaSirayeli aba namava enkcaso nentiyo emangalisayo, awugqiba umsebenzi ayezimisele ukuwenza. Emva kokugqitywa kodonga, uNehemiya wonyula ibamba laseYerusalem (umzalwana wakhe uHanani) nenkokheli yenqaba (uHananiya). Omabini la madoda onyulwa ngokwentembeko, ukunyaniseka, nokuhlonela uThixo (Neh. 7:2) endaweni yomnombo. Udonga lwagqitywa ngenyanga ka-Elul (inyanga ye-6th; Neh. 6:15).

Yintoni eyayizakuba sisicwangciso esi landelayo somsebenzi? Izahluko ezilandelayo zikaNehemiya (Nehemiah 8–10) zichaza udederhu lweziganeko zenyanga ka-Tishri, inyanga ye-7th (Neh. 8:2). Kwezi ndima singabona imizekelo yendlela abantwana bakaSirayeli ababezimisele ngayo ukuthobela iLizwi likaThixo nendlela abalivuyela ngayo.
