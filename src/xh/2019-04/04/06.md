---
title:  Ukwenza “Umsebenzi Omkhulu”
date:  25/07/2019
---

`Funda uNehemiya 6:1–13. Kutheni uNehemiya ewubona umsebenzi awenza eYerusalem “njengomsebenzi omkhulu” (Neh. 6:3)? Aba yintoni amalinge kule imeko okummisa?`

Isahluko 6 sichaza amalinge amininzi ebomini bukaNehemiya. USanebhalati noGeshem bathi gqolo bethumela iincwadi kuNehemiya ukuze eze kubo phantsi kweqhinga lentlangano. Noko kunjalo, intlangano le yayikwithafa le-Ono, elalikumda wotshaba, yaza ngolo hlobo yanikezela injongo eyiyo yesimemo. USanebhalati, uTobhiya, noGeshem babona inyoba yethuba elizakuma de udonga lugqitywe namasango avalwe. AmaYuda anokhuseleko lokumkani wamaPersi, ngoko ke iintshaba zawo azinako ukuweyisa ngohlaselo olupheleleyo oluza ngaphambili.  Kodwa ukuba bangashenxisa inkokheli, bazakuyiphazamisa inkqubela okanye mhlawumbi bade bawamise amaYuda ngonaphakade. Abanikezeli. Nokuba uNehemiya akaphenduli, bayakhondoza ukuzama. Makube kwakuphazamisa kuNehemiya ukujongana nenkcaso nkalo zonke. Uphendula kubo ngokuthi, “Ndenza umsebenzi omkhulu” (Neh. 6:3).  Ngokwamanqanaba ehlabathi, uNehemiya wayesenza umsebenzi omkhulu njengomngcamli kakumkani, nowawungumsebenzi oliqhayiya, omnye yeyona isemanqwanqweni kwilizwe awayesebenza kulo njengomcebisi kakumkani. Kodwa ukwakha isixeko esasidiliziwe, oko kwakungenankangeleko yakubaluleka elizweni? Oko koku akubiza njengomsebenzi omkhulu? UNehemiya wawuqonda umsebenzi kaThixo “njengomkhulu” nobaluleke ngakumbi ngenxa yokuba waqaphela ukuba isigxina segama likaThixo sisemngciphekweni eYerusalem.

Kwakhona, xa uThixo wayemisela iinkonzo zomnquba, waseka wasungula ububingeleli. Ukuze agcine umnquba ungcwele yaye ubalulekile ezingqondweni zabantu, wavumela kuphela ababingeleli ukuba benze imisebenzi yangaphakathi etempileni. Ngokunokwethu, sisokole ukubona ubungcwele bukaThixo; ngenxa yoko, uThixo wenza umsindleko wokunceda amaSirayeli eze kwindawo enoThixo ngentlonelo. UNehemiya wayesazi ukuba iintendelezo zetempile zezikawonkewonke, kodwa hayi amagumbi angaphakathi. Ngamazwi akhe, ngokuhlangana etempileni, uShemiya akazibonisi nje kuphela ukuba ungumprofeti wobuxoki ngokucebisa into ephikisanayo nendlela kaThixo, kodwa uyazidiza kanjalo njengomngcatshi.

`Zeziphi iindlela thina, namhlanje, singenamnquba osemhlabeni, esinokubugcina ngayo phambi kwethu ubungcwele bukaThixo? Ukuqonda ubungcwele bukaThixo, ngokukhabanayo nokuba nesono kwethu, kusikhaphelela njani emnqamlezweni?`
