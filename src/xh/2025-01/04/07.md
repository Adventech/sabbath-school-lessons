Funda uEllen G. White, “ ‘The Sower Funda Ellen G. White, “The Beatitudes,” pp. 6–44, in Thoughts From the Mount of Blessing.

“Bonke abo babuqondayo ubunzulu bobuhlwempu babo basemphefumlweni, abaziva ngathi akukho nto ilungileyo kubo, banokufumana ubulungisa namandla ekukhangeleni kuYesu. Uthi, ‘Yizani kum, nina nonke nibulalekayo nisindwayo ngumthwalo.’ Mateyu 11:28. Uthi kuwe nanisa ngobuhlwempu bakho ngenxa yobutyebi bobabalo lwaKhe. Asilufanelanga uthando lukaThixo, kodwa uKristu, ingqiniseko yethu, ufanelwe, kwaye unako ngokugqithisileyo ukusindisa bonke abazakuza kuYe. Nokokuba ebeyintoni na amava akho adlulileyo, nokuba zityhafisa kangakanani na iimeko zakho zangoku, ukuba uzakuza kuYesu njengoko unjalo, ubuthathaka, athalala, yaye ungenathemba, uMsindisi wethu onemfesane uzakuhlangabeza usekude lee,  yaye uzakuthi wambu ngeengalo zaKhe zothando nengubo yaKhe yobulungisa. Usazisa kuYise sambathiswe ingubo emhlophe yesimilo saKhe. Uyabongoza phambi koThixo endaweni yethu, esithi: Ndithathe indawo kamoni. Sukujonga kulo mntwana ulahlekayo, koko jonga Mna. Ingaba uSathana wenza isankxwe ukuchasa imiphefumlo yethu, esimangalela ngesono, kwaye esibanga njengexhoba lakhe, igazi likaKristu lithethela ngamandla amakhulu.”—Ellen G. White, Thoughts From the Mount of Blessing, pp. 8, 9.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo**

`“Qwalasela oko impefumlelo engentla ikuthethileyo ngendlela, enkosi kuYesu, esimiswa ngayo phambi koBawo.“Usazisa kuYise sambathiswe ingubo emhlophe yesimilo saKhe.” Nokuba sityhafiseka kangakanani na ngamanye amaxesha ngeziphene namagqwidigqwidi ethu, okanye ngendlela esisoloko singabonakalisi ngayo kwabanye uhlobo lothando uThixo asithululela lona, kutheni le nto kufuneka sisoloko sibuyela kwiindaba ezimangalisayo zokuba samkeleka kuBawo kuba uYesu “usazisa kuYise sambathiswe ingubo emhlophe yesimilo saKhe”?`

`Cinga ngendlela umama awayesiva ngayo kwityala labafazi ababini abaya phambi koSolomon bebanga ukuba umntwana omnye ngowabo. Gqala kwakhona ulwimi lwemvakalelo oluchazwe koo-1 Kumkani 3:26. Oku kukuthi chapha njani ukukhanya kuhlobo olunye lolwimi olusetyenziswe ukuchaza iimvakalelo zikaThixo ngakubantu baKhe, kuHoseya 11:8?`

`Kuzo zonke iiGospile, sibonile ukuba uYesu wayesoloko echukumiseka zizidingo zabanye.Yaye wenza ntoni Yena? Wenza ngendlela eyaahlangabezana nezidingo zabantu. Zeziphi iindlela eziphathekayo onokuthi njengesiqu, okanye mhlawumbi nkqu njengeklasi, ezinokuhlangabezana nezidingo zabo badinga intuthuzelo?`
