F Ellen G. White, “The Reward of Earnest Effort,” pp. 285–288, in Testimonies for the Church, vol. 9.

“Konke oko kusididileyo kwizibonelelo zikaThixo kuyakucaciswa gcaa kwilizwe elizayo. Izinto ekunzima ukuziqonda ziyakufumana inkcazo ngoko. Iimfihlelo zobabalo ziyakutyhileka phambi kwethu. Apho iingqondo zethu eziphelelwayo ziqaphele kuphela ukudideka nezithembiso ezophuliweyo, siyakubona olona lungelelwano lugqibeleleyo noluhle. Siyakwazi ukuba uthando olungaphele ndawo lwaacwangcisa amava ayekhangeleka evavanya ngeyona ndlela. Ngokuya siqonda inkathalo ethantamisayo yaLowo owenza zonke izinto zisebenziselane okulungileyo, siyakuvuya ngovuyo olungathethekiyo zizaliswe nabubuqaqawuli.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 9, p. 286.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo**

`Ukhe wakhohlwa uzama ukuqonda izibonelelo zikaThixo? Ikuthuthuzela njani eyokwazi ukuba`

`Cinga ngoko uKristu wakuncamayo ukuze abe ngumntu nokuze afele eli hlabathi. Yongeza ukuphonononga into esiyixelelwa koku ngothando lukaThixo kwanokuba uThixo unako na ukuthenjwa. Yintoni engenye ebenokuyenza?`

`Yintoni ebaluleke ngolo hlobo “ngegama” likaThixo? Miphumela mini oku okunayo ngakwabo bethu bazibiza ngamaKristu? Kungaziphi iindlela athe amaKristu ngamanye amaxesha azisa ihlazo kwigama likaKristu, kwaye singenza ntoni kwiindawo esihlala kuzo ukubonisa abantu into ekukhangeleka kuyiyo ngokuphathekayo ukulandela uKristu?`

`Ekugqibeleni, nkqu nezona “mpendulo” zingcono zingqamene nengxaki yobubi aziphelelanga ngokwangoku. Singenza ntoni ngokuphathekayo ukusondela kwabo babulalekayo sibe zizenzeleleli zokususa ukubandezeleka kweli lizwe lo gama silinde isisombululo sokugqibela, kwingxaki yobubi esinokuziswa nguThixo kuphela?`

`Gxila ngakumbi kuIsaya 53:4, kwinto yokuba uKristu ethwele “iintlungu” “neentsizi” zethu. Yintoni eyenzekayo ngokusebenzisana eMnqamlezweni esinceda siqonde icebo losindiso kwanento ekwamdla yona uThixo ukusisindisa?`
