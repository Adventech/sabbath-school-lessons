---
title:  Ngumntu Olutshaba Lo Wenze Le Nto
date:  23/02/2025
---

`Funda kuMateyu 13:24-27. Lo mzekeliso unceda njani ukuba sibuqonde ububi obusehlabathini?`

UYesu ubalisa ibali lomnini-mhlaba ohlwaye imbewu elungileyo kuphela entsimini yakhe. Noko kunjalo, umdiza wakhula phakathi kwengqolowa. Esakukubona oku, izicaka zomnikazi zambuza, “ ‘ “Nkosi, ubungahlwayelanga imbewu entle yini na entsimini yakho? Phofu iwuzuze phi na umdiza? ?” ’ ” (Mat. 13:27). Oku kuyafana nombuzo obuzwayo namhlanje malunga nengxaki yobubi: Ukuba uThixo walidala ihlabathi lalunga konke konke, kutheni le nto kukho ububi kulo?

`Funda kuMateyu 13:28-30 ujonge ingcaciso kaKristu kuMateyu 13:37-40. Oku kukuzisa ukukhanya kwimvelaphi yembambano yelimiweyo?`

Inkosi iphendula kumbuzo wabakhonzi bayo: “ ‘ “Ngumntu olutshaba owenze le nto” ’ ” (Mat. 13:28). UYesu kamva uchaza lo “ ‘uhlwayele imbewu entle’ ” “ ‘njengoNyana woMntu,’ ” onguYesu Kristu ngokwaKhe (Mat. 13:37), aze achaze ukuba “intsimi ke lihlabathi” (Mat. 13:38), luze “ ‘utshaba ke olwawuhlwayelayo’ ” umdiza “ ‘nguMtyholi’ ”(Mat. 13:39), ebonakalisa ngokuselubala imbambano eselizweni phakathi koKristu noSathana. Kutheni le nto kukho ububi ehlabathini? Ububi ngumphumela wotshaba (umtyholi) oluchase inkosi. “ ‘Ngumntu olutshaba owenze le nto’ ” (Mat. 13:28).

Le mpendulo, noko kunjalo, ixhokonxa umbuzo olandelayo, “ ‘Uyathanda na ke ukuba siye siwubuthe?’ ” Ngamanye amazwi, kutheni ungabusiphuli ububi ngoko nangoko? “ ‘ “Hayi” ’ ” yaphendula inkosi “ ‘ “hleze nithi, xa niwubuthayo umdiza, nincothule nengqolowa kunye nawo. Yekani kukhule ndawonye kokubini” ’ ” (Mat. 13:29, 30; thelekisa noMarko 4:29). Ngokomzekeliso, uThixo ekugqibeleni uyakubuphelisa ububi, kodwa ukuwuncothula phambi kwexesha kuzakukhokelela kumonakala wesibini ongenakulungiseka owonakalisa okulungileyo.

`Zintoni ezinye  zeengozi zokuzama ukuncothula umdiza engqoloweni ngoku? Kwangaxesha linye, kutheni le nto oku kungathethi ukubethisa ngoyaba ububi esiqubisana nabo?`
