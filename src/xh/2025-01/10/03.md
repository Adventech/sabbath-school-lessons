---
title:  Inamba YesiTyhilelo
date:  03/03/2025
---

Eyona nkalo itshatsheleyo yezilawuli zezulu kule mbambano yelimiweyo ishwankathelwe kwincwadi yesiTyhilelo, apho umtyholi adandalaziswa “njengenamba enkulu” echasa uThixo ize “ilahlekise ihlabathi liphela” (IsiTyhi. 12:9, NKJV).

`Funda kwisiTyhilelo 13:1-8. Oku kutyhila ntoni ngobungakanani bolawulo lwenamba? `

Inamba (uSathana) akalwi nje kuphela noThixo (IsiTyhi. 12:7–9) (umzekelo, IsiTyhi. 12:1–6), kodwa uchazwa njengomlawuli osemva kwezikumkani zomhlaba ezitshutshisa abantu bakaThixo kuwo onke amaphakade.

Inamba “yalinika . . . amandla ayo, netrone yayo, negunya elikhulu” irhamncwa eliphuma elwandla (IsiTyhi. 13:2; thelekisa nesiTyhi. 13:5; IsiTyhi. 17:13, 14). Eli rhamncwa liphuma elwandle “lanikwa umlomo othetha izinto ezinkulu, nezenyeliso; kanjalo lanikwa igunya lokulwa iinyanga ezimashumi mane anesibini” (IsiTyhi. 13:5).

Ngoko ke, uSathana (inamba) unikezela ngamandla negunya elilawulayo kwirhamncwa (igunya lomhlaba lenkolo nepolitiki). La mandla asetyenziselwa ukuphanga unqulo olulolukaThixo. Irhamncwa lenyelisa igama likaThixo; likwalwa ngokuchasene, de leyise, abangcwele bakaThixo (abangcwele), ubuncinane kangangexesha. Olu lawulo negunya lehlabathi lonke lilinikwa yinamba, umlawuli ophangayo weli hlabathi.

Noko kunjalo, kukho imida ecacileyo ngakuSathana nezenzeleli zakhe, kanjalo, kuquka nemida yethutyana. “ ‘Ngenxa yoko yibani nemihlali, nina mazulu, nani ninomnquba kuwo. Yeha, abo bawumiyo umhlaba nolwandle! Ngokuba uhlile weza kuni uMtyholi, enomsindo omkhulu, esazi nje ukuba ixesha analo lincinane’ ” (IsiTyhi. 12:12).

USathana “uyazi ukuba ixesha analo lincinane (IsiTyhi. 12:12),” kwaye iziganeko ezichazwe kwisiTyhilelo ziqhubeka ngokwezigaba zesiprofeto, ezibonakalisa imida ethile (bona IsiTyhi. 12:14, IsiTyhi. 13:5) kulawulo lwalemikhosi ingendawo.

`Nokuba kunzima kangakanani kuthi ukuyibona ngoku, ekugqibeleni ukulunga kuzakubeyisa ngonaphakade ububi. Kutheni le nto kubalulekile ukuba singaze siyilibale le nyaniso imangalisayo?`
