### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Eksod. 20:1–17; Roma 6:1–3; Roma 7:7–12; Yer. 31:31–34; Mat. 23:23, 24; Yakobi 2:1–9..

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Musani ukuba natyala la nto mntwini, lingelilo elokuthandana; kuba omthandayo omnye uzalise umthetho” (Roma 13:8).

Lo gama babesajamelene nelungu eliyingxaki, umntu wathi kwiliso lebandla (church board) wathi kumfundisi, “Asinakwenza izigqibo ezisekelezwe kwimfesane.” Asinako? Umfundisi waxakwa wazibuza ukuba uthini nam umqondo wakhe ngoThixo nomthetho kaThixo. Imfesane ngokuqinisekileyo kufuneka ibe sembindini wendlela esijamelana ngayo nabantu, ngoku kodwa abo bagqwidizayo. Imfesane iyinxalenye yothando, njengoko amaRoma 13:8 esixelela, ukuba ukuthanda ummelwane wakho kukuzalisekisa umthetho.

Ukuba uthando ngokwenene likukuzalisekisa umthetho, ngoko ke kufuneka silumke singacingi ngomthetho ngendlela eyohluliweyo kumthetho okanye sicinge uthando ngendlela encothuliweyo kumthetho. KwisiBhalo, umthetho nothando zihamba kunye. Ummisi-mthetho ongcwelo luthando, yaye kanjalo, umthetho kaThixo ngumthetho wothando. Kunjengokuba, uEllen G. White eyibeka, umbhalo wesimilo sikaThixo (Bona kwi-Christ’s Object, p. 305.)

Umthetho kaThixo awuloqela lemimiselo ezizicatshulwa koko yimithetho nemiyalelo elungiselelwe ukuphumelela kwethu. Umthetho kaThixo, yinxalenye, yimbonakaliso yothando njengoko uThixo ngokwaKhe elubonakalisa.

_*Funda isifundo Sale Veki Ukulungiselela iSabatha, Matshi 29._
