---
title:  Ukunyulwa Nokwamkeleka KuKristu
date:  02/07/2023
---

Incwadi yombulelo ikholisa ukuquka inkcazo yezipho okanye izipho ezifunyenweyo. UPawulos uquka udederhu lwezipho Kwabase-Efese 1:3–14 xa ebulela uThixo ngeentsikelelo zeendaba ezilungileyo.

UPawulos uyambonga uThixo ngale nto yokuba uThixo “wasisikelelayo ngeentsikelelo zonke zikaMoya sikuKristu” (Efese 1:3). Into yokuba iintsikelelo zizezikaMoya (isiGrike, pneumatikos [numatikos]) inika into ethi ziza ngoMoya (pneuma [numa]), isalatha kwintsikelelo avala ngayo uPawulos, ethi ivuyele umsebenzi kaMoya Oyingcwele ebomini bamakholwa (Efese 1:13, 14).

Kwabase-Efese 1:3–6 kukho ulwimi olunika inkuthazo olungendlela uThixo asibona ngayo xa sikuKristu. Ngaphambi kokudalwa kwehlabathi, uThixo wasinyula sikuKristu wenza isigqibo sokuba siya kuma “singcwele singenasiphako” phambi kwaKhe (Efese 1:4, ESV; thelekisa noEfese 5:27) njengabantwana, oonyana neentombi, abaxabisekileyo ngokuhle kwako kokubini, ukuDalwa nokuHlangulwa kuKristu (Efese 1:5). Kususela phambi kokuba ilanga likhanye, yaye ilicebo laKhe ukuba size “samkeleke kOthandiweyo” (Efese 1:6). Ngamafutshane, yinjongo kaThixo ngathi ukuba sisindiswe. Silahlekwa lusindiso kuphela ngenxa yokwethu ukukhetha kwesono.

`Ibinzana elithi “kwezasezulwini iindawo” lithetha ntoni Kwabase-Efese (indawo ekuphela kwayo apho lisetyenziswe khona kwiTestamente eNtsha)? Kufunde ukusetyenziswa kwaso eli binzana. (Bona:ee Efese 1:3, 20; Efese 2:6; Efese 3:10; Efese 6:12; thelekisa ukusetyenziswa [kuka]“emazulwini”: Efese 3:15, Efese 4:10, Efese 6:9).`

Kwabase-Efese, ibinzana “kwezasemazulwini iindawo” okanye “ezulwini” lalatha izulu njengendawo yokuhlala kukaThixo (Efese 1:3, Efese 6:9), endaweni yamandla kamoya (Efese 1:10, 20, 21; Efese 3:10, 15; Efese 6:12), nendawo aphakanyiselwe kuyo uKristu, ngasekunene kukaYise (Efese 1:20). Amakholwa ayakwazi ukufikelela kwezi ndawo “zasemazulwini” apho iintsikelelo zikaMoya zinikwa khona ngoKristu (Efese 1:3, Efese 2:6). Nangona “iindawo zasemazulwini” ziye zayindawo yentsikelelo yamakholwa, zisekwayindawo yamandla [yamagunya] amabi alwa ukuba yinkosi kukaKristu (Efese 3:10, Efese 6:12).

`Gxila Kwabase-Efese 1:4, athi siye sanyulwa kuYe, uKristu “ngaphambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi.” Kuthetha ukuthini oko? Kusityhilela njani uthando lukaThixo ngakuthi nolangazelelo lwaKhe ngathi ukuba sisindiswe?  `
