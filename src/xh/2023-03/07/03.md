---
title:  Ukuba Kunye Njengomnye KoMnye
date:  07/08/2023
---

Ndiza kusebenzisa igama ‘umnqongo’ endaweni ka‘nye’ kwezinye iimeko, phofu la magama mabini esebenzisana, ‘umnqongo emi endaweni ka‘nye’]

`Zingaphi izixhenxe zemi“nqongo” azicaphulayo uPawulos ukuxhasa umxholo wakhe wobunye ebandleni? Ithini ingongoma afuna ukuyibethelela ngolu ludwe? (Efese 4:4–6).`

Uludwe lukaPawulos lwesixhenxe “seminqongo” luvakala okombongo kulo kwaye lunokuvakala njengengoma yokuqinisekisa elapha Kwabase-Efese. Uludwe luqala ngokukhankanya “iminqongo” emibini ikunye: “Mnye umzimba” (ebhekisa bandleni njengomzimba kaKristu, Efese 4:12, 16; Efese 1:23; Efese 5:23, 29, 30) no“mnye uMoya” (Efese 4:4). Owesithathu umnqongo li“themba elinye lobizo lwenu” (Efese 4:4; thelekisa: Efese 4:1).

Emva koko uludwe lunika isithathu esingaphezulu seziqalo,  “inye iNkosi” (ukubhekisa kuKristu), “lunye ukholo” (ukuthetha ngokukholelwa ngamaKristu, Efese 4:13; Kol. 1:23; Kol. 2:7; Gal. 1:23; 1 Tim. 4:1, 6), kunye no“lunye ubhaptizo” (thelekisa: Efese 5:26) phambi kokugqibezela ngenkcazo enabileyo kaThixo ethi “mnye uThixo onguYise wabo bonke, ophezu kwabo bonke, okubo bonke, ophakathi kwenu nonke” (Efese 4:6).

Yintoni ayigqithisayo uPawulos ngale nkcazo ibubonga ngoThixo uYise? Ngokuba engu“Yise wabo bonke,” uThixo unguMdali. Okunye kwisivisa kuchaza ukuba, wakuba udaliwe umhlaba, uThixo uhlobana njani “nezinto zonke,” kwizinto zonke azenzileyo. UPawulos akagxampuzi kwiinkohliso zonqulo loothixo abaninzi (okuthi indalo yonke inguThixo), okanye panentheism (exoxa ukuthi ihlabathi liqukwe kwinto enguThixo, nangona ingazi kakuhle ukuba injani loo nto inguThixo). Endaweni yoko, uvakalisa ukunyukela ngasentla (“kophezu kwako konke”), ulawulo olusebenzayo (“okhoyo kuko konke . . .”) nosondeleyo (“okuko  . . . konke”) kaThixo.

Qaphela kakuhle, iingcamango ezimbini ngobunye bebandla (Efese 4:1–6). Okokuqala, ubunye buyinyaniso kamoya, emile kule “minqongo” isixhenxe, inyaniso ekufuneka ivuyelwe (Efese 4:4–6). Okwesibini, obu bunye bufuna inzondelelo yethu ukubondla sibukhulise (Efese 4:3). Kuya kukholisa ukuba kubekho isizathu sokukhala ekusileleni kwethu ukubufumana obu bunye. Phofu ke, ekusileleni kwethu, kufuneka sivuye ekusebenzeni kukaThixo kuKristu ekulimanyeni ibandla, sivuye kubunyaniso bokwazi ngoThixo “”ubunye boMoya” (Efese 4:3). Ukwenza oko kuya kusinika amandla okubuqhubela phambili obu bunye, kodwa ngengqiqo entsha yokuba, ekwenzeni oko, siya kuba sifeza okaThixo umsebenzi.

`Funda Kwabase-Efese 4:4–6. Le ndawo ikwenza uzive njani? Kufanele ukukwenza uzive njani, ukwazi ukuba ithini ngobunye bethu kuYe nakunye naYe uThixo ngoKristu?`
