---
title:  Imfihlelo Ekade Iihliwe YeGospile
date:  31/07/2023
---

`Yimfihlelo ethini awayinikwayo uPawulos? Efese 3:1–6.`

Xa ufunda Kwabase-Efese 3:1–6, qaphela oku kulandelayo:

Okokuqala, uPawulos uyibhala le ndawo yeleta ebhekisa ngqo kumakholwa eeNtlanga kumabandla asemakhaya ase-Efese (Efese 3:1).

Okwesibini, uPawulos ubanga ukuba ungozuze into ayibiza ngokuthi “bubugosa bobabalo lukaThixo,” abunikiweyo “ngenxa yenu,” ngenxa yamakholwa eeNtlanga (Efese 3:2, ESV). Obu bugosa, okanye olu lungiselelo lobabalo, yindlela kaPawulos yokuchaza ukuthunywa akunikiweyo ukuba ashumayele iindaba ezilungileyo (“ubabalo lukaThixo”) eziNtlangeni (thelekisa: Efese 3:7, 8, ESV).

Okwesithathu, uPawulos ubanga ukuba imfihlelo ityhiliwe kuye, umxholo asele ebhalile ngawo eleteni (bona ngakumbi: Efese 1:9, 10; Efese 2:11–22), “imfihlelo kaKristu” (Efese 3:3, 4). UPawulos akanqweneli ukuba aqondwe njengomvelisi weendaba ezilungileyo, kodwa ulibeka ibango kulungiselelo olunikwe nguThixo lokuba azivakalise.

Okwesine, uPawulos akayedwa ekufumaneni isityhilelo esiphambili ngale mfihlelo, njengoko uMoya ekwayityhile “kubapostile nabaprofeti abangcwele” ngendlela edlula isityhilelo secebo likaThixo kwizizukulwana zangaphambili (Efese 3:5). Isaci abaprofeti apha sinokuba sibhekisa kwabo banaso kwaye besisebenzisa isipho sesiprofeto phakathi kwamabandla okuqala obuKristu asemakhaya kunokuba kubaprofeti beTestamente eNdala. Imfihlelo, eyayikade ifihliwe, ngoku ithe yaba yile singathi “yimfihlo esekuhleni.”

Ekugqibeleni uthi: “Le mfihlelo yeyokuba iiNtlanga nazo zikwazindlalifa, amalungu omzimba omnye, zikwanaso nesabelo kumadinga kaKristu Yesu ngazo iindaba ezilungileyo” (Efese 3:6, ESV).

UPawulos uyatshiseka ngeendaba ezilungileyo ngakumbi ngendlela ezibekwe ngayo ebandleni, elihlanganise bonke, amaYuda neeNtlanga. La maqela mabini aye azizitena zokwakha uluntu olutsha lukaThixo, uluntu olutsha kraca, ibandla (bona: Efese 2:14–16). Singathi, ngoku enziwe kumila kumbi ukusuka ekubeni zintshaba kuse ekubeni “ziindalifa kunye,” “ukuba ngumzimba kunye” (behlanganiswe kumzimba omnye, umzimba kaKristu), aba “nenxaxheba kunye” kwisithembiso seendaba ezilungileyo (bona: Efese 3:6).

`Zeziphi iingqondo ezingentle, ukuba zingakho, mhlawumbi emfihlakalweni, onokuba unazo ezichasana noku kuhlanganiswa [kwentlanga zonke] kufundiswa ziindaba ezilungileyo? Ungazisusa njani?`
