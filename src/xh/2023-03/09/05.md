---
title:  Ukurhwaphiliza Izisulu
date:  23/08/2023
---

UPawulos ugqibezela ileta eya Kwabase-Efese 5:1–20 ngezihloko ezibini zezikhuthazo, Kwabase-Efese 5:15–17 naKwabase-Efese 5:18–20, ayigqibe indawo ngomdla omileyo wobunyulu ngokwesondo. Isihloko sokuqala siqala ngemvuselelo, “Khangelani ngoko, ukuba ningathini na ukuze nicokiseke ukuhamba kwenu; ningabi njengabaswele ubulumko, yibani njengezilumko” (Efese 5:15), ebekwe ngenye indlela ethi, “musani ukuba zizibhanxa, kodwa kuqondeni ukuba kuyintoni ukuthanda kweNkosi” (Efese 5:17, ESV). Phakathi kweendima ezimbini kukho ubizo lokuba lisetyenziswe ngeyona ndlela iyiyo ixesha (Efese 5:16, ESV).

`Qahela iimvuselelo zikaPawulos zokuphila ngendlela ebonisa ubulumko obunomthandazo nobuqondayo (Efese 5:15–17). Uyintoni umahluko phakathi kokungahambi njengezidenge kodwa “ngokwezilumko”? kwakhona, kuthetha ukuthini “ukuzongela ixesha”? `

Kwabase-Efese, uPawulos uphindaphindile ukusebenzisa ufaniso oluqhelekileyo lweTestamente eNdala lo“kuhamba” kwindlela athi umntu aphile ngayo (Efese 2:2, 10; Efese 4:1, 17; Efese 5:2, 8). Apha usebenzisa ufaniso ukukhuthaza ubufundi obusezingqondweni. Kanye njengokuba kufuneka uqaphele ukunyathela kwakho, “xa uhamba endleleni enezigingqi, amakholwa kufuneka “ayijonge ngononophelo indlela ahamba kuyo” (Efese 5:15, ESV). Kuba Kwabase-Efese 5:15 kuhambela naKwabase-Efese 5:17, singayikhangela apho ingcaciso yokuba kuthetha ukuthini ukuphila ngokwabantu abalumkileyo. Asibukhangeli apha ngaphakathi ubulumko. Ukulumka kukufikelela ngaphaya kwethu, “ukuqonda ukuba iyintoni intando kaThixo” (Efese 5:17, ESV).

UPawulos ukwakhuthaza ubufundi obusezingqondweni ngomfanekiselo ocacileyo. Kwibinzana “nilisebenzisa ngeyona ndlela iyiyo ixesha” (Efese 5:16, ESV; thelekisa “nizongela ixesha”), uPawulos usebenzisa isenzi uexagorazō (thelekisa: Kol. 4:5). Sithatyathwe kwindawo yokonwaba likwimo yogxininiso yesenzi “ukuthenga” nesithetha “ukuxhumela izisulu” ezifumanekayo njengoko silindele ukubuya kukaKristu. “ixesha” apha ligama lesiGrike ukairos, elichaza umzuzu wethuba. “Ixesha” kude kuse ekupheleni lithuba lesithembiso ekufuneka lisetyenzisiwe ngokupheleleyo. Likwalixesha lomngeni kuba “imihla ayindawo” (Efese 5:16, ESV; thelekisa: Efese 6:13, Gal. 1:4) nokuba ngenxa “yendlela yeli hlabathi” ilawulwa “yinkosi yamandla omoya” (Efese 2:2, ESV).

Njengokuba amakholwa ekhangele phambili ekubuyeni kukaKristu, aphila kwixesha elinzima, alizobe uPawulos njengendawo eyingozi kodwa enomvuzo. Kufuneka aqaphelisise ekulisebenziseni kwawo ixesha njengabazingeli bezisulu ngexesha elifutshane lentengiso elinika izaphulelo eziphakame kakhulu. Nangona singenako ukuluthenga usindiso, umfanekiselo usafanelekile: Masikuthabathe ngokukhawuleza oko sikunikwa okanye sikuphiwayo kuKristu.
