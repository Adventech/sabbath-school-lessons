---
title:  Icebiso Kumakhosikazi angamaKristu
date:  27/08/2023
---

UPawulos uqala ngesicatshulwa esingundoqo, Kwabase-Efese 5:21, ehlanganisa Kwabase-Efese 5:1–20 naKwabase-Efese 5:22–33, apho axhasa khona ukuba amalungu ebandla alulamelane, elinye nelinye (thelekisa: Marko 10:42–45; Roma 12:10; Filipu 2:3, 4). Amakholwa kufuneka akwenze oko “ngokoyika uKristu” (Efese 5:21, ESV), kukokuqala kumaxesha amaninzi uPawulos eya kububona ubudlelane noKristu ngoboba bubaluleke kakhulu nobuqinisekileyo kumakholwa.

`Uthetha ukuthini uPawulos ngokukhuthaza amalungu ebandla ukuba athobelane elinye nelinye? Kufuneka siyiqonde njani le ngcamango? Efese 5:21.`

UPawulos ukwawamema amakhosikazi angamaKristu ukuba abalulamele “abawo abayeni, ngokwaseNkosini” (Efese 5:22, ESV), ecacisa ukuba uxoxa ukuthoba kwamakhosikazi iyileyo kumyeni wayo (bona naku-1 Pet. 3:1, 5). Xa uPawulos esithi amakhosikazi mawakwenze oku “ngokwaseNkosini,” ingaba uthetha ukuba inkosikazi imthobele umyeni wayo ngokungathi unguKristu; okanye, endaweni yoko, ingaba uthetha ukuthi uKristu ungoyena uyinyaniso noyena uphezulu emakuqwalaselwe kuYe ekuzithobeni kwayo?

Ngokubona Kwabase-Efese 6:7, apho amakhoboka kuthiwa mawakhonze “ngokwaseNkosini kungabi ngokwasemntwini” (RV), nabaseKolose 3:18, apho amakhosikazi kuthiwa makathobele awabo abayeni “ngokoko kulunge eNkosini” (NKJV), le mbono yamva, yemayikhethwe. Amakhosikazi nawo angamakholwa ekufuneka, ekugqibeleni, nawo amzukise uKristu ngaphezu kwabayeni bawo.

Kwezi leta zombini, eyaseKolose neyabase-Efese, uKristu ubonwa eyiNtloko yebandla, elingumzimba waKhe (Efese 1:22, Efese 5:23, Kolose 1:18): “UKristu uyintloko yebandla; ekwanguMsindisi womzimba” (Efese 5:23). Ngokwemfaniso, umyeni uyintloko yenkosikazi” (Efese 5:23), ngokuthembeka kwebandla kuKristu likhonza njengomfanekiselo wokunyaniseka kwenkosikazi kumyeni wayo. Eli binza licatshulweyo libona umtshato onothando, onenkathalo, hayi ongasebenziyo (okanye impasalala). Le ndima akufanele ukuba ichazwe ngendlela enokuthi ivumele uhlobo lwempatho-mbi ekhayeni.

`Ngokoku sisanda ukukufunda, kungani ukuba kubaluleke kakhulu ukulikhumbula eli cebiso? Ukuba umyeni ungumntu “orhabaxa, ongqwabalala, olwayo, onomona, noyindinisa, makangaze alithethe nokulithetha igama elithi umyeni uyintloko yomfazi, nokuba kufuneka ayithobele kuyo yonke into; kuba ayiyiyo iNkosi, asinguye nomyeni esimfaneleyo eso saci.”—Ellen G. White, The Adventist Home, p. 117.`
