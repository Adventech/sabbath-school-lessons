---
title:  Amakhoboka KaKristu
date:  06/09/2023
---

`Ufuna enze ntoni uPawulos amakhoboka angamaKristu kwimiyalelo yakhe ecakaciweyo kuwo? Efese 6:5–8.`

UPawulos uwacela amakhoboka angamaKristu ukuba azithobele iinkosi zawo, anike inkonzo ephuma entliziyweni nebalaseleyo. Okuqaphelekayo kukuphindaphinda kwakhe ukuthetha ngokuhle okumi endaweni yoku athi mawakwenze. Awayi kubeka inkosi yawo [yamakhoboka] endaweni kaKristu, ayinike intlonipho okanye intembeko elunge kuKristu. Endaweni yoko, ekuzinikeleni nasekunyanisekeni okukhuthazwa yinkonzo ephuma entliziyweni nebalaseleyo, aya kuba amisa uKristu, iNkosi yenkosi yamakhoboka. Ekukhuthazeni esi simiso sibalulekileyo, uPawulos unika ingqiqo eyenziwe yantsha yobuKristu yolwalamano nenkosi yamakhoboka.

Qaphela iindlela eziliqela uPawulos acinezela ngazo esi simiso phezu kwawo [amakhoboka]:

* Ezawo [amakhoboka] iinkosi ziyathotywa nguPawulos “njengeenkosi zasemhlabeni,” esalatha kuqobo lweNkosi yasezulwini (Efese 6:5, ESV, ucinezelo lufakelwe).
* Kufuneka asebenze “ngokoyika nokungcangcazela, ngentliziyo enyanisekileyo “njengoko ninokwenza kuKristu” (Efese 6:5, ESV, ucinezelo lufakelwe).
* UPawulos uliqaphela eli bamba ngokucace kakhulu ekuxoxeni ngokuba amakhoboka angamaKristu kufuneka kuwo inkonzo eyiyo njengamakhoboka, kungekuko kwiinkosi zawo, kodwa “njengamakhoboka kaKristu” (Efese 6:6, NIV).
* Ekwenzeni umsebenzi wawo, kufuneka akwenze “ukuthanda kukaThixo ngokuphuma entliziyweni,” enikela inkonzo ephuma entliziyweni ekhokelelwe kuThixo (Efese 6:6, NIV).
* UPawulos ufuna inkonzo entle yentumekelelo enikelwa “njengaseNkosini kungekuye umntu” (Efese 6:7, ESV).

Ngenkonzo yawo ephuma entliziyweni, amakhoboka angalindela umvuzo wawo opheleleyo kuKristu xa ebuya. Awenzile umsebenzi wawo kwaye angakwazi ukulindela umvuzo kuYe, ingcamango enomtsalane ngendlela eyodwa kumakhoboka angazange ahlawulwa. Ikhoboka lalinokuva lingaxatyiswanga okanye kakubi kakhulu yinkosi yasemhlabeni (thelekisa: 1 Pet. 2:19, 20). Ikhoboka elikholwayo, lona, lineNkosi ekumamelayo, ikuqaphele, “konke okulungileyo okwenziwa ngumntu ngamnye” (Efese 6:8, NASB), inike umvuzo oqinisekileyo.

`Nokuba besinganqwena kangakanani ukuba sibe isiBhalo sasigweba ekuhleni esi senzo sibi kakhulu [ngala makhoboka,] asikwenzi oko. Sekunjalo, yeyiphi imithetho-siseko ekhoyo esiyikhuphayo kule meko ngendlela esalamana ngayo nabantu esisebenza nabo kweyethu imeko? `
