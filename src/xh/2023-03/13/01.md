### Fundela Isifundo Sale Veki
Efese 6:10–20; 1 Pet. 4:1; 1 Pet. 5:8; Isa. 59:17; Isa. 52:8–10; 1 Thess. 5:16–18.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Kwiimeko zonke qubulani ikhaka loxolo, lona nothi ngalo nibe nako ukuzicima zonke iintolo ezinomlilo zakhe ongendawo; nithabathe isigcina-ntloko sosindiso, nekrele loMoya, eliliLizwi likaThixo” (Kwabase-Efese 6:16, 17, ESV).

Encwadini yakhe yomthandazo, Uhambo Lomhambi, awayibhala esentolongweni, uJohn Bunyan ubhala ngomfo ogama linguMkristu. Uthi uMkristu waxhagwa esiwa kwindawo ekugcinwa kuyo izixhobo zokulwa. Zazikho zonke iintlobo ezalungiswa yinkosi ukuba zisetyenziswe ngabahambi – ikrele, ikhaka, umnqwazi, isigcina-sifuba, wonke umthandazo, nezihlangu ezingagugiyo. Okwakulapho kwakwanele wonke amadoda awayesebenzela inkosi, njengoko zazikho neenkwenkwezi zezulu ezanele isihlwele. “Ngaphambi kokuba ahambe uMkristu, uphinda angeniswe kule ndlu yezixhobo apho “wanxitywa izixhobo ukususela entloko kuye elunyaweni, nto leyo eyayiqinisekisa ukuba akayi kulimala nokuba angahlangana nokuhlaselwa endleleni.”

Umbhalo kaBunyan ngowe-1678 kuzisa engqondweni uxwebhu olwabhalwa kwi-1600 leminyaka ngaphambili ngumpostile uPawulos, incwadi eya kwabase-Efese, ekwayibhala naye esentolongweni. Kuyo apho, umvangeli omkhulu ongumpostile wayenombono womkhosi omkhulu, ibandla lihambele kwindawo yezixhobo zikaThixo, linxiba izixhobo zezulu, ngokwesiGrike, isixhobo esisuka entloko siye ezinyaweni. Indawo yezixhobo zikaThixo izele ntli zizo zonke iintlobo zezixhobo nezilingene nokuba mangaphi amasoldati, anxiba intsimbi yasentla ukuqala entloko kuye eluzwaneni,” xa ephuma ukuya kuqala imfazwe yoxolo egameni laKhe [uYesu].

_*Funda esi sifundo ukulungiselela iSabatha kaSeptemba 23._
