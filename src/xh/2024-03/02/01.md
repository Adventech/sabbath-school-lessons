### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Marko 1:16–45, Yohane 1:29–42, Marko 5:41, Luka 6:12, Levitikus 13.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Wathi kubo uYesu,Yizani apha nindilandele; ndonenza nibe ngababambisi babantu’ ” (Marko 1:17).

Gospile nganye izisa ukuqaleka kolungiselelo lukaYesu ngendlela ethile. UMateyu umthi thaca uYesu ebiza abafundi aze emva koko ashumayele iNtshumayelo yaseNtabeni.

ULuka ubalisa ibali lentshumayelo yokumiselwa kukaYesu ngeSabatha endlwini yesikhungu eNazarete.

UYohane ubalisa ukubizwa kwabanye babafundi bokuqala kwakunye nomsitho waseKana, nalapho uYesu enza khona umnqondiso wokuqala.

IGospile kaMarko ibalisa ukubizwa kwabafundi abane ize idandalazise neSabatha eKapernahum noko kwaalandelayo.

Le “Sabatha enoYesu” ekuqaleni kuMarko inika umfundi into yokuba ungubani uYesu. Kulo lonke icandelo lesifundo sale veki ambalwa kumazwi aKhe ashicilelweyo: ubizo lobufundi olungabheke phi, ukuyalelwa kwedemon, isicwangciso sokutyelela eminye imimandla, kwakunye nokuphiliswa koneqhenqa okunemiyalelo yokuba azibonakalise phambi kombingeleli ukuba uhlambulukile. Kugxilwe kwisenzo, ngokukodwa ukuphilisa abantu. Umbhali weGospile uyakholwa kukusebenzisa igama ngoko nangoko ukubonakalisa intshukumo ekhawulezayo yolungiselelo lukaYesu.

_*Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Julayi 13._
