### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Isa. 14:12–14, Eph. 5:5, Yoshuwa 7, Yohane 12:1–8, Zenzo 5:1–11, 1 Kor. 10:13.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “‘Lumkani, nizilinde ngasekubaweni; ngokuba ubomi bomntu abumi ngabuninzi bempahla yakhe’ ” (Luka 12:15).

Ukubawa kuchazwa njengomnqweno oqatseleyo wobutyebi okanye ubuncwane obungebubo obakho ncakasana. Ukubawa yinto enkulu, enkulu ngokwaneleyo, eneneni, iba phezulu phaya kunye nokungaxoki, ukuba, okanye ukubulala; iyonakalisa ngeyona ndlela kangangokuba uThixo wakhetha ukulumkisa ngokuchasene nayo kumthetho waKhe omkhulu wesimilo. “Uze ungawunqweneli umzi wommelwane wakho. Uze ungamnqweneli umfazi wommelwane wakho, nesicaka sakhe, nesicakakazi sakhe, nenkomo yakhe, ne-esile lakhe, nanye into eyiyeyommelwane wakho” (Eksod. 20:17).

Ukubawa kusoloko kudweliswa kuluhlu lwezono ezikhohlakeleyo eziyakumvalela umntu ngaphandle ebukumkanini bukaThixo. “Anazi na, ukuba abengemalungisa abayi kubudla ilifa ubukumkani bukaThixo? Musani ukulahlekiswa; abenza umbulo, nabakhonzi bezithixo, nabakrexezi, nezifebe, namadoda alalanayo, namasela, namabawa, namanxila, nabatshabhisi, nabaphangi, abayi kubudla ilifa ubukumkani bukaThixo” (1 Kor. 6:9, 10).

Ukubawa, kunye phaya nokuphanga, ukunqula izithixo, umbulo, kunye nokrexezo? Leyo yinto ethethwa yindima, kwaye kule veki sizakuqwalasela imizekelo yendlela okubi ngayo nento esinokuyenza ukukoyisa.

_*Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Matshi 4._
