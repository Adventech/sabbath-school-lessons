title:  Gqala Indoda EnguYobhi
date:  16/02/2023
---

`Funda uYobhi 1:8. UYobhi wachazwa njani nguThixo buQu? `

Kuhle oko kakhulu, ukude uThixo athi uYobhi “ugqibelele” kwaye “uthe tye” (Yobhi 1:8), ugqibelele wathi tye ngolona hlobo de kungabikho mntu ehlabathini ngelaxesha onokulingana naye. Kwakhona, la ngamazwi kaThixo ngoYobhi, atsolile, ngoYobhi.

Kwanasemva kokuba uYobhi ejamelene nesithwakumbe emva kwesinye, uThixo wakuphinda oko wayekuthethile ngoYobhi, ukuba akukho unje ngaye ehlabathini, ugqibelele uthe tye njalo njalo, ngaphandle kokuba kongezwa elinye isolotya elitsha. UYobhi wayesekwazizo zonke ezi zinto,  ” (Yobhi 2:3).

Kwaye nangona sifumana ukrwaqu omandla wengqibelele nokuthi tye kukaYobhi ngendlela awalandula ngayo ukulahla phakathi kwako konke okwehlayo kwaye naphakathi kokunyhukuthywa kwakhe kwelishwa yinkosikazi yakhe, “‘Usabambelele na ekugqibeleleni kwakho? Mlahle uThixo, ufe!’ ”(Yobhi 2:9), incwadi le ityhila enye inkalo ngobomi bukaYobhi ngaphambi kokuvezwa kwalo mboniso apha.

`Funda kuYobhi 29:12-16. Yintoni edandalaziswe apha esinika umbono ngakumbi kwimfihlo yesimilo sikaYobhi?`

Mhlawumbi okona kubonakalayo ngakumbi apha ngamazwi kaYobhi, “Netyala endingalaziyo bendiligocagoca” (Yobhi 29:16). Ngamanye amazwi, uYobhi akazange alinde nje, umzekelo, ukuba umngqibi othile udlakazelayo aze kukhongozela kuye. Endaweni yoko, uYobhi wayetsiba kuqala ukuya kukhangela iintswelo aze emva koko asebenze ngazo.

U-Ellen G. White waacebisa, “Sukubalinda [amahlwempu] ukuba batsalele ingqalelo yakho kwiintswelo zabo. Yenza njengoko weenzayo uYobhi. Into awayeyazi hayi ayiphandileyo. Yiba kutyelelo lokuhlola uze ubone ukuba yintoni edingekayo kwakunye nendlela engabonelelwa ngcono ngayo.”—Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 151. Lo ngumgangatho wokugcina imali kwakunye nobugosa bemithombo kaThixo ongaphaya kokwenziwa luninzi lwabantwana bakaThixo nomhlanje.
