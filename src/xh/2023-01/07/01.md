### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Luka 4:16–19; Isa. 62:1, 2; Dut. 15:11; Mat. 19:16–22; Luka 19:1–10; Yobhi 29:12–16.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “ ‘Uya kuthi ke ukumkani kwabangasekunene kwakhe, Yizani, nina nisikelelweyo nguBawo, nibudle ilifa ubukumkani obalungiselelwa nina kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi” ’ ” (Mateyu 25:34).

IBhayibhile isoloko ithetha ngabaphambukeli (ngamanye amaxesha babizwa abasemzini), iinkedama, kunye nabahlolokazi. Eli qela ingangabo abo uYesu walatha kubo “nakumnye waba bazalwana bam bangabona bancinane” (Mat. 25:40).

Aba bantu singababona njani namhlanje? Abaphambukeli bexesha leBhayibhile yayi ngabantu ekwakufuneka bashiye amazwe abo okuzalwa, mhlawumbi ngenxa yemfazwe okanye indlala. Abafana nabo namhlanje ingazizigidi ngezigidi zeembacu ezibe ziindwadunge ngenxa yeemeko ezingakhethanga kubo kuzo.

Iinkedama ngabantwana abaphulukene nootata babo ngenxa yemfazwe, ingozi, okanye ukugula. Eli qela lisengaquka abo banootata baba ababhadla eziseleni okanye ngandlel’ithile abekho. Uhlobo olubanzi ngolo hlobo lomsebenzi oludandalaziswe apha.

Abahlolokazi ngabo baphulukene nabalingane babo kwangenxa yesizathu esinye njengeenkedama. Uninzi lwabo luyintloko kwikhaya elimzali-mnye kwaye bangalusebenzisa uncedo ibandla elinokubonelela ngalo.

Njengoko sizakubona kule veki, ngenxa yokuba singabalawuli bomsebenzi kaThixo, ukunceda abahlwempuzekileyo asiyonto nje oyikhethayo. Kukulandela umzekelo kaYesu uthobele imiyalelo yaKhe.

_*Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Februwari 18._
