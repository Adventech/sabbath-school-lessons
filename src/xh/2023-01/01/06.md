---
title:  Ubutyebi Emazulwini
date:  05/01/2023
---

> <p></p>
> “Ningaziqwebeli ubutyebi emhlabeni, apho konakalisa inundu nomhlwa, nalapho amasela agqobhozayo ebe; ziqwebeleni ke ubutyebi emazulwini, apho kungonakalisi nundu namhlwa, nalapho amasela angagqobhoziyo ebe; kuba apho bukhona ubutyebi benu, yoba lapho nentliziyo yenu’ (Mat. 6:19–21).

`Zeziphi iinyaniso ezibalulekileyo azithethayo uYesu apha? `

Ngubani ongakhange afunde ibali emva kwebali elingabo baqwebe intabalala yobutyebi, suke ngandlel’ithile baphulukane nabo? Ihlabathi lethu yindawo engazinzanga kakhulu: iimfazwe, ulwaphulo-mthetho, ubundlobongela, iintlekele, nantoni ingagaleleka ngesiquphe itshayele konke ebesikusebenzele kwaye, mhlawumbi, nkqu noko besikuzuze ngokunyanisekileyo nangokuthembakeleyo. Ize, kanjalo, ngesiquphe, kufike ukufa, ngenxa yoko ezi zinto zingabi namsebenzi kuthi kakade.

Eneneni, isiBhalo asisixeleli nakanye ukuba akulunganga ukuba sisityebi okanye ukuba nobutyebi obuqwetyiweyo; ndaweni yoko, kwezi ndima uYesu uyasiyala ukuba konke masikugcine kusemgceni.

Kuthetha ukuthini, ngoko, ukuziqwebela ubutyebi emazulwini? Kuthetha ukubeka uThixo nomsebenzi wakhe phambili ebomini bakho, endaweni yokubeka imali phambili. Phakathi kwezinye zezinto, kuthetha ukusebenzisa oko sinako emsebenzini kaThixo, ukuqhubela phambili ubukumkani baKhe, ukusebenzela abanye, nokuba yintsikelelo kwabanye.

Umzekelo, xa uThixo wayebiza uAbram, wayenesicwangciso sokusebenzisa uAbram nosapho lwaKhe ukusikelela zonke iintsapho zehlabathi. UThixo wathi kuAbraham, lowo “wabizwa ngokuba sisihlobo sikaThixo” (Yakobi 2:23), “ ‘Ndikwenze uhlanga olukhulu, ndikusikelele, ndilikhulise igama lakho, ube yintsikelelo; ndibasikelele abakusikelelayo, ndimtshabhise okuqalekisayo; zisikeleleke ngawe zonke izizwe zehlabathi’ ” (Gen. 12:2, 3). “Ngoko ke abaselukholweni bayasikelelwa ndawonye noAbraham okholwayo” (Gal. 3:9). Sinomceli-mngeni omnye obonakaliswe kuthi njengoko wawubonakaliswe kuye.

“Imali inexabiso elikhulu, kuba inokwenza okulungileyo okukhulu. Kwizandla zabantwana bakaThixo ikukudla kolambileyo, isiselo konxaniweyo, nemapahla koze. Ilukhuseleko kocinezelweyo, kwakunye nento yokunceda kogulayo. Kodwa imali ayinaxabiso ngaphezu kwentlabathi, kuphela kuxa isetyenziselwa ukuhlangabezana neentswelo zobomi, ukusikelela abanye, nokuqhubela phambili umsebenzi kaKristu.”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Isifundos, p. 351.

`“Kuba apho bukhona ubutyebi benu, yoba lapho nentliziyo yenu” (Mat. 6:21). Intliziyo yakho ikuxelela ukuba buphi ubutyebi bakho?`
