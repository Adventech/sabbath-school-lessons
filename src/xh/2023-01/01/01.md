### Funda Ukulungiselela Isifundo Sale Veki
Gal. 3:26, 29; Ndum. 50:10–12; 1 IziKroni. 29:13, 14; Fil. 4:19; 1 Yohane 5:3; Mat. 6:19–21.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Bonani uthando olungaka asenzele lona uYise, olu lokuba sibizwe ngokuba singabantwana bakaThixo!” (1 Yohane 3:1).

NjengamaKristu, uphawu olumangalisayo ngobudlelane bethu noThixo, kukuba usithembile ekugcineni imicimbi yaKhe emhlabeni. Ekuqalekeni kanye kwembali yomntu, uThixo ngokuselubala wanikela ku-Adam no-Eva ukukhathalelwa ngokukodwa kwendalo engenachaphaza. (Bona kwiGen. 2:7–9, 15.) Ukususela ekuthiyweni kwezilwanyana, ukusinga ekulondolozweni koMyezo, nokuya ekuzaliseni umhlaba ngabantwana, uThixo wazisa ukuba sizakusebenza endaweni yaKhe apha.

Yena uyasisikelela kanjalo ngemithombo, kodwa sithi abagosise ngayo ukuba siyigcine, efana nokuqokelela imali, ukubhala iitshekhi, ukuthumela imali ngobuchwephesha, ukwenza uhlahlo-lwabiwo-mali, okanye ukuzisa izishumi zethu neminikelo enkonzweni ngeentsasa zangeSabatha. UThixo uyasikhuthaza ukuba sichithe imithombo asinike yona kwiintswelo zethu, iintswelo zabanye, nokuqhubela phambili umsebenzi waKhe. Ingakholeleki injalo, sithi abagosiswe nguThixo ngokukhulisa abantwaba baKhe, ukwakha izakhiwo zaKhe, nokufundisa izizukulwana ezilandelayo.

Kwisifundo sale veki, sizakuphonononga amalungelo namaxanduva okuba yinxalenye yosapho lukaThixo.

_*Funda isifundo sale veki ukulungiselela iSabatha, Janyuwari 7._
