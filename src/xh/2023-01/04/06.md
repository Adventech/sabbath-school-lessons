---
title:  'Amaphulo Akhethekileyo: Ukunikela Komphanda Omkhulu'
date:  26/01/2023
---

Uphando lubonakalise ukuba kuphela kangane-9 yepesenti yobuncwane babantu bungamanzi nje yaye bunokubandakanywa njengomnikelo ngesibhengezo sethutyana. Imali ezinkozo, indlela yetsheki, ukonga imali, ingxowa-mali zokuhambisa imali, nezinye, zithathwa ngoba bubuncwane obungamanzi, ubuncinane kwabo banezinto ezifana nezi. Inkoliso yobuncwane bethu, kangange-91 yeepesenti, “zityaliwe” kwizinto zexabiso, ezifana namakhaya, imfuyo yethu (ukuba sisemaphandleni), okanye ezinye izinto ezingengomanzi.

Umahluko kwiipesenti zobuncwabe obungamanzi nobungengomanzi unokubonakaliswa ngokufaka iipeni ezili-1,000 kwiijagi zeglasi ezimbini, iipeni ezi-10 zimele inqanaba lepesenti nganye. Ngoko ke uzakuba neepeni ezingama-90 kwijagi encinci emele i-9 pesenti yobuncwane obungamanzi uze ube neepeni ezingama-910 kwijagi elingana nekwati enkulu emele i-91 pesenti yobuncwane obungengomanzi.

Inkoliso yabantu ikhupha iminikelo yayo okanye amagalelo ayo kule jagi incincikubuncwane babo obungamanzi. Oku koko banako kwii-akhawunto zabo zetshekhi okanye ipoketibhuku. Kodwa xa ubani enomdla ngento ethile, banikela bethatha kwijagi enkulu. IBhayibhile ibalisa amabali amaninzi anjalo.

`Funda kuMarko 14:3-9 nakuYohane 12:2-8. Ngobani abantu ababephambili kwisidlo sikaSimon? Laliyintoni ixabiso lesipho sikaMariya? Kwakutheni ukuze athambise uYesu ngeli xesha?`

Isipho sikaMariya sasixabisa iidenariyo ezingama-300-umvuzo wonyaka gunqu. Yayi, phantse ikakhulu, sisipho “sejagi enkulu”. Ukulandela esi siganeko, uYuda wangcatsha uYesu ngenxa yesixa esingaphantsi kwisahlulo sesithathu-isipho “sejagi encinci”, amaqhosha angama-30 esilivere (Mat. 26:15). Kungenxa yothando lokwenene nokuzibophelela ukwenza izipho zejagi enkulu-eziphuma kwizinto esityale kuzo. Kodwa xa sinyoluka, njengoYuda, singayithegisa imiphefumlo yethu ukulandela kungekho nto.

Umsebenzi nezenzo zikaBarnabas zikhankanywe izihlandlo ezingama-28 kwiTestamente eNtsha. Siyamazi ekuqalekeni njengehlakani likampostile uPawulos kwakunye nomvangeli omkhulu. Kodwa isiseko sako konke oku sibekwe kwisicatshulwa sokuqala apho akhankanywa khona. KwiZenzo 4:36, 37, sifunda ngokunikela kwakhe, ngokwenene, umnikelo “wejagi enkulu”. Umzekelo omandla ngolo hlobo wamazwi kaKristu: “ ‘kuba apho bukhona ubutyebi benu, yoba lapho nentliziyo yenu’ ” (Mat. 6:21).

`Kutheni ukunikela kokuzibingelela kubaluleke kwabo banikelayo njengakwabo baxhamlayo?  `
