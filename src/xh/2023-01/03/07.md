Funda u-Ellen G. White’s most comprehensive tithe document in volume 9 of Testimonies for the Church, pp. 245–252. Funda Section III of Counsels on Stewardship, pp. 65–107.

“Ukuba zonke izishumi zabantu bethu bezithontelana ukusinga kwindyebo kaYehova njengoko zimele ukuba njalo, iintsikelelo ezinjalo beziyakwamkela kangangokuba izipho neminikelo yeenjongo ezingcwele beziyakuphindaphindeka kalishumi, ize ngaloo ndlela ijelo eliphakathi koThixo nomntu lihlale livulekile.”— Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 4, p. 474. Esi sisicatshulwa esimangalisayo. Ukuba sonke besingabarholi-zishumi abathembekileyo, uThixo ebezakusisikelela ngeemali ukwandisa iminikelo yethu nge-1000 pesenti.

“Kwisahluko sesithathu sikaMalaki kufumaneka isivumelwano uThixo asenze nomntu. Apha uThixo ubek’elubala indima Yena ayakuyenza ukuthulula izipho zaKhe ezikhulu kwabo bathe babuyisela kuYe ngokuthembekileyo izishumi neminikelo.”— Ellen G. White, Review and Herald, DISEMBA 17, 1901.

“Bonke kufuneka bakhumbule ukuba ibango likaThixo kuthi lisekela naliphi elinye ibango. Usinika ngokuphuphumayo, yaye isivumelwano asenzileyo nomntu sesokuba okweshumi kubuncwane bakhe makubuyiselwe kuThixo. INkosi ngokunemfesane igcinisa kumagosa aYo ubutyebi baYo, kodwa kokweshumi ithi: Oku koKwam. Ngokohlobo uThixo anikezele ngayo ubuncwane baKhe emntwini, nomntu kufuneka abuyisele kuThixo isishumi esithembakeleyo sako konke anako. Esi sicwangciso sicacileyo seenziwa nguYesu buQu.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 6, p. 384.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo**

`.Gxilani ngakumbi kule ngcamango yokuba isenzo sokubuyisa isishumi asizange sivele kuSirayeli wamandulo. Le nyaniso isinceda njani ukuqonda ukutyhutyhumeza kolu xanduva ngakwindima yethu phambi koThixo?`

`Eklasini, xoxani ngombuzo obuzwe ekupheleni kwesifundo sangoMvulo. Cingani ngokuba bekuyakwenzeka ntoni xa abantu bebenokugqiba ngokuthumela isishumi sabo kwenye indawo. Bekuyakwenzeka ntoni ebandleni lethu? Ngesisenalo phofu ibandla? Yintoni engalunganga kumoya othi, Kakade, isishumi sam sincinci kakhulu xa usithelekisa nayo yonke enye into, noba kunjalo? Xa wonke umntu ebecinga njalo?`

`Yabelana nabanye ngoko ukufundileyo wanamava ako ngokuhlawula isishumi. Yintoni onokuyifundisa abanye ngesi senzo?`
