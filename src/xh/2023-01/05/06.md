---
title:  Imida Yexesha leMboleko kwakunye nama-Nqaku/ Iingongoza okuboleka Zokuboleka
date:  02/02/2023
---

`Funda kwiDuteronomi 15:1-5. Yintoni uYehova awayeyifuna kubantu baKhe ngokutyhilwe kwezi ndima? `

Ngokuhambisana neminye imiyalelo eminyaka isixhenxe (Eksod. 21:2; Lev. 25:3, 4), yayingengomakhoboka odwa okanye izicaka kunye nomhlaba ezazimiselwe, koko nababolekisi. Ngenxa yokuba ababolekisi babengafuni ukuxolela nawaphi amatyala, elona lide nawuphi umntu awayenokuba setyaleni kulo yiminyaka esixhenxe. Nokuba ziintoni ezingezinye esinokuzithabatha kwezi ndima, zibonisa ukuba uYehova uyakhathala ngezintlobo zemiba yezezimali, ngokukodwa xa yayi bandakanya amaSirayeli afanayo. Ezi ndima ziyabonisa ukuba uYehova wayamkela inyaniso yetyala, nokuba yayimbi kangakanani na. Wagxininisa kanjalo ukuba maliphetshwe kangangoko.

Namhlanje, ngokwahlukileyo, iindawo ezininzi ehlabathini zineemali-mboleko zeminyaka engama-30 nama-40 zokuthengwa kwezindlu. Kukhangeleka ngathi isizathu ekuphela kwaso sokuba izindlu zixabise kakhulu kukuba ityala likho ukubonelela ngeemali-mboleko zokuthenga zona.

Kwelinye icala, intlaninge yabantu, abazali, nabafundi bacinga ngokuboleka imali yokufunda. Njengomqathango, ukuzuza isidanga sekholeji kuzakuqinisa isakhono somvuzo womntu ubomi bakhe bonke. Abanye abantu kufuneka baboleke imali ethile ukuze bahlawulele imfundo yabo, kodwa gcina engqondweni ezingongoma. Kufuneka uyibuyise inenzala. Zama ukufumana zonke izibonelelo okanye iinkxaso-mali onokuzifumana. Sebenza uze wonge konke onako ngenxa yesikolo. Thatha kuphela amakhondo emfundo azakukhokelela empangelweni. Quka abazali bakuncede. Ngamaxesha eBhayibhile, abazali babenika abantwana umhlaba wokulima ukuze baziphilele. Namhlanje elo “lifa” lingayimfundo ukuze babengabantu abadala abazimeleyo.

Kwihlabathi elililo, bekungazokubakho kuboleka natyala. Kodwa ngenxa yokuba singaphili kwilizwe elililo, angakhona amaxesha apho kudingeka ukuba siboleke. Qiniseka kuphela ukuba ukwesona sivumelwano singcono unokuba kuso, yaye nawona maqondo enzala ayafumaneka. Emva koko boleka owona myinge usezantsi uwudingayo, uze uwuhlawule msinyane kangangoko ukuphungula iindleko zenzala. Ngokommiselo, noko kunjalo, masizame ukuliphepha ityala, size ngokulandela imimiselo yezezimali yeBhayihbile kubomi bethu bemihla ngemihla, singahamba indlela ende esingisa ekuphepheni ityala elingeyomfuneko kwakunye nobunzima obubi elinokububeka kuthi neentsapho zethu.

`Ukuba uboleke abantu imali, uthembeke wanemfobe nobubele kanjani ngendlela obaphethe ngayo? Ungaxabisa ntoni phambi koThixo xa kufuneka uphendule ngezoompatho? (Bona kwiNtshuma. 12:14.) `
