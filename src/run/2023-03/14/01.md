### Ibisomwa Vy’Icigwa Ca Rino Yinga Aho Biboneka:
Abanyefeso 1–Abanyefeso 6

> <p>Umurongo wo gufata ku mutwe</p>
> “Ubuntu ni bwo bwabakijije kubw'ukwizera: na vyo nyene ntivyavuye kuri mwebwe, n'ingabire y'Imana; ntivyavuye ku bikorwa, kugira ngo hoye kugira uwirarira. Kuko tur'ibiremwa vyayo, twaremewe muri Kristo Yesu ngo dukore ibikorwa vyiza, ivyo Imana yateguye kera ngo tubigenderemwoˮ (Abanyefeso 6: 8-10).

Ingenzi zitemberera mu gisagara ca Londres (mu Bwongeleza) zikunda kwurira icubako citwa London Eye, ikintu kimeze nk‘umuzingi munini kizunguruka. Uhagaze ku metero 1300 hejuru y‘uruzi Thames urashobora kubona ico cubako cose: Big ben, amazu y‘inama nshingamateka, n‘inyubakwa nyinshi zahoze ari ingoro z‘abami muri kahise hamwe n‘amasengero manini y‘abakatolika. Nk‘uko umuhinga mu vy‘iyobokamana yanonosoye ibijanye n‘Isezerano Rishasha yitwa Nicholas Thomas ‟Tom" Wright, avuga, icete c‘Abanyefeso kirafise ico gihuriyeko n‘ayandi makete yose ya Pawulo kuruta iyo London Eye. Si co kirekire kuruta ayandi makete yose canke cuzuyemwo inyandiko ziwe gusa, ariko kiratanga indabo yagutse y‘amakete yiwe yose. Duhereye aha, uko iyo pine izunguruka, ubona umuntu hama ukagenda ubona ingingo imwe inyuma y‘iyindi." —_Paul for Everyone: The Prison Letters_ (London: S.P.C.K. [Society for Promoting Christian Knowledge Publishing], 2004), p. 3.

Mu cete c‘Abanyefeso, Pawulo ntiyashimikiye gusa ku bibazo vyo mu karere kamwe kanaka. Ico cete cumvikana nk‘aho Pawulo yariko avugana n‘abizera b‘ahantu aho ari ho hose n‘amashengero y‘Abakristo aho ari hose. Kuba iryo kete ritagenewe ikiringo c‘umwanya munaka cumvikana ko gitanga akaryo ko gufata impwemuPawulo atanga kugira ngo kinyengetere no mw‘isi no mu vyiyumviro vyacu muri kino gihe cacu bwite. Mu gihe dusubiramwo buri gice cose, mureke tugume twiyumvira kur‘iki kibazo ngo: Mbega ni ukuhe kuri ngirakamaro kwanyegejwe mur‘iki cete c‘Abanyefeso dukwiye kubandanya dukomeza mu buzima bwacu nk‘abizera?

_Nimwige icigwa ca rino yinga kugira ngo mwitegurire Isabato, yo kw’igenekerezo rya 30 Nyakanga 2023._
