---
title:  Igihe Cose Indirimbo
date:   02/01/2024
---

`Soma Zaburi 3, Zaburi 33:1-3, na Zaburi 109:6-15. Mbega n'izihe mpande zitandukanye z'ivyo umuntu ahura na vyo biri muri ayo Mazaburi ?`

Amazaburi amenyesha umugwi w'abizera umugwi w'ivyo bahura n'umuntu kandi akerekana ko abizera bashobora kuguma basenga Imana mu gihe ico ari coc ose, c'ubuzima bwabo. Dusangamwo ibi bikurikira:

1. Indirimbo zihayagiza Imana ku bukuru bwayo bw'agakura no ku bushobozi bwayo mw'irema, ku bwami bwayo, ku rubanza rwayo n'ubudahemuka bwayo.

2. Indirimbo zo gutazira zigaragaza ugutahura vyimbitse imigisha y'umusesekara y'Imana.

3. Ugucura intimba, ari ugutakambira Imana kutakira uburyarya kugira ngo idukize umubabaro.

4. Amazaburi y'ubwitonzi ubwenge nyabwo, atanga imirongo yo gukorerako kugira ngo ubuzima bube bugororotse (butunganye)

5. Amazaburi ntimatima yerekana Kristo, Umwami Nyen'inganji n'Umubohozi w'ubwoko bw'Imana.

6. Amazaburi ndangakahise yibutsa kahise ka Isirayeli kandi yerekana ubwizigirwa bw'Imana n'uguhemuka kwa Isirayeli, kugira ngo yigishe urunganwe n'urunganwe rwo muri kazoza kugira ngo ntirusubire gukora, amakosha nk'ayo ba sekuruza babo bakoze, ariko ko boguma bizigiye Imana kandi bakaguma ari intahemuka kw'Isezerano ryayo.

Ukwisiga (poesie) kw'Amazaburi gufise ubushobozi bwo kwifatira neza neza abagusoma ngo bayagumizeko umutima. Naho bimwe mu bisabwa ku vyese vyagiye biratakara mu kuyashira mu zindi ndimi, dushobora misi yose, uko ururimi rw'amavukiro yacu rwoba rumeze kwose, guha agaciro igitigiri nyamwishi.

1. _Ibangabangwa (parallélisme)_ rigizwe no gushira hamwe amajambo amungane, canke ivyiyumviro bisa. Ibangabanganwa rifasha gutahura insiguro y'ibice bihura. Nk'akarorero: " Mutima wanje, himbaza Uhoraho: namwe ibimbamwo vyose, muhimbaze izina ryiwe ryera " (Zaburi 103:1). Muri iryo bangabanganwa, " mutima wanje " ni kandi " na mwebwe ibimbamwo vyose "

2. _Ingereranyo nyavyese (imagerie poétique)_ ikoresha imvugo ngereranyo kugira ngo ihamagarane inkomezi umusomyi gutahura ibifadika nk'akarorero, uguhungira ku Mana buvugwa " mu gitutu c'amababa yawe "(Zaburi 17:8).

3. _Igaburagabura (mérisme)_ ryerekana umurava biciye ku kuwugaburamwo kabiri kabiri mu bice bidahuza " ntakambira imbere yawe ku murango no mw'ijoro " bisigura kuri ubudahengeshanya (Zaburi 88:1, ni twebwe tubiciyeko akarongo).

4. Imvugo mudiho (calembour) ikoresha umudiho w'amajambo kugira ngo igire urukino rw'amajambo kandi itsimbataze ubutumwa mu vya Mpwemu. Muri Zaburi 96:4,5 amajambo y'igiheburayo 'elohim, " imana nyinshi " na 'elilim, " ibigirwamana " irema, urukino rw'amajambo kugira itange ubutumwa ko imana z'amahanga zisa na élohim " imana nyinshi ", ariko ari élilim (ibigirwamana gusa gusa " " Kuk'Uhoraho ahambaye, abereye gushimwa cane. Nuw'igitinyiro kuruta ibigirwamana vyose. Kukw'ibigirwamana vyose ari ubusa, arik'Uhoraho we ni we yaremye amajuru. "

5. Ari bwo bwa nyuma, ijambo Selah (Sela) ikiruhuko ryerekana akanya gatoyi k'akaruhuko haba ku guhamagara, akaruhuko bwite canke ku kwiyumvira ku butumwa bw'igice kidasanzwe c'izaburi, canke ku guhindura injano y'umuziki (Zaburi 61:4).
