---
title:  Ishengero Rya Mbere N’uburere Ryari Rimererewemwo Neza
date:   18/10/2023
---

`Soma Ivyakozwe n’Intumwa 8 :1-4. Mw’ishengero rya mbere, ni igihe catumye abakristo basabagira bakaja kure y’aho bari bamererewe neza?`

Gushika ico gihe, ishengero rya mbere ryari rigwiriye cane i Yerusalemu (canke kubutaka kiyuda kandi mu bwoko bw’Abayuda). Igihe agahamo katangura, muri ko Sawuli, Umuyuda w’intwari ku myizerere kandi w’Umufarisayo, yari ashimitse cane, Ishengero ry’i Yerusalemu ryahavuye risabagira i Yudaya hose n’i Samariya. Yesu yari yarabivuze imbere y’igihe mu Vyakozwe n’Intumwa 1:8, ati: "Muzoba ivyabona vyo kunshingira intahe i Yerusalemu, n’i Yudaya n’i Samariya".

Ubu butumwa bwarasohoye, nk’uko tubisoma mu Ivyakozwe n’Intumwa 8:4 : "Arikw’Abasabagiye bagendagenda bavuga Ijambo ry’Imana."

N’igihe ishengero ryari ritanguye gushingwa kure y’i Yerusalemu, bakomeje kwigisha mu bisagara vy’i Yudaya no mu makaritiye y’ubwoko kiyuda mu bindi bisagara. Ivyakozwe n’Intumwa 11:19 herekana ko ishengero ryasabagiye gushika muri Foyinike, muri Libani n’i Kupuro, ariko bakabwira ubutumwa "Abayuda bonyene". Abigishwa ba Yesu n’ishengero rya mbere ntibashaka ko Abapagani (baza kuri Yesu nk’abanyamahanga. Bari bagifise imbono ya hafi cane ku categerezwa kuba ku gikorwa c’ishengero.)

Petero intumwa ya Yesu, yabaye umwe mu barongozi b’ivyamamare vy’ishengero rya mbere, yarabaye uwudashigikiye ku kujana ubutumwa bw’ubutumwa bwiza ku banyamahanga (Abapagani), n’igihe Pawulo yari yatanguye kubikora. Petero yari azwi nk’intumwa y’abakebwe (bisigura Abayuda), Pawulo na we intumwa y’abanyamahanga (abapagani). (Ab’i Galatiya 2 :8). Vyarageze n’aho Petero atagomba ko bamubona ari kumwe n’abanyamahanga (Ab’i Galatiya 2 :11,12). Ariko kandi, Imana yarasohoye Petero muri ako karere amererewemwo neza, hanyuma ihindura umutima wiwe. Yahavuye atangura gutegera ico bisiguye vy’ukuri igikorwa c’ivugabutumwa yahawe, atahura n’ico urupfu rwa Yesu rwaronkeye isi yose agakiza.

`Soma Ivyakozwe n’Intumwa 10:9-10,28,29. Ni ubutumwa ki Imana yahaye Petero kandi ni gute dutegerezwa muri iki gihe cacu, gukoresha iyo ngingo nshimikiro mu gikorwa cacu c’ukuvuga ubutumwa?`
