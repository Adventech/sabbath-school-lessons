---
title:  Ibindi Vyo Kwiga No Kuzirikana
date:   13/10/2023
---

Igishingantahe kumahanga yose. Amajambo y’umukiza, "Muri umuco wisi” ashaka kuvuga ko Yahaye intumwa igikorwa c’ivugabutumwa kw’isi yose, nkuko imishwarara y’izuba icengetera mumfuruka za kure z’isi niko Imana ishaka ko Umuco w’ubutumwa bwiza ucengetera mumitima yabantu bose baba kw’isi. Iyo ishengero rya Kristo rishitsa intumbero y’Umukiza wacu, Umuco uba waramaze gukwira kubari mumwiza no mugitutu c’urupfu, hakwicara bakirinda kugumbira bonyene ubushobozi bw’umusaraba, abizera bategerezwa gukwira isi yose,mukureka umuco wa Kristo ukakira bose nkuko Yesu yabikoze kugakiza k’imitima “Kandi ubu butmwa bwiza bw’ubwami buzovugwa kwisi yose”.

Mubihugu vyose akamo k’I Masedoniya karumvikana: “Ni muze mudutabare”, Imana irakorera mumaso yacu, ibiremwa vyo mw’Ijuru birashirahamwe n’abantu. Ivy ahishuwe biri imbere yacu, ubushobozi bw’Imana burakorana n’inguvu z’umuntu, amaso atabona ibikorwa vya Yesu n’amaso ahumye, n’amatwi atumva ijwi ry’umwungere w’intama nayo yarazivye. Bamwe barumvise akamo k’Imana kandi barakitavye, Umutima wose wejejwe uk wiye kwitaba akamo uyumusi mukurondera gutangaza inkuru nziza y’ubuzima. Iyo abagabo n’abagore mukwicisha bugufi no mubwizigirwa, bafata nka nkama igikorwa bahawe kandi batumwe n’Imana; Ubushobozi bw’Imana bwokwigaragaje muguhinduka kwa benshi bahindukirir a mukuri. Umwimbu w’inguvu zabo wobaye munini cane kandi mwiza" Ellen G. White, _The Adventist Review & Sabbath Herald_, 14 Novembre 1912.

GUHANAHANA IVYIYUMVIRO:

`Ubwizigirwa bw’ubwaku bw’ishengero mukibano bwerekanwa nuko twebwe umubiri wa Kristo tugaragaza urukundo rw’Imana mubuzima bwacu, Mugushitsa ibango twahawe ry’ubutumwa. Ni gute wokwishura kugiti cawe kuruwo muhamagaro?`

`Kubwanyu ni gute ishengero ryanyu ribonwa nabo mukibano batari Abadiventiste? Mubiziko iki? Nimba ishengero ryanyu ribonwa neza, ni gute mwoshora gukora kandi kugira rigire ubwaku bwiza gusumba? Nimba ribonwa nabi, nigiki mwokora kugira ishengero ryongere ryizerwe mukibano?`

`Ni kubera iki ari ngombwa kugumya “Inkuru nziza ihoraho” kuntimatima y’igikorwa c’ivugabutumwa mw’isi? Nibihe bi ndi vyizigiro dushobora kwereka umuntu, ahantu ahariho hose utisunze Ivyizigiro bihambabaye dufise kubw’inkuru nziza, amakuru meza yivyo y’ivyo Yesu yakoze kumusaraba?`
