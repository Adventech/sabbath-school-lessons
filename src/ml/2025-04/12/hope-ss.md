### Part #12 God Is Faithful

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are continuing a series of studies on Lessons of Faith from Joshua. Today’s topic: GOD IS FAITHFUL.

**Scripture Song:** Be strong and of good courage – Deut. 31:6

- **The faithfulness of the LORD**
	- Joshua 21:43-45 What impresses you as you read this testimony of Joshua? (see also Joshua 23:14)
	- While every promise of the LORD to the children of Israel was fulfilled, not every Israelite received the promised blessings. Why? (Achan, Korah, etc.)
	- Who chose to believe the promises of the LORD and become a recipient of His blessings? (Joshua, Caleb, Rahab, etc.)
	- What was the Apostle Paul’s testimony about the faithfulness of God to keep His promises? 2 Timothy 2:11-13, 2 Corinthians 1:18-20
	- What promises of the LORD are especially meaningful for you? John 3:16-17, Matthew 11:28-30, John 10:27-29, John 11:25, John 14:1-3, etc.
- **The importance of human cooperation**
	- Joshua 23:1-5 Where do you see the need for human cooperation in the victories the LORD had promised to the children of Israel?
	- Joshua 23:6-10 Why was an obedient relationship with the LORD essential for His promises to be fulfilled for the children of Israel? (see also Deuteronomy 6:3-5)
	- What teachings of Jesus emphasize the importance of an obedient relationship with God? Luke 6:46-49, John 14:15, etc.
	- Share some miracles in the ministry of Jesus where human cooperation was important. John 9:7, 11:39, Matthew 10:5-8, 14:19-20, etc.
	- Share a time when you witnessed a miracle of God as you responded in obedience to His invitation to join Him in His work.
- **Warning against unfaithfulness to the LORD**
	- Joshua 23:7,12-13 What warning did Joshua give in his farewell address to the children of Israel?
	- How does the warning of Joshua relate to our lives today? (see 1 John 2:15-17)
	- What promises in the Scriptures encourage you when you are tempted to be unfaithful to the LORD? 1 Corinthians 10:13, James 4:7-10, etc.
	- Joshua 23:15-16 How do you explain the anger (wrath) of the LORD if He is a God of love? (see also 1 John 4:8, 2 Peter 3:9, etc.)
- **Loving the LORD with all your heart**
	- Joshua 23:11 Why is the simple appeal of Joshua so important? (see also Joshua 22:5, Deuteronomy 6:5)
	- What teaching of Jesus reinforced the importance of loving God with all our heart? Matthew 22:37-38
	- How is it possible to love God with all your heart?
