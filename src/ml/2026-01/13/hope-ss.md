### Participating In The Mission Of Jesus

Welcome to Hope Sabbath School—an in-depth interactive study of the Word of God. We are concluding our series on Inspired Letters from Prison. Today’s topic from Colossians: PARTICIPATING IN THE MISSION OF JESUS

**Scripture Song:** Rejoice always – 1 Thessalonians 5:16-18, Philippians 4:6

- **Mission through multiplication**
	- Where was the Apostle Paul located when writing this inspired letter to Christians in Colossae? Colossians 4:3, Acts 28:16,30-31
	- Colossians 4:7-9 How did the impact of Paul’s ministry continue to expand even during his time in prison? (see also Philippians 1:12-14)
	- What do you know about the individuals the Apostle Paul sent to deliver his letter to Christians in Colossae?
		- Who was Tychicus? Acts 20:2-4, Ephesians 6:21
		- Who was Onesimus? Philemon 10-16
- **Fellowship with other followers of Jesus**
	- Colossians 4:10-11 Who were some other followers of Jesus supporting the Apostle Paul during his imprisonment in Rome?
	- What additional information do we know about Aristarchus, a fellow prisoner with the Apostle Paul? Acts 19:29, 27:2
	- What additional information do we know about Jesus called Justus? Is this the same Justus mentioned in Acts 18:7
	- What lesson can we learn from the life experience of John Mark, the cousin of Barnabas? Acts 13:4-5,12, 15:36-39, 2 Timothy 4:11
	- What lessons can we learn from Luke, the beloved physician who traveled with the Apostle Paul?
	- What sad lesson can we learn from the life of Demas? 2 Timothy 4:10, 1 John 2:15-17
- **The testimony of Epaphras**
	- Who was Epaphras? Colossians 4:12a, 1:3-8
	- What are your thoughts about his prayer for his fellow believers in Colossae? Colossians 4:12b
	- What can we learn from Epaphras about our mission assignment? Colossians 4:13, Acts 1:8
	- How is God impressing you to expand your mission impact beyond your immediate circle of influence?
- **A message for Laodicea**
	- What instruction did the Apostle Paul give regarding the sharing of his letter to Christians in Colossae? Colossians 4:16
	- What was the content of Paul’s letter to Laodicea? (Colossians 2:1-3, related to Revelation 3:17-21)
	- What admonition did Paul give to Archippus, a church leader in Laodicea? Colossians 4:17
	- How does this admonition relate to our lives today?
