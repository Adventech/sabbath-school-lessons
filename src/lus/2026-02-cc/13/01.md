> <p>Chângvawn</p>
> “Chutichuan, tûnah mikhual leh mi cham mai in ni tawh lo va, mi thianghlimte nên chuan tualkhat leh Pathian chhûngte in ni ta zâwk a ni; Krista Isua nge chu a kila lung pawimawh ber niin, tirhkohte leh zâwlneite lungphûm chunga sak in ni a; nangni pawh amahah chuan Thlarauva, Pathian awmna tûra rem khâwm in ni” (Ephesi 2:19–22, NKJV).

### Chhiar Tûrte
Matthaia 16:18; Ephesi 2:19–22; Thupuan 7:9–17

### Zir Zauna
Tirhkohte Thiltih, bung 58

### Thu Êng

“Krista chuan kohhran hnênah tih tûr pawimawh tak a pe a. Krista hausakna chhuisen rual loh, a khawngaihna ro hlu tak chu khawvêl mite hnêna Pathianin a hrilh ve theih nân, member tinte hi hmanrua an ni tûr a ni. Khawvêl hnêna a Thlarau leh a nungchang aiawh ni tûra aiawhtute a duh ang hi thil dang eng mah a awm lo. Chhandamtu hmangaihna mihring hmanga tihlanna hi khawvêlin a mamawh ber chu a ni. Pathian thiltihtheihna, Kristiannaa mihringte hmanga tihlan a nih theih nân, vânte chuan mihringte min ngâichâng a ni.”—Tirhkohte Thiltih, pp. 538, 539.

### Engtin Nge I Ngaih?

`“Tirhkoh” tih thu i hriatin, thumal awmze belhchhah thei (adjective) rilrua lo lang apiang chu ziak chhuak zêl ang che:`

“Tirhkoh” tih thu i ngaihtuah a mize tha lam rilrua lo lang apiang chu ziak chhuak zêl ang che.

### I Hria Em?

Kohhran chu biak in a ni lo. Pathian biak inkhâwmna pawh a ni lo va, leirawhchan lungphûm chunga rem pawh a ni hek lo. Khawvêl thima eng chhuak tûra koh che chhuak pâwl a ni a. A awlsam zawng chuan nang leh kei hi kan ni. Mi dangte rawngbâwlsak a, hmangaih a, thil pe a, fuih phur a kan en chhuah chuan—kohhran kan n! A awmzia chu keini chiah hi A kohhran tâna tirhkoh a neihte chu kan ni. Kum zabi hmasa tam vak lo chhûng kha chuan kohhran awm dân tûra Krista ruahman dân kha miten an manthiam a. Lo ralthlîrtute pawhin, Kristianten an rinna leh khawngaihna mi chi tin—damlo te, mi rethei te, endawng tuarte chunga an lantîr zêl mai chu mak an ti a. Chutiang chuan, khâng ringtute khân hming mumal pawh an nei lo va. ‘Koh chhuah’ [ekklesia] tih an ni mai. A awmzia chu “assembly” tihna a ni a, chu chu kohhran chu niin, mi naran lo pung khâwm, an kalna apianga Isua tâna eng chhi chhuak zêl tûra inpete an ni.

### Bible Thawnthu

“Nang Petera i ni tih ka hrilh bawk a che; he lungpui chungah hian ka kohhranho ka rem chho vâng a, Mitthi Khaw kulh kawngkhârte chuan chu chu an ngam lo vang.

Chutichuan, tûnah mikhual leh mi cham mai in ni tawh lo va, mi thianghlimte nên chuan tualkhat leh Pathian chhûngte in ni ta zâwk a ni; Krista Isua nge chu a kila lung pawimawh ber niin, tirhkohte leh zâwlneite lungphûm chunga sak in ni a; amahah chuan lung rem hrang theuhte, rem taka chuktuahin, Lalpa-ah chuan biak in thianghlimah a châng zêl thîn a; nangni pawh amahah chuan Thlarauva, Pathian awmna tûra rem khâwm in ni.

Chung hnu-ah chuan ka han en a, tin, ngâi teh, mipui tam tak tu ma chhiar sên loh, hnam tin zînga mi te, chi tin zînga mi te, mi tin zînga mi te, tawng tin zînga mite puan vâr sina tûmkau kengin, lalthutphah hmaah leh Berâm No hmaa ding ka hmu a; chungte chuan aw ring takin,

“Kan Pathian lalthutphaha thua hnênah leh Berâm No hnênah chhandamna,” tiin an au va.

Vântirhkoh zawng zawngte chu lalthutphah leh upate leh thilnung palite vêlah chuan dingin an awm a; lalthutphah hmaah chuan bawkkhupin,

“Amen: Malsâwmna te, ropuina te, finna te, lâwmthu sawina te, chawimâwina te, thiltihtheihna te, chakna te, chatuanin kan Pathian hnênah awm rawh se,” Amen, tiin Pathian chibai an buk a.

Upa zînga pakhat chuan ka hnênah, “Hêng puan var sinte hi tute nge ni a, khawi atanga lo kal nge an nih?” tiin mi bia a.

Kei chuan a hnênah, “Ka pu, nangman i hria alâwm,” ka ti a. Ani’n ka hnênah, “Hêng hi hrehawm nasa tak ata chhuakhote kha an ni a, an puante Berâm No thisena suin an tivar ta a. Chuvângin, Pathian thutphah hmaah an awm a ni; a chhun a zânin a biak inah chuan a rawng an bâwl thîn; tin, lalthutphaha thua chuan an chungah a puan in chu a zâr ang. An riltam leh tawh lo vang a, an tuihal leh tawh hek lo vang; niin a em hek lo vang a, lum reng reng pawhin a tihrehawm hek lo vang. Berâm No, lalthutphah lâia awm chu an vengtu a ni ang a, nunna tuikhurahte a hruai ang; Pathianin an mit ata mittui zawng zawng a hru fâi tawh ang,” a ti a. (Matthaia 16:18; Ephesi 2: 19–22; Thupuan 7:9–17, NIV)

Isua Krista chungchâng sawina thu apiangah arsi dah zêl ang che. Hêng Bible châng tinin an sawi tum ber theuh chu eng nge ni?

`Thupuan 7:9–17`

`Engtin nge tûn kâr chhûng hian khawvêl thim zînga eng i nih theih ang?`

### I Hria Em?

“Khawvêl êng in ni e. Tlâng chhîpa khaw awm chu thuhrûk theih a ni lo. Miin khâwnvâr halin hrai hnuaiah an dah ngâi lo, a dahnaah an dah zâwk thîn; tichuan, in chhûnga mi zawng zawng a en thîn. Chutiang bawkin in eng chu mi mit hmuhin eng rawh se, chutichuan, in thil tih thatte an hmu ang a, in Pa vâna mi an chawimâwi thei ang,” (Matthaia 5:14–16).

“Inthâwina duh lovin khawngaihna ka duh si a, hâlral inthawinate ai chuan Pathian hriatna ka duh zâwk a ni,” (Hosea 6:6, NKJV).

“Aw, Lalpa hnênah lâwmthu hril ula, a hming lam rawh u, mite zîngah chuan a thiltihte hriattîr rawh u,” (1 Chronicles 16:8, NKJV).

“Nangni pawh lung nungte angin thlarau lam ina rem chhohvin in awm, Isua Krista zâra Pathian lâwmzâwng thlarau lam inthawina hlan tûrin puithiam thianghlimho in lo nih nân,” (1 Petera 2:5, NKJV).

“Mi zirtute ni rawh u, kei pawh Krista zirtu ka ni ang bawk hian,” (1 Korinth 11:1, NKJV).

### Hriat Zui Atân

“Khawvêl hnêna a Thlarau leh a nungchang âiawh ni tûra aiawhtute a duh ang hi thil dang eng mah a awm lo.”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 538.

Mahni Nun Nêna Dawh Zawm:

#### Sabbath

**Chhiar tûr:** Hosea 6:6

Engtin Nge I Ngaih? tiha tirhkohte i ngaih dân kha en nawn leh la. Bible chuan rawngbâwltu/tirhkohte ni tûrin min ko va. Thuhretua i tan dân bîk kha hmu chhuak tûrin, a hnuaia zawhnate hi chhang la. Chutah nangma tih dân pângngâi renga thuhretua tanna remchâng zawng ang che.

#### Sunday

**Chhiar tûr:** 1 Chronicles 16:8

I rawngbâwl dân tûr hriat theih nân a hnuaia zawhna hi chhang ang che:

1. Bus-a i chuannaah i kianga hmeichhe thu chuan in kohhran chungchâng a zâwt che a. A khawi tih dân phung hi nge i zuia i inrin?
	- a. In thurinte nemngheh nân Bible châng eng emaw zât i hrilh a.
	- b. In tualchhûng kohhranin beihpui thlâk a huaihawta inkhâwm tûrin chu mi chu i sâwm.
	- c. Chu nu chu thutak hmangin i bei.
	- d. Bus atanga a ruala in chhuknaah a bag rit tak i khaipui.
	- e. Kristian i lo nih dân i hrilh.
2. I pa chu ringtu a ni ve ngâi hauh lo va. Thlarau lam thilte a tuipui ve theih nân engtia dâwr nge thaa i rin?
	- a. A rilru ngaihtuah tuipui zawng sawipui.
	- b. Inkhâwm ve tûra sâwm.
	- c. Pathian thilthlâwnpêk chhandamna pawm tûra thutlukna kan siam theuh a ngâi tih sawi fiah a, duhthlanna siam tûra ngen.
	- d. Mahni nun dân hmanga Isua hmêlhriattîr.
	- e. Mahni mimal testimoni hrilh.
3. Kristian mi hip thei tak nih chungchâng class-a zirtîr lo ni ta lang, engtia tih dân tûr nge i sawi uar ang?
	- a. Mahni thurinte sawifiah thei tûra Bible chângte hriat.
	- b. Beihpui thlâknaa timna nei lo va thiante sâwm ngam tûra anmahni nêna inkungkaihna tha lo siam lawk.
	- c. Remchânna neih apianga huaisen taka thutak sawi hmiah hmiah zêl.
	- d. Miten nangmah hmanga rawngbâwlna sermon chu an hmuh thiam theih nâna retheite rawngbâwlsak tûra mi inpea tan.
	- e. Remchânna neih apianga sawi nghâl thei tûra mahni testimoni hriat.

Scoring: ‘A’ deuh hlîr i ti a nih chuan: ‘Ngaihtuahna hmang chi’ deuh i ni tihna. Mite ngaihtuahna i chawhthawhsak thei ang. Chutiang chi chu Paula kha a ni (Tirhkohte 17:17 en la).

‘B’ deuh hlîr i ti a nih chuan: ‘Mi sâwm chi’ i ni tihna. Thuhril ngâithla tûrin mi dangte i sâwm ang a. Chutiang chi chu tuichhunchhuaha hmeichhia kha a ni (Johana 4:28–30, 39 en la).

‘C’ deuh hlîr i ti a nih chuan: ‘Mi inhnialpui chi’ deuh i ni tihna. Thutak hmanga mite hmachhawnin i bei ang. Chutiang chi chu Petera tih dân pawh a ni (Tirhkohte 2:14 en la).

‘D’ deuh hlîr i ti a nih chuan: ‘Rawngbâwlsak’ lam i thlang tihna. Mi dang i tanpui ang. Chutiang chi chu Dorki kha a ni (Tirhkohte 9:36 en la).

‘E’ deuh hlîr i ti a nih chuan: ‘Testimoni sawi chi’ i ni tihna. Isuan thil a lo tihsak tawh che chu mi dangte hnênah i hrilh zêl mai ang. Chutiang chu ramhuai–pai pa kha a ni (Marka 5:18, 19 en la).

A dinhmun leh boruak awm dân azirin, Pathian hmangaihna thuchah mite hnêna hrilh tûrin tih dân hrang hrang hi i hmang vek pawh a ni thei e.

#### Thawhtanni

**Chhiar tûr:** Ephesi 2:19–22

Tûn kâr Chângvawn chhiarin vawng la, chutah Life Application Bible thu hi chîk takin ngaihtuah bawk ang che:

Biak in hi eng emaw châng chuan Pathian in tih a ni a. A nihna takah chuan, Pathian chhûngte chu biak in hi a ni lo va, [a chhûnga lawi] mipuite hi an ni. Ani chu keimahniah a lo chêngin, keimahni hmangin lo thlîrtu khawvêl hmaah A intâr lan zâwk thîn a ni.*

<sup>*</sup> Life Application Bible, New International Version (Wheaton, Ill.: Tyndale House Publishers, 1999), p. 2134.

#### Thawhlehni

**Chhiar tûr:** 1 Petera 2:5

Thu Êngtiha Ellen White-i thusawi leh helâia thu lak chhuah hi a danglamna leh inânna hre tûrin khaikhin ang che:

“Khawvêl hmaa Krista aiawhtua tan hi rawngbâwltu tih tûr bîk mai ni lovin, kohhran member tinte tih tûr a ni zâwk. Isua hnên atanga eng zung lo kal chu dawngin, thurin dik lo ngainatna leh dik lohnain an mit a tihdelsak tawhte chhun eng tûra en chhuahtîr tûr a ni.” (Ellen G. White, Pastoral Ministry, p. 153).

#### Nilaini

**Chhiar tûr:** Matthaia 7:13

Tûn kâr zirlâia Thu Pawimawhte tih kha chhiar la, nangma hnêna thu han sawi bîk deuh nia i hriat chu thai bial ang che. Engati nge chu châng chuan tûnah hian i tan awmzia a neih riau chhan?

#### Ningani

**Chhiar tûr:** 1 Korinth 11:1

Ellen G. White-i lehkhabu ziah, Tirhkohte Thiltih bung 58 chhiar la. “Kohhran hruaitu kal tate hma lo lak tawhnate kha chhunzawm zêl dân kawng min kawhhmuh ang che,” tiin tawngtâi ang che.

**Chhiar tûr:** Matthaia 5:14–16
