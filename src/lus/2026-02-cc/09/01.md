> <p>Chângvawn</p>
> “Tûnah chuan felna lallukhum chu ka tân dahin a awm tawh a, chu chu Lalpa ngaihtuahtu fel tak chuan chu mi ni-ah chuan mi pe ang; ka hnênah chauh ni lovin, a inlârna ngâi apiangte hnênah pawh” (Timothea 4:8).

### Chhiar Tûrte
Luka 21:12; 2 Timothea

### Zir Zauna
Tirhkohte Thiltih, bung 48, 49.

### Thu Êng

“Grik mite leh Rom mite dânin mi an heka chuan dikna rorêlnaah, a aiawh tûrin sawipuitu a râwih an phalsak thîn a. Hnial thiamna avâng te, mi rilru hnehtheihna tûra thusawi thiamna te, ngenna te, tawngtâina te leh mittui tlâk zâwih zâwihna chuan aiawha thusawitu chu nasa takin a pui a, mi tang pawh chu thiam a chantîr thei thîn a ni. Chuti a nih loh pawhin hremna tûr chu a tinêp thei hle thîn a ni.... Chu hun pawimawh takah chuan Rom khuaa Kristian tu mah a bula lo dinpui tûrin lo kal an awm hek lo.”—Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 439.

### Engtin Nge I Ngaih?

`Tirhkoh Paulan Isua Krista hminga chhandamna thu a puan khân tihduhdahna namen lo tak a tawrh phah a. Bible-a midang pahnih, Pathian thu âwih a, rinna an neih vânga tihduhdah tuartute chu han târ lang teh. An thil tawn chu tlêm tal ziak zêl ang che.`

### I Hria Em?

“Paula chuan tûnah hian mipui makti taka lo entute hmaah kros chu a châwisâng leh ta a ni. A hmaa mi lo awmte: Juda te, Grik te, Rom mite leh ram hrang hrang atanga lo kal mikhualte a han en chuan, an chhandamna duhsak êm êmin a thlarau chu a lo phur leh ta êm êm a. Eng thil ti tûrin nge helâia hian a lo awm a, eng thil hlauhawmin nge rawn hual vêl a, a chan tûr rapthlâk tak la awm mai tûr chu eng nge ni tih reng a hre châng lo. Mi sualte tâna Pathian hmaa dîlsaktu leh sawipuitu Isua chauh chu a hmu a. Mihringte tawngkam thiamna leh thiltihtheihna aia nasa zâwkin Paula chuan thutak chanchin tha chu a han sawi ta a ni.”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltih, pp. 441, 442.

### Bible Thawnthu

“Chutichuan, kan Lalpa thu hriattîrna chu zahpui suh la, kei, ama mi tang pawh hi mi zahpui hek suh ang che. Chanchin Tha avânga hrehawm hi Pathian thiltihtheihna angzia ziain mi tuarpui zâwk ang che. Ani chuan kan thiltih angte ni lovin, ama ruat leh khawngaihna ang zâwkin min chhandam a, kohna thianghlimin min ko bawk a; chu khawngaihna chu leilung pian hmain Krista Isua-ah chuan min pêk a ni tawh, nimahsela, min chhandamtu Krista Isua inlârna avâng hian tûnah tihlanin a awm ta. Ani chuan thihna chu a tiboralin Chanchin Thain nunna leh boral theih lohna a tilang ta; chu mi Chanchin Tha thu chu hriattîr tûrin tlângaupuituah leh tirhkohvah leh zirtîrtuah ruat ka ni. Chuvâng chuan a ni, hêng thil pawh hi ka tuar ni; nimahsela, ka zak lo ve; ka rina chu ka hria, a hnêna ka kawltîr chu, chu mi ni atân chuan a vawng tha thei tih dik takin ka hre bawk a.

Hei hi hre rawh, ni hnuhnungahte chuan hun khirh takte a lo awm ang. Mi, mahni inhmangaihtu te, tangka ngainatu te, intivei te, chapo te, Pathian sawichhetu te, nu leh pa thu âwih lo te, lâwm nachâng hre lo te, thianghlim lo te, hmangaihna pianpui nei lo te, huaikawmbâwl te, hektu te, insum thei lo te, mi kawlhsen te, tha duh hauh lo te, vervek te, thuhnudâwn lo te, inngaihlu te, Pathian hmangaih aia mahni lâwmna mai hmangaih zâwktute an ni dâwn si; Pathian ngaihsak anna an nei ang a, nimahsela, a thiltihtheihna chu an âwih lo vang; chung ang chu hawisan rawh.

Pathian mithmuh leh mi nung leh mitthi ngaihtuahtu tûr Krista Isua mithmuhah chuan a inlârna leh a ram chhâlin, thahnemngâi takin ka hriattîr a che. Thu chu hril rawh; a hunah te, a hun lovah te pawh bei zêl rawh; dawh thei taka zirtîr chungin an thiam loh hriattîr la, zilh la, fuih rawh.

Intihsiakna tha chu ka bei zo tawh a, ka tlansiakna kawng ka hlen tawh a, rinna chu ka vawng reng tawh a ni; tûnah chuan felna lallukhum chu ka tan dahin a awm tawh a, chu chu Lalpa ngaihtuahtu fel tak chuan chumi ni-ah chuan mi pe ang; ka hnênah chauh ni lovin, a inlârna ngâi apiangte hnênah pawh.” (2 Timothea 1:8–12; 3:1–5; 4:1, 2, 7, 8, NIrV)

### Thawnthu ATangin

`2 Timothea 1:8-ah Paulan Timothea hnênah Lalpa tâna thuhretua tan leh, amah Paula pawh chu Lalpa tawrhnate tawnpuitu a nih avângin zahpui lo tûrin a hrilh a. Tute emaw chu thuhretua an lo tan vânga rilru buaina lo neih chângte i neih tawh em? Mi dangte thuhreua an tanna avânga an thlavâng lo hauhsak a ngaih châng i tawng tawh em? Sawi fiah la.`

`Thu Êng tiha thu awmte kha chhiar leh la. Paula chu Nero-a hmaa a din dâwn khân sawipuitu (ukil) pawh a nei lo. Amah tan zawnga sawipuitu pawh an awm lo. Sawipuitu kan mamawh ve hunah tuin nge min sawisak ang? (Chhiar tûr: 1 Johana 2:1.) Chu chu engtik hunah nge a lo thlen ang?`

`Paulan a sawi chanchin tha chu eng nge ni?`

`Engtin nge Paula ang khân, i chhûngte, thiante leh thenawmte hnênah chanchin tha chu i puan ve theih ang?`

`Engati nge chanchin tha hril avângin miten an tawrh thîn?`

`Hmasialna te, chapona te, awhna te, thuâwihlohna te, lâwmnachâng hriat lohna, ngainatawm lo leh rilru nei lo nihna laka fihlîm theihna tûr eng nge Bible chângin a sawi?`

### I Hria Em?

“Tichuan, in tluk loh nâna veng thei che u leh a ropuina hma taka nasa zeta hlimin bawlhhlawh kai lova dah theitu che u,” (Juda 24, NIrV).

“Remna Pathian ngei chuan in pumin tithianghlim che u rawh se; tin, in thlarau leh, in rilru leh, in taksa chu kan Lalpa Isua Krista lo kal hunah chuan sawisêl bovin apuma humhimin awm rawh se. A kotu che u chu a rinawm a, tih pawh a ti bawk ang.” (1 Thessalonika 5:23, 24, NIrV).

“Chuvângin, Krista Isua, Lalpa chu in lâwm ang khân, amaha zung kai leh rem chhoh deuh deuhva awmin, an zirtîr che u ang khân in rinnaah chuan nghet taka awmin, lâwmthu sawi lo ngahin, amahah chuan awm rawh u,” (Kolosa 2:6, 7, NIrV).

“Lâwm fo ula; bang lovin tawngtâi ula; engkimah lâwmthu hril fo rawh u; chu chu nangmahni lam thuah Krista Isuaah chuan Pathian duhzâwng a ni si a. Thlarau chu timit suh ula,” (1 Thessalonika 5:16–19, NIrV).

“Hnathawktu, thutak thu fel taka hmang chu zak tûr a ni lo angin, Pathian ngaiha thaa inêntîr tûrin thahnem ngâi rawh,” (2 Timothea 2:15, NIrV).

### Hriat Zui Atân

“Finna Tâwploneian a sawi tawh chuan mihring tan hnial tûr a awm tawh lo va, a tan rem fel leh chuan tûr thil rinhlelhawm a awm tawh bawk hek lo”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 453.

#### Zirtâwpni

**Chhiar tûr:** Kolosa 2:6, 7

Tirhkoh Paula kha Krista tâna dopuitu ropui tak a ni. Kum tam tak chhûng chu Krista chanchin tha leh Pathian Thua târ lan thutakte puang tûrin a hmei a pate an lo chhuak tawh thîn a. Mi tam tak chu an rinna avânga tihduhdah tuarte pawh an ni bawk. Pi Ellen White-i chuan Indona Ropui buah chutiang Pathian mi rinawm, an rinna thu dinchhuahpui a, thutak puangtute chanchin thenkhat a ziak a. A hnuaia John Wycliffe-a chanchin târ lan pawh hi chîk takin ngaihtuah ang che. A ngaihdân chuan mipui nawlpuite hian Pathian thu chhiar tûr nei sêla, mithiam leh sakhaw hruaitute zirtîrna dik lo leh hrilhfiah dân chu pawm ngawt lovin, mahni ngeiin zir chiang thei se a duhsak a ni.

“Wycliffe-a chuan College-a a awm lâi atangin Pathian thu a zir tan a. Chutih lâi chuan Bible hi hman lâi tawng chauhva a la awm avângin, lehkha thiamte chauhvin thu dik tuikhur chu an thleng phak a, mi dangte tan chuan khar a ni. Chuta tang chuan Kohhran siamthatna hnaah a lo inbuatsaih tawh reng a ni. . . .

“Wycliffe-a’n Pathian Lehkhabu thu a lo ngaihven hian, a lehkha zirlâi a beih nasat ang bawk khân a bei nasa a....

“Hetah hian Pathian chhandamna leh Krista chauh chu mihringte tâna ngensaktu a ni tih chiang takin a intâr langin a hmu a. Krista rawngbâwl tûrin a inpe ta a, thu dik a hmuh chhuah chu puan darh a tum ta bawk a ni....

“Thu dik kawng zawhna hian dik lohna nên mi a chaltauhtîr ngei thîn. Pope dik lohna a hmu chiang deuh deuh va, Bible thu zirtîrte a sawi uar ve sauh sauh va. Rom kohhran chu Pathian thu kalsanin mihring dân siam chawpah an pakai hlauh tih a hmu a. Huai takin puithiam–hote chu Pathian Lehkhabu hnawl bovah a puh a, mipuite hnênah Bible pêka, a thu Zirtîrte chu kohhranah thuneihna sâng bera hman tûr tiin a phut a.”—Indona Ropui, pp. 80, 81.

`Pathian Thua târ lan thutak anga i din ngheh a ngaihnaa i dinhmun leh i chhanlêtna chu eng nge ni?`
