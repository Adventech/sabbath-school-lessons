> <p>Chângvawn</p>
> “Ngâi teh u, Pain min hmangaihna chu a va nasa em! ‘Pathian faahte’ min vuah tak hi; chutiang chu kan ni reng e. He mi avâng hian khawvêlin min hre lo a ni, amah chu an hriat loh avângin. Duh takte u, tûnah hian Pathian fate kan ni a, engtin nge kan la awm dâwn tih erawh chu a la lang rih lo ve; amah a lo inlâr chuan amah angin kan awm dâwn tih zawng kan hria, amah chu a awm pangngâi ang takin kan hmu dâwn si a” (1 Johana 3:1, 2, NIV).

### Chhiar Tûrte
1 Johana 1:5–10; 4:7–16; 2 Johana 1:7–11

### Zir Zauna
Tirhkohte Thiltih, bung 53, 54, 55

### Thu Êng

“Johana nuna tihthianghlimna lo awm ta nêna inang lo deuh mai chu, a zirtîrpui Juda nunah khân a lo awm ve thung a. A thian angin Juda chuan Krista zirtîr a inti ve tho va, mahsela Pathian ngaihsak anna chauh a nei a ni. Krista nungchang mâwina chu a hre ve tak tak lo, Chhandamtu thute a ngâithla fo va, a ring hle bawk thîn; mahse, a thinlung chu a tihniam duh lo va, a sualnate pawh a puang duh bawk hek lo. Chutiang boruak tha tak mai chu hnarin, Hotupa, hmangaih ve ngei nia a inchhâl thîna chu a chawimâwi lo a ni.”—Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 500.

### Engtin Nge I Ngaih?

A hnuaia thu hi i pawmpui chuan a zawnah ‘D’ ziak la, i pawmpui loh erawh chuan ‘X’ ziak thung ang che:

- Taksa tâna boruak a pawimawh ang chiahin hmangaihna hi i rilru tan a pawimawh.
- Hmangaihna dik chu tuin emaw ‘a pal thluk hunah che’ a lo thleng thîn.
- Mi tam zâwkte chuan hmangaihna dik awmzia hi Bible chhiar atangin an zir thîn.
- Hmangaihna hi mimal (tperson) a ni.
- Hmangaihna leh thutak hi thil hrang pahnih a ni.
- Pathian leh khawvêl hi a rualin i hmangaih kawp thei lo.
- Hmangaihna chu rilrua hriatna (feeling) a ni.
- Hmangaihna dikin a tidanglam thîn che.

### I Hria Em?

“Johana chuan Krista a hmangaihna a thuk em avâng chuan a bul hnaia awm reng mai hi a duh a. Chhandamtu chuan sâwm leh pahnihte kha a hmangaih theuh a, mahsela Johana hian a hmangaih lêt ve lehna rilru a nei nasa ber a ni. Mi dangte aiin a naupang a, naupangte rinna, mi dang aia nei tam zâwkin, a thinlung chu Isua hnênah a hawng a ni. Chutiang chuan Krista khawngaihna pawh alo nei tam zâwk a, amah hmangin Chhandamtu thlarau lam zirtîrna thuk ber mai chu mipuite hnênah hriattîr a lo ni ta a ni.”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltihte, pp. 488, 489.

### Bible Thawnthu

“Thuchah a hnên ata kan hriata in hnêna kan hrilh chhawn tûr che u chu hei hi a ni: Pathian chu eng a ni a, amahah chuan thim reng reng a awm lo. Thima awm siin, “Amah ka pâwl,” kan tih chuan dawt kan sawi a ni, thutak pawh chu kan zawm lo; amaherawhchu amah chu engah a awm angin keini pawh enga awm kan nih chuan kan inpâwl tlâng a, tin, a Fapa Isua thisenin kan sual zawng zawng min tlenfâisak thîn a ni.

“Sual ka nei lo,” kan tih chuan mahni leh mahni kan inbum a, thutak chu keimahniah a awm lo a ni. Kan sualte thupha kan chawi chuan kan sualte ngâidam tûr leh kan fel lohna zawng zawng tlengfâi tûrin amah chu a rinawmin a fel a ni. “Thil ka ti sual lo,” kan tih chuan amah chu dawtheiah kan siam a, a thu pawh keimahniah a awm lo a ni.

Duh takte u, i inhmangaih tawn theuh ang u; hmangaihna hi Pathian laka chhuak a ni si a; mi tin hmangaihna nei apiang Pathian hrin an ni tawh a, Pathian an hre bawk a. Hmangaihna nei lo chuan Pathian a hre lo; Pathian chu hmangaihna a ni si a. He mi-ah hian Pathian hmangaihna kan chungah a lo lang ta, amah avânga kan nun theihna tûrin Pathianin a Fapa neih chhun khawvêla a rawn tirhah hian.

He mi-ah hian hmangaihna a awm, keimahnin Pathian kan hmangaih ka ti a ni lo va, amahin min hmangaih a, kan sualte thupha chawina ni tûrin a Fapa a rawn tîr ka ti a ni zâwk e.

Duh takte u, Pathianin chuti kauva min hmangaih chuan keini pawh kan inhmangaih tawn theuh tûr a ni. Tu man eng lâi mahin Pathian an hmu ngâi lo. Kan inhmangaih tawn chuan Pathian keimahniah a awm reng ang a, a hmangaihna pawh keimahniah a famkim bawk ang.

Amaha kan awm reng leh amah chu keimahniah a awm reng tih he mi-ah hian kan hria, a thlarau min pêk tawh avâng hian.

Pain Fapa chu khawvêl Chhandamtua awm tûrin a rawn tîr tih kan hmu tawh a, kan hriattîr a ni. Tu pawh, “Isua Pathian Fapa a ni,” tia tan apiang chu, amahah chuan Pathian a awm reng a, ani pawh chu Pathian-ah a awm reng bawk a ni. Pathianin keimahnia hmangaihna a neih chu kan hria a, kan ring bawk a ni. Pathian chu hmangaihna a ni; hmangaihnaa awm reng chu Pathian-ah a awm reng a, Pathian pawh amahah a awm reng a ni.

Bumtu tam tak-isua Krista chu tisain a lo kal tih tan lotute kha–khawvêlah hian an lo chhuak ta si a. Chutiang mi chu a ni, bumtu leh Krista dodaltu chu.

Kan thawh chhuah sate kha in hloh loh va, a man chu a kima in hmuh theih zâwk nân inveng tha rawh u. Tu pawh Krista zirtîrna thua awm reng lova khum chuan Pathian a nei lo ve. Chu mi zirtîrna thua awm reng erawh chuan chu mi ngei chuan Pa leh Fapa a nei kawp a ni. Tu pawh chu mi zirtîrna thu chu rawn keng lovin, in hnênah lo kal sêla, in inah thlentîr suh ula, tawngkam tha pawhin thlah suh u; tawngkam thaa thlahtu chu a thiltihsualah chuan a tel ve si a.” (1 Johana 1:5–10; 4:7–16; 2 Johana 7–11, NIV)

### Thawnthu ATangin

`Bible thua thil thar, a awmzia tak pawh i la hriat ngâi loh nia i hriat chu thai bial hnân zêl ang che.`

`Bible thu hlawm tina thu pawimawh bîk lâi deuh nia i hriat chu chhinchhiah zêl ang che.`

`Engati nge Johanan a lehkhathawnah hian Pathian chu ‘eng’ anga sawi nia i rin? Engati nge Pathianah chuan thimna reng a awm lo a tih?`

`Johana hian sual chungchâng eng nge a târ lan?`

`Johana sawi dânin, engtin nge Pathianin a hmangaihna min târ lansak?`

`Engati nge Pathian ringtua inchhâlte tan thutaka awm ‘zui zêl’ chu a pawimawh?`

### I Hria Em?

“Krista Isua-ah chuan serh tan eng mah a sawt lo, serh tan loh pawh eng mah a sawt hek lo; rinna, hmangaihnaa thawh erawh chu a sawt a ni,” (Galatia 5:6, NIV).

“Chatuan nunna neihtîrna khawp kan Lalpa Isua Krista khawngaihna chu nghâk chungin, Pathian hmangaihnaah chuan invawng tha rawh u,” (Juda 21, NIV).

“Thim thiltih rah nei ngâi lovahte chuan telna reng nei suh ula, tel a hnekin a thiam lohzia hriattîr zâwk rawh u,” (Ephesi 5:11, NIV).

“Thu chu tisaah a lo châng a, kan zîngah a awm ta a, (Pa fa mal neih ropui 	angin a ropuizia kan hmu,) khawngaih leh thutakin a khat,” (Johana 1:14, NIV).

“Ka kal hnuin chinghne kawlhte in zîngah an lo lût ang a, pâwlho chu an zuah lo vang tih ka hria e.” (Tirhkohte 20:29, NIV).

### Hriat Zui Atân

“Mimal nungchangah chuan dik lohnate chu hmuh tûr an awm mai thei a, mahsela Krista zirtîr dik tak a lo nih chuan khawngaihna ropui thiltihtheihna chuan amah chu a tidanglamin, a tithianghlim ang.”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 502.

### Mahni Nun Nêna Dawh Zawm Tûr

#### Sabbath

**Chhiar tûr:** 1 Johana 4:16; 2 Korinth 5:14

Engtin Nge I Ngaih? tih kha ti zo lawk la. In tih zawh tlan vêleh in chhannate chu in sâwiho dâwn nia. Hmangaihna hi kan khawvêla a nih dân dik tak hriat sual hlawh ber a ni awm e. Kan hmuh chuan kan hre mai a, mahse engtin nge hmangaih dân kan zir tak ang? Ni e, Zirtîr dang zawngte aiin Johana khân hmangaihna awmzia hrilh fiah leh min siam tha tûra thiltihtheihna a neihzia sawi chian a tum ber awm e.

`1 Johana 4:16 chhiar leh la. Johana sawi dânin, hmangaihna chu tu emaw nge ni a thil eng emaw zâwk?`

`Johanan hmangaihna lo chhuahna hnar a sawi behchhanin, Pathian nêna inlaichînna tel lovin hmangaihna dik a neih theih reng em? I chhanna sawi fiah la.`

`Pathian hmangaihna chuan eng thiltihtheihna bîk tak nge a neih? (2 Korinth 5:14).`

#### Sunday

**Chhiar tûr:** Luka 9:49

Bible Thawnthu chhiar la, chutah Thawnthu Atangin tih chhûnga zawhnate kha chhang zui ang che. Johana lehkhathawn pakhatna bung khatna tîr lamah, Pathian chu ‘eng’ a ni tih a puang a. Eng avângin thil kan hmu thei thîn a. Chu chuan tisa leh thlarau lama thimna a hnawt bo bawk thîn.

`Luka 9:49 chhiar la, Johana nungchangah khân ‘thimna’ eng nge i hmuh?`

`Tûn kâr zirlâi Bible thawnthu-ah hi Johanan Isua kianga hun tam tak a hman hnu, Thlarau Thianghlim tanpuina zâra a nungchang thimna tam tak a hneh hnua a ziah a ni. Nangma nun thimna eng lâiah khân nge Pathian enna i mamawh ang le?`

#### Thawhtanni

**Chhiar tûr:** 1 Johana 3:1, 2

Chângvawn kha chhiar la, hei hi Bible pum puia thu thiltithei ber berte zînga pakhat chu a ni.

Chîk takin ngaihtuah zui vâng vâng la. Tûnah chuan Pathian fate kan lo ni ta tih inhriatna, chhandam nih hlimawmna a taka kan channa tûr chu eng thilten nge min dal thîn?

`Isaia 59:2 chhiar la. Sualna hian Pathian nên min tihrang thîn a. Pathian hlat taka awm angin kan inngâi thîn. Pathian hian min kalsan ngâi em (1 Johana 1:9)? Pathianin min hmangaih reng lâi hian, sual a ni tih kan hriat sa reng tih luih zêlnaah hian eng thil hlauhawm nge awm (1 Timothea 4:1, 2)?`

#### Thawhlehni

**Chhiar tûr:** 1 Johana 1:1–4

Tûn kâr zirlâia Thu Êng tih chhûnga thu lâk chhuahte hian Isua Krista nêna hmangaih taka inlaichînna neihpuinain mi a siam that theihzia fiah takin a târ lang a. Johanan Isua a hmangaih a, Isua a zui lâi khân mi tha famkim chu a ni bîk lo, mahse ni tina Isua kianga a awm thînna chuan zawi zawiin a nun chu a siam tha deuh deuh a ni.

`1 Johana 1:1–4 chhiarin, mi dangte hnêna a hrilh chhawn thîn Isua chungchâng engtin nge a sawi mithla tum ang che. Isua chu a hre ngei a ni tih finfiah thei eng thumalte nge a hman ziak chhuak ang che.`

`Johanan a takin Isua kha lenpuiin, a titipui a, a chenpui bawk thîn. Tûnah Isua chu taksa chuan leiah hian a awm ta lo nain, engtin nge Johana ang khân amah ‘hriatna’ kan neih ve theih ang? Johana 16:12–15 en la.`

#### Nilaini

Tûn kâr zirlâia Thu Pawimawhte tiha eng thu ber hian nge thil hrilh che a, i rilru han luah deuh bîk le? Johana-te hunlâi khân, zirtîrtu der thenkhatten Isua kha Pathian Fapa ni lo awmin an sawi a. Khâng mite khân thil reng reng hi thil tha lo vek a ni tawh a, chutah chuan mihring taksa pawh a tel a, chuvângin Pathian chuan sual tel lo tha famkim a nihna chu suala khat mihring nên chawhpâwlh a phal lek lo vang an ti thîn a ni.

`Engati nge hetiang thurin hi thil hlauhawm a nih i rin? Isua sual tel lo nun, thihna leh thawhlehna kha sirah hnawl ni ta mai se, mihrinnaah hian eng nge lo thleng thîn?`

`Isua kha a taka awm leh sual tâna inthawina a nih hnartute kha Johanan engtin nge a lo sawi (1 Johana 2:22, 23)? Chutiang mi chu tûnlâi khawvêlah hian an la awm cheu em? Sawifiah la.`

#### Zirtawpni

**Chhiar tûr:** Johana 17:3

Hmangaih Johanan Pathian hriatna neih chungchâng heti hian a ziak: “Hei hi chatuana nunna a ni, nang Pathian dik awm chhun leh i tirh Isua Krista hriat hi,” (Johana 17:3, NIV).

Hêng zawhna pahnihte chhanna tûr chîk taka inngaihtuah nân hun tlêm tal hmang teh: Pathian i hria em? Pathianin a hria che em?
