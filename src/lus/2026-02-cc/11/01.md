> <p>Chângvawn</p>
> “Kei Johana, in unaupa leh, Isuaa hrehawmna te, khua leh tui te, chhelna te tâwmpuitu che u hi, Pathian thu avâng leh, Isua hriattîrna thu avângin Patmos Thliarkâr an tihah chuan ka awm a” (Thupuan 1:9).

### Chhiar Tûrte
Thupuan 1:9.

### Zir Zauna
Tirhkohte Thiltih, bung 56

### Thu Êng

“Krista hnaa a rinawm em avângin, Juda rorêltute chuan Johana chu an haw êm êm mai a. Johana hriattîrna chu mipuite benga a rik chhûng chuan, Kristiante an dodalna chuan awmzia a nei lo vang an ti a ni. Isua thiltih makte leh zirtîrnate chu theihnghilh a nih theih nân, hriatpuitu huaisena aw chu tihreh tûr a ni. Johana chu a rinna avânga thubuai nei tûrin Rom khawpuiah koh a lo ni ta hial a. Hetah hian tirhkohte thurinte chu rorêltute hmaah dik lo taka sawi a ni a. Hretu derte chuan sorkâr laka hel leh thil dik lo zirtîr angin amah chu an hek a. Hetianga an hek hian a hmêlmate chuan zirtîr chu tihhlumtîr theih an inbeisei a ni.”—Tirhkohte Thiltih, pp. 510, 511.

### Engtin Nge I Ngaih?

Hêng thil hi i ngaihdâna 1 (buaithlâk tak) atanga 10 (engto ngâi lo) i tih dân tûr angin nambar pe zêl ang che.

- Chabi tih bo
- I unauten eng mah khek lovin eitûr an ei zawhsan vek che
- Cell phone a thi tlat
- Exam result “D” lek i hmu a
- Paper tan
- I iPod an rûksak che
- I thian pakhatin i chungchâng dâwt a sawi
- Fender-bender accident
- Ni thum chhûng zet internet i hmang thei lo

### I Hria Em?

Tirhkoh Johanan inlârnaa Thupuan a hmuhna Patmos Thliarkâr kha tûn thlengin a la awm reng tih i hria em? Turkey leh Grik inkâra Aegean Tuipuia thliarkâr pakhat niin, mihring 3,000 vêl an chêng a. Tûnlâi hian khualzin tam takin an tlawh ruih ruih reng mai a ni. Tirhkoh Johana hunlâite kha chuan, Patmos Thliarkâr chu sorkâr huatzawng titute an tantîrna hmun niin, mi tangte chuan lung lakna khur atangin lung an thiar thîn a. Mi sual tawngtawraw ber berte pawh an chhawr zawi hnapna hmun a ni.

### Bible Thawnthu

“Isua Krista thu puan chhuah, Pathianin a bâwihte hriattîr atân a hnêna a pêk, thil lo awm lawk tûr thu chu; tin, chu chu a vântirhkoh hmangin a bâwih Johana a rawn hriattîr a; chu Johana chuan Pathian thu leh Isua Krista hriattîrna thu, a thil hmuh zawng zawngte thu chu a hriattîr a. He hrilh lawkna bu thu chhiartu leh ngâithlaa a chhûnga thu ziak pawmtute chu an eng a thâwl e; a hun chu a hnai tawh si a.

Johana chuan, Asia rama kohhran pasarihte hnênah chuan: Awm mêka leh awm tawha leh lo la awm tûra hnên atang te, a lalthutphah hmaa Thlarau pasarih awmho hnên atang te, Isua Krista, thu hretu rinawm tak, mitthi zîng ata piang hmasa bera leh leia lalte rorêltute hnên atangte chuan, khawngaihna leh remna in hnênah awm rawh se. Min hmangaiha a thinsenin kan sualte lak ata min tlan chhuak a, khua leh tuiah leh a Pathian leh a Pa lama puithiama min siamtu hnênah, a ni, ama hnên ngeiah ropuina leh rorêlna chu chatuanin awm rawh se. Amen.

Ngâi teh u, ani chu chhumte nên chuan a lo kal dâwn e; mit tin rengin a hmu ang a, a chhuntute ngei pawh khân; khawvêla hnam zawng zawngin amah an tah bawk ang. A ni, Amen.

“Kei Alpha leh Omega ka ni,” Lalpa Pathian, awm mêka leh awm tawha leh lo la awm tûra, Engkimtitheia chuan a ti a ni. Kei Johana, in unaupa leh, Isuaa hrehawmna te, khua leh tui te, chhelnate tawmpuitu che u hi, Pathian thu avâng leh Isua hriattîrna thu avângin Patmos Thliarkâr an tihah chuan ka awm a, Lalpa ni-ah chuan Thlarauvin ka lo awm a, tin, ka hnung lamah tawtawrawt ri ang, aw ring takin, “I thil hmuh tûr chu lehkhabuah ziak la, kohhranho pâwl sarihte hnênah Ephesi khuaah te, Smurna khuaah te, Pergamos khuaah te, Thuatîra khuaah te, Sardis khuaah te, Filadelfia khuaah te, Laodikei khuaah te chuan thawn rawh,” a tih ka hria a.” (Thupuan 1:1–11, NIV)

### Thawnthu ATangin

`Thupuan bu khawilâi bung pawh hi i lo chhiar ve tawh ngâi em? Johanan thu ziak a tan dân eng hian nge han khawih deuh bîk che?`

`Johana khân tu tân nge a ziah sak? He thuchah lo ngâithlatu chu tu nge ni?`

`Eng rilru pu chungin nge a ziah? Lungngâi tak chungin nge, hlim tak chung zâwkin? A thu ziah thuchah bultan dân rilru sûkthlêk nia i hriat ang chu tlêm han ziak teh.`

`Pathian hnên atanga inlârna a hmuh lâiin khawiah nge Johana kha a awm? Eng nge a tih mêk? Châng 9-na chhiar leh la. Engati nge Johana kha Patmos Thliarkâra thawn a nih? An hêkna thubuai chu engte nge ni? Thupuan bu thuhmahruai lam atang hian a ziaktu chungchâng eng thilte nge i zir thar? Johana thuchah hian fiahna huna chhel taka awm chungchâng eng nge min zirtîr?`

### I Hria Em?

“Ani chuan ka hnênah, “Ka khawngaihna i tan a tâwk e; ka thiltihtheihna hi chak lohnaah asin tihfamkimin a awm ni,” a ti ta a. Chutichuan, Krista thiltihtheihna chu ka chunga a awm theih nân lâwm êm êmin ka chak lohnate hi ka chhuang tawh zâwk ang. Chuvângin, Krista avângin chak lohnaah te, chhiatnaah te, neksawrnaah te, tihduhdahnaah te, hnawhchepnaah te ka lâwm thîn a ni; ka chak loh apiangin a ni ka chak thîn ni,” (2 Korinth 12:9, 10, NIV).

“Tihlung nihlohva in awm lohna tûrin hêng hi in hnênah ka sawi tawh hi. Inkhâwmna inahte an hnawntîr ang che u a; a ni, tu pawh a tihlum tûr che u apiangin Pathian chungah thil tha tiin an inhria ang, chutiang hun chu a thleng dâwn,” (Johana 16:1, 2, NIV).

“Lalpa thu chu, chu mi ram khawvêlah chuan a darh ta a. Nimahsela, Judate chuan hmeichhe zahawm Pathian ngaihsakte leh khuaa upahote chu an fuih a, Paula leh Barnaba chu an tihduhdahtîr a, an ram ata an hnawt chhuak ta a,” (Tirhkohte 13:49, 50, NIV).

“Mi fel hrehawmnate chu a tam a, nimahsela, Lalpan chung zawng zawngah chuan a chhan chhuak thîn a,” (Sam 34:19, NIV).

“Lalpa chuan mitdelte mit a tivar thîn a, Lalpa chuan kun ngâwih ngâwihte chu a kai tho thîn a; Lalpa chuan mi felte chu a hmangaih thîn a,” (Sam 146:8, NIV).

### Hriat Zui Atân

“Tawrhna leh tihduhdahna atang hian, Pathian nungchang ropuina chu a mi thlanteah hian tihlanin a awm thîn.”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 518.

### Mahni Nun Nêna Dawh Zawm Tûr

#### Sabbath

**Chhiar tûr:** 2 Korinth 4:17; 11:25

Engtin Nge I Ngaih? tiha tih tûrte kha i ti zo tawh em? Khâng zînga a khawi ber emaw kha i tawng tawhin, i buai ve viau em? Ni tina i rilru tihah deuh thilte i tawh chângin engtin nge i chinfel thîn?

`2 Korinth 4:17 chhiar la. Engtin nge amah Paula leh thuhretua a tanpuiten chonate an hmachhawn thîn?`

`Tûnah 2 Korinth 11:25–28 chhiarin, Paulan a sawi tawrhna ‘nêp tak’ a tih chu mitthla thiam tum ang che. Miten chutiang hremna chu pumpelh an tum deuh chawk a. Enga ti nge Paulan a pal tlang si?`

Engtin nge Paula chu rinawm taka thuhretua a tanna zârah Johana pawhin tihduhdahna chhel taka a tawrh theih phah nia i rin? Harsat buaina tawh chânga tu emaw entawna hmanga chakna i lo neih phah tak an awm em?

#### Sunday

**Chhiar tûr:** Sam 34:19

`Bible Thawnthu chhiar la, chutah Thawnthu Atangin tih chhûnga zawhnate kha chhang ang che. Johana khân Patmos Thliarkâra tantîr leh rim taka hna thawhtîr a ni tûra ngâi chungin, a thuziah sûkthlêk chungchâng ngaihtuah tûra tih i nih kha. Thuhmahruai lam kha chîk taka ennawn lehin, Johanan Isua Krista leh Pathian chungchânga thil hrang hrang a ziahte chu târ lang la. Midangte pawh i hrilh ve dâwn nia.`

`Johanan Pathian lam leh inlârna a thlîr khân a buaina zawng zawng a bo ta duak mai tih i manthiam em? Hei hian fiahna leh harsatnate hmachhawn dân tûr chungchâng eng nge a zirtîr che?`

#### Thawhtanni

**Chhiar tûr:** Thupuan 1:9

Chângvawn hian Johanan a tantîr a nih chhan chiang takin a hria tih a târ lang a. Pathian Thu a puang thîn a, Isua Krista chu Hriakthiha a ni tih pawh mite hnênah a hrilh bawk thîn a ni.

`Thupuan 1:9-a eng thu hian nge Johanan a rinna fiahna a paltlâng tih min hrilh?`

`Tûnah Thupuan 14:9–12 chhiar la. Hêng chângte hian Isua lo kal leh dâwn têpa Krista hnungzuitu la damte chungchâng a sawi a. Engtin nge chu huna harsatna lo thleng chu an hmachhawn dâwn?`

Tûna i harsatna lian ber chu eng nge ni? A ching fel tûrin Pathian i rinchhan ngam em? Amah chu i nghâk fan fan dâwn em?

#### Thawhlehni

**Chhiar tûr:** Luka 6:22

Thu Êng tiha Ellen G. White thuziah lak chhuahte kha chhiar la. Dik lo taka hêk niin, Johana chu Emperor Domitian-a rorêlna hmaa dintîr a ni a. Eng thil dik lo mah a ti lo, chutichungin mi suaksualten dik lo taka an hekna chu a chhiatpui dâwn ta si a ni.

`Dik lo taka min hek leh sawisêltute (Luka 6:22) chu eng tianga ti tûrin nge Isuan thurawn min pêk?`

`Engtin nge min tihnat tumtute (Luka 6:27–36) hi kan chhan ang?`

He thurawn hi a taka hlen theih chi a ni reng em? Mahni tawngkauchhehin, engtin nge remna duhtu ni chungin, sikula mi chhaih nawmnah ang chi chu kan nih loh theih tho dân tûr?

#### Nilaini

**Chhiar tûr:** Johana 16:1, 2

Tûr kâr zirlâia Thu Pawimawh tih huam chhûnga thute hian fiahna leh hun harsa hnuaia chona min siamsakte chu en chhuak la. Johana 16:1, 2 chhiar la.

`Mi thenkhat chuan Pathian rawngbâwla inngâiin zirtîrte chu tihnat an la tum dâwn si a ni tih Isuan a sawi a. Pathian hming chhâl chungin thil chi tin reng mai an ti a. Kristian tu emaw chuan, nau tihtlâk hi tualthat a ni a, chuvângin nau tihtlâksaktu doctor chu tualthat tho a nih avângin, amah thah ve pawh chu thil tih awm tak a ni ve tho titu hnênah eng thurawn nge i pêk ang?`

#### Ningani

**Chhiar tûr:** 2 Timothea 3:12

A rinna avânga tirhkoh Johana inhlanna ang nei ve tûrin kan zînga tam zâwk hi chu koh kan ni ve lo vang. Mahse, eng emaw chenah, “tu pawh Krista Isuaa Pathian ngaihsaka awm tum apiang chuan tihduhdahnate an tuar bawk ang” (2 Timothea 3:12, NKJV) tih a ni tho.

Tûna Pathian nêna in len dun dân behchhanin, he thu kim lo lâi hi han dah kim dâwn teh:
