> <p>Chângvawn</p>
> “Bâwih ni lo tûrin Krista chuan min chhuahtîr tawh kha, chutichuan, ding nghet ula, bâwih nghâwngkawlah chuan tâng leh tawh suh u” (Galatia 5:1, NIV).

### Chhiar Tûrte
Rom; Galatia.

### Zir Zauna
Tirhkohte Thiltih, bung 35, 36.

### Thu Êng

“An nuna Pathian thiltihtheihna lo hre tawhte chu chanchin tha thutak an lo hmangaihna hmasa ber lamah chuan kir leh tûrin Paulan a ngên a. Kristaa mipa leh hmeichhe zalên tak an lo nih theihna hamthatna an neihna chu an hmaah hnial theih miah lohin a târ lang a; a khawngaihna tlanna azâra inpumpêkte chu a felna puana tuam an nih dân pawh chu a hrilh a. Chhandam tûrte reng reng chuan Pathian thilteah chuan nuntawng dik tak mai chu an lo kal tlang ngei tûr a ni.”—Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 346.

### Engtin Nge I Ngaih?

Y (aw), N (ni lo), or M (maithei)?

- Ka duhthlanna hi Pathianin a ngâi pawimawh a, ka hma lam tan nghawng thui tak a nei bawk.
- Han bei sa teh mah ilang, ka thil chîn tha lo lamah bawk ka tlu leh thîn
- Kristian ka ni a, ka duhthlanna siamah hian mawh ka phur.
- Pathian tanpuina zârah chiah thil dik ka ti thei tih ka hria.

### I Hria Em?

“Chutiang chuan Paula chuan Judate leh Gentailte chu inang rengin Pathianin an thinlung tidanglam thei a ni tih a entîr a. Israelte hnêna malsâwmna tiamte chu Krista ringtu tinte hnênah phalsak an ni bawk a. Pathian mite chanchin Isaian a lo puan chu a sawi nawn leh bawk a, ‘Israel mite chu tuifinriat tiau zâtin tam mah sêla, a hrem bâk chauh chu chhandamin an awm ang, Lalpa chuan rang taka ti zo diamin lei chungah a hrem tûr thu a hlen dâwn si a’ a tih kha. Isaia vekin, ‘Mipuite Lalpa chuan chithlahtu min lo zuahsak suh sêla chuan, Sodom khua angin kan lo awm tawh ang a, Gomorra khua anga siamin kan lo awm tawh ang’ ti a a lo sawi lawk ang bawk khân,’ tiin.”—Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 339.

### Bible Thawnthu

“Pathian hriat an duh loh avângin, thil mâwi lote ti tûra a duh loh zâwng rilru neih phalin Pathianin a hawisan ta; chutichuan, fel lohna zawng zawng te, sualna zawng zawng te, duhamna zawng zawng te, suahsualna zawng zawng tein an lo khat a; itsikna te, tualthahna te, inhauna te, bumna te, nungchang that lohna tein an khat a; sesea thu lak hmang te, mi rêl hmang te, Pathian huatzawng te, intivei te, chapo te, mi uang te, thil sual thar tichhuaktu te, nu leh pa thu âwih lo te, rilru nei lo te, thutiam bawhchhetu te, hmangaihna pianpui nei lo te, khawngaihna nei lo te an lo ni a; chutianga thil titute chu thi zia reng an ni tih Pathian ruat chu hre rengin, anmahni chânga ti pawh ni lovin, mi dang ti thînte chungah chuan an lâwm bawk thîn.

“Chuvângin rinna avânga thiam chânga awmin, kan Lalpa Isua Krista avângin Pathian nên inremin i awm ang u; amah avâng vek chuan he khawngaihna kan din nghehnaah hian rinnain kan lût ta a; Pathian ropuina beiseiin lâwmin i awm bawk ang u. Chuti chauh chu ni lovin, kan hrehawmnaah pawh lâwmin i awm ang u, hrehawmna chuan chhelna a siam a, chhelna chuan hriatfiahna a siam a, hriatfiahna chuan beiseina a siam tih kan hre si a; tin, Thlarau Thianghlim kan hnêna min pêk khân Pathian hmangaihna chu kan thinlunga a leih tawh avângin beiseina chuan a timualpho lo va.

Kan la chak loh lâi khân, a hun tein Krista chu mi sualte ai chuan a lo thi ta si. Mi fel aia thih miin a duh awm lo hle si a, mi tha ai erawh chuan mi tu emaw tal chuan thih a ngam mial mahna. Nimahsela, Pathian chuan a mi hmangaihna chu fak tlâkin a lantîr a, mi sualte kan la nih lâia Krista kan aia a thih avâng khân.

Chutichuan, a thisena thiam chantîra kan awm tawh hnu hian, amah avângin Pathian thinurna lak ata chu chhandamin kan awm ngei ang. Hmêlma kan nih lâi maha a Fapa thihna avânga Pathian nêna lo inrem tawh kan nih si chuan, inrema kan awm tawh hnu hian a nunna avângin chhandamin kan awm ngei ang. Chuti chauh pawh a ni lo va, inremna tûna min hmuhtîrtu kan Lalpa Isua Krista avâng chuan Pathian-ah kan lâwm bawk a ni.

“Bâwih ni lo tûrin Krista chuan min chhuahtîr kha, chutichuan, ding nghet ula, bâwih nghawngkawlah chuan tang leh tawh suh u.... Nangni unaute u, bâwih ni lo tûrin koh in ni si a; nimahsela, in bâwih nih lohna chu tisa tâna remchângah hmang suh ula, hmangaihnain rawng inbâwlsak tawn theuh zâwk rawh u.

“Nangmah i inhmangaih angin i vengte i hmangaih tûr a ni,” tih thu kamkhatah hian dân thu zawng zawng chu a famkim tawh a ni si. Nimahsela, in inseh a, in inei tawn si chuan in intihboral tawn loh nân fimkhur rawh u. (Rom 1:28–32; 5:1–11; Galatia 5:1–15, NIV)

### Thawnthu ATangin

Bible Thawnthu tih hi chhiar la, hêng zawhnate hi chhâng nghâl ang che:

`Paulan, “Chutichuan, amah Siamtu aiah Pathian thilsiamte chu chibai bukin, rawng an bâwlsak ta zâwk a ni,” a ti a. Chu chu hmânlâi khawvêlah khân eng tihna ni tûrin nge i ngaih? Tûnlâi atâna a awmzia chu eng nge ni ang?`

`Pathian chungchânga thutak eng aiah nge dawt thu chu an thlan zâwk?`

`Engati nge miten Pathian hriatna neih chu thil atthlâka an ngaih?`

`Mi zual zawng zawngte tân Pathian thiamchantîrna hi a awm thei em? Sawifiah la.`

`Kristaa zalênna chu a taka châng tûrin eng nge ka tih theih ang?`

### I Hria Em?

“Inngaih hi tlânsan rawh u. Mihring thil tihsual tinreng hi taksa pâwn lamah a ni; mi ngaih mang erawh chuan ama taksa chungah thil a tisual a ni. A nih leh, in taksa chu, in chhûnga awm Pathian hnên atanga in hmuh, Thlarau Thianghlim in a ni tih in hre lo em ni le? Mahni ta pawh in ni lo va, Mana lei in ni tawh asin, chuvângin in taksaah chuan Pathian châwimâwi rawh u.” (1 Korinth 6:18–20, NIV).

“A ringa baptisma châng chu chhandamin an awm ang a; a ring lo erawh chu thiam loh chângin an awm ang” (Marka 16:16, NIV).

“Krista hmangaihna ata chu tuin nge min then ang? Hrehawmnain emaw, lungngaihnain emaw, tihduhdahnain emaw, tamin emaw, saruaknain emaw, hlauhawmnain emaw, khândaihin emaw min then ang em ni?” (Rom 8:35, NIV).

“Nimahsela, Pathian chuan a mi hmangaihna chu fak tlâkin a lantîr a, mi sualte kan la nih lâia Krista kan aia a thih avâng khân” (Rom 5:8, NIV).

### Hriat Zui Atân

“Tûnah hian Krista chu chawimâwi niawm taka lang siin, Kristiana inchhâlte chu bumin, rilru thatna dân chu hmuhsittîrin, a thupêkte chu bawhchhe mahse hrem an ni chuang lo vang tiin a zirtîr a. Pathian chhiahhlawh tinte chuan nghet taka dingin, he rinna tikhawlotute hi an do tlat tûr a ni a, an dik lohnate chu thutak thu hmangin hlau miah lovin an thâi lang tûr a ni.”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 345.

### Mahni Nun Nêna Dawh Zawm Tûr

#### Sabbath

**Chhiar tûr:** 1 Petera 2:21.

`Piang thar tih hian i tan eng awmzia nge a neih? Engtin nge a chunga Bible thu hian Kristaa nun thar nei tûra min hruai dân a sawi?`

`Engtin Nge I Ngaih? tih chhûng thu kha en nawn leh la. Zawhna chhan nân eng nge behchhana i hman: Mahni ngaihdân nge, Pathian Lehkha Thu nge? Chanchinbu? Thiante em ni zâwk? Chûng chu rinna nghah nân a tha tâwk bawk ang em? Sawifiah la.`

#### Sunday

**Chhiar tûr:** Rom 1:28

He châng, “Pathian hriat an duh loh avângin, thil mâwi lote ti tûra a duh lohzawng rilru neih phalin Pathianin a hawisan ta” (Rom 1:28, NLT) tih hi ennawn leh la.

`Engtin nge he châng (Rom 1:28) hian Galatia 5:1-a Paula thusawi, “Zalên ni tûrin Krista chuan min chhuahtîr kha, chutichuan, ding nghet ula, bâwih nghawngkawlah chuan tang leh tawh suh u,” tih hi hriatthiamna min neihtîr? Hêng thu pahnih: âtna leh bâwih nihna tih inzawmna chu eng nge ni?`

#### Thawhtanni

**Chhiar tûr:** Galatia 5:1

`Chângvawn thu kha chhiar lehin, tûnlâi mahni tawng kauchhehin a awmzia ziak ang che.`

#### Thawhlehni

**Chhiar tûr:** Marka 16:16.

Thu Êngtih kha ennawn lehin, hêng zawhnate hmang hian târ lang nawn leh ang che:

- “Chanchin tha thutaka hmangaihna hmasa” chu a takin i lo châng ve tawh em? Chuti ni se, eng nge ang i tih?
- Kristaa mi zalên (a hmei a pain) ni tûra chona siamsak nân Paulan eng tanfungte nge a hman?
- Ellen White-i thusawi: “Pathian thilte mimal taka a tak ngeia channa” tel lovin chhandamna a awm thei lo a tih atanga manthiam tûr kan ni em? Engvângin nge a nih a, a nih loh bawk? Chutiang thil Pathiana a taka channna châwm puitling tûrin eng nge kan tih theih ang?

#### Nilaini

**Chhiar tûr:** Galatia 4:4–7.

Thu Pawimawhte tih thute kha chhiar la. Pathianin kan taksa hi a Thlarau Thianghlim temple ni tûrin a duh tih hriain, engati nge kan thlarau dinhmun tin reng hi veng tha viau tûr kan nih?

`Engtin nge hur leh bawlhhlawhna hian Thlarau Thianghlim temple kan nih a, Isua nêna inlaichînna dik a taka kan channa tûr chu min dâl thîn?`

#### Ningani

**Chhiar tûr:** Kolosa 3:11

Chhandamna a taka chan thu-ah hêng Bible chângte hi chhiarin, a thu tum khâikhâwm zêl ang che:

- Jeremia 31:34
- Ezekiela 33:11
- Marka 9:23
- Johana 3:3–8, 16
- Rom 5:6–10
- 2 Korinth 5:17–21
- Ephesi 2:8, 9

`Krista chhandamna thilthlâwnpêk chungchâng eng nge hêng Bible châng atang hian i zir chhuah? Engtin nge i kiang vêla mite hnêna Pathian khawngaihna chu i lantîr zêl theih ang?`

#### Zirtawpni

**Chhiar tûr:** Rom 8:25

I thiantuten emaw, chhandamna nih rinngamna leh a taka i channa chungchâng zâwt ta che sêlang, eng nge i hrilh ang? Bible zirtîrna hnuk hniam chuang si lovin, engtin nge chanchin duh awm lo tak nei hnênah delhkilh nei lova pawm kan nih dân chu i hrilhfiah ang?
