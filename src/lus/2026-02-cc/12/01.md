> <p>Chângvawn</p>
> “He hrilh lâwkna bu thu chhiartu leh ngâithlaa a chhûnga thu ziak pawmtute chu an eng a thâwl e; a hun chu a hnâi tawh si a” (Thupuan 1:3)

### Chhiar Tûrte
Tirhkohte 18:18–28; 1 & 2 Thessalonika

### Zir Zauna
Tirhkohte Thiltih, bung 25; 26

### Thu Êng

“Thupuana chhinchhiahna tin rengte hi an hrilhfiah vek theih loh avângin ‘a chhûnga thutak awmzia hre tûra he lehkhabu zir hi eng mah a ni lo’ tiin tu man ngâi suh se. Johana hnêna hêng thurukte tilangtu hian thutak tihtak zeta zawngtute hnênah vân thil temlawkna A pe ang. Thutak pawm tûra thinlung hawngtute chu a zirtîrnate hriatthiamtîr an ni ang a, ‘He hrilhlâwkna thute hria a, a chhûnga thu ziak pawmtute,’ hnêna malsâwmna tiam chu phalsak an ni ang.”—Tirhkohte Thiltih, pp. 524, 525.

### Engtin Nge I Ngaih?

A khawi zâwk nge:

- Johanan Thupuan Bu a ziah lâia hrilhlâwkna thilpêk a dawng ang khân, Pathianin tûnlâi mite pawh hi hrilhlâwkna thilpêk hmangin mal a sâwm tih ka ring. Nge, Johana khân hrilhlâwkna thilpêk danglam bîk tak chu a dawng a, chuta chin chu tu mahin khatiang kha chu an dawng ve ta lo a ni tih ka ring.
- Thupuan Bu hi Isua chungchâng a ni ber. Nge, Thupuan Bu hi hrilhlâwkna chungchâng a ni ber zâwk.
- Thupuan Bu-a Asia rama kohhran pasarihte hnêna thuchah kha a chhe lam aiin a tha lam a ni zâwk. Nge, Thupuan Bu-a Asia rama kohhran pasarihte hnêna thuchah puan kha a tha lam aiin thil duhawm lo lam a ni zâwk.
- Thupuan sawi dânin, Seventh–day Adventist Kohhran hi a la bang kohhran chu a ni. Nge, Thupuan sawi dânin, kohhran la bang chu Krista zuitute–kohhran leh pâwl sawi chuang lovin–Pathian thupêkte vawng a, Isua hriattîrna thu neitute an ni.

### I Hria Em?

“Johana hnawhchhuah a nih khân thutak hmêlmate chuan Pathian thuhretu rinawm taka aw chu chatuan atâna hmehmih an lo beisei a; mahse, Patmos Thliarkârah chuan zirtîr chuan thuchah a dawng a, chu chuan hun tâwp thlenga kohhran awm chu a tichak zui zêl dâwn a ni. An thiltih dik lohah chuan mawh an phur tho dâwn nachungin, Johana hnawtchhuaktute chu vân thil tum kenkawhtîr tûra Pathian hmanrua an lo ni a, eng tihmih an tumna ngei chuan thutak chu rinna langsar takah a dah a ni.”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltih, pp. 521, 522.

### Bible Thawnthu

“Isua Krista thu puan chhuah, Pathianin a bâwihte hriattîr atân a hnêna a pêk, thil lo awm lawk tûr thu chu; tin, chu chu a vântirhkoh hmangin a bâwih Johana a rawn hriattîr a; chu Johana chuan Pathian thu leh Isua Krista hriattîrna thu, a thil hmuh zawng zawngte thu chu a hriattîr a. He hrilh lawkna bu thu chhiartu leh ngâithlaa a chhûnga thu ziak pawmtute chu an eng a thâwl e; a hun chu a hnai tawh si a.

Johana chuan, Asia rama kohhran pasarihte hnênah chuan: Awm mêka leh awm tawha leh lo la awm tûra hnên atang te, a lalthutphah hmaa Thlarau pasarih awmho hnên atang te, Isua Krista, thu hretu rinawm tak, mitthi zîng ata piang hmasa bera leh leia lalte rorêltute hnên atangte chuan, khawngaihna leh remna in hnênah awm rawh se. Min hmangaiha a thinsenin kan sualte lak ata min tlan chhuak a, khua leh tuiah leh a Pathian leh a Pa lama puithiama min siamtu hnênah, a ni, ama hnên ngeiah ropuina leh rorêlna chu chatuanin awm rawh se. Amen.

Ngâi teh u, ani chu chhumte nên chuan a lo kal dâwn e; mit tin rengin a hmu ang a, a chhuntute ngei pawh khân; khawvêla hnam zawng zawngin amah an tah bawk ang. A ni, Amen.

“Kei Alpha leh Omega ka ni,” Lalpa Pathian, awm mêka leh awm tawha leh lo la awm tûra, Engkimtitheia chuan a ti a ni.

Kei Johana, in unaupa leh, Isuaa hrehawmna te, khua leh tui te, chhelnate tawmpuitu che u hi, Pathian thu avâng leh Isua hriattîrna thu avângin Patmos Thliarkâr an tihah chuan ka awm a, Lalpa ni-ah chuan Thlarauvin ka lo awm a, tin, ka hnung lamah tawtawrawt ri ang, aw ring takin, “I thil hmuh tûr chu lehkhabuah ziak la, kohhranho pâwl sarihte hnênah Ephesi khuaah te, Smurna khuaah te, Pergamos khuaah te, Thuatîra khuaah te, Sardis khuaah te, Philadelfia khuaah te, Laodikei khuaah te chuan thawn rawh,” a tih ka hria a.

Tichuan, dragon chu hmeichhe chungah chuan a lo thinur a, a thlah la awm Pathian thupêkte zawma Isua hriattîrna thu pawmte chu do tûrin a kal ta a.

Leia awmte hnênah leh hnam tin reng te, chi tin reng te, tawng tin reng te, mi tin reng te hnêna tlângaupui tûra chatuana Chanchin Tha nei vântirhkoh dang vânlâizawla thlâwk ka hmu a; tin, ani chuan aw ring takin, “A rorêl hun chu a lo thlen tak avângin Pathian chu hlau ula, chawimâwi rawh u; vân te, lei te, tuifinriat te, tuihna te Siamtu chu chibai buk rawh u,” a ti a.

Vântirhkoh dang a pahnihna chuan, “Babulon khaw ropui, a mi ngaih chin vânga thinurna uain hnam zawng zawng intîrtu chu a tlâwm ta, a tlâwm ta,” tiin a rawn zui a.

Vântirhkoh dang a pathumna chuan aw ring takin, “Tu pawhin Sakawlh leh a lem chibai bukin an chalah emaw an kutah emaw chhinchhiahna an neih chuan, chu mite ngei chuan Pathian thinurna uain, a thinurna nova eng mah pâwlh lova siam chu an in ang a, vântirhkoh thianghlimte mithmuh leh Berâm No mithmuhah chuan mei leh kata nghaisakin an awm bawk ang.

Chung mite nghaisakna meikhu chu chatuanin a chho vâng a; sakawlh leh a lem chibai buktute, a hming chhinchhiahna nei apiangte pawh chuan a chhun a zânin chawlh reng an nei lo vang,” tiin anni chu a rawn zui a. Mi thianghlim, Pathian thupêk âwihtute leh Isua zuitu rinawmte tan hetah hian chhel a tulzia a lang.

Tichuan, Pathian chibai buka buk tûrin a ke bulah ka bawkkhup a. Ani chuan ka hnênah, “Heti chuan ti suh khai; kei chu nangmah leh i unau, Isua hriattîrna thu pawmte bâwihpui ka ni a lâwm; Pathian chibai buk zâwk rawh; Isua hriattîrna thu chu thu hrilhna thlarau a ni si a,” a ti a.” (Thupuan 1:1–11; 12:17; 14:6–12; 19:10, NIV)

### Thawnthu ATangin

Bible Thawnthu hi chhiar la, chutah Pastor Morris Venden-a thuziak lak chhuah hi chhiar zui ang che:

“[Seventh–day Adventist] kohhran thurin danglam bîk tak pakhat [a awm]. Mi dang tute emaw Sabbath thutak ring a, kum eng emawti chhûng lo vuan tlat tawhte an awm a. Mi dang, keini ang tho va mihring thihna dinhmun ring an awm. Mi dang, hrilhlâwkna thilpêk pawm ve te an awm a; mi dang tam tak, kohhran pâwl tin deuhthawah hian Krista lo kal lehna tûr ringtu thahnem tak an awm a ni. Sakhuana khawvêla kan thawh chhuah ve danglam bîk deuh tak awm chu, vântirhkoh pathum thuchah, biak buk leh rorêlna chungchâng zirtîrna nêna inzawmna nei chu a ni.”2

Ngaihtuah zui tûr zawhnate:

`Vântirhkoh pathum thuchah lâimu tak chu eng nge ni?`

`Engtin tak chuan nge in tualchhûng kohhran khân Thupuan 14-a vântirhkoh pathum thuchah puan chhuah kawngah hma in lak?`

`Engtin nge hêng thuchahte hian kan nunphung a nghawng?`

`Hêng thuchahte hi keima nunah bel dâwn ta ilang, ka nun chu eng nge a an ang?`

### I Hria Em?

“Mihring tawngte leh vântirhkoh tawngtein thu sawi mah ila, hmangaihna ka neih si loh chuan dâr ri mai mai emaw, dâr benthek, ri mai mai emaw ka lo ni ang. Thu hrilh theihna neiin, thuruk zawng zawng leh hriatna zawng zawng hre vek mah ila, tlâng sawn theihna khawp hial rinna famkim nei mah ila, hmangaihna ka neih si loh chuan eng mah ka ni lo vang” (1 Korinth 13:1, 2, NIV).

“Israel mite chu tuifinriata tiau zâtin tam mah sêla, a hrem bâk chauh chu chhandamin an awm ang,” (Rom 9:27, NIV).

“Chutiang bawkin tûn lâi pawh hian khawngaiha thlan ang zia ziain paih thlâk bâk chu an la awm,” (Rom 11:5, NIV).

“He ram Chanchin Tha hi hnam zawng zawng hriattîrna tûrin khawvêl zawng zawngah hrilhin a awm ang; chu mi zawhah chuan tâwpna chu a lo thleng ang,” (Matthaia 24:14, NIV).

“Amaherawhchu, amah chu engah a awm angin keini pawh enga awm kan nih chuan kan inpâwl tlâng a, tin, a Fapa Isua thisenin kan sual zawng zawng min tlenfâisak thîn a ni,” (1 Johana 1:7, NIV).

### Hriat Zui Atân

“Khawvêl mi maktaduaih tam takte nêna khaikhin chuan Pathian mite chu, an nih dân thîn angin, mi tlêm te an ni a; mahsela, a thua tihlan thutak tan rinawm taka an din chuan Pathian chu an inhumhimna a ni ang.”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 529.

#### Sunday

**Chhiar tûr:** Thupuan 12:17

‘Hriattîrna’ emaw, ‘Isua Krista hriatpuina’ tih emaw hi eng nge a awmzia? “Grik tawng chuan ‘hriattîrna’ (or ‘thuhretu’) anga hriat niin, Kristiante chuan Isua thupuia neiin, a thuhretuah an tang emaw, ‘hriattîrna’ an puan emaw a ni thîn. Isua hnên atanga lo zichhuak chu zâwlneite hmangin a kohhranah puan chhuah a ni ta a ni (1:2). Thupuan 19:10-ah ‘Isua hriattîrna thu’ chu ‘hrilhlâwkna thlarau’ tia sawi fiah a ni.

“Isua hnên atanga thuchahte dawng a, mipuite hnêna puan chu zâwlneite hna bîk deuh a nih thîn avângin, Isua hriattîrna thu tih hrilh fiahna pawh chu, Isuan kohhran hnêna a hriattîrna thu a ni tih hi a pawmawm hle a. Seventh–day Adventist-te chuan he thu hi chutiang zawng chuan hrilh fiahin, ‘a la bang’ [kohhran] pawh chu an zînga hrilhlâwkna thilpêk hmanga a inpuan chhuahna chu a danglamna tûr tak a ni ang tih an ring.”—The Seventh–day Adventist Bible Commentary, vol. 7, p. 812.
