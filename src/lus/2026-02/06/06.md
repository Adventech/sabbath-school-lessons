---
title:  Mosia’n Israel hnam a dîlsak
date:   07/05/2026
---

`Exodus 32:31, 32 chhiar la. He thu hian Mosia chanchin leh tawngtâina chungchâng eng nge min hrilh?`

Mosia hian huaisen takin Pathian mite tân a dîl fo va. Israel fate’n tui in tûr an tlâkchham tum (Exod. 15:25; Exod. 17:2– 6) te, ei tûr an neih loh châng (Num. 11:21, 22) te leh amah ngei pawh a beidawn êm êm lâi (Num. 11:11–15) te khân Pathian lam a hawi vat thîn.

Israel fate’n Pathianin thu a rawn thlunpui hnu deuh lawka rangkachak bâwngno lim an lo siam ngawt tum pawh khân heti hian an hnênah a lo sawi a: “Nangmahni tiboral tûra LALPA thinurna, a thinrimna leh lungnih lohna nasa tak chu ka hlau si a. Nimahsela, chumi tum pawh chuan LALPA’N ka thu chu mi lo ngaihthlâksak a” (Deut. 9:19) tiin.

Ram Tiam va en thlatute an lo kîr leh pawh khân, “Chutichuan, LALPA’N, ‘Ka tiboral dâwn’ a tih che u avâng khân LALPA hmaah ka bawkkhup a, ni sawmli leh zan sawmli ngawt ka bawhkhup kha” (Deut. 9:25) tiin a lo sawi bawk a.

Tin, Biak bûka rawngbâwl tûra Levi chi a tihhran khân heti hian a lo sawi leh bawk: “Tin, a hma ang bawk khân tlângah chuan ni sawmli leh zan sawmli ka han awm a; tin, chumi tum pawh chuan LALPA’N ka thu chu a ngâithla a, LALPA chuan a tiboral duh ta lo che u a nih kha” (Deut. 10:10) tiin. Hetiang hian Pathian chuan Mosia tawngtâinate kha a lo ngâithlâksak thîn a ni.

Mosia nun atang hian tawngtâina leh Pathian vawn tlat a pawimawhzia chungchângah zirlâi zir chhuah theih eng emaw zât a awm a, chûng zînga thenkhat chu i lo târ lang teh ang:

- **Mosia hian Pathian a hmangaih êm êm a, a nungchang pawh a hre chiang hle bawk.** Pathian chuan Mosia hnênah hian, “LALPA, LALPA Pathian chu zahngaihnaa khat leh mi khawngaih thei tak, thinnel, thatna leh thutak ngah êm êm” a nih thu sawi (Exod. 34:6, NKJV).
- **Mosia hi a huaisenin a rinawm hle.** Ram Tiam pana an zin kawngah khân harsatna tawh châng a ngah hle a, chutiang hunah pawh chuan Pathian a vuan tlat zêl tho. Keimahni ang bawka buaina tâwk ve thîn a ni a; mahse, Pathian thiltihtheihna, a awmpuina leh a hruainaah a rinna a nghat tlat thîn (Exod. 33:13).
- **Pathian hnênah a thu lo thlun tawh chu hre reng tûra ngênin (Exod. 32:13), a mite hnêna thu a tiamte pawh hlen chhuak ngei tûrin a phût thîn a (Deut. 7:8); tin, tûn hmaa Pathian hruaina an lo chan thin tawh dân pawh a hre rêng thîn bawk (Deut. 8:2).**
- **Pathianin a tawngtâina a rawn chhânna chu eng pawhni se, lâwm takin a pawm mai thîn.** Pathian nên inzawmna nghet tak kan neih vâng chuan kan duh apiang kan nei zêl dâwn tihna a ni chuang lo va (Deut. 3:23–29). Kan duh ang chhânna kan dawng emaw, dawng lo emaw a nih pawhin bâng lova tawngtâi chhunzawm zêl tho tûr kan ni (Luka 18:1–8).

`Tûnah khân i tawngtâisak ngâi tu nge awm? A tâna i tawngtâi nghâl theihna tûr dâltu a awm em?`
