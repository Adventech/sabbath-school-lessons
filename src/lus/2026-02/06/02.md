---
title:  Mi rinawm Daniela
date:   03/05/2026
---

Daniela hi Bible-a kan hmuh rinna pasaltha ropui tak takte zînga pakhat a ni a. A chanchin sawina hmasa berah chuan hetiang thu hi kan hmu a: “Daniela chuan a rilruin lal chaw leh a uain in thinte chuan intihbawlhhlawh loh a tum tlat a” (Dan. 1:8). Tin, amah leh a thian pathumte hnênah hian Pathian chuan “hriatna bâkah, thu leh hla leh finna lama thiamna pawh a pe bawk a; Daniela phei hi chuan inlârna leh mumang awmzia zawng zawng a hrethiam nghe nghe (Dan. 1:17, NKJV). Bible chuan Daniela hi mi fing niin a sawi a (Dan. 1:20; Dan. 2:14, 21, 23, 48), a chhan pawh amahah hian Pathian Thlarau a awm vâng (Dan. 4:9, 18; Dan. 5:14; Dan. 6:3) leh Pathianin a hmangaih êm vâng a ni (Dan. 9:23, Dan. 10:11). Hêngte hian Pathian nêna inzawmna nung leh nghet a neihzia a tilang chiang hle.

Lal Nebukadnezzara’n Babulon rama mi fing zawng zawngte tihhlum vek an nih tûr thu a chhuah khân Daniela hian Nebukadnezzara mumang chungchângah Pathian khawngaihna a dîl a (Dan. 2:18). Khatia Pathianin Nabukadnezzera mumang leh a awmzia a rawn hriattîr veleh khân Pathian fakin a tawngtâi nghâl a nih kha.

`Daniela 2:20–23 chhiar la. Daniela tawngtâi chhan hi eng nge ni a, he a tawngtâi thu chham atang hian eng nge zir chhuah theih kan neih?`

Hun lo inher liam chho zêlah khân Babulon chu lal eng emaw zât rorêlna hnuaiah a awm hman a, chutiang kârah pawh chuan Daniela hi lalte hnêna thurâwn petu a ni chhunzawm zêl tho. “Rilru tha filawr pu a nih avângin hotu dangte leh thuneitute lakah langsâr bîk larh a, lal pawhin ram pum pui chunga thuneitu-ah dah a rîlrûk hial a ni” (Dan. 6:3, NKJV). Tin, “a rinawm êm êm bawk a, amahah chuan tihsual emaw, dik lohna emaw hmuh tûr rêng rêng a awm lo” (Dan. 6:4, NKJV). A hotu nihpuite’n îtsîkin amah tihhlum dân kawng zawng nasa thîn mah se (Dan. 6:5–9), Pathian lakah a rinawm tlat reng tho a, hlau miah lovin a tawngtâi chhunzawm zêl thîn.

`Daniela 6:10, 11 chhiar la. Hêng thute hian Daniela chanchin eng nge min hrilh?`

Daniela hi harsatna a tawh chângin a tawngtâi thîn a. Lal thupêk chhuah hi amah tihhlum duhtute ruahmanna mai a ni tih chu hre tho mah se, a tawngtâi thin dân pângngâiin a la tawngtâi chhunzawm zêl tho (ni tin vawi thum a tawngtâi ziah a; tin, a tawngtâi hian a tukverh a hawng a, Jerusalem lam a hawi kher thîn bawk). Tin, thingthi ngeiin a tawngtâi thîn a, Pathian hnênah lâwmthu sawiin a hnênah dîlna a thlen thîn a ni.

`Daniela chanchin atang hian tawngtâi loh chhualam atâna kan hman thinte dawrâwmzia kan lo hmu thiam em?`
