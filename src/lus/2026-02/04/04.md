---
title:  Bible thutak
date:   21/04/2026
---

Kum 1960 chho vêla liberal theologian thenkhatte khân a tum ber chu Pathian thu zirna leh hrilhfiahna (theology) atanga Pathian dah chhuah a ni ber a. Kum 2017-a chhuak Time chanchinbua thupui berah pawh khân “Thutak Hi A Thi Ta Em Ni?” tih chu hman a lo ni tawh bawk. Hei hi thil mak leh bengverh angreng tak a nihna chen a awm a, a chhan chu kan tûnlâi khawtlâng nun leh ram dinhmun a târ lan chian êm vâng a ni. “Thutak” han tih hrim hrim pawh hian mi ngaihven a hlawh tawh mang lo va, thutak hriaa lawi pawh kan awm tawh lo niin a lang hial a nih hi. Kan tûnlâi khawtlâng nunah hi chuan thil dik leh dik lo hriat theihna tûr tehfung mumal a awm hran tawh lo va, innghahna lungphûm nghet leh hun lo inher danglam zêlah pawh rin tlâk ni tûr kan nei tawh hek lo. Isua erawh kha chuan “Kei hi . . . thutak ka ni” tiin a lo sawi chiang hle a (Joh. 14:6). A Thu hian thutak a nih ziah chiang leh fiah takin a lantîr rêng bawk a ni.

**A hnuaia Bible chângte hi zâwimuangin chhiar la, i chhiar zawhah vawi khat dang chhiar leh ang che. Tichuan, eng thu nge min hrilh i sawi dâwn nia.**

`Joh. 17:17`

`Thuf. 30:5,6`

`Sam 12:6`

Bible hian thutak bulpui ber Isua chu a danglam ngâi lo tih a puang a. Chutih rual chuan, Pathian Thu kan han chhiar hian Pathian mizia leh nihphung mai bâkah, a thutak kan hriatthiam dân erawh a thang danglam thei thung. “Tih tak zeta zawngtute’n an la hmuh chhuah belh zêl tûr thutak tam tak a awm a.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 704. Ellen G. White-i “thutak” tih a sawi rêng rêng hian a rilrua awm chu Pathianin a Thu kal tlanga min pêk thutak hi a ni thîn. Kan lo la hriat ngâi loh êng dang chhar chhuak tûrin Bible kan zir thîn tûr a ni a, a chhan pawh chûng kan thutak hriat tharte chu tûn kan lo hriat tawh sate nên inkalh lovin, chûngte chungah chuan inrem chho a nih vâng a ni.

`1 Thessalonika 2:13; Sam 33:4, 5; leh Ephesi 1:13 chhiar la. Hêng Bible chângte’n an sawi chu eng nge ni?`

A tâwp berah chuan, Bible, Bible chauh hi thutak nia kan pawm leh hriat innghahna hnâr a ni tûr a ni. Thu hnâr dang zawng zawng chu Pathian Thu hmanga fiah leh teh vek tûr a ni a. Keimahni ngaihdân leh thil chhût thiamna ngei pawh hi Pathian Thu hmanga fiah a tûl tho mai.

`Mi thenkhat chuan thutak a awm hran lo ni âwm te’n an sawi mai thîn a. Enga ti nge chutiang ngaihdân chu a dik loh mai bâkah, mahni inkalh a nih hial nghe nghe zâwk le? A chhan pawh thutak a awm lo tia an sawi chu thutakah an puang a ni ve tho tho si atin a ni.`
