---
title:  Zir belhna
date:   24/04/2026
---

Dârkâr 24 liam ta maia i thu sawite kha ngaihtuah lêt leh ta la, eng ang thute nge i lo sawi le? Hmangaihna, khawngaihna, lâwmna, mi dangte châwikânna, beidawnna, chauhna, hlauhthâwnna, thinrimna, mi dangte rêlna leh an chhiatna tûr thute a ni em? Bible chuan, “Thinlunga khat liam chu kâin a sawi chhuak thîn asin” (Mat. 12:34) tiin a lo sawi a. Kan thinlungah thil tha lo tak tak a awm a nih chuan kan thu sawiah a rawn lang chhuak thîn ang.

A tute pawh hian han hnual châng, han chauh êm êm tum leh rilru hah châng te kan nei theuh a, chutianga kan awm tum chuan kan sawi duh loh, a hnua kan la inchhîr lehna tûr thute kan sawi chhuak fo thîn. Chutih lâiin, kan thinlungah mi tu emaw hmangaihna a awm a nih erawh chuan, kan thil sawiah hmangaihna thu a tel tam hle thîn thung ang.

Chutiang chiah chuan Pathian Thu pawh hian Pathian thinlunga thil awmte leh kan lama tih a tumte chu a sawi chhuak a. Pathianin a sawi ngei, ama thinlung atanga lo chhuak thute kan Bible-a a lo awm hi a ropui ngawt mai. Pathian thute hian thiltihtheihna ropui tak a nei a ni tih chu chanchin kal tawh kan chhui kîr pawhin hmuh tûr tam tak a awm a, keimahni ngei pawh hi a chang tawh kan lo ni ve fo tawh nghe nghe ngei ang.

Mi thenkhat chuan Bible hi nun dân tha inzirtîrna tûr lehkhabu, kan hun lâi leh kan dinhmun chelh nêna inrem ang zêla pawm emaw, pawm lo emaw mai tûr niin an ngâi a. Mahse, chutiang ngaihdân chu Pathian thu nung a ni tia pawm nên chuan inang lo tak a ni. He thu hi kan nunna a nih avângin kan thiltih, kan thu sawi leh kan ngaihtuahna zawng zawng kâihruaitu leh thununtu a ni tûr a ni a. Hemi âia hniam leh nêp zâwk anga Pathian thu pawm chu Pathian thu hnâwlna a ni. Tin, Pathian thu ringtu nia inchhâlte’n Pathian thuin thu a neihna an hnâwl tlat si thîn hi thalâite zînga Bible rin lohna leh Pathian laka rinawm lohna thlentîrtu langsâr ber a ni bawk.”—Ellen G. White, Education, p. 260.

**Sawi ho tûrte:**

`Bible leh Pathian i rin chhan hrang hrang awmte chueng nge ni? I rin âiin a tam daih maithei.`

`Engtin nge Bible chhiar leh tawngtâi hi Pathian nênain inzawmna tinghettu a ni tih chiang takin i tihlan theih ang? Bible chhiar leh tawngtâi lovin Pathian nên inzawmna nghet tak in nei thei ang em?`

`Pathian nêna inzawmna thûk zâwk neih duhtupakhatin, Bible-a eng lehkhabu ber hi nge chhiar tûr ni ang tiin rawn zâwt ta che se la, eng bu nge i lo kawhhmuh ang?`

`Engtin nge Lalpa kaa thu tin chhuak (Deut. 8:3) hi a takin i lo nunpui theih ang? I nunpui ngei a nih tih engtin nge i nunah a lo lan chhuah ang?`

`Hêng Bible chângte hian Pathian thu chungchângengtin nge an sawi? Heb. 11:3; Sam 33:6; Mat. 11:4, 5; 1 Thes. 4:16; Eph. 6:17; Jak. 1:21.`

**Zirlâi khâikhâwmna:** Bible hi a nungin thil a ti thei a, Pathian nêna kan inzawmna a lo than zêl theih nân kan chhiar thîn tûr a ni. Pathian nungchang leh hun hrang hranga mite hnêna thu a lo sawite min hrilh a ni mai lo va, kan hnênah pawh rilru inngâitlâwm taka kan chhiar a nih chuan thu a la sawi zêl a ni.
