### Chhiar tûrte
Gen. 3:1–5, Lev. 20:26, 1 Sam. 2:2, 1 Joh. 4:7–19, Gen. 1:1, Gen. 2:7, Mat. 1:23, Mat. 28:20.

> <p>Châng vawn</p>
> “Hei hi chatuana nunna a ni, nang Pathian tak chauh leh i tirh Isua Krista hriat hi” (Johana 17:3).

Pathian nêna inzawmna nghet tak neih theihna tûra a lungphûm pawimawh êm êm chu fiah leh chiang taka a nungchang hriatna neih a ni. Chu vâng chuan, tûn kârah hian Bible-in Pathian nungchang a lo sawi dân ngun takin kan zir ho dâwn a. Chutia kan zir pah chuan “khawvêl hi Pathian nihna dik tak hriat lohna thim chhah takin a bawh chûn mai a ni” tih hi rilru-ah kan lo hre reng dâwn nia.

“Mihringte hian Pathian nungchang hriatna hloh mêk zêl a. A hriat thiam dân a dik tâwk loh bâkah, dik lo takin an lantîr thîn bawk. Hetih hunah tak hian Pathian atanga lo chhuak thuchah, ênna rawn thlentu leh chhandam tûra thiltihtheihna nei ngei chu puan chhuah tûr a ni a. A nungchang chu mi tin hriat theih tûrin lantîr ni rawh se. Khawvêl thimah hian a ropuina êng, a thatna, a zahngaihna leh a thutak êng chu tâi darhtîr ni se la. . . . Khawvêl hnêna hriattîr tûr khawngaihna thuchah hnuhnûng ber chu Pathian hmangaihna nungchang puan chhuahna a ni.”—Ellen G. White, Krista Tehkhin Thute, p. 368.

Pathian mizia leh a nungchang hi a zawng a za chuan sawifiah zawh rual a ni hauh lo va; chu vângin, kan tih theih awm chhun chu Bible-in a chanchin a lo sawite sawi chhâwn ve leh mai a ni lo thei lo vang. Tûnah hian Pathian nungchang ropui tak chungchânga hriat tûr awm zawng zawng chu hre vek rih dâwn lo tho mah ila, a chanchin kan hriatna leh amah kan hmangaihna a lo thûk leh zual theih nân i tawngtâi thîn ang u. Chutichuan, mi dangte hnêna a hmangaihna leh a nungchang kan lantîr theih nân amah hnaih leh zual deuh deuh duhna thinlung kan lo nei thei dâwn nia.
