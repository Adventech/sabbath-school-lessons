---
title:  Pathian chu a thianghlim a ni
date:   06/04/2026
---

Thianghlimna tih thumal hi—kan chheh vêlah hian thil thianghlim nia ngaih theih a tlêm tham êm avâng pawh a ni maithei e—kan hmang zen zen hlawm lo niin a lang. Sabbath hi ni thianghlim a ni a, chutiangin, Pathian pawh a thianghlim tih kan hre bawk. Nimahsela, kan ni tin nun hi chuan thianghlimna a tlachham hle si a nih hi.

Pathian nungchang nêna inzawm a zepui hrang hrangte hi i zir chiang a nih chuan, a laipui ber leh pawimawh ber ni bawk chu thianghlimna a ni tih i hre ngei ang. Nimahsela, thianghlimna tih chu eng nge a awmzia ni le?

`Hêng Bible chângte hian engtin nge Pathian nihna an sawi: Leviticus 20:26, 1 Samuela 2:2, Isaia 57:15, leh Ezekiela 38:23?`

Bible-in thianghlimna entîrna anga Pathian a sawi rêng rêng hian thil tha lo leh sual laka fihlîm leh hrang hlauh a ni a tihna thîn a. Pathian chu a tîr ata a tâwp thlengin za-ah za tha a ni tawp mai. Chutiang chu a nih avângin, a thianghlimna hi a zepui dang zawng zawngte innghah chhan pawh a ni nghâl bawk.

Entîr nân, Pathian hmangaihna chu hmangaihna thianghlim leh fîr—mahni hmasialna leh mahni inngaihhlutna pakhat mah tel lo a ni a. Engkim a hriatna pawh hi engkim hriatna thianglim, thil tha lo leh sual tih tumna laka fihlîm a ni bawk. Pathian hi engkim hria, nimahsela, thianghlim si lo ni ta se i ring ngam ang em? Rin chu sawi loh, a hlauhin kan hlau êm êm zâwk lo vang em ni?

Tin, engkim ti thei a nihna pawh hi engkim tih theihna thianghlim tho a ni leh bawk a. Engkim ti thei Pathian, nimahsela, thianghlim si lo ni tûrin angin an suangtuah chhin teh? Pathian nunrâwng tak, thil ti thei êm êm bawk si a ni dâwn tlat. A thianghlimna chauhin amah hmangaih min phalsak a, mi a hmangaihtîr thei bawk, a chhan pawh a tîr ata a tâwp thlenga tha a nih vâng a ni. Thianghlimna hi Pathian nungchang hrang hrang zînga kan hriatthiam tûr pawimawh ber a ni e pawh kan ti hial thei a. Chutiang chu ni mah se, kan hriatthiam loh berte zînga mi a ni lâwi si maithei bawk.

Bible-a an chanchin kan hmuh, Pathian nên pawha lo inhmu tawh, hêng—Mosia te, Isaia te, Ezekiela te, Daniela te leh Johana te hi ngaihtuah la. Pathian an hmuh ruala an tih chu eng nge ni? An pheikhawk an phelh a, an hmâi an hup a, a nih loh leh, thi ang dêrin leiah an tlu a ni lo’m ni? Mihringte hi sual tak leh thianghlim lo êm êm kan nih avângin Pathian hmaa ding tlâk kan ni lo va. Pathian hmêl hmu tawh phei chu dama chhuak leh pakhat mah an awm lo. Hetiang chiah hian, inlârnaa Ellen G. White-i hruai luh a nih pawh khân, “Thianghlim . . . thianghlim . . . thianghlim” tiin a au thîn a, a chhan pawh he tawngkam hian a thil hmuhte chu eng ang nge ni tih hriatna min neihtîr vâng a ni. Tin, thil nung palite pawh khân a chhûn a zân châwl lovin, “A thianghlim e, a thianghlim e, a thianghlim e, Lalpa Pathian, Engkimtitheia, awm tawha leh, awm mêka leh lo la awm tûra chu” an ti a nih kha (Thup. 4:8, NKJV).

`Pathian chu thianghlimnaa khat a ni a, a hmaa kan din hunah pawh chutiang tak chuan kan hmu tûr a ni ang. Chumi hriatna chuan engtiangin nge a tihphûr che? Chutih rual chuan, engtiangin nge i chona a pêk bawk che?`
