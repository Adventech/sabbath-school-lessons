---
title:  Chapona hlauhawmzia
date:   12/04/2026
---

Chapona. He thumal i hriat châng hian i rilru-ah politician chapo tak te, mi hausa tawntâw emaw, mi hmingthang tak emaw, a nih loh leh ârâwn emaw a lo lang maithei. Chapona chu mi dangte âia pawimawh zâwk emaw, tha zâwk emaw nia inhriatna neih hi a ni ber a. Chutiang chu a nih avângin innghahna atâna hman theih a nih loh bâkah, hman pawh kan tum tûr a lo hrim hrim bawk.

Chapona chu vêngtu cherub hriak thih, Pathian rawngbâwltu ropui tak Lucifera-ah khân a lo intan daih tawh a. Chûng mahni hmasialna rilrute chuan engtikah nge a thinlung a lo luh chilh a, engtiang chiahin nge a fan chhuah tih hre lo mah ila, chumi chuan vânah khian indona ropui tia kan hriat tâk hi a thlentîr ta tih erawh kan hre chiang hle. Setana chu Pathian dodâltu a ni tih kan hria a (Isaia 14:12–14 leh Phil. 2:5–11 khâikhin rawh.) Chumi avâng chuan kan khawvêl hian—Setana’n Evi leh Adama rilrua rinhlelhna chi a tuha, Pathian âia anmahni inhmangaih zâwk tûr leh omring zâwk tûra a thlêm tâk achin kha—sualin a nghawng thil tha lo tak takte chu a tuar chho ta zêl a nih hi.

`1 Johana 2:15–17 chhiar la. He thuin chapona leh khawvêl hmangaihna chungchânga thil pathum a sawite chu eng nge ni?`

Chapona emaw, mahni inngaihhlutna emaw hi thil tha a nihna chen a awm ve tho em? He thumal hi a tha zâwnga kan hman châng chu a awm ve tho ngei mai a, mi tu emaw chuan hlawhtlinna a chang, a nih loh pawhin thil ropui tak eng emaw a ti a, chutiangah chuan, “Ka chhuang lutuk che!” tiin kan sawi thîn. Thil tha leh ropui tak kan tih leh, Pathianin thiamna leh theihna bîk min pêk lo ngaihhlut hi chapona a ni hran rêng rêng lo tih kan hre tûr a ni a. Bible kan en pawhin mahni inhmangaih dân dik leh tha a awm ngei a ni tih pawh kan hria (Marka 12:31-a Isua thupêk, keimahni kan inhmangaih ang bawkin mi dangte pawh kan hmangaih ve tûr a ni tih te hi ngaihtuah teh). Hetiang hi chu mahni hmasialna tel lo hmangaihna a ni a. Pathianin min rawn chên chilh a, keimahni’n thil tha tih tlat duhna kan nei (1 Tim. 3:1 en la) bawk a nih chuan kan chapo thei lo ang. Chutih lâiin, kan tâna thil a tihte avânga Pathian kan châwimâwi lo a nih erawh chuan kan lo chapo mai thîn thung.

Kan thil neihte, kan theihnate leh hlawhtlinna kan chante hian kan hlutna a sawi fiah hran lo tih kan hriat reng a pawimawh a. Kan thil neih engkim, chapona rilru min puttîr theite ngei pawh hi Pathian hnên atanga kan dawn vek a nih avângin kan hlutna pawh ama hnên ata lo thleng tûr a ni zâwk. Hei hi engtik lâi mahin kan theihnghilh tûr a ni lo.

`Heti hian han inzâwt teh: Engtiang taka chapo leh inngâihlu nge kan lo nih ve rêng le? Keimah ka inngaihhlutna hian Pathian nên leh mi dangte nêna kan inlaichînna kawngah engtiangin nge nghawng a neih theih?`
