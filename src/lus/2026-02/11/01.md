### Chhiar tûrte
Marka 4:35–41; Marka 5:21–34; Rom 5:3–5; Joba 19:23–27; Joba 23:8–12; Luka 24:13–27; Rom 8:18, 28.

> <p>Châng vawn</p>
> “Tin, chuti chauh chu ni lovin, kan hrehawmnaah pawh lâwmin i awm ang u, hrehawmna chuan chhelna a siam a, chhelna chuan hriat fiahna a siam a, hriat fiahna chuan beiseina a siam tih kan hre si a; tin, Thlarau Thianghlim kan hnêna min pêk khân Pathian hmangaihna chu kan thinlunga a leih tawh avângin beiseina chuan a timualpho lo” (Rom 5:3–5).

Tlâi khat chu ni tlâk dâwn ruai tawhah hmeichhe naupang pakhat hi an in lam panin a tlân haw chan chan a. Hmanhmawh hle mah sela, thli thaw vûk vûk hnuaiah chuan kal chak harsa a ti hle mai. Nakinah chuan ruah fang pakhat hian a biang a rawn dêng ta nge nge a, chumi hnu chuan a dang pakhatin a rawn dêng ve leh a, rei lo te chhûngin a hu zawrh ta vek mai le! Tichuan, a theihtâwpa chakin a han tlân leh ta a, an in a va thlen chuan a pa’n kawngka a lo hawnsak nghâl a. Puan chhah leh lum tha taka lo tuamin, “Tûn maiah khân ruah sûr hnuaiah ka hmu che a. Mahse, khatianga kâwl phe zawr zawr leh têk tla ri thuai thuai kârah khân enga ti nge chung lam thlîra i nuih? tiin a zâwt a.

Hmeichhe naupang chuan, “Aw, chung lam ka thlîr a, ka nui tak e. A chhan chu Pathianin ka thla a rawn la alâwm!” tiin a chhâng a.

Buaina thlipuite’n kan nun a rawn nan chîn lâi emaw, Pathian nêna kan inzawmna kawngah bahthlahna eng eng emaw a lo thlen châng emaw hian engtin nge kan awm thin le? Lungngâi taka indawmin, kan hnungzângah ruah mal lian tak takte chu kan tlâktîr fawk vawk mai nge ni a, chung lam kan thlîr chho a, kan tân Pathian a awm a ni tih hriain amahah rinna kan nghat thîn zâwk?

Tûn kârah hian kan nunin harsatna a tawh chânga kan lo dawnsawn dân thenkhat kan thlîr ho dâwn a. Chûng kan harsatna tawh hrang hrangte chu Pathian nêna kan inzawmna tichak lotu ni lovin, tichaktu zâwk atâna kan hman theih dân tûr pawh kan ngaihtuah tlâng bawk dâwn a ni.
