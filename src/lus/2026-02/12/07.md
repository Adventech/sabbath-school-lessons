title:  Zir belhna
date:   19/06/2026
---

“Nia kan lo inchhâl chu eng pawh lo ni se, tu mah hian mahni hmasialna tel miah lova kan unaunu/unaupa hmangaihna pawh kan neih chuan si loh chuan Pathian hmangaihna tak tak kan nei thei lo vang. Nimahsela, chumi rilru chu mi dangte va hmangaih tumna hmanga neih ngawt theih a ni chuang lo va. Kan mamawh ber chu kan thinlunga Krista hmangaihna neih phawt mai a ni. Isua Kristaa kan inchhûn luh hunah hmangaihna chu amahin a lo lang chhuak nghâl mai dâwn thung a ni.”—Ellen G. White, Krista Tehkhin Thute, p. 339.

“Isua Krista tâna thlarau seng khâwm hna thawhna kawnga phûr tak leh rinawm taka thawkte hi thlarau nun hausa leh Pathian hnêna inpe thûk ber ber an ni zêl thîn.”—Ellen G. White, Evangelism, p. 356.

“Sual do tûra chakna neih theihna kawng tha ber chu tih tak zeta Pathian rawngbâwl a ni.”—Ellen G. White, Tirhkohte Thiltih, p. 105.

“Krista lâwmnaa—a inhlannain a tlan chhuah thlaraute hmuh avânga hlimna—lût tûr chuan krawsa a lo thih chhante’n chhandamna an chan theih nâna chanchin tha hril hna kan thawh ve a ngâi.”—Ellen G. White, Chatuan Nghahfâk, p. 142.

“Rawngbâwlnaa Krista nêna inpâwl theihna hun tha an neih hnâwltute chuan Krista ropuina tâwmpui ve thei tûra anmahni chhertu awm chhun chu an hnâwl a ni.”—Ellen G.White, Education, p. 264.

**Sawi ho tûrte:**

`Enga ti nge hmangaihna hi thu hretu hlawhtling ni tûraa lungphûm leh pawimawh ber a nih?`

`Engtik khân thlarau hneh hna thawh hi Pathian nênalên dunna nên thil inzawm tlat a ni tih hi a dikzia i lo hriat chhuah ve?`

`Mi dangte hnêna chanchin tha hrilh tûr hian thil engemaw hriat hmasak phawt ngâi a awm em? A awm a nih chuan eng nge ni?`

`Ring lo mi tu emaw hnênah Bible study i lo pe dâwn taa, eng thu atangin nge i tan ang? Eng thu nge i ngaih pawimawh hmasak ni thîn: kan thurin thenkhat hi a dikzia finfiah i tum nghâl mai nge ni a, Isua lo hre ve tûrin i sâwm hmasa phawt thîn zâwk?`

`“Ka Puang Châk E Min Tlantu Thu Hi!” tih hla(Adventist Hla Bu, No. 189) kha sa la, a nih loh pawhin chhiar tal ang che. Tichuan, he hla thuin a lo sawi i lo puan chhuah ve dân chu i ngaihtuah dâwn nia.`

**Zirlâi khâikhâwmna:** Pathian hmangaihna leh, a Thu nung leh thiltitheihna ropui tak neiin kan nun a luah khah hunah chuan kan bul hnâia awmte hmangaih leh an hnêna Pathian chanchin sawi lo thei lova siam kan lo ni ang. Krista tâna thu hretu nih hna kan thawhna kawngah hian ngaihtuahna fîm tak leh tum nghet tak kan neih a tûl a, a kâ atanga lo chhuak a Thu chu engmah tih nei lovin a hnênah a lêt leh lo vang a, a duh ang chu a hlen chhuak zâwk ang a, tin, thil ti tûra a tirhnaah chuan a hlawhtling zêl bawk ang tih kan ring bawk tûr a ni (Isaia 55:11).
