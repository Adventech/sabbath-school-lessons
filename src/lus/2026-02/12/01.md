### Chhiar tûrte
Mat. 28:18–20, 2 Pet. 3:18, 1 Pet. 3:8–15, Hosea 7, Zakaria 10.

> <p>Châng vawn</p>
> “Mi hah tak chu thua ka tihhahdam thiam nân Lalpa PATHIAN chuan zirtîrte lei mi pe a; ani chuan a tûk a tûk tin a kâi harh a, zirtîrte’n an hriat anga hre tûrin ka beng hi ka kâi harh thîn” (Isaia 50:4).

Sabbath tûk a ni a, Pastor G-a chuan tih tûr a ngah hle mai. Hma takah a tho a, Sabbath School atân leh Divine Service-a a thu sawi tûr a buatsaih a; tin, hemi ni chawhnu hian an kohhranin chanchin tha hril beihpui thlâk program an huaihawt chu a kaihhruai a ngâi bawk. A motor chabi a la a, hmanhmawh takin a inkhalh chhuak ta nghâl a.

Khawpui chhûng a va lût chu mi a lo tam êm êm mai a, a duh ang pawha a tlân chak theih loh avâng chuan biak in a thlen tlâi phah pawh a hlau hman hle. Khawia kal tûr nge maw an nih zawh zâi rêng rêng le? tiin a ngaihtuah a. Chutih lâi tak chuan car pakhat hi a hma zâwn takah a rawn dawk chhuak zawk mai a. Chu veleh a car brake a rap a, thinrim êm êmin a kuttum a lek chhuak a, chu car driver chu a hrâwk ta chiam mai a.

A tâwp a tâwpah biak in chu a va thleng ta hrâm a. Sabbath School zirlâi zirtîr tûra a din chhuah pah chuan a han hawi kual a, a class member-te zîngah chuan minute 20 emaw vêl lek liam taa a hauh hrep car driver kha a lo thu ve rân mai le! Chutah kohhran member pakhat hi lo ding chhuakin, chu car driver chu Adventist pawh ni ve chuang lo, a chhûngkhatte tlawha lo zin remchâng mai a ni tih a han sawi a. Pastor G-a chu a zak kher mai. Chuta tang chuan kan hmêlhriatte leh hmêlhriat ni lêm lote laka kan awm dân—kan thil sawite leh thil tihte— engkim mai hi Pathian awmpuina kan channa atanga lo luang chhuak hmangaihna tuiin a bual huh a ni tûr a ni tih a hre chhuak thar leh ta a. Ni e, kan thil tih, a bîk takin ringtu kan nihna anga kan tihte hian mi dangte nunah nghawng lian tak a nei thei a ni tih hi kan hriat reng a pawimawh hle rêng a ni.
