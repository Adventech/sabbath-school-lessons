---
title:  Berâm No chu zui rawh
date:   24/06/2026
---

“Vânram i thlen huna tih i châk ber eng nge ni?” tia zawhna i tawng tawh ngâi em? He zawhna hi naupang hnênah zâwt ta la, “Sakei chunga chuan,” “Giraffe nghâwnga tawlh thlâk,” “Planet hrang hrang fan kual” ti te’n an chhâng maithei che a ni. Tleirâwl hnênah i zâwt a nih chuan, “Lehkha zir ngâi tawh lo tûr” tiin emaw, “Ka thiante nên intina lova vânram hmun hrang hrang fan kual” tiin emaw an chhâng ve thung maithei che a. Tin, puitling tawhte hnênah i zâwt a nih chuan, “Natna, hrehawm tawrhna leh thihna awm tawh lohna hmuna awm” tiin emaw, “Kan hmangaih tak takte nêna intawh khâwm leh” tiin emaw an chhâng maithei bawk che. Hêng chhânna hrang hrangte hi a dikin a tha vek a, lei thar leh vân tharah chuan thil nghahhlelhawm leh tih châkawm tak tak a tam rêng dâwn a ni. Kan thinlung chhûngah hian chatuan hun nghahhlelhna a awm ve hrim hrim a, tûna he leia kan neih loh leh tih theih loh tam tak hi vânah chuan kan neiin kan ti thei dâwn a ni tih kan hria.

`Lei thar leh vân thara malsâwmna kan dawn tûr dangte chu eng engte nge ni? Isaia 25:8, Thupuan 7:17, leh Thupuan 21:4 chhiar ang che.`

Vânrama malsâwmna kan dawn tûr ropui ber mai chu hmâi chhan ngeia Isua kan hmu thei tawh tûr leh, suala tlu tawh he leia kan tâna thil a lo tih tawh avânga lâwmthu hrilh a ni ang. Krawsa a tawrhna hmanga chatuana thihna lak ata min chhandam avângin theihtâwpa châwimâwi leh chibai bûk kan duh dâwn a. “Berâm No talh tawha hi thiltihtheihna te, hausakna te, finna te, chakna te, châwimâwina te, ropuina te, malsâwmna te hmu tlâk a ni e” (Thup. 5:12) tiin bâng lovin kan zâi ang.

Baptistu Johana chuan Isua hi “Pathian Berâm No” tiin a lo sawi a (Joh. 1:35–37). Zirtîrte khân hnâi takah zui thînin, chutiang chiah chuan keini pawhin kan la ti ve dâwn a ni. “Chûng chu Berâm No a kalna apianga zuitute chu an ni a” (Thup. 14:4). Amaherawhchu, vânah khian kan duh tak tak dâwn a nih chuan, leiah hian kan lo zui thin phawt a ngâi a ni.

Isua, Berâm No hi min vêngtu Berâm Pu pawh a ni bawk a, mi tu mahin an tih ve theih loh thilte chu tiin kan kawng min hruai ang. Harsatna tâwkin buaina hrang hrang han hmachhawn thîn mah ila, ani chuan min hruai zêl dâwn a ni tih hi a rin ngamawm êm êm a, he leiah mai pawh ni lovin vânramah pawh min hruai zêl dâwn a ni. Thupuan 7:17 chuan, “Berâm No, lalthutphah lâia awm chu an vêngtu a ni ang a, [ani chuan] nunna tuikhurahte chuan a hruai ang” tiin a lo sawi a. A mite, a berâmte kan nih ang ngeiin vânah pawh Isua zui zêl kan duh dâwn a, chatuana a bula awm kan thlahlel êm êm ang. Pathian mite nihna tichiangtu pakhat chu an chala Isua hming a chuang tûr hi a ni a (Thup. 22:4). A awmzia chu, engtik lâi pawhin amah kan ngaihtuah reng dâwn tihna a ni.
