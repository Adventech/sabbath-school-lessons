> <p>Chângvawn</p>
> “Mitinin thilpêk in hmuh ang zêlin Pathian khawngaihna tinrêng enkawltu thate angin chûng chuan rawng inbâwlsak tawn theuh rawh u,” (1 Petera 4:10).

> <p>Chakna thu</p>
> Mi dangte tanpui nâna Thlarau thilpêk kan dawnte kan hman hian Pathian hna kan thawhpui tihna a ni.

### Chhiar tûrte
Tirhkohte 21:1–16; Tirhkohte Thiltih, bung 37, pp. 389-398; The Bible Story (1994), vol. 10,pp. 106–110; kan Kohhran Thurin, no. 17, 21, 14.

_Thian hlun hmu tûra zin angah inchan la. Hmuh ngei i tum si, i kalna tûr i thlen hunah hlauhawm i tâwk dâwn tih inhre bawk la. I thulh leh ang nge i kal lui ang? Hetiang deuh dinhmuna ding chunga Paula tih dân hi han chhiar ve teh._

Tura khaw lawngchawlhnaa châwl mêk lawng lianpui bathlârah chuan Paula chu a ding vang vang a. Lawng bungrua an hlân thlâk chhûng ni sarih zet chu an châwl tawh a. Amah thlahtute mittui tla zawih zawihte chu a hmu reng a. Thlarau Thianghlim hriattîrin chûng ringtute chuan a kalna tûr Jerusalemah chuan hlauhawm a tâwk dâwn tih an hre lâwk a. Paula chuan a khualzin kawng tâwp lam a thleng mêk a, Kaisari khuaah rei lo te châwlin a thian hlui Tirhkoh Phillipa leh a fanute tlawh pah a tum a. Chutah Pathianin kal tûra a tihna lam Jerusalem chu a thianten hlauhawm a awm ti mah se a pan zêl dâwn a ni.

Vawi thum a zin khual chhûng khân mi tam takin Messia Isua chu an ring a. Grik Pathian zînga thupuangtu Mercury pathian ni hiala sawite pawh an awm a. Dik tak chuan Isua tâna thupuangtu a ni zâwk. Mahse amah chauh chu Pathian kohhran hmasate din tûra hman chu a ni bîk hauh lo. Thilpêk hrang hrang nei mi tam takin Pathian hnathawh zahngaihna leh thutak Isua chanchin puanna hna chu an rawn zawm ve a ni.

Entîrn nan, Paula thianpa Phillipa pawh hi zirtîr 12-te tanpui tûra thlan mi 7-te zînga mi kha a ni ve a. Jerusalema ringtute an lo pun khân mi enkawl tûr leh ei tûr ngaihtuah lamah a tangkâi hle a ni. Zirtîr dang Petera te, Jakoba te, Johana te phei chu thuhril leh zirtîrin an buai em em reng a. Phillipa leh a thiante chuan an thilpêk dawn chu mi awp khâwm a, tha taka enkawl nân an hmang a. Vawikhat phei chu Pathian Thlarauvin Ethiopia milian, thlaler paltlanga zin mêk chu a thu chhiar lâi hrilhfiah a, baptis tûrin a tîr hial a ni. Tûnah hian a fanûte chuan thu an hril a, inlârnate hmuin kohhran thannaah an tangkâi hle a ni. Pathian tâna serhhran chhûngkua an ni a. Paula pawhin rei lo te tal chu an thatnate va chhim ve a tum a ni.

Paulate ho chuan Phillipa ina thlen a, thiante bula zalên taka chawlh chu nuam an ti hle a. Ni khat chu mikhual pakhat, a hming Agaba, Pathian thlarau hruai, vawi tam tak thil lo thleng tûr sawi lâwk thîn hian a rawn tlawh a. Rome rama tamtlak tûr thû a lo sawi lâwk dik tawh a. Chumi tum chuan Paula tlawh rama mite chuan Jerusalema ringtu tam tuarte chhawmdawl nân thil tam tak an thawn nghe nghe a ni.

Agaba chu Phillipa inah lûtin Paula chu a pan dîng nghâl a. A bula dingin Paula kawng hren chu a la a, a kut leh ke phuara Paula en chungin, “He kawnghren neitu hi Jerusalem-ah a kut leh ke-a Judaten kawl buntîrin Gentelte kutah, rorêlsak leh tihhlum tûrin an pe dâwn. Chû chu Thlarau Thianghlimin mi hriattîr dân a ni,” a ti a.

Paula thiante chuan chu pawh chu Jerusalema thil hlauhawm lo thleng tûr hrilhlâwkna a ni tih an hre thar leh a. Paula chu kal lo tûrin an ngên leh chiam a. Ani erawh chuan, “Ka lung in tichhia e, Pathian chuan Jerusalem-ah min tîr ngei niin ka hria a. Thil lo thleng tûr min hriattîr avângin ka lâwm em em a ni. Mahse ka thulh dâwn tawh lo,” a ti tlat a.

A thiante chuan, “Lalpa thu thu ni rawh se,” an ti a. Phillipa hnêna an cham tâwp lam ni rei lo te chu hahdam nan leh mi tlawh kual vêl nân hun tha takah an hmang ral ta zâwk a ni.

Jerusalem lam pan a lo hun leh ta. Kaisari khua an panna ni hmasa berah chuan Gentel ringtu hmasa zînga mi Kupra thliarkâr atanga Jerusalem hnaih tuma pem Nasona inah an châwl a. A pindan zau nuam tak mai chu ringtu Jerusalem kal lam leh haw lamin chawlh nân an hman ber a ni. Amah hi Isua kohhran thar zînga mi pawimawh ber pâwl a ni.

Paula chuan Jerusalemah engin nge lo hmuak dâwn a hre lo va. Mahse ringtute chuan Pathian thilpêk hrang hranga thuam an ni tih erawh a hria. Thilpêk tinte chu mite hnêna Pathian zahngaihna hlan chhâwn nâna hman tûr a ni. Chu kohhran thang mêka tel tawh phawt chu vân atanga lo chhuk Pathian zahngaihna khâidiat, chhandam tûr leh rawngbâwl tûra tel an ni si a.

### Tih Tûrte

#### Sunday

- Ephesi 4:11–13 chhiar la, tûn kâr zirlâi hi chhiar bawk ang che.
- Pathian hnên atagna thilpêk i dâwn nia i hriatte chu lehkha themah ziakin khâidiat angin siam ang che.
- In chângvawn, 1 Peter 4:10 hi en lova sawi thei tûrin zir tan la.
- Pathian hnênah i thilpêk dawn hmanga a rawng i bâwl theih dân tûr rawn khawhmuh tûr chein tawngtâi rawh.

#### Thawhtanni

- Tirhkohte 21:1–6 chhiar la.
- Tura khuaa ringtute khân Paula tanpui nân eng thilpêk nge an hman?
- In khuaah Paula ang deuh thuhriltu pastor, tirhkoh, etc. rawn kal ta se, engtin nge i thilpêk dawnte hmangin i lo tanpui theih ang?
- Thlarau Thianghlim a hruai che tih i hriat reng theih nân Pathian hnênah tawngtâi thîn ang che.

#### Thawhlehni

- Tirhkohte 21:7–14 chhiar la.
- Hêng chângahte hian eng thilpêkte nge langsâr êm êm?
- Tûn kâr chhûng hian mikhual laka that chhuahna thilpêk i hman theih dân tûr ngaihtuah ang che.
- Duhthlanna siam kawnga kâihruaitu che Thlarau Thianghlim aw i chu i lo ngâithla tawh ngâi em? Pathianin i tâna ruahmanna a lo siam chu engtin nge i hriata i tihhlawhtlin theih ang?
- Mi dangte tâna i tangkâi theih hun remchâng dîlin tawngtâi ang che.

#### Nilaini

- Tirhkohte 21:15–16 chhiar la.
- Eng vângin nge Paula hunlâi khân Thlarau thilpêk pakhat, “mikhual chunga that chhuah” tih hi an lo uar viau thin le? Tûnlâi hian mikhual chungah kan la tha viau tho nge tha tawh vak lo?
- Mite chunga that chhuah tûrin ke pen nghâl rawh (Entîr nân, i thiante chu eng emaw tihpuiahte sâwm la).
- I thilpêk dawnte huaisen taka hmang ngam tûrin Pathian hnênah huaisenna dîl ang che.

#### Ningani

- Ephesi 4:11–12; 1 Korinth 12:28–31 khâikhin la. Thlarau thilpêk an sawite hi a danglamna leh inanna eng nge ni?
- Heta Thlarau thilpêk a ziahte hi i lo nei ve hlawm em?
- Lehkha puanah thilpêk hmanga rawngbâwl chungchâng thua i buainate chu Pathan nêna tîtî angin ziak la.
- Thilpêk i dawn avângin Pathian hnênah lâwmthu sawi bawk ang che.

#### Zirtawpni

- Chhûng inkhâwmnaah 1 Korinth 13 chhiar ang che u.
- In thilpêk dawn theuhte chu sawi ho ula.
- Khâidiata i thilpêk dawn i ziah kha hrilh ve la, anni pawhin an thilpêk dâwn chu ziah belh ve rawh se. Fakna hla sa ho ula.
- I thilpêk dawn leh i thil ruahmannate chu Pathian hnênah hlân ang che.
