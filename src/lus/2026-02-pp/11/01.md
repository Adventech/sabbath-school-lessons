---
title:  Thu sawi zêl rawh
date:   07/06/2026
---

> <p>Chângvawn</p>
> “Hlau suh la, ngâwi lovin thu sawi zêl rawh; kei i hnênah ka awm alâwm” (Tirhkohte 18:9, 10).

> <p>Chakna thu</p>
> Khawi hmunah pawh awm mah ila, Pathian khawngaihna chanchin i sawi zêl ang u.

### Chhiar tûrte
Tirhkohte 9:15, 16; Tirhkohte 20:24; 2 Timothea 4:6–8; Tirhkohte Thiltih, bung 50, pp. 509–513; The Bible Story (1994), vol. 10, pp. 149–151; kan Kohran Thurin, no. 10, 17, 11.

_Ding chhuak la, i kut kha i theih anga sâng leh zauvin han phar kual vêl teh. A nuam i ti em? I kut leh i kete kha khâidiata thlun ni ta se, nuam i ti ang em? I duh angin i kut leh i kete i tiche thei ang em? Chutiang chuan Paula kha a awm asin. Chutiang dinhmunah chuan keini hi ding ve ta ila, kan rilru a hah viau ngei ang. Nimahsela, Paula chuan beiseina a nei tlat a. Chu bâkah, mi dangte hnênah pawh Pathian khawngaihna chanchin a la sawi zêl zâwk bawk a ni._

Paula awmna tân in chu a tawpin a thim hle mai. Tukverh te tak tê atang chuan êng chhe tê a rawn lût ve a. Chhuat dâia mu mai a nih avângin a thawmhnaw hâk pan tak chuan a tuam lum zo hlawl lo. A chêt apiangin a kut leh ke an tâwmna thîr khâidiat chu a ri rawl rawl zêl mai lehnghâl a. Amah an tihhlum tûr nghâka awm mai a nih tawh avângin a pen a la chhuak a, a thiante hnênah beiseina leh fuihna thu an chhiar tûr a ziak tan ta a ni.

Hei hi tân ina khung a nih vawi khatna a ni lo. Pathian khawngaihna thu a puan thin avângin hian harsatna a tâwk tam tawh hle mai a. Chûngte chu: “Juda-te’n hruaihrualin vawi nga an vaw tawh a; tiangin vawi thum an vaw tawh a; lunga den vawi khat a tâwk tawh a; lawnga chhiatna vawi thum a tâwk tawh a, tum khat phei chu nilêng leh zankhuain tuiah a awm ngeh nghe. A dam chhûng khân hlauhawm chi hrang hrang a tâwk châmchi a—Juda-te lak atang te, Jentail-te lak atang te, tui lianah te, suamhmang lak atang te, khawpuiah leh ramhnuaiah te bâkah, thian rinawm lo lak atangte pawhin hlauhawm a tâwk bawk.

Chhîn khat pawh chhîng lova a tlâivâr zak zân te, ei leh in tûr neih miah loh tum te pawh a lo tawng tawh bawk a. Saruaka awm châng leh vawt tih êm êm tum pawh a tawng ngun mai. Tân inah tân phei hi chu a tân thil thar a ni lo. Nimahsela, hêng zawng zawngte hi mi dangte hnêna Isua chanchin sawina hun tha takah a chhuah zêl thei thung a. Thlipuiin a chuanna lawng a tihchhiat avânga thla thum chhûng ngawt Malta thliarkâra an khawtân tum pawhin a thu sawi avânga lo piangthar ta eng emaw zât an awm. Tin, Rom khawpuia kum hnih chhûng a tân lâi pawh khân amah vêngtute leh amah rawn tlawhtute hnênah Pathian khawngaihna chanchin tha bawk a lo hrilh thîn a. A tîrah chuan salte leh mi retheite hnênah hril ber thînin, a hnu-ah erawh a thuchah ken chuan mi hausate leh Kaisara chhûngte hnên pawh a thleng ve ta hial a ni.

A tâwpah chuan lalber Nero-a hmaa inlan tûra hruai a ni ta nge nge a. Nero-a kha lal nunrâwng tak, thîr kut nêna khawvêl zawng zawng deuh thawh lo awm tawh thîntu a ni a. Amah kalha thu sawite rêng rêng chu, ama nu ngei pawh ni se khawngaihna tel miah lovin a lo tihlum hmiah hmiah mai thîn a ni. Kristiante phei chu a haw hle a, Rome khawpuiah hruai khâwmin sâng tam tak a lo tihlum tawh bawk. Paula pawh Rome sawrkâr laka hel nia puh a nihna laka a thiamthu sawi tûrin an rawn hruai ta a, mahse, atân dama chhuah a beiseiawm loh hle. Nimahsela, rorêlna hmaa a inlan hi Pathian ruahmanna a lo ni zâwk a, Pathian chuan lal hmaa a thu hretu atân a lo ruat sa diam a lo ni.

Rom sawrkâr hnuaia mi tângte chu a thiamthu sawipuitu tûr mi tu emaw an rawn hruai ve phalsak an ni thîn a, tûnlâia ukil kan tih ang deuh hi a ni an ni ang. Nimahsela, Paula hi chu tu mahin Nero hmaah an dinpui ngam ve lo va. Timothea hnêna lehkha a thawnah pawh, “Ka thiamthu ka sawi hmasak tum chuan tu mah rêngin min tanpui lo va, min thlahthlam ta vek zâwk a ni. . . . Chanchin Tha chu keimah avânga hrilh kimin a awm theih nân leh, Jentail-te zawng zawng chuan an hriat theih nân Lalpa chuan mi tan a, mi tichak ta zâwk a ni; tin, sakeibâknei kâa mi kha chhan chhuahin a lo awm ta a” tiin a lo ziak (2 Timothea 16, 17).

Mi dangte hlauva an khawrh zawih zawihna tûr rorêlna hmaah chuan Paula erawh hahdam takin a ding thei a, rorêlna pindan khat hmura thute hnênah chuan Isua chanchin a sawi ta dap dap mai le. Ram bial hrang hrang atanga lo kal khâwmte chuan ngaihnawm takin Isua lo pian dân leh kan sualte lak tlan chhuah kan neih theh nân man a pêk dân a sawi chu ngaihnawm ti takin an ngâithla a. A thu sawite leh a hmêla Isua thlamuanna lo lang chuan mi hausa leh ropui tak takte kha a tipiangthar ta hial a ni.

Nero-a ngei pawhin Paula thu sawi chu tha a ti a, chuvâng chuan, mite hmuha sakeibâkneite she hlumtîr lovin, a tânna inah bawk a tîr lêt leh ta zâwk a.

Nimahsela, a tâwp a tâwpah chuan a lu tan tûrin a thu a titlu ta nge nge a ni. Paula hian a lu an tânna tûr hmun lam a pan lâi pawhin Isua chanchin a la sawi ta zêl tho a. A lu tan a ni lâi lo thlîrtu sipai thenkhatte chuan khawngaihnaa khat thu a sawite chu an lo hriatin Pathian Thlarau hnathawh zârah an thinlung a lo tui ral ve ta nghe nghe bawk.

Paula khân a tânna in pindan thim leh rim chhe taka a awm lâi khân Pathian hnênah a vui thu a thlen ngâi lo rêng rêng a. Pathian a rinna pawh a nghîng ngâi hek lo. Timothea hnêna lehkha a thawn hnuhnûng zâwkah chuan heti hian a lo ziak a: “Intlânsiakna tha chu ka bei zo tawh a, ka tlânsiakna kawng ka hlen tawh a, rinna chu ka vawng reng tawh a ni; tûnah chuan felna lallukhum chu ka tân dahin a awm tawh a, chu chu Lalpa, ngaihtuahtu fel tak chuan chumi niah chuan mi pe ang; ka hnênah chauh ni lovin a inlârna ngâi apiangte hnênah pawh” (2 Timothea 4:7, 8).

Paula kha harsatna chi hrang hrang hnuaiah pawh Pathian khawngaihna chanchin tha hril tûra koh a ni a. Tân in chhûngah te, khawvêla lal hmingthang leh nunrâwng ber berte hmaah te, a thihna tûr hmun a pan lâi ngei te pawh khân mi dangte hnêna Pathian khawngaihna chanchin tha hrilh theihna remchâng a zawng hrâm hrâm zêl a. Dinhmun khirh ber bera a din lâi pawhin Pathianin a awmpui a ni tih hriatna nei zêlin, chûngte chu mi dangte hnêna khawngaihna chanchin tha sawina remchângah a hmang zêl thîn.

Kan chungah eng nge lo la thleng dâwn kan hre lo va. Nimahsela, eng pawh lo thleng se Pathian chuan kan hnêna awm zêl a tiam asin. “In hnênah ka awm zêl ang” tiin min lo tiam a nih kha (Matthaia 28:20). He thuthiam hi, Paula ang bawk khân kan awmna hmun apiangah kan hre reng dâwn nia.

### Tih Tûrte

#### Sunday

- Tirhkohte 9:15, 16, 20–24 chhiar la, tûn kâr zirlâi, “Thu Sawi Zêl Rawh” tih hi chhiar chhuak bawk ang che.
- Pathianin a vêng che a ni tih i lo hriat reng theih nân in chângvawn, Tirhkohte 18:9, 10 hi paper-ah ziak chhuak ang che.
- In chângvawn hi en lova sawi thei tûrin zir tan rawh.
- Hun harsaah pawh Pathian tâna thu hretu i nih theih nân a hnênah chakna dîl ang che.

#### Thawhtanni

- Tirhkohte16:22–34 chhiar la.
- Map i neih chuan a tum hnihna atâna Paula a zin chhuaha a khawpui tlawhte chu zawng chhuak ang che.
- Paula’n hrehawm a tawrh thenkhatte hi tuar ve ta la, engtin nge i awm ang?
- Isua khawngaihna chanchin an sawi avânga hrehawm tuarte tân Pathian hnênah vênhimna dîl ang che.

#### Thawhlehni

- Tirhkohte 21:20–40.
- Dinhmun khirh taka i awm lâi i rilru put dân khân engtiangin nge Pathian khawngaihna a lan chhuahtîr le?
- Dik lo taka puh i hunah pawh Pathian khawngaihna chanchin i hril zêl tho theih nân Pathian hnênah a tanpuina dîl rawh.

#### Nilaini

- Tirhkohte 23:9–11 chhiar la.
- I Bible note bu-ah khân Pathianin mi dangte hnêna a khawngaihuna chanchin sawi tûra huaisena a lo pêk tawh dân che kha ziak ang che.
- Paula thil tawn chanchin hi hlaah han phuah teh.
- Isua hnênah i hlauhna hmachhawn tûra huaisenna i neih theih nân a puihna dîl ang che.

#### Ningani

- Tirhkohte 28:17–20 chhiar la.
- Dik lo taka puh emaw ni ta la, Pathian khawngaihna chanchin eng nge i sawi ang?
- I chhânna chu i Bible note bu-ah khân i ziak dâwn nia.
- Dik tak lo taka puh nite tân tawngtâi rawh.

#### Zirtawpni

- In chhûngkuain Tirhkohte 9:15 leh Rom 5:3–5 chhiar rual ang che u.
- Pathianin chanchin tha hril tûra Puala a koh chanchin leh chu hna chu tha taka a heln chhuah theih nâna Pathianin a humhim thin dân sawi ho ula. Pathianin tih tûra a tih ve che u chu eng nge ni ang?
- Paula anga tuar chhel tute emaw hnênah lâwmthu sawi la, an rinna chuan nangmah pawh a fuih phûr hle che a ni tih i hrilh dâwn nia.
- Pathian hnênah a khawngaihna i chan avângin lâwmthu sawi la, a beiseina thu puangtu i lo nih theih nân a tanpuina dîl ang che.
