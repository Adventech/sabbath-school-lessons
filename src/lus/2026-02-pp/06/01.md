---
title:  Hrilh chhâwn loh atân chuan uiawm lutuk
date:   03/05/2026
---

> <p>Chângvawn</p>
> “Kan Pathian tanpuinain dodâlna nasa tak kârah pawh chanchin tha chu kan hrilh ngam che u kha” (1 Thessalonika 2:2).

> <p>Chakna thu</p>
> Dodâlna kârah pawh mi dangte kan tanpui hian Isua hnung kan zui tihna a ni.

### Chhiar tûrte
Tirhkohte 14:1–7; Tirhkohte Thiltih, bung 18, pp. 177–179; The SDA Bible Commentary, vol. 6, pp. 295, 296; The Bible Story (1994), vol. 10, pp. 69–75; kan Kohhran Thurin, no. 11, 17, 12.

_Pathian i rin chhan kha mi hnênah hrilh fiah i tum tawh ngâi em? Kristian i nih avânga tihthaih i ni tawh em? Ringtu i nih avânga miin an pâwng ngeina che hmunah Isua zuiin mi rawng engtin nge i bâwl ang? Paula’n chanchin tha hrila a zin tual tuma a thil tawnte hian Isua zuitute’n harsatna kan tawh chânga kan awm dân tûr min hriattîr a ni._

An hmêl en pawha chau tih hriat tak mi pahnih chu ni tlak dâwn ruaiah Iconium khaw lam panin an kan hnak hnak a. A lian zâwk chuan nui chungin, “Kan kalsan tak khua kha kan kea vaivut hlîrin a paw leh tawh niin ka hria,” a ti a. A fiamthu chu a thianpa’n engtin nge a ngaih tih hriat tumin a melh zauh bawk a. Antiokei khua chhuahsana tlang an lawn liamna kawng hi a hahthlâk viau reng a ni.

Paula chu thian a nei tih hre thar thut ang mai hian a dâk vat a. A bula mite theihnghilh hi a chîng fo rêng a. “Antiokei khuaa kan kalsan tak ringtute kha zawng an hlim teh kha le! Messia chanchin kan hrilh awihtute hmêl hmu leh tûr kha chuan eng pawh huamin ka kal leh ngei ngei ang. Krista zârah Pathian nên inremin an awm leh ta tih an hriat lâia an hmêl awm dânte kha i lo hmu ve em?” tih pah chuan hmun hla tak a thlîr vang vang a.

Barnaba chuan, “Iconium kan thleng dâwn e. Sunagogue i zawng teh ang,” tih pahin khaw tawntirh kawng sira dâwr inhawngte chu a thlîr kual a. Rome khua Antiokei-a an tih angin he Grik khuaah pawh hian rawngbâwl an tum a. Gentel tam tak an kal vena Sunagogue, Messia nghâktute inkhâwmna hmunah thû an hril hmasa dâwn a ni.

Sunagogue-a inkhâwmho chuan Jerusalem atanga lo zin thuchah sawi tûr nei inti bawk si chu an lo lâwm hle mai a. He khuaah hian Grik tlangrama Juda-ho chanchin hriat châk zâwng tak hre tûr mikhual Jerusalem atangin an rawn kal ve zauh zauh a. Sâwma pakhat leh thawhlâwm dang, Jerusalem lama mi tûr khawn chu an la rawn lam zing khawp mai. Mikhual an lo ti tha em em a, chuvângin Paula leh Barnaba-te pawh chu Sabbath hmasa bera Messia chanchin hril tûrin an ti ta a.

Rei vak lo chu thil pawh a kal tluang hle. Paula chuan Sunagogue-ah chuan thu tam tak a sawi hman a. Gentel leh Judate chu thliar hrang hauh lovin an inah te, dâwrah te, sumdâwnna hmunah te an tlawh zêl a. Isua chanchin hre duh apiangte hnênah an sawi zêl a. Sunagogue-a mite leh in lama thusawi ngaithlatute ngaihdân a inang lo fo a. Chutiangah chuan inhnial buaina a thleng fo thîn a ni.

Juda hruaitu eng ngemaw zâtin Paulan Messia thu a sawi chu an pawm lova. Paula te thusawi mi tam takin an pawm chu thîk tâwk an awm bawk a. An thilmak tihte an hmuhin an thinur zual sauh bawk a. A duh lotute chuan khawtlâng hruaitute hnênah an thlen ta nge nge a. Khua atanga hnawtchhuak tûrin an duh a. Paulate rawn zin hma ang khân thil chu awm leh se an tih bâkah, Sunagogue hruaitute chuan an dinhmun ngai an awhleh thei dâwn a ni.

Mahse rorêltute chuan Paulate thian dûn leh ringtute chu kovin an heknate chu ngaihtuah mah se thiam loh chantîrna tûr engmah an hmu si lo. Mi tam tak chuan Pathian thuâwihin khua leh tui tha tak an nih phah si a. Chu chu rorêltute tan hai rual a ni lo. A hawtuten an hêk nasat poh leh hrechiang duha ngaihven leh pianthar phah an tam ting mai a ni.

A tâwpah phei chuan Juda hruaitute chuan an buaina chu anmahni ngeia chinfel an tum ta a. Engmah hrethiam pha ve lo mi eng emaw zât hui khâwma fuihpawrhin buaina an siam a. Chu chu Paula-te thian dûn chuan an thih phah hial loh pawhin, rorêltute hnêna an zualko hman pawh an ring tawh lo a ni.

Mi thenkhatin Juda-ho phiarrûk dân chu an lo hre ru kiau si a. Paula leh Barnaba hnênah, “Mipui thinrim hi inmantîr miah suh u. In thuhril Messia hi a ropui lutuk a nih hi. Thu tam zâwk min hrilh zêl theih nân biru rih mai rawh u,” an ti a.

Paula leh Barnaba chuan an thiante thu sawi chu an pawm a. Mipui thinrimin an tlâng chîl hmain an bibo ta a. Mahse an rawn kîr leh ang. An chanchin tha ken chu ngawih bopui mai atan chuan a ui awm lutuk si a.

### Tih Tûrte

#### Sunday

- 1 Chronicles 29:11 chhiar la, tûn kâr zirlâi hi chhiar bawk ang che.
- I Bible hnung lama map awm kha en la, Iconium khaw awmna zawng rawh.
- Chângvawn, 1 Thessalonia 2:2 hi en lova sawi thei tûrin zir tan la.
- Isua zirtîrna lo duh lo sa nghâl ngawtute an aw mem? Chu mite chu i tanpui theih nân Pathian hnênah finna dîl ang che.

#### Thawhtanni

- Tirhkohte 1:8 leh Tirhkohte14:1 chhiar la.
- Tirhkohte 14:1 hian Tirhkohte 1:8-a Isua lo hrilh lâwk a rawn thlen famkimtîr tâk dân chu eng nge ni?
- Eng vângin nge Paula leh Barnaba-te kha Iconium khuaa Juda-te inkhâwmna in sunagogue-ah khân an va inkhâwm ve? Enga ti nge Tirhkohte 14:1-ah hian “an tih thin dân pângngâiin” tih tawngkam hman a nih?
- Pathian hnênah “i tih thin dân pângngâia” chibai i bûkna chu ngâwi renga a thu hretu nihna kawng remchâng taka rawn chantîr tûrin dîl ang che.

#### Thawhlehni

- Tirhkohte 14:2–7 chhiar la.
- Paula leh Barnaba te hian dodâlna an tawh khân engtin nge an rilru a awm i rin?
- Paula hian a hnampui Judahote’n amah sawichhe tûr ringawta khaw hrang hranga an zui thîn khân eng thu nge a lo hrilh i rin?
- Internet-ah “sakhua avânga tihduhdahna” tih han zawng chhin la.
- Isua chanchin an sawi avânga tihduhdah tuartute tân tawngtâi ang che.

#### Nilaini

- Tirhkohte 14:13; Tirhkohte 19:11, 12 leh Johana 4:48 chhiar la.
- Pathianin thilmak leh chhinchhiahna eng eng emaw a rawn hman chhan hi eng nge ni?
- Tirhkohte 5:15, 15 chhiar la, i Bible zirna note bu-ah khân Paula leh Petera te inan lohna lâi ziak chhuak ang che.
- Pathian awmpuina changa thil i tihte khân Pathian hnêna mi a hruai thlen ngei theih nân tawngtâi ang che.

#### Ningani

- Tirhkohte 13 leh 14 chhiar la,
- Khaw hrang hranga Paula duh lohna boruak tisosângtute kha tute nge?
- Thuthlung Thar map en la, Juda-hote’n Paula zui tûra Antiokei atanga Iconium leh Lustra thleng an zin kawng hlat zawng chu chhût ang che.
- Nangmah leh mi dang tiduhdahtute tân tawngtâi rawh.

#### Zirtawpni

- In chhûng inkhâwmah Tirhkohte 14:1–7 leh Acts of the Apostles, p. 179 chhiar ang che.
- I thian pakhat chu a keartui bang siin dintîr la. Chhuatah chuan, a ke hma inches 18 vêlah pawisa thîr dah ang che. Tichuan, kûn hniam a, pawisa thîr chu tlu miah lova la a, a lâk hnuah ding ngîl leh at tûrin hrilh la. Chumi zawhah chuan in pahnihin ti dûn leh ang che u. Engin chu thil tih chu tiharsa a, tiawlsam tih in sawi dûn dâwn nia
- Harsatna i tâwk chung pawha Pathian rawngbâwl tlat tho tûrin intiamkamna siam rawh u.
- Nangmah puitu thiante leh chhûngte i neih avângin Pathian hnênah lâwmthu sawi ang che.
