---
title:  Rebɔ Bible sua ho mmɔden
date:   28/04/2026
---

Wonhia sɛ mpo wobɛyɛ nwomanimfoɔ ansa na woasua Bible no, na so wobɛyɛ dɛn atumi agyina abɔ mmɔden Bible no sua mu?

**Bɔ mpaeɛ:** Wo Bible sua berɛ mu no adetitire paa ara a ɛnni sɛ wokwati wɔ ne mfitiaseɛ ne n’awieeɛ mu (ne akenkan no mu nwennweneho mu) ne mpaebɔ. Ellen G. White kyerɛ yɛn sɛ, sɛ yɛhyia Bible sua mu a, asrafoɔ bi bɛhyɛn yɛn mu bi. Na sɛ yɛsrɛ Honhom Kronkron no sɛ ɔmmra mmɛdi yɛn anim a, yɛnam so si sɔretia biara kwan, na ɔtamfo no dwane firi yɛn mu. “Ɛnni sɛ yɛkwati mpaebɔ wɔ yɛn Bible sua mu. Honhom Kronkron no nkutoo na ɔbetumi ama yɛn nneɛma akɛseɛ a ɛmu yɛ yɛn den mu nhunumu, anaasɛ aboa yɛn na yɛanwowɔ ntwɛtwɛdɛ wɔ nneɛma a ɛmu yɛ yɛn hunu na ho.”- The Great Controversy, pp. 599, 600.

**Kenkan na Twerɛ:** Yɛbɛtumi aka sɛ nsonsonoeɛ titire a ɛda sɛ worekenkan Bible no ara kɛkɛ, ne sɛ woresua paa no gyina dwumadie titire baako pɛ so: atwerɛ (woretwerɛ). Atwerɛtwerɛ no boa na ɛtumi twe yɛn adwene si Nyankopɔn asɛm no so, na yɛanya berɛ adwennwene, apɛnsɛmpɛnsɛmu, de ayɛ adwuma na yɛakɔ nkan mmɔdenmmɔ mu. Ɛboa ma yɛtumi siesie nneɛma a ayɛ yɛn adwenem bebree no na yɛada no adi-afiri yɛn tirim, aba yɛn nsa ano atwerɛm, na atena yɛn akomam damu no nyinaa. Ana ɛyɛ mmerɛ sɛ yɛbɛkae nea yɛatwerɛ no ntɛm so (Nwm. 119:15, 16). Sɛ wonnim atwerɛ a, kenkan Bible no (anaasɛ tie akenkan no), na akyire yi wadwennwene ho Nyankopɔn anim mpaebɔm.

**Kyɛ:** Ɛne obi nkyɛ nea wasua no. Yei bɛma aseɛ atim w’adwenem na akanyan obi foforɔ nso.

Paw Bible mu nwoma nkumaa no bi (te sɛ Yona, Marko, Filipifoɔ, anaa 1 Yohane) fa firi aseɛ, na sua no nkakrankakra. Akwan a ɛmmerɛ a wobetumi apaw yɛ sɛ wobɛsua no nkyekyɛmu (nkyɛkyɛmu-nkyekyɛmu kwan so), nyitohɔsɛm-nyitohɔsɛm, anaa etire (ti) no nyinaa:

1. Bɔ mpaeɛ srɛ sɛ Honhom Kronkron no mfa w’adwene na ɔnni w’anim na ɔmmubu w’akoma adesua no mu.
2. Paw Bible no nwoma anaa nyitohɔsɛm bi a wopɛ sɛ wosua.
3. Twerɛ nyitohɔsɛm no anaa emu nsɛntitire a wohunuiɛ berɛ a na worekenkan no gu wo nwoma mu.
4. Fa mpaebɔ kenkan nyitohɔsɛm no mu bio na sensan nsɛntitire no ase.
5. Twerɛ nea nsɛntitire no da no adi no.
6. Gyina nsɛntitire no so na bɔ mpaeɛ srɛ sɛ ɛnnya wo ne Nyankopɔn ntam ayɔnkofa no so nsunsuansoɔ.
7. Paw obi a nnɛ wo ne no bɛkyɛ.

“Berɛbiara sɛ Nyankopɔn nkurɔfoɔ renyini adom mu a, wɔkɔ so ara nya n’asɛm no mu nteaseɛ. Wɔnya nhunumu foforɔ ne nokorɛ kronkron no fɛ. Yei yɛ asafo no abakɔsɛm mu nokwasɛm a ɛfiri mfirisanten, na ɛbɛkɔ so atoa so ara akɔsi awieeɛ.”-EGW, Counsels to Writers and Editors, pp. 38, 39.

`Akwan bɛn so na w’ankasa wahu sɛ nyitohɔsɛm a ɛwɔ atifi ha yi yɛ nokorɛ? Sane hwɛ Dw. 119: 105.`
