title:  Imanuel, Onyankopͻn Ne Yεn Wͻ Hͻ
date:   09/04/2026
---

Sε worehwehwε sε wo ne obi a ͻnyε Kristoni bεkyε Onyankopͻn suban baako firi Bible no mu a, εhe na wobεbue akͻ?

Nnyiano papa no ara bεyε Yesu. Twerε no ka sε, Yesu nkyerε yεn sεnea Onyankopͻn suban teε nko na mmom, ͻyi Nyankopͻn adi nso. Abasεm bebree na εkasa fa saa adeε yi ho wͻ Bible no mu, na nea εma nteaseε no yε mmrε ne Yohane 14:9. Ɛha na Yesu ka sε, “Nea wahu me no ahu Agya no. Sε εbεyε na yεahu sεnea Onyankopͻn teε no, εwͻ sε yεtwa yεn ani hwε Yesu, N’asεm, Ne dwumadie, Ne nneyεε ne N’anwanwa dͻ a Ɔnam Ne wuo ne Ne sͻreε mu da no adi kyerε adasamma.

Agya no dɔ ne Ne banbɔ no da adi yie wɔ Ne Ba Yesu mu. Nea εma Bible no yεfε koraa ne sε, Onyankopͻn ama yɛn sɛnnahɔ nnan a nnipa hunu twerɛ too hɔ fa Yesu abrabͻ ho sεnea εbεyε na yεbɛhunu sεnea Ɔteε no yie. Mateo nwoma mu no (Yudani na ͻtwerε maa Yudafoͻ), yεhunu Yesu a Ɔyε Mesaia a na yεatwεn No abrε a Ɔbεhyɛɛ bͻhyε a na εda hͻ no ma. Marko nwoma mu nso, yεhu Yesu a Ɔreyε ɔsom ne afɔrebɔ nnwuma. Na Ɔdwene afoforͻ ho berɛ biara, afei nso na Wayε krado dͻn biara sε Ɔbεyε N’Agya apεdeε. Luka nwoma no mu nso, yεbεtumi akenkan afa sεnea Yesu nyaa atenka no ho, Ne nipayɛ a mfomsoͻ biara nni ho na ahummͻborͻ ahyε ma no. Yεbεtumi akenkan saa sɛnnahɔ yi anya anidasoͻ bi sε, nea yεkenkan no yε nokorε (Lk. 1:3,4). Yohane nwoma mu nso, yεhunu Nyankopͻn Ba no a Wabɛyɛ nipa, na wͻto nsa frε yεn ma yɛgye di sε Yesu yɛ oniiko a Ɔka sɛ Ɔyε no, sεnea εbεkanya yεn honhom fam abrabͻ no. Ɛwom sε Nsεmpa nwoma nnan no nhwehwεmu kura fapem korͻ deɛ, nanso “wͻmfa kwan korͻ so na εda nneɛma a εsesε no adi. Ɔtwerεfoͻ biara nyaa n’ankasa suahunu, na saa nsonoeɛ yi boa ma adwene biara nya nimdeε a emu dɔ.” - EGW, Manuscript 105, 1900. Asεmpa nwoma no mu nea εwͻ he na wokenkaneeɛ nkyεree koraa?

`Mat. 1:23 mu no, wͻde edin pͻtee bi ma Yesu. Adεn nti na yei ho hia saa ma nteaseɛ a yɛwɔ fa Onyankopͻn suban ho no? Kenkan Mat. 28:20, berε a wode w’adwene resi ͻfa a εtwa toɔ no soɔ no. Fa nyitohͻsεm mmienu yi toto ho. Jdeɛn na wohunu?`

Yεde yεn nsa akeka asεmpɔ kɛseɛ, a ɛyɛ Onyankopͻn suban no ani. Onyankopͻn yε kεse na Ne nneyεε mu yɛ duru sene sε yεbεtumi ate aseε, na yεbεkͻ so asua Ne ho adeε kͻsi daapem no mu.

`Onyankopͻn fata yεn ayeyie wͻ sεnea Ɔteε, nea Wayε, ne nea Ɔreyε wͻ yεn abrabͻ mu. Fa simma kakra bͻ mpaeε yi Onyankopͻn ayε fa sεnea Ɔteε ho. Gyina nea Bible no ka kyerɛ wo fa Onyankopɔn ho no so pɔtee. (Nhwεsoͻ: “Onyankopͻn, meda Wo ase sε ..., sεnea Woka kyerε me wͻ... .”).`
