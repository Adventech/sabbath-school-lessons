---
title:  Nyankopͻn Yε Kronkron
date:   06/04/2026
---

Kronkronyε nyε asεmfua a nnipa taa de di dwuma wͻn daa daa nkitahodie mu, ebia ɛsiane sɛ akronkronneε kakraa bi na atwa yεn ho ahyia no ntira. Homeda no yε ɛda kronkron bi wͻ ɛberε mu, na Nyankopͻn nso, sεnea Ɔteɛ no, Ɔyε kronkron. Nanso kronkronyɛ biara nni yεn daa daa abrabͻ mu.

Sε woyε nhwehwεmu bi fa Nyankopͻn abodin horoɔ no ho a, wobεhu sɛ kronkronyε ne N’abodin nyinaa nnyinasoɔ. Na ɛdeɛn na yei kyerɛ mmom?

`Sɛn na akenkansεm a ɛdidi soɔ yinom si ka Onyankopͻn ho asɛm: Lev. 20:26; 1 Sam 2:2; Yes. 57:5; Hes. 38:23?`

Sε Bible kasa fa Nyankopͻn ho sε, Ɔyε kronkron mapa a, na εkyerε sε, Ne ho nni amumuyɛ anaa bɔne biara, Ɔte Ne ho firi ho korakora. Onyankopɔn papayɛ no kura ɔha mu abupɛn no nyinaa firi ahyɛaseɛ kɔsi awieeɛ. Saa kwan yi so no, Nyankopͻn kronkronyε no ne fapem ma N’abodin a aka no nyinaa ara.

Nhwεsoͻ bi ne sε, Onyankopͻn dͻ no yε ͻdͻ kronn, ͻdͻ kronkron – ͻdͻ bi a εnyε pεsεmenkomenya ne ahopɛ. Nim a Ɔnim ade nyinaa no yɛ kronkron, kyerɛ sɛ, adwemmɔne biara mmata akyire. So wode wo ho bɛto Onyankopɔn bi a Ɔnim ade nyinaa no so berε a Ɔnyε kronkron? Jnneε anka yεbεsuro No mmom.

Onyankopͻn “tumi-nyinaa-wura” abodin no yε kronkron. Fa no sɛ Onyankopɔn bi wɔ hɔ a Ɔkura tumi nyinaa nanso Ɔnyε kronkron. Anka Ɔbεtumi ayε ɔhoͻdenfoͻ tirimuͻdenfoͻ. Nyankopͻn kronkronyε na εhyε yεn den ma yεdͻ No, εfirisε Ɔyε papa firi ahyεaseε kͻsi awieeε. Yei nti na, ebia, kronkronyε yε suban titire bi a εhia sɛ yɛte aseɛ fa Onyankopɔn suban ho no. Nanso, asɛ ɛyɛ nea yɛnte aseɛ koraa no mu baako.

Dwene saa nkurͻfoͻ yi a Bible bobͻ wͻn din yi ho; Mose, Yesaia, Hesekiel, Daniel ne Yohane a wͻbaa Nyankopͻn nkyεn no. Na wͻn nnyesoͻ no te sεn? Wͻyii wͻn nan mu mpaboa, de wͻn anim butuu fam, anaa wɔhwehwee ase sε awufoͻ. Sε nnipa a yεyε no, yεyε nnebͻnyεfoͻ na yεn ho nte koraa sε yεbεgyina Nyankopͻn anim. Onipa biara a ͻhwε Nyankopͻn anim no ntumi ntena nkwa mu. Afei, berε biara a E. G. White bεkͻ anisoadehunu mu no, na ͻteam sε, “Kronkron … kronkron … kronkron …,” εfiri sε, na εno ne asεmfua a ɛbɔ nea ɔhunu no nyinaa tɔfa. Na nokorεm mu no, ateasefoͻ baanan no nhome awia ne anadwo berɛ a wɔnka sε, “Kronkron, kronkron, kronkron ne Awurade Nyankopͻn, ade nyinaa so Tumfoɔ a Ɔtenaa hɔ, na Ɔwͻ hͻ, na Ɔrebɛba!” (Adi. 4:8).

`Nokwasεm ne sε, Nyankopͻn kronkronyε no yɛ kronn, na sε yεba Ne nkyεn a, εtwa sε yεhunu sε saa ara na Ɔteε. Ɔkwan bεn so na hunu a worehunu yei no kanyan woɔ?`
