---
title:  Awurade Nokorɛdie A Ɛtoɔ Ntwa
date:   03/03/2024
---

`Kenkan Dw. 78. Abakɔsɛm mmerɛ titire mmiɛnsa bɛn na wɔka ho asɛm wɔ saa Nnwom yi mu? Adesua ahoroɔ a ɛsisi bɛn na Asaph nya firi mmerɛ no biara mu?`

Israelfoɔ abrabɔ a atwam mu nhwehwɛmu da Onyankopɔn nokorɛdie ne Israel nokorɛ a wɔanni adi. Na ɛsɛ sɛ wɔkyerɛ awoɔntoatoasoɔ a ɛreba no sɛ wɔnnyɛ wɔn agyanom mfomsoɔ no bi na mmom wɔnya Onyankopɔn mu ahotosoɔ na wɔnni nokorɛ mma N’apam no. Ɔdwontotoɔ no de abakɔsɛm yɛ abebuo (Dw. 78:2), a ɛkyerɛ sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ nkurɔfoɔ no dwene nnwom no nkrasɛm no ho yie, na wɔhwehwɛ ne nteaseɛ no mu yie, na wɔhwehwɛ ne nteaseɛ no fa. Dw. 78:2 yɛ nkɔmhyɛ nkyerɛkyerɛmu a ɛfa kwan nan a Yesu fa so kyerɛkyerɛ wɔ abebuo mu ho (Mat. 13:34, 35).

Nnwom nwoma no nso sane kyerɛ Tukorɔ berɛ no (Dw. 78:9-54), berɛ a wɔtenaa Kanaan (Dw. 78:55-64), ne Dawid berɛ no (Dw. 78:65-72). Ɛda Awurade animuonyam nnwuma ne nsunsuansoɔ a ɛwɔ nnipa no bu a wɔbu wɔne Onyankopɔn apam no so adi. Israel abakɔsɛm no kyerɛ mmerɛ ahoroɔ a nkurɔfoɔ no annya ahofama amma Onyankopɔn, ne titire no wɔn abosonsom no (Dw. 78:58).

Nanso ɔdwontoni no si nea amma Israel anni nokorɛ no farebae so dua; wɔn werɛ firii nea Onyankopɔn ayɛ ama wɔn no, na wɔnni Onyankopɔn mu ahotosoɔ biara, wɔsɔɔ Onyankopɔn hwɛeɛ (Dw. 78:18, 41, 56), na wɔtee Ne so atua, na wɔpoo sɛ wɔbɛdi Ne mmara, N’apam ne N’adansedeɛ so (Dw. 78:10, 37, 56). Berɛ a ɔdwontoni no resi saa ahofama a wɔannya yi so dua no na ɛkyerɛ sɛ po a Ɔpoo Israel wɔ abakɔsɛm mu no firi bɔne titire a wɔyɔeɛ, ɛne sɛ, nkurɔfoɔ no poo sɛ wɔbɛnya Onyankopɔn mu ahotosoɔ (Dw. 78:7, 8).

Sɛ worekenkan nnwom nwoma no a, wo ho bɛdwiri wɔ nkurɔfoɔ no asoɔden a na wɔtaa yɛ ne wɔn honhom mu anifira a ɛne Onyankopɔn boasetɔ ne adom a ɛnni ano no bɔ abira no ho. Adɛn nti na awoɔntoatoasoɔ foforɔ no biara twentwɛnee wɔn nan ase sɛ wɛbɛsua no?

Ansa na yɛbɛbu awoɔntoatoasoɔ a atwam no atɛn no, ɛsɛ sɛ yɛdwene yɛn ankasa ho. So yɛn nso werɛ mfirii Onyankopɔn anwanwadeɛ a atwam no na yɛmpoo N’apam ahwehwɛdeɛ no? Nnwom no ntu nnipa fo sɛ wɔmfa wɔn ho nto wɔn nneyɛɛ so. Na mmom Dw. 78 kyerɛ kwa a onipa pɛ yɛ kwa gye sɛ yɛkaakae yɛn ho wɔ Onyankopɔn nokorɛdie na yɛgye N’adom no. Ako ahoroɔ a Onyankopɔn nkurɔfoɔ dii mu nkoguo (Dw. 78:9, 62-64) kyerɛkyerɛ nnwom no adesua mu sɛ onipa mmɔdemmɔ biara a Onyankopɔn nokorɛdie nka ho no bɛwie wɔ nkoguo mu.

`Adesua ahoroɔ bɛn na woasua, anaa ɛsɛ sɛ wosua firi wo mfomsoɔ a atwam no ho?`
