---
title:  Toa so dwennwene ho
date:   15/03/2024
---

Dwennwene nkransɛm a ɛwɔ Yes. 40 ne 51:1-16 mu no ho.

Sion ho nnwom no boa yɛn ma yɛtumi nya ahofama sononko ma Sion ne anidasoɔ amapa wɔ Onyankopɔn ahennie a ɛgyina hɔ ma no. Ɛwom Onyankopɔn ntomadan mu nhyira ahoroɔ pii kɔ so wɔ saa abrabɔ yi mu deɛ, anidasoɔ a ɛwɔ abrabɔ mu ne anigyeɛ a ɛwɔ Sion no da so ara wɔ daakye mu. Onyankopɔn mma bebree de nisuo hwehwɛ ɔsoro Sion (Dw. 137:1). Sɛ wobɛkae Sion no nkyerɛ adwene bi a ɛba kɛkɛ, na mmom sɛ yɛde anidaho si agyinaeɛ sɛ yɛbɛtena ase sɛnea saa nkaedeɛ no teɛ (Exod. 13:3; 20:8).

Ɛno nti, sɛ yɛreto Sion ho Nnwom no boa ma yɛtumi gye tom sɛ yɛbɛtena anidasoɔ a ɛwɔ Onyankopɔn ahennie no mu wɔ asase foforɔ no so (Adi. 21:1-5). Ɛno nti, nnipa a wɔnwu da bɛdwene anigyeɛ a ɛtena hɔ daa no ho afa adebɔ tumi, ɔgyeɛ dɔ ahintasɛm no ho. Ɔtamfo daadaafoɔ tirimuɔdenfoɔ biara nni hɔ a ɔbɛma yɛn werɛ afiri Onyankopɔn. Akwaa biara bɛtu mpɔn, na ahoɔden biara bɛyɛ kɛse. Sɛ yɛrepɛ nimdeɛ no renha adwene no anaasɛ ɛrema yɛmmrɛ. Ɛhɔ na yɛbɛbuebue nnwuma akɛseɛ, na yɛaduru botae akɛseɛ ho, na yɛbɛduru anisoadehunu akɛseɛ ho; nanso mpuntuo afoforɔ bɛkɔ so awɔ hɔ, nneɛma a ɛyɛ nwanwa foforɔ bɛwɔ hɔ a yɛn ani bɛgye ho, nokorɛ afoforɔ a yɛbɛte aseɛ, asuadeɛ afoforɔ a yɛbɛtwe adwene ne ɔkra ne nipadua no asi soɔ.” – EGW, GC 677.

Sɛ yɛbɛbɔ yɛn adwene pɔ sɛ yɛmma yɛn werɛ mfiri Sion no yɛ anohoba bi a Awurade akwantufoɔ no bɔ, sɛ ɛnyɛ adeɛ a wɔbɛgye saa wiase yi atom da sɛ ɛha na yɛfiri na mmom yɛtwɛn ɔsoro foforɔ ne asase foforɔ no.

Ɛno nti, Sion ho Nnwom no agyidifoɔ bɛtumi ato wɔ awoɔntoatoasoɔ nyinaa a wɔn kɔn dɔ sɛ wɔbɛtena Yerusalem Foforɔ no mu no (Adi. 3:12). Sion ho Nnwom no hyɛ yɛn nkuran sɛ yɛmfa anidasoɔ nhwɛ daakye wiase bi anim kwan, na ɛsane nso hyɛ yɛn sɛ yɛnyɛ Onyankopɔn adom ananmusifoɔ wɔ saa wiase yi mu.

**Nkasaho nsɛmmisa**:

`Sɛn na yɛbɛsi afa honhom mu ne gyidie nkyerɛkyerɛ nhyehyɛɛ a ɛgyina Onyankopɔn nkurɔfoɔ so wɔ Sion no, beaeɛ pɔtee bi a ɛwɔ Yerusalem, na yɛde ayɛ adwuma ama Asafo no ne ne somdwumadie wɔ wiase mu?`

`Ɔkwan bɛn so na agyidifoɔ bɛtumi atena Onyankopɔn ntomadan mu ɛnnɛ yi? (Yoh. 1:14-18; Heb. 12:22-24).`

`Ɔkwan bɛn so na Sion bɛba abɛyɛ aman nyinaa kuropɔn sɛnea ɛda adi wɔ Dw. 87 mu no? (Rom. 5:10; Efe. 2:11-16; Kol. 1:19-23).`

`Mmuaeɛ bɛn na wode bɛma obi a ɔbisa nea enti a nnebɔneyɛfoɔ di yie na “ateneneefoɔ” pii hunu amane wɔ saa wiase yi mu? Ɛdeɛn na wobɛka? Yɛbɛyɛ dɛn agye atom sɛ ɛnyɛ nneɛma a ɛkɔ so wɔ ha seesei no nyinaa na yɛwɔ ho mmuaeɛ?`
