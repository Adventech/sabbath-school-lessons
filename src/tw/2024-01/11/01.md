### Nnawɔtwe yi adesua
Dw. 84; Adi. 21:3; Dw. 122; 87; Gal. 3:28, 29; Mat. 28:18-20; Dw. 46; Dw. 125.

> <p>Nkaebɔsɛm</p>
> “Me kra ani gyina, ɛtɔ piti mpo, Awurade adihɔ no nti, m’akoma ne me honam di ahurisie ma Onyankopɔn teasefoɔ” (Dw. 84:2).

Sion nnwom no yɛ anigyeɛ sankunnwom a ɛhyɛ Sion ahoɔfɛ ne Awurade a Ɔdi hene firi Ne bepɔ kronkron no soɔ tumidie animuonyam. Saa nnwom yi taa fata Awurade fie no ayɛyie, na ɛkyerɛ kronkronbea no ho dɔ a yɛtumi hunu wɔ nnwom foforɔ mu nso. Kora mma no a wɔdii kan nyaa Awurade fie nhyira mu suahunu sɛ asɔrefie no adwontofoɔ (1 Bere. 6:31-38) ne asɔrefie apono no awɛnfoɔ (1 Bere. 9:19) na wɔhyehyɛɛ saa nnwom yi mu pii.

Deɛn na ɛma Sion yɛ anidasoɔ ne anigyeɛ farebae? Sion gyinaa hɔ maa Onyankopɔn a Ɔte Ne nkurɔfoɔ mu. Sɛnea Israelfoɔ no yɛ Onyankopɔn nkurɔfoɔ a Wayi wɔn (Deut. 7:6) no, saa ara nso na Sion yɛ bepɔ a Onyankopɔn ayi (Dw. 78:68; 87:2). Onyankopɔn firi Sion di hene (Dw. 99:1, 2) na Ɔkyekyeree N’asɔrefie nso wɔ Sion (Dw. 87:1). Saa nti na Sion yɛ ɔsoro nhyira ne dwanekɔbea no. Wɔtaa de Sion toto Yerusalem ho, anaa mpo wɔfrɛ no Yerusalem ne kronkronbea, beaeɛ a Onyankopɔn yɛɛ nkwagyeɛ ho adwuma maa tetefoɔ no.

Sion nhyira trɛ kɔpem asase ano ɛfiri sɛ Awurade nipa ne adom boro kronkronbea biara ahyeɛ so. Sion ne asase nyinaa anika (Dw. 48:2), de foa so sɛ asase nyinaa yɛ Onyankopɔn dea.
