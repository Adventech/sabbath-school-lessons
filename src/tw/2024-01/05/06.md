---
title:  Na Wɔansɔ Teneneeni No Anhwɛ
date:   01/02/2024
---

`Kenkan Dw. 37:1, 8; 49:5-7; 94:3-7; ne 125:3. Apereapereɛ bɛn na ɔɔdwontoni no hyia?`

Saa Nnwom nwoma ahoroɔ yi nwiinwii fa abɔnefoɔ yiedie ne ɔhaw a saa nokwasɛm yi de ma ateneneefoɔ ho. Ɛnyɛ sɛ abɔnefoɔ no nya wɔn ho nko ara, na mmom ɛtɔ mmerɛ bi a wɔkasa tia Onyankopɔn na wɔhyɛ afoforɔ so nso. Nea ɛha adwene ne sɛ berɛ a “bɔne ahempoma” (Dw. 125:3) afa wiase nyinaa no, “tenenee poma” (Dw. 45:6) no deɛ asɛ ɛredi nkoguo. Adɛn nti na ɛnneɛ yɛnni bɔne akyi sɛnea afoforɔ yɛ no?

`Kenkan Dw. 73:1-20, 27. Ɛdeɛn na ɛma ɔɔdwontoni no tumi fa saa ɔhaw yi mu? Wɔn a wɔde wɔn ho to nneɛma hunu so no awieeɛ ne sɛn (hwɛ 1 Pet. 1:17 nso).`

Berɛ a ɔdwontoni no de n’ani sii amumuyɛ a ɛrekɔ so wɔ wiase yi so wɔ Dw. 73 mu no, wantumi anhunu Onyankopɔn fam deɛ. Ɔhaw a abɔnefoɔ yiedie de maa ne gyidie no na ɛboro nteaseɛ so; na na ɔgye di nso sɛ n’akyinnyeɛ a ɛfa gyidie a ɛso nni mfasoɔ no nso gyina nokwasɛm so.

Nanso Dw. 73 kyerɛ sɛ “saa nsɛm yi di wɔn a wɔpo saa nnwom yi nkyekyɛmu a ɛdi kan no ho fɛw no, a ɛyɛ nnwom no nyinaa tɔfabɔ no: “Hwɛ sɛnea Israel Nyankopɔn no yɛ ma wɔn a wɔtene wɔ akoma mu no” – Reformation Commentary on Scripture, p. 11.

Wɔde ɔɔdwontoni no kɔɔ kronkronbea hɔ, baabi a Onyankopɔn di soɔ no, na ɔtenaa hɔ sɛ “nnɛ” yɛ mosaic no mu baako, na ɛsɛ sɛ ɔdwene “awieeɛ” ho berɛ a abɔnefoɔ bɛhyia Onyankopɔn atemmuo. Ɔɔdwontoni no te a ɔtee saa nokwasɛm yi ase wɔ kronkronbea hɔ, na ɔpaee mu kaa ne nkwaseasɛm a na wadi no kyerɛ sɛ, wobɛtumi afa honhom mu nhunumu so ate nokwasɛm ase na mmom ɛnyɛ onipa nyansa so.

`Sɛn na Onyankopɔn atemmuo a ɛbɛba wiase ne emu bɔne so ho bɔhyɛ no ma wo abotɔyam berɛ a bɔne bebree nnya asotwe biara no?`
