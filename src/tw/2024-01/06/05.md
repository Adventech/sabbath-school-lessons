---
title:  Fa w’abufuw kɔ wɔn so
date:   07/02/2024
---

`Kenkan Dw. 58:6-8; 69:22-28; 83:9-17; 94:1, 2; 137:7-9. Adwene bɛn na Nnwom yinom da no adi? Hwan na Ɔbu atɛn wɔ Nnwom yinom mu?`

Nnwom nwoma binom koto srɛ Onyankopɔn sɛ Ɔntɔ werɛ wɔ ankorankorɛ ne aman binom a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔha anaasɛ wɔaha Nnwom atwerɛfoɔ no anaasɛ wɔn nkurɔfoɔ. Nnwom atwerɛfoɔ yinom bɛtumi ayɛ basaa esiane wɔn kasa a ano yɛ den ne sɛnea ɛne Twerɛsɛm mu nhyehyɛeɛ, dɔ w’atamfo bɔ abira no ho (Mat. 5:44).

Nanso Nnwom twerɛfoɔ no anibere wɔ nhyɛsoɔ ho no yɛ papa. Ɛkyerɛ sɛ Nnwom atwerɛfoɔ no fa nokorɛ ne mfomsoɔ anibere so sene nnipa bebree. Ɔdwene bɔne a ɛkɔ so wɔ wiase ho kɛse, ɛnyɛ nea ɛtia no nko na mmom nea ɛtia afoforɔ nso.

Nnwom twerɛfoɔ no nsusu wɔ baabiara sɛ awerɛtɔ wɔ ne nsam. Mmom ɔgyae awerɛtɔ no hyɛ Onyankopɔn nko ara nsa. Nnwom nwoma no kanyan ɔsoro nnome apam no (Deut. 27:9-16). Ɔfrɛ Onyankopɔn sɛ Ɔnkeka Ne ho sɛnea Wahyɛ bɔ no.

Nnwom nwoma no yɛ nkɔmhyɛ dawubɔ a ɛfa Onyankopɔn atemmuo a yɛrehwɛ no kwan ho; Ɛnyɛ ɛno nko ara ne Nnwom twerɛfoɔ no mpaebɔ.

Dw. 137 bu nkɔmpɔ wɔ amannebɔ a ɛfa ɔsoro atemmuo wɔ Babel so no ho; sɛnea ɛda adi wɔ adiyifoɔ no mu no. Ɔsɛeɛ a Babelfoɔ no de baa aman nkaeɛ so no bɛba wɔn so. Nnwom nwoma no de ɔsoro kɔkɔbɔ to dwa sɛ bɔne nkwati asotwe daa.

Atɛntenenee ne adom na wɔde susu Onyankopɔn awerɛtɔ. Wɔfrɛ Onyankopɔn mma sɛ wɔmmɔ mpaeɛ mma nkurɔfoɔ a wɔteetee wɔn na wɔnnya wɔn adwensakra ho anidasoɔ (Dw. 83:18, Yer. 29:7).

Berɛ a yɛrehwɛ sɛ Nnwom yi bɛfata Twerɛsɛm mu suban pa mmara a ɛfa ɔdɔ a yɛnya ma atamfoɔ ho no, ɛsɛ sɛ yɛhwɛ yie sɛ yɛngo yeadie mu adwennwene suahunu a ɛwɔ mu no mu. Onyankopɔn hunu ne mma amanehunu na ɔma wɔn awerɛhyɛmu sɛ, “Awurade ahotefoɔ wuo deɛ, ne boɔ yɛ den N’anim (Dw. 116:15). Ɔsoro atemmuo hyɛ Onyankopɔn nkurɔfoɔ sɛ wɔmma wɔn nne so ntia bɔne biara na wɔnhwehwɛ Onyankopɔn ahennie no wɔ ne mayɛ mu. Nnwom nwoma ma wɔn a wɔhunu sɛ Onyankopɔn nim wɔn amanehunu na da koro bi atɛntenenee bɛba.

`Hwan na ɛtɔ da a, ɔnnya adwene anaasɛ nsusuiɛ mfa nkurɔfoɔ a wɔafom no anaasɛ n’adɔfoɔ binom no awerɛtɔ ho? Sɛn na ebia Nnwom yinom bɛboa wo ama wode saa atenka ahoroɔ no ato yie?`
