---
title:  Toantšano e Kholo Mangolong a Paulosi
date:  12/09/2023
---

`Bala Baroma 13:11–14, 1 Bathesalonike 5:6–8, le 2 Bakorinthe 10:3–6. Litemana tsee li tšoana joang le Baefese 6:10–20? U nahana hobaneng Paulosi a sebelisa mofuta oo oa litšoantšo?`

Mangolong a hae, Paulosi o lutse a sebelisa puo ea sesole le papiso, ho mema balumeli ho etsisa mohlala oa boitšoaro ba masole. Ha Baefese 6:10–20 e emetse tšebeliso e telele ho feta tse ling e bileng e tletseng puo eo haholo, puo ea sesole e bonahala e le mokhoa o moholo o mo thusang ho utloisisa pale ea evangeli. Kamora ho hlola “ matla le mebuso” sefapanong (Bakolo. 2:15), Kreste ea phahamisitsoeng joale o sebeletsa litholoana tsa tlholo eo a le maemong a hae a hoba Morena ea phahamisitsoeng holima matla (Bafilipi 2:9–11). Ka ho memela balateli ba hae hoba bahlabani ntoeng ea ka lefatše lohle, Kreste o etella pele makhotla a leseli ho a isa tlholong e kholo (1 Bakori. 15:54–58, 2 Bathes. 2:8, Baro. 16:20). Ha re nka tšebeliso ea Paulosi ea tšoantšiso ea sesole, re hlokomela hore o utloisisa ntoa pakeng tsa botle le bobe e le “ntoa ea nako e telele ea ka lefatše lohle: ntoa li fihlela tlhorong le ho kupa pakeng tsa mabotho a mabeli a tjamelaneng hara mengoaha eohle ho fihlela le leng le hapa ntoa ea ho qetela.”—Petrose W. Macky, St. Paulosi’s Cosmic War Myth: A Military Version of the Gospel (New York: Petrose Lang Publishing, Inc., 1998), Leq. 1.

’Moko-taba oa Paulosi o lutseng o hlaella oa ntoa ea ka lefatše lohle o boetse ke karolo e entseng Baefese. Boipiletsong ba hae ba ho nka lihlomo (Baef. 6:10–20), Paulosi o kopanyana likarolo tsa ntoa ea ka lefatše lohle, tseo a seng a li sebelisitse: ho matlafatsa balumeli ka “matla” a mangata ho etsoang ke Molimo (Baef. 1:18–20; Baef. 3:16,20); tlholo ea Kreste le phahamiso holima matla (Baef. 1:20–23); balumeli e le lebotho le tsositsoeng bafung la ea neng a shoele, empa joale ba matlafalitsoe ke hˆo ba ba Kreste ea phahamisitsoeng, ’me ba khona ho loana khahlanong le monga bona oa mehleng oa lefifi (Baef. 2:1–10); seabo sa kereke sa ho senolela matla tšenyeho ea oona e tlang (Baef. 3:10); tšebeliso ea Pesalema 68:18 ho bontša Kreste e le mohlabani ea halalelang ea hlolang (Baef. 4:7–11); le pitso ea balumeli ea ho “apara” liaparo tsa evangeli (sheba Baef. 4:20–24). Ha re bitsetsoa ho apara “lihlomo tsohle” tsa Molimo tsa Moea, re se re loketse ho utloisisa khubu ea seabo sa rona ntoeng ea ka lefatše lohle, empa, hape, re lokela ho tiea tšepisong eo re nang le eona ea ho ba le seabo tlholong ea Kreste ea mapomela.

`Tse ling tsa tsela tseo u le motho, tseo u bileng le maiphihlelo, eseng a ’nete ena ea ka lefatše lohle, empa ea tlholo eo re ka inkelang eona ho Jesu? Ke hobaneng ha ho utloisisa tlholo eo a re fumanetseng eona e le motheo oa tšepo le maiphihlelo a rona?`
