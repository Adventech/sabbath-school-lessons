---
title:  Re Kereke ea Molimo O Phelang
date:  26/09/2023
---

`Hobaneng ho thabisa ho bile ho le bohlokoa hoba karolo ea kereke ea Molimo? Baefese 3.`

Rea khothala ha re utlo litho tsa kereke li buoa ntho tse ntle ka kereke. Leha ho le joalo, ea nang le morolo o moholo hara rona o sitoa ho fihlela motlotlohali oa bopaki ba Paulosi ho Baefese 3 ka kereke. Paulosi o qala ka tlaleho ea lithapelo tsa hae ho balumeli ba Baefese (Baef. 3:1; bapisa le Baef. 1:15–23) empa o khaola taba o buoa ka tlholo ea Molimo ea kereke (Baef. 3:2–13), ebe joale o fera tlaleho ea thapelo ea hae (Baef. 3:14–21). Hara nako re qetella re utloisisa ntho tsa bohlokoa ka “leano” kapa “sephiri” sa Molimo:

* Ho tloha bosafeleng, Molimo O phuthulla “sephiri” kapa “leano” la kereke (Baef. 3:3–5, 9, 11).
* Ka bophelo le lefu la Jesu, leano lane leo esale le patiloe lea “sebelisoa” (Baef. 3:11; bapisa le Baef. 2:11–22).
* Ka Tšenolo, Paulosi o ithuta “sephiri” sa kereke le taba e makatsang ea hore le Balichaba ba tlameha ho nka seabo ka botlalo ho lona (Baef. 3:3–6).
* Paulosi o nka karolo ho haseng taba tsena tse monate ele moreri oa “monono o sa fihleloeng oa Kreste” (Baef. 3:8,9).
* Ka batho ba bangata ba hapetsoeng ho Kreste, kereke, e bopiloeng ka Bajude le Balichaba, e bonahala “ho baholo le ho mebuso maholimong” (Baef. 3:10), o phatlalatsa tšenyeho ea eona e haufi (bapisa le Baef. 6:10–20). Leano la ho boelanya sohle ho Kreste (Baef. 1:10) le tseleng, ’me nako ea eona e khutšoane.

Kutloisiso ena ka kereke e chesehellisa Paulosi ho rapella balumeli. Hobaneng u sa nahane ka eena a u rapella thapelo e tsoang botebong ba pelo ho Baefese 3:14–21? Hobaneng u sa nahane a u rapella hore u “tlale botlalo bohle ba Molimo” (Baef. 3:19) le ho nka karolo ka botlalo, sephiring sa kereke e kopaneng?

`A mang a marako a teng pakeng tsa balumeli kerekeng ea rona ao, re ikamahantse le se ngotsoeng ke Paulosi, tse sa lokeleng hoba teng? Ke sefe seo u ka se etsang ho thusa ho a tlosa?`
