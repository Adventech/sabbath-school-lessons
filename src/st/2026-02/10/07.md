---
title:  Ho Ithuta Haholoanyane
date:   05/06/2026
---

Hangata Bibele e sebelisa lipapiso tsa temo ho hlalosa boemo ba rona ba moea. Hosea 10:12 ke mohlala o mong o bolelang seo re se buileng bekeng ena:

“Itjalleleng ho loka; le tle le kotule mohau;\
Itemeleng thite,\
Hobane ke nako ea ho batla Jehova,\
Ho fihlela a tla, a le nesetsa ho loka”.

Re jala, re kotula, re pshatla mobu o thata, ’me re batla Molimo ho atamela ho eena. Mobu oa lipelo tsa rona o tlameha ho lokisoa le ho itokisetsa pula (Moea o Halalelang) ho na. Molimo a ka re fa takatso ea ho lokisa mobu, empa, qetellong, kamano le Molimo ke kopano (sheba Ba-Filipi 2:12, 13). Re tlameha ho lebisa lihloho tsa rona ho Eena, ho nanabela ho Eena, le ho Mo khomarela. Ebe O sebetsa ka ho rona ho etsa tse ling.

Mohlala o moholo oa rona oa seo ho khomarela Molimo ho se bolelang o ka fumanoa litemaneng tsena: “Mahlo a lona a bone seo Jehova a se entseng ka baka la Baale-peore, kamoo Jehova, Molimo oa hao, a timelitseng har’a lona bohle ba neng ba latetse Baale-Peore kateng. Athe lona ba ileng ba khomarela Jehova, Molimo oa lona, le sa ntsane le phela le kajeno kaofela ha lona” (Deut. 4:3, 4).

**Lipotso tsa Puisano:**

`“U se ke ua re isa molekong, u mpe u re lopolle ho e mobe” (Mattheu 6:13). Jesu o ile a ruta barutuoa ba hae ka ho toba hore ba rapele ka tsela ena, empa na re boloka mohopolo oo lithapelong tsa r na tsa letsatsi le letsatsi? U rapela khafetsa hakae bakeng sa tšireletso khahlanong le moleko le sebe?`

`U ka hlalosa mpho ea bohlokoa ea seaparo sa Kreste sa ho loka joang ho motho eo e seng Mokreste kapa molumeli e mocha?`

`Kobo ea Kreste ea ho loka e amana joang le molaetsa oa sehalalelo, oo kaofela ha oona o buang ka ho tšoarela le ho hloekisa moetsalibe ea bakileng? U utloisisa hantle hakae botle le monono oa molaetsa oo?`

**Kakaretso:** Ho ipolela libe tsa rona ka lebaka la khothatso ea Moea o Halalelang le ho inehela pakong ke likarolo tsa bohlokoa tsa ho ba le likamano tse atlehileng le Molimo. Ho tseba hore re tšoaretsoe ka ho feletseng le ho apesoa ka seaparo sa Jesu sa ho loka ke phihlelo e fetolang ka ho fetisisa ho motho. Hase feela hore re utloa boima ba sebe bo tlosoa, empa re utloa lerato la Molimo le re pota-potile ha re ntse re atamela ho Eena. Sena se re tlamahanya le Molimo, sea re matlafatsa moeeng, ’me se re qobella ho Mo rata ka matla ’ohle a botho ba rona.
