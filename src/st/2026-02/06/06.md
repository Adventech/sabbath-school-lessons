---
title:  Moshe o Rapella Sechaba
date:   07/05/2026
---

`Bala Exoda 32:31, 32. See se re ruta eng ka Moshe le thapelo?`

Moshe o ne a buella batho ba Molimo ka sebete nako le nako. Moshe o ile a retelehela ho Molimo ha batho ba nyoriloe (Ex. 15:25; Ex. 17:2–6), ha batho ba ne ba lapile (Num. 11:21, 22), le ka ho tsieleha (Num. 11:11 –15).

Ha Baiseraele ba ntse ba iketsetsa namane ea khauta hang ka mor’a hore Molimo a etse selekane le bona, Moshe o ile a hopola: “’Etsoe ke ne ke tšohile bohale le khalefo e tukang eo Jehova a neng a le halefetse ka eona, hore a tle a le timetse. Empa Jehova a nkutloa le ka nako eo’” (Deuteronoma 9:19.

Ha lihloela li khutla Leatseng la Pallo, Moshe o ile a hopola, “‘Ke ile ka itihela fatše ka sefahleho ka pel’a Jehova; Ka ’na ka itihela fatše ka matsatsi a mashome a mane le masiu a mashome a mane, hobane Jehova o ne a itse o tla le felisa’” (Deuteronoma 9:25).

Ha Levi a ne a aroloa ho tsoa melokong e meng hore a sebeletse sehalalelong, Moshe o ile a hopola: “‘Joalo ka lekhetlo la pele ke ile ka lula thabeng matsatsi a mashome a mane le masiu a mashome a mane; Jehova le eena o ile a nkutloa ka nako eo, ’me Jehova a khetha hore a se ke a le timetsa’” (Deuteronoma 10:10). Molimo o ile a utloa kopo ea Moshe.

Re ka ithuta ho hongata bophelong ba Moshe mabapi le thapelo le ho khomarela Molimo ka tieo:

- **Moshe o ne a e-na le lerato le tebileng ho Molimo, le setšoantšo se hlakileng sa botho ba Hae.** Molimo o itlhalositse ho Moshe ho Exoda 34:6: “Jehova, Jehova, Molimo o mohau, o lereko, o liehang ho halefa, o tletseng mohau le ’nete”.
- **Moshe o ne a e-na le sebete a bile a tšepahala ha a ntse a tšoarella ho Molimo linakong tse monate le tse bohloko** tsa leeto le khathatsang ho ea lefatšeng la pallo. Leha a ne a sokola joalo ka rona bohle, Moshe o ile a tšepa matla a Molimo, boteng ba hae, le boetapele ba hae bophelong ba hae (Exod. 33:13).
- **Moshe o ile a hopotsa Molimo ka selekane sa Hae** (Exoda 32:13), a bolela litšepiso tsa Molimo molemong oa batho ba Hae (Deut. 7:8), ’me a hopola tataiso ea Molimo nakong e fetileng (Deuteronoma 8:2).
- **Moshe o ile a amohela likarabo tsa Molimo lithapelong tsa hae, ebang ke E kapa Che.** Ho ba kamanong e haufi le Molimo ha ho bolele hore re tla lula re fumana seo re se batlang (Deut. 3:23–29), empa re lokela ho rapela ka phehello leha ho le joalo (Luka 18:1–8).

`Ke mang ea hlokang lithapelo tsa hau tsa bo rapelli hona joale? Ke eng e u sitisang ho rapela hona joale?`
