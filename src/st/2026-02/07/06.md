---
title:  Lipotso Tse Ling Mabapi le Thapelo
date:   14/05/2026
---

**Ke hobane’ng ha u ka rapela empa Molimo a se a ntse a tseba?** Ke ka baka la’ng re tšoanetse ho rapela Molimo ha e le moo a tseba tsohle? Ellen G. White o e hlalosa tjena: “Eseng hore hoa hlokahala hore re tsebise Molimo seo re leng sona, empa e le hore re khone ho mo amohela. Thapelo ha e theolele Molimo ho rona, empa e re nyollela ho Eena.”— Steps to Christ, p. 93. Ka sebele, Molimo o tseba litakatso tsa rona le litlhoko tsa rona, ’me o bala sohle se ka lipelong tsa rona. Leha hole joalo, ho rapela ho molemo ho rona. E re memela ho emisa har'a lepotlapotla le ka har'a maphelo a rona, ho khefutsa le ho amohela hore Molimo o na le borena holim'a tsohle, le ho ipeha maotong a Hae. Ho ka boela ha buloa litsela hore Molimo a nke khato ha re Mo mema ho etsa joalo. Moea o Halalelang oa re rapella ha re sa tsebe ho rapela kamoo re lokelang ho rapela kateng (Ba-Roma 8:26, 27).

**Ke hobane'ng ha u rapela ha lintho li tsamaea hantle?** Ho khotsofala le boikho-homoso (sheba Thuto ea 3) e ka ba e 'ngoe ea litšitiso tse kholo ho bophelo bo matla ba thapelo. Ha feela re ne re ka elelloa hore na re hloka Molimo hakae, re ka tla ho Eena haholo! Haeba mangeloi a phethahetseng aa Mo khumamela le ho Mo rapela, ke hobane’ng ha rona, joaloka batho ba baetsalibe, re ka nahana hore ha re hloke hakaalo? Matheu 5:6 le Esaia 44:3 li re’ng ka ’nete ee?

**Karolo ea tumelo thapelong ke efe?** Bala Ba-Heberu 11:6 ’me u nahanisise ka mantsoe ana: “Thapelo le tumelo li amana haholo-holo, ’me li hloka ho ithutoa ’moho. Thapelong ea tumelo ho na le saense ea bomolimo; ke saense eo e mong le e mong ea neng a tla etsa hore mosebetsi oa hae oa bophelo o atlehe o tlameha ho o utloisisa. Kreste o re, ‘Se seng le se seng seo le se kopang ka thapelo, lumelang hoba le se bone, ’me se tla ba ho lona.’ Mareka 11:24. O hlakisa hore ho kopa ha rona ho tlameha ho lumellana le thato ea Molimo; re tlameha ho kopa lintho tseo a li tšepisitseng, ’me eng kapa eng eo re e amohelang e tlameha ho sebelisoa ho etsa thato ea Hae. Maemo a ile a phethahala, tšepiso ha e na pelaelo. Ha ho hlokahale hore re batle bopaki bo ka ntle ba tlhohonolofatso.”—Ellen G. White, Education, leqepheng la 257, 258.

**Ke lokela ho rapela le bo-mang?** Haholo-holo re lokela ho rapela boinotšing (Molimo le uena feela), kaha thapelo le ho ithuta Bibele ke tsona tse matlafatsang kamano ea hao le Molimo. Behella nako kathoko ea ho hlahloba pelo ea hau ha u bua le mamela Molimo (Matheo 6:6). Hape re lokela ho rapela le malapa a rona kapa ka lihlopha tse nyane (Liketso 12:12), hobane moo ba babeli kapa ba bararo ba bokaneng teng, Molimo o teng (Matt. 18:20). Qetellong, re lokela ho rapela le liphutheho tsa rona (Jakobo 5:13–16). Mefuta ena e meraro ea thapelo e bohlokoa.

**Ke lokela ho mamela joang?** Thapelo hase feela ho bua le Molimo; hape re hloka ho mo lumella ho re “hlaolela” le ho bua maphelong a rona. Mokhoa o hlakileng le o bolokehileng oa ho etsa sena ke ho bala Bibele le ho kopanya thapelo le thuto ea Bibele nakong ea borapeli. Hlokomela hore u se ke ua ntša maikutlo a hao kapa ua mamela maikutlo a hao ho e-na le ho batlisisa ka Bibeleng.

`Ke lintho life tse u thatafallang ka ho fetisisa lintlheng tse ka holimo?`
