### Bakeng sa Thuto ea Beke ena Bala
Lipes. 105:5, Bakolo. 3:16, Jakobo 5:13, Pesalm 44, Pesalm 22, Pesalm 13, Lipes. 60:1–5.

> <p>Temana ea khopolo</p>
> “Mohlomong, ha Jesu a le sebakeng se seng A rapela; eitse hobane a qete thapelo ea hae, e mong oa barutuoa a re ho eena: Morena, re rute ho rapela joalokaha Johanne le eena a rutile barutuoa ba hae hae.’” (Luka 11:1).

Tumelo ea hore ke thapelo ea maikutlo e sa ithutoang eo eleng eona ea makoea e tumme hara Bakreste ba bang. Leha ho le joalo, barutuoa ba Jesu ba ile ba putsoa haholo ha ba ne ba kopa Jesu ho ba ruta ho rapela. Molimo

O ila a fana ka buka ea thapelo, Lipesaleme, tse pelong ea Bibele, eseng feela ho re bontša hore na batho ba Molimo mehleng ea khale ba ne ba rapela joang, empa le ho re ruta ho rapela kajeno.

Ho tloha lilemong tsa pele, Lipesaleme li file lithapelo tsa bahalaleli sebopeho, ho kenyeletsa le thapelo tsa Jesu (1 Likro. 16:7, 9; Neh. 12:8; Mattheu 27:46; Baefese 5:19). Bekeng ena re tla shebanang le seabo seo Lipesaleme li bileng le sona ho thuseng batho ba Molimo leetong la bona la bophelo le ho hola likamanong tsa bona le Molimo. Re lokela ho hopola hore Lipesaleme ke lithapelo, ka hona, li bohlokoa- hlokoa, eseng feela bakeng sa ho chabisa thuto-Molimo empa le kamoo li ka fetolang thapelo tsa motho ka mong le tsa rona kaofela.

Ho rapela Lipesaleme ho thusistse balumeli ba bangata ho etsa tloaelo e molemo ea thapelo ea kamehla-le mehla.

Bekeng ena re tla tsoela pele ho sheba Lipesaleme, haholo-holo nakong tseo re leng maemong ao ntho li sa re tsamaeleng hantle.
