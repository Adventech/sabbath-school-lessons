---
title:  Selekane Senai
date:  29/09/2021
---

Phallo le sohle seo e neng e se fupere, e tloha maling a likoseneng tsa mamati Egepeta, ho fihlella mehlolong ea leoatle le lefubelu—ebile maiphihlelo a makakang! Haho potang e ile ea etsa maiphihlelo a matla ho ba ileng ba phela hara tsona. (Ba ileng ba shoa bona, ho tloha ka matsibolo a Maegepeta ho isa masoleng shoeletseng mpeng ea leoatle, Molimo o tla ba ahlola ka ho loka.) Joalokaha Molimo a itse: And those who died, from the first-born children in Egypt to the soldiers at the bottom of the sea, God will judge them fairly.) As the Lord said: “ ‘ “Le bone seo ke se entseng ho ba Egepeta, le kamoo ke le pepileng kateng, ka mapheo a ntsu, ka le tlisa ho ’na” ’ ” (Exod. 19:4,).

Ke hobaneng ha Morena a ile a etsa pholoso ee mahlonoko e hlollang, haele hantle, a ntša sechaba se seng hara se seng, kapa, joalokaha Moshe a ile a ba bolella: “ ‘ “Kapa o teng Moliom o mong o kileng oa leka ho tla inkela sechaba sa kahara sechaba se seng, ka meloko, le ka lipontšo, le ka mehlolo, le ka ntoa, le ka letsoho le matla, le ka letsoho le otlolohileng, Molimo oa lona a le etselitse tsohle tse joalo Egepeta, pela mahlo a lona na?” ’ ” (Deut. 4:34,)?

`Bala Exoda 19:4–8. Ke hobaneng Molimo a ile a ntša batho baa Egepeta?`

Hone hole bonolo joalo. Molimo o ile a ba ntša, peo, litloho-tloholoana tsa bo ntat’a bona, Aborahama, Isaka le Jakobo. ’Me Molimo o ile a thakhola selekane le litloholo tsena, tsona e n’e tlaba, “‘“letlotlo la ka la sebele hara lichaba tse ling kaofela, etsoe lefatše lohle e le laka”’” (Exod. 19:5,). Likamano tsena e n’e le khubu ea selekane.

Taba ena ea Molimo ea “letlotlo la sebele” (segullah), leha hole joalo, e kaba (’me ho no fela hole joalo) e sa utloisisehe. Ho khetheha ha bona ho ne ho sa tsoe ho letho le teng ka hare ho bona le ikhethileng le halalelang. Empa, e n’e le ka baka la mohau oa Molimo oo ba o filoeng le ka baka la linnete tse babatsehang tseo a ba fileng tsona, linnete tseo ba neng ba tlameha ho li latela, hape, joaloka “’muso oa baprista,” ba qeteletseng ba li phatlalletsa lefatše.

Joale eaba Molimo o ba fa tse ling tsa litaelo tsa selekane, (qetello ea bona ea tumellano, re ka rialo), Melao e leshome (Exoda 20), ’me selekane sena se ile sa tiisoa. Kamora ho fafatsa aletara e ncha ka mali a mahlabelo, Moshe o ile “a nka buka ea selekane, a e balla sechaba” (Exod. 24:7). Batho ba boela ba ikana ka hore ba tla mamela.

`“Hobane Moshe, enere mohlang a boleletseng sechaba kaofela litaelo tsohle tsa molao, a nka mali a manamane le a liphooko... ’me a foka buka le sechaba sohle ka tsona. Are, ‘ke ana mali a selekane seo Molimo o se laetseng bakeng sa lona’” (Baheb. 9:19, 20,). Mali a emetse eng, ’me ke hobaneng seo se le bohlokoa, le ho rona kajeno?`
