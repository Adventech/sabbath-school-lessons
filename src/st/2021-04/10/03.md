---
title:  Malebana le Matsasti a Fetileng
date:  29/11/2021
---

Ho Deuteronoma 4, re balile likhalemelo tse ntle tseo Jehova a li fileng batho ba hae ka Moshe malebana le menyetla e meholo eo ba nang le eona joaloka batho ba Molimo ba khethiloeng. O ne a ba lopolotse Egepeta “‘ka meloko, le ka lipontšo, le ka lipontšo, le ka mehlolo, le ka ntoa, le ka letsoho le matla, le ka letsoho le otlolohileng, le ka matsoho a maholo, le ka matsoho a maholo, joalokaha Molimo oa lona a le etselitse tsohle tse joalo Egepeta, pel’a mahlo a lona na?’” (Deut. 4:34). Ka mantsoe a mang, ha se hore feela Molimo o le etselitse ntho tse kholo feela, o li entse ka tsela tseo tse ka u thusang ho hopola, u se lebale, ntho tse kholo tseo a u etselitseng tsona.

`Bala Deuteronoma 4:32–39. Ke sefe seo Morena a neng a ba bolella ho hopola, ’me hobaneng ho ne ho le bohlokoa hore ba hopole ntho tsee?`

Moshe o supisa batho morao nalaneng eohle, le tlholehong ka bo eona, e be o oa ba botsa, potso seikarabi, ebang hona le ntho e tšoanang le ena eo ba e etselitsoeng e kileng ea etsahala nalaneng eohle. Haele hantle, o re ba botse; ke hore, hore ba ipatlele ka bo bona, hore na ntho e joalo e se e kile ea etsahala nalaneng. Ka ho ba botsa lipotso tse ’maloa, Moshe o ne a leka ho etsa hore ba bone seo Molimo a ba etselitseng sona, ’me ka hona, qetellong, ba itumela le ho leboha Molimo ha kaakang ka liketso tsa hae tse matla maphelong a bona.

Khubu ea liketso tsena e n’e le topollo Egepeta, ’me joale, mohlomong ka tsela tse ling tse makatsang ho feta, Ho buoa le bona Sinai, eleng se ba lumellang ho utloa “Mantsoe ea Molimo hara mollo.”

`Bala Deuteronoma 4:40. Ke qeto efe, joale, eo Moshe a neng a batla hore batho ba e utloe mantsoeng aa malebana le seo Molimo a ba etselitseng sona?`

Jehova h’a ka a etsa ntho tsena tsohle ntle le lebaka. O ne a pholositse batho ba hae, a boloka karolo ea hae ea selekane seo a neng a se thakhotse le bona. Ba ne ba lokolotsoe ho tsoa Egepeta, ba le mathuleng a ho kena Lefatše la Pallo. Molimo o ne a entse karolo ea hae; joale ba ne ba bitsetsoa ho etsa ea bona, e neng e le bonolo feela, ho mamela.

`Ke joang moralo oo o bontšang leano la topollo jolokaha le Testamenteng e Ncha? Jesu o entseng bakeng sa rona, ’me re ka arabela seo a re etselitseng sona joang? (Sheba Tsen. 14:12.)`
