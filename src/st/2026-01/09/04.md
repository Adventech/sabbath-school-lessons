---
title:  Leano la Molimo la Ka Ho Sa Feleng
date:   24/02/2026
---

`Bala Bakolose 1:24, 25. Paulose o reng ka ho sotleha hoa hae bakeng sa Kreste?`

Leha Paulose a ngotse Bakolose a koaletsoe ka tlung Roma, mohlomong ho sotleha ha hae ho hoholo ha hoa tlisoa ke ho sitoa ho sebetsa ka thata bakeng sena le sane, le ho kena ntlo le ntlo joalokaha a ne a etsa (Liketso 20:20). Matšoenyeho ana (kapa litlhoriso), tseo Kreste re hlokomelisitseng ka tsona (Matt. 24:9, Johanne 16:33), “ha a lokele ho lekanngoa le khanya e tlang ho bonahala ho rona” (Baro. 8:18). Sena ke setšoantšo se seholo. Joalokaha Paulose a ngoletse Bakreste ba Filipi, joale ho Bakolose o thabela matšoeneyeho a hae, a molemong oa bona (Bakol. 1:24).

Paulose a ka ba teronkong, empa “Lentsoe la Molimo lona ha lea tlangoa” (2 Tim. 2:9). Nakong ea Paulose a leng mona litlamong, Bafilipi, Baefese, le Filemone li bile tsa ngoloa. Kamora ho lokolloa, Molimo O ile A mo fa tšusumetso ea ho ngola likeletso tsa bohlokoa tse fumanoang ho 1 Timothea le Tite. Eaba, nakong ea ho koalloa hoa hae la ho qetela teronkong ea Roma, a ngola Timothea o bobeli. Ka bokhutšoanyane, lilemo tsena tsa ho qetela li file Paulose monyetla oa ho ngola karolo e kholo ea Testamente e Ncha, eo e tlamehang e kenyeletsa le Baheberu.

Leano la Molimo la ka ho sa feleng le ile la bona tsena le tse ling tse ngata. Lentsoe la Segerike le sebelisoang ke Paulose ho Bakolose 1:25, leo tolokoang e le “boqhoku,” ke oikonomia. Ha le sebelisoe haholo (mohlala, ho, 1 Tim. 1:4), le bontša “tsela eo Molimo A etsang lintho ka eona.”—Luke Timothy Johanneson, The First and Second Letters to Timothea (New York: Doubleday, 2001), Leq. 164. Sena se ka kenyeletsa boapostola ba Paulose. Empa ka moelelo o pharaletseng, le kenyeletsa tsohle tseo Molimo A faneng ka tsona leanong la topollo. Bosebeletsi ba Paulose, ba Apostola ba bang, le baporofeta ba Testamente ea Khale (Baef. 2:20, Baef. 3:5), ho kenyeletsa Moshe, tsohle li ne li reretsoe “ho isa Lentsoe la Molimo ka hohle” (Bakol. 1:25), sohle ka sepheo sa ho phetha leano lena le halalelang.

Leha re tla sheba sehlooho sena ka botebo thutong ea hosane, ho bohlokoa hore re lemohe hore Paulose o nkile bosebeletsi ba hae e le karoloana e nyenyane ea leano le halalelang le qalileng ho kenngoa tšebetsong “ho tloha pele lefatše le thehoa” (Matt. 13:35, Baef. 1:4).

`Nahana ka bophelo ba hao. Ke joang liqeto tseo u li etsang (tse kholo le tse nyenyane) li ka kenellang leanong le leholo la Molimo? Na re ka fela ra tseba hore na qeto e “nyenyane”? Ke joang see se ka bang le litlamorao tse kholo tse tla bonahala hamorao?`
