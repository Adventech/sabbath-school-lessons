---
title:  Hopola
date:   14/10/2025
---

`Bala Joshua 4. Hobaneng Molimo A ile A re Baiseraele ba ahe sehopotso?`

Sepheo sa majoe ana ke ho ba “letšoao o o.” Lentsoe la Seheberu ’ot hangata le amahanngoa le lentsoe “meeka” ’me le ka bontša ketso tsa mohlolo tse etsoang ke Molimo (sheba thuto ea maobane), joaloka likotlo holima Egepeta (Exoda 7:3, Deut. 4:34). Le ka boela la nka moelelo oa “letšoao o o” e le letšoaola kantle la ’nete e holimo-limo. Mohlala, mookoli ke “letšoao o o” la selekane (Gen. 9:12, 13); mali likoseneng tsa matlo a Baisraele a bitsoa “letšoao o o” (Exoda 12:13); ka holimo ho tsohle, Sabatha ke “letšoao o o” la tlholeho le la boteng ba khalalelo ea Molimo (Exoda 31:13, 17; Ezek. 20:12).

Mona, letšoaole sebetsa e le sehopotso, ho hopotsa meloko e tlang ka ho tšela ka mohlolo. Lentsoe Sehopotso (zikkaron) le tsoa lentsoeng zakar, “ho hopola,” se bontšang se fetang ketso e nthithi feela ea ho hopola se itseng. E bontša ho hopola ho lateloang ke ketso e nepahetseng (Deut. 5:15, Deut. 8:2). Ho aha majoe a sehopotso (Gen. 28:18–22) le tšebeletso tse neng li tlisa lipotso (Exoda 12:26, 27; Deut. 6:20–25) ho ne ho tloaelehile Testamanteng ea Khale. Ho e na le ho pheta mehlolo khafekhafetsa, Molimo o etsa liemahale tse tlisang khopotso ea ketso tsa Hae tse kholo le ho thokola likarabo tse utloahalang. Ka hona, letšoaole lokela ho ba teng “ka ho sa feleng,” ho bontša bohloki ba ho boloka mohlolo ona oa Jehova hara batho ba Hae ka ho sa feleng.

Potso e ka tlang melokong e mecha e bohlokoa hobane e e bopetsoe motho ka mong: “Majoe aa areng ho uena?” Moloko o mong le o mong o mocha o lokela ho thuisa le ho utloisisa se boleloang ke majoe ana ho motho ka mong. Tumelo ho Molimo o etsang mehlolo e ka lula e tuka ha fela moloko o mong le o mong o lemoha bohlokoa ba ketso tse matla tsa Yahweh bakeng sa bona. Tumelo e joalo e tla tlisa phapang e kholo pakeng tsa ho phela meetlo ea Bibele, le semoeetlo, bolumeli bo shoeleng e senang boleng le ’moko ea moloko o phelang kajeno. Qetellong, re hloka ho etsa tumelo e thomiloeng Bibeleng ea rona. Ha hona mang kapa mang, haholo- holo baholo-holo ba rona, ea ka lumelang bakeng sa rona.

`Tse ling tsa lihopotso, tsa hao o le motho, leetong la hao le Molimo tse u thusang ho hopola tseo a u etselitseng tsona ke li fe?`
