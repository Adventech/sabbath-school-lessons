### Bakeng sa Thuto ea Beke ena Bala
1 Bakorinthe 8; Liketso 15:20; 1 Bakor. 9:1–6; 1 Bakor. 10:5–22; Deut. 6:4, 5; Mareka 12:28–31.

> <p>Temana ea khopolo</p>
> “Empa leha le e ja leha le e noa, leha le etsa ntho efe kapa efe, etsang tsohle ho tlotlisa Molimo ka tsona” (1 Bakorinthe 10:31).

Bakorinthe ba pele 8–10 e tlisa taba ea thobalano sehlohlolong (khaolong ea 5–6) e bile e lelekella likarabo tsa Pauluse lipotsong tse ikhethang tse botsitsoeng ka lengolo (1 Bakor. 7:1) ke Bakorinthe. Likarabo tsena li tla karolo e kholo e setseng ea Bakorinthe ba pele.

Mokhoa oa ho fetela maemong a latelang oa 1 Bakorinthe 7 o bontša hore bohlola (khaolo tsa 5–7) le khumamelo ea litšoantšo (khaolo tsa 8–10) li’a amana. Ehlile, hangata ho buuoa ka tsona li le peli Testamenteng e Ncha (sheba Liketso 15:20, 29; Liketso 21:25; 1 Bakor. 6:9; Baef. 5:5; Bakol. 3:5; Tšen. 21:8; Tsen. 22:15).

Ka kakaretso, ha ho 1 Bakorinthe 5–7 Pauluse a sebetsana le bothata ba bofebe, ho 1 Bakorinthe 8–10 khathatseho ea hae e kholo ke taba ea borapeli ba litšoantšo. O re Bakreste ba lokela ho li baleha li le peli (1 Bakor. 6:18, 1 Bakor. 10:14).

Bekeng e fetileng, re bone hore ka ho ba tempele ea Moea O Halalelang (1 Bakor. 6:19, 20), ke joalo motho a ka balehang bofebe. Bekeng ena, re tla bona hore motho a ka baleha khumamelo ea litšoantšo ka ho “etsa sohle tlotlisong ea Molimo” (1 Bakor. 10:31).

_Ithute thuto ea beke ena ho lokisetsa Sabatha, Phato 1._
