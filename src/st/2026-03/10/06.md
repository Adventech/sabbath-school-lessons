---
title:  Khothalo le Thabo
date:   03/09/2026
---

`Bala 2 Bakorinthe 7. Maikutlo a Paulose ke a eng ha a uttloa hore Bakorinthe ba sokolohile?`

Ke larato le le kae le utloahalang polelong “le lipelong tsa rona” (2 Bakr. 7:3; bona hape 2 Bakr. 6:11). Takatsong ena e tebileng ea hore lerato la hae le qhatsetsanoe, Paulose o boetse o re, “Re buleleng lipelo tsa lona” (2 Bakr. 7:2). Ha mantsoe “ka lipelong” a sa totobala mongolong oa Segerike, boholo ba liphetolelo sa sekhooa li na le ona, e leng se nepahetseng hobane ke ona moelelo.

Ruri, Bakorinthe ba etse ba buletse Paulose le bo mphato’ae lipelo tsa bona. Ke ka hona temana ea 4 e leng khaphatseho ea thabo. Polelo tsa Paulose li bontsa kamoo boikutlo ba hae bo leng monate lekhatheng lena: “Ke le tsepile haholo, ke ikotla sefuba ka lona, thabo ea ka eea khaphatseha. Matsoenyehong ohle a rona, ke ntse ke tletse tsepo” (2 Bakr. 7:4). Paulose o khothetse o tletse Tsepo le thabo. Ke hakae kereke tsa rona li ka tlisetsang baruti ba tsona leboso le thabo kaho itahlela ka setotsoana ho Kreste.

Ho 2 Bakorinthe 7:5–16, Paulose o tsoela apele ho malosa mabaka a khothalo ea hae le thabo. Tsena tse peli ke tsona boholo sekheng sena. Kutu-ketso _parakaleo_ (“ho thaba”) kapa lereho _paraklesis_ (“thabo”) le hlahella makhetlo a supileng kaoela ho 2 Bakorinthe 7. Karolo ena ea lengolo e qetella ka tsela eo e qalileng ka eona, eleng, ka motlotlo o moholo ho Molimo (2 Bakr. 1:3–7). Thabo ea Paulose ho 2 Bakorinthe 7 e tsoaloa ke tokoloho eo a bileng le eona ka lebaka la lengolo le bohale leo a neng a le entse le soettse litholoana tse hlabosang.

Leha khothalo ena e tlisitsoe ke tlaleho ea Tite e monate, qetellong, Molimo ke Eena monga khothalo ea Pulose (2 Bakr. 7:6). Molimo ruri ke “Molimo oa matseliso ohle, ea re tselisang le matsoenyeong ohle” (2 Bakr. 1:3, 4).

Se monate, le hoja Paulose a “tletse matseliso,” o “thabile ka ho fetisisa” (2 Bakr. 7:4, 7, 13). Leha lengolo la hae le bohloko le ne le bakile masoabi a maholo, e ne le masoabi ka lebaka la thato ea Molimo a reretsoeng pako (2 Bakr. 7:9–11). Bakorinthe ba “soabile kamoo Molimo a ratang” (2 Bakr. 7:11), masoabi a tlisang “pako e isang pholohong” (2 Bakr. 7:10). Ke eng se neng se ka tlisa thabo e fetang ena pelong ea moruti oa Molimo?

`Na u kile oa ba le masoabi a bo-Molimo bophelong ba hao? U ile oa tseba joang hore e ne le masoabi a batloang ke thato ea Molimo ho isa pakong?`
