Ellen G. White, “The Message Heeded,” lq. 323–334, ho The Acts of the Apostles.

Beke e fetileng, re balile sekha sena se qotsutsoeng ho The Acts of the Apostles. Ekare se ka baloa hape. Tebisa kepi likarolong tse buang ka lengolo la Paulose le bohale, boikutlo ba hae nakong eo a neng a le ngola, le thabo ea ha’a amohela tlaleho ea litaba tse monate tsa pako ea ‘nete ho baamoheli ba lona. Joale, nahana ka se ka boleloang ka bo’nete ba bosebeletsi ba Paulose le lithuto tseo re ka li hapellang bosebeletsing ba rona bakeng sa Kreste.

“Re lokela ho senolela lefatse le oeleng le libopuoa tse sa oelang, hore poelano e teng ho Molimo, hore ka baka la lerato la Molimo re ka boelanngoa le Eena. Ha motho a baka, a hloeka pelong, a lumela ho Kreste joalo ka sehlabelo se hloekisang, ‘me a lemoha hore Molimo o boelane le eena.”—Ellen G. White, Special Testimonies On Education, lq. 223.

“Re le kereke, re se re amohetse leseli le lengata. Lesli lena Molimo o re neile lona molemong oa ho hlohonoloatsa lefatse. Re neiloe borumuoa ba poelano. Ka matla a tsoang holimo re lokela ho qeka batho hore ba khutlele ho Molimo.”—Ellen G. White, Letter 32, 1903.

Hoba batho khutlele ho Molimo, ba tla batlana le khalalelo. Haa bua ka 2 Bakorinthe 7:1, Ellen G. White o hlalosa seo Paulose a se bolelan ka “ho phethahala khalalelong, re ntse re tsaba Molimo” (2 Bakr. 7:1). O re Paulose o na batla ho thusa basokolloa ba bacha “ho fihlela boikemelo, ha hola Bokresteng, ho ba matla tumelong, ho ba le cheseho, ho ithukhubetsa ka lipelo tsohle tlhonolofalong ho Molimo le bosebeletsing kholisong ea ‘muso oa Hae.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 201.

**Lipotso tsa Ho Qoqoa:**

`Paulose o re reka “lipitsa tsa letsopa” tse t etseng letlotlo la evangeli (2 Bakr. 4:7). Ke joang taba ea hore boemo ba motho bo mefokolo, bo selehe bo tletse likhaello bo ka matlafatsang ho ena le ho fokolisa evangeli?`

`Ho bolelang ho ba “‘mopuoa e mocha” (2 Bakr. 5:17)? Sena se ka ama joang maphelo a rona a ka mehla? Kreste o u entse joang ‘mopuoa e mocha?`

`Ho 2 Bakorinthe 6:4, 5, Paulose o fana ka lethathamo le lelelele la liholotso tsa evangeli. O arabela oang qholotsong tsee (bona 2 Bakr. 6:6, 7)? Sena se u thusa joang ho arabela liqholotsong tsa hao?`

`Paulose o bapisa masoabi a bomolimo le masoabi a lefat e (2 Bakr. 7:10). Ke joang masoabi a ka amanang le pako? Uka hlalosa joang masoabi a bomolimo le masoabi a lefat e?`
