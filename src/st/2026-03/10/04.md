---
title:  Borumuoa Ba Kreste Ba Poelano
date:   01/09/2026
---

`Bala 2 Bakorinthe 5:11–15. Sekha se se bontsa joang hore borumuoa ba Paulose ke ba Kreste?`

Paulose o ne a tseba hore ho ne ho lokela hore a nke boikarabello bosebeletsing ba hae ho Kreste (2 Bakr. 5:10). O ne a tseba “tsabo ho Morena” 'me a batla ho khothaletsa batho ka evangeli ea Kreste (2 Bakr. 5:11). Tsabo ena ke tlhompho le tsabo ho Kreste 'me, ka hona, e kopantsoe le lerato la Paulose bakeng sa Kreste le tsepo leratong la Kreste ho eena. Selekaneng sa Khale, ho tšaba Morena ho bolela ho tsamaea ka litsela tsa hae, ho mo rata, le ho mo sebeletsa ka pelo ea hau eohle le moea oa hau ohle (Deut. 10:12).

Bosebeletsi ba Paulose ha bo a shebana le eena empa bo shebane le Kreste. Ha a ithoke. Lebaka la boikonko ba hae ke Kreste (2 Bakr. 12:9). O itse, “Empa ke se ithorise ka letho haese ka sefapano sa Morena oa rona Jesu Kreste” (Gal. 6:14). Kahoo, monyetla oa Bakorinthe oa ho ikhantša ka eena (2 Bakr. 5:12) o bolela motlotlo ka bosebeletsi ba Kreste ho fapana le bosebeletsing bo shebaneng le boikhabo ba lira tsa hae. Bagl 6:14).

`Bala 2 Bakorinthe 5:16–21, Bakolosi 1:19–23, le Baefesi 2:13–16. Paulose o na bolelang ka “bosebeletsi ba poelano”?`

Kreste ke mampoli oa poelano. Ka hona, O “re file mosebetsi oa ho boelanya” (2 Bakorinthe 5:18). Mohopolo oa khotsofalo o hlahella khafetsa bukeng ea 2 Bakorinthe 5:16–21. Ona ke mohopolo o hlokahalang ho Paulose, mme le rona re lokela ho o boloka le ho o utloisisa.

Molimo o boelantse batho ka sebele ka lefu la Mora oa Hae le hlasoang sebe. Bao e leng ba boelantsoeng le Molimo ke libopuoa tse ncha (2 Bakorinthe 5:17). Hona joale, ba lokela ho phatlalatsa “molaetsa ona oa poelano” ka ho phatlalatsa evangeli ea Kreste (2 Bakorinthe 5:19). Ka tsela ena, “re maqosa a Kreste, joaloka ha Molimo a le kopa ka rona” (2 Bakorinthe 5:20).

`Nahana ka seo Kreste a o etselitseng sona. Nahana molato, sebe, sekhobo seo e ka beng e le sa hao ha ene ese ka lebaka la seo a se entseng seapanong. ‘Nete ee ke joang eka amang likamano tsa hao le ba bang, haholo ba ntseng ba eso mo tsebe?`
