### Bakeng sa Thuto ea Beke ena Bala
2 Bakr. 3:1–9, 2 Bakr. 4:7–18, 2 Bakr. 5:11–15, Bakl. 1:19–23, Eph. 2:13–16, 2 Bakr. 6:11–7:1, 2 Bakorinthe 7.

> <p>Temana ea khopolo</p>
> “Re patisoa ka nqa tsohle, empa ha re bjaramane; rea tsieleha, empa ha re nyahame lipelo; rea hlorisoa, empa ha re furalloe ke bohle; re ratlanngoa fatse, empa ha re pshatlehe; kamehla re jere mahloko a lefu la Jesu ‘meleng ea rona, hore bophelo ba Hae bo tle bo bonahatsoe le 'meleng ea rona” (2 Bakorinthe 4:8–10).

Bekeng e fetileng, re bone kamoo Paulose, ka ho tsitlallela bonolo le ho tsitsa saleng, o ila itsireletsa liqosong tsa hoba leqitolo le ho hloka lerato ho Bakorinthe. O na sebelitse ka boitelo molemong oa bana ba hae ba semoea. A qala letoto la mehopolo e ho 2 Bakorinthe 2:12–17 ho ea ho 2 Bakorinthe 7. Ka ho etsa joalo, o bonaletsa seo bosebeletsi ba sebele ba Kreste bo leng sona. Re ka fumana thuto tse ‘maloa mehopolong ena ea Paulose.

Beke ena, re tla sheba 2 Bakorinthe 3–7, moo Paulose a buang ka bosebeletsi ba hae ba ho hapela Kreste meea. Ellen G. White o re, “Tsokoloho ea baetsalibe le khalaletso ea bona ka 'nete ke bopaki bo matla haholo boo moefangeli a ka bang le bona hore Molimo o mo bitselitse ho sebeletsa. Bopaki ba boapostola ba hae bo ngotsoe lipelong tsa bao ba sokolohileng, 'me bo bontšoa ke maphelo a bona a nchafalitsoeng. Kreste o hlaha ka hare, tšepo ea khanya. Mosebeletsi o pakoa matla haholo ke litholoana tsa tšebetso ea hae.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, lq. 328.

_Ithute thuto ea beke ena ho lokisetsa Sabatha, Loetse 5._
