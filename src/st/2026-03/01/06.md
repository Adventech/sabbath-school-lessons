---
title:  Mangolo a Pauluse ho Bakorinthe
date:   02/07/2026
---

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 1:11–13; 1 Bakorinthe 4:14; 1 Bakorinthe 5:11; 1 Bakorinthe`

7:1; le 1 Bakorinthe 14:37, 40. Hape le 2 Bakorinthe 1:12, 2 Bakorinthe 2:9, 2 Bakorinthe 11:3, le 2 Bakorinthe 13:10. Ke joang litemana tsee li re thusang ho utloisisa hore na hobaneng Pauluse a ngoletse Bakorinthe mangolo aa?

Pauluse o ne a le Efese ha a ngola Bakorinthe ba pele (1 Bakor. 16:5–9). Lelapa la Klooe la fihla ho eena ka tlaleho ea hore ntho ha li tsamae hantle morao koana Korinthe (1 Bakor. 1:11). Ho 1 Bakorinthe 1–6, Pauluse o buoa ka litaba tse tlisitsoeng ke Klooe. Mathata a ne a akha, likarohano, ho qosanoa, le bohlola. Pauluse o ne a boetse a fumane lengolo le nang le lipotso tse otlolohileng (1 Bakor. 7:1). Karabo ea hae e nka boholo ba ho tloha khaolong ea bosupa ho ea pele. Lipotso li ne li le malebana le lenyalo, ho hlala, ho nena thobalano ka baka la tumelo, lijo tse hlabetsoeng melingoana, boitšoaro khumamelong, tšebeliso ea lineo tsa Moea, le kutloisiso e mpe ea tsoho. Kereke ea Korinthe e n’e sokolisa e sa hole. Mohlomong kereke ea hao ea lehae e na le mathata a mangata. Empa ea Korinthe e ne le pele.

Lengolo la pele la Pauluse ho Bakorinthe le bohlokoa haholo nakong tsa rona ha joale le rona. Ka holimo ho tsohle, na ha se rona bohle, ka tsela e itseng, re tobanang le le ntho tse tšoanang kerekeng tsa rona kajeno? Lengolo lena le na le ntho tse ngata tseo le ka re rutang tsona. Ke le leng la “mangolo a nonneng, a tletseng thuto, le matla ho feta boholo ba mangolo a hae.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, leq. 301.

Mohlomong Pauluse a ka be a ngoletse Bakorinthe mangolo a mararo kapa a mane (bapisa le 2 Bakor. 10:9). O ngotse lengolo pele ho 1 Bakorinthe (1 Bakor. 5:9), empa le lahlehile. Pele ho Bakorinthe ba bobeli, o ngola leo libali-bali li reng ke “lengolo le bohale” (2 Bakor. 2:3, 4, 9; 2 Bakor. 7:8), empa le lona le lahlehile. Bang ba nahana hore e ka nna eaba o buoa ka Bakorinthe ba pele, kapa mohlomong karolo ea lengolo lena e atlehile ho bolokeha ho Bakorinthe ba bobeli.

Ho tloha ho Bakorinthe ba bobeli, re lemoha hore litho tsa Korinthe li ne li hahlametsoe hampe ke meetlo e li pota-potileng. Ba ne ba rata ntho tse kang ho hlolisana, matla, le borui, eleng ntho tse ka qholotsang kereke ea rona le kejeno. Ka nqe’ngoe, Pauluse o ne a batla ho etsa tloaelo ea ho tsepamisa maikutlo ho Jesu, tsela ea ho bona lefatše ka khalase ea evangeli. Ho bohlokoa ha ka kang hore le rona, re bone lefatše la rona ka khalalse ea eavangeli.

`Bala 2 Bakorinthe 2:4 hape. Seo se re bolellang malebana le hore na Pauluse o ne a tsotella batho hakae? Ho fapana le seo, ke joang pelo tsa rona li ka rekolohelang ho ba bang?`
