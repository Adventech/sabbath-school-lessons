---
title:  Tlholo ea Ho Qetela Holima Lefu
date:   20/08/2026
---

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 15:54–57. Seratsoana see se re bolell’ang malebana le tlholo ea rona ho qetela ea lefu?`

Pauluse o qala seratsoana sa ho qetela ho 1 Bakorinthe 15 ka polelo e thahasellisang: “Nama le mali li ke ke tsa ja lefa la ’muso oa Molimo” (1 Bakor. 15:50). Bongata ba babali ba Bibele ba sebelisa polelo ena ho emela ntlha ea hore Pauluse o sireletsa ho phela ha moea fela leholimong. Empa maemo mona aa ba latola. Nyallano ea 1 Bakorinthe 15:50 e hlahisa hore “nama le mali” li tšoana hantle le “ho bola,” bo kang “’muso oa Molimo” o nyallana le “ho se bole.” Hantle joaloka ha ho etsahetse ho 1 Bakorinthe 15:42–49, le hona Pauluse o bapisa ’mele oa hona joale (kapa sona setopo) le ’mele o tsositsoeng bafung. ’Mele o tsetetsoeng/patuoeng o bonoa ka ho bola le ho shoa, ha ’mele o tsositsoeng bafung o bonoa ka ho se bolele ho se shoe (1 Bakor. 15:50, 53, 54). Re ka re, Pauluse o re ’mele ea rona e lokela ho feta hara phetoho e kholo hore e tle ruhe leholimo.

Ka bokhutšoane, Pauluse o sebelisa taba ea ho bola le ho shoa ho bontva tlhao ea rona ea sebe. Lingoloeng tsa Sejuda, “nama le mali” ke polelo e buoang ka batho ba oeleng, ke ka hona ’mele ea rona e lokelang ho fetoloa e hloekisoe limpe tsohle mohla a khutlang.

Ke fela ha tlhaho ea rona ea sebe e tlositsoe (1 Bakor. 15:54) ’me re tsamaeang hara maiphihlelo a khalaletso (1 Bakr. 15:51–53, 1 Bathes. 4:13–17) moo hoo oa hore “‘Lefu le metsitsoe ke hlolo’” (1 Bakor. 15:54) e tla phethahala. E be, sefela sena se sa itšabelleng sea binoa:

“‘Na lefu, tsenene ea hao e kae na? Uena lebitla ho hlola ha hao ho kae na?’” (1 Bakr. 15:55). Tsena tsohle li tla etsahala mohla Kreste A khutlang labobeli (1 Bakr. 15:51, 52).

E nahane: re koala mahlo a rona lefung, e be ha rea bula re bona ho tla ha Jesu khetlo la bobeli, ha A re tsosa bafung, ho sa natsoe hore na molumeli o shoele neng, le haeba ke likete-kete tsa lilemo tse fetileng, “hang, ka ho panya ha leihlo,” ba tla phelisoa “ka terompeta ea morao; hobane terompeta e tla lla, ’me bafu ba tla tsoha, ba se ke ba boela ba bola, ’me le rona re tla fetoha” (1 Bakor. 15:52).

`Ke mang ea e-so utloisoe bohloko ke kamoo bophelo bo fetang kapele kateng? Maiphihlelong a rona, ho tla labobeli ha Jesu ho tla bonahala ho le kapele joalo. Mohlomong monahano oa rona oa pele mohlang a khutlang e tla ba “Khele! Morena, kannete U tlile kapele!” ke joang taba ee e re thusang thusang ho amohela se bonahalang e ka ke “ho lieha”?`
