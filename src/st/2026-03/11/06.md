---
title:  Kopano
date:   10/09/2026
---

Se re bone hore Paulose o khothaletsa litho Korinthe ho kenya letsoho letloleng la likereke tse hlophehileng tse Judea. Sepheo se seng sa ha eke ho aha moea oa bonngoe. O batla ba phehhise, ba be le seabo thomong. O rata ho bonahhale hore likereke ttsa balichaba ke karolo ea lelapa le le leng la Molimo ‘moho le balumeli ba Bajuda ba Jerusalema. Ke hohre, battho ba na, bao ekileng ea ba basele, ruri joale ke baena ‘moho selekaneng se seha sa Masala a Molimo. Paulose o battla ho bona lelapa lohle la Bokreste, Bauda le Balichaba, ba kopane ka tsela e ka sieang mohlala o matla bakeng sa kereke melokong e tlang.

Tite le baena ba bang ba babeli ba tseptjoang ba ne ba ikarabella matlotlong. Molimo a bea tlhokomelo ena ea kereke ea Hae pelong ea Tite (2 Bakr. 8:16). Ka likereke, Molimo A boetse a khetha baena ba babeli (2 Bakr. 8:18–23). Ba bitsoa “baromuoa ba likereke, tlotlisong ea Kreste” (2 Bakr. 8:23). Hore na “tlotlisong ea Kreste” e bolela baena bana ba tsepahalang kapa likereke bo tsona, ha se lebaka. Phano ea linyehelo qetellong ke letsoao la tlotliso ho Kreste, hlooho ea kereke (Eph. 4:15).

Ea bobeli Bakorinthe 8–9 e bonts’a hore linyehelo li lokela ho neoa ba khethiloeng ke Molimo ka kereke. Mantsoe “Likereke tsohle” (2 Bakr. 8:18 “bakhethoa ba likereke” (2 Bakr. 8:19), le “baromuoa ba likereke” (2 Bakr. 8:23) ke sona se boleloang. Ke hore, polelo e latelang ha e makatse, “ba bontseng lerato la lona” (2 Bakr. 8:24).

Ho tlisa linyehelo kerekeng—sesebelisoa sa Molimo leats’eng—ho totisoa bonngoe, hape ke letsoao la kopano (2 Bakr. 8:13, 14). Chelete e ka bopa bonngoe. Ka lehlakoreng le leng, haeba batho ba sa shebele lintho ‘moho tlotlisong ea Molimo, chelete e ka boela ea baka likarohano.

Ke joang Baroma 15:26, 27 e senolang takatso ea Paulose ea bonngoe moo?

Qetellong, Paulose o bua ka pokello eo joalo ka Tsebeletso kapa bosebeletsi, ketso ea mohau, tlhoholonofatso, ketso ea borapeli, le kopano ea lerato. Tsohle tsena ho tsoa nyehelong? Nahana ka sena.

`Nyehelo ea rona likerekeng tse ling le borumuoeng mose ho maoatle, hangata le libakeng tse hole, e thusa joang ho tlisa bonngoe kerekeng ka lefat e lohle?`
