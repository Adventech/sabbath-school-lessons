`Bala 2 Bakorinthe 8:1, 5 le 2 Bakorinthe 9:7, 9, 13, 15. Khubu ea molaetsa oo ke efeng?`

Puo ea ho ana e tletse 2 Bakorinthe 8 le 9: “Mohau oa Molimo... o fanoe” (2 Bakr. 8:1); “Li iphile Molimo” (2 Bakr. 8:5); “E mong le e mong a fane kamoo a lokisitseng pelong ea hae... hobane Molimo o rata motho ea fanang a thabile” (2 Bakr. 9:7); “O fana ho mafutsana” (2 Bakr. 9:9); “Ba ttla tlotlisa Molimo ka hobane... le ana le sa khanane” (2 Bakr. 9:13); “Liteboho ho Molimo ka mpho ea hae e senang ho lekanngoa!” (2 Bakr. 9:15). 2 Bakorinthe 8 le 9 li qala le ehho qetella ka puo ea ho fana (2 Bakr. 8:1 hape 2 Bakr. 9:15). Re lokela ho bala khaolo tsena ka kutloisiso ea ho bala likelellong. Bonyane li fana ka mabaka a mane a ho fana ka linyehelo tsa rona.

**Teboho ka mohau oa Molimo** (2 Bakr. 8:1; 2 Bakr. 9:14,15). 2 Bakorinthe 8–9 e lelekela ka “mohau oa Molimo” (2 Bakr. 8:1). Ha u theosa, Paulose o re, “Le ttseba ohau oa Morena oa rona Jesu Kreste” (2 Bakr. 8:9). Mohau oa Molimo le Kreste o hlahisoa e le lebaka la mantlha la ho nyehela. Molimo o re etselitse se seholo ka ho re fa Kreste. Ka ho nyehela, re ananela mohau oa Molimo maphelong a rona.

Joalo ka ho fana, lentsoe “mohau” (se-Greek, _charis_) le lona le hlaha hangata 2 Bakorinthe 8–9. Ka ho tsoana, lentsoe _charis_ le hlahella qalong le qetellong ea likha tsena (2 Bakr. 8:1; 2 Bakr. 9:14, 15). Paulose o sebelisa lentsoe lena meelelong e fapaneng ka hara sekha ho hlalosa hore mohau oa Kreste maphelong a rona o tsoala mohau ho ba bang le teboho.

**Lerato la ho etsisa mohlala oa Jesu** (2 Bakr. 8:9). Jesu o ne a ruile a khetha ho futsaneha (hopola hore hona ke lipapiso ka boteng ba hae bo senang qalo le ho tsoaloa e le motho). Tsela e ‘ngoe feela ea ketsahalo ena: O tetse sohle. Rona, ka ho arolelana linyehelo, re bula tsela tsa ho phatlalatsa Kreste.

**Lerato la ho phatlalatsa tlhohonolofatso tsa Molimo** (2 Bakr. 9:10, 11). Re fana ho ba bang hobane re fuoe pele ke Molimo. O re abetse hore re tle re be liabi.

**Lerao la Sebele** (2 Bakr. 8:8, 24). Ho fana ke teko ea lerato la ‘nete le sebele. Ke bopaki bo ts’oarehang bah ore lerato le teng pelong. Ts’ebeliso ea maelana a Sekhooa ea re ke ho “bea chelete ea hao haufi le molomo oa hao.”

`U fana hakae? Ha u bapisa le seapano, u fana hakae papisong le seo u neng uka se fana?`
