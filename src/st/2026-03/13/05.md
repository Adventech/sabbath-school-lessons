---
title:  Kopano ea Moea o Halalelang
date:   23/09/2026
---

Mohau oa Jesu ha o senole feela lerato la Molimo ho rona, empa o boela o tlisa kopano ea Moea o Halalelang e le sephetho se eketsehileng sa lerato leo. Ka nako e tšoanang, kopano ena ea Moea e tsoa leratong la Molimo. ’Me sena ke hobane ntle le lerato, ha ho kopano. Joalo ka ha Paulose a ngola: “Ka baka leo, ekare ha tšeliso e le teng ho Kreste, ha ho le khothatso ea lerato, ha ho le kopano ea Moea, ha ho le lereko le mehauhelo, le ke le phethise thabo ea ka, ka ho ba le khopolo e le ’ngoe, le lerato le le leng, le moea o le mong, le ho ikutloa ho le hong feela” (Phil. 2:1, 2, NKJV).

Batho ba bang ba re Moea o Halalelang ke matla kapa tšusumetso feela, empa seo e ke ke ea ba ’nete. Ha e le hantle, hobaneng Paulose a ka bua ka batho ba babeli—Ntate le Mora—hammoho le “matla” feela ka mokhoa oa boraro bo bong? Hoo ha ho etse kelello. Joalo ka ha Ntate le Mora ba bontšitsoe ka kamano ea botho (2 Bakr. 1:3, 2 Bakr. 11:31), kamano ea Moea le batho e re isa qetong ea hore le eena ke motho (Barom. 8:15, 16; bona hape Joh. 14:16, 17, 26; Joh. 15:26).

Poleloana ea “kopano ea Moea” (Ba-Fil. 2:1) e ka utloisisoa ka litsela tse peli. E ka bolela kopano ea ba bang e fanoeng ke Moea, kapa kopano le Moea ka bo oona. Bafetoleli ba Bibele ba ’maloa ba bolela hore kutloisiso tsena ha li fapane ka ho felletseng. Kamoo ho nkoang, kamohelo ea ba bang e lateloa ke kamohelo le Moea.

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 2:10, 11; 1 Bakorinthe 3:16; 1 Bakorinthe 12:11; le 2 Bakorinthe 3:6, 17. Paulose o rutile eng ho Bakorinthe ka Moea?`

Paulose o bua haholo ka mosebetsi oa Moea o Halalelang. Ho 1 le 2 Bakorinthe, ho na le litšupiso tse fetang mashome a mane ho Moea o Halalelang. Moea o Halalelang o: khothaletsa kholo ea kereke (1 Bakr. 14:12), nolofalletsa batho mosebetsi oa thomo (1 Bakr. 2:4, 5), O senola liphiri tse tebileng tsa Molimo ho rona (1 Bakr. 2:10, 11), 'me O re ruta tsona (1 Bakr. 2:13), O lula ho rona (1 Bakr. 3:16; 1 Bakr. 6:19), O sebetsa le Kreste bakeng sa toka ea rona (1 Bakr. 6:11), O fa kereke lineo tsa semoea (1 Bakorinthe 12–14), O re tiisa bakeng sa pholoho (2 Bakr. 1:22), O kenya molao ka lipelong tsa batho (2 Bakr. 3:3), O fana ka bophelo bo bocha ho Kreste (2 Bakr. 3:6), le tokelo ea ho lokolloa sebeng (2 Bakr. 3:17).

Ruri, re ke ke ra phela ntle le Moea o Halalelang.

`Hobaneng, hape, kutloisiso ea bomolimo ba Moea o Halalelang bo le bohlokoa ho utloisisa ka ho phethahala lerato la Molimo bakeng sa r na?`
