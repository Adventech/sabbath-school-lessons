---
title:  Molimo oa Rona oa Boraro-bo-bong
date:   24/09/2026
---

Ha motho a bala 2 Bakorinthe 13:14, a ka nahana hore Kreste ke eena feela mohloli oa mohau, Molimo ke eena feela mohloli oa lerato, ’me Moea o Halalelang ke eena feela mohloli oa kopano — empa ha ho letho le ka bang hole le ’nete ho feta seo.

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 1:3, 4, 9; 1 Bakorinthe 10:16; 2 Bakorinthe 1:2, 12; Baroma 8:35; Baroma 15:30; Ba-Galata 2:20; le Ba-Efese 3:19. Litemana tsee li reng ka mohau, lerato, le kopano malebana le boraro bo bong?`

Ntate, Mora, le Moea o Halalelang ba sebetsa ’moho bakeng sa pholoho ea rona. Mohau, lerato le kopano ha li tsoe ho e mong feela, empa li tsoa ho bona ba bararo. Leha ho le joalo, e mong le e mong o na le mosebetsi o itseng nalaneng ea pholoho. Paulose o tseba sena ’me o totobatsa thuto ena mangolong a hae. Ka mohlala, morero oa pholoho o hlalositsoe ka bokhutšoanyane bo makatsang ho Bagalata 4:4–6, moo litho tse tharo tsa Bomolimo li nkang karolo. Molimo Ntate o rometse Jesu, ho bontša hore Ntate ke Mohloli oa morero oo (Bagal. 4:4). Mora o hlahile ho mosali (Bagal. 4:4), e leng pontšo ea ho tsoaloa ha hae e le motho le phethahatso ea tšepiso ea khale (Gen. 3:15). Mora o re lopolotse ’me a re khutlisetsa likamanong tse nepahetseng le Ntate, eo Satane a ileng a bua leshano ka eena (Gen. 3:5). ’Me Moea o Halalelang o tiisa boitsebiso ba rona re le bana ba Molimo (Bagal. 4:6).

Ho na le liqotsulo tse ’maloa tse ling tse buang ka Boraro-bo-bong mangolong a Paulose. Ba bararo bana ba sebetsa ’moho ho lokisetsa kereke mosebetsi oa borumuoa (1 Bakorinthe 12:4–6), ho re fa matla a semoea (Baefese 3:14–19), le ho khothalletsa bonngoe bo tebileng har’a litho tsa kereke—bonngoe bo etsisang kamano e phethiloeng e teng pakeng tsa litho tsa Boraro-bo-bong (Baefese 4:4–6). Ho ea ka kutloisiso ea Paulose, hase feela hore Molimo ke Boraro-bo-bong, empa le hore Batho ba bararo ba Boraro-bo-bong ba sebetsa ’moho bakeng sa pholoho ea rona (Baefese 1:3, 13, 14). Bukeng ea Baefese, Paulose o bile a bontša hore re lokela ho tlala ka botlalo ba Ntate (Baefese 3:19), Mora (Baefese 4:13), le Moea o Halalelang (Baefese 5:18).

Ha a tlisa puisano le Bakorinthe qetellong (2 Bakr. 13:14), Paulose o sitoa ho fana ka qetello e betere—tšepo ea hore Batho ba Boraro-bo-bong ba tlholeho, Boraro-bo-bong ba leholimo, ba tla ba le rona hona joale le nakong e tlang.

`Ke joang kopano har’a litho tsa kereke e lokela ho bontša kamano e ntle ea Boraro-bo-bong?`
