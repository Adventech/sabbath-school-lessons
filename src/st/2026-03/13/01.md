### Bakeng sa Thuto ea Beke ena Bala
2 Bakr. 8:9; Ba-Rom. 16:20; 1 John 4:8–11; 2 Bakr. 13:11; Ba-Fil. 2:1, 2; Ba-Gal. 4:4–6.

> <p>Temana ea khopolo</p>
> “Mohau oa Morena Jesu Kreste le lerato la Molimo le kopano ea Moea o Halalelang li be le lona bohle” (2 Bakorinthe 13:14).

Paulose o koala 2 Bakorinthe ka tiisa lintlha tseo a buileng ka tsona lengolong la hae. O etsa sena ka melao e mehlano ea litaelo (2 Bakr. 13:11). Molao-taelo oa pele, “Thabang” (ESV), ho hopotsa litemana tse tlileng pele lengolong.

Oa bobeli molao-taelo, “le phethehe boikutlong bo le bong” (NIV), e toloka lentsoe le le leng segerikeng (_katartizo_), le hlahang mona le ho 1 Bakorinthe 1:10.

Oa boraro, “Le khothatsane” (NIV), e tsoela pele ho 2 Bakorinthe 1:3–7. Paulose o qala le ho koala lengolo la bobeli ka khothatso. Re amohela khothatso ea Molimo e le hore re khothatse ba bang (2 Bakr. 1:4, 6).

Molao-taelo oa bone le oa bohlano, “E-bang le mohopolo o le mong, phelang ka khotso” (2 Bakr. 13:11, NKJV), ke pitso ea bonngoe. Tikoloho ena ea thabo, ho tsosolosa, khothatso, bonngoe, le khotso ke maemo a bo teng ba Molimo, “Molimo oa lerato le khotso” (2 Bakr. 13:11, ESV). Sena se tsoa mosebetsing oa Molimo oa Boraro ka pelong ea motho (2 Bakr. 13:14).

Mohau, lerato le kopano, li tsoa mosebetsing oa boraro ba Molimo bakeng sa rona. Lintlha tsena tse tharo tsa Kreste li khothaletsa tikoloho e bontšang bo teng ba Molimo.

_Ithute thuto ea beke ena ho lokisetsa Sabatha, Loetse 26._
