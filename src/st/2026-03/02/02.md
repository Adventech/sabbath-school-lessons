---
title:  Evangeli ea Sefapano
date:   05/07/2026
---

Pauluse o re molaetsa oa sefapano ke matla a Molimo ho rona. Haho makatse hore “Jesu Kreste le hona ea thakhisitsoeng” ke khubu ea shomaelo ea hae (1 Bakor. 2:2).

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 1:17–31. Ke ntlha efe ea bohlokoa e etsoang ke Pauluse moo?`

Ho 1 Bakorinthe 1:18–31, Pauluse o sebetsana le phapang pakeng tsa bothoto ba motho le bohlale bo halalelang. Sefapano se na le matla a ho senola batho ba senyehing ka ho fetisisa le boipabolo ba Molimo. Karolo ea 1 Bakorinthe e lelekelloa ke polelo e ho 1 Bakorinthe 1:17. Kaha sefapano sa Kreste ha sea lokela ho mongoa matla a sona (1 Bakor. 1:17), molaetsa oa sefapano o tlameha ho ba khubu ea ho shomaela ha rona (Sheba le 1 Bakor. 2:2).

Pauluse o re h’a rongoa ho kolobetsa, empa ho ruta evangeli ea sefapano. Polelo ena e hloka re ele hloko taba tse peli tsa bohlokoa tseo re li lemohang. Ea pele, leetsi la Segerike e tolokiloeng “ho roma” ke _apostello_, e hlomelang kutung e tšoanang joaloka ho moapostola. Ka hona, mosebetsi o moholo oa boapostola ba Pauluse e n’e le khaso ea evangeli. Taba ea bobeli, mantsoe a Pauluse malebana le kolobetso a bolele hore kolobetso ha e bohlokoa, kapa eseng hore ha e bohlokoa joaloka evangeli. Empa, o ne a khalemela ba neng ba etsa malebana le hore na ke mang ea kolobetsang ho e-na le oa bohlokoa-hlokoa, eleng Jesu, eo ba kolobelitsoeng ho Eena.

Ka “bohlale ba mantsoe” (1 Bakor. 1:17), Pauluse ha a bontše hore hase hore lipuo tse monate ke ntho’e mpe hakalo-kalo. Taba ke hore bohlale ba motho ha bo’a lokela ho sira molaetsa oa sefapano. Poleloana ena e malebana le potso seikarabi ea Magerike le Maroma. Athene, Pauluse o sebelisitse kelello ho kopanya lintlha (lojike), saense, le filosofi, empa sena se tsoetse litholoana tse seng kae. Ka hona, “a etsa qeto ea ho latela leoa le leng la ho sebetsa Korinthe boitekong ba hae ba ho hohela thahasello ea ba sa tsotelleng le ba sa ikamahanyeng hohang. O ile a itjoetsa hore o tla qoba likhang le lipuisano tse telele, le ‘ho se tsebe letho’ hara Bakorinthe ‘haese Jesu Kreste, le hona ea thakhisitsoeng.’ ”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, leq. 244.

`Ke ka tsela life lipuisano tse telele li ka thunthetsang molaetsa oa sefapano? Ke hobaneng ha phatlalatso ea Jesu Kreste ea thakhisitsoeng e tlisitse litholoana Korinthe, tse fetang tse tlisitsoeng ke kelello, saense, le filosofi Athene? Leha ho le joaloa, na ho na le moo lojiki, filosofi, le saense li ka thusang teng khasong ea evangeli?`
