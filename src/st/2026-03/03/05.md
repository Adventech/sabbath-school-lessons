---
title:  Bohlanka Bo Kang ba Kreste
date:   15/07/2026
---

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 4:1, 2. Ke sefe se rutoang ke seratsoana see malebana le hore na mokhoa o motle oa ho sheba baetapele ba batho ke ofe?`

Ho 1 Bakorinthe 3:1–4, Pauluse o fana ka leseli la hore maquloana a tsoalo ke ho se hole moeeng. Leha ho le joalo, pele a buoa ka taba ena, o tiisa hore “re na le kelello ea Kreste” (1 Bakor. 2:16. Khonahalo ke hore poleloana ena e tlameha e buoa ka mokhoa oa Kreste oa nahana le ho etsa. Ka mantsoe a mang, Molumeli o na le “kelello ea Kreste” ha a nahana le ho etsa joaloka Kreste. Ho kenya kelello ena tšebetsong likarolong tsohle tsa bophelo ha ho bonolo haholo, kapa ho bonolo? Lefatšeng la Magerike-Maroma, ho ne ho e na le tlholisano e kholo hara bapolotiki, lifilosofara, linahani, le baetaple ba tsa bolumeli. Lenyora la litho la ho amoheleha sechabeng ho bonahala le ile la etsa hore kereke ea Korinthe e latele maemo a lefatše. Taba ena e ka ba kotsi e ka hlahelang kereke le kajeno.

`Bala Bafilipi 2:5–8. Ke joang temana ee e re thusang ho utloisisa poleloana “kelello ea Kreste” (1 Bakor. 2:16)?`

Hantle joaloka Korinthe, liphapang li ne li boetse li nkile sebaka ka kerekeng ea Filipi (Bafil. 2:1–4), mohlomong li se mepe hakaalo. Bafilipi 2:1–8 e re ruta hore bohlanka bo kang a Kreste bo re qosa ho shoa bonna le litaba-tabelo tsa bonna, empa ho batla ho hlohonolofatsa ba bang pele ho rona, joalokaha Jesu A entse.

Bosebeletsi bo kang ba Kreste ke bona bo neng bo boleloa ke Pauluse ka poleloana “bahlanka ba Kreste” (1 Bakor. 4:1). Poleloana ena e ka tlisa moelelo oa hore ba sebeletsa Kreste e le bathusi kapa ba ka tlasana ho Eena. Ho hlakile hore katamelo e ntle ea boetapele ea boetapele ba motho e thonngoe holima mohlala oa Kreste oa boetapele. Bahlanka ba boetse ba bontšoa e le “liqhoku” (1 Bakor. 4:1, 2). Qhoku ke motho ea tšepetsoeng ho hlokomela thepa ea motho e mong. Hakere sohle seo re nang le sona, haele hantle ke sa Kreste!

`Ka thapelo, tsepamisa maikutlo molaetseng oa Bafilipi 2:5–8. Re utloisisa seo see se re bolellang sona malebana le lerato la Molimo le iketsang sehlabelo bakeng sa rona? Hape, ke joang re ka ithutang ho shoa bonna ka tsela eo, hohle moo re teng, re ka bontšang lerato lee?`
