---
title:  Bothata ba Maquloana ka Kerekeng
date:   12/07/2026
---

Boipiletso ba Pauluse ba ho re “ho se be likarohano hara lona, empa le be ntho e le ’ngoe kelellong e le ’ngoe le kahlolong e le ’ngoe” (1 Bakor. 1:10) e nka karolo e kholo khaolong tse ’ne tsa pele ho Bakorinthe ba pele. Haele hantle, bongata ba baithuti bo lumela hore bonngoe ke ’moko-taba o kenyeletsang sohle se kopanyang khalo tsena kaofela.

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 1:12–17. Ke joang serapa see se re thusang ho utloisisa hore na ke taba e senang kelello hakae ho theha maquloana malebana le baetapele ba kereke ea lehae? Tharollo ea Pauluse ke efe?`

Pauluse o sebelisa mantsoe a matla ho bontša tlhokeho ea bonngoe hara litho tsa kereke Korinthe. O sebelisa lentsoe la Segerike _schisma_ (“likarohano,” 1 Bakor. 1:10) le _eris_ (“ho qhoebeshana,” 1 Bakor. 1:11). Lereho _schisma_ (’moho le leetsi _schizo_, “ho arola”) le sebelisoa kae kae Testamenteng e Ncha ho hlalosa ho fapana ka maikutlo ho tlisang likarohano. Ka ho tšoana, lereho _eris_ (“liqhoebeshano”) hangata le hlaha hara lethathamo la libe tse sa lokeleng ho etsoa ke Bakreste.

`Bala Baroma 1:29, Baroma 13:13, 1 Bakorinthe 3:3, 2 Bakorinthe 12:20, le Bagalata 5:20. Libe tse thathamisitsoeng ’moho le eris (“qhoebeshano,” “ho loantšana”)? See se bolellang malebana le hore na ke ntho’e mpe hakae?`

Ho fapana ka maikutlo ka kerekeng ea Korinthe ho bile pepenene—hona hoo ho bileng ha isanoa makhotleng. (1 Bakor. 6:1–3). “ke rialo ho le hlabisa lihlong,” o ile a ba joetsa (1 Bakor. 6:5) ka ho qosana hona ha litho tsa kereke. Haele hantle, ba ne ba lese liphapang tsa bona le nakong ea selallo (1 Bakor. 11:17–22).

Bothata ba tlhokeho ea bonngoe hara litho e ea tšosa, ’me e tšoentse Pauluse haholo, hona hoo a qalang ka eona ha a ngolla Bakorinthe.

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 1:12–27 hape. E be o nahana ka kamoo serapa sena se re thusang ho utloisa hore na hobaneng maquloana a le kotsi bonngoeng ba kereke. Ke sefe seo kereke ea heno ea lehae e ka se etsang e le ho qoba bothata boo?`
