### Bakeng sa Thuto ea Beke ena Bala
1 Bakor. 1:12–17, Baro. 1:29, 1 Bakor. 1:10, 1 Bakor. 3:1–4, Bafil. 2:5–8, 2 Bakor. 11:23–28, Bakol. 1:24.

> <p>Temana ea khopolo</p>
> “Kea le rapela banab’eso, ka lebitso la Morena oa rona Jesu Kreste ke re: le ke le bolelle bohle taba e le ’ngoe; likhaohano li se ke tsa e ba teng hara lona, le mpe le phethehe boikutlong bo le bong le kho-polong e le ’ngoe” (1 Bakorinthe 1:10).

Ba shebisisang bophelo ba naheng ba tseba hantle tse ling tsa libupuoa li phela ka hlopha tse kholo, tse sa lekaneng. Ho tloha ka liphiri, (ho joalo), ho ea lidolfining, le koana maroaneng, ke libupuoa tse phelang ’moho.

Litšoene tsa lichimpanse li tumme haholo ka likamano tsa tsona tsa phelisano, ka nako tse ling li phela ka lihlopha tse ka bang 15 ho ea ho 150. Leha ho le joalo, ha se hore kamehla likamano tsena li lula li tiile, ka nako tse ling li chimapse li loantšana e le tsona.

Batho le bona ba joalo; ke hore, ha ba phele ka lihlopha feela, ka nako tse ling b’a loantšana hona lihlopheng tseo. Taba ena ke ’nete le ka likerekeng tsa rona! Ho ba le maquloana, lihlopha li’a thomeha hangata ka tlasa tšusumetso ea moetapele oa kheleke. Ka bomalimabe, maquloana ana ha a utloane. Na use u kile oa bona seo ka kerekeng ea hao?

Ebang ho joalo, u utloisisa taba eo Pauluse a neng a kopane le eona hantle Korinthe. Bekeng ena re tla shebang 1 Bakorinthe 1–4, moo moapostola Pauluse a sebetsanang le bothata ba ho qhoebeshana ka kerekeng le ho li hlola, eleng ka, bonngoe ho Kreste.

_Ithute thuto ea beke ena ho lokisetsa Sabatha, Phupu 18._
