---
title:  Marema-tlou A Ntsoe-leng
date:   03/08/2026
---

Puo ea bonngoe e khakotsoeng ho 1 Bakorinthe 12:4–6 (“moea o le mong,” “Molimo o mong” le “Morena o mong”) e holisoa hantle likarolong tse latelang tsa 1 Bakorinthe 12. Sena se pakoa ke tšebeliso ea Pauluse ea lipoleloana tse kang “Moea oo o leng mong feela” (1 Bakor. 12:11), “’mele o mong” (1 Bakor. 12:12), “’mele o mong” (1 Bakor. 12:12, 13, 20), “Moea O mong” (1 Bakor. 12:13 [makhetlo a mabeli]), “litho li mpe li baballane” (1 Bakor. 12:25).

’Moho le taba ea bonngoe, Pauluse o bontša ho fapana ha litho ’meleng oa Kreste ka lipolelo tse kang “litho tse ngata” (1 Bakor. 12:12, 20), “bohle re kolobelitsoe” (1 Bakor. 12:13), “ha se setho se le seng empa ke litho tse ngata” (1 Bakor. 12:14), “’mele oohle” (1 Bakor. 12:17), “se seng se le seng” (1 Bakor. 12:18), “litho tsa ’mele” (1 Bakor. 12:22, 23), “litho tsohle” (1 Bakor. 12:26). Thatiselletso ena ea bonngoe le ho fapana e bontša hore sepheo sa lineo tsa Moea ke ho holisa bonngoe ka ho se tšoane.

Bonngoe hara ho se tšoane hona ho bontša semelo sa Molimo. Ntate ke motho a le mong, Mora le eena ke motho e mong, le Moea O Halalelang ke e mong. E mong le e mong o boloka boeena ba Hae, ha ba ntse ba sebetsa ’moho ho holisa kereke le ho e matlafatsa bakeng sa borumuoa (1 Bakor. 12:4–6, Baef. 4:11–13).

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 12:12–31. Ke hobaneng ha papiso ea ’mele le litho tsa oona e fita hantle bakeng sa ho emela kereke le litho tsa eona?`

E ’ngoe ea taba tsa mantlha ho 1 Bakorinthe 12 ke hore leha litho tsa ’mele li fapane hakaalo (1 Bakor. 12:15–20), tsohle li itšetlehile ho tse ling (1 Bakor. 12:21–26). Maoto a itšetlehile ho mahlo hore a bone na a tsamaea kae; ka ho tšoana, mahlo a ke se tšoare letho—ke matsoho fela a ka etsang joalo. Hape, taba ea hore litho tse ling li’a fokola (1 Bakor. 12:22) kapa ha li’a eiseha (1 Bakor. 12:23) joaloka tse ling ke monahano feela hobane tsohle li’a hlokeha (1 Bakor. 12:22).

Ka bomali-mabe, ho ne ho e na le tloaelo hara bakorinthe ea ho nkela lineo tse ling holimo ho feta tse ling. Ho thibela Bakorinthe ho etsa phoso ena, Pauluse o ile a isa maikutlo a bona leratong, eleng, “tsela e fetisisang” (1 Bakor. 12:31). Ka mantsoe a mang, ho sa natsoe hore na mpho ke efe, ebang e sebelisoa ka bohlale le ka lerato, e ea khahlisa ho Molimo.

`Sheba lethathamo la lineo ho 1 Bakorinthe 12:8–10, 28; Baroma 12:6–8; le Baefese 4:11. Neo ea hao ke efe? U ka e sebelisa joang ho holisa ’mele oa Kreste?`
