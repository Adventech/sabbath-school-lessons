### Bakeng sa Thuto ea Beke ena Bala
1 Bakorinthe 12, Baef. 4:11–13, 1 Bakorinthe 13, 1 Pet. 4:8–11, 1 Bakor. 14:27, Amose 3:7.

> <p>Temana ea khopolo</p>
> “Phehellang lerato. Leha ho le joalo, le chesehelle lineo tsa Moea, haholo ea ho porofeta” (1 Bakorinthe 14:1).

Joaloka ’mele oa motho, kereke e ’ngoe empa e na le litho tse ngata, se seng le se seng se bapala karolo, ho etsa mosebetsi, lineo tse ikhethang. Ha li etsoa ka lerato, lineo tsena tsa Moea li phahamisa bonngoe bo bontšang semelo sa boraro bo kopaneng ba Molimo.

Bekeng ena re tla lekolang 1 Bakorinthe 12–14 le lithuto tsa tsona ka lineo tsa moea. Karolo ena ke karoloana ea karolo e kholo, moo Paulose a sebetsanang le se lebelletsoeng ka boitšoaro ba Mokreste liphuthehong (1 Bakorinthe 11–14). Khathatseho ea mantlha ea Pauluse e malebana le phutheho tse se nang taolo. Karabo ea hae ke hore kereke ke ’mele oo likarolo tsa ona li nang le mesebetsi e fapaneng e kenya letsoho ho “ho hahisa ’mele oa Kreste” (Baef. 4:12). Ka bokutšoane, Molimo O file kereke lineo tsa moea ho phahamisa bonngoe ka hara ho se tšoane.

E, Pauluse o ntse a beile ka kelellong bothata ba likarohano boo a buileng ka bona khaolong tse ’ne tsa pele tsa 1 Bakorinthe, moo karabo ea ho se utloane hara litho tsa kereke eleng bonngoe ho Kreste. Joale, o holisa taba eo ka ho teka kutloisiso ea hae ea mosebetsi oa lineo tsa moea. Ho ea ka Pauluse, bonngoe ho Kreste le Moea ke oona fela mokhoa oa qoba likarohano.

_Ithute thuto ea beke ena ho lokisetsa Sabatha, Phato 8._
