---
title:  Neo ea Maleme
date:   05/08/2026
---

Neo ea maleme eona? Re ipapisitse le moo ho buuoeng ka neo ena kae kae ka Bibeleng (Mareka 16:17, Liketso 2:1–13, Liketso 10:44–48, Liketso 19:6), neo ea maleme ho 1 Bakorinthe ho na le khonahalo ea hore ke bokhoni bo fanoang ke Moea ba ho buoa lipuo tseo eseng tsa hao.

Pauluse o buoa ka neo ea maleme lethathamong la lineo tse ho 1 Bakorinthe 12:8–10 (Sheba le 1 Bakor. 12:28, 30; 1 Bakor. 13:1, 8). Leha ho le joalo, o buoa ka tsona hangata ho 1 Bakorinthe 14. Ehlile, Lentsoe la Segerike glossa (“leleme”) le hlaha makhetlo a fetang mashome a mabeli ho 1 Bakorinthe 12–14, moo makhetlo a leshome le metso e mehlano a leng ho 1 Bakorinthe 14 fela. Ntle ho mona, lentsoe la Segerike, heteroglossos (“leleme lesele”) le boetse le hlaha, ho 1 Bakorinthe 14:21. Qotso tse ngata tsena tse buoang ka neo ea maleme e hlahisa hore taba ena ke taba ho Pauluse. Ho sebelisa neo ena hampe ho neng ho etsoa ke kereke ea Korinthe ho ne ho bakile tlhokeho ea taolo le mofere-fere khumamelong ea phutheho (1 Bakor. 14:23, 27, 33, 40).

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 14:5, 13, 26, 27 le 1 Bakorinthe 12:10, 30. Ke litaelo life tse hlakileng tseo Pauluse a ileng a fana ka tsona malebana le neo ea maleme?`

Lebaka leo neo ea ho buoa maleme e lokelang ho tsamaea ’moho le ea ho a fetola ke hore maleme a tlameha a utloisisehe (1 Bakor. 14:9); ho seng joalo, ha hona thuso ea ho sebelisa neo eo (1 Bakor. 14:6). Sena se hlalosa hore na hobaneng Pauluse a toboketsa taba ea ho fetola le ho utloisisa. Ho hlakile, hore ha a hlasele neo ea maleme ka boeona, empa, joalokaha re tla bona hosane, kamoo Bakorinthe ba neng ba se ba e nketse holimo kateng, eleng se neng se entse hore ba se natse neo ea ho porofeta.

Hona mona, ho bohlokoa ho ela hloko hore leha pauluse a ne a lakatsa ha Bakorinthe kaofela ba ka buoa lipuo tsa balichaba (1 Bakor. 14:5), o ne a sa lebella hore seo se ka etsahala (1 Bakor. 12:10). Ka hona, taba ea hore “bohle ba buoe ka maleme pele ba kopa ho kolobetsoa ka Moea O Halalelang ke ho sotha thuto ea Pauluse ho 1 Bakorinthe 12 le 14.”—Raoul Dederen, Handbook of Seventh-day Adventist Theology, electronic ed., vol. 12 ea Commentary Reference Series (Hagerstown, MD: Review and Herald Publishing Association, 2001), leq. 620.

`Na ka kerekeng ea hao ho na le batho ba buoang lipuo tse ling? Ke joang ba ka sebelisang tsebo ee ho fihlela ba bang bakeng sa Kreste? Ke joang taba ee e ka re thusang ho utloisisa tlhaho ea ’nete ea maleme, eo Pauluse a buoang ka eona?`
