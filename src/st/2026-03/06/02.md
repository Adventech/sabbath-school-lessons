---
title:  Lineo tse Fapaneng
date:   02/08/2026
---

Pauluse o hlahisa sehlooho se secha ho 1 Bakorinthe 12:1, ka hore “ha e le ka.” Barutehi ba ngangisana ka hore na o buoa ka “lineo tsa Moea” kapa “batho ba semoea” hobane poleloana ea Segerike ton pneumatikon e lumella botoloki bona ka bobeli. “Lineo tsa moea” ke eona e utlohalang ho latela 1 Bakorinthe 12:4, moo ka ho hlaka Pauluse a buoang ka lineo tsa Moea. Ho 1 Bakorinthe 12:2, 3, Pauluse o supa hore neo ea pele ea Moea ke sebete sa ho phatlalatsa hore Jesu ke Morena (1 Bakor. 12:2, 3). Mehleng ea Testamnete e Ncha, ho re Jesu ke Morena ke ho re Sisare ha se morena (Liketso 17:7; le Johanne 19:12, 15). Sena se ne se nkoa e le nyeliso ea matla a maholo a morena, ke ka hona, kahlolo ea teng e neng e le lefu.

Jesu le Pauluse ba tiisitse hore, tumelo ho Molimo—le hara litlhoriso le tšoso tsa lefu—ke neo ea Moea. Haele hantle, tumelo ke motheo oa lineo tsa Moea. Ha ho makatse hore e be, tumelo ke eona ea pele lethathamong la lineo tsa moea ho 1 Bakorinthe 13:13. Hore tumelo ke neo ea Moea ho hlakisoa ke polelo ea Pauluse ho 1 Bakorinthe 12:9. Leha ho le joalo, ho na le neo tse ling tse ngata. Taba ea hore moea O Halalelang o fana ka mefuta e fapaneng ea lineo “O abela e mong le e mong kamoo O ratang kateng” (1 Bakor. 12:11) e tiisa hore tsohle li bohlokoa.

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 12:1–6. Se toboketsoang ke seratsoana see ke sefe?`

Pheta-pheto ea “mefuta” ho toboketsa bongata ba lineo. Seo Pauluse a reng ke “lineo tsa Moea” ho 1 Bakorinthe 12:1 se nontšoa temaneng tsa 4–6, ka ho lihla mahlakoreng a mararo a fapaneng: “lineo” (charisma), “litšebeletso” kapa “bosebeletsi” (diakonia), le “tse etsuoang” (energema). Leha mantsoe ana a bolela ntho tse fapaneng, ho bohlokoa ho se etse phapang e hlakileng ho tsona ka baka la ho tšoana ha seratsoana sena. Motho o lokela ho ela hloko hore lineo tsa Moea li etselitsoe ho tlisa bonngoe bo thehiloeng holima semelo sa Molimo o hararo (Sheba le Baef. 4:8–11). Ha Moea o fana ka lineo ho balumeli, ke Molimo ea ba fang matlafatsang hore ba sebeletse Kreste motseng oa balumeli (1 Bakor. 12:5, 6). Molumeli ka mong o fuoa lineo (1 Bakor. 12:11), empa kaofela li lokela ho tsoala balumeli ka moka molemo.

`Hlokomela ka moo Pauluse a boelang a toboketsa bonngoe kateng. Ke hobaneng ha see se le bohlokoa ho kereke?`
