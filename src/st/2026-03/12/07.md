Ellen G. White, “The Laodicean Church,” p. 125, in The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, Loetse 30, 1873.

“Morena o sireletsa batho ba Hae khahlanong le ho pheta liphoso tsa nako e fetileng. E sa le ho na le baruti ba bohata ba neng, ka ho khothaletsa lithuto tse fosahetseng le mekhoa e sa halaleleng, ’me ba sebetsa ka melao e fosahetseng ka mokhoa o khahlang, o patiloeng, o khelosang, ba leka ho khelosa, haeba ho ka khoneha, le bona bakhethoa.”—Ellen G. White, in The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, January 7, 1904.

“Morena o rerile hore maikutlo a rona a hlahlojoe, hore re bone tlhokahalo ea ho hlahloba ka hloko melaetsa e phelang hore re bone hore na re ntse re le tumelong kapa che. Ba bangata ba ipolelang hore ba lumela ’nete ba lutse meriting oa tebalo ba re, ‘Ke ruile, ’me ke eketsehile ka lintho, ebile ha ke hloke letho’.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 36.

“Batho ba rata liphoso, ha ’nete e hlakile ebile e totobetse; ’me haeba ba ne ba ka tlisa lithuto tsa bona lentsoeng la Molimo, ’me eseng ho bala lentsoe la Molimo ho lumellana le lithuto tsa bona ho leka ho bontša hore mehopolo ea bona e nepahetse, ba ne ba ke ke ba tsamaea lefifing le bofofung, kapa ba ananele liphoso. Batho ba bangata ba fa mantsoe a Bibele moelelo o lumellanang le maikutlo a bona, ’me ba iphosisa le ho khelosa ba bang ka kutloisiso e fosahetseng ea lentsoe la Molimo. Ha re qala ho ithuta lentsoe la Molimo, re lokela ho etsa joalo ka lipelo tse nang le boikokobetso. Boithati bohle, lerato lohle la ho ba le maikutlo a ikhethang, li lokela ho tlosoa. Maikutlo a nako e telele a lokela hore a se ke a lumelloa ho nkoa a sa fosahala.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, pp. 36, 37.

**Lipotso tsa Ho Qoqoas:**

`Bala 2 Bakorinthe 10:1–6 hape. Mokhoa oa Paulose ke ofe oa ho tobana le “lintoa” tsa moea bakeng sa ’nete ea Molimo, ’me ke joang re ka o sebelisang lintoeng tsa r na tsa semoea?`

`Bibele e re pele bofelo bo tla, baruti ba bangata ba bohata ba tla leka ho hohela batho ho ba tlosa ’nete. Kereke ea hao ea lehae e ka etsa eng ho thibela litho hore li se ke tsa susumetsoa ke baruti ba bohata bao e kang ’nang eaba le kerekeng ’moho? Hobaneng sena se bohlokoa bakeng sa phethahatso eat homo ea kereke?`

`Hobaneng Paulose a fumana ho le bohlokoa ho ithorisa ka letoto la mahlomola (2 Bakr. 11:16–33)? Hape, ho bolela eng “ho ithorisa moreneng”?`

`Hobaneng ho le bohlokoa bakeng sa litho tsa kereke ho itlhatlhoba ho bona hore na li ntse li le tumelong (2 Bakr. 13:5)? Ho etsang phapano ea eng?`
