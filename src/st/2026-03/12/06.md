---
title:  Boipiletso ho ba Bakang
date:   17/09/2026
---

Ho 2 Bakorinthe 12:14–13:10, Paulose o tsebisa phutheho ka ketelo ea hae ea boraro (2 Bakr. 12:14, 2 Bakr. 13:1). O ne a bontšitse hore ha a monyenyane ho baapostola ba bohata, ’me joale o na le kholiseho ea ho tla Korinthe hape ho leka ho khutlisa litho tse sa bakang. Ha e le hantle, lena e ne e le lebaka la mathomo la leeto lena. Tsohle tseo Paulose a li entseng le tseo a li buileng li ne li reretsoe ho haha phutheho (2 Bakr. 12:19).

`Bala 2 Bakorinthe 12:20, 21. Ke libe life tse sentseng boemo ba semoea ba kereke ea Korinthe?`

Lethathamo la libe ho 2 Bakorinthe 12:20, 21 le tšoana le le fumanoang libakeng tse ling mangolong a Paulose (Baroma 1:29–31, Bagalata 5:19–21). Lintho tse peli tsa pele li hlaha ho 1 Bakorinthe 3:3, moo Paulose a buang ka mohono le likhohlano har’a litho tsa phutheho ea Korinthe.

Paulose o tšoenyehile hore lintho li ke ke tsa fapana haholo ha nako ea ho ba etela lekhetlo la boraro. O re, “Kea tšaba hore mohlomong ha ke tla ho lona ke tla le fumana le se joalo ka ha ke lakatsa.” Ka lehlakoreng le leng, o re, “Le tla mphumana ka tsela eo le sat lo e lakatsa” (2 Bakorinthe 12:20, ESV). Sena se bolela hore ho e-na le hore a sebetsane le bona “ka bonolo le boikokobetso ba Kreste” (2 Bakorinthe 10:1, ESV), joale o tla be a “ikemiselitse ho otla bohle ba hlokang kutlo” (2 Bakr. 10:6, ESV).

Tšoenyeho ea hae e kholo ke hore litho tseo tse amehang “litšileng, bofebeng, le hloleng” mohlomong ba so ka ba baka (2 Bakorinthe 12:21, ESV). ’Me ke libe tse joalo tse bakang karohano ka kerekeng.

Ka mor’a moo, Paulose o shebana le mosebetsi oa kereke oa khalemo ho khutlisetsa ba entseng sebe tseleng e nepahetseng (2 Bakorinthe 13:1–4). Bofokoli ha se lebaka la ho phela bophelo bo tletseng sebe. Ho teng matla bakeng sa ba batlang ho phela bophelo bo hlolang (2 Bakorinthe 13:4). Taba ea hore ba bang ba Korinthe ba ne ba etsa sebe sa bohlola e bontša hore matla a Molimo e ne e se nnete maphelong a bona. Paulose o ne a batla hore ba bake, ’me ba utloe matla a etsang hore motho a mamele. Ho ba khalemela e ne e le ntho eo a neng a sa e batle. O re, “Re rapela Molimo hore le se ke la etsa phoso . . . empa hore le etse se nepahetseng. Rea le rapella hore le tsosolosoe” (2 Bakorinthe 13:7–9, ESV). Thapelo e ntle hakakang! O ba kopa hore ba itlhahlobe hore ba bone hore na ba ntse ba le tumelong.

`Bala 2 Bakorinthe 13:5. Ho boleloa eng ka ho ba tumelong? U ka tseba joang hore u tumelong?`
