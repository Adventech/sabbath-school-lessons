---
title:  Lenyalo le Ho ba Leqhalaha
date:   23/07/2026
---

Thatiselletso ea Pauluse ea hore ’mele ea rona “ke tempele ea Moea O Halalelang” (1 Bakor. 6:19) e hlaha maemong ao a khalemelang thobalano e litšila. Ho ba tempele ea Moea O Halalelang ke eona fela tsela ea ho phela bophelo ba khalalelo. Kereke ke sehlopha sa Bakreste se iqollang hara tikoloho e se pota-potileng. Ke boteng ba Moea O Halalelang bo etsang hore seo se khonahale.

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 6:19–7:9. Ke joang seratsona see se chabisang leseli malebana le kamoo motho a ka kenyang tšebetsong taelo ea ho “baleha bofebe” (1 Bakor. 6:18)?`

Ho na le thuto tsa bohlokoa malebana le bofebe ho 1 Bakorinthe 7. Re habanya feela, khaolo ena e ka aroloa koto tse peli: (1) litaelo tsa lenyalo (1 Bakor. 7:1–24) le (2) litaelo tsa ho se nyaloe kapa ho nyala (1 Bakor. 7:25–40). Bakorinthe ba pele 7 e re thusa ho utloisisa hore ho buoa ka thobalano ho’a lokela ebile ho bohlokoa. Leha ho le joalo, ha re bala 1 Bakorinthe 7, re lokela ho hopola hore Pauluse o araba potso tseo a li etselitsoeng ka kotloloho malebana le taba tse neng li etsahala kerekeng ea Korinthe. Ho seng joalo, tse ling tsa lipolelo li ka etsa ho utloahale e ka ha a khothaletse lenyalo, eleng phoso. (1 Tim. 4:1–3, 1 Tim. 5:14; hape le Baheb. 13:4).

Ka ho khetholoha, taelo ena “balehang bofebe” ho 1 Bakorinthe 6:18 e fuperoe ke taba ea ho amohela Kreste (1 Bakor. 6:17) le ho ba tempele ea Moea (1 Bakor. Na ho na le tsela e betere ea ho baleha bofebe? Ruri ha e eo.

Hape, Molimo o entse thobalano, empa e tlameha ho natefeloa lenyalong feela. Thobalano ke mpho ea e motona le motšehali lenyalong, ke oona feela mofuta o lumeletsoeng ka Bibeleng.

Ha a re, “Balehang bofebe,” e kanna eaba Pauluse o na le pale ea Josefa ka kelellong (Gen. 39:6–18). Bibele e re pele mosali oa Potifaro ea neng a cha ke litakatso a atamela, Josefa “a balehela ka ntle” (Gen. 39:18. Taba e phetoa makhetlo a fetang bone ho Genese 39:6–18. Bibele ha e e toboketse hantle, empa hoa utloahala hore Josefa o ne a emetse ho etsa thobalano lenyalong (Gen. 41:45). E ne e le monna ea tletseng Moea O Halalelang (Gen. 41:38) ’me a batla ho etsa se nepahetseng ka pela mahlo a Molimo oa hae.

`Ke joang rona, re le kereke, re ka itšireletsang maikutlong a fapaneng malebana le thobalano a laolang meetlo ea rona?`
