---
title:  Ho Sireletsa Boitsebiso ba Kereke
date:   21/07/2026
---

Ho 1 Bakorinthe 6:1–11, Pauluse o tsoela pele ka ho buoa ka kamoo Bakreste ba lokelang ho tobana le taba tse amanang le batho ba ka kerekeng.

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 5:3, 12, 13 le 1 Bakorinthe 6:1–13. Seo Pauluse a lekang ho se ruta Bakorinthe, le rona, ke sefe?`

Lentsoe la Segerike pragma ho 1 Bakorinthe 6:1, le tolokiloeng “taba” Phetolelong e Ncha ea Morena Jakobo, ke lentsoe le akaretsang le bolelang “ntho.” Mona, le buoa ka taba ea molao. Ho bohlokoa ho hopola hore 1 Bakorinthe 6:1–11 ha e buoe ka tlolo ea molao ea botlokotsebe. Matla a makhotla a molao bakeng sa litlolo tsa molao tsa botlokotsebe e boela e toboketsoa ho Baroma 13:1–5. Pauluse o sebetsana le nyeoe e ka makhotleng a molao hang kamora nyeoe ea litšila tsa thobalano, hantle joalokaha Moshe a ile a etsa ho Deuterenoma 22:22-24. Sena se bontša kamoo mokhoa oa Pauluse oa ho sebetsa le taba tse ka kerekeng o neng o itšetlehile holima Mangolo kateng.

Taba ea hore nyeoe e ho 1 Bakorinthe 6:1–11 e lika-likelitsoe ke lirapa tse buoang ka litšila tsa thobalano (1 Bakorinthe 5 and 1 Bakor. 6:12–20) e ka nna ea bolela hore “taba” ho 1 Bakorinthe 6:1 e malebana le litšila tsa thobalano hape. Ha re tsebe hantle hore na taba mona e ne e le efe, hore na e n’e le nyeoenyana e nyenyane, joaloka ho tseka thepa, kapa bothata ba thobalano.

Ho sa khathelehe hore na pragma hantle e n’e leng, Pauluse o ne a sa thabisoa ke ho bona litho tsa kereke li e isa lekhotleng la ’maseterata. Na, joaloka baena le likhaitseli Moreneng, ba ka be ba sa e sebetsa ka bo bona, ho e na le ho hulela taba ena ka pela “ba khopo” (1 Bakor. 6:1)?

Khonahalo e teng, joalokaha ba bang ba nahana, hore linyeoe tsa 1 Bakorinthe 6:1 ke mora le ntate ba 1 Bakorinthe 5:1. Leha ho le joalo, ho bohlokoa ho utloisisa taba hore re utloisise se boleloang. Pauluse o ne a khathalla boitsebiso ba kereke e le sehlopha sa Bakreste se bonoang ke ba kantle. Bakreste ha b’a lokela ho aneha phahlo tsa bona tse litšila terateng (1 Bakor. 6:6). Hape ha b’a lokela ho sebelisa makhotla a lefatše ho sebetsana le taba tse ka kerekeng. Lefatšeng la Roma, batho ba boemong bo holimo kapa barui kapa ba nang le matla a bopolotiki ba ne ba hlomphuoa ka khotla. Ho fapana le seo, Bakreste ba lokela ho etsa kahlolo e kang ea Kreste, hape ba ikhetholle maemong a lefatše.

`Nahana ka kamoo Pauluse a ththamisang libe ho 1 Bakorinthe 5:10, 11 le ho 1 Bakorinthe 6:9, 10. Hobaneng a thathamisa libe tsa thobalano ’moho le tse ling tse kang khumamelo ea litšoantšo, bosholu, meharo, le ho feteletsa kahlolo?`
