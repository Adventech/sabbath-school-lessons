---
title:  Ho Sebetsana le Mahlabisa-Lihlong
date:   20/07/2026
---

Ho sebetsana le taba tsa thobalano ha ho mohlang ho le bonolo. Ho ne ho le thata ho Pauluse, ho thata le ho rona. Maemong ana, re lokela ho tšepahalla Mangolo ’me re sebetse taba ena ka thapelo le lerato. Le ka mohla re se lebale hore sepheo sa rona ke ho khutlisetsa.

`Bala 1 Bakorinthe 5:1–13 hape. Pauluse o re ba sebetsane le taba ee joang?`

Pauluse o hlakisa hantle ho 1 Bakorinthe 5 hore litšila tsa thobalano li hloka kereke e li khalemele. O re monna enoa ea robalang le ’m’ae a khaoloe (1 Bakor. 5:2), a ahloloe (1 Bakor. 5:3), a fuoe Satane (1 Bakor. 5:5), e be “‘o lahleloa ntle’” (1 Bakor. 5:13. Litho tsa kereke li ile tsa bolelloa “ho se ikamahanye le eena” (1 Bakor. 5:9, 11, le ho “ja le ea joalo” (1 Bakor. 5:11). Pauluse o sebelisa puo e thata e ka nnang ea hlohlontša litsebe ho mobali oa kajeno, empa mantsoe ana a hae a lokela ho utloisisoa ho ipapisitsoe le maemo a nako eo. Hape, motho o lokela ho hopola hore o sebetsana le manyala a ferolang libete, a bophelo ba sebe. Hangata maemong a mabe haholo, ho hlokeha puo e thata. Leha ho le joalo, tlhaloso e khutšoane ea lipolelo tse ling e ea thusa.

**“ho tlosoe . . . hara lona”** (1 Bakor. 5:2; hape le 1 Bakor. 5:13). Taba ena e buoa ka khalemelo ea kereke.

**“a neeloe Satane”** (1 Bakor. 5:5). Hobane monna enoa h’a khetha ho ba tlasa tšireletso ea Molimo ka ho phela bophelo ba ho mo mamela, o tlokotsing ea hloibiloa ke Satane. Ka hona, taba ena e ka bolela ho kang “Ha a khole litholoana tsa qeto tsa hae.”

**“Le se ke la kopana”** (1 Bakor. 5:9, 11) “le se n’o ja le eena” (1 Bakor. 5:11). Ho tloelana le batho ba nang le likamano tse litšila tsa thobalano ho ne ho nkuoa ho le kotsi kaha ba ne ba ka ba mehlala e mebe ho ba bang. Mehleng ea khale, ho ja ’moho ho ne ho boetse e le mokhoa oa ho arolelana litšiea tsa ho phela. Bohle re kotsing ea tšusumetso tse re pota-potileng, ’me re lokela ho itšireletsa kahohle kamoo re ka khonang kateng, haholo-ha re tobane ntho ea mofuta ona.

**“Moea o tle o pholohe”** (1 Bakor. 5:5). Khalemelo ea kereke e ea khutlisetsa. Sepheo sa eona ke ho khutlisetsa moetsalibe kelellong e nepahetseng le mo thusa ho khoa bophelong ba hae ba sebe. Sena e tlameha e ne e le sona se neng se buuoa ke Pauluse ka ho “senyeha ha nama” (1 Bakor. 5:5). Hape, ho ka boela ha etsahala hore ebe, monna enoa ea robalang le ’m’ae ho 1 Bakorinthe 5, ke monna ea sokolohileng eo ho buuoang ka eena hamorao ho (sheba 2 Bakor. 2:5–10). Khalemelo ea kereke e fihlela sepheo sa eona ha setho se fositseng se boela se kenngoa setsoalleng sa kereke.
