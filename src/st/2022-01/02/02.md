---
title:  Jesu ke Morena oa Rona
date:  02/01/2022
---

Taba ea sehlooho ea Ba-Heberu ke hore Jesu ke ‘Musi, ea lutseng letsohong le letona la Ntate (Ba-Heb. 8:1). E le Molimo, esale Jesu e le ‘musi oa maholimo le lefatše. Empa ha Adama le Eva ba etsa sebe, Satane o ile a fetoha ‘musi oa lefatše lena (Johanne 12:31, Johanne 14:30, Johanne 16:11). Leha ho le joalo, Jesu o tlile ‘me a hlola Satane sefapanong, ‘me a buseletsa tokelo ea ho busa ba mo amohelang e le Molopolli oa bona (Ba-Kol. 2:13–15).

Khaolo tse peli tse qalang tsa Ba-Heberu li tsepame ka ho khetholloha tlhomamisong ea Jesu e le Morena.

`Bala Ba-Heberu 1:5–14. Ho etsahala eng moo?`

Litemana tsena li hlophisitsoe ka likarolo tse tharo. Karolo ka ‘ngoe e lelekella ntlha ea tšebeletso ea tlhomamiso ea Mora. Pele, Molimo o amohela Jesu e le Mora oa Hae oa borena (Ba-Heb. 1:5). Bobeling, Molimo o tsebisa makhotla a leholimo Mora, ba mo khumamelang (Ba-Heb. 1:6, 8) ha Jehova a phatlalatsa puso ea ka ho sa feleng ea Mora (Ba-Heb. 1:8–12). Borarong, Molimo o hlomamisa Mora—phetisetso ea matla ka kotloloho (Ba-Heb. 1:13, 14).

E ‘ngoe ea litumelo tsa bohlokoa ka ho fetisisa Testamenteng e Ncha ke hore ka Jesu Molimo o phethahalitse litšepiso tsa Hae ho Davida (sheba 2 Sam. 7:8–16 le Luka 1:30–33). Jesu o hlahile lelokong la Davida a hlahela motseng oa Davida (Matt. 1:1–16; Luka 2:10, 11). Nakong ea bosebeletsi ba Hae, batho ba ne ba atisa ho mo bitsa “mor’a Davida.” O ile a ahloloa ka qoso ea hore o ipitsa “morena oa Bajuda” (Matt. 27:37). Petrose le Pauluse ba ile ba ruta hore Jesu o tsohile bafung e le phethahatso ea tšepiso e entsoeng ho Davida (Liketso 2:22–36, Liketso 13:22–37). Hape Johanne o hlalosa Jesu e le “Tau ea leloko la Juda” (Tšen. 5:5).

Ba-Heberu, e fela, ea lumellana. Molimo o phethahalitse litšepiso tsa Hae ho Davida ka Jesu: Molimo o mo neile “lebitso” le leholo (Ba-Heb. 1:4), a mo ananela e le Mora oa Hae (Ba-Heb. 1:5), a tiisa terone ea Hae ka ho sa feleng (Ba-Heb. 1:8–12), hape a mo lulisa “letsohong le letona” la Hae (Ba-Heb. 1:13, 14). Ho feta moo, ho ea ka Ba-Heberu 4, Jesu o isa batho phomolong ea Molimo hape e re hopotsa hore Jesu ke eena moahi oa ntlo ea Molimo (Ba-Heb. 3:3, 4).

Jesu, ha ho le joalo, ke Eena ‘musi oa molao ea lokeloang ke bolateli ba rona, ea keneng ntoeng le Satane, oa moipehi.

`Ke joang re ka fumanang khothatso, haholo har’a liteko, ka ho tseba hore Jesu ke ‘musi oa maholimo le mafatše?`
