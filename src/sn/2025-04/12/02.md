---
title:  Zvose Zvakaitika
date:   14/12/2025
---

`Muna Joshua 21:43–45, ndeupi mufananidzo unoiswa nebhuku iri waMwari? Ko mashoko aya anei nezvekuita munhoroondo yeNyika Yechipikirwa sei nemuidi reruponeso wo? (2 Timoti 2:11–13)?`

Mavhesi aya anotaura nezvezvinotisvitsa pamusoro-soro pebhuku nepakupenderwa kwaro. Anotaura nezvemisoro yenyaya mikuru  yebhuku rose: sungano yekutendeka kwaYahweh, anochengeta zvipikirwa zvake nekuzadzisa mhiko dzake. Ndima pfupi iyi inepfupiso dzezviri mubhuku rose kusvika apa. Joshua 21:43 inotaura nezvekugovewa kwenyika nekugaramo (Joshua zvitsauko 13–21), apo Joshua 21:44 ichitaura nezvekukurirwa kwevavengi nekuva nesimba pamusoro penyika (Joshua zvitsauko 1–12). Kutarisa kumashure kose uku kurikuitwa kuburikidza nekutendeka kwaMwari. VaIsraeri vanofanira kurangarira nguva dzose kuti havagoni kukukurira vavengi vavo voga kana kutora nyika senhaka yavo voga—kunze kwekutendeka kwaMwari kushoko rake.

Akapa “nyika yose” (Joshua 21:43) kuisa “vavengi vavo vose mumavoko avo” (Joshua 21:44)  uye “kuburikidza nezvose zvaakavapikira” (Joshua 21:) “zvose zvakaitika” (Joshua 21:45). Kudzokororwa kweizwi rekuti kol, “zvose,” kanokwana ka 6 mumavhesi aya (Joshua 21:43-45), zvinosimbiswazve muidi rekuti nyika chipo chinobva kunaYahweh, Israeri haigoni kuva nechaingati yakazviwanira. NdiMwari akapika “kupa”  “nyika” nevavengi vake mumavoko avo. 

Kubudirira kwaIsraeri yose kunozivikanwa kuti kunobva kunaMwari chete uye kwakatendeka. Ndizvo zvazviri neruponeso rwedu: “Nekuti makaponeswa nenyasha, nekutenda; izvo zvisingabvi pamabasa, kuti kurege kuva nemunhu unozvikudza” (VaEfeso 2:8, 9).

Hongu, kusimbisa kutendeka kwaMwari, Pauro anonyora achiti: “Ishoko razvokwadi, rekuti kana tichifa pamwe naye, tichararamavo pamwechete naye. Kana tikatsungirira tichabata vushe pamwe chete naye; kana tikamuramba, iye uchatirambavo. Kana isi tisakatendeka, iye unoramba akatendeka, nekuti haangazvirambi iye” (2 Timoti 2:11–13).

`Ko kutendeka kwaMwari mukuchengeta zvitsidzo zvake kwatiri kunotipa sei rusimbiro rweremangwana risingakundikani? (VaHebheru 1 VaKorinde 10:13, 2 VaKorinde 1:18–20.)`
