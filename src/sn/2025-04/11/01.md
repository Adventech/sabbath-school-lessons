### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Joshua 22; VaEfeso 6:7; Johani 7:24; Numeri 25; Zvirevo 15:1; 1 Petro 3:8, 9.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Mhinduro nyoro inodzora kutsvamwa, asi shoko rinorwadza mwoyo rinomutsa” (Zvirevo 15:1).

Kugara nevamwe dzimwe nguva zvinogona kutungamirira kunetsana. Ichokwadi zvikuru sei kugara nevanhu vekereke, munowanikwa vanhu vanobva kwakasiyana-siyana nekuzvimiro zvakasiyana—dzimwe nguva vanotowunganidzwa munzvimbo imwe asi tsika dzavo dzakasiyana—ivo vachishanda nekugara panzvimbo imwechete nekuda kwechinangwa chakafanana.5-   

Vhiki ino tichadzidza Joshua 22 nedambudziko rakamuka kubva mukusanzwisisana kukuru pakati pevanhu. Panotangira bhuku, Joshua akaraira mamwe marudzi kuyambukira mhiri kwaJordani kunotora nyika, pamwechete nemarudzi ekumadokera kwaJordani (Joshua 1:12–18). Zvino basa rapera, vasununguka kudzokera havo. Asi kumavira zuva kwaJordani, vanovaka aritari inokonzera kushushikana kwemarudzi ekumadokera kwaJordani. 

Nemhaka yei zvinjodzi kumhanya kutaura zvausingazivi nezvezvinoitwa nevamwe? Tingakurudzira kubatana sei mukereke? Nemhaka yei zvakakosha kugara munhu achifunga nezvazvo nekusakanganiswa? Iyi ndiyo mimwe mibvunzo yatichatarisa vhiki ino. 

_*Dzidzo chevhiki ino kugadzirira Sabata December 13_
