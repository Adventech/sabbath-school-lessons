---
title:  Jehova Achakurwira
date:   22/10/2025
---

`Kana takatarisa pana Ekisodho 14:13, 14, 25, Mwari aida kuti vaIsraeri vapinde muhondo sei kubva pakutanga?`  

Munguva yedambudziko, vana veIsraeri pavakapinzwa parumananzombe, , “Mosesi akavapindura achiti, ‘Musatya henyu. Mirai makasimba mugovona sununguro yaJehova yaachakuitirai nhasi, nekuti vaEgipita vamunovona nhasi hamuchavavonezve’ ” (Ekisodho 14:13, 14). Kana takatarisa muBhaibheri, kana vaEgipita pachavo vakaziva zvazvaireva: “Ngatitize pamberi pevaIsraeri, nekuti Jehova inovarwira ivo, asi unorwa nevaEgipita” (Ekisodho 14:25).

Kurwira kwaMwari kunoshamisa kwevaIsraeri vakanga vasina chavaigona kuzviitira, vasina kudzidzira kurwa, vakanga vasina kana zano. Ekisodho yakaramba ichiinda mberi nenzira yayo, kushanduka kwenzira yaMwari achirwira vaIsraeri. Apa hondo haisi  kurwiwa naYahweh chete, asi Israeri inotarisirwa kurwavo (Ekisodho 14:14). Mwari ndiye murwi wacho; ndiye anotanga nyaya yacho. Anotara nzira yacho, nekuita zvaakaronga. Kana Yahweh akasarwira Israeri, havakundi. 

Ellen G. White anodudzira sekuti Mwari “akanga akaronga kuti vasawana nyika yechipikirwa nekurwa, asi nekuzviisa pasi nekuteerera mirairo yake.”—Ellen G. White, The Signs of the Times, September 2, 1880. Sekudzikinurwa kwekubva Egipita, Mwari aizovarwira. Zvavaifanira kungoita chete kumira vakasimba vachivona kusunungura kwaJehova. 

Nhoroondo inoratidza kuti nguva dzose Israeri payaivimba naMwari zvakazara, vakanga vasingafaniri kurwa (tarisa 2 Madzimambo 19, 2 Makoronike 32, Isaya 37).

Muzano raMwari rekutanga, Israeri yakanga isingambofaniri kurwa. Zvakabva mukusatenda kwavo, kwavaratidza shure kweEkisodho, Mwari paakaregerera kuti vave nerutivi muhondo nevaKenani. Nenzira imwechete, vakanga vasingambofaniri kusimudza kana munondo wekurwisa vaEgipita muEkisodho; vakanga vasingatofaniri kumborwa zvavo mukukunda vaKenani (Deuteronomio 7:17–19).

`“Kudai vaIsraeri vasina kunyunyutira Jehova, aingadai asina kubvumira vavengi vavo kurwa navo.”—Ellen G. White, The Story of Redemption, p. 134. Ko kunyunyuta kwaigona kukanganis avupenyu hwedu sei nhasi?`
