---
title:  Hondo Mudenga
date:   20/10/2025
---

`Joshua anonzwisisa kuti kurwisana uku chikamu chehondo huru. Tinozivei nezvehondo iyo Mwari Pachake ari mairi? Verenga Zvakazarurwa 12:7–9, Isaya 14:12–14, Ezekieri 28:11–19, and Danieri 10:12–14.`

Mwari akazadza nyika nezvisikwa zvaakaisamo zvinesimba resarudzo, chinhu chekutanga chaidiwa kuti vakwanise kuda. Vanogona kusarudza kuita kuda kwake kana kupikisana zvidiso zvake. Mumwe wevatumwa vaiva nesimba guru, Rusifa, akapandukira Mwari, akatora vatumwa vakawanda chaizvo.

Isaya na Ezekieri vanotaura nezvekurwisana, kana nepo vamwe vanodudzira vanoidza kuramba vachiti Isaya 14 na Ezekieri 28 ndedza mambo weBabironi newe Tire. Asi zviri pachena kuti Bhaibheri rinotaura nezvezvinhu zvekumusoro. Mambo weBabironi anoita seaiva kudenga pachigaro chaMwari (Isaya 14:12, 13), na mambo weTire anoita sekuti akagara muEdeni sekerubi inodzivirira gomo dzvene raMwari (Ezekieri 28:12–15). Hakuna chechokwadi pazviri zvose izvi nemadzimambo eBabironi neTire. 

Kana kuti zvinganzi nezvamambo wepanyika akanga asina chaanopomera uye kuti “iwe wakaisa chisimbiso chako pachiyero.” Zvimiro izvi zvinonongedzera kuhushe hunopfuvura hweBabironi neTire.

Isaya anotipa “mufananidzo” (VaHebheru  mashal), unotaura nezvaitaurwa zvaipfuvura nyaya iripo. Munyaya iyi, mambo weBabironi anomiririra shanduko yezvapanduka, kuzvikudza, nekunzwa asina chaanoshaya. Ezekieri anoisawo mupatsano wakafanana pakati pemuchinda weTire (Ezekieri 28:2) namambo weTire (Ezekieri 28:11, 12), apo muchinda, ane zvaanoita muvushe hwedenga. 

Kana takatarisa Danieri 10:12–14, zvisikwa izvi zvemudenga zvinokanganisa kuzadziswa kwezvinangwa zvaMwari panyika. Tinofanira kunzwisisa hukama pakati pedenga nenyika nekuda kwehondo idzi dzaIsraeri. Tinofanira kudzivona semabasa emasimba epanyika nehondo pakati paMwari naSatani, zvakaipa nezvakanaka—zvose zviine chinangwa chekudzorera kururamisira kwaMwari nerudo munyika yakatadza.

`Munzira dzipi umo tinoona, munyika ino pose patiri muhupenyu hwedu, hondo iyi ichitsviriridza pakati pezvakanaka nezvakaipa?`
