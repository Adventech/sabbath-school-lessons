---
title:  Mukuru Wehondo YaJehova
date:   19/10/2025
---

`Verenga Joshua 5:13–15. Ko vhesi iyi iri kuti kudini nezviri seri kwekukurirwa kwe Kenani?`

Israeri yakanga ichangoyambuka Jordani nekutsika nyika yevavengi. Rusvingo rukuru rweJeriko ruri pamberi pavo, masuwo akapfigwa chose (Joshua 6:1). Apa, vanhu vakanga vasingazivi nzira yehondo. Zvaitonyanya kunetsa, ndezvekuti vaIsraeri vane zvipfura mambwe, miseve, nemapfumo kunotora guta rakakomberedzwa nedzivirira kukombwa.

Mibvunzo yaJoshua kumunhu aakanga asingazivi uyu inopindurwa zvisina kujeka: “Kwete.” Mhinduro dzemutorwa uyu dzinoratidza kuti haadi kupinda muzvimiro zvose zvataurwa naJoshua. Saka mubvunzo chaiwo wakanga usiri wekuti ari kurutivi rwaJoshua here asi kutoti Joshua ari kurutivi rwake here?

`Enzanisa Joshua 5:14, 15 na 2 Madzimambo 6:8–17, Nehemia 9:6,  na Isaya 37:16. Ko unodzidzei nezvezita remukuru wehondo yaJehova?`

Kana nepo zita rekuti  “mukuru wehondo yaJehova” risinganzwisisiki muBhaibheri rechiHeberu, mubatanidzwa wemazwi aya “mukuru” ne “hondo” anowanza kuva nechekuita nemukuru wezvekurwisana. Izwi rekuti “hondo” muMagwaro rinogonawo kureva vatumwa kana zvisikwa zvekudenga.

Kristu asati aberekwa anovuya kuna Joshua asiri shamwari chete, kana kuva Mutungamiriri wezvokwadi wehondo yeIsraeri, asi seMutungamiriri weboka risati ravonekwa revatumwa rinebasa guru rakakurisa muhondo inopfuura yaJoshua ne vaKenani. Mhinduro yaJoshua inoratidza pachena kunzwisisa kwake kwezita reMukuru wehondo uyu. Akainzana naMwari, Joshua anowira pasi nechiso pamberi pake sechiratidzo chekuremekedza kukuru nekunamata (Joshua 5:14, Genesi 17:3, 2 Samueri 9:6, 2 Makoronike 20:18). Joshua akagadzirira kugashira mazano ekupinda muhondo zvinobatsira muhondo huru kwazvo iyo Mwari weHondo Pachake ari mairi. 

`Kunyaradzwai kwatinogona kuwana, kana tava kuziva kuti “Mukuru wehondo yaJehova” aripo kudzivirira vanhu vake?`
