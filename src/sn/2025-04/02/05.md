---
title:  Kupikisana Kwezvinokosheswa
date:   08/10/2025
---

`Verenga Joshua 9:1–20. Zvii zvakafanana nezvakasiyana munyaya dzaRahabi nevaGibeoni? Nemhaka yei dzine basa?`

Ichi chitsauko chaJoshua chinotanga nekutiudza kuti madzimambo mashanu eKenani aiwanzotonga maguta maduku akasarudza kuumba mubatanidzwa kurwisa vaIsraeri. Vagari veGibhiyoni vakasarudza kuita sungano nevaIsraeri.

Mukuda kunyengera vaIsaraeri kupinda musungano navo, vaGibeoni vakaronga kuita zano rekuva vamiririri vaibva kunyika yavatorwa. Kana takatarisa kuna Deuteronomio 20:10–18, Mwari akaisa musiyano pakati pevaKenani nevanhu vaigara kure neNyika Yechipikirwa. 

Izwi rinodudzirwa kuti “kungwara” kana kuti “kunyengera” rinogona kushandiswa mune zvakanaka, richireva uchenjeri kana kungwara (Zvirevo 1:4; Zvirevo 8:5, 12), kana mune zvakaipa, richireva kuita chitsotsi (Ekisodho 21:14, 1 Samueri 23:22, Mapisarema 83:3). Panyaya yevaGibhiyoni, kuseri kwechiito chavo chekunyepedzera pane chinangwa chisina kuparadza chekuzvidzivirira.

Mashoko evaGibeoni akafanana chaizvo neaRahabi. Vose vakati vaiziva simba raMwari wevaIsraeri, vose vaiziva kubudirira kwevaIsraeri kwakanga kusiri kwekugona kwevanhu. Kusiyana kwazvo nevamwe vaKenani, ava havapandukiri mazano aYahweh ekupa vaIsraeri nyika, uye vakabvuma kuti Jehova chaivo ndivo vaiva vachidzinga ndudzi idzi pamberi pevaIsraeri. Nhau dzekudzikinurwa kubva kuEgipita, kukurirwa kwaSihoni naOgi, zvakasunda vose Rahabi nevaGibeoni kutsvaka kudyidzana nevaIsraeri. Asi pane kutsvaka kuzivisa kwakazara kuzvipira kwavo kuna Mwari wevaIsraeri, sezvakaitwa naRahabi, vaGibeoni vakasarudza kushandisa vungwaru.

Mutemo waMosesi waibvumira kutsvaka kuda kwaMwari panyaya dzakadai (Numeri 27:16–21). Joshua ingadai akabvunza kuda kwaShe nekuzvidzivirira kunyengedzwa nevaGibeoni. 

Chikuru muhutungamiriri hwaMwari, kana hwemutungamiriri wese weChikristu, kutsvaka kuda kwaMwari (1 Makoronike 28:9, 2 Makoronike 15:2, 2 Makoronike 18:4, 2 Makoronike 20:4). Kurega kuzviita, vaIsraeri vakamanikidzwa kutyora mirairo mikuru yekukukurira nyika kana kutyora mhiko muzita raShe, chinenge chasungwa. 

`Kangani uchitambudzika nesarudzo pakati pezviri zvakakosha zvinotaurwa neBhaibheri?`
