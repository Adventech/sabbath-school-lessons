---
title:  Kudzidza Kuenda Mberi
date:   03/10/2025
---

“Muzvitsidzo zvake neyambiro, Jesu anotaura ini. Mwari akada nyika, nekudaro wakapa Mwanakomana wake wakaberekwa mumwe woga kuti ani-nani anotenda maari arege kufa asi ave nevupenyu husingaperi. Zvinowanikwa mushoko raMwari ndezvangu. Munamato nechipikirwa, mutemo neyambiro, ndezvangu. . . . Kutenda kuchigashira nekurarama zvokwadi iyi, zvinova chimiro nesimba rekuraramira. Shoko raMwari, rakagashirwa mumwoyo, rinogadzirisa pfungwa, nekupinda mukubatsira kukura kwechimiro.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, pp. 390, 391.

“Hakuna chinhu chinofanira kufungwa zvakanyanya nekuperera, kudzokororwa nguva nenguva, kana kusimbiswa mupfungwa mevanhu vose sekukwanisa kuzviruramisa kwemutadzi nekumabasa ake akanaka. Ruponeso runovuya kuburikidza nekutenda munaJesu woga.”—Ellen G. White, Faith and Works, p. 19.

**Mibvunzo Yekutaurirana**: 

`Zvisinei nekusiyana kwevupenyu hwedu nehwaJoshua, zvidzidzo zvipi zvatingazviwanira kubva muhupenyu hwake zvatingashandisa mune hwedu? Nemhaka yei hazvo, tichifanira kugara takachengeta munguva nezvaiitika mo apo tinoda kufananidza zviviri izvi?`

`Tauriranai hukama pakati pezvitsidzo zvaMwari nekumuteerera kwedu. Zvinobatsirana sei? Ndeipi njodzi iri mukusimbira chimwe tichisiya chimwe? Kumhanya nemutemo tichisiya nyasha kana kumhanya nenyasha tichisiya mutemo?`

`Takatsamira pachidzidzo chevhiki ino, ungati kubudirira kuitei kana takatarisa muBhaibheri? Ko muChikristu chii chinonzi kubudirira?`
