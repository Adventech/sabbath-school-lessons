### Verenga  Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Deuteronomio 18:15–22; Joshua 1; VaHebheru 6:17, 18; VaEfeso 6:10–18; Mapisarema 1:1–3; VaRoma 3:31.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Asi usimbe nekutsunga mwoyo kwazvo, kuti uchenjere kuita murairo wandakaraira muranda wangu Mosisi; usatsauka kwauri, kana kurudyi kana kuruboshwe, kune uve nemufaro pose paunoenda” (Joshua 1:7.) 

Benjamin Zander, mutungamiriri wemimhanzi weBoston Philharmonic Orchestra, akadzidzisa kirasi yekududzira mimhanzi. Akaona kushushikana kwevadzidzi pavakatarisana nekuongororwa kwekuita kwavo. Kuti aise vadzidzi vakasununguka uye kuvavhura kune kugona kwavo kuzere, akazivisa pazuva rekutanga rekirasi kuti munhu wese aizowana "A." Iyi "A" yakanga isiri tarisiro yekurarama maererano  "asi mukana wekurarama mairi." Chinhu chega chinodiwa chaive chekuti vadzidzi vanyore tsamba mukati memavhiki maviri ekutanga esemester asi yakanyorwa pakupera kwekirasi. Tsamba yacho yakatsanangura kuti nei vaikodzera kukwidziridzwa kwepamusoro.

Bhuku raJoshua rinotaura nezvemikana mitsva. Mosesi, uyo akanga atonga makore 40 enhoroondo yevaIsraeri, akanga ari mutungamiri wenyika. Ekisodho kubva muIjipiti uye kudzungaira murenje, zvinosuwisa zvakaratidzirwa nekupanduka uye kusindimara, zvakanga zvapera. Chizvarwa chitsva, chakanga chagadzirira kuteerera Mwari, chakanga chakagadzirira kupinda muNyika Yakapikirwa, kwete setarisiro yekurarama maererano nacho asi semukana wekurarama mairi. 

Ngationgororei kuti Mwari akazarura sei chitsauko chitsva muupenyu hwevaIsraeri, uye kuti angaitewo sei saizvozvo muupenyu hwedu.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo chevhiki ino kugadzirira Sabata, October 4._
