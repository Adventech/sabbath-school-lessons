---
title:  Mosesi Mutsva
date:   28/09/2025
---

`Verenga Deuteronomio 18:15–22 na Joshua 1:1–9. Zvakakosherei kuti bhuku raJoshua ritange nekudzokorora chitsidzo chinevukama nezvaizoitika Mosesi afa?`
 
Kunyange zvazvo Mosesi akanga afa uye mutungamiri mutsva, Joshua, akanga agadzwa naMwari, pane zvakafanana pakati pavo. Mwari akaudza vanhu vake kuti vapinde munyika yakapikirwa madzitateguru avo. Jehovha akati kuna Joshua, "Nzvimbo dzose dzinotsika tsoka dzenyu, ndakakupai, sezvandakaudza Mozisi." (Josh. 1: 3, NKJV). Joshua aizopedzisa basa rakanga rapiwa Mosesi pakutanga. Zvechokwadi akanga ari mutsva Mosesi.


`Verenga Ekisodho 33:11; Numeri 14:6, 30, 38; Numeri 27:18; Numeri 32:12; Deuteronomio 1:38; Deuteronomio 31:23; na Deuteronomio 34:9. Ko matsamba aya anotivudzei nezva Joshua?`

Apa, chitsidzo chaMwari chaiva “chekutsvaga” muprofita akafanana naMosesi (Deuteronomio 18:15) chaikwanisika chakanga chisiri chinhu chatoitika. Mashoko ekutanga ebhuku raJoshua anotirangaridza nezvechitsidzo, panguva imwechete iyo, kutipa chishuvo chekutarisira kuzadziswa kwacho. 

Kunyange zvazvo Mosesi akafa, achiri kutonga chitsauko chekutanga. Zita rake rakataurwa kagumi, asi Joshua anongova mana chete. Mosesi anodaidzwa kuti “muranda waJehova” asi Joshua achinzi mubatsiri “waMosesi” (Joshua 1:1). Zvichatora vupenyu hose hwekutendeka nekushanda nekuteerera kuti Joshua agashire zita rekuti “muranda waJehova” (Joshua 24:29).

Kana nepo chitsauko chekutanga chaJoshua chichibata kusiirana pakati pevatungamiriri vaviri vakuru vaIsraeri ava, mukuru munyaya dzose idzi ndiJehova Pachake, uyo anemashoko anovhura bhuku iri nekutungamirira kwake kunovonekwa zvikuru. Hazvina mubvunzo kuti mutungamiriri chaiye waIsraeri ndiani.

`Kwemazera nemazera, Mwari akadana vanhu kuti vatungamirire vanhu vake. Nei zvakakosha kurangarira kuti mutungamiriri asingavonekwi wechokwadi chaiye ndiani?`
