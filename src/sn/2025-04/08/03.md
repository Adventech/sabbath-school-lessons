---
title:  Ndipei Gomo Iri 
date:   17/11/2025
---

`Verenga Joshua 14:6–14, Numeri 14:24, Numeri 32:12, Deuteronomio 1:36, na Ruka 6:45. Ungatsanangura Karebi na Joshua sei? Zvinorevei kutevera Mwari zvakazara?`

Karebi haana kumbokanganwa chitsidzo chaakanga apiwa naMwari kuburikidza naMosesi: chekuti iye aizopinda munyika umo tsoka dzake dzakanga dzatsika (Numeri 14:24). Makore 40 apera, anotaura zvaakambotaura nezvenyika seshoko “zvariri mumwoyo make” (Joshua 14:7). Shoko rake raibva mune zvaaidaira, nekutungamirira nekubatsira kwaMwari, Israeri yaitora nyika.

Mukusiyana neshoko revamwe vashori 10, vakaisa kutya pakati pevaIsraeri, Karebi akaratidza kuvimba kwakaperera nekuzvipira kuchitsidzo chaMwari. Mazwi echiHeberu, anoreva kuti “Ndakanga ndakazara muna Mwari” (Joshua 14:8), inzira pfupi yenyaudzosingwi: “Mwoyo wangu waitevera Mwari zvakazara,” kana kuti “Ndakazadza mwoyo wangu ndirikumashure nekutevera Mwari.” Mukusiyana nevamwe vakafamba vachitevera zvimwari zvisingazivikanwi vasingateveri  zvakazara, mwoyo waKarebi wakanga wakazvipira kuna Mwari. 

Mazwi mamwechete anodzokororwazve pamberi apo kaviri, achisimbisa kutendeka kwemwoyo waKarebi (Joshua 14:9, 14). Paakazviisa panowirirana nezvinonzi naMwari Pachake “mweya wakasiyana” (Numeri 14:24, ) ndizvo zvaiva Karebi mukusiyana nevamwe vashori 10. Kana pamakore 85, akaramba ari muenzaniso wezvingaitwa naMwari kuburikidza nevanhu vane mwoyo yakaperera kuita kuda kwake.

Karebi akanzwisisa kuti nyika yaipiwa rudzi rumwe nerumwe yaizowirirana nezvavaitarisira kubva pazvitsidzo zvaMwari nekukura kwenyika yavaida nekutenda. Zvitsidzo zvaMwari hazvingoitiki, zvisinei nekuda kana kusada kwedu. Asi zvinotoda kuperekedzwa nekutenda nemabasa. Izwi rechiHeberu ’ulay, “zvimwe” (Joshua 14:12), rinogona kuratidza kutya kana kunyunyuta, asi rinowanza kureva tariro nekutarisira kuti pane zvakanaka zvichaitika (Gen. 16:2; Numeri 22:6, 11; Numeri 23:3).

`Ndezvipi zvikanganiso, “zviduku” zvinowumba, zvinogona kutikanganisa kutevera Mwari?`
