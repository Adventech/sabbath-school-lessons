---
title:  Kutanga Sungano
date:   09/11/2025
---

`Verenga Joshua 5:1–7. Nemhaka yei Mwari akaraira Joshua kudzingisa vana vezera rechipiri veIsraeri panguva yehondo?`

Mushure mekunovona nyika yose, mashoko ekukurudzira evashori, nekuyambuka Jordani zvinoshamisa, tinogona kutarisira kutopinda muhondo nemuvengi. Asi kwete kune zvimwe chakanyanya kukosha kupfuura kukurira vavengi: sungano yeIsraeri naMwari. 

Chizvarwa chitsva chisati chapinda mukutora ivhu, vaifanira kunyatsoziva nezvehukama hwavo hwakakosha neMuridzi wenyika. . Rumutsiriro rwezviratidzo zvesungano runoitwa mushure mekuyambutswa Jordani zvinoshamisa. 

Sungano yedu naMwari inofanira kugara iri mhinduro yekutenda kwedu zvaakatiitira, isamboita sekuda kuwana zvinhu izvi nekuda kwemabasa edu ekuteerera zvaakatema. (Pfungwa imwecheteyo, pasina mubvunzo, yaive yakakosha pakurwisana kwaPauro nevaya vakasimbirira kuti varume vechiHedheni vatendeuke vadzingiswe, sezvinoonekwa zvakajeka mutsamba yake kuvaGaratia.)

Israeri yaive padanho remushandirapamwe mukuru wemauto munhoroondo yayo, uye isu taizotarisira kuti musasa wese uve wakabatikana nekugadzirira kwehondo. Zvaive, asi kwete mupfungwa yechinyakare. Panzvimbo pekusunga mabhiza uye kurodza minondo, vakaita tsika yakasiya vazhinji vemauto ehondo vari panjodzi kwemazuva angangoita matatu. 

Vakazviitira kupemberera hukama kwavo naMwari, akavadzikinura kubva kuEgipita. Nemhaka yei? Nekuti vakaziva kuti hondo haisi yavo asi ndeya Mwari. Ndiye anovapa kukurira. Jesu akatanga zano rakadarovo asi rakasiyana zvishoma: “Tangai kutsvaka vushe hwaMwari nekururama kwake, izvi zvose zvichawedzerwa kwamuri” (Mateo 6:33). Nguva zhinji, hupenyu hwose hunoita sehunotimanikidza kuti tikurumidze muzvinhu zvakakosha zvakawanda zvekuti tinokanganwa kuisa nguva kune zvakakosha chaizvo muvupenyu hwedu: kuzvipira zuva nezuva kunaKristu.

`Funga nezvenguva dzawakashaya hako nguva naMwari nekuda kwezvinhu zvawaiti “zvakanyanya kukosha”. Nemhaka yei izvi zvinyore kuita, Uye tingazvikurira sei.`
