---
title:  Kushuvira Kuvapo Kwake
date:   13/11/2025
---

`Verenga Joshua 18:1, 2. Chii chakaitwa na Joshua chakambomisa kugovewa kwenyika?`

Kutsanangura nzvimbo dzakapiwa marudzi makuru maviri kwapera kumadokera kweJordani nehafu yerudzi rwaManase, ndima iyi inoratidza wungano yeboka rose paShiro, apo nyika inogovewa kumarudzi maduku manomwe aanga asara. 

Kudzikwa kwesenjuwari, “Imba yangu My,” kunomiririra kuzadziswa kwechitsidzo chaMwari kwekugara pakati pevanhu vake (Ekisodho 25:8; Revhitiko 26:11, 12) nekuratidza musoro wenyaya yebhuku rose: kuvapo kwaMwari pakati pevaIsraeri kwaita kuti nyika ive yavo nekuramba iri nzira yavo yekuwana maropafadzo, achibva asvitswavo kunyika yose (Gen. 12:3). Kunamatwa kwaMwari ndiko kunobva kwava pakati pezvinhu zvose, kusanganisira kukurirwa kwenzvimbo dzakagovewa! Kuvapo kwesenjuwari, neTembere yakazovakwa pamberi apo, zvingadai zvakabatsira vanhu kuvona huvepo hwaMwari pakati pavo nezvavaitarisirwa kuchengetedza sungano.

`Verenga VaHebheru  6:19, 20; VaHebheru  9:11, 12; na VaHebheru  10:19–23. Ko isu Makristu asina senjuwari pakati pedu achivonekwa sekuvapo kwaMwari pakati pedu, tingadzidzei kubva kuna Joshua?`

Kuvapo kwesenjuwari hakufaniri kushamisa, nekuti nyaya yesenjuwari yakagara irimo muzvinyorwa zvaJoshua kuburikidza neareka yesungano. Ichi chivezwa chaiva chakakosha kwazvo muImba Tsvene-tsvene, uye yairatidza miganhu yezvikamu zviviri zvebhuku: kuyambuka kwekukurira. Zvino pakugadzika tabernakeri panzvimbo iyi munguva yekukovewa kwenyika, Joshua anoratidza kuti hupenyu hweIsraeri hwose hunotenderera pasenjuwari, muzinda mukuru waYahweh panyika. 

Zvakatonyanya kukosha kwatiri, seMakristu anorarama mumazuva ari pedyo neZuva Chairo Reruchenuro,  kuti tiise meso edu pasenjuwari yekudenga apo tinoramba tichitambudzana nemitimbanyoka yemazuva ano inodenha kutenda, tariro nemhaka yedu yekunamata. Patinoramba takatsamira panezvakaitwa naKristu paMuchinjikwa nemusenjuwari yekudenga, tinogona kutarisa mberi nekutenda kunguva iyo Mwari achazogarazve pakati pevanhu vake, asi apa kuchava nekusingaperi. (Enzanisa na Zvakazarurwa 21:3.)
