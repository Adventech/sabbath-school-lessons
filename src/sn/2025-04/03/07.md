---
title:  Kudzidza Kuenda Mberi
date:   17/10/2025
---

“Dzidza zvakanaka nyaya yevaIsraeri  murwendo rwekuKenani. Dzidza zvitsauko 3 ne 4 zvebhuku raJoshua, uchinyora gadziriro dzavo nekuyambuka kwavo Jordani vachipinda munyika yechipikirwa. Tinofanira kuchengeta mwoyo nepfungwa dzakadzidziswa, nekugara tichifunga zvidzidzo izvo Ishe vakadzidzisa vanhu vavo. Nezvazvinorevavo kwatiri sekwavari, sekudzidzisa kweshoko rake kunofadza nekupa zvidzidzo zvinogara.”—Ellen G. White Comments, The SDA Bible Commentary, vol. 2, p. 994.

“Israeri yakapinda mudambudziko guru kwazvo rekukanganwa Mwari nekutungamirirwa kuzvifananidzo kupfuura vanhu vekare. ZviMwari zvakawanda zvirikunamatwa, kunyange nevanochengeta Sabata chaivo. Mwari akaraira vanhu vake zvakasimba kuti vazvichenjerere kuti vasanamata zvifananidzo, nekuti vaigona kubviswa kwaari, kutuka kwake kwaizogara pamusoro pavo, nepo kana vaizomuda nemoyo wavo wose, nemweya wavo wose, uye nesimba ravo rose, aizovakomborera zvakanyanya mudengu uye muchitoro,  uye achabvisa zvirwere pakati pavo."—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 1, p. 609.

**Mibvunzo Yekutaurirana**:

`Tauriranai mukirasi chishamiso chekuyambuka Jordani? Ungatsanangura sei zvishamiso? Nemhaka yei zvichiita sekuti Mwari haasi kumboita zvishamiso zvakadaro mazuva ano?`

`Ungapa mazano api mukirasi ekudzivirira kukanganwa zvakaitwa naMwari kwauri kana kwamuri sekereke? Zvakakosha hazvo kuti tive nevukama hwakasimba naMwari, uye kuti kutenda kwedu hakutsamiri pane zvikuru zvatakaitirwa kare naMwari?`

`Ko Sabata unofunga kuti kune rumwe rutivi rungatibatsira sei kurangarira kupindira kwaMwari muhupenyu hwedu, uye kune rimwe divi zvingatipa sei zvishoma kubva muzvidzidzo zvezororo rehumambo? Ko Sabata inonongedza sei kune zvatinofanira kurangarira nezvatinofanira kutarisira mune remangwana?`
