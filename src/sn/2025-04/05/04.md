---
title:  Kubvisa Panzvimbo Kana Kuvuraiwa?
date:   28/10/2025
---

`Enzanisa Ekisodho 23:28–30; Ekisodho 33:2; Ekisodho 34:11; Numeri 33:52; na Deuteronomio 7:20 na Ekisodho 34:13; Deuteronomio 7:5; Deuteronomio 9:3; Deuteronomio 12:2, 3; and Deuteronomio 31:3, 4. Ko mavhesi aya anoratidzei nezvechinangwa chakukurira nekwaisvika kuparadza?`

Chinangwa chaMwari chepakutanga kuva Kenani chakanga chisiri chekuvavuraya, asi kuvabvisa pavaigara. Kutarisisa matsamba anotaura nezvenzira iyo vaIsraeri vaifanira kuita nayo hondo dzavo inoratidza kuti kwaiva kubviswa pavagere, kudzingwa kwevagari veNyika Yechipikirwa. Mamwe mashoko anoratidza kuparadza ane Israeri saiyo iripanyaya sezvinhu chisina vupenyu, chakafanana nezvinhu zvainamatwa  nezvinhu zvaifanira kuparadzwa. Zvinoratidza pachena kuti nzvimbo dzainamatirwa zviMwari nemaaritari ndidzo dzaiva nzvimbo dzakakosha muchinamato chevaKenani. 

Hondo tsvene dzaiva dzekubvisa tsika dzakawora dzevaKenani munzvimbo dzavaigara. Kudzivirira kusvibiswa, Israeri yaifanira kuparadza zvose zvaingavakonzera kuvoravo. Asi kune vamwe vagari veKenani vakavona simba raMwari rekutonga vakabva munyika dzavo vachiinda kunedzimwe nyika semunhu mumwechete kana seboka guru (Joshua 2:9–14; enzanisa  na Vatongi 1:24–26). Nzvimbo chete yevaKenani yakanzi ngaiparadzwe ndedze avo chete vakatizira kumaguta aiva nerusvingo, vachuratidza kuramba zano raMwari revaIsrari, nekuwomesa mwoyo yavo (Joshua 11:19, 20).

Asi izvi zvinomutsa mubvunz: kana zano rakutanga raiva rekubvusa vaKenani kubva munzvimbo dzavaigara, risiri rekuvavuraya, saka vaIsraeri vakavurairei vanhu vakawanda kudaro?

Wongororo yematsamba anotaura nezvehondo dzekubvisa vaKenani inoratidza kuti chinangwa chekutanga chehondo idzi chairwisa huipi hweruzhinji rwevaKenani. Asi ruzhinji rweKenani saFarao rwakawomesa mwoyo, zvekuti vakava setsika dzavo dzakaipa dzaifanira kuparadzwavo.

`Ndezvipi mauri nemutsika dzako zvinofanirwa kudzurwa kana kuvuraiwa?`
