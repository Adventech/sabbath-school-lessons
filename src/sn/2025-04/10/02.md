---
title:  Mufananidzo YemuBhaibheri
date:   30/11/2025
---

`Dzidza Magwaro anotevera anotaura nezvemifananidzo ugoedza kutsanangura chinonzi mifananidzo yemuBhaibheri: VaRoma 5:14, 1 VaKorinde 10:1–13, VaHebheru 8:5, na VaHebheru 9:23.`

Ndima dzemuBhaibheri idzi dzinoshandisa izwi rekuti “mufananidzo” (Giriki typos) kana “chinhu icho chirimumvuri wechinhu chaicho” (Giriki antitypos) kutaura nezvenzira idzo munyori weTestamende Itsva anotsanangura vukama pakati pendima yemuTestamende Yakare nechiitiko, nezvachichareva munguva yake, kana munguva inotevera.

Mifananidzo yemimvuri inodudzira nezvevanhu, zviitiko, kana hurongwa hunomiririra Jesu kana chimwe chezvakaitika muevhangeri. Mufananidzo unowirirana nechinhu chaicho seforoma inoratidza chimiro chechinhu chichabuda imomo, kunyange zvachichazova, nekuzadziswa kwacho naicho. Saka, mufananidzo weBhaibheri wakanga wakaita sezvaidiwa nedenga zvakasimba kana zvichivonekwa mupfungwa dzaMwari, zvichibatsira kugadzirisa zvemangwana (chaizvo izvo). 

Zvakakosha kuziva kuti vanyori veTestamende Itsva havana kungogashira chero chinhu chavaingofungira kuti chinomiririra zvirimuTestamende Yakare. Mufananidzo wose weTestamende Yakare unozadziswa nezvinyorwa zvechiprofita zviri muTestamende Itsva zvisati zvasvika.

**Tarisa kuti Davidi anovonekwa sei muTestamende Yakare, nezvaaimirira muItsva. Tinodzidzei nezvemashandiro emifananidzo kubva pamuenzaniso uyu?**

`a. Davidi (Mapisarema 22:1, 14–18): `

`b. Davidi mutsva (Jeremia 23:5; Zakaria 9:5, 6; Zakaria 11:1–5): `

`c. Davidi chaiye (Johani 19:24): `

Nekutarisa pandima idzi, tinovona kutiTestamende Yakare pachayo inotibatsira kunzwisisa zvinomiririrwa muMagwaro. Kureva kuti, vanyori veTestamende Itsva, avo vane Magwaro avo aiva Testamende Yakare, vaifemerwa neMweya Mutsvene kushandisa mifananidzo yeTestamende Yakare kuratidza “zvokwadi zvanhasi” (2 Petro 1:12), zvikuru sei pamusoro paJesu nebasa rake.
