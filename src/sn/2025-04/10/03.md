---
title:  Mufananidzo Nechinhu Chaicho 
date:   01/12/2025
---

Vadudziri veBhaibheri havagoni kupa chaizvo-izvo zvinofanira kuva mufananidzo kana kuti ungazadziswa sei muTestamende Itsva nekumberi uko. Bhaibheri pacharo rinopa miganhu nemirairo yekushandisa mifananidzo.

Nenzira imwechete iyi, Testamende Itsva inoisa pachena kuzadziswa kwemifananidzo nenzira nhatu: (1) muvupenyu hwaKristu (mukurarama kwaJesu) (2) muvupenyu hwekereke (mubasa rekereke), ne(3) munguva yekupera kwenguva (mukuzadziswa kwenguva).

Tinogona kuwana mifananidzo iyi nezvayainomiririra muBhaibheri rose, uye zvinobatsira chaizvo mukuratidza vaverengi kunzwisisa Bhaibheri nezvokwadi zviri muShoko raMwari nedzidziso dzaJesu, ruponeso, netariro yatinayo pakupedzisira.

**Tarisa pamifananidzo yeTestamende Yakare inotevera: Israeri, Ekisodho, nesenjuwari. Ko chimwe nechimwe chazvo chinozadziswa sei muzvinhu chaizvo zvitatu: munaKristu, mukereke, nemuzviitiko zvemazuva ekupedzisira?**

1. Muna Israeri
	- a. Munguva yaKristu (Mateo 2:15): 
	- b. Munguva yekereke (VaGaratia 6:16): 
	- c. Muzviitiko zvemazuva ekupedzisira (Zvakazarurwa 7:4-8, 14): 
2. MuEkisodho	
	- a. Munguva yaKristu (Mateo 2:19–21): 
	- b. Munguva yekereke (2 VaKorinde 6:17): 
	- c. Muzviitiko zvemazuva ekupedzisira (Zvakazarurwa 18:4): 
3. Musenjuwari 
	- a. Munguva yaKristu (Johani 1:14, Johani 2:21, Mateo 26:61): 
	- b. Munguva yekereke (1 VaKorinde 3:16, 17; 2 VaKorinde 6:16): 	
	- c. Muzviitiko zvemazuva ekupedzisira (Zvakazarurwa 3:12; 11:19, 21:3; 21:22).

“Sezvo Magwaro aneMunyori mumwechete, ndima dzakasiyana-siyana dzeMagwaro dzinowirirana chaizvo. . . . Dzidziso dzose dzemuBhaibheri dzinoerera mukufamba kwadzo; dudziro dzendima imwe neimwe dzinonopindirana nendima imwe neimwe mudzidziso huru yeMagwaro ose.”—Handbook of Seventh-day Adventist Theology, p. 65.
