---
title:  Joshua, Mufananidzo 
date:   02/12/2025
---

`Takatarisa nzira yemifananidzo yemuBhaibheri ndezvipi zvinofambidzana nekuwirirana muhupenyu hwaMosesi nehwa Joshua?  Ekisodho 3:1, 2; Joshua 1:1–3; Numeri 13:1, 2; Joshua 2:1; Ekisodho 3:5; Joshua 5:15.`

Sezvatakaona muvhiki rekutanga, Joshua anoratidzwa saMosesi mutsva uyo, muhupenyu hwechizvarwa chechipiri, anodzokorora nhanho dzakakosha dzeEksodho kubva kuEgipita. Kungofanana naMosesi, akatumirwa kusangana naMwari pachake. Pasi pehutungamiriri hwevaIsraeri, mukurumbira weIsraeri pakati pemarudzi unokonzera kutya. Mosesi akatungamirira vaIsraeri kuyambuka Gungwa Dzvuku, uye Joshua akatungamirira vaIsraeri kuyambuka Jorodhani nenzira inoshamisa. Vatungamiriri vaviri ava vanoyeuchidzwa nezvekukosha kwekudzingiswa uye kukosha kwePaseka. Mana yakatanga kudonha munguva yaMosesi, uye inopera naJoshua. Vose vari vaviri vanorayirwa kuti vabvise shangu dzavo. Ruoko rwakatambanudzwa rwevaviri runoratidza kukunda kweIsraeri. Mosesi akapa mirayiridzo yokugoverwa kwenyika uye kuvaka maguta ekupotera. Joshua akateerera mirayiridzo yacho. Vose vari vaviri vanopa hurukuro yekukwazisa rudzi uye vanovandudza sungano yevanhu pakupera kwehushumiri hwavo.

`Dzidza Deuteronomio 18:15–19, Deuteronomio 34:10–12, Johani 1:21, Mabasa 3:22–26, na Mabasa 7:37. Ndiani anozadzisa chiprofita ichi chaMosesi nezvemuprofita akafanana naye? Ko Joshua anopinda sei mumufananidzo uyu? `

Vupenyu hwaJoshua hwaiva kuzadzisa chimwe chezviprofita zvakaitwa naMosesi (Deuteronomio 18:15, 18). Asi chiprofita chakaitwa naMosesi hachina kuzadziswa muzvinhu zvose. Asi muna Messia ndimo munozadziswa zvose. Aiziva Baba murudo rwavo (Johani 1:14, 18); Akanga ari wazvokwadi sezvakaratidzwa naMwari (Ruka 10:22, Johani 14:6, Mateo 22:16). Mwari zvechokwadi akaisa mashoko ake mumuromo wake (Johani 14:24). Saka, vupenyu hwaMosesi nehwaJoshua hwakava mifananidzo inomiririra Messia, Jesu aizovuya. 

`Ko Jesu akakosha sei murwendo rwako naMwari? Ko nemhaka yei Jesu, nezvaakakuitira, ari hwaro hwevupenyu hwako hose hweChikristu?`
