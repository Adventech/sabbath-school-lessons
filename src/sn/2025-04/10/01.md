### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
1 VaKorinde 10:1–13; Mateo 2:15; Joshua 1:1–3; Mabasa 3:22–26; VaHebheru 3:7—4:11; 2 VaKorinde 10:3–5.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Zvino izvi zvakavawira, kuti zvive zviratidzo; zvakanyorwa kuti tinyeverwe isu, takasvikirwa nekuguma kwenyika” (1 VaKorinde 10:11).

Mubhuku raJoshua, kune zvinoratidza sekuti vupenyu hwemunhu anotaurwa nezvake zvakanyanya, hunotaura nezvemumwe asiri iye pachake. Tinovona murairo uyu muBhaibheri rose, sezvakaita nyika yeKenani, mufananidzo wetariro isingaperi munyika yaitwa itsva. Hongu, senjuwari yepanyika yainongedzera kune yakakura chaiyo: “Asi Kristu wakati asvika ari muprisita mukuru wezvinhu zvakanaka zvinovuya, akapinda napatabernakeri yakpfuvura pakukura nepakukwana, isina kuitwa nevoko ndiko kuti, isati iri yekusikwa kuno” ( 9:11, ).

Asi mubvunzo inobva wamuka: Munzira dzipi umo Joshua anonongedzera kune zvichazadziswa kumberi uko? Tingaziva sei kuti kududzira Bhaibheri kwakadai kwakanaka? Ndeipi mirairo yekudzidza Bhaibheri inotungamirira kushanda kwebhuku raJoshua muTestamende Itsva nezviripo nezvichazovuyawo?

Vhiki ino tichatarisa pamirairo yekudzidza Bhaibheri munyaya dzemifananidzo inomiririra zvimwe zvinhu. Tichadzida kuti Bhaibheri pacharo rine zvinoratidza mifananidzo inoratidza zvimwe zvazvinomiririra nekuzadziswa mukereke, nemukuratidzwa kwezvinhu zvose zvemunhoondo yevanhu.

_*Dzidzo chevhiki ino kugadzirira Sabata, December 6._
