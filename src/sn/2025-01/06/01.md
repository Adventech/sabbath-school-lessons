### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Mapisarema 33:5, Mapisarema 85:10, Deut. 32:4, Jakobho 1:17, Tito 1:2, Ekisodo 32:14, Mateo 5:43–48

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Asi anozvirumbidza ngaazvirumbidze pamusoro pechinhu ichi, kuti anondinzwisisa, nekundiziva Ini, kuti Ndini Jehova unoita vunyoro nekururamisira, nekururama panyika, nekuti ndinofarira zvinhu izvi-ndizvo zvinotaura Jehova” (Jeremia 9: 24).

Kumabvazuva Kumusoro uko, “zvimwari” zvendudzi zvakanga zvisingashanduki,
kana kuita zvakaipa kana kusazivisa zvazvinoda, asi zvairairawo kuitirwa
zvinorwadza, zvakafanana nekuda kupisirwa vana vaduku. Kunyange zvakadaro
ruzhinji rwevahedheni rwakanga rusingaregi kuita zvazvaida, saka vakanga
vasingadi kuzvitsamwisa “zvimwari izvi”.

Muna Deuteronomio 32:17, kuseri “kwezvimwari” izvi kwaiva nemadhimoni (tarisawo  1 Vakorinde 10:20, 21). Nenzira dzavo dzakunamata dzaiva dzakanakira kuzviita, zvichisiya vanhu vari murima guru rezvakaipa.

Mwari wemuBhaibheri akanga akasiyana chaizvo nemasimba aya emadhimoni. Yahweh akaperera murunako nechimiro chake hachishanduki. Saka chikonzero chete chekunaka kusingaperi kwake chinogona kutipa tariro, yazvino neyekusingaperi. 

Mukusiyana kukuru nezvimwari zvenyika dzekare, kana “zvimwari” zvemazuva ano, Yahweh anehanya zvakanyanya nezvakaipa, kutambudzika, kusaruramisira nekudzvinyirira—zvose zvaaigara achishora zvisina mubvunzo. Uyewo, zvakanyanya kukosha, nerimwe remazuva achazviparadza wo.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo chevhiki ino kugadzirira Sabata, February 8_
