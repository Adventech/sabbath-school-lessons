### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Jobho 30:26; Mateo27:46; Jobho 38:1–12; Mapisarema 73; Genesisi 2:16, 17; Zvakazarurwa. 21:3, 4.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Nekuti Mwari achapukuta misodzi yose pameso avo; rufu haruchavipo, kana kuchema kana kurira, kana kutambudzika hazvingavipo, nekuti zvekutanga zvapfuvura” (Zvakazarurwa 21: 4).

Zvimwe dambudziko guru kwazvo kuMakristu idambudziko rezvakaipa-kukwanisa kutsanangura kunaka kwaMwari, nerudo rwake zvichienzaniswa nezvakaipa zviri munyika, zvanyanyisa wo ikozvinowo?

Iri idambudziko revadzidzi asi rinonetsawo vanhu vakawanda richikonzerawo vamwe kuti vasasvika pakuziva nekuda Mwari. 

“Kupfungwa dzakawanda chikonzero chezvivi nechikonzero chekurarama zvinhu zvinovapa kupokana kukuru mupfungwa. Vanovona basa rezvakaipa, namatuso aro akaipisisa nekuparadza kukuru, nekubvunza kuti zvose izvi zvingavapo sei iye wMasimba ose, anoziva zvose, unesimba rose nerudo achiriko. Ichi ndicho chakavanzika chavasina mhinduro netsanangudzo machiri.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 492.

Vakawanda vanoti hakuna Mwari vanoti dambudziko rezvivi ndicho chikonzero chakavaita kuti vasatenda kuti Mwari ariko. Asi sezvatichavona muchidzidzo chevhiki ino, Mwari weBhaibheri wakanakisisa kwazvo, uye tinogona kuvimba maAri—kunyange zvakaipa zvakazara munyika yedu yakatadza.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo chevhiki ino kugadzirira Sabata, February 15._
