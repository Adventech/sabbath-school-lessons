---
title:  Mutongi (Wechinguvana) Wenyika Ino 
date:   05/03/2025
---

Takavona muzvidzidzo zvakapfuvura, kuti muhondi yekunetsana yepasi rose, Satani nevari kurutivi rwake vanotonga nyika ino nguva duku, vaine miganhu yezvavanotenderwa kuita.

Mirairo iyi yekuratidza zvavanotaura haina miganhu mune zvinoitwa chete nemuvengi—diaborosi nevamwe vake—asi inotarirawo Mwari pekugumira kuti asaita seanombobvumira zvakaipa (kwenguva duku) zviri zvewakaipa. Nekuti Jehova havamboputsi zvitsidzo zvavo, pavakazvimisira mukuratidza—zvinopa mukana wenguva duku yekutonga kwadiaborosi—Mwari akazvidzora mukuita kwake (asingabvisi simba rake).

`Verenga Johani 12:31, Johani 14:30, Johani 16:11, 2 VaKorinde 4:4, na Ruka 4:6. Ko mavhesi anodzidzisei nezvekutonga kwemuvengi munyika ino?`

Testamende Itsva inoratidza kudumana kwehushe, humambo hwechiedza nehwerima, rima richibva kuna Satani nekupanduka kwake. Rimwe rebase raKristu nderekukurira hushe hwaSatani: “Mwanakomana waMwari wakavoneswa nemhaka iyi, kuti aparadze mabsa adiaborosi” (1 Johani 3:8). 

Kunyange zvakadaro pane “mirairo” inopa miganhu yezvingaitwa naMwari asi achiramba akatendeka kumirairo yake yehushe. Miganhu inosanganisira (1) kupa simba resarudzo kuzvisikwa ne (2) mirairo yesungano yekuratidza, iyo yatisingazivi, ikozvino hako. Zvinorambidza nekumisa denga kuita zvimwe zvinhu zvinechekuita nechimiro chaMwari mukudzikisa kana kuparadza huipi munyika. Saka tichiramba tichiona huipi huchienderera mberi nekurwadziwa, izvo zvinogona kukonzera vazhinji kutanga kunyunyuta kuvapo kwaMwari kana kunaka kwake. Asi kana zviri seri kwehondo huru zvikanzwisiswa chete, nemiganhu yaMwri ikavonekwa yezvaachaita nekuda kwezvakaipa, tinogonawo kumbonzwisisa kuti nemhaka yei zvinhu zviri sezvazviri—hongu kusvikira pakukurira kwaMwari zvakaipa. 
	 
`Ko pfungwa yekuti Jesu anodaidza Satani kuti “mutongi” wenyika ino zvinotibatsira seiwo hazvo kunzwisisa kuvapo kwezvakaipa munyika ino ikozvino? Zvinonyaradza zvakadini kungozivazve kuti kutonga kwenguva duku hako!`
