---
title:  Kudzidza Kuenda Mberi
date:   28/03/2025
---

“Avo vanoshandira vamwe mukuita zvakanaka vachashandirwawo zvakanaka neMufudzi Mukuru. Ivo pachavo vachanwa kubva pamvura yevupeyu, uye vachagutswa. Havazofariri zvinofadza nyama chete, kana zvimwe zvingashandura vupenyu. Nyaya huru yakataura ndeyekuti vangaitei kuti vamwe vaponeswe avo varikuparara. Kutaurirana nevamwe kuchabatsira. Rudo kuMudzikinuri ruchakakatira mwoyo pamwechete mukubatana.

“Kana tavakuziva kuti tiri vashandi pamwechete naMwari, zvitsidzo zvake hazvizotaurwi zvisina hanya. Zvichapisa mumwoyo yedu, nekupfutidza miromo yedu. Kuna Mosisi, paakadaidzwa kunoshandira vanhu vakanga vasingazivi, vasina hanya, vasingazvidzori nevaipanduka, Mwari akapa zvitsidzo, ‘Ndichava newe uye ndichakupa zororo.’ Akazotizve, ‘Zvirokwazvo ndichava newe.’ Ekisodo 33:14; 3:12. Chitsidzo ichi ndechevanhu vose vanoshanda pachinzvimbo chaKristu kune vanotambudzika vake.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 641.

**Mibvunzo Yekutaurirana**:

`Muna 1 VaKronde 13:4–8. Ko 1 VaKorinde 13 inojekesa sei mhando yevanhu vatinofanira kuva?`

`Chii chinoparadzanisa hwai kubva kumbudzi muna Mateo 25:31–46? Ko tinganzwisisa sei zvinotaurwa naJesu apa senzira yekusavona seanodzidzisa ruponeso nemabasa? `

`Ko zvinorevei kwauri kuti “kana tavakuda nyika sezvaakatida, kwatiri basa rake rapera. Takagadzirirwa denga; tave nedenga mumwoyo yedu” (tarisa chidzidzo chechishanu)? Ko izvi zvinoratidzei nezvechimiro chaMwari nechedenga pacharo? Ko tingararama sei sevagari vekudenga asi tiripano, munyaya dzekuparadzira rudo rwaMwari nenzira inounza chiedza nekururamisira kune varikumanikidzwa?`

`Ndezvipi zvingaitwa nekereke yepenyu kuratidza hanya yaMwari  yerudo nekururamisira munzvimbo yamunogara? Chii chamungada kuita zvirinani uye nguva dzakawanda? Ndezvipi zvinobatika zvaungaita iwe woga nezvamungaita nevamwe pane zvatakadzidza nezverudo rwaMwari nekururamisira kwake?`
