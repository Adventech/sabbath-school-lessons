---
title:  Mufananidzo WaMwari Wakajeka Kwazvo
date:  05/04/2026
---

Bhaibheri rinotopa mufananidzo waMwari nenzira yakajeka kwazvo. Bhaibheri rose rinozarura mucheka wakavhara zvisingavonekwi nezvinovonekwa zviri munyika yedu; kutiratidza kwatakabva nekwatinoinda; nekutizivisazve kuti ndiani anotonga kudenga nepasi uye zvaakaita.

Kubva kuna Genesi kusvika kunaZvakazarurwa, tinoverenga nezvaMwari mumwechete wazvokwadi, uyo anozvizivisa kuburikidza neBhaibheri uye naJesu Kristu, Mwari akava munhu. Tinogona kuverenga nehukuru-kuru hwaMwari (Jobho 1:12), kuziva kwake zvose (Isaya 46:9, 10), kururama kwake (Isaya 30:18), ngoni dzake (Deuteronomio 7:9), rudo nevunyoro hwake kwatiri (VaRoma 2:4), huchenjeri hwake (1 VaKorinde 2:7), nyasha dzake (2 VaKorinde 12:9), kuregerera kwake (Mateo 6:14), chinangwa chake muvupenyu hwedu (Jeremia 29:11), simba rake rekukurira rufu (Johani 11:25), hushe hwake (Mapisarema 47:8), chimiro chake chekugara nekusingaperi (Deuteronomio 33:27), nezvimwe zvimiro zvakawanda zvinotipa zvikonzero zvekuva nevukama naye. Patinonyanya kuziva nezvaMwari nezvaari, zvichatiita kuti timudewo zvakanyanya nekuda kuva pedyo naye muvukama hwakasimba.

NdiLusifa akatanga kuva nemibvunzo muchimiro chaMwari. Zvaainyunyutira nezvechimiro chaMwari zvakabva zvatungamirira kuhondo huru kwazvo mupasi rose. Kubva ipapo, “Satani avakugara achidzidza kuti angaitei kuti vanhu vasaziva zvakawanda nezvaMwari.”—Ellen G. White*.* Satani haana hanya kuti vanhu vanovona Mwari akaita sei (kuva muzvinhu zvose, kuva musiki akasika akasiya zvinhu kuti zvizvifambire zvoga, kuwanda kwezvimwari kana zvimwe zvakadaro), kana zvisingazivisi chokwadi chaicho nezvaMwari chete.

`Verenga Genesi 3:1–5. Lusifa aiva nechinangwa chei muhurukuro yake naEva? Inhema dzipi dzaakavudza Eva nezvechimiro chaMwari?`

Shoko raSatani chairo kuna Eva raiva rekuti: *Mwari anezvakawanda zvaakakuvanzirai. Hamugoni kuvimba naye.* Ellen White anowedzera achiti, “Kubva pakutanga kwehondo huru chaiva chinangwa chaSatani chekumonyorora chimiro chaMwari nekukurudzira kupandukira mutemo wake.”—*Ellen G. White*.

`Ko chimiro chaMwari chinomonyororwa sei munyika yedu? Ko iwe ndedzipi nguvawo dzaunombo ratidza chimiro chaMwari zvisiri izvo kune vamwe?`
