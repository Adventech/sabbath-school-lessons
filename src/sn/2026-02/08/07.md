---
title:  Kudzidza Kuenda Mberi
date:  22/05/2026
---

Tinoruramiswa nekutenda (VaRoma 5:1). Toitwa vatsvene nekucheneswa nekutenda (Mabasa 26:18). Kana tichikoka Jesu muvupenyu hwedu, tinovawo vana vaMwari nekutenda (Johani 1:12). Tinorarama nekutenda muMwanakomana waMwari (VaGaratia 2:20).

“Hakuna chimiro chinoita sechisina basa nekupererwa, asi chinokurira, chingaita semunhu anonzwa asingabatsiriki ari woga asi anotsamira panezvakaitwa naMuponesi. Nemunamato, nekudzidza shoko rake, nekugara maari nekutenda, munhu asina simba zvachose anogona kurarama muhumwe naKristu, nekumubata neruvoko rusingaregedzi.”—Ellen G. White.

“Kutenda kwavo kunofanira kusimbiswa neminamato yakaperera yakasimba inekuzvinyima, nekuzvininipisa. Vanofanira kuzvidurura, nekuzadzwa neMweya nesimba raMwari. Kukumbira kwakaperera kunekushinga kuna Mwari nekutenda—kutenda kunotungamirira kukutsamira pana Mwari, kuzvipira zvachose kubasa rake—ndizvo zvoga zvinogona kusvitsa vanhu kurubatsiro rwaMweya Mutsvene muhondo nemasimba, nevakuru, nevatongi verima renyika ino, nemweya yakaipa iri munzvimbo dzakakwirira.”—Ellen G. White.

**Mibvunzo Yekutaurirana**:

`Ndezvipi zvinhu zvishanu zvakaratidzwa pamusoro zvinobatsirana naMweya Mutsvene kurwisana nemuvengi?`

`Ko kutenda kunoita basa rei muhondo iyi?`

`Ko unozvivona sei muvupenyu hwako ikozvino?`

`Verenga VaHebheru 10:23. Nemhaka yei zvakakosha kubatirira pakutenda kwedu?`

`Unofungisisa nguva dzakawanda zvakadini neidi iri rekuti kana wavakunzwa usina simba, ndiwo mukana wekutsamira zvakazara pana Jesu?`

**Pfupiso:** Mwari anopa munhu wose chiyero chekutenda sehwaro hwevukama naye. SeMuvambi neMukwanisi wekutenda kwedu, Jesu akatiratidza simba rekutenda. Kana kutenda kwedu kwavakuduku, tichivuya nekuchema nekukumbira nemwoyo yakazvipira, Mwari achaita minana muvupenyu hwedu (tarisa Jeremia 31:2–4, 9, 11, 12). Achatitungamirira munzira dzake dzakarurama kuti tisagumburwa zve, uye tichava nerugare. Jesu ndiye muenzaniso wedu wakaperera muzvinhu zvose, nekuva nekutenda kwake achativona sevanhu vake mukupera kwenguva.
