---
title:  Kudzidza Kuenda Mberi
date:  24/04/2026
---

Kana ukafunga mashoko awataura zuva rose rapfuvura, ungazviisa papi? Anga anerudo here, vunyoro, achifadza, nekusimudzira kana kukuhunisa kana kuratidza kuneta, kushuvira zvimwe, kutsamwa, ari emakuhwa kana ruvengo? Bhaibheri rinoti, “ ‘Nekuti muromo unotaura zvizere mumwoyo’ ” (Mateo 12:34). Kana tine marara mumwoyo, anobuda nemashoko.

Tose takambokuhunika, takamboneta, takamboshungurudzwa mupfungwa nezvakabuda mumuromo medu (zvikuru sei mashoko atinozozvidemba). Asi kana tichida munhu zvinongobuda mumuromo zvoga.

Mwari anotaurawo nemwoyo yedu saizvozvo. Zvinoshamisa kuti mashoko aya tinawo muBhaibheri, aine simba nguva dzose kubva kare.

“Kutora Bhaibheri sebhuku retsika dzakanaka ndezvimwe, asi kuriteerera nguva dzose ndeimwe nyaya nekuritora seshoko benyu raMwari, vupenyu hwedu chaiho, shoko rinesimba mumaitiro edu, mashoko edu nepfungwa dzedu. Kusatora shoko raMwari neimwe nzira kuriramba. Kuramba izvi kungaitwa nevanoti vanozvitenda, ndiko kunokonzera kushaya kutenda nekurasa kutenda munevechidiki.”—Ellen G. White, *Education*, p. 260.

**Mibvunzo Yekutaurirana**:

`Ndezvipi zvaunazvo zvinokupa kutenda? Zvimwe zvingava zvakawandisa kupfuvura zvaungafunga.`

`Ungava nechokwadi sei chekuti kudzidza Bhaibheri nekunamata ndihwo hwaro hwehukama naMwari? Ungava here nevukama naMwari pasina kudzidza Bhaibheri nekunamata?`

`Kana mumwe akada kusimbisa vukama hwake naMwari, ungati atangire kuverenga papi?`

`Ko ungararama sei neshoko rose rinobuda mumuromo waMwari mazuva ose? (Deuteronomio 8:3). Izvi zvingaitika sei muvupenyu hwako?`

`Ko ndima dzinotevera dzinotivudzei nezvemashoko aMwari? VaHebheru 11:3; Mapisarema 33:6; Mateo 11:4, 5; 1 VaTesaronika 4:16; VaEfeso 6:17; Jakobo 1:21.`

**Pfupiso:** Bhaibheri ibenyu rinesimba, uye kuriverenga hwaro hwakanakira kukura muhukama naMwari. Haringotidzidzisi nezvechimiro chinoshamisa chezvaatira vanhu kwemazera-nemazera—rinotauravo nemumwe-nemumwe wedu nhasi kana tichivuya kwariri nekuzvininipisa.
