---
title:  Maropafadzo Akapetwa
date:  29/04/2026
---

Kune nzira zhinji dzatingadzidza Bhaibheri redu nadzo, dzakafanana nekubata vhesi imwe panguva, kana kudzidza chitsauko, kana kusarudza musoro wenyaya umwechete, kana kudzidza izwi rimwechete, kana bhuku rimwechete. Tinogona kudzidza tichishandisa duramazwi reBhaibheri, kana kuverenga mabhuku aEllen G. White kukubatsira kujekesa dzimwe ndima. Tinogona kumbofamba zvedu kunze uko, kana kuteerera Bhaibheri richiverengwa kwatiri kana kusangana neshamwari kana kaboka kaduku modzidza pamwechete.

Sezvatinoita kuchengetedza shamwari dzedu nekuitira zvitsva, tinofanira kuchengetedza nguva dzatinosangana naMwari zuva nezuva tichishandisa nzira itsva dzakudzidza Bhaibheri. Kune zvakawanda zvekudzidza nguva dzose!

Imwe nzira inoita kuti kudzidza Bhaibheri kunakidze kuvudza vamwe zvawawanamo. Kana tichitsanangura zvakatadzidza, kuisa mumazwi mashoma, nekutsvakurudza zvidzidzo kunosimbisa pfungwa dzedu. Zvinotibatsira kuchengeta zvataverenga. Maropafadzo akapetwa ndeekuti kana tichivudza vamwe nekukurukura navo, hurukuro dzeChikristu dzinowanzo denha nekusimbisa mose. Kazhinji kana tichivudza vamwe kana kuvadzidzisa ndipo patinonyanya kunzwisisa zvichipinda mupfungwa dzedu.

Unosvikawo pakuvona kuti zvaunodzidza zuva rimwe nerimwe harisi shoko raMwari chete kwauri asi ishoko revamwe vo.

`Verenga Isaya 50:4. Ko vhesi iri inotivudzei nezvevukama hwedu naMwari nezvahungabatsira muvukama hwedu nevamwe wo?`

Kudzidza kwako Bhaibheri uriwega hakukusimbisi chete asi kunobatsira kukurudzira vamwe vatinosangana navo zuva iroro. Anogona kuva makomborero akapetwa.

Vupenyu hwedu hweChikristu inhange-mutange. Kumbira Tenzi kuti vakubatsire kuramba uchimhanya senguva dzose, meso akaiswa pamubairo (VaFiripi 3:14). Usakuhunika kana ukaita sewavakudzikira kwekanguvana, asi shanduka sezvingadiwa muvupenyu hwako kuti uchengetedze vukama, zvikuru sei mukudzidza Bhaibheri rako nenguva yemunamato, wakasimba. Nekuti zvechokwadi uhu hupenyu husingaperi, kuti timuzive Mwari nhasi (Johani 17:3). Kuzvipira kwedu kwezuva nezuva kugara maari nemuShoko rake zvinoshandura vupenyu.

`Verenga chemusoro chevhiki ino utarisise zvachinoreva. Urikudzidzei izvozvi? Ungazvitaurira ani izvi?`
