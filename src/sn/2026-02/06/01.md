### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Danieri 2:20–23; Danieri 6:10, 11; Mabasa 20:36; Genesi 5:22–24; Ekisodo 33:15–23; Ekisodo 32:31, 32.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Ndinoda Jehova nekuti unonzwa izwi rangu nekukumbira kwangu. Zvaakarerekera nzeve yake kwandiri, ndichadana kwaari mazuva angu” (Mapisarema 116:1, 2).

Fungidzira zvazvingava kana ukatora nguva yakareba usina kumbotaura neshamwari yako kana mumwe wako muwanano. Nenguva isipi vukama uhu hunopera, kova kutanga kwedambudziko guru kwazvo. Nemunamatowo unokosha kwazvo muvukama naMwari. Itsika yakakosha kwazvo yekuzvinamatira nguva dzawakatara, kuti aninani awane simba. Kana tisinganamati nguva dzose nekukasika, tichatetereka kubva kunaTenzi.

MuBhaibheri, tinodzidza nezvevupenyu hwevanhu vakasiyana-siyana vakanamata nenzira dzakasiyana. Tinogona kumbodzokera kumashure nekuedza kutarisako kuti vaitaura sei naMwari zvakasimbisa vukama hwavo naye, uye kuti nesuwo tingaitei uye sei kuti tinamatevo, sezvakaita minamato yavo ichavashandura nevamwevo. Ichokwadi: tsika yekunamata kwedu inezvainotiita isu nevamwe vo.

Sezvinoita kudzidza Bhaibheri, nyaya iyi yekunamata yakakura yakakosha uye yakafara zvekusakwanisika kubatwa mumavhiki maviri. Vhiki ino tichadzidza zvidzidzo zvinobva kune vamwe vakanamatavo muBhaibheri vakatiratidza kukosha kwekunamata muvukama hwakasimba naMwari. Ngatidzidzei kubva kumienzaniso yavo.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo chevhiki ino kugadzirira Sabata, May 9._
