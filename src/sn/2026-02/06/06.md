---
title:  Mosesi Anomiririra Rudzi
date:  07/05/2026
---

`Verenga Ekisodo 32:31, 32. Ko tinodzidzei nezvaMosesi nemunamato?`

Mosesi akashinga kumiririra vanhu vaMwari nguva nenguva. Mosesi akainda kuna Mwari vanhu vaita nyota (Ekisodo 15:25; Ekisodo 17:2–6), vanhu vava nenzara (Numeri 11:21, 22), nemune mamwe matambudziko ekupererwa (Numeri 11:11–15).

VaIsraeri pavakawumba chimhuru chendarama Mwari vachangopedza kuita sungano navo, Mosesi akarangarira, “ ‘Nekuti ndakatya hasha nekutsamwa kukuru kwamakatsamwisa nako Jehova, nekuti wakanga achida kukuparadzai. Asi Kehova wakandinzwa vo nenguva iyo’ ” (Deuteronomio 9:19).

Vashori pavakodzoka kuNyika Yechipikirwa, Mosesi akarangarira, “ ‘Zvino ndakawira pasi pamberi paJehova mazuva anemakumi mana nevusiku makumi mana, ndiyo nguva yandakawira pasi; nekuti Jehova wakati: Ndichakuparadzai’ ” (Deuteronomio 9:25).

Revi paakaparadzana nemamwe marudzi ose kuti anoshumira musenjuwari, Mosesi anorangarira, “ ‘Ini ndakagara mugomo mazuva anemakumi mana nevusiku hunamakumi mana sepakutanga; Jehova vakandizwa nenguva iyo vo; Jehova vakasakuparadzai’ ” (Deuteronomio 10:10). Mwari akanzwa kukumbira kwaMosesi.

Tinogona kudzidza zvakawanda kubva muvupenyu hwaMosesi kana takatarisa munamato nekubatirira kwake kuna Mwari:

- * Mosesi aida Mwari nemwoyo wose, achiziva Mwari chimiro chake zvakanaka. Mwari anotsanangurira Mosesi muna Ekisodo 34:6: “ ‘Jehova, Jehova, Mwari anetsitsi nenyasha huru nemwoyo murefu akazara nenyasha nezvokwadi’ ”.
- * Mosesi akanga akashinga akatendeka mukubatirira pana Mwari munguva dzakanaka nedzakaipa murwendo rwairema kuinda kuNyika Yechipikirwa. Achitambudzika sezvatinoitavo isu, Mosesi akavimba musimba raMwari, kuvapo kwake nekutungamirira muvupenyu hwake iye (Ekisodo 33:13).
- * Mosesi akatondedza Mwari zvitsidzo zvavo (Ekisodo 32:13), akakumbira zvitsidzo izvi akamiririra vanhu vake (Deuteronomio 7:8), nekurangarira kutungamirira kwake kumashure (Deuteronomio 8:2).
- * Mosesi akagashira mhinduro dzeminamato yake kuna Mwari, kana dzaiva hongu kana kwete. Kuva muvukama hwakasimba naMwari hazvirevi kuti zvose zvatinoda tinongobva tazviwana ipapo nguva dzose (Deuteronomio 3:23–29), asizve tinofanira kushingirira kunamata zvakangodaro (Ruka 18:1–8).
