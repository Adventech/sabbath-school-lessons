---
title:  Kudzidza Kuenda Mberi
date:  19/06/2026
---

“Hazvinei kuti munhu anotendei, hakuna munhu angava nerudo rwaMwari rwakaperera kunze kwekuti aratidza kuzviramba nekuda kwehama yake. Asi hatigoni kuva nemweya wakadai nekuedza kuda vamwe. Chinodiwa kuda Kristu mumwoyo. Kana iwe wakabatanidzwa naKristu, rudo runotubuka rwega.”

“Avo varimubasa rekuwana mweya nekutendeka kuna Kristu, vanokura zvirinani pamweya nepakuwadzana napakuzvipira.”

“Simba rekukurira zvakaipa rinowanikwa zvakanyanya nekushanda nesimba.”

“Kuti upinde murufaro rwake—rufaro rwekuvona mweya yadzikinurwa nekuzvipira kwake—tinofanira kupindawo mubasa rake rekuvadzikinura.”

“Avo vanoramba mukana wekuwadzana naKristu mubasa rake, vanoramba kudzidziswa kunovakwanisa kuva naye mukubwinya kwake.”—Ellen G. White.

**Mibvunzo Yekutaurirana**:

`Nemhaka yei rudo rwakakosha zvakadaro mukupupura kose kungaitwa?`

`Wakazvivona zviri zvechokwadi sei: zvekuti kuwana mweya kwakabatana nekusimba kwevukama hwako naMwari?`

`Pane zvinotodiwa kuti munhu agone kuparidza Mwari kune vamwe? Kana zviripo chii?`

`Kana takatarisa kudzidza Bhaibheri nemunhu asinganamati, ungatangira papi? Chinangwa chako chikuru ndechei : kuratidza here chokwadi chedzidziso kana kukokera munhu kuti azive Jesu?`

`Imba kana kuteerera kumashoko erwiyo “Ndaponeswa!” ugofungisisa nzira dzaungazvitaurira vamwe nadzo.`

**Pfupiso:** Kana rudo rwaMwari nekurarama kwake, Shoko rake rakasimba zvikava munguva yedu zhinji pazuva, tichamanikidzwa kuparidzira vakatipoteredza rudo rwake. Tinofanira kugara mumunamato, nekufungisisa, nekuronga kuita basa rekupupura, nekudaira Shoko rake, rinobuda mumuromo wake, risingadzoki risina kuita basa raakarituma kunoita, uye richakurira muzvinhu zvose zvaakaritumira (Isaya 55:11).
