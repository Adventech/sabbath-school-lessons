---
title:  Kubva Pakupfachukira
date:  14/06/2026
---

`Verenga Kutumwa Kukuru muna Mateo 28:18–20. Nyora mashoko aJesu akasiyana-siyana paanoti “rose/yose” kana “nguva dzose” (muchiGiriki riri izwi rimwechete, pas).`

Jesu akatipa basa rekuparidza shoko rake kunyika: “Endai mupasi rose mudzidzise marudzi ose.” Basa rekereke yeMasabata nderekuita vateveri, avo vanoitavo vateveri. Nenzira iyi tinoparidza evhangeri isingaperi neshoko rengirozi nhatu (Zvakazarurwa 14:6–12) kugadzirira nyika yedu kudzokazve kwaJesu kwavapedyo.

Ani nani agashira vupenyu hutsva muna Kristu anodaidzwa kuva chapupu. Asi nguva zhinji, vanhu vanofunga kupupura sechinhu chavasingagoni kuita kana chavasingadi kuita. Unogona kuzvivona uchiparidza mumugwagwa wemudhorobha kana kudzidzisa Bhaibheri uchitaura nyaya yakawoma kwazvo, wobva wakazunza musoro wako. “Kwete ini! Hazviiti izvi! Ndiri munhu akazvinyararira; kupupura hachisi chinhu chandakasununguka kuita.”

Asi kupupura kwechokwadi kunongobva mumunhu akavona zvinoitwa naMwari muvupenyu hwake, kuvona zvaanokudzidzisa paunenge uchikura maari, nekutaurira vamwe zvawakaitirwa. Mwari wakanaka, nezvaakatiitira inyaya yakanakisisa inofanira kunzwisiswa nenyika. Hatigoni uye hatifaniri kunyarara! Akatidzikinura; akakudaidza nezita rako—uri wake. Kungava neimwe nyaya yakanaka kupfuvura iyi here kuna ani nani kose kose?

Kana nepo vadzidzi vekereke yekutanga vakanga vasina kudzidza kana kugona kutaura zvinonwisa mvura, zviripo zvatinodzidza kubva kwavari.

`Verenga Mabasa 1:8 na Mabasa 4:13. Ko kupupura kwakanga kwakamira sei mukereke yekutanga? Ko Petro na Johani vakashanda zvakabata sei avo vakavanzwa vachipupura?`

Petro na Johani vakasvika kutaura pachena zvinesimba, “ ‘Nekuti hatingaregi kutaura zvatakavona nezvatakanzwa’ ” (Mabasa 4:20). “Vakanga vafamba naJesu” (Mabasa 4:13) vakasundwa kumupupurira. Mweya Mutsvene akavapa kushinga nesimba rekubata pfungwa dzevamwe nemashoko avo.

`Iva nenguva uri pamunamato izvozvi. Kumbira kushinga kuti Mwari akakutungamirire kune vanhu vaunofanira kushinga kuparidzira, nevuchenjeri hwekuziva paunofanira kutaura nezvekutaura. Verenga 1 Johani 4:7–11 ugonamatira rudo rwakadai.`
