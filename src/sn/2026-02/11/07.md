---
title:  Kudzidza Kuenda Mberi
date:  12/06/2026
---

Tinoda kubatirira kuna Mwari kana tasangana nemadambudziko makuru evupenyu. Misoro yenyaya yatabata mukota ino yose inobatsirana kumutsidzira nekuchengetedza vukama hwekufamba naMwari. Kana wasangana nezvigumbuso zvakafanana nevutano hwaparara, dambudziko remari, kana dambudziko muwanano, kana kuda mumwe munhu pedyo newe, kana mumwe wemitoro inokubira mufaro wako, imbofungisisa nezvemibvunzo inotevera uchifungazve nezvezvidzidzo zvatabata kusvika zvino.

**Mibvunzo Yekutaurirana**:

`Ko dambudziko rauri mariri kana rawakasangana naro, rakakusiya uchivona Mwari sei? Ko ungavona chimiro chaMwari zvirinani sei?`

`Wakaguma riini kunamatira kunzwa izwi raMwari muvupenyu hwako rakasimba kupfuvura remuvengi? Rangarira kuti mbavha (Satani) anovuya kuzoba nekuvuraya nekuparadza, asi Mwari kuzopa vupenyu hwakawandisa (Johani 10:10).`

`Ko mwoyo wako wakazvininipisa here? Unobvuma here kuti Mwari achiri mukuru uye anesimba rose muvupenyu hwako, zvisinei nematambudziko? Kana zvisinakudaro, ungadzidza sei kuzvininipisa nekuvimba nekunaka nerudo rwaMwari kwauri iwe?`

`Unogara wakasimba here muShoko raMwari zuva nezuva? Kumbira Mwari kupfutidzazve rudo rwako rwekutanga kwaari paunopfuvura nemumatambudziko.`

`Wakaguma riini kutarisa kuna Mwari seMunyaradzi, Mupi wemazano wako mumunamato, uchivimba kuti akagara achingochengeta zvitsidzo zvake zvekusakusiya kana kukurasha (VaHebheru 13:5)?`

`Kana kutenda kwako kuri kushoma, namata, uchiti,“ ‘Tenzi, ndinodavira; batsirai kusatenda kwangu!’ ” (Marko 9:24). Zvipoteredze nevanhu vanogona kukukurudzira pane vanokukuhunisa.`

`Nyika haina hanya nevasina simba nguva zhinji, vasingazivi, vakakuvara, kana vakatyoka. Shoko raMwari “Kana usina simba, Ini ndinaro” ishoko rinogona kushandura vanhu ipapo-ipapo. Funga nezvemumwe munhu waungakurudzira neshoko ranhasi.`

**Pfupiso:** Tichiri kurarama munyika yechitema yakazara nekurwadza nekutambudzika, mumwe nemumwe wedu anematambudziko ake pano nepapo, zvinhu zvinogona kutikonzera kubvunza rudo rwaMwari. Patinovona zvakaitwa nevamwe vari muBhaibheri munguva dzekushungurudzwa, tinogona, kushinga kuti mazviri tinosimbisa vukama hwedu naMwari, asingashanduki (Maraki 3:6) uye anerudo runoramba rwakadaro nguva dzose.
