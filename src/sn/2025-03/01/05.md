---
title:  Mosesi Anozvarwa
date:   02/07/2025
---

`Verenga Ekisodo 2:1–10. Ndekupi kutungamirira kwaMwari nekudzivirira kunowanikwa mukuzvarwa kwaMosesi?`

Zvakanga zviripo munguva yakazvarwa Mosesi nemuvupenyu hwake zvinoshamisa nekuti akararama munguva dzehushe hwechiEgipita hwaiva nemukurumbira hwechi 18. Mumwe wemadzishe munguva iyi—Thutmose III, anodaidzwa kuti “Napoleon weEgipita”—ndiye anozivikanwa kupfuvura vamwe vose. 

Kana nepo aifanira kufa pakuberekwa (tarisa Ekisodo 1:22), Mosesi akazvarwa se mwanakomana “akatsanangurika; chiHeberu tob, zvinoreva “kunaka”; (Ekisodo 2:2). Izwi rechiHeberu rinoreva kunaka kunopfuvura kwekunze. Rinowanza kushandiswa kutsanangura vhiki reKusika raMwari, apo akati “zvakanaka kwazvo” (Genesi 1:4, 10, 31). 

Sechisikwa chitsva, mwana uyu “akanaka,” muzano raMwari, akazova mutungamiriri weVaHebheru kubva kuhubatwa. Pakuberekwa kwemwana uyu, zvikuru sei muchimiro chaivapo, ndiani aingaziva remangwana rake? Asi Mwari anozadzisa mashoko ake kuna Abrahama, Isaka naJakobo. Akatsidza kuti aizopa vana vavo Nyika Yechipikirwa (Ekisodo 2:24, 25). Hongu, aizishandisa kamwana katob aka, kuzviita. 

Kusvika mukunda wamambo weEgipita Hatshepsut atora Mosesi semwana wake. Zita rakapiwa Mosesi rinobva muchiEgipita, kureva “mwanakomana wa,” sezvingavonekwa muzita rekuti Ah-mose (“mwanakomana waAkh”) kana kuti Thut-mose (“mwanakomana waThoth”). Saka zita rake, muchiHeberu riri Mosheh, zvinoreva “kumunyukura.” Vupenyu hwake hwakachengetedzeka apo “akanyukurwa” kubva mumvura nenzira inoshamisa kwazvo.

Zvishoma kwazvo zvatinoziva nezvevupenyu hwake. Abva kudzikinurwa nenzira inoshamisa, nekutorwa kuva mwana waHatshepsut, Mosesi akararama makore ake 12 ekutanga nemhuri yake chaiyo (Ekisodo 2:7–9; Ellen G. White, PP, p. 244). Mosesi akawana dzidzo yakanakisisa yemuEgipita, zvose zvichiitirwa kuti agova Farao aizotonga Egipita pamberi apo, (PP, p. 245). Zvinoshamisa sei kuti, pakupedzisira dzidzo iyi haina kuzoshanda chinhu, asi chakanyanya kukosha: kuziva Mwari nezvokwadi yake.

`Urikudzidzei hako izvo zvichashaya basa muzvinhu zvakakosha chaizvo? `
