---
title:  Zvihuta NeManna
date:   11/08/2025
---

Nerombo rakaipa pane kupanduka kwakarongeka munyaya dzerwendo. Vanhu vaitokanganwa zvinotyisa ruvoko rwaMwari rwesimba rwakavabatsira kare nekuvapa mhinduro mumatambudziko avo. Vakasiya matambudziko enguva yavaiva vachitadza kuvona chinangwa chechitsidzo chavaivafanira kusvika. Iri idambudziko rakajairika pakati pevanhu vaMwari nanhasiwo.

`Verenga Ekisodo 16:1–36. Chii chaikonzera Israeri kunyunyuta, uye chii chaizotevera?`
 
Zvakakosha kuvona kuti miyedzo muBhaibheri inechekuita sezvekudya. MuBindu reEdeni, Kutadza Kwekutanga kwaibva pakudya zvaiva zvarambidzwa zvemuti wekuziva zvakanaka nezvakaipa (Genesi 2:16, 17; Genesi 3:1–6). Mukuyedzwa kwaJesu murenje naSatani, museve waSatani wekutanga waibva panezvekudya (Mateo 4:3). Esau akarasikirwa nehudangwe hwake nekuda kwekusagona kudzora mumukanwa make (Genesi 25:29–34). Kakawanda zvakadini kusateerera kwaIsraeri kwaibva mune zvekudya kana zvekunwa! Ndicho chikonzero nei Mosesi akayeuchidza zera rakatevera: 

“Munhu haararami nechikudya bedzi, asi munhu anorarama neshoko rinobva mumuromo waJehova” (Deuteronomio 8:3).

Manna, hongu, chaiva chingwa chekudenga chapiwa vaIsraeri naMwari murwendo rwavo rwemakore makumi mana rwemurenje. Kuburikidza nechipo ichi, akavadzidzisa kuti ndiye Musiki neMupi wazvose. Uyezve, Mwari akashandisa simba rake risingaperi kuvapa manna kuvaratidza kuti vanochengeta sei Sabata rezuva rechinomwe. 

Vhiki imwe neimwe mavhiki mana zvakaitika: (1) kwemazuva mashanu, Mwari vakapa manna yezuva nezuva; (3) manna haina kuvora neChitanhatu (Friday) kusvika Sabata; uyezve (4) hakuna manna yakanaya neSabata. Mwari akagara achitumira manna kuti vanhu varangarire zuva reSabata nekupemberera kunaka kwaMwari nezuva iroro. Mwari akati: “Tarirai, Jehova zvaakakupai Sabata,” (Ekisodo 16:29). 

`Vanhu vanoda kudya. Takasikirwa kudya. Zvekudya zvakakora zvinobva muvhu (ndizvo zvekudya zvekutangisisa), hazviratidzi chete kuti Mwari anoda kuti tidye, asi kuti tide zvatinodyavo. Asizve chipo ichi chinoshamisa chezvekudya (nekuda kwedu kudya), chingashandiswa zvisiri izvo sei?`
