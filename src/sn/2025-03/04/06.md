---
title:  Chimvuramabwe, Mhashu, Nerima
date:   24/07/2025
---

`Verenga Ekisodo 9:13–10:29. Matenda aya anobudirira zvakadini kushandura pfungwa dza Farao?`

Nut chaiva chimwarikadzi chedenga uye chaizivikanwa nekuchengetedza zvaiitika muchadenga nepanyika. Osiris chaiva chezvirimwa nekubereka. MuBhaibheri, chimvuramambwe chaiwanzo fambidzana nerutongo rwaMwari (Isaya 28:2, 17; Ezekieri 13:11–13). Mudenga iri, avo vakaviga fuma yavo pakavanzika yakachengeteka (Ekisodo 9:20, 21). Munhu wose zvino avakuidzwa: vachateerera here kana kwete, vachadaira Shoko raMwari here nekuita zvarinoreva? 

Mwari anozivisa chinangwa chake chekuraramisa Farao kuti nyika yose imuzive (Ekisodo 9:16). Mambo weEgipita zvino anobvuma kuti akatadza, asi pamberi apo anoshandura pfungwa dzake. 

Chimwari cheEgipita chemurowe, chehondo nechenyongano chainzi Seth. Pamwechete naIsis, zvaitorwa sezvekurima. Shu chaiva chemumhepo. Serapis chairatidza simba dzvene, vukuru, kubereka, kuporeswa nevupenyu hweseri kweguva. Hakuna kana chimwari chimwechete cheEgipita chakagona kumisa Mwari (Ekisodo 10:4–20) nekuti zvimwari hazvisi chinhu (Isaya 44:9, 10, 12–17).

Varanda vaFarao vakamukurudzira kurega vaIsraeri vaende, asi akaramba. Akada kunyengera Mosesi akazviramba izvi, nekuti madzimai nevana vakakosha uye havabviswi pakunamata nekuva nemhuri yekunamata.

Chekupedzisira, Ra ndicho chaiva chimwari chikuru pazvimwari zvose zveEgipita, chimwari chezuva. Thoth chaiva chemwedzi. Chakatadza kana kupa chiedza. Farao akaedzazve kunyengerera, asi hazvina kubatsira. Nguva ingaita mazuva matatu kuine rima guru paEgipita, asi kwaigara vaIsraeri kwaiva nechiedza. Kusiyana uku kwakava kukuru kwazvo. 

Asi zvisinei nekuparara kwaiva kwaita nyika yake, Farao akashinga kurwisa. Kana nepo tisingazivi zvaiva mukati mepfungwa dzake, asi tinogona kungovona kuzvikudza chaiko. Zvisinei nesimba rehuchapupu, kana varanda vake vachimuudzawo, “ ‘Ko munhu acharamba arimarwadzo kwatiri kusvika riiniko? Regai vanhu ava vaende, kuti vanoshumira Mwari wavo. Ko hamusati maziva kuti Egipita yaparara?’ ” [Ekisodo 10:7], zvisinei kuti chakanaka chekuita chaivapo—nekumbotaura-taura, Farao akarambisisa kubvuma kukundwa naMwari nekubvumira vanhu ava kuti vaende. 

Mashoko anoshamisa sei, “kuzvikudza kunoteverwa nekuparara, nekudada kunoteverwa nekudonha” (Zvirevo 16:18).
