Fungisisa nemashoko arimuna Isaya 40 naIsaya 51: 1-16.

Nziyo dzeZioni zvinokonzera kuzvipira kwakazara kwekuramba munhu achifunga nezve Zioni nekurarama mutariro yemukubata vushe kwaMwari dzakunomiririra. Kana nepo maropafadzo akawanda esenjuwari yaMwari achiwanikwa muvupenyu huno, tariro mukuzara kwevupenyu nemufaro muZioni zvichiri kunguva ichavuya. Vana vaMwari vakawanda vatarisira kwenguva yakareba Zioni nemisodzi (Mapisarema 137: 1). Kurangarira Zioni hachisi chinhu chinongouya mupfungwa kwenguva duku, asi zvinotora nguva yekutofunga nekusarudza kurarama nazvo mupfungwa mhenyu (Ekisodho 13: 3; 20: 8).

Saka kuimba nziyo dzeZioni zvine kubatikana nekundangariro mhenyu mutariro yekudzorerwa kuvushe hwaMwari panyika itsva (Zvakazarurwa 21: 1-5). “Apo, pfungwa dzisingafi dzichafunga nekufara kusingaperi zvishamiso zvesimba rekusika, zvakavanzika zverudo runodzikinura. Hapana hutsinye, mhandu inonyengera kukanganwa Mwari. Pfungwa dzose dzichakura, nekukwanisa kose kuchiwedzerwa. Zvichazikanwa hazvichanetesi pfungwa  kana kupedza simba. Hurongwa hukuru apa huchaitwa, zvidiso zvikuru zvichasvikwa; asi pacharamba pane kumwe kunokwanisa kusvikwa, zvimwezve zvinoshamisa zvichamuka, zvokwadi itsva yekuziva, zvinangwa zvitsva kudaidzira mamwe masimba epfungwa nemweya nemuviri.”-Ellen G. White, _Hondo Huru,_ p. 677.

Kuzvimisira kusakanganwa Zioni chichava chitsidzo chikuru  chevafambi vaJehova, avo vacharamba zvachose kugashira nyika ino semusha wavo asi vachamirira nyika itsvaka denga idzva.

Ndizvo, MaMapisarema eZioni achigona kuimbwa nevatendi vemazera ose vanoshuva kugara muJerusarema Idzva (Zvakazarurwa 3: 12). Nziyo dzeZioni dzinotikurudzira kutarisira nyika ichavuya netariro, asi zvichatifadza kuva vashandiri venyasha dzaMwari munyika ino.

**Mibvunzo Yekutaurirana**:

`Tingawana sei mirairo yezvemweya neyeruzivo rwezvamwari inotsamira pavanhu vaMwari muZioni, nzvimbo chaiyo muJerusarema, nekuzviisa pakereke nebasa rayo kunyika?`

`Ko vatendi vangararama sei musenjuwari yaMwari nhasi? (Johani 1: 14-18, VaHebheru 12: 22-24).`

`Ko Zioni richava guta renyika dzose sei sezvinovonekwa muMapisarema 87? (VaRoma 5: 10; VaEfeso 2: 11-16; VaKorose 1: 19-23).`

`4.	Ungapindura sei munhu anonongedza zvokwadi yevasakarurama vanobudirira munyika ino apo vakawanda “vakanaka” vachitambura? Ungati kudini? Nemhaka yei zvakakosha kubvuma kuti hatina mhinduro dzezvinhu zvose ikozvino?`
