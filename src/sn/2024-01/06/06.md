---
title:  Rutongo RwaShe Nesenjuwari
date:   08/02/2024
---

`Verenga Mapisarema 96:6-10; 99:1-4; na Mapisarema 132:7-9, 13-18. Rutongo rwaMwari ruchaitikepi, uye zvichatipa mhinduro ipi? Ko senjuwari inotibatsira sei kunzwisisa mabatiro anoita Mwari  zvakaipa?`

Rutongo rwaMwari runevukama hwakasimba nesunjuwari. Musenjuwari ndimo munzvimbo umo dambudziko remuimbi rechitema rakashandurwa (Mapisarema 73:17-20). Senjuwari yaiva nzvimbo yaiva yakagadzirirwa rutongo rutsvene sezvaiva zvakagadzirirwa Urimi (Numeri 27:21) uye nechifunhiro chechifuva rutongo rwemuprisita mukuru (Ekso. 28:15, 28-30). Naizvozvo Mapisarema akawanda anoratidza Mwari agere pachigaro musenjuwari akagadzirira kutonga nyika nekuda kwezvivi zvayo.

Pasenjuwari zano reruponeso rakaratidzwa. Muchinamato chechihedeni, chitema chaitorwa segwapa pamuviri, raibviswa nemishonga. Kune rumwe rutivi, Bhaibheri rinoratidza chitema sekukanganiswa kwemutemo waMwari wekuzvidzora. Hutsvene hwaMwari hunoreva kuti anoda kururama uye nekumunamata muvutsvene hwake. Kuita izvi, vanofanira kuchengeta mutemo waMwari, uyo unoratidza hutsvene hwake. 

Saka, senjuwari inzvimbo yekuregererwa kwezvivi nekudzorerwa kwekururama sezvinoratidzwa nechigaro chaMwari chemutsa  “nezvibairo zvekururama” (Deut. 33:19; Mapisarema 4:5). 

Asi “Mwari anoregerera” anoranga mabasa asina kunaka evanhu vasina kutendeuka (Mapisarema 99:8). Zvinoita chaizvo musenjuwari nekuti inzvimbo yerutongo zvinovonekwa muhutsvene hwaMwari hunogara huripo nekuda kwevupenyu hwakarurama zvinowirirana nezvidiso zvesungano. Kutonga kwaJehova kunobva paZioni kunoperera mukuitirwa zvakanaka kwevakarurama nekukurirwa kwezvisakarurama Mapisarema (Mapisarema 132:13-18). Senjuwari yaikurudzira mufaro mukuru wekutarisira kudzokazve kwaShe seMutongi, zvikuru sei Zuva Reruchenuro. Saizvozvovo, Mapisarema anosimbisa chokwadi chekuuya kwerutongo rutsvene (Mapisarema 96:13; 98:9), Jesu musenjuwari yekudenga (Zvakazarurwa 11:15-19).

`Verenga VaRoma 8:34. Ko vhesi iyi  inoratidza sei kwatiri kuti zvirikuitwa naKristu musenjuwari yekudenga inyaya yakanaka kuvanhu vake?`
