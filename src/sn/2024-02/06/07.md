title:  Kudzidza Kuenda Mberi
date:   10/05/2024
---

Bhaibheri parakarambidzwa nemasimba enyika; zvapupu zvaro zvikakanganiswa, nezvose zvikaedzwa kuita nevanhu nemadimoni kuti pfungwa dzevanhu dzibve kwariri; apo avo vakada kuparidza zvokwadi zvaro zvitsvene vakavhimwa, vakatengeswa, vakarwadziswa, vakavigwa mumakomba ematiringo, vakavurairwa kutenda kwavo, kana kumanikidzwa kutizira kumakomo, nemapako eshumba nemakomba enyika-ipapo chapupu chakatendeka chakaparidza chakapfeka masaga. Asi zvakaramba zvichipupura kwenguva yose yemakore 1, 260. Munguva dzerima guru kwaiva nevarume vakatendeka vaida Shoko raMwari vaine godo nekukudzwa kwake. Kuvaranda ava vaiteerera huchenjeri hwakapiwa, simba nechikuriri chekutaura zvokwadi zvake munguva iyi yose.”—Ellen G. White, Hondo Huru, pp. 267, 268.

Furanzi payakaramba pachena nekutsveta Bhaibheri parutivi, vanhu vakaipa nemweya yerima vakafara mukukwanisa chidiso chavo chamakore-vushe husina kurambidza kunobva mumutemo waMwari. . . . Kudzivisa kweMweya waMwari, unogara wakarinda hutsinye hwemweya waSatani, wakabviswa nechikamu chikuru kwazvo, uya anofarira kutambura kwevanhu akabvumirwa kuida madiro. Avo vakanga vasarudza kupanduka vakasiiwa kuti vakohwe michero yako kusvikira nyika yazara nehuipi hwakaipisisa zvekusakwanisa kunyorwa. Kubva mumatunhu akabvarurwa-bvarurwa nemaguta akanga ava matongo kuchema kunorwadza kwakanzwikwa-kuchema kwekurwadziwa kukuru. Furanzi ikazunguzwa nezvakaita sekudengenyeka kwenyika. Chinamato, mutemo, kugarisana kwevanhu, dzimhuri, Hurumende, nekereke-zvose zvakakandirwa pasi neruvoko rwakaipa kwazvo urwo rwakanga rwasimudzirwa kurwisana nemutemo waMwari .”—Hondo Huru, p. 286.

“Kunze kwekuti kereke yatevera kutungamirira kwake [Mwari], nekugashira chiedza chose, nekuita basa rose rinogona kuratidza, chinamato chichadzikira zvinotyisa kusvika dzangova tsika, nemweya wehuMwari hune hupenyu huchanyangarika.”—Hondo Huru, p. 316.
