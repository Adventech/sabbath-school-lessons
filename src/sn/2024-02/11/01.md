### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Zvakazarurwa 14:7, 9, 12; Zvakazarurwa 4:11; Zvakazarurwa 13:1, 2; Zvakazarurwa 12:3–5, 17; Zvakazarurwa 13:11–18.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Muvaite vatsvene muzvokwadi; Shoko renyu izvokwadi” (Johani 17:17).

Kwave nekanhu kava kushandiswa ikozvino mukurapa kanonzi Bhayochipi kana kuti Vherichipi, kakakura kuenzana nenzanga yemupunga, kanogona kudyarwa mumurwere. KaBhiyochipi kanenge kane ruzivo rwemurwere zvakaitika kwaari kare izvo zvinogona kuwanikwa nekupfuudza mushina kunze uchingotarisa muviri wemunhu uchipfuvura nepane kaBhiyochipi. Mamwe Makristu anovona izvi senzira yekuda kutanga kumanikidzira munembo wechikara pavanhu. Kune vamwewo, munembo wechikara unechekutia nemitsetse unoiswa pazvinhu zvinotengeswa muzvitoro; kana namba isinganzwisiswi iri pamari inogona kuti kana ikahwerengedzwa inobudisa 666. Kune vamwe zvine chekuita nehurongwa hwemaMasoni, Iruminati, zvikopokopo zvitema zveUN (Mubatanidzwa Wenyika Dzepasi Rose), neUN pachayo.

Chinagwa chechidzidzo chevhiki ino kuratidza kurwisana kuchavuya munyaya yezvekunamata. Satani acharwisa simba raMwari nekuedza kutarisira pasi mutemo waMwari. Zvikuru sei weSabata ndiwo uchava pakati pehondo iyi yekunamata. Satani anovenga Sabata nekuti anovenga Musiki. Achashandisa kumanikidza, simba, nekubvisa kuzvipira kwedu kunaKristu. Pachava nekudhumana kwezvinotendwa pamusoro pezuva rechokwadi rekunamata nesiri. Rukoko rwaMwari rwekupedzisira kune vanoteerera Kristu zvisinei nekutambudzwa, kurambidzwa kutenga kana kutengesa, kusungwa kana rufu chairwo. Chidzidzo chevhiki ino chinosimbisa simba raJesu rekutitungamirira mukati mehondo yekupedzisira yepanyika.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo ichi, chakatsamira muHondo Huru, zvitsauko 35 ne 36  kugadzirira Sabata, June 15._
