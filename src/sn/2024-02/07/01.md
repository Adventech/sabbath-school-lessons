### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
1 VaTesaronika 4:13–18; Mat. 24:27, 30, 31; 2 Petro 1:19, 21; Dan. 8:14; Dan. 9:20–27; Ezra 7:7–13.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Nezuva iro vachati ‘Tarirai uyu ndiye Mwari wedu; takanga takamumirira, iye
uchatiponesa; uyu ndiye Jehova takanga takamumirira, tichafara nekufarira ruponeso rwake ’
” (Isaya 25:9).

Kudzoka kwechipiri kwaKristu ndiyo imwe nyaya Huru muMagwaro. Rushinda
rwendarama rwakasonanidza mapeji ose eBhaibheri. Mumwe mudzidzi
weBhaibheri akafungidzira kune ndima dzinosvika 1, 845 dzinotaura nezve kudzoka
kwechipiri kwaKristu muTestamende Yekare. Muzvitsauko 260 zveTestamende Itsva,
mune ndima 300 dzekudzoka kwaKristu. Vhesi imwe mu25 ndizvo zvainotaura
Mumabhuku 27 eTestamende Itsva 23 acho anazvo zvechiitiko chikuru ichi. 

Mushure mekunyudzwa kwenguva yeRumutsiriro muUropu nekukanganiswa
Ane makakatanwa, MaProtesitendi (Vakaramba Dzidziso DzeRomu) vakasimba Munyika
Itsva, kusanganisira America, umo vakawanda vakatsvaka zvokwadi, kusanganisira
zvokwadi yeKudzoka Kwechipiri.

Pakati pavo paiva nasapurazi aipinda Baptist ainzi William Miller. Kubva mukudzidza
kwake Bhaibheri, akadaira kuti Jesu aizodzokazve nekukurumidza, iye achiri mupenyu,
saka akatanga kuparidza shoko iri, akatanga boka, kana nepo rakakuhunika,
zvakazarurira vanhu zvimwe zvokwadi zvichiri kukosha nanhasi.	 

Muchidzidzo chevhiki ino tichatarisa kuti nei kudzoka kwechipiri kwaKristu
kwakazadza mwoyo yevatendi nerufaro kwemazana emakore uye nekuti tingagadzirira
sei chiitiko chikuru ichi.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo ichi, chakatsamira muHondo Huru, zvitsauko 18-21 kugadzirira Sabata, May 18._
