---
title:  Mapere Anoparadza
date:   15/04/2024
---

`Verenga Mabasa 20: 27-32. Ndeipi yambiro yakatsanangurika iyo Pauro akapa vatungamiriri vekereke yepaEfeso nezvekupanduka kwaivuya?`

Chinagwa chezano raPauro chaiva chekugadzirira kereke zvaizovuya. Mundima idzi anotsanangura nyaya mbiri dzakakosha.

Yekutanga yaiva “yemapere anoparadza aizovuya pakati pavo, asingazosiyi boka” (Mabasa 20:29). Mune mamwe mazwi, vatendi vaizosangana nekutambudzwa kunotyisa, asi achibva mukati mekereke. Chiprofita ichi chakazadziswa muzana nezana rechipiri remakore. Makristu akaramba kunamata mutongi nekumupfugamira nekunamata chimwari cheRoma vaishungurudzwa zvikuru, vaiiswawo mujere nekupiswa pamurwi hwehuni. 

Muapostora akataurazve rimwe dambudziko achiti, “kubva pakati penyu pane vachamuka, vachitaura zvekurasika, kuti vatsause vadzidzi vachizvitsvakira vadzidzi” (Mabasa 20:30). Manyepo aizopinda mutemberi. Dzidziso dzenhema dzaizotsiva zvokwadi tsvene. Tsika dzechihedheni dzaikunda. Mumakore ezana rechina nerechishanu kusanganisa zvokwadi kwakapinda kukereke yeChikristu nenzira yahuchenjeri, zvichinzi ndiko kufamba kweevhangeri. Asi matuso akaipisisa akava ekubva muidi reShoko raMwari. 

`Verenga 2 VaTesaronika 2:7–12. Ko Pauro anotsanangura sei kurasika kwaizovuya? Ndezvipi zvimiro zvavaizotarisira?`

Mashoko aPauro, “chakavanzika chekusarurama chavakutobata basa racho” zvakakosha. Kana munguva yaPauro kwaiva nekubva kuzvokwadi yaMwari zvishoma- zvishoma mukuteerera mutemo waMwari. Kwaizonyanyisa mazuva ekupedzisira. 

Mukupisana nemurairo wechipiri, zvifananidzo zvakaunzwa muchinamato cheChikristu. Mamirioni ezvifananidzo akasusukidzwa pamberi pekushumira. Kuitira kugashirwa kwechinamato cheChikristu nevahedheni vaivuya kukereke yeChikristu, zvimwari zvechihedheni zvakapiwa mazita sevatsvene (vasande). Svondo zuva rekunamata zuva sechimwari, rakatanga kugashirwa mukushumira kweChikristu kuremekedza rumuko. Zuva iri renhema, risiri muMagwaro, richipo nanhasi. 

`Ndekupi kugashira zvakaipa kwaunovona kuchipinda mukereke nhasi ? zvakanyanya kukosha, ndezvipi zvawava kuita iwe sezviregerera? Kusanganisa zvokwadi nenhema here?`
