---
title:  Kudzidza Kuinda Mberi
date:   20/06/2025
---

“Mumazera ose, Mwari akarwira vanhu vake kuburikidza nevatumwa. Zvisikwa zvekudenga zvakapinda munyaya dzevanhu. Zvakavonekwa zvakapfeka nguvo chena dzainya semheini; vakavuya sevaeni. Vatumwa vakavonekwa vakaita sevanhu vaMwari. Vakazorora kuita sevanoneta, pasi pemioki masikati. Vakabvuma rukoko rwevanhu mudzimba dzavo, vakava vatungamiriri vevafambi. Nemavoko avo vakatungidza moto paaritari. Vakazarura matirongo vakasunungura varanda vaSheThey have acted as guides to benighted travelers. They have, with their own hands, kindled the fires at the altar. They have opened prison doors and set free the servants of the Lord. Vakapfeka zvekudenga, vakavuya kuzokungurutsa dombo paguva raShe.

“Muchimiro chavanhu, vatumwa vakava pakati pevanhu vakarurama; vakashanyirazve vungano dzevasakarurama, pavakainda Sodoma, kunonyora zvakaitika kuti vavone kuti vakanga vadarika muganhu here wemutsa waMwari. Jehova vanofarira ngoni; uye nekuda kwevashoma vanovashumira nemwoyo wose, vanorambidza matambudziko makuru nekuwedzera rugare kuzviuru. Vatadzi havatozivi kuti vapenyu nekuda kwevashoma vakatendeka avo vavanofarira kuseka nekumanikidza.”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, pp. 631, 632.

**Mibvunzo Yekutaurirana**:

`Verenga 2 Timoti 1:7. Tauriranai zviprofita zvakakunetsa zvikuru. Ko tingabvisa sei mweya wekutya nekuwana tariro iri mushoko raMwari pakati pezviprofita zvekuvurairwa kutenda kwedu?`
 
`Kana nepo ikozvino zvakawoma kuvona kuti Sabata neSvondo zvichava nyaya huru sei mumazuva ekupedzisira, tarisa kuti nyika inogona kushanduka nenguva duku sei. Saka izvi zvinofanira kutivudzei nezvekusaisa rudaviro rwedu pazvinhu zvirikuitika izvozvi, asi zvichigon akushanduka hazvo nguva ipi zvayo, asi kusimbira paShoko Mwari chete?`

`Funga nezva Danieri 2 (kana na7). Hushe hose hwakauya hukainda sezvakataurwa kare. Nhasi, tichitarisa mashure kune zvakaitika, tinovona kuti zvakaitika saizvozvo. Kubva mumavonero edu nhasi, kwasara hushe humwechete. Ndehupi uhu, uye nemhaka yei tineidi rekuti hurikuuya sezvakaprofitwa?`
