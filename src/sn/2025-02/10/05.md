---
title:  Mutongi Wenyika Yose
date:   04/06/2025
---

Sodoma risari raparadzwa, kune nyaya inoshamisa inoita mumapani eMamura. Mwari anozviratidza kuna Abrahama, ane vatumwa vaviri. Abrahama paanovavona, anokoka vaenzi vekudenga kuzodya, ipapa ndipo apo Mwari akavimbisa Abrahama naSara kuti vaizova nemwanakomana waizotungamiriria kuinda kuna Messia. Jesu zvechokwadi akabva muimba yaAbrahama (enzanisa naVaGaratia 3:16). Nyaya inobva yashanduka yainda kukusarurama kwemaguta emumapani. 

`Verenga Genesi 18:17–32. Ko tinodzidzei nezvechimiro chaMwari nenzira iyi yaanoranga nayo kuita nekuda kwezvakaipa panyik ayedu kubva mumavhesi aya?`

Mwari haamanikidzwi kutitsanangurira, asi haasi kufukidza pfungwa dzake kubva kuvanhu. “Zvirokwazvo Ishe Jehova havangaiti chinhu vasina kuzivisa varanda vavo vaprofita,” (Amos 3:7).

Mwari vasati vaparadza Sodoma neGomora, chinhu chavanokoshesa kuzivisa zvavanoda kuita kuna Abrahama, nezvaifanira kutarisira nenguva duku.

Mwari anotanda-tandara naAbrahama vatumwa vaviri vachinobudisa avo vaizonzwa yambiro yavo. Nanhasi kune vatumwa vanoita basa iri, kudaidzira vanhu vaMwari kubuda muBaibironi (Zvakazarurwa 14:6–12, Zvakazarurwa 18:1–4). Panopiwa yambiro yekupedzisira, Mwari vanotaurirana naAbrahama zvavakuda kuitika, nekuzviisa pasi pemibvunzo yemutana. 

“ ‘Kure nemi kuti mungaita chinhi ichi, kuparadza vakarurama nevasina kururama,’ ” Abrahama akabvuma, “ ‘Ko Mutongi wepasi rose haangaiti zvakanaka here?’ ” (Gen. 18:25). Abrahama haasi kutaura nyaya yeSodoma chete, asi ari kuwongorora chimiro chaMwari. Ndizvo zvichaitikawo kuhuma kusati kwasvika, Mwari achazarura mabhuku ekudenga (Zvakazarurwa 20:4, 11–15) nekutitendera kuawongorora moto wekupedzisira wekuparadza usati wavuya panyika. Tichava nemakore chiuru kuti mibvunzo yakawanda ipindurwe, iyo ikozvino isina mhinduro.

`Jehova vasati vaparadza vakaraswa, vanopa makore 1000 kukunzwisisa kuti chii chichaitika kunani. Ko izvi zvinozivisa chii nezvechimiro chaMwari uye nekubvumira zvisikwa kumuwongorora—vanhu vanoraramiswa naye-vasina kodzero iri mavari yekuziva zvinhu izvi?`
