---
title:  Rurongo Kuguma Kusati kwasvika
date:   05/06/2025
---

Tsanangudzo yerutongo runowanikwa Danieri 7 runoratidza zvishoma zviri sei kwechidzitiro—kuratidza kushamisa kwemagadzirirwe achaita dambudziko rechitema naMwari asi achidzikinura avo vanoda kupinda musungano yevukama naye.  

`Verenga tsanangudzo yerutongo rwekufeya-feya inopiwa muna Danieri 7:9, 10, 13, 14, 22, 26, na27. Chii chakanyanya kunangwa murongo? Zvinonzi kudini pakupera kwerutongo? Ko izvi zvinotivudzei nezvezano reruponeso?`

Hazvina mubvunzo kuti vanhu vachatongwa naMwari. Muparidzi 12:14 inotivimbisa izvi “Mwari achatonga mabasa ose” naPauro anotiyevuchidza kuti tisatongane, nekuti “tose tichamira pamberi pechigaro chaKristu” (VaRoma 14:10). Mwari, hongu, haadi zvakanyorwa kuziva vakaponeswa kana vakaraswa, asi vatumwa-vakanga vachiwongorora zano reruponeso kubva kare (1 Petro 1:12)–vangava nemibvunzo. Vaiva zvapupu zvekupanduka kwaSatani vakavona chitatu chevatumwa chichidzingwa kubva kudenga (Zvakazarurwa 12:4)—zvino Mwari avakutiisa pamberi pake. Mwari anozarura mabhuku nekutendera vatsvene kuvona zvose. 

Nyaya yaAbrahama achikumbirira Sodoma neGomora—rudzi rwerutongo—inotiratidza zvakakosha. Zvivi zveSodoma zvechokwadi zvakawongororwa; Mwari vanozvitaura kuti zvivi zveguta zvanzvikwa zvikuru kwazvo (Gen. 18:20). Asi akanga asiri Sodoma neGomora akawongororwa iwo asati aparadzwa. Mwari akavhurirazve Abrahama mukana wekudzidza kuti Mwari waiita zvakarurama here mukuparadza vasakarurama. 

Panguva iyi ndiani anovonekwa parutongo mudenga, muna Danieri 7, hakuna asi  “Mwanakomana wemunhu,” Jesu (Dan. 7:13, tarisa woMateo 20:28), kuvonekwa kwake kuri iko chikonzero choga nei rutongo urwu “ruchiitira zvakanaka vatsvene veweKumusoro-soro” (Dan. 7:22). Kururama kwake koga kunonatsa vanhu vake parutongo.  

`Imbofungidzira wakamira parutongo nezvakavanzika zvako zvose zviri pamberi paMwari mutsvene. Tariro yako iripapi panguva yerutongo? (Tarisa Friday.)`
