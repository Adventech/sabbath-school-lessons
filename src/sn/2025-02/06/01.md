### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Isa. 1:2–15, VaHebheru 10:3–10, Ekisodo 12:1–11, 1 VaKorinde 5:7, Hag. 2:7–9, Isa. 6:1–5, Zvakazarurwa 4:7–11.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Vakaimba rwiyo rutsva vachiti: Ndimi makafanira kuti mutore bhuku nekuzarura zvisimbisa zvayo; nekuti makabaiwa mukatengera Mwari neropa renyu vanhu vanobva kumarudzi ose, nendimi dzose nevanhu vose, nendudzi dzose” (Zvakazarurwa 5: 9). 

Jesu akavuya kwaari, Johani Mubhapatidzi anotaura achiti: “ ‘Tarirai Gwaiana raMwari rinobvisa zvivi zvenyika!’ ” (Johani 1:29). Pakanga pasina kukanganisa kureva zvemuhuka dzaibairwa, zvaimiririra basa rekumiririra raKristu pachinzvimbo chevanhu vose. 

MuBhaibheri, hatigoni kutiza nyaya yekubairwa kwemhuka; inovonekwa muBhaibheri rose uye inebasa rakakosha kwazvo munyaya yemuna Zvakazarurwa, umo Johani anotungamirirwa kuinda pachigaro chaMwari (Zvakazarurwa 4 na5). Kuvonekwa kwaJesu apa kwakakosha, akaita segwaiana rakabaiwa (Zvakazarurwa 5:6), zvakakoshera kunzvisisa zviprofita zvose.
 
Vhiki ino tichatarisa pane mimwe misoro yenyaya dzezvibairo zvinotibatsir akunzwisisa Jesu, Gwaiana rakabaiwa, gamba remuimba yerutongo. Anogashirwa seakafanira, pasina mumwe, nekukodzera kwakatsanangurika kunotaura zvinhu zvikuru nezvaTenzi mutsika dzezvibairo. Zvinomuratidza saMwari anerudo rusingaperi akaita chibairo chakazara chekupedzisira, izvi isu kana mumwe akachenjera zvaachashamiswa nazvo nekusingaperi.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo ichi kugadzirira Sabata, May 10._
