---
title:  Jesu Patembere
date:   07/05/2025
---

Mune kunetsana kukuru munyaya yose yeruponeso. Mwari anoda kudzorera kudyidzana kwaivapo pakati pedu naye. Asi kukwevera vatadzi pedyo naye kunovavuraya. “Nekuti hamusi Mwari anofanirira zvisakarurama,” Davida anonyora, “kana zvakarurama hazvingagari nemi” (Mapisarema 5:4). Anonyorazve achiti, “Asi kana ndiri ini, ndichavuya mumba menyu nekuwanda kwekwevunyoro hwenyu; Ndichanamata ndikarangarira kutembere yenyu tsvene ndichikutyai” (Mapisarema 5:7). 

`Verenga Haggai 2:7–9. Paivakwa tembere yechipiri, muprofita Haggai akaita chitsidzo chinoshamisa: tembere itsva ichavayakanaka nekubwinya kupfuvura yekutanga. Chiprofita ichi chairevei?`

Solomoni paakapa tembere yaakanga avaka kuna Mwari, mbiri Shekaina—inoratidza kuvapo kwaMwari yakanga iripo vaIsraeri vachibva Kenani—yakazadza tembere, zvekuti parisita havana kukwanisa kupedza basa rekushumira ravaiita (1 Madzimambo 8:10, 11). Pakapirwa temberi yechipiri areka yakanga isipo nekuti Jeremia akanga aiviga. Kuvapo kwaMwari kunovonekwa hakuna kuvatembere apa, zvakarwadza chaizvo. Ko chiprofta chaHaggai chaingabva chaitika nemo-nemo? 

Maiva mutembere yechipiri umo Jesu, Mwari avamunhu, akavonekwa Pawuzima, nenyama neropa. Mwari Pachake akabuda seri kwechidziro kuvuya kuzova mumwe nesu munyika yakabvaruka. Nekuti Mwanakomana waMwari zvino akanga avaMwanakomana wemunhu, taigona kuchivona huso hwake, kunzwa inzwi rake, nehuchapupu hwake, semuenzaniso, paakabata munhu aiva nemaperembudzi akamunatsa (Mateo 8:3). Pane kutiswededza pedyo naye, Mwari akaswedera pedyo nesu. Akaburuka, Iye mbune kwatiri. Saka hazvishamisi kuti Bhaibheri nezvaJesu rinoti: “ ‘Tarirai mhandara ichava nemimba, igozvara Mwanakomana, vachamutumidza zita rekuti Emanuweri,’ ndokuti kana zvichishandurwa, ‘Mwari unesu’ ” (Mateo 1:23). Funga kuti zvinorevei: Musiki kuzvipeta kusiri kuti agare nesu chete, asi kutifirawo. 
	
`Muchinjikwa chiratidzo chikurusa cherudo rwaMwari. Munzira dzipi umo tinogona kuvona idi rerudo rwaMwari?`
