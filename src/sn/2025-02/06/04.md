---
title:  Gwaiana RePaseka
date:   06/05/2025
---

Bhuku raZvakazarurwa rinoti Jesu aiva “Gwaiana” kanokwana ka 30. Kubva mumazuva ekutanga muzano reruponeso, vanhu vaMwari vakashandisa hwai semufananidzo waMessia aizovuya. Abero akabaira “dangwe rezvipfuyo zvake” (Gen. 4:4), uye Israeri isai yabuda muEgipita ichiinda kunyika yechipikirwa, vakarairwa kudzikinura matangwe evanhu kana mhuka negwaiana regore rimwechete (Ekisodo 12:5).

`Verenga Ekisodo 12:1–11; Isaya 53:7, 8; 1 VaKorinde 5:7; na Zvakazarurwa 5:6. Ko mavhesi aya anotidzidzisei nezvaJesu seChibairo chePaseka? Ko izvi zvinorevei kune mumwe nemumwe wedu?`

Makore mazhinji mushure mekufa, nekumuka nekukwidzwa kwaKristu, Petro akataura nezvakanga zvaitika, akanyora achiti, “muchiziva kuti hamuna kudzikinurwa nezvinhu zvisina maturo kubva munzira dzemadzibaba enyu, hamuna kudzikinurwa nezvinovora sesiriveri kana ndarama, asi neropa raKristu rinoyera, segwaiana risina charinopomerwa kana chipomerwa”” (1 Petro 1:18, 19).

Jesu akararama vupenyu hwemunhu achifadza Mwari; vamwe tose takatadza, nemararamiro edu echitema anonyepera vupenyu hwakachena hweMusiki wedu. Jesu, akava “Adamu wekupedzisira” (1 VaKorinde 15:45). 

Patakadonha, Iye akapagona. Muhunhu hwake, akaita zvose zviri munhu. Akaratidza mbiri yaMwari yakaperera. “Avona ini,” akavudza Firipi kudaro, “avona Baba” (Johani 14:9l). 

Jesu, akaroverwa panguva yePaseka, kuradza kuti ndiye Gwaiana. Muna Johani 18:19, 20, Jesu “akataura pachena” nedzidzi iyi. VaIsraeri vaisarudza hwai yePaseka voichengeta kusvika nguva yacho Ekodo 12:5, 6. Jesu paakabvunzwa nemuprisita mukuru nezcedzidziso dzake, akati ndaiva mutemberi muzava ose ndichidzidzisa izvozvo kuti vanhu vose vazvivone. Vupenyu hwake, mabasa, dzidziso—zvose zvairatidza kuti Iye ndiani. Gwaiana risina charingapomerwa, mashoko makuru kwazvo anoratidza kururama nembiri yaMwari. 

`Munzirai umotingaratidza zvirinani chimiro chaJesu muvupenyu hwedu?`
