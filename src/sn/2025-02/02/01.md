### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Isa. 40:7, 8; Gen. 22:1–13; Johani 3:16; Zvakazarurwa 5:5–10; 1 VaKorinde 15:15–19; Zvakazarurwa 12:1–9.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Ava mangwana wakavona Jesu achivuya kwaari akati: ‘Tarirai Gwaiana raMwari rinobvisa zvivi zvenyika” (Johani 1: 29).

Rimwe rematambudziko edudziro dzemazuva ano ezviprofita zveBhaibheri 
semuna Zvakazarurwa, nderekutadza kuziva mavambo ekare aZvakazarurwa.
Munyori anofungdzira kuti muverengi anoziva Testamende Yekare saka
Achishandisa nzira iyi. Kana nepo kutsvakurudza muBhaibheri rose ndima
dzinofanana nedziri muna Zvakazarurwa, zvinoshandazve kungoziva kuti kune
mavhesi akakoshera hwaro hwekudzidza Zvakazarurwa. Iri idi semuna Genesi,
ratinowana hwaro hwekutanga kwebongozozo rechitema. Zvinoita sekuti zvose
zvinotaurwa muna Zvakazarurwa zvinovonekwa—pane imwenzvimbo mune
chimwe chimiro muBhaibheri. 

Vhiki ino, tichadzidza pfungwa dzakakura pakati paZvakazarurwa. Dzakawanda chaizvo ndima idzi, saka tichasarudza shomanana kuratidza kuti zvakakosha kunzwisisa nheyo dzekare dziri seri kwaZvakazarurwa dzinobatsira vadzidzi kuvona zvakawanda kwazvo mundima, chimwe nechimwe chazvo chichiburitsa zvidzidzo zvakakosha zvehunhu hwevanhu, chimiro chaMwari, nehondo irikutsviriridza kudenga nepanyika, zvichisvika muvupenyu hwedu wo.
 
_*Dzidza chidzidzo ichi kugadzirira Sabata, April 12._
