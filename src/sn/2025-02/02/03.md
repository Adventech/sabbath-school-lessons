---
title:  Kunzwisisa Rudo RwaMwari 
date:   07/04/2025
---

Nhaka imwe yechitema kuzvikudza nechindini. Tinemiganhu mukuvona kwedu nyika tisina kufanana naMwari anoziva zvose. “Rudo” rwakakanganiswa kupinda zvimwe zvose nechitema. Dzimwe tsika dzavakukurudzira chindini kupfuvura kuda vamwe. Saka zvinotikanganisa kunzwisisa Mwari. 

Kunzwisisa rudo kwakakoshera kunzwisisa zviprofita. Nyaya huru kwazvo muhondo huru ndeyekusanzwisisa chimiro chaMwari. Ellen G. White, anopeta bhuku rake Hondo Huru achiti: “Denga nenyika zvachena. Kuwirirana kukuru nemufaro zvazara muzvisikwa. Kubva kuna Iye akasika zvose, kwavakuyerera chiedza, vupenyu nemufaro munzvimbo isina muganhu. Kubva kukanhu  kaduku-duku kuzvika kukura kwenyikka zvinhu zvose zvinofema nezvisingafemi, murunako rwazvo rusina kufukidza nerufaro, zvotaura kuti Mwari rudo.”—The Great Controversy, p. 678.

`Verenga Genesi 22:1–13. Panotanga kutaurwa nezve”rudo” muBhaibheri zviri muna Genesi 22:2. Ko nyaya iyi inotidzidzisei nezvechimiro cherudo rwaMwari? `

Kuwedzera pakuvona pfungwa kekutanga muBhaibheri zvakakosha kuvonawo painotangira muabhuku eBhaibheri–zvikuru sei muMavhangeri. Muna Mateo 3:17, Mark 1:11, Ruka 3:22, naJohani 3:16 tinowana kutanga kweizwi rinoti “rudo”. 

Semuenzaniso, muna Johani rudo rwuri muna (Johani 3:16): kutaura nezvaIsaka paaritari. Abrahamaa akatenda muna Mwari, achidaira kuti Mwari aizomutsa wake kana (VaHebheru 11:19). Zvakaratidza rudo rwaMwari kuvanhu. Akatida “zvekutipa Mwanakomana wake mumwe woga” (tarisa Gen. 22:2, 12, 16)—nekumumutsa kubva kune vakafa. Saka tinoratidzwa mhando yerudo, rwekuzvipira, urwo anotiratidza.
 
`Tinotanga sei kuratidza vamwe rudo rwekuzvipira urwi rwakaratidzwa naMwari kwatiri? Nemhaka yei rudo rwakadai rusiri matiri?`
