---
title:  Murairo Wekutanga Chaiwo
date:   27/04/2025
---

Munda weEdeni wakanga uri imba yekudzidzira yevanhu vaMwari vekutanga, apo vaisanga neMusiki wavo ivo nevana vavo. “Vaviri vatsvene ava vakanga basiri vana chete pasi pababa vanehanya Mwari,” Ellen G. White anodaro, “asi vadzidzi vari pasi peanenjere dzose Musiki. . . . Zvakavanzika zvezvisingavonekwi—‘mabasa anoshamisa aiye aneruzivo rwakaperera rwezvose’ (Jobho 37:16)— achivapa rairo isingaperi  yerufaro.”—Patriarchs and Prophets, pp. 50, 51. 

`Verenga Genesi  2:9–17. Rairo yekutanga yaiva yekuti kudini, yekurambidza, iyo Mwari akapa vanhu, uye nemhaka yei yakanga yakakosha?`

Kutanga kushandiswa kweizwi rekuti tswh, “kurairo kunesimba,”  uko Mwari vanhu kuri muna Genesi  2:16, rairo yekuti vasadya kubva pamuti wekuziva zvakanaka nezvakaipa. Ko rumwe ruzivo rungarambidzirwei? Ko hazivna kunaka here kungoziva zvakawandisa?  Hazvina kudaro muMagwaro. Chinangwa chaMwari chaiva chekusavaziva zvakasimba achivadzivirira kubva mukutambudzika kwenguva yakareba nekuda kwerumwe ruzivo, sezvaizoitika pamberi apo vanhu pavakasarudza kuzvitonga panekutangwa naJehova Pachavo. 

Nguva yakareba yapfuura, Israeri yakakumbira mambo, Jehova ndokuvavudza matuso ose, uye kuti zvaizogara navo nekusingaperi. Madzimambo paaiipisisa, sungano nevanhu vaMwari yakabva yaparara, ndokuvabvisa pazvironga, ndokuvapa zvakanyanya zvezvavaida: hurumende yevanhu.

Kutarisa bhuku raDanieri tambonzwa izvi zvakakosha: zvisiri zvekufamba kwevushe hunotaurwa mo “nekuhupa mhosva”—kutarisawo kukanganisa kwaIsraeri, kuramba mitswot (mirairo) yake. Mazana emakore ekutongwa, pane kuva murufaro rwakapiwa muEdeni pakutanga, aizova dzimba itsva dzekudzidzira umo mwoyo inoda yaizozvivonera kusiyan akwevutongi hwaMwari nehwevanhu.
 
`Funga nemarudzi eruzivo nhasi, zvaiva nani vanhu vasingazvizivi. Ko izvi zvinotibatsira sei kunzwisisa zvakarambidzwa muEdeni?`
