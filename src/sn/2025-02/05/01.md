### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Gen. 2:9–17; Dan. 2:31–35; Isa. 17:12, 13; Dan. 7:1–3; VaRoma 3:10–19; Zvakazarurwa 12:15, 16; Zvakazarurwa 10:1–11.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Nyararai kuti muzive kuti ndini Mwari; Ndichava mukuru pakatii pavahedheni, ndichava mukuru panyika” (Mapisarema 46: 10).

Kwemazera nemazera kune vanhu vanopikisa vachiti Mwari aida kuti vanhu vatadze, vanhu vadzikire pasi, zvinovatungamirira kuna—Jesu—nekumuchinjikwa. Iyewo iangadai akaratidza rudo rwake kuvanhu sei pane kuvafira pamuchinjiwa? Vanoti Mwari aingoda kuti vanhu vatadze chete.

Ipfungwa dzawora kwazvo idzi. Hachina kumbova chinangwa chaMwari chekuti Satani ave zvaari kana kuti vanhu vatadze. Kupanduka kwaSatani, nekwevanhu yakavatsaoma yakaipisisa kwazvo, nerufaro rwedu maari rungadai rwakaramba rwakanakisisa kudai vabereki vedu vasina kutadza. 

Vhiki ino ticharamba tichitarira matambudziko akakonzerwa neKutadza nechidiso chehurumende yevanhu vachiramba yaMwari. Zvokwadi izvi zvinonyatsoratidzwa mubhuku raDanieri, rinoratidza kuti Mwari haana chaakakanganisa paakayambira vanhu kusiyana nekuda kutongwa nevanhu vachibva kwaari. Izvi ndizvo chaizvo zvavakawana: madzishe epanyika, vatadzi vachiremedza vatadzi—pasina chakanaka.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo ichi kugadzirira Sabata, May 3._
