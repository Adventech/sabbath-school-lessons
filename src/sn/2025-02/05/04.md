---
title:  Danieri 7
date:   29/04/2025
---

Chiroto chaDanieri 2 chakaratidzwa kuna mambo weBabironi. Chiratidz chemuna Danieri 7, kune rumwe rutivi, chakapiwa kumuprofita wechiHeberu, nhengo yesungano yevanhu vaMwari.

Danieri anoratidza nyaya imwechete yakaratidzwa Nebukadinezari, asi kubva kune mamwe mawonero. Pachinzvimbo chekutombi, anovuya nyika dzichitevedzana dzichibuda mugungwa, zvichikonzerwa nemhepo yaimutsa mvura. Nyika idzi dzakaramba dzichingorwisana, zvichikonzera kushanduka kwemasimba pakati pavo. Mavhesi akafanana na Mapisarema 65:5–8; Isaya 17:12, 13; na Jeremia 46:7, 8 anoshandisa mufananidzo wemurowe nemafungu kuratidza mhirizhonga pakati pemarudzi. 

Asi Nyika Yechipirwa yaivapo, kwekanguva hako, chiri chitsuwa cherugare nekuchengetedzeka pakati pegungwa rehushe hwevaHedheni—rudzi rwakasimba rwaiswa panheyo yakasimb yehurumende yaMwari, zvasiyana nehwevanhu husina mutemo. 

`Verenga Danieri 7:1–3. Pane kufamba kwakanyanya apa. tinodzidzei kubva mumufananidzo uyu, sekubva mugungwa kwechikara chekutanga?`

Danieri anotarisa mhirizhonga dzehushe hwechiHedheni ari pamahombe-kombe kusvika chikara chasvika panyika—munyika yacho! Matambudziko evahedheni zvino ava ake. Vakanga vada kurarama sevaHedheni, saka vava pasi pavo. Kutangira paBabironi, vanhu vesungano yaMwari havana kuzofarazve mukuvitonga. 

Kurasikirwa uku kwekuzvitonga hakuchadzokizve kusvika kuguma kwenguva, apo Kristu achadzorera hutongi hwake hwakanaka saMambo. MuTestamende Itsva vanhu vaMwari vakatambura pasi peHushe hweRoma nemushungurudzwa munguva yekarunyanga kaduku, rwakatevera Roma yechihedheni. 

Kananepo hushe hwakasiyana-siyana, humwe huchifara humwe huchitambudza. Zvese izvi nekuda kwendini chehushe. Kusiyana kwakadini nehwaMwari kudai vanhu vake vakangohusadza. 

`Ko VaRoma 3:10–19 inobatsira sei kutsanangura nyika yedu? Vhesi 19, zvikuru sei, inoratidza kudiwa kweevhangeri muvupenyu hwedu?`
