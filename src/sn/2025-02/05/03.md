Muhunhapwa hweBabironi, kuburikidza neurofita Danieri, Mwari anotsanangura zvinokwezvera zvevukamwa pakati pake nevanhu vake nemadzishe enyika. Kuzvitonga kwevanhu vake kwakanga kwapera; vakanga vava kukohwa matuso esarudzo dzavo. (Zvimwe vachidzidza kubva maari).

`Verenga Danieri 2:31–35, inopa mamiriro ematongerwe enyika nenzira inoshamisa kwazvo kusvika kuguma kwenyika. Tinodzidzei kubva muzviprofita zvinoshamisa zvakadai?`

Pakupera kwekuma 1900, vanhu vazhinji vakanga vave kuratidza ruvimbo rutsva mubudiriro dzevanhu. Pepa rekuParisi “The Paris Exposition (1900), semuenzaniso, rakaratidza izvi zveremangwana. Zvokwadi nevuchenjeri hose hwesainzi, matambudziko mazhinj evanhu avakupera! Vanhu pavakapinda muma2000, makava nekujekerwa kukuru—kwakafanana nekuperera kwevanhu nesimba rekufunga—raizounza nguva itsva kuvanhu.

Hondo Yepasi Rose I, sezvinei yakapwanyira zviroto zvose izvi pasi, uye pakupera kwe 2000, takanga tarisikirwa nevanhu vanopfuura 200 muhondo. Tinogona kuva taiva tafamba chaizvo muhuchenjeri, asi pasina kuzvidzora. Kuisa mumazwi mashoma mashoko Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., tine mamisairi asina anoatungamirira. Izvi zvinotyisa zvikuru kwazvo.

Vadzidzi vezviprofita vakawanda vakawona Danieri 2 zvichibva mukukosha zvichiinda mukusakosha: ndarama kuinda kusiriveri, siriveri ichiinda kundarira, ndarira ichiinda kusimbi, kusvika tapedzisira tiine simbi neivhu remuumbi.

Charles Darwin, Karl Marx, nevamwe vafungi vema1900 vakaedza kutibvumisa kuti vanhu varikuita zvirinani—kuti taiva tichishanduka zvishoma nezvishoma mumiviri nemuhukama. Kana nepo kurarama kwevanhu kwasimukira mune dzimwe nzira, (takatarisa zvagadzirwa) mumwe anogona kutarisa kune remangwana renyika ino, ichitongwa, nekubudirira nerugare nerunyararo? 

`Jesu akayambira, “ ‘Muchanzwa nezveguhu rekurwa nehondo. . . . Nekuti nyika ichamukira nyika nevushe huchamukira hushe. Uye kuchavako nenzara, nezvirwere, nekudengenyeka kwenyika munzvimbo zhinji’ ” (Mateo 24:6, 7). Zvisinei neyambiro idzi, tinogona kuwana kunyaradzwa sei mukuziva kuti takagara tayambirwa nezvazvo kare?`
