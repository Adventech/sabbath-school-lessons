---
title:  Kudonha KwaLusifa
date:   15/05/2025
---

Zvinouita sekuti Lusifa haana kumbova kerebu yaifukidza, anechigaro chepamusoro-soro pedyo naMwari. Kuvapo kwake kwafanira kubatsira kuratidza mbiri yaMwari kudenga nepanyika. Asi iye akatanga kufunga nembiri yake, isiri yeMusiki wake; kana kutoti akatanga kufunga kuti akanga asiri kupiwa rukudzo rwaakodzera. 

`Verenga Ezekieri 28:11–17 na Isaya 14:12–14. Chii chakakonzera kuwa kwaLusifa? Enzanisa ndima idzi na Zvakazarurwa 14:1–12. Ko muenzaniso uyu pakati pekuwa kwaLusifa nechigaro chemunhu chakakwirira muna Kristu zvinozivisa kunzwisisa kwako kwezvakaitika muna Zvakazarurwa 14 sei?`
	
Tarisa kuti Lusifa akabviswa sei pagomo dzvene, apo vatsvene vachamira paGomo reZioni neGwaiana raMwari. Apo paiva naLusifa muEdeni; nevanhuwo vaimbovapo, asi kana zvichiinzaniswa nezvakaitika kuna Lusifa, vanhu vachadzorerwa muparadiso kuburikidza naKristu. (Tarisa Zvakazarurwa 22:1–3.)

Ipapa, chinyorwa chinobva kuna Ellen G. White chinodzidzisa chaizvo: “Denga richakurira, nekuti nzvimbo dzakasiiwa naSatani nevatumwa vake dzichazadzwa nevakadzikinurwa vaJehova.”—The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, May 29, 1900.

Uye vakava kudenga nekuda kwevhangeri chete. Shoko guru revhangeri, rudzikinuro, rakazara nezvichaitika mukamuri reimba yechagaro zviri muna Zvakazarurwa 4 na5. Semuenzaniso vatumwa vanodaidzira: “Ndimi makafanira kuti mutore bhuku nekuzarura  zvisimbisa zvayo; nekuti makabaiwa mukatengera Mwari neropa renyu vanhu  vanobva kumarudzi ose, nendimi dzose nevanhu vose nendudzi dzose”  (Zvakazarurwa 5:9). Mufananidzo wakanaka zvakadini weevhangeri, rufu rwaJesu rwekudzikinura rudzi rwevanhu!

Tarisa kuti ndizvo zvatinodaidzirwawo kuti tiparidze shoko remutumwa wekutanga “evhangeri isingaperi. . . . kune vanogara panyika—kumarudzi ose, nendimi dzose nevanhu vose nendudzi dzose” (Zvakazarurwa 14:6). Zvakakosha zvatakaitirwa naKristu zvekutifira zvinongoda kuti wose azvidzidze nekutenda.

`Ibasa ripi iro kereke kana iwe woga raunaro mukuzivisa vanhu nezvatakaitirwa naKristu?`
