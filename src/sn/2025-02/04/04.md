---
title:  Kupiwa Zvawakakumbira
date:   22/04/2025
---

Sezvakaitwa kare, Israeri yakanga isngafaniri kuva nemunhu ari mambo, sedzimmwe ndudzi. Asi kutenda kweIsraeri kwakadzikira, vakada kufanana nedzimwe “nyika,” dzechihedheni.

`Verenga 1 Samueri 8:4–18. Unofunga kuti chii chakavonekwa chakanakira kuna namambo nevakuru ava? Ko nesuwo tinowira mumusungo wakafanana sei?`

Zvakakosha kuvona kuti kukumbira mambo kwaiva kuramba kutongwa naMwari. Senyika yakanga yatangwa, vaifanira kuzvidavirira kuMusiki, nevukama hwavo naye hwakaratidwa musenjuwari nemumarusando ayo. Kukumbira mambo kwaizounza matambudziko edzimwe nyika pamusoro pavo: kutorwa vachiiswa kuhondo, kutorerwa zvavo, mitero nezvimwe zvinorwadza. Vaizovona kuti madzishe evanhu aitonga achiitira hama dzavo zvakanaka, pane kuenzanisira vanhu vose saMwari. 

Uyezve zvavakanga vakumbira zvaizogara zvakadaro nekusingaperi: kunyange Israeri yaivona kuzvidzikisira kwazvo, vaizogara nazvo nekusingaperi “ ‘Nezuva iro muchachema kwazvo nekuda kwamambo wenyu wamunenge mazvitsaurira; asi Jehova haangakupinduriyi nezuva iro’ ” (1 Sam. 8:18).

Mwari anoziva kushaya simba kwevanhu vake, akazviturira Israeri zvisati zviitika. Ndizvo zvatinovona kunhoroondo tsvene matuso esarudzo.

Verenga Deuteronomio 17:14–20. Tarisa kuti Mwari haasikuti, “Ndichavapa mambo”, asi vanhu vake vakazvisarudzira kuda mambo. Mwari akanga vadzivirira kuzvakaipa zvinoitwa nemadzishe vari vanhu—sezviri munhoroondo yemadzimambo, yambiro yakarambwa.

Kungotarisa zvakaitika kwaIsraeri nemadzimambo aya zvinoratidza kuipa kwawo. Kana nepo mamwe madzishe aiva nani kupinda mamwe, “akanaka”, acho akaitawo zvakaipa (funga nezveDavidai naBethsheba). Nguva dzakawanda kwaivuya mambo iota “zvakaipa pamberi paJehova” (tarisa 1 Madzimambo 11:6, 1 Madzimambo 15:26, 1 Madzimambo 16:30, 2 Madzimambo 3:2.) Kareko, kana nanhasi, hurumende dzose dzevanhu dzakafanana: vatadzi vanotonga vatadzi. _Asi chii chakakanganisika?_
