### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Gen. 10:1–12, Gen. 12:1–9, 1 Sam. 8:4–18, Mateo 20:25–28, Zvakazarurwa 18:1–4.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Akapiwa simba nekubwinya nevushe kuti vanhu vose nemarudzo ose nemarimi ose vamushumire; simba rake revushe isimba risingaperi; risingazopfuuri , vuye vushe hwake haungazoparadzwi” Danieri 7: 14.

Bhuku raZvakazarurwa rinoratidza mhinduro yaMwari kunyika yakatadza. Muzvitsauko zvekupedzisira, vanhu vanotenderwa kunodya muti wevupenyu, chituko chabviswa, tadzorerwazve pamberi paMwari. Zvakazarurwa, ibhuku raGenesi rainda nhendeshure, ndicho chikonzero nei Genesi rakakosha kwazvo mukunzwisisa matambudziko enyika nezvakaitika pamberi.

Imwe nyaya huru iri mose muna Danieri na Zvakazarurwa ndeyehurumende yepanyika, kuedza kwevanhu kutorerana simba rekutonga panyika iri yaMwari uyo pakupera kwezvose izvi achaitonga nekururama.

Hurongwa hwakareba kwazvo kusvika apa, hunotora zviuru zvemakore vanhu vachiidza kuzvitonga. Havasati vashanda, kunyange avo vanoidza kusvika pane zvinotarisirwa vanokundikana chaizvo. Zvinosuwisa kuti kwemakore mazhinji hapana chabuda asi nhamo nematambudziko ndizvo nhaka yedu. Uye zvicharamba zvichiipisisa kusvika “vushe husingaperi” hwaMwari hwagadzwa (Dan. 7:27) pakupedzisira.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo ichi kugadzirira Sabata, April 26._
