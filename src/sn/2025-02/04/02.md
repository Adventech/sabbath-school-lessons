---
title:  Nimrodi NeNinivah
date:   20/04/2025
---

Edeni yakasikwa iri musha wakaperera wevanhu. Chitema changopinda, Mwari akanga asisina zvimwe zvaaingaita asi kubvisa munhu muMumunda weEdeni nepamuti wevupenyu, panguva ino hapo. 

Kunze kwemunda vanhu vanotarisirwa kushanda nesimba kuti vararame. Vupenyu hwakabva hwawoma; munhu anofanira kudya cheziya (Gen. 3:16–19). Vabereki vedu vekutanga vakavimba kuti mambo wakanaka waizovadzorera muEdeni,  vakaunza zvibairo pamasuwo eEdeni vachitarisira mukutendeka rudzikunuro rwakavimbiswa naMwari kubva pakutanga chaipo. 

“Munda weEdeni wakaramba uripanyika makore nguva yakareba munhu adzingwa. . . . Ndipo paivuya Adamu nevanakomana vake kuzonamata Mwari. Vakabaira mukurevurura kudarika kwavo mutemo wakavadzingisa muEdeni. Pakapararira kusarurama munyika, vanhu vakazviwunzira kuparadzwa nemurowe, ruvoko rwakadyara Edeni rukaibvisa panyika. Asi muzvitsva, muchava “nenyika itsva nedenga idzva’ (Zvakazarurwa 21:1), zvichanakisisa kupfuvura zvekutanga.”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 62. Asi vakashanda “nemazano” evanhu kugadzirisa matambudziko edu, saka tichivona maguta makuru, kuedza kugadzira vupenyu hwakareruka, zvimwe kuidza kudzorera zvakarasika muEdeni.

`Verenga Genesi 10:1–12. Bhaibheri apa ndipo parinozivisa zvimwe zvakaita mabasa makuru kusanganisira NInivah neBabironi. Nekuziva zvakazoitika mumaguta aya, unodzidzei kubva mundima idzi?`
 
Vamwe vakaverenga nezvaNimrodi vakati aiva gamba semagamba emungano dzechihedheni. Asi kana Bhaibheri richimutsanangura rinoti “muvimi mukuru panyika,” kana kuti “muvimbi mukuru pamberi paJehova,” hakusi kurumbidza. Nimrrodi aiva mukuru muzvinhu zvake, achimira “pamberi” paJehova mukuzvidza Mwari. Zvatinovona apa kupandukira Mwari kurikupararira, kuchainda mberi kusvika kumagumo, kusvika kose kwazoparadzwa zvachose.
 
`Nemhaka yei chitema chekumukira Mwari chichinyengera kwazvo kupfuura zvinovonekwa navanhu? Ko tingazvidzivirira sei kubva kutsika yevanhu iyi? `
