---
title:  Mukadzi Wevufeve WaHosea
date:   15/04/2025
---

Chikumbiro chaMwari kuna muprofita Hosea chinogona kuva pasina chakafanana nacho chakakumburwa kuti muranda wake aite: kuwana chifeve—zvine chinangwa! Asi Mwari aitibatsira kunzwisisa mavonero ake zvatinoita Hosea achishandiswa, kurwadza kwechitema chevanhu nekupanduka. Mwari murudo rukuru kwazvo akanga asarudza mukadzi, Israeri, uyo akanyengera nguva nenguva, asi zvinoshamisa kuti Mwari akamutorazve kwaari akamudzorera kwaari. 

`Enzanisa Hosea 1:2; Hosea 3:1; Zvakazarurwa 17:1, 2;  na Zvakazarurwa 18:1–4. Vufeve hupi hurikutaurwa apa? Sangano reMakristu ringadzidzei kubva munyaya yaHosea? Munzira dzipi umo kereke yadzokorora zvivi zveTestamende Yekare?`

Bhaibheri rinoisa pachena kuti zvivi zveIsraeri muTestamende Yekare zvichadzokororwa nekereke yaKristu yeTestamende Itsva. Vanhu vaMwari vakapengereka nekutevera zvimwari zvendudzi dzaiva dzakavapotedza vakazvipinza muhunhapwa. Kereke yeChikristu yakanyengerera Konistantino mutongi weRoma kuzogadzirisa kunetsana kwavo. Zvikakonzera vanhu vaMwari kuzvitsvakira “mhinduro” dzematambudziko avaita sevanovona. 

Matauriro aMwari ndeekutiratidza kuti zvaanonzwa kana tamutadzira. Avo vakambonyengedzwa nemudikani vanogona kunzwisisa kurwadziwa kwakaita Kristu nezvakwakaita kudenga. Chinonyanya kushamisa munyaya yaHosea kuvona zvaakapindana nazvo achitevera mukadzi wake akapata. 

Patinovona kuchema kwaMwari achidaidza vana vake kubva muBabironi, zvakakosha kuvona kuti anodaidza vanhu vake, asingadaidzi vatorwa. He knows them intimately. Anovada. Nyika ichiipisisa, anomira nemutengo waakatitenga neropa kuti tidzoke kwaari. Muchinjikwa waKristu kupfuura zvimwe zvose unotiratidza shungu dzaShe wedu dzekuponesa vanhu vake vakarasika.
  
`Munzira dzipi umo kereke imwe wo zvayo kana yedu ingadaro ichipata pamweya?`
