---
title:  Hamani NaSatani
date:   11/06/2025
---

Munyaya yaEsteri, tinosanga naHamani, anenzara yechigaro nesimba. Anokudzwa chaizvo muvushe, kupfuvura mamwe makorokota ose (Esteri 3:1). 

Kana tikaverenga muna Ezekieri 28:11–15 na Isaya 14:12–15 nezva Lusifa, tinogona kuvona zvakafanana naHamani, muvengi wevanhu vaMwari akaipa, anoramba kuwira pasi pevukuru hwaMwari. Kushingirira kunyengera kwaSatani kunovonekwa  munyaya yekuedza Kristu, paanomuisa pakakwirira penyika nekumuratidza vushe hose hwepanyika (Mateo 4:8–11). Kristu, sezvatinovona, akavuya kuzodzikinira nyika nekuitora seyake—akazviitira isu. Jesu ndiye hama mudzikinuri, hongu mubhadharo wakaaita kutidzinura nyika wakakurisa.

Mubhuku raZvakazarurwa, tinovona ruchiva rwaSatani rwekuda kutonga nekunamatwa rinopinza nyika ino mudambudziko. Kunyengera kwake kunogashirwa nenyika “nekushamisa” nekutevera chikara (Zvakazarurwa 13:3, 4), kusara vashoma vanoramba kumunamata. Ndivo vaanorwisa nechisimba. 

Hamani anovona Mordekai—mumwe wevanhu vaMwari vakasanangirika—haana kubvuma kusiya zvaaidavira nekuchengetedza kodzero yake “yekunamata”.  “Anoshatirwa “ (Esteri 3:5, 6), anobva azvimisira kuvuraya vanhu vose verudzi rwaMordekai nekuvabvisa panyika.
 
`Verenga Esteri 3:1–14, Zvakazarurwa 12:14–17, na Zvakazarurwa 13:15. Ndezvipi zvaunovona zvakafanana mundima idzi? Ko tsangudzo yaJohani yekereke yaMwari yemusaririrwa inofanana here neyaHamani yevanhu vaMwari?`

 Diaborosi akati nyika iyi ndeyake, asi vanhu vanogara pamberi paMwari vanomuteerera-vanocheengeta mirairo yake-vanoramba zvinotaurwa nadiaborosi. “Sabata parichava nyaya huru muhondo muChikristu, kuramba kwekaboka kaduku kubvumza kune zvinenge zvisikunakidzirwa pavanhu zvichakonzera kuti varwiswe zvakaipisisa.”—Ellen G. White, Signs of the Times, February 22, 1910.

`Funga zvinhu “zviduku” zvinozunungusa kutenda kwako ikozvino. Kana ukazvishaira hanya “zvinhu zviduku,” ko unozoita sei muzvikuru?`
