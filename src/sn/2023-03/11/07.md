---
title:  Pfungwa yokuwedzerera
date:   08/09/2023
---

Ruremekedzo rwaPauro kuvana sezvo vari vatendiwo (VaEfeso 6:1-3) runosimudzira hanya yedu pamusoro pemabatirwe evana munyika yedu nhasi. Shoko rake kumadzibaba (VaEfeso 6:4) rinotidaidza kuti tifungisise basa revabereki. Rairo yaPauro kuvaranda (VaEfeso 6:5-8), pamwe nerairo kunavatenzi vevaranda (VaEfeso 6:9) inopa chitsvambe chikuru, nokuda kokuva kure kwechimiro ichi kuvazhinji vedu, uye nokuti tinoziva kuti hutapwa, hwemhando ipi zvayo, ndechimwe chezvinhu zvakaipisisa. Kunyangwe zvakadaro, mashoko aya akafemerwa sezvo ari muBhaibheri, tinofanira kufunga kuti tingaaisa sei mukurarama kwedu nhasi. Pamwe chete nevatendi venguva yekare iyi, tine mukana nebasa rokurarama dzidziso idzi muhukama pakati pedu. Mibvunzo yekukurukura inotevera yakaitirwa kusimudzira basa iri rakakosha:

**Mibvunzo Yekukurukura**:

`Zvinorevei kuMasabata kuti rudo kuvana runoonekwa sehumbowo hwekuva “vanhu vakamirira Ishe”? Ruka 1:17 (ichibva pana Maraki 4:6).`

`Ruremekedzo ruri pachena rwaPauro kuvana runobudisa mubvunzo wekuzvinzvera: Basa rako nderipi pakuratidza hanya yaKristu kuvana vakasangana nemhirizhonga, kubatwa chibharo, nekunyadziswa pahudiki hwavo? Tichitarisa tsvakurudzo yakaitwa pamusoro pezvisingafadzi zvinosangana nevana (ona https://www.cdc.gov/violenceprevention/), basa rako ringava ripi kwavari?`

`Senzira yekuratidza ruremekedzo rwaPauro kuvana, nehanya yaJesu pamusoro pavo, ibasa ripi ringaitwa nekereke pakurera nekuchengetedza vana vari mukereke? Ndezvipi zvinofanira kugadziriswa kuti basa iri ribudirire?`

`Paul’s counsel to slaves and slave masters, Ephesians 6:5–9, is often applied to the relationships between employees and employers. In what ways might this be appropriate? What dangers present themselves in doing so?`

`Nyaya yekutora nhapwa iri kuitika zvinorwadza munyika yedu, vanhu vanodarika mamirioni makumi mana (4 million) vari muhutapwa (tichitevera zvinotaurwa ne “The Global Slavery Index,” http://www.globalslaveryindex.org/). Sevanhu vakasununguka, vateveri vemadzibaba ezvomweya akazvipira kare kubvisa zvachose hutapwa, basa redu nderipi kuvana vaMwari vakatapwa, sezvo tichiimba, “Makashu uchadimbura, nokuti nhapwa ihama, muzita rake, hudzvinyiriri hwose huchapera”?`
