---
title:  Bhanire neChidzitiro cheChifuva
date:   18/09/2023
---

`Pauro anoona sei vatendi vachitanga kugadzirira hondo nezvakaipa? VaEfeso 6:14; ona 1 Petro 4:1, 1 Petro 5:8, VaRoma 8:37-39.`

Yambiro yaPauro pamusoro pehondo huru (VaEfeso :13) inogadzirira vaverengi kurukoko rwekupedzisira rwekumira (rwechina, enzanisa VaEfeso 6:11, 13) uye rukoko rwacho rwakadzama rwekukokera kuhondo (VaEfeso 6:14-17). Pauro anotsanangura chiito cheku“sunga chiuno” (enzanisa Isaya 11:5). Nhumbi dzekare, dzaiva dzisingabati pamuviri dzaida kusungwa muchiuno munhu asati apinda pabasa kana pakurwa (enzanisa Ruka 12:35, 37; Ruka 17:8). Pauro anoona mutendi achipfeka nhumbi dzokurwa sezvaiita murwi wechiRoma, kutanga nebhanire rechiuto redehwe nezvishongo zvaro nezvisungiso. Pabhanhire apa, pairembera zvitambo zvematehwe zvakaputirwa nemakwati ematare, pamwe chete, zvaibudisa chinhu chakapamhama chaipfekwa sechiratidzo chenyembe. Bhanhire raishanda kusunga zvipfeko kana kuchengeta zvimwe zvinhu panzvimbo. 

Zvokwadi haibvi kuvatendi, asi chipo chaMwari (enzanisa ruponeso muna VaEfeso 2:8). Haifaniri hayo kugara kure, sechinhu chakachengetwa chiri kure, chisina shanduko yachinounza muhupenyu hwavo. Vanofanira “kushonga” zvokwadi yaMwari, kuti vararame nokushandisa chipo ichi chaMwari. Handivo vanotakura nokuchengeta zvokwadi yaMwari asi zvokwadi yaMwari ndiyo inavo uye inovachengeta. 

Pauro anoenda mberi, achikurudzira vatendi kupfeka “chidzitiro chechifuva chokururama” (enzanisa 1 VaTesaronika 5:8). Sezviri bhanire rezvokwadi, naicho chinobva kunaMwari, iri imwe yenhumbi dzaJehova pabasa rake semurwi (Isaya 59:17). Nhumbi dzokurwa ndadzo dzaipfekwa nevarwi pamazuva aPauro dzaigadzirwa nezvindori zvakapindana, makwati endarira kana dare, kana matare akapfekaniswa pamwe chete. Chipfeko ichi chaive chidzitiro chaidzivirira nhengo dzakakosha kubva kuzvombo zvomuvengi. Kana tichifananidza, vatendi vanofanira kuwana dziviriro pamweya, inobva pakudzivivira kwechipo chaMwari chokururama. Muna VaEfeso, Pauro anobatanidza kururama noutsvene, kunaka, nezvokwadi (VaEfeso 4:24, VaEfeso 5:9), achizvitora sezvinhu zvakakosha pakubata vamwe nokururama, zvikuru sei avo vatendi pamwe chete navo.

`Wakaona nenzira dzipi kuti kunaka, utsvene, nezvokwadi zvinogona kuva chidziviriro?`
