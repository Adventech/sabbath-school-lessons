---
title:  'Kupupura kuna Vakadzidza: Nikodimo'
date:   28/11/2023
---

Nikodimo aiva murume akadzidza. Bhaibheri rinomutsanangura semutongi wevaJudha (Johani 3:1). Jesu aomudaida achiti mudzidzisi weIsraeri (Johani 3:10). Aiva nekunzwisisa Bhaibheri kwakanaka uye aivanenzara pamweya yekuda Tenzi. Pamaonero evanhu, zvaiita sokuti aiva muteveri waMwari. Aichengeta mirairo yose, uye aivamutungamiri airemekedzwa pakati peVaJudha. Aiva nesimba ue akanga akapfuma. Vazhinji vanoona izvi sehumbowo hwekuti Mwari akanga amukomborera. Asi tikazotarisa, zvinoratidza kuti zvaingooonekwa kunze ndizvowo zvaiva kunze.

`Verenga Johani 3:1–12. Nyaya iyi inoduritsei pamusoro pehupenyu hwepamweya hwaNikodimo uye Jesu akamubatsira sei?`

Apo Nikodimo akauya kuna jesu, akaedza kuzvimisa nehushingi, sezvaasiita asi Mwari aiziva moyo wake. Zvimwechetezvo, Mwari anoziva moyo yevapfumi nevane masimba, zvisinei neranezuro ravo. Nikodimo akauya kuna Jesu nokuti dzidziso dzaJesu dzakana dzamubata. Kuzvikudza kwake kwakanga kwamukonesa kuuya pachena kubvuma kuti Jesu Kristu ndiye Tenzi, asi madekwana aya akashandura hupenyu hwake zvachose. Kunyangwe akanga abvuma kuti Jesu akabva kunaMwari, asi haana kugona kubuda pachena kuti akanga va muteveri waJesu Kristu.

`Verenga Johani 7:43–52 and Johani 19:39. Ndima idzi dzinotidzidzisei pamusoro paNikodimo naJesu?`

Tinoona pachena mundima idzi tinoona kuti Nikodimo akanga abatwa neshoko raJesu. Akatsvaga kumuchengetedza achiri mupenyu uye kumuremekedza mushure mekunge afa. Hazvina mubvunzo, kuti Niodimo akanga asvikrwa zvakazara naJesu, nokuti, aida ruzivo rwaMuponsei kunyangwezvo akanga akadzidza chose, sezvatinongitawo isu.

`Sei tichifanira kungwarira musungo wekufunga kuti sezvo tiine chokwadi (icho chatinacho) saka ruzivo rwechokwadi ichi rwega rwuchatiponesa? Mweya mingani icharaswa avo vakanga vaine ruzivo rwakawanda kunyangwe rweshoko rengirozi nhatu, asi vasingaponeswi?`
