### Ndima dzeChidzidzo Vhiki rino
Dhanieri 1:1–12; Dhanieri 6:1–9; Esteri 2:1–10, 20; Esteri 3:1–15; Esteri 4:1–14; Esteri 9:1–12.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Ndichapawo vuye chiedza kuvahedheni, kuti kuponeswa kwangu kusvikire kumugumowenyika.” (Isaya 49:6).

Imwe yenyaya dzinofadza muBhaibheri, myaya inechekuita nekuyambuka miganhu yetsika nevhangeri, inowanikwa mubhuku raEsteri. Zvizhinji zvakanyora mukufamba kwemakore pamusoro pebhuku iri, uye kusvikira nhasi vaJudha vanopemberera mutambo wePurim sezvavakauwana muBhuku raEsteri 9:26–31.

Esteri nehama yake Modhekai vaiava maJudha aigara muguta guru reSusa muhushe hwe VaPersia. Nekuda kwezvimwewo zvikonzero, ivo nevamwe vaJudha havana kudzokera kumusha asi vakaramba vari munyika yehutorwa.

Mushure, nekuda kwekukomborerwa, Esteri anova Mambokadzi. “Mambo akada Esiteri kupfuura vamwe vakadzi vose, akanzwira tsitsi novunyoro naye kupfuvura dzimwe mhandara dzose, naizvozvo akaisa korona youhosi pamusoro wake, akamuita vahosi panzvimbo yaVashiti” (Esteri 2:17).

Muchinzvimbo ichi ndimo Esteri, kunyangwe aitya, akakwanisa kuita rupandi rukuru munhoroondo yebhaibheri. Mukusiyana kwayo, nyaya iyi inoratidza kuita kwevanhu vaMwari, kunyangwe munyika dzeutorwa, kuti vanogona kupupura zvokwadi.

Nenguva iyoyo yaunayo, edza kuverenga bhuku raEsteri rose pachidzidzo chevhiki rino.

_*Dzidza chidzizo chevhiki rino uchigadzirira Sabata ra, Zvita 23._
