Verenga Ellen G. White, “The Great Commission,” pp. 25–34, and “A Seeker for Truth,” pp. 131–142, mubhuku The Acts of the Apostles.

“Asati akwira kudenga, Kristu akapa vadzidzi vake kutumwa kwavo. Akavaudza kuti vaifanira kuva vagoveri venhaka yake yaakanga akagadzirira nyika mufuma yehupenyu husingaperi. Akavaudza kuti ivovakanga vari zvapupu zvehupenyu hwake hwekuzvipira vakamirira nyika yose. Maona kushanda kwangu pakati paIsraeri. Asi kunyangwe vanhu vangu vasina kuuya kwandiri kuti vawane hupenyu, kunyangwe vaPrisita nevatongi vandiitira zvavakaita, kunyangwezvo vandiramba, vachawanazve mukana wekugamuchira Mwanakomana waMwari. Maona avo vose vanouya kandiri vachireurura zvivi zvavo, ndichivagamuchira pachena. Kwamuri vadzidzi vangu, ndinokupai shoko iri retsitsi. Rinofanira kupiwa kunavose, vaJudha neVahedheni-kuIsraeri, kutanga, tevere kundudzi dzose, ndimi dzose, navanhu vose. Vose vanotenda vanofanira kuunganidzwa muSangano rimwe chete,”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, pp. 27, 28.

Shoko reKutuma Kukuru riri pachena: ‘Endai naizvozvo mudzidzise marudzi ose’ ” (Mateo 28:19). Nokudaro, zviripachena kuti kunofanira kuenda kune vamwe, kunyanya kunemamwe marudzi.

Kutumwa nevhangeri ndicho chinhu chikuru chinoonekwa chichifanira kuitwa muhumambo hwaKristu. Vadzidzi vaifanira kushanda nesimba kutsvaka mweya, vachipa kuna ose rukoko rwetsitsi. Havaifanira kumirira kuti vanhu vauye kwavari; asi vaifanira kuenda kuvanhu neshoko.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 28.

“Kune vazhinji munyika nhasi vari pedyo nehumambo hwaMwari kufuura zvatinofunga. Munyika yerima nhasi, Jehovha ane maparera ake anokosha, uko achatungamirira veranda vake kuenda. Kwese kwese kunevanhu vanogona kumirira Kristu. Vazhinji vachakoshesa uchenjeri hwaMwari kupfuura nyika, vachasimua kuva vasimudzi vemwenje. . . . Vachagutsikana kuti basa raPetro rakanga riri kuzadzisa zano raMwari, uye kuti kusagamuchirika kwavo nepfunga dzavo zvakanga zvichipikisana nemweya wevhangeri, vacharumbidza Mwari vachiti, ‘Zvino Mwari watenderawo VaHedheni kutendeukira kuvupenyu.’ Zvino, pasina hondo, midhuri inoparadzanisa yawira pasi, kukosha kwevamwe kupfuura vamwe kwakanga uri mumazera kwasiiwa, zvino nzira yazaruka kuti vhangeri iparidzwewo kuVahedheni” —Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, pp. 140–142.

**Mibvunzo Yekuzeya**:

`Ungatsanangura sei vara rinoti “basa raMwari” uye rinorevei muhupenyu hwako?`

`Ungaratidza sei, mazuva ese, nekaitiro kako, kuti uri mubasa? Ungapa sei pfungwa dzako kuti dzigare dzichifunga basa mukuita kwako mabasa emazuva ose?`

`Zvakakosha sei kuti tinzvere moyo yedu nekutsvaga simba rinobva kumusoro kuti ribvise maonero asiwo atinoita vamwe vanhu vasina tsika sedzedu?`
