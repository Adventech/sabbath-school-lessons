---
title:  Rudo Rukuru
date:   23/11/2023
---

Sekuziva kwedu tose, uye tinonyatsoziva kuti zvidiso zvehupenyu hazviperi. Kana uchida kubatsira vamwe, uchawana mikana mizhinji yokudaro. Dzinogona kunge dziri shamwari dzepedyo kana vapoteri vari kure, asi vanu vanoda rubatsiro, uye tinofanira kubatsira, pose patinogona napo. Jesu aibatsira avo vasingakwanisi kuzvibatsira. Panedzimwe nguva, iye ndiye aitanga kutora danho racho, otoenda kwavari, asi munedzimwe nguva, sezvakitwa neshamwari dzemurume akanga akaremara, vanhu ndivo vakatanga kutora danho rekuuya nemurwere kwaari iye akamubatsira.

“Hakuna rudo rukuru kuno urwu, kuti munhu unorasha vupenyu bgwake nokuda kwehama dzake” (Johani 15:13).

`Tingashandisawo sei chimiso ichi mubasa redu rekushumira?`

Imwe mhuri yechiMishinari yakashanda kwemakore matanhatu munyika yeTrinidad ne Tobago. Makore matatu ekutanga vaigara munharaunda yevanhu vezvitendero cheHindu nechiMuslim. MaHindu mazhinji vanonyunyuta kuti maKristu havadi kuuya kana vakokwa kumarusando ekutenda anoitwa kamwe pagore. Rimwe zuva maKristu aya akaenda kurusando urwu rwakaitwa neimwe shamwari yavo yechiHindu. Vakazviita izvi vachitevera muenzaniso waJesu: uyo aishanyira shamwari dzake kana vamukoka kumitambo yavo. Chitenderi chechi Hindu chinodzidzisa kuti vaenze neshamwari vanouya nezvikomborero mumusha wako kana wavakoka.

Ngatiite hurongwa hwekutanga kuita hushamwari neumwe munhu vhiki rino. Chekutanga, tsvagurudza pamusoro penharaunda yako, musha wako kana guta rako. Unoziva here nezve mutorwa kana mupoteri anogara imomo? Ko vanhu vanogara mumugwagwa wemusha wako? Unovaziva vose here? Zvisinei nechimiro chavo, ziva kuti kuita hushamwari nevanhu vausingazivi hakuzi nyore. Ngatinamate tikumbire Mwari kuti abatsire. Ndiye anoziva vanhu vose kusanganisira nemutorwa uyo waurikuda kuita hushamwari naye. Rangarira chinangwa, ndechekuti uite hushamwari naye wozomutungamirira kuna Mwari kuti awane rubatsiro.

`Kurudziro: Dzidza pamusoro pevatorwa nevanhu vasiri vechiKristu vanogara munyika yako. Dandemutende re Joshuaproject.net inzvimbo yakanaka kuita tsvagurudzo pamusoro pemapoka evanhu vasati vasvikirwa neshoko murudzi rwako.`

`Chitsvambe: Tsvaga munhu umwechete munharaunda yako. Tanga kumunamatira zvakarongeka mushure mekupindura mibvunzo inotevera: Munhu uyu ishamwari yangu here maringe nezvimiso zva Jesu zvehushamwari?  Ndinoziva here zvidiso zvehupenyu hwake?  Ndingamutungamirira sei kuti anopreswa naJesu?`
