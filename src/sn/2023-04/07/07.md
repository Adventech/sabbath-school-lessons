Verenga Ellen G. White “The Good Samaritan,” pp. 497–505, mubhuku The Desire of Ages.

Kunevanhu vazhinji nhasimunyika vanenzara, vanoshaiwa, vasinagbatwi zvakanaka. Unogona kuita rupandi rwako, chero nepaduku paunokwanisa. Hatigoni kupedza kugadzirisa matambudziko ose enyika Jesu asati adzoka. Hazvisizvo zvatakadanirwa kuita. Asi, kusvikira ipapo, basa redu rinogona kutangira pakubatsira umwemunhu waunoziva asina zvokudya, kana kubatsira imwe nhengo yesangano isina kuitirwa zvakarurama, kana rusarura, rinova dambudziko guru munyika yedu kusvikira nhasi.

“Kutenda kwakachena kusina kusvibiswa pamberi paBaba ndekuku: “Kushanyira nherera nechirikadzi pakutambudzika kwavo, nekuvabatsira pakusasvibisva nenyika.’ Mabasa akanaka ndiwo muchero uyoKristu anoda kwatiri: mashoko manyoro, mabasa emutsa, kuitira varombo zvakanaka, vanoshaiwa, nevanotambudzika. Kana moyo ichinzwira avo vakaremerwa, vakakuhunika nekutambudzika, kana ruoko rwukatambanudzirwa kune anoshaiwa, kana vasina chokufuka vakapfekedzwa, kana mweni akapiwa pokugara mumba mako nenzvimbo mumoyo mako, ngirozi dzinoswedera pedyo, uye mhinduro inobva kudenga. Kuita kose kwokururamisira, tsitsi, nokupa zvinosvika sorwiyo rwunofadza kudenga. Baba vokudenga vari pachigaro chavo, vanoonaavo vanoita mabasa etsisti, vanovaverenga pakati peavo vanokosha vavo. ‘Vachava vangu, ndizvo zvinoytaura Jehovha wehondo, nezuva iro randichazviita.’ Mabasa ose etsitsi kunevanoshaya, vanotambudzika, anoverengwa seakaitirwa Jesu pachake. Kana ukabatsira murombo, ukachema neanoshungurudzwa neanodzvinyirirwa, ukava shamwari yenherera, unozviisa pedyo pedyo naJesu.” —Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 2, p. 25.

**Mibvunzo Yekuzeya**:

`Tingaita sei kuti tizive kuti murairo wekuda Mwari nevamwe haurevi kuponeswa nemabasa? Kana tichicherechedza kuti jesu ndiyani, nezvaakatiitira pamuchinjikwa (ona VaFiripi. 2:5–8), zvinotiratidza sei kuti chese chatingaita semabasa ekuti tiponeswe imhosho huru? Tingapatsanisa sei pakati pekushandira ruponeso, inova mhosho huru, nekuburitsa kubva muhupenyu hwedu kuti tineruponeso rwatakawana kuna Jesu?`

`Tingadzidza sei kuona zvimwe zverusarura zvinogonakuitika matiri kuneavo vakasiyana nesu?`

`Kunze kwendima idzi dzatadzidza vhiki rino, ndedzipi dzimwe ndina muBhaibheri dzinoratidza kuti tinofanira kuratidza hanya nevamwe vanhu, zvisinei kuti ndivanani?`
