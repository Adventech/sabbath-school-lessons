---
title:  Makereke EpaFiripai NepaVaKorose
date:  01/01/2026
---

`Verenga VaFiripi 1:1-3 na VaKorose 1:1, 2. Makereke ari paFiripi naKorose anotsanangurwa sei, uyezve pfungwa ino yakamira sei?`

Kwaziso yaPauro mumatsamba ake aanodana muKristu nemaguta iwayo kuti “Vatsvene”; kureva kuti muthabhatidzo vakasiyanitswa sevanhu vaMwari vake, sekuti vanhu veIsiraeri, kuburikidza nechirairo chekudzingiswa (Eksodho 19:5, 6; enzanisa na 1 Petro 2:9, 10), vakanga vakasiyaniswa se “rudzi rutsvene.” (Izvi hazvinei nemaitiro ekereke yeRoma ekutsvenesa vanhu sezvavanoti “vatsvene.”)

Chinochitadzisa zvakare husingadzikisike pakati pekwaziso dze tsamba idzi mbiri. Pauro anotaura nezve “vatariri nevadiyakoni” (VaFiripi 1:1) paFiripi neku “vatendi muKristu” (VaKorose 1:2) paKorose. Kana Testamende Itsva ichitaura nezve “vashandi,” vanobvisira pabasa rekereke (tarisa VaEfeso 6:21, VaKorose 4:7, 1 Petro 5:12). Saka zvinoita sekuti Pauro haasi kungoparidzira kune nhengo dzereke chete asi kune vatungamiri vezvikoro zvekumaguta aya. Kurehwa kwemahofisi anotsanangurwa pane dzimwe nzvimbo (semuenzaniso muna 1 Timoti 3:1–12, Tito 1:5–9) kunoratidza kuitika nekukosha kurongeka kwekukura kwekereke.

Kurasira kuvadzidzi vakaita sa Timoti na Epafrasi nekubatsira vatungamiri vemakereke ekunzvimbo idzodzo kune zvazvaikoshesa zvose nekufamba kwe evhangeri. Nemamwe mazwi, kwaiva neronga mukuenda nemukuchengetedza. Vatangi vedu vechi Adventist vakatevedza mufananidzo weTestamende Itsva wekurongeka kwekereke, sezvotinoona munyaya dzeAdvent Review neSabbath Herald dzemakore dze 1850. Chiratidzirwa na James White akati, “Hurongwa hwaMwari hweTestamende Itsva hwakakwana kuronga kereke yaKristu. Dai zvimwe zvaidiwa, zvaizouya.” —“Gospel Order,” *Advent Review and Sabbath Herald*, Dec. 6, 1853, p. 173. Nguva refu Pauro asati anyora kune makereke aya, vaapostora vakanga vatanga kuva nemahofisi ekereke muJerusarema (tarisa Mabasa 6:11-6, Mabasa 11:30), uko “kwaishandiswa senzira yekurongeka kwemakereke ari kunzvimbo idzo nhume dzechokwadi dzaibva nadzo.”—Ellen G. White, *The Acts of the Apostles*, p. 91.

Zvakajeka kuti Pauro aishandisa vabatsiri dzimwe nguva pakunyora tsamba idzi. Timoti anodomwa nemumwe anotumirawo kune idzi tsamba (semuenzaniso 2 VaKorinde 1:1, Firimoni 1). Paya Pauro paanoshandisa mazwi ekuti “Ndiri” tichasiya pfungwa dzake kuti simba rake ndiro rinenge riri shure kwe tsamba idzi.

`Tinonzwisisa sei nheyo dzerekereke patinovona mabasa akaitwa naPauro aya?`
