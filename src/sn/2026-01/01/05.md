---
title:  Pauro NevaKorose
date:  31/12/2025
---

Hatina nhoroondo inoratidza kuti Pauro akashanyira Korose, zvakare izvi zvinotitira nhoroondo yekugona kwaakanga akaita pabrasa rake. Chekutanga, waiva Epafrasi, mugari wepaKorose (VaKorose 4:12), uyo akaenda nevhangeri kuguta iri (VaKorose 1:7). Ko iye akatendeuka sei? Zvinonzi akatendeuka makore epakati dze 50, apo Pauro akanga ari kuEfeso “vose vakanga vakagara muAsia vakanzwa shoko raShe Jesu” (Mabasa 19:10; enzanisa naMabasa 20:31).

Bhuku raZvakazarurwa rinovonesa kupararira kwakaita evhangeri munzvimbo ino (Zvakazarurwa 1:4). Dzidzo inogamuchirika pakubudirira uku, kusanganisira kusvika kwaro paKorose, mhedzisiro yebasa revakatendeuka avo vakanzwa vhangeri vari paEfeso, rinova guta rakanga rakakosha muAsia Minor. Epafrasi ainzwa kuparidza kwaPauro kuEfeso akazova mumwe wevashandi pamwe naye uyo akazoenda nevhangeri kumusha kwake kwaiyenzvimbo yeKorose.

Guta iri pacharo raiva makiromita 15 kuenda zasi kwemabvazuva kweRaodhikia, richangoti kucherwa pari zvino pamafongore aro ekarekare, saka ruzivo rwacho ishoma tichienzanisa nemamwe maguta. Chatinongoziva ndechekuti raiva nevaJudha vakawanda “vanosvika 10,000 vaigara munzvimbo iyi yeFrigia.”—Arthur G. Patzia, *New International Biblical Commentary: Ephesians, Colossians, Philemon* (Peabody, MA: Hendrickson Publishers Inc., 1990), vol. 10, p. 3. Mari yaishandiswa paKorose inoratidza kuti vanhu vepo, sezvaingogara zviri muumambo hweRoma, ainamata vamwari vakawanda. Tsika dziye dzekunze nepesvedzero yeadzo dzairemedza maKristu epanguva iyi mubasa rekuparadzira denga uye nekusimbisa kutenda kune evhangeri yechokwadi. Mumwe muKristu wepaKorose waiva Firimoni, uyo angave akatendeuka panguva imwe chete na Epafrasi.

`Verenga Firimoni 15, 16. Tarisazve VaKorose 4:9. Pauro anokurudzira chii nenzira munyoro kuna Firimoni nezve Onisimo?`

Zvisinei nekuti mitemo yeRoma yaida kuti Pauro adzose Onisimo kuna Firimoni, Pauro anoita zvese panyaya yeuranda hwacho kuti Pauro achimutora semukoma nemunun'una. (Firimoni 16).

`Asizve chose chose patinovona chivi cheuranda, tichishuvira kuti dai Pauro akambochituka, tinogamuchira sei zvakataurwa naPauro pano? (Zvinofadza kuvona kuti panguva dzehuranda kuAmerica, Ellen G. White akakurudzira maAdventist kuti vasateerera murairo waiti vadzosere varanda vainge vatiza.)`
