title:  Kudzidza Kuenda Mberi
date:  02/01/2026
---

“Mwari akakusarudzai kuti muponeswe nekuve vatsvene mumweya nekutenda chokwadi. Saka, mirai makasimba. . . . Kana muchishandira Mwari nevatendi, muchasangana nematambudziko neruperuko; asi musaputswa moyo kana muchitambudzwa zvisina mhosva. Musatsiva. Rumbidzai kuzvipira kwenyu muna Jesu Kristu. Setai chiso chenyu kuenda kudenga. Regai vamwe vataure zvavo kutaura kwavo, voita basa ravo sevanoda; zvenyu kuri kupfuurira mberi nehunyoro nekudzikira kwe muna Kristu. Itai basa renyu nechinangwa chakasimba chakasvunuka, nekuchena kwe mwoyo, nesimba renyu rose, makazendama paruoko rwa Mwari. Kunaka kwehunhu nebasa renyu unogona usakatinyatsochivona. Kukosha kwe vupenyu hwenyu unongogona kukuyera nevupenyu hwawakapiwa ne weponeso hwenyu. . . .

“Kumweya kose kuri kukura muna Kristu, kune munguva apo panowonekwa matambudziko nekukunda; nekuti rima raSatani rakasimba mukudzivisa nzira yekukura nadzo. Asizve chose patinotarisira pamuchinjikwa wa Kristu nekuda kwe denga, hatichagoni kukundikana. Chidzidzo cheMudunhurirwi ndicho, ‘Handizokusiyi kana kukurasai.’ ‘Ndinemi mazuva ose, kusvika pakuguma kwenyika.’ ”—Ellen G. White, mu *The Youth’s Instructor*, Nov. 9, 1899.

**Mibvunzo Yokutaurirana**:

`Pauro akavharirwa mujere kazhinji, asi ari asina mhosva. Ko iwe unopindura sei kana ukaitirwa zvakashata? Ko chipikirwa che Bhaibheri chipi chaungadome panguva dzakadai?`

`Pakutambudzwa kwechiKristu, Tertullian, mumwe wemutungamiri wekereke yekutanga wakati, “Chipeneti patinoita basa renyu tinoramba tichikura; ropa ravaKristu riri mhodzi.”—Alexander Roberts and James Donaldson, eds., Ante-Nicene Fathers, vol. 3, (Peabody, MA: Hendrickson Publishers, Inc., 1999), p. 55. Panguva dzimwe chete, kutambudzwa munzvimbo dzimwe munguva idzodzo nekureretesa basa rekereke. Ndedzipi dzimwe nzira dzatingatsigira nadzo vaya vanotambudzika nekuda kwekuteerera Mwari?`

`Funga nezve chemusoro chevhiki ino tichitarisa miedzo yakasangana naPauro. “Farai nguva dzose.” Ko izvozvi zvinorevei? Ko tinogona kuzviita sei? Mumwe waunoda ari kurwara kana kufa. Warasikirwa nebasa. Une kurwadziwa kwemuviri. Chipeneti nheyo iripo panyaya iyi ndeyekubvunza kuti, “Farai nguva dzose muna chii?” kureva kuti, chose chose chakatipoteredza, chichatidyi chinotiita kuti tigare tichifara?`
