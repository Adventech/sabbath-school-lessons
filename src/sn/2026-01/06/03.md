---
title:  Vupenyu HwaPauro “Hwekare”
date:  02/02/2026
---

Chinhu chakajairika muvupenyu hweChikristu kune vakatendevuka kufunga nezvevupenyu hwavo hwekare vasati vagashira Jesu nevupenyu hwake, sezvinoitwa naPauro munaVaFiripi 3. Dzimwe nguva tinogona kutaura zvakanaka kana zvakaipa nezvevanhu vasiri Makristu vachinzi “munhu akanaka,” uyewo kana zvichitariswa neziso remunhuwo zvake kune vanhu vakatonaka vo. Kune rumwe rutivi kana zvikaenzaniswa nekuvona kwaMwari, hakuna akanaka kunyange Makristu chaiwo.

`Muna VaFiripi 3:4–6, Pauro anotaura nezvinhu zvakawanda zvevupenyu hwake zvaaimbodada nazvo. Ndezvipi zvinhu izvi? Ungatsanangura sei “zvakanaka” muvupenyu hwako (hwekare kana hwanhasi)?`

Pauro anonyatsoenzanisa pakati pemaJudha anotenda vanoparadza vangeri renhema nevasina kudzingiswa vakatsamira pana Kristu nemwoyo wose vasingadairi kuti vangawana ruponeso kune mumwe munhu kunze kwemuna Kristu chete (tarisa VaHebheru 6:1, VaHebheru 9:14; Enzanisa VaRoma 2:25–29). Kana po vupenyu hwaPauro hwekare hwaimbofadza chaizvo kuhama dzake dzechiJudha, hakuna chakabatsiridza ruponeswo rwake. Asi zvakatorukanganisa nekuti zvakato mukonzera kusavona Kristu zvakanaka kwekanguva.

Pauro akanga asina kudzingiswa chete—akanga ari munhu “wezuva rerusere,”, muIsraeri akaberekwa nekurerwa muvanhu vechipikirwa, akadzingiswa nezuva rechinomwe. Uyezve aibva muimba yaBenjamini, avo vaiva nenyika yaisanganisira maguta akakosha eIsraeri. Pauro akanga asingozivi chiHeberu chete, semudzidzi waGamari Eridha (Mabasa 22:3, Mabasa 26:4, 5) uye muFarise, anogona kuva akanga akamwirira mumutemo nezvawaiva, zvichienderana netsika, nemararamiro.

Pauro aibatirira pamutemo zvekutotambudza kereke nekuti yaivonekwa seyaiva isina basa nemararamiro echiJudha, izvo zvaaiti zviripachena mumutemo. Zvinofadza chaizvo, kananepo hazvo, “kushaya mhosva” kana tichitarisa sevanhu “kururama,” Pauro akazovona kuti mutemo wakanga wakadzama kwazvo nekupfuurisa uye uchida kutevedzwa kupfuvura zvaaifunga, uye kuti muchiyero chaKristu aiva asina kana chaanogona asi kuva nemhaka huru kwazvo.

`Enzanisa VaRoma 7:7–12 naMateo 5:21, 22, 27, 28. Nderipi shoko rakakosha iri vose Jesu naPauro vanotipa nezve mutemo, uye nemhaka yei “kutenda munaKristu” (VaFiripi 3:9), kusiri kuchengeta mutemo, iri iyo nzira yoga yekuwana kururama? Zvitarise nenzira iyi: Unogona kuchengeta mutemo zvakadini iwe, zvikuru sei sekutarisirwa kwazvo naJesu?`
