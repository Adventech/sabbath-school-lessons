---
title:  Chakavanzika Chekunamata
date:  22/01/2026
---

Vhesi rakajairika kwazvo muBhaibheri ndi 1 VaKorinde 8:2: “Kana munhu achiti ndinoziva chinhu, ugere kutongoziva zvaanofanira kuziva.” Hakuna hurukuro ine zvatinoziva zvose. Tinogona kuwana chimwe chatinotodzidzivo pane zvimwe. Ichokwadi chakakura sei pazvokwadi yezvinhu zvisingaperi yezveHumwari nekuva munhu? Pauro anotaura kakawanda nezvekuzvidzwa kwaKristu kunoshamisa kwekuva munhu. Inyaya isingapedzwi kutaurwa kana nekusingaperi.

`Verenga VaRoma 8:3, VaHebheru 2:14–18, naVaheberu 4:15. Chii chakaitika pakuzvidzikisira kwaJesu nekuva munhu?`

Zvakaitika sei kuti Mwanakomana waMwari anogara nekusingaperi, kuburikidza nekushanda kwaMweya Mutsvene (Tarisa Ruka 1:35), kuti angava munhu-Mwari mumimba yaMaria? Zvinoshungurudza pfungwa kuti uyo anogara nekusingaperi angava munhu anofa. Ndizvo zvinonzi naPauro “zvakavanzika zvehumwari” (1 Timoti 3:16).

Murwiyo rwakanakisisa rwemuna VaFiripi 2, Pauro anotambunudza apa kuzvidzikisira zvakazara kupfuvura mune dzimwe ndima dzose dzemuMagwaro.

- “Kuva muchimiro chaMwari” (VaFiripi 2:6). Izwi rekuti *morphē* (chimiro) rinoreva chimiro chake chitsvene, umo Jesu aiva akainzana naBaba (enzanisa Johani 1:1).
- “Wakazvidurura” (VaFiripi 2:7). Kuvanzika kwechimiro chaJesu chezvidurura achibvisa mikana—kuti ave munhu chaiye agoedzwa sesu—zvinoshamisa kwazvo.
- “Akazvininipisa” (VaFiripi 2:8). Mukuva munhu, Jesu akabva pahukuru hwepasi rose akava muranda zvake, kusiyana kukuru nechinagwa chaLusifa.
- “Rufu, kunyange rwepamuchinjikwa” (VaFiripi 2:8). Kwakanga kusina kufa kunonyadzisa kungapfuvura kwakasarudzwa naJesu, mushure mekuzvironga naBaba “mudare rerugare” (Zekaria 6:13), inozviisa pachena pamberi paMosesi nekusimudzwa kwenyoka (Numeri 21:9, John 3:14), zvakamuita kuti ave “zvivi nekuda kwedu . . . kuti isu tive kururama kwaMwari maari (2 VaKorinde 5:21).

`Ko kutarisa pane zvatakaitirwa naJesu pamuchinjikwa—nemuchinjikwa semuenzaniso wedu wekuzvininipisa—zvingatibatsira sei kuzvininipisa zvakanyanya, uye nekunyanyisa kuzviisa pasi paMwari?`
