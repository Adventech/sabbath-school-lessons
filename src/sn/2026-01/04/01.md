### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
Firimoni 2:1–11, Jeremia 17:9, VaFiripi 4:8, 1 VaKorinde 8:2, VaRoma 8:3, VaHebheru 2:14–18.

> <p>Chemusoro:</p>
> “Zadzisai mufaro wangu nekuva nemundangariro wakafanana, murudo rumwechete, muchiwirirana, nemufungo mumwe” (VaFiripi 2:2).

Kubatana kune simba. Asi kuziva zvokwadi hazvina kufanana nekuzviita. Tose tinombotadza pano nepapo, zvisinei nekuedza kwedu kuita zvakanaka. Asi hazvina kufanana nekuzvidza kubatana tichizviziva zvedu. Saka hazvishamisi, kuti Pauro anoramba achinyorera VaFiripi, anoda kuti vave “humwe, nemupfungwa dzimwe.”

Pauro anosimbisa kudiwa kwekubatana zvichibva padzidziso dzaJesu nemuenzaniso wake. Musoro wenyaya watinovona muTestamende Yose zvikuru sei mumatsamba. Kutanga kwekusabatana mupasi rose kwakabva mukuzvikudza nenyota nechigaro nesimba remutumwa mumwechete kudenga, pfungwa yakatekeshera nekukasika, kunyange munzvimbo yaiva yakaperera (Tarisa Isaya 14:12–14). Ikasimbiswa muEdeni kuburikidza nekusagutsikana kwakanofananawo mumirairo yaMwari yaaiva aisa nechidiso chekusimukira kupfuvura zvakanga zvatarwa naMwari (Genesi 3:1–6).

Vhiki tichatarisa zvinobva muBhaibheri zvinosimbisa kukosha kwekubatana mukereke, takanyanya kutarisa pakuzvidzwa kwaJesu, zvidzidzo zvatingawana kubva mukumutarisa, nekuti tingakura sei kufanana naye.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo chevhiki ino kugadzirira Sabata, January 24._
