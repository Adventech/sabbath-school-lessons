---
title:  Chipiriso Chipenyu
date:  27/01/2026
---

`Verenga VaFiripi 2:17; 2 Timoti 4:6; VaRoma 12:1, 2; na 1 VaKorinde 11:1. Ko Pauro arikuti kudini mundima idzi?`

Pauro anoratidza kushamiswa nekusamira zvakanaka kwekurarama kwake kana kufira mubasa raKristu (VaFiripi 1:20–23). Zvino ava kusarudza kuratidza chaizvo zvinogona kuitika “kudururwa sechipiriso” (VaFiripi 2:17). Mufananidzo uyu unobva pane zvaiitwa kareko muzvinwiwa, zvaisanganisira kudirwa kwezvinhu zvakafanana (nemafuta, waini kana mvura) sechipo kuna Mwari (tarisa, semuenzaniso, Genesi 35:14, Ekosodo 29:40, 2 Samueri 23:15–17). Zvinoita “sekutambisa” zvinodururwa zvakakosha mukunamata zvinogona kutirangaridza zvakaitwa naMaria pakuzora musoro netsoka dzaJesu “nemafuta aikosha kwazvo chenardo” (Marko 14:3–9; Johani 12:3). Kana nepo chakanga chisiri chechinwiwa hacho, zvinomiririra kuzvipira kukuru kunonyatsopinda pamufananidzo wekuzvipira kwaKristu nekuda kweruponeso rwedu.

Kana Pauro aingavuraiwa nekuda kwekuparidza evangeri, aingafara nekuti vupenyu hwake “hwaiva hwadururwa” sechipiriso kuna Mwari. Nekuti zvaidirwa muchiheberu hazvimiri zvoga asi zvinofamba nechibairo (tarisa Numeri 15:1–10, Numeri 28:1–15), Pauro aingatora kupira vupenyu hwake se “chibairo kubasa” rekushandira vatendi muguta reFiripai, avo kuburikidza nekutenda, vaiva vasarudza kupa vupenyu hwavo kuna Mwari “sechibairo chipenyu” (VaRoma 12:1).

MaKristu ekutangisisa, kusanganisira vepa Firipai (VaFiripi 1:27–29), vairarama vupenyu hwekuparidza. Vaifamba vachiparidza evangeri kubva muimba imwe neimwe vachiinda kune imwe (Mabasa 5:42). Dzimba dzavo vaidzivhurira kudzidzwa kweMagwaro (Mabasa 12:12; 1 VaKorinde 16:19; VaKorose 4:15; Firimoni 1, 2), vachikwanisa kupa chikonzero chekutenda kwavo kubva muMagwaro (Mabasa 17:11, Mabasa 18:26, 1 Petro 3:15). Vakatanga sangano reMasabata vakaitavo izvozvo. Pane kumirira vafudzi kuti vaparidze kuvavakidzani vavo, vaitaurira vamwe zvavaitenda, vachipa zvidzidzo zveBhaibheri, nekugadzirira vanhu rubhapatidzo kana vafudzi vadzoka.

Kureva kuzvipira kukuru kwavaiita, ndizvo zvinonzi “chibairo chipenyu,” vakashanda kuparidza evhangeri. Tingatadza kuzviitavo izvi here?

`Fungisisa nezvazvingareva muvupenyu hwako kuva “chibairo chipenyu.” Unozvipira zvakadini kuitira humambo hwaMwari, uye mhinduro yako inoti kudini nezvako?`
