### Verenga Kugadzirira Chidzidzo Chevhiki Ino
VaKorose 4:7–18; VaEfeso 6:21; Mabasa 15:36–40; 2 Timoti 4:10, 11; 2 Petro 3:10–14; Isaya 60:1–3.

> <p>Chemusoro</p>
> “Pazvinhu zvose vongai; nekuti ndiko kuda kwaMwari, muna Kristu Jesu kwamuri” (1 VaTesaronika 5:18).

Chikamu chekupedzisira chaVaKorosi chinoratidza kuwanda kweshamwari dzaPauro pabasa. Bhuku raMabasa rinotaridza achitanga kushanda naBarnabhasi, kozoti naSirasi, uyezve rinopa nhoroondo dzenzendo dzake nhatu dzeumishinari.

Vhiki ino tichatarisa zano raPauro reumishinari, rinosanganisira kushandiswa kwenguva zvakanyanyisa kuparidzira maguta makuru eUshe hweRoma, pamwe chete nekudzidzisa vatendiwo zvavo kuti vasvitse vhangeri kumaguta akanga ashingasvikwi naPauro, akaita seColossae, Raodhikeia, neHierapori.

Kuburikidza nekushanya kwaaiita munzendo dzake, kunyanya sevasungwa, kuburikidza nematsamba, Pauro aigara achibatanidza vanhu nekereke. Akaziva kuti kubudirira kwevhangeri kwakadzamira pakuti munhu mumwe nemumwe ashande pamwe chete—MaJudha neVahedheni; vanhurume nevanhukadzi; vanhu vakaita saTikiko, Aristako, Justo, Epafra, Ruka, naNimfa. Zvinonakidza, tinonzwawo nezvetsamba yaakanyorera kuRaodhikeia iyo isina kuchengetwa kwatiri. Pauro anoronga zvakawanda mundima dzekupedzisira idzi, kusanganisira kurudziro kumurume ainzi Akipo. Akaita zvose zvaigwera kuti asimbise kereke achiri kugona.

_*Dzidza chidzidzo chevhiki ino kugadzirira Sabata, March 28._
