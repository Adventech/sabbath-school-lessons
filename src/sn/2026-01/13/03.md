---
title:  Kubatana Kwekereke
date:  23/03/2026
---

Munyika yakabatana neidaneti, masocial media, nemidziyo yakawanda, zvakaoma kufungidzira dambudziko rakasangana naPauro mukubatsira makereke kuti anzwe ari chikamu chechinhu chikuru kupfuvura ungano dzavo dzekumaruwa.

`Verenga VaKorose 4:10, 11. Kunze kwekutumira shoko nenhume (VaKorose 4:7–9), ndedzipi dzimwe nzira idzo Pauro aimutsidzira nadzo kubatana? Tichitarisa mamwe matambudziko akabatwa naPauro mutsamba iyi, ishoko ripi ringave rakaendeswa nekukwazisa uku?`

Nekukwazisa uku, Pauro anosika nekusimudzira kubatana pakati pevatendi. Tinodzidza pano kuti Marko aiva muzukuru waBarnabhasi. Pauro aivhura nzira yekushanya kwaMarko kuKorose. Aristako anotsanangurwa “semusungwa pamwe chete naye”; kureva kuti, akasungwa pamwe chete naPauro. Vose vaiva mauto “akashonga zvombo zvaMwari” (VaEfeso 6:10, 11), vachirwira kusunungura vasungwa vaSatani kuti vashandire ushe hwaMwari (tarisa  2 Timoti 2:1–4). Jesu/Justo (Mazita echiJudha neechiRoma anonzwika zvakafanana muchiGriki, saSauro/Pauro) vanotaurwawo nezvavo sevashandi vakavimbika muvhangeri.

Pauro anoita shungu dzekutaura kuti Aristako, Marko, naJusto vatendi vechiJudha (“vekudzingiswa”). Anozoenderera mberi achidura VaHedheni vatatu: Epafra, Ruka, naDhema (VaKorose 4:12–14). Zvakakosha kuti, kunyange paine kusawirirana mukereke pakati pevaJudha neVahedheni, vashandi pamwe chete ava vanogona kushanda zvine simba pamwe chete, vakabatana. Pakuti “chete,” zvisinei, Pauro anoita seanoratidza kuva neshungu dzekuti maKristu echiJudha haana kumira naye mumatambudziko ake. Kunyange zvakadaro, zvakakosha kuti panguva ino, Johani Marko, uyo akanga amboramba Pauro naBarnabhasi parwendo rwavo rwekutanga (Mabasa 13:13), haana kungoratidza kuvimbika asi “kuvaraidza” kuna Pauro (Mabasa 15:36-40).

Hutyisidzi kuumwe hachisi chinhu chitsva. Mumakore achangopfuura, Kereke yeAdventist yakapfuura mukushanduka kukuru payakapararira pasi rose, uye pane mweya iri kurova kubatana kwayo. Dambudziko iri ririko pamatanho ose ekereke.

`Mungaita seni mukereke menyu, ekuderedza zvinhu zvinotyisidzira kubatana kwedu? Ndeapi matambudziko aripo, uye chingaitwa chinyi pamusoro pawo?`
