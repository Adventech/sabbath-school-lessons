---
title:  Mashoko KuRaodhikeia
date:  26/03/2026
---

`Verenga VaKorose 4:16–18; enzanisa VaKorose 2:1–3. Uchifunga nezve shoko raJesu kuRaodhikeia (tarisa chidzidzo chezuro), kuwirirana kupi kwaunowana neari muna VaKorosi, iyo yaifanira kuverengwa zvakare kukereke yeRaodhikeia yemazuva aPauro?`

Tichitarisa nhoroondo dzevanhu vaMwari nekufamba kwenguva, matambudziko mamwechete anongodzokorora. Vaprofita vaitsiura Israeri nekuda kunamata senyika vachivakurudzira kutendeuka nguva isati yapera. Isaya akachema, “Guta rakatendeka raita sei chifeve!” (Isaya 1:21) uye akakurudzira vanhu kudzokera kuna Mwari kuti varegererwe nekuchenurwa (Isaya 1:16–20). Zvose Johani Mubhapatidzi (Mateo 3:2, 8–10) naJesu (Mateo 4:17, Mateo 12:33–37) vakadaidzira kuIsraeri kuti vatendeuke vabere michero inomira pamutongo pamazuva ekupedzisira. Vaapostora vakatakura shoko rakafanana (Mabasa 2:38; Mabasa 3:19; Mabasa 17:30; 2 VaKorinde 7:9, 10).

`Enzanisa-mima inotevera: Isaya 60:1–3 na Zvakazarurwa 18:1–4, uye Isaya 62:1–5 na Zvakazarurwa 19:7, 8. Zvakafanana zvipi zviripo pakati pemashoko ari mumabhuku maviri aya?`

Mwari vachabatanidza denga nenyika. Asi nekuda kwekukakavara kukuru, zvinofanira kuitwa nezvikamu:

1. PaKarivhari, Satani akarasikirwa nerudo rwevanhu vekudenga (Johani 12:31).

2. Kuburikidza nebasa rekutonga rwaKristu Musenjuwari yekudenga, vanhu vaMwari “vakwana pamabasa ose akanaka kuita kuda kwake” (VaHebheru 13:21) uye vakakodzerana nedenga.

3. Kuburikidza nekutonga kwemakore chiuru nemutongo wekupedzisira mushure memakore, mibvunzo yose inopera zvachose, uye chivi nevatadzi vasingatendeuki vachaparadzwa mugungwa remoto, uyo uchanatsa nyika (Zvakazarurwa 21:8).

4. Nekupera kwechivi chete ndipo denga nenyika pazvichabatanidzwa (Zvakazarurwa 21:3).

`Chinyi chatingaite pachedu (usina kutarisa mumwe) kuti tirambe takatendeka kuna Mwari nekuchokwadi chaakatipa? Kureva kuti, isarudzo dzipi dzauri kuita dzinoratidza kuti ndiani muridzi wemwoyo wako?`
