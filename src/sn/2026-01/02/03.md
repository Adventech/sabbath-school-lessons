title:  Zvikumbiro Zveminamato YaPauro
date:  05/01/2026
---

Makore adarika akapfuura; kuita kwemufudzi aimbotura nheyo dzekunamata dzimene panyaya yezvake, zvake, zvake, nezvinyorwa zvake kana dze ruzivo. Akaita pfungwa pazviri achiti “minamato yeumbimbi nehu dyire” nekuti Mwari ane zvinhu zvikuru mumwoyo pake.

`Verenga munamato waPauro muna VaFiripi 1:9–11. Chidzidzo chei chiripo, uyezve chipikirwa chikuru chipi chaanoita? Ko iwe unodzidziswa chii pazviri?`

Munamato uyu une mazwi 43 muchiGiriki, asi rudo rwasanganiswa rudo rwaPauro rwese, uyo acharamba achikurisa kune dzimwe tsamba: rudo, ruzivo, rudo, unhu, kusagumbura, nekusatadza kwavo chose chose muna Jesu Kristu. Mumunamato uyu, sezvamwe mazwi a Pauro ekutenda denga; pane nheyo panyaya yekereke chose chose. Munamato wa Pauro wakarerekera kune vamwe; uchiitirwa kereke yose nekunaka kwayo chose. Handei tivone pamberi panyaya idzi:

Rudo ngarukure chose chose (VaFiripi 1:9). Pauro hamonaiti munamato werudo chete asi kuti rudo rurerekerere mune chimwe chimiro: “muruzivo nerunyararo chose” (VaFiripi 1:9). Ruzivo ruzivo urwu harusi rwe ungano chete asi ruzivo rwe zvinhu zvesimba rinongowanikwa mucheshanyiro dzaMwari ne kudzidza Shoko rake (VaEfeso 1:17, VaEfeso 4:13, 1 Timoti 2:4).

Pfungwa dzekuziva chose ndidzo dzinopa kukurira pane (kusiyanisa nezvakaipa) “uye nekuchena chose” (VaFiripi 1:10).

Kusevenza chokwadi nemazwi echiGiriki anoreva kuti “kucherechedzwa nechiedza” anotarisana nekusataura pfungwa panyaya ino “Chose chinogona kuitwa namaKristu chinogona kuvonwa sezvichasimbiswa nechiedza vezuva.”—Ellen G. White, Reflecting Christ, p. 71.

Kusagumburwa. Izvi zvinorevei kusava chigumburo kune vamwe; kusataura nekusaita chimwe chipi chinogona kuremetesa mumwe panyaya yekutenda.

Nhoroondo ya Mwari kuburikidza muna Kristu. Pauro anoramba akadzika nheyo mu tsamba dzeva Roma neva Garatia achiramba achikura kune VaFiripi 3. Hatina nhoroondo kune yedu timene kusara kweicho chatinodzimura muna Kristu.

`Kuzorodzwa muchiito chapi chose, rudo rwedu rungaramba runodarika chose sei pamberi nezvose? (VaFiripi 1:9)? Ko ndechipi chinopa hutano pamberi pedu? (1 VaKorinde 13:1–8).`
