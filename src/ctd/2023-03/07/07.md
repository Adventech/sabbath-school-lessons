Thunih in Ephesians 4:7–10 sung i sinna pan theihna hong khang sakthei ding hi:

**1. Ephesians 4:9 letkhiatna**. Letkhiatna pawlkhat in tuaksukna pen kahtohna maa in ommasa hi ci a, “Amah hongtuaksuk masa hi” ci uhhi. Adang letkhia mite in Greek ngaihdaan bang zui in, a kahtoh a tuaksuk hunte nusiaziau lel hi. A letkhia dangte in Greekpau nakzuihzaw uh a, a kahtoh leh atuahsuk gentello (bangci kahto cihpen, leitungah hong tuaksuk keileh kahtoh kik omlo ding) hi a, Tuesday sinna sungah, Late 68:18 zui hihtuak a, Christ in vantung kahtoh khit ciangin, Khasiangtho in hong tuaksuk kik hi cihi.

**2. Salte paipih**. Laisiangtholui Late 68:18 bulphuh in, Septuagint acihuh (Greek pau lasiangtholuibu) pan, Paul in Ephesian 4:8 sungah “salte a paipih hi” acih pen acihnopna taktak ah “salmat bangin amat mite” cihbang in kilet zaw hi. SDA pawlpi in, Christ in a thawhkiklai in thawhkikna tuam in a thokikte pen, vantung a kahtoh ciangin tonpih hi cihi (Matt. 27:51–53). Hihpen in “buhlom viikna” tatkhiatte anpal hi a, vantung Pa’ maiah va pulakto hi. (see The SDA Bible Commentary, vol. 6, p. 1022; The Desire of Ages, p. 834, compare The Desire of Ages, pp. 785, 786). Colosian 2:15 tawh enkak leng, Christ adingin a galte maiah a gualzawhna khat mah hi a, Satan leh ama’ vanmite matsa salte ahi uhhi.
