### Tukalsung Simding
Eph. 3; Job 11:5–9; Ezek. 43:13–16; Amos 7:7, 8; Mang. 11:1, 2.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Ei a dingin na a hong sepsak, eite’ ngetzahval, leh i ngaihsut zawhloh zah dong in a vangliatna tawh tampitak ahong hihsak thei; eite Pasian pen pawlpi mite khempeuh leh Christ Zeisu tawh a kipawl mi khempeuh in a khang a khang in a tawntung in phatciat ta uh hen. Amen” Ephesian 3:20, 21.

Ephesian 3:6 ah, Paul in “Gentile mite in Jew mite tawh Pasian’ thupha ngah khawm thei a, amaute tawh pawlkhat suak in Pasian in piakding a ciam thute, Christ hang in a ngahkhop theihna uh ahi hi” cihi. Paul hun a pawlpi in Gentile mite tawmzaw ihih manin, ama’laisim mite ading in thulamdangpi khat bang hong suakliang zawzen hi.

Paul in humuapsa kammal ombang in zompaisuak in, Gentile mite’ tungah Zeisu’ lungdamthu hilhzomsuak hi.

Tuabang in a nasepna ah haksatna, adiakin Rome ah thongkiatna, cihbang in tampi thuak khazel hi.

Lungdamthu thuthuk ading in ama’ kipiakkhiatna, pawlpi’ thuthukpen ahi Gentile mite in Jew mite tawh unau sanggam in a omtheihna cihte zong izaksa vive ahi hi. Pawlpi leh vanuai bup huamin avaipuak ading, a lung hihmawhna zong kimuhsak ding hi. Pasian phat in, pawlpi tungtawn in Ama’ hehpihna hongpiak ding a thungetna zong izaksa ahi hi.

Tomgen leng, Paul in lungdamthu aveii mahmah bangin eite’n zong hong veiisak zo hi.
