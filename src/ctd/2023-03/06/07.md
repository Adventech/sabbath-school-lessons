Ellen G. White, “Modern Revivals,”The Great Controversy, laimai 461–478 simin.

“Eima khalam dimun zawngkhalna pen Ephesian 3:14-19 sunga, eite ngah ding apicing pilna tawh koibangin lemtuah thei ding ihi hiam? Khalam leh tumah in hehpihna sungah khantoh theihna’ng neinapi in, eima hamphatna nangawn a zangsiamlo eite pen Vantung in hong koici muh tam maw? Sawltakpa in hihthute pen eite hong lawpsuahna maimai ding leh, eima phutkhakte lungkiatna hangin lam-etna hongkhangsak maimai ding lel in a gelh hilo hi. Eite in Pasian’ gamh i luahleh bang hih ding cih khawng hong theisak hi. I theihna tawmkhialua leh koi bangin Pasian tawh maapangkhawm thei ding ihi hiam? Christian hamphatna ding ahi, Topa in hong deihsak Pasian tawh kisai nate khempeuh thuktakin i zong ding uhhi.”

“Laisiangtho i umtakpi hiam? Hihteng ah hong pulak Pasian theihtelna hangin ngah dingteng zong i umtakpi hiam? Pasian’ kampan hong paikhia kammal khempeuh i umkhin hiam? Kamsangte, sawltakte leh khuvak leh thupha apiangsak a, hauhna leh picinna sungah a ompa Zeisu Christ kammalte i umtakpi hiam? Pasian leh Ama’ Tapa i umtakpi hiam?”—Ellen G. White, The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, October 1, 1889.

**Kikupding Dotnate**:

`Ephesian 3:20, 21 sungah Paul phatna lagui leh laisiangthothak sung phatna dangte simkak dih in—Rom. 11:33–36; Rom. 16:25–27; Phil. 4:20; 2 Pet. 3:18b; Jude 24, 25. Bang thubul leh ngaihdaan tawh pai naci hiam? Amau hong hilh praise and worship pen koici bangin zatsak thei ding ihi hiam?`

`Paul in Greek pau plçroma (“hunhoih”) acihpen namli in a zatna teng enkak in (Eph. 1:10, 23; Eph. 3:19; Eph. 4:13). Paul in banghang in thupi sathei hiding hiam?`

`Ephesians 3 sunga Pasian’ nasepna Paul in a phatna teng lakpan, koipen in nang hong huzaappen naci hiam? Banghang hiam?`
