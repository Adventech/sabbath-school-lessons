---
title:  Nicodemus Teci Panna
date:   24/10/2024
---

`John 3:1–21 simin. Nicodemus’ teci panna in John’ Lungdamthu tawphah koibang in thapia hiam?`

Nicodemus pen Israel te mi zahtak leh a hau Sanhedrin lutpa khat ahi hi. Ama’ teci panna in John’ Lungdamthu pen thu tampi mah tawh thapia hi. Amah in Zeisu’ bawl limte leh Ama’ nasep ading in “Rabbi” naci hi. Nicodemus mapek in Zeisu ana theikhin hi a, Zeisu in Messiah ahihna thapia khinzo hi.

Nicodemus a hihleh, Zeisu in hong sam ci ziau lel a, vantung in Zeisu pen Lai siangtholui sung a Messiah’ thu a genkholsa teng picinna ahihlam thei selo hi. Tua hang mahin, Nicodemus in lunghiang takin hong pai hi a, tualai ciangdong pen Zeisu in Christ ahihlam phawk zo nailo hi.

`John 3:3–21 sim in. Nicodemus in, Zeisu tungtawn in Amah amuh theihna’ng Zeisu in bang gen hiam?`

Zeisu in mimal’ in lungsim hong tel hi. Zeisu in Nicodemus a dawnkikna pen simmawhna bang hiphial taleh, Ama’ deihna a tut dong genpha hi. Jew te in, Gentile mite peuhmah pianthak kul ci in umuh a, amau Israel mite, Pasian’ teeltuam mite mah zong a pianthak uh kul ding cih theikha vetlo uhhi. Hotkhiatsa in kuamah piangkhia lo a, minam ahi a, pawlpi tawh ahi zongin kihonkhia lo hi.

Jew mite’ luah gamh pen tanglai Abraham tungpan hi a, hamphatna tuamtuam nei cih dot kullo lel hi (Rom. 3:1, 2). Hi taleh tua bek tawh hong kicing samlo hi. Nicodemus pen Israel ukpa leh siapa khat hiliang napi, tunglam pan a pianthak mah kul veve ding cih Zeisu in sawl hi.

Nicodemus in khalam a theihlahna tengmah tawh Zeisu in talsik pah in, “Nang, Israelte siapa khat hi napi in hih thute thei lo zawzen na hi hiam?” (John 3:10) cihi. A zahtakhuai siapa khat in koibang in hi thute theilo thei ding nahi hiam? Kitaai dan khauhgiap lel hi. Nicodemus in Zeisu tungah bangthu peuh dong taleh, a nunglam ciangin Ama’ nungzui khatmah nahi veve lel hi (John 19:39 en in).

`“Piangthak” cih in bang cihna hi a, Zeisu in banghang in hibang cisese hiding hiam?`
