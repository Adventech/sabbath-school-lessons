### Tukalsung simding
John 1:19–23; Isa. 40:1–5; John 1:29–37; Rom. 5:6; John 1:35–39; John 1:43–51; John 3:1–21.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Zeisu in, ‘Aman takpi kong cih in ah, mi khat a suahkik kei leh Pasian kumpigam sungah a lut thei kei ding hi,’ a ci hi.” (John 3:3 ZSV).

Zeisu in, “Aman takpi kong cih in ah, kei hong um mi in nuntak tawntungna a neizo hi. Keimah in nuntakna anlum ka hi hi” (John 6:47) a cihcih pen Laisiangtho mah tawh kipsak sitset cih kician mahmah hi.

Tua thamlo, Tui – lengtui suahna; tulsim mi khomun tawm bektawh vakna; ulian pa’ tapa damsakna; Bethesda bual a mipa damsakna; pianpih mittawpa khua muhsakna; sihna pan Lazarus thawhkik sakna cihte omlai hi. Thuhilhpa in thudang tampi kaikhawm lai hi – Jew, Gentile, mihau, mizawng, pasal, numei, mi maimai, mipil, mipillo – tuate khempeuh in Zeisu kua hiam cih teci pang ngeingai uhhi.

John in, Pa ngiat in a teci panna, laisiangtho in a teci panna cihte Zeisu a ding teci panna in nei hi.

Tukal pen Tuiphum John teci panna vanglian tawh kipan hi. Teci dangdang te zong hong kipan uhhi: Andrew leh Simon Peter, Philip leh Nathaniel banah lam-et hetloh teci ahi Pharisee Nicodemus cihte ahi uhhi. A nunglam ah muhloh simtham in teci a pang John (John 1:35, 40) amah mahmah hi ngiat hi.
