### Tukalsung simding
John 5:17, 20, 36–40, 46, 47; John 13:18; John 17:12; Jer. 2:13; Zech. 9:9; John 8:12–30.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Ahih hangin John teci panna sanga a lianzaw teci khat ka nei hi. Banghang hiam cihleh Pa in ka zawh dinga hong piak nasepte ka sem a, Pa in kei a hong sawl lam tua nasepte in teci a pang uh hi.” (John 5:36 ZSV).

Lungdamna John sungah, Zeisu in Messiah (Hammashiach), Christ hi a, Israel ading in hongpai ahihna lahna in asep abawl khempeuh om hi. Amah in, Laisiangtho gen kholhsa om bangin, Jew mi khatmah bangin Bethlehem ah hong suak hi.

Tua kawmkal ah John mah in “Amah leitung ah hong om a, ama tungtawn in leitung a kibawl hi napi leitung in amah a thei kei hi” (John 1:10) cihi.

Amah leitung ah hong om a, hih leitung zong Amah tawh a kibawl hi napi leitung in Amah theilo hiam? Lamdang lua himai. John’ Lungdamthu leh a dangte sungah i muh mahbang in, Ama’ sepna bawlna tampi takmah mu uh hi napi, mi tampi in Amah thei tuanlo uhhi. Bek thamlo, laisiangtholui in zong amah pulak den sam hiven.

Tukalsung in John in, Zeisu pen Messiah hi ci in hong lahna sungpan, anial tentan lai pawlkhat te in – Amah Christ hi cih kipkho sakna mah hi veve cih sin ding hihang.

Amaute’ khialhna tungtawnin bang pilna ngah thei ding ihi hiam?
