---
title:  Kha Siangtho
date:   11/12/2024
---

Kha Siangtho pen Lungdamthu John sungah Pa leh Tapa zah in tamgen khollo hi. Ahihhang Zeisu nasepna a lawhcin na’ng Kha Siangtho mapanna phamawh mahmah hi,

`John 1:10–13 sim in. Kikhel nadingin Kha Siangtho a phatmawhna hihmun in bang teng gen hiam?`

John 1 ah, Kha Siangtho in bangzah in laigil hiam cih kimuthei hi. John in tua Thu a sang mi peuhmah Amah a um hi a, Pasian tate suak in “cilehsa tawh hilo, sisan tawh hilo, mihing deihna tawh hilo, Pasian’ deihna tawh suahna” (John 1:13) cihi. Tua zong Kha Siangtho nasepna bekpan hongpai ahi hi.

`Anuai a munte in Khasiangtho gamtatna bang hong gen hiam? John 3:5–8; John 6:63; John 14:26; John 15:26; John 16:7–11.`

“Zeisu in nungzite kiangah Kha Siangtho nasepna, gen hi. Ama’ lungsim teekin angah nopna leh lam-etna teng tawh huzaap nuam hi. Pawlpi ading Ama’ huhna tampite ading in zong Amah lungdam hi. Ama’ mite a pahtawi theihna lianpen silpiak in Kha Siangtho ahi hi. Tua Kha Siangtho pen ki puahphatna hi a, Amah lo in Christ’ sihna in a mawkna suak hi. Gitlohna vang in zalom tampi sung thahat a, mihing in Satan leh mawhna tungah ki-aap in thongkia uhhi. Mawhna pen, Kha siangtho Pasian huhna tawh do leng kizo hi. Pasian in pawlpi tungah Kha Siangtho dim takin hong buak hi.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 671.

Pasian in thumaan hi cih teci a pang Kha Siangtho ngahkhakna in hamphatna ahi hi (John 3:33). Mawhna hong zo sak, thumaan leh thukhenna ah hong gual zawhpih pen Kha Siangtho mah ahi hi (John 16:8–11). A dik, a thumaan, a hoihte hong theisak a, Pasian’ Thu sungah hong ki-aapsak pen, Kha Siangtho in hong thuzawhna ahi hi.
