### Tukalsung Simding

1Cor. 10:1–13; Mate 2:15; Josh. 1:1–3; Sawl. 3:22–26; Heb. 3:7—4:11; 2 Cor. 10:3–5.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Leitung beikuan sunga om eite a dingin, hih thupiangte in etteh ding hi a, eite hilhkholna dingin lai sungah a kiciamteh ahi hi” (1 Corinth. 10:11).

Joshua’ laibu sungah, ama’ nundan taktak leh amah’ hihna taktak sangin nakpi takin a lianzaw thukhat om khinkhian hi. Tua thukhun in Laisiangtho sungah adim ahaa in om a, Canaan in tawntung leithak lam-etna limpua ahi hi. Tua banah, leitung biakbuk ngeina te in a sangzaw hihna kawk a: “Ahizong in tu in a hoihzaw thu asem ding Siampi Lianpen Christ hong tungkhin a, Ama’ lutna biakbuk pen, aki piangsak tawm, khut tawh akibawl biakbuk hilo in, a lianzaw a cingtaakzaw mun ahi hi” (Heb. 9: 11).

Dotna khat hong piang pen: Nung ciangin picing ding cih Joshua in koici pulak hiam? Hih laibu hibang in a letkhiatna in koibang in kikipsak thei ding hiam? Joshua’ lai bu’ thukhun te in laisiangthothak sunga thupiang taktak leh hun nunung thute koibang in hong genkhol hiam?

Tukal sungteng in, Laisiangtho letkhiatna ah limlahna te thukhun teng ending hi hang. Laisiangtho in a limlahnate leh Joshua’ nundan in Messiah’ nasepna a limlah kholna te in pawlpi sungah leh mihing’ tangthu sungah a tangtunna teng kisin ding hi hang.
