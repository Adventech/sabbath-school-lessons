---
title:  A Nam Lahna leh A Taktak
date:   01/12/2025
---

Laisiangtho tawh kisai Laisiangtho thak sunga limlahna khat peuhpeuh letkhiat ciangin, eima deihbang peuh in lilet mawkmawk theilo hi. Laisaingtho mahin tua limlah nate gentelna ding limlahna kammal hong piakhin hi.

Tua mahbang in laisiangthothak limlahna khat a picinna ahi a, a nung picin pan dingte pen thuthum in khen in pulaak hi: (1) Christ nuntakna tawh (Chrit sungah a picin na) (2) Pawlpi phutkhak thute sungah (pawlpi sungah a picinna) (3) Hun bei ciang (nitawp ni ciang picinna) cihte ahi hi.

Hih namlahna leh a nam taktakte pen Laisiangtho sung khempeuh ah om hi. Lai siangtho simte in zong thumaan thutak icihpen Zeisu leh Ama’ hotkhiatna sangpen ahih lam leh eima’ lam-etna sangpen ahihlam telcian theihna dingin hong huh hi.

**A nuai a Laisiangtholui nam lahnate entel in: Israel, Paikhiatna leh Biakinnpi. Christ sungah picinna, Pawlpi sungah a picinna, leh nitawpni ciang apicinna.**

1. Israel
	- a. Christ sungah picinna (Mate 2:15)
	- b. Pawlpi sungah picinna (Gal. 6:16)
	- c. Nitawpni ciang picinna (Mang. 7:4-8, 14)
2. Paikhiatna
	- a. Christ sungah picinna(Mate 2:19–21)
	- b. Pawlpi sungah picinna (2 Cor. 6:17)
	- c. Nitawpni ciang picinna (Mang. 18:4)
3. The Sanctuary
	- a. Christ sungah picinna(John 1:14; 2:21, Mate 26:61)
	- b. Pawlpi sungah picinna(1Cor. 3:16, 17; 2Cor. 6:16)
	- c. Nitawpni ciang picinna(Mang.3:12;11:19;21:3; 21:22)

“Laisaingtho in laipu khatbek nei ahih manin, mun tuamtuam panin kikhel them thum cih omlo hi. Laisiangtho sunga upna thugui khempeuh in kizawitawn mahmah uh a, a khennono te khempeuh in zong hong kipia khinsa bulphuh khat tungah kinga in a vekpi in tonkhawm thei ding hi”—Handbook of Seventh-day Adventist Theology, p. 65.

`Laisiangtho mun khatpeuh a khiatna telhak nasak ciangin na koicih hiam?`
