---
title:  Maavang in Nasep Tangtun
date:   02/10/2025
---

`Joshua 1:7–9; Pian. 24:40, Isa, 53:10 leh Late 1:1–3 simin. Hiteng siksan leng maavang in nasep tangtun cihin bang khiatna neithei ding hiam?`

Hebrew laimal tsalakh, pen “maavang” (Josh. 1:8) hi a, geelsa thukhat picingsak, lungkim huai sak cihna ahi hi.

Tuakhit sakal, in “pilvaang” (Josh. 1:8) hi a, “nasep tangtun” cihna zong ahi hi. Tua banah “pil, ciim, navak” cihte ahi hi. Job, Paunak leh Late laibu sungah tamveipi om a, Pasian’ Thu mangin Pasian mahmah kihtakna tawh gamtatna tawh kizom hi.

Hih muhdaan tawh hileh gualzawhna pen van hauhna ci samlo napi pampaih tuan lo hi. Gualzawhna pen, Pasian in leitung a piansakna thubulphuh Ama’ thukham sungah kha thukham a koihsa tawh kituak in nuntak kalsuan hangbek hiding hi. Upna hangbek in hotkhiatna leh Ama’ thukham zuihna in kilangdo hetlo ahih manin, Ama’ kamciam peuh mah mung photphot in. Maitangnih a nei sumtang tawh kibang lel hi.

`Rom. 3:31 simin. Hihmun in thukham leh upna koibang in kizomsak hiam?`

Pasian’ thukham nialnop manpeuh in Zeisu’ hong sihna leh hong kipiak khiatna teng nindum sungah khiatsukna in phelnih a suah tawh kibang in lauhuai hi. Thukham leh hehpihna in tonkhawm kinken hi. Thukham’ vaipuak pen a tungtham bekmah theihna in “thukham leh hehpihna” hong kikeelki sak zawsop hi.

Laisiangtholui langkungpu te in thukham pahtawi mahmah in, a lungnopna bulpi uh hisak hi (Late 1:2; 119: 70, 77, 174). Thukham zahtak in pahtawina in eima’ mawhna a thukzia hong telsak (Rom. 7:7) a, Christ’ dikna kisaplam zong hong musak (Gal. 3:24) hi.

`Pasian hong hehpihna tawh Ama’ thukham nazuih ciangin Christ’ dikna in Nang hong tuam ding nakisapna koibangin namukhia hiam?`
