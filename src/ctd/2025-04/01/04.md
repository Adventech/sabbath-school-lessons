---
title:  Kamciam Luahte
date:   30/09/2025
---

Joshua 1:2, 3 sungah Topa in Joshua kiangah tua gam kong pia hi cihi. Thu dang khat panin, Kong piakhin zo hi cihi. Bang a cihnopna aitam?

Tua gam pen a Neipa taktak Topa in a piakkhong ahi hi. Joshua 1: 2, 3 sungah sepna kammal “pia ding” cih zang a, gam luahna ding thu thupi namnih om hi. Amasa in tua gam a piak daan hi. Israel in Jordan gunpang tengbek luahzo photphot hi. Kamciam Gam a tamzaw a laklai dinguh ahi hi.

Joshua 1:3 sungah sepna pen a picing khin ci a, tua gam amaute tungah kipiakhin cihi. Tua nasepte pen Pasian nasep hi a, tuate pen “kamciam picing” kici hi. Ama’ kam mal sunga Ama’ kamciamte in tuni tuhun a dingin muanhuai mahmah hi.

A taang 3 ah “Note leh Note’aa “ cihte in mitam hi a, tua kamciam in Joshua tung bek ah kipia hilo a, Israel mipi buppi tungah kipia ahi hi. Moses tungah apiak kamciam a genphatna pen Pasian’na semzom suak cihna ahi hi.

Hebrew pau kol, “khempeuh” “malsim” cihna hi a, a lian khatna sungah tamveipi ki pulak hi. A zaipi in ahuam tua kammal in avekpi kigawm, leh Joshua in ama’ vaipuak nasep pen zokhin sitset cihna zong ahi hi. Pasian, Joshua leh Israel mpite in thum kigawm in a thutuak uhleh bek gualzo thei dinguh a Kamciam Gam lazo thei ding uhhi.

`Joshua 1:4–6 leh Heb. 6:17, 18 simin. Tua laitak lianin, tua Kamciam Gam in kamciam bekmah hiphotphot lai hi. Hinapi, Pasian in gamh cihi. Pasian’ gamh luah cihteh bang acih nopna ahi diam?`

Pasian kamciamna sungah bum leh ai kihel vetlo hi. Amau mahmah kihuut ding leh picin theihna’ng bang vang mahmah neilo uhhi. Tuate a picintheihna dingin Pasian’ ompihna “note tawh kong omkhawm ding hi” cihthu bekin lungmuanna neisak hi. Pasian’ ompihna in Israel kipkhona dingin phamawh mahmah hi. Tualo peuhmah tawh amaute in zong minam tampi lakah, saptuam ahilo, mintuam, nasep tuam a neilo namkhat mah hilel ding uhhi (Pai. 33:12-16). Joshua in a gualzawhna dingin Topa ompihna kisam bel hi.

Mate 28:20 sunga Zeisu’ kamciam omsa mahbangin tuni in zong bangmah kikhel tuanlo hi.
