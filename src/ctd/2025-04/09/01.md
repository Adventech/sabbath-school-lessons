### Tukalsung Simding

Pian. 3:17–24; Thkna. 6:3; Josh. 13:1–7; Heb. 12:28; Siam. 25:1–5, 8–13; Ezek. 37:14, 25.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Lam-etna a om no salte aw, ciahkik unla, a muanhuai mahmah na munngei uh zuankik un. Kote’ thuaknasa a zahnih in thupha ka hong piakna dingtu in note kiangah kahong gen hi” (Zechariah 9:12).

Joshua 13–21 sungah Israel te munpiakna ah leitang leh inn-gii lo-gii a cingdildel in ciaptehna laipi ahi hi. Tuni a laisimte in, hih sazian saupite pen a hitheilo dingzah in mukha sop ding uh hinapi; biakna vai tawh Kamciam Gam bulphuh peuh leng kician mah mah lel hi. Israel te in mangman peuh ahihlohna Pasian in hihthute tawh Pasian in hilh nuam hi. Muhtheih nate leh tehtheih nate tawh kamciam hi. Tua pen amau un gamtaang takpi in tua kamciamna te la ding uhhi.

Pasian in tua gam pen gamhluah dingin paikhia nuam hi; piakkhong hiding a, apu apate tungah Ama’ kamciamna a tangtutna ahi hi. “En un, note’ maiah leitang ka koihzo hi. Topa in napate uh Abraham, Isaac leh Jacob leh amau’ nunga a suanlehkhakte uh tung ah piak dingin a ciam leitang luah un” (Thkna. 1:8) hongci hi. Hi tamahleh amau’ lampan zong a sepkhiat uh kul hi.

Tukalsung in Kamciam Gam tawh kipawl biaknavai upna pawlkhat leh Khalam thute leh Zeisu hong kamciamna tetawh a kipawlte sin ding hihang.
