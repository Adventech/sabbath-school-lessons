### “Bang Deih Na Hiam?”

_Thupuak: Laurie Denski-Snyman_

Misssionary khat in khuatual mi khat tawh Khristian laibute Southern Asia-a khuapi khat sung ah Khristian a hilote’ kiang ah va zuak hi. Tuate’ kizep dan a et ciang bang bia uh hiam cih kithei thei hi. Numeite a lu uh khuh, pasalte khamul khawi uh hi. Pawl khatte sual tung ah a tal uh tat-in thu sawtpi a nget man un a pawn bang vom liang hi.

Inn khat bang ah, pasal khat hehsa-in hong daak in “bang deih na hiam?” ci giap hi. Kong hon’lo hi.

Missionary pa’n zong “ko pen’ na omna veeng ah project khat geel ung-a laibute zong zuak ung. Laibute na en nuam diam? ci hi.

Innteekpa zong “hongpai kik ning ci hi. Missionary ahuh nu Maria bel lau hi. “Thungen ni. Ameel lauhuai lua, a upna ah cihtak tuak lua” ci hi.

David in bel “a gilkial hi-in cia” cih san hi. Hih hun pen zehtan hun hi. Innteekpa hong kileh kik-in “inn sung ah luut un” ci hi. Inn sung ah “no bang deih na hiam?” ci giap kik hi. David zong laibu tampi hong lakhia hi.

Innteek pa’n hong en-in “ka zi-in hih laibute deih thei mah inteh, amah pen an huan siam hi” ci-in a zi sam in veel sak hi.

A nu’pa un laibu veel in, innteek pa in “hih bek hoih tuak ei” ci hi. Tua khitteh “no kua aa ding nasem nai’ huam?” ci-in tangdot hi.

David in, “ko pen SDA hi ung” ci hi.

Innteekpa zong hong ding in, paikhia hi. Hong kileh kik in hong tu hi. Leenglate mit sal hi.

A zi-in “ken’ Adventist lawm nei ing. Amau pen hoih mahmah uh hi. Ken’ bel amau thu tel khin keng” ci hi. Innteekpa bel a ci’ bang liing hi. A zi-in “laibu dangte bang bute hiam?” ci hi.

David zong The Great Controversy bu dok in “hih bu pen itna maan, mawh mai na maan, lung nopna maan thu a gen laibu hi” ci hi.

Innteeknu zong lungluut in David in Step to Christ, Thoughts form the Mount of Blessing bute zong lak hi. Innteekpa bel ding zel, tu zel hi. Innteek nu in “nang’n bang um na hiam?” ci hi.

Innteekpa bel hong ding kik hi. A kigen khempeuh ngaih sawm tuak hi. Cell phone tawh aw khum sawm ta hi.

_Leitung bup-a gospel nasemte aa ding thunget sak in. Missionary nasep Sabbath School sumpi tawh na huh man un lungdam hi. Tangthu om laite mai kaal ah simin._
