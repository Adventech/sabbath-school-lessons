---
title:  Kizopna Lim Khat
date:   15/12/2025
---

Josh. 21: 43-45 sungah, thumanna hangin tangtung cihthu tawh thukhup hi. Gual zawhna cihpen piakkhiatna in ngaihsut ding hi tuanlo a; Pasian’ Thu zuihna tawh kizom den tawntung zaw hi. Gam a hawmna in zong Israel tungah Pasian’ cihtakna (Neh. 9: 8) hizaw a, amau’ mailam ah a khantoh laina ding uh mun awng a kipatna leh a tawpna pen amau Israel mite omzia tungah kinga hizaw hi. Israel in amau’ ngahsa a lenkip zo ding uh hiam?

`Joshua 23:1–5 simin. Joshua’ thupatna ah adeihna bulpi bang teng hiam?`

Joshua’ thugenna sungah khangham ahihna nihvei sitset genkhia in, mipi khem peuh in Pasian thupiak nasep sempaisuak ding cih hanthawn hi. Gam a kialaakzawh theih na zong: Topa’n amau a ding dosak cihi. Paikhiat khit nungsang amau’cihtakloh leh uploh te hilo in galphual ah vakihel teitei ding uh a, amau’ galhatna hang hilo, Pasian in a lailutsak manin tua gam a neithei hibek uhhi.

Galvai panin Pasian in Israel tawlngak pia khin ahihhang, a hawlkhiat dinguh minam pawlkhat omlai veve hi. Galzawhna cih Israel a dingin picinna, akikhel theilo amau’ dinmun cihbang hituanlo a; Pasian in a huhtheih na’ngin Ama’ tungah kinga tawn tung dinguh ahi hi.

`Joshua’ makaihna tawh Israel in Canaan a laakna leh tuni ei Christiante in khalam gualzo nuntakna i neihtheihna in koibang in kizawitawn mahmah thei naci hiam? Josh. 23:10; Col. 2:15; 2 Cor. 10:3–5; Eph. 6:11–18.`

Israel’ galzawhna teng pen amau’ thahatna leh galsiamna kici theilo hi. Tua mah bangin mawhna leh ze-etna tungah khalam galzawhna pen Zeisu’ hong sihna leh hong thawhkikna hangbek hinapi; tuni Pasian’ mite in a tumdang phadiak khalam galzawhna ngah theihna dingin Kha Siangtho vaihawmna tungah kinga zaw ding uhhi.

`Eima’ maimah ah kamciam tampi takmah omkhin zo hinapi a, banghang in mawhna bawl ding baihlam thei mahmah naci hiam?`
