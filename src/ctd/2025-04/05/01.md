### Tukalsung Simding

Pian. 15:16; Siam. 18:24–30; 2 Tim. 4:1, 8; Pai. 23:28–30; Thkna. 20:10, 15–18; Isa. 9:6.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Isael mite’ Topa Pasian in Israel a dingin dosak ahih manin, Joshua in hih kumpite khempeuh leh amau’ leitang teng hunkhat sungin a lakhin hi” (Joshua 10:42).

Joshua’ laibu sungah amuh thadah huai pawlkhat om hi. Siangtho Pasian’ galkap te in Pasian’ thupiakna mahtawh midang pawlkhat vasim sese cih khawng dotna om hi.

Laisiangtholui sunga siangtho gal cihmawk pen tona lianpi khatmah ahi hi. Pasian in vannuai bup ah sangpen Topa ahih manin; a piang na khempeuh pen, Ama’ deihna bang linlian ahi a, a tungtawn hi hamtang ding hi. Tua hi a dotna omthei khatpen “Pasian in hibang thute bang dingin phalthei hiam? cihpen om teitei hi.

Nungkal in, Pasian tektek in, mihing’ tangthu sungah a piang gal lellel sangin a lianzaw tham, eima nuntakna vai bang ahilian gal khat do hi cih sinkhin hihang. Hih kido na tungtawn bekin Laisiangtho leh leitung tangthu kitelcian thei bek cih i muhsa zong ahi hi.

Tukalsung in, vantung in hong ciangtanna galdona leh siangtho galdona, Laisiang tholui kamsangte gen siangtho galdona leh tua galdona huzaapna teng kisin ding hihang.
