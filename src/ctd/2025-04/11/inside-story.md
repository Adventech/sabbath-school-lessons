### Nasepna: Pasian’ Huhna

Kim Sun pen 1000 Missionary Movement tawh Philippines gam nawl lam ah kha 10 bang missionary va sem ding ahih manin a lungtang bang kisai hi. Kha 10 koici zang ding a hiam kici hi. Anute tawh kum nga bang kinial-in bangmah tunlo hi. Tu’n kha 10 bek ding ahih ciang hi.

Thu a nget kawm in “Lai Siangtho tawh pan masa leng’ ka nute mah bangin hong kinial ding. Zeisu zia lah masak kul ding hi” ci-in ngaihsun hi.

Sun in missionary ahihlam kua mah kiang ah gen sawmlo hi. Lai Siangtho thu zong gen sawmlo hi. Kha thum sung kilawm bawl phot sawm hi.

Nasep nading khuano a tun’ ciangin Sun in a kiim-a inn 20-30 bang va huh hi.

Amasa inn khat-a nupi kiang ah “huhna na kisam hiam?” va ci hi. “Huhna na kisapleh hong huh nuam ing” ci hi. Tua nu zong na nuam mahmah hi.

Mi a huhna thu hoih veeng sungah hong thang hi. A sawtlo-in huhna a deihte hong kigual uh hi. Amuh masak nu in amah sun an vak in Amah’n zong tua nu-in mi 17 huh hi.

Sun zong inn a ciah teh tawlta hi. Ahih hangin “kei pen missionary hing” kici hi.

Sun zong a sawtlo-in ama huhna a deih mi’ min teng hong nei hi. Monday pan Friday dong zingsang inn nih, nitaak inn nihte va hawh hi.

Khua sung mite tawh lawmtaat theihna dangte zong hong mu hi. Amah’n vaimiim ciing in khol khin hi. Mite’ bangzah ba ka hih uh hiam a cihteh bang mah ba kei uhteh cih san hi. Vaimiim pen Pasian piak hi ci hi. Pasian in no taang in hong piak sak hi. Khat vei bang meima pi a nei naupang khat mu-in damsak hi. Meima a dam khit ciangin a nu a pa te in “ka lungdam dan uh kigen zolo hi” ci hi.

Sun in kha thum sung bang khua sung mite kiang ah missionary ahih lam kigen lo hi. Tua pen a kisel hilo hi, kua mah’n na donglo uh hi. Amah zong mai kha thum aa ding thungen hi.

_Kim Sung pen 1996 Sabbath 13na sumpi tawh Silang, Philippines zumpi ah 1000 Missionary Movement’ associate director sem hi. Mai kaal ah sim beh in._
