### Tukalsung Simding

Josh. 5:1–7; Pai. 12:6; 1 Cor. 5:7; Josh. 8:30–35; Thkna. 8:11, 14; Heb. 9:11, 12.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Pasian Gam leh Ama’ dikna zong masa un. Tua hileh, na kisapna dangte khempeuh uh hong piathuah veve ding hi” (Mate 6:33).

Tukal sungteng in, Israel in Kamciam Gamsunga teengsa mahin, lauhuaina hong tungding bang ahih ciangin, Pasian kiangah ki-aap phatkik hun teng kisin ding hihang. Joshua in thusau ngaihsun khalo in Israel’ galte gamgii tenga Israel mite vun aatsak tadih dih hi (Josh. 5:1-9). Gal omkuan laitak in Paisanpawi khaamna ding (Josh, 5:10-12); gam laakna a kizawhkuan ciangin biaknatau khat lamin biakna pia ding (Josh. 8:30-35); Israel nam sagih in amau’ gamh uh zong ngahsak nai ngaplopi Topa’ biakbuk lam ding cita dih zen hi (Josh. 18:1, 2).

Eima nuntakna’ng buaimahmah sungin nuntakna sungah a thupizaw kisapnate awlmawh kul hi. Pasian in eima a ding hong hihsaknate lungdamko kawmin amau’ zong ki-aap phakik in thaksuahsak ding mangngilh pahpah uhhi. Vanman aval aliap in zuakna, sumzuakna ah daupaina leh gualzawhna lamah manawhna cihte zingsang nitak biakpiak na ah innkuan biaknatau tungah tun ding ki mangngilh pahpah hi. Pasian tawh kikhawlh na ah i itmahmah innkuanpihte pen, tua hun a dingin lawm hoih penpente ahi uhhi.
