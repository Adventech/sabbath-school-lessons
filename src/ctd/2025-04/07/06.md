---
title:  Ama’ Ompihna Lunggulh
date:   13/11/2025
---

`Joshua 18:1, 2 simin. Joshua in gam a hawm kimlai bang sepding khat in hong lailut dihdih hiam?`

Jordan nitumna lam gundung tengpen, a lianpeh minam nih a ding leh Manasseh namlang a dingin aseh khit ciangin, minam a tawmzaw sagih teng a dingin a sehsa uh munteng hawm ding in Israel mipi in Shiloh ah hong kikaikhawm uhhi.

Biakinnpi lamna “Ka Biakinn” in Amite lakah Pasian om ding kamciam picing sak (Pai. 25:8; Siam. 26:11, 12) a, tua laibu’ thulaigil zong pulaak hi: Israel lakah Pasian ompihna in gam lazo sakthei a, Israel leh leitung khempeuh a dingin thupha om paisuak na ding ahi hi (Pian. 12:3). Pasian a biakpiakna uh pen gam laakna sungah tatlehhekna khempeuh’ laigil ahi hi. Biakbuk panin a nungciang in Biakinn a omna hangin, amau lak ah Pasian ompihna leh amaute’ bawlsa thuciamnate zuihkul ahihna phawksak hi.

`Heb. 6:19, 20; Heb. 9:11, 12; leh Heb. 10:19–23 simin. Ei Christiante in Pasian ompihna biakinn neiselo mah taleng, Joshua tungtawn in bang pilna kingah thei naci hiam?`

Biakinn leh a thukhunte in, thukham singkuang tawh kisai in Joshua kiangah aki gensiangsa ahih manin upmawhloh in hong piangvat zong hi tuanlo hi. Tuazong in Siang thobel mun sungah van omteng a deihna bulpi hi a; tua laibu khennih lakah khenmasa a teng: kaantanna leh gamlaakna ahi hi. A kihawmuh leitang laizang ah biakbuk a lamna uh tawh, leitung ah Yahweh zumhpi ahi biakbuk kiim tengah Israel omkha cihthu Joshua in a pulaakna ahi hi.

Mawhthoihna Ni mantaktak a thei ei Chrisrtiante a dingin athupi semsem lai khat in, van biakbuklam mitsuan gige sa in, eima nuntakna sungah i uptentan thute, lam-etna leh khalam gamh i luah dingte maingatsa in mainawt ding hihang. Christ in singlamteh tung leh van biakbuk sungah hong zawhsaksa khempeuh muang in om ding hang a; khat vei mah eite lakah Pasian hong omkik ding ngak in om ding hihang. Tua in a tawntung hita ding hi (Mang 21:3).
