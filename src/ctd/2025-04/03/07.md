Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets lai. 483, 484 “Crossing the Jordan” simin.

“Israel in Canaan zuan a khualzinna teng kuhkal takin sim in. Joshua thumna leh lina sim lecin, Jordan gun galkhat lam a kamciamgam sung lutna ding a kisakna teng uh ahi hi. Topa in tanglai a Ama’ mite tungah a thuhilhna te phawkna tawh eima lungsim sungah tengsak in ki thuhilh sak ding kisam hi. Amaute mahbang in eite’n zong Ama’ thu hilhna te lunglut in pibawl ding hong deihsak hi”—Ellen G. White Comments, The SDA Bible Commentary, vol. 2, p. 994.

“Tanglai Israel te sangin, tuhun Israel te in Pasian mangngilh in milim biakna sung a tun ding lauhuai zawtham hi. Thu um Sabbath tang a kicite nangawn in milim tampi bia uhhi. Pasian in tanglai a Amite milim biak ding khaam a, amaute in a nungta Pasian biakna peelkha peuhmah leh, Pasian’ samsiatna in amau tungah om ding a; amau un a lungsim khempeuh, a kha khempeuh leh a thahatna khempeuh tawh a itnakvua leh, Topa in amau seeng leh amau ansaal sungah thupha pia ding a, amau lakpan natna khem peuh hepkhiat sak ding hi”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 1, p. 609.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Na khansung vuah Jordan kaantanna thu lamdang kikum un. Na lamdang pen koibang na gentel ding hiam? Tuabang na lamdangte mah tuhun in Pasian in banghang in bawllo tawh kibang hiam?`

`Khalam thute mangngilh pahpahna teng khaktan theihna dingin a mimal ahi a, pawlpi in ahi zongin koibang in semthei ding ihi hiam? Pasian tawh kizopna a thupi hun sungin, a beisa hun sungpan pilna lalo in, Pasian hong hihsak sate phawk kik in koibang in zangthei ding ihi hiam?`

`Eima’ nuntakna sungah Pasian hong lailutna te Sabbath tungtawn in koi bang in hong kiphawkkik sak a, Ama’ kumpi gam sungah tawlngakna hisak thei hiam? Sabbath in eite a dingin phawkna lel ciangbek hilo in mailam lam-etna nangawn koibang in hong neisak thei hiam`
