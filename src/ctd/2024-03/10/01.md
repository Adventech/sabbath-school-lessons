### Tukalsung Simding
Mark 12:41–44; 13:1–32; Dan. 9:24–27; 7:25; 1Thess. 4:13–18.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Tua ciangin Mihing Tapa in vangliatna le minthanna lianpi tawh meii lakah a hong pai amaute in a mu ding uh hi. Tua ciangin amah in a vantungmite sawl ding a, lei kiuli pan lei mong khat pan van mong khat donga om a teelsa mite a kaikhawmsak ding hi.” (Mark 13:26, 27).

Tukal sinna pen, Mark 12 tawpna lama meigongnu khat gamtatna tangthu tomno khat ahi hi. Tukal sinna khenpi teng pen Mark 13 sungpan, Jerusalem biakinn leh a mailam thupiang ding teng leh adangdang kihel ding hi. Hih chapter pen Mate 24 leh Luke 21 tawh tonkhawm uh a, Jerusalem a pukna ding leh leitung a beina ding nangawn om ding hi.

Mark 13 in zong hih genkholna pen a genkhol kamsang Zeisu pan kipan in hun beina Ama’ hong paikik dong hiding cihi. Hihteng pen “hun nunung ading genkholna teng a letkhiatna” hi a, pawlkhat in, tuate khawng a beisa hi aci tawh mailam ading hi aci tawh om ziazua hi.

Mark sunga Zeisu’ thuhilhna mahbang in, Topa’ thuhilhna in a nungzuite’ telkhialhna khat a hilhcianna ahi hi. Hih dotna leh telkhialhna tengin, Zeisu ading in Christian nuntakna sungah a kisam thumaan thutak hilhtheih na’ng hunlem khat ngahsak hi. Zeisu in mailamthu a genkhol bek hilo, hong pai ding haksatna ading kigin khol theihna ding hilhcian thei pah hi.
