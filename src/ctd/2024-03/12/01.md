### Tukalsung Simding
Mark 15; Luke 13:1; Late 22:18; John 20:24–29; John 1:1–3; Dan. 9:24–27.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Nitak nai thum laitakin Zeisu in, ‘Eloi, Eloi, lama sabachthani?’ ci in nakpi in a kiko hi. A khiatna in, Ka Pasian, ka Pasian aw, banghang in hong nusia na hi hiam? a cihna ahi hi.” (Mark 15:34).

Mark 15 pen thuaksiatna bulpi ahi hi. Zeisu haksatna thusitna, a siatsakna, a zahkona, a kikhaina, a sihna leh a kiphumna cihte om hi. Hih chapter sung thupiang peuhmah khauhpai in lung hong nuamsak a, laikungpu laigelhdan pen a kihel peuhpeuh in amau munciat ah pausak hi.

Hih chapter sung tawntung ah, thu lehgenna vive ultung sak hi. Tuabang in lehgenna tawh bang gen nuam hiam cih zong kithei sak hi.

Lehgenna sungah thu namthum om hi: (1) khiatna namnih (2) tua khiatna namnih te in a kilehngat vive hi a (3) tuabang in thu a lehgen a lehpau mite in amau kampau gamtatna sulzui bang teng hiding cihzong theise mello uhhi.

Tukalsung in Pilate dotna “Jew mite kumpi nahi hiam?” a cihpen panin, singlamteh tungah a zahko a simmawh galkapte leh biakna makaite, “midangte honkhia hi; Amah kihonkhia zolo” cihna te kipan, lam-et lohpi in Arimathea khuami Joseph hong kidawkna leh adang lehtatna thuaksiatna, Zeisu’ sihna in bang khiatna nei cihte om ding hi.
