Ellen G. White, _The Desire of Ages_ laimai. 426–446 sungpan “Ministry” leh “Who Is the Greatest” simin.

“Pahtawina maiah kiniamkhiatna omhi. Mite’ maiah munsang ngahna dingin, vantung in Tuiphumpa John bang in Pasian’ maiah a kiniamkhiat mite teel hi. A naupang vaipen nungzuipa in Pasian’ nasepna ah muibun pen hi. Vantung pilna in amah aki pahtawi ding sangin kha hotkhiat ding a veiizaw nasempa tawh nasem khawm thei zaw hi”—Ellen G. White, _The Desire of Ages_, p. 436.

“Midang tungah septheih ding hong kipia teng; - pilna leh siamna, gamtat hoihna, Christian kihilhna, biakna lam theihna ah – awlmawh ngahlo tuabang mite tungah leibaa hihang a, eima tha neihzah tawh amau ading sepsak ding hihang. Tha ineih nakleh, thanemte’ khut laptoh sak ding hi hang. Pasian’ maipha a muden minthang vanmite in, a neute panpihna sungah nuamsa uhhi. A dah kha, gamtat hoih gamtate don phadiak uhhi. Vanmite in amau’ kisapna munmun, angsung a khual nuamlo te galhan penna mun leh lungkiatna a dimna mun ah om den uhhi. Tuabang nasepna sungah Christ’ nungzui diktak te in panpih uhhi”—P. 440.

**Kikupding Dotnate**:

`Mark 8:27–29 simkik in. Zeisu in Christ hi cih nangma upna midangte tungah bang zahvei na hawmsawn ngei hiam?`

`Leitung in lian a sakte leh Pasian muhna koibang in kilamdang nasa hiam? Leitung in liansa mahmah taleh Pasian in a sakloh kuabang te ahi tam maw? Leitung in a awlmawh melloh hang, Pasian in lian a sak mahmah kua bangte hiam? Hih kilamdanna teng panin leitung muhna in bangzah in bangzah in kawi in buaihuai naci hiam?`
