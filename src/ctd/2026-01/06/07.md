---
title:  Ngaihsutbeh ding
date:   06/02/2026
---

“A thahat mahmah, Christian nundan tawh akidim gamtatna in nakhempeuh nusia in Christ a dingin piakhin ding hi; Tankhiapa in lungsim phellang peuhmah sanglo ding hi.

Ki pumpiakzia nisim in kisin ding hi. Pasian’ kammal sim ding a, akhiatna leh thupiakte zuihding zong kisin ding hi. Christian dinmun hoihpen dong tung ding hi. Pasian tawh na semkhawm ahih manin, sittelna nunungpen ah zong dingtang zo dingin picing sak hi. A puksa leimite a ding lungdamthu in hong hihsak nate pen; thu ummite in midangte leh vanmite’ maiah pulak den ding uhhi”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 483.

“Mopipa hong pai ding a ngak mite in, midangte kiangah ‘enun na Pasian’ ci ding uhhi. A nunung peh hehpihna khuavak, leitung a ding a nunungpen hehpihna thupiak pen Amah’ hong itna omzia ahi hi. Pasian’ tate in Ama’ minthanna pulaak ding uhhi. Pasian in amaute a ding a hihsak sa khempeuh pen amau’ nundan leh gamtatna tawh lahkhia ding uhhi.

“Thumaanna Ni in nahoih sepna sungah taang ding a—i kammal hoih leh gamtat hoih a siangtho sungah taang ding hi”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, pp. 415, 416.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Topa sungah lungdam ding lungngai un. Nomau’ haksatna te sung khawngah lungdam ding cilo; Topa sungah cihi. Eima’ mai ah, Topa, Ama’ hoihna, Ama’ vang liatna, hong itna leh hong hotkhiatna banghang in koihden ding ci hiam?`

`Atunga thu kisungte in Christian te peuhmah “na hoih” hong semsak nuam cih thei in. A sawtlo in Christ hong pai ding a lam-en mite a ding tua hehpihna in banghang in thupi thei hiam? Eima nasep hoihna tawh hotkhiat ihihloh tei hang, tuate neikei taktak leng hotkhiat ihi thei diam?`

`Cilehsa tungah lungmuanlohna mah lungngai phot in. Bang a cihnopna hiam? Banghang in tua sungah lungmuang lo se ding ihi hiam? Cilehsa zong Pasian hong piak mah hilo maw?`
