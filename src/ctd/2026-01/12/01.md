### Tukalsung Simding
Col. 3:18–4:6; Eph. 5:22–25, 33; Pau. 22:6, 15; 1 Pet. 2:16; 1 Thess. 5:17.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Kam napau uh ciangun, mi zaknop mi ngaihnop dingin napau un. Tua hileh na kihopihte khempeuh a dingin, phattuamna anei kam a pau nahi ding uhhi.” (Colossians 4:6).

Mihing in teengkhawm nasepkhawp ciangin haksatna tampi takmah kithuak hi. Ngaihdan tuamtuamte hangin kiseelna peuh omthei hi. Ki naikhawlh semsem leng, tua a kikhawl khempeuh ki thutuah ding thupi semsem hi.

Kinaikhawlh penpen na in innkuan sung ahi hi. Innkuan sungzong “innkuan nasep phual” peuh zong kici hi. Nasep khat vaihawm dan leh innkuan vaihawm dan pen kibang lel hi. Manphatna te, ngiimnate leh mungtupte neih ding kisam limlim hi. Na khempeuh a neel dikdek theihna dingin, mimalkim ki thutuak in sepkhawp ding kisam hi. Tua mahbang in thukhunte zong pawlpi sungah zat ding hi a, pawlpi in innkuan lianpi mah ahi hi.

Tukal sinna panin, Paul in, Christian innkuan a hoihsak penpen thukhunte pawlkhat kisin ding hihang. Christian innkuan pen Laisiangtho thukhun tetawh ki makaih kul ahih manin Rome mite innkuan tawh kilamdanna themkhat hong om hi. Paul in innsung innpua lamah lawmlehgual kikhawlhna a dingin hong panpih thei ding a dang amanpha thubulpi te zong hongpia hi.
