### Tukalsung Simding
Thkte. 14; Maku 9:42–48; Rom. 3:20; Mate 5:17, 18; Rom 3:28; Mate 7:24–29.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Nangma thuhilhna te ka mangngilh ngeikei ding hi. Banghang hiam cihleh tuate tawh nong nungta sak hi. Keipen Nangma aa’ kahi hi. Nangma thuhilhnate zuihding hanciam kahih manin Kei hong hon in” (Late 119: 93, 94).

Pasian leh eite kikhawlhna hong daalthei gal lianpen in lunghianna hi. Mawhna in Pasian tawh hong khenkhia bek thamlo (Isa. 59: 2), hong khem, hong liamsak, hong nawmvalh in hong beisiangsak thei hi. Mawhna leh ang galphual ki maingat den a, a lian pen a lauhuaipen leh tawntung hun hong sukha penpen zong ahi hi.

Mawhna cihpen nunna’ khenkhat hi ci in lungsim sungah a koihlo zong om uhhi. Mi hihna tawh nopsakna lamah paiziau uhhi. Lawmlehgual tawh nuamsalua in mawhna peuh i neuseh kha hiam? Mawhna’ thulu geiteng sikdikdek napi in, mawhna pen ahibang in pulaak khia leng, mikhat peuh sukkhak ding peuh lau ihihleh; a tawpna ah gualnopna bekmah a teelte in, Pasian tawh a cidam kikhawlhna panin lampialkhia thei ding uhhi.

Mikim in mawhbawl hang a, i ngaihdan, lungsimpuak, tatlehhek, paulehham te in eimah, midang leh Pasian nangawn sukhasak hihang. Mawhna in Pasian tawh kikhawlh na hong suksiatsak tei hang, Pasian in ahihleh Ama’ Thukham theihna tawh Amah hong kilangh in eima’ nunna sungah khuavak hong taansuk sak hi.

Tukal sungteng in, Pasian in AThukham hong piakna hangthu leh mikhat peuh in thukham a palsat in amawh ciangin, a kua peuhmah in Pasian tawh kizopna hong puah phat sakthei ding cih kisin ding hihang.
