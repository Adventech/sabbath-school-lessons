---
title:  Ngaihsutbeh Ding
date:   29/05/2026
---

Thukham’ thulu in kitukalh gawp in a kitelkhialh zong lamdang sakluat ding hilo hi. Satan in Pasian a langdona bulpi pen a thukham mah hipah hi.

Zeisu’ hunlai a mi pawlkhat in, thukham hemkhia dingin Zeisu hongpai cih a um zong om uh hitaleh, tuabang bel hithei samlo hi. Tua thukham a picinsak theihna dingin Zeisu un tua thukham leh Pasian hoihna khuavak pia dingin hongpai (Mate 5: 17, 18) a, Pasian omzia taktak hong lak hi.

“Pasian’ kammal siangthote pen zahtakna bek tawh mihing’ lungsim sungah teng thei a, vantung’ deihna zong picing sakthei bek hi. David maan hun sung leh Solomon’ hun masalam sungteng in Israel mite thahatna tawh a kidimsak thei pen Pasian’ thukham mah hi a; Elijah leh Josiah hun sunga kipuahphatna teng zong tua thu nungta upna mah tawh hizel hi. Hih thumaan thutak mahpen Israel mite gamhpi hi a, Jeremiah in zong tua thu tawh kizui in kipuahphat ding nakpi takin hanthawn hi”—Ellen G. White, Prophets and Kings, pp. 465, 466.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Mipi ngeina in mawhna koici muh hiam? I pawlpi in e koici dawnkik hiam?`

`Mawhna in Pasian leh mihing, milehmi kikhawlhna hong suksiatna cikin namuh masakpen ahi hiam?`

`Pasian’ thukham zuihna haksa nasa ngei hiam? Bangthu tengpeuh in a thu kipsak hiam?`

`Pasian i theihna a thanem ciangin Amah tawh kikhawlhna koibang in hong sukkhaksak thei hiam? (Pau. 24: 3, 13, 14).`
