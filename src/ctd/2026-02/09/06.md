---
title:  Thei in Sem Knowing and Doing
date:   28/05/2026
---

Mualtung thuhilhna sungah, Zeisu in kikhawlhna thu – Amah tawh leh milehmi kikhawlhna thu tampi gen hi. A thukhupna ah lungsim hong khoih mahmah thukhat gen hi: “Kei kiangah, Topa, Topa hong ci mi khempeuh vantung gamsung ah lutlo ding uhhi. Vantung ah ka Pa’ deihna bangin a gamta teng bek lut thei ding uhhi” (Mate 7: 21) cihi.

Zeisu in, mi pawlkhat in Ama’ thu thei napi in Amah thei taktak lopi in a sammi om ding hi cihi. Thu theihna zon ding a thupit mahbang in, Laisiangtho in zong, Pasian’ mite in, Pasian a theihlohna uh tawh a nialkhakna uh omthei hi cihi (Hosea 4: 1, 6, 10). A hun a bei ngeilo Laisiangtho thumaan pen nengniam pampaih ding hilo hi. Ahihhang tua theihna in eite kheel in ki-aapna leh Pasian tawh hong tonkhawp saktheih keileh, tua in bangmah kimanna neilo hi.

“Nangbek amaan Pasian nahihna thuleh, Nangmah in na hong sawl Zeisu Christ hong theihna pen amaute a dingin tawntung nunna ahi hi” (John 17: 3). Vantung luttheih na dingin a kisam masa penin, Ama’ deihna bang hihna hi a, Amah theician lopi in lah Ama’ deihna kihih thei tuanlo hi. Tua in a khiatna letna ding a kilawmpen mah hiding hi.

Na tate in nang hong it hong cih a, na sawlna a manleh; amau’ tatlehhek tengin amau’ hong itna leh hong zahtakna a thukna hong lak hi. Tua mahbang in eite’n Pasian i it ciangin, Ama’ deihna bang i semnuam a; tua sangin hihding a hoihzaw dang omlo cih i thei hi. Amah i dawnthuk kikna in Ama’ thu manna pen, eima’ itna panin hong luangkhia Amah tawh kikhawlhna omzia diktak ahi hi.

`Zeisu in mualtung thuhilhna pen, a thungai mipite’ lungsim a khoihkha mahmah tona khat tawh thukhup hi. Bang aitam? Mate 7: 24-29 simin.`

Zeisu’ thupuakte i zak taktak ciangin zong kito sakzolo in zong kikheel zo tuanlo hihang. Hitaleh, amasa in, i bilte beek kihong henla, i lungsim beek in sangthei photphot leh; tua ciangin, Pasian tawh kinaikhawlh theihdan ding pen lungsim sungah husan simin hong omthei ding hi. Tua ciangin i nunna in tua Suangpi tung leh Pasian’ hong ngiimsak na tungah hong kinga theipan ding hi.

Kinaikhawlh theihna pen thusimlua zong hikhollo hi. Tua thupen Pasian’ Thu sungah laimaite tawh hong pulaak khin a, mikhempeuh hong piaksa ahi hi. A cingtaak Christ’ dikna pen upna tawh sang in, tua dikna tawh nuntakpih theihna pen mimal deih teelna tungah kinga hi.
