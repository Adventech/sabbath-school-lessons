---
title:  Thukham leh Lungdamthu
date:   27/05/2026
---

Zeisu mahmah in Amah leh thukham kikhawlhna pen vangneitak leh kiciantak in hong hilhcian khinzo hi.

`Mate 5:17, 18 sungah, Zeisu in thukham koici gen hiam?`

Tua mahbang in nulehpa te in zong tate a ding manpha a sakte uh pen daikai uhhi. Thukham icih zong Pasian’ omzia hi a Amah’n manpha sa hi. Eite in thukham tungtawn in Pasian tawh kikhawlhna sungah khangto ding ihih manun; Amah tawh ahi a, milehmi ihi zongin i kikhawlhna a kemcing dingin thukham hong pia ahi hi. Thukham palsatna hangin mawhna’ sulzuite a thuakkhalo eite lakah kua om peuhmah ding hiam?

Zeisu i itna pen thukham’ thulaigil hi. Zeisu in “Kei nong it uhleh, Ka thukham na zui ding uhhi” (John 14: 15) hong cihi. Zeisu it taktak peuhleng, Ama’ thukham zuinuam lelpeuh hihang. Ama’ thukham kiteltak in i muhtheih ciangin, Zeisu itloh hong phamawh ta hi. Tua sang a thupi zawsem khat in, singlamteh mitsuan gige in; Christ in eite a dingin aiawh hong sihsakna thu i mit tawh muh gige tawntungna in Pasian tungah itna neihtheih na’ng lampi hoihpen ahi hi.

Tua ahih manin lungdamthu leh thukham tonkhawm ngitnget hi. Tuhang mahin thukham leh eima’ zuihna bangzah in thupi taleh, Pasian’ maiah hong kidinsakna munah thukham mahin eite hong mawhsak veve hi. Thukham in hong maisak ngeilo, hong diksak ngeilo in hong lemtuah ngeilo hi.

Tua sangsik in, mawhmaina, diksakna leh teptuahna te ikisap lam hoh hong musak zawsop hi. Tua hi a, thukham theihcian na’ng thu tawphahpi in langdamthu, eite’ aiawh in Christ hong sihsakna pen thukham zuihna tawh hi zawlo in, upna hangbek tawh eima aa hihsakna ahi hi.

`Anuai a munteng simin: Rom. 3:28; Rom. 4:13–16; Gal. 2:16; Gal. 3:13; Philippian 3:9. Thu ummite a dingin, thukham zuihna pen kalhna hilo in upna hizaw cih bangthu tawh hong hilh hiam?`
