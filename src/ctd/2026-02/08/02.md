---
title:  Limkhat Hongpia in!
date:   17/05/2026
---

“Tuipi San kikhenkham, manna hong kiatsuk, Zeisu in mittaw mipa a damask cihte muleng um petmah ning” cih khawng za ngeiphial in nangmah’n zong ciphial thei ding hiteh.

A langkhat ah, tanglai mite sangin tulai mite in upna i neihding banghang in baih zaw ding hiam? Israel mite in laisiangthobu zong neilo, eite bangin tangthu saupibu zong neilo uhhi. Pasian’ makaihna leh hoihna teng nung leh-et kik dingin Moses in hanthawn pha mahmah bek hi (Thkna. 4: 7-10; 8: 2, 3). Israel mite tawh kibanglo in eite in kum 6000 petmah a sau laisiangtho tangthu neikhin hihang (John 20:30, 31).

Khangsim in lim a deih uh mahbang in ei’ khang in zong kideih lel hi. Mate 24 sim lecin, eima kimkot teng mahah tua limte tangtung pha mahmah pahlel hi.

`Zeisu’ hunlai amite in lim tampi mukhin napi un Zeisu in Pasian Tapa ahihna lim mah deihlai veve uhhi. Zeisu in koi dawn hiam? (Maku 8:11, 12).`

Pharisee mite hihbang in eite’n Zeisu sittel in i nial hiam? Up dingin hong piaksa khempeuh uplohna tawh Zeisu peuh i “khuisa sakkha hiam” (Maku 8: 12)?

“Jew mite a ding hih limte kisam khollo hi. Apuatham thupiang te in amau phat tuamna piazolo hi. Amaute un ngaihsutna pilna Khanna hizawlo in, khalam tha guatna hi zaw hi”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 406. Tua mahbang in eite in thumaan tawh khalam khantohna, Pasian tawh ahunhun in kikhawlhna taktak i kisam thei ding hiam? Eite in Laisiangtho tungtawn in eima khutsungah pilna tampi neikhin ihih manin lim khawng peuh kisam nawnlo lel ihi hiam?

Tua hi a, eima uplahna hangin Zeisu “khuisa liangin” i omsak sangin: Zeisu mah in Thomas kiangah “Kei hong mulo napi in hong umte a dingin lungdamhuai mahmah zaw ding hi” (John 20: 29; Heb. 11: 1). Pasian in mittaw liangin upna hong kallo a – up theihna ding thu tampi zong hong piakhinzo zaw hi. Tua thu tampite kawmkal mahah uplah theihna’ng mun tampi mah na om veve lai hi. A thubulpi in lunghianna a piangsak theite hilo, upna a khauhsak theite bulphuh ding hizaw hi.

`Pasian sungah na upna teng Second 60 sung bekbek gen ding hong kici leh koibang teng na genkhia tammaw? Nangma dawnna in Pasian leh nangma’ kikhawlhna bangteng pulaak hiam?`
