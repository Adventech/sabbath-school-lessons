Eite in upna hangin diksakna Pasian tawh kilemna kingah hi (Rom. 5: 1). Upna hang mah in siangtho sakna (Zeisu tawh kibat theihna vang) kingah hi (Sawl. 26: 18). Eima’ nunna sungah Zeisu lutsak ciangin, upna hangmah in Pasian’ tata i suak uhhi (1 John 1: 12). Pasian’ Tapa sungah upna tawh i nungta uhhi (Gal. 2: 20).

“Eite in amawkna taktak ihihlam theitel in, honkhiapa hoihna bekmah a muang kuamah a huzaap zolo kha sangin, a hehpihhuai zaw kha dang omlo hi. Thungetna, Ama’ Thu simna, upna tawh Ama’ sungah omna, mihing’ thanemna in a nungta Christ sungah a nuntak leh; amaute a khahsuah ngeilo ding khut tawh Christ in zong lenkip ding hi” —Ellen G. White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 182.

“Amaute’ upna pen, deihtak thungetna leh antanna, lungsim kiniamkhiatna in tha hat sakthei ding hi. Angsung hawmsak ding a, Kha leh Pasian’ vang tawh kidimsak ding hi. Deihtak in hahkatna upna tawh Pasian ki-aap in – Pasian bekmah muan ngeungauna leh Ama’na sepna sungah siit neilo in ki pum-aapna bekin – vantung huihlak a om ukna aana neite, hi leitung khuamial a uk a tuamtuamte leh a sangna a om kha gilote dona ah kipkho thei bek ding hi”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 431.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Galte langkhat ah Kha Siangtho tawh pangkhawm ding a cihna ah, bang a thupi thu nga hong pulaakkhia hiam?`

`Tua galphual ah upna in koimun panin maapang hiam?`

`Hih thute nangma nunna sung ngeiah koibang in hong tung namu hiam?`

`Hebrew 10:23 simin. Eima’ upna letkip ding banghang in thupi nasa hiam?`

`Nangmah thanem nakisak ciangin Zeisu tungah na ki pum-aap ding hunlem cih bangzah vei bang na ngaihsun kha ngei hiam?`
