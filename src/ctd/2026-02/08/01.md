### Tukalsung Simding
Maku 8:11, 12; Mate 15:21–28; Luka 7:1–10; Eph. 2:8; Hebrews 11; Rev. 14:12.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Upna nei icih ciangin i muhtheih nailoh i lam-ette ngah takpi ding ci in muanna lungsim neihna ahi hi” (Hebrews 11:1).

Mikhat in “Upna icih WiFi tawh kibang hi. Ki mutheilo hinapi, na kisapna bangin hong kizomsak thei hi” cihi. Himah hi: Upna omlopi in Pasian tawh kikhawlhna zong om theilo hi.

Tuni in nang’ upna koibang hiam? Pasian na upna a liing ngei hiam? Pasian tawh kikhawlhna sungah koibang in mainawt ding cih natheihlohna munah tona khatpeuh na tuakkha ding hi. Tua a hihkeileh na upna pen, amom nocik panin a san zeezaa melpua in hong palhkhia a, innsung khempeuh a namtui sak mahmah lingpaak bang ahi hiam?

“Upna nei i cih ciangin i muhtheih nailoh i lam-ette ngah takpi ding ci in muanna lungsim neihna ahi hi” (Heb. 11: 1). Eima’ thu tawh a kikalsuan thei mawkmawk hilo a, “Pasian in note tungah upna hong piak zah tawh kizui in” (Rom. 12: 3) cihi. Upna pen Pasian hong piakkhong hi (Eph. 2: 8, 9) a, tua upna mahmah pen Pasian in eima’ sungah hong piaksa ombang hibek hi.

Tukalsung upna thulu tawh zonkhiat ding tengpen: lunghianna leh uplahna koicih ding; Upna khauh in bang hi a, “Zeisu’ upna” in bang hiam cihte ahi hi.
