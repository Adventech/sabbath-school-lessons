title:  Upna ah Etteh dingte
date:   20/05/2026
---

Tuni in Hebrew 11 upna chapter simin hun tawmno zangin. Agingkhia in simmasa inla khawl hetke’n. Anihveina simkiklai inla, tuakhit ciang, anuai adotnate nangma ngaihdan tawh dawng in gelh in:

- Taang 1 na simkik in. Na muh nailohte tuni in bangteng na lam-en hiam? (Tu leh tu a kisappah leh sunmangsau khentel siam in).
- Nangma mimal teci panna leh nangma kikheelna sungah upna in koibang in maapang hiam?
- Taang 3 na a, Pasian leh a Piansakna simin. Pasian om takpi ahihna lampi tampi taktawh uptheihna dingin banghang in baihpen thei hiam?
- Taang 6 sim inla, a thupuak teng nangma kammal tawh gelhsuk in.
- Taang 7-40 in, laisiangtho’ mi tampi takte’ nundan teng pulak hi.

` `

Upna in hih mite Pasian tawh akikhawlhna a thahatsak thubulpi banghang in hithei hiam?

Pasian thei in, Amah tawh a nungta kikhawlhna neihna in upna hong kal hi. Upna kiliing khat koibang in thapia in upna thahatsak thei ding nahiam? Anuai ah ngaihsut piak na tawm om hi:

1. Ankamtang bang upna neucik in vangliatna nei a, Pasian tawh nakikhawlhna hong khangto sakthei hi (Mate 17:20). Pasian tawh na kikhawlhnop nakleh Pasian in na upna khangto sak ding hi.
2. Upna in Pasian hong hopihna A Thu zakna panin hongpiang hi (Rom. 10:17). Nisim Laisiangtho simin thungetna tawh ki-aap in.
3.	Na upna khangsak dingin Pasian kiangah ngen in (Luka 17: 5). Dawivei tapa iipa in Zeisu kiangah hong pai in khitui luangsa in “Topa aw, ka um hi, ka uplahna hong huh in” (Maku 9: 24) acih mahbang in eima uplahna kithei in upna hong khangsak dingin Pasian kingen thei hi.
4. Upna leh uplahna omkhawm thei hi (Maku 9: 24). Nangmah’n dotna naneih ma’n peuhin Pasian kiangpan paikhia mawk ke’n. Eima hotkhiatna zong launa leh liinna tawh sepkhiat ding thupi hi (Phil. 2: 12-16). Mi kiangpan upna na leitawi sangin nungakpil ngate bangin (Mate 25: 8) nangma’ upna neipah in.
5. Kha Siangtho dawngthuk inla, Nangma nunna a ding a tamzaw ngen in.
6. Na upna gamtaang sak in. Upna icih feeling hilo a, up ding khensatna cih phawkzel in. Khuamial namuh theihlohna ah Pasian om cih phawkin (2Cor. 5:7).
7. Pasian’cihtakna a dingin lungdamko in thungen in. “Na Cihtakna Lian hi” cih la a lamal ngaihsun in.
