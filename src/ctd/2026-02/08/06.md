---
title:  Zeisu’ Upna
date:   21/05/2026
---

Hih leitung in tawplam manawh in vanmi thumte thupuak in Pasian’ thukham len kip in Zeisu’ upna a nei mite acih hun hong tungta hi.

`Mangmuhna 14:12 simin. “Zeisu’ upna” in bang acihna hiam?`

Seventh-day Adventist pawlpi in upna tawh diktanna a teldan kisin lecin, 1890 kiimteng in Zeisu’ upna leh vanmi thumte thupuak in pawlpi kipkhosak cih namu ding hi. Tua hun ciangdong in pawlpi in thukham mah bulphuh in pibawl uhhi.

Tuabang ding hilo a, lungdamthu bulphuh ding hizaw hi. Tuathu EGWhite in a tomgenna ah: “Pasian’ thu-kham bel kitangko khinzo hitaleh, Zeisu’ upna zong a kikim in thupi cihleh thukham leh lungdamthu in tonkhawm cihthu - Seventh-day Adventist pawlpi in tangko nailo hi” —Selected Messages, book 3, p. 172.

Hebrew 11 in Pasian teeltuam, upna thahat mite pulaak khinzo hitaleh, kuamah peuhmah in Zeisu’ upna bang upna nei peuhmah nailo uhhi.

`Mate 26:36–42 simin. Hih hun thupi sungahkoibangin ki pulaak hiam?`

Zeisu’ upna kanei hi icih ciangin, thupiak zuihna tawh Pa tungah Zeisu’ upna peuhmah khengval sak ding bek hilo; Zeisu tawh nisim kikhawlhna zong ahi hi. Eima’ ni sim nunna a ding thubul hisak lo in Pasian tawh kikhawlhna om theilo cih theihtelna a hi hi.

Zeisu’ upna nei cih ciangin, Zeisu eima sungah omsak in, Zeisu mahin upna thu bulpi ahih manin, Ama’ upna zong eima lungtang sungah omsakna ahi hi. Khatveivei ei’ upna zong thanem theikha ding hi taleh, Zeisu in kilawm (Mang. 5: 9) ahih manin, Ama’ upna zong kineithei ta bek thamlo, hehpihna silpiak bangin eima’ deihang in kizangthei ta hi.

Zeisu’ upna bangzah nadeih hiam? Nang hong piakna dingin kiniamkhiat takin ngen inla, Heb. 11: 6 sunga ombang in nangma mimal a ding thungetna ah, “Topa aw, upna omlopi in nang hong kilungdam sakzolo hi. Nangmah omtakpi cih umin nakiang zuan kongpai hi. Lungsim takpi tawh kong zon ciangin, thaman tawh hong thukkik ta in” ci in.
