---
title:  Zeisu in Eima’ Upna Mu hi
date:   18/05/2026
---

`Maku 4:40 sunga Ama’ nungzuite upna leh Mate 15:21–28 sunga numeinu’ upna Zeisu in koibang in tehkak hiam?`

Zeisu’ nung zuih manpeuh in upna thahat cihtheihna zong omtuanlo hi. Zeisu um inga ci mi pawlkhat mahmah zong Zeisu in amau’ lungsim tawng sittel lailai hi (John 2: 23-25).

`Luka 7:1–10 simin. Hiteng pan upna thu bang kisinkha hiam?`

Maku 9 sungah, a tapa’ sungpan in, dawi a hawlkhiatsak dingin Zeisu cial dingin hongpai pa kiangah upna kicing neih leh neihloh a sittel theipa kiangah “Topa aw, ka um hi; ka upna akicinna dingin hong huh in” (Maku 9:24) cihi. Hih a kihona malkim tengah, Zeisu in mihing’ upna entel a, a kicinlahna mun ah tua upna a thahat sakna dingin lim tawh panpih hi.

Tua mahbang in Kha Siangtho in eite’ upna hong khanglo sak hinapi, kha’ galpa in ahihleh, eima nunna sungah Pasian’ kihelna lunghiang, phawkkhalo cihbang hong hih sak nuam hi.

“Lungsim in a zongsat mahmah uplahna in hong khimzo mahmah hi. Amaute’n a vawh uh khaici in amau’ khai mah aatkik ding uh a, amau mahun tua uplahna khaikung abul abal khempeuh longkhia khin ding uhhi. Hih gunei khaikungte a kibawhkhiat khit ciangin, amau’ kampau gamtatna ding a thapia omnawnlo ahih manun, hong khangto thei nawlo ding uhhi. Kha in upna leh itna khaikung tepen lungtang sungah posak in, kumpi tokhom luahsak ding hi” —Ellen G. White, Faith and Works, p. 17.

Pasian, Ama’ omzia leh Ama’ Thu tungah lunghianna i neih ciangin, tuate bang cih ding ihi hiam? Pasian in Abraham, Moses leh Job tetawh a kiholim mahbang in; eite tawh zong holim thei dingin, Ama’ limmel suunin hong bawl ahih manin, mihing tung tang thu om peuhmah paisan hetlo hi. Pasian in ciangtan omlo Ama’ zaina limmel sungah semkhawm ding in hong deih a, telcian kim zokei phial leng zong, sangthei in ki-aap ding in hong sam hi.

`Nangmah upna hong guan ding tawh akibang thute ngaihsun in. Tua laitak mahin, bang hun, bangthu hangin tua thute khawlthei a, a kipkho upna koibang in khanglo sakthei ding nahi hiam?`
