---
title:  Thupha Azahnih
date:   29/04/2026
---

Laisiangtho simzia nam tampi om a, atung a bangin tangkhat khit tangkhat, chapter khat simna, thulu khat tawh simna, kammal khat sinna leh a bubup sinna cihbang in om hi. Laisiangtho zon-olna (concordance) leh Laisiangtho dictionary tawh kisin thei hang a, Khang tawntung Kitukalhna tangthu (conflict of the ages) tawh zong simbeh thei lai hi. Khua pualam singlak ah pai in, mi’ Laisiangtho sim zong kingai thei a, lawmkhat gualkhat leh nih peuhtawh kihonsim theilai hi.

Tuabang danin lawmlehgual tawh thuthak khawng bawlin kikhawlhna kikemcing thei a, Laisiangtho simdan tuamtuam te tungtawn in Pasian hong thupiak sate thaksuah kikkik thei ding hihang. Kisin ding tampi omlai hi.

Na Laisiangtho simhun a nungta sakden ding khatpen, na muhsa khat peuhpeuh midang hawmsawn zel in. Eima kisinsa khat hilhciankik ciangin, tomgenna, gawmkhawp kikupna hangin eima’ ngaihsutna zong hong kipsak thei hi. Hih in eima theihna hong kep cing sak hi. Thupha a zahnih cihpen midangte tawh kikum in hawpsawnna pan hi a, kha lam kiholimna in a langnih mahin tha hong hatsak ding hi.

Midangte tungah hawmsawn in hilhcianna panin eima’ lungsim sungah hilhcianna thukpen kingah thei hi. Nisim in na kisinden thute pen nang a dingbek hilo, midang a dingin zong Pasian’ thupuak ahihlam natheita ding hi.

`Isaiah 50:4 simin. Pasian tawh kikhawlhna in midangte tawh na kikhawlhna zong huzaap thei cih hihmun in koibang in hong gen hiam?`

Eima’ mimal Laisiangtho simna in eimah mah tha hongpiak banah, tuani in kimuh pih khat peuh zong thapia theilai hi. Thupha a zahnih hong piangsak hi.

I khalam nunna limlim in khawllo tainna ahi hi. Tai kidemna ah hong kemcing ding Topa tung thungen inla, mungtup kung mitsuan gige in (Phil. 3:14). Tawlkhat sung peuh thadahin na vakvai hangin lungkia pah ke’nla, na kikhawlhna uh kepcin na’ng kikheel theih teng, a diak in Laisiangtho simhun leh thunget hun, kheel lel in. Eite in tuni in Pasian i theihloh tawntung nunna hipah hi (John 17: 3). Amah leh Ama’ Thu sungah nisim ki-aapna in nundan kikheelna ahi hi.

`Tukal kamngah sim inla a khiatna ngaihsun in. Tu laitak bangsim nahiam? Kua hawmsan thei ding nahiam?`
