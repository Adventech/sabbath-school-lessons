---
title:  Laisiangtho Thuthuk Kisinna
date:   28/04/2026
---

Laisiangtho hilhcian siam a omloh hang, koibang in Laisiangtho thuthuk kisin thei ding nahi hiam?

**Thungen in:** Lasiangtho kisinna peuhmah thungetna tawh kipan in thungetna tawh tawp na pen a thupitna genzawhloh hi. EGWhite in, Laisiangtho sim ding ciangin eibek kihilo hi. Kha Siangtho lamlahna ngen leng, hong nawngkai sakthei khempeuh nialna hi a; galpa taaikhia cihi. “Laisiangtho peuhmah thungetna lotawh sim ding hilo hi. Tuate a thu pitna teng pen Kha Siangtho bekin hong theitel sak thei a, a telhak thumaan telzawhna dingin hanciamna pan hong dalzolo hi”—The Great Controversy, pp. 599, 600.

**Sim in Gelh:** Laisiangtho simsuk maimaina leh hoihtak in kisinna pen kibang theilo a, gelh ngiatna in kilamdang sak hi. Gelhna in ngaihsutna hong khiapsak in, Pasian kammal hong phawksak, zonphatna, letkhiatna, zatkhiatna leh kamciam piaknate hong piangsak hi. Tua in eima’ lungsim sungah ngaihsutna tuamtuamte pen, i khuak sungpan in laikung ah leh i lungtang sungah, tua panin, tua ni sun niloh genthei ding in hong ciamteh sak hi. A thupi ciapteh dingteng zong hong gelhsuksak hi (Late 119: 15, 16). Na gelh siam kei phial leh zong, a gingkhia in sim im ngai inla, Pasian tungah thungetna kawm in.

**Hawmsawn in:** Na theihkhak peuhpeuh mikhat gensawn in. Na ngaihsutna hong kipsak in, midang zong thahatsak ding hi. Laisiangtho laibu a topmen khat Jonah, Maku, 1 John, Phillippians cihte khat peuh tengkhia inla nasep kipan in. Taangkhat tung nahih ding dan, a khawk in leh lian buppi in (tangkhat khit tangkhat hihdan):

1. Na simsuk kawmin Kha siangtho in na lungsim hong makaih in na lungtang a nipsakna ding thungen in.
2. Laisiangtho taang khat or khawkkhat tengkhia in.
3. Tua natenkhiat pen nangma simphat dingin laidal tungkhat ah gelh in.
4. Thungetna tawh tua mun simpha kik in a deihna bulpi zongkhia in.
5. A deihna bulpi in nang bang hong hilh cih gelhsuk leuleu in.
6. Hih deihna bulpi in nang leh Pasian kikhawlhna koibang in huzaap cih thungetna tawh zong in.
7. Tuni kua hawmsawn thei ding cih ngaihsun in.

“Pasian’ mite in hehpihna sungah a khantoh laisiah, Ama’ Thu a kitelcian zaw in tellai ding uhhi. Tua thumaan sungah khuamuhna thak nei ding uhhi. Khang tawntung in pawlpi’ tangthu sungah zong tuamah bang hi a, a tawp dongin paisuak lai ding hi “—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, pp. 38, 39.

`A tunga thupiak omte in, nangma a dingin koibang dan in thumaan hithei naci hiam? Late 119:105 simlai in.`
