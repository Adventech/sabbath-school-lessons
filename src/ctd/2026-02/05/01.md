### Tukalsung Simding
John 15:1–8; Maku 1:35; 1 Khang. 16:11; Late 119:105; Isa. 50:4; Isa 55:1–13.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Keima’ kampan a paikhia Ka Thu in, a hawmpi in Kei kiangah hong tung kiklo ding a; Keima’ ngiimnasa bangin, tangtung sakin, amah kasawlna thu tangtun ding hi” (Isaiah 55:11, NKJV).

Na laisiangthobu na ngahcillai ngaihsun kikin. Na neulai a Christian meltheih khatpeuh; na khangham ciang a na leitawm peuh hikha ding hi. Laisiangthobu naneihna bangtan a sawta, bu zangzah keek nanei zongin; tua laibu na manphat sakna lungngai zaw in. Manpha nasak pen mah leh Pasian’ kammalte khawng neikha cihzah in naki ngaihsun hiam? Tua laibu simden ning naci hiam?

Hihthu nangaihsun ngei hiam: Koipan in kipan sim ding? Pasian tawh kinaih theihna dingin koibang in sim ding maw?

Martin Luther in zong, “Kum tamveipi sung mah kumkhat in nihveita kasim hiven. Laisiangthobu pen, singkung golpi khat hi henla; a kammal khempeuh pen a hiang te vive hi zenleh: tuate sungah a omte thei nuam in a vekpi in teep mang khin zo ding hi ing” cihi.

Nisim Laisiangtho simna ah thupi takin simcha nahi a, na laisiangthobu zong laibu’tau tungah omcip hileh, Pasian tawh Laisiangtho simkhawpna tawh khangto zaw sem thei ding hihang. Tu kalsung tengin Pasian’ Thu hoihtak in simtheihna ding lampi zong ding hihang.
