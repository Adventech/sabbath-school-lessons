---
title:  Ngaihsutbeh Ding
date:   01/05/2026
---

Laisiangtho simna a deihna bulpi in Pasian thei in Amah tawh kikhawlhna sung ah khangto ding; tua in tawntung nunna hi a, eite’n i itpa omkhawm thei ding hi (John 5: 39; John 17: 3).

Kikhawlhna cih peuhmah langnih om hi. Mangmuhna 3: 20 sungah, Zeisu in tua kikhawlhna zongin hong pai a, ei piansak mite in Piangsakpa’ thu kisin kul cih thei ding hihang. Suangmanpha a to suangtote mahbang in eite’n zong Laisiangtho zongden ding hihang. Laisiangtho thu leh tangthute tamveipi simkhin ihih hang a sangh kaihkhop ding omlai veve hi.

“Mihing’ pilna bangzah in khangto hitaleh, a lianzaw khuavak zong in Laisiang- tho sim kul nawn peuhmah kei ci in ngaihsun kei uh hen. Mihing i hihna tawh, eite in gen kholnate a kisin sangnaupangte ihi uhhi”—Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 41.

Laisiangtho khawng eima ngaihdaan tawh kituak in puah mawkmawk lo ding hihang. “Laisiangtho koibang in zong ding hiam? Upna thukhun khatkhitkhat bawltawm in, Laisiangtho zong eima’ ngaihdan tawh kituaksak pongmawk ding hiam? Eima’ ngaih-dan muhdan tawh Laisiangtho en in, Laisiangtho thumaan khawng eima’ tehna tawh sittel mawkmawk ding hiam? Laisiangtho a sim ahilh mi tampi takmah in, amau’ hilh thute manphatna zong ngaihsun kha ngeilo uhhi. Laisiangtho kammal khawng amau’ ngaihdan tawh a letkhia mawkmawk tammahmah hi” —Ellen G. White, Counsels to Writers and Editors, p. 36.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Bang lunggulh in Laisiangtho va zuanzel nahiam? Kikheel ding khatpeuh na nei hiam? Ki niamkhiatna tawh Thu sungah ki aapna banghang in thupi hiam?`

`Laisiangtho hong paikhiat theihna dingin, hepkhiat ding a kisam nangma ngaihdan khatpeuh a omkha diam? Tuate a dingin tumahmah in thunget koici kipat ding nahiam?`

`Pasian tawh a puatham kikhawlhna ah pianpih ngeina in tukna’ng koibang in hithei ding hiam? Cihnopna in, a thak lunggulhna leh puahphat nopna lungsim in angkhualna tawh a sep leh, mikhat lampial sakthei ding hiam?`
