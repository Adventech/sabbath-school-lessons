---
title:  Khum Mahmah!
date:   30/04/2026
---

Nang duhpen bang a khum hiam. Cidamna tawh a kituak beek hiam? A khum na’ngin khuaizu peuh nazang hiam? Cidamna na’ng ahoih Manuka khuaizu peuh zong na zangkha ding hi. Khuaizubu khawng nanekngei khakleh, khum dekdak in leii tungteng ah tuisuak pah cih natheihsa hiding hi.

Late 119:103, 104 sungah, Laisiangtho in amah mah khuaizubu tawh kigenteh ngeu hi: “Nangma’ Thute khum mahmah a, khuaizu sangin khumzaw hi. Na thuhilhna te hangin thu theihtelna kangah hi” cihi.

`“Na thuhilhnate tungtawn in thu theihtelna kangah hi” cihpen bang acihna hiam? (Late 119:104). Laisiangtho simna ah hihthu banghang in thupi hiam?`

Himah, Pasian’ kammal in kha a dingin leitung in hong piaktheih te sangin khum zaw hi. A khum dangte bang hilo in, Pasian’ Thu in kha cidamna leh nundan tatdan kikheelna ahi hi. Pasian tawh naki gamlaat ciangin, khukdin in, laisiangthobu hong inla, dang a cimsakthei tua tui nungta dawn in.

**Isaiah 55:1–13 sungah, kamsangpa in a tunga thute zaisak hi. Chapter bup in sim inla a nuaia dotna teng dawng in:**

- Ama’ Thu a “ne” dingin hongpaite; Topa in bang pia hiam?
- Hihteng ah bang hihding in hong sam hiam?
- Ama’ hong tona bang hiam?
- Ama’ kamciam bang hiam?

` `

A nungta a vanglian Pasian’ Thu in lungtangte, lungsimte leh khate bunsuk in Christ sungah hong khangto sak hi. Tuabang ahih theihna dingin, eimah mah in hun la in hahkat kuhkal takin Thu sung sawk in, kiniamkhiatna, thuhilhna khempeuh zuihnopna leh ki-aapna tawh zon tentan zong kul kisam hi.

`“A kimuhthei hunlai in Topa zong” ding koibang dan hiding hiam (Isa. 55:6)?`
