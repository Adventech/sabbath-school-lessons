`Zeisu in nakhempeuh sungah etteh pipen hi a, mimal vai ah zong ki lamdang tuanlo hi. Zeisu in Pasian tawh a hunzatna Maku 1: 35 in bang gen hiam?`

Hihmun pen taangkhat linlian bek ahih hang Zeisu etteh theihna ding ahi hi. Ni suah mapek in, nisim nasep buaina tepan in Amah kihemkhia in gamdaihna munah pai in A Pa tawh kimu uhhi. Zeisu in Ama’ kimkot a leitung mite thawhma in, Galilee bualtui gei mualpang ah tu in, thungen in a Pa tawh kiho dingin na mitkha in mu in.

Hihmun pen Zeisu in thunget hun alakna mun hi a, Amah’n zong a deihpen hun mah zong ahi hi. Hih hun in ama’ thuakna sung khempeuh ah kipkhosak hi. Nikhat hun kipatna dingin Zeisu in hibang kisam ahihleh, ei e leh kisam sem lo ding maw?

Pasian in “Kamai hong zong in” hong ci a, “Topa aw, na Mai ka hong zong ding hi” (Late 27: 8) ci in dawngthuk ding hihang.

`Ama’ mai i zon ding 1 Khang. 16:11 in bang gen hiam?`

Pasian tawh kimu dingin zingsang simin paina ding mun nanei hiam? Tawlet gei a tokhom khatpeuh, a pualam gamdaihna munkhat, aihke”h anbuk sung sabuai khat peuh ah Pasian’ Thu kisin dingin Zeisu’ khe phungah na tut theihna ding mun khatpeuh e. Zeisu’ khephung in tutna’ng mun hoihpen ahi hi (Luka 10: 39-42).

Tuabang in ciangtan na munkhat peuhah, Pasian tawh nisim in hunzang zel lecin, tualai mun zongsang in nazuan zel ding hi. Thu phamawh khatpeuh a om manin Nikhat peuh pelhkha in Pasian tawh nakimuh theihloh hang, lungkia pah nai ke’n. Tua bangin Pasian tawh hunzang khawmlo in, ni tampi kha tampi beisak ke’n. Pasian tawh nisim kikhawlhna pen, nisim in khantat phatzel hi a, deihteelna ahi hi.

`Abeisa kaal sungin thungetna leh Laisiangtho simna in hun bangzah nazang khin hiam? Na kikheel dingin bangteng nangma lungdeih teelna hiding hiam?`
