### Tukalsung Simding
Pian. 3:1–5; Siam. 20:26; 1 Sam. 2:2; 1 John 4:7–19; Pian. 1:1; Pian. 2:7; Mate 1:23; Mate 28:20.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Nangmah bek a maan Pasian nahihna leh Nangmah in na hongsawl Zeisu Christ hong theihna pen; amaute a dingin tawntung nuntakna ngahna ding lampi ahi hi” (John 17:3).

Pasian’ omzia theihtel taktakna pen Amah tawh a kip kikhawlhna a dingin thubul pi ahi hi. Tua ahih manin, tukal sungteng in Laisiangtho in Pasian’ omzia a genna teng en ngiatngiat ding hihang. Theih ding thu in,

“Pasian telkhialhna khuamial in leitung tuamcip hi. Mihing in zong Ama’ omzia theihna mansuahta uhhi. Ki telkhial in ki letkhial khin hi. Tuhun in Pasian kiangpan thu puak khat tangko ding om a, hotkhiatna khuavak hong taan thupiak khat tangko ding hihang. Leitung khuamialna sungah Ama’ minthanna, hoihna, hehpihna leh thumaan khuavak taangsak ding hihang. Leitung ah tangko lai ding thupiak nunungpen in; Ama’ hong itna omzia ahi hi” —Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 415.

Pasian thupen akhawng akhek in ki pulaak zolo ding hitaleh, Laisiangtho sunga teng beek pulaakkhia thei sam hihang. Lah theizo ngeikei ni in, a diakin Pasian omzia lamdang i theihna dingin, thungen in i kisin ciangin, Amah kithuktheih semsem ding a, Amah tawh kinaikhawlh nopna tha leh midang tungah Ama’ itna lengkik sak nopna tha te hong suak semsem ding hi.
