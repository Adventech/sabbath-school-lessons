---
title:  Ngaihsutbeh Ding
date:   10/04/2026
---

Pasian in Amite peuhmah Ama’ omzia lak dingin a sap hi a, tuabang hihtheihna dingin, eimah ngiat tawh i kitheih kul hi. Mawhnei mite hihang a, mawhna mit in Ama’ picinna muzolo ahih manin; Amah teltak in muhtheihna ding pen Ama’ kammal laisiang tho simna ahi hi.

“Mihing’ lungsim kiguizui in hong piangsuksuk pate’ itna pen mihing lungsim sungah dikna luitui bangin hong luangsuksuk zel hi. Beihun aneilo hong luangsuk den mong neilo tuipi bang ahi Pasian’ hong itna pen a tung a luitui luang tawh etkak ciangin lui hong neulua keei lel hi, Kam tawh genzawhloh, laikung tawh gelh zawhloh hi. Na nun sungteng tua tungah ki-aap den thei ding hiteh. Laisiangtho na telcianna dingin deihtak in na zong thei ding hi. Vantunga Pa’ hong itna leh hong veiina teng telcianna dingin vang liatna zong samthei ding hiteh. Ahihhang telzawhloh tampi omlai ding hi.

Tua hong itna pen khangtawn in na sinsin hangin Amah leitung ah siding in hong piak ngamna hong itna azaina, athukna, asanna telkim zo tuan nailo ding hiteh. Tawn tung hunin zong tuathu pulaak khinzolo ding hi. Ahihhang, Laisiangtho simin Zeisu’ nunna tungah ki-aap den leng hihthute telsemsem theihna dingin hong kihong zawsem ding hi” —Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 740.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Tukal sungin kisinna sungpan Pasian’ omzia lungngai lecin, koipen in Pasian hong telcian sakpen nasa hiam?`

`Nang leh Pasian nakikhawlhna a thahatsak thei dingin Pasian’ omzia bangteng nakisin beh thei ding hiam?`

`Innkuanpih khat ahi a, lawmkhat peuh tawh ahi zongin ‘Steps to Christ’ sim khawm unla, chapter 1 na kikumkhawm un. Hih chapter sungpan Pasian leh Zeisu’ amaute’ omzia namuhthak bang om hiam?`

`Mi tampi in Pasian’ lim susia mahmah uh a, tuapen Zeisu in puahpha kik dingin hong tuaksuk hi. Nangma huzaap zaw peuhmah tungah Pasian’ lim leh omzia maan taktak koibang in hawmsawn thei ding nahiam?`
