---
title:  Pasian Siangtho hi
date:   06/04/2026
---

Siangthona cih limlim pen mihingte in nisim kampauna sungah kihelselo a, i kiim leh pam ah a siangtho a tawmluat hang ahi hi. Sabbath in a siangtho ni hi a, Pasian zong siangtho hi. Hinapi eima’ nisim nundan ah siangthona kisampha hihang. Pasian’ omzia tawh kisai kisin limlim lecin, Ama’ siangthona pen a thu laigil pen mah hong hiding hi. Bang a cihnopna hiding hiam?

`Anuai a munteng in koibang in Pasian pulak uh hiam: Siam. 20:26; 1 Samuel 2:2; Isaiah 57:15; leh Ezekiel 38:23?`

Laisiangtho in Pasian pen siangthona mualsuang in pulak khin a, tuapen gitlohna peuhmah tawh kipawl kilkel lo a cihna hipah hi. Pasian pen a kipatna pan a tawp dongin 100 in hoih hi. Tuabang icih ciangin Ama’ siangthona in Ama’ omzia bulpi ahi hi.

Gentehna in, Pasian’ itna in siangtho itna hi a, angkhualna leh kiliatsakna tawh kipelh hi. Ama’ pilkimna zong siangtho pilna hi a, gitloh nopna tawh kipelh sitset hi. A pilkim Pasian in siangtho kei tazen leh na muangngam ding uh hiam? Tua sangsik in kihta mahmah zawsop ding hihang.

Pasian’ vangliatna picing in zong a siangtho vangliatna ahi hi. A vangliankim Pasian in siangtholo hileh, Amah in a vangliankim a gilopa hibek ding hi. Pasian’ siang thona bekmah in Amah hong itsak thei hi. Amah a kipatna pan a tawpna dong hoihkim hi. Tua hi a, Pasian’ omzia teltak in kisin theihna ding in siangthona’ omzia ahi hi. A kitel khial penpen tuathu mah ahi hi.

Laisiangtho sunga Moses, Isaiah, Ezekiel, Daniel leh John cihte in Pasian’ maiah a omzia uh ngaihsun un. Amau’ dawnthuk masakna koibang hiam? Amau’ khedapte uh zong suahkhia, a maitang uh seel in misi bangin bawkkhup uhhi. Mihing i hihna tawh ki siangtholo ahih manin Pasian’ maiah ki dingzolo hi. Mihing in Pasian’ maitang a etleh nungta lo ding hi.

Tua mahbang in EGWhite in zong, mangmuh ciangin, ama’ muhna banglian in “Siangtho, Siangtho, Siangtho” ci in kiko zel hi. Tuabek thamlo, a nungta ganhing lii te in zong sunlehzan khawlhun neilo in “Siangtho, Siangtho, Siangtho; A beisa, tuhun leh a tawntung a om vanglian Topa Pasian” (Mang. 4: 8) ci in phatden uhhi.

`Pasian in a siangtho sitset hi a, Ama’ kiangah i pai ciangin zong tua mahbang in mu ding hihang. Tua in nang koibang in hong huzaap nasa hiam? Nangma omzia tawh kisai in bang tona hongpia naci hiam?`
