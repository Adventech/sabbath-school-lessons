---
title:  Pasian in Itna hi
date:   07/04/2026
---

Itna pen Christiante in Pasian’ omzia pulaakna ah a zatpen kammal ahi hi. Itna in 1 John 4; 8 sung “Pasian in itna hi” acih mahbang in, Pasian’ omzia pulakna hi a, John in “Pasian in hong it hi” ciziau lo in, “Pasian in itna hi” cihi. Itna pen Ama’ omzia, Amah ahihna thu laigil ahi hi.

Mi tampi a dingin, Pasian’ lim amau’ muhdan pen, mihing ki-itdan peuh tawh hilel a, a kisiasa itna pi hilelpeuh hi. Tua ahih manin itna i pulaak ding ciangin Pasian hong lahsa lim leh Ama’ kammal sunga Ama’ pulaakna bang bekmah hiding hi.

`1 John 4:7–19 in itna pen koibang in hong hilhcian hiam?`

Pasian’ itna in picing, suakta in kikhawlhna hi a, 1 John sungah a pulaak “kizom” acih bangin “Pasian in eite hong itna thu eite mahmah in thei in i um uhhi. Pasian in itna hi a, itna a neite pen Pasian tawh omkhawm uh a, Pasian zong amaute tawh omkhawm hi” (1 John 4: 16). Pasian in ki itna hi a, Ama’ lim-mel sunin hong bawl hi (Pian. 1: 27). Hebrew pau in itna pen hesed hi. Tua in mihing tawh kithuciamna itna pulak a, akilang cihtakna, huutna, kipkhona leh citna cihte ciaptehna ahi hi.

Tanglai Hebrew leh Greek pau sungah Pasian acihna pen a khiatna tawh kizui in Pasian’ hoihna pulaakna min tuamtuam om hi. Hiteng ah nih bekbek mah kipulaak hi.

- **Adonai:** thuciamna tawh akipawl, a mang tawntung, na khempeuh’ Topa, (Pian. 15: 2; Thkte. 6: 15; Mal. 1: 6; Late 97: 5).
- **Yahweh Yireh:** Topa’n hong sik ding hi (Pian. 22: 13, 14).

A lianpen Pasian’ hong itna pen Ama’ Tapa mawhneite a ding sihna thuak dingin (Rom. 5: 8) leitung ah hong piaksukna sungah kilang hi (John 3: 16). Pasian in tuathu pen mihing lakpan in dokkik thei phial hi taleh, lungsim picing takin, angkhuallo itna tawh leitung ah hong sawlsuk a, eite a ding aiawh hong sihsakna suakta takin dangthuk kikthei dingin hong deihsakna ahi hi. Pasian leh eite’ kikal kihalna tungah Zeisu in lei hong dawh sak (Isa. 59: 1, 2) bek thamlo, a picing Pasian’ itna hong lakkhia dingin nungta (John 14: 9); Heb. 1: 3) a, mi khempeuh Ama’ lamah huup ding hi (John 12: 32).

`Pasian’ min tampitak in Ama’ Siangthona leh itna bulphuh uhhi. 1 Cor. 13:4–8 sim inla, “itna” a omna munteng pen “Pasian” ci in simin. Pasian’ omzia na theihtelna koibang in hong khangtosak thei naci hiam? “Itna” omna munteng ah nangma’ min guang ding hileng, bangzah nakilawm thei ding hiam?`
