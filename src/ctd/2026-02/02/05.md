---
title:  Piansakna Sungah Pasian God in Creation
date:   08/04/2026
---

Laisiangtho kammal masapen teng thei sam veni cin: “A kipatcil in Pasian in” hi. Hebrew pau in Pasian pen Elohim hi. Hih kammal pen pasian maanlote nangawn ki min lawh thei hi. Pasian maan taktak minlawhna in zat ciangin, vanglianpenpa, vangliatna khempeuh tawh na khempeuh a Piangsakpa, mihing tel zawhloh ngeina khengval ahoih nakhempeuh a uk Pasian ahi hi. Vangliatna tawh a kampau ciangin, Ama’ aw in na khat peuh piangsak thei hi.

Piancil 2 sungah, Pasian’ minmah a dangkhat omkik hi: Yahweh. Tua minmah in Elohim (Yahweh Elohim) pen zong, tua a vangliatna khempeuh anei pa Pasian ahi hi. Pasian maan taktak minpen Yahweh hizaw a, Amah in a piansaksa mihingte itna tawh ki khawlhpih a, thuciam itna tawh it cihnopna hipha diak hi.

`Piancil 1:1 leh Piancil 2:7 sungah Pasian a ki pulaakna teng saikak in. Bang peuh na phawkkha hiam?`

Piancil 2:7 sungah, Pasian in khukdinsa in leivui panin Ama’ khut tawh mihing masa penpen bawl cih ngaihsun in. “Topa Pasian in leilak panin leivui tawh mihing bawl a, anak sungah nuntakna hu sangsuk hi. Tuami pen leitung nuntakna nei mi hongsuak pah hi.” Tua Pasian pen Adam’ naksungah husansukna tawh hong kinaikhawlh Pasian ahi hi. Amin Yahweh pen Pasian’ min hizawdeuh napi Moses in bel Piancil 1 leh 2 sung ah Pasian’ omzia namnih a pulaakna in min nih hong zang hi.

Lamdang lua! Pasian in Elohim in hong kiniam suksak, Ama’ mun ahilona, eite tawh kinaitak in Yahweh in hong om hi. Hih Pasian’ omzia namnih lungngai lecin: Amah’n nakhempeuh uk hinapi, eite tawh hong kinaikhawlh mahmah hi. Paul in Greekmi Athen khuamite tungah “eite kiang panin tua Pasian gamla om hetlo hi. Pasian hangin ei te piangin, hingin i gamtang hi” (Sawl. 17: 27, 28) cihi.

Tua hi a, Amah tawh kikhawlhna sungah i khantoh theihna dingin, Laisiangtho in honghilh Pasian’ omzia leh Ama’ limmel taktak a kitel mahmah in zong ding hihang. Tua ahih manin, Laisiangtho munkhat peuh bulphuh in simvat lel sangin, a buppi mah simsuk ngiatngiat ding thupi hi. Tua hi a, Pasian’ thu tam simsemsem leng, Amah itna khang semsem hi.

`Job 36:24–33 leh Job 37 sungah Elihu in Pasian omzia pawlkhat a pulaakna teng simsuk in. Tua khitteh Pasian in zong Amah vangliankim ahihnaa pulaakna Job 38, 39 sungah simlai in. Hihmunte in Pasian’ thu bangteng hong pulaak hiam?`
