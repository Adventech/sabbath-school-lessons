---
title:  A Kitelzaw Pasian’ Lim
date:   05/04/2026
---

Pasian’ lim taktak a kitelpen leh a kippen, Laisiangtho in hong lak hi. Laisiangtho buppi sungah muhtheih hih leitung leh muhmawh leitung tegel kikal a puandalna hong liipkhiat sak nuam hi a, eite zong koipan hong pai a, koilam zuan cihte; kua in eite hong uk, Amah koibang hi cihte hong laknuam hi.

Pasian in Amah leh Amah Laisiangtho sungah Zeisu Christ, sunglut Pasian tung tawnin hong kipulaakna Piancil pan Mangmuhna dongah om hi. Vanglian Pasian ahihna Job 1:12; theikim ahihna, nakhempeuh theihna Isa. 46: 9, 10; A thumaanna Isa. 30: 18; A hehpihna Thkna. 7: 9; hong itna leh hong thuakzawhna Rom. 2: 4; a pilna 1 Cor. 2: 7; A hehpihna 2 Cor. 12: 9; A mawhmaisakna Mate 6: 14; eima’ nuntakna dingin hong deih sakna Jer. 29: 11; sihna tungah a gualzawhna vanglian John 11: 25; a kumpi dinmun Late 47: 8; A tawntung pianzia Thkna 33: 27; leh Amah tawh kizom in kikhawlhtehihna dingin hong piak omzia dangdangte zong tampi omlai hi. Pasian’ omzia koibang cih thei tel semsem leng, Amah it semsem in Amah tawh kinaikhawlh semsem nuam ding hihang.

Pasian’ omzia a lunghiang masapen Lucifer ahi hi. Pasian tungtang a lunghianna in vannuai tangthu sungah gal kidona lianpen tungsak hi. Tualai pan kipan in “mihingte’ lungsim khempeuh Pasian theihna panin a khak-hal thei ding nate tawh Satan in kidim sak hi”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 740.

Eite in Pasian’ lim i muhna ah, nakhempeuh pasian, pasian omlo, pasian tampi om cihbang khawng umlel lengzong a taktakpa a hihkei ngal leh, Satan in phamawh saselo hi

`Piancil 3:1–5 simin. Eve tawh a kihona vuah Lucifer’ mungtuppi in bang ahi diam? Eve kiangah Pasian’ omzia bang zuau phuak hiam?`

Eve kiangah Satan’ thupiak in hibang ahi hi: Nang theihloh in Pasian in seelsim nei hi. Pasian in nang a ding ahoihpen hong deihsaklo hi. Muang kha ke’n. EGWhite in zong tuavai pen “kidona lianpi kipatcil limlim in Pasian’ omzia telkhialhna ding leh Ama’ thukham langdo ding pen Satan’ ngiimna bulpi hipah hi”—Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 338.

`Leitung in Pasian’ omzia koibang in telkhial hiam? Tua sangin a thupizaw thamlai, nangmah ngei in midang tungah Pasian’ omzia koibang in lahkhial zel nahi hiam?`
