---
title:  Immanuel, Eite tawh Hong Omkhawm Pasian
date:   09/04/2026
---

Christian ahilo mikhat kiangah Laisiangtho sungpan in Pasian’ omzia pulaak ding hilecin, koipan nong kipan tammaw?

A dawnna hoihpen in Zeisu mah hipah lel hi. Zeisu in Pasian’ omzia lengkik bek hilo, pulaak ngiat hi, Laisiangtho in cihi. Laisiangtho mun tampi mahin hihthu pulaak ahihhang, John 14: 9 mah kicianpen hi. Tualai ah Zeisu in “Kei nong muh uhleh Pa namu khin uhhi” cihi. Pasian a mutel semsem nuamte in Zeisu ending hilel hi – a kammal, a tat leh hek, a omdan leh sihna, thawhkikna tawh mihing hong itna lianpite en ding hihang.

Pa’ hong itna leh hong kepcinna pen a Tapa Zeisu sungah hong kilangsak pen hi. Laisiangtho hoihna pen Pasian in Ama’ omzia pen, Zeisu’ nuntakna sungah muhdan nam lii tawh hong pulaaksak hi. Mate (Jew mikhat in Jewte a ding agelh) sungah Zeisu pen, sawtpi pekpan in kamciamsa Messiah in akingak densa cihi. Maku in zong Zeisu pen na hong sepsak leh hong kipiakhia dingin nungta cihi. A Pa’ deihna tawh kizawitawn in mi dangte a ding vive ngaihsut sakden hi.

Luka sungah, mihing taktak hong hihna leh hong veiina tawh Zeisu’ thuakna, Luka 1: 3, 4 simleng tua thute thumaan taktak ahihna kimu hi. John sungah, hong sunglut Pasian Tapa kimu zel a; eima’ khalam nundan a khanlawh theihna dingin Zeisu pen Amah mah ahihna um dingin hong sam den hi.

Tua lungdamthu bulii tengin deihna mun tungciat uhhi; “a kibang lel nate tawh lim hong lakziau hilo hi. Laikungpu khatsim in ama’ muhdan tawh gelh a, thu tuamtuam vive tawh lungsim tuamtuam te sungah pilna zaisak, thuksak thei uhhi”—Ellen G. White manuscript 105, 1900. Bang lungdam thubu nasim tampen hiam?

`Mate 1:23 sungah, Zeisu pen minkhat kivawh tuam hi. Pasian’ omzia telcian theihna dingin hih min banghang in thupi hiam? Mate 28: 20 a tawpna lamteng bulphuh in simin. Hih minnih ne etkak ciangin bang naphawk kha tammaw?`

Hih thulu tawh kisai a tungtham, Pasian’ omzia bek sawkkha pan hihang. Pasian in ei tehmaimai theih sangin lianzaw tham lamdang zaw tham lai a, tawntung hun sungah zong Ama’ thu mah kisinsin lai ding hihang.

`Eima nunna sungah Ama’ hong hihsaksa teng leh Ama’ hihna limlim pahtawi dingin Pasian in kilawm ngiat hi. Pasian’ hihna tungtang pahtawi nadingin thunget hun tawm la ngiat in. Laisiangtho hong gen bang teek in a malmal in lo in. (Gentehna, “Lungdam ing Topa …… nong hihsak manin; …….. sungah nong cihsa bangin.”)`
