### Pasian tawh Kikhawlhna sungah Khangto

Nangmah in SDA pawlpi sungpan khangto nahi a, thu umthak nahi zongin, hih Lesson bute tampi nasim a tawm nasim zongin; tuni nangma’ kha dinmun natheih leh theihloh hizawlo a, Pasian tawh khiatna nei taktak in kikhawlhna in thupilua zaw ding hi.

Hih i thulu in mi khempeuh huam hi. Na lungsim sungah Pasian pen kitel mial muallo peuh hithei ding hi. Tuabang ahih khaleh siangtak namuhtheih na ding kisin kawmin thungen in. Na kha nundan (Laisiangtho sim thungen) koibang in thaksuah ding cih ngaihsut kawmin (or) kiphatsakna leh kiniamkhiatna, upna leh theihna, mawhna leh Pasian thukham, kisikkikna leh maisakna, muanhuai mun te lauhuaina te, koibang in zo ding, midangte in Pasian tawh a kikhawlhna vuah koibang in thapia ding cihte ahi hi.

Pasian tawh na kikhawlna pen nangmah mimal a dingina a thupipen kikhawhna ahi hi. Tubang kikhawlhna neih ding, kipsak ding, thahatsak ding zaikai ke’n. Mailam ah cihloh ding – na mopawi hun ding, nulehpa na suahna ding, na lawm na gual, na sumvai khentatna, na hun koihtuam te, na ngiimna te leh na mailam tawntung hun khempeuh huamgai pah ding hi.

Pasian in mihing tawh a kikhawlh nopna thu in Laisiangtho sungah adim aha in om hi. Muhdan tuamtuam, tangthu leh lasiangtho mun tuamtuam te teeltheih ding om hi. Khangham Laisiangtho sinna dingin hong kipia hih ciangtansa munteng mah zui ngeu ngau phot ding hihang.

Tuni in Pasian tawh na kikhawlhna koibang peuh hitaleh, hih sinna teng pen nang mah hong kiveiina tawh a kigelh ahi hi. Hih sinna sawmlehthum teng pen tu quarter thak sungteng in Zeisu na itna leh na ki-aapna khanglosak ding cih ngaihsutna ahi hi.

Hihthu pen kikhawlhna ahih manin, hih Khangham Laisiangtho sinna pen abeisa a tetawh themkhat hong kibanglo deuh ding hi. Hih sinna sungah Pasian in nangmah na mimal in hong theitel nuampa ahih manin, eima mimal tawh kisai phadeuh hi.

Ellen G. White in zong “Christ’ nung zuih cintenna pen lim lianpi khat ahi hi”—The Desire of Ages, p. 407. Laisiangtho in Pasian kiang zuatna khualzinna pen tai kidem na tawh genteh hi. Eite’ thaman pen a kisia theilo lukhu ahi hi (1Cor. 9: 24, 25). Khalam tai kidemna zong pen, hat kidemna (sprint) hilo; khawlngeilo taaina (marathon) ahi hi. Khat veivei, bawkkhup tuk in khawlkha phial ding hihang. Tuabang hun ciangin zong, thokik ziau in taaikik pah hihang. Eite in phuutloh phamawh haksatna te phu tentan ding hihang (Heb. 12: 4-11).

Hih kidemna sungah eibek a tai kihilo a; Zeisu leh Ama’ Thu a it teng tawh tai khawm hihang. A thupipen in, Zeisu in huhpa hong kamciam hi. “Keimah in Pa kiangah ka ngen ding a, tawh a omkhawm tawntung dingin, thumaan a hong lak Khasiangtho note hong huhpa ding a hong pia ding hi. Leitung mite in tua Khasiangtho mutheilo, theitheilo uh ahih manun, sangtheilo ding uhhi. Ahi zongin note sungah Khasiangtho hong om ding ahih manin note in nathei ding uhhi” (John 14: 16, 17).

Nuntakna taina ah eibek tailo hihang. Hong Huhpa in eite tawh omkhawm hibeklo eite sungah teeng ngiat in “Zeisu eima’ upna hong cingtaak sakpa” (Heb. 12: 2) hong mitsuansak in tha hong hatsak den hi.

Hihlai ka hong gelhkawmin, eite mimalkim tungah Khasiangtho in na hong sem in, leitung bup huam i pawlpi zong, nidang sangin Pasian tawh hong kinaizawsak dingin thu kangen hi. Pasian tawh kikhawlhna sangin a thupizaw thudang peuhmah omlo hi. Hih thute sinkhawm kawm in ki it ding leh Amah sungah om ding kisin ding hihang.

_Nina Atcheson pen General Conference zumpi ah curriculum manager leh Alive in Jesus Sabbath School Curriculum endiknu ahi hi. Pasian in hong humuapsa Thu mah tawh midangte in Pasian a theihcianna dingin nungta hi. Atcheson’ lawmpa prn Matt hi a, ta khangno thum nei uhhi._
