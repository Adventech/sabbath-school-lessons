---
title:  Ngaihsutbeh Ding
date:   12/06/2026
---

Nuntakna’ haksatna te in hong to laitak pen Pasian belh ding a kisampen hun ahi hi. Tu quarter sung sinna thulu khempeuh teng zong, Pasian tawh kikhawlhna khauh neih na ahi hi. Cidamna, sum haksatna, innkuan kisiatna, tanaute sihna leh a dang nangma lungnopna hong susia khat peuhpeuh a om ciangin: anuai a dotna teng ngaihsun inla, i sinsa sinna teng lungngai kik in.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Haksatna peuh na phukhakte in Pasian namuhna bangzah kikheelsak zo hiam? Pasian’ omzia maantaktak koibang in mutel thei ding nahiam?`

`Na galte sangin na thahatzawk na dingin na thunget nunung banghun hiam? Guta (Satan) in gu ding, that ding leh susia dingin hongpai a; Pasian in nuntakna hongpia cih phawk in (John 10:10).`

`Na lungsim a kiniamhiat hiam? Haksatna kawmkal mahah Pasian lianpen a, nangma’ nunna hong makaih gige cih namuang hiam? Na muan kei khakleh, Pasian hoihna leh itna pen nanmah mimal a ding cih nakoici theih thei ding hiam?`

`Nisim in Pasian’Thu sungah zung nakha hiam? Tonate na kantan zawhna dingin; na itna masa kuangkik sak ding Pasian kiangah ngen in.`

`Pasian in Ama’ kamciam tungah citak in hong nusia ngeilo cih muangin; Huhpa leh Ngaihsutna piapa ci in Ama’ kiang na zuat nunung bang hun hiam? (Heb. 13:5)?`

`Na upna a thanem leh, thungen in “Topa aw hong um ing, ka uplahna hong huh in!” (Maku 9:24) ci in. Hong thanemsak theite sangin hong thahatsak thei dingte lakah omzaw hi.`
