---
title:  Nuntakna Huihpite
date:   07/06/2026
---

Zeisu in Galilee bual’ gei panin mihonpi te tungah sunthapai in thuhilh hi. Tua Zeisu’ kammal tengin mihing te lungsim sungah tawmvei sung ahi a, a tawntung ahizong in omkha thei ding hi.

Nitaklam ciangin, Zeisu in khualzin khia dingin nungzuite sam hi. “Tui kantan in galkhat lamah paini” (Maku 4: 35) aci hi. Huihpi hong nung ding cih Zeisu in thei gige napi zinpih veve hi. A nungzuite a nuntak sunguh pilna thupi pawlkhat hilhnuam hi. A thupiang theikhin phial niteh.

`Maku 4:35–41 sungah hih huihpi’ thu simpha kik in. Upna tawh kipawl bang sinna ki neithei naci hiam?`

Hihthu teng lungngai in:

1. Zeisu in gunkuang sunga lukham khat kham in ihmu hihtual hi. Nga-man gun kuang te in lukham khat nei hamtang uh a, gunkuang hawlpa tutphah zong hi ahih manin a nunglam a hawlna mun ah om hi. Gunkuang nunga ompa in gunkuang paina ding mun ah hawl hi. Hitengah Zeisu in gunkuang “hawlpa” ommun ah om hinapi, Amah ihmu hih tuak hi.
2. Nungzuite khempeuh gunkuang tawh kisai mithal hikhin lo uhhi. Peter, James leh John tebang pen ngaman siammahmah uhhi. Galilee bualtui khawngpen khut tung lumleh bangin a lumleh uh hi a, huihpi khawng pial siamlua lel uhhi.
3. Lungdamthu sungah Zeisu’ ihmutna hiteng bekah om hi. Nungzuite nuntak sunga huihpi suksia pen khat hi a, amaute’n sih ding a lau mahmah laitak in Zeisu ahihleh na ihmu hithiat lelngel hi.
4. Nungzuite in amau’ haksat hun ciangin “Hong donlo na hiam” ci uhhi. Amau tungah Zeisu’ itna leh khoina dong ta uhhi. Eite’n zong haksatna i thuak ciangin tua mah bangin dawngthuk thei zel hihang.

Lam-et bei dinmun i tun ciangin eileh ei peuh ki honkhia nuam pongmawk thei hi. Natlehsat supna a om ciangin, Pasian hong itna leh hong donna peuh ki dongpha nuam pong hi. Mihing lampan eima’ muhdan leh ngaihdan bangin Amah’n hong panpih dingin ki um pongmawk hi. Nungzuite mahbang in nuntakna huihpi lakmah ah Pasian in lim lianpen hong hihsak thei hi. Pasian citak den hi. A puatham in Ama’ tatna muhtheih loh hang khiatna neilo in omkhong lo hi. Eima’ huihpi lakah Amah hong om a, bangmah ihih theih nawnloh ciangin Amah’n huihpi daisak hi.

`Nangma nuntakna huihpi na phutkhak ciangin na koici dawnthuk zel hiam? Hih hunte in Pasian tawh kikhawlhna koici huzaap thei hiam? 2 Corinth. 5:7 sunga teng cik in na nuntakpih hiam?`
