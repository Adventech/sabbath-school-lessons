---
title:  Emmaus paina Lampi
date:   10/06/2026
---

Tua nungzui nihte a dingin nipikal tawmno sungteng a lung hihmawh pen hun uh ahi hi. Amau’ phutkhak thuteng leh aki holimna teng lungngai phapha in om uhhi. Gual zo in Jerusalem hong lutna, biakinnpi siansuahna cihte, inndei tungnung ah Paisan pawi Gethsemane ah Zeisu’ thungetna, a zumhuai mahmah Judas in a lehhekna; thu kisitna, a ki simmawhna, saatgawpna, liammaa kidim Zeisu singlamteh tungah khaina leh nitaklam ciangin Zeisu’ laitahna hangin vanpi hong mialna. Biakinnpi deina puandal kikeek ging beeba. Misiangthote’ han kihong. Singlamteh tungpan in Zeisu’ siluang damtak in akilak sukna leh Sabbath hong tunma in hankhuk sungah koihna. Nungzuite’ buai ngeingaina, a lungkiatna leh amau’ lungsim sungvuah dotna tuamtuam te.

Zeisu’ nungzuite lungkia, thanem in lungkham uhhi. Amau’ nunna sungvuah haksatna lianpen mah ahi hi. Hibang teng pen khang tawntung in a lianpen thupiang khat ahihna amaute’n mukhalo uhhi. Amau tegel Emmaus lamzuan hong pai laitak in Zeisu hong kilang in amaute tawh tonkhawm hi.

`Luka 24:13–27 sunga kiholimna teng sim inla, a kibanglo muhdan namnih lungngai in: Nungzui nih leh Zeisu.`

Amaugel’ mi thong kihon takciangin, tua nungzui nihin Jerusalem ah tai vingveng in lampi tungah thupiang teng hong pulakkhia uhhi (Luka 24: 33, 34). Tua laitak mahin amaute lakah Zeisu hong kilang ahih manin amaute patau mahmah uhhi. Zeisu’ dotna ngaihsun lecin: “Banghang in lungmang? Banghang in lungka?” (Luka 24: 38).

Himah pen tuni eite a ding Zeisu’ thupuak higige hi. Eima’ kimkot mahah Zeisu in eimah tawh tongige ahihlam kimangngilh kha den hi. Amah phawklo tawntung hihang. Tua thupiang teng sangin a lianzaw na tampi omcih mangngilh den hihang. Zeisu in eima’ nunna ama’ khut tawh lenkip gige cih mangngilh in, lungmang pahpah hang a, lunghiang denkeei hihang. Eima’ tungah thupiang tepen Zeisu sang in theizaw thei pong mawk hihang (Luka 24: 18).

Haksatna leh tona peuhmah Christian te dawnthukna ding Laisiangtho in ngaihsutna hong piaksa om hi. Hihmun teng hunla in kisin in: Rom. 8:28; Phil. 4:4–13; James 1:2–4, 12; 2 Cor. 12:9, 10. 2 Corinthians 1:4 na lungsim sungah koih inla, a tunga thuteng nakisin kawmin, nangmah bangin haksatna thuak khat kiangah thu thum hawmsawn in.
