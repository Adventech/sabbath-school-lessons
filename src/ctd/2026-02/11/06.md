---
title:  Zeisu Mu
date:   11/06/2026
---

Na lungkiat mahmah ciangin Zeisu na munuam ngei hiam? Hih sunmang sungah nangmah kiguang dih in.

“Lungkia thanem in ka maitang khutpeek tawh hum in khua kangaihsun a, hih lei- tungah Zeisu hong omleh, Ama’ kiangah zuan in, a khephung ah bokin, ka thuaksiatna teng ko nuam buang ing. Kei hong taisan lo ding a, hong hehpih in, ke’n zong Amah it in Ama’na kasem ding hi. Tua laitak in kong hong kihong a puanhoih a silh linlian a zahtak huai khat hong luttum hi. Kei hong hehpih tuak mahmah a: “Zeisu munuam maw? Amah hiteng ah om hiven. Na neihsa khempeuh la inla kei hong zui in.”

“Genzawhloh lungdamna tawh hihthu kazak ciangin, tawmno taleh keima neihsun teng kaikhawm in, tua lamlahpa’ nung kazuipah hi. Amah in, keen bangin a kitung zen zan kahlei khat kiang hong tun hi. Kahlei kahtoh kakipat lianleh, luvai in ka tuk khak ding dah ahih manin, a tunglam bek ento dingin hong sawl hi. Tua keen lampi a kahto mi tampite zong a dawn tungzolo in akia atuk tampi om uhhi.”

“A nunung pen kahlei cial tung in kongpi mai ah kading uhhi. Hiteng ah lamlahpa in ka vanpuak khempeuh nusia ta ding in thu hongpia hi. Kei zong lungdam takin ka khia suk a, amah’n kongpi hong hon in a sungah lutding in hong sam hi. A sawtlo in Zeisu’ maiah ka ding ta hi. Tua melhoihna pen haihtheihna ding peuhmah hilo hi. Hehpihna leh vangliatna hong taangkhiatna tua maitang pen kuamah dang peuhmah hitheilo hi. Ama’ mittangte keitung ah hong tukciang in, kei nunna sunga thupiang teng khempeuh a theipa a hihlam ka theipah hi.”

“Ama’ hong et-telna pan kiseel kasawm hangin, Ama’ et-telna mitte thuakzawh ding hilo hi. Amah’ nuihmai tawh kei hong naihzawsop in ka lu tungah akhut nga in: ‘lau ke’n’ hong cihi. Ama’ awkhum in, ka lungtang sungah khatvei zong a omngeilo nuamna tawh kidim sak hi. Pauthei nawnlo liang in ka nuam a, genzawhloh in lungdamna tawh Ama’ khe-phungah bawk in ka bia hi.”

“Thanem takin bawkkhup in ka om laitak in, hoihna leh minthanna in kamai ah hong kantan a, bitna leh daihna vangam a tung ka nahi hi. Tua khit ciangin katha hong dimkik ahih manin ka thoto hi. Zeisu’ itna mitte in kei hong engige lai a, Ama’ nuihmai in kakha sungah kidim hi. Hong ompihna in siangtho zahtakna leh gen zawhloh itna tawh hong kidim sak hi.”

“Hih mangin lam-etna leh upna hong guan hi. Pasian muanna in khuamial kakha a dingin khuavak hong taan hi.”—Ellen G. White, Early Writings, pp. 79–81.

Nuntak haksatna sungah, Zeisu leh Ama’ honglah Pasian hong itna mitsuan gige ding hihang.

`Rom. 8:18, 28 sung ombang in nangma a ding bang lam-etna tumahmah in naneithei ding hiam?`
