---
title:  Khialhna Lianpen
date:   15/04/2026
---

Zeisu’ nungzui dingin kingaihsun in. Amah tawh zinkhawm, an nekhawm, ihmu khawm, nang nundan pan kipan mi tampi nundan a puahphatna pan pilna kisin ci o. Mi dangte’n gamlum liangin Amah zuinuam kawmkal ah nungzui 12 lakah nang Ama’ naipen ah hong koih sese. Nungzuite lakpan kua lianpen ding hiam cih lungngaih kipan in.

`Luka 22:24–27 sunga, nungzuite’n a lianpen ding a kinial keeina uh Zeisu’ dawnthukna sim in. Zeisu kammal sungah koipen in thubulpi hi nasa hiam?`

Zeisu kiangah a kinaipen in omthei khin a kisakkhit uh ciangun, hibang kinialna in amau’ lungsim sungah a piang nunung pen ding hinapi, tuabang na hilo hi. Amau a kisamkhak zong lungkim lel ding hinapi, kiphatsakna lutsak uh a, midang sangin amah hoih kisak zawkna lungsim hong nei uhhi. I lungsim sungah tuabang thute omsak ding baih mahmah lel hi.

“Kiphatsakna tawh eileh ei omthei lel cih lungsim tawh Pasian tung ah khialhna sangin a lauhuaizaw khialhna dang omlo hi. Mawhna khempeuh lakah, lam-etna a beisak penpen leh bawldam zawhloh penpen ahi hi”—Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 154.

Hihthu ei a dingin lauhuai mahmah hi. Thu dangte sangin kiphatsakna in Pasian sukha penpen a, muh theihloh haksatna haksatna khatmah ahih manin zawh hak hi. Eileh ei hi kisaklelna tawh eimah kisittel meelo in, kiphatsakna kumpi kisuaksak mai hi. Tuate khawl in, eimah ki sittel in, eima’ dinmun taktak muhtheihna ding, mi thong honsak ding in ngen ding hihang. Pasian tawh kikhawlhna peuhmah hong suksiat sakthei gal lauhuai pen in tua kiphatsakna ahi hi.

Pasian bekin tuabang hong hihsakthei, kiphatsakna leh angkhualna hong hepkhiat sakthei cih natheih nakleh, tu mahmah in hunla inla, “Topa aw, ka lungsim kong kipia thei kei hi. Hong laksak in, Nanga hizaw hi. Ke’n kemcing siam lo kahih manin, hong siangtho sak in. Keimah in thanem in Christ ka hong suun kei hi, Hong hon in. Hong zuun inla, hong puah in. Ka lungsim sungah Nangma itna hong luang suksak inla kei hong siansuah sak in” ci in thungen in.—Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 159.
