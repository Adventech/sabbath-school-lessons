“Zeisu tawh kinai semsem in i pai ciangin, Ama’ omzia siangthona kimu semsem a, eimah kilapsang nopna khawng peuhmah pen mawhna baanglua ahihlam kitelcian zaw sem hi. Vantung in a siangtho in a ciapteh mite a ding anunung pen gal in eimah hoih kisakna a hihi” —Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 160.

“Pahtawi maa in kiniamkhiatna om masa hi. Mite’ maiah munsang ngah dingin, vantung in Tuiphum John bangin Pasian’ maiah a kiniamkhiatte teel masa hi. Naupang a bangpen n ungzuipa in Pasian’ nasepna sungah a muibun pen nasempa ahi hi.

Amah leh mah aki lamsanglo a, kha hotkhiat dingbek a zong mite pen, vantung pilna tawh nasemkhawm thei uhhi.

Mite in, Pasian’ ngiimna lianpi a gualzawhna ding in amau kisampha cih upna tawh amau leh amau a kilapsang uh ciangun; Pasian in hemkhia zawsop hi.

“Vantung ah Ama’ gam bangin nungzuite a thuhilhna tawh kicing zolo hi. Van-tung thukhun tawh kituakin amau’ lungsim uh a kikheel sakuh kul hi. Gen olna, angkhual lohna leh naupang bangin itna neihna pen vantung omzia ngeina ahi hi. Milian taktak te’ dinmun zong ahi hi.

“Kisikna maan taktak pen Pasian’ maiah manpha hi. Pasian in Ama’ ciaptehna zungbuh a khetna tepen, hauhna tawh hilo, pil ciimna tawh hilo, Christ tawh pumkhat theihna hang bek ahi hi”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, pp. 436, 437.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Anuai a Laisiangtho munteng in kiniamkhiatna koibang in genzom lai hiam? Mate 23:12; Late 25:9; Late 149:4; leh James 4:6, 10.`

`Cihtakna tawh ngaihsun in: Nangma’ hoihna ngeei na hepkhiat a nunungpeh bang hun hiam? Pasian tawh kikhawlhna leh nalawmleh nagual tawh na kikhawlhna koibang in huzaap nasa hiam?`

`Pasian tawh nakikhawlhna a thahatsak dingin Ama’ maiah na kiniamkhiat theihna ding bangteng peuh nakikheel ding om hiam?`
