---
title:  Kiphatsakna tawh Akigak Khutmeete
date:   12/04/2026
---

Kiphatsakna. Hih kammal lungngaih ciangin gam ulian kiphasak khat, mihau khat peuh leh utongbaal khatpeuh nangaihsun kha ding hi. Kiphatsakna cihlimlim pen mi sangin kei hoihzaw ing cih ki upna hi photphot hi. Kiphatsakna pen lungsim thuakna hi a, up ding muan ding peuhmah hi vetlo hi.

Kiphatsakna cihpen, Cherub te a ukpa, Pasian tawh kinai takin nasempa Lucifer panin hong kipan ahi hi. A banghun ciangin tua kiphatsakna in ama’ lungsim sungah Lutman hiding hiam i theihloh hangin, tua lungsim in hih vannuai leitung khempeuh buaina lianpi sung tun cihbel theilua hihang. Satan pen Pasian langdopa cih mukhin hi hang (Isa. 14: 12-14 leh Phil. 2: 5-11 simkhawm in). Tua hangmah in, Satan in, Adam leh Eve sungah lunghianna guanmasa in, Pasian sangin amau mah kimuan zawkna sulzui tengpen leitung in do thapai in thuakdikdek hihang.

`1 John 2:15–17 simin. Kiphatsakna leh leitung itna tawh kisai bang thu thum hiteng in hong hilh hiam?`

Kiphatsakna a hoihlam a hithei diam? I theihsa tangthu tawh kisailo in bel, ahoih lam in kizangthei hi. Mikhat peuh’ gualzawhna, mikhat peuh nasepna lungdampih mah mahna (hong angtang pih ing) kicithei hi. A thupi mahmah telcian dingin, Pasian hong piak ahoihpen te zongin, kipsakna, manphatsakna, cihbangte kuamah in bawlngei hetlo cihzong phawkhuai hi.

Laisiangtho sungah, eimah leh eimah ki-itna namkhat om citciat hi. Maku 12: 31 sungah “nomau’ pumpi naki-it zah tawh midangte zong it un” cihi. Tuapen angkhuallo itna ahi hi. Mihing peuhmah eima nunna sungah ngiimna khatpeuh tangtut zawhna dingin Pasian in hong kihel ciangin angtang keei cihzong omkhollo uhhi (1Tim. 3: 11). Pasian in amau nunna sungah a hihsakte khawng Pasian pahtawina in zanglo uhhi.

I neihsa te, pilna siamna te, gualzawhna te khawngpen, eima’ manphatna zong hi tuanlo cih zong ciamteh ni. Eima’ manneihna peuhmah Pasian’ kiangpan hi teitei ding a, eima pilna siamna khempeuh pen Pasian kiangpan hong pai chiding ahi hi. Mangngilh het kei ni maw.

`Nangmah kidong in: Kei, bangzah kiphasak kahiam? Eima mimal kiphatsakna lel in Pasian leh midangtawh kikhawlhna bangzah huzaap thei ding naci hiam?`
