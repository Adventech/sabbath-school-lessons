### Tukalsung Simding
1 John 2:15–17; Luka 18:9–14; 1 John 1:9; Heb. 11:24–26; Luka 22:24–27; Phil. 2:3–8.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “A kiliansakte khempeuh in niamkhiatna thuak ding uh a, akiniam- khiatte khempeuh a lianin kibawl ding hi” (Luka 14:11).

Amah khial ngeilo kici in, kiphatsakna lianpipi a neite theingei hihang. Ahih kei phial leh zong, ukna nuai ah a omnuam lelmi, thuhilhna leh puahphatna khawngah paulo phunlo lelmi khawng zong om uhhi. Midang khawng tawh kisia kinken in, mi khawng a tuancil gawp theimi zong om hi. Tuabang icih ciangin midang peuh nakawk nuam kha ding a, ahihhang: eimah mimal khat sim in e koici? Khutme zungkawk tawh mi i kawk ciangin adang thumteng in eimah hong kawkkik hi a, eileh eimah a kikhemkhem hihang.

Kiphatsakna gal mah dociat hihang. I kimlehpam a mite sangin eimah hoih kisa zaw ihih manin; amau’ dinmun khawng en in, gamtang in pautheihna ding lampi peuh ki zong mawkmawk thei hi. Min theihloh mikhat in, eima nunna ki pahtawi nopna panin ki phatsakna hong piang hi cihi. Eite pen Pasian in hong piansak ngiat ahih manin eima nuntakna in manpha mahmah hi.

Tukalsung in Pasian, nangmah leh midangte tawh na kikhawlhna uh hangin, ki phatsakna in hong huzaap theilua cih kisin ding hang a; midangte’ mai leh Pasian’ maiah kiniamkhiatna tawh kisai Laisiangtho hong hilhna teng kisin ding hihang.
