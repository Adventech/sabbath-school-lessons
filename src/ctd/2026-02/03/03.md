---
title:  Know Yourself
date:   13/04/2026
---

Minih biakinn paikhawm uhhi. Khatpa a zahtakhuai upa hi a, biakpiak kipat ciang in mipi’ maiah ding lenlan hi. A thunget ging lualua a, ama’ hoihna teng muhna tawh Pasian lungdam ko hi.

Khatpa leuleu, mi mawkmawk hilel a, biakinn sung anunglam ah ding peuh hi. Amah in mawhna vangikpi hong pua ahih manin a mitte zong khitui tawh kidim pelhpalh hi. Biakinn nunglam kiu ah khukdin khilkhial in, Topa huau a, “Hehpihna tawh Topa aw, mawhnei kei hong hehpih in” cihi.

`Luka 18:9–14 simin. Hih minih tegel na koic ngaihsut hiam? Zeisu in e, a koici muh tam? Eite khempeuh a dingin bang bang pilna omthei naci hiam?`

Eileh ei kilaptoh ding baih mahmah hi. Khatveivei ciangin eima’ gualzawhna leh eima’ hoihna peuh mite’n musak hihang. Tuabang tetawh vantung in ei hong pahtawi na’ng bangmah piangsak zolo hi. Luka 18: 14 sunga Zeisu in “a kiliansakte pen kiniam sak ding a, a kiniamkhiatte pen a lianin kibawl ding hi” a cihtawh kilehbulh lua hi. Zeisu in a niampenna munah tu photphot le uhcin innteekpa in a sangna ah hong koihnop leh ama’thu cihi (Luka 18:8-10). Zeisu hong hilhna bang in, alulam-nuainung zaw kumpigam peuhmah pen ei’ lam-etna tawh kilehbulh kilkel hi. “Christ bekin hong hotkhiat theih, amah mawhneimi ahihlam a kitheite bek hiding hi” —Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 158.

Mawhneimi i hihna eima dinmun taktak leh Christ i kisap luatna telcian ciangin, lungmuanna tawh Ama’ kiangah paithei ding hang a, “i mawhnate vapulaak lehang, Amah citak in thuman ahih manin, i mawhnate hong maisak in, i hoihlohna te khempeuh hong siangsak ding hi” (1John 1: 9).

Christ tawh i kinaih semsem ciangin, mawhnei i hihna, i kilawmlohna teng i ki phawk sansan hi. “Eimah ki theihtheihna lampi khatbek om hi. Christ en ding hihang. Amah Christ phawklohna tawh mihing in amau’ hoihna dikna peuh lamto sak hi” —Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 159.

`Tua ahihleh a kiphasak mite Pasian bangci ngaihsut aitam? 1 Peter 5: 5 sungah “Pasian in akiphasak mite nial a, a kiniamkhiat mite hehpih hi” cihi. A kician zaw bang om lai?`

Na nunna sungah Pasian’ hehpihna na tuahkhak nunungpen banghun hiam? (Nisim mahin tuakkha hihang) Midangte zong hehpih thei ding hihang. Pasian khut thahat in hun a sak ciangin hong laptoh theih dingin, tu mahmah in hunla inla thu ngen in.
