---
title:  Moses, a Kiniamkhiat Silapa
date:   14/04/2026
---

A thupi a nasia mahmah Egypt kumpi inn sungah hauhna manna nopsakna tawh kiphasak mahmah uhhi. “Moses in Egypt gamsunga om pilna khempeuh kisin ahih manin thugen leh siamna lamah milian mahmah khat asuak hi” (Sawl. 7: 22). Vangliatna, hauh na leh minthanna cihte pen Moses a ding hinapi, amah in a hilozaw khat teel hi.

“Tangthupu, la pu, thupilpa, galkap mangpipa, ukpipa in demzawh lohin ding kha hi. Ama’ maiah leitungbup om hinapi, amah in hauhna, liatna leh minthanna ding teng nialzawh na’ng lungsim thahatna nanei a; ‘tawlkhat sung bekmah mawhna sungah nopsakna sang in, Pasian’ mite tawh thuaksiatna thuak ding teelzaw hi’”—EG. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 246.

`Hebrews 11:24–26 in kiniamkhiatna tawh a hilozaw khat Moses in a teelna thu koibang in gen hiam?`

Moses hong piankhiatna limlim enleng, a nunglam ciangin, a kiniamkhiatna in ciapteh huai hi. Amah ki ukzolo pak in mawhna bawl a (Pai. 2: 12), ama’ vang kemzolo pak hi. Mual kawmlakah sang vakah a, kiphatsakna hemsiang in; Kamciam Gam dong minam khat makaih zawhna’ng in kum 40 sungteng Pasian in thu hilhsak hi. Moses in tawntung nunna a ngaihsut ciangin; Egypt gamsung a vangliatna leh hauhna tengpen mial mialmual salel hi. Pasian in amah a saptuam vilvel hi a, Moses in zong a zui hi.

Hitawh a kizawitawn theipen dingin, Gamlakvakna 12: 3 sungah “Tu in, Moses a lungnem mahmah hi a, leitung a om mihing khempeuh sangin a lungnemzaw hi” cihi. Lai siangtho sungah tuan pupate lakah khat ahi Moses pen kiniamkhiatna, lungnemna tawh minthang vangvang hi. Ama’ nuntak sunga: sawlpang meikuang, Egypt gimnate, Tuipi san kantanna, vantungpan manna hong kiatna, Pasian tawh maitang kimu kiho theihna, Thukham sawmte a sanna, suangpi a saat khit ciangin Pasian’ aw a zaakna cihte kiphat- sakpih kha zen leh; ama’ nundan koibang in a kilamdang zen tam maw!

Nangma nundan lungngai kikin. Mikhat peuh in nangmah hong pulaak ding hileh, “kiniamkhiat, lungnem” cihte a kihel diam? A Kihel/ kihelloh a hangthu gen in. A taktak in eimah kiniamkhiat tawm theingiat lo hihang. Mawhna nuntakpih ngiat ihih manin Zeisu kisampha hihang. “Ke’n Zeisu deihzaw ing” cih la, a lamal mahmah sim inla, Moses’ nundan leh nang’ nundan a kizawitawn hiam enin.
