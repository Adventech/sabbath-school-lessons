“Pasian’ Thu tawh kivaak in Christ’ biakna i saankei peuhmah leh, Pasian’ khuapi sunga lut dingte lakah ei’ min na omlo ding hi. Leitung an bek ne in, leitung’ nate theihna leh duhna sungbek ah hong kikhantoh sakleh, vantung zumhpi ah kilawmna kineilo ding a; vantung khempeuh ki ukna sungah a siangthona zong nuam kisalo ding hi.

“Vanmite’ aw leh amau’ gawsem tumging te in hong lungnuamsak zolo ding hi. Vantung’ pilna pen eima lungsim a dingin thu lamdangpi khat hiding hi. Vantung mite lakah kihel theihna dingin, Christ’ dikna gilkial dangtak ding hang a; Ama’ hehpihna in eimah zuunsak puahpha sak ding hihang.”

“Tua ciangin tua minam in vantung’ thukham lobuang adang thukham neilo ding uhhi. Lungdamna vive hiding a, innkuan kigawmkhawm kik, phatna leh lungdamna puan paak silh ding uhhi. Tuate khempeuh tungsiah ah zingsang aksi te in zong lasa khawm uh in, Pasian’ tate in lungdamna tawh kiko ding uh a, tua ciangin Pasian leh Christ in zong “Mawhna omnawnlo ding hi, sihna zong omnawnlo ding hi” ci in hong tangko khawm ta ding uhhi.”

“A hoih vantung te kiholimna sungah a lutnuam ciat hihang. Pasian a nuntakna lai siah hih thu bekmah tawh kiho hileng; leitung haksatna te in hong neulua lelpeuh ding hi. Na lungsim in Pasian lunggulh sakden in.”—Ellen G. White, The Faith I Live By, p. 363.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Early Writings, pp. 14–20 sunga, vantung’ thu EGWhite mangmuhna a thu ngai or simin. A thupi penpen bangthu om naci hiam?`

`Zeisu tawh maitang kimuh dong in, Pasian tawh i kikhawlhna a kipkho sak pen bangthu cih tu quarter sinna sungpan koi hoihsa pen nahiam?`

`Na nunsung in kua in vantung thu a zak ding kisam nasa hiam? Tuathu hawmsawn dingin kamciam in. Nangmah’n naneih lohpi lam-etna khat peuh khawng midang tungah hawmsawn theilo ding cih phawk in.`
