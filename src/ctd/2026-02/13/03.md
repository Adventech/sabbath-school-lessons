---
title:  Atawpna ah, Maitang Kimu
date:   22/06/2026
---

Eite pen Pasian kiang naih dingin hong bawl ahi hi (Pian. 2: 7). Pasian in mihing hong bawlsa ahih nungsang, a kitatsa Pasian tawh ikikhawlhna hong puahphatsak kik ding hi (John 3: 16). Eima’ lungsim sungah tawntung hun hongpia khin himah taleh, mite in Pasian nasepna a kipatna pan a tawp dong kuamah’n tehzolo hi (Thsia. 3: 11).

Eite in zong i kimmot ah a laang kidona lianpi sungah a kihel bek hilo – eima’ sungmah ah om gige hi a – Pasian tawh i kikhawlh theihna’ng dinmun tunkik theihna dingin hong kipiaksak a manteng nangawn zong i lungngai kha ngeikei hi.

Leitung vai haksatna galte tawh buai ngeingai in ki om denkeei a “vantung gammi ihi a, vantung panmah in hong paisuk ding Topa Zeisu Christ eite’ honpa lawptak in a ngak ihi hi. Amah in nakhempeuh Ama’ khutnuai ah koihna in a zat vangliatna tawh a ginalo leitung pumpi pen, Ama’ aa mahbang in Ama’ pumpi tawh hong kheelsak ding hi” (Phil. 3: 20, 21).

Hih leitung in huntawpna lam manawh ta a, nisuahna lampan in meiilum kang no khat hong kidawk ding thei khin hihang. Tua meii hong naisemsem a, tua meii tungah “Mihing’ Tapa tawh akibang khat tu in, khamlukhu khu in, a khut ah phiaukawi hiam khat a tawi kamu hi” (Mang. 14: 14). Vanmite tul tampi tawh hong tonkhawm suk ding hi) (Mate 25: 31. Hong tuaksuk ciangin Ama’ kikona aw, Pasian’ pengkul muutna kiza ding a, tua ciangin, Christ sung a ihmu misiangtho te han kihong in hong tho masa ding uhhi (1 Thess. 4: 16). Tuate in amaute a sam Pa’ aw thei ding uhhi (John 5: 28).

`A zom bang apiangtam? 1 Thess. 4:17 simin. Phil. 2: 10, 11 sungah a bangthu pen in vannuai bup thawn ding ci hiam?`

Hihthu bel uphuai hetlo liang hiven. Nikhat ni ciangin Amah eima’ mitting tawh mu in, Ama’ zong za in, Amah Topa hi ci in kiko ding hihang. Tuni in Ama’ thu i kisin a, thungen in midang tungah zong i tangko pa; lungsim sungah i lunggulh mahmah pa – tua Pa tawh maitang kimu ding hihang. Pasian citak a, Ama’ kamciamna kip ahih manin, hih thute tangtung taktak ding hi (Mang. 22: 6).

Tua hun ciangin, pengkul hong kimuut ciangin, mihing mitting khempeuh in Zeisu mu ding uh a; ngak taak kisa mahmah lel ding hi. Lungkialo in thunget nilohna, Amah i masak tawntung na, Ama’ a dingin hangsan takin paukhiatna te leh i thuak haksat na te khempeuh in, Ama’ maitang muhtheih na’ng kilawmna vive ahi ding uhhi (Mang. 22: 4).
