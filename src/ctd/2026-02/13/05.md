---
title:  Tuuno Nung Zui
date:   24/06/2026
---

Tawntung hunsung ah bang lam-en pen nahiam ci in hong kidong ngei hiam? Nau pang khat dong leng, “Sahang tuang ding, Sa-ngawngsau ngawngah tolhsuk ding, Aksi te tungah leng kawikawi ding” peuh ciding uhhi.

Kum sawm valdeuh khangthak te dong leng, “innlai bawl kul nawnlo ding, liam na baaina om hetlo in lawmte tawh vantung ah zinkawikawi ding” peuh ci ding uhhi.

Khanghamte dong leuleu leng, “natna satna, thuaksiatna, sihna om nawnlo, itleh ngaihte tawh kimuhkik ding” peuh hong ci ding uhhi.

Hih dawnna khempeuh hoih in dik a, vanthak leh leithak ah lam-et ding na tampi takmah om lai hi. Tawntung hun in i lungsim sungah kuang a, hih leinun tawh kibang nawnlo a, eimah teekmah in a khengval nate tampitak mah theihbeh lai ding hihang.

`Tawntung hun sungah bang thupha dang peuh ki lam-en theilai ding hiam? Isaiah 25:8; Mang. 7:17; leh Mang. 21:4.`

Vantung thupha a lianpen in, Zeisu mitmuh ding, a puk hih leitung a ding Amah’n hong hihsak na khempeuh a dingin mimal in lungdamko ding hihang. Singlamteh tungah Ama’ thuakna ngiat tawh, tawntung sihna panin hong tatna hangin lungsim takpi tawh phat in bia nuam ding hihang. “A kigo Tuuno in vangliatna leh hauhna, pilna leh thahat na pahtawina, minthanna leh phatna a ngah dingin kilawm hi” (Mang. 5: 12).

Tuiphum John in Zeisu pen “Pasian’ Tuuno” (John 1: 35-37) cihi. Nungzuite in a nung naizuih mahmah uh a, Mang. 14: 4 in, eite’n zong tuabang in hih ding cihi. “Hihte in Tuuno’ paina peuhpeuh a zuite a hi uhhi” (Mang. 14: 4) cihi. Vantung ah Ama’ nung i zuihnop leh leitung ah Amah zuipah ding hihang.

Zeisu, Tuuno in, eite hong cing Tuucingpa hi a, midang tawh kibanglo in hong makaih hi. Haksatna sungah kitomtom laitakin Zeisu in lungmuanna hong guan a, van- tung dongin hong makaih paisuak ding hi. Mang. 7: 17 sungah “Tokhom laizang a om Tuuno, amaute a kempa hiding a, nuntakna tui omna ah amaute paipih ding hi” cihi.

Ama’ mite, Ama’ tuute, eite in vantung dongin Zeisu zui in, Ama’ omna ah om den nuam ding hihang. Pasian’ mite omzia a kitelcianna khat pen “amau’ taltang tungah Ama’ min kigelh ding hi” (Mang. 22: 4). Amah bekmah ngaihsun tawntung ding cihna ahi
