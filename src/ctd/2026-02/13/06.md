---
title:  “Hongpai un!”
date:   25/06/2026
---

Tuni in zong hong sam hi: “Hongpai un.”

`Anuai a munteng sim inla, Ama’ kiangah pai ding sapna teng lungngai in: Mate 11:28–30; Isa. 55:1–3; John 6:44.`

Kha Siangtho in Zeisu kiangah bang hong kaai nuam hi. Zeisu in zong Ama’ hong paikik ni dong Ama’ sungmah ah nisimin beel dingin hong sam hi. Ama’ hong sapna pen lungsim niamkhiat in ki-aapna tawh na dawnthuk ciangin; nangma’ kilawmna bangmah omhetlo hinapi, ni nunung ni ciangin nangmah hong phongkik ding cih theihna tawh na lungnuam mahmah ding hi. Zeisu in “Kei kiangah hong pai peuhmah ka hawlkhia kei ding hi” (John 6: 37) cihi.

Zeisu’ hotkhiatna sungah kikhawlkhawm thei dingin Kha Siangtho tawh pang khawm in midang te sam ding hihang. “Kha Siangtho leh mothak in Zeisu kiangah ‘hongpai in’ aci uhhi. Hihthu a zami khempeuh in zong Zeisu aw hong pai in ci uhhi. A dangtak mi khempeuh hong pai uh henla, a deih khempeuh un nuntaknatui a khong in hong la ta uhhen” (Mang. 22:17).

Sapna pen a khong hi a, hehpihna tawh hong kipia hi. Eima’ nuntakna sungah Amah sangin lungsim, ngaihsutna, kha leh thahatna khempeuh tawh Amah i it ciangin (Thkna. 6: 5) tu a leinun leh mailam nun in a tawntung in hong kikheel hiau ding hi,

Laisiangtho kammal nunungpen sungah Zeisu’ hongsapna ah hih kamciam mah om hi. “Kei kahong paibaih taktak ding hi” “Amen Topa Zeisu aw hong paita in” (Mang. 22: 20).

Bangzah hong paibaih tam? Mihing’ muhna pan ahihleh, sihna sung mitsi suk in i honkik lianleh Christ hong paisuk mupah ding hihang. I leinun bangzah in beibaih cih ngaihsun lecin, tuapen Zeisu hong paibaih ding dan hipah lel hi. Thawhkik ni ciangin mi’ lungsim sungah a ngaihsut masak pen ding in “Kalaisai Topa, Hong paibaih mah ciai” cih ding ahi hi.

Tu in a hihleh, limlang sungah et bang mialmual in kician mello ahihhang, tuani ciangin Amah tawh maitang kimu ding hihang. Na ngak cimlua khol kei in. Pasian hong itna leh hoihna sungah upna leh muanna tawh hih na lunggulhna nungta sakden in. Topa Zeisu aw, hong paita in!Nangma a dingin a sipa leh a sawtlo in hong paikik dingpa tungah naki pum-aapna upna a kipkho tawntung theihna dingin tu mahmah in thungen in.
