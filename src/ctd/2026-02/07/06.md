title:  Thungetvai Dotna Dangte
date:   14/05/2026
---

Pasian theihkhitsa khawng bang ngetnget kul? Pasian in nakhempeuh thei hi ven nget kul lailai maw leh? EGWhite in hong hilhcianna ah “Eima’ dinmun khawng Pasian kiangah pulak kul samlo a, eimah’n Amah i saan theihna ding bek ahi hi. Thunget na in Pasian hong paipih suklo zaw in, eimah Ama’ kiang hong paipihto zaw hi”—Steps to Christ, p. 93.

Pasian in eima deihna leh kisapna thei a, i lungsim’ geelna khempeuh simkhin zo hi. Hinapi, thunget pen eima a dingin hoih hi. Nisim nasep buai nianuana lakpan tawm veino khawlpak in, Pasian lianbel a, nakhempeuh Ama’ khe-nuai ah koih cih phawk ding in hong deihsak hi. Tuabang in hong hihsak ding Pasian cial peuhleng Amah’n lampi hong honsak thei hi. Eima’ thunget ding zia nangawn i theihloh ciangin Kha Siangtho in hong lailutsak lailai hi (Rom. 8: 26, 27).

I Hoihkim teknakleh Banghang thungen sese? Eileh eimah kicinglel sakna pen kiphatsakna hi) (sinna 3 enkik a, a thahat thungetna nundan hong daalthei thu lianpen hi. Pasian i kisapzia theitel hipeuhmah leng, Ama’ kiangah naipai mahmah ding hihang. A picing vanmite in zong bia uh ahihleh; ei mawhnei mite lellel, kisam saklohna ding bang om? Mate 5: 6 leh Isa. 44: 3 in hih thumaan koici gen hiam?

Thungetna sungah upna in bang mapang hiam? Hebrews 11:6 sim inla, athu teng ngaihsun in: “Thungetna’ lawmhoih in upna hi a, kisin khawm ding hihang. Thunget na sungah vantung’ pilna om hi; tua pilna a neimi in, ama’ nunsung nasep gualzawhna dingin telcian kul hi. Christ in “Nakhat peuhpeuh nanget uh ciangun ngah ding cih lam-en un. Tua hileh nanget peuhpeuh uh nan gah ding uhhi” (Maku 11: 24).

I nget ding teng zong Pasian’ deihna tawh kituak in ngen ding a; Ama’ hong kam-ciamsa teng ngen ding hang a, i ngah peuhmah zong Ama’ deihna bangin zangkik ding hi. Ahun zong lem linlian, kamciam zonglah hong piaksa … thupha saanna ding in a puatham teci peuh zongzong lo ding hi”—Ellen G. White, Education, pp. 257, 258.

Kuatawh thungen ding? A tamzaw mimal thunget kul a (Pasian leh nang) thu-ngetna leh Laisiangtho simna pen Pasian tawh kizopna sisan ahi hi. Pasian tawh nakiho kawmin nangma lungsim na sittelna dingin hun latuam in (Mate 6: 6). Innkuan tawh ahi a, pawlneu tawh ahi zongin thungen in (Sawl. 12: 12). Nihlehthum kikaihkhopna munah Pasian hong om hi (Mate 18: 20). A tawpna ah pawlpi kipawlna tawh thungen ding hi hang (James 5: 13-16). Hih thungetzia a namthum mahin thupi hi.

Koibang in ngai ding? Thungetna pen Pasian tawh kihona sangin sangzaw hi: Eimah hong zuun dingin phalna pia in i nunna sungah pausak ding hihang. A tunga bang in hihtheihna dingin Laisiangtho sim ding leh thungetna leh Laisiangtho simna gawm khawm ding hi. Laisiangtho simzawlo in, na lungsim awngsak in nangma ngaihdan peuh tawh hih mawmawk kei in.

`Atung a thute lakpan koipen in nang hong topen hiam?`
