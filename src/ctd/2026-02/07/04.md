---
title:  Zeisu in Thungetzia Hong Hilh
date:   12/05/2026
---

Zeisu’ hunlai in, thungetna pen sau in, siamna tawh aki phuaktawm kammal hoih nonote khawng lotngahsa in sim duatduat laang mahmah hi. Zeisu in tuabang thungetnate ahoihna bangmah genlo hi (Mate 6: 5-8). Tuabangte pen biakna um mahmah kineihna lahna pawi hilel hi cihi.

Nungzuite in zong Zeisu’ thunget mu uh a, Ama’ thungetna in Ama’ nunna ahih zong thei uhhi (Luka 5:16; 6:12; 9:18; 22:41; 24:30; Maku 1:35; Maku 6:46). Zeisu a et kawmun, amau’ biakna makaipite tawh kibatlohna mu uh a; amau’ ngaihsutzah sangin thunget ding tamzaw tham cihzong mukhia uhhi. Tua ciangin nungzuite hongpai uh in Zeisu kiangah, “Topa aw, thunget zia hong hilh in” (Luka 11: 1) ci uhhi.

Zeisu in Ama’ nungzuite leh eimah zong nisim i kampauna te mahtawh thunget theihna dingin limhoih honglak hi. Thungetna peuhmah lungsim tawngpan hi hamtang ding cihi. Luka 11: 2-4 leh Mate 6: 5-15 sim inla, a nuaia Zeisu’ hong hilhnate phawk in:

1. Vantunga om kote’ Pa: Mi khempeuh’ Pa tawh mimal kikhawlhna kipsakna.
2. Na min siangtho tahen: Pasian’ sianthona in eite’ zahtakna piangsak cih kipsak.
3. Na gam hong piang tahen: Pasian hong kumkik ding leh Kha Siangtho hong teen ding lunggulhna.
4. Vantungah adeihna akicin bangin leitung ah kicing ding: eima’ nunna sungah Pasian’deihna hong kicingsak ding ki-aapna, Eima’ deihna peuh ngetziau lel sang in Amah’n a hoihpen thei cih upna.
5. Tuni khamkhop ding an hong pia in: Nuntak khopna ding kulkisam bang ngetna, taksa a ding anlehtui, kha a ding Zeisu leh a nungta Ama’ Thu.
6. Mite’ mawhna ka maisak bangun, ko’ mawhna zong hong maisak in: Kisik na, mawhmai na’ng zong, Pasian in hong maisak bangin hong sukhate zong maisak ding thei ding.
7. Ze-etna sung hong thuaksak ke’n, gilopa kiangpan hong honkhia in: Leitung gitlohnate khempeuh pan kepcin ding ngetna (Late 91).
8. Ukzawhna, vangliatna, minthanna neitawntung ta in: Amen: Eimah, i neihsate leh na khempeuh Pasian aa hi a, Amah bek phat taakcing cih theihna (1 Khang. 29: 11).

Thu tawmbek ngetna khawl zaw in, zingsang simin na lawm it khat tawh na kiho bangin Pasian tawh kiho lo ding na hiam? Hih ding theihsa gige, bang in hong daal hiam? Zeisu hong sapna bangin tu mahmah in thungen in.
