---
title:  Ngaihsutbeh Ding
date:   15/05/2026
---

“Eimah kikhual selo in Zeisu’ thu tam gen zawlel leng, Ama’ hong ompihna zong tamsemsem lai ding hi”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, p. 102.

“Upna omlo in, lunghianna leh launate tungpan ngaihsutna la in, khua mulo pipi in nakhempeuh na siansuah nopleh, na lungsim buaina khangin thuk zawsem ding hi. Eima dinmun mahtawh kiniamkhiat in muanna tawh Pasian kianglam zuan lecin; eima’ lungdeih teng a theikhin sitset, Ama’ piansak sate mugige in, Thu tawh nakhempeuh a uk Topa kiangah, upna tawh i vanaih ciangin, eima kahna awte Amah’n hong zaksak ahih manin, Ama’ khuavak in eima’ lungsim sungah hong taang hi”—Steps to Christ, pp. 96, 97.

“Phatna kha tungah eima’ kha tuangto in vantung naihthei hi. Vantung ah zong Pasian pen la leh tumging tetawh kibia a, eite’n zong i lungdamna i pulaak uh ciangin vanmite biakpiakna sungah akihel bang kihipah hi. Zahtak lungnopna leh lungdamkohna la aw tetawh Piangsakpa’ mai ah hongpai un” (Isaiah 51:3) “—Steps to Christ. p. 104.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Atunga ngaihdante lakpan koipen in nang hong khoihkha pen hiam? Koipen in nang hong topen naci hiam?`

`Laisiangtho sunga midangte thunget nundan panin bang pilna ngah Nakisa hiam? (Ezra 10:1; 2 Kumpite 13:4; Jon. 4:2, 3; Hab. 3:1; 2 Kumpi 19:14–19; Jer. 32:16–25, Neh. 1:4–11, 1 Kumpite 8:22–54).`

`Thungetna sungah antanna in koimun panin maa hongpang hiam?`

`Tukal sinna hangin, nangma’ thungetna puahphatna dingin thuthak bang peuh nanei hiam?`
