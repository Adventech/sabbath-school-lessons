### Tukalsung Simding
1 Kumpi. 19:1–18; Mate 6:5–8; Luka 11:2–4. Mate 6:5–15; Dan. 9:4–19; Rom. 8:26, 27.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Mite aw, Amah muang tawntung unla, na lungkhamna tenguh Ama’ tungah gen un. Pasian in, eite’ bukna ahi hi” (Late 62:8).

Nangma thunget nundan koibang hiam? Bangzah vei thungen, bangzah deihna nei in ngen? Bangciang lam-en maw? Nisim in thungen maw? Phatmawh hunciang bekin ngen maw? Na thungetna te in ngetna bekmah maw, Pasian zong na phat kawm hiam?

Nikhat nasepna nakuankhiat ma, an nanekma in zingsang thungetna peuh nanei hiam? Anai anai in thungen pawl sungah kihel in thungen tawntung pawl nahi hiam? Na nunna sungah na khempeuh hong laihsak thei Pasian ompihna nan gah ngei hiam?

Thungetna in lengguipi Zeisu leh lenghian eimah kizopna ahi hi. “Eite nungta in a dawng nuamte in a Nungta Guipi panin atui dawn tawntung ding hi a; Guipi tawh kikhen nakleh thahatna neilo hi”—Ellen G. White, Early Writings, p. 73.

Tua in Ama’ sungah omna thupha ahi hi. Pasian in eite hong za in; a hunlap in Ama’ lampi tawn in hong dawngzel a – eima’ lam-etna bang lianlian hi selo hi.

Tukalsung in, Laisiangtho sungah thungen mimalte leh amau’ thunget dan in eima nisim nunna hong thapia thungetna ahihna kisin ding hihang.
