---
title:  Phatna, Pulakna, Thuumna, Lungdamkohna
date:   13/05/2026
---

Thunget zia Zeisu’ hong hilhna bangin, thu paizia teng tawh Pasian’ kiangah mimal, innkuan, pawlpi in kizuan thei a, tua holimna pen lawm khat tawh holim bangin Pasian tawh kihona ahi hi. Zeisu in thunget zia ding hong hilh khin hinapi i thungetna te sungah ngetna vive tawh kidim zel hi.

`Daniel 9:4–19 sunga Daniel’ thungetna sim inla a tuampianna teng zongin.`

Na thungetna sungah a nuaia teng na hel hamtang ding ngaihsun in:

**1. Phatna:** Phatna cihpen Pasian omzia leh Ama’ omzia pulak pahtawina ahi hi. Pasian phatna lahoih Late 100 simin. Pasian’ min tuamtuam teleh Ama’ omzia lungngai in. Na Tankhiapa, Honpa, Huhpa, Damsakpa, Tuucing Hoihpa, Alpha leh Omega leh Suangpi a hih manin phatna pia in.

**2. Mawh pulakna leh Maisakna:** Pasian tawh kiho in Ama’ sungah i om ciangin, Amah tawh hong khenkhia thei nakhat peuhmah in hong kaikhia zo nawnlo tahi. Amah tawh ki naisemsem leng, eima ginatlohna leh kilawmlohna mupha semsem hihang. Tua mahin ei’ mawhna teng hong hepkhiat sakding hong ngensak in Amah hong suun nuamsak hi. Ei’ mawhna hong maisak ding i lam-et leh, midangte zong maisak sawm ding hihang.

“Khatlehkhat tungah namawhna uh kipulaak in, thu naki ngetsak ta un. Mihoihte thungetna in vangnei a, na lianpi semzo hi” (James 5: 16).

**3. Thuumna:** Na innkuan, lawmlehgual, cidamna, sumlehpai, nasep leh sangkahna ah bang haksatna maingat nahiam? Koisantak lian ah Pasian’ makaihna khut lunggulh pen nahiam? Nang’ huhna kua’n kisam a, koici huhthei ding na hiam? Hibang mite a dingin thungen inla, Pasian’ deihna a picin ding ngen in.

**4. Lungdamkohna:** Philipi 4: 6 sim inla, na nunsunga na thupha saan teng simkik in. Na thupha lianpite na simcha zelzel ding ahih hang, thupha nengneng na saansa te eleh? Eite in Pasian’ hehpihna a saangden hinapi hi taleng, pulaakna mel zong kineilo, lungdamna zong kiko mello lel hi.

`Pasian phatna napiak nading, mawh napulak ding nan get ding leh lungdam nakoh ding bangteng omhiam? Tu mahmah in hihlo ding maw?`
