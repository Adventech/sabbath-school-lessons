---
title:  Thungetnate Hong Kidawnglobang Ahihciang
date:   11/05/2026
---

`Nakhat a ding sawtveipi a kum kailiang in thu nanget khin in zong Pasian in hong dawnglo bang hithei hi. Laisiangtho in “ngen un hong kipia ding hi” (Mate 7:7) “Ama’ deihna tawh kituak in i ngetleh” (1 John 5:14) cihi. Hih kamciam na koici tel hiam?`

Hannah in thu khat tangtawh in thungetna ah, etteh huai nu ahi hi (1Sam. 1: 10-17). Acil lamin Pasian in ama’ thungetna dawnglo bangkeek hiphot napi, ngen tenton ahih manin, Pasian in a hunlap in Ama’ deihna tawh kituak in dawng hi. Khatveivei ciang in i ngak khakna in Pasian tawh kikhawlhna thuksak in, Amah hong muang sakzaw hi.

Late 62:8 sungah “Mite aw, Amah muang tawntung unla, na lungkhamna tenguh Ama’ tungah gen un. Pasian in eite’ bukna ahi hi” cihi. Muang. Eima’ thungetna hong dawnpah loh ciangin Amah’n a hoihpen thei hi cih i muang beek hiam? Ama’ hun cin ciang leh Ama’ lampi tawnin hong dawng ding cih i muang hiam?

Khatveivei ciang, i thungetnate pen, eima’ deihdan leh a manlang theithei leh eima’ lam-etna bangin hong kidawng mello hi. Hihthu tawh kisai Laisiangtho in koibang in ngaihsut hong pia hiam?

1. Nangma’ deihna hilo; Pasian’ deihna zong zaw in (Mate 6: 10; 1 John 5: 14, 15).
2. Na ngiimna tupnate ngaihsun in. (Pau. 16: 2; James 4: 3).
3. Zongsat mawhna nanei hiam ki sittel in. (Late 66; 18; 1 Pet. 3: 12; Pau. 15: 29).
4. Pasian leh Ama’ Thu sungah om in (John 15: 7).
5. Na thunget ciangin upna nei in (Heb. 11: 6; James 1:6; Maku 11: 24; Mate 21: 22).
6. Na lungsim kiniamkhiat maw kiphasak ngaihsun in (James 4: 6; 1 Peter 5: 6).
7. Thuak haat in (1 Thess. 5: 17, 18).
8. Mi’ mawhna maisak in (Maku 11: 25, 26).
9. Pasian in nakhempeuh mukhin a, eima’ a ding ahoihpen thei hi (Rom. 8: 28; Eph. 3: 20; Jer. 29: 11-13). Khatveivei ciangin Ama’ hong dawnna pen Paul a dawnna bangin “Ka hehpihna in nang a dingin kicing hi” (2 Cor. 12: 9) peuh hithei hi.

Dawnlohsa in hong nutsiat thungetna tepen eima’ dawnthukna in eimah’n Pasian i muhdan tawh kizui hi. Pasian in gamlapi a om in hong donmello cih muhdan i neihleh, Amah tawh kikhawlhna thanem ding hi. Tuabang hun ciangin Ama’ hong donna leh hong itna, Laisiangtho sungah zong inla, Pasian na muhkhialhna teng, hong siangtho kik dingin thungen in.
