---
title:  Elijah—Haksathun in Thungen
date:   10/05/2026
---

Citakpa Elijah in Ahab hun gitlohna sungah omkha a “Ahab in ama’ maiah om kumpite khempeuh sangin; Israel Pasian Topa hehsakna ding tam hihzaw hi” (1 Kumpi 16: 33). Ama’ nunsung mahin a ciapteh taak khat in Carmel mualtung ah kimaitang tuah na khat hong piang hi (1 Kumpi. 18). Tua thupiang a sangpenna ah Ahab leh ama’ gam in Pasian in thungetna dawng takpi cih mitmuh uhhi. Israel tangthu sungah mangngilh theih loh leh lam-et lohpi in kikheel vatna thupiang a hi hi.

`1 Kumpi 19:1–18 sim inla, Elijah’ thungetna leh Pasian in amah tawh a holimna teng enpha diak in. Elijah’ lungkiatna bulpi bang hiam? Pasian’ dawnthuk na in Carmel mualtunga tawh koibang kilamdang hiam?`

Nikhat sungteng, Elijah’ thungetna khempeuh Pasian in dawngkim ahih manin, kamsangpa lungsim, ngaihsutna leh taksa mahmah kilaamsang hi. Tgawua ni in Elijah in gual zawhna lianpi khat ngah hinapi; tawmveino sungmah in, gentheihna, sih ding launa in Pasian sungah a upna a zogawp sak kha hi.

Hih tangthu sungah a kitelcian mahmah thukhat in, Elijah in lungkia dikdek in, a lungsim tha a beidikdek laitak in, Pasian in lungnem takin hong tuaksuk in an leh tui hong vaihawm sak a (1 Kumpi 19: 5, 6), sun 40 leh zan 40 a sawt hi (1 Kumpi. 19: 8). A tawpna ah Pasian in zong Amah a kipulaak ciangin, nidang aa tawh hong kibang nawn hetlo hi.

Khatveivei ciangin Pasian in munkim pan in tangtak, vangliantak leh nialtheihloh zah in hong dawng thei hi. Tua in zong upna hong thakhauhsak in, eima’ nunna sungah Pasian hong naihna zong hong theisak hi. Khatveivei ciangin lah ze-etna i thuak ciangin, kiliing hetlo in Pasian sungah upna neih ding khawng haksa peuh kisathei hi.

Pasian’ ngaihsutna leh a lampite in, eite a sangin sangzaw cihzong, phawk khalo in eima’ lam-etna bangin hong dawn ding khawng lam-en khathei hihang (Isa. 55: 8, 9). Pasian bawlsa nate ei telzawhloh tampi a om mahbang in, Pasian’ lampi te zong i tel zawhloh pen zong a lamdanglua zong hikhollo hi.

Pasian, eima a dingin ahoih a dik i Pa, in eima kisap mahmah nate thei hi. Tuate khempeuh tungtawn in Amah muang in Amah koibang in mitsuan thei ding nahiam? Tu mahmah in Amah hopih in.
