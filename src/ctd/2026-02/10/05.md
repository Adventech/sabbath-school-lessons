---
title:  A Kicing Hehpihna
date:   03/06/2026
---

Eima’ mawhna vangikpua in, singlamteh phungah Kha Siangtho kimakaihsak ciangin, “Topa dikin hehpihna tawh kidim a, hehhak in a lianmahmah itnakip tawh akidim hi” (Late 103: 8) cih thei in; Pasian’ hong maisakna ngen in. Hih mun mahmah pen Pasian Ama’ teeltuam mite in a khasiat sakkhit uh ciangin, Amah ngiatin a paukhiat (Pai. 34: 6) ahi hi.

`Paikhiatna 34:1–10 simin. Bang thumaan thupi om hiam?`

Topa in hehpihna tawh kidim, hehhak, a hehpihna in a zaihuamna in, eimah leh Pasian kikhawlhna a hoihsak kik dingin, singlamteh tungah Zeisu hong sihna thu mah ahi hi. Mawhna phawk in i pulak ciang in Aw Topa hiah om ing “Kei mawhnei hong hehpih in” (Luka 18: 13) icih ciangin; eima’ sapmaa nangawn in a Kha Siangtho tawh eima a ding a semsem khinsa i Topa in eima’ vangik hong muhsak in hong hepkhiatsak hi.

Calvary ah eima’ vangik teng hong laptoh sakkhin a, Amah kiang i zuat ciangin Zeisu in hong nai om mahmah hi. Tuucing hoihpa in hong zong niloh a, kongkhak ah dingin hong kiu hi (Mang. 3: 20). Pasian gal-et lelpeuh dingin singlamteh kiangpan gamla om sedah ni maw. Zeisu’ kiangah tai vingveng in eima’ vangik teng leh Ama’ dikna kheek ni (Zech. 3: 4).

**Anuai a munteng damtak in simin. Nang a ding Pasian hehpihna a pulaak teng nangma’ kammal tawh gelhsuk in:**

“Mawhna’ thaman in sihna hi a, ahi zong in Topa Zeisu tawh kipawl manin Pasian hong piakkhong pen tawntung nunna ahi hi” (Rom. 6:23).

“Khialhna a tamna mun ah Pasian hong hehpihna lianzaw zel hi. Mawhna thahatna in hong sihsak mahbang in, i Topa Zeisu Christ hangin Pasian’ hehpihna thahat zawlai a, mihoih in hong sangin nuntak tawntungna sung hong tun hi” (Rom. 5:20, 21).

“Eite mawhna nei i hih laitak in, eite a dingin Christ hong si ahih manin Pasian in eite hong itmahmahna thu hong lak khin hi” (Rom. 5:8).
