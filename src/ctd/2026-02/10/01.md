### Tukalsung Simding
Isa. 61:10; Hosea 6; Sawl. 3:18, 19; Pai. 34:1–10; Rom. 6:23; Mate 22:1–14.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Pasian kiangah i mawhna teng vapulak lehang, Amah citak in thu maan ahih manin, i mawhnate hong maisak in, i hoihlohnate khem peuh hong siangsak ding hi” (1 John 1:9).

Israel mite in meiikhuam nuai ah giahphual saat in om uh a, Kamciam Gam zong amau a dingin gamla mahmah bang ahi hi. Ni tampi laipek panin Moses in tua meii in a khuhcip mualtung khuamial sungah va kahto khin hi. Tua mualdawn pek khawngah gilkial dangtak leh meikuang peuhtawh sikhin zo phial ding hi ci uhhi. Tua mihon tangpi in tuamun ah om nuamsa nawnlo in bawngnawi leh khuaizu a luanna gamah lutta dingin kiging ngeingai uhhi.

A sawtnailo khatin tuamipi in Pasian’thu ka mang ding uhhi ci in thuciamna kip khat a bawl uhpen mangngilh in, amau’ muhtheih lim khat bawlnuam uhhi. Amau a ding in lim khat a bawlsak dingin Aaron giahbuk kiimah hong kikaikhawm ngeingai uhhi. Ama’ nunna a ding launa tawh Aaron in thukimpih hi. A dahhuai hih thupiang teng Pai. 32-34 sungah om hi.

Hih thupiang pen, tukal sinna thubulpi; mawh kisikna leh mawhmaina hilhcianna Laisiangtho sungah tangthutom khat bek hipan hi. Nisim sinnate nasim suksuk kawmin tukal kamngah mahmah na lungsim sungah omsak den in. Mawhnei hinapi hang in, sing lamteh leh hotkhiatna ngiimna tungah lungdam un; tualai ah mawhna pulak te a dingin mawhmaina om tawntung hi
