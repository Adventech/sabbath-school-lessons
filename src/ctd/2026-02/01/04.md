---
title:  Tawntung Itna
date:   31/03/2026
---

I lunglut mello khawng zong pen zawh ding thu phamawh khat ahihlam Zeisu in hong hilh hi. “Keimah in zong gualzo in Ka Pa tokhom ah ka tutkhawm uh mahbang un, a gualzote khempeuh in Keimah tawh Ka tokhom tungah hong tutkhawm theihna dingun thu kapia ding hi” (Mang. 3: 21). Pawlkhat a dingin eima’ thanemna leh kicinlahna phawkkhakna: Zeisu’ hong taina sangin, kisik, Zeisu; dikna puanpi in tuamsak in muhna taktak tawh Amah’n kimuhsakna pen, pawlkhat a dingin gal a hanpen peuh hi kha sop ding hi.

Eima’ gitnat lohna, lumleltak in omna cihte khempeuh Zeisu in telpha mahmah a, eimah khat bangin hong omsuk cihthu lamdang mahmah hi. Amah in “Kei ka gualzawh mahbang in, a gualzo mite” (Mang. 3: 21) cihi. Ei hong hon dingin hong si ahih mahbang in mawhna leh a thaman zong zokhin hi. Mawhna galdona zong telcian ahih manin huh ding kamciam hong pia hi.

Laisangtho sungah mi tampi takin Pasian tawh kikhawl ding kamciam tawh hong sapna dawng thuk uhhi. Laisaingtho buppi tawh a kiguizop thuthuk te zong ahi uhhi. Tua te khempeuh huam-et leng; a mimal mal in Pasian’ dawnthuk kikna in a tuamtuak vive uh ahi uhhi.

**Hun leh omzia tuamtaum sungah hih mite tungah Pasian in a dawnthuk kikna tuamtuam koibang in kiciamteh hiam?**

`Pian. 2:7; 3:8–10`

`Pian. 5:24`

`Pian. 6:13`

`Pian. 12:1–4`

`Paik. 34:29`

Pasian in, taksa mahtawh a tate tawh kikhawl kiho cihbang ahihlian loh hang, mi tawh kinai khawlh nuam mahmah takpi hi. Tuni in Pasian tawh na kikhawlhna bangciang peuh hitaleh, Pasian in nang tawh kinainuam hi. Tua tawh kisai Jeremiah 31: 3, 4 sungah “Gamlapi pek panin Topa kakilang hi. Keimah in nang kong it tawntung a, tua ahihman in, nang tungah kong thumaan tawntung hi. Nang kong bawlpha kik ding hi”

Nangma’ nite a kipan ahi a, a tawp ahiphial zongin, Pasian in hong zong a, nang tawh kinainuam hi. Nang hong lampha in hong lamphakik nuam hi. Tuabang a hihkei leh zong, mawhna peuhmah nang’ lampan hizaw hamtang hi.

`A kizawhzawh keileh, Pasian leh nang’ kikhawlhna hong khak hong halthei ding nangma nunna sungah bangpeuh omthei ding hiam?`
