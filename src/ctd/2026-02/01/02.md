---
title:  Eima’ Dinmun
date:   29/03/2026
---

Zeisu in tu laitak in nang tawh a kikhawlhdan hong pulak ding hileh koibang ahi tam cih nangaihsun ngei hiam? Thahat mahteh peuh hong cimaw; a beisa sangin hatzaw deuh peuh hong cithei ding hi. Hih hun nunung a Ama’ mite koibang in gen ding cihpeuh na lungngai kha ngei hiam? Mangmuhna 3: 14-22 sungah Zeisu in pulak khin hi. Tualai ah Amah pen “Thumaan leh citak in teci a pang, Pasian’ piansakte khempeuh a kipatna” (Mang. 3: 14) cihi. Thumaan leh citak in teci a pangpa in zuaulo in thumaan taktak gen hi.

`Mangmuhna 3:14–17 sungah, Zeisu in tuni a Ama’ mite omzia pulak khia hi. Hihthu te in nang koibang in hong pulak naci hiam?`

Zeisu in, hun nunung mite hong theitel mahmah cihi. Eite in salo votlo in ki om a, lumlel in eima dinmun panin bangmah kisam kisa nawnlo hihang. Hun tomno la in Pasian tawh kikhawlpak zelzel ihih manin kicing lel dingin ki um hihang. Kicing deksuai lo hi. Eima ngaihsutzah sangin Amah kisam zawtham hihang.

Lungsim khempeuh tawh Zeisu it in, Ama’ a ding lungsim khempeuh tawh nung ta zokei phial lengzong Pasian’ muhna ah lumlel sangin hoihzaw mah ding hihang. Zeisu in bel a gim hua kahih manin siakhia ding hi’ng cihi. Himah taleh, hong tuacih nailo deuh lai a, teeltheih ding thu pawlkhat mah hong pia hi.

`Mang. 3:18, 19 sungah koibang in hanthotna hong pia hiam?`

Tanglai in sum kilei ciangin van leh van kikheek thei uhhi. Hihtengah zong Zeisu in kikheek theih ding van hong zuak hi: eima’ gitnatlohna pen Ama’ kham tawh, Ama’ puanpak tawh, Ama’ mitzaa tawh kikheek ding cihi. Ama’ dikna puanpi tawh eite hong tuamnuam hi. Thumaan thutak mu in, Ama’ sungah beel in Amah tawh kikhawl siamna hangin na khempeuh kikheel cih Amah’n munuam hi. Eima kisap peuhmah eimah’n nei zolo ihih manin, tuate khempeuh Amah’n hong zuak hi. Amah bekin hihthei a, eilam pan ut ding kisam hi.

`Nangma khalam nundan zong nangmah’n na muh thadah keeileh tuni in bang lam-etna peuh hong guanlai hiam?`
