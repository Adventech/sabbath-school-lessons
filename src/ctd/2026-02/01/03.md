---
title:  Thuhilh, Kisik, Thaman
date:   30/03/2026
---

Zeisu in “Ka it mite thu hilh in katai hi” (Mang. 3: 19); “Tua ahih manin na lungsim takpi uhtawh na mawhna uhpan kisik un” cihi. Eite khatpeuh in setkan khatpeuh zong tu leh mailam, Zeisu in hong kem hetkei citheilo ding hihang. Mihing a dingin hih leitung ah hong tuaksuk in lam haksapipi hong tawnsuk dah lelleh zong baihzaw tham sam ding hi. Ama’ ngeina bangin hong itluat manpeuh in hong thuhilh zel hilel hi. Eite tawh a khauh mahmah kikhawlhna deih hi. Ei’ omdan silhzel suahkhia zel in, “Ka kisap hunhun in Ama’ kiang paimai ning” cihlel tawh lungkim zolo hi.

Eima’ hoih theihna dingin Zeisu in hong tai hi. Kisik un hong ci hi. Eima khialhna i muhzawh keinakleh lah kisik thei tuanlo hihang. Eima khialhna taktak Amah’n hong hilh zo hi. Hau mahmah i kisak hang a taktak in, “a zawng, a mittaw, puansilh neilo, a hehpih huai, a lungkham huai” (Mang. 3: 17) hong ci hi.

`Mangmuhna 3:20 simin. Bang kamciam om hiam? Tua kamciam i saan theihna dingin bang hih ding hiam?`

Hih kamciam in hoihna tawh kidim hi. Vannuai bup Pasian in eitawh anne khawm nuam zawzen hi. A mimal in kikhawl ngiat in holimna leh anlim ne-khawm nuam hi. Ki nai khawlhna, ki ompih ngiatna Zeisu in Amah tawh hong neisak nuam hi.

Zeisu in nangma lungsim kongkhak hong kiu in hong ngak ngiungeu hi. Tua lim zong naupang laibu sungah Mukhin phial nuteh – a thasang a ithuai Honpa in hong kiu keuhkeuh hi. Amah tawh kiho dingin hong zawhthawh lutlo hi. Na manlah hunte a dingin hong mawhpaih hetlo hi. Hun tom ahih manin, nazak nakleh na kong hon lel in. Nangma’ nunna sungah hong lut ding hi.

Hih thugentehna zong pen Zeisu in eitawh a kikhawlh nopna thu hong hilhna hi. Nikhat Amah tawh maitang nakimuh ciangun, pahtawina tawh Ama’ khephung ah na lukhu na ngaksuk ciangin, tulthen tampite in zong Amah Piangsakpa bia ding uhhi (Mang. 4:9-11; 5; 11-14). Tua ciangin, a beisa hun in na leitung haksatna teng pen hong thuneu luakeei lel ding a, tua hun ciangin, Zeisu tawh leitungah nakikhawlh hun teng peuh kisik omse ding hiam?

`Tu mahmah in Zeisu’n hong kiu hi. Hong sam hi. Nang nahihleh, na lungsim kongkhak hon ding na khentat ding bekmah hi. Singlamteh na et ciangin tua khentatna neithei dingin na lungsim sungah koibang in hong suak hiam?`
