### Tukalsung Simding
Mang. 3:14–22; Mang. 4:9–11; Pian. 2:7; Pian. 3:8–10; Jer. 31:3, 4; John 15:1–11; Rom. 8:9–11.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Ka Pa in Kei hong itzah in note hong it Kahih manin; Keima’ hong itna sungah om un” (John 15:9).

Tuni in Pasian tawh na kikhawlhna koibangin pulak ding nahiam? Kiliing in a hat mahmah hiam? Ama’ hong hatthotna kammalte zongin khawlhpih lawm khat bangin na hopih hiam? Ama’ a ding nahun bangzah nazang hiam?

Pasian tawh na kikhawlh khak pen na khansungin a lamdangpen kikhawlhna ahih manin midangte hawmsawn dingin lawpna nanei kha hiam? Ahih keilehzong, tua no’ kikhawlhna pen a sawt the kiamsuksuk mai hiam? Hi thei ahih manin a hunzui in sittel kinken in. Maantak in cileng thahat nawn vetlo hi. Laisiangtho in “lumlel” a cihbang nahi khadiam? (Mang. 3: 16).

Lungsim gilkial taktak leh lawptak in, Pasian tawh nisim kinaiden, Tankhiapa phatna la nisim nasakloh hangin, vanmite in hong encik gige ahihlam naphawk kha hiam? Pasian tawh kikhawlhna in tu leh a tawntung in na khempeuh hong kheelsak thei hi.

Tukal sungteng Pasian tawh kikhawlhna taktak leh Laisiangtho in ngaihsut hong piakna teng kisin ding hihang. Eima omzia taktak, eimah mahin sittel in Zeisu hong thu piakna bang i zuihkei nakleh; eima tunna peuh panin koimah ah kikhiin zolo ding hihang,
