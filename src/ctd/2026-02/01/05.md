---
title:  Kizom
date:   01/04/2026
---

Inndei tungnung pan kipan lampi tungdongin nungzuite in Zeisu’nung zui dau dau uhhi. Gethesemane ah a paikhawm laitak un, leitung tangthu sungah a kitelcian pen zan in, inndei tungnung ah Zeisu’ kampau nunungte in bangzah thupi cih peuhmah phawk khalo uhhi.

`John 15:1–11 sungah Zeisu in bang hong gen hiam?`

Hih kammal Zeisu teekin a paikhiat tengin Pasian tawh kinaikhawlhna thu gen hi. Tualai ah sawmvei val a gen kikik “kizom” cih pen ngaihsun un. Zeisu tawh kizom cihin Amah tawh kizom in nuntakna ahi hi. Singlamteh a maingat laitak in, Pa tawh a kizopna ciang bek hilo a, eima’ nuntakna a ding mahmah in a phamawh mahmah thukhat ahihna hong pulaak hi.

Zeisu in, lengguipi hi a, eite a hiangte ihi uhhi. Amah tawh i kizom ciangin, eite a hiangte in gah gahthei hihang. Ei leh ei kigah mawkmawk theilo hi. Amah tawh a kizom gige bang kihi napi; ataktak in gah gahlo ihih manin eimah a hiangte khawng keu thei hi hang. Tuabang in i keu leh, huan puahpa in hong aatkhia ding hi. Gah i gah leh gahloh ci lo in hong heuhkhia ding hi.

Tuabang hun laitak in, tona leh natna tampi maingat kha ding hihang. Amah tawh i kizop nakleh, tuabang hunte in sawtpi gahpha sem ding hihang. Gah gahna in nungzuite ihihna hong kipsak hi. Ama’ minthanna dingin gah i gah uhhi. Zeisu tawh kizom cihpen Ama’ thukhamnate kepcinna, angkhualna omlo Ama’ omzia hoih lengkik sakna ahi hi. Zeisu tawh kizopna in lungnopna lianpi hong tun hi.

Zeisu tawh kizom cihpen Ama’ hong sawlna bang sepna tawh Amah dawnthukna ahi hi. “Pasian i it ciangin Ama’ thu piakte a zui ihi hi. Ama’ thupiakte in eite ading in a zuih haksalua zong hilo hi” (1John 5:3). Tuathu hoihtak ngaihsun lecin, Zeisu tawh kizopna pen, Laodicea dinmun ah omna pan donghu zatui ahi hi (Mang. 3:20; John 15: 4). Hih leitung nunna leh tawntung nunna dongin khiatna nei gamtatna neihtheihna ding thusim hi napi, Zeisu’ thupiakte khawng mangngilh kha pahpah hihang.

Zeisu mah in ‘Pa in Kei hong it zahmah tawh note kong itzaw ahih manin; Keima itna sungah om un’ (John 15: 9). Zeisu hong itna in Ama’ lamah hong kai thei itna khaugui a hi hi. Tua itna i theih ciangin, Pasian tungah leh midangte tungah itna hong nei sakthei hi.
