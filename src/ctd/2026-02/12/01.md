### Tukalsung Simding
Mate 28:18–20; 2 Pet. 3:18; 1 Pet. 3:8–15; Hosea 7; Zechariah 10.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “A lunggimte ka thahatsak theihna dingin Topa Pasian in ka gen ding thu hong hilhkhin hi. Ama’ hong hilhna te a ngai dingin zingsang sim in lawpna hong guan hi” (Isaiah 50: 4).

Sabbath zingsang khat Pastor G. buailua mahmah hi. Zing thobaih, Sabbath lesson leh sermon a ding kiging keei hi. Nitaklam in zong thu tangkona khat makaih lailai hi. Mawtaw tawh manphei in kongpi pan taikhia zangzang in, hat liang hi.

Khuapi sung mawtaw phuul mahmah ahih manin, tua Sabbath zingsang in biakinn a tuntheihna ding mi tampi in buaisak ahih manin zekai thei phial ding hi. Hiteng koi lam teng pai zuaizuai ding aitam maw cihi. Tua laitak in koilam pan cih theihkhak loh mawtaw khat a mai ah hong tumvat hi. Brake tuansuk vat in patau leh heh in khut-tum bang hong zial liangin, mawtaw hawlpa tawngphei pah hi.

Tuabang in, Pastor G biakinn tungthei ta hi. Lesson hilh ding hong din ciangin, a muhngeisa maitang khat tungah a mi thong tu mahmah hi: a beisa minute 20 lai khawng a hehin a totgawp mawtaw hawlpa ahi hi.

Tua khit pawlpimi khatin, mi kipulakna ah tua mawtaw hawlpa pen Adventist ahi lo tua pawlpi a tanaute kiang honghawh pak cihi. Pastor G. in, meltheih leh mikhual tung ah tatlehhek peuhmah; Pasian tawh kizopna tungtawn in hong lungsuk itna tui sungah kawtsak ding cih hong phawksaan hi.

Thu um nangma tatlehhekna in midangte huzaapzo cih theikha ngeilo ding hiteh.
