---
title:  Hong Leetkhia
date:   14/06/2026
---

`Mate 28:18–20 sunga a Lian Thupiakna simin. Zeisu’ thupuakna sungah “khempeuh acih leh tawntung” (Greekpau in pas hikhawm) cih tegel a kibatlohna gelhsuk dih in.`

Zeisu in leitung ah puakloh phamawh Ama’ thupuak hongpia hi: “Pai unla nung zui suaksak un” cihi. SDA pawlpi’ vaipuakpi pen eima’ nungzui zong ding a, tuate in zong amau’ nungzui ding mah a zongte hileuleu ding hi. Tuabang in tawntung thumaan leh vanmi thumte thupuak tawh Zeisu’ hong paikik na’ng leitung kiging dingin tangko ding hihang (Mang. 14: 6-12).

Christ sungah nuntakna thak a ngahsa peuhmah teci pang dingin hong kisam hi. Mite in teci panpen amau’ hihtheihloh dan in ngaihsun uh a, zong pang nuamlo uhhi. Lampi kiukhat khawngah nangmah Laisiangtho hilhcian dingin kingaihsun in; “Kei hilo, Ke’n hihtheilo, Keimawh keilehkei omthei bek. Teci pan lel khawng kei’n nopsak nam hi lo” ci in lupei keei dingin kituat in.

Citateu zongin. Teci panna taktak pen, nangma nunsung a Pasian hong hihsak te mitmuh ngiat, phutkhak ngiatte; Ama’ sunga na khantohtoh kawmin midangte zong na hawmsawn theih ding thute ahi hi. Pasian hoih mahmah a, Ama’ hong hihsaksa te zong leitung mite zak dingin thuthang hoihpen ahi hi. Gamdai in ki omtheilo ding hi. Amah’n nang hong tankhia khin hiven; Ama’ aa nahih manin naming in hong samta hi. Koikoi ah om mikhat peuh a dingin hisang thuhoih zaw bang omthei lai ding hiam?

Pawlpi masalai a nungzuite pen mipil misaim zong ahih kholloh uh hang, amau’ kiangpan in pilna tampi sinthei ding hihang.

`Sawl. 1:8 leh Sawl. 4:13 simin. Pawlpi masate a dingin tecipanna in bang ahi hiam? Peter leh John te thugenna a za mite tungah bang huzaap om hiam?`

Peter leh John te in a tangkona vuah, “Ka muhsa uhleh kazaksa thute genlo in ka omthei kei ding uhhi” (Sawl. 4:20) ci uhhi. “Amaute Zeisu tawh kikhawl khin” (Sawl. 4:13) ahih manun hawmsawn lo in omtheilo uhhi. Kha Siangtho in zong hangsanna leh amau kammal sungah gualzawhna vanglian pia hi.

Na teci panna ding mi kiangah, hangsanna leh gensiam theihna pilna Pasian makaihna ngen in. 1 John 4:7–11 sim inla, tuabang dan itna te ngen in.
