---
title:  Zeisu Hawmsawn na’ng Ngaihdante
date:   16/06/2026
---

I mimalsim a ding dotna khat pen: Laito pan maw, store sai lai-aat tawh, nisim in na muhmuh zel mikhat tawh ahi zongin Zeisu na hawmsawn ngei hiam? Thu um mimal simin hih nasep sungah kihel dingin Pasian in hong sam den hi. Ama’ hong kamciamna in “A lunggim mite ka thahatsak theihna dingin, Topa Pasian in ka gen ding thu hong hilh khin hi” (Isa. 50: 4). Christian in ama’ upna leh lam-etna te (1 Pet. 3: 15) hong dong te tungah dawngsiam dingin kisaden ding hi.

`1 Peter 3:8–15 simin. Hihmun in Pasian’ Thu bangci gen hiam?`

Midang tungah Zeisu hawpsawn theihna dingin phawkhuai ngaihdan pawlkhat:

1. Mikhat peuhpeuh theihsawm inla lawmbawl pah in. Nangma’ itna, hoihna leh donna diktak te in Pasian’ lamah amaute kaai uhhi. (Pawlbawlna tawh Pasian’na sepna).
2. Tua mi’ lungsim sungah Kha Siangtho nasepna dingin thungen in. Amaute tawh hoihtak holim theih hun ngahna ding zong thungen in.
3. Nangma’ upna na pulak theihna ding lampi olno khat zonglel inla amaute thunget sak in. Hangsan tak leh lungnem takin na vanaih theihna’ng Pasian kiangah ngen in.
4. Tua na lawmthak leh pawlpi lamte kimuh theihna’ng vaihawm inla, na pawlpi ki uapna ciamkha sak masa in. Kikhawlhna leh Laisiangtho sinkhawpna tawh kalsuanpih in.
5. Na lawmthak kisapna tangtawh in thungetsak inla, Laisiangtho pan hamphatna, hehnepna, ngsihsut piakna leh makaihna te hilhcian in. Laisiangtho kamciam khatpeuh hawmsawn masa aihke’h dotna dawngsak lecin, ama’ lungsim hong kihong khathei ding. Amau zong thungetsak in.
6. Tua nalawm in kalkhat suanbeh nuam khathei ding hi (Laisiangtho kisinna, kitui phumna cihbang). Hiteng ah kalsuan nawhlua ke’nla, uaikaai lua ke’n. Thungen in.
7. Eima gamtatna in kua ihih pulak ding hi. Mite tungah ihihdan te in a aw ging hi. Eima’ omzia te pen Ama’ omzia tawh kibang peuhleh (siangthona) mikhempeuh ama’ kianglam zuatpihthei ding hihang.·
