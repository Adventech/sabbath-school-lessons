---
title:  A Lampial Tano khat
date:   17/06/2026
---

Innkuan sungpan in khalam ah hoihtak in aki makaih tano khat – khalam thahat innkuan pan hong khangto napi – Topa tawh kikhawlhna pan lampial ding teelzaw cih bang tampi omkhin zo hi.

`Ephraim in, Pasian’ teeltuam minam hinapi, Topa’ kiangpan lampial hi. Hosea 4:17 leh Hosea 7 in Ephraim’ mawhna bangteng gen hiam?`

Tua banah, Ephraim in Topa tawh kikhawlhna panin lampial ahih manin, a pi Rachel kap ci in gentehna khat om hi (Jer. 31: 15). Topa in ama’ kahtapna teng a dawn kikna zong Jer. 31: 16, 17 ah “Kap nawnkei unla, na khitui uh nul un; Topa in kong cih in ah, na nasep thaman uh nangah uhhi. Amaute galte gampanin hong ciahkik ding uhhi. Na mailam a ding uh in lam-etna a om hi. Na tate uh zong na gam mah uhah hong ciahkik ding hi” cihi.

`A lampial a tano a ding akah sangin, lam-etna nei dingin Rachel kisawl zaw hi. Hihmun in bangteng hong hilhbeh nacilai hiam? Jer. 31: 18, 19 simin.`

Hih tangthu te tungtawn in (Ephraim leh Gomer mahbang in) Pasian in hong khah suah ngeilo ahih manin lam-etna om den cih i thei uhhi. A lampial mite Pasian in a hun hunin taiden hinapi, Pasian’ hehpihna in tawp ngeilo a, Ama’ thupuak zong hiteng mahah kizomto suaklai hi (Jer. 31: 20).

Tanau te khawngin Pasian tawh kikhawlhna nusia uh ahih manin, lungsim natna, lungmanna, lungkiatna, kam hoihlo paukhakna cihbang omthei khaphial ding hi. Ahih hang Pasian in lampial a tano mangngilh ngei vetlo cih hong theisak hi. Tuabang mite a ding Pasian’ ngiimna pen tawmveino sung a ding hilo; lungsim takpi tawh a geelsak ahi hi. Pasian in tuabang mite ngai ngeungau cihi. Ama’ hong hehpihna lian ahih manin Ama’ kianglam zuan ding in deih hi.

`Ephraim’ lampialna hangin, Rachel tamlawhna teng Pasian’ dawnthukna cih theihna in; nangmah zong Pasian kiangpan in lampial nahihna koibang in hong theisak naci hiam? Hihthu in nangmah hong to maw, hong thapia zaw hiam?`
