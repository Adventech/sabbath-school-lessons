“Koibang peuh in ki pulak taleh, angkhual hetlo in a sanggampa a itkei ngal leh, Pasian tungah zong itna siangtho neilo ding hi. Midang itsawmna ciang lelbek tawh tua bang lungsim kineithei ngeilo ding hi. A kisampha penin lungsim sungah Christ’ itna neih ding ahi hi. Eima’ pumpi Christ sungah phum ciangin, itna hong phulkhia ziahziah ding hi” —Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 384.

“Christ kiangah kha tampi tutna dingin thumaan takleh citak takin nasemte pen, khalam leh ki-aapna lamah a khangto pente ahi hi”—Ellen G. White, Evangelism, p. 356.

“Gitlohna nanzawhna ding thahatna pen nasia takin hahkat in nasepna bek tawh ahi hi” —Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, p. 105.

“Ama’ nopsakna – Christ hong kipiakna tawh a tatkhiat khate muhna ah nopsak na sungah luttheihna dingin; tatkhiatna dingin Ama’ nasepna sungah kihel in sem ding hihang” —Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 142.

“Christ tawh nasepna sungah kihel ding anial mite in, Ama’ minthanna sungah kihel theihna ding a nialmi ahi uhhi” —Ellen G. White, Education, p. 264.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Teci panna a muibutna dingin, banghang it itna in a bulpi hithei hiam?`

`Kha hotkhiatna pen, lawp takin Pasian tawh kikhawlhna a hihlam cik in na theikha hiam?`

`Midang tungah Pasian hawpsawn na dingin bulphuh ding pilna cihbang a om hiam? A omleh bang ahi diam?`

`Thu umlo khat Laisiangtho nahilh ding ciangin koipan na kipan hiam? Zeisu a theih theihna dingin bang upna thugui na bulphuh masa pen hiam?`

`Tedim labu 113 na “Gupkhiat” cih phatna la sa in a lamal ngai in. Nangma tangko dan lungngai kik in.`
