---
title:  Lungtang Dinmun
date:   23/04/2026
---

`Pasian’ kammal hong thuhilhna bang saantheihna pen (Job 22: 22) Laisiang tho lam zuan i pai laitak lungtang dinmun tungah kinga hi. 1Cor. 2: 14 in koibang in gencian hiam?`

Khalam thu theihna neihna cihpen khalam khuamuhna tel thuthiehna ahi hi. Kha’ lungsim kihongte in Laisiangtho a simnopna tha uhleh; Khalam mah lungsim kikhakbit te Laisiangtho simnopna tha hong kibang hetlo ding hi. Laisiangtho khawng haivai a saluate in Laisiangtho laimai tunga thumaan zong hetlo ding hi.

`Tua hi a, Laisiangtho tungah i lungsim puak leh i simnopna tha in; Pasian tawh kikhawlhna a khantohna dingin thupi hi. Paul in 1 Thess. 2:13 sungah koibang in gentel hiam?`

Pasian’ Thu i up ciangin eisung ah na hongsem hi. Laisiangthobu nahon ciangin, tua laimaite ah Pasian in nang tungah hong gennop khat om hi cih na upleh, Amah’n hong hopih takpi ding a nangma nunna sungah na hong sem ding hi. Cihtei hang tuate in nangma’ upna leh lam-etna tungah kinga uhhi. Lungdamthu khat panin, na upna a neu a, ankamtang (Luka 17: 6) ciahbek a phakphial hang, Pasian in khangsak thei veve hi (Maku 9: 24).

Laisiangtho’ deihna lianpi khat in, eite leh Pasian kikhawlhna dinmun hong tha hatsak theihna ding in eite hong kihopihna ahi hi. Khasiangtho a ding na lungsim kong hong in, kiniamkhiatna tawh Thu zuan lecin, nisim mahin kuama theihloh kalin kikheelna neizel in khantohna mah hiding hi. Ahihhang awlmawh mello khawngin mawhna peuh kemkem in kikheelnuam keileng Laisiangtho simna in, ei a dingin hoihna tawmcik bek mah hong piangsak thei ding hi.

Khasiangtho in Zeisu Christ kiang naih dingin hong hanthawn den hi. A kinaizaw dingin i kalsuan nuam hiam? Tua cihleng “hotkhiatna ngah theihna ding pilna” (2 Tim. 3: 15) nei ding hang a, muh ngeiloh nate zong mu ding hihang.

`Laisiangtho lam ka zuat laitak in ka lungtang leh lungsim dinmun in koibang hiamm? Laisiangtho mungtupte khawng keima ngaihdan peuh tawh zuun leltak ka hiam? Pasian in keimah hong thuhilhnop teng naupang bangin lungsim kihong in ka zuan hiam? Tua dawnna bangzah in thupi hiam?`
