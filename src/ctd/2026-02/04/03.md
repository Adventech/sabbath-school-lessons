---
title:  "Laisiangtho: A Thuneihna"
date:   20/04/2026
---

`Laisiangtho’ thuneihna leh a nasep bulpi pen a laimai khempeuh tungah ki pulak hi. 2 Tim. 3:15-17 sim inla tei in. Laisiangtho’ nasep bulpi teng gelh in.`

Mimal in Laisiangtho i sim ciangin, Pasian deihna tawh a kizawitawn mello eima’ ngiimna lam-etna khatpeuh picin sakna in zat khakloh ding kidawm ni. Gentehna pak in “mitsi in munkhat kawk” cihbang lel zatloh ding hi. Pasian in Ama’ kammal tungtawn in tuabang dan in kizop ding a deihna omlo hi. Pasian pen, khau tawh akikhai milimneu, deih bangbang in laamsak theih ding hilo hi. Ama’ lampi leh ngaihsutna te in eite aa sang in sangzaw tham (Isa. 55: 9) ahih manin, eite’n Ama’ kammal khawng uk mawkmawk ding hilo hi.

Eima deihna bangin hong lungnuam sak khawk khatpeuh simpak ding zong hilo hi. A baihtata leh ei tawh a kituakteng peuh simlel in; hong kitona peuhmah nutsiat ding hilo, Laisiangtho buppi pen pumkhat in saan ding ahi hi. Pasian in eima nunna sungah hong pau ding i deihleh, Laisiangtho buppi pumkhat in sangin, lammaan tawh sim in, ei’ theih zawhloh teng Pasian in hong lahkhia ding cih muang ding hihang.

Zeisu ngiat in zong, “Na Topa Pasian na lungsim khempeuh, na nuntakna khem-peuh leh na pilna khempeuh tawh na it ding hi” (Mate 22: 37) cihi. Pasian in, i lungsim koihdak ding deihlo hi. Azai mahmah amuibun Ama’ pilna theihna tepen Ama’ Thu sung ah hong pulaak ahih manin, tua thute hong hilhnuam hi. Zeisu in mitampi taktawh ana kiho mahbang in; Pasian in zong Enoch, Abraham, Moses leh Job cihte tawh a kiholimna Laisiangtho sungah tampi mah simcha hihang. Pasian in mihing peuhmah hong koihdak hetlo in, Ama’ Thu sungah ki-aap ding; eima’ hotkhiat na ding ciat “semkhia” dingin hong cial hi.

Mihing’ lungsim pen mihing mah hiphot ihih manin khialhtheihna leh khemna kihel lai hi. Tuukmawh ngeilo hi. Eima mimal a ding peuhin Pasian i nawtnawt zong pen, mihing dinmun hiphot cihphot ding hitaleh; eimah Pasian’ tungsiah khawng ah leh Amah tawh kikim in omna higige hi.

Mihing in Laisiangtho i vanaih ciangin, tungnun nopna lungsim tawh, zakzaksa vive hi e, thuthak omkei e cih ding hilo hi. Eimah i kimuan laitak, kicing i kisak laitak, Pasian tawh kizopliang kulkei e ci in Pasian tawh kizop ding awlmawhloh laitak, eima’ pilna peuh tawh a hilolo teng peuh ngaihsut laitak in; Laisiangtho vanaih ding kisampha penpen zaw hi.
