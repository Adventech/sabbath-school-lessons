### Tukalsung Simding
Kahla. 3:22, 23; 2 Tim. 3:15–17; John 17:17; Eph. 1:13; Late 119:11; 1 Cor. 2:14.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Pasian thu nungta in a gamtang hi a, langnih a hiam namsau sangin hiamzawlai hi. Kha leh lungsim, guh leh tang kituahna, guh leh guhngek dongin khenkhia thei hi. Mihingte’ lungsim ngiimna leh a ngaihsutnate zong theithei hi” (Hebrews 4:12).

Laisiangtho bu, bukhat bek hilo bu tampi zong neikha niteh. Khang tawntung in hih laibu manpha kikhen kikkik, kizuak sim in kikhaam zel hi. Kam nam khempeuh tawh a kumlui pen leh a kikhawngpen laibu ahi hi. Laisiangtho bu a suahtak na’ng in a sihlawh zong om uhhi.

Na Laisiangtho bu in nangma nuntakna sungah koi ah om hiam? Simzel maw ahih keileh na lupna gei ah koih dak maw, laibu tau tungah vuikai sakmaw? Pasian’ kammal sim manlo liangin na nuntakna manlah mahmah maw? Laisiangthobu hon ding khawng thadah kei maw?

Pasian’ kammal in nungta in vanglian hi. Pasian in, nang hong hanthawn ding, hong to in hong kheel ding, hong makaih in lam-etna hong pia dingin nang hong sam gige hi.

Laisiangthobu cihpen, sang laibu bek thamlo, tangthu kigawmkhawpna peuh hilo hi. Piangsakpa in, Amah tawh kinaih theihna dingin ei hong sapna tangthu ciaptehna ahi hi. Pasian tawh nakikhawlhna na khantoh saknop leh, a hoihpenpen in; thungetna tawh Ama’ kammal sim in Ama’ hong hilhna bang ki pum-aapna in hun zatna ahi hi.
