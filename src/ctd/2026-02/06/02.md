---
title:  A Citak Daniel
date:   03/05/2026
---

Daniel zong Laisiangtho sungah galhang khat ahi hi. Ama’ tangthu masa Daniel 1 sungah, “Daniel in kumpi inn sunga an leh lenggahzu tawh a pumpi a thaanghuai sakloh na dingin a lungsim a khensat hi” (Daniel 1: 8). Daniel leh a lawm thumte tungah zong “Pasian in, lai theihsiamna leh pilna apia hi. Daniel pen mangmuhna leh mangkhiat siam na apia hi” (Daniel 1: 17).

Laisiangtho in Daniel pen pil hi (Dan. 1: 20; 2: 14, 21, 23, 48) ci a, Pasian Kha Siangtho in ama’ sungah om (Dan. 4: 9, 18; Dan. 5: 14; Dan. 6: 3) a, vantung in amah it mahmah hi (Dan. 9: 23, Dan. 10: 11). Hitengpen, Pasian tawh kizopna khauh anei mipa khat a kipulaakna ahi hi.

Daniel 2 sunga, Kumpipa Nebuchadnezzar in, Babylon mipil khempeuh sihdan a piak ciangin, Daniel in tua ipcip mang a dingin Pasian’ hehpihna zong hi (Dan. 2: 18). Pasian in kumpipa ‘ mang Daniel kiangah a pulaak phet leh, amah’n thakhat in thungen pah hi.

`Daniel 2:20–23 simin. Banghang in Daniel thungen a, hih thungetna pan bang pilna peuh kingah thei hiam?`

Hunte bei in kumpite kahto in pukkik uh a, Daniel ahihleh kumpite tungah ngaih- sutna piapa in kip paisuak a, “a lungsim pil mahmah ahih manin; kumpipa in zong gam buppi tungah koih dingin ngaihsun hi” (Daniel 6: 3). “Daniel thuman citak in, mawhna leh khialhna khat peuhpeuh ama’ sungah ki mulo hi” (Daniel 6: 4). A naseppih te in hazatna tawh thaang siah zel hinapi (Daniel 6: 5-9), Daniel in ama’ thungetna mahtawh lauhetlo in om hithiat lel hi.

`Daniel 6:10, 11 simin.Hiteng ah Daniel’ thu bang gen hiam?`

Haksatna a thuak ciangin Daniel thungen hi. Lauhuaina in ama’ nuntakna lauh-thawn hinapi, amah ahih leh (Jerusalem lamnga in tawlet hongin nikhat thumvei thungen den in) om hithiat hi. Ama’ thungetna sungah thatang kipiakna in khukdin a, lungdamna leh hehpihna zonna bulphuh hi.

`Hibang thute i et ciangin, thunget theihlohna dingin nangma’ kamzonna maimai te in bangzah khebai bang hithei naci hiam?`
