### Tukalsung Simding
Dan. 2:20–23; Dan. 6:10, 11; Sawl. 20:36; Pian. 5:22–24; Pai. 33:15–23; Pai. 32:31, 32.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Topa ka it hi. Banghanghiam cihleh, Amah in ka aw hong za in ka thumnate hong ngai hi. Amah ka sap sialin ka sapna aw hong ngai hi” (Late 116: 1, 2).

Na lawm hoihpeh ahi a, nazi/pasal tawh tam kiho khollo dingin kingaihsun in. A sawtlo in na kikhawlhna uh kitatsat ding a, buaina peuh omthei ding hi. Pasian tawh kikhawlhna ah zong thungetna in mun thupi khat panin mapang hi. Ki-aapna ngeina khat hi a, mimalsim hong thahatsak dingin i kisap khat zong ahi hi. Thu ngenlopi khawngin sawtveipi khawng i omkhak leh Topa kiangpan gammang ding hihang.

Laisiangtho sungah, thungetdan tuamtuam tawh thungen mimal tampi om hi. Tua te in Pasian tawh kikhawl uh ahih manun, Pasian leh amau’ kikhawlhna, amaute’ thunget na, tua amaute’ thungetna in zong midangte nundan kikheelna piangsak dingin huzaap nei cih nung leh-et leng kimu hi. Eima’ thungetna in zong eimah bek hilo, midangte zong huzaap thei hi.

Laisiangtho simna mahbang in, hih thungetna thulu zong lian in thupi a, kaalnih sungbek in muhzawh zah sangin zaizaw tham hi. Tukal sungteng in, Laisiangtho sunga thungen mite leh thungetna pen Pasian tawh kikhawlhna ah thubulpi ahihna kisin ding hihang. Amau’ hong limlahna panin thu kisin ni.
