---
title:  Moses in Minam khat Lailutsak
date:   07/05/2026
---

`Paikhiat. 32:31, 32 simin. Moses leh thungetna koibang in hong hilh hiam?`

Moses in hangsan takin Pasian’ mite a dingin lailutsak zelzel hi. Mipite a dangtak uh ciangun Moses in (Pai. 15: 25; 17: 2-6), mipite a gilkial ciangun (Gam. 11: 21, 22) lunggulhluatna aneih uh ciangun (Gam. 11: 11-15) Pasian kiang lutsak hi.

Pasian tawh thuciamna bawlkhin phetlian in, Israel in kham-bawngno lim hong bawl takuh ciangun, Moses in thu-nung khat pulakkik in, “Note’ tungah Topa’ hehna leh a lungkim hetlohna note susia dingin a kithawina hangin kalua hi. Ahihhang in tuahun in zong Topa in kathu hong ngai hi” (Thkna. 9: 19).

Thukante in Kamciam Gampan hong kilehkik ciangin, Moses in thu-nung khat mah pulaak kikin “Topa in note susia ding ci ahih manin, sun sawmli leh zan sawmlii sung Topa’ maiah bawkkhup in ka om hi” (Thkna. 9: 25) aci hi.

Levi mite pen biakinn sungah nasem dingin aki koihtuam ciangin, Moses mahin tua thu-nung mah pulaak kik in “Amasa a mahbang in, mualtungah sun sawmli leh zan sawmli sung ka om a, Topa in tua hun in zong kathu hong ngai a, Topa in note hong susia nawnlo hi” (Thkna. 10: 10) cihi. Pasian in Moses’ thuumna za hi.

Moses’ nundan panin thungetna tawh Pasian kiplet ding dan i kisinkha uhhi.

1. Moses in itna thuk tawh Pasian it in, Ama’ omzia lim pulaak hi. Pasian ngiatin Moses kiangah a kipulakna Pai. 34: 6 “Topa, Topa, hehpihna nei in adik Pasian; lung- duai in itna kip leh thutak tawh a dim” cihi.
2. Moses in Kamciam Gam lamah Pasian tawh tonsuk tontohna sungah hangsan citak hi. Eimah mahbang in, haksatna a phuut ciangin, ama’ nundan makaihna sungah Pasian’ vangliatna, ompihna muang ngiat hi (Pai. 33:13).
3. Moses in Pasian Ama’ thuciamna phawkkik sak a (Pai. 32: 13), Ama’ mite tungah Ama’ kamciamte pulaak phakik (Thkna. 7: 8) a, a beisa a Pasian makaihna te phawkkik sakzel hi (Thkna. 8: 2).
4. Moses in ama’ thungetna Pasian’ dawnna yes ahi a, no ahi zongin saang hi. Pasian tawh kinaikhawlh mahmahna in, lungdeih teng liibang hong cingsak pahpah lo a (Thkna. 3: 23-29), thungen tawntung ding cizaw hi (Luka 18: 1-8).

`Tu mahmah in nangma’ lailut thunget sakna kua in kisampha hiam? Tu mahmah in thunget sakding bangin hong daal hiam?`
