---
title:  Moses, Pasian Teelkhiat Makaipa
date:   06/05/2026
---

Enoch in Pasian tawh kinaikhawlh mahmah citateu zongin, Pasian leh Moses’ kikhawlhna kitam muhzaw a, a kihona uh bang kitam ciapteh mahmah zawsop hi. Hih a kiniamkhiat mahmah makaipa Moses khualzinna zuisuk zuito leng, Pasian’ teeltuampa a hih mahbang in a gualzawhna thusim pen, a hunhun in Pasian tawh kihosimna tawh a kikhawlh tawntungna kimu thei hi.

`Paikhiatna 33:15–23 simin. Moses leh Topa koibang in kiho a bangteng kiho uh hiam?`

Pasian tawh kihokha in Ama’ aw kitel takin za ding bangin kingaihsun in. Israel mite in Pasian tawh tuabang in kiho ding lampi zonglo zaw in, Moses tungtawn in kiho zawk ding thuum zawsop uhhi (Pai. 20: 18-21). A tua ding mahin lah Pasian in Moses pen tua mualtung mahah sawlpang sung meikuang lakpan in hopih in kisakhol sak khinzo hi. Moses’ mimal thungetna tampi a kisimkhak teihang, a tamzaw peuhmah pen; ama’ makaih ahi a mipite a dingin makaihna leh mawhna a ding lailut sakna tawh Pasian’ maiah ding kinken ahihna zong muh gige ahi hi.

**Thupiang nih hangin, Moses in innkuante a dingin lailutsak hi. Ama’ lailut sak lai in innkuante un koibang dinmun ah tunguh a, tuabang in leidawhsak kei zenleh bangthu a piangthei tam maw?**

`Aaron : Paikhiatna 32:1–14, 31–34 Thuhilhkikna 9:20`

`Miriam : Gamlakvakna 12:13`

Miriam tawh a thupiang uh ah a lamdang mahmah thu in; Moses mahmah pen Miriam’ simmawhna leh hazatna a thuakngiatpa ahi hi. Amah a nunglam ah ding hithiat lel henla, Pasian in Miriam leh Aaron tungah amau tawh akituak in gaamsak lelleh hithei lel hi. Ahihhang Moses in manlang takin a mawhna maisak zaw in Miriam’ cidamna ding in lailutsak hi. Moses’ gamtatna sungah, Pasian in mawhneite a dingin mawhmai na’ng hehpihna lianpi hong neihna hong kilangsak hi.

`Mate 5:44 leh Colossians 3:13 simin. Hitak ah hong sawl thute na koici hih thei ding hiam? Na hihna in banghang in thupi ding hia?`
