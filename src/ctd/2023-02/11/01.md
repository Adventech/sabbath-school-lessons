### Tukalsung Simding Mang. 14:12, Eph. 2:8–10, Mate27:45–50; Mang. 13:15–17; 14:4, Luka 5:18–26.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Tuaciang in a nungta Pasian’ ciamtehna a tawi vantungmi khat nisuahna lampan hong paito kamu hi. Leitung leh tuipi suksiat theihna ding vangliatna a kipia vantungmi lite kiangah amah in, nakipitak in gen a, ‘I Pasian nasemte a taltang tungah ciaptehna ibawlkhit matengin, leitung tuipi leh singkungte susia naikei in’ aci hi.” (Mang. 7:2, 3).

Sapi ciamtehna tawh kisai in hun nunung thupiang dingte i sinciangin, Pasian in koici sep a, khate’ galpa in koici sep cihpen hong kibanglo ding hi. Kidona lianpi a thubulpi taktak pen Christ leh Satan kikalah cihtakna, ukzawhna leh biakpiakna ahi hi. Mangmuhna 13 sungah sapi, Daniel 7 sunga kiineu leh 2Thessalonian 2 sunga “kisiatna’ tapa” cihte khempeuh in, Pasian’ vang liatna buluhna leh langdo biakpiakna piansakna piangsak uhhi. Amau un hihlo phamawh in zawh thawh thutawh hih uh a, khatveivei golhguk leh thaman tawh biakpiakna sawl uhhi.

Tua’ langkhatah, Pasian’ gampen itna bekmah in thuzawh hi. Sapi biakpiak ding sangin, Pasian’ mite in Amah mah biakpiakna sungah a lianpen lungnopna leh a sangpen kipaakna nei uh hi. Amah in amau tungah kipia mahmah ahih manin, amauti in zong Ama tungah ki-aap pha mah mah uhhi. Hun nunung ciangin sapi’ciaptehna ngahna pan hong dalthei ding galmuanna khatbek om a – kuamah’n a suktat zawhloh Zeisu tungah itna in Amah hong lenkipsak hi.
