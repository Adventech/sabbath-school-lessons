title:  Piansakna, Sabbath, leh Hunbeina
date:   17/05/2023
---

Kum tultampi lai in akipan kidona lianpi in, Pasian’ ukzawhna langdona ahi hi. Tua tona in tuni ciang dongmah kiplai hi.

**Mangmuhna 14:7, 9 leh 12 simin. Anuai a munte a tomcing in behlap in.**

`Mangmuhna 14:7 hongsapna`

`Mangmuhna 14:9 hihloh ding sawlna`

`Mangmuhna 14:12 kumite cihna`

Hihmun tengin hun nunung a sialehpha, Satan leh Christ kikal a kidona lian thubulpi pen biakpiakna mah ahi hi. Eite in Piangsakpa maw sapi maw i biakpiak? Piansakna in eite’ upna tawphahpi mah hi a, nisagih Sabbath zong Piancil lai a Piansakna sungmah ah abulphuh hi (Pian. 2:1–3) a, a kiseelzolo a tawntung tua Piansakna limpua ahi hi. Hilhcianna bulpi limpua bulpi ahi hi. Tua sangin a bulzaw ding icihleh Pasian Amah ngiat mah ahi hi.

Tua hi a, nisagih Sabbath i buluh ciangin, Piangsakpa Topa’ukna aana asangpen mah a buluh ihi pah hi. Na khempeuh pampaihsak in a laigil pan alongkha ihi hi. Pasian dinmun mahmah a laksak ihi hi (2Thess. 2:4).

Bangbang hileh nitawp cianga buaipih dingpen Zeisu tungah itna leh cihtakna ahi hi. Ahih hang laisiangtho in tua itna zongpen thukham zuihna tawh kilakkhia (1John 5:3; Mang. 14:12) a, thukham sawmlakpan Sabbath bekmah in adangteng khempeuh’ nungah omin, tuabekmah in Pasian pen Piangsakpa ahihna honglak hi (Pai. 20:8–11). Topa abia mipawl leh sapi abia mipawl khentel theihna ding limpua ahi hi (Mang. 14:11, 12). Sabbath pen nakhempeuh ading in abulpi mah ahih hang, buaina nunungpen in Piangsakpa biakpiakna ahihlam telsiam haksa mahmah hi.  
  
`Mi tampitak in nikhat peuhpeuh Sabbath bangin tangleng zong bangmah kilamdang tuanlolel hi cih upna nei uhhi. Tuathu laisiangtho siksan in koici dawn ding hiam?`
