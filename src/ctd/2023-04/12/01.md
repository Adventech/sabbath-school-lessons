### Tukalsung Simding
Dan. 1:1–12; 6:1–9; Esther 2:1–10, 20; 3:1–15; Esther 4:1–14; 9:1–12.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Keima’ hotkhiatna in leimong dong aphak theihna ding in, minam dangte tungah khuavak a hiding in nang kongpia hi” (Isa. 49:6).

Laisiangtho sungah a lungluthuai penpen thu khat in “ngeina tuamtuam lakah nasepna” hi a, Esther laibu sungah kimuthei hi. Hih laibu tawh kisai tampi mah kigelh khin zo a, tuni in Jew mite tampi takin, Esther 9:26-31 siksan uh in Purim pawi khaam zel uhhi.

Esther leh apano Mordecai in Persian khuapi Susa ah teeng uhhi. Mi tampite Judah gamah ciahkikta uh hinapi un, amaute un saltanna gamsung mahah omsuak uhhi. Thu tuamtuam tawh Esther zong kumpinu hongsuak hi. “Kumpipa in ama’ numei dangte khem peuh sangin limbawl zaw hi. kumpi lukhu amah khusak in, Vashti munah kumpinu suaksak hi” (Esther 2:17).

Tua zong, Esther in laisiangtho tangthu kician khatpeuh a bawlkhak nading deihna ahi hi. Tuabang in, amaute’ munciat pan un, Pasian’ mite in gamdang mite lakah thumaan teci pangthei uhhi.

Tukal sung hun nangah, ngahloh cilo in Esther laibu sim hamtang in aw.
