---
title:  Sihlupmun pan Migitna
date:   08/03/2023
---

**Hih anuai amunte pan sumvai bang thukhun i ngahthei ding diam?**

`1Tim. 6:17`

`2Cor. 4:18`

`Pau. 30:8`

`Thuhilh. 5:10`

Sumin mihing hong ukzo mahmah a, siatna atundong mah mi tampi tak ukcip zo hi. Sum tampi nei khinzo napi in – sum hang mahin gitlohna abawl mite’ thu a za ngeilo omse ding hiam? Tuabang ding hi hetlo hi. Ama’ hong piaksa thuphate khawng samsiatna peuh i suahsak loh nadingin, Pasian’ vangliatna mah tawh zozo ding hihang. 

Sih hun adingin kemcing siam hih nading ngiimkholhna ah, a lauhuai khatpen, “ka sih ciangin ka neihsa teng ka piakkhiat theih nading” ci in neihlehlam kholsim khinkhian uhhi. Nuntak sungin zangtum gawp cih lawmlawm sang hoihzaw (sumhaupa khatin ka siluang vuina’ng sum a omman bekin a nungta kahi hi cihi) a, tuabang lel sangin a hoihzaw in hih buangding hihang.

“Ka muhna ah, mi tampi takin nasepna ding pan ton uh a, asih uh ciangun mizawngte piak ding mangngilh uhin; amau nuntak sungbeek Pasian upna sungah piakkhiat ding kisin hetlo uhhi. Ahih hang, Khazih in hih sih/lupmun pan migitna tawh piakkhiatna hong ngenlo hi. Tua in a nungtalai mite angkhualna vengsak theilo hi. Amau’ neihsate, Pasian nasepna ah piakkhiat ding sangin, amau sih dongmah kem tentan mi zong omhi. Supna hong kizom daudau ding hi. Bank pan sumh ding a, a neihsa peuhmah lampi tuamtuam tawh kisia ding hi. Bawlnop tampi tak om napi, ziakailua uh ahih manun, sumkholna mun hongtun lohna’ng Satan in daltansak pah hi. Pasian kiang aki-apkik maa in mangthang ta a, Satan kipaak mahmah tahi.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 154.

`Thupha i saansa neihlam khempeuh zatzia ding banghang in kidop mahmah kul hiam?`
