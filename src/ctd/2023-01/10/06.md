---
title:  Khalam Gamh
date:   09/03/2023
---

Mihing in mawhneikei zenleh leitung nunna koibang ahizen tam cih ngaihsut haksa a, a kitelcian khat bel: kisaktheihna, deihgawhna, zawnna leh leitung a susia thei peuhmah omlo ding hi. Na i sep khempeuh, a neitaktakpa theitel in, thumanna leh cihtakna tawh sem leng, eima a taktak hi cih apuksa leitungah lahkhiatna ahi hi. Bangzah nasem in na semkei phial zongin, a tawpna ah ciapteh tawntung ding a thupi khat om hi.

`Anuai a munte sim in. Bang thubul khat nei uh a, tua thubul in na khatpeuh zangin na nasep ciangin Pasian in thupha hongpia hi cihna hiveve cih koibang in honglak hiam? (Late 24:1; Heb. 3:4; Late 50:10; Pian. 14:19; Col. 1:15–17).`

Eite pen Ama hong piaksa tengbek akemcing leh azeekkhia ihi a; anei taktak Pasian ahi hi. Amah’n lei nunna, om theihna leh nasep zawhna’ng thahatna hong pia hi. A thupaizia taktak bel Pasian hong piaksa khempeuh i zatkhit ciang, innkuan kivakna kivaakkhit ciangin, a omlaiteng Amah aapkik tading hihang.

“Pasian’ nasepna ah na piakkhiat peuhmah, vantungah sum akholkhol na hi hi. Tua na kholsa khempeuh zong leitung siatna leh supna tawh kipelh a, tawntung hun adingin meet in kip hi. Tua teng khemmpeuh nangma min mahin kiciamteh ngiat hi”—Ellen G. White, Counsels on Stewardship, p. 342.

Eima nuntak sungmah in piakkhiat theihna’ng hunpha tampi om hi.

1. A piakhiate muhtheih pah – biakinn thak, college ah sangnaupang, Pasian’ thu genna’ng sum kingah.
2. Kisapna lianpenpen khat ahihleh nasepna leh asemte a hampha.
3. Nangma sihkhit innkuan sung kitotna omnawnlo.
4. Innkuan ading a ettehhuai Phalna leh midang itna.
5. Phiangsiah tawmzaw pah.
6. Nangmah’n piakding na lunggulhna mun tung taktak (tanaute leh thuneite’n sawk theilo).
7. A piakhiate lungsim in angkhualna pan angkhuallohna ah kikhelta.
8. Vantungah neihsa khol.
