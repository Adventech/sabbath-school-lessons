---
title:  Pasian Innkuanpihte Vaipuakte
date:   04/01/2023
---

Pasian hong piak leitung thupha leh silpiakte hangin lunguam mahmah hihang. “Pasian inn kuanpihte” i hihlam theihna in koibang lungnopna hongpia hiam?

`Thuhilhkik. 6:5 leh Mate 22:37 sim in. Bang a cihnopna hi a, bang icih hiam?`

Pasian “na lungsim khempeuh, na kha khempeuh leh na ngaihsutna khempeuh” tawh koici it na hiam (Mate 22:37)? Laisiangtho hong hilhcianna kicing mahmah napi, eite lamet hikhollo zaw phial hi. 

`Thuhilhkik. 10:12, 13 leh 1John 5:3 simin. Laisiangtho bangin, vantunga i Pa tawh kizopna koibang in dawngkik ihi hiam?`

Thukham zui maw? Thupiak mang maw leh? Christian tampitak ading in thukham zuihna pen (a lina phadiak) khauh salua uh a, Pasian it ding leh vengte eimah ki-it bangin it ding ciangbek hi ci uhhi. Pasian genna kitelcian mahmah lel hi: Pasian leh vengte itna in thukhamte kemcing ihi uhhi.

“Pasian i it ciangin ama’ thupiakte azui ihi hi” (1John 5:3). Hih mun teng isim ciangun, Pasian i it manin a thukham izui hi. Hoihmah hi. Ahihhang, “Ama’ thukham zuihna tawh Pasian itna telcian” ci in zong kisimtheilai hi. 

Mate 7:21-27 ah, Pasian kammal za a, a nuntakpih mite in, suangpi tungah innlampa tawh kibang cihi. Za napi in a nuntakpih lote leuleu, sehnel tungah innlampa hi a, lauhuai mahmah tawh kibang cihi. Zakhawm uh hinapi; khat in nuntakpih, khat in nuntakpih lo citciat hi. Tua in nuntakna leh sihna dong tun hi.

`Pasian it in, A thukham zuihna a kizopzia lungngai in. Banghang in Pasian itna tua tawh kipulak sese hiam? Thukham zuihna in bangtaktak hi a, itna pulak taktak hiam? (Lahkiauna: thukham zuihlohna hangin bangpiang cih ngaihsun in.)`
