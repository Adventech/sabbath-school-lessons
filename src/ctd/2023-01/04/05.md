---
title:  Pasian in Ei Sumpite Awlmawh
date:   25/01/2023
---

`Maku 12:41–44 sim in. I hau a haukei zong in, hih tangthu pan bang thupuak kilathei hiam? Hih thukhun in bang hong hilh a, eima biakpiakna ah koibang in zangthei ding hiam?`

Zeisu leh a nungzuite biakinn huang sung sum donna sabuai geiah hong pai uh a, Amah in sumpi hong paipihte en gige hi. Meigongnu khat in dangkasan tangnih a tawi teutau mukha citciat hi. Tua nu in aneih khempeuh a piakhia hi. “Zeisu in tuanu omzia telpah hi. Amah in biakbuk nasep teng peuhmah Pasian’ deihna bang a kisem cih thei a, ama’ hihzawh ding hisa mello it. Ama’ hihzawhzah ciangbek hih pen, ama’ hihna teng khangtawntung in kiciamteh ding a, tawntung hunah ama’ lungnopna’ng ahi hi. Ama’ lungsim a silpiak tawh tonsak a, a man akiseh ciangin, sumtang tawh hizawlo in, Pasian a itna leh Ama’ nasep lunglut in asepna hizaw hi.”—Ellen G. White, Counsels on Stewardship, p. 175.

A thupitna a kiciatna dangkhat ah, piakkhiatna limlim Zeisu in hihthu bek genngei a – pawlpi tungah piakna pan Amah nialna a piangkhia, hongsapna leh mission nasepna pan a pialkhia pawlpi ahi hi.

`Sawl. 10:1–4 simin. Rome galkapmangpa kiangah, banghangin vantungmi khat vahawh thei hiam? Vantungah a kiciapteh sakliang ama hihna nam nih bangteng hiam? `

Vantung in i thungetna bek hong za hilo a, i piakkhiatzia nangawn hong awlmawh lai hi. Cornelius in cingh takin apiakkhiat ciapteh sak hi. “Na sum omna uhah na lungsim uh om tawntung ding hi” (Mate 6:21). Cornelius in a piakna vazui hi. Zeisu’ thu sinbeh nuamlai hi. Thungetna leh piakkhiatna in kinaizop mahmah a, Pasian leh vengte itna lahna zong hi a, tuagel zong pen – Pasian thukham thubulphuh nihte ahi: “Na Topa Pasian na lungsim khempeuh, na nuntakna khempeuh leh na pilna khempeuh tawh it inla, nangmah na ki-it bangin na vengte it in” (Luka 10:27) a cih tegel kek ahi hi. Amasa pen thungetna hi a, a nihna bel piakkhiatna hi.
