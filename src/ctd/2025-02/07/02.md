---
title:  “Hiah Om ing, Kei Hongsawl in”
date:   11/05/2025
---

Khatvei lai in, pawlpi khatin kikhawpna hall thak khat lam lozaw in alui puah zaw leng ci uhhi. Hall sung a muhnop theihna ding meivak a thak bulh uhhi. Tuabang in meivak thak a bulh vualeh, alui teng hong kidawk lemlam zawsop in a mel hong sia zawsop hi.

Isaiah’ mangmuhna lamdang sungah, Pasian’ tokhom in ama’ mawhna teng nakpi takin hong phawksak hi. “Gimngahpa hita ing, kei in a mawknapi kahi hi. Ka kam in ka gen khempeuh a mawknapi hi a, a thugen khempeuh uh mawhna le nitna ahite lakah kateng kha hi. Vanglian Kumpipa ka mukha khinta hi” (Isa. 6:5) cihi. Pasian’ maiah hong ki paipih vat peuhmah leh eizong tua mahbang hiding hihang. Eima’ paulam khempeuh a hemkhia zo dingin khuavak tanglua zen hi. Pasian’ maiah eite a mangthang kihi lel hi. Ama’ nuntak sungin thu lamdang khatmah hipah hi.

`Isaiah 6:6–8 simin. Isaiah in mawhna cihpen eimah “a mawkna” hong suah cihi. Mawhna thaman ahihleh sihna hinapi, mawhna sungah hong nutsiat ding sangin Pasian in Amah kinaizaw hong kaai hi. Hih kikhawpna pan bang thu piang a, bang hangin thupi thei hiam?`

Vanmi seraph khatin biakna tau tungpan meii-am khat la in Isaiah’ muk bi in ahihleh ama’ mawhna tengpan hong siangtho pah hi. Tuapen Pasian’ mite aki lemtuahna paknamtui tau (Mang. 8:3, 4) hihtuak pen hi. Ama’ mawhna teng kimaisak khin a, tu in Pasian’ maiah ding thei dingin kilawmta hi a – bek thamlo, amah pen leitung ah Pasian aiawh theihna thu ngahta hi.

“Seraph” cih kammal pen “meikuang” cihna ahi hi. John 5:35 sungah Zeisu in Tuiphum John thu a genna ah “Amah in meivak bangin kuang in a taang hi a, ama’ khuavak sungah tawlkhat nuamsa in om ding na ut uhhi” cihi. John mahmah pen mawhnei hotkhiat ngaipa hinapi, ama’ nasepna tengin hehpihna tawh hotkhiatna hong tun theipa kawk hi. Zeisu in a cingtaak Pa’ minthanna teng pulak dingin hongpai hi a – Pasian in zong mawhnei kamsang khat in sawl a, vanmi seraphim te mahbang in nasep thupi a sem hi.

`Isaiah in a mawhna teng ki siansuah cih a theih ciangin, Hiah om ing, kei hong sawl in cihi. Eite mimalkim mawhna teng Zeisu’ sisan in hong siansuah khin ahih manin Isaiah mahbang in koibang in dawngthuk thei ding ihi hiam?`
