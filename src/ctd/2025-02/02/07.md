Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles laimai 578-581 “The Revelation” simin.

Leitung biakna tampi in ngaihdan vive zang uh a; Christian biakna ngaihdan ahih leh, tangthu thupiang tungah kipkho mahmah hi. Laisiangtho pen mihing’ tangthu sungah Pasian leh mihing mapan khopna hi a, tuabang in kum tulsim in mapan khopna pan, Pasian’ omzia kipkho cihthu kitel thei hi.

Khat veivei, Christian te in hih thute zak ding nangawn cimtak liang thei uhhi. Eima kamsang thupuak peuh mipi lakah a kihilh ciangin, i zaksak sa peuh hi e, thuthak zong om sam kei e kici hi.

Eima thupuak in kipkho a kikhel ngeilo cihtei hang, thu neucik aihke’h tona omlo cihna zong hi tuanlo hi. Tua tawh kilehbulh in: Pasian’ thupiakna khat kisin ding ciangin pilna picing Pasian in hong piak ziakai ahih manin, tua thu atawp dongin tun zolo ding cih thei pah niteh.

One of the reasons Ellen G. White in Mangmuhna laibu pen khang tawntung in Christian te belh thei sikkip ahi ding tangthu thupuak in kigelh hi cihi. EGWhite hunlai a “Pasian nasem khangno pawlkhat in hih thumaan teng bekbek a hilh den uh cimtak uhhi. Thuthak a lamdang deuh ahi dingin upna thak liklek khawng hong lutpih sawm uhhi” —The Acts of the Apostles, p. 580. Hihthu tawh kisai in, hihte pen mailam thupiang ding ciangbek hilo in; a piang khinsa tangthu te zong hi ahih manin, a gen masa peuh hihnopna cele nopna peuhtawh lampial lo in, laisiangtho tangthu sungah upna zungkhak ding kisam hi.

**Kikupding Dotnate:**

`Laisiangtho in thuthak leh pilna thak hong piakhial ngei nailo hi . Eima saansa thuman te lenkipsa in thuthak theihnopna koibang in kemcing siam ding ihi hiam?`

`Genkholna te letkhiatna thak a omleh pawlpi in koibang in dawngthuk ding hiam? Sin beh ding tampi omlai cihtei hang, tua khuavak thak pen thu phamawh maw, a siahuai thu laang pak maw cih koibang in khentel thei ding ihi hiam?`

`Galpi nihna lai in Pacific tuipi tung khualzin khat in, a laitah madeuh in, a siavuan pa kiangah “Keipen tagah hipeuh ing. Ka sihkhit ciangin kua’n hong phawk nawn ding hiam?” ci in kiko hi. Siavuanpa in “Ke’n hong phawk tawntung ning” ci in dawng hi. Siavuanpa’ dawnna khum mahmah napi a sawtlo in amah leh a ki phawkkhakna si lel hi. Hih leitung ah nuntakna pen, sihna hong tung peuhleh, koibang khiatna nei mello suakthei ding hiam?`
