---
title:  Dawpkholh Thukhenna
date:   05/06/2025
---

Daniel 7 sungah puandal nunga thukhenna a kimu vat pen—tatsate Pasian tawh kinai takin anungta nuam mite a dingin Pasian in amau’ mawhna a siansuah sakna paizia ahi hi.

`Daniel 7:9, 10, 13, 14, 22, 26, leh 27 sunga dawpkhol thukhenna hong hilhcianna simin. Tua thukhenna sunga thu bulphuh bang hiam? Atawpna ah thu khensatna bang hiam? Hotkhiatna ngiimna tawh kisai bang hong theisak hiam?`

Pasian in mihing thukhen ahihna dot ding tawmlua hi. Thuhilhsia 12:14 in “Na khempeuh Pasian in thukhen ding” cihi. Paul in zong, khatlehkhat thu kikhen lo ding, “Eite khempeuh Christ’ thukhenna maiah ading ding hihang” (Rom. 14:10) cihi. Pasian in gup ngah leh mangthang khawng laikung laidal kisam selo ahihtei hang, vanmite mahmah in zong hotkhiat ngiimna a sittel-phapha lai uh hi a (1Pet. 1:12)– dotnop khatpeuh nei khathei ding uhhi. Satan’ langdona sung mahah vanmi thumkhen khenkhat vanpan ki hawlkhia cih zong teci khatmah hipah a (Mang. 12:4)—tu in zong Pasian in eite Ama’ maiah hong koih hi. Pasian in misiangthote muhtheih dingin laibute hong hi.

Abraham in Sodom leh Gomorrah ading a thumsakna pen—thukhenna limpua—thukhenna sungah a thupi muhna thuk hongpia hi. Sodom’ mawhna teng kitel takin sittel in: Pasian in, tua khuapi pen a siatna lian mahmah ci in tangko liang hi (Pian. 18:20). Aki suksiatma in aki sittel khua pen Sodom leh Gomorrah tegel bek hilo hi. Pasian in migilote a suksiat ding ciangin, thumaan thutang in thukhen ahihna, Abraham in a theihna dingin lampi honsak hi.

Tua laitak lianmah in Daniel 7 sung, vantung thukhenna ah “mihing Tapa” (Dan. 7:13; Mate 20:28) Zeisu hong kilang lian a, Ama’ hong paina pen hih thukhenna in “Sangpenpa’ misiangthote maipha piakna” (Dan. 7:22) cihi. Ama’ dikna picing bekmah in Ama’ mite thukhenna kantan zosak hi.

`A siangtho eima Pasian maiah nangma kel-luphum teng khempeuh tawh dingin ki ngaihsun in. Tua thukhen hun in bang lam-etna khatbek neithei ding nahiam?`
