### Tukalsung Simding

Heb. 9:11–15; Late 122; Late 15; Late 24; Pai. 33:18–23, Late 5; Late 51:7–15.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Tua ciangin ka khuadak leh, Zion Mual tungah Tuuno a ding kamu hi, Amah tawh mi tul za leh sawmli leh tul li ding khawm uh a, tua mite a taltang tungah Tuuno leh a Pa’ min kigelh hi” (Mangmuhna 14:1).

Seventh-day Adventist te in, Mangmuhna sunga limpua teng telcian theihna ding in Laisiangtholui tangthu sungah zong uhhi. Hih tangthu te a bulpi gamla mahmah ahih hang. Laisiangtholui sungah om khin lel hi.

A kicing mahmah bul khat in Late hi a, mihing in Pasian tawh a kikhawlhna bul-phuh in kiphuak la siangtho – kiniamkhiatna pan kipan, Pasian’ ompihna, mawh maisak ding leh hotkhiat ding kamciamte hangin gen zawhloh lungdamna late ahi uhhi.

Late laibu kuhkal takin sim lecin, Mangmuhna laibu, a diakin leitung ah a beibang Pasian’ pawlpi nasep ding teng a pulak, chapter 14 in hong nungta phadeuh ding hi. Hun nunung Pasian’ mite tungah, tanglai Israel te a piak mawhpuak mah kipia a: minam dangte tungah khuavak hiding, mihing khempeuh in hong Piangsakpa biakna pia dingin sapna cihte ahi hi.

Pasian’ labu sung mun dangdang ah a kicing zawtham in telcian theihna dingin lampi thak hongpia ding hi.
