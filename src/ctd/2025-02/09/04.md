---
title:  Ama’ Khe Nuai ah
date:   27/05/2025
---

`Late 47:1–4 simin. Christ Kumpi gam sungah eima om mun koibang ci hiam?`

Hunsau ahihleh mailam taang hi. Hih leitung ah mihing in ama’ ukna pen Lucifer tungah apiak pan kipan, Satan in vantung kikhopna sungah hong kihel pah a, hih leitung in kei aa hi cita hi. Pasian in “koipan a hongpai nahi hiam” aci hi. Satan in zong “leitung khempeuh alu pan ataw dong vak kawikawi in ka phehlehna munpan a hongpai kahi hi” cihi” (Job 1:7).

Satan in a neipa hi ing cita hi; khe pen neih zawhna lahna hisak hi. Pasian in Abraham kiangah “Tho inla, gamdung gamvai kantan in pai in, Nangmah kong pia ding hi” (Pian. 13:17) cihi.

`1Thess. 4:13–17 leh Zechariah 14:4 enkak inla, Christ’ khe encian in. Hih munnih tegel kilamdanna bang om a, Christ in hi leitung ukpipa ahih manin, hih kilamdanna tegel in bangthu hong hilh naci hiam?`

Kul tul maan hun bei ciangin Christ nasep ding, EGWhite in a genna ah: “Christ in Ama’ thawhkik khit van a kahtohna mun Olive mual tung mahah vanmite kamciam omsa bangin hong tuaksuk hi. Kamsangpa in “Topa ka Pasian hongpai ding a, misiang khempeuh hong tonpih ding hi. Ama’ khe in Jerusalem nisuahna lama om Olives mual hong sik ciangin, Olives mual kitam in, zaangkuam lianpi khat suak ding hi. Tua ciangin Topa pen leitung khempeuh’ kumpi suak ding a, tuani ciangin Topa khatbek leh Ama’ min khatbek om ding hi” (Zechariah 14:5, 4, 9).

Tua ciangin Jerusalem thak in zong tee ziazua in vantung pan hong kumsuk a, ama ading Christ in a siansuahna munah hong kinga in, Christ in Amite leh vanmite tawh Khuapi Siangtho sungah lut ding uhhi”—Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, pp. 662, 663.

`Zeisu sungah hongpiak lam-etna en dih un. Na khempeuh sihna sungah tawpgai leh bangzah in nuntakna haksa ding cih ngaihsun un. Na khempeuh amawkna himai lo ding maw?`
