Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages lai. 662–680 “Let Not Your Heart Be Troubled” simin.

“A mite amau leh amau tualniam a kisak uh manin Topa dah hi. Ama’ teel tuamna gamh ngahna dingin man tampi a piaksa ahih manin, amau’ manphatna theisak nuam hi. Pasian’ deihna hi a, hikei peuhmah leh A Tapa sawl liang in, amau tatna dingin tua zah man tampipi piakhia loding hi. Topa minthan sakna dingin amau tungah a kikal amau’ hihtheih zah ahihna tungtawn in Topa lungdam mahmah hi. Topa’ kamciamna sungah upna kip peuhleh na lianpipi lam-et ding om hi.”

“Christ min tawh thungetna vanglian hi. Ama’ gamtatna, Ama’ lungsim leh Ama’ sepnate sang ding hihang. Honpa’ kamciamna pen eima’ omzia tungah kinga hi. ‘Kei nong it uhleh Ka thukhamna zui un’. Topa in mawhna sungmah ah hong hon hilo a, mawhna sungpan in hong honkhia hi a; Amah a itte peuhmah in amau’ itna uh itna mahleh thumanna tawh lahkhia ding uhhi.”

“Thumanna diktak pen lungsim sungpan ahi hi. Christ tawh kop in lungsim nasep khopna hi. Eimah lungkim hipeuh leng, eima ngaihsutna leh ngiimna sungah Amah kilangh sak ding a, eima’ lungsim pen Ama’ lungsim tawh luang khawm sak in; Ama’ thu manna sungah eima’ sepnopna hong omsak ding hi. A kisiansuah eima deihna in, Ama’ na sepna sungah lungkimna sangpen ngah ding hi. Pasian i theihkhak hampha i kisak ciangin, eima’ nuntakna in thuman tawntungna nuntak hiding hi. Christ’ gamtatna manphat sakna leh Pasian tawh kikhawlhna hangin mawhna pen hong huathuai mahmah ta ding hi.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 668.
