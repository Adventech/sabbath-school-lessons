Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 9 lai. 285–288 “The Reward of Earnest Effort” simin.

“Pasian hong vaihawm sakna tawh kisai lungsim hong buaisak khempeuh pen leitung thak ciangin ki telkheh mahmah ding hi. Tel zawhloh thute zong telta ding hihang. Pasian lainatna hehpihna eite mai ah kihong khia ding hi. Mihing pilna lel in lungbuai in kamciam a palsat na munah, a kicing a hoihkim lung kituahna kimu ding hi. Mongneilo itna in a haksapen mun ah nasem cih kimu ding hi. Na khempeuh ah eima’ hoihna dingin Ama’ hong kepcinna i phawk ciangin, genzawhloh in lungdam ding hang a, minthanna tawh kidim ding hihang”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 9, p. 286.

**Kikupding Dotnate**:

`Pasian vaihawmna thei nuamlua in na lungsim a buai ngei hiam? Atawpna ah tuateng khempeuh kilangkhia ding cih na theihna in bangzah hong lungnuam sak hiam?`

`Christ in hih leitung a dingin a si dingin mihing hong suak cihthu ngaihsun in. Hihthu in Pasian itna hong pulakna leh Pasian muanhuai na hong gen hi. bang hihthei lai ding hiam?`

`Pasian’ “min” tawh kisai bang thupi hiam? Christian a kici mite a dingin bang mawhna hong phawksak naci hiam? Christian te in koici bangin Christ’ min khawng daisak thei a, eima kimkot a mite tungah Christ’ nungzui hihna koibang in lakthei ding ihi hiam?`
