---
title:  Pasian Hoih Kicing in Thudik
date:   03/02/2025
---

Pasian in thutang it ding leh itna tawh thutang ding in mi samziau hilo in, Pasian mahmah in kicingtak leh sawi hetlo in tuate hong lak hi. Lasiangtho in Pasian pen avek khawzang sangtho, citak, thudik in itna ahi hi.

`Thuhilhkikna 32:4 leh Late 92:15 simin. Hihmun tegel in Pasian’ Cihtakna leh Thudikna bangteng gen hiam?`

Hite bek thamlo a dang tampi in Pasian thutang in itna ci a – “Ama’ sungah thumaan lohna omlo” (Late 92:15 leh etkak ding Late 25:8; Late 129:4) cihi. Pasian in “thumaan lo in gamta ngeilo a, a thumaan lote in maizum theilo” (Zeph. 3:5) cihi. Pasian in a thumaan a gamtatna leh thumaan a itlote’ gamtatna enkak dih in.

Pasian in mimalsim a ding a hoihpen thei a, mimalkim zong hoihsak nuam in, mi khempeuh kihelkim na tawh a hoihpen suaksak nuam hi.

`Late 9:7, 8 leh Late 145:9–17 simin. Pasian tawh kisai bang gen hiam?`

Laisiangtho Pasian pen “a thutang thukhenpa” (Late 7:11), Ama’ sungah gitlohna omlo hi (Late 5:4). 1John 1:5 in “Pasian in khuavak hi a Ama’ sungah khuamial omlo hi”. Pasian in hoih kicing bek hilo, James 1:13 in “Siatna tawh Pasian ki ze-et theilo” cihi (Hab. 1:13 enkak in). Tua hi a, Pasian’ hoihna leh minthanna zong a kikai-okcip ahi hi. Milim tampi in vanglian teleh Pasian in vanglian kicing a, ahihhang Ama’ vangliatna pen a thutang leh a ithuai te a ding bek ahi hi. Moses in kuama nget ngei hetloh, “Na vangliatna hong lak in” acih ciangin, Pasian in “Keima hoihna khempeuh namai ah kapai sak ding hi” (Pai. 33:18, 19) cihi.

`Hizah ahoih kicing Pasian in banghang in hih leitungah gitlohna omding in phalthei hiam? Kikum un.`
