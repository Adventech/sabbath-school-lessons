### Tukalsung Simding

Late 103:13; Isa. 49:15; Hos. 11:1–9; Mate 23:37; 2Cor. 11:2; 1Cor. 13:4–8.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Nupi khat in ama’ nawi ateep naungek mangngilh thei ding ahi hiam? Mangngilh kha thei mah ding hi; Ahi zongin Keimah in note kong mangngilh kei ding hi” (Isa. 49:15).

Lungsim phawktheihna pen pelhhuai hetlo kisaciat hi. Pawlkhat in tua phawktheihna pen pianpih zong hi khollo ahih manin, mi khat hoihna pen tua phawktheihna tawh khen ziau ding zong hituanlo a ci om uhhi. Tanglai Greek thupilna sungah “theih theihna” pen khasiatna lamah tung pahpah lo a, ama’ phawktheihna khawng a thulian lualo-te ah a kemcing zo tepen etteh huai ci uhhi.

Phawktheihna kamkaih kei leng buaina hong tun thei hi. Pasian in mihing hong bawl ciangin phawktheihna hong guan a, Pasian mahmah zong Laisiangtho sungah tua phawktheihna mah nei in phawk baih mahmah zaw hi. Laisiangtho in Pasian in zong tua phawktheihna thukpi mah nei ci ahih manin, a sia pianpih hetlo ding hi – Pasian pen hoihna picing leh pilna picing tawh kidim Laisiangtho in cihi.

A thuk mahmah phawktheihna tawh a kidim Pasian hong itna panin kaihkhop theih ding thumaan thu hoih tampi om hinapi; a kidophuai thu khat pen, Pasian hong itna in a picing itna ahih manin mihing in tua bang linlian neithei lo ding cih thu ahi hi.
