---
title:  Ciangtante leh Thukhunte
date:   06/03/2025
---

Vantung leitung kinialna pipen Pasian’ gamtatzia, Pasian’ hoihna, thutangna leh a ukzawhna tungah dawimangpa in a mawhmatna teng ahi hi. Vantung leh leitung kamciam thukham thu ahi hi. Vantung leitung a ding ki ukna thukhun ahi hi. Hibang thusia peuhmah pen vangliatna tawh kizo theilo a, sepkhiatsak phot kul hi.

Mi khat’ vangliatna tungah mawhmatna khat peuh a om ciangin, thu veng sakna dingin, hong mawhmat mite suakta tak leh thutang takin semkhia sak ding a, mipi muh in thu dawp ding hihang. Tua mawhsakna in ki ukna (itna tawh) lokzo phial ahihleh, amah ninbung sungah ki mawkpaih theilo hi.

Kidona lianpi telcianna in gitlohna buaina telcianna tawh koibang in kizom hiam? Pasian in kamciam apiak leh a palsat diam? Tuabang hilo hi. Pasian in a nasepna sungah thukhun om ding a thukim mahbang in, Ama’ mailam nasepna peuh ah ciangtanna neipah ding hi. Tuabang ciangin, khuamial ukna nuai ah tawlkhat sung gitlohna omthei mah ding hi.

`Maku 6:5 leh Maku 9:29 simin. Hih munte in Pasian’ nasepna peuhmah upna leh thungetna tawh ki sawhhel ding cih bangteng tawh hong lak hiam?`

Hihmun tegel in, nasepna peuhmah ciangtan om hamtang ding a, thungetna leh upna tawh tonkhawm ding cihi. Mun khempeuh ah thungetna in thu lamdangsak cih muhsa hi a, Pasian’ nasep theihna ding lampi honsak maw ahih keileh zong kisang zolo maw cih hipah hi. Ahihhang, upna leh thungetna bekmah tawh cih tentan tawh khialhna bawlloh ding thupi hi. I Phawkkhak loh theih ding thu tampi mah omlai hi.

Hipen bel i muhsa ahi nasepna ading thukhun ahi hi. I nasepna ading thukhun te Pasian hong thukimpih a omnakleh, Ama’ mailam nasepna ah ciangtan zang pah ding hi. Tua mahbang in khuamial ukna gam sungah zong gitloh nasepna tawlkhat sungteng ompah ding hi.

`Rom. 8:18 leh Mang. 21:3, 4 simin. Hihmun te tungtawn in, ei theih khakloh thu tampi om a; Pasian in a hoihpen ding thei, a hoihpen zong deih pah a, gitlohna tawpsak in tawntung nopna sung hong tun ding cih bang lungmuanna ki neithei ci hiam?`
