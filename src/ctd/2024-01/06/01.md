### Tukalsung Simding
Late 18:3–18; 41:1–3; Thuhilhkik. 15:7–11, 82; 96:6–10, Late 99:1–4; Rom. 8:34.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Mizawngte aneihsa ki-hawkkhia a, mi cimawhte thum in tau uh ahih ciangin tu in dingin amau lunggulh lungmuanna munah amaute kikoih hi” a cihi. (Late 12:5).

Eite khang pen gitlohna, thumaan lohna, leh nenniamna khang ahi hi. Laphuakte zong tuabang khang hun sungmah ah nungta kha uhhi. Bang hun peuh hi taseleh, Late pen Pasian in, leitung mite nenniam thuakna, laphuakte mahmah in zong thuakkha uhhi.

Topa in lungduai takin thuak kenkon a, Ama’ hehna in zong, kuamah kisiat ding  deihsak lo in, amau lampite khel ding deihsak hi (2Peter 3:9–15). Pasian hong lutsuk hun pen mihingte deih hun tawh a kituak lianlian theihloh hangin, Pasian’ thukhenna ni hongpai teitei ding hi (Late 96:13; 98:9). Amah, Ama’ kamciamte tuani dong i muan ding hiziau bek hi.

Piangsakpa bekmah in a tokhom pen dikna leh thutanna tungah kiphut hi (Late 89:14; 97:2) a, Ama’ thukhenna in leitungah kipkho in khangto hi. Pasian’ thukhenna muhdan namnih pen – nenniamte _Khahkhia_ leh, mi gilote _susia_ cihte ahi hi (Late 7:6-17). Hihpen eite hong kamciamsa hi a, khatvei hong tung teitei ding, nikhat, Pasian’ hun ahi a, ei’ hun hi lo, laphuakpa in cihi.
