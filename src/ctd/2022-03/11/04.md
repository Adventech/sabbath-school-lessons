---
title:  'David: Ngakna’ng Muhtheih Pilna'
date:  06/09/2022
---

1 Samuel 16:1-13 sungah, tangval David pen kumpi dingin Samuel in sathau buak hi. Jerusalem kumpi’ inn pen, a pa’ Jesse tenna lo pan a gamla mahmah Jerusalem kumpi inn atung hi. Khatveivei haksa samahmah thei ding hi.

Amasa in, tangvalpa in, tumging tumna tawh, Saul’ kha niin hawlkhiatsak dingin kisam hi (1Samuel 16). Tua khit teh Goliath a thahtheih manin Israel’ galhangpa hong suak hi (1Sam. 17). Tua khit in David in a nuntakna adingin kum tampi bumang hi. Saul leh Jonathan a pata in David kumpi suakding cih theikhawm uhhi (1Sam. 23:17; 1Sam. 24:20). David in Pasian piaksa a mailamthu peuhmah khuattawm tuanlo lelhi. Tua sangin, lehtatzaw bang hiliang hi. Saul in amah thahding a zonlai in a puanmongtep tawmcik a aatkhiatsak nangawn aatkhiatloh hoihsazaw hi (1Sam. 24:5-7). Saul in thah dingin zongkik leuleu a, tua hunin, amah in Saul thah theih nading hunlem ngahzawsop hinapi nialzaw hi (1Sam. 26:7-11).

`1 Samuel 26:1-11 sim in. Banghang in David in Saul athah ding nial hiam? Pasian in eite a ding a ngiimna hong tangtun nading lampi koibang in hong theisak naci hiam?`

`1 Samuel 26:12-25 simkik in. David in Saul athahding a nialna in, koici bangin Saul sukha zaw hiam? Pasian ngaklelna tawh hamphat zawkna koibang in hong hilh hiam?`

ian in hoih asak hunkhat a hongpiak bang bang pen hoihpen tawntung hi. Sawtpi zong kingak khathei hi. Be-tang in naitawmvei sung in hongpo thei mah taleh, gel-tang in kum tampikhit ciangin pokhia panthei hi. Ahih hangin huihpi nakpi’n nungtaleh gelsing kilongkhia lo ding hi.

`David in Saul thatziau lelleh zong thumaan kicilel peuh ding cih ngaihsun in. (David in kumpi ding hi a, Saul pen gilomahmah a kicimah hilel hi). Cihteei hang ama’ gamtatna in Pasian upna taktak nei cihna ahihi. Ngak tawntung lelding ahoihna hihteng ah nang etteh theih ding bang limhoih na mu hiam?`
