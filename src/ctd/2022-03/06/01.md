### Tukalsung Simding
John 16:5-15; Col. 1:28, 29; 1 Peter 1:13; Mate 5:29; Piancil 32.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Hihbang a tangtun theih nadingin, Christ in kei tungah a hong piak ka thahatna khempeuh zangin, nakpi takin nasem in ka hanciam hi” Colossians 1:29.

Papi khat leh nupi khat tukhawmin kiho uhhi. Naupangno khat a kithahna thu kikum uh hi. Nupinu’ tapa pen tunung kum 20 lai in kithat a, a hehna leh thangpaihna nasialua hi. Papipa’ apen thu tuampi khat hileuleu hi. A sawt nailo deuhin a tanu pen mi ngongtatte in thatlum uhhi. Mithatte ading mawhmaina leh Pasian in ama’ thuakna pan a puahphatna thu gen hi. A thuakna pen suksialua mahmah taleh, hih mipa in, i nuntaksung a khuamialpen hun sung nangawnin Pasian in damna hong piaktheihzia lim honglak hi.

Hih mi nih tegel in a kibanglo dawnna koibang in nei thei uhhiam? Nuntaksung in i thuak haksatnate in hong lungkiacip saklo in koici bangin khalam kikhelna hong piathei zaw ding hiam?
