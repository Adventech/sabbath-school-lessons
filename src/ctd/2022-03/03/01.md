### Tukalsung Simding
Pai. 14; 15:22-27; 17:1-7; Paunak 3; Luka 4:1-13; 1Peter 1:6-9.

> <p>Kamngah</p>
> “Tu laitakin mite’ bawlsiatna na thuak manun tawlkhat na lungkham uh ahi zongin, hihbang thuakna hangin lungdamzaw un.” 1 Peter 1:6.

“Sun nitangpi mah, awging gamlum tuamtuam lakah, buk sunga kikhumcip vasa in, a topa hilhsa la leh awte ciingkik theilo hi. Tua awte manthei vatmah ahihhang, khentel sitset theilo hi. A topa in a buk siin in, ama’ hilh la bekmah a zaktheih ding munah koih in hilhleh zui thei bek hi. Khuamial ciangin a maan sinsen in a sak theih dong hilhpha kikkik hi. Tuabang in a siamsitset khit ciang in a pualamah pulak panleh khuavak sungah zong sathei suak hi. Pasian in zong a ta-te tuabangin hilh hi. Hong hilh ding la khat nei a, haksatna tampite kawmkal mahah i saktheih kimkhit nakleh, satheisuak ding cihi”—Ellen G. White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 472.

Phawk dingthu pen, tua vasa khuamial sungah a paipih pen a topa mah hipah hi. Satan in natna hongpia mah taleh, tua haksatna teng khempeuh sungah Pasian in makaih a hihmanin, buaina haksatna khempeuh kantanzo hi.
