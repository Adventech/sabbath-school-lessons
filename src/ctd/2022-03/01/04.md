> <p></p>
> “Kei hong ompih na hih manin, khuamial thukpi ka nawk hangin kalau kei hi. Na molhtum leh na ciangkhut in kei hong huu hi.” Late 23:4.

Thumaan lampi in lonona mun tuivot luannagei tengbek nawk lehbel nuam mahmah lel ding hi. David in tua bang cilo hi. Sihna gamkuam khuamialna zong omzel a, painophuai hetlo lel hi. Israel gam a luiluangte leh hawkguamte pen amau uthunhun in tui adim in hong luangpak zel thei hi. Hih munte pen kawcik in, keen mahmah ahih manin khuavak liahthei mahmah hi. Tua a hih manin “sihna gamkuam” cihpen “khuamial thukpi” cihna ahi hi.

`Nangma aituam “sihna gamkuam sung” na om hun ngaihsun in. Bang tawh a kibang hiam? Tuucingpa om gige cih thei napi na lau veve hiam? Bang Laisiangtho mun in banghang in nang ading manphapen thei naci hiam?`

`Tua gamkuam ah tuute thukhupna koibang hiding na-um hiam? Tuute amau thuthu in pailel ding maw, Tuucingpa ngiat in makaih ding maw, ngaihsun in.`

Elisabeth Elliot in, “sihna gamkuam sungah a tunlam a thei tuupen, kimakaih khial cih a theih kul hi. Khuamial pen lau mawkmawk ding hi lo a, kisin zawk ding ahihi. Tuucingpa in ama kiangah om gige lel hi.”–Elisabeth Elliot, Quest for Love (Grand Rapids, MI: Revell Books, 1996), p. 218.

`Tuabang gamkuam ah “thumaanlo takin” hong kimakaih ngei hiam? Tuabang hun ciangin Pasian hong sapna koibangin nadawng hiam? Thu tellopi in khuamial thukpi sungah i lutding pen Tuucingpa in bangzah in hongvei ding in na-um hiam?`
