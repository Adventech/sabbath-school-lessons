EGWhite, Signs of the Times, Nov. 18, 1903 sungpan “Effectual Prayer,” simin; The SDA Bible Commentary, vol. 4 laimai 1182 ah EGWhite kammal simin; My Life Today laimai 92 ah “God Permits Trial and Affliction to Purify Me” simin.

“Lungsim a simpa in amau’ gamtatna pen amaute sangin telpha semzaw hi. Pawl khatte a lungsim uh tei mahmah, thumaan khentat thei in Ama’ nasepna ah kizang thei mahmah ding cihzong mugige hi. Ama’ vaihawmna sungah hihmite muntuamtuam pia a, dinmun tuamtuamte panin amau’ theihzawhloh in, amau’ sunga a kiseel khinkhian, amau gamtat hoihlohna teng phawkkhia sak hi. Tua siatna teng pan in kipuahphat na’ng hun zong pia in, Ama’ nasepna sungah kilawm sak hi. Amaute a kisiansuah na’ngin meikuang haksate zong pia zelzel hi.”–Ellen G. White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 471.

**Kikupding Dotnate**

`Mawhna in suul hong zuih ciangin haksa sa mahmah veve ding hihang. “Pasian tawh ka kilemkik theilai ding hiam” bang ciliang ding hihang. Tuabang hun ciangin kipkho thei ding leh lelhlo ding in Pasian in bang hong kamciam khin hiam? (Paul kamal Rom. 5:1-11 simin). Hihbang lianin hong dongte tungah koici dawn ding nahi hiam?`

`EGWhite in “Pasian’ vaihawmna” acihpen bang acinuam hiam? Tua in koibang in nasem hiam? Thupiang khatpeuh pen Pasian’ vaihawmna a hihlam koibang in kithei hiam? Pasian “vaihawmna” in nangma nuntak sungin haksatna sungah koibang in hong makaih hiam?`

`Haksatna a thuak mikhat natheih ciangin, bangzah phamawh thei ding in namu hiam? Bangbang a piang zongin tuapa thuakna koibang in huhthei ding nahiam?`
