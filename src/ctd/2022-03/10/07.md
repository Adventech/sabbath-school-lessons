EGWhite The Ministry of Healing; Laimai 453-454 ah “The Importance of Seeking True Knowledge”; The Desire of Ages; Laimai 298-314 sungah “The Sermon on the Mount” leh Evangelism Laimai 630 ah “The Worker and His Qualifications” simin.

“I phutkhak ding haksatna tengpen lungnemna tawh Christ sungah i bukna in tampitak mah kiamsak tuam ding hi. I Topa’ kiniamkhiatna neizen hileng maw, nisim in i thuak elzel ahi simmawhna, nialna, buai sakna cihte khempeuh tungah kahto vingveng in, tuate maimialsak zawsop ding hihang. Christiante’ thupitna sangpen in pumpi ukzawhna ahi hi. Gamdaihna leh lungmuanna ngahna’ng in ngongtatna a zang mite in, Pasian’ picinna hong lahkhiat na’ng a buluh uh ahi uhhi. Lungsim kiniamkhiatna pen Christ’ nungzuite gualzawhna thahatna hi a; vantung thukhen zumh tawh kizopna zong ahi hi.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 301.

**Kikupding Dotnate**

`Kiniamkhiatna in “natsakna leh buaisakna-te” tungah koibangin hong lamto thei hiam? Tuabang i hihtheih nadingin a thupipen kiniamkhiatna gamtatzia in bang hiding in na um hiam?`

`Nomau ninam ngeina sungah, kiniamkhiatna leh lungnemna koibang in kilak zel hiam? Ki pakta maw, ki simmawh maw bangci? Tuabangte naneih sawm ciangin bang peuh in hong nengcip thei ding naci hiam?`

`Tuate pen tuhun a dingin lungnemna leh kiniamkhiatna’ limhoih naci hiam? Nacihleh, kuateng peuh hi a, amau kiangpan bang pilna nangah thei ding hiam?`

`Banghang in lungnemna leh kiniamkhiatna kibangsak denthei ihi hiam?`
