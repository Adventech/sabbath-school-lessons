---
title:  Gamtatna leh Vengsung
date:  21/07/2022
---

La khat in: “Keimah in Suangpi, Keimah in Tuikulh” ci hi. Tua bangin nangmah bek ding teta ding cih nalungngai ngei hiam? Midangte khawng in zong, “Pasian tawh ka tonkhopna pen keima aituam hi a, ka gengen nuam tuankei hi” acih uh zong nazangei ding uhhi.

`Ephet 4:11-16 simin. Paul in bang thubulpi agennuam hiam? Veng sung adingin bang tantuam hongpia hiam?`

Paul in Ephet mite lai akhak lai in, a pawlpi uhpen pumpi khat in gen hi. Zeisu in a lutang hi a, a mite uhpen a dangteng tawh genteh hi. Ephet 4:13 en lecin, tuabang vengsungah nuntak pen “Christ’ cingtaakna baan” cihi. Tua ahih manin khatleh khat i kingaih kul hi.

Christian khat in khattang in omtheih luatlel hi. A khang a khang in mi tampi takte in pampaihna bawlsiatna thuak uh a, khatguakin tang-om thei uhhi. Pasian vangliatna hangin, mite in amau’ kiimkot a nenniamna nuai ah omciplo uhhi. Tuabel himah taleh, Paul in thumaan khat hong pia a: mihing kipawlkhawm in sepkhopna hangin Christ cingtaakna i baanthei ding hi.

`Ephet 4:11-16 sim lecin, Paul in i vengsungah Christ’ cingtaakna i lahkhiat theihma in bang thuteng piang masa ding ci hiam?`

`Mimal in Christ’ picinna i pulaak bang tawh a kibanglo in veng buppi in Christ’ pi cinna koibang in pulaak thei ding hiam? Hih thute lakah, kidona lianpi sungah kihelna ahi sak bangteng omhiam? Eph. 3:10 simin.`
