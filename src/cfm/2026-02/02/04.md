---
title:  Pathian cu duhdawtnak a si
date:   07/04/2026
---

Duhdawtnak timi cu Khristian pawl in Pathian ziaza an relnak ih an hman uar zet mi a si men thei. Asan cu 1 Johan 4:8 ih Pathian thuhla ah “Pathian cu duhdawtnak a si” tiin simfiangnak a neihmi ruangah tla a si ko ding. Johan cun “Pathian cu duhdawtnak neitu a si” tin a tilo ih, “Pathian cu duhdawtnak a si” tiah a ti. Duhdawtnak cu a ziaza, asinak bulhram bik a si.

Mi tampi hrang ahcun Pathian an hmuhdan cu minung ih duhdawtnak an simfiangdan ihsin a ra suak theu, cumi ruangah famkim lo, le dik tawk loin an thei ringring theu. Duhdawtnak simfiangnak ding ah Pathian sinak le A kamsuak thu ih a phuangawknak pawl ihsin simfiangnak kan tuah ding a si.

`1 Johan 4:7-17 ahhin duhdawtnak thuhla ziangsaw in zirh?`

Pathian duhdawtnak cu a famkim, a zalen zet ih, pehzom awknak thuk zet a nei. Cumi cu 1 Johan ah Amah ih “um” ringring dingih in sawmnak ahhin a langfiang zet. Ziangahtile “Pathian in kan parih duhdawtnak a neihmi cu kan thei ih, kan zum. Pathian cu duhdawt-nak a si; duhdawtnak ih a umtu cu Pathian ah a um ih, Pathian khal amah ah a um a si” (1 Johan 4:16). Pathian cu duhdawtnak a si, cule duhdawtnak le ngilneihnak kan neih ve theinak dingah a hmuihmel keng ih tuah mi kan si (Sem 1:27). Hebrew tong ahcun duhdawtnak relnak thupi zet cu hesed ti mi a si. Himi cun minung hrang Pathian ih thukam duhdawtnak thuhla a rel, cumi cun rinumnak, kilhimnak, a hmun mi le pekawknak pawl a huaptel thluh a si.

Hlanlai Hebru le Grik tong ahcun Pathian relnak ah hmin dangdang tamzet an hmang, Cui hmin pawl cun Pathian ziaza mawi zet a dangdang in an tleu ter thluh. Tahthimnak ah hitawk ah pahnih lawng kan rel ding:

- **Adonai:** Ziangkim ih Bawipa, thukamnak thuhla ah kumkhua ih laireltu (uktu). (Sem. 15:2, Thut. 6:15, Mal. 1:6, Saam. 97:5).
- **Yahweh-Yireh:** BAWIPA cun in bawm ding (Sem. 22:13, 14, NKJV).

A netabik ih Pathian ih duhdawtnak ropi zet cu misual pawl hrangih thi (Rom. 5:8) A Fapa leitlun ih a run peknak hmangin tarlang a si (Johan 3:16). Pathian cun minung hnen ihsin himi thil cu a thup hlo thei ko, asinan a duhdawtnak a nasat tuk ruangah Pathian cun leitlun ah Jesuh a run thlah, cutin cun kan ai-awh ih thihnak a tuarnak ihsin A duhdawtnak zalen zetin kan rak let thei a si. Kanmah le Pathian karlak ih thenhrang awknak sual a ummi (Isa. 59:1, 2) ah Jesuh cun leilawn in don sak men lo ih, a famkim mi Pathian ih duhdawtnak (Johan 14:9, Heb. 1:3) ziaza pawl in hmuh dingah leitlun ah a um, cule mipi pawl hmuahhmuah cu amai lamah a hruai theinak ding khalah a si (Johan 12:32).

`Pathian hmin tamzet cun A thianghlimnak le A duhdawtnak cu thupi bikah an ret thluh. 1 Korin 13:4-8 siar awla, cule ziangvek dinhmun khalah “duhdawtnak” timi tongfang ahkhan “Pathian” tin vun thleng aw. Himi cun Pathian ziaza na theihthiamnak ziangtluk in a lo kauter? “Duhdawtnak” timi tongfang umnak ahhin na hmin vun ret ve ta awla, a mil aw kem?`
