---
title:  Pathian cu A Thianghlim
date:   06/04/2026
---

Thianghlimnak timi tongkam hi mipi in an nitin nun ih an hman mi tongkam a si tuk lem lo, ziangahtile kan kiangkap ih thil thianghlim malte lawng a um ruangah a si men thei. Sabbath hi caan liamcia ihsin ni thianghlim a si. Cule Pathian cu A thianghlim ti kan thei. Asinan nitin kan nun ahhin thianghlimnak hi kan tlasam zet.

Pathian ziaza hi na zirfiang ahcun Pathian a sinak laimu bik cu thianghlimnak hi a si timi na hmu suak ding. Asinan cumi sullam cu ziangsaw a si?

`A hnuai ih thu um mi pawl in ziangtin saw Pathian an simfiang: Puithiam hnatuan 20:26, 1 Samuel 2:2, Isaiah 57:15, le Ezekiel 38:23?`

Bible in Pathian ih thianghlimnak a rel tikah Anih cu sualnak le thiltha lo ihsin famkim zetih thenhran, lole hrial tu a si timi sullam a nei. Pathian cu a hramthok ihsin a nettiang za ah za a tha famkim a si. Himi thu ahhin Pathian ih thianghlimnak cu a ziaza dang pawl hmuahhmuah ih lungphum a si.

Khawthim sungah Pathian duhdawtnak cu a thiangfai, a thianghlimmi duhdawtnak a si ih, mai tanghmai sialnak le ngai thupi awknak hmuahhmuah lak ihsin a thiar fihlim aw thluhmi duhdawtnak a si. Anih cu tinkim theitu (ziang hmuahhmuah theitu) asinak cu ziangkim a theihnak ah a thianghlim thluh tinak a si. Cumi sullam cu sual tuahduhnak lak ihsin thiar fihlim awknak a si. Ziangkim thei thluhtu Pathian cu thianghlim lo bangta sehla, na rinsan thei kem?

Pathian ih huham (Thiltitheinak hmuahhmuah neitu)cu thianghlimnak huham a si. Ziangkim tithei khal sehla, thianghlim cuang riai lo Pathian cu vun mitthlam hnik awla, thilti theinak ih khat, misualral zet hruaitu a si tengteng ding. Pathian ih thianghlimnak lawng in Amah duhdawt dingah in tuah thei, ziangahtile Anih cu a hramthok ihsin a net tiang ih mi tha zet a si. Curuangah thianghlimnak cu Pathian ziaza thuhla ih kan theihfiang theinak dingih ziaza thupi zet a si men thei. Asinan hithil cu theihsual olzet mi lak ih pakhat a si.

Pathian hmaika ih feh suaktu Moses, Isaiah, Ezekiel, Daniel, le Johan vekih Bible sung ih a ummi pawl thuhla vun ruat aw. Pathian an sawnletnak tongkam hmaisabik kha ziangsaw a si? An kedam an phelh, an hmai an hup, asilole mithi vekin lei ah an tlu. Minung kan si vekin misualral le thianghlim lo kan si ruangah Pathian hmaika ah kan ding thei lo. Pathian hmel a zohtu milai zokhal an nung lo ding. Hivek deuh nawn in Ellen G White in langnak a hmuh tikah, “Thianghlim…Thianghlim…thianghlim” tin a au theu. Ziangahtile cumi cu a hmuh mi ihsin a run relsuak mi tongkam pawl an si. Cule thilnung pali pawl khal sun le zan cawl loin, “Thianghlim, thianghlim, thianghlim, Bawipa Pathian Ziangkimtithei, um zo, um lai, um lai ding a si, tiin an au (Thup 4:8).

`Pathian cu a thianghlim ngaingai, A hnen ih kan rat tikah, cuvek ciah in kan hmu tengteng ding. Himi na theihnak cun ziangsaw thazang a lo pek? Himi cun nangmai ziaza thawn pehpar awin ziangvek lamzin in saw sawmngiarnak a lo pek?`
