---
title:  Ruahbet ding
date:   10/04/2026
---

Pathian cun A ziaza langter tu dingah A mi pawl a ko, asinan himi tuah thei ding cun kanmah ten Amah kan theihfiang a tul. Fiangzetin Amah kan hmuh theinak ding lamzin thabik cu A thianghlimnak le A famkim mi lamzin pawl theihsual theunak sualnak ih khat minung mit pawl cu kan tanta ding ih, Bible sungih A kamsuak thu ah kan hawl ding a si.

“Minung thinlung ihsin sankhat hnu sankhat a luangsuak mi duhdawtnak pawl hmuahhmuah, minung thinlung ihsin cerhti a luangsuak mi pawl cu cemni nei lo Pathian duhdawtnak thawn vun tahthim ahcun tifinriat lamih a fate zetmi tiva te vek men fang a si. Cumi cu tongkam in a simfiang thei lo, cati khal in a tarlang thluh thei lo. Nitin na nun ah na ruat ringring men thei, cumi thei thiam dingin taima zetin Bible sungah na hawl tla a tul men thei; vancung Pa ih duhdawtnak le zangfahnak theithiam dingih na zuamnak ah Pathian in a lo pekmi huham le thiamnak hmuahhmuah na ko thei; asinan cumi tlun ah cemni nei lo a um hrih lai. Kum upa pawl ih duhdawtnak na zir men thei; asinan Pathian in leitlun hrangah a Fapa thi dingih a peknak duhdawtnak cu a thuk lam le sanglam, a kauhlam le sau lam hi famkim zetin zianghman na theithiam thei lo ding. Kumkhua nunnak khal in a famkim in a tarlang thei dah lo. Asinan Bible kan zir ih, Khrih ih nun le tlennak khawkhannak ah kan thinlung kan pek ahcun himi thulu ropi zet pawl cu kan theihthiamnak ah an ong aw deuhdeuh ding.”—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 5, p. 740.

**Relkhawm ding thusuhnak pawl:**

`Tuizarh na zir mi Pathian thuhla na ruat tikah, a khuimi in saw Pathian na theihthiamnak hmual lo nei ter?`

`Pathian thawn nan pehtlaihawknak thukter le cakter dingah Pathian ih ziaza dang ziangso na zir thei ding?`

`Na innsang lole na rualpi thawn Khrih hnen thlennak karbak timi ih bung 1-nak kha siar uh, lole ngai tlang uh. Cule rel tlang uh si. Himi bung 1-nak ih Pathian ziaza le Jesuh ih thuhla ah ziangvek theihthiamnak thar saw a lo ruat ter mi?`

`Mi tampi in Pathian hmuh diklonak an nei. Cumi tuahtha dingin Jesuh a ra. Hivek ih ruahnak diklo a neitu pawl hnenah Pathian ziaza dikzet sim thei dingin ziangsaw na tuah ding?`

`Nikhat ni zirlai na zir ngah mi kha vun ruat sal aw. Kannih cu misual le thianghlim lo si hman ningla, Bible cun fiangzet in Pathian minung pawl thianghlim ih an nun thu in rak hmuh. Siar ding: 1 Piter 1:13–16, Rom 6:22, le Hebru 12:14. Pathian cu a thianghlim ih, thianghlim ve dingah in sawm a si. Asinan thianghlim ten nun hman ding timi sullam ngaingai hi ziangsaw a si?`

**A tawizawng in:** Sersiamnak a thok ihsin, Pathian cun kanmah thawn laicin awknak nai zetih neih a duh. A ziaza kan theihthiamnak cu Satan ih in do tum mi bik si khal sehla, Pathian cun A kamsuak thu le A Fapa Jesuh ih nun hmangin fiangzetin kan hnenah a tarlang aw a si. A netnak ahcun Pathian thawn laicin awknak thuk zet neih kan duh ahcun Pathian sinak mawi zet le fiang zet theihthiam hi a thupi zet a si.
