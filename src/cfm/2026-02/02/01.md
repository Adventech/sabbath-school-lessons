### Tuizarh zirlai hrang siar ding
Sem. 3:1–5, Pui. 20:26, 1 Sam. 2:2, 1 Johan 4:7–19, Gen. 1:1, Sem. 2:7, Matt. 1:23, Matt. 28:20.

> <p>Bible cangken</p>
> “ ‘Cun nang Pathian dik umsun, le na thlahmi Jesuh Khrih an theih hi kumkhaw nunnak cu a si’ “ (John 17:3, NKJV).

Pathian ziaza theihfiangnak cu Amah thawn laicinawknak hnget khohzet neihnak hi a si. Curuangah tuizarh ahcun Bible in Pathian ziaza thuhla a rel mi ralring zetin kan zoh ding, “Pathian theihsualnak thim sahzet cun leitlun hi a khuh thluh. Minung cun Pathian ziaza an theihnak an hloh thluh. An theisual ih, an simfiang sual theu. Tui caan ahcun Pathian hnen ihsin thucah um mi cu simphuang ding a si ih, a thucah tleu mawi zet le a huham rundamnak cu thehzarh ding a si. A ziaza cu mikip ih an theihnak dingah phuanzar ding a si. Leitlun thim sahzet ahhin A sunglawinak eng, A thatnak, zangfahnak, thudik eng pawl tleu ter in a um ….. Leitlun hnenih pek dingmi zangfahnak thucah netabik cu A duhdawtnak ziaza langternak hi a si”—Ellen G. White, Christ’s Object Lessons, p. 415.

Pathian thuhla simfiang thluh ding cu thil cang thei lo vekin tla a lang, kan tuahthei mi umsun cu Amai thuhla Bible ih a rel mi khihhmuh men hi a si. A hleice in tu ahcun Pathian ih ziaza mangbangza thuhla kan theih dingmi pawl hman hi thei thluh hrih lo khal sehla, A thuhla tamsin ih kan zir lai ahhin Amah kan theihfiangnak le kan duhdawtnak thuk sinsin ih a um theinak dingah thlacamnak nei uh si. Cuticun netabik ah midang hnenah A duhdawtnak kan langter theinak dingah A kiangnai ih um kan duhnak a thanglian ding a si.
