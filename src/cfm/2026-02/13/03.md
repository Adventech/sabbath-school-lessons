---
title:  A netnak ahcun hmaiton rori in
date:   22/06/2026
---

Pathian kiangnai ih um dingah sersiam mi kan si (Semtirnak 2:7). Milai a sersiam ihsin Amah thawn kan laicin awknak a siatbal mi tuahtha sal dingin ziangkim a pe suak (Johan 3:16). Kan thinlung ah kumkhaw hmunram a ret, cumi cing khal in milai cun Pathian in a hramthok ihsin a cemnet tiang ih a tuah mi pawl cu kan theithiam thei lo (Thus. 3:11). Kannih cu kan kiangkap – kan sung rori ih a ummi doawknak ropi ahcun kan tel – cumi cing khal in Pathian cun Amah thawn kan um tlang theinak dingah kan hrangih laicin awknak tuahtha sal thei dingih a cemzat man khungzia pawl ruat ve ciamciam ta dingin kan cawl duh lo theu. Kan leitlun ih doawknak le hniksaknak pawl ah kan khum bet aw ringring theu, asinan “kan nuntuzia cu van nuntuzia a si; cui van ihsin Rundamtu Bawipa Jesuh Khrih a rat ding kan thlir: Anih cun a thuthu ih thil ziangkim umter theinak a huham ih hnatuannak thawn hi kan taksa tawntai hi, a sunglawimi amai’ taksa vekih thuam a si theinak dingah a thleng leh ding a si” timi hi kan hngilh theu” (Filipi. 3:20, 21).

Leitlun sancemnak lam kan naih cuahco thlang vek in nikhat ni ah nisuaknak lam ihsin mero fate a ra lang ding ti kan thei. A ra nai deuhdeuh ding ih, cui mero par ahcun, “A lu ah sui lallukhuh khum in, le a kut ah favah hriam zet keng in minung Fapa vek a to mi” kan hmu ding (Thup. 14:14). Jesuh cu vancungmi thawngtel in an run thlun ding (Matt. 25:31), cule mit hmuahhmuah in an hmu ding (Thup. 1:7). Cutin a tum lai ahcun A au thawm, Pathian ih kawhnak tawtawrawt aw kan thei ding, Khrih ih a itthat mi pawl ih thlan cu an ongaw dingih, annih pawl cu an tho hmaisa ding (1 Thess. 4:16). Cule anmah a ko tu ih aw cu an thei ding (Johan 5:28).

`Ziangthil a um lai ding? siar aw: 1 Thessalonika 4:17. A netabik ahcun Paul in Filipi 2:10,11 ih a ngan mi cu leitlun pumhuap ah a thangkhawk thluh ding.`

Ruahnak ropi zet le rinsan tlak zet a si! Nikhat ni ahcun Jesuh kan hmu ding – thungai in – Amah cu kan hmu ngaingai ding a si. A aw kan thei ding ih, Anih cu Bawi a si timi kan phuang ding. A thuhla kan siar theu mi, a hnen ih thla kan cam theu mi, midang hnen ih a thuhla kan rel theu mi; Kan thinlung ih a rak duh zet mi cu … hmaiton rori in kan hmu ding. Himi cu fiang ngaingai thei ding, ziangahtile Pathian cu a rinum ih, a thutiam pawl cu thutak hlir an si (Thup. 22:6).

Tawtawrawt a awn ih, mi hmuahhmuah ih Jesuh an hmuh tikah kan rak hngah tento ih man a neih zia kan thei ding. Taima zet ih thlacamnak kan neihnak pawl, Amah thawn tikcu caan kan hmannak pawl, Amai hrangih raltha zetih kan tongsuahnak pawl, hniksaknak kan tonnak pawl hmuahhmuah cu A hmel kan hmuh tikah an cemral thluh ding (Thup. 22:4).
