---
title:  Ralthuam huham nei bik
date:   19/04/2026
---

Bible ih man a neih zia le bulpak ih Bible kan zirnak ah ziangtin thuk zetin kan zir thei ding timi kan rel hlan ah thil ziang simaw kan theih tengteng tul mi a um: Satan in a lo donak langsar zet pakhat cu Pathian thu ah Pathian thawn tikcu na hmannak lo khap ding hi a si. Mipi pawl cun an sumdawnnak, an ngaihthahnak, an cauhnak, lole an ringhlelnak pawl ruangih an Bible ihsin hmundang zetih an um ding hi Satan ih khawkhannak pakhat a si. Pathian thu ah tikcu caan kan hmang tikah kan nun a tuahthar ih, kan thlarau a cawm nung a si timi Satan cun a thei ringring. Curuangah hivek thil um lo dingah ziangkim tuah a huam a si.

“Satan cun minung in Pathian theihnak an neih lonak dingah hmanrua a um theimi pawl hmuahhmuah a hmang rero; ziangahtile cui thil cun a mibumnak pawl hmuahhmuah an langter thluh ruangah a si” tiah simthan in kan rak um zo —Ellen G. White, The Great Controversy, p. 593

Satan in Huham neizet Pathian kamsuak thu cun huham nei lo ah a tuah thei timi a thei. Satan cun Thlacamnak le Bible zirnak cu minung in amah an dokalhnak dingih an hman theimi ralthuam huham neizet a si timi a thei (Efe. 6:17, 18; Heb. 4:12), curuangah thlacamnak le Bible siarnak kan neih thei lonak dingah a cang theitawk in tan a la ve. Pathian kamsuak thu hi huham a nei zet ih, hi leitlun um dingin thu a pek lawng hman si loin (Saam 33:6), mithi a kai tho thei (Johan 11:41-44) cule sual neh dingah thazang in pe a si (Matt. 4:1–11).

Satan cun Pathian minung pawl hi an Bible ihsin hladeuh ih a um ternak thawngin Pathian thawn kan laicin awknak lawng siloin midang thawn kan laicin awknak tiang khal in a siatbal thluh a si. Kan neihawknak cu zianghman lo men a si ih, kan fale khal sullam nei loin kan hro ih, kan rualpi le kan hnatuanpi pawl par khalah thinsaunak kan nei lo. Nun hi a buai tuk ih, thinbang le phurrit phur in, tlansuaknak ding lamzin khal zianghman um lo vekin tla a lang. Cule thil a hungcang mi pawl theifiang dingin tikcu caan khal kan la tam dah lo. Pathian thawn naiaw te ih um vekin kan ruat aw theu, asinan a ngaingai ahcun Pathian kamsuak thu zianghman kau (siar) loin ni a liam ih, zarh a liam cuahco, nitin in kan cak lo deuhdeuh.

`Pathian thawn kan laicin awknak ahhin nitin kan nun cu a sukso in a um rero, asinan mangbangza in Pathian cu Tahhla 3:22,23 ih a sim vekin a dinnak hmunah a um ringring. Himi bungcang ahhin ziangsaw kan theih mi um, ziangtin saw kan minung sinak thawn an tahthim?`

A tluk hlan ih Cherub khuhtu a si vekin (Ezek 28:14-17), Lucifer cun Pathian kamsuak thu le cui Kamsuak thu pawl ih huham an neih zia khal a thei. Tu ahcun himi thudik hi a hua tuk, curuangah nitin kan nunnak ah Pathian thu la lut loin, A thu ngai loih kan um caan ah kan thinlung a cak thei lo ih, kan ruahnak pawl khal an hit thluh.

`Ziangtluk in so na nunnak ah duhthusam nei le duhthusam nei lo na si? A tul asile, na letnak in danglamnak ziangtin a lo sim?`
