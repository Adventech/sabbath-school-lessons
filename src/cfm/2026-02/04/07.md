---
title:  Ruahbet ding
date:   24/04/2026
---

Nazi 24 liamzo ih na tongmi tongkam pawl na vun ruatsal a si ahcun, ziangtin na rem khawm thluh ding? Duhdawtnak, lainatnak, lungawinak, thazang peknak, thinhengnak, thinbangnak, lungawi lonak, mi thuhla relnak, asilole that lonak pawl an si maw? Bible cun, “ziangahtile kaa cun thinlung sung ihsin a ummi a tong suak’ “ tiah a ti (Matt. 12:34, NKJV). Kan thinlung ih thil tha lo a um tikah, kan tongkam in a suak a si.

Kan zaten thinsiat, thinbang, thinpit caan tivek tonnun kan nei cio thluh, Cuvek ih kan ruahnak cun kan ka ihsin a suak mi a thleng danglam theu (khuimaw caan ahcun tongkam hrekkhat pawl cu neta ah kan sir aw theu). Cumi thawn kalh aw ciah in, kan thinlung cu mi pakhat duhdawtnak ih a khat tikah kan tongkam ihsin a luangsuak a si.

Cuvek ciah in Pathian kamsuak thu khal in A thinlung le A tumtah mi pawl kan hnenah in sim. Pathian thinlung ihsin a ra suak mi tongkam pawl cu Bible ahhin kan nei a si timi vun ruat ding cu mangbangza thil a si. Pathian kamsuak thu hi thuanthu pawl hmuahmuah ah thititheinak nasa zet a nei timi hi hmangbangza ngaingai a si.

“Bible hi nun ziaza tha zirhnak, kan leitlun dinhmun thawn a milaw dingih a um lawng ah a tikcu te ih kan thlun cu thuhran sisehla; asinan a ngaingai cu hitin khal in kan zoh thei – A nungmi Pathian thu, kan nunnak Pathian thu, kan cangvaihnak, tongkam, le ruahnak in kilkhawitu tongkam an si. Pathian thu cu himi hnakih a hnuailam ih ret ding cu hnong ding a si. Cule cumi zumtu pawl ih hnongnak hi mino pawl ih rinhlelnak le rin umlonak a suaktertu a si”—Ellen G. White, Education, p. 260.

**Relkhawm ding thusuhnak pawl:**

`Na zumnak hrang na neih mi thuruahnak lungput kha ziangsaw a si? Na theih ai hnak hmanin a tam sawn men thei.`

`Thlacam le Bible siar cu Pathian thawn laicinawknak nan neihnak lungphum a si tin ziangtin saw na fiang thei ding?`

`Mi pakhat in Pathian thawn laicin awknak ah thuk sin ih um a duh ahcun, Bible siar thok dingin ziangtin thurion na pe ding?`

`Bawipai ka ihsin a suak mi tongfang ziangkim ahhin ziangtin na nunpi thei ding (Daan. 8:3)? Cumi cun na nun ah ziangmi saw a si?`

`A hnuai ih Bible cang pawl in Pathian kam suak thuhla ziangsaw in sim cio? Heb. 11:3; Saam. 33:6; Matt. 11:4, 5; 1 Thess. 4:16; Efesa. 6:17; James 1:21.`

**A tawizang in:** Bible cu a nung ih huham nasa zet a nei, cumi siar cu Pathian thawn kan laicin awknak a thansoh theinak ding lungphum a si. Cumi cun mangbangza Pathian ziaza le leitlun san thuanthu ih a tangdor zia lawng hi in zirh lo ih, tuini ah tangdor zetin Pathian hnenih kan rat tikah kan zate hnenah a tong a si.
