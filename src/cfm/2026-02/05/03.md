---
title:  A hmun
date:   27/04/2026
---

`Jesuh cu ziangkim ih kan zoh thim dingmi mi famkim a si. Bulpak pekawknak thuhla rel ding khalah zohthim tlak a si thotho. Marka 1:35 ah Jesuh le Pathian ih tikcu caan an hmandan thuhla ziangsaw in sim?`

Himi bungcang hi pakhat lawng si khal sehla, hitawk ahhin Jesuh ih zohthim ding a tuahnak ihsin tamzet kan zir thei. Ni a suah hlanah A Pa thawn a um theinak dingah hmun reh zet ih um dingin a poksuak (Nitin nun buainak ihsin a thiar fihlim aw). Himi te hi vun mithlam awla – Jesuh cu Galilee tipi kap ah a to, asilole tlang kuam ah to in a kiangkap ih mipi an thawh hlan ah A Pa thawn pawlkom awknak neih le thlacam a duh timi na vun ruat thiam maw? Himi bungcang cu thlacam dingih Jesuh a apawk thluhnak thuhla si khal sehla, cui thil cu A hrangih thil thupi zet a si timi khal kan hmu thei. Himi tikcu caan cu a tuarnak ding pawl hmuahhmuah a hmaiton ding mi ih thazang rak petu a si ding timi cu rinhlehnak zianghman um loin a fiang zet. Jesuh cun nitin bulhram a thoknak dingah hivek hi a tulmi a si ahcun kannih cun ziangtluk in saw kan tul bet ding?

Pathian cun, “ ‘Ka mithmai hawl uh” tiah in sim, cule kannih in, “ Bawipa, Na mithmai ka hawl ding a si” tiih kan rak let ding hi a ruahsan a si (Saam. 27:8, NKJV).

`A mithmai kan hawldan thuhla ah 1 Korin 16:11 in ziangsaw a rel?`

Pathian kiangih um dingah zingtin fehnak ding hmun na nei maw? Sangkate kiangih tohkham ah to in, lenglam hmun reh zet na hmu men thei, asilole coka ih cabuai, nitin Jesuh kehram ah to in Pathian kamsuak thu na zir theinak hmun ah na feh men thei. Jesuh kehram ih to hi umnak ding hmun thabik a si (Luka 10:39–42). Pathian thawn tikcu caan hmang dingin hmun pakhatkhat ah nitin na feh ahcun, cui hmun ah nitin ten na kir sal ringring men thei. Manhlap thil tivek a um ruangah nitin na feh theunak hmun ih na feh ngah lo caan le Pathian thawn tikcu caan na hmang man vak lo caan tivek a um asile na thanau hlah. Asinan Pathian thawn tikcu caan hmang lo in ni tamzet, zarh tamzet le thla tamzet sung um hlah. Pathian thawn laicin awknak ih um ringring cu nitin duhhrilnak a si ih, Amah na hril ahcun tuini khal ih na thok kirsal theih mi thil a si timi kha cing ringring aw.

`A liamcia zarh ahkhan Bible siarnak le thlacamnak ah caan ziangzat na hmang? Na letnak cun a hmaisabik ih na tuah tul mi thlengdanglamnak pawl ziangsaw a lo sim?`
