---
title:  Ruahbet ding
date:   03/04/2026
---

Kan suah hlan ihsin Pathian cun in duhdawt; kanmah in theihnak dingah le Amah kan theihtheinak dingah khawkhannak a nei zo. Tuu khaltu tha vekin in hawl ih, nitin ten Amah ih um ringring dingah in sawm. Amah let dingah duhrilnak kan tuah a tul, cule A thu kan ngaihsak lonak le kan Laodicean dinhmun cu A thatnak laksawng thawn thleng ding a si (zoh aw, Thup. 3:18, 19).

Sabit kung ih a hnget pawl nuamnai teten an thanlian vek ciah in Pathian thawn kan laicin awknak cu nuamnai teten a thang lian ding, asilole ruahpi tulsam zet ruangah a hung thanglian thei. Kan nunnak ah rah tampi rah in kan thansohnak cu ziang ava si hman ah nitinten “thinghnai” asilole Thiang Thlarau cu Jesuh thawn kan feh zom aw ringring lai timi theihnak ah kan tul ringring a si.

“Khrih sungah um ringring timi sullam cu A thlarau rak dong ih, phulo hringnun hi A hnatuannak hnuai ih va pumpek awk hi a si. Pehtlaih awknak pawl cu minung le A Pathian karlak ah a um ding a si. Sabit kung hngetek in cawl loin sabit kung a nungmi ihsin a hnai a pek suah bangtuk in, Jesuh ah kan um a tul, cahnak le a ziaza famkimnak hi zumnak thawngin Amai hnen ihsin kan dong a si.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 676.

“Ziangtin saw a romi thingkung cu a nu le pa thawn kan pehzom awk thei ding? A nungmi sabitti le a rah mi cu ziangtin na tep thei ding? Sabit kung thawn an pehzom awknak thawngin laicinawknak nai zetah a run suah thei ding a si. Thing hram ihsin thinghram, thing hnah thahri ihsin thinghnah thahri cun nunnak a pekmi sabit kung cu a hnge thawn a pehzom aw ih, a hnge cun sabit kung vekin rah a rah.”—Ellen G. White, manuscript 67, 1897.

**Relkhaw din thusuhnak pawl:**

`Na nun zoh kirsal aw. Na nun ah thlarau lam ih Laodicean dinhmun ih a lo umtertu ziangvek in saw na nun a lo tham ngah? Ziangmi in saw Pathian thawn nai aw deuhdeuh dingin a lo tuah?`

`Ellen G White in “Thlarau thianghlim rak cohlangnak” timi thuhla a rel. Thiang Thlarau hrangah ziangtluk thlacamnak na rak nei dah zo?Nitin ten Thiang Thlarau dong ringring bangta awla, ziang thil saw thleng aw ding?`

`Kannih hi kawhhran kan si vek in nitin ten nasa zet in Thiang Thlarau ngah dingin thlacamnak nei bangta sehla, ziangmi thlengawknak saw um ding?`

`Na tuarnat khalah Pathian thawn na laicin awknak ahcun lailan zetin um aw. Amah thawn nai aw zetih na um theinak dingah ziangmi sia le tha theihnak nei in saw duhhrilnak na tuah ding?`

**A tawizawng in:** Pathian thawn laicin awknak sungih kan thanlen hlan ihsin, Amah thawn tui dinhmun ih kan laicin awknak hi ziang a si timi ruat hmaisa dingin ngaicang ta aw. Laodicean asilole kan hnget pawl cu luangsuak thei lo ih a um asile, Jesuh cun kan thlarau dinhmun hrang relfelnak famkim a nei a si: Amah ih um ringring ding hi a si.
