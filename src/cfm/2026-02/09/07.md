---
title:  Ruahbet ding
date:   29/05/2026
---

Daan hi thleng linglet le theihsual in a um timi thupi cun kan mang in bangter lem lo, Satan in Pathian a donak netabik cu A Daan hi a si ding timi thu theihter in kan um.

Jesuh san lai ahkhan mi hrekkhat cun daan hloral ter dingin a ra ah an ruat, asinan cumi cu thudik a si lo. Pathian cu ziangvek a si timi in hmuh dingah Jesuh cun daan le Pathian ziaza mawi zet a run ti eng, a run ti famkim a si (Matt. 5:17, 18).

“Mipi thinlung ah Pathian kamsuak thu Thianghlim tihzah upatnak an neih lawngah Pathian tumtahnak hlen famkim dingin ruahsannak an nei thei ding. David ih a uk lai le Solomon ih a uk thok hrawng ih Israel pawl ih cahnak kha Pathian daan an ngai thupi ruangah a si: Elijah le Josiah san lai ih harhtharnak khal kha Pathian thu nung an rinsan ruangih um thei mi a si. Israel pawl ih ro sunglawi himi Pathian thudik rinsannak thawngin Jeremiah in tuahtharnak hna a rak dil a si”—Ellen G. White, Prophets and Kings, pp. 465, 466.

**Relkhawm ding thusuhnak pawl:**

`Nunphung tum deuh pawl in sual hi ziangtin an thlir? Kan kawhhran in ziangtin kan thlir ding?`

`Ziangtik ahsaw sual cun Pathian thawn laicinawknak le midang thawn laicin awknak a siatsuah timi na hmuh hmaisabik?`

`Na nun ahhin Pathian daan thlun hi a har maw, a ol saw? Himi thuah ziangsi relbet duh mi na neih?`

`Adventist kan si vekin kan pawlkom hmin men hman in daan ka ngai thupi zia a tarlang thluh, cule ziangtin saw daan thlunnak cun in rundam ding timi ah thum aw ih, daan uar ciamconak thang ihsin kan luat thei ding? (Na ruahnak hmang lawk awla : thuthen ni ah, na sualnak cu a thianghlim le famkim Pathian hmaiah sunbung in a um tikah, ziangmi ahsaw na rundamnak na thum awk ding? Na daan thlunnak ah maw? Asilole na ai-awh ih Jesuh ih felnak famkim ah saw a si ding?`

`Ziangtin saw theihnak (lole theihnak tlasam)cun mi pakhat cu Pathian thawn an laicin awknak ah sullam a neih?`

**A tawi zawngin:** Kan nun cu sualnak in a kaitang, cumi cu Pathian ihsin in thenhrang a si. Asinan Pathian cun Amah cu ka thinlung, ruahnak, cahnak pawl hmuahhmuah thawn duhdawt le thei dingah in sawm a si. Hivek ih kan tuah thei tikah midang par khalah duhdawtnak kan nei sinsin ding. Cuvek ih Pathian le midang duhdawtnak cun Pathian daan a tarlang, cumi cu Amah le kan kiangkap um pawl thawn kan laicin awknak kilkhawi le humhimnak dingih pek mi a si. Pathian daan cu a mawi emem ih, a ziaza a tarlang thluh a si, A daan kan theihfiang ahcun Amah thawn kan laicin awknak cu a cak sinsin ding a si.
