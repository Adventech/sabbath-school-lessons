### Tuizarh zirlai hrang siar ding
Marka 8:11, 12; Matt. 15:21–28; Luka 7:1–10; Efesa. 2:8; Hebru 11; Thup. 14:12.

> <p>Bible cangken</p>
> “Zumnak in kan ruahsanmi cu a ngaingai a si timi a langter; cumi cu hmuh lomi thil pawl theihfiangnak a si” (Hebru 11:1, NLT).

Mi pakhat cun, “Zumnak cu Wifi vek ciah a si. Hmuh theih a si lo, asinan cui thil cu na tulmi thawn pehzom awk ter theinak huham a nei a si” tiah a ti. Rinhlelnak um riai loin: Zumnak tel lo cun, Pathian thawn laicin awknak a um thei lo a si.

Na zumnak cu tuini ah ziangvek a si? Pathian na zumnak cu a hnin derdo dah maw? Pathian thawn nan laicin awknak ah hmailam ziangtin na feh so vivo ding timi hman thei mumal loin na rak um dah zo ko ding. Asilole na zumnak cu rose (hnen-si) pangpar hnahhring fate zet ihsin hung pitling vivo mi, a netnak ih pangpar tha zet, rong dangdang ih hung suak, khaan sung khal ih theihhngilh siang lo ding khawp ih rimthaw teh a si pang maw? Ngaingai te cun, “Zumnak in kan ruahsanmi cu a ngaingai a si timi a langter; cumi cu hmuh lomi thil pawl theihfiangnak a si” (Hebru 11:1, NLT). Himi cu thil ziangmaw kanmah te ih kan tuah cop thei mi a si lo, ziangahtile “ Pathian in mikip hnenah zumnak tahfung a pe thluh a si” (Rom. 12:3, NKJV). Zumnak cu Pathian ih thilpek a si (Efesa. 2:8, 9), cumi cing khal in Pathian kan zumnak cu Pathian in kan hrangah thil a rak tuah zo ruang ah a si.

Tuizarh ahcun zumnak timi thupi kan hliakhlai ding: Rinhlelnak le zum lonak thuhla ah ziangsaw tuah ding; Jesuh relvek ih zumnak cakzet cu ziangsaw a si; “Jesuh ih zumnak” neih timi cu ziangsaw a sullam timi tiangin kan zoh ding.
