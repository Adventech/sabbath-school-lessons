---
title:  Ruahbet ding
date:   22/05/2026
---

Kannih cu zumnak (Rom 5:1)thawngin thiamcotermi kan si(ngaidam le Pathian thawn um tlan). Cule zumnak thawngin thianghlimter (Jesuh vek si dingih huham pek mi)mi kan si (Thirh 26:18). Jesuh cu kan nun ah um dingih kan sawm tikah, kannih khal zumnak thawngin Pathian fale ah kan cang (Johan 1:12). Pathian Fapa zumnak thawngin kan nung (Gal. 2:20).

“A tlaktlai lonak a thei awtu thlarau le rundamtu ih thatnak ih thum aw thluh mi thlarau ai hnakih bomtul zetih a langmi thil zianghman a um lo. Thlacamnak thawngin, A kamsuak thu zirnak thawngin, a umpitnak sungih zumnak thawngin minung tawntai cu a nungmi Khrih thawn pehzom in a nung thei ih, a thlah dah lo dingmi kut in a kai hnget ding”—Ellen G. White, The Ministry of Healing, p. 182.

“An zumnak cu beidong duh lo thlacamnak le thinlung tangdornak hmangin an cakter tengteng ding a si. Anmah le mah an kawlawng aw ter ding ih, Pathian kamsuak thu le Thlarau thawn silhkhat ter an si ding. Zumnak in Pathian hnenah thungai thlak in thlacamnak nei aw – Zumnak cun Pathian rinsannak le A hnatuannak ih pumpe aw thluh dingin a lo hruai ding – Cumi lawng in hi leitlun ih thimnak uktu pawl, le hmunsang ih um thlarau suak pawl ih thiltitheinak pawl hmuahhmuah donak ah minung cu Thiang Thlarau lamah bomnak ngah dingin a hruai thei a si”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 431.

**Relkhawm ding thusuhnak pawl:**

`Doral beihnak ih Thiang Thlarau thawn tantlannak thuhla a tlun ih tarlang mi ihsin ziangmi thil thupi zet pa 5 saw na lak thei ding?`

`Himi doawknak ahhin zumnak in ziang a rak tuah ve?`

`Himi hi a tu na nun ah ziangtin saw na hmuh?`

`Siar aw: Hebru 10:23. Zumnak ih kan phuangawknak cu ziangah kan kaih hnget a thupi?`

`Bomtul ih na theih awk caan ah, cui thil cu na hrangah Jesuh hnenih na pekawk thluhnak ding tikcu caan a si timi thudik cu ziangtin na ruat theu?`

**A tawi zawngin:** Pathian cun Amah thawn laicinawknak hrangih thumawknak dingah zumnak tahfung hi mikip hnenah a pe thluh. Kan zumnak neitu diktak a si vekin Jesuh cun kan zate hrangah zumnak ih huham in pe thluh. Kan zumnak a tet tuk lai caan ah, thinlung pekawknak mitthli thawn kan rat caan ahhin Pathian cun kan nunah mangbangza a tuah ding a si (zoh aw, Jer. 31:2–4, 9, 11, 12). Amai lamzin dik ah in hruai ding, cuticun kan bah nawn lo ding ih, daihnak kan nei ding. Jesuh cu thil ziangkim ih zohthim ding mi famkim a si. A zumnak neihnak cun caan neta ih Amai minung kan sinak a nemhnget ding a si.
