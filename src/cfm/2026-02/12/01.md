### Tuizarh zirlai hrang siar ding
Matt. 28:18–20, 2 Pet. 3:18, 1 Pet. 3:8–15, Hosea 7, Zekhariah 10.

> <p>Bible cangken</p>
> “ ‘A caumi hnenah a tikcute ih thusimdan ka theih theinak dingah Bawipa Pathian in mithiam ih lei i pe zo: anih in zing tin i kai tho, mithiam vekih thu thei dingin ka hna a thangharhter’ “ (Isaiah 50:4).

Pastor G. hrang ahcun Sabbath zinglam hi a buaizet ni a si. Zingpit in a tho ih, Sabbath sikul le sermon ding a timlam rero. Cule tlailam ah thuthangtha simnak a nei sal lala ding. A tawh a la ih, inn ihsin a suak ih, a feh lan hngal.

Zarhte ni zinglam ah minung tamzet leng an suah ruangah zamrang zetih a feh thei lo ih, biakinn khal a thlen tlai phah. Khuitawk lamah saw an feh ciamco ding? Khuitawk ihsin a ra tihman theih lomi motor cu a hmaiah a ra ding. Motor a dingter vukvi ih, a kut pharh phah in motor mawngtu cu a hro ciamco.

A netnak ahcun Pastor G cu biakinn a thleng. Sabbath sikul zirlai zirh dingin a hung ding, a zirh dingmi pawl a vun zoh kual ih, hmel theihfiang mi pakhat a hmu: Minute 20 liamzo ih motor mawngtu a hro ciamco mi kha a si.

Cumi hnuah kawhhran member pakhat in Pastor hnenah motor mawngtu cu Adventist a silo timi a simfiang tikah, Pastor G cun mi zovek an va si khalah duhdawtnak langter ding a si timi a theisuak. Cumi cu Pathian thawn laicin awknak ih um ringringnak ihsin a luangsuak mi a si. A hleice in zumtu na si vekin na cangvaihnak in ziangtluk in midang a hngong thei ti na thei lo.
