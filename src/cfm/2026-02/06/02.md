---
title:  Rinum Daniel
date:   03/05/2026
---

Daniel cu Bible sungih pacang tha ropi zet lakih pakhat a si. A thuanthu ihsin kan thei (zoh aw, Daniel 1): “Daniel cun siangpahrang ih rawl le a in mi sabitti thawn borhbahawter lo dingin a thinlung sungah tumtahnak a nei” (Daniel 1:8), Cule Daniel le a rualpi pathum pawl hnenah, “Pathian in zirnak le fimnak tinkim theihnak le thiamnak a pek hai: cun Daniel cun langnak le mang hmuahhmuah ah theihnak a nei” (Dan. 1:17). Bible cun Daniel cu mifim tiah a phuang (Dan. 1:20; Dan. 2:14, 21, 23, 48), ziangahtile Pathian Thlarau cu amah ah a um a si (Dan. 4:9, 18; Dan. 5:14; Dan. 6:3), cule vanlam in nasa zetih an duhdawt mi a si (Dan. 9:23, Dan. 10:11). Cuvek pawl cun Pathian thawn pehzom aw ringring tu mi pakhat relnak a si.

Daniel 2 ahkhan Siangpahrang Nebuchadnezzar cun Babilon ih um mifim pawl hmuahhmuah hrang thihnak thusuah a tuah, Daniel cun Pathian hnenah Nebuchadnezzar ih um thuthup thawn pehpar aw in Pathian zangfahnak a rak dil (Dan. 2:18). Pathian in Daniel hnenah siangpahrang ih mang a langter thluh tikah Daniel cu zamrang zetin thlacamnak a nei hngal.

`Siar aw: Daniel 2:20-23. Ziangah Daniel thla a cam, cule Himi thlacamnak ihsin ziangsaw kan zir thei ding?`

Kum a hung herliam vivo ih, siangpahrang pawl an tlu ih, an tho ve vivo, Daniel cu siangpahrang pawl ruahnak petu a si ringring lai ih, mi ropi zet vekih rel a si, “a sung ih thlarau tha hleice a um ruangah siangpahrang in anih cu ram pumpi tlun ah ret a tum” (Dan. 6:3, NKJV). “Mi rinum zet a si ih; amah ah palhnak le mawhnak zianghman hmuh a si lo” (Dan. 6:4, NKJV). A rualpi pawl in a parah iksiknak le khawkhannak sual ral zet nei khal sehla (Dan. 6:5–9), Daniel cun a thlacam nun ah tihnak zianghman a nei lo ih, rinum zetin a um ringring.

`Siar ding: Daniel 6:10, 11. Himi bungcang pawl in Daniel thuhla ziangsaw in sim?`

Harsatnak a ton caan ah Daniel cun thlacamnak a nei. Cui harsatnak cun a nun dokalh khal sehla, thlacamnak ah hnget zetin a um (Nikhat ah veithum thlacamnak neih hi a ti theudan kel a si), cule a relcia vekin thlacamnak a nei (Jerusalem lam hoi in a sangka ong in nikhat ah veithum thlacamnak a nei). A thlacam ah taksa cangvaihnak (A khuk a bil) a tel ih, lungawi thu simnak le bom dilnak lam a hmuitin theu.

`Hivek thuanthu eng ahhin thlacamnak na neih lonak san ih na puhmawh mi kha ziangtluk thil tha lo saw a si?`
