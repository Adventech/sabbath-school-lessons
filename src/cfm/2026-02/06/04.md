---
title:  Enok cu Pathian thawn an lengtlan, an bia aw
date:   05/05/2026
---

`Siar ding: Semtirnak 5:22-24. Enok ih thuhla hi ziang ciah saw kan theih mi?`

Bible cun Enok ih thuhla hi a rel tam vak lo, asinan Pathian in van ih a lak so hlan ah kum 300 sung rori Pathian thawn an lentlan thu in sim. Miin Pathian hnen ih pumpek awknak hnget zet an neih ahcun cumi cu an nun relfiang tu a si timi hi a va mawi ve!

Kan theih mi thil pakhat cu Enok kha “ cat banglo ih thlacam ringring” (Rom. 12:12, NKJV) theutu a si ko ding, nitin nun ih a hmuhton mi pawl ah zumnak thawngin Pathian naihnak lamah a thangso vivo. Amai san ahcun leilung cu sualnak a pung vivo, Enok cu Pathian riantuan in a buai zet. Asinan himi khal hi Amah Pathian ih a um ringring lo ahcun a ti thei ve ce lo.

“Hnatuan buai zet ih a hringnun a um lai khalah Enok cun Pathian thawn an pehzom awknak cu a kilkhawi tha zet. A hnatuan a tam deuhdeuh ih, a nasat deuhdeuh tikah a thlacamnak khal nasa sinsin in a pehzom vivo…Mipi lakah caan tawkfang zet a um ih, an hrangih zohthim ding le zirding tam zet a tanta hnuah Pathian lawngih a pek thei mi Pathian theihnak lam thuk sinsin ih a neih theinak dingah amah te lawng ih um dingin a feh suak. Pathian thawn pehzom awknak an neih tikah Enok cu Pathian hmuihmel bangnak ah a thang vivo…Pathian ngaihsak lo pawl khalin a hmelah vanlam hmel engmawi zet an hmu a si”—Ellen G. White, Patriarchs and Prophets, pp. 86, 87.

Pathian cun leitlun ih hman theih lo dingin leilung ihsin a hrante ih kan um theinak dingah sakhuanak ruangih mahte lawng a hran ih um, asilole phungki pawl vekih um dingah in fial lo. Enok vek ciah in kan kiangkap ih um pawl ih tulsam mi kan bawm thei ih, kan theihsak thei, asinan Pathian thawn laicin awknak tha zet neiin Amah ih kan um ringring a si ahcun kanmah hmangin A ziaza mangbangza kha kan langter thei ding a si.

Ziangtik lai khalah, khuitawk hmun khalah thlacamnak kan nei thei ko. Leitlun ahhin Pathian ih in hmuh thei lonak le in theihthei lonak hmun a um lo (Saam. 139:7–12); Khuitawk ih kan um khalah kan thinlung ih auaw a thei ringring a si (Siar aw, Tahhla. 3:55–57). Asinan thinlung ih thlacam men ai hnakin ringzet ih thlacamnak neih a tha tiih rel mi khal a um. Daiziar ten thlacamnak kan neih tikah kan ruahnak lole kan reltum mi catluan pawl kan theihsuak thei lo dingih, kan ruahnak hmuihngil vivo ding a harsat men thei. Asinan aw suak ih thlacamnak kan neih tikah, cui thil cun Pathian a um ngaingai ih, kan thlacamnak in ngai sak ringring a si timi in theihter, cule thil thupi khal Amah thawn kan ruah aw thei timi in theihter a si.

`Tuini ih tikcu caan na hman tikah Jesuh thawn pawlkom awknak nei dingin khuitawk ah asilole ziangtin saw aw suah ih thlacamnak na neih?`
