---
title:  Moses, Hruaitu Pathian mi
date:   06/05/2026
---

Enok cu Pathian thawn laicinawknak nai zet nei mi a si timi fiang zet khal sehla, Moses le Pathian ih laicin awknak cu tamzet theih ding a um. Amah Pathian thawn an biakawknak pawl khal Moses ih thuanthu ah tamzet siar ding a um. Hi hruaitu nunnem zet Moses thawn kan khualtlawnnak sukso ahhin Pathian mi hman hruaitu a si vekin a nun ih thil thupi bik le a hlawhtlinnak thuthup cu Pathian thawn laicin awknak ih a um ringring ruangah le pehtlaih awknak a neih ringring ruangah a si timi kan hmu rero thei.

`Siar ding: Suahhlannak 33:15-23. Bawipa le Moses karlak biaknak dan cu ziang veksaw a si?`

Pathian thawn biakawk ih, A aw fiangzet ih theih hi ziangvek a si ding timi vun ruat aw. Israel fate pawl in Moses hnenah Pathian ai-awih in an hnenah thu sim sawn dingin an ngen ih, anmah rori in Pathian thawn pehtlaih awknak an hawl lo cu mangbangza thil a si (Suah. 20:18-21). Asinan Pathian cun Moses cu himi hrangah a rak timlam cia thluh zo, himi tlang rori ahhin hrambur alh kiangih an ton awknak ihsin a rak thok zo. Moses ih bulpak thlacamnak pawl kan siar khal ahhin anih cu Pathian hmaiah a um ringring ih, a kaihhruaimi mipi pawl hrangah kaihhruainak le thlacam dilnak a neihsak ringring theu.

**Moses cun an innsang pawl hrangah veihnih rori thlacamnak a neih sak. Cui dinhmun ah ziangthil a thleng, rak bawm lo bangta sehlah ziangthil saw thleng ding?**

`Aaron: Suah 32:1–14, 31–34, Daan. 9:20`

`Miriam: Mipum. 12:13`

Miriam thawn an pehtlaih awknak cu mangbangza thil a si. Miriam cun Moses cu a hmuhsuam ih, a iksik. Moses cu dung tawlh in, Aaron le Miriam ih an phutawk hremnak cu Pathian in a pe thei a si. Cumi cu ngai ngam loin, Moses cun zamrang zetin a ngaidam hngal, cule a unau nu a dam theinak dingah thlacamnak a neihsak. Hitawk ih Moses ih cangvaihnak ahhin misual pawl hrangih Pathian ih zangfah lainatnak rak zohter tu huham nasa zet hmuh theih in a um.

`Siar ding: Matthai 5: 44 le Kolose 3:13. Hitawk ih simmi tuah dingah ziangtin na zir thei ding? Ziangruangah na tuah a thupi?`
