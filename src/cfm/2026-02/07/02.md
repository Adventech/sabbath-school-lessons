---
title:  Elijah – Harsat lai ih Thlacam
date:   10/05/2026
---

Mi rinum zet Elijah cu san siava ah a nung, Siangpahrang Ahab cun thil tam zet a tuah ih, “amai’ hlanih Israel siangpahrang pawl hmuahhmuah hnakin Israel ih Bawipa Pathian cu thinhengter dingin a tuah tam sawn” (1 Siang 16:33). A nun ih thil danglam zet a thleng mi cu Karmel tlang ahkhan a si (zoh aw, 1 Siang 18). Himi lunglut za thuanthu ahhin Ahab le a lalram cun an mit rori in Pathian cun thlacamnak a let timi thudik an hmu. Himi cu Israel thuanthu ih hngilh theih lo mi a si, himi thuanthu ih thil danglam vukvi mi cun mangbangza in in tuah hai.

`Siar ding: 1 Siang 19:1-18, a hleice in Elijah ih thlacamnak le Pathian thawn an pawl awknak kha hminsin aw. Hitawk ahhin Elijah ih thinlung riahsiatnak kha ziangsaw a si? Pathian ih sawnletnak cu Karmel Tlang ih a cang mi thawn ziangtin a danglam?`

Nikhat pum Pathian in Elijah ih thlacamnak pakhat hman hrelh lo in let thluh khal sehla, profet ih thinlung ruahnak le taksa dinhmun cu a thleng aw lohli. Cui ni ah Elijah cun Pathian thawn nehnak ropi zet tonnun nei khal sehla, a cau zetih thih a tih ruangah Pathian a zumnak khal a hloh thluh. Himi thuanthu ih mangbangza thil um mi cu Elijah kha beidong le anhai in um khal sehla, mi zangfah thiam le lainatnak neitu Pathian cu a hnen ah a ra ih tidai le in ding a ra pe kirsal (1 Siang 19:5, 6)—cutin ni 40 le zan 40 sung a awh (1 Siang 19:8). A netnak ah Pathian a phuang aw ih, Elijah cun a hlan ih a rak neih mi ai hnakin danglam zetin zumnak a nei.

Khuimaw caan ahcun kan nun ahhin Pathian cun huham nei zet, fiang zet le hrial theih riai lo lamzin in tla in let. Himi cun kan zumnak a cakter ih, kan nun ah Pathian thawn nai aw zet vekin kan thei a si.

Zumnak hnget zet thawn Pathian thlun cu thil harsa lutuk tiah kan ruat ih, cutin thlemnak sungah kan barh lut aw theu. Kanmai duhdan in Pathian ih in let ding ruahsannak thawn kan hngak, asinan kan ruahnak ai hnakin A ruahnak le A lamzin pawl cu an sang ih an fim sawn timi tla kan thei lo theu (Isa. 55:8, 9). Pathian ih sersiammi pawl hin kan theih thiam thei lo mi thil tampi a um vek ciah in, Pathian ih lamzin dangdang khalah kan theih thiam thei lomi tampi a um hi mangbangza tin kan ruat ding a si cuang lo.

`Pathian, thatnak le hngilnei zet Pa cun kan tulmi ngaingai hi a thei a si. Harsatnak na ton caan ah ziangtin Pathian hnen ah na thinlung na pe thei ding? Tu rori ah Pathian hnen ah thusim aw.`
